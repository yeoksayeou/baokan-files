Title: The Battle Song of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution – The Shanghai Spring (Pictures)
Author: Dong Lianbao
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 6

    The Battle Song of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution – The Shanghai Spring
    The large-scale performance 《Forward, Glorious Shanghai Workers!》 vividly demonstrates the heroic spirit of Shanghai workers relying on their own efforts, striving for self-improvement, and vowing to reach the peak of world science and technology.
    The Shanghai Gaoqiao Chemical Plant presents the female vocal performance 《Quotations from Chairman Mao Shine with Golden Light》
    Pingtan 《Farewell to the God of Plague》 Seven-character律诗 two poems
    The Shanghai Workers' Amateur Art Troupe performs 《The Painter》
    Workers from the Shanghai Refinery sing their own battle song 《Song of the Refinery Workers》
    《Three Electric Wires Extend to the Countryside》
    The Shanghai County Hongqiao Commune female vocal performance 《Planting Vegetables While Thinking of Those Who Eat Them》
    Actors participating in the "Shanghai Spring" perform a large medley for soldiers of the East Sea Fleet 《Singing of Wang Jie, Learning from Wang Jie》
    《Chairman Mao's Quotations are Distributed to Us》        Dong Lianbao’s Sketch from Haitong Nonferrous Alloy Factory
    The song performance 《Song of the Eagle》 by the singing group of a certain Air Force unit stationed in Shanghai
    A Shanghai Conservatory of Music detachment performs 《Devoting All Our Red Hearts to the People》 for the workers
      Photos and sketches on this page by reporters from the People's Liberation Army Daily, reporters from Wenhui Daily, reporters from Liberation Daily, and reporters from Xinhua News Agency
