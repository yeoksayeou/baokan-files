Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Visits Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-06-11
Edition: 5

    New China News Agency, June 9th—Invited by the Japan Youth Organizations Council, the Chinese Youth Delegation of seven members, led by Wen Chi, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, has left Beijing for a visit to Japan.
    Yang Zhenya, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, and others saw them off at the airport.



**Named Entities**


中华全国青年联合会    All-China Youth Federation
文迟    Wen Chi
杨振亚    Yang Zhenya
日本青年团协会    Japan Youth Organizations Council
北京   Beijing
新华社   Xinhua News Agency

### 中国青年代表团去日本访问

1966-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    中国青年代表团去日本访问
    新华社九日讯　应日本青年团协会邀请，由中华全国青年联合会副秘书长文迟率领的中国青年代表团一行七人，已离开北京前往日本访问。
    到机场送行的有中华全国青年联合会副秘书长杨振亚等。
