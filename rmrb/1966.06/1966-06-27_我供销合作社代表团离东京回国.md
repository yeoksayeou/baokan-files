Title: Chinese Supply and Marketing Cooperative Delegation Departs Tokyo for Home
Author:
Date: 1966-06-27
Edition: 5

The Chinese Supply and Marketing Cooperative Delegation, led by its head and Vice Chairman of the Chinese National Supply and Marketing Cooperative Council, Liang Yao, concluded its friendly visit to Japan. On the 22nd, the delegation boarded a plane in Tokyo and returned to China.

On the evening of the 21st, the delegation held a farewell banquet to express gratitude to Japanese friends for their warm welcome and friendly reception. Attendees included the Chairman of the Japan Consumer Cooperative Union, Ishiguro Takejyu, the Chairman of the National Federation of Agricultural Cooperatives, Mitsuhashi Sei, and representatives from various sectors.

Vice Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Kurosaki Toshio, Diet member of the Socialist Party, Suikei Shiro, and Chairman of the Tokyo Chinese Association, Gan Wenfang, also attended the banquet.

### 我供销合作社代表团离东京回国

1966-06-27
第5版()
专栏：

    我供销合作社代表团离东京回国
    据新华社东京电　中国供销合作社代表团在团长、中华全国供销合作总社理事会副主任梁耀率领下，结束了对日本的友好访问，二十二日乘飞机离开东京回国。
    二十一日晚上，代表团举行了告别酒会，答谢日本朋友对代表团的热烈欢迎和友好接待。出席酒会的有日本生活协同组合联合会会长石黑武重、全国购买农业合作社联合会会长三桥诚和各界代表。
    日中友好协会副会长黑田寿男、社会党众议院议员穗积七郎、东京华侨总会会长甘文芳等也出席了酒会。
