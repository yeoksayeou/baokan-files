Title: Never Forget Class Struggle
Author: Hu Lühan
Date: 1966-06-11
Edition: 6

     Never Forget Class Struggle
　　 Red Star Brigade Member of Luofang Commune, Xinyu County, Jiangxi Province  Hu Lühan
    Knife against knife, gun against gun,
    Never forget class struggle.
    Enemies with guns have been overthrown,
    Enemies without guns are still rampant.
    Demons and monsters dare to oppose the Party,
    Grasp them firmly and never let go!
    Knife against knife, gun against gun,
    Never forget class struggle.
    Arm ourselves with Mao Zedong Thought,
    Become a vanguard in the Cultural Revolution.
    Overthrow that black line and black gang,
    We will not leave the battlefield until complete victory.

罗坊公社  Luofang Commune

江西省  Jiangxi Province
毛泽东思想 Mao Zedong Thought
文化革命 Cultural Revolution
黑线 black line

### 阶级斗争永不忘
胡履汉
1966-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

     阶级斗争永不忘
　　 江西新余县罗坊公社  红星大队社员   胡履汉
    刀对刀，枪对枪，
    阶级斗争永不忘。
    拿枪的敌人已打倒，
    不拿枪的敌人还猖狂。
    牛鬼蛇神敢反党，
    狠狠抓着他决不放！
    刀对刀，枪对枪，
    阶级斗争永不忘。
    毛泽东思想来武装，
    做个文化革命的闯将。
    斗垮那黑线和黑帮，
    不获全胜不下战场。
