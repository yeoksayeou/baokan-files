Title: Albanian "People's Voice" Condemns New Soviet-U.S. Deal; People of All Countries See Clearly the Traitorous Face of the Soviet Leadership; The "International Treaty" on the So-called Peaceful Use of the Moon Hides the Conspiracy of Soviet-U.S. Cooperation to Dominate the World
Author:
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 11th. The Albanian newspaper *People's Voice* published a commentary on the 11th, exposing a new conspiracy of Soviet-U.S. collusion and condemning the Soviet government's recent proposal to conclude an "international treaty" with the United States on the so-called peaceful use of the moon and other celestial bodies.

    The commentary said that many facts increasingly show that the Soviet revisionist leaders are going further and further down the road of betraying the interests of the people of all countries, and they are stepping up their rapprochement and collusion with U.S. imperialism. Not long ago, the Soviet Union's Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Fedorenko, delivered a letter from Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko to UN Secretary-General U Thant, suggesting that the UN General Assembly discuss the issue of the so-called peaceful use of the moon and other celestial bodies and conclude relevant international agreements.

    The commentary said that the purpose of this proposal is to further develop the multifaceted cooperation between the Khrushchev revisionists and the U.S. imperialists, and to strengthen their ties in accordance with the policy of Soviet-U.S. great power hegemony over the world.

    The commentary said that just as U.S. imperialism is frantically and barbarously invading Vietnam, expanding aggression around the world, and attempting to suppress all national liberation movements of the people of all countries, the Soviet revisionist leaders continue to demonstrate with their actions that they are friends and allies of U.S. imperialism, and continue to implement the policy of the 23rd Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union of expanding Soviet-U.S. cooperation and bringing important international issues into the orbit of Soviet-U.S. cooperation. After openly betraying Marxism-Leninism and the interests of the people of all countries, the Soviet revisionist leaders, in order to carry out deceptive propaganda and reach new deals with U.S. imperialism, are attempting to use the latest achievements in the fields of science and technology to also bring the issue of exploring outer space into the notorious orbit of Soviet-U.S. cooperation.

    The commentary said: While U.S. imperialists are talking about "peaceful use" of the moon and other celestial bodies, they are using artificial spy satellites to spy on and survey the territories of other countries, seriously violating the sovereignty of these countries.

    The commentary said that the people of all countries are well aware of the aggressive nature of U.S. imperialism and the traitorous face of modern revisionists. Therefore, they will not fail to understand that behind this "new peace initiative" lies a conspiracy by the two great powers of the Soviet Union and the United States to dominate the world and plot against the people of all countries.

    The commentary sternly pointed out that today, the aggressive policies and actions of U.S. imperialism seriously threaten international peace and security. The Soviet revisionists are uneasy about the establishment of military bases on the moon and other celestial bodies, but they are not only not uneasy about the United States establishing a series of military bases around socialist countries that threaten their security and sovereignty, but instead cooperate with U.S. imperialism. This once again proves that their betrayal has no limit. The Soviet revisionists are strengthening their sinful cooperation with the U.S. imperialists, but the people of all countries in the world will use their tit-for-tat and resolute struggle to further isolate them and achieve new victories in the struggle.

### 阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》谴责苏美新交易  各国人民看清了苏联领导的叛徒嘴脸  所谓和平利用月球的“国际条约”背后隐藏着苏美合作主宰世界阴谋

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》谴责苏美新交易
    各国人民看清了苏联领导的叛徒嘴脸
    所谓和平利用月球的“国际条约”背后隐藏着苏美合作主宰世界阴谋
    新华社地拉那十一日电　阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》十一日发表评论，揭露苏美勾结的新阴谋，谴责苏联政府最近建议同美国缔结一个所谓和平利用月球和其他天体的“国际条约”。
    评论说，许多事实越来越清楚地表明，苏联修正主义领导人在背叛各国人民利益的道路上越走越远，他们正在同美帝国主义加紧靠拢和勾结。不久前，苏联驻联合国常任代表费德林向联合国秘书长吴丹递交了一封苏联外交部长葛罗米柯的信，建议联合国大会讨论所谓和平利用月球和其他天体的问题，并缔结有关的国际协议。
    评论说，这一建议的目的是为了进一步发展赫鲁晓夫修正主义者和美帝国主义者之间的多方面的合作，加强他们根据苏美两大国称霸世界的政策，而进行的联系。
    评论说，正当美帝国主义疯狂和野蛮地侵略越南，在世界各地扩大侵略，并企图镇压各国人民的一切民族解放运动的时候，苏联修正主义领导人继续以自己的行动表明了他们是美帝国主义的朋友和同盟者，继续执行苏联共产党第二十三次代表大会的扩大苏美合作和把重要国际问题纳入苏美合作轨道的方针。苏联修正主义领导人在公开背叛了马克思列宁主义和各国人民利益以后，为了进行蛊惑宣传和同美帝国主义达成新的交易，企图利用科学和技术领域中的最近成果，把探测宇宙空间的问题也纳入臭名远扬的苏美合作轨道。
    评论说：美帝国主义者在谈论“和平利用”月球和其他天体的同时，通过人造间谍卫星，窥测和侦察其它国家的领土，严重地侵犯了这些国家的主权。
    评论说，各国人民十分了解美帝国主义的侵略本性和现代修正主义者的叛徒嘴脸。因此，他们不会不理解到，在这一“新的和平倡议”的背后，掩藏着苏美两大国企图主宰世界而策划的反对各国人民的阴谋。
    评论最后严正指出，今天，美帝国主义的侵略政策和行动严重地威胁着国际和平与安全。苏联修正主义者对在月球和其他天体上建立军事基地感到不安，但是他们对美国在社会主义国家周围建立一系列威胁着它们的安全和主权的军事基地却不但不感到不安，反而同美帝国主义进行合作。这就再一次证明了他们的背叛是没有止境的。苏联修正主义者正在加强同美帝国主义者的罪恶合作，但是世界各国人民将以自己针锋相对的坚决斗争，使他们进一步孤立起来，并且在斗争中取得新的胜利。
