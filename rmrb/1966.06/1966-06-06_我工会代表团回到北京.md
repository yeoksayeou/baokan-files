Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Chinese trade union delegation led by Kang Yonghe, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, returned to Beijing by plane on the 3rd, after attending the 32nd Executive Committee meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions held in Cyprus.

### 我工会代表团回到北京

1966-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团回到北京
    新华社四日讯　由中华全国总工会副主席康永和率领的中国工会代表团，参加了在塞浦路斯举行的世界工会联合会第三十二次执行委员会会议后，三日乘飞机回到北京。
