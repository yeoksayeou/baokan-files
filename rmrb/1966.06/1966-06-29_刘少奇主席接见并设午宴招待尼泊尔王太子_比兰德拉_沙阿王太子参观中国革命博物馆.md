Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receives and Hosts a Luncheon for the Nepalese Crown Prince
Author:
Date: 1966-06-29
Edition: 1

Chairman Liu Shaoqi received and hosted a luncheon for the Nepalese Crown Prince

    Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah today at noon and had a friendly conversation with him.

    Also present for the reception were Prince Himalaia Shah and his wife Princess Prinsip Shah, who accompanied the Crown Prince on his visit, as well as other accompanying personnel.

    Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qie, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, and others.

    Nepalese Ambassador to China Rendi Su Ba and his wife were also present.

    After the reception, Chairman Liu hosted a luncheon for the distinguished guests.

    The Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah, along with Prince Himalaia Shah and his wife Princess Prinsip Shah, and other Nepalese dignitaries, visited the Chinese Revolutionary Museum this afternoon, accompanied by Vice Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Commission Shi Huai Bi. In the morning, the dignitaries visited the Beijing Arts and Crafts Factory and the Wangfujing Department Store. The dignitaries were warmly welcomed wherever they went.

    June 28, Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah. The photo shows a group picture taken during the reception. In the front row, fifth from the left is Crown Prince Birendra Shah, and seventh from the left is Prince Himalaia Shah. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong.

### 刘少奇主席接见并设午宴招待尼泊尔王太子  比兰德拉·沙阿王太子参观中国革命博物馆

1966-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席接见并设午宴招待尼泊尔王太子
    比兰德拉·沙阿王太子参观中国革命博物馆
    新华社二十八日讯　刘少奇主席今天中午接见尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿，同他进行了友好的谈话。
    同时被接见的，还有随同王太子来访的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主，以及其他随行人员。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅和夫人张茜，外交部副部长韩念龙等。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴和夫人也在座。
    接见后，刘主席设午宴招待贵宾。
    新华社二十八日讯　尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿，以及随行的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主等尼泊尔贵宾，今天下午由对外文委副主任史怀璧陪同，参观了中国革命博物馆。上午，贵宾们参观了北京工艺美术工厂和王府井百货商店。贵宾们所到之处，受到热烈欢迎。（附图片）
    六月二十八日，刘少奇主席接见尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿。图为接见时合影。前排左五是比兰德拉·沙阿王太子，前排左七是喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王。　新华社记者　刘长忠摄
