Title: Two Cambodian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-20
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, June 18th -- Lieutenant Colonel Bok Sam An, Director of the Technical Bureau of the Cambodian Sports Bureau and Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the Cambodian National Organizing Committee for the First Asian Games for Emerging Forces, and another person, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight at our invitation for a friendly visit to China.
    Guo Lei, Deputy Director of the International Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, He Zhenliang, Secretary General and Treasurer of the Asian Committee for Emerging Forces Games, and others went to the airport to warmly welcome them.
    Diplomatic officials from the Cambodian Embassy in China also went to the airport to greet them.

### 柬埔寨两位客人到京

1966-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨两位客人到京
    新华社十八日讯　应邀来我国进行友好访问的柬埔寨体育局技术局长、第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会柬埔寨全国组织委员会执行局主席卜森安中校等二人，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    国家体委国际司副司长郭雷、新兴力量运动会亚洲委员会秘书长兼司库何振梁等前往机场热烈欢迎。
    柬埔寨驻华大使馆外交官员等也到机场迎接。
