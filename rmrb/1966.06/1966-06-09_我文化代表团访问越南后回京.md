Title: My Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-06-09
Edition: 5

    Our Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Chinese Cultural Delegation, headed by Li Qitao, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, returned to Beijing by plane today after concluding its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.  Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director, were among those who greeted the delegation at the airport. Hoang Bac, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, was also present to greet them.

### 我文化代表团访问越南后回京

1966-06-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团访问越南后回京
    新华社七日讯　以对外文委副主任李琦涛为首的中国文化代表团一行三人，结束在越南民主共和国的访问后，今天乘飞机回到北京。前往机场迎接的有对外文委主任张奚若、副主任陈忠经等。越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北也前往迎接。
