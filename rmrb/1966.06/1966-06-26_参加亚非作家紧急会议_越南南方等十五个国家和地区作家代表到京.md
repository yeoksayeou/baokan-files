Title: Writers' Representatives from Fifteen Countries and Regions, Including South Vietnam, Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Meeting

Author:

Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 6

　　To Attend the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Meeting
　　Writers' Representatives from Fifteen Countries and Regions, Including South Vietnam, Arrive in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 25th – The delegation of writers from the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, led by head of delegation Chen Tingyun, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening to attend the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Meeting.
　　Welcoming them at the airport were Chinese writers Ba Jin, Jin Jingmai, Cong Shen, Xu Huaizhong, as well as Vietnamese and Laotian writers who had already arrived in Beijing. The Permanent Mission of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front in China and the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China also sent representatives to the airport to greet them.
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 25th – Tonight, fourteen more writers' representatives from Afro-Asian countries and regions arrived in Beijing to attend the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Meeting.
　　These writers' representatives from countries and regions are: Ahmed Gora Ebrahim from Azania (South Africa); Si Mukhahhel from Basutoland; Ndala Sielle from Congo (Brazzaville); Abdou Bakari Boina from the Comoros; Mamadou Gologo, Mamadou Lamine Cisse, Oumar Traore from Mali; Absalom Bahule from Mozambique; Siddhi Charan Shrestha, Govinda Bhatta from Nepal; Mrs. Salimatu Turay, Mrs. Nancy Steele Grant from Sierra Leone; Charles Kauraisa, Wattja Koukuetu from Southwest Africa; Zadat Ricabi, Salamah Obeid from Syria; Necat from Turkey; Elias Rusike, G. Shoanhwe from Zimbabwe, as well as writers' representatives from Jordan and Uganda.
　　Welcoming them at the airport were Chinese writers Ba Jin, Liu Baiyu, Cao Yu, and Lin Yu, as well as some Afro-Asian writers' representatives who had arrived in Beijing earlier.

### 参加亚非作家紧急会议  越南南方等十五个国家和地区作家代表到京

1966-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    参加亚非作家紧急会议
    越南南方等十五个国家和地区作家代表到京
    新华社二十五日讯　前来北京参加亚非作家紧急会议的越南南方民族解放阵线作家代表团，在团长陈庭云率领下，今天晚间乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中国作家巴金、金敬迈、丛深、徐怀中等，以及已经到达北京的越南和老挝作家。越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团和越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆也派代表到机场迎接。
    新华社二十五日讯　今天夜间又有十四个亚非国家和地区的作家代表到达北京，前来参加亚非作家紧急会议。
    这些国家和地区的作家代表是：阿扎尼亚（南非）的艾哈迈德·戈拉·易卜拉欣；巴苏陀兰的西·穆克海赫尔；刚果（布）的恩达拉·西埃勒；科摩罗的阿布杜·巴卡里·布瓦纳；马里的马马杜·戈洛戈，马马杜·拉米纳·西赛，乌马尔·特拉奥雷；莫三鼻给的阿布萨隆·巴胡尔；尼泊尔的西迪·查兰·什雷斯塔，戈温达·巴塔；塞拉勒窝内的萨利玛图·图莱夫人，南西·格兰特夫人；西南非的查尔斯·考赖萨，瓦特贾·考库埃图；叙利亚的扎达特·里卡比，萨拉马赫·奥贝德；土耳其的聂乍德；津巴布韦的伊利亚斯·鲁西克，格·绍安胡，以及约旦和乌干达的作家代表。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国作家巴金、刘白羽、曹禺和林雨等，以及先期到达北京的一些亚非作家代表。
