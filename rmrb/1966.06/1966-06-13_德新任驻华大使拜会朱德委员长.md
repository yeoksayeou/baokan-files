Title: New East German Ambassador to China Pays Courtesy Call on Chairman Zhu De
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 12th -- The new Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Martin Bierbach, paid a courtesy call on Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, this afternoon. Chairman Zhu De had a friendly conversation with him.

### 德新任驻华大使拜会朱德委员长

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    德新任驻华大使拜会朱德委员长
    新华社十二日讯　德意志民主共和国新任驻华大使马丁·比尔巴赫，今天下午拜会全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德。朱德委员长同他进行了友好的谈话。
