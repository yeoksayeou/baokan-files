Title: List of Personnel of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-06-17
Edition: 1

    List of Personnel of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation
    (Xinhua News Agency, 16th dispatch) The list of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation going to Romania for a friendly visit:
    Head of Delegation: Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
    Members:   Zhao Yimin, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
              Qiao Guanhua, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs
              Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Socialist Republic of Romania



**Named Entities**


周恩来	Zhou Enlai
赵毅敏	Zhao Yimin
乔冠华	Qiao Guanhua
曾涌泉	Zeng Yongquan
中国共产党中央委员会	Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
中华人民共和国国务院	State Council of the People's Republic of China
外交部	Ministry of Foreign Affairs
中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚社会主义共和国特命全权大使	Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Socialist Republic of Romania
罗马尼亚	Romania
新华社	Xinhua News Agency
