Title: Indonesian Rightists Intensify Collusion with Chiang Gang Both Openly and Covertly; Chiang Gang Faction "Key Members" Go to Jakarta to "Improve Relations" with Indonesian Rightists
Author:
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 4

    (Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd dispatch from Taipei) Under the instigation of U.S. imperialism, the Indonesian right-wing military regime and the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang have been in frequent contact in recent months, engaging in a series of conspiratorial activities in preparation for establishing "diplomatic" relations.

    According to a June 13th report in Taiwan's *Zhengxin News*, the Chiang gang has dispatched a "central-level key member" secretly to Jakarta to discuss "improving bilateral relations" with the Indonesian right-wing authorities. Before leaving Taipei, this "key member" received "important instructions and considerable authorization" from the Chiang gang and held multiple talks with "Minister of Foreign Affairs" Wei Daoming and "Director of the Department of East Asian and Pacific Affairs" Liu Zonghan.

    According to reports from Taipei's *China Post* and Associated Press dispatches from Taipei, Indonesian Foreign Minister Adam Malik, through Philippine diplomatic officials, informed the Chiang gang of his hope that the Chiang gang would send "senior officials to Jakarta" to help the Indonesian right-wing authorities carry out anti-China and anti-Chinese activities, and eliminate so-called "Chinese Communist Party elements" among Indonesian overseas Chinese. Malik also expressed to the Chiang gang through this diplomatic official that Indonesia is anti-communist and very friendly to the Chiang gang.

### 印尼右派同蒋帮明来暗去加紧勾搭  蒋帮派“要员”去雅加达同印尼右派“改善关系”

1966-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼右派同蒋帮明来暗去加紧勾搭
    蒋帮派“要员”去雅加达同印尼右派“改善关系”
    据新华社二十二日讯　台北消息：在美帝国主义指使下，印度尼西亚右派军人政权同蒋介石匪帮，最近几个月来接触频繁，为准备建立“外交”关系进行了一系列阴谋活动。
    据台湾《征信新闻报》六月十三日报道，蒋帮已派遣一名“中央级要员”秘密去雅加达，与印度尼西亚右派当局商讨“改善双方关系”。这个“要员”离开台北前，曾得到蒋帮的“重要指示与相当的授权”，并与“外交部长”魏道明和“亚东太平洋司司长”刘宗翰进行了多次会谈。
    据台北市《中国邮报》和美联社从台北发出的消息透露，印度尼西亚外交部长阿达姆·马利克，通过菲律宾的外交官员转告蒋帮，希望蒋帮派遣“高级官员去雅加达”，帮助印度尼西亚右派当局搞反华排华活动，消灭印度尼西亚华侨中的所谓“中国共产党分子”。马利克还通过这个外交官员向蒋帮表示，印度尼西亚是反共的，对蒋帮十分友好。
