Title: Down with the Counter-Revolutionary Revisionists
Author:
Date: 1966-06-05
Edition: 2

    Down with the Counter-Revolutionary Revisionists
    Comrades Nie Yuanzi and others from Peking University: We firmly support your revolutionary actions, learn from your revolutionary spirit of daring to struggle, and vow to fight alongside you to thoroughly overthrow all Khrushchev-style counter-revolutionary revisionists, and smash the reactionary fortress of the "Three-Family Village" black gang. The broad masses of workers, peasants, soldiers, and revolutionary intellectuals unite, pledge to defend the Party Central Committee, defend Mao Zedong Thought, and defend the dictatorship of the proletariat. The great socialist cultural revolution must be carried through to the end; we will never stop until we achieve complete victory.
                      Shanghai Jiaotong University 150 Teaching and Research Section

### 打倒反革命修正主义分子

1966-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

    打倒反革命修正主义分子
    北大聂元梓等同学：我们坚决支持你们的革命行动，学习你们敢于斗争的革命精神，誓与你们并肩作战，彻底打倒一切赫鲁晓夫式反革命修正主义分子，粉碎“三家村”黑帮的反动堡垒。广大工农兵和革命知识分子团结起来，誓死保卫党中央，保卫毛泽东思想，保卫无产阶级专政。伟大的社会主义文化大革命一定要进行到底，不获全胜，决不收兵。
                      上海交通大学一五○教研室
