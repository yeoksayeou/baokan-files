Title: Helpless Confession

Author:

Date: 1966-06-25
Edition: 5

    A Helpless Confession
    "U.S. News & World Report" wrote that American intelligence sources believe that since the United States carried out its first bombing sixteen months ago, "North Vietnam" has almost made itself into a country that no bomb can destroy.
    Here, U.S. imperialism admits two important facts: 1. The U.S. attempt to use bombing to force the Vietnamese people to submit has gone bankrupt; 2. The people of North Vietnam are becoming stronger and stronger under the frenzied bombing of the American bandit planes.

### 无可奈何的供状

1966-06-25
第5版()
专栏：三言两语

    无可奈何的供状
    《美国新闻与世界报道》写道，美国情报人士认为：自从美国进行第一次轰炸的十六个月来，“北越”差不多已经使它成为什么炸弹都炸不垮的国家。
    在这里，美帝国主义承认了两个重要的事实：一、美国企图用轰炸来迫使越南人民屈服的战争讹诈政策破产了；二、越南北方的人民在美国强盗飞机的狂轰滥炸中越来越坚强。
