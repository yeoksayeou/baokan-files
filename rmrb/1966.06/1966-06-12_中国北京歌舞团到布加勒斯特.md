Title: Chinese Beijing Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Bucharest
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 10th - The Chinese Beijing Song and Dance Troupe, led by Wang Qian, arrived in Bucharest by train on the 11th. The troupe is visiting Romania for a friendly performance tour at the invitation of the Romanian State Committee for Culture and Art. Floria, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Romanian State Committee for Culture and Art, G. Daya, Manager of the Performance Company, heads of literary and artistic groups in Bucharest, and Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, welcomed the troupe at the station.
    Representatives of literary and art workers in the Romanian capital presented flowers to the members of the song and dance troupe.

### 中国北京歌舞团到布加勒斯特

1966-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    中国北京歌舞团到布加勒斯特
    新华社布加勒斯特十日电　由王前率领的中国北京歌舞团十一日乘火车到达布加勒斯特。歌舞团是应邀来罗马尼亚进行友好访问演出的。罗马尼亚国家文化和艺术委员会对外司司长弗洛里亚、演出公司经理格·达亚、布加勒斯特的文艺团体的负责人，以及中国驻罗马尼亚大使曾涌泉到车站欢迎。
    罗马尼亚首都文艺工作者代表向歌舞团团员们献了鲜花。
