Title: Vietnamese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 4

    Vietnamese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. A four-member Vietnamese Cultural Delegation, invited to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane and train respectively. The delegation was led by Phạm Ngọc Thuần, Director of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Those who went to the airport and railway station to welcome the delegation included Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director; Sun Xiaocun, Vice President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association; and Yan Baizhen, Deputy Director of the Foreign Languages Publishing and Distribution Administration.
    Hoàng Bắc, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also went to greet them.

### 越南文化代表团到京

1966-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    越南文化代表团到京
    新华社七日讯　应邀前来我国进行友好访问的越南文化代表团一行四人，在团长、越南民主共和国对外文化联络委员会主任范玉纯率领下，分别乘飞机和火车到达北京。
    前往机场、车站欢迎的有对外文委主任张奚若、副主任陈忠经，中越友协副会长孙晓村，外文出版发行事业局副局长阎百真等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北也前往迎接。
