Title: A Telegram from Premier Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1966-06-29
Edition: 1

	The People's Daily reports that on June 28, a telegram from Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Asia-Africa Writers' Emergency Conference reads as follows:

	To the Asia-Africa Writers' Emergency Conference in Beijing:

	On the occasion of the opening of the Asia-Africa Writers' Emergency Conference, I extend my warm congratulations and respect to your conference and to all the progressive writers from Asia and Africa.

	I am confident that your conference will contribute to further enhancing the role of writers in actively supporting the Vietnamese people's struggle against American imperialist aggression, as well as in strengthening the unity and struggle of the Asian and African peoples for anti-imperialist national liberation and social progress. I sincerely wish for the success of the conference.

	Kim Il-sung
	Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
	June 27, 1966, Pyongyang

### 金日成首相的贺电

1966-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成首相的贺电
    新华社二十八日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成致亚非作家紧急会议的贺电，全文如下：北京亚非作家紧急会议：
    值此亚非作家紧急会议开幕之际，我谨向你们的会议和所有来自亚洲和非洲的进步作家致以友好的祝贺和敬意。
    深信你们的会议将对进一步提高作家在积极支持越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争中，以及在加强亚非人民争取反帝民族解放与社会进步的团结和斗争中的作用，作出贡献。衷心地祝会议的工作成功。
              朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相　金日成
                          一九六六年六月二十七日于平壤
