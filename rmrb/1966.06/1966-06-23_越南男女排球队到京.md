Title: Vietnamese Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-23
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd – The Vietnamese Democratic Republic men's and women's volleyball teams, a group of thirty-one, led by Nguyen Ngoc Yin, the vice-principal of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Sports Technology School, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning for a friendly visit to our country.
    Greeting them at the airport were Mou Zuoyun, Deputy Director of the Ball Games Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and others.
    The visiting team will go to Shenyang on the 23rd for a visit.

### 越南男女排球队到京

1966-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    越南男女排球队到京
    新华社二十二日讯　应邀来我国进行友好访问的越南民主共和国男女排球队一行三十一人，在越南民主共和国体育技术学校副校长阮玉寅的率领下今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的，有国家体委球类司副司长牟作云等。
    客队将在二十三日去沈阳访问。
