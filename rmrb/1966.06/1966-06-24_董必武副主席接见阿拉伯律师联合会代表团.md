Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu Receives Delegation from the Arab Lawyers Union
Author:
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd – Vice Chairman Dong Biwu received this morning the delegation from the Arab Lawyers Union, including the head of the delegation, Mustafa Baradai from the United Arab Republic, and members: Faiq Samarraye from Iraq, Adnan Jaser from Lebanon, Sabah Rikabi and Ihsan Kayali from Syria, Ibrahim Shahawi from the United Arab Republic, and Abdullah Najib from Sudan. Vice Chairman Dong Biwu had a friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the meeting were Wang Jiren and Yang Huanan. (Attached picture)
    [Picture Caption:] Group photo during the meeting. Third from the left in the front row is the head of the delegation, Mustafa Baradai. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Jingde.

### 董必武副主席接见阿拉伯律师联合会代表团

1966-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    董必武副主席接见阿拉伯律师联合会代表团
    新华社二十三日讯　董必武副主席今天上午接见了阿拉伯律师联合会代表团团长、阿联的穆斯塔法·巴拉迪，团员：伊拉克的法依克·萨姆拉耶，黎巴嫩的阿德纳·杰斯尔，叙利亚的萨巴赫·里卡比、伊桑·卡雅里，阿联的易卜拉欣·夏哈威，苏丹的阿卜杜拉·纳吉布。董必武副主席同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有王吉仁、杨化南。（附图片）
