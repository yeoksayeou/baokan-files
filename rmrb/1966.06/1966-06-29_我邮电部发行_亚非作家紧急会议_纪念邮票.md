Title: Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Issues "Asia-Africa Writers' Emergency Conference" Commemorative Stamps
Author:
Date: 1966-06-29
Edition: 6

The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications will issue a set of "Asia-Africa Writers' Emergency Conference" commemorative stamps starting today. This set of commemorative stamps consists of two stamps (see the image on the right). The first stamp has a face value of eight fen, and the design features the front half of the "Wind and Thunder" sculpture. The second stamp has a face value of twenty-two fen, and the design features the emblem of the Asia-Africa Writers' Emergency Conference.

### 我邮电部发行“亚非作家紧急会议”纪念邮票

1966-06-29
第6版()
专栏：

    我邮电部发行“亚非作家紧急会议”纪念邮票
    新华社二十七日讯　邮电部从今天起发行一套“亚非作家紧急会议”纪念邮票。这套纪念邮票共二枚（见右图）。第一枚面值八分，图案是“风雷”雕塑的前半部分；第二枚面值二十二分，图案是亚非作家紧急会议证章。（附图片）
