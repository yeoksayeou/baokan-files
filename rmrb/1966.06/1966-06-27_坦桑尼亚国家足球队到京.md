Title: The Tanzanian National Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-27
Edition: 5

The Tanzanian national football team, which is visiting China for the first time for a friendly tour, arrived in Beijing last night. The team is led by A. J. Mwangosi, the vice-chairman of the Tanzanian Football Association and the head of the welfare department of the Tanzanian Ministry of Rural Development and National Culture. They flew into Beijing. Guo Lei, deputy director of the General Administration of Sport of China, and Li Fenglou, vice-chairman of the Chinese Football Association, as well as Chinese football players, went to the airport to welcome them and presented them with flowers. Ali Mwinyikoko, the acting charge d'affaires of the Tanzanian Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 坦桑尼亚国家足球队到京

1966-06-27
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚国家足球队到京
    据新华社二十六日讯　第一次来我国进行友好访问的坦桑尼亚国家足球队，由坦桑尼亚足球协会副主席、坦桑尼亚乡村发展和国民文化部福利事业负责人爱·约·姆旺戈西率领，昨晚乘飞机到达北京。国家体委国际司副司长郭雷和中国足球协会副主席李凤楼，以及我国足球运动员们前往机场迎接，并向客人们献了鲜花。坦桑尼亚驻我国大使馆临时代办阿里·姆维尼可可也前往机场迎接。
