Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives British Chargé d'Affaires in China
Author:
Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 11th—Vice Premier and concurrently Foreign Minister Chen Yi, at the request of British Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. Hopson, received him on the morning of the 9th and held talks with him.

### 陈毅副总理接见英国驻华代办

1966-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见英国驻华代办
    新华社十一日讯　陈毅副总理兼外交部长应英国驻中国代办霍普森的请求，九日上午接见了他，和他谈了话。
