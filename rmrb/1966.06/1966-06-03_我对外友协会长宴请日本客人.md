Title: China-Foreign Friendship Association President Hosts Banquet for Japanese Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-03
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations and Friendship with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet tonight for all members of the Japanese Kyoto Arts and Crafts Artists Delegation led by Asami Ryuzo. Wang Xiaoyun, Liu Kaiqu, Lin Lin, Wang Yuanfang, and others were present. Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Saionji Kinkazu, was also invited to attend.

### 我对外友协会长宴请日本客人

1966-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我对外友协会长宴请日本客人
    新华社二日讯　中国人民对外文化友好协会会长楚图南今晚设宴招待浅见隆三率领的日本京都工艺美术家代表团全体成员。出席的有王晓云、刘开渠、林林、王元方等。在京日本和平人士西园寺公一也应邀出席。
