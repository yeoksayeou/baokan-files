Title: Former Lebanese Minister Othman Dana Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-20
Edition: 6

    Former Lebanese Minister Othman Dana Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, June 19th) Former Lebanese Minister and Member of Parliament Othman Dana and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Those who went to the airport to greet them were Lian Guan, Sha Mengbi, and Wang Yinpu.

### 黎巴嫩前部长欧斯曼·达纳到京

1966-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩前部长欧斯曼·达纳到京
    新华社十九日讯　黎巴嫩前部长、议员欧斯曼·达纳和夫人今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的。前往机场迎接的有连贯、沙梦弼、王荫圃。
