Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Meets Japanese Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-25
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 24th – Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met today with the Japanese Socialist Party Fisheries Delegation led by Yamazaki Noboru. The guests and hosts had a friendly conversation. Present at the meeting were Li Yingshu and Wang Yunxiang, Vice Presidents of the China Fisheries Association, and Committee Member Han Juli, among others. The delegation will leave Beijing tonight to visit other parts of the country. The China Fisheries Association held a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the guests.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社二十四日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天接见了由山崎昇率领的日本社会党渔业代表团，宾主进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有中国渔业协会副会长李英叔、王云祥，委员韩巨礼等人。代表团今天晚上离开北京去外地访问，中国渔业协会今晚举行宴会欢送客人。
