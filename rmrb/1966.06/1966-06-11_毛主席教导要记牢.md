Title: Keep Chairman Mao's Teachings Firmly in Mind
Author: Guo Chonghua
Date: 1966-06-11
Edition: 6

　　Keep Chairman Mao's teachings firmly in mind,
　　The east wind blows strongly, the west wind falls,
　　No matter how fierce the storms arise,
　　We stand firm with heroic spirit.
　　Keep Chairman Mao's teachings firmly in mind,
　　The enemy sharpens his knife, we sharpen ours,
　　We counterattack tit-for-tat, without fear,
　　And eliminate demons and monsters with one shot.
　　Keep Chairman Mao's teachings firmly in mind,
　　Charging forward to eradicate poisonous weeds,
　　Let the flames of revolution spread throughout the world,
　　And burn all the monsters and demons together.
　　Keep Chairman Mao's teachings firmly in mind,
　　Holding the revolutionary banner high,
　　With the winds and thunders of the five continents surging in our hearts,
　　We will not waver on the road ahead.

### 毛主席教导要记牢
郭崇华
1966-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

    毛主席教导要记牢
　　北京部队某部战士　郭崇华
    毛主席教导要记牢，
    东风劲吹西风倒，
    任凭狂飙阵阵起，
    岿然不动胆气豪。
    毛主席教导要记牢，
    敌人磨刀我磨刀，
    针锋相对不畏缩，
    妖魔鬼怪一枪挑。
    毛主席教导要记牢，
    冲锋陷阵铲毒草，
    革命烽火遍四海，
    牛鬼蛇神一齐烧。
    毛主席教导要记牢，
    革命大旗擎得高，
    五洲风雷涌满胸，
    前进路上不动摇。
