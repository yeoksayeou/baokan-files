Title: Ambassador Park Se-chang Holds Film Reception to Commemorate the Sixteenth Anniversary of the "June 25th" Korean Fatherland Liberation War
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-25
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 24th—Park Se-chang, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, held a film reception this afternoon to commemorate the sixteenth anniversary of the "June 25th" Korean Fatherland Liberation War.
    Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and leading figures from various fields, including Wu Xiuquan, Han Nianlong, Shi Huaibi, Zhang Tianmin, and Wei Chuantong, were invited to attend the reception.
    Cultural officials from various countries' embassies in China were also invited to the reception.
    A Korean feature film was screened at the reception.

### 纪念“六·二五”朝鲜祖国解放战争十六周年  朴世昌大使举行电影招待会

1966-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    纪念“六·二五”朝鲜祖国解放战争十六周年
    朴世昌大使举行电影招待会
    新华社二十四日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使朴世昌，今天下午举行电影招待会，纪念“六·二五”朝鲜祖国解放战争十六周年。
    人大常委会副委员长林枫，以及各方面负责人伍修权、韩念龙、史怀璧、张天民、魏传统等应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有各国驻中国大使馆的文化官员。
    招待会上放映了朝鲜故事影片。
