Title: Danish Professor Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd – Professor Miltos, head of the Department of Agricultural Economics at the Royal Veterinary and Agricultural College of Denmark, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences to conduct a study tour in our country.

### 丹麦一教授到京

1966-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    丹麦一教授到京
    新华社二十二日讯　应中国农业科学院邀请来我国考察访问的丹麦皇家兽医农学院农业经济系主任米尔托斯教授和夫人，今天下午乘飞机到京。
