Title: Fear No Ghosts, Believe No Evil, Dare to Revolution, Dare to Struggle
Author: Zhang Mingren, Dong Quanyou, Yu Xiuzhu, Ding Fenglan, Du Guifang
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 3

    Fear No Ghosts, Believe No Evil, Dare to Revolution, Dare to Struggle
    To Comrades Nie Yuanzi and others of the Philosophy Department of Peking University:
    We were extremely excited when we heard your big-character poster, "What Exactly Have Song Shuo, Lu Ping, and Peng Peiyun Been Doing in the Cultural Revolution?" on the radio. Your revolutionary spirit of daring to revolt, daring to struggle, fearing no ghosts, believing no evil, and being willing to "dare to unhorse the emperor" for the sake of defending socialism and Mao Zedong Thought, the lifeline of the Chinese people and the people of the world, is the correct attitude of revolutionary intellectuals and is a model for us to learn from. We firmly support you! We will fight alongside you to completely defeat all anti-Party and anti-socialist monsters and demons!
    Currently, a vigorous Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is sweeping the country. This is a life-and-death class struggle. All revolutionary people are holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and launching a fierce attack on the anti-Party and anti-socialist black gangs. However, some agents of the bourgeoisie are stirring up trouble, creating various "restrictive rules and regulations," suppressing the revolutionary masses, opposing the revolutionary masses, and singing a tune contrary to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao.
    But no matter what, all their conspiracies cannot escape the eyes of the people armed with Mao Zedong Thought. Let us hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, defend the Party Central Committee, defend Chairman Mao, defend our great socialism, eliminate all "restrictive rules and regulations" and conspiracies, completely, thoroughly, and cleanly smash the attacks of all anti-Party and anti-socialist elements, and resolutely carry the socialist Cultural Revolution through to the end!
    Long live the great Communist Party of China!
    Long live our great leader Chairman Mao! With revolutionary greetings,
      Zhangjiakou Prospecting Machinery Plant, Ministry of Geology
      Zhang Mingren, Dong Quanyou, Yu Xiuzhu
                              Ding Fenglan, Du Guifang

### 不怕鬼　不信邪　敢革命　敢斗争
张明仁  董全有  于秀珠  丁凤兰  杜桂芳
1966-06-04
第3版()
专栏：

    不怕鬼　不信邪　敢革命　敢斗争
    北京大学哲学系聂元梓等同志：
    当我们在广播里听到你们写的《宋硕、陆平、彭珮云在文化革命中究竟干些什么？》的大字报后，十分激动。你们这种敢于革命、敢于斗争、不怕鬼、不信邪，为了保卫社会主义，为了保卫毛泽东思想这个中国人民和世界人民的命根子，“舍得一身剐，敢把皇帝拉下马”的革命精神，是革命的知识分子的正确态度，是我们学习的榜样，我们坚决支持你们！与你们并肩战斗，为彻底打垮一切反党反社会主义的牛鬼蛇神而奋斗！
    目前，全国各地都掀起了轰轰烈烈的文化大革命，这是一场你死我活的阶级斗争，一切革命的人民都在高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，向反党反社会主义的黑帮展开了猛烈的进攻。然而，有些资产阶级的代理人却在兴风作浪，制造种种“清规戒律”，压制群众革命，反对群众革命，和党中央、毛主席唱对台戏。
    但是，不管怎样，他们的一切阴谋诡计，逃脱不了用毛泽东思想武装起来的人民的眼睛。让我们高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，为保卫党中央、保卫毛主席、保卫我们伟大的社会主义，肃清一切“清规戒律”、阴谋诡计，全部、彻底、干净地打垮一切反党反社会主义分子的进攻，坚决把社会主义文化大革命进行到底！
    伟大的中国共产党万岁！
    我们伟大的领袖毛主席万岁！致以革命的敬礼
      地质部张家口探矿机械厂　张明仁　董全有　于秀珠
                              丁凤兰　杜桂芳
