Title: Our Photography Delegation Leaves Albania for Home

Author:

Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 9th – The Chinese photography delegation led by Gao Fan, editor-in-chief of the *Liberation Army Pictorial* and editor-in-chief of *China Photography*, left Tirana by plane on the 8th to return home.



**Named Entities**

### 我摄影代表团离阿回国

1966-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我摄影代表团离阿回国
    新华社地拉那九日电　由《解放军画报》总编辑和《中国摄影》主编高帆率领的中国摄影代表团八日乘飞机离开地拉那回国。
