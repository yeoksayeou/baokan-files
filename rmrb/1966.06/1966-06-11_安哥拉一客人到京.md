Title: Angolan Guest Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-11
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 10th. Moisés Sebastião, Social Affairs Secretary of the Angolan National Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country at our invitation.

### 安哥拉一客人到京

1966-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    安哥拉一客人到京
    新华社十日讯　应邀来我国访问的安哥拉全国工人联合会社会事务书记莫依斯·赛巴斯丁今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
