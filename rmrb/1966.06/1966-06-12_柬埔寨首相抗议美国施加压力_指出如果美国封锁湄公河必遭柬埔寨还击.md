Title: Cambodian Prime Minister Protests US Pressure, States Cambodia Will Retaliate if the US Blockades the Mekong River
Author:
Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 5

    According to a New China News Agency dispatch from Phnom Penh on June 10th: The Cambodian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Prince Kantol, sent a telegram to US Congressman Charles Chamberlain, protesting his request that US President Johnson exert pressure on Cambodia by blockading the Mekong River.
    The telegram stated that such an action would be yet another violation of international law, following the US’s countless violations of various treaties, agreements, and the UN Charter. The telegram warned: You must understand that Cambodia will certainly be able to deal with this move, just as it has dealt with your troops and your mercenaries' constant aggression. Do not think that this measure can undermine Cambodia's independence.
    The *Cambodge Soir* newspaper published a commentary on June 10th entitled "The Mekong Blockade," stating: "Such a blockade would be a blatant violation of the principles of international law regarding the freedom of navigation on international waterways."
    The commentary stated: The American plot reveals to us the frustration of certain US Congressmen who resent Cambodia for not approving of their anti-communist crusade for the "free" world. The commentary emphasized that Prince Sihanouk has built the port of Sihanoukville from scratch, which is Cambodia's response to hostile plots. The commentary stated: The plan to blockade the Mekong River is just another bluff after so many previous bluffs.

### 柬埔寨首相抗议美国施加压力  指出如果美国封锁湄公河必遭柬埔寨还击

1966-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨首相抗议美国施加压力
    指出如果美国封锁湄公河必遭柬埔寨还击
    据新华社金边十日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十日报道，柬埔寨内阁首相兼外交大臣康托尔亲王打电报给美国众议员查尔斯·钱伯林，抗议他要求美国总统约翰逊通过封锁湄公河对柬埔寨施加压力。
    电报说，这种做法将是美国无数次破坏各种条约、协定和联合国宪章之后，对国际法的又一次破坏。电报警告说，你必须明白：象对付你们的军队和你们的雇佣军的不断侵略一样，柬埔寨一定能对付你们这一手。你不要认为这种措施能够破坏柬埔寨的独立。
    《柬埔寨电讯报》十日就这一问题发表题为《封锁湄公河》的评论指出：“这样的封锁将是明目张胆地破坏国际法关于国际河道航行自由的准则。”
    评论说：美国的阴谋向我们揭示了因为柬埔寨不赞成他们为“自由”世界搞反共十字军而怀恨在心的某些美国国会人员的沮丧。评论强调指出，西哈努克亲王已经从无到有把西哈努克城这个港口建设起来，这是柬埔寨对敌对阴谋的反击。评论说：封锁湄公河的计划，只不过是继那么多次虚张声势之后的又一次虚张声势。
