Title: Vice Premier Bo Receives Pakistani Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-06-19
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Vice Premier Bo Yibo received Machali, Chairman of the West Pakistan Industrial Development Corporation, and his wife this morning, and had a friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Li Yingji, Wu Qingchan, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Pakistani Ambassador to China, Sultan Muhammad Khan, was also present. (Attached photo)
    June 18, Vice Premier Bo Yibo receives Machali (front right two), Chairman of the West Pakistan Industrial Development Corporation, and his wife. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Jingde.

### 薄副总理接见巴基斯坦客人

1966-06-19
第6版()
专栏：

    薄副总理接见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社十八日讯　薄一波副总理今天上午接见巴基斯坦西巴工业发展公司主席马查利和夫人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有李应吉、武庆产等有关部门负责人。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也在座。（附图片）
    六月十八日，薄一波副总理接见巴基斯坦西巴工业发展公司主席马查利（前右二）和夫人。　新华社记者　王敬德摄
