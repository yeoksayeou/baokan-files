Title: Chairman of Tribhuvan University Student Union in Nepal Says: Mao Zedong Thought Points Out the Direction and Path for Us
Author:
Date: 1966-06-19
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 16th. The Tribhuvan University in Nepal held a reception on the 13th to welcome the Chinese Youth Sports Delegation. Nimar Kumar Aryal, Chairman of the Tribhuvan University Student Union, said at the reception that the Chinese people have achieved great accomplishments under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. He emphasized: "Chairman Mao Zedong's thought points out the direction and path for us." He expressed his desire to learn from China's experience in national construction.
    Yang Gongsu, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, and his wife held a reception for the Chinese Youth Sports Delegation on the afternoon of the 15th.
    Prince Basundhara, other members of the Nepalese royal family, and senior military and government officials attended the reception. People from all walks of life and diplomatic officials, about two hundred people in total, attended the reception. The reception was conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

### 尼泊尔特里布文大学学生会主席说  毛泽东思想为我们指出了方向和道路

1966-06-19
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔特里布文大学学生会主席说
    毛泽东思想为我们指出了方向和道路
    新华社加德满都十六日电　尼泊尔特里布文大学十三日举行招待会，招待中国青年体育代表团。特里布文大学学生会主席尼马尔·库马尔·阿里亚尔在招待会上讲话说，中国人民在毛泽东主席的英明领导下取得了巨大的成就。他强调指出：“毛泽东主席的思想为我们指出了方向和道路”。他表示要学习中国在国家建设方面的经验。
    中国驻尼泊尔大使杨公素和夫人十五日下午为中国青年体育代表团举行招待会。
    巴森达拉殿下、尼泊尔王室其他成员和高级军政官员出席了招待会。出席招待会的有各界人士和外交官员等约两百人。招待会是在友好的气氛中进行的。
