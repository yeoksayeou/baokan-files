Title: China and Pakistan Sign Protocol on China's Assistance to Pakistan in Building a Heavy Machinery Plant
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, June 23rd. China and Pakistan signed a protocol in Karachi on the 23rd regarding China's assistance in building an integrated heavy machinery plant.

    According to the protocol, the Chinese government will provide machinery and technical expertise for the construction of this project. This integrated factory will produce complete sets of equipment for cement and sugar production, low-pressure boilers, road construction machinery, cranes, and railway materials.

    This project is being built using an interest-free loan provided by China to Pakistan.

    Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Ding Guoyu and Usman Ali, Secretary of the Economic Affairs Department of the Presidential Secretariat of Pakistan, signed the protocol on behalf of the Chinese Government and the Pakistani Government respectively.

### 中巴签订中国援助巴兴建重型机器厂议定书

1966-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    中巴签订中国援助巴兴建重型机器厂议定书
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十三日电　中国和巴基斯坦二十三日在卡拉奇签订了一项由中国援助兴建一座综合重型机器厂的议定书。
    根据这项议定书，中国政府将为这一工程的建设提供机器和技术知识。这一综合工厂将生产制造水泥、食糖用的全套设备、低压锅炉、筑路机器、起重机和铁路材料等。
    这项工程是利用中国提供巴基斯坦的无息贷款兴建的。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰和巴基斯坦总统秘书处经济事务部秘书奥斯曼·阿里分别代表中国政府和巴基斯坦政府在议定书上签了字。
