Title: Beijing Song and Dance Troupe to Albania and Romania

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-06
Edition: 4

    Beijing, June 2nd (Xinhua News Agency) A 77-member Chinese Beijing Song and Dance Troupe, led by Director Wang Qian, left by train on the 2nd for a performance tour of Romania and Albania. Among those who saw them off were Yang Chun, Jin Guangzu, Ding Bo, and other responsible persons from relevant departments. Albanian Ambassador to China, Vasil Nathanaili, and his wife, Counselor Meleq Babani and his wife, and Second Secretary of the Romanian Embassy in China, Betrescu, also saw them off at the station.

### 北京歌舞团赴阿罗

1966-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    北京歌舞团赴阿罗
    新华社讯　中国北京歌舞团一行七十七人在团长王前率领下，二日乘火车赴罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚访问演出。前往送行的有杨纯、金光祖、丁波等有关方面负责人。阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利和夫人、参赞梅莱契·巴巴尼和夫人，罗马尼亚驻华大使馆二等秘书贝特勒斯库等也到车站送行。
