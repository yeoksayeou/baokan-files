	All the people of the world, unite and defeat the American aggressors and all their running dogs! The people of the world should have courage, dare to fight, be not afraid of difficulties and press on wave upon wave. Then the whole world will surely belong to the people. All monsters will be destroyed.

		From the "Statement on Supporting the People of the Congo (Leopoldville) in Their Struggle Against U.S. Aggression"
