Title: Root Mao Zedong Thought in the Xiaoxing'an Mountains
Author:
Date: 1966-06-06
Edition: 3

    Root Mao Zedong Thought in the Xiaoxing'an Mountains
    When the news of the CCP Central Committee's reorganization of the Beijing Municipal Party Committee reached the Xiaoxing'an Mountains, the people here were overjoyed and spread the word. This is a welcome and gratifying piece of good news, another great victory for Mao Zedong Thought. In the Socialist Education Movement, we deeply realized that Mao Zedong Thought is the fundamental of fundamentals for carrying out the Socialist Education Movement well, and it is an invincible ideological weapon. We are determined to firmly plant the roots of Mao Zedong Thought on the peaks of the Xiaoxing'an Mountains. Let the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought fly forever. Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!
                    The Entire Team of the Tielin Work Team of the Xinqing Forest Area Socialist Education Work Group, Yichun Special District

### 把毛泽东思想扎根在小兴安岭

1966-06-06
第3版()
专栏：

    把毛泽东思想扎根在小兴安岭
    当中共中央关于改组北京市委的消息传到小兴安岭的时候，这里的人们欢欣鼓舞，奔走相告。这是大快人心的捷报，是毛泽东思想又一伟大胜利。我们在社会主义教育运动中，深切地体会到，毛泽东思想是搞好社会主义教育运动根本的根本，是战无不胜的思想武器。我们决心把毛泽东思想的根子牢牢的扎在小兴安岭顶峰。让毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，永远飘扬。战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！
                    伊春特区新青林区社会主义教
                    育工作团铁林工作队全体队员
