Title: Speech by Kalmi, Head of the Palestinian Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-06-28
Edition: 6

Dear friends:

We have come to Beijing, to the side of our great friends, the friends of all peoples fighting for freedom, with longing in our hearts. Here, we hear her great heart beating with ours, just as we hear it in Palestine, just as peoples of all countries hear it everywhere. In this heartbeat, there is the great history of her people's struggle against imperialism.

Friends:

I have come to participate in the emergency meeting of Asian and African writers held in Beijing, a meeting to express support for the struggle of the Vietnamese people for the freedom and unity of their country. If everyone has the responsibility to support the Vietnamese people, then writers, especially Asian and African writers, have an even greater responsibility, for this is a struggle to defend freedom. The great Arab prophet said: "Whoever among you finds evil, let him change it with his hand; if he cannot, then with his tongue; if he cannot, then with his heart—this is the minimum of faith."

Is there any crime more brutal than the evil war waged by American imperialism to prevent the Vietnamese people from achieving freedom?

The young generation can use their strong hands to eradicate this crime. We, writers and poets, those among us who cannot directly participate in the war, should at least wield our pens as swords to defend the freedom of the Vietnamese people. The writer's pen must be ignited by the flames of Vietnam, until its fragrance spreads far and wide, and the spring breeze carries it to all directions.

The ink of writers, like the blood of martyrs, both can radiate light, dispel the darkness of night, and guide the heroes fighting for liberation towards dawn.

Dear friends:

The unity in struggle, the unity on the path to freedom and unity, has united the great Chinese people with the Palestinian people. The sufferings endured in the entire history of struggle against the common enemy have united our two peoples. Friends, before I met you, I already knew you. Revolutionary comrades, even if we have not met, we are comrades who share life and death.

Before I met you, I was with you; during the Opium War, I was with you; on Jinggangshan, in the caves of Yan'an, I was with you; in your Long March, in the trench warfare, I was with you. When I saw you, I understood how you achieved liberation from the rule of imperialism and the disasters it brought, how you built a new society through self-reliance, and how you embodied the great thoughts of your leader Mao Zedong. I also understood that you achieved your own liberation, but even after liberating Taiwan, you do not consider your mission completed. You will not lay down your arms until all peoples of the world, first and foremost the Vietnamese and Palestinian peoples, achieve liberation from imperialist rule. This is the revolutionary mission pointed out by Chairman Mao Zedong for you.

Dear friends:

Our greatest enemy is American imperialism. It wears various masks, sometimes the mask of West Germany, sometimes the mask of Israel. It has many masks, but we recognize all its masks. If it thinks we do not know its various faces, it is more foolish than the ostrich, a small-headed, large-bodied animal living in our Arab deserts. This ostrich, when hunters try to catch it, buries its small head in the sand, leaving its large body exposed. It foolishly thinks that if it hides its head, the hunter will not see it, thus becoming an easy prey.

### 巴勒斯坦代表团长卡尔米的致词

1966-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦代表团长卡尔米的致词亲爱的朋友们：
    我们怀着渴慕之情来到了北京，来到了伟大的朋友、一切为自由而战斗的人民的朋友身边。在这里，我们听到，她那伟大的心脏和我们一起跳动，就象我们在巴勒斯坦所听到的一样，就象各国人民在一切地方所听到的一样。在这心的搏动中，包含着她的伟大人民反帝的历史。
    朋友们：
    我来参加亚非作家在北京召开的紧急会议，这个会议是为了对越南人民为争取祖国的自由和统一而进行的斗争表示支持。如果说，支持越南人民人人有责的话，那末作家，特别是亚非作家，就有更大的责任，因为，这就是保卫自由。伟大的阿拉伯先知说过：“谁在你们之中发现了罪恶，则以手更易之，若不成，则以舌更易之，若不成，则以心更易之，这是最起码的信仰”。
    难道还有比美帝国主义为阻止越南人民取得自由而向他们发动的罪恶战争更残暴的罪行吗？
    年轻的一代是能够用他们强有力的双手来清除这一罪行的。我们，作家和诗人，我们中间不能直接参战的人，至少应该拔出他们的笔来，代替宝剑，来保卫越南人民的自由。作家之笔必须用越南的战火来点燃，直至其芳香四溢，并让春风把它带到四面八方。
    作家的笔墨，犹如烈士的鲜血，两者都能放射光芒，驱散夜的黑暗，把争取解放的英雄引向黎明。
    亲爱的朋友们：
    斗争中的团结，在向着自由和统一的道路上行进中的团结，把伟大的中国人民和巴勒斯坦人民联结在一起了，在为反对共同敌人而斗争的整个历史中所受的苦难，把我们两国人民联结在一起了。朋友们，在我和你们相会之前，我就已认识了你们，革命的同志，即使尚未相遇，也是生死与共的同志。
    在我和你们相遇以前，我就和你们在一起；在鸦片战争中，我和你们在一起；在井冈山上，在延安的窑洞中，我和你们在一起；在你们的长征中，地道战中，我和你们在一起。当我看到了你们，我就了解了你们是怎样从帝国主义者统治下、从帝国主义者带来的灾难中取得了解放的，我就了解你们是如何自力更生建设新社会，如何体现你们伟大的领袖毛泽东的思想的。我还了解，你们自己取得了解放，但即使是在解放台湾以后，你们也不认为已经完成了你们的使命，你们决不会放下武器，除非是在全世界人民，首先是越南人民和巴勒斯坦人民从帝国主义的统治下取得了解放以后。这就是毛泽东主席为你们指出的你们的革命使命。
    亲爱的朋友们：
    我们最大的敌人就是美帝国主义，它戴有各种各样的假面具，有时戴上西德的面具，有时戴上以色列的面具。它们假面具是很多的，但是我们认识它的一切面具，如果它认为我们不知道它的种种面貌，那它就比鸵鸟还要愚蠢，鸵鸟是头小身体庞大的动物，它生活在我们阿拉伯沙漠。这种鸵鸟，当猎人要捕捉它时，它把小小的头埋进沙里，它那庞大的身体却露在外面，它愚蠢地认为，如果把头藏起来，猎人就看不见它了，这样，它就成了很容易捕获的猎物。
