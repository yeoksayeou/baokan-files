Title: Farewell Banquet on June 7th
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 4

    On June 7th, Tanzanian Minister for Economic Affairs and Development Planning, Paul Bomani, and his wife held a farewell banquet.
    The hosts and guests had a cordial conversation at the banquet. From the left: Premier Zhou Enlai, Minister Paul Bomani, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Ambassador Wazir Juma, and Vice Premier Li Xiannian. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Yuanliu photo)
