Title: Guinean Artists Cheer "Long Live Chairman Mao" During Performance in Harbin; Mao Zedong Thought is the Forever Unsetting Sun

Author:
Date: 1966-06-01
Edition: 4

    Guinean artists cheer "Long Live Chairman Mao" during performance in Harbin
    Mao Zedong Thought is the Forever Unsetting Sun
    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 31st – During the Guinean National Djoliba Dance Company's premiere performance in Harbin, the performer Keita Mounceri passionately invited the audience on stage to shout with him: "Long Live Chairman Mao!" At that moment, shouts of "Long Live Chairman Mao" resounded throughout the theater, from both the stage and the audience, fully reflecting the African people's sincere love for Chairman Mao.
    The Guinean National Djoliba Dance Company arrived in Harbin on May 29th from Changchun. Tonight was their premiere performance in Harbin. When the Guinean artists sang the song "Fighting Africa Praises Mao Zedong and His Great Cause," the audience of over a thousand erupted in enthusiastic applause. The envoys of the awakened African people sang and danced beneath Chairman Mao's portrait. They sang with warmth and passion:
        Ah, Mao Zedong, I will forever
            sing your praises,
        I will forever sing your praises.
        Your works, ah, are a source of
            endless strength,
        Your thought, ah, is the forever
            unsetting sun!
        It illuminates the vast expanse of
          China, and also illuminates the
          hearts of fighting people all
          over the world...
    The Deputy Head of the dance company, vocalist Kouyate Sory Kandia, wearing a large red flower on his chest, enthusiastically sang "Wear a Big Red Flower, Listen to the Party's Words" in Chinese. The performer Keita Mounceri also sang "The East is Red" in Chinese. At that moment, the emotions of the performers and the audience intertwined in their love for Chairman Mao, and the excited audience could not help but clap enthusiastically along with the rhythm of their singing.
    Bai Wei, Deputy Director of the Heilongjiang Provincial Cultural Bureau, held a banquet on the evening of the 29th to warmly welcome the Guinean friends. Before the banquet, Sun Xiqi, Vice Governor of Heilongjiang Province, and Hu Chuanjing, Vice Mayor of Harbin, met with the Guinean guests.

### 几内亚艺术家在哈尔滨演出时欢呼毛主席万岁  毛泽东思想是永远不落的太阳

1966-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    几内亚艺术家在哈尔滨演出时欢呼毛主席万岁
    毛泽东思想是永远不落的太阳
    新华社哈尔滨三十一日电几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团在哈尔滨首场演出的时候，演员凯塔·芒塞里满怀激情地在台上请观众和他一起高呼：“毛主席万岁！”这时，台上台下“毛主席万岁”的口号声响彻整个剧场，充分反映了非洲人民对毛主席的真挚热爱。
    几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团是在五月二十九日由长春到达哈尔滨的。今天晚上举行了在哈尔滨的首场演出。几内亚艺术家在演唱《战斗的非洲歌颂毛泽东和他伟大的事业》这首歌曲时，一千多名观众爆发出一阵阵的热烈掌声。觉醒的非洲人民的使者在毛主席画象下面边歌边舞。他们热情奔放地歌唱：
    啊，毛泽东，我要永远把
        您歌唱，
    我要永远把您歌唱。
    您的著作啊，就是无穷的
        力量，
    您的思想啊，是永远不落
        的太阳！
    它照亮了辽阔的中国，也
      把全世界战斗的人民心
      儿照亮……。
    舞蹈团副总团长、歌唱家库雅特·索里·康迪亚胸前带着大红花热情洋溢地用中文演唱了《戴花要戴大红花，听话要听党的话》。演员凯塔·芒塞里也用中文演唱《东方红》，这时，演员和观众对毛主席热爱的感情交织在一起，激动的观众情不自禁地跟着他们演唱的节拍热情地鼓掌。
    黑龙江省文化局副局长白韦二十九日晚上举行宴会，热情欢迎几内亚朋友。宴会前，黑龙江省副省长孙西岐、哈尔滨市副市长胡传经接见了几内亚客人。
