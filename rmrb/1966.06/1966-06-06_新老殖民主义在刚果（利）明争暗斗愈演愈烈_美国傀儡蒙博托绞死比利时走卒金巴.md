Title: Intensifying Open and Covert Struggles Between New and Old Colonialism in the Congo (Kinshasa): U.S. Puppet Mobutu Executes Belgian Stooge Kimba

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-06
Edition: 5

    According to a Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Brazzaville on the 3rd, the Mobutu puppet regime of the Congo (Kinshasa) publicly hanged former puppet regime "Prime Minister" Kimba and three others in Leopoldville on the 2nd, accusing them of plotting to overthrow Mobutu's rule. This is an ugly farce in which the intense contradictions between American neocolonialism and Belgian old colonialism in their scramble for the Congo (Kinshasa) are becoming increasingly visible.

    The United States and the Mobutu puppet regime have been making a great show of "uncovering a conspiracy" and executing Kimba and the three others this time in order to achieve two goals: on the one hand, to weaken Belgium's position and influence in the Congo (Kinshasa), and on the other hand, to transform the Congolese people's anti-American sentiment into anti-Belgian sentiment. It is worth noting that when the Mobutu puppet regime's "Foreign Minister" Bomboko announced the Kimba case at a press conference, he accused the Belgian embassy of participating in the conspiracy and said that a Belgian diplomat had been expelled, while praising the "well-intentioned" American embassy for refusing to get involved in the conspiracy.

    Since Mobutu was installed in power last November, American neocolonialism has intensified its control over the Mobutu puppet regime by providing weapons and "economic aid," and has directed it to take a series of measures to weaken and squeeze out the influence of Belgian old colonialism in the Congo (Kinshasa). At the end of May, the Congolese (Kinshasa) pseudo-parliament controlled by the Mobutu clique also passed a bill to cancel the mining and forestry rights previously leased by the Belgian colonial government.

    The Mobutu clique also declared the financial agreement signed between Belgium and the former Tshombe puppet regime in February of last year invalid, and held negotiations with the Belgian authorities on financial and economic issues in Brussels from May 12 to 26.

    During the negotiations, the radio stations and newspapers controlled by the Mobutu clique clamored that the "kinship" between Belgium and the Congo (Kinshasa) no longer existed, and demanded the confiscation of the "Union Minière du Haut Katanga" and the Belgian mining "General Company." The Mobutu clique also held anti-Belgian demonstrations in some cities of the Congo (Kinshasa).

    In this situation, Belgian Foreign Minister Harmel temporarily put aside the negotiations between the Belgian authorities and the Mobutu regime and flew to Washington to negotiate directly with U.S. Secretary of State Rusk.

    During the two-week Brussels negotiations, although the Belgian authorities made some compromises to avoid a "complete rupture" in relations with Mobutu, they were unwilling to show weakness in the face of the American and Mobutu offensive. Its propaganda machine threatened that if the Congo (Kinshasa) could not maintain "order and prosperity," Katanga might "secede" from the Congo (Kinshasa). In this way, the open and covert struggles between new and old colonialism are intensifying.

    At the same time, the more the tentacles of American imperialism stretch into the Congo (Kinshasa), attempting to plunge the Congolese people further into misery, the more intense the anti-American sentiment it arouses. The armed struggle of the patriotic people is developing in a more in-depth direction, and the people's hatred for American imperialism is growing deeper and deeper.

    Therefore, American imperialism and the Mobutu puppet regime are trying to use the "uncovering of the conspiracy" case to strike at Belgian forces and transform the anti-American sentiment of the Congolese people into anti-Belgian sentiment. These developments reveal that American imperialism's days in the Congo (Kinshasa) are getting more and more difficult. (Attached picture)

### 新老殖民主义在刚果（利）明争暗斗愈演愈烈  美国傀儡蒙博托绞死比利时走卒金巴

1966-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    新老殖民主义在刚果（利）明争暗斗愈演愈烈
    美国傀儡蒙博托绞死比利时走卒金巴
    据新华社布拉柴维尔三日电　刚果（利）蒙博托傀儡政权二日在利奥波德维尔把前傀儡政权“总理”金巴等四人公开绞死，并指责他们阴谋推翻蒙博托统治。这是美国新殖民主义和比利时老殖民主义争夺刚果（利）的激烈矛盾日趋表面化的一出丑剧。
    美国和蒙博托傀儡政权这一次大肆宣传“破获阴谋”和绞死金巴等四人，是为了达到一箭双雕的目的：一方面削弱比利时在刚果（利）的地位和势力，同时又把刚果（利）人民的反美情绪转变为反比情绪。值得注意的是，蒙博托傀儡政权的“外交部长”邦博科在记者招待会上宣布金巴等的案件时，一方面指责比利时大使馆参与了这一阴谋，并且说一个比利时外交官已经被驱逐出境，同时他又称赞“好心的”美国大使馆拒绝卷入这个阴谋。
    自从去年十一月蒙博托被扶植上台以来，美国新殖民主义通过提供武器和“经济援助”等办法加紧控制蒙博托傀儡政权，并指使它采取一系列措施，削弱和排挤比利时老殖民主义在刚果（利）的势力。五月底，蒙博托集团控制的刚果（利）伪议会还通过一项取消以前由比利时殖民政府出租的矿产和森林的开采权。
    蒙博托集团还宣布去年二月比利时和前冲伯傀儡政权签订的财政协定无效，并从五月十二日到二十六日在布鲁塞尔同比利时当局就财政经济问题举行谈判。
    在谈判期间，蒙博托集团控制的电台和报纸大声叫喊，比利时和刚果（利）之间的
  “亲戚关系”不存在了，并要求没收“上加丹加联合矿业公司”和比利时矿业“总公司”。蒙博托集团还在刚果（利）一些城市举行反比利时示威。
    在这种情况下，比利时外交大臣阿麦尔就把比利时当局和蒙博托政权的谈判暂时搁置一边，而飞往华盛顿同美国国务卿腊斯克直接谈判。
    在持续两个星期的布鲁塞尔谈判中，比利时当局虽然作了某种程度的妥协，以免同蒙博托的关系“完全破裂”，但是它对美国和蒙博托的进攻也不甘示弱。它的宣传机器威胁说，如果刚果（利）不能保持“有秩序和繁荣”，加丹加可能从刚果（利）“脱离”出来。就这样，新老殖民主义的明争暗斗，愈演愈烈。
    同时，美帝国主义的魔手越要伸进刚果（利），企图把刚果（利）人民进一步投入水深火热之中，也越激起激烈的反美情绪。爱国人民的武装斗争向更加深入的方向发展，人民对美帝国主义的仇恨也越来越深。
    因此，美帝国主义和蒙博托傀儡政权就企图利用“破获阴谋”案来打击比利时势力和把刚果（利）人民的反美情绪转变为反比情绪。这些事态的发展暴露了美帝国主义在刚果（利）的日子越来越不好过。（附图片）
