Title: Japanese Scientist Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th.  Mr. Saburo Shimohira, a Japanese scientist, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the China Association for Science and Technology of the People's Republic of China.


**Named Entities**


新华社 Xinhua News Agency
下平三郎	Shimohira Saburo
中华人民共和国科学技术协会 Zhonghua Renmin Gongheguo Kexue Jishu Xiehui (China Association for Science and Technology of the People's Republic of China)
北京	Beijing
