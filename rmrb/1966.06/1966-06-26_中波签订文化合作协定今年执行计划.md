Title: China and Poland Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement This Year's Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 6

    China and Poland Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement This Year's Implementation Plan
    (Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 24th) The 1966 implementation plan for the China-Poland Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Warsaw on the 24th.
    Wang Guoquan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and Lucjan Motyka, Polish Minister of Culture and Arts, signed the implementation plan on behalf of their respective governments.
    Wu Feng, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, and Garstecki, Vice Minister of Culture and Arts of Poland, attended the signing ceremony.

### 中波签订文化合作协定今年执行计划

1966-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    中波签订文化合作协定今年执行计划
    新华社华沙二十四日电　中国和波兰文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划二十四日在华沙签字。
    分别代表两国政府在执行计划上签字的是：中国驻波兰大使王国权和波兰文化艺术部部长芦·莫提卡。
    中国驻波兰大使馆文化参赞吴枫和波兰文化艺术部副部长加尔斯特茨基等出席了签字仪式。
