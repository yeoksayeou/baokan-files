Title: Sino-Romanian Party and Government Delegations Continue Talks
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-20
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Constanta, 19th – The Chinese Party and Government Delegation and the Romanian Party and Government Delegation continued formal talks this morning in North Eforie, near Constanta, Romania. The talks were conducted in a comradely and sincere atmosphere.

**Named Entities**

中国    Zhōngguó (China)
罗马尼亚  Luómǎníyǎ (Romania)
康斯坦察  Kāngsītǎnchá (Constanta)
北埃弗利耶  Běi Āifúlìyē (North Eforie)
新华社  Xīnhuá shè (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中罗两国党政代表团继续会谈

1966-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗两国党政代表团继续会谈
    新华社康斯坦察十九日电　中国党政代表团和罗马尼亚党政代表团，十九日上午在罗马尼亚康斯坦察市附近的北埃弗利耶继续举行了正式会谈。会谈是在同志式的诚挚气氛中进行的。
