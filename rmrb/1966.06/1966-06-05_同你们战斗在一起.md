Title: Fighting Alongside You

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-05
Edition: 2

    Comrades Nie Yuanzi and others, we firmly support your revolutionary actions and learn from your fearless revolutionary spirit. We firmly stand with you, and will fight to the end to defend the Party Central Committee, defend Mao Zedong Thought, and defend the dictatorship of the proletariat. Let us unite, hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, and resolutely, thoroughly, completely, and cleanly eliminate all monsters and demons, and carry the socialist cultural revolution through to the end!
                                Fuzhou University Student Union

**Named Entities**

毛泽东思想	Mao Zedong Thought
党中央	Dang Zhongyang (Party Central Committee)
福州大学	Fuzhou University

无产阶级专政	Dictatorship of the Proletariat

### 同你们战斗在一起

1966-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

    同你们战斗在一起
    聂元梓等同志，我们坚决支持你们的革命行动，学习你们这种大无畏的革命精神。我们坚决同你们站在一起，为保卫党中央，保卫毛泽东思想，保卫无产阶级专政，而斗争到底。让我们团结起来，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，坚决、彻底、全部、干净地消灭一切牛鬼蛇神，把社会主义文化大革命进行到底！
                                福州大学学生会
