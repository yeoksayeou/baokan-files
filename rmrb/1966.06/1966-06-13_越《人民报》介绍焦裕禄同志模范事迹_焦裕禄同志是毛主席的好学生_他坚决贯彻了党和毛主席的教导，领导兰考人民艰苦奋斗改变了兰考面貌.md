Author:

Edition: 4

    Vietnam's "People's Daily" Introduces Comrade Jiao Yulu's Exemplary Deeds
    Comrade Jiao Yulu is Chairman Mao's Good Student
    He Resolutely Implemented the Teachings of the Party and Chairman Mao, Leading the People of Lankao to Work Hard and Change the Face of Lankao
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 11th. The Vietnamese "People's Daily" published an article on June 11th entitled "Jiao Yulu - An Example of a County Party Secretary," introducing the exemplary deeds of Comrade Jiao Yulu, a good student of Chairman Mao.
    The article stated that due to the resolute implementation of the teachings of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, hundreds of thousands of Chinese Communist Party members have become outstanding cadres. Comrade Jiao Yulu is one of the good examples.
    The article said that with the communist's thrift, fearlessness of hardship, fearlessness of difficulties, and the spirit of not seeking fame or profit, Comrade Jiao Yulu served the Party and the people wholeheartedly, determined to lead the people of Lankao County to overcome all difficulties and eliminate the three natural disasters. The article stated that although Comrade Jiao Yulu's life was short, he devoted all his energy to completing the tasks assigned by the Party. He led the masses in arduous struggle and changed the face of Lankao.

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    越《人民报》介绍焦裕禄同志模范事迹
    焦裕禄同志是毛主席的好学生
    他坚决贯彻了党和毛主席的教导，领导兰考人民艰苦奋斗改变了兰考面貌
    新华社河内十一日电　越南《人民报》十一日发表了一篇题为《焦裕禄——县委书记的榜样》的文章，介绍了毛主席的好学生焦裕禄同志的模范事迹。
    文章说，由于坚决地贯彻中国共产党和毛泽东主席的教导，成千上万的中国共产党党员变成了出色的干部。焦裕禄同志就是其中的一个好榜样。
    文章说，焦裕禄同志以一个共产党人的勤俭、不怕苦、不怕困难、不为名、不为利的品德，全心全意为党、为人民服务，决心领导兰考县人民克服一切困难，消除三种自然灾害。文章说，焦裕禄同志的一生虽然短暂，但是他竭尽全部精力来完成党交给的任务。他领导群众进行艰苦的奋斗，改变了兰考的面貌。
