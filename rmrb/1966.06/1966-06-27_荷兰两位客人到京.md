Title: Two Dutch Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-27
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the Chinese Society of Shipbuilding Engineering, Professor Fan La Mi Lun, Director of the Wageningen Ship Model Basin in the Netherlands, and Dr. Fan Man Neng, Deputy Director, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 荷兰两位客人到京

1966-06-27
第5版()
专栏：

    荷兰两位客人到京
    新华社二十六日讯　应中国造船工程学会的邀请，荷兰瓦格宁根船模试验水池主任范拉米伦教授和副主任范曼能博士，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
