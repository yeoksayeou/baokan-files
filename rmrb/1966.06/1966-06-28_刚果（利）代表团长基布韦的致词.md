Title: Speech by the Head of the Congolese (Leopoldville) Delegation, Kibwe
Author:
Date: 1966-06-28
Edition: 6

Chairman Comrade, Representative Comrades, Dear Friends:

We are representatives from the African continent, where aggression, exploitation, and both old and new imperialism run rampant. We have come to Beijing to participate in this important conference of Asian and African peoples uniting and opposing our common enemy. We feel greatly honored to be here. This conference is being held in the beautiful capital of the People's Republic of China. The People's Republic of China is the first country in Asia to have liberated itself from imperialist rule through armed struggle. It is the vanguard of the international proletariat and the unparalleled defender of the interests of the oppressed peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

In the name of the African continental national liberation movement, we extend our warmest greetings to this emergency conference of Asian and African writers. We also extend our fraternal greetings to the presidium and the representatives.

We also extend our warmest greetings and heartfelt thanks to the Communist Party of China, the Chinese writers, and the Chinese people. Under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, they have provided all the favorable conditions for this historic conference. We thank them for their consistent and unconditional support for the patriotic and just struggles of the African people.

Representative Comrades, Dear Friends, the African people have suffered under the brutal rule of imperialism and colonialism. Exploitation, colonial shackles, entire villages bombed, people shot for not submitting to the oppressive system, massacres, racism, laboring for wages that cannot feed or clothe us, nor educate our beloved children, and invasion and tyranny—all these have sparked a great revolutionary force among the people of our continent to oppose imperialism and colonialism.

The Congo (Leopoldville), Southern Rhodesia, Portuguese Guinea, Spanish Guinea, Angola, Mozambique, South West Africa, South Africa, and many other countries are victims of imperialism and colonialism. The birth of the world socialist system and the revolutionary movement have inspired all these oppressed peoples to stand up with increasing strength to attack the fortresses of imperialism and colonialism. We have a common enemy and a common struggle. The most important thing is to unite as one to continue our decisive struggle against imperialism, old and new colonialism, and especially against the leader of the forces of aggression and enslavement that aim to reconquer our countries and turn them into new colonies—the American imperialists.

The era of slavery and colonialism has passed. The once backward continents of Asia and Africa have awakened and are increasingly becoming masters of their own destiny. The victory of the Chinese people's Long March has changed the balance of power in the world. The enemy's camp is filled with contradictions, while the Asian and African peoples are holding high the banner of a protracted war to save humanity, the most noble and glorious cause, which will save all of humanity, save Asia and Africa, and ultimately achieve the elimination of classes and enter an era of permanent peace and happiness for all humanity.

The world colonialist system is collapsing, and this is a great historical victory for the African people. This victory announces the complete failure of imperialism, colonialism, and the colonial slave system. The Congolese people have learned from the collapse of this shameful system, and standing at the forefront of the armed struggle in Africa, just as the victory of all those who are not satisfied with superficial independence and engage in armed struggle is certain, the Congolese people are certain to win. Why do we always beg for money from Europe and America? Why are we always seen as beggars? We possess extremely rich agricultural and mineral resources. We should seize these resources from the hands of foreigners and make them serve our people. We have made too many promises to our people in the past.

Comrades, Friends!

This conference bears great responsibility for our people's destiny. The resolutions of this conference should be strong and powerful, and our unity should be indivisible. Since we have been able to mobilize the people of our continent to fulfill the historic mission of achieving victory in the national liberation struggle, we should ensure that this emergency conference in support of the Vietnamese people and against imperialism achieves the greatest success. This will challenge all those who, using various cunning methods, attempt to sabotage the conference from within or without.

We should show the world that, in order to isolate and condemn American imperialism, modern revisionism, and all their counter-revolutionary lackeys who have consistently betrayed the noble aspirations of our people around the world, we are united and indivisible, just as we have always done in our struggles.

The delegation of the National Liberation Committee of the Congo (Leopoldville) will do everything possible to achieve the following goals of this emergency conference:

Support the Vietnamese people, strongly condemn the number one enemy of the world's people, American imperialism, and modern revisionism, strive for the development of the culture of the fighting Asian and African peoples, strive for national liberation, and achieve socialist construction.

Long live the constructive work of our various committees!

### 刚果（利）代表团长基布韦的致词

1966-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（利）代表团长基布韦的致词主席同志，代表同志们，亲爱的朋友们：
    我们是来自侵略、剥削盛行、新老殖民主义横行霸道的非洲大陆的各国代表，到北京来参加这次亚非各国人民团结和反对共同敌人的重要会议，感到十分荣幸。这次会议是在中华人民共和国美丽的首都北京举行的。中华人民共和国是亚洲大陆第一个用武装斗争摆脱了帝国主义统治的国家，是国际无产阶级的旗手，也是亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲各国被压迫人民的利益的举世无双的保卫者。
    我们以非洲大陆民族解放运动的名义，向这次亚非作家紧急会议致以热烈的敬意，向大会主席团和代表同志们致以兄弟般的敬意。
    我们也向中国共产党、中国作家和中国人民致以热烈的敬意和衷心的感谢，感谢他们在毛泽东主席英明领导下，为这次具有历史意义的大会，提供了一切有利条件，感谢他们一贯毫无保留地支持非洲人民的爱国正义斗争。
    代表同志们，亲爱的朋友们，我们非洲人民遭受帝国主义和殖民主义的残酷统治。剥削，殖民枷锁，整个村庄被轰炸，枪杀不愿屈服于压迫制度的人们，屠杀，种族主义，劳累的工作换得的工资不能使我们吃饱穿暖，也不能使我们的可爱的孩子受教育，还有侵略和暴政，都促使我们大陆的人民迸发出反对帝国主义和殖民主义的伟大革命力量。
    刚果（利）、南罗得西亚、“葡属”几内亚、“西属”几内亚、安哥拉、莫三鼻给、西南非洲、南非和其他许多国家，都是帝国主义和殖民主义的受害者。世界社会主义体系的诞生和革命运动，促使所有这些国家被压迫的人民站起来，以日益强大的力量冲击帝国主义和殖民主义的堡垒。我们有着共同的敌人，我们有着共同的斗争。最重要的问题是把我们团结得象一个人，继续我们反对帝国主义、新老殖民主义、特别是反对以重新征服我们这些国家并把它们变为新殖民地为目的的侵略和奴役势力的魁首——美帝国主义的决定性斗争。
    奴役的时代和殖民主义的时代已经一去不复返了。过去落后的亚洲和非洲大陆已经觉醒，并日益成为自己命运的主人。中国人民长征的胜利改变了世界力量的对比。敌人的营垒中充满着重重矛盾，而亚非人民则高举着拯救人类命运的持久战的旗帜，这是最崇高、最光荣的事业，它将拯救全人类，拯救亚洲、非洲，从而达到消灭阶级，进入使全人类幸福的，永久和平的时代。
    世界殖民主义制度正在大瓦解，这是非洲人民的具有伟大历史意义的胜利，这个胜利向我们宣布了帝国主义、殖民主义和殖民奴役制度的彻底失败。刚果人民从这个可耻制度的瓦解中吸取了教训，并且站在非洲武装斗争的前列，正象一切不满足于表面独立而进行武装斗争的人们的胜利将是肯定的一样，刚果人民是肯定要胜利的。为什么总是向欧洲和美洲乞求金钱，为什么我们总是被人看作是穷鬼，我们拥有极其丰富的农矿财富，我们应当把这些财富从外国人手里夺回来，使它们为我们的人民服务。我们过去对我们的人民已许下了过多的诺言。
    同志们，朋友们！
