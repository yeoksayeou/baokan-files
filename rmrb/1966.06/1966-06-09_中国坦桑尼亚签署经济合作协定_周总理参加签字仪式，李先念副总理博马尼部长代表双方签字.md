Title: China and Tanzania Sign Economic Cooperation Agreement; Premier Zhou Attends Signing Ceremony; Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Minister Bomani Sign on Behalf of Respective Sides
Author:
Date: 1966-06-09
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 8th – The government of the People's Republic of China and the government of the United Republic of Tanzania signed an economic cooperation agreement in Beijing this morning.
    During the visit of the Tanzanian government economic delegation to Beijing, they held sincere, cordial, and friendly talks with the Chinese government. Both sides reached a consensus agreement on economic cooperation between the two governments. This marks a further development of the friendship between the two peoples and the economic cooperation between the two countries.
    Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai attended the signing ceremony this morning.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian and the head of the Tanzanian government economic delegation, Minister of Economic Affairs and Development Planning Paul Bomani, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.
    Chinese attendees at the signing ceremony included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Deputy Director of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries Yang Lin, and others.
    Tanzanian attendees at the signing ceremony included Ambassador to China Wazir Juma, member of the Tanzanian government economic delegation and Deputy Chief Secretary of the Ministry of Communications and Works Chopeta, and others.
    After the signing, both sides raised their glasses in warm congratulations. (Attached picture)

### 中国坦桑尼亚签署经济合作协定  周总理参加签字仪式，李先念副总理博马尼部长代表双方签字

1966-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中国坦桑尼亚签署经济合作协定
    周总理参加签字仪式，李先念副总理博马尼部长代表双方签字
    新华社八日讯　中华人民共和国政府和坦桑尼亚联合共和国政府，今天上午在北京签署了一项经济合作协定。
    坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团在访问北京期间，同中国政府进行了诚挚、亲切和友好的会谈，双方就两国政府经济合作问题达成了一致的协议。这标志着两国人民之间的友谊和两国经济合作关系有了进一步的发展。
    国务院总理周恩来参加了今天上午的签字仪式。
    国务院副总理李先念和坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团团长、经济事务和发展计划部长保罗·博马尼，分别代表两国政府在协定上签字。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，还有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外经委副主任杨琳等。
    坦桑尼亚方面参加签字仪式的，有驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马，坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团团员、交通工程部副首席秘书乔沛塔等。
    签字以后，双方举杯热烈祝贺。（附图片）
    李先念副总理和博马尼部长在协定上签字。
