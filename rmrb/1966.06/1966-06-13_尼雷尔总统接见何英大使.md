Title: President Nyerere Receives Ambassador He Ying
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam, 10th. Tanzanian President Nyerere received Chinese Ambassador to Tanzania, He Ying, here on the 10th and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.



**Named Entities**


尼雷尔	Nyerere
何英	He Ying
达累斯萨拉姆	Dar es Salaam
坦桑尼亚	Tanzania

### 尼雷尔总统接见何英大使

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    尼雷尔总统接见何英大使
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆十日电　坦桑尼亚总统尼雷尔十日在这里接见了中国驻坦桑尼亚大使何英，并同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
