Title: Zhou Enlai Thanks Ho Chi Minh, Hoxha, and Shehu
Author:
Date: 1966-06-29
Edition: 4

	Zhou Enlai Thanks Ho Chi Minh, Hoxha, and Shehu
	Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 28th — When the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, led by Comrade Zhou Enlai, left Albania on the 28th, he sent a telegram of thanks to the Albanian Party and Government leaders. The text of the telegram is as follows:

	To Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Party, Comrade Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, and Comrade Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania:

	As we depart from your heroic country, carrying with us the sincere and deep friendship of the Albanian people towards the Chinese people, I, on behalf of the Communist Party of China, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the Chinese people, once again express our heartfelt gratitude to you, the leaders of the Albanian Labour Party and the state, and the Albanian people for the extremely warm and enthusiastic reception and cordial, friendly treatment given to our Chinese Party and Government Delegation.

	We wish that the revolutionary friendship and combat unity between the two parties, the two countries, and the two peoples of China and Albania, which are based on Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, will shine even more brightly in the common struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism, and in the common cause of socialist revolution and socialist construction in the two countries.

		Chinese Party and Government Delegation Leader, Zhou Enlai
		June 28, 1966

### 周恩来同志电谢霍查列希谢胡同志

1966-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来同志电谢霍查列希谢胡同志
    新华社地拉那二十八日电　中国党政代表团团长周恩来同志二十八日离开阿尔巴尼亚时，向阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人发出一封感谢电。电文如下：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩·霍查同志，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈·列希同志，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆·谢胡同志：
    在我们满载着阿尔巴尼亚人民对中国人民的真挚、深厚的友谊，离开你们英雄的国家的时候，我谨以中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府和中国人民的名义，对你们，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和国家领导同志们和阿尔巴尼亚人民给予我们中国党政代表团的极其热烈隆重的欢迎和亲切友好的接待，再一次表示衷心的感谢。
    祝中阿两党、两国和两国人民建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义基础上的革命友谊和战斗团结，在反对帝国主义和现代修正主义的共同斗争中，在两国社会主义革命和社会主义建设的共同事业中，放射出更加灿烂的光芒。
            中国党政代表团团长　周恩来
                一九六六年六月二十八日
