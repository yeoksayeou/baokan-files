Title: My Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending the World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd – The Chinese delegation to the World Peace Council meeting in Geneva, led by Li Chuwen and Zheng Senyu, returned to Beijing on the 22nd.

### 我代表团出席世界和理会会议后回京

1966-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我代表团出席世界和理会会议后回京
    新华社二十三日讯　由李储文、郑森禹率领的出席世界和平理事会日内瓦会议的中国代表团，已在二十二日回到北京。
