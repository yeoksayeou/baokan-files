Title: Delegation from Chinese Ministry of Petroleum Industry Arrives in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 2nd – The delegation from the Chinese Ministry of Petroleum Industry, headed by Vice Minister Tang Ke, arrived in Tirana by plane on June 2nd for a friendly visit to Albania at the invitation of the Albanian Ministry of Industry and Mining.
    The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Hamiti and Spahiu, Deputy Ministers of the Albanian Ministry of Industry and Mining, and Spase, Vice Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, among others.
    Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and Li Mingshan, Economic Counselor, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 我石油工业部代表团到地拉那

1966-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我石油工业部代表团到地拉那
    新华社地拉那二日电　由中国石油工业部副部长唐克率领的中国石油工业部代表团一行十二人，二日乘飞机到达地拉那。
    代表团是应阿尔巴尼亚工业和矿产部的邀请前来阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问的。
    代表团在机场上受到阿尔巴尼亚工矿部副部长哈米蒂和斯巴修，阿中友协副主席斯帕塞等人的热烈欢迎。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川和经济参赞李鸣山也到机场迎接。
