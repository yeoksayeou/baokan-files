Title: Record of the American Imperialist's Defeat in the Korean War
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-25
Edition: 5

    美帝侵朝战争的失败纪录
    美帝发动侵朝战争的结果，连美国侵略者自己也供认，是“美国从未遭到的最完全的失败”。
    在三年零三十二天的战争期中，美国动员了全部陆军的三分之一，空军五分之一和海军的大部分；连同附庸国家军队，投入了二百几十万兵力；花了两百亿美元以上的直接军费；运了七千三百万吨以上的作战物资到朝鲜战场。
    但是，美帝仍然被朝中人民军队打得落花流水。美国及其帮凶国家军队被歼总数为一百零九万三千八百三十九名，其中美军占三十九万七千五百四十三名（美军在这次战争中的兵力损失，超过了它在第一次世界大战中的伤亡总数——三十六万余名，比它在第二次世界大战太平洋战争中伤亡十八万一千余人多一倍以上）；美军飞机被击落击伤共一万二千二百多架，美国的各种舰艇被击沉击伤共二百五十七艘。最后，美国侵略者不得不接受停战谈判，在发动侵略的地方停了下来。美国侵朝战争的这个结果，戳穿了美国军事力量“强大无敌”的神话，完全证明了美帝国主义是外强中干的纸老虎。

Record of the American Imperialist's Defeat in the Korean War
    The result of the American imperialists launching the war of aggression against Korea, even the American aggressors themselves admit, was "the most complete failure the United States has ever encountered."
    During the war period of three years and thirty-two days, the United States mobilized one-third of its entire army, one-fifth of its air force, and most of its navy; together with the armies of its vassal states, it投入了两百万兵力; spent more than twenty billion U.S. dollars in direct military expenses; and transported more than 73 million tons of war materials to the Korean battlefield.
    However, the American imperialists were still utterly routed by the Korean and Chinese People's Armies. The total number of American and accomplice nations' troops annihilated was 1,093,839, of which 397,543 were American troops (the American troop losses in this war exceeded its total casualties in World War I -- more than 360,000, and more than double its casualties of over 181,000 in the Pacific War of World War II); a total of more than 12,200 American aircraft were shot down or damaged, and a total of 257 American ships of various types were sunk or damaged. In the end, the American aggressors had no choice but to accept armistice negotiations and stopped where they launched their aggression. The result of the American imperialist war of aggression against Korea punctured the myth of the "powerful and invincible" American military strength and fully proved that American imperialism is a paper tiger, strong in appearance but weak in reality.
