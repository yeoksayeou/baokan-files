Title: UN Force's Mandate in Cyprus Extended: Soviet and US Representatives Once Again Vote in Favor at the Security Council
Author:
Date: 1966-06-18
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 17th: The United Nations Security Council passed a resolution on the 16th, deciding to extend the mandate of the so-called "United Nations Peacekeeping Force" stationed in Cyprus for another six months, until December 26, 1966. The Soviet and American representatives once again voted together in favor. Since this UN force entered Cyprus on March 27, 1964, to interfere in the internal affairs of the country, this is the eighth time its mandate has been extended. Every time the Security Council discusses this issue, the Soviet representative has invariably voted in favor.

### 联合国部队延长驻塞浦路斯期限  苏美代表在安理会又一起投赞成票

1966-06-18
第5版()
专栏：

    联合国部队延长驻塞浦路斯期限
    苏美代表在安理会又一起投赞成票
    新华社十七日讯　纽约消息：联合国安全理事会十六日通过一项决议，决定把驻在塞浦路斯的所谓“联合国维持和平部队”的驻扎期限再延长六个月，到一九六六年十二月二十六日届满。苏联和美国代表又一次一起投了赞成票。自从联合国这支部队在一九六四年三月二十七日进驻塞浦路斯干涉这个国家的内部事务以来，这已经是第八次延长它的驻扎期限了。安理会每次讨论这个问题时，苏联代表都毫不例外地投赞成票。
