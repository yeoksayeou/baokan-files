Title: List of Personnel of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-25
Edition: 1

    Chinese Party and Government Delegation Personnel List
    Xinhua News Agency, June 24th – The list of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation going to Albania for a friendly visit:
    Head of Delegation: Zhou Enlai
  　Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
    Members: Zhao Yimin
  　Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    Qiao Guanhua
  　Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs

**Named Entities**

中国    China
阿尔巴尼亚  Albania
周恩来  Zhou Enlai
中国共产党中央委员会  Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
中华人民共和国国务院  State Council of the People's Republic of China
赵毅敏  Zhao Yimin
乔冠华  Qiao Guanhua
外交部  Ministry of Foreign Affairs
新华社  Xinhua News Agency
