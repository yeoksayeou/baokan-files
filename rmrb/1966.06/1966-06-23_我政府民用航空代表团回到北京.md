Title: Our Government Civil Aviation Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-23
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency: The Chinese Government Civil Aviation Delegation, led by Shen Tu, Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, returned to Beijing by plane on the 17th after negotiating with the French Government Civil Aviation Delegation in Paris on the issue of air traffic between the two countries and signing the Sino-French Air Transport Agreement.
    On their way to and from Paris, the delegation paid friendly visits to Pakistan, Syria, Iraq, the United Arab Republic, and other countries. Responsible persons from relevant departments, including Liu Jinping, Li Ming, and Xue Gongchuo, were at the airport to welcome the delegation. Also present at the airport to welcome them were Chayet, First Secretary of the French Embassy in China, and Richard, Commercial Counselor.

### 我政府民用航空代表团回到北京

1966-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府民用航空代表团回到北京
    新华社讯　由团长、中国民用航空总局副局长沈图率领的中国政府民用航空代表团，在巴黎同法国政府民用航空代表团就两国通航问题进行谈判并签订了中法航空交通协定后，十七日乘飞机回到北京。
    代表团在往返途中，曾在巴基斯坦、叙利亚、伊拉克、阿联等国家进行了友好访问。到机场迎接代表团的有：刘锦平、黎明、薛公绰等有关方面负责人。法国驻华使馆一等参赞沙耶和商务参赞里沙尔也到机场欢迎。
