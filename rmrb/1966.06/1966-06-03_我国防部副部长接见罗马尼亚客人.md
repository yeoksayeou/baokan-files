Title: Chinese Vice Minister of National Defense Receives Romanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-06-03
Edition: 5

    Chinese Vice Minister of National Defense Xu Guangda received Romanian guests this morning. The guests were led by Lieutenant General Nicolaescu Marin, Commander of the Artillery of the Romanian People's Army, and his wife, who are in China for vacation. The host and guests had a cordial and friendly conversation. Tonight, the General Staff Department and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a banquet to bid farewell to the Romanian guests. Deputy Chief of Staff Li Tianyou and his wife, Deputy Director Xu Liqing and his wife, and others attended the banquet. Xu Liqing and Lieutenant General Nicolaescu Marin spoke successively. They praised the increasingly consolidated and developed fighting friendship between the people and armies of China and Romania. Present at the reception and invited to the banquet were Romanian Ambassador to China Aurel Duma and Military Attaché Colonel Solescu.
    Also joining the Romanian guests tonight were Wu Kehua, Jia Ruoyu, Pan Zhenwu and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    During their vacation in China, the Romanian officers visited factories, people's communes, and military academies in Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Nanjing, and other places, and watched militia performances. They were warmly welcomed wherever they went.

### 我国防部副部长接见罗马尼亚客人

1966-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我国防部副部长接见罗马尼亚客人
    新华社二日讯　国防部副部长许光达今天上午接见了由罗马尼亚人民军炮兵司令尼考列斯库·马林中将和夫人率领的来我国休假的罗马尼亚军官及夫人。宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。今晚，中国人民解放军总参谋部和总政治部举行宴会，为罗马尼亚客人饯行。副总参谋长李天佑和夫人、副主任徐立清和夫人等出席了宴会。徐立清和尼考列斯库·马林中将先后讲话。他们颂扬中罗两国人民和两国军队之间战斗友谊日益巩固和发展。接见时在座和应邀出席宴会的，有罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马和大使馆武官索列斯库上校。
    今晚同罗马尼亚客人们欢聚的，还有吴克华、贾若瑜、潘振武等有关部门负责人。
    罗马尼亚军官们在我国休假期间，曾去广州、杭州、上海、南京等地参观了工厂、人民公社和军事院校，并观看了民兵表演。他们所到之处，都受到热烈欢迎。
