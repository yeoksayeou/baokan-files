    Above Picture: Workers at the Xiaoshan County Agricultural Machinery Repair Factory in Zhejiang Province, through studying Chairman Mao's works, have further strengthened their awareness of serving the peasants. They organized a mobile repair boat team to go to the countryside and to the doors of production teams to repair irrigation and drainage equipment, which has been welcomed by the broad masses of commune members. This is the scene of workers repairing water pumps for the Yuzhuang Production Brigade of Zhaodong Commune.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Gengxin Photo
