Title: New Malian Ambassador Calls on Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1966-06-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: Gise Tidia Ni, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Mali to the People's Republic of China, paid a visit to Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi this afternoon to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Vice Premier Chen Yi held a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

### 马里新任大使拜会陈毅副总理

1966-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    马里新任大使拜会陈毅副总理
    新华社四日讯　马里共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使吉塞·提迪亚尼，今天下午拜会陈毅副总理兼外交部长，商谈递交国书事宜。陈毅副总理同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
