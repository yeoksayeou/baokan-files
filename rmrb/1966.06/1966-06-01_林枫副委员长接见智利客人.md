Title: Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives Chilean Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, May 31st -- Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, this morning received all members of the Chilean National Technical University delegation, led by Professor Jorge Soto, Vice President and Secretary-General of the Chilean National Technical University, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the meeting were Chu Tunan, Lian Guan, and others.

### 林枫副委员长接见智利客人

1966-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    林枫副委员长接见智利客人
    新华社三十一日讯　人大常委会副委员长林枫今天上午接见由智利国立技术大学副校长兼秘书长豪尔赫·索托教授率领的智利国立技术大学代表团的全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有楚图南、连贯等。
