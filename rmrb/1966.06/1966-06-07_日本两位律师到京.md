Title: Two Japanese Lawyers Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, June 6th -- Two Japanese lawyers, Sekihara Isamu and Yokota Satoshi, who are visiting China at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 日本两位律师到京

1966-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    日本两位律师到京
    据新华社六日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的日本律师关原勇和横田聪一行二人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
