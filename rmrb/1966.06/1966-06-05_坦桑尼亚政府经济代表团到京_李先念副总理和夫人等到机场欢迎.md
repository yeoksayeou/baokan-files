Title: Tanzanian Government Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Vice Premier Li Xiannian and his wife welcome them at the airport

Author:

Date: 1966-06-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 4th – The Tanzanian government economic delegation, led by Paul Bomani, Minister of Economic Affairs and Development Planning of Tanzania, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight for a friendly visit to our country at our invitation. Mrs. Paul Bomani and Mrs. Kunabi, Executive Committee Member of the Tanzanian National Women's Federation, also arrived on the same plane tonight at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian and his wife Lin Jiamei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei and his wife Xu Hanbing, other responsible persons from relevant departments Nan Hanchen, Luo Qingchang, Lei Renmin, Yang Lin, Shen Zijiu, Le Songsheng, and more than 1,500 people from all walks of life in the capital.
    When the distinguished guests stepped off the plane and met with the welcoming crowds, people beat drums and gongs, waved colorful flags, ribbons, and bouquets, warmly welcoming the distinguished Tanzanian guests, and shouted slogans such as "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Tanzanian people!"
   "Long live the solidarity of the Asian and African people!" Young women presented flowers to Minister Bomani and his wife, as well as other distinguished Tanzanian guests. Tanzanian Ambassador to China Waziri Juma and embassy officials also went to the airport to greet them. When the delegation passed through Shanghai this afternoon, Vice Mayor of Shanghai Shi Ying and others went to the airport to welcome them. (Attached picture)
    The picture shows the distinguished guests, including Head of Delegation Paul Bomani (front, third from right), accompanied by Vice Premier Li Xiannian, meeting the welcoming crowds at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Jingde.

### 坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团到京  李先念副总理和夫人等到机场欢迎

1966-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团到京
    李先念副总理和夫人等到机场欢迎
    新华社四日讯　应邀前来我国进行友好访问的坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团，在团长、坦桑尼亚经济事务和发展计划部长保罗·博马尼率领下，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。保罗·博马尼夫人和坦桑尼亚全国妇联执委库纳比夫人，应全国妇联的邀请前来访问，也在今晚同机到达。
    前往机场欢迎的，有国务院副总理李先念和夫人林佳楣，外交部副部长姬鹏飞和夫人许寒冰，其他有关方面负责人南汉宸、罗青长、雷任民、杨琳、沈兹九、乐松生，以及首都各界群众一千五百多人。
    当贵宾们走下飞机同欢迎群众见面时，人们敲锣打鼓，挥舞着彩旗、彩带和花束，热烈欢迎坦桑尼亚贵宾，并高呼“中坦两国人民友好万岁！”
  “亚非人民团结万岁！”等口号。女青年向博马尼部长和夫人以及其他坦桑尼亚贵宾献鲜花。坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马和使馆官员也到机场迎接。代表团今天下午路过上海的时候，上海市副市长石英等曾到机场迎送。（附图片）
