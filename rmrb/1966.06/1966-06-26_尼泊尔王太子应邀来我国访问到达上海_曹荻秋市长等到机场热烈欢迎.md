Title: Nepalese Crown Prince Arrives in Shanghai for Visit to China at Invitation; Mayor Cao Diqiu and Others Warmly Welcome Him at the Airport

Author:

Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 25th.  At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai, the Crown Prince of the Kingdom of Nepal, Birendra Bir Bikram Shah Dev, arrived in Shanghai by plane this afternoon en route to Beijing for a visit to China. Accompanying the Crown Prince on his visit to China were Prince Himalaya Shah and his wife, Princess প্রিন্সেপ শাহ, along with ten others.

    Shanghai Mayor Cao Diqiu and his wife, Shi Bin, Shanghai Vice Mayor Zhang Chengzong, and over one thousand people from all walks of life in Shanghai warmly welcomed the distinguished Nepalese guests at the airport.  Zhou Min, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who had made a special trip to Shanghai to welcome the guests, was also at the airport.

    The Ambassador of the Kingdom of Nepal to China, Randhir Subba, made a special trip to Shanghai to welcome them.

    As the plane carrying the distinguished guests landed, the welcoming crowds beat drums and gongs, waved bouquets, and shouted "Oppose imperialism and old and new colonialism!" "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Nepalese people!" Cao Diqiu and his wife, along with others, stepped forward to warmly shake hands with the distinguished guests. A group of children presented flowers to the guests.

    In the evening, Cao Diqiu hosted a banquet to welcome the Crown Prince of the Kingdom of Nepal and his entourage.  In his toast at the banquet, Cao Diqiu extended a warm welcome to Crown Prince Birendra and other distinguished guests to visit China. He expressed his firm belief that this visit would make a positive contribution to the development of friendly relations between China and Nepal. In his speech, Crown Prince Birendra also expressed his belief that the historical friendship existing between Nepal and China will continue to develop.

### 尼泊尔王太子应邀来我国访问到达上海  曹荻秋市长等到机场热烈欢迎

1966-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔王太子应邀来我国访问到达上海
    曹荻秋市长等到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社上海二十五日电  应周恩来总理邀请前来我国访问的尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦，在赴北京途中今天下午乘飞机到达上海。陪同王太子一起来我国访问的有喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主等十一人。
    上海市长曹荻秋和夫人石斌，上海市副市长张承宗以及上海各界一千多人在机场热烈欢迎尼泊尔贵宾。专程来上海欢迎贵宾的外交部礼宾司副司长周敏也到机场迎接。
    尼泊尔王国驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴专程来上海迎接。
    贵宾们乘坐的飞机着陆时，欢迎的人们敲锣打鼓，挥舞花束，高呼“反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义！”“中国—尼泊尔人民友好万岁！”曹荻秋和夫人等迎上前去和贵宾热烈握手。一群少年儿童向贵宾们献花。
    晚间，曹荻秋设宴欢迎尼泊尔王国王太子及其随行人员。曹荻秋在宴会上祝酒的时候，对比兰德拉王太子等贵宾来到中国访问表示热烈欢迎，他表示深信，这次访问对发展中尼两国之间的友好关系将会作出积极贡献。比兰德拉王太子在讲话中也表示相信，尼中两国间存在着的历史性友谊必将不断得到发展。
