Title: Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Hosts Banquet for Two Japanese Delegations
Author:
Date: 1966-06-23
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch The Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Hu Yuzhi, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 20th to welcome the Japanese Socialist Party Yamagata Prefecture Activists' Visit to China Delegation and the Japanese Socialist Party Hokkaido Activists' Visit to China Delegation. Accompanying them at the banquet were relevant responsible individuals including Mei Gongbin, Luo Shuzhang, Li Jinde, Zhao Anbo, and Yong Longgui.

### 我外交学会欢宴日本两代表团

1966-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    我外交学会欢宴日本两代表团
    新华社讯　中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之二十日晚设宴欢迎日本社会党山形县活动家访华代表团和日本社会党北海道活动家访华代表团。出席宴会作陪的，有梅龚彬、罗叔章、李金德、赵安博、勇龙桂等有关方面负责人。
