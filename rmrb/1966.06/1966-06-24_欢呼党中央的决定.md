Title: Hail the Decision of the Party Central Committee
Author: Zhang Xiaochong, Zhang Pin
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 6

    Hailing the Decision of the Party Central Committee
    Zhang Xiaochong, Zhang Pin, Senior Grade One (1) Class, Beijing No. 4 Middle School
    The torrent of the Cultural Revolution,
    Is mighty and vast.
    Another clap of spring thunder,
    Shakes all directions.
    Hail our beloved Chairman Mao,
    The great Communist Party.
    Thoroughly reform the education system,
    And bury all old ideologies.
    This wise decision,
    Smashes the examination hall like the imperial examination system,
    And destroys the breeding ground for spiritual aristocrats.
    Today,
    The spring breeze sweeps across the campus,
    Everywhere is a scene of new life.
    This decision,
    Greatly enhances the ambition of revolutionary youth,
    Making us full of fighting spirit.
    Invincible Mao Zedong Thought,
    Is the source of our victory,
    Is the direction of our advancement.
    Dear Chairman Mao,
    It is you who gave us powerful ideological weapons,
    It is you who taught us to be revolutionary pioneers.
    Hail, jump,
    Our hearts are singing.
    Integrate with workers, peasants, and soldiers,
    And grow in struggle.
    Sweep away all monsters and demons,
    And march towards the bright future!

### 欢呼党中央的决定
张小冲　张品
1966-06-24
第6版()
专栏：

    欢呼党中央的决定
    北京四中高一（一）班　张小冲　张品
    文化革命的洪流，
    浩浩荡荡。
    又一声春雷，
    震动四面八方。
    欢呼敬爱的毛主席，
    伟大的共产党。
    彻底改革教育制度，
    把一切旧意识埋葬。
    这英明的决定，
    打碎了科举式的考场，
    砸烂了产生精神贵族的温床。
    今天，
    春风吹遍了校园，
    处处一片新生景象。
    这决定，
    大长了革命青年的志气，
    使我们斗志昂扬。
    战无不胜的毛泽东思想，
    是我们胜利的源泉，
    是我们前进的方向。
    亲爱的毛主席，
    是您，给了我们强大的思想武器，
    是您，教导我们做革命的闯将。
    欢呼呀，跳跃呀，
    我们的心在歌唱。
    与工农兵相结合，
    在斗争中成长。
    横扫一切牛鬼蛇神，
    奔向光辉的前方！
