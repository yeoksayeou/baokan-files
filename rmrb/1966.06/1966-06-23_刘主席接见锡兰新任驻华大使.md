Title: Chairman Liu Receives New Ceylonese Ambassador to China
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-23
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd – Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, received the new Ceylonese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China, Mr. D.B.R. Gunawardena, this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with him. Present at the reception were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and others. (Photo attached)
    June 22nd, Chairman Liu Shaoqi receives the new Ambassador of Ceylon to China, Gunawardena.
                                Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Bin photo

### 刘主席接见锡兰新任驻华大使

1966-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见锡兰新任驻华大使
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天下午接见锡兰新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使唐·本·鲁·古纳瓦德纳，同他进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有外交部副部长韩念龙等。（附图片）
    六月二十二日，刘少奇主席接见锡兰新任驻中国大使古纳瓦德纳。
                                新华社记者　张彬摄
