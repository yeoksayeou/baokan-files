Title: Albanian Youth Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-27
Edition: 5

	Albanian Youth Football Team Arrives in Beijing
	Xinhua News Agency, June 26th — At the invitation of our country, the Albanian youth football team, consisting of 24 members, arrived in Beijing by airplane this afternoon. They were led by Vice Chairman of the Albanian State Sports Committee, Anton Mazrek. They were warmly welcomed by Vice Chairman of the State Sports Commission, Li Menghua, and Deputy Director of the Ball Games Department of the State Sports Commission, Zhao Xiwu, as well as our country's football players.

	The Albanian Charge d'Affaires to our country, Melichi Babani, and others from the Albanian Embassy also came to greet them.

### 阿尔巴尼亚青年足球队到京

1966-06-27
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚青年足球队到京
    新华社二十六日讯　应邀来我国进行友好访问的阿尔巴尼亚青年足球队一行二十四人，在阿尔巴尼亚国家体委副主任安东·马兹列库的率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    客人们在机场上受到了国家体委副主任李梦华和国家体委球类司副司长赵希武，以及我国足球运动员们的热烈欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使馆临时代办梅莱契·巴巴尼等也前往迎接。
