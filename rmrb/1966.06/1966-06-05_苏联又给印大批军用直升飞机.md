Title: The Soviet Union Again Gives India a Large Number of Military Helicopters
Author:
Date: 1966-06-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch: New Delhi dispatch: Following the Soviet revisionist clique's supply of a large quantity of military supplies, transported by five Soviet ships, to the Indian reactionaries, it has recently provided India with a large number of military helicopters, "fully satisfying" India's needs.
    According to the May 28th report in the *Times of India*: The Soviet Union has now fully met India's needs for Mi-4 helicopters, which can carry at least ten passengers at a time and are extremely useful for reconnaissance, airdrops, supplies, and evacuation of the sick and wounded. The Indian Air Force has also received some Tu-124 aircraft from the Soviet Union for the air transportation of the President, Prime Minister, Minister of Defense, Minister of the Interior, and the heads of the Army, Navy, and Air Force.

### 苏联又给印大批军用直升飞机

1966-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联又给印大批军用直升飞机
    新华社三日讯　新德里消息：苏联修正主义集团紧接着向印度反动派提供由五艘苏联轮船装运的大批军用物资以后，最近又向印度提供了大批军用直升飞机，“充分满足了”印度的需要。
    据《印度时报》五月二十八日报道：苏联现在已经充分满足了印度所需要的米—4式直升飞机，这种飞机一次至少能载十个乘客，在侦察、空投、供应和撤运伤病员方面极为有用。印度空军还从苏联得到一些图—124式飞机，作为总统、总理、国防部长和内政部长以及陆海空军首脑的空中交通工具。
