Title: Government Trade Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting UAR, Zambia, and Tanzania
Author:
Date: 1966-06-03
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Chinese Government Trade Delegation, led by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after concluding its friendly visits to the United Arab Republic, the Republic of Zambia, and the United Republic of Tanzania.
    Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others were present at the airport to welcome them. The Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, Zakaria Adli Imam, and the Ambassador of the United Republic of Tanzania to China, Wazir Juma, also came to the airport to welcome them.

### 访问阿联赞比亚坦桑尼亚后  我政府贸易代表团回京

1966-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    访问阿联赞比亚坦桑尼亚后
    我政府贸易代表团回京
    新华社二日讯　由对外贸易部副部长雷任民率领的中国政府贸易代表团，结束了对阿拉伯联合共和国、赞比亚共和国和坦桑尼亚联合共和国的友好访问，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    前往机场迎接的有对外贸易部副部长李强等。阿拉伯联合共和国驻华大使查卡里亚·阿德利·伊马姆和坦桑尼亚联合共和国驻华大使瓦齐里·朱马也到机场迎接。
