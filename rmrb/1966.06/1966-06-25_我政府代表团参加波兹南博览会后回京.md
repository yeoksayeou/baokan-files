Title: Our Government Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending the Poznań Fair
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-25
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: The Chinese government delegation that participated in the 35th Poznań International Fair, led by Jia Linfang, Vice Minister of the Coal Industry, returned to Beijing today. In addition to visiting the fair, Jia Linfang also led the Chinese Scientific and Technological Delegation to participate in the 13th session of the Sino-Polish Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, and signed a protocol with the Polish side on this meeting.

### 我政府代表团参加波兹南博览会后回京

1966-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团参加波兹南博览会后回京
    新华社二十四日讯　参加第三十五届波兹南国际博览会的中国政府代表团，在煤炭工业部副部长贾林放率领下，今天回到北京。贾林放除参观了博览会外，还率领中国科学技术代表团参加了中波科学技术合作委员会第十三届会议，并同波兰方面就这次会议签订了一项议定书。
