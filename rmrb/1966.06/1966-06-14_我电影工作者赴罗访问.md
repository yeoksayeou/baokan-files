Title: Our Film Workers Visit Romania
Author:
Date: 1966-06-14
Edition: 4

    According to the executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Qiao Yi, Deputy Director of the Pearl River Film Studio, and Qian Yunda, Deputy Director of the Shanghai Animation Film Studio, left Beijing by plane today for a visit to Romania.

### 我电影工作者赴罗访问

1966-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我电影工作者赴罗访问
    新华社十三日讯　根据中罗两国文化合作协定执行计划，珠江电影制片厂副厂长乔屹、上海美术电影制片厂副导演钱运达今天乘飞机离开北京赴罗马尼亚访问。
