Title: Our Government Delegation Led by Bo Returned Home

Author:

Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 22nd. The Chinese government delegation and the scientific and technological delegation, led by Jia Linfang, Vice Minister of the Coal Industry, left Warsaw by plane on the 22nd to return home after attending the 35th Poznan International Fair and the 13th session of the Sino-Polish Joint Standing Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation.
    Among those who saw off the Chinese delegation at the airport were An. Czechowicz, Vice-Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Economic and Scientific and Technological Cooperation of the Polish Council of Ministers, and others.
    Wang Guoquan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and others also saw them off at the airport.

### 我政府代表团等离波回国

1966-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团等离波回国
    新华社华沙二十二日电　由煤炭工业部副部长贾林放率领的中国政府代表团和科学技术代表团，在参加了第三十五届波兹南国际博览会和中波科学技术合作联合常任委员会第十三届会议后，于二十二日乘飞机离开华沙回国。
    到机场欢送中国代表团的有波兰部长会议对外经济和科学技术合作委员会副主席安·切霍维奇等。
    中国驻波兰大使王国权等也到机场送行。
