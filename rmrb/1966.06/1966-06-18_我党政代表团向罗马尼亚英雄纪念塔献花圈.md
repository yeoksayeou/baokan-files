Title: Our Party and Government Delegation Lays Wreath at Romanian Heroes Monument
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 17th -- The Chinese Party and Government Delegation led by Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, laid a wreath at the "Monument to the Heroes Who Fought for the Freedom of the People and the Motherland, and for Socialism" in Bucharest on the morning of the 17th, accompanied by Emil Bodnăraș, Member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Member of the Standing Presidium of the Central Committee, and First Vice-Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers.
    The ribbon on the wreath read: "Eternal glory to the heroes who sacrificed themselves for the freedom of the Romanian people and country, and for the cause of socialism!"
    "Presented by Zhou Enlai, Head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, and all members of the delegation"
    Accompanying Comrade Zhou Enlai in laying the wreath were: Dumitru Razea, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and Deputy Minister of the Party Central Committee; Gheorghe Macovescu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Octavian Duma, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and Romanian Ambassador to China.
    Ion Gheorghe Maurer, Alternate Member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Vasile Patilineț, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party; responsible persons from the Party Central Committee and state organs; representatives of the Bucharest City People's Council; and senior generals of the armed forces greeted the distinguished Chinese guests in front of the Heroes Monument. After laying the wreath, the comrades of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation stood in silent tribute in front of the monument.
    Head of Delegation Zhou Enlai and the members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation also entered the monument and stood in silent tribute before the tombs of Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej and Dr. Petru Groza.

### 我党政代表团向罗马尼亚英雄纪念塔献花圈

1966-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我党政代表团向罗马尼亚英雄纪念塔献花圈
    新华社布加勒斯特十七日电  中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来率领的中国党政代表团，十七日上午在罗共中央执委、中央常设主席团委员、罗马尼亚部长会议第一副主席埃·波德纳拉希陪同下，向布加勒斯特“为人民、祖国的自由和社会主义而斗争的英雄纪念塔”献花圈。
    花圈的缎带上写着：“为罗马尼亚人民和国家的自由、为社会主义斗争而牺牲的英雄们永垂不朽！”
    “中国党政代表团团长周恩来及全体团员敬献”
    陪同周恩来同志献花圈的还有：罗共中央候补委员、党中央副部长杜·拉泽尔，外交部副部长乔·马科维斯库，罗共中央候补委员、罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥·杜马。
    罗共中央候补执委、部长会议副主席约·邦克，罗共中央书记瓦·帕蒂利内茨，党中央和国家机关的负责人，布加勒斯特市人民会议的代表以及武装部队的高级将领等在英雄纪念塔前迎候了中国贵宾。献花圈后，中国党政代表团同志在纪念塔前默哀。
    周恩来团长和中国党政代表团团员们并进入纪念塔内，在乔治乌—德治同志和彼·格罗查博士的陵墓前默哀。
