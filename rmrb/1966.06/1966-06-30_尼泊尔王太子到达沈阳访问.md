Title: Nepalese Crown Prince Visits Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1966-06-30
Edition: 4

	Nepalese Crown Prince Visits Shenyang

	New China News Agency, June 29th

	Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, departed Beijing by special plane this morning to visit the northeastern region of China.

	Accompanying Crown Prince Birendra Shah to the northeast were Prince Himalaia Shah and his wife Princess Prinesep Shah, as well as other Nepalese dignitaries, and Nepalese Ambassador to China, Rendra Subba.

	Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Han Nianlong, and others also accompanied the dignitaries to the northeast.

	According to New China News Agency, Shenyang, June 29th: Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, arrived in Shenyang by special plane from Beijing this morning for a visit.

	Accompanying the Crown Prince to Shenyang were Prince Himalaia Shah and his wife Princess Prinesep Shah, as well as other accompanying personnel. Nepalese Ambassador to China, Rendra Subba, also accompanied the dignitaries to Shenyang.

	Governor of Liaoning Province, Huang Oudong, Deputy Governors Che Xiangqin, Qiu Youwen, Chen Xianzhou, Gong Tianmin, Zhang Zhengde, Mayor of Shenyang Li Qing, Deputy Mayor Song Guang, and over a thousand representatives from various sectors warmly welcomed the Nepalese dignitaries at the airport. As Crown Prince Birendra Shah walked towards the welcoming crowd accompanied by Huang Oudong and others, people danced with red ribbons and beat drums to express their enthusiastic welcome to the Nepalese guests.

	In the morning, the dignitaries, accompanied by Huang Oudong and Li Qing, toured the city of Shenyang and the Tiexi Workers' Residential Area by car. In the afternoon, the dignitaries, accompanied by Huang Oudong, Qiu Youwen, Li Qing, and others, visited the Liaoning Industrial Exhibition Hall and the Shenyang Conservatory of Music.

	In the evening, Huang Oudong hosted a banquet to welcome Crown Prince Birendra Shah and his entourage. After the banquet, the hosts and guests together enjoyed a splendid performance by the Shenyang Acrobatic Troupe.

### 尼泊尔王太子到达沈阳访问

1966-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔王太子到达沈阳访问
    新华社二十九日讯　尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿由陈毅副总理陪同，今天上午乘专机离开北京前往我国东北地区参观访问。
    随同比兰德拉·沙阿王太子到东北参观的，有喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主等尼泊尔贵宾，以及尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴。
    外交部副部长韩念龙等也陪同贵宾前往东北参观。
    据新华社沈阳二十九日电尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿，今天上午在国务院副总理陈毅、外交部副部长韩念龙陪同下，由北京乘专机到达沈阳访问。
    随同王太子一起到沈阳访问的有喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主以及其他随行人员。尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴也陪同贵宾到沈阳。
    辽宁省省长黄欧东，副省长车向忱、仇友文、陈先舟、巩天民、张正德，沈阳市市长李青、副市长宋光以及各界群众一千多人在机场热烈欢迎尼泊尔贵宾。比兰德拉·沙阿王太子在机场上由黄欧东等陪同走向欢迎群众的行列时，人们跳起红绸舞，击着腰鼓，向尼泊尔贵宾们表示热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们上午由黄欧东、李青陪同乘车浏览了沈阳市容和铁西工人住宅区。下午，贵宾们由黄欧东、仇友文、李青等陪同参观了辽宁工业展览馆和沈阳音乐学院。
    晚间，黄欧东举行宴会欢迎比兰德拉·沙阿王太子及其随行人员。宴会后，宾主一起观看了沈阳杂技团的精采演出。
