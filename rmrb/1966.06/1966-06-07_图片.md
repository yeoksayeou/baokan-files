Title:  Bozhou, Anhui Province, Builds a New Fertilizer Plant
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 3

    A new chemical fertilizer plant in Bozhou County, Anhui Province, with an annual output of 15,000 tons of ammonium bicarbonate, has recently been officially put into production. This is a batch of fertilizer produced by the fertilizer plant ready to be shipped to the countryside. Photo by Wang Xingcai (Xinhua News Agency draft)
