Title: Let Us Join Hands and Fight Shoulder to Shoulder
Author:
Date: 1966-06-05
Edition: 2

    To the Proletarian Revolutionaries of Peking University:
    We, all the poor and lower-middle peasants and the broad masses of cadres, have seen your big-character posters and are extremely excited. We greatly admire your revolutionary spirit of daring to struggle and daring to win, and express our firm support for your revolutionary actions.
    We support you in continuing the struggle to resolutely overthrow the anti-Party, anti-socialist black gangs hidden in every corner until final victory is achieved. We, all the poor and lower-middle peasants, are determined to join hands with you, fight side by side, until these black gangs surrender.
    Together, we will resolutely fight to the end to defend the Party Central Committee, defend Chairman Mao, and defend socialism! We will never stop until we have won a complete victory!
                  The Entire Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants of the Xishan Brigade, Si Ji Qing Commune, Beijing

### 携起手来并肩作战

1966-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

    携起手来并肩作战
    北大无产阶级革命派：
    我们全体贫下中农和广大干部，看到了你们的大字报，心情万分激动，对你们这种敢于斗争、敢于胜利的革命精神十分敬佩，对你们这种革命行动表示坚决支持。
    我们支持你们继续斗争，坚决打倒隐藏在各个角落的反党反社会主义黑帮，直到取得最后的胜利。我们全体贫下中农决心与你们携起手来，并肩战斗，直到这些黑帮缴械投降为止。
    我们共同为保卫党中央、保卫毛主席、保卫社会主义坚决战斗到底，不获全胜，决不收兵！
                  北京四季青公社西山大队全体贫下中农
