Title: Chinese Experts Arrive in Somalia
Author:
Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 6

    Our Experts Arrive in Somalia
    (Xinhua News Agency, Mogadishu, 23rd) The first group of Chinese experts assisting Somalia in the construction of a national theater and the Chinese agricultural expert group arrived here by plane on the afternoon of the 23rd.
