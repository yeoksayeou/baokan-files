Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Invade Our Territorial Waters and Airspace in Fujian and Guangdong; We Issue the 404th Serious Warning

Author:
Date: 1966-06-17
Edition: 5

    U.S. Warships and Aircraft Invade Our Territorial Waters and Airspace in Fujian and Guangdong
    We Issue the 404th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th June: At 07:20 to 12:12 on June 16th, a U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters south of Dongshan Island, Fujian Province. On the same day, from 13:17 to 13:21, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace south of Nanao Island, Guangdong Province. Regarding these military provocations by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 404th serious warning.



**Named Entities**


福建省  Fujian Sheng (Fujian Province)
东山岛  Dongshan Dao (Dongshan Island)
广东省  Guangdong Sheng (Guangdong Province)
南澳岛  Nanao Dao (Nanao Island)
外交部  Waijiaobu (Ministry of Foreign Affairs)
新华社  Xinhua News Agency

### 美舰美机侵入我福建广东领海领空  我提出第四百零四次严重警告

1966-06-17
第5版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我福建广东领海领空
    我提出第四百零四次严重警告
    新华社十六日讯　六月十六日七时二十分至十二时十二分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省东山岛以南地区领海。同日十三时十七分至十三时二十一分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省南澳岛以南地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百零四次严重警告。
