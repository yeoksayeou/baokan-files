Title: Malian Delegation Arrives in Yan'an for Visit
Author:
Date: 1966-06-14
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 13th – The Malian delegation, led by Minister Mamadou Madeira Keita, arrived in Yan'an today morning from Hohhot City, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, by special plane for a visit, accompanied by Lai Yali, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Tu Jinzhang and Tian Enxiang, Deputy Commissioners of the Yan'an Commissioner's Office of Shaanxi Province, as well as Li Ning, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of Shaanxi Province, who came to Yan'an specifically to greet them.
    When the delegation left Hohhot this morning, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi went to the airport to see them off. Also at the airport to bid farewell were Wang Yilun and Shen Xinfa, Vice Chairmen of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

### 马里代表团到达延安访问

1966-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    马里代表团到达延安访问
    新华社延安十三日电　由马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔部长率领的马里代表团，在外交部礼宾司司长赖亚力陪同下，今天上午从内蒙古自治区呼和浩特市乘专机到达延安访问。
    到机场欢迎的有陕西省延安专员公署副专员土金璋、田恩祥，以及专程来延安迎接的陕西省外事办公室副主任李宁等。
    代表团今天上午离开呼和浩特的时候，国务院副总理陈毅曾前往机场送行。到机场欢送的还有内蒙古自治区副主席王逸伦、沈新发等。
