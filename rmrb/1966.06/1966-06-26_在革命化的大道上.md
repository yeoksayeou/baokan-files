Title: On the Road to Revolutionization

Author:
Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 5

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　阿尔巴尼亚通讯

　　在革命化的大道上
　　冲破困难胜利前进
　　在阿尔巴尼亚全国各地革命化运动的高潮中，我们访问了发罗拉列宁水泥厂。
　　在风景如画的亚得里亚海滨，一片银色的厂房在朝阳下闪烁着金灿灿的光芒。一踏进这座巨大的水泥厂，第一个突出的印象是厂房内外，分外清洁整齐，有条不紊。洁白明亮的厂房周围，一排排新植幼林，一块块碧绿的花圃和草坪，十分美观。这个厂的职工用自己辛勤的双手，打破了“水泥厂必脏”的老观念，五年如一日，保持了工厂经常清洁的光荣传统。一九六四年一月，周恩来总理访问阿尔巴尼亚时曾经参观了这个工厂。他赞扬了这个水泥厂经常保持清洁的好经验，并且指示正在帮助建设石棉管板厂的中国专家，好好学习阿尔巴尼亚同志的这方面的先进经验。阿尔巴尼亚同志说，这个工厂今春以来，在革命化措施的推动下，比以前更加清洁美观了。
　　水泥厂厂长埃特赫姆同志指着那隆隆滚动的回转窑、震耳轰鸣的粉碎机、轳轳转动的传送带向我们说：“赫鲁晓夫修正主义者企图用封锁和停止援助扼杀我们的工厂。他们对我们说，‘没有我们的帮助，你们的工厂就得关门’。但是，我们给他们的回答是，我们把它建设得更美丽了。”他告诉我们，当苏联修正主义者背信弃义，在工厂扩建中途撤走专家，并且停止供应工厂生产必需的备件和材料时，水泥厂确实经历过一段困难道路。但是，阿尔巴尼亚人民发扬了“宁愿站着死，不愿跪着生”的英雄气概和不畏强暴、不怕困难的革命志气，用自己的双手把扩建部分完成了，自力更生地制造了三百多种备件，保证了工厂机器继续运转，使赫鲁晓夫修正主义者的阴谋化成了泡影。也就从那个时候起，全厂职工就立下了把工厂建设得清洁美丽的夙愿，并且胜利地实现了。而今天，不仅水泥厂的生产乘风破浪，胜利前进，在它的旁边又建立起一座附属于这个厂的崭新的石棉管板厂。水泥厂生产的水泥经过就地加工，制成石棉管和石棉板等珍贵的建筑材料。
　　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党所采取的一系列革命化措施，在全厂职工中激起的革命热情，象春潮一样汹涌澎湃。他们提出了一项宏伟的任务：在第四个五年计划期间（一九六六年至一九七○年），超额生产三万九千吨水泥，这差不多等于水泥厂半年的生产任务。而石棉管板厂的职工提出，把原在一九六八年实现年产四十万平方米石棉板和十五万米石棉管的任务提前一年完成。此外，为了响应党提出的自力更生、大力发展农牧业、争取粮食自给的号召，四十多名家在农村的工人返回了农业生产第一线。
　　群众路线遍地开花
　　在减少人力八分之一的情况下，怎样实现这项宏伟的保证呢？这个问题在全厂职工中热烈地展开讨论。大会，小会，挖潜力，想办法，一个群众性的提倡议运动在全厂各个车间、工段进行着。全厂职工热火朝天地争论，辩论着。在很短时间里就涌现了五百二十多项建议，有二百七十项可以立即行之有效，并且马上定出了贯彻方案。
　　群众发动起来了，群众积极性的鲜花在全厂遍地开放。回转窑工长阿里夫·赫巴和他的同伴在讨论，能不能再提高两座回转窑焙烧水泥半成品——熟料的生产能力。现在两座窑的生产能力经过一再提高，已经分别达到了每小时六点四吨和六点三五吨。是不是没有潜力可挖了呢？在讨论中窑工弗尼提出了一项创议：提高窑温，降低生料湿度以提高回转窑的生产能力。于是阿里夫和弗尼立即去找生料工段工长塔尔塔里，一起研究，共同提出了一项建议：在石灰石粉和粘土加水搅拌，制成生料的过程中，把生料湿度从百分之四十五降低到百分之四十。阿里夫和弗尼还建议改变回转窑内加热焙烧生料的链锁的配置以提高窑温。这两项倡议使回转窑的生产能力可以提高百分之五，也就是说，两座窑每小时能多生产三百九十公斤熟料，而一年就可增产熟料二千多吨。提出来就干，这两项建议在三月份都已经实现了。
　　是不是可以缩短回转窑的维修时间？机修工人们也在这个生产水泥的最关键的部分上打主意。回转窑每年要进行一次中修和四次小修。中修一次需时二十天，小修一次需时七天。这样，回转窑每年要停工维修四十八天之久。机修工人经过讨论，提议把中修时间缩短两天，小修时间缩短一天，让回转窑多为人民服务六天。六天，这意味一年多生产一千八百吨熟料！二月下旬，机修工们面临着是否能兑现自己诺言的考验。他们经过事先充分讨论，合理分工，细致准备，结果，第一座窑的小修只用了五天，而三月末，第二座窑的小修才用了四天半。
　　为了使石棉管板厂提前一年达到设备生产能力的关键是提前实现两班制。然而这需要采取紧急措施培养工人。于是几十名年青女工被召来了，保教保学的保证提出来了。老工人手把手教女工们在实践中掌握技术，工程师和技术员则发扬革命精神，为学徒工义务教课，保证帮助她们成为有业务知识的工人。我们参观中看到，一群群女工虽然只经过短期学习，已经在独立地熟练地进行操作。
　　革命精神结成硕果
　　党提出的“以革命精神去劳动和生活”的号召在发罗拉列宁水泥厂掀起了一个风起云涌的革命热潮。在水泥厂的车间和工段悬着醒目的标语：“不完成任务不离开岗位！”这就是全厂职工对党的号召的回答。
　　一个阴雨天，机修工们已经换好衣服，撑起雨伞，准备回家。这时，机修组长伊利阿·穆奇突然得到通知：传送带断了，必须立即修复。这位劳动党员、先进工作者和他的三个组员立即拿起工具，奔到现场，换上了备带，开始了修理。有人劝他们，断带留到明天再修，但是他们毅然回答说：“不！我们一定要把它修好。”当他们把断带修好时，抬头一看，东方已经映照着红彤彤的曙光，朝阳正在徐徐升起，又到了他们的新的一班。工厂领导让他们回家休息，但是他们又坚决地回答说：“不！我们的任务一定要由我们来完成！”这个先进机修组坚持战斗了四班，三十六小时，实现了自己的保证。
　　埃特赫姆同志告诉我们，这仅仅是无数类似事迹中的一个例子。现在在水泥厂中只有“超产”这个词，没有完不成任务而离开岗位的人。
　　“革命化”这个词在列宁水泥厂职工的心中已经成为了一种动力。在它的推动下职工的工作、生活和精神面貌都在起着变化。在革命精神的感召下，工人主动提出取消加班费，主动找工作做；工程师自动不要业余教课津贴，争取把自己的技术知识早日传授给新工人。二十名行政干部自愿走上了生产岗位，大鼓革命干劲，仅仅在两个月期间就掌握了生产技术，现在已经能独立工作，成为了名副其实的工人。在革命精神的鼓舞下，石棉管板厂的职工星期天自动地来到工厂，维修设备，调换零件，以保证平常机器不停止转动。在革命精神的支持下，管板厂的职工研究用玻璃丝和人造棉代替石棉以减少进口。他们经过数十次失败，终于取得了最后成功。
　　在发罗拉列宁水泥厂的意气风发、斗志昂扬、蓬蓬勃勃的革命气象中，我们看到了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党坚持发扬不断革命精神，把社会主义革命进行到底而采取的一系列革命化措施，结出了丰硕的果实。

　　新华社记者

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Albanian Dispatch

　　On the Road to Revolutionization
　　Breaking Through Difficulties and Advancing Victoriously
　　Amidst the high tide of the revolutionization movement throughout Albania, we visited the Vlora Lenin Cement Plant.
　　On the picturesque Adriatic coast, a silver-colored factory building shimmered with golden light in the morning sun. Stepping into this enormous cement plant, the first striking impression was the extraordinary cleanliness and orderliness inside and outside the factory buildings. Surrounding the gleaming white factory buildings were rows of newly planted saplings and patches of lush green flowerbeds and lawns, which were very beautiful. The factory workers, with their diligent hands, broke the old notion that "cement factories must be dirty," and maintained the glorious tradition of keeping the factory clean at all times, day after day for five years. In January 1964, Premier Zhou Enlai visited this factory during his visit to Albania. He praised the good experience of this cement plant in maintaining cleanliness at all times, and instructed the Chinese experts who were helping to build the asbestos pipe and board factory to learn well from the advanced experience of the Albanian comrades in this regard. Albanian comrades said that since this spring, driven by the measures for revolutionization, this factory has become even cleaner and more beautiful than before.
　　Comrade Ethem, the director of the cement plant, pointed to the rumbling rotary kilns, the deafening crushers, and the constantly turning conveyor belts and told us: "The Khrushchev revisionists attempted to strangle our factory through blockade and the cessation of aid. They said to us, 'Without our help, your factory will have to close down.' However, our answer to them is that we have built it even more beautifully." He told us that when the Soviet revisionists broke their promises, withdrew their experts midway through the factory expansion, and stopped supplying the spare parts and materials necessary for the factory's production, the cement plant did indeed experience a difficult period. However, the Albanian people displayed the heroic spirit of "Better to die standing than live on one's knees" and the revolutionary determination to defy the powerful and not fear difficulties, completed the expansion with their own hands, manufactured more than 300 kinds of spare parts through self-reliance, ensured the continued operation of the factory's machinery, and turned the Khrushchev revisionists' conspiracy into a bubble. It was from that time that all the factory workers made a long-cherished wish to build the factory clean and beautiful, and they have victoriously realized it. And today, not only is the cement plant's production advancing victoriously against the wind and waves, but a brand new asbestos pipe and board factory affiliated with this plant has also been built next to it. The cement produced by the cement plant is processed locally to produce valuable building materials such as asbestos pipes and asbestos boards.
　　A series of revolutionization measures taken by the Albanian Party of Labour aroused revolutionary enthusiasm among all the factory workers, like a surging spring tide. They put forward a grand task: during the Fourth Five-Year Plan period (1966 to 1970), to overproduce 39,000 tons of cement, which is almost equal to half a year's production task for the cement plant. The workers of the asbestos pipe and board factory proposed to complete the original task of achieving an annual output of 400,000 square meters of asbestos board and 150,000 meters of asbestos pipe in 1968 one year ahead of schedule. In addition, in response to the Party's call for self-reliance, vigorous development of agriculture and animal husbandry, and striving for self-sufficiency in grain, more than 40 workers whose families lived in rural areas returned to the front line of agricultural production.
　　The Mass Line Blossoms Everywhere
　　How can this grand guarantee be realized while reducing the workforce by one-eighth? This question was hotly debated among all the factory workers. Large meetings, small meetings, tapping potential, finding solutions – a mass movement to propose ideas was underway in every workshop and section of the factory. The entire factory was abuzz with lively discussions and debates. In a very short period of time, more than 520 suggestions emerged, and 270 of them could be implemented immediately and effectively, and implementation plans were immediately formulated.
　　The masses have been mobilized, and the flowers of mass initiative are blooming everywhere in the factory. Arif Hiba, the foreman of the rotary kiln, and his colleagues were discussing whether they could further increase the production capacity of the two rotary kilns that bake cement semi-finished products – clinker. The production capacity of the two kilns has been repeatedly increased and has reached 6.4 tons and 6.35 tons per hour, respectively. Is there no more potential to be tapped? In the discussion, kiln worker Frani proposed an initiative: increase the kiln temperature and reduce the humidity of the raw material to increase the production capacity of the rotary kiln. So Arif and Frani immediately went to Tartari, the foreman of the raw material section, to study together and jointly proposed a suggestion: in the process of mixing limestone powder and clay with water to make raw material, reduce the humidity of the raw material from 45 percent to 40 percent. Arif and Frani also suggested changing the configuration of the chain lock inside the rotary kiln that heats and bakes the raw material to increase the kiln temperature. These two initiatives could increase the production capacity of the rotary kiln by 5 percent, that is, the two kilns could produce 390 kilograms more clinker per hour, and the annual output of clinker could be increased by more than 2,000 tons. What is proposed is done, and these two suggestions have already been implemented in March.
　　Is it possible to shorten the maintenance time of the rotary kiln? The mechanics are also racking their brains on this most critical part of cement production. The rotary kiln needs to undergo one medium repair and four minor repairs every year. A medium repair takes 20 days, and a minor repair takes 7 days. In this way, the rotary kiln has to be shut down for maintenance for as long as 48 days a year. After discussion, the mechanics proposed to shorten the medium repair time by two days and the minor repair time by one day, so that the rotary kiln can serve the people for six more days. Six days, which means producing 1,800 tons more clinker a year! In late February, the mechanics faced the test of whether they could deliver on their promise. After full discussion, reasonable division of labor, and meticulous preparation in advance, the minor repair of the first kiln took only five days, and the minor repair of the second kiln only took four and a half days at the end of March.
　　In order for the asbestos pipe and board factory to reach the equipment production capacity one year ahead of schedule, the key is to achieve a two-shift system ahead of schedule. However, this requires taking emergency measures to train workers. So dozens of young female workers were called in, and guarantees of teaching and learning were put forward. Old workers taught the female workers hand-in-hand to master the technology in practice, and engineers and technicians carried forward the revolutionary spirit, teaching apprentice workers voluntarily, and ensuring that they would help them become workers with professional knowledge. During our visit, we saw that groups of female workers, although they had only undergone short-term study, were already operating independently and skillfully.
　　Revolutionary Spirit Bears Fruit
　　The Party's call to "work and live with revolutionary spirit" has set off a surging revolutionary upsurge in the Vlora Lenin Cement Plant. Conspicuous slogans are hung in the workshops and sections of the cement plant: "Do not leave the post without completing the task!" This is the answer of all the factory workers to the Party's call.
　　On a cloudy and rainy day, the mechanics had already changed their clothes, put up their umbrellas, and were ready to go home. At this time, Ilia Muqi, the leader of the mechanical repair team, suddenly received a notice: the conveyor belt was broken and must be repaired immediately. This labor party member, advanced worker, and his three team members immediately picked up their tools, rushed to the scene, replaced the spare belt, and began repairs. Someone advised them to leave the broken belt to be repaired tomorrow, but they resolutely replied: "No! We must fix it." When they fixed the broken belt, they looked up and saw that the east was already reflecting a red dawn, the morning sun was slowly rising, and it was time for their new shift. The factory leaders asked them to go home to rest, but they firmly replied: "No! Our task must be completed by us!" This advanced mechanical repair team persisted in fighting for four shifts, thirty-six hours, and realized their guarantee.
　　Comrade Ethem told us that this is just one example among countless similar deeds. Now in the cement plant, there is only the word "overproduction," and no one leaves the post without completing the task.
　　The word "revolutionization" has become a driving force in the hearts of the workers of the Lenin Cement Plant. Under its impetus, the workers' work, life, and mental outlook are all changing. Inspired by the revolutionary spirit, workers voluntarily propose to cancel overtime pay and actively seek work; engineers voluntarily refuse part-time teaching subsidies and strive to pass on their technical knowledge to new workers as soon as possible. Twenty administrative cadres voluntarily took up production posts, greatly boosting revolutionary energy, and mastered production technology in just two months. Now they can work independently and become genuine workers. Encouraged by the revolutionary spirit, the workers of the asbestos pipe and board factory automatically came to the factory on Sundays to repair equipment and replace parts to ensure that the machines would not stop running on weekdays. Supported by the revolutionary spirit, the workers of the pipe and board factory studied the use of glass fiber and rayon instead of asbestos to reduce imports. After dozens of failures, they finally achieved final success.
　　In the spirited, high-spirited, and thriving revolutionary atmosphere of the Vlora Lenin Cement Plant, we have seen that the Albanian Party of Labour has adhered to and carried forward the spirit of continuous revolution, and the series of revolutionization measures taken to carry the socialist revolution through to the end have yielded fruitful results.

　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter

    在风景如画的亚得里亚海滨，一片银色的厂房在朝阳下闪烁着金灿灿的光芒。一踏进这座巨大的水泥厂，第一个突出的印象是厂房内外，分外清洁整齐，有条不紊。洁白明亮的厂房周围，一排排新植幼林，一块块碧绿的花圃和草坪，十分美观。这个厂的职工用自己辛勤的双手，打破了“水泥厂必脏”的老观念，五年如一日，保持了工厂经常清洁的光荣传统。一九六四年一月，周恩来总理访问阿尔巴尼亚时曾经参观了这个工厂。他赞扬了这个水泥厂经常保持清洁的好经验，并且指示正在帮助建设石棉管板厂的中国专家，好好学习阿尔巴尼亚同志的这方面的先进经验。阿尔巴尼亚同志说，这个工厂今春以来，在革命化措施的推动下，比以前更加清洁美观了。
    水泥厂厂长埃特赫姆同志指着那隆隆滚动的回转窑、震耳轰鸣的粉碎机、轳轳转动的传送带向我们说：“赫鲁晓夫修正主义者企图用封锁和停止援助扼杀我们的工厂。他们对我们说，‘没有我们的帮助，你们的工厂就得关门’。但是，我们给他们的回答是，我们把它建设得更美丽了。”他告诉我们，当苏联修正主义者背信弃义，在工厂扩建中途撤走专家，并且停止供应工厂生产必需的备件和材料时，水泥厂确实经历过一段困难道路。但是，阿尔巴尼亚人民发扬了“宁愿站着死，不愿跪着生”的英雄气概和不畏强暴、不怕困难的革命志气，用自己的双手把扩建部分完成了，自力更生地制造了三百多种备件，保证了工厂机器继续运转，使赫鲁晓夫修正主义者的阴谋化成了泡影。也就从那个时候起，全厂职工就立下了把工厂建设得清洁美丽的夙愿，并且胜利地实现了。而今天，不仅水泥厂的生产乘风破浪，胜利前进，在它的旁边又建立起一座附属于这个厂的崭新的石棉管板厂。水泥厂生产的水泥经过就地加工，制成石棉管和石棉板等珍贵的建筑材料。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党所采取的一系列革命化措施，在全厂职工中激起的革命热情，象春潮一样汹涌澎湃。他们提出了一项宏伟的任务：在第四个五年计划期间（一九六六年至一九七○年），超额生产三万九千吨水泥，这差不多等于水泥厂半年的生产任务。而石棉管板厂的职工提出，把原在一九六八年实现年产四十万平方米石棉板和十五万米石棉管的任务提前一年完成。此外，为了响应党提出的自力更生、大力发展农牧业、争取粮食自给的号召，四十多名家在农村的工人返回了农业生产第一线。
    群众路线遍地开花
    在减少人力八分之一的情况下，怎样实现这项宏伟的保证呢？这个问题在全厂职工中热烈地展开讨论。大会，小会，挖潜力，想办法，一个群众性的提倡议运动在全厂各个车间、工段进行着。全厂职工热火朝天地争论，辩论着。在很短时间里就涌现了五百二十多项建议，有二百七十项可以立即行之有效，并且马上定出了贯彻方案。
    群众发动起来了，群众积极性的鲜花在全厂遍地开放。回转窑工长阿里夫·赫巴和他的同伴在讨论，能不能再提高两座回转窑焙烧水泥半成品——熟料的生产能力。现在两座窑的生产能力经过一再提高，已经分别达到了每小时六点四吨和六点三五吨。是不是没有潜力可挖了呢？在讨论中窑工弗尼提出了一项创议：提高窑温，降低生料湿度以提高回转窑的生产能力。于是阿里夫和弗尼立即去找生料工段工长塔尔塔里，一起研究，共同提出了一项建议：在石灰石粉和粘土加水搅拌，制成生料的过程中，把生料湿度从百分之四十五降低到百分之四十。阿里夫和弗尼还建议改变回转窑内加热焙烧生料的链锁的配置以提高窑温。这两项倡议使回转窑的生产能力可以提高百分之五，也就是说，两座窑每小时能多生产三百九十公斤熟料，而一年就可增产熟料二千多吨。提出来就干，这两项建议在三月份都已经实现了。
    是不是可以缩短回转窑的维修时间？机修工人们也在这个生产水泥的最关键的部分上打主意。回转窑每年要进行一次中修和四次小修。中修一次需时二十天，小修一次需时七天。这样，回转窑每年要停工维修四十八天之久。机修工人经过讨论，提议把中修时间缩短两天，小修时间缩短一天，让回转窑多为人民服务六天。六天，这意味一年多生产一千八百吨熟料！二月下旬，机修工们面临着是否能兑现自己诺言的考验。他们经过事先充分讨论，合理分工，细致准备，结果，第一座窑的小修只用了五天，而三月末，第二座窑的小修才用了四天半。
    为了使石棉管板厂提前一年达到设备生产能力的关键是提前实现两班制。然而这需要采取紧急措施培养工人。于是几十名年青女工被召来了，保教保学的保证提出来了。老工人手把手教女工们在实践中掌握技术，工程师和技术员则发扬革命精神，为学徒工义务教课，保证帮助她们成为有业务知识的工人。我们参观中看到，一群群女工虽然只经过短期学习，已经在独立地熟练地进行操作。
    革命精神结成硕果
    党提出的“以革命精神去劳动和生活”的号召在发罗拉列宁水泥厂掀起了一个风起云涌的革命热潮。在水泥厂的车间和工段悬着醒目的标语：“不完成任务不离开岗位！”这就是全厂职工对党的号召的回答。
    一个阴雨天，机修工们已经换好衣服，撑起雨伞，准备回家。这时，机修组长伊利阿·穆奇突然得到通知：传送带断了，必须立即修复。这位劳动党员、先进工作者和他的三个组员立即拿起工具，奔到现场，换上了备带，开始了修理。有人劝他们，断带留到明天再修，但是他们毅然回答说：“不！我们一定要把它修好。”当他们把断带修好时，抬头一看，东方已经映照着红彤彤的曙光，朝阳正在徐徐升起，又到了他们的新的一班。工厂领导让他们回家休息，但是他们又坚决地回答说：“不！我们的任务一定要由我们来完成！”这个先进机修组坚持战斗了四班，三十六小时，实现了自己的保证。
    埃特赫姆同志告诉我们，这仅仅是无数类似事迹中的一个例子。现在在水泥厂中只有“超产”这个词，没有完不成任务而离开岗位的人。
    “革命化”这个词在列宁水泥厂职工的心中已经成为了一种动力。在它的推动下职工的工作、生活和精神面貌都在起着变化。在革命精神的感召下，工人主动提出取消加班费，主动找工作做；工程师自动不要业余教课津贴，争取把自己的技术知识早日传授给新工人。二十名行政干部自愿走上了生产岗位，大鼓革命干劲，仅仅在两个月期间就掌握了生产技术，现在已经能独立工作，成为了名副其实的工人。在革命精神的鼓舞下，石棉管板厂的职工星期天自动地来到工厂，维修设备，调换零件，以保证平常机器不停止转动。在革命精神的支持下，管板厂的职工研究用玻璃丝和人造棉代替石棉以减少进口。他们经过数十次失败，终于取得了最后成功。
    在发罗拉列宁水泥厂的意气风发、斗志昂扬、蓬蓬勃勃的革命气象中，我们看到了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党坚持发扬不断革命精神，把社会主义革命进行到底而采取的一系列革命化措施，结出了丰硕的果实。
