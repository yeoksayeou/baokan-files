Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Osaka Prefecture Federation Holds Membership Meeting, Warmly Welcomes the Guangdong Province Friendship Delegation of China
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-14
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 13th—The Japan-China Friendship Association Osaka Prefecture Federation held a membership meeting on the 11th to warmly welcome the Chinese Guangdong Province Friendship Delegation led by Zeng Sheng.

　　President of the Osaka Prefecture Japan-China Friendship Association and President of Osaka University of Foreign Studies, Jiro Kaneko, said in his speech that the Japanese people eagerly hope for friendship with the Chinese people and are striving to restore diplomatic relations between Japan and China. Kaneko stated that no matter how difficult the situation, the Japanese people will never succumb to the enemy's plot to undermine Japan-China friendship.

　　Head of the Delegation, Zeng Sheng, then spoke. He said that U.S. imperialism is the common enemy of the Chinese and Japanese people. He pointed out that the Japanese people's great struggle to demand the recovery of Okinawa and the Ogasawara Islands, to oppose U.S. imperialism establishing military bases in Japan, and to oppose U.S. nuclear submarines entering Japanese ports, is a support for the Chinese people.

　　Zeng Sheng said that the Chinese people are holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and are working hard to build socialism. The Chinese people must drive U.S. imperialism out of Taiwan.

　　Zeng Sheng concluded by saying that the flames of anger ignited by the Chinese and Japanese people will surely burn U.S. imperialism and its running dogs to death.

　　After the meeting, members of the Japan-China Friendship Association performed national dances of China and Japan and sang revolutionary songs.

　　Prior to this, the Osaka City League of Local Assembly Members for Promoting International Trade and the Osaka Prefecture Council of Leagues of Local Assembly Members for Promoting International Trade hosted banquets for the Chinese Guangdong Province Friendship Delegation on the mornings and evenings of the 9th, respectively. Mayor of Osaka City, Kaoru Nakama, and Speaker of the Osaka Prefectural Assembly, Nobuo Nakai, also attended these two banquets.

　　The delegation also visited Sakai City in Osaka Prefecture, Kobe City, and Nara Prefecture. Wherever the delegation went, it was warmly welcomed by friendly people from all walks of life and local overseas Chinese.

### 日中友协大阪府联合会举行会员大会  热烈欢迎我广东省友好代表团

1966-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协大阪府联合会举行会员大会
    热烈欢迎我广东省友好代表团
    新华社东京十三日电　日中友好协会大阪府联合会十一日举行会员大会，热烈欢迎由曾生率领的中国广东省友好代表团。
    大阪府日中友好协会会长、大阪外国语大学校长金子二郎在会上致词说，日本人民热望同中国人民友好，并且为争取恢复日中两国的邦交而努力。金子表示，不管局势怎样困难，日本人民决不会屈服于敌人破坏日中友好的阴谋。
    曾生团长接着讲话。他说美帝国主义是中日两国人民的共同敌人。他指出日本人民要求收复冲绳和小笠原群岛，反对美帝国主义在日本设立军事基地，反对美国核潜艇开进日本港口的伟大斗争，是对中国人民的支持。
    曾生说，中国人民正高举毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，努力建设社会主义，中国人民一定要把美帝国主义从台湾赶走。
    曾生最后说，中国人民和日本人民燃起的怒火，定将烧死美帝国主义及其走狗。
    会后，日中友协的会员们表演了中日两国的民族舞蹈，歌唱了革命歌曲。
    在这以前，大阪市促进国际贸易地方议员联盟、大阪府促进国际贸易地方议员联盟协议会分别于九日上午和晚上宴请中国广东省友好代表团。大阪市市长中马馨和大阪府议会议长中井信夫也出席了这两次宴会。
    代表团还访问了大阪府堺市、神户市和奈良县。代表团所到的地方，都受到各界友好人士和当地旅日华侨的热烈欢迎。
