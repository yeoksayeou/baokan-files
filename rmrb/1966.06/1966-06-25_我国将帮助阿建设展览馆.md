Title: China Will Help Algeria Build an Exhibition Hall
Author:
Date: 1966-06-25
Edition: 5

    China will help Algeria build an exhibition hall in the capital to be used for holding national exhibitions and the Algiers International Fair.
    The Chinese expert group, which arrived here last September, has formulated the design plan for the exhibition hall in cooperation with Algerian technicians. The minutes of the talks on this construction project were signed on the afternoon of the 20th. Huang Bingwen, the leader of the Chinese expert group, signed on behalf of the Chinese side, and Larabi, the president of the Algiers Chamber of Commerce, signed on behalf of the Algerian side.
    The total construction area of ​​the exhibition hall will be more than 36,000 square meters.

### 我国将帮助阿建设展览馆

1966-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我国将帮助阿建设展览馆
    据新华社阿尔及尔电　中国将帮助阿尔及利亚在首都建设一座展览馆，以便用来举办全国性的展览会和阿尔及尔国际博览会。
    去年九月到达这里的中国专家组，在阿尔及利亚技术人员的合作下，已经制定了这个展览馆的设计方案。关于这项建设工程的会谈纪要已在二十日下午签字。代表中国方面签字的是中国专家组组长黄炳文，代表阿尔及利亚方面签字的是阿尔及尔商会会长拉腊比。
    这座展览馆的建筑面积总计为三万六千多平方米。
