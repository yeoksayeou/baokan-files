Title: Sino-Romanian Party and Government Delegations Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 1

    Sino-Romanian Party and Government Delegations Continue Talks
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 23rd - The Chinese Party and Government Delegation and the Romanian Party and Government Delegation continued formal talks this morning, June 23rd, at the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Building.
    The talks were conducted in a comradely and sincere atmosphere.


**Named Entities**

中国    Zhōngguó (China)
罗马尼亚  Luómǎníyà (Romania)
新华社  Xīnhuá shè (Xinhua News Agency)
布加勒斯特 Bùjiālèisītè (Bucharest)
中国党政代表团 Zhōngguó dǎngzhèng dàibiǎotuán (Chinese Party and Government Delegation)
罗马尼亚党政代表团 Luómǎníyà dǎngzhèng dàibiǎotuán (Romanian Party and Government Delegation)
罗共中央委员会大厦 Luó gòng zhōngyāng wěiyuánhuì dàshà (Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Building)

### 中罗党政代表团继续会谈

1966-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗党政代表团继续会谈
    新华社布加勒斯特二十三日电　中国党政代表团和罗马尼亚党政代表团二十三日上午，在罗共中央委员会大厦继续举行正式会谈。
    会谈是在同志式的诚挚气氛中进行的。
