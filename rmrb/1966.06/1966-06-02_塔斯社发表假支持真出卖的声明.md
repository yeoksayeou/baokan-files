Title: TASS Publishes a Statement of False Support and Real Betrayal
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-02
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st—Following the new provocative actions by U.S. imperialism against the Cuban people, the Soviet TASS published a statement on May 29th that falsely supports but truly betrays the Cuban people, loudly clamoring for the need to quickly realize the "normalization of the situation in the Cuban region."
    The statement hypocritically expresses support for the Cuban people, repeatedly calling itself a "loyal and reliable friend" of the Cuban people, but does not mention the five demands of the Cuban people to safeguard their independence and sovereignty. The entire statement does not contain the words "U.S. imperialism." The central content of the statement is:
    "The new provocations of the American aggressive cliques against the Republic of Cuba" "once again demonstrate the need to quickly realize the just demands of the Cuban people and government regarding the normalization of the situation in the Cuban region."

### 塔斯社发表假支持真出卖的声明

1966-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    塔斯社发表假支持真出卖的声明
    新华社一日讯　在美帝国主义对古巴人民进行新的挑衅事件发生以后，苏联塔斯社五月二十九日发表了一个对古巴人民假支持、真出卖的声明，大喊什么必须尽快实现“古巴地区局势正常化”。
    声明假惺惺地对古巴人民表示支持，口口声声自称是古巴人民的“忠实可靠的朋友”，但是根本不提支持古巴人民维护独立和主权的五项要求。声明通篇没有美帝国主义字样。声明的中心内容是：
    “美国侵略集团对古巴共和国的新的挑衅”“再次说明必须尽快实现古巴人民和政府的关于使古巴地区局势正常化的正义要求。”
