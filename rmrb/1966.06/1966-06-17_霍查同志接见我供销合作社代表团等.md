Title: Comrade Hoxha Receives Delegation from Chinese Supply and Marketing Cooperative, etc.
Author:
Date: 1966-06-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana -- Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, received here on the 14th all members of the Chinese Supply and Marketing Cooperative Delegation, the Chinese Track and Field Team, and the Chinese Agricultural Delegation, and had a very cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    When Comrade Hoxha received the Chinese Supply and Marketing Cooperative Delegation and the Chinese Track and Field Team at noon on the 14th, present were: Politburo member and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor, Alia; Central Committee member Shtulla; Chairman of the Central Union of Consumer Cooperatives, Fega; and Chairman of the Sports Commission, Doda.
    When Comrade Hoxha received the Chinese Agricultural Delegation on the afternoon of the 14th, present were: Politburo member and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor, Kambo; Central Committee member Shtulla; and Minister of Agriculture, Dodbiba.
    When Comrade Hoxha received the Chinese guests this day, Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, was also present.

### 霍查同志接见我供销合作社代表团等

1966-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    霍查同志接见我供销合作社代表团等
    新华社地拉那电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查十四日在这里接见了中国供销合作社代表团、中国田径队和中国农业代表团的全体成员，并同他们进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
    霍查同志十四日中午接见中国供销合作社代表团和中国田径队时，在座的有：劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记阿利雅，中央委员什图拉，消费合作社中央联盟主席费加和体育运动委员会主席多达。
    霍查同志十四日下午接见中国农业代表团时，在座的有：劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记卡博，中央委员什图拉和农业部长多德比巴。
    霍查同志这一天接见中国客人时，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川也在座。
