Title: Our Government Cultural Friendship Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1966-06-20
Edition: 6

    Our Government Cultural Friendship Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting the Middle East
    (Xinhua News Agency, 18th) The Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation, which visited Syria, the United Arab Republic, Yemen, and Iraq, returned to Beijing by plane on the 17th, led by Deputy Head Chen Gongqi, after concluding its visits to the four countries.
    Greeting them at the airport were the head of the delegation, Li Chang, who had returned earlier, as well as responsible comrades from relevant departments, including Chen Zhongjing, Gu Wenhua, Cao Shenggong, and Wang Xianzong. Diplomatic officials from the Syrian, UAR, and Iraqi embassies in China were also at the airport to welcome them.

### 我政府文化友好代表团访中东后回京

1966-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府文化友好代表团访中东后回京
    新华社十八日讯　前往叙利亚、阿联、也门、伊拉克进行友好访问的中国政府文化友好代表团结束对四国的访问以后，在副团长陈公琪率领下，十七日乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的，有先期回国的代表团团长李昌以及有关方面负责人陈忠经、顾文华、曹胜功、王显宗等。叙利亚、阿联、伊拉克等国驻中国大使馆的外交官员也到机场迎接。
