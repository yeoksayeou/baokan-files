Title: World People Praise Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1966-06-01
Edition: 4

    World People Praise Mao Zedong Thought
    Chairman Mao's brilliant thesis that imperialism and all reactionaries are paper tigers is not only the weapon of victorious struggle for the Chinese people, but also the weapon of victorious struggle for the Lao people and the people of the world. We heard this sentence very early and learned to use it. The road of struggle over the past fourteen years has fully demonstrated that the seemingly powerful imperialists and reactionaries want to eliminate us at all times, but they are not terrible. Our struggle is just.
            -- General Singapo, Representative of the Supreme Command of the Lao People's Liberation Army

    Mao Zedong's words are a guide for Asia, Africa, Latin America, and all oppressed people and oppressed nations. Without Mao Zedong Thought, it would be impossible to defeat imperialism and revisionism, and it would be impossible to build socialism in China.
              -- T.N. Nadongge, Head of the Delegation of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation

    To understand contemporary reality, one must first understand the pinnacle of Marxism-Leninism. We must study and absorb the theories and viewpoints put forward by Mao Zedong, because it is a guide for us to clearly understand problems when US imperialism and modern revisionism create chaos, a key to solving problems, and a weapon for promoting the revolutionization of the masses.
                -- Floreal Cavallo, a well-known Argentine ophthalmologist

    Mao Zedong is a great and wise leader. Everyone who wants to understand the truth should read Chairman Mao Zedong's books and listen to Chairman Mao Zedong's words.
    I like to read Chairman Mao Zedong's works because Chairman Mao Zedong's works are the truth. I was inspired after reading it.
      -- Ali Mdaohoma Mmlinda, Head of the Workers' Delegation of the Zanzibar Afro-Shirazi Party

    The precious works of the great leader of the Chinese people will guide me directly to the road of Marxism-Leninism. Comrade Mao Zedong is a great man. Who in the world dares to deny this fact? He has made immeasurable contributions to his motherland and the people of the world. I am very interested in Chinese revolutionary publications. I admire China, its people, and its strong leaders. In the future, I will read Comrade Mao Zedong's works more and better.
    Long live Mao Zedong! -- Mauritius Su Gubiewei

    Many experiences can be learned from Volume 4 of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," which are very valuable for revolutionary and construction work in Africa. -- Guinea Yalo Lelu Ma

    The "Selected Military Writings of Mao Zedong" is a powerful backing for the people who dare to fight against imperialism and reactionaries. Our African students appreciate this book very much.
                                      -- Togo D. Emmanu

    Mao Zedong Thought is the most valuable contribution to the revolutionary cause. Studying and applying his theories will greatly benefit the anti-imperialist struggle.
                      -- Dominican Republic Sul Belles Penia

    Due to Mao Zedong and other Chinese leaders defending the truth indicated by Marx and Lenin, it aroused my deepest and most sincere admiration for them.
                                      -- Chile Ma. Liles

    Comrades, it is not surprising that I call Comrade Mao Zedong the leader of the world proletariat, because that is the fact. All true Marxists-Leninists and all true proletarian revolutionaries regard Comrade Mao Zedong, the great organizer and leader of the Chinese revolution, as their recognized leader. -- Greece Mi. Ostasigis

    Chairman Mao is a contemporary Marxist-Leninist theorist. His writings use clear, vivid, and simple language to speak out Marxist-Leninist truths, making them understandable to everyone. This is because Chairman Mao is a true people's leader who emerged from the people. Chairman Mao's writings have armed and cultivated revolutionary youth with revolutionary theory, and this is the case in Puerto Rico, in Latin America, and in the world. -- Puerto Rico La Beier

### 世界人民歌颂毛泽东思想

1966-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    世界人民歌颂毛泽东思想
    毛泽东主席关于帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎的英明论断，不仅是中国人民的胜利斗争武器，也是老挝人民和世界人民的胜利斗争武器。我们很早就听到这句话，而且学会运用它。十四年来的斗争道路充分表明，貌似强大的帝国主义和反动派每时每刻都想消灭我们，但是他们并不可怕。我们的斗争是正义的。
            ——老挝人民解放军最高指挥部代表辛加坡将军
    毛泽东的话是亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲和一切被压迫人民和被压迫民族的指针。没有毛泽东思想就不可能战胜帝国主义和修正主义，也不可能在中国国内建设社会主义。
              ——锡兰工会联合会代表团团长蒂·恩·纳东格
    要了解当代现实，首先要了解马克思列宁主义的顶峰。我们要很好地学习和吸取毛泽东所提出来的理论和观点，因为它是美帝国主义、现代修正主义制造混乱时引导我们清楚地认识问题的指南，是解决问题的钥匙和促进群众革命化的武器。
                ——阿根廷著名眼科专家弗洛雷阿尔·卡瓦略
    毛泽东是一位伟大、英明的领袖。每一个想了解真理的人都应该读毛泽东主席的书，听毛泽东主席的话。
    我喜欢阅读毛泽东主席的著作，因为毛泽东主席的著作是真理。我读了以后，得到了鼓舞。
      ——桑给巴尔非洲设拉子党工人部代表团团长
                                  阿里·姆达霍马·姆林达
    中国人民伟大领袖的宝贵作品将引导我直接走向马列主义的道路。毛泽东同志是一位伟大的人物，世上有谁敢否认这一事实呢？他已为他的祖国和全世界人民做出不可估量的贡献。我对中国革命的出版物是十分感兴趣的，我羡慕中国、她的人民及其坚强的领袖们。今后，我要更多更好地阅读毛泽东同志的著作。
    毛泽东万岁！——毛里求斯　苏·古毕威
    从《毛泽东选集》第四卷里可以学到许多经验，这些经验对非洲的革命和建设工作是非常宝贵的。——几内亚　亚洛莱鲁马
    《毛泽东军事文选》是敢于进行反对帝国主义和反动派斗争的人民的有力后盾，我们非洲学生对这部著作十分赞赏。
                                      ——多哥　D·艾曼努
    毛泽东思想是对革命事业最宝贵的贡献，研究和运用他的学说，将大大有利于反帝斗争。
                      ——多米尼加　苏尔·贝列斯·培尼亚
    由于毛泽东及其他中国领导人捍卫了马克思和列宁所指示的真理，激起了我对他们最深切的和由衷的敬佩。
                                      ——智利　玛·利莱斯
    同志们，我称毛泽东同志为世界无产阶级的领袖是不足为奇的，因为事实就是如此。一切真正的马克思列宁主义者，所有的真正的无产阶级革命者都把中国革命的伟大组织者和领导者——毛泽东同志看成自己公认的领袖。——希腊　米·奥斯塔西吉斯
    毛主席是当代一位马克思列宁主义理论家。他的著作鲜明、生动、朴素的语言，说出了马克思列宁主义真理，使一切人都懂得。这是因为毛主席是从人民群众中产生的真正的人民领袖。毛主席著作用革命理论武装和培养了革命青年，在波多黎各，在拉丁美洲，在全世界都是这样。——波多黎各　拉贝尔
