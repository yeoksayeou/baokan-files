Title: Vietnamese Acrobat Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 12th – The Acrobat Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Đoàn trưởng [Head of Delegation] Su Hai, arrived in Beijing today by special train from Wuhan at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit and perform in our country.
    Those who welcomed them at the station included Yang Chun, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Sun Xiaocun, Vice-President of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association; Cheng Haofei, Acting Director of the Department of Foreign Liaison of the Ministry of Culture; responsible persons from relevant departments; and hundreds of people from literary and artistic circles in the capital.
    As the Vietnamese artists stepped off the train, gongs and drums resounded at the station, and people welcomed their comrades-in-arms from the front lines of the anti-American struggle with warm applause. Literary and artistic workers from the capital presented flowers to all the Vietnamese artists.
    Tran Van Khien, Cultural Attaché of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also welcomed them at the station.

### 越南杂技团到京

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    越南杂技团到京
    新华社十二日讯　应中国人民对外文化联络委员会邀请，来我国进行访问演出的越南民主共和国杂技团，在团长苏海率领下，今天乘专车从武汉到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的，有对外文委副主任杨纯、中越友协副会长孙晓村、文化部对外联络司代司长程浩飞等有关方面负责人，以及首都文艺界人士数百人。
    当越南艺术家们走下火车时，车站上锣鼓齐鸣，人们以热烈的掌声欢迎来自反美斗争前线的战友们。首都文艺工作者向全体越南艺术家献了花。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆文化专员陈文献也到车站欢迎。
