	Pitiful

	According to British newspapers, a certain British "China expert" named McFarquhar, when discussing the revolutionary situation in Asia, said: "The main danger in Asia is not the Chinese army, but the pitiful economic and social conditions that foster domestic revolutionary movements."

	McFarquhar's remarks actually admit that the so-called "threat of China to Asia" fabricated by imperialism and the reactionaries of various countries is sheer nonsense. They also have to admit that the revolutions of various countries arise from the maturation of revolutionary conditions within those countries. These revolutionary conditions are the result of imperialist exploitation and oppression; the more imperialism strengthens its exploitation and suppression, the more fiercely the flames of revolution will burn.

	For imperialists, this is indeed quite pitiful.

### 可悲

1966-06-27
第5版()
专栏：三言两语

    可悲
    据英国报纸报道，一个英国的所谓中国问题专家麦克法夸尔在谈到亚洲的革命形势时说：“亚洲的主要危险不是中国军队，而是培育本国革命运动的可悲的经济和社会条件。”
    麦克法夸尔这段话，实际上承认了帝国主义和各国反动派捏造的所谓“中国对亚洲的威胁”完全是鬼话，并且不得不承认各国人民的革命是出于本国革命条件的成熟。这种革命条件是帝国主义的剥削和压迫造成的；帝国主义越是加强剥削和镇压，革命的烈火也就燃烧得越是旺盛。
    对于帝国主义者来说，这的确是十分可悲的。
