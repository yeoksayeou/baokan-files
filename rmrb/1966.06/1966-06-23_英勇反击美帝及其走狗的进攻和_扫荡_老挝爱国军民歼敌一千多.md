Title: Valiant Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Counterattack the U.S. Imperialists and Their Puppets' Attacks and "Sweeps," Annihilating Over a Thousand Enemies

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-23
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, as reported by Pathet Lao Radio: In the first five months of this year, the patriotic military and civilians in the Nam Tha region of Laos, while counterattacking the attacks of the U.S. imperialists and their puppet troops on the liberated areas, annihilated a total of 819 enemies, captured 76, and accepted the surrender of 195; recovered 62 villages originally belonging to the liberated areas, liberating tens of thousands of people; shot down two enemy planes, and captured a large quantity of weapons and military supplies.

    On May 30, militiamen and guerrillas in Sayaboury Province, in a valiant counterattack against two battalions of the U.S. and its puppets carrying out a "sweep" in the area north of Sayaboury City, killed 21 enemies, wounded 19, and seized a batch of weapons and military supplies.

    On May 26, the U.S. imperialists and their puppets dispatched bandits to sneak into some areas of Heum District in Xam Neua Province to carry out looting and sabotage activities. The local militia immediately launched a fierce counterattack, killing 43 enemies and wounding many.

### 英勇反击美帝及其走狗的进攻和“扫荡”  老挝爱国军民歼敌一千多

1966-06-23
第5版()
专栏：

    英勇反击美帝及其走狗的进攻和“扫荡”
    老挝爱国军民歼敌一千多
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯，据巴特寮电台广播：老挝南塔地区爱国军民今年头五个月在反击美帝国主义及其走狗军队进攻解放区的战斗中，共歼灭敌人八百一十九名，俘虏七十六名，受降一百九十五名；收复原属解放区的六十二个村庄，解放成万人口；打落两架敌机，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    沙耶武里省民兵和游击队五月三十日在英勇反击美国及其走狗的两个营军队在沙耶武里市以北地区进行的“扫荡”中，打死敌人二十一名，打伤十九名，缴获一批武器和军用物资。
    美帝国主义及其走狗五月二十六日派遣土匪潜入桑怒省赫芒县一些地方进行掠夺和破坏活动。当地民兵立即予以痛击，打死敌人四十三名，打伤多名。
