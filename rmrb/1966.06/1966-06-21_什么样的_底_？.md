Title: What Kind of "Bottom"?
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-21
Edition: 5

    What Kind of "Bottom"?
    U.S. President Johnson clamored at a press conference: The United States is determined to "go all the way" in the war of aggression against Vietnam.
    This statement by Johnson is actually completely superfluous. No one doubts that U.S. imperialism will continue to act recklessly in South Vietnam. The Johnson administration's continued increase in troops proves this point. The question is, what is the "bottom" that Johnson refers to when he says "go all the way?" It is very clear that this "bottom" can only be the complete defeat of the American aggressors. Johnson's words once again show that, like all reactionaries, U.S. imperialism will never shed tears until they see the coffin.

**Named Entities**

约翰逊  Johnson
美国    United States
南越    South Vietnam
美帝国主义  U.S. imperialism

### 什么样的“底”？

1966-06-21
第5版()
专栏：三言两语

    什么样的“底”？
    美国总统约翰逊在记者招待会上叫嚷：美国在侵越战争中决心“干到底”。
    约翰逊的这种表示，其实完全是多余的。谁也不怀疑美帝国主义要在南越继续蛮干下去。约翰逊政府继续增兵就证明了这一点。问题是约翰逊所谓“干到底”的“底”是个什么东西。事情很明白，这个“底”只能是美国侵略者的彻底失败。约翰逊的话再一次表明，美帝国主义和一切反动派一样，总是不见棺材不落泪的。
