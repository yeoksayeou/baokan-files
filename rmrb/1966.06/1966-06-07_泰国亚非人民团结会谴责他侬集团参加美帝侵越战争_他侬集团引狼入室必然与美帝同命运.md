Title: Thailand-Asia-Africa People's Solidarity Association Condemns Thanom Group for Participating in US Imperialist War of Aggression Against Vietnam; Thanom Group's Invitation of Wolves Will Inevitably Share the Same Fate as US Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency: Individuals from the Thailand-Asia-Africa People's Solidarity Committee in Beijing provided Xinhua News Agency with a statement issued by the Thailand-Asia-Africa People's Solidarity Committee on May 22. This statement strongly condemns and resolutely opposes the Thai ruling group's dispatch of combat troops to participate in the US imperialist war of aggression in South Vietnam. The statement points out that this action by the Thanom group demonstrates that it has openly become a war criminal.
    The statement points out, "The Thanom reactionary group has sold out the national sovereignty to US imperialism, willingly acting as US imperialism's slave, placing itself in a position of enmity with the Thai people, the people of neighboring countries, the people of Asia, and all peace-loving and justice-upholding people in the world." The statement says that Vietnam and Thailand are close neighbors, and the people of the two countries have a long-standing fraternal relationship. "Therefore, the Thai people express great indignation at the Thanom puppet government's willingness to act as a running dog of US imperialism, sending troops to join US imperialism and its other lackeys in aggression, slaughtering the fraternal Vietnamese people, suppressing their just struggle for national salvation, and committing despicable crimes as cannon fodder for their master, US imperialism."
    The statement says: "The actions of the Thanom puppet government can only be described as inviting wolves into the house, bringing the disaster of war upon the country. US imperialism is increasingly suffering disastrous defeats in South Vietnam, to the point of nearing its demise. The Thanom reactionaries cannot save US imperialism from its fate of failure. They themselves will inevitably be condemned by the Thai people, the Vietnamese people, and all peace-loving and justice-upholding people in the world, and will suffer the punishment they deserve, certainly meeting the same fate as its master, US imperialism."

### 泰国亚非人民团结会谴责他侬集团参加美帝侵越战争  他侬集团引狼入室必然与美帝同命运

1966-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    泰国亚非人民团结会谴责他侬集团参加美帝侵越战争
    他侬集团引狼入室必然与美帝同命运
  据新华社讯　泰国亚非人民团结委员会在京人士向新华社提供了泰国亚非人民团结委员会五月二十二日发表的声明。这项声明强烈谴责和坚决反对泰国统治集团派遣作战部队参与美帝国主义在南越进行的侵略战争。声明指出，他侬集团的这一行动说明，它已经赤裸裸地成了战争罪犯。
    声明指出，“他侬反动集团把国家主权出卖给美帝国主义，甘心充当美帝国主义的奴才，把自己放在与泰国人民、邻国人民、亚洲人民和全世界爱好和平正义的人民为敌的位置上。”声明说，越南和泰国两国是亲密的邻邦，两国人民有着悠久的兄弟关系。“因此，泰国人民对他侬伪政府甘心充当美帝国主义的走卒，出兵同美帝国主义和它的其他仆从一起进行侵略，屠杀兄弟的越南人民，镇压他们正义的救国斗争，为它们的主子美帝国主义充当炮灰而犯下的卑鄙罪行，表示极大的愤慨。”
    声明说：“他侬伪政府的所作所为，只能是引狼入室，把战争的灾祸引到国家。美帝国主义在南越日益惨败，以至快接近灭亡了。他侬反动派不可能挽救美帝国主义失败的命运。他们自己也必然遭到泰国人民、越南人民和全世界爱好和平正义的人民的谴责和罪有应得的惩罚，一定落得和它的主子美帝国主义一样的命运。”
