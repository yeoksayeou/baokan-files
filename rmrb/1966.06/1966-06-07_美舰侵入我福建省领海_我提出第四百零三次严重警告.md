Title: U.S. Warships Intruded into the Territorial Waters of Fujian Province; I Issued the 403rd Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 6th—On June 5th, from 05:09 to 09:07, a U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters southeast of Chongwu, Fujian Province. On the same day, from 12:56 to 16:48 and from 19:24 to June 6th at 02:30, a U.S. warship intruded twice into the territorial waters northeast of Dongshan Island, Fujian Province. On June 6th, from 08:58 to 18:53, a U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters southeast of Chongwu, Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. warships, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 403rd serious warning.

### 美舰侵入我福建省领海  我提出第四百零三次严重警告

1966-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    美舰侵入我福建省领海
    我提出第四百零三次严重警告
    新华社六日讯　六月五日五时零九分至九时零七分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省崇武东南地区领海。同日十二时五十六分至十六时四十八分和十九时二十四分至六日二时三十分，美国军舰一艘，两次侵入我福建省东山岛东北地区领海。六日八时五十八分至十八时五十三分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省崇武东南地区领海。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百零三次严重警告。
