Title: Tirana Hosts Chinese Photography Exhibition; Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations Hosts Reception for Chinese Photography Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-06-10
Edition: 5

    Tirana Hosts Chinese Photography Exhibition
    Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations Hosts Reception for Chinese Photography Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 7th - The Chinese Photography Exhibition, co-sponsored by the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations and the Albanian Journalists Association, opened in Tirana on the 6th.
    Albanian Minister of Education and Culture Dejana, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Malile, Vice Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association Spase, Chairman of the Albanian Journalists Association Vogeli, and others attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition.
    Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and all members of the Chinese photography delegation currently visiting here were invited to attend the opening ceremony.
    Gyulameti, Editor-in-Chief of the Albanian newspaper "Warrior," spoke at the opening ceremony.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana - The Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations and the Albanian Journalists Association held a reception on the evening of the 5th to bid farewell to the Chinese photography delegation led by Gao Fan, Editor-in-Chief of the "PLA Pictorial" and Editor-in-Chief of "China Photography."
    Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations Chairman Treska, Journalists Association Chairman Vogeli, Director of the Albanian Telegraph Agency Simijtiu, and Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, attended the reception.
    The reception proceeded in an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 地拉那举办我摄影展览会  阿对外文委等宴请我摄影代表团

1966-06-10
第5版()
专栏：

    地拉那举办我摄影展览会
    阿对外文委等宴请我摄影代表团
    新华社地拉那七日电　由阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会和阿尔巴尼亚新闻工作者协会举办的中国摄影展览会，六日在地拉那开幕。
    阿尔巴尼亚教育和文化部部长德雅纳、外交部副部长马利列、阿中友好协会副主席斯帕塞、阿尔巴尼亚新闻工作者协会主席沃格利等出席了展览会开幕式。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川和正在这里访问的中国摄影代表团全体成员应邀出席了开幕式。
    阿尔巴尼亚《战士》报总编辑居拉麦蒂在开幕式上讲了话。
    新华社地拉那电　阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会和阿尔巴尼亚新闻工作者协会五日晚上举行招待会，欢送由《解放军画报》总编辑、《中国摄影》主编高帆率领的中国摄影代表团。
    阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席特雷斯卡，新闻工作者协会主席沃格利、阿尔巴尼亚通讯社社长西米季乌以及中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川出席了招待会。
    招待会在极为热烈和友好的气氛中进行。
