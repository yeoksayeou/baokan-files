Title: Executive Body of China-Mongolia Science and Technology Cooperation Agreement Signs Protocol
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-02
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 31 – The fourth session of the executive body of the China-Mongolia Science and Technology Cooperation Agreement was held in Ulaanbaatar from May 24 to 30, and a protocol was signed.
    Signing the protocol on the Chinese side was Zhao Jichang, Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission, and on the Mongolian side, Demchig, Chairman of the Central Council of the Mongolian Handicraft Cooperative.
    Gombojab, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia and Chairman of the Economic Cooperation Committee, and Zhang Canming, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, attended the signing ceremony.
    The Chinese science and technology delegation led by Zhao Jichang left Ulaanbaatar by train on the 31st to return home after attending the fourth session of the executive body of the China-Mongolia Science and Technology Cooperation Agreement.
    Xinhua News Agency, June 1 – The Chinese science and technology delegation, which went to the Mongolian People's Republic to participate in the fourth session of the executive body of the China-Mongolia Science and Technology Cooperation Agreement, returned to Beijing by train this afternoon, led by Zhao Jichang, head of the delegation and Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission. Yue Zhijian, Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission, and others were at the station to greet the delegation.
    Luo Qing, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, also greeted them at the station.

### 中蒙科技合作协定执行机构签订议定书

1966-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    中蒙科技合作协定执行机构签订议定书
    新华社乌兰巴托三十一日电　中蒙科学技术合作协定执行机构第四届会议五月二十四日到三十日在乌兰巴托举行，并且签订了议定书。
    在议定书上签字的，中国方面是科学技术委员会副主任赵继昌，蒙古方面是蒙古手工业合作社中央理事会主席德姆奇格。
    蒙古部长会议副主席兼经济合作委员会主席贡布扎布和中国驻蒙古大使张灿明出席了签字仪式。
    由赵继昌率领的中国科学技术代表团在参加了中蒙科学技术合作协定执行机构第四届会议后在三十一日乘火车离开乌兰巴托回国。
    新华社一日讯　前往蒙古人民共和国参加中蒙科学技术合作协定执行机构第四届会议的中国科学技术代表团，在团长、科学技术委员会副主任赵继昌率领下，今天下午乘火车回到北京。到车站迎接代表团的有科学技术委员会副主任岳志坚等。
    蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办罗青也到车站迎接。
