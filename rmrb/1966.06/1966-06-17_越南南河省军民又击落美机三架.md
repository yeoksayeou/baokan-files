Title: The Army and People of Nam Ha Province in Vietnam Shot Down Three More US Aircraft

Author:
Date: 1966-06-17
Edition: 5

	Hanoi, June 16 (Xinhua) – According to supplementary news released here on the 16th, the army and people of Nam Ha Province in North Vietnam shot down a total of three American bandit aircraft on the 15th in the battle against invading American bandit aircraft, and captured American pilots alive.
	Hanoi, June 16 (Xinhua) – It was announced here: At 5:30 a.m. on the 16th, three American bandit commando boats invaded the territorial waters south of Ha Tinh Province in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. One of them was sunk by the local army and people, and the other two fled in panic.

### 越南南河省军民又击落美机三架

1966-06-17
第5版()
专栏：

    越南南河省军民又击落美机三架
    据新华社河内十六日电　据这里十六日公布的补充消息说，越南北方南河省军民，十五日在反击入侵的美国强盗飞机的战斗中，共击落美国强盗飞机三架，并活捉了美国飞行员。
    新华社河内十六日电　据这里公布：十六日清晨五时三十分，美国强盗别动艇三艘侵入越南民主共和国河静省南部领海，其中一艘被当地军民击沉，另两艘仓惶逃走。
