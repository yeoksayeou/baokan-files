Title: Chairman Williams of the National Committee of the New Communist Party Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1966-06-03
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Wellington, 2nd - Comrade Mervyn Harry Williams, an outstanding fighter of the international communist movement, an excellent son of the working people of New Zealand, and Chairman of the National Committee of the Communist Party of New Zealand, unfortunately passed away here on the evening of the 1st after a long illness.

    Comrade Williams' funeral is scheduled to be held on the 4th.

    Comrade Williams was born on July 13, 1904. He served for a long time as a member of the Political Committee and Secretary of the National Secretariat of the Communist Party of New Zealand. In April 1963, at the National Congress of the Communist Party of New Zealand, Comrade Williams was elected Chairman of the National Committee of the New Communist Party. He was re-elected at the 21st Congress of the New Communist Party this April.

### 新共全国委员会主席  威廉斯同志逝世

1966-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    新共全国委员会主席
    威廉斯同志逝世
    新华社惠灵顿二日电　国际共产主义运动的卓越战士、新西兰劳动人民的优秀儿子、新西兰共产党全国委员会主席默文·哈利·威廉斯同志在长期患病之后，不幸于一日晚在这里逝世。
    威廉斯同志的葬礼定四日举行。
    威廉斯同志生于一九○四年七月十三日。他曾长期担任新西兰共产党政治委员会委员和全国书记处书记。一九六三年四月，在新西兰共产党全国代表大会上，威廉斯同志当选为新共全国委员会主席。今年四月在新共二十一大上他再度当选连任。
