Title: Chairman Shehu Says at a Banquet Held for the Albanian Party and Government Delegation's Return from China: Comrade Mao Zedong Belongs Not Only to China But Also to the Whole World; The Friendship Between the Chinese and Albanian People Is Great, Eternal, and Unbreakable

Author:
Date: 1966-06-14
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 12th - Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Political Bureau member and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Shehu, held a grand banquet here on the evening of the 11th for the return of the Albanian Party and Government delegation from its visit to China, to entertain all the diplomatic officials of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, all members of the four Chinese delegations visiting Albania, and other Chinese comrades in Albania.
    Kabo, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Kellezi, member of the Central Committee and Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, and Nasse, alternate member of the Central Committee and Minister of Foreign Affairs, who participated in the Albanian Party and Government delegation's visit to China, attended the banquet.
    Comrades Shehu and Kabo proposed toasts at the banquet.
    In his toast, Comrade Shehu warmly praised the great, eternal, and unbreakable friendship between the two parties, the two countries, and the two peoples, jointly forged by the Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong and the Albanian Labor Party headed by Comrade Hoxha, and based on Marxism-Leninism. He warmly wished Comrade Mao Zedong health and longevity like the high mountains of Albania. He said that Comrade Mao Zedong belongs not only to China but also to Albania, to the international communist movement, and to the entire world. He proposed that we drink to the victory of Mao Zedong Thought and doctrines.
    Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Zhu Rong, head of the Chinese Agricultural Delegation and Vice Minister of Agriculture, Tang Ke, head of the Petroleum Industry Ministry Delegation and Vice Minister of the Petroleum Industry Ministry, Cheng Hongyi, head of the Supply and Marketing Cooperative Delegation and Vice Chairman of the Board of Directors of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, and Liu Dexiu, leader of the track and field team and Vice Chairman of the Track and Field Association, all proposed toasts at the meeting, for the unbreakable and eternal friendship between the Chinese and Albanian people, and for the good health of Comrade Hoxha, the beloved leader of the Albanian people and the close friend of the Chinese people.
    Representatives of Chinese experts and students in Albania also attended the banquet. The banquet proceeded in a fraternal, extremely cordial, warm, and friendly atmosphere.
    After the banquet, the guests watched the documentary film "Great Friendship, Fighting Unity" about the Albanian Party and Government delegation's visit to China.

### 谢胡主席在为阿党政代表团访华归来举行的宴会上说  毛泽东同志不仅属于中国也属于全世界  中阿两国人民友谊是伟大的永恒的和牢不可破的

1966-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    谢胡主席在为阿党政代表团访华归来举行的宴会上说
    毛泽东同志不仅属于中国也属于全世界
    中阿两国人民友谊是伟大的永恒的和牢不可破的
    新华社地拉那十二日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席谢胡为阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团的访华归来，十一日晚上在这里举行盛大宴会，招待中国驻阿尔巴尼亚使馆全体外交官员、在阿尔巴尼亚访问的中国的四个代表团全体成员和在阿尔巴尼亚的其他中国同志。
    参加阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团访问中国的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员卡博、中央委员、阿中友协主席凯莱齐、中央候补委员、外交部部长纳赛等出席了宴会。
    谢胡同志和卡博同志在宴会上祝了酒。
    谢胡同志在祝酒时，热情赞扬了以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党和以霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党共同缔造的两党、两国和两国人民之间伟大的、永恒的、牢不可破的、建立在马克思列宁主义基础上的友谊。他热烈祝愿毛泽东同志象阿尔巴尼亚的高山一样健康长寿。他说，毛泽东同志不仅属于中国，而且属于阿尔巴尼亚，属于国际共产主义运动，属于整个世界。他提议让我们为毛泽东思想和学说的胜利而干杯。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚使馆临时代办王锦川、中国农业代表团团长、农业部副部长朱荣、石油工业部代表团团长、石油工业部副部长唐克、供销合作社代表团团长、中华全国供销合作总社理事会副主任程宏毅和田径队的领队、田径协会副主席刘德修等都在会上祝酒，为中阿两国人民的牢不可破的永恒的友谊，为阿尔巴尼亚人民敬爱的领袖、中国人民的亲密朋友霍查同志的身体健康干杯。
    出席宴会的还有中国在阿尔巴尼亚的专家和留学生的代表。宴会在兄弟般的极为亲切、热烈和友好的气氛中进行。
    宴会后，宾主观看了阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团访华的纪录电影片《伟大的友谊，战斗的团结》。
