Title: Smash the Anti-Party Black Line
Author: Shi Yuxin
Date: 1966-06-11
Edition: 6

    Smash the Anti-Party Black Line
    Shi Yuxin, Shanghai Silicon Steel Sheet Factory
    Anger burns in our hearts,
    Hatred is poured into our pens,
    We open fire on the "Three-Family Village" black den,
    We will never stop until we achieve complete victory!
    We use the surging steel flow,
    We use the rolling oil,
    We are a torrent rushing thousands of miles,
    Washing away the anti-Party, anti-socialist black line!
    We hold a hammer in one hand and a pen in the other,
    Rushing to the forefront of the struggle,
    Whoever dares to oppose the Party and oppose socialism,
    Our iron fists will beat them until their heads are bloody!

**Named Entities**

上海矽钢片厂  Shanghai Silicon Steel Sheet Factory
三家村    Sanjia Cun (Three-Family Village - a derogatory term for intellectuals and writers criticized during the Cultural Revolution)

史玉新
1966-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

    冲垮反党黑线	上海矽钢片厂　史玉新
    愤怒燃烧在胸口，
    仇恨倾注在笔头，
    向“三家村”黑店开火，
    不获全胜决不罢休！
    我们用奔腾的钢流，
    我们用滚滚的石油，
    我们是一泄千里的激流，
    把反党反社会主义的黑线冲走！
    我们一手拿锤一手拿笔，
    奔赴斗争的最前头，
    谁胆敢反党反社会主义，
    铁拳把他打得头破血流！
