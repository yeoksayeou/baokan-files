Title: Cambodian Newspapers Expose Increasingly Obvious and Close Soviet-U.S. Cooperation; Soviet Leaders Reverse the Enemy-Friend Relationship and Step Up Collaboration with the U.S. Against China; Pakistani Figures Condemn the Soviet Union for Simultaneously Promoting the "Tashkent Spirit" and Intensifying Aid to India Against Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-06-09
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh dispatch: The "Cambodge Soir" published an editorial on the 3rd, saying: "U.S.-Soviet cooperation has never been as obvious and close as it is today. The idea of nuclear monopoly has linked the two major powers through the notorious Moscow Treaty. The agreement reached between Kennedy and Khrushchev on the Cuban issue in 1962 fully illustrated the problem at that time, so there is no need to talk about it again. Now Moscow has proposed a draft international agreement to explore and utilize celestial bodies, which is of course warmly welcomed by Washington. Only the United Nations' endorsement is awaited to make the Soviet-U.S. 'wedding' even more grand."

    The editorial said: "It is indeed a wonderful program, but on the condition that it can be applied to the United States."

    The editorial exposed the Soviet revisionist leaders' reversal of the enemy-friend relationship. It said that the Soviet leaders hinted that "the Soviet Union's enemy is People's China, not the United States. What an absurdity."

    The editorial emphasized: "Who should be held responsible? This does not need an answer, because people have already seen through the faces of the accomplices of U.S. imperialism. People all over the world have made judgments about them. People all over the world know from their own personal experience that they must never trust the aid of those so-called friends who embrace you in order to make it easier to strangle you." The editorial exposed the Soviet revisionist leaders' position of false support and true betrayal on the Vietnam issue, saying: "What is the use of even the best military assistance if it is not accompanied by firm, sincere, and constant political support? If we do not try to isolate U.S. imperialism, but seek to compromise with it, then even the most modern nuclear missiles cannot defeat it."

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi dispatch: Responsible officials, prominent figures, and newspapers in Pakistan and Kashmir continue to condemn the Soviet Union's recent arms aid to India, which seriously endangers Pakistan's national security. Some prominent figures also pointed out that this action by the Soviet leadership is also a step in its collusion with U.S. imperialism and Indian reactionaries against China.

    Sardar Mohammad Aslam Khan, Secretary-General of the ruling Pakistan Muslim League, said at a press conference in Quetta, West Pakistan, on the 3rd: The Soviet supply of weapons to India poses a huge threat to Pakistan. These weapons will obviously be used against Pakistan.

    A leader of the National Awami Party in Lyallpur, West Pakistan, said that the new Soviet leaders' provision of new arms aid to India shows that they are no different from Khrushchev in uniting with U.S. imperialism and Indian reactionaries against socialist China. They are implementing the same revisionist policy.

    Abdul Hamid Khan, President of the Azad Kashmir government, said at a press conference in Muzaffarabad on the 3rd that the weapons newly given to India by the Soviet Union will strengthen India, and India has already used the military supplies previously given to it by the Soviet Union to intensify its tyrannical rule over the Indian-occupied area of Kashmir.

    In addition to pointing out that this action by the Soviet Union will greatly encourage the Indian leaders to adopt a more belligerent attitude than before, some newspaper editorials also condemned the Soviet leaders for playing a double game with Pakistan. An editorial in the Karachi "Achievement" on the 3rd pointed out: It is "truly strange that the Soviet Union is 'promoting the "Tashkent Spirit" all over the world,' saying that this is the 'best example' of peacefully resolving international differences, while on the other hand, it is supplying India with a large amount of war materials for use against Pakistan."

### 柬埔寨报纸揭露苏美合作更加明显和密切  苏联领导颠倒敌我关系加紧联美反华  巴基斯坦人士谴责苏联一面宣扬“塔什干精神”一面加紧援印反巴

1966-06-09
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨报纸揭露苏美合作更加明显和密切
    苏联领导颠倒敌我关系加紧联美反华
    巴基斯坦人士谴责苏联一面宣扬“塔什干精神”一面加紧援印反巴
    据新华社金边电　《柬埔寨电讯报》三日发表的社论说：“美苏合作从来没有象今天这样明显和密切。核垄断的主意通过臭名昭著的莫斯科条约已经把两个大国联结在一起。肯尼迪和赫鲁晓夫一九六二年就古巴问题达成的协议，当时就充分说明问题，无需再去谈它了。现在莫斯科提出一项探索和利用天体的国际协定草案，这个草案当然受到华盛顿的热烈欢迎。只待联合国的赞同来使苏美‘婚礼’更为隆重。”
    社论说：“确是美妙的纲领，但是以能适用于美国为条件。”
    社论揭露苏联修正主义领导颠倒敌我关系。它说，苏联领导暗示“苏联的敌人是人民中国，而不是美国。咄咄怪事。”
    社论强调指出：“谁该负责呢？这无须回答，因为人们已经识破了美帝国主义的帮凶的面目。全世界人民已经对他们作出了判断。全世界人民从自己的切身经历中懂得，决不能信赖为了更便于把你卡死而同你拥抱的那些所谓的朋友的援助。”社论揭露苏联修正主义领导在越南问题上的假支持、真出卖的立场说：“如果不同时在政治上给予坚决的、真诚的和经常的支持，即使是最好的军事援助还有什么用处呢？如果不设法孤立美帝国主义，而是力求同它妥协，那么即使有最新式的核导弹也决不能把它打倒。”
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第电巴基斯坦和克什米尔的负责官员、社会知名人士和报纸，继续谴责苏联最近给印度的武器援助，严重危害了巴基斯坦的国家安全。一些社会知名人士还指出，苏联领导的这一行动也是它勾结美帝国主义和印度反动派反对中国的一个步骤。
    执政的巴基斯坦穆斯林联盟总书记萨达尔·穆罕默德·阿斯拉姆汗三日在西巴基斯坦的基达举行的记者招待会上说：苏联向印度供应武器对巴基斯坦形成了巨大的威胁。这些武器显然会用来反对巴基斯坦。
    西巴基斯坦莱雅普尔民族人民党的一位领导人说，苏联新领导向印度提供新的武器援助，表明了他们在联合美帝国主义和印度反动派反对社会主义的中国方面同赫鲁晓夫没有丝毫区别。他们执行着同样的修正主义政策。
    查谟和克什米尔自由政府主席阿卜杜勒·哈米德汗三日在穆扎法拉巴举行的记者招待会上说，苏联新施舍给印度的武器将会使印度得到加强，印度已经用苏联先前给它的军火加紧了对克什米尔印占区的暴虐统治。
    一些报纸的社论除了指出苏联的这一行动，将大大地鼓励印度领导人采取比以前更加好战的态度以外，还谴责苏联领导在对巴基斯坦玩弄两面手法。卡拉奇《成就报》三日的社论指出：苏联一方面“在全世界宣扬‘塔什干精神’，说这是和平解决国际分歧的‘最好的榜样’，而另一方面却又给印度大量战争物资供反对巴基斯坦之用，这听来真是奇怪。”
