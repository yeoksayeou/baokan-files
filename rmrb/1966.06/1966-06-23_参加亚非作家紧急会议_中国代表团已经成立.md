Title: Chinese Delegation Established to Attend the Asian-African Writers' Emergency Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-23
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd—The Chinese delegation to attend the Asian-African Writers' Emergency Meeting has been established.
    The list of the delegation is as follows:
    Head of Delegation:
    Guo Moruo
    Deputy Heads of Delegation: Xu Guangping, Ba Jin, Liu Baiyu
