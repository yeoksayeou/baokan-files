Title: Representatives of the Asian-African Writers' Emergency Conference Watch the Peking Opera "The Red Detachment of Women"
Author:
Date: 1966-06-30
Edition: 4

Representatives from various countries and regions attending the Asian-African Writers' Emergency Conference watched the new achievement of Chinese opera reform, the Peking Opera "The Red Detachment of Women," which reflects contemporary struggle life, tonight.

Representatives from various countries and regions warmly welcomed and highly praised this modern Peking Opera, which was produced and performed by the Shandong Peking Opera Troupe. During the performance, the guests frequently applauded and cheered. After the performance, the entire audience stood up, and the applause was thunderous and unending. The performers took their bows four times. A representative from Korea said, "This is a great play that reflects the people's war. It praises the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Korea in their common struggle against American imperialism and also praises the revolutionary heroism and internationalism of the Chinese and Korean people's armies." A representative from Congo (Leopoldville) said: "This is a very excellent performance. It has such superb stage settings, such superb acting skills, and such content full of fighting spirit. Watching it will inspire people."

### 亚非作家紧急会议代表观看京剧《奇袭白虎团》

1966-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非作家紧急会议代表观看京剧《奇袭白虎团》
    新华社二十九日讯　出席亚非作家紧急会议的各国和各地区代表，今晚观看了中国戏曲改革的新成果、反映现实斗争生活的京剧《奇袭白虎团》。
    各国各地区代表对这个由山东京剧团编导和演出的现代京剧，表示热烈欢迎并给予高度赞扬。在演出过程中，来宾们不断发出喝采声和掌声。演出结束后，全场起立，掌声雷动，经久不息，演员谢幕四次。一位朝鲜代表说，“这是一出反映人民战争的好戏，它歌颂了中朝两国人民在反美共同斗争中的战斗友谊，也歌颂了中朝人民军队的革命英雄主义和国际主义精神。”刚果（利）的一位代表说：“这是十分精采的演出。它有这么出色的布景，这么高超的演技，这么充满战斗精神的内容，看了会使人受到鼓舞。”
