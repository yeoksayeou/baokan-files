Title: Swedish "Sickle" Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Beijing for Shenyang to Visit and Perform
Author:
Date: 1966-06-15
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: The Swedish "Sickle" folk song and dance troupe left Beijing by train this evening to visit and perform in Shenyang. Song Changhua and others were at the station to see them off. (Photo attached)
    June 14th, after Premier Li Xiannian of the State Council watched the performance of the acrobatic troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, he went on stage to take a group photo with the performers. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen.

### 瑞典“镰刀”歌舞团  离京去沈阳访问演出

1966-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞典“镰刀”歌舞团
    离京去沈阳访问演出
    新华社十四日讯　瑞典“镰刀”民间歌舞团今晚乘火车离开北京，前往沈阳访问演出。到车站欢送的有宋常华等。（附图片）
    六月十四日，国务院副总理李先念观看越南民主共和国杂技团的演出后，上台和演员合影。　新华社记者　郑小箴摄
