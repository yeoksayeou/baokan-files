Title: Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau Hosts a Banquet for Swedish Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-06-03
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Gu Wenhua, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Bairentz, Director of the Swedish Broadcasting Corporation Television, and his wife, as well as Haigel, Consultant to the Swedish Broadcasting Corporation, Dean of the Swedish Dance Academy, Director of the Swedish Dance Museum, and Chairman of the Swedish Dance Association. Meng Qiyu, Dai Lingfeng, Sheng Jie, and others attended the banquet.

### 我广播事业局副局长宴请瑞典客人

1966-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我广播事业局副局长宴请瑞典客人
    据新华社一日讯　广播事业局副局长顾文华今晚设宴欢迎瑞典广播公司电视台台长拜仑茨和他的夫人以及瑞典广播公司顾问、瑞典舞蹈学院院长、瑞典舞蹈博物馆馆长、瑞典舞蹈协会主席海格尔。出席宴会的有孟启予、戴临风、盛婕等。
