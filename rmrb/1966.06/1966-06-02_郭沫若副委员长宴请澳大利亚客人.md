Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman, Hosts Australian Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-02
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 31st - Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and his wife, received and hosted Professor Christenson of the Department of Electrical Engineering of the University of Sydney, Australia, Academician of the Australian Academy of Sciences, and his wife, and their son, Stephen Christenson, this afternoon. Guo Moruo had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Among those present at the banquet were Du Runsheng, Zhou Peiyuan, Cheng Maolan, Pan Chun, and Xiao Guangjia.

### 郭沫若副委员长宴请澳大利亚客人

1966-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长宴请澳大利亚客人
    新华社三十一日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若和夫人，今天下午接见并设宴招待澳大利亚悉尼大学电气工程系教授、澳大利亚科学院院士克里斯琴森和夫人以及他们的儿子斯蒂芬·克里斯琴森。郭沫若同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    宴会时在座的有杜润生、周培源、程茂兰、潘纯、萧光甲等。
