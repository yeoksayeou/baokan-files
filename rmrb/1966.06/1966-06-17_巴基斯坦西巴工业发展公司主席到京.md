Title: Chairman of West Pakistan Industrial Development Corporation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-17
Edition: 4

    Chairman of West Pakistan Industrial Development Corporation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: Mr. Machali, Chairman of the West Pakistan Industrial Development Corporation, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this morning for a visit to our country at our invitation.
    Greeting them at the airport were Li Yingji, Vice-Minister of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries, and others. Mr. Durrani, Minister of the Pakistani Embassy in China, and Mr. Malik, Commercial Secretary, also went to the airport to welcome them.
    In the evening, Li Yingji hosted a banquet for the guests.

### 巴基斯坦西巴工业发展公司主席到京

1966-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦西巴工业发展公司主席到京
    新华社十六日讯　巴基斯坦西巴工业发展公司主席马查利和夫人应邀来我国访问，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的，有对外经委副主任李应吉等。巴基斯坦驻华大使馆公使杜拉尼、商务秘书马力克也前往机场迎接。
    晚上，李应吉设宴招待客人。
