Title: China Oriental Song and Dance Troupe Returns to Beijing After Ending Performance Tour in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-06-14
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th - The Chinese Oriental Song and Dance Troupe, comprising 56 members and led by its director Zhang Xing, returned to Beijing on the 13th after concluding its performance tour in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    They were greeted at the station by Yang Chun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Yao, Deputy Manager of the China Performing Arts Agency, and others.

    Huang Bei, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Su Hai, head of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Acrobatic Troupe currently in Beijing, along with embassy officials and acrobatic troupe performers, also welcomed them at the station and presented flowers to the leaders of the Oriental Song and Dance Troupe.

    The Oriental Song and Dance Troupe visited the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for performances according to the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement. From February 15th to April 19th, they visited 19 provinces, cities, and some front-line positions in Vietnam, performing 157 shows with a total audience of 257,000, receiving a warm welcome from the Vietnamese people fighting against American aggression.

    President Ho Chi Minh and Prime Minister Pham Van Dong both received all members of the song and dance troupe. The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam also awarded the troupe the First Class Labor Medal, praising its contribution to enhancing the fighting solidarity and friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China.

### 结束对越南的访问演出后  我东方歌舞团回到北京

1966-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    结束对越南的访问演出后
    我东方歌舞团回到北京
    新华社十三日讯　中国东方歌舞团一行五十六人结束了在越南民主共和国的访问演出后，已经于十三日在团长张星的率领下回到北京。
    到车站迎接的有：对外文委副主任杨纯，中国演出公司副经理张耀等。
    越南驻华使馆临时代办黄北、正在北京的越南民主共和国杂技团团长苏海以及使馆官员和杂技团演员也到车站迎接，并向东方歌舞团负责人献了花。
    东方歌舞团是根据中越文化合作协定前往越南民主共和国访问演出的。他们从二月十五日到四月十九日期间，在越南访问了十九个省、市和一些前线阵地，演出一百五十七场，观众达二十五万七千人次，受到了抗美战斗中的越南人民的热烈欢迎。
    胡志明主席和范文同总理都曾接见了歌舞团的全体人员。越南民主共和国政府还授给歌舞团一级劳动勋章，表扬它为增强越中两国人民之间的战斗团结和友谊作出的贡献。
