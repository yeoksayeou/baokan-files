Title: Tanzanian Government Economic Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Home

Author:

Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 12th—The Tanzanian Government Economic Delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Paul Bomani, Minister of Economic Affairs and Development Planning of Tanzania, concluded its friendly visit to our country and left Shanghai by plane today to return home.
    Shi Ying, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Yang Lin, Deputy Director of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission, and responsible persons from relevant departments warmly saw them off at the airport. Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Wazir Juma, and Mrs. Bomani and Mrs. Kunabi, who remained in China for further visits, also saw them off at the airport.
    During their stay in Shanghai, the distinguished guests visited the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition and some factories, and visited the suburban people's commune, etc. After visiting the Spark Electrical Appliance Factory, which was founded two years ago by twenty-one housewives with self-reliance spirit, Minister Paul Bomani praised the achievements of the factory. He said: "Here we have seen the tenacious working spirit of Chinese women and the Chinese people. Your spirit of self-reliance is worth learning from."

### 坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团离沪回国

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团离沪回国
    新华社上海十二日电　坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团结束了在我国的友好访问，在团长、坦桑尼亚经济事务和发展计划部长保罗·博马尼率领下，今天乘飞机离开上海回国。
    上海市副市长石英、对外经委副主任杨琳以及有关方面负责人到机场热情欢送。坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马和留在中国继续访问的博马尼夫人、库纳比夫人也到机场送行。
    贵宾们在上海期间，参观了上海工业展览会和一些工厂，访问了郊区人民公社等。保罗·博马尼部长在参观了两年前由二十一个家庭妇女以自力更生精神创办的星火电器厂后，赞扬这家工厂所取得的成就。他说：“在这里我们看到了中国妇女和中国人民的顽强工作精神，你们这种自力更生精神值得我们学习。”
