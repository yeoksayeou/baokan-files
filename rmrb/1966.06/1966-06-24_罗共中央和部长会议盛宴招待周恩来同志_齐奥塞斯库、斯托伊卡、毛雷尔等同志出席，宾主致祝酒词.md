Title: Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Council of Ministers Hold Grand Banquet in Honor of Comrade Zhou Enlai, Comrades Ceaușescu, Stoica, Maurer, etc. Attend, Guests and Hosts Exchange Toasts
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 22nd – The Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and the Romanian Council of Ministers held a grand banquet in the Council of Ministers Hall on the evening of June 22nd to honor Comrade Zhou Enlai, head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Premier of the State Council.

    Attending the banquet were: Nicolae Ceaușescu, General Secretary of the Romanian Communist Party; Chivu Stoica, Member of the Executive Committee and Permanent Presidium of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Chairman of the State Council; Ion Gheorghe Maurer, Member of the Executive Committee and Permanent Presidium of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Gheorghe Apostol, Member of the Executive Committee and Permanent Presidium of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Alexandru Bârlădeanu, Member of the Executive Committee and Permanent Presidium of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Emil Bodnăraș, Member of the Executive Committee and Permanent Presidium of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Central Secretary, Alexandru Drăghici.

    Members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation Zhao Yimin, Qiao Guanhua, Zeng Yongquan and delegation staff were also invited to the banquet.

    At the beginning of the banquet, the band played the national anthems of China and Romania. Comrade Nicolae Ceaușescu and Comrade Zhou Enlai delivered toasts during the banquet.

    Also participating in the banquet were: Members and Alternate Members of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Secretaries of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Members of the Romanian State Council, Ministers of various ministries of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Government, senior generals of the Romanian Armed Forces, and leaders of various mass organizations, as well as more than 500 well-known figures from all walks of life. All diplomatic officials of the Chinese Embassy in Romania and diplomatic envoys from various countries also attended the banquet.

    During the banquet, Romanian artists performed the music "The East is Red," praising Chairman Mao, and sang folk songs from China and Romania. The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere. (Attached picture)

### 罗共中央和部长会议盛宴招待周恩来同志  齐奥塞斯库、斯托伊卡、毛雷尔等同志出席，宾主致祝酒词

1966-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    罗共中央和部长会议盛宴招待周恩来同志
    齐奥塞斯库、斯托伊卡、毛雷尔等同志出席，宾主致祝酒词
    新华社布加勒斯特二十二日电　罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会和罗马尼亚部长会议二十二日晚在部长会议大厅举行盛大宴会，招待中国党政代表团团长、中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来同志。
    出席宴会的有：罗共中央总书记尼·齐奥塞斯库，罗共中央执行委员、中央常设主席团委员、国务委员会主席基伏·斯托伊卡，罗共中央执行委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议主席扬·格·毛雷尔，罗共中央执行委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席格·阿波斯托尔，罗共中央执行委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席亚·伯尔拉德亚努，罗共中央执行委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席埃·波德纳拉希，罗共中央执行委员、中央常设主席团委员、中央书记亚·德勒吉奇。
    中国党政代表团团员赵毅敏、乔冠华、曾涌泉和代表团随行人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会开始时，乐队高奏中罗两国国歌。尼·齐奥塞斯库同志和周恩来同志先后在席间致了祝酒词。
    参加宴会的还有：罗共中央执行委员、候补执行委员，罗共中央书记处书记，罗马尼亚国务委员会委员，罗共中央和政府各部部长，罗马尼亚武装部队高级将领和各群众团体负责人以及各界知名人士等五百多人。中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆全体外交官员和各国外交使节也出席了宴会。
    宴会上，罗马尼亚艺术家们演奏了歌颂毛主席的乐曲《东方红》，演唱了中罗两国的民歌。宴会充满了热烈、友好的气氛。（附图片）
