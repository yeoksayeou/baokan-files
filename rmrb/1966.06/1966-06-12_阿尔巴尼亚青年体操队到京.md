Title: Albanian Youth Gymnastics Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 10th: The first group of ten members of the Albanian Youth Gymnastics Team, invited to visit our country, led by Vasil Naji, member of the Albanian Sports Commission, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    Zhu Debao, Director of the Sports Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission and Chairman of the Chinese Gymnastics Association, and Chinese gymnasts warmly welcomed them at the airport.
    The second group of seven members of the Albanian Youth Gymnastics Team will arrive later.

### 阿尔巴尼亚青年体操队到京

1966-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚青年体操队到京
    新华社十日讯　应邀来我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚青年体操队第一批人员十人，由阿尔巴尼亚体委委员瓦西里·纳吉率领，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    国家体委运动司司长、中国体操协会主席朱德宝和我国的体操运动员等到机场热烈欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚青年体操队的第二批人员七人将在晚些时候到达。
