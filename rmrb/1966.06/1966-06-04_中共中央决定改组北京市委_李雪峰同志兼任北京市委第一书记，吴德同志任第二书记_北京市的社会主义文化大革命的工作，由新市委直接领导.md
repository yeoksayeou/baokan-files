    The CCP Central Committee Decides to Reorganize the Beijing Municipal Party Committee
    Li Xuefeng Concurrently Serves as First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Party Committee, Wu De Serves as Second Secretary
    The Socialist Cultural Revolution Work in Beijing Municipality is Directly Led by the New Municipal Party Committee
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd – The CCP Central Committee has decided that Comrade Li Xuefeng, First Secretary of the CCP Central Committee's North China Bureau, will concurrently serve as First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Party Committee, and Comrade Wu De, First Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Party Committee, will be transferred to serve as Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Party Committee, to reorganize the Beijing Municipal Party Committee. Comrades Li Xuefeng and Wu De have already taken up their posts and begun work. The Socialist Cultural Revolution work in Beijing Municipality will be directly led by the new Municipal Party Committee.

### 中共中央决定改组北京市委  李雪峰同志兼任北京市委第一书记，吴德同志任第二书记  北京市的社会主义文化大革命的工作，由新市委直接领导

1966-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央决定改组北京市委
    李雪峰同志兼任北京市委第一书记，吴德同志任第二书记
    北京市的社会主义文化大革命的工作，由新市委直接领导
    新华社三日讯　中共中央决定：由中共中央华北局第一书记李雪峰同志兼任北京市委第一书记，调中共吉林省委第一书记吴德同志任北京市委第二书记，对北京市委进行改组。李雪峰、吴德两同志业已到职工作。北京市的社会主义文化大革命的工作，由新市委直接领导。
