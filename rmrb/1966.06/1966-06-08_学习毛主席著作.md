Title: Studying Chairman Mao's Writings
Author: Liu Dahai
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 6

    Studying Chairman Mao's Writings
    Liu Dahai
    The Weishanzhuang People's Commune in Daxing County, Beijing, recently held a grand meeting of nearly ten thousand people to discuss and apply what they have learned from studying Chairman Mao's writings, pushing the movement of studying Chairman Mao's writings to a new high. These are three quick sketches from the meeting. (Attached pictures)
    The broad masses of poor and lower-middle peasants are vying to purchase Chairman Mao's writings.
    Model students in studying Chairman Mao's writings speak and exchange their learning experiences.
    Holding high the red flag, they march to the meeting venue.
