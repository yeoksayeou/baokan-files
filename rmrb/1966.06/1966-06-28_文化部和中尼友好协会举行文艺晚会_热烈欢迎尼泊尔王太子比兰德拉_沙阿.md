Title: Cultural Department and China-Nepal Friendship Association Host Art Evening
Author:
Date: 1966-06-28
Edition: 3

The Ministry of Culture and the China-Nepal Friendship Association held an art evening to warmly welcome Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah, as well as the accompanying Himalaya Shah Prince and his wife Princess Prineshpa Shah and other Nepalese dignitaries.

The evening featured a performance by the Beijing Peking Opera Troupe of the modern Peking opera "Sha Jiahamp."

Vice Premier Li Xiannian of the State Council, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association Zhou Jianren, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, and Vice Minister of Culture Zhao Xinchu accompanied the dignitaries to watch the performance.

Nepalese Ambassador to China Rendel Subba was also invited to attend the evening.

After the performance, Crown Prince Birendra Shah, accompanied by Vice Premier Li Xiannian, went on stage and shook hands with the performers, expressing his greetings.

This morning, Crown Prince Birendra Shah and other accompanying Nepalese dignitaries, escorted by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, visited the Beijing First Machine Tool Factory and toured the Summer Palace.

In the afternoon, Crown Prince Birendra Shah and other dignitaries attended a welcoming tea party hosted by the All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation, where they met with Chinese youth from various sectors. The hosts and guests engaged in warm and friendly conversations. The Chinese youth present also performed Chinese music, dance, and Nepalese folk songs for the dignitaries.

Princess Prineshpa Shah, wife of Himalaya Shah Prince, visited a children's hospital this afternoon and met with the President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, Qian Xinzhuo.

### 文化部和中尼友好协会举行文艺晚会  热烈欢迎尼泊尔王太子比兰德拉·沙阿

1966-06-28
第3版()
专栏：

    文化部和中尼友好协会举行文艺晚会
    热烈欢迎尼泊尔王太子比兰德拉·沙阿
    新华社二十七日讯　文化部和中国尼泊尔友好协会今晚举行文艺晚会，热烈欢迎尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿，以及随行的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主等尼泊尔贵宾。
    晚会上，北京京剧团为贵宾们演出现代京剧《沙家浜》。
    国务院副总理李先念，人大常委会副委员长、中尼友协会长周建人，外交部副部长韩念龙，文化部副部长赵辛初等陪同贵宾们观看了演出。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴也应邀出席了晚会。
    演出结束后，比兰德拉·沙阿王太子由李先念副总理陪同，走上舞台，同演员们握手，向他们致意。
    今天上午，比兰德拉·沙阿王太子和随行的其他尼泊尔贵宾，由外交部副部长韩念龙陪同，参观了北京第一机床厂，游览了颐和园。
    下午，比兰德拉·沙阿王太子等贵宾在全国青联和全国学联举行的欢迎茶会上，会见了中国各界青年，宾主进行了热烈友好的交谈。参加茶会的中国青年还为贵宾们表演了中国音乐歌舞和尼泊尔民歌。
    喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主今天下午参观了儿童医院，拜会了中国红十字会会长钱信忠。
