Title: Swedish "Sickle" Folk Song and Dance Troupe Performs in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 11th -- The Swedish "Sickle" Folk Song and Dance Troupe gave its premiere performance at the Capital Theater tonight.
    Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and responsible persons from relevant departments, including Li Shubin, Song Changhua, and Wang Yuanfang, watched the troupe's performance.
    Also in attendance was the Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Petri.

### 瑞典“镰刀”民间歌舞团在京演出

1966-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典“镰刀”民间歌舞团在京演出
    新华社十一日讯　瑞典“镰刀”民间歌舞团今晚在首都剧场举行首次演出。
    对外文化友好协会会长楚图南，以及有关方面负责人栗树彬、宋常华、王元方等观看了歌舞团的演出。
    观看演出的，还有瑞典驻中国大使佩特里。
