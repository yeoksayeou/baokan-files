Title: Mass Rally Held in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1966-06-29
Edition: 5

On June 27, a mass rally was held in Tirana. Comrade Zhou Enlai delivered a speech at the rally. To his left is Comrade Hoxha and to his right is Comrade Lishi.

**Named Entities**

**People:**
地拉那	Tirana
周恩来	Zhou Enlai
霍查	Hoxha
