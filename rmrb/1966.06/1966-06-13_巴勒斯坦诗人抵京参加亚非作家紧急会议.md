Title: Palestinian Poet Arrives in Beijing to Attend Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 12th: Palestinian poet Abdul Karim Karmi arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 11th to attend the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Meeting to be held in Beijing on the 27th.

### 巴勒斯坦诗人抵京参加亚非作家紧急会议

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦诗人抵京参加亚非作家紧急会议
    新华社十二日讯　巴勒斯坦诗人阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡尔米十一日晚到达北京，前来参加将于二十七日在北京召开的亚非作家紧急会议。
