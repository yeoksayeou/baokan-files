Title: Chinese and Albanian Party and Government Leaders Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1966-06-28
Edition: 1

Chinese and Albanian Party and Government Leaders Continue Talks

	New China News Agency, Tirana, June 26 — A delegation led by Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai continued talks with the Party and state leaders of Albania this afternoon.

	The talks were conducted in a very sincere and friendly atmosphere.

### 中阿党政领导人继续会谈

1966-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中阿党政领导人继续会谈
    新华社地拉那二十六日电　由中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来同志率领的中国党政代表团同阿尔巴尼亚党和国家领导人二十六日下午继续举行会谈。
    会谈是在十分真挚和友好的气氛中进行的。
