Title: Mao Zedong's Literary and Art Soldiers Serve the World Revolution (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1966-06-15
Edition: 6

    Mao Zedong's Literary and Art Soldiers Serve the World Revolution
    Actors of the China Oriental Song and Dance Troupe, with excitement and carrying the Chinese people's high respect and fraternal greetings, went to comfort the heroic Vietnamese people fighting on the front lines of the anti-American struggle. Wherever the song and dance troupe went, the Vietnamese military and civilians cheered, "The friendship between the Vietnamese and Chinese people in unity and struggle will last forever!" The picture shows the China Oriental Song and Dance Troupe performing programs in the hometown of President Ho Chi Minh in Nghe An Province, Vietnam, for the local military and civilians, reflecting the heroic deeds of Vietnamese women in the anti-American and national salvation struggle.　Xinhua News Agency Reporter An Kang

    During a friendly visit to Congo (Brazzaville) in February of this year, the art workers in the Chinese Youth Delegation performed "Bow Dance" on the streets of Pointe-Noire, a famous seaport of Congo (Brazzaville). The Congolese youth expressed great admiration for the Chinese people's indomitable fighting spirit.
                      Xinhua News Agency Reporter Gao Liang

    The China Folk Art Troupe performs the dance "Female Militia" in Dubreka, Guinea. Here, the Guinean people saw the heroic image of the valiant Chinese female militia. They kept applauding the revolutionary aspirations of Chinese women who love military uniforms more than elegant dresses.
                                       Xinhua News Agency Reporter Meng Xianquan

    The Mauritanian people received the folk art troupe from China with the highest courtesy, and they greatly appreciated the Chinese songs and dances with strong national characteristics. The picture shows the China Folk Art Troupe performing "Harvest Dance" in Nouakchott, the capital of Mauritania.　		Xinhua News Agency Reporter Meng Xianquan

    During a friendly visit to Tanzania by the Chinese Youth Delegation, the artists in the delegation often performed for the broad masses of working people in the streets and in the countryside, and were warmly welcomed by the local people. The picture shows the art workers of the Chinese Youth Delegation singing loudly for the young laborers in the fields of the first youth reclamation team in Zanzibar.　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Liu Zhiguang Learning from each other and improving together. Actors of the Yunnan Acrobatic Troupe learn the Lao folk instrument "Drumming" from Lao youth. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Xue Tiejun Actors of the China Oriental Song and Dance Troupe learn Vietnamese dance from art workers in Thanh Hóa Province, Vietnam.
                                Xinhua News Agency Reporter An Kang

    During the China Folk Art Troupe's performance tour in Mali, the actors' wonderful performances won praise from the Malian audience, and their eagerness to learn and humility were also highly praised by the local people. The picture shows a Malian artist patiently teaching Chinese actors how to play the drums.
                                  Xinhua News Agency Reporter Meng Xianquan

    The Yunnan Acrobatic Troupe is the first art group sent by the Chinese people to visit and perform in the Lao liberated areas. Carrying forward the fighting spirit of the "Ulanmuqi," they often risked the dangers of bombing and strafing by American imperialist aircraft, carrying props on their backs, crossing mountains and ridges, and going deep into the front lines and the countryside to perform for the broad masses of soldiers and civilians. The Lao audience praised them: "They are worthy of being Mao Zedong's literary and art soldiers, and outstanding sons and daughters of the new China."　		Xinhua News Agency Reporter Xue Tiejun

    The China Folk Art Troupe's visit and performance tour to Mali was warmly welcomed by the Malian people everywhere. Whenever the performance ended, enthusiastic audiences always rushed to the front of the stage, vying to shake hands with the Chinese actors, expressing their love for Mao Zedong and their congratulations on the vigorous development of Chinese revolutionary literature and art.
                                         Xinhua News Agency

### 毛泽东的文艺战士为世界革命服务（图片）

1966-06-15
第6版()
专栏：

    毛泽东的文艺战士为世界革命服务
    中国东方歌舞团演员怀着激动的心情，带着中国人民的崇高敬意和兄弟般的问候，前往慰问战斗在反美斗争最前线的英雄的越南人民。歌舞团所到之处，越南军民一片欢呼：“越中两国人民团结战斗友谊万古常青！”图为中国东方歌舞团在越南义安省胡志明主席的故乡，为当地军民演出反映越南妇女在抗美救国斗争中的英雄事迹的节目。	　新华社记者　安　康摄
    今年二月中国青年代表团到刚果（布）进行友好访问，代表团中的文艺工作者在刚果（布）著名海港——黑角的街道上，表演《弓舞》。刚果（布）青年对于中国人民不屈不挠的战斗精神，表示十分敬佩。
                      新华社记者　高　梁摄
    中国民间艺术团在几内亚杜博雷卡演出舞蹈《女民兵》。在这里，几内亚人民看到了英姿飒爽的中国女民兵的英雄形象，他们不断为中国妇女不爱红装爱武装的革命志气热烈鼓掌。
                                       新华社记者　孟宪全摄
    毛里塔尼亚人民以最高的礼遇接待来自中国的民间艺术团，他们对富有浓厚民族色彩的中国歌舞非常赞赏。图为中国民间艺术团在毛里塔尼亚首都努瓦克肖特演出《丰收舞》。　		新华社记者　孟宪全摄
    中国青年代表团在坦桑尼亚进行友好访问期间，代表团中的艺术家经常在街头和农村为广大劳动人民演出，受到当地人民的热烈欢迎。图为中国青年代表团的文艺工作者在桑给巴尔第一个青年垦荒队的田间，为青年劳动者高声歌唱。　新华社记者　刘治光摄互相学习，共同提高。云南杂技艺术团演员向老挝青年学习老挝民间乐器——“打鼓”。新华社记者　薛铁军摄中国东方歌舞团的演员向越南清化省的文艺工作者学习越南舞蹈。
                                新华社记者　安　康摄
    中国民间艺术团在马里演出期间，演员的精彩表演博得马里观众的好评，他们的好学和谦虚精神也深为当地人民所称赞。图为马里艺术家耐心地教中国演员击鼓。
                                  新华社记者　孟宪全摄
    云南杂技艺术团是中国人民派到老挝解放区进行访问演出的第一个艺术团体。他们发扬“乌兰牧骑”的战斗精神，经常冒着美帝国主义飞机轰炸和扫射的危险，身背道具，翻山越岭，深入阵地与农村，为广大军民演出。老挝观众夸奖他们：“不愧是毛泽东的文艺战士，新中国的优秀儿女。”　		新华社记者　薛铁军摄
    中国民间艺术团到马里访问演出，到处受到马里人民的热烈欢迎。每当演出结束时，热情的观众总是涌向台前，争着和中国演员握手，表示他们对毛泽东的热爱，对中国革命文艺蓬勃发展的祝贺。
                                         新华社发
