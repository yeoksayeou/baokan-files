Title: Australian "Vanguard" Exposes US-Soviet Collusion in Planning New "Peace Talk" Scam; Soviet Revisionists Exert All Efforts to Help US Imperialism Salvage Defeat in Vietnam; Seoul Anti-Communist Conference Exposes New US Imperialist Plot of "Using Asians to Fight Asians"
Author:
Date: 1966-06-25
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, June 23rd: The "Vanguard" of Australia pointed out in a commentary on June 23rd, commenting on the new "peace talk" conspiracy planned by US imperialism on the Vietnam issue: In this conspiracy, standing on the side of US imperialism is the Soviet revisionist clique.
    "Vanguard" said that US imperialism is desperately trying to use the slogan of "peace" to suppress the demand for it to withdraw from South Vietnam. "With the help of Soviet revisionists, it is trying to use the peace sentiment of the people of the world to consolidate its position (in South Vietnam)." "Vanguard" emphasized that if it were not for the help of Soviet revisionists, the American aggressors would be more isolated and suffer greater defeat than they are now.
    Referring to the US imperialists' conspiracy to engage in a "truce" scam in Vietnam, "Vanguard" pointed out that this is because it has suffered heavy defeats there. "Its position in South Vietnam will soon be untenable. Therefore, if it can devise some kind of negotiation plan without withdrawing its troops, it will have time to consolidate its military and political position. A pause in the war will help ease its own domestic situation. The American people are launching a powerful offensive against the Johnson administration. A 'peaceful' pause is very necessary for Johnson."
    "Vanguard" emphasized: "The correct policy is to launch a struggle against US imperialism—to fight it on all fronts until it is crushed."
    The newspaper also pointed out that behind the "peace" scam, US imperialism is gathering greater strength to continue and expand the war. With the cooperation of Soviet revisionists, it is now transferring troops from Europe to South Vietnam.
    Another commentary published by "Vanguard" on the same day condemned the conspiracy of the so-called "Ministerial Conference of the Asian and Pacific Region" recently held in Seoul to plan to organize a new "anti-communist alliance."
    The commentary said that this conference gathered a group of Asian reactionary forces to form an aggressive bloc, directing its spearhead at the Asian people who are fighting for liberation and independence.
    The commentary said that this new "alliance" is also part of the crazy conspiracy to "contain China," and behind it lies the conspiracy to establish an aggressive system of using Asians to fight Asians. The commentary pointed out that US imperialism, through the Holt puppet government, has already dragged Australia into this "alliance" in order to involve the country more deeply in the dirty war of aggression against Vietnam.

### 澳《先锋报》揭露美苏勾结策划“和谈”新骗局  苏修竭力帮助美帝挽救侵越败局  汉城反共会议暴露了美帝“用亚洲人打亚洲人”的新阴谋

1966-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    澳《先锋报》揭露美苏勾结策划“和谈”新骗局
    苏修竭力帮助美帝挽救侵越败局
    汉城反共会议暴露了美帝“用亚洲人打亚洲人”的新阴谋
    据新华社墨尔本二十三日电　《先锋报》二十三日在评论美帝国主义就越南问题策划新的“和谈”阴谋时指出：在这一阴谋中，站在美帝国主义一边的是苏联修正主义集团。
    《先锋报》说，美帝国主义拼命想用“和平”的口号来压制要它撤出南越的要求。“它在苏联修正主义者的帮助下，试图利用世界人民的和平情绪来巩固它（在南越）的地位。”《先锋报》强调指出，要不是苏联修正主义者的帮忙，美国侵略者将比现在更加孤立和遭受到更大的失败。
    《先锋报》在谈到美帝国主义者阴谋在越南搞“休战”的骗局时指出，这是因为它在那里遭到了惨重的失败。“它在南越的地位不久将守不住。因此，如果它能策划某种谈判方案而不撤出它的军队，那么，它将有时间巩固它的军事地位和政治地位。战争的暂停将有助于缓和它自己的国内局势。美国人民正在对约翰逊政府发动强大的攻势。一次‘和平’暂停对于约翰逊说来是十分必要的。”
    《先锋报》强调说：“正确的政策是要发动反对美帝国主义的斗争——在各条战线上同它斗争，直到它被粉碎为止。”
    报纸还指出，美帝国主义在“和平”骗局的背后，正在集结更大的力量来继续和扩大战争。在苏联修正主义者的合作之下，它现在正在从欧洲调动军队到南越去。
    《先锋报》同日发表的另一篇评论，谴责最近在汉城举行的所谓“亚洲和太平洋地区部长会议”策划组织新的“反共联盟”的阴谋。
    评论说，这次会议纠集一批亚洲反动势力组成侵略集团，把矛头指向为争取解放和独立而斗争的亚洲人民。
    评论说，这个新的“联盟”也是疯狂的“遏制中国”阴谋的一部分，它的背后隐藏着建立以亚洲人打亚洲人的侵略体系的阴谋。评论指出，美帝国主义通过霍尔特傀儡政府，已经把澳大利亚拉入了这个“联盟”，目的是要使这个国家更深地卷入侵略越南的肮脏战争。
