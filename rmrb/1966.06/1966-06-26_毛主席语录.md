Chairman Mao's Quotations

    Class struggle, production struggle, and scientific experiment are the three great revolutionary movements for building a powerful socialist state. They are a positive guarantee that the Communists will be rid of bureaucratism, avoid revisionism and dogmatism, and remain invincible. They are a reliable guarantee that the proletariat can unite with the broad masses of the people and exercise democratic dictatorship.

    Comment on "Seven Good Materials on Cadres Participating in Labor in Zhejiang Province"
