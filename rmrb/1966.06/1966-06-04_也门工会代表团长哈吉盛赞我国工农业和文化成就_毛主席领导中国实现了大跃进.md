Title: Yemeni Trade Union Delegation Leader Hajj Praises China's Achievements in Industry, Agriculture, and Culture; Chairman Mao Leads China to Achieve the Great Leap Forward
Author:
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 3rd. The leader of the Yemeni Trade Union Delegation, Muhammad Abdullah Hajj, warmly praised China's tremendous achievements in industry, agriculture, and culture in an interview with our agency's reporter here regarding his impressions of his visit to China. He said that all the achievements made by the Chinese people, especially the Great Leap Forward since 1958, are the result of implementing the policy of self-reliance, the result of the continuous struggle of workers and peasants, and even more so, the result of the teachings and guidance of the Chinese Communist Party and Chinese leaders, especially Chairman Mao Zedong.
    Muhammad Abdullah Hajj especially highly praised China's People's Communes. He said that the establishment and existence of the People's Communes are very necessary for China. He pointed out that the People's Communes have shown their superiority even more in overcoming natural disasters.
    He said: "When we understand the suffering of Chinese peasants in the old society, and today see the commune members working hard to increase production and thus improve their living standards, it makes us realize that the existence of the People's Communes has enormous importance."
    Hajj said that the Yemeni people support the Chinese people's struggle to liberate Taiwan, support the Chinese people's assistance to the Vietnamese people, and express their gratitude for the Chinese people's support for the Palestinian people's struggle to return to their homeland and support for the South Yemeni people's anti-imperialist struggle.

### 也门工会代表团长哈吉盛赞我国工农业和文化成就  毛主席领导中国实现了大跃进

1966-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    也门工会代表团长哈吉盛赞我国工农业和文化成就
    毛主席领导中国实现了大跃进
    据新华社广州三日电　也门工会代表团团长穆罕默德·阿卜杜拉·哈吉在这里向本社记者畅谈访华观感时热烈赞扬中国在工业、农业和文化各方面所取得的巨大成就。他说，中国人民所取得的一切成就，特别是一九五八年以来的大跃进，是由于执行自力更生方针的结果，是由于工人和农民不断进行斗争的结果，更是由于中国共产党和中国领导人特别是毛泽东主席的教导和指引的结果。
    穆罕默德·阿卜杜拉·哈吉特别高度评价了中国的人民公社。他说，人民公社的建立和存在，对中国来说是非常必要的。他指出，在克服自然灾害方面，人民公社更显示出了它的优越性。
    他说：“当我们了解到中国农民在旧社会里所遭受的苦难，而今天又看到公社社员为增加生产从而改善自己的生活水平而进行着辛勤劳动时，使我们认识到，人民公社的存在有着它巨大的重要意义。”
    哈吉说，也门人民支持中国人民解放台湾的斗争，支持中国人民对越南人民的援助，并对中国人民支持巴勒斯坦人民重回家园的斗争、支持南也门人民的反帝斗争表示感谢。
