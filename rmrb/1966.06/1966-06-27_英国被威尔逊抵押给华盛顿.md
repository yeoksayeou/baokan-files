Title: Britain Mortgaged to Washington by Wilson
Author:
Date: 1966-06-27
Edition: 5

The British newspaper *The Guardian* recently published an article asking: “Why can’t the government decide overnight to cut all our expenses east of Suez? … Why can’t Britain resolutely declare that the Americans have gone mad in Vietnam, and Britain will not participate in their adventure?” *The Guardian* said: “The answer is that Britain is strapped into a straitjacket for madmen, and the straps are controlled by the Americans.” The “political illness” of Britain originates from “Britain’s dependence on the United States.”

The editorial of *The Guardian* on the 15th said: Britain is now “hopelessly mortgaged out,” and its foreign policy “is under the control of President Johnson.”

*The Financial Times* said: “Britain’s overseas defense expenditure has become a heavy burden in the balance sheet… It is increasingly doubtful whether this country can continue to bear the burden (east of Suez).”

Labour Party MP Alun recently wrote in a British newspaper that the Wilson government is “Johnson’s lapdog.” He believes that the $3 billion loan from Washington and other foreign banks to support the pound since November 1964, “is the reason for our shameful support for America’s invasion of Vietnam—the dirtiest war in history.”

### 英国被威尔逊抵押给华盛顿

1966-06-27
第5版()
专栏：外论摘要

    英国被威尔逊抵押给华盛顿
    英国《卫报》最近一篇文章问道：“政府为什么不能在一夜之间决定削减我国在苏伊士以东的全部费用呢？……英国为什么不能够毅然决然地说美国人在越南患了杀人狂，英国不会参与他们的冒险呢？”《卫报》说：答案是“英国是套在给疯子穿的紧身衣里，而衣服上的纽带是受美国控制的”，英国的“政治病”起源于“英国对美国的依赖”。
    《卫报》十五日的社论说：英国现在是“毫无希望地被抵押出去了”，它的外交政策“受约翰逊总统的支配”。
    《金融时报》说：“英国的海外防务开支成了支付账目中的一大负担……这个国家能否吃得消继续承受（苏伊士以东的）负担，是越来越令人怀疑了。”
    工党议员阿龙最近在英国报纸上发表的一篇文章中说，威尔逊政府是“约翰逊的卷毛狗”。他认为，一九六四年十一月以来华盛顿和其他外国银行用来支持英镑的三十亿美元贷款，“是我们卑贱地支持美国侵略越南——历史上最肮脏的战争——的原因所在”。
