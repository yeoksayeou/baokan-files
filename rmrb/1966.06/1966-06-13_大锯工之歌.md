Title: Song of the Great Sawmill Worker
Author: Sun Wenkun
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 6

    Song of the Great Sawmill Worker
　　Harbin Northeast Forestry Bureau Worker Sun Wenkun
    Great saw table,
    By my side,
    Dragon-like, tiger-like strength,
    Unleash it!
    Look, each and every one,
    Truly like tigers returning to the mountains,
    Truly like dragons stirring the sea!
    Great band saw,
    Silver and shimmering,
    Just like my own zither,
    Truly lovely!
    Great logs,
    Thick and sturdy,
    Just like my own bow,
    Truly reliable!
    We swing our arms,
    Wave our hands,
    A battle song is played out!
    Look at that transmission belt,
    Just like the Yangtze River's flowing water,
    Forever so rapid.
    Look at it, not wide,
    We say it’s not narrow—
    Millions of meters of shipbuilding material,
    It transports;
    Millions of meters of construction material,
    It carries!
    Look at it, not long,
    We say it’s not short—
    Near, it connects Beijing and Guangzhou,
    Far, it connects the Five Lakes and Four Seas.
    Great saw table,
    By my side,
    We use it as a stage every day,
    How much heroic feeling is expressed!
    Produce high-quality materials quickly, well, and economically,
    The motherland strides forward!
    The brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought,
    Shines on our hearts every day!

### 大锯工之歌
孙文库
1966-06-13
第6版()
专栏：

    大锯工之歌
　　哈尔滨东北林业局工人　孙文库
    大锯台啊，
    身边在，
    如龙似虎的力气，
    使出来！
    你看，一个个，
    真象虎归山，
    真象龙闹海！
    大带锯啊，
    银闪闪，
    就象咱的一把琴，
    真可爱！
    大原木啊，
    粗墩墩，
    就象咱的一只弓，
    真不赖！
    咱们两臂挥，
    双手摆，
    一曲战歌奏出来！
    你看那传导带，
    就象长江的流水，
    永远那样急湍。
    你看它不宽，
    咱说它不窄——
    百万米造船材，
    它给运；
    百万米建筑材，
    它给载！
    你看它不长，
    咱说它不短——
    近连北京、广州，
    远连五湖、四海。
    大锯台啊，
    身边在，
    咱天天用它作擂台，
    多少豪情抒出来！
    多快好省制良材，
    祖国双脚大步迈！
    毛泽东思想的光辉，
    天天照着咱心怀！
