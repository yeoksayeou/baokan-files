Title: Our Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Poland to Participate in the 35th Poznań International Fair
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 6th -- The Chinese Government Delegation to the 35th Poznań International Fair, led by Jia Linfang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry, left Beijing by plane today for Poland.

    In addition to visiting the Expo, Jia Linfang will also lead the Chinese Scientific and Technological Delegation to the 13th meeting of the Sino-Polish Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.  Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry Zou Tong, Deputy Director of the Scientific and Technological Commission Zhao Jichang, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Boqing, and others saw them off at the airport. Polish Ambassador to China Jerzy Knothe and others also saw them off at the airport.

### 参加第三十五届波兹南国际博览会  我政府代表团离京去波兰

1966-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    参加第三十五届波兹南国际博览会
    我政府代表团离京去波兰
    新华社六日讯　参加第三十五届波兹南国际博览会的中国政府代表团，在煤炭部副部长贾林放率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京前往波兰。
    贾林放除参观博览会外，还将率领中国科学技术代表团参加中波科学技术合作委员会第十三届会议。到机场送行的有煤炭部副部长邹桐、科学技术委员会副主任赵继昌、外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等。波兰驻中国大使耶日·克诺泰等也到机场送行。
