Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Separately Receive Distinguished Guests from Mali
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi received Mamadou Madeira Keita, member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union Party of Mali and Minister of Justice, and members of the Malian delegation led by him, this afternoon, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Minister of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission Wang Daohan, among others.
    Malian Ambassador to China, Guisse Tidiani, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Mamadou Madeira Keita, member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union Party of Mali and Minister of Justice, and members of the Malian delegation led by him, this afternoon, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Minister of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission Wang Daohan, among others.
    Malian Ambassador to China, Guisse Tidiani, was also present. (Attached picture)
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi receives Mamadou Madeira Keita (second from right), head of the Malian delegation, member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union Party of Mali, and Minister of Justice, and Oumar Ly (first from right), member of the National Planning and Economic Leadership Committee of Mali and member of the Malian delegation.
  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Yuanliu photographed

### 刘主席周总理分别接见马里贵宾

1966-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席周总理分别接见马里贵宾
    新华社七日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、司法部长马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔和由他率领的马里代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有陈毅副总理和对外经委副主任汪道涵等。
    马里驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼也在座。
    新华社七日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、司法部长马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔和由他率领的马里代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有陈毅副总理和对外经委副主任汪道涵等。
    马里驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼也在座。（附图片）
    刘少奇主席接见马里代表团团长、马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、司法部长马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔（右二）和马里代表团团员、马里全国计划和经济领导委员会委员乌马尔·利（右一）。
