Title: 阿越柬报纸谴责美机悍然袭击中国渔船 美帝如胆敢对中国玩火必遭惩罚 (Newspapers in Albania, Vietnam, and Cambodia Condemn US Aircraft's Outrageous Attack on Chinese Fishing Boats; If US Imperialism Dares to Play with Fire Against China, It Will Surely Be Punished)
Author:
Date: 1966-06-02
Edition: 4

    据新华社地拉那三十一日电　阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》和《团结报》三十一日发表编辑部文章，谴责美帝国主义者最近在公海上袭击中国渔船的野蛮罪行。
    Both newspapers emphasized that this crime was not an accidental event, but an extremely dangerous and serious provocation by the American aggressors against the People's Republic of China.

    《人民之声报》着重指出，阿尔巴尼亚人民对美国侵略者的这一新的血腥罪行感到万分愤慨，最强烈地谴责美国侵略者的这一罪行，并且同兄弟的中国人民在一起，坚决抗议美国政府的这种严重威胁各国人民安全和世界和平的行径。
    The *People's Voice* emphasized that the Albanian people are extremely indignant at this new bloody crime of the American aggressors, most strongly condemn this crime of the American aggressors, and stand with the fraternal Chinese people in resolutely protesting this act of the American government, which seriously threatens the security of the people of all countries and world peace.

    这家报纸强调指出，帝国主义侵略者对中国普通劳动者的生命和财产犯下的这一新罪行，是美帝国主义者侵略和好战的罪恶本性的又一次典型的表现，是华盛顿为了对中国进行包围、挑衅和发动进攻的整个计划的一个部分。
    The newspaper emphasized that this new crime committed by the imperialist aggressors against the lives and property of ordinary Chinese laborers is another typical manifestation of the aggressive and bellicose evil nature of US imperialism and is part of Washington's entire plan to encircle, provoke, and launch attacks against China.

    报纸指出：世界正处在帝国主义者及其走狗的罪行一定要受到惩罚的时代。万恶的美国强盗必须懂得，如果他们继续对社会主义中国采取挑衅和侵略行动，那就是在危险地玩火；他们的野蛮罪行现在和将来都是一定要受到惩罚的。
    The newspaper pointed out: The world is in an era in which the crimes of the imperialists and their lackeys must be punished. The wicked American bandits must understand that if they continue to take provocative and aggressive actions against socialist China, they are dangerously playing with fire; their barbaric crimes will certainly be punished now and in the future.

    文章说，美国侵略者尽管得到赫鲁晓夫修正主义者在苏美合作主宰世界的范围内给予他们的多方面的支持，绝对吓不倒也压不倒伟大的中国人民。在光荣的中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，中国人民在发展社会主义革命和国家的社会主义建设方面取得了光辉成就，已成为人类进步发展的重大因素，成为国际和平和安全的强有力支柱。
    The article said that the American aggressors, despite receiving multifaceted support from the Khrushchev revisionists in the scope of Soviet-American cooperation to dominate the world, absolutely cannot intimidate or suppress the great Chinese people. Under the leadership of the glorious Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people have made brilliant achievements in the development of the socialist revolution and the socialist construction of the country, becoming a major factor in the progress and development of mankind and a powerful pillar of international peace and security.

    《人民之声报》最后说，每一个真正的革命者，每一个真诚的民主人士，每一个正直的人，都必须坚决和公开地谴责美帝国主义者及其仆从的野蛮罪行和反华阴谋。
    The *People's Voice* concluded by saying that every true revolutionary, every sincere democrat, and every upright person must resolutely and openly condemn the barbaric crimes and anti-China conspiracies of US imperialism and its servants.

    新华社河内三十一日电　越南《人民报》三十一日发表评论，强烈谴责美国飞机二十八日在公海上悍然袭击中国渔船、打死打伤中国渔民的严重挑衅行动。评论指出，这一挑衅行动证明：美国对中华人民共和国的基本政策仍然是一贯采取的敌视和战争政策。
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 31st - The Vietnamese newspaper *Nhan Dan* published a commentary on the 31st, strongly condemning the serious provocative act of American aircraft outrageously attacking Chinese fishing boats on the high seas on the 28th, killing and wounding Chinese fishermen. The commentary pointed out that this provocative action proves that the United States' basic policy toward the People's Republic of China remains a consistent policy of hostility and war.

    评论说，自从美帝国主义大举向越南南方增兵和轰炸扫射越南北方以来，美国对中国的军事挑衅接连发生。美国飞机和军舰曾多次侵犯中国的领空和领海，轰炸扫射中国的渔船和商船。五月二十八日的新的挑衅事件是极其严重的事件。
    The commentary said that since US imperialism massively increased its troops to South Vietnam and bombed and strafed North Vietnam, US military provocations against China have occurred one after another. American aircraft and warships have repeatedly violated China's airspace and territorial waters, bombing and strafing Chinese fishing boats and merchant ships. The new provocative incident on May 28 was an extremely serious event.

    评论说，约翰逊集团尽管受到中国人民的严厉警告和遭到多次应有的惩罚，它们仍然顽固地继续推行反对中国的阴谋，扩大对亚洲的侵略战争。评论强调说：“越南人民坚决谴责美帝国主义加紧对中国的挑衅、粗暴地践踏国际法的政策。我们决心同兄弟的中国人民并肩战斗，打败美国侵略者的一切疯狂战争行动。”
    The commentary said that despite the Johnson clique receiving stern warnings from the Chinese people and suffering repeated due punishments, they stubbornly continue to pursue conspiracies against China and expand the aggressive war in Asia. The commentary emphasized: "The Vietnamese people resolutely condemn US imperialism's policy of intensifying provocations against China and crudely trampling on international law. We are determined to fight shoulder to shoulder with the fraternal Chinese people to defeat all the crazy war actions of the American aggressors."

    新华社金边一日电　柬埔寨《祖国报》一日就美国飞机在公海上轰炸扫射中国渔船的暴行发表评论说：美帝国主义在越南问题上找不到符合自己的卑鄙利益的解决办法，就竭力对中华人民共和国和柬埔寨进行挑衅。评论指出：“这种行径使美帝国主义越来越遭到全世界的谴责和痛恨，这也表明美帝国主义在政治和军事方面的失败。”
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 1st - The Cambodian newspaper *Khmers United* published a commentary on the 1st on the outrage of American aircraft bombing and strafing Chinese fishing boats on the high seas, saying: US imperialism, unable to find a solution to the Vietnam issue that suits its despicable interests, is doing its utmost to provoke the People's Republic of China and Cambodia. The commentary pointed out: "This kind of behavior makes US imperialism increasingly condemned and hated by the whole world, which also shows the political and military failure of US imperialism."
