Title: The Emergency Meeting of Asian and African Writers Continues
Author:
Date: 1966-06-29
Edition: 1

The emergency meeting of Asian and African writers continued today. The meeting heard reports from representatives from the frontlines of the anti-American struggle in South Vietnam and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

At the meeting, Chen Tingyun, the head of the delegation from the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, and Chun Chang, the head of the delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, gave reports. (Summaries of the reports will be published on page six.)

At today's morning meeting, congratulatory telegrams and letters from Kim Il-sung, the Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mrs. Bandaranaike of Ceylon, the Pakistan Writers' Association, the External Affairs Committee of the South West Africa People's Organization, the Kashmiri Writers' Association, and the Ceylon Journalists' Association were read out.

Today's morning meeting was chaired by representatives from Iran, Iraq, Japan, Bechuanaland, Cameroon, and the Canary Islands.

### 亚非作家紧急会议继续举行  南越民族解放阵线代表团长陈庭云和越南代表团长春长作报告

1966-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    亚非作家紧急会议继续举行
    南越民族解放阵线代表团长陈庭云和越南代表团长春长作报告
    宣读了金日成首相班达拉奈克夫人等给会议的贺电和贺信
    新华社二十八日讯　亚非作家紧急会议今天上午继续举行全体会议。会议听取了来自反美斗争前线的越南南方和越南民主共和国的代表分别作的报告。
    在会议上作报告的是越南南方民族解放阵线代表团团长陈庭云和越南民主共和国代表团团长春长（报告摘要另发，见第六版）。
    在今天上午的会议上，宣读了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成、锡兰的班达拉奈克夫人、巴基斯坦作家协会、西南非民族联盟对外委员会、克什米尔作家和锡兰新闻工作者协会等给会议的贺电和贺信。
    今天上午的会议由伊朗、伊拉克、日本、贝专纳、喀麦隆和加那利群岛的代表担任执行主席。
