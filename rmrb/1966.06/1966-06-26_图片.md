Title: Lü Houmin
Author: Lü Houmin
Date: 1966-06-26

    Students of the Department of Political Science at Nanjing University Liyang Branch, creatively study and apply Chairman Mao's works during the Great Cultural Revolution. Holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, they thoroughly expose and criticize the crimes of the anti-Party and anti-socialist counter-revolutionary element Kuang Yaming. (Fax photo) Xinhua News Agency reporter Lü Houmin.
