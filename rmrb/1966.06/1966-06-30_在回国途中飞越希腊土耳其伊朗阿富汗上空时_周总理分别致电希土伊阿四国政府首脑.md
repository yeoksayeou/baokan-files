Title: Premier Zhou Sends Telegrams to Leaders of Greece, Turkey, Iran, and Afghanistan During Flight Home
Author:
Date: 1966-06-30
Edition: 4

	In the course of returning to China after visiting Romania and Albania, Premier Zhou En-lai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent telegrams to the heads of state of Greece, Turkey, Iran, and Afghanistan while flying over their territories. The telegrams were addressed to: Prime Minister Stefanopoulos of the Kingdom of Greece, Prime Minister Suleiman Demirel of the Republic of Turkey, Prime Minister Amir Abbas Hoveyda of the Kingdom of Iran, and Prime Minister Muhammad Hashim Maiwandwal of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, expressing good wishes.

	Premier Zhou En-lai has received a reply from Turkish Prime Minister Suleiman Demirel, thanking him for the telegram expressing good wishes.

### 在回国途中飞越希腊土耳其伊朗阿富汗上空时  周总理分别致电希土伊阿四国政府首脑

1966-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    在回国途中飞越希腊土耳其伊朗阿富汗上空时
    周总理分别致电希土伊阿四国政府首脑
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十八日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在访问罗马尼亚和阿尔巴尼亚以后回国途中，今天飞经希腊、土耳其、伊朗和阿富汗领空时，打电报向这些国家的政府首脑表示良好的祝愿。这些电报分别打给：希腊王国政府首相斯特凡诺普洛斯，土耳其共和国总理苏莱曼·德米雷尔，伊朗王国政府首相阿米尔·阿巴斯·胡韦达和阿富汗王国政府首相穆罕默德·哈希姆·迈万德瓦尔。
    周恩来总理已经收到了土耳其总理苏莱曼·德米雷尔的复电，答谢他的表示良好祝愿的电报。
