Title: Smashed the Myth of American Bandit "Air Superiority": Vietnam's Military and Civilians Have Shot Down 1,100 American Planes
Author:
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 7th. It was announced here on the afternoon of the 7th that militiamen of Nghi Lộc County, Nghệ An Province, North Vietnam, shot down an American jet fighter with light weapons on the 6th.

    On June 4th, the military and civilians of Quảng Bình Province also shot down an American plane.

    Up to this point, the military and civilians of North Vietnam have shot down a total of 1,100 American bandit planes since August 5, 1964.

    The General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army sent a telegram to the militiamen of Nghi Lộc County on the 7th, warmly commending them for their meritorious service in shooting down the 1,100th American bandit plane on the 6th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 7th. According to a supplementary report by the Vietnam News Agency, the military and civilians of Quảng Bình Province, North Vietnam, shot down two American bandit planes invading the skies over Đồng Hới City on June 5th.

    The military and civilians of Nghệ An Province shot down two American bandit planes on May 28th and June 1st respectively.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th. It was announced here on the 6th that the military and civilians of Nghệ An Province, North Vietnam, shot down two American bandit planes on the afternoon of the 5th.

    According to supplementary news, the military and civilians of Nghệ An and Hà Tĩnh provinces each shot down an American plane on the 3rd.

    On May 31st, the military and civilians of Nghĩa Lộ Province and Yên Bái Province each shot down an American plane.

### 粉碎美国强盗“空中优势”的神话  越南军民击落美机已达一千一百架

1966-06-08
第5版()
专栏：

    粉碎美国强盗“空中优势”的神话
    越南军民击落美机已达一千一百架
    新华社河内七日电　据这里七日下午公布：越南北方义安省宜禄县民兵，六日用轻武器击落美国喷气式飞机一架。
    六月四日，广平省军民还击落美机一架。
    至此，越南北方军民从一九六四年八月五日以来共击落美国强盗飞机一千一百架。
    越南人民军总司令部七日打电报给宜禄县民兵，热烈嘉奖他们六日击落第一千一百架美国强盗飞机的功勋。
    新华社河内七日电　据越南通讯社补充报道，越南北方广平省军民六月五日击落入侵洞海市上空的美国强盗飞机两架。
    义安省军民在五月二十八日和六月一日先后击落美国强盗飞机两架。
    新华社河内六日电　据这里六日公布：越南北方义安省军民，五日下午击落美国强盗飞机两架。
    另据补充消息说，义安、河静两省军民三日各击落美机一架。
    五月三十一日，义路省和安沛省军民各击落美机一架。
