Title: Representatives of Writers from 47 Countries and Regions Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-27
Edition: 1

In the history of the Asian-African Writers' Movement, the unprecedentedly large-scale Asian-African Writers' Emergency Conference will open tomorrow in Beijing. By tonight, representatives from 47 Asian and African countries and regions have arrived in Beijing.

This Asian-African Writers' Emergency Conference holds great significance in the united struggle of Asian and African peoples against imperialism. The Soviet revisionists, who advocate splitting, have cut themselves off from the ranks of Asian and African writers and are engaged in sabotage and splitting activities. However, more Asian and African writers have come to Beijing. The number of representatives attending this conference and the breadth of countries and regions represented are unprecedented in the history of the Asian-African Writers' Movement.

The people of Beijing have welcomed the representatives from various countries with great enthusiasm. On the main streets of Beijing, huge banners welcoming the Asian-African writers' representatives are hanging. The venue for the Asian-African Writers' Emergency Conference is set in the magnificent Jingxi Guesthouse. On the plaza of the guesthouse, there is a red signboard written in Chinese, Arabic, English, and French that reads, "Welcome to the friends attending the Asian-African Writers' Emergency Conference!" Colorful flags flutter in the wind on both sides of the signboard.

### 四十七个国家和地区的作家代表到北京

1966-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    四十七个国家和地区的作家代表到北京
    新华社二十六日讯　在亚非作家运动的历史上规模空前的亚非作家紧急会议，明天将在北京开幕。到今天夜间为止，已有四十七个亚非国家和地区的作家代表到达北京。
    这次亚非作家紧急会议在亚非人民团结反帝事业中，有着重大的意义。制造分裂的苏联修正主义者自绝于亚非作家的队伍，进行破坏分裂活动，但是更多的亚非作家来到了北京。这次出席会议的代表人数之多，国家和地区之广，在亚非作家运动的历史上是前所未有的。
    北京人民以极大的热情欢迎各国代表。在北京的主要街道上，悬挂着欢迎亚非作家代表的巨幅标语。亚非作家紧急会议的会场设在宏伟的京西宾馆，在宾馆广场上，树立着用中文、阿拉伯文、英文、法文写的“欢迎参加亚非作家紧急会议的朋友们！”的红色字标。字标两旁彩旗迎风招展。
