Talks Between Chinese and Albanian Party and Government Leaders Conclude

New China News Agency, Tirana, June 27 — The Chinese Party and Government Delegation, led by Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, concluded its talks with the leaders of the Albanian Party and State on the morning of the 27th.

The talks were conducted in an atmosphere of fraternal unity and sincere friendship.

### 中阿党政领导人会谈结束

1966-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中阿党政领导人会谈结束
    新华社地拉那二十七日电　由中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来率领的中国党政代表团二十七日上午结束了同阿尔巴尼亚党和国家领导人的会谈。
    会谈是在兄弟团结和十分真挚友好的气氛中进行的。
