Title: Newly Appointed Malian Ambassador to China Calls on Chairman of the National People's Congress, Zhu De
Author:
Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 6

The newly appointed Malian Ambassador to China, Gi Se Ti Dian Ni, paid a call on Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhu De, this afternoon. Zhu De Chairman had a friendly conversation with him.

### 马里新任驻华大使拜会朱德委员长

1966-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    马里新任驻华大使拜会朱德委员长
    新华社二十五日讯　马里共和国新任驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼今天下午拜会全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德。朱德委员长同他进行了友好的谈话。
