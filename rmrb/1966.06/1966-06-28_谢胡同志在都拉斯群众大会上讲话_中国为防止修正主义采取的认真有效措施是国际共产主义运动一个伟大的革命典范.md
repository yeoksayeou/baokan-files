Title: Comrade Xie Hu Speaks at the Durres Mass Rally
Author:
Date: 1966-06-28
Edition: 2

Comrade Xie Hu Speaks at the Durres Mass Rally
The measures taken by China to prevent revisionism are a great revolutionary example for the international communist movement

Dear Comrade Zhou Enlai, dear Chinese comrades and friends, citizens of Durres:

The Chinese party and government delegation led by Comrade Zhou Enlai is visiting Durres today. We are very happy about this. Durres is Albania's largest port. It is an important factor in breaking the blockade of imperialism and revisionism against the People's Republic of Albania. Together with China's great port of Guangzhou, it plays an important role in strengthening the friendly ties and economic exchanges between the People's Republic of Albania and the People's Republic of China. For these two ports are the two pillars of the "sea bridge" between Albania and China.

The friendship between our two peoples is indeed a great friendship. It has withstood the test of the times. It is eternal and great because it is based on the principles of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, because it is created by the Marxist-Leninist parties of our two countries. It is a fighting friendship because it is forged and tempered in the common revolutionary struggle for common revolutionary goals, for the victory of socialism and communism, against common enemies, the imperialists and revisionists.

The Albanian people and the Albanian Party of Labour highly respect the seven hundred million Chinese people, the great Chinese Communist Party, and Comrade Mao Zedong. The great people of China, who make up a quarter of humanity, have already become a powerful and indestructible fortress of socialism. Over it, the red flag of Mao Zedong's Marxist-Leninist thought is flying in the wind; there, the revolutionary struggle for socialist production is flourishing; there, a class struggle is being waged to bury bourgeois ideas, defeat class enemies, prevent the emergence of revisionism, and prevent the restoration of capitalism; there, scientific experiments are advancing like an avalanche, and the basis of this scientific experiment is the creative efforts of the working masses to smash old things and open the way for everything new and revolutionary.

Facing the invasion and enslavement plots of imperialism led by the United States, facing the great betrayal of modern revisionism centered on the leadership of Khrushchev in the Soviet Union, the great Chinese people and the glorious Chinese Communist Party are fully aware of their responsibility to the socialist camp and the international communist movement. The Chinese people and the Chinese Communist Party have made up their minds not to allow revisionists to usurp the leadership of the party and the state, not to allow the restoration of capitalism; not to allow the great tragedy that occurred in the Soviet Union after the death of the great Stalin to be repeated in China. The Chinese people and the Chinese Communist Party have taken and are taking serious and effective measures to accomplish this historic task of ensuring the victory of socialism in China and not only in China. This is a great revolutionary example for all socialist countries and for the entire international communist movement.

The great proletarian cultural revolution that is being carried out and is victorious in the People's Republic of China once again proves the universal truth of Marxism-Leninism: throughout the entire historical period of socialist construction, in socialist countries, the class struggle between the two roads of socialism and capitalism continues to exist, and the danger of the emergence of revisionism and the restoration of capitalism continues to exist; it proves that in order to achieve the victory of socialism and communism, it is not enough to overthrow the bourgeoisie and establish the dictatorship of the proletariat, to abolish private ownership, and to establish socialist ownership of the means of production, but it is also necessary to carry out a thorough socialist revolution in the fields of ideology and culture, and to maintain and strengthen the dictatorship of the proletariat throughout this entire historical period, and to carry the revolution through in all aspects of life.

The great proletarian cultural revolution being carried out in the People's Republic of China has torn off the masks of the bourgeois elements, revisionists, and anti-socialist elements hiding under the red flag of Marxism-Leninism. We are happy and inspired by the achievements of this revolution and believe that this revolution will achieve complete victory because it is guided by the Marxist-Leninist thought of Comrade Mao Zedong.

The great achievements of the People's Republic of China in all aspects of life and its consistent, principled, and revolutionary policy are a brilliant example of the creative development and application of Marxism-Leninism under the specific conditions of China today, a great school and source of inspiration for all Marxist-Leninists. We are happy and proud of these achievements of our great and loyal ally, the People's Republic of China, and we regard them as our own achievements and victories, and we wish our brotherly Chinese people greater and greater achievements on the road of revolution, for the victory of socialism in China, for the socialist camp and the international communist movement, and for all mankind.

Comrades!

The Chinese party and government delegation led by Comrade Zhou Enlai, at the invitation of our Party Central Committee and our government, has come to our country at a time when the entire people of Albania, under the loyal leadership of our Party of Labour and its Central Committee led by Comrade Enver Hoxha, are mobilizing all forces, displaying revolutionary enthusiasm, and relying on their own efforts to accomplish the great task of socialist construction; at a time when our people are not only developing socialist production but are also in a period of high revolutionary tide in the revolutionary tempering of people's thinking, in educating people with the immortal great works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, in raising the revolutionary banner of class struggle, and in blocking all channels for the emergence of revisionism and the restoration of capitalism in our country.

The revolutionary measures taken and to be taken by our Party and government, such as: rooting out bureaucracy in our country's organs, thoroughly implementing the mass line, further continuously promoting socialist democracy, giving young people comprehensive revolutionary tempering, strengthening the army in all aspects of thought, politics, and technology; always emphasizing proletarian politics; waging a struggle against bourgeois ideology and revisionist ideology (these ideologies are embodied by the remnants of the exploited classes who have been overthrown and had their property confiscated, and by the wavering and weak elements in our ranks who are influenced by bourgeois ideology and revisionist ideology); taking concrete measures to gradually narrow the gap between mental and manual labor, between urban and rural areas, between workers and peasants, and other revolutionary measures, have great historical significance for our country. Our Party has learned a great deal from the revisionist tragedies that occurred in the Soviet Union and some socialist countries where revisionists came to power. Our Party is determined to carry out the Party's revolutionary line to the end and will not allow what happened in the Soviet Union and other socialist countries to happen in our country.

The Party of Labour of Albania and the Chinese Communist Party are closely cooperating and exchanging valuable historical experiences in this regard.

It has been only a few weeks since the signing of the Albanian-Chinese Joint Statement in Beijing. This statement embodies the unity of thought and action of our two peoples, two parties, and two governments. Every day, new facts prove the correctness of the Marxist-Leninist theses of this statement.

There is no denying that the current international situation is characterized by: the deepening of the general crisis of the world capitalist system, which is causing the further collapse of imperialism. This crisis, both in quantity and quality, has caused a change in the balance of international forces that is favorable to the struggle of the peoples of all countries and unfavorable to imperialism. The internal and external contradictions of the capitalist system are becoming increasingly acute, leading to the disintegration of the imperialist alliance. The American imperialism, which acts as the international gendarme to brutally suppress the liberation movements of the peoples of all countries, is facing inevitable failure. The more it plays this role, the more serious its situation becomes, and the more fiercely the flames of the struggle of the peoples of all countries for freedom and independence burn.

Today, the weaknesses of the main imperialist power in the world, the United States, are more clearly manifested in the Vietnam War than anywhere else in the world. The people's war of the heroic Vietnamese people is becoming the grave of the living forces of the American imperialist army, and it is burying the myth of the invincibility of American imperialism. The peoples of all countries in the world are supporting the struggle of the Vietnamese people. The Albanian people have supported and will continue to support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people until final victory. We are fully convinced that final victory will belong to the Vietnamese people.

The leadership of Soviet revisionism is striving to serve the global strategy of the United States to "contain China," "surround and isolate China," and then "attack China." The American imperialists and the Soviet revisionists, in order to realize their cooperation to dominate the world, are trying in every possible way, through various military and political alliances, to plot conspiracies and tricks, to play open or covert conspiracies, in order to organize a circle of fire around China.

In many aspects of international life, the collusion between the leadership of Soviet revisionism and American imperialism is clearly visible, especially in their attempt to extinguish the flames of the revolutionary struggle of the Vietnamese people.

The Soviet revisionists and American imperialists are plotting a second Munich conspiracy against the heroic people of Vietnam. They are trying to achieve this goal through "peace talks." To achieve this conspiracy, they have used all kinds of means. However, the heroic people of South Vietnam and their leader, the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, and the heroic people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and their leader, the Vietnamese Workers' Party, have seen through the enemy's purpose and will not fall into the trap of this great conspiracy of imperialism and revisionism. We have full confidence in this. The Vietnamese people have declared that the only way to solve the Vietnamese problem is to implement the five-point declaration of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the four-point proposal of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. We fully support this revolutionary position without reservation.

The leadership of Soviet revisionism, in collusion with the imperialists, is plotting a conspiracy to eliminate the German Democratic Republic under the pretext of "European security," with the aim of making imperialism have no worries in Europe, so that it can freely plot wars against the People's Republic of China in Asia.

However, no matter what tricks the imperialists and revisionists play, they will fail. Reactionary and treacherous actions, imperialism and revisionism, cannot turn back the wheel of history. The great people of China, under the guidance of the Marxist-Leninist teachings of Comrade Mao Zedong, have become an invincible giant and the strongest fortress of the socialist camp and the world revolution. No force can destroy this fortress. In the struggle against imperialism, revisionism, and the reactionaries of all countries, all progressive peoples of the world and all true Marxist-Leninists stand on the side of the people of China. Our people, our Party, and our government fully support the position and correct foreign policy of the People's Republic of China. We stand on the same front line with the People's Republic of China in the struggle against imperialism, revisionism, and the reactionaries of all countries. Our two countries are fighting to expand the anti-imperialist united front, but this united front must not include the revisionists but must wage open struggle against them.

The revisionist front, like the imperialist front, is in the process of being completely weakened.

Today, the dividing line between revisionism and Marxism-Leninism is very clear. Whoever does not see this dividing line, does not see this chasm, does not know who is a friend and who is an enemy, cannot distinguish right from wrong, is sleeping soundly, is walking with his eyes closed towards an abyss. If one does not wake up immediately, there is a danger of falling into this abyss. Some people pretend to be "neutral" in the face of this life-and-death struggle between Marxism-Leninism and modern revisionism. In this struggle, "neutrality" will only benefit modern revisionism. It is time for every Marxist-Leninist party to take responsibility for its position towards its own people and the international communist movement. Those politicians who try to persuade us with their revisionism under the cover of "neutrality" to stop the open struggle, to "unite in action against imperialism" with the Khrushchev revisionists, to "seek common ground while reserving differences," and who have taken other deceitful positions, have not and will not deceive the Party of Labour of Albania.

The Party of Labour of Albania, led by Comrade Enver Hoxha, will steadfastly advance side by side with the great Chinese Communist Party led by Comrade Mao Zedong and all true Marxist-Leninist parties and forces in the world, and will unswervingly carry the struggle against modern revisionism centered on the leadership of Soviet Khrushchevism and with the Tito group as the vanguard to the end.

Just as our party and government delegation's visit to China, the visit of the Chinese party and government delegation led by Comrade Zhou Enlai to our country will further strengthen the great, indestructible friendship between our two peoples, two parties, and two governments. In the common cause of revolution and socialism, in the common struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism, the indestructible and vigorous fighting friendship between Albania and China will be further consolidated and strengthened. At any time and under any circumstances, no matter how complex the situation is, our two peoples, two countries, and two parties will advance together with the same revolutionary steps, will do their utmost to support and help each other, because our two parties are parties guided by the immortal teachings of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism.

Long live the indestructible, revolutionary friendship between Albania and China for generations to come!

Long live the great fighters for socialism, revolution, and the freedom of the peoples of all countries—the heroic seven hundred million Chinese people!

Glory to the great Chinese Communist Party led by Comrade Mao Zedong!

Glory to Marxism-Leninism!

### 谢胡同志在都拉斯群众大会上讲话  中国为防止修正主义采取的认真有效措施是国际共产主义运动一个伟大的革命典范

1966-06-28
第2版()
专栏：

    谢胡同志在都拉斯群众大会上讲话
    中国为防止修正主义采取的认真有效措施是国际共产主义运动一个伟大的革命典范亲爱的周恩来同志，亲爱的中国同志们、朋友们，都拉斯市的市民们：
    以周恩来同志为首的中国党政代表团今天访问都拉斯市。我们感到非常高兴。都拉斯是阿尔巴尼亚最大的港口。它是阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国打破帝国主义、修正主义封锁的一个重要因素。它和中国的巨大港口广州港一起，在加强阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国和中华人民共和国之间的友好联系和经济来往方面起着重要的作用。因为，这两个港口是阿中“海上桥梁的两座桥墩”。
    我们两国人民之间的友谊的确是伟大的友谊。它经受了时代的风浪的考验。它永远是伟大的，永恒的，因为它是建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义原则基础之上的，因为它是我们两国马克思列宁主义政党缔造起来的。它是战斗的友谊，因为它是在为了共同的革命目的，为了社会主义和共产主义的胜利，反对共同敌人帝国主义者和修正主义者而进行的共同革命斗争中建立和锤炼起来的。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党十分敬佩七亿中国人民、伟大的中国共产党和毛泽东同志。占人类四分之一的伟大的人民中国，已经成了社会主义的强大的和不可摧毁的堡垒。在它的上空，毛泽东同志的马克思列宁主义思想红旗迎风飘扬；在那里，为社会主义生产而进行的革命斗争正在蓬勃发展；在那里，进行着一场埋葬资产阶级思想、战胜阶级敌人、防止产生修正主义和防止复辟资本主义的阶级斗争；在那里，科学实验象雪崩一样势不可挡，这个科学实验的基础是劳动群众为粉碎旧事物、给一切新事物和革命事物开辟道路而作出的创造性努力。
    面对着以美国为首的帝国主义的侵略和奴役阴谋，面对着以苏联赫鲁晓夫主义的领导为中心的现代修正主义的大背叛，伟大的中国人民和光荣的中国共产党完全意识到他们对社会主义阵营和国际共产主义运动所担负的责任。中国人民和中国共产党已经完全下定决心，不许修正主义者在他们的国家里篡夺党和国家的领导，不许资本主义复辟；不许苏联在伟大的斯大林逝世后所发生的大悲剧在中国重演。中国人民和中国共产党已经采取了，并正在采取认真的和有效的措施，以完成这个决定着社会主义在中国，并且不仅在中国取得胜利的具有历史意义的任务。这对所有社会主义国家，对整个国际共产主义运动，都是一个伟大的革命典范。
    正在中华人民共和国掀起并胜利开展的无产阶级文化革命，再一次证明马克思列宁主义的这一普遍真理：在社会主义建设的整个历史时期，在社会主义国家里，继续存在着社会主义和资本主义两条道路之间的阶级斗争，继续存在着产生修正主义和复辟资本主义的危险；它证明为了取得社会主义和共产主义的胜利，推翻资产阶级和确立无产阶级专政，消灭私有制，建立社会主义的生产资料所有制，都是不够的，还必须彻底进行思想和文化领域中的社会主义革命，也必须在这整个历史时期内，维护和加强无产阶级专政，并在生活领域的各个方面把革命进行到底。
    中华人民共和国正在进行的无产阶级文化革命，撕破了隐藏在马克思列宁主义红旗下的资产阶级分子、修正主义分子和反社会主义分子的假面具。我们为这一革命的成就而感到高兴和受到鼓舞，并相信这一革命将取得完全胜利，因为这次革命是以毛泽东同志的马克思列宁主义思想为指南的。
    中华人民共和国在生活的各方面取得的宏伟成就和她遵循的始终一贯的、原则性的、革命的政策，是当代在中国具体条件下创造性地发展和运用马克思列宁主义的光辉典范，是所有马克思列宁主义者的一所大学校和鼓舞源泉。我们为我们伟大而忠诚的同盟者中华人民共和国的这些成就感到高兴和骄傲，我们把它们看作是我们自己的成就和胜利，并祝兄弟的中国人民在革命的道路上取得越来越大的成就，以有利于社会主义在中国的胜利，有利于社会主义阵营和国际共产主义运动，有利于全人类。
    同志们！
    由周恩来同志率领的中国党政代表团应我们党中央和我国政府的邀请，来到我们国家，这正是阿尔巴尼亚全国人民在我们劳动党和以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的中央委员会的忠诚领导下，一手拿镐，一手拿枪，全面动员起来，发扬革命干劲，自力更生地完成社会主义建设的伟大任务的时候；正是我国人民不仅在发展社会主义生产方面，而且还在人的思想革命锻炼方面，在用马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的不朽的伟大学说来教育人方面，在高举阶级斗争的革命旗帜，堵塞在我国产生修正主义和复辟资本主义的一切孔道方面，都处在革命浪潮的高涨时期。
    我们党和我国政府所采取和将要采取的革命措施，如：根除我国机关中的官僚主义，完善地贯彻群众路线，进一步不断发扬社会主义民主，对青年进行全面的革命锻炼，在思想、政治和技术等方面全面加强军队；永远突出无产阶级政治；进行反对资产阶级思想和修正主义思想的斗争（这一些思想的体现者是被推翻和被没收了财产的剥削阶级残余，我们队伍中由于受资产阶级思想和修正主义思想影响而产生的动摇和脆弱分子）；采取具体措施，不断缩小脑力劳动和体力劳动的差别、城乡差别、工农差别，以及其它革命措施，这对我国有着伟大的历史意义。我们党从苏联和修正主义者上了台的一些社会主义国家发生的修正主义悲剧中得到了很大的教训。我们党决心彻底执行党的革命路线，不许在苏联和其它一些社会主义国家中所发生的事情在我国重演。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和中国共产党在这方面紧密合作，互相交流具有历史意义的宝贵经验。
    在北京签署阿中联合声明仅仅过去几个星期。这一声明体现了我们两国人民、两党和两国政府的思想一致和行动一致，生活每天都在以新的事实证明这一声明的马克思列宁主义论点是正确的。
