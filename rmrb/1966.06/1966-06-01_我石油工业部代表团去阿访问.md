Title: Delegation of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry to Visit Albania
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency: At the invitation of the Ministry of Industry and Mines of the People's Republic of Albania, a delegation from the Chinese Ministry of Petroleum Industry, led by Tang Ke, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, left Beijing by plane on the 28th for a friendly visit to Albania.
    Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Li Yingji, Vice Director of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries, Sun Xiaofeng, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Albanian Ambassador to China, Vasil Nathanaili, also saw the delegation off at the airport.

### 我石油工业部代表团去阿访问

1966-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我石油工业部代表团去阿访问
    新华社讯　应阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国工矿部的邀请，由石油工业部副部长唐克率领的中国石油工业部代表团，二十八日乘飞机离开北京前往阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问。
    到机场送行的有对外经济联络委员会副主任李应吉、石油工业部副部长孙晓风等有关方面负责人。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利也到机场送行。
