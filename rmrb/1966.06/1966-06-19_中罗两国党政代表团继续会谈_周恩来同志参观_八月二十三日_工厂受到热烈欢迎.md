Title: Sino-Romanian Party and Government Delegations Continue Talks Comrade Zhou Enlai Visits "August 23" Factory and Receives Warm Welcome
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-19
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 18th – The Chinese Party and Government Delegation and the Romanian Party and Government Delegation continued to hold formal talks at the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Building on the 18th.
    The talks were conducted in a comradely and sincere atmosphere.
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 18th – The Chinese Party and Government Delegation, led by Comrade Zhou Enlai, visited the "August 23rd" Heavy Machinery Manufacturing Plant in Bucharest on the afternoon of the 17th, accompanied by Emil Bodnăraș, Member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Member of the Standing Presidium of the Central Committee, and First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and was warmly welcomed by all the factory's workers.
    When Comrade Zhou Enlai and delegation members Zhao Yimin, Qiao Guanhua, and Zeng Yongquan arrived at the factory, the workers gathered in front of the factory gate, waving Chinese and Romanian national flags and flowers, and warmly cheered the distinguished Chinese guests. Young female workers presented Comrade Zhou Enlai and the members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation with flowers.
    The "August 23rd" factory is one of the largest comprehensive machinery manufacturing plants that has rapidly developed since Romania's liberation. It has more than 11,000 employees and can produce complete sets of equipment for cement and sulfuric acid plants, manufacture blast furnaces, rolling mill equipment, diesel locomotives, and so on.
    After the factory director, Petre Tănăsie, introduced the factory's development history and production situation to Comrade Zhou Enlai, he led the distinguished Chinese guests to visit the complete equipment workshop and the diesel locomotive workshop. Comrade Zhou Enlai was warmly welcomed by the workers in the workshops. The workers cordially shook hands with Comrade Zhou Enlai.
    After the visit, the factory leaders invited Comrade Zhou Enlai to write an inscription in the factory's guestbook. Comrade Zhou Enlai wrote: "Your factory has left a deep impression on me. With your outstanding labor, you have made significant contributions to Romania's socialist construction. You are worthy of the glorious and historically significant name 'August 23rd.'"
      "We heartily congratulate you on achieving new and greater successes in realizing the new Five-Year Plan!"
    Factory director Petre Tănăsie, on behalf of all the factory workers, presented Comrade Zhou Enlai with a model of a 700-horsepower diesel locomotive produced by the factory, as well as a collection of photographs of various machines produced by the factory, as souvenirs. Comrade Zhou Enlai also presented gifts to the factory.
    When Comrade Zhou Enlai and the delegation members left the factory, the workers gathered in the factory yard once again cheered loudly and waved enthusiastically to see off the distinguished guests.
    Afterward, the Chinese Party and Government Delegation took a car tour of Bucharest. The streets along which the delegation passed were lined with welcoming crowds. They warmly cheered and applauded the distinguished Chinese guests.
    Accompanying the Chinese Party and Government Delegation on the factory visit and city tour were also Dumitru Popa, Member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and First Secretary of the Romanian Communist Party Bucharest City Committee; Gheorghe Macovescu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Dumitru Ghizela, Alternate Member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Deputy Minister of the Party Central Committee; and Aurel Duma, Alternate Member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Ambassador to China.

### 中罗两国党政代表团继续会谈  周恩来同志参观“八月二十三日”工厂受到热烈欢迎

1966-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗两国党政代表团继续会谈
    周恩来同志参观“八月二十三日”工厂受到热烈欢迎
    新华社布加勒斯特十八日电　中国党政代表团和罗马尼亚党政代表团十八日在罗共中央委员会大厦继续举行正式会谈。
    会谈是在同志式的诚挚气氛中进行的。
    新华社布加勒斯特十八日电　由周恩来同志率领的中国党政代表团，十七日下午在罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席埃·波德纳拉希陪同下，参观了布加勒斯特以罗马尼亚解放日“八月二十三日”命名的重型机器制造厂，受到工厂全体职工的热烈欢迎。
    当周恩来同志和代表团团员赵毅敏、乔冠华和曾涌泉来到工厂时，职工们聚集在工厂门前挥舞中罗两国国旗和鲜花，热情地向中国贵宾欢呼。青年女工向周恩来同志和中国党政代表团团员献了鲜花。
    “八月二十三日”工厂是罗马尼亚解放后迅速发展起来的最大的综合性机器制造厂之一，它拥有一万一千多名职工，可以生产水泥和硫酸等工厂的成套设备，制造高炉、轧钢设备和柴油机车等等。
    工厂厂长彼·图尔库向周恩来同志介绍了这家工厂发展历史和生产情况之后，引导中国贵宾参观了成套设备车间和柴油机车车间。周恩来同志受到车间工人们的热烈欢迎。工人们亲切地同周恩来同志握手。
    参观结束后，工厂负责人请周恩来同志在工厂贵宾留言簿上题词。周恩来同志写道：“你们的工厂给我留下了深刻的印象。你们以自己出色的劳动，为罗马尼亚的社会主义建设事业作出了重大贡献。你们无愧于‘八月二十三日’这个光荣的、具有历史意义的名称。”
      “我们衷心地祝贺你们在实现新的五年计划中取得新的、更大的成就！”
    工厂厂长彼·图尔库代表全厂职工把这家工厂生产的七百匹马力的柴油机车模型以及出产的各种机器的照片集送给周恩来同志留念。周恩来同志也向工厂赠送了礼品。
    当周恩来同志和代表团员们离开工厂时，聚集在工厂院子里的职工们再一次高声欢呼，热情地招手欢送贵宾。
    随后，中国党政代表团乘车游览布加勒斯特市容。代表团经过的街道两旁站满了欢迎的人群。他们向中国贵宾热烈欢呼和鼓掌。
    陪同中国党政代表团参观工厂和游览市容的还有罗马尼亚共产党中央委员、罗马尼亚共产党布加勒斯特市委员会第一书记杜米特鲁·波帕、外交部副部长乔·马科维斯库、罗马尼亚共产党候补中央委员、党中央副部长杜·拉泽尔和罗马尼亚共产党候补中央委员、驻华大使奥·杜马。
