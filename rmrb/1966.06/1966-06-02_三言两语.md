    Brief Comments
    Misjudging the Target
    U.S. Senator J. William Fulbright, Chairman of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee, said he thought Defense Secretary McNamara was "overly optimistic" about the situation in Vietnam. However, he said, "I don't blame the Defense Secretary for making a wrong judgment about the situation. Our policy might have made different enemies beg for peace."
    It turns out the problem still lies in misjudging the target. The Vietnamese people will not be intimidated by the war blackmail of U.S. imperialism, but McNamara believes that the Vietnamese people can be forced to submit by force. Isn't this a wrong judgment of the situation?
    Not Contradictory
    Japanese Foreign Minister Shiina shamelessly defended the intrusion of U.S. nuclear submarines into Japan, saying that "the work of promoting peace in Vietnam and the entry of nuclear-powered submarines into port are two separate issues." This means that Japan's permission for U.S. nuclear submarines to be stationed in Japan is not contradictory to the Sato government's peddling of the U.S. "peace talks" swindle.
    The Sato government has consistently echoed and supported the U.S.'s two-pronged strategy. Its introduction of U.S. nuclear submarines, making Japan a base for the U.S. war of aggression against Vietnam, is not contradictory to its active peddling of the U.S. "peace talks" swindle. However, they are not two separate issues, but two sides of the same issue. That issue is that the Japanese government has become an accomplice of U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam.

### 三言两语

1966-06-02
第4版()
专栏：三言两语

    三言两语
    看错对象
    美国参议院外交委员会主席富布赖特说，他认为国防部长麦克纳马拉对越南局势“过分乐观”。但是，他说：“我并不怪国防部长对局势作出错误判断。对不同的敌人，我们的政策可能已经使他们乞求和平了。”
    原来毛病还是出在看错对象上面。越南人民是不会被美帝国主义的战争讹诈吓倒的，而麦克纳马拉却认为可以用武力迫使越南人民屈服。这不是对局势作出的错误判断又是什么呢？
    并不矛盾
    日本外务大臣椎名恬不知耻地为美核潜艇闯进日本辩解，说什么“促进越南实现和平的工作与核动力潜艇入港，是两个问题”。这个意思是说，日本允许美国核潜艇进驻，同佐藤政府为美国兜售“和谈”骗局并不矛盾。
    佐藤政府一直附和和支持美国的两手策略，它引进美国核潜艇，让日本成为美国侵越战争基地，同它积极兜售美国“和谈”骗局的活动，本来就不矛盾。不过，它不是两个问题，而是一个问题的两面。这个问题就是日本政府已经成为美帝国主义侵略越南的帮凶。
