Title: Guo, Vice Chairman, Receives Mexican Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd. Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and his wife, Yu Liqun, received Adolfo Orive Alba, former Mexican Minister of Water Resources and renowned engineer, along with his family of nine, this afternoon, and held a friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and others.

### 郭副委员长接见墨西哥客人

1966-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    郭副委员长接见墨西哥客人
    新华社二十三日讯　郭沫若副委员长和夫人于立群今天下午接见墨西哥前水利部长、著名工程师阿杜勒富·奥里维·阿勒巴和他的夫人等一家九人，并同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南等。
