Title: Guo Vice-Chairman Receives Romanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, June 7th, Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the Romanian journalists delegation led by Nestor Ignat, Chairman of the Romanian Journalists Association, this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Wang Yi, Zhou Xinwu, Bi Jilong and other responsible persons from relevant departments. Also present at the reception were diplomatic officials from the Romanian Embassy in China.
    On the afternoon of the 5th, the All-China Journalists Association held a banquet to bid farewell to the Romanian journalists delegation.
    The banquet was presided over by Zhu Muzhi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association.
    Present at the banquet were Yin Da, Hu Chi, Chang Zhiqing, Zhou Xinwu, Gu Wenhua, Bi Jilong, Sun Xiaocun, Xing Fangqun, responsible persons from the capital's press circles and related departments, as well as Wang Yi, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Journalists Association, and Secretaries Deng Gang and Li Wu. Romanian Ambassador to China, Aurel Duma, and embassy staff were invited to attend the banquet.
    Zhu Muzhi and Nestor Ignat spoke at the banquet.

### 郭副委员长接见罗马尼亚客人

1966-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    郭副委员长接见罗马尼亚客人
    据新华社七日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，今天下午接见了由罗马尼亚新闻工作者协会主席内斯托尔·伊格纳特率领的罗马尼亚新闻工作者代表团，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有王揖、周新武、毕季龙等有关方面负责人。接见时在座的，还有罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆外交官员。
    五日中午，中华全国新闻工作者协会举行宴会，欢送罗马尼亚新闻工作者代表团。
    宴会由中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之主持。
    出席宴会的，有首都新闻界和有关方面的负责人尹达、胡痴、常芝青、周新武、顾文华、毕季龙、孙晓村、邢方群，以及记协书记处第一书记王揖，书记邓岗、李伍。罗马尼亚驻我国大使奥列尔·杜马和使馆人员应邀出席了宴会。
    朱穆之、内斯托尔·伊格纳特在宴会上讲了话。
