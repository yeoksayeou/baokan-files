Title: Our Islamic Friendship Delegation Leaves Damascus for Beirut
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-14
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, June 11th. The Chinese Islamic Friendship Delegation, led by Zhang Jie, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, left here for Beirut, the capital of Lebanon, on the 11th after a ten-day visit to Syria.
    The delegation will visit Lebanon as guests of the Lebanese Association of Islamic Scholars. During their stay in Syria, the delegation was received by Syrian Head of State Atassi, Prime Minister Zayen, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Mahous, and other ministers. The delegation visited the areas of Aleppo, Latakia, Hama, and Homs and was warmly received by local government leaders.
  President of the Sudanese Medical Association Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, June 12th. Dr. Muhammad Hamad Sati, President of the Sudanese Medical Association and Director of the Medical Research Institute of the Sudanese Ministry of Health, who was invited by the Chinese Medical Association to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 11th.

### 我伊斯兰友好代表团离大马士革前往贝鲁特

1966-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我伊斯兰友好代表团离大马士革前往贝鲁特
    据新华社大马士革十一日电　由中国伊斯兰教协会副主任张杰率领的中国伊斯兰友好代表团在对叙利亚进行十天访问后，十一日离开这里前往黎巴嫩首都贝鲁特。
    代表团将作为黎巴嫩伊斯兰学者协会的客人在黎巴嫩进行访问。代表团在叙利亚逗留期间，受到叙利亚国家元首阿塔西、总理扎因、副总理兼外交部长马胡斯和其他部长的接见。代表团访问了阿勒颇、拉塔基亚、哈马和霍姆斯地区，并受到了当地政府领导人的亲切接待。
  苏丹医学会主席到京
    新华社十二日讯　应中华医学会邀请来我国访问的苏丹医学会主席、苏丹卫生部医学研究所所长穆罕默德·哈马德·萨提医师，已在十一日晚乘飞机到达北京。
