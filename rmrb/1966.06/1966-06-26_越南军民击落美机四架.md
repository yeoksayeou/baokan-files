Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Four U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 6

Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down four U.S. aircraft
	New China News Agency, Hanoi, June 25
	According to a report released here on June 25: The military and civilians of Nghe An and Thanh Hoa provinces in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft each on June 25.

	According to a supplementary report: The military and civilians of Nghe An Province also shot down one U.S. aircraft each on the afternoon of June 16 and the morning of June 19.

### 越南军民击落美机四架

1966-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机四架
    新华社河内二十五日电　据这里二十五日公布：越南北方义安省和清化省军民，二十五日各击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    另据补充报道：义安省军民在十六日下午和十九日上午，还分别击落美国飞机各一架。
