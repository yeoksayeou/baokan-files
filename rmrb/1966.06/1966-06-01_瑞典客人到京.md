Title: Swedish Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, May 31st – Mr. and Mrs. Karl-Birger Blomdahl, a Swedish composer who was invited by the Chinese Musicians Association to visit our country, arrived in Beijing today.
