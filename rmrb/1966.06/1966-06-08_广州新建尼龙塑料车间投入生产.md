Title: Guangzhou's Newly Built Nylon Plastic Workshop Goes into Production
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 3

    Guangzhou's newly built nylon plastic workshop has formally gone into production.
    Nylon plastic possesses excellent chemical and physical properties. It can not only replace non-ferrous metals in manufacturing parts for precision machinery, instruments, and meters, but also be drawn into silk for weaving cloth, making its applications very broad.
                              (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 广州新建尼龙塑料车间投入生产

1966-06-08
第3版()
专栏：

    广州新建尼龙塑料车间投入生产
    广州新建的尼龙塑料车间已正式投入生产。
    尼龙塑料具有优良的化学性能和物理性能，既能代替有色金属制造精密机器、仪器、仪表的零件，又能抽丝织布，用途很广泛。
                              （据新华社）
