Title: Japanese Professor Shigeru Machida Praises China's Science for Reaching a High Level; China's Nuclear Explosion is a Victory of Self-Reliance
Author:
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch from Tokyo: Shigeru Machida, a professor at Rikkyo University in Japan, recently published an article in the Japan-China Friendship Association's newspaper, "Japan-China Friendship News," praising China's successful third nuclear test as a "victory of self-reliance."
    Machida pointed out that Chinese science has reached a high level in the short period of less than twenty years since the founding of the People's Republic of China. This achievement is attributed to the superior social system, and also to the principle of self-reliance that has been consistently implemented since the withdrawal of Soviet experts. He said: China has insisted on self-reliance and has unexpectedly achieved success in nuclear testing ahead of schedule.

### 日本教授町田茂赞扬我国科学达到高度水平  中国核爆炸是自力更生的胜利

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    日本教授町田茂赞扬我国科学达到高度水平
    中国核爆炸是自力更生的胜利
    新华社九日讯　东京消息：日本立教大学教授町田茂最近在日中友好协会机关报《日中友好新闻》上发表文章，赞扬中国第三次核试验成功是“自力更生的胜利”。
    町田茂指出，中国的科学在建国不到二十年的短时期里达到了高度的水平，这种成就要归功于优越的社会制度，同时也要归功于自从苏联专家撤退之后一直坚持贯彻的自力更生的原则。他说：中国坚持自力更生，在核试验方面出人意料地提早获得成就。
