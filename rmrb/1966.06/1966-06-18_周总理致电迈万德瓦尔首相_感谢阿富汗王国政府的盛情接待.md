Title: Premier Zhou Sends a Telegram to Prime Minister Maiwandwal Thanking the Royal Afghan Government for its Hospitality
Author:
Date: 1966-06-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 16th -- Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram on the 16th to Mohammad Hashim Maiwandwal, Prime Minister of the Royal Afghan Government, thanking him and the Royal Afghan Government for the warm reception given to Premier Zhou Enlai during his brief stay in Afghanistan. The telegram said, "During my brief stopover in your country en route to Romania, we received a warm reception from Your Excellency and the Royal Afghan Government. Now that we are about to fly out of your country's borders, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude. I wish that the friendship between the peoples of China and Afghanistan will continue to be consolidated and developed."

### 周总理致电迈万德瓦尔首相  感谢阿富汗王国政府的盛情接待

1966-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理致电迈万德瓦尔首相
    感谢阿富汗王国政府的盛情接待
    新华社喀布尔十六日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来十六日打电报给阿富汗王国政府首相穆罕默德·哈希姆·迈万德瓦尔，感谢他和阿富汗王国政府对周恩来总理在阿富汗短暂逗留期间所给予的盛情接待。电报说，当我前往罗马尼亚途中在贵国作短暂逗留期间，我们受到了阁下和阿富汗王国政府的盛情接待。现在，我们即将飞离贵国国境，谨对此表示衷心的感谢。祝中国阿富汗两国人民的友谊不断巩固和发展。
