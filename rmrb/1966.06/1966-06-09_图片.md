    On June 8, Mamadou Madeira Keita (seventh from right), member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union Party of Mali and Minister of Justice, and the Malian delegation he led, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, traveled by helicopter to Zunhua County, Hebei Province, one of the national Dazhai-style agricultural model units, and visited the Xipu Brigade of the Jianming Commune, which resolutely listened to Chairman Mao's words and adhered to the "poor stick" spirit in running the commune. This is Wang Guofan (fifth from right), representative of the National People's Congress, director of the Jianming Commune, and secretary of the Party Branch of the Xipu Brigade, accompanying the distinguished guests on a tour of the wheat fields.
                                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Jingde

专栏：

    六月八日，马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、司法部长马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔（右七）和由他率领的马里代表团，在陈毅副总理陪同下乘直升飞机到全国大寨式农业典型单位之一的河北省遵化县，访问了坚决听毛主席的话，坚持“穷棒子”精神办社的建明公社西铺大队。这是全国人民代表大会代表、建明公社社长、西铺大队党支部书记王国藩（右五）陪同贵宾在麦田参观。
                                      新华社记者　王敬德摄
