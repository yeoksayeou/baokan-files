Title: Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Condemns US Imperialism for Plotting to Assemble a New Aggression Bloc; Firmly Smash US Imperialist Schemes to Expand the War; South Vietnam National Liberation Front Points Out that the Seoul Meeting Cannot Save US Imperialism from Defeat
Author:
Date: 1966-06-15
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 13th (Report) - The Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on the 12th, severely condemning and exposing the conspiracy of US imperialism to hold the so-called "Ministerial Conference of the Asian and Pacific Region" in South Korea.

    The statement said that Japan, the Philippines, Thailand, "Malaysia," Australia, New Zealand, the South Korean puppet clique, the Chiang Kai-shek clique, and the South Vietnamese puppet clique, all participating in the conference, are servants or puppets of US and British imperialism. They have all participated in various US military aggression blocs, or have bilateral military alliances with the United States or Britain. The upcoming meeting in Seoul is obviously an attempt by US imperialism to gather all its servant and running dog cliques in the Asian and Pacific region into a new military alliance from northern Asia to Oceania.

    The statement pointed out that this conference also attempts to extricate US imperialism, which is suffering a disastrous defeat in Vietnam militarily and politically, and to urge its servant and running dog cliques to further participate in its plot to strengthen and expand the war of aggression against Vietnam and Indochina. This is the true purpose of the so-called "Ministerial Conference of the Asian and Pacific Region."

    The statement said that in order to escape the condemnation of world public opinion, US imperialism and its running dog cliques have had to put up the signboard of "economic and cultural cooperation" to conceal the ugly face of this conference. However, this cunning trick can absolutely deceive no one.

    The statement emphasized that the Vietnamese people and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam severely condemn the above-mentioned conspiracy of US imperialism and its running dog cliques. The Vietnamese people and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam resolutely oppose the so-called "Ministerial Conference of the Asian and Pacific Region" directed by US imperialism and organized by the Park Chung-hee puppet clique in Seoul.

    The statement pointed out that US imperialism is the most ferocious enemy of mankind. The people of Asia and the people of the world clearly see this, and strongly condemn all the aggressive plots of US imperialism, resolutely oppose their various military alliances, and resolutely expose their dangerous deceptive means such as "economic aid and economic cooperation."

    The statement concluded that the Vietnamese people, standing on the front line against the US, will resolutely carry the struggle against US imperialism and its running dogs through to the end, and smash all their aggressive and bellicose plots against Asia and the world. The Vietnamese people firmly believe that the struggle of the Asian people and the world people for peace, independence, democracy, and social progress will surely triumph. No matter how desperately US imperialism and its running dogs struggle, they will eventually suffer a shameful defeat.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 13th (Report) - According to a report by the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the External Liaison Committee of the Central Committee of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front issued a statement on the 10th, strongly condemning the US-manipulated "Ministerial Conference of the Asian and Pacific Region" to be held in Seoul.

    The statement said that in order to deceive public opinion, the United States and its servants claim that the "Seoul Conference" will discuss issues related to "economic and cultural cooperation." However, the composition of the conference shows that this is another conspiracy planned by the United States, with the aim of strengthening the collusion between the US's servants and pawns in the Asian and Pacific region, intensifying and expanding the war of aggression against South Vietnam and Indochina, thereby promoting the US's policy of enslavement and aggression in these regions.

    The statement said that the "Ministerial Conference" held by the United States in Seoul is another bellicose and aggressive act against the peace and independence of the people in the Asian and Pacific region, seriously aggravating the tense situation in these regions.

    The statement warned US imperialism and its servants that their death throes cannot save US imperialism from complete defeat in South Vietnam, nor can their alliance contain the enormous revolutionary wave that is surging in Asia and Oceania.

    The statement concluded that through unyielding struggle, through firm solidarity with the people of Indochina, Asia, and the Pacific region, and with the support of the people all over the world, the people of South Vietnam will completely defeat the US imperialist war of aggression.

### 越南外交部谴责美帝策划拼凑新侵略集团  坚决粉碎美帝扩大战争的阴谋  南越民族解放阵线指出汉城会议挽救不了美帝的败局

1966-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    越南外交部谴责美帝策划拼凑新侵略集团
    坚决粉碎美帝扩大战争的阴谋
    南越民族解放阵线指出汉城会议挽救不了美帝的败局
    新华社河内十三日电　越南民主共和国外交部十二日发表声明，严厉谴责和揭露美帝国主义策划的、即将在南朝鲜举行所谓“亚洲和太平洋地区部长会议”的阴谋。
    声明说，参加这次会议的日本、菲律宾、泰国、“马来西亚”、澳大利亚、新西兰、南朝鲜傀儡集团、蒋介石集团和南越傀儡集团，都是美英帝国主义的仆从或傀儡。它们都参加了美国的各个军事侵略集团，或者同美国或英国缔结有双边军事联盟。即将在汉城召开的会议，显然是美帝国主义企图把它在亚洲和太平洋地区的所有仆从和走狗集团纠集到一个从亚洲北部到大洋洲的新的军事同盟之中。
    声明指出，这次会议还企图使正在越南从军事上到政治上都遭到惨败的美帝国主义摆脱困境，促使它的仆从和走狗集团更进一步参与它加强和扩大对越南和印度支那的侵略战争的阴谋。这就是所谓“亚洲和太平洋地区部长会议”的真正目的。
    声明说，美帝国主义及其走狗集团为了逃避世界舆论的谴责，不得不打出“经济和文化合作”的招牌来掩饰这次会议的丑恶面目。但是，这种奸诈的伎俩是绝对欺骗不了谁的。
    声明强调说，越南人民和越南民主共和国政府严厉谴责美帝国主义及其走狗集团的上述阴谋。越南人民和越南民主共和国政府坚决反对由美帝国主义指挥和由朴正熙傀儡集团在汉城组织的所谓“亚洲和太平洋地区部长会议”。
    声明指出，美帝国主义是人类最凶恶的敌人。亚洲人民和世界人民清楚地看清了这一点，并且强烈谴责美帝国主义的一切侵略阴谋，坚决反对它们的各个军事联盟，坚决揭露它们的关于“经济援助，经济合作”等危险的欺骗手段。
    声明最后说，屹立在反美前线的越南人民，将坚决把反对美帝国主义及其走狗的斗争进行到底，粉碎它们对亚洲和世界的一切侵略和好战阴谋。越南人民坚信：亚洲人民和世界人民为和平、独立、民主和社会进步而进行的斗争一定胜利。美帝国主义及其走狗不论怎样进行疯狂的挣扎，最后必将遭到可耻的失败。
    据新华社河内十三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会对外联络委员会十日发表声明，强烈谴责将在汉城举行的、由美国操纵的“亚洲和太平洋地区部长会议”。
    声明说，美国及其仆从为了欺骗舆论，说什么“汉城会议”将讨论有关“经济和文化合作”的问题。但会议的组成说明，这是美国策划的又一个阴谋，目的是要加强美国在亚洲和太平洋地区的仆从和爪牙之间的勾结，加紧和扩大侵略越南南方和印度支那的战争，从而在这些地区推行美国的奴役和侵略政策。
    声明说，美国在汉城举行的“部长会议”，是又一个反对亚洲和太平洋地区人民的和平与独立的好战侵略行径，严重地加剧了这些地区的紧张局势。
    声明警告美帝国主义及其仆从说，它们的垂死挣扎决不能使美帝国主义在越南南方免遭彻底失败，它们的联盟也遏制不了正在亚洲和大洋洲高涨的巨大的革命浪潮。
    声明最后说，越南南方人民通过不屈不挠的斗争，通过同印度支那、亚洲和太平洋地区各国人民的牢固团结，在全世界人民的支持下，将彻底击败美帝国主义的侵略战争。
