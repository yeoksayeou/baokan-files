Title: Albanian People's Assembly Deputy Candidate Nomination Work Successfully Concluded; Comrades Hoxha and Shehu, etc., Nominated as Candidates

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-19
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 17th - The nomination work for candidates for the next session of the Albanian People's Assembly has concluded victoriously. The Albanian Labor Party and government leaders Hoxha, Shehu, etc., have all been nominated as candidates for the next People's Assembly. Also nominated as candidates are model workers and outstanding representatives from all fronts. In this candidate list, the number of workers and peasants has significantly increased.
    The quota for candidates for this People's Assembly is two hundred and forty, twenty-seven percent more than the previous session. According to Albanian election law, one candidate is produced for every eight thousand voters.

### 阿人民议会代表候选人提名工作胜利结束  霍查谢胡等同志被提名为候选人

1966-06-19
第5版()
专栏：

    阿人民议会代表候选人提名工作胜利结束
    霍查谢胡等同志被提名为候选人
    新华社地拉那十七日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会下一届议会代表候选人提名工作已经胜利结束。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府领导人霍查、谢胡等都被提名为下届人民议会代表的候选人。被提名为候选人的还有劳动模范和各个战线上的优秀代表。在这次候选人名额中，工人、农民的名额显著增多。
    这次人民议会代表候选人的名额为二百四十名，比上届候选人名额多百分之二十七。按照阿尔巴尼亚选举法规定，每八千名选民产生一名候选人。
