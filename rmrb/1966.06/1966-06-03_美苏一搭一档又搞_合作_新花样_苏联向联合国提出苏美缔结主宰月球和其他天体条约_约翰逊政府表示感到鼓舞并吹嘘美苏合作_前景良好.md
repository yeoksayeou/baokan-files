Title: The US and the Soviet Union Collaborate Again with New Tricks; The Soviet Union Proposes a Treaty to the UN with the US to Dominate the Moon and Other Celestial Bodies; The Johnson Administration Expresses Encouragement and Boasts of “Good Prospects” for US-Soviet Cooperation

Author:
Date: 1966-06-03
Edition: 5

    The US and the Soviet Union Collaborate Again with New Tricks
    The Soviet Union Proposes a Treaty to the UN with the US to Dominate the Moon and Other Celestial Bodies
    The Johnson Administration Expresses Encouragement and Boasts of “Good Prospects” for US-Soviet Cooperation
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 2nd – Following its line of US-Soviet cooperation to dominate the world, the Soviet government proposed a suggestion to the United Nations on May 31st, requesting a treaty with the United States on the so-called peaceful use of the moon and other celestial bodies, at a time when US imperialism is intensifying aggression and war on Earth. In its proposal, the Soviet government openly stated that if an agreement could be reached in this regard, it would be “an important step towards achieving ‘complete and thorough disarmament’ globally and promoting ‘mutual understanding and strengthening friendly relations between countries and peoples.’”
    The Soviet government’s proposal, in response to the US President’s call, was announced by the Soviet ambassador to the UN, Fedorenko, at a press conference. He said that he had delivered a letter from Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko to U Thant, in which he requested that the UN General Assembly, scheduled for September, discuss this “very important issue.”
    The Soviet proposal hypocritically stated that “all countries” could “freely explore and use” the moon and other celestial bodies; that the moon and other celestial bodies should only be used for “peaceful purposes” and “no military bases or weapons of mass destruction should be established”; that “the moon and other celestial bodies cannot be objects of annexation or territorial ambition”; and that “relevant countries should cooperate and assist each other” in the process of exploration, etc.
    The US government immediately welcomed the Soviet proposal. White House Deputy Press Secretary Fleming said on the 1st that the Soviet proposal “supports the position of US President Johnson’s statement on May 7th.” The US government maintains that an agreement should be drafted without delay. US State Department spokesman McCloskey also said that the Soviet government’s “clear and positive interest” in the US proposal of May 7th was “encouraging to us.” He said that the US had requested the UN Committee on Outer Space to convene a meeting as soon as possible to begin drafting the relevant treaty immediately.
    In a statement on May 7th, Johnson proposed “concluding a treaty for the peaceful exploration of the moon and other celestial bodies.” Johnson hypocritically said that the US advocates that “the moon and other celestial bodies should be freely explored and used by all countries,” that “no country should be allowed to make claims of sovereignty,” and that “no weapons of mass destruction should be placed on celestial bodies,” and that “all countries should cooperate in scientific activities related to celestial bodies,” etc. According to a report from Washington by Associated Press reporter Hightower: US officials say that despite the Vietnam War on Earth, the “prospects” for the US to negotiate and conclude such a treaty with the Soviet Union seem “good.”

### 美苏一搭一档又搞“合作”新花样  苏联向联合国提出苏美缔结主宰月球和其他天体条约  约翰逊政府表示感到鼓舞并吹嘘美苏合作“前景良好”

1966-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    美苏一搭一档又搞“合作”新花样
    苏联向联合国提出苏美缔结主宰月球和其他天体条约
    约翰逊政府表示感到鼓舞并吹嘘美苏合作“前景良好”
    新华社二日讯　纽约消息：苏联政府从它奉行的美苏合作主宰世界的路线出发，在五月三十一日向联合国提出一项建议，要求在美帝国主义正在地球上加紧进行侵略和战争的时候，同美国缔结一个所谓和平利用月球和其他天体的“国际条约”。苏联政府在建议中公开表示，如果在这方面达成协议，这将是朝着在全球实现“全面彻底裁军”增进“国家和人民之间的相互了解和加强友好关系的方向跨出的一个重要步骤”。
    苏联政府这项响应美国总统主张的建议是由苏联驻联合国大使费德林在一次记者招待会上宣布的。他说，他已经向吴丹递交了一封苏联外交部长葛罗米柯的信，在这封信中要求订于九月举行的联合国大会讨论这一“非常重要的问题”。
    苏联的建议伪善地提出：“一切国家”可以“自由探索和使用”月球和其他天体；月球和其他天体应当只用于“和平目的”，“不得设军事基地和大规模毁灭性武器”；“对月球和其他天体不能作为霸占或领土野心的对象”；在探索过程中，“有关国家应当合作和互助”等等。
    美国官方立即表示欢迎苏联的建议。白宫副新闻秘书弗莱明一日说，苏联的建议“是对美国总统约翰逊五月七日声明的立场的支持”。美国政府主张应当毫不迟延地着手订立这样一项协议。美国国务院发言人麦克洛斯基也说，苏联政府对美国五月七日提出的建议“表示了明显的和积极的兴趣，我们对此感到鼓舞”。他说，美国已经要求联合国外层空间委员会早日开会，以便马上开始制订有关条约的工作。
    约翰逊在五月七日发表声明，建议“缔结一项和平探索月球和其他天体的条约”。约翰逊虚伪地说，美国主张“月球和其他天体应供所有国家自由探索和使用”，“不允许任何国家提出主权要求”和“不得在天体上放置大规模毁灭性武器”，“所有国家都应在有关天体的科学活动方面进行合作”等等。据美联社记者海托华从华盛顿发出的消息说：美国官员们说，尽管地球上有着越南战争，看来美国同苏联谈判订立这样一个条约的“前景良好”。
