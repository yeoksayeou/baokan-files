Title: Singing Loudly in Front of Tiananmen
Author: Ma Heping
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 6

    Singing Loudly in Front of Tiananmen
    Ma Heping, Class 6, Grade 1, Beijing No. 4 Middle School
    I hold the bright red flag,
    Marching in Tiananmen Square,
    Looking back on my teens of growth,
    My heart surging!
    I remember that year,
    Wearing my red scarf, I marched for the first time,
    Seeing Chairman Mao waving to us kindly,
    Hoping we would grow up quickly!
    I remember that year,
    Under the Monument to the Heroes,
    We recited Chairman Mao's poem "The Long March,"
    Swearing to follow in the footsteps of the martyrs,
    To advance forever, to make a thorough revolution!
    How can we allow
    The shackles of the old examination methods?
    How can we tolerate
    The deception of the old education system?
    Smash it! Purge it!
    Sweep away! Eliminate it!
    With an excited heart,
    I stand in the ranks of the revolution.
    To the north are Tiananmen and Chang'an Avenue,
    To the south are the Monument to the Heroes and solemn green pines.
    Chairman Mao is the sun in our hearts,
    Our best teachers are the revolutionary workers, peasants, and soldiers!
    We must temper ourselves and grow in the three great revolutionary movements,
    To meet the heavy responsibility that history gives us!

### 天安门前放声唱
马和平
1966-06-24
第6版()
专栏：

    天安门前放声唱
    北京四中高一（六）班　马和平
    我举着鲜艳的红旗，
    在天安门广场游行，
    回顾我十几年的成长，
    心潮滚滚翻腾！
    回想那一年，
    我戴着红领巾第一次游行，
    看见毛主席向我们亲切招手，
    希望我们快快长成！
    回想那一年，
    我们在英雄碑下，
    朗诵毛主席的《长征》诗，
    宣誓要沿着先烈的足迹，
    永远前进，彻底革命！
    我们怎么能允许
    旧考试方法的束缚？
    我们怎么能容忍
    旧教育制度的愚弄？
    砸烂！把它肃清！
    横扫！把它除净！
    我怀着激动的心，
    站在革命队伍当中。
    北面是天安门和长安大街，
    南面是英雄碑和庄严的青松。
    毛主席是我们心中的太阳，
    我们最好的教师是革命的工农兵！
    我们要在三大革命中锻炼成长，
    去迎接历史给我们的重任！
