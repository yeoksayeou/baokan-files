Title: Vietnamese Acrobatic Troupe Leaves Beijing for Visit to Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1966-06-22
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 21st -- The acrobatic troupe from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Su Hai, concluded its performance tour in Beijing and departed this morning for a visit to Tianjin.
    Responsible persons from relevant departments, including Yang Chun, Sun Xiaocun, Fu Bingyou, Lu Ming, and Zhang Yue, as well as people from the capital's literary and art circles, were at the station to see them off. The platform was filled with the sound of gongs and drums, and Chinese and Vietnamese comrades embraced warmly. Literary and art workers from the capital presented flowers to the guests. The artists from both countries sang songs such as "Unity is Strength" and "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman."
    Huang Bei, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also came to see them off.

### 越南杂技团离北京去天津访问

1966-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    越南杂技团离北京去天津访问
    新华社二十一日讯　由苏海率领的越南民主共和国杂技团结束了在北京的访问演出后，今天上午离开北京前往天津访问。
    到车站欢送的有杨纯、孙晓村、傅兵友、鲁明、张跃等有关方面负责人，以及首都文艺界人士。月台上锣鼓齐鸣，中越两国同志热烈拥抱。首都文艺工作者向客人们献了花。两国文艺工作者高唱《团结就是力量》、《大海航行靠舵手》等歌曲。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞黄北等也到车站欢送。
