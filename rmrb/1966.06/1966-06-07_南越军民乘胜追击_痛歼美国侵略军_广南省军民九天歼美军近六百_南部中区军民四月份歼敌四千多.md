Title: South Vietnamese Armymen and Civilians Pursue the Victory and Fiercely Annihilate the American Invaders: Quang Nam Province Armymen and Civilians Eliminate Nearly Six Hundred American Troops in Nine Days; Armymen and Civilians in the Southern Central Region Eliminate More Than Four Thousand Enemies in April

Author:
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 5

    According to a Hanoi dispatch from Xinhua News Agency on the 4th, the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency reported that the armymen and civilians in the northern part of Quang Nam Province, South Vietnam, enthusiastically launched a killing-the-enemy competition, eliminating 596 American invading troops and 141 puppet troops in the nine days from May 14th to 22nd, shooting down six enemy planes and destroying or damaging ten military vehicles of various types. At the same time, thirteen "strategic hamlets" were destroyed.
    During this period, the Liberation Armed Forces launched many active attacks, annihilating enemy forces. On May 14th, the Liberation Armed Forces fiercely intercepted two groups of American invading troops in Dien Ban district, wiping out more than 100 enemy soldiers and shooting down two enemy planes. On the morning of May 17th, the Liberation Armed Forces used landmines to destroy five military vehicles and one amphibious armored vehicle fully loaded with American invading troops in Jinshan Village, Dien Tien Township, Dien Ban district. At noon on May 21st, the Liberation Armed Forces intercepted American invading troops from E Ba going to the Phu Son area, and then successively intercepted enemy reinforcements three times, killing or wounding 160 enemy soldiers. In addition, they completely wiped out American invading troops airdropped by four helicopters in Xishan Village, destroying and damaging four M-113 amphibious armored vehicles, and wounding one helicopter.
    Guerrillas in various parts of northern Quang Nam Province also launched a competition to eliminate American and puppet troops with simple weapons.
    According to a Hanoi dispatch from Xinhua News Agency on the 3rd, the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency reported that the armymen and civilians in the southern central region of South Vietnam eliminated and disintegrated a total of 4,805 enemies in April, including 2,434 killed or wounded, 152 captured, and 2,219 puppet soldiers who fled the enemy ranks. Seven enemy companies and eight platoons were completely wiped out.
    The armymen and civilians eliminated nine enemy outposts and strongholds, as well as two military training centers, forcing the enemy to withdraw from one stronghold, destroying or damaging seventeen enemy military vehicles, sinking one enemy warship, shooting down eight enemy planes, and damaging nineteen. A large number of weapons and military supplies were seized.
    During this period, the armymen and civilians in the southern central region also continuously attacked the enemy on transportation lines. They cut off sixteen highways, destroyed twenty-seven bridges and thirty culverts, and set up obstacles on the transportation lines, paralyzing the enemy's transportation.

### 南越军民乘胜追击　痛歼美国侵略军  广南省军民九天歼美军近六百　南部中区军民四月份歼敌四千多

1966-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    南越军民乘胜追击　痛歼美国侵略军
    广南省军民九天歼美军近六百　南部中区军民四月份歼敌四千多
    据新华社河内四日电　越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广南省北部军民热烈开展杀敌竞赛运动，在五月十四日到二十二日的九天中，歼灭美国侵略军五百九十六名和伪军一百四十一名，打落敌机六架，击毁击伤各种军车十辆。同时捣毁了十三个“战略村”。
    在这期间，解放武装力量多次主动出击，歼灭敌军有生力量。五月十四日，解放武装力量在奠盘县猛烈截击两股美国侵略军，歼敌一百多名，打落敌机两架。五月十七日上午，解放武装力量在奠盘县奠进乡锦山村用地雷炸毁了满载美国侵略军的军车五辆和两栖装甲车一辆。五月二十一日中午，解放武装力量截击从鹅巴前往富山地区的美国侵略军，接着又连续三次截击敌人的援军，打死打伤敌人一百六十名，另外还全歼由四架直升飞机空投到西山村的美国侵略军，击毁、击伤Ｍ—113型两栖装甲车四辆，打伤直升飞机一架。
    广南省北部各地游击队也展开用简陋武器歼灭美伪军的竞赛运动。
    据新华社河内三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方南部中区军民，四月份共歼灭和瓦解敌人四千八百零五名，其中打死、打伤敌人二千四百三十四名，俘虏一百五十二名，另有二千二百十九名伪军士兵逃离敌人的队伍。全歼敌军七个连和八个排。
    军民们铲除了敌人的九个哨所和据点，以及两个军事训练中心，迫使敌人撤出一个据点，击毁、击伤敌军车十七辆，击沉敌战船一艘，击落敌机八架，击伤十九架。缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    在这期间，南部中区军民还不断在交通线上袭击敌人，他们切断了十六条公路，炸毁桥梁二十七座和涵洞三十个，并在交通线上设置障碍物，使敌人的交通运输经常处于瘫痪状态。
