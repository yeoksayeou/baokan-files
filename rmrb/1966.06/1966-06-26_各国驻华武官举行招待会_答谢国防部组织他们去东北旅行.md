Title: Foreign Military Attaches in China Hold Reception to Thank the Ministry of National Defense for Organizing Their Trip to the Northeast

Author:

Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, June 25th. Foreign military attaches stationed in China held a reception this evening to thank the Chinese Ministry of National Defense for organizing a trip to the Northeast region of China for them and their wives.
    Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and responsible persons from relevant departments, including Cao Lihuai, Jia Ruoyu, Pan Zhenwu, Zhang Minsheng, Fu Jize, and Tian Tao, were invited to attend the reception. At the reception, Colonel Solescu, military attaché of the Romanian Embassy in China, and Peng Shaohui spoke successively. The hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses, wishing the friendship between the people and armies of all countries would develop day by day. The foreign military attaches and their wives, at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, travelled to Northeast China from the end of May to the middle of June. Accompanied by Tian Tao, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, they visited Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, and other places, and were warmly welcomed by relevant departments.

### 各国驻华武官举行招待会  答谢国防部组织他们去东北旅行

1966-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    各国驻华武官举行招待会
    答谢国防部组织他们去东北旅行
    新华社二十五日讯　各国驻华武官，今晚举行招待会，答谢我国防部组织他们和他们的夫人去我国东北地区旅行。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉和有关方面的负责人曹里怀、贾若瑜、潘振武、张敏生、傅继泽、田涛等，应邀出席招待会。会上，各国驻华武官代表、罗马尼亚驻华大使馆武官索列斯库上校和彭绍辉先后讲了话。宾主频频举杯，共祝各国人民和军队之间的友谊日益发展。各国驻华武官和夫人是应我国防部邀请，在五月底至六月中旬前往我国东北旅行的。他们在我国防部外事局副局长田涛陪同下，参观访问了沈阳、长春、哈尔滨等地，受到有关方面的热烈欢迎。
