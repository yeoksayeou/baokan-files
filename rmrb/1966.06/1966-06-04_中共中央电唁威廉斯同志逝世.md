Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Condolences on the Death of Comrade Williams
Author:
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the National Committee of the Communist Party of New Zealand on the 2nd, mourning the death of Comrade Mervyn Williams, Chairman of the National Committee of the Communist Party of New Zealand. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the National Committee of the Communist Party of New Zealand:
    We are shocked to learn of the unfortunate death of Comrade Mervyn Williams, Chairman of the Communist Party of New Zealand. We express our deep condolences to you and the family of Comrade Williams with great sorrow.
    Comrade Williams was an outstanding son of the working class and working people of New Zealand, and a staunch fighter of the international communist movement. He dedicated his life to the noble cause of the working class and working people of New Zealand in their struggle for socialism. He made active contributions to opposing American imperialism and modern revisionism centered on the leading group of the Soviet Communist Party, to defending the purity of Marxism-Leninism, and to safeguarding the unity of the international communist movement. His death is a major loss to our common cause.
    We firmly believe that the fighting life of Comrade Williams will inspire the working class and working people of New Zealand to strive for New Zealand's genuine national independence, people's democracy, and socialism.
              The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                 June 2, 1966

### 中共中央电唁威廉斯同志逝世

1966-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电唁威廉斯同志逝世
    新华社三日讯　中国共产党中央委员会二日打电报给新西兰共产党全国委员会，吊唁新西兰共产党全国委员会主席默文·威廉斯同志逝世。电报全文如下：新西兰共产党全国委员会：
    惊悉新共主席默文·威廉斯同志不幸逝世。我们怀着十分悲痛的心情，向你们和威廉斯同志的家属，表示深切的哀悼。
    威廉斯同志是新西兰工人阶级和劳动人民的优秀儿子，国际共产主义运动的坚强战士。他把毕生的精力献给了新西兰工人阶级和劳动人民争取社会主义的崇高事业，他为反对美帝国主义和以苏共领导集团为中心的现代修正主义，为保卫马克思列宁主义的纯洁性和维护国际共产主义运动的团结作出了积极的贡献。他的逝世是我们共同事业的一个重大的损失。
    我们深信，威廉斯同志斗争的一生，将鼓舞新西兰工人阶级和劳动人民为争取新西兰真正的民族独立、人民民主和社会主义而奋斗。
              中国共产党中央委员会
                 一九六六年六月二日
