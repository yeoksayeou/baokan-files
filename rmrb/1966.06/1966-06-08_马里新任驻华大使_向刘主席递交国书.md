Title: New Malian Ambassador to China Presents Credentials to Chairman Liu
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 1

    New Malian Ambassador to China
    Presents Credentials to Chairman Liu
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th - The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Mali to the People's Republic of China, Gisse Tidiani, accompanied by members of the embassy, presented his credentials to Chairman Liu Shaoqi this afternoon.
    After the presentation of credentials, Chairman Liu Shaoqi had a cordial and friendly conversation with Ambassador Gisse.
    Present were Wang Bingnan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.



**Named Entities**

### 马里新任驻华大使  向刘主席递交国书

1966-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    马里新任驻华大使
    向刘主席递交国书
    新华社七日讯　马里共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使吉塞·提迪亚尼，今天下午偕同大使馆馆员，向刘少奇主席递交国书。
    递交国书以后，刘少奇主席同吉塞大使进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    在场的有外交部副部长王炳南等。
