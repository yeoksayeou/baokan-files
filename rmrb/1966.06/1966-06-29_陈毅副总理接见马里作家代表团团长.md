Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with the Head of the Malian Writers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-06-29
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi today morning received the head of the Malian delegation to the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers, Mamadou Gologo, Minister of Information and Tourism of the Republic of Mali, and had a friendly talk with him. Mali's Ambassador to China, Gise Tidianni, was also present at the meeting.

### 陈毅副总理接见马里作家代表团团长

1966-06-29
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见马里作家代表团团长
    新华社二十八日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了出席亚非作家紧急会议的马里共和国作家代表团团长、马里新闻游览部部长马马杜·戈洛戈，并同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时，马里驻华大使吉塞·提迪亚尼也在座。
