Title: Three Japanese Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. A Japanese Socialist Party Fisheries Delegation of six people, led by Yamazaki Noboru, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the China Fisheries Association. Wang Yinpu, Wang Xiaoyun, Xiao Xiangqian, Han Juli and others welcomed the delegation at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. A Japanese Socialist Party Yamagata Prefecture Activists Visiting China Delegation of five people, headed by Watanabe Saburo, and a Japanese Socialist Party Hokkaido Activists Visiting China Delegation of six people, headed by Saito Naomi, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Responsible persons from relevant departments, including Wang Yinpu, Xiao Xiangqian, Wang Xiaoyun, and Wang Jiyu, went to the airport to greet them.

### 日本三个代表团到京

1966-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    日本三个代表团到京
    新华社十六日讯  由山崎升率领的日本社会党渔业代表团一行六人，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中国渔业协会邀请来我国进行友好访问的。到机场欢迎代表团的有王荫圃、王晓云、萧向前、韩巨礼等人。
    新华社十六日讯以渡边三郎为首的日本社会党山形县活动家访华代表团一行五人，和以斋藤直已为首的日本社会党北海道活动家访华代表团一行六人，应中国人民外交学会邀请，今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场迎接的，有王荫圃、萧向前、王晓云、王继钰等有关方面负责人。
