Title: Australian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-01
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, 30th, Professor Christiansen of the Department of Electrical Engineering of the University of Sydney, Australia, Academician of the Australian Academy of Sciences, and his wife and their son Stephen Christiansen, who were invited by the Chinese Academy of Sciences to visit China, arrived in Beijing on the 26th.

斯蒂芬·克里斯琴 Stephen Christiansen

### 澳大利亚客人到京

1966-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚客人到京
    据新华社三十日讯　应中国科学院邀请来我国访问的澳大利亚悉尼大学电气工程系教授、澳大利亚科学院士克里斯琴森和夫人以及他们的儿子斯蒂芬·克里斯琴，已于二十六日到达北京。
