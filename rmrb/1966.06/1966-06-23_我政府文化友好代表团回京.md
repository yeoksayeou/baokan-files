Title: Our Government Cultural Friendship Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-23
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency: The Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation, led by Shi Huai-bi, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, returned to Beijing on the evening of the 17th after concluding its friendly visits to Afghanistan and Pakistan.  Li Chang and Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, were at the airport to greet them. Officials from the Afghan and Pakistani Embassies in China were also present at the airport.

### 我政府文化友好代表团回京

1966-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府文化友好代表团回京
    新华社讯　由对外文委副主任史怀璧率领的中国政府文化友好代表团，结束了对阿富汗和巴基斯坦的友好访问，十七日晚回到北京。到机场迎接的，有对外文委副主任李昌、陈忠经。阿富汗、巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆官员也到机场迎接。
