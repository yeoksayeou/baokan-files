    Tanzanian Government Economic Delegation Leaves Beijing for Shanghai
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th June. The Tanzanian Government Economic Delegation headed by Paul Bomani, Minister of Economic Affairs and Development Planning of Tanzania, as well as Mrs. Paul Bomani and Mrs. Kunabi, Executive Committee Member of the Tanzanian National Women's Union, left Beijing by plane this afternoon for Shanghai for a visit, accompanied by Yang Lin, Deputy Director of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission, and others. Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Wazir Juma, traveled with them.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian and his wife Lin Jiamei, along with 1,500 people from all walks of life in the capital, went to the airport to see them off.
    When Minister Paul Bomani and others bid farewell to the well-wishers, drums and gongs resounded at the airport. People waved colorful flags, ribbons, and bouquets, shouting slogans such as "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Tanzanian people" and "Long live the solidarity of the Asian and African people."
    Those who went to the airport to see them off also included Vice Foreign Minister Ji Pengfei, and other responsible persons from relevant departments, including Nan Hanchen, Luo Qingchang, Lei Renmin, Shen Zijiou, and Le Songsheng.
    Officials from the Tanzanian Embassy in China also went to the airport to see them off. (Attached picture)
    On June 8, Paul Bomani, Minister of Economic Affairs and Development Planning of Tanzania, and the Tanzanian Government Economic Delegation headed by him, as well as Mrs. Bomani and Mrs. Kunabi, Executive Committee Member of the Tanzanian National Women's Union, left Beijing by plane. Vice Premier Li Xiannian and others, along with people from the capital, warmly saw off the distinguished Tanzanian guests at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Yuanliu.

### 坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团离京去上海

1966-06-09
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团离京去上海
    新华社八日讯　坦桑尼亚经济事务和发展计划部长保罗·博马尼率领的坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团，以及保罗·博马尼夫人和坦桑尼亚全国妇联执委库纳比夫人，在对外经委副主任杨琳等陪同下，今天下午乘飞机离开北京去上海访问。坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马随机前往。
    李先念副总理和夫人林佳楣以及首都各界一千五百多人到机场欢送。
    保罗·博马尼部长等同欢送的群众告别时，机场上响起了锣鼓声，人们挥舞着彩旗、彩带和花束，高呼“中坦两国人民友好万岁”、“亚非人民团结万岁”等口号。
    到机场欢送的，还有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，其他有关方面负责人南汉宸、罗青长、雷任民、沈兹九、乐松生等。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使馆官员也到机场欢送。（附图片）
    六月八日，坦桑尼亚经济事务和发展计划部长保罗·博马尼和由他率领的坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团，以及博马尼夫人和坦桑尼亚全国妇联执委库纳比夫人乘飞机离开北京。李先念副总理等和首都群众到机场热烈欢送坦桑尼亚贵宾。　新华社记者　吴元柳摄
