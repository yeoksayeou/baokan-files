Title: Hasegawa Praises Chinese Table Tennis Players' Noble Style and Superb Skills: Mao Zedong Thought Gives Our Athletes Spiritual Strength
Author:
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 4

    (Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo) Kiyotaro Hasegawa, Director-General of the Japan Table Tennis Association and a renowned table tennis coach, recently made a statement to Chinese reporters, highly praising the tremendous achievements of Chinese table tennis players in studying Mao Zedong Thought, and warmly commending the Chinese athletes' noble style and superb skills.
    Hasegawa recently watched three official matches between the visiting Chinese table tennis team and the Japanese table tennis team in Tokyo, Osaka, and Nagoya, as well as friendly matches in Yori, Saitama Prefecture, and Tokyo.
    Hasegawa said that the Chinese table tennis team thoroughly studied Mao Zedong Thought, enabling Mao Zedong Thought to deeply penetrate the hearts of every team member. This was reflected in every match. He said that this time, the Chinese table tennis team visiting Japan emphasized the study of Mao Zedong Thought more than ever before, and put forward strict and considerate requirements for the athletes' spiritual aspects. Through the matches, I deeply felt the achievements of Chinese players in studying Mao Zedong Thought.
    Hasegawa said that the techniques of Chinese and Japanese table tennis players have reached a fairly high level. Under these circumstances, it is necessary for athletes to gain spiritual strength and confidence. On this point, I think China's guidance method is good.
    Hasegawa praised the style and polite attitude shown by Chinese athletes in the competition. He said that Chinese athletes left an excellent impression on Japanese athletes and the general public. Their exemplary actions have played a good role in strengthening Japan-China friendship.
    When talking about the strength of the Chinese and Japanese table tennis teams and the situation of this competition, Hasegawa said that, on the whole, the level of the Chinese team has improved compared to the past.
    Hasegawa expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the Chinese table tennis team's visit to Japan. He said that through this friendly competition between the Chinese and Japanese teams, the friendship between the people and athletes of the two countries has been further strengthened.

### 长谷川赞扬我乒乓球运动员高尚风格和精湛球艺  毛泽东思想使我运动员获得精神力量

1966-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    长谷川赞扬我乒乓球运动员高尚风格和精湛球艺
    毛泽东思想使我运动员获得精神力量
    据新华社东京电　日本乒乓球协会理事长、著名乒乓球教练长谷川喜代太郎最近向中国记者发表谈话，高度评价中国乒乓球运动员在学习毛泽东思想方面所取得的巨大成就，并且热烈称赞中国运动员的高尚风格和精湛的球艺。
    长谷川最近观看了访问日本的中国乒乓球队同日本乒乓球队在东京、大阪和名古屋进行的三场正式比赛和在埼玉县的寄居和东京进行的友谊赛。
    长谷川说，中国乒乓球队彻底地学习了毛泽东思想，使毛泽东思想深入到每个队员的心里。这一点体现在各场比赛中。他说，这次访问日本的中国乒乓球队比任何时候都强调学习毛泽东思想，对运动员的精神方面提出了既严格又体贴的要求。通过比赛，我深深感到中国队员在学习毛泽东思想方面已收到的成效。
    长谷川说，目前中国和日本乒乓球运动员的技术都已经达到了相当高的水平。在这种情况下，必须使运动员获得精神力量和信心。在这一点上，我认为，中国的指导方法是好的。
    长谷川赞扬中国运动员在比赛中所表现的风格和有礼貌的态度。他说，中国运动员给日本运动员和广大观众留下了极好的印象。他们的模范行动对加强日中友好起了很好的作用。
    长谷川在谈到中日两国乒乓球队的实力和这次比赛的情况时说，整个说来，中国队的水平比过去又有了提高。
    长谷川对中国乒乓球队访问日本表示衷心的感谢。他说，通过这次中日两国球队的友好比赛，进一步加强了两国人民和运动员之间的友谊。
