Title: Sudanese Writer Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-17
Edition: 4

    Sudanese Writer Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, June 16) Awad Hassan Ahmed, a Sudanese poet and writer attending the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Meeting scheduled to open in Beijing on June 27, arrived in Beijing by plane today.


**Named Entities**

苏丹  Sūdān (Sudan)
北京  Běijīng (Beijing)
亚非作家紧急会议  Yà Fēi Zuòjiā Jǐnjí Huìyì (Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Meeting)
阿瓦德·哈桑·艾哈迈德  Āwǎdé Hāsāng Àihāmài dé (Awad Hassan Ahmed)
新华社  Xīnhuá Shè (Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏丹一作家到京

1966-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏丹一作家到京
    新华社十六日讯　出席定于六月二十七日在北京召开的亚非作家紧急会议的苏丹诗人和作家阿瓦德·哈桑·艾哈迈德，今天乘飞机到达北京。
