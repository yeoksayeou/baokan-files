Title: North Vietnamese Army and Civilians Shot Down Thirteen U.S. Aircraft

Author:
Date: 1966-06-06
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 4th Telegram. It was announced here on the 4th that the army and civilians of Quang Binh Province, North Vietnam, shot down one U.S. aircraft on the 3rd.
    According to supplementary reports, the army and civilians of Quang Ninh Province shot down one U.S. aircraft on the 2nd; the army and civilians of Quang Binh Province, in addition to the one enemy aircraft already announced as shot down on the 2nd, also shot down another U.S. aircraft; the army and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down one U.S. aircraft each on May 29th and 30th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 2nd Telegram. It was announced here on the 2nd that on the evening of the 1st, the army and civilians in the southern region of Lang Son Province shot down one invading U.S. bandit aircraft.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd Telegram. It was announced here on the 3rd that the army and civilians of Quang Binh, Nghe An, and Thanh Hoa Provinces in North Vietnam shot down three invading U.S. bandit aircraft on the 2nd. According to supplementary reports, the army and civilians of Quang Binh and Ha Tinh Provinces shot down three invading U.S. bandit aircraft on the 1st, of which the army and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down two, and the militia of Thach Ha County, Ha Tinh Province, shot down one.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 5th Telegram. According to supplementary news announced here on the 5th, the army and civilians of Thai Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down one U.S. aircraft on the 3rd while fighting back against invading enemy aircraft.
    According to additional news, the enemy aircraft shot down by the army and civilians of Thanh Hoa Province in the early morning of May 31st, which was previously announced, was a U.S. C-130 transport aircraft, and all seven enemies on board were killed.

### 越南北方军民又击落美机十三架

1966-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民又击落美机十三架
    新华社河内四日电　据这里四日公布：越南北方广平省军民三日击落美国飞机一架。
    另据补充报道，广宁省军民二日击落美国飞机一架；广平省军民二日除前已公布的击落敌机一架外，还击落了另一架美国飞机；义安省军民在五月二十九日和三十日两天各击落美国飞机一架。
    新华社河内二日电　据这里二日公布：一日晚上，谅山省南部地区军民击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社河内三日电　据这里三日公布：越南北方广平、义安、清化三省军民，二日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机三架。另据补充报道，广平、河静两省军民，一日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机三架，其中广平省军民击落两架、河静省石河县民兵击落一架。
    新华社河内五日电　据这里五日公布的补充消息说，越南北方太平省军民三日在反击入侵敌机的战斗中，击落美国飞机一架。
    另据补充消息说，前已公布的清化省军民在五月三十一日凌晨击落的一架敌机，是美国C—130型运输机，机上的七名敌人全部毙命。
