Title: China and Pakistan Sign Annual Cultural Exchange Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-06-03
Edition: 5

    China and Pakistan Sign Annual Cultural Exchange Plan
    (Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 1st dispatch) A China-Pakistan annual cultural exchange plan (from July 1966 to June 1967) was signed here on the morning of the 1st. The plan stipulates that the two countries will send educational, scientific, and cultural delegations, as well as sports teams, for visits.
    Shi Huaibi, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, signed the plan on behalf of the Chinese government. Qadri, Secretary of the Pakistani Ministry of Education, signed on behalf of the Pakistani government. After the signing, Shi Huaibi and Qadri warmly congratulated each other on the further development of cultural cooperation between the two countries.
    Shi Linfeng, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, and Zafar Aslam, Assistant Advisor to the Pakistani Ministry of Education, attended the signing ceremony.
    The Pakistan-China Friendship Association of Rawalpindi held a reception here on the afternoon of the 1st to welcome the Chinese government cultural friendship delegation led by Shi Huaibi. Ayub Mirza, Secretary of the Association, spoke at the reception, expressing gratitude for China's assistance to Pakistan during India's armed aggression last year, and thanking China for supporting the Kashmiri people's struggle for self-determination. The Rawalpindi Education Workers' Club also held a reception for the delegation here on the 1st.

### 中巴签订文化交流年度计划

1966-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    中巴签订文化交流年度计划
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第一日电　中国同巴基斯坦文化交流年度计划（从一九六六年七月到一九六七年六月）一日上午在这里签字。计划规定两国互派教育、科学和文化代表团以及体育队进行访问。
    中国对外文化联络委员会副主任史怀璧代表中国政府在计划上签字。巴基斯坦教育部秘书卡德里代表巴基斯坦政府签字。签字后，史怀璧和卡德里对两国文化合作的进一步发展互相热烈致贺。
    参加签字仪式的有中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆文化参赞史林峰和巴基斯坦教育部助理顾问扎法尔·阿斯拉姆。
    拉瓦尔品第的巴基斯坦—中国友好协会一日下午在这里举行招待会，招待由史怀璧率领的中国政府文化友好代表团。协会秘书阿尤布·米尔扎在招待会上讲话，表示感谢中国在去年印度武装侵略期间给予巴基斯坦的援助，感谢中国支持克什米尔人民争取自决权的斗争。拉瓦尔品第教育工作者俱乐部一日也在这里为代表团举行招待会。
