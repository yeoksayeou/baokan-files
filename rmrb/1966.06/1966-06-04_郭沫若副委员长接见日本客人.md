Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives Japanese Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd -- Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received all members of the Japanese Socialist Party Activists Delegation to China, led by Sakai Ikuo, this afternoon, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Lian Guan, Wang Xiaoyun, Xiao Xiangqian, and others.
    Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Saionji Kinkazu, was also present.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社三日讯　郭沫若副委员长今天下午接见了由酒井郁造率领的日本社会党活动家访华团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有连贯、王晓云、萧向前等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
