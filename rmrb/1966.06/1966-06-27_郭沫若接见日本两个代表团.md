Edition: 5

Guo Moruo Meets with Two Japanese Delegations

	New China News Agency, June 26th

	The Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Guo Moruo, received two Japanese delegations this morning and had a friendly conversation with the guests.

	The two Japanese delegations visiting China are: the Japan Socialist Party Yamagata Prefecture Activists Delegation led by Watanabe Saburo and the Japan Socialist Party Hokkaido Prefecture Activists Delegation led by Saito Naomi.

	Present at the reception were relevant officials such as Lian Guan and Wang Yinfu.

### 郭沫若接见日本两个代表团

1966-06-27
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本两个代表团
    新华社二十六日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天上午接见了两个日本访华代表团，和客人们进行了友好的谈话。
    这两个日本访华代表团是：以渡边三郎为首的日本社会党山形县活动家访华代表团和以斋藤直已为首的日本社会党北海道活动家访华代表团。
    接见时在座的，有连贯、王荫圃等有关方面负责人。
