Title: Not Afraid of "Authority," Not Fearing Suppression
Author: Han Boquan, Pan Deyi, Bi Qingxiu
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 3

    Not Afraid of "Authority," Not Fearing Suppression
    Comrades Nie Yuanzi and six others from Peking University: In this life-and-death class struggle, you have held high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, broken through the heavy obstacles of the black gang elements, exposed the deceptive tricks of Song Shuo, Lu Ping, Peng Peiyun and their ilk, not afraid of "authority," not fearing suppression, and posted the first big-character poster at Peking University. It was like a bomb, thrown at the reactionary stronghold of the "Three-Family Village" black gang. After seeing this news, on the one hand, we felt extremely angry at the counter-revolutionary methods of black gang elements such as Song Shuo, Lu Ping, and Peng Peiyun; on the other hand, we were very happy for the heroic struggle of you seven comrades.
    Currently, our country is in the torrent of the socialist cultural revolution that touches people's souls. From the big-character poster of the seven comrades of Peking University, we have once again deeply realized what Chairman Mao taught us: No destruction, no construction; no blockage, no flow; no stopping, no progress; the enemy will not fall if not attacked. "A trapped beast still fights," this is precisely the nature of all ghosts and monsters such as Song Shuo, Lu Ping, and Peng Peiyun. Song Shuo, Lu Ping, Peng Peiyun, we warn you: your set of deceptive, oppressive, and intimidating counter-revolutionary methods will absolutely not deceive or suppress us. "The devil is one foot tall, the Tao is ten feet tall." No matter how cunning you are, you will not escape the judgment of the people. Comrades Nie Yuanzi and six others, we firmly stand by your side, support you, and together with you, actively throw ourselves into the torrent of this great proletarian cultural revolution, drag out all the big and small ghosts and monsters, uproot them, completely defeat them, and never stop until complete victory.
    Guangdong Nationalities Institute Han Boquan Pan Deyi Bi Qingxiu

### 不怕“权威”不畏压制
韩伯泉  潘德义　闭清秀
1966-06-04
第3版()
专栏：

    不怕“权威”不畏压制
    北京大学聂元梓等七位同志：你们在这一场你死我活的阶级搏斗中高举毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，冲破黑帮分子的重重阻拦，拆穿宋硕、陆平、彭珮云之流那一套骗人的伎俩，不怕“权威”，不畏压制，在北大贴出第一张大字报。它象炸弹一样，投向“三家村”黑帮们的这个反动堡垒。我们看到这则消息后，一方面对宋硕、陆平、彭珮云等黑帮分子的反革命手法感到无比的愤怒；另一方面，又为你们七位同志的英勇斗争感到万分高兴。
    目前，我国正处在触及人们灵魂的社会主义文化大革命的洪流之中。我们从北大七位同志的大字报中，又一次深刻地认识到毛主席教导我们的：不破不立，不塞不流，不止不行，敌人不打不倒。“困兽犹斗”，这正是宋硕、陆平、彭珮云等一切牛鬼蛇神的本性。宋硕、陆平、彭珮云，我们警告你们：你们这一套骗、压、吓的反革命手法是绝对欺骗不了，压制不倒我们的。“魔高一尺，道高一丈”。不管你们怎样鬼计多端，也逃脱不了人民对你们的审判。聂元梓等七位同志，我们坚决站在你们一边，支持你们，和你们一道，积极投身到这一场伟大的无产阶级文化革命的洪流中，把一切大大小小的牛鬼蛇神，统统揪出来，连根拔掉、彻底打垮，不获全胜，决不收兵。
    广东民族学院　韩伯泉  潘德义　闭清秀
