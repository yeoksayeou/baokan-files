Title: Chinese Government Delegation and Science and Technology Delegation Arrive in Poland
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 7th. The Chinese Government Delegation, led by Jia Linfang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry, and the Science and Technology Delegation arrived in Warsaw by plane on the morning of the 7th.
    The Chinese Government Delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Polish government to attend the 35th Poznań International Fair. The Chinese Science and Technology Delegation will participate in the 13th meeting of the Sino-Polish Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.
    Greeting the Chinese delegation at the airport were An. Chekhovich, Vice Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Economic and Scientific and Technological Cooperation under the Polish Council of Ministers, as well as responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Wang Guoquan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, also greeted them at the airport.

### 我政府代表团和科技代表团抵波兰

1966-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团和科技代表团抵波兰
    新华社华沙七日电　由煤炭工业部副部长贾林放率领的中国政府代表团和科学技术代表团七日上午乘飞机到达华沙。
    中国政府代表团是应波兰政府的邀请前来参加第三十五届波兹南国际博览会的，中国科学技术代表团将参加中波两国科学技术合作委员会第十三届会议。
    到机场欢迎中国代表团的有波兰部长会议对外经济与科学技术合作委员会副主席安·切霍维奇以及有关部门负责人。
    中国驻波兰大使王国权也到机场迎接。
