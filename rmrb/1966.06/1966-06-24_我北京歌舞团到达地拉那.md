Title: Our Beijing Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 22nd (Electric Dispatch) – The Chinese Beijing Song and Dance Troupe, led by Wang Qian, arrived in Tirana by plane on the 22nd. The song and dance troupe is visiting Albania for a friendly performance at the invitation of the Albanian side.
    Those who welcomed the troupe at the airport were: Abdi Hoxha, Deputy Minister of Education and Culture of Albania; Spase, Vice Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association; Hobdari, Secretary of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations; Qiu, Vice Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Tirana People's Assembly; Krantia, Director of the Opera and Ballet Theater and Head of the National Folk Song and Dance Troupe; Avkaci, Head of the People's Army Song and Dance Troupe; and representatives of Tirana artists. Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, also greeted them at the airport.

### 我北京歌舞团到达地拉那

1966-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我北京歌舞团到达地拉那
    新华社地拉那二十二日电　由王前率领的中国北京歌舞团二十二日乘飞机到达地拉那。歌舞团是应邀前来阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问演出的。
    到机场欢迎的有：阿尔巴尼亚教育和文化部副部长阿布迪霍查，阿中友好协会副主席斯帕塞，阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会秘书霍布达里，地拉那市人民会议执行委员会副主席齐乌，歌剧芭蕾舞剧院院长、国家民间歌舞团团长克兰蒂亚，人民军歌舞团团长阿夫卡奇和地拉那艺术家的代表。中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川也到机场迎接。
