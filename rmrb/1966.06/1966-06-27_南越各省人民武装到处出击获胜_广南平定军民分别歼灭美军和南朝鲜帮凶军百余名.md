Title: People's Armed Forces in Various Provinces of South Vietnam Launch Attacks and Achieve Victories
Author:
Date: 1966-06-27
Edition: 5

The liberation armed forces and guerrilla units in the provinces of Quang Nam, Binh Dinh, Dac Lac, and My Tho in South Vietnam have recently launched continuous attacks and ambushes against the enemy, achieving significant victories.

On the night of June 15 to the early morning of June 16, the liberation armed forces in Quang Nam Province launched an attack on the American special forces stationed on Vu Mountain to the northeast of Xuan Loc town. They annihilated an entire American platoon. On the morning of June 16, when the enemy airlifted two companies of American troops to this area, they were again intercepted and bravely attacked by the liberation armed forces. Preliminary statistics show that in these two battles, the liberation armed forces killed and wounded more than 100 American invaders and shot down four helicopters.

From June 2 to 3, the liberation armed forces and guerrilla units in Binh Dinh Province launched fierce ambushes against the South Korean mercenary troops conducting "sweeping" operations in the Ho Thi area of Phu Ky District. Preliminary statistics show that they annihilated 108 enemy soldiers and forced this batch of South Korean mercenaries to withdraw from the area entirely by the afternoon of June 3.

On May 21, the guerrilla units in the southern region of Dac Lac Province simultaneously ambushed two enemy convoys on Highway 14 and a puppet security company attempting to clear this transportation line. They killed and wounded 53 enemy soldiers on the spot and destroyed or damaged six military vehicles and armored cars. On June 1, the guerrilla units in the villages of Long Khanh and Ho My in My Tho Province's Go Cong District shot down three American helicopters in battle.

According to the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the liberation armed forces in the Tam Ky region and the provinces of Quang Nam, Thua Thien, Bien Hoa, Quang Ngai, and Phu Yen in South Vietnam have continued to attack enemy outposts and transportation lines, achieving new victories.

On the night of June 17 to the early morning of June 18, the liberation armed forces in the Tam Ky region launched a surprise attack on two outposts near My An town, seizing these two outposts and annihilating two enemy platoons, seizing all their weapons and military supplies.

In Quang Nam Province, the guerrilla units launched fierce ambushes against the American puppet troops conducting "sweeping" operations in the districts of Dien Ban and Dai Loc on May 29 and June 2. They killed and wounded 45 American soldiers, annihilated an entire puppet platoon, and severely damaged two other puppet platoons, seizing a batch of weapons and ammunition.

On the night of June 14, the guerrilla units in the Luc Ngan region of Thua Thien Province attacked an enemy outpost on Highway 13, 20 kilometers from Luc Ngan town, annihilating 20 enemy soldiers and seizing all their weapons.

From May 26 to 28, the guerrilla units in Dong Xuan District and Phu Phuong District in Bien Hoa Province actively attacked the enemy conducting search operations, killing 34 enemy soldiers. On June 2, the guerrilla units in Phu Yen Province's Phu My District used landmines to destroy an American invader's military vehicle, annihilating all 32 American invaders on the vehicle.

On June 2, the liberation armed forces in Phu Yen Province blew up the Chau Oi iron bridge on the Mang Mang-Lang Son road, destroying an enemy locomotive and two carriages.

According to the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the liberation armed forces in the provinces of Dac Lac and Binh Dinh in South Vietnam have recently achieved new victories in attacking enemy outposts and anti-"sweeping" battles, annihilating more than 300 enemy soldiers.

On the night of June 14, the liberation armed forces in Dac Lac Province launched a fierce attack on the American "advisors'" quarters and the puppet government offices in Dac Lac City, killing and wounding 134 enemy soldiers (including seven American invaders), and burning down a fuel depot.

On June 4, the local forces and guerrilla units in Binh Dinh Province launched a fierce ambush against two puppet battalions conducting "sweeping" operations 15 kilometers northeast of Phu Cat town. They fought the enemy so fiercely that they fled in disarray, killing and wounding 150 enemy soldiers in the area of Giac Nghia alone.

### 南越各省人民武装到处出击获胜  广南平定军民分别歼灭美军和南朝鲜帮凶军百余名

1966-06-27
第5版()
专栏：

    南越各省人民武装到处出击获胜
    广南平定军民分别歼灭美军和南朝鲜帮凶军百余名
    据新华社河内二十五日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广南、平定、多乐、美萩等省解放武装力量和游击队，最近连续袭击和伏击敌军，取得很大胜利。
    广南省解放武装力量在六月十五日深夜到十六日凌晨，对驻守在先福县城东北武山上的美军别动队发动袭击，全歼美军一个排。十六日早晨，敌人用直升飞机空运两个连美军前来这个地区时，又遭到解放武装力量的英勇截击。据初步统计，解放武装力量在这两次战斗中共打死打伤美国侵略军一百多名，击落直升飞机四架。
    平定省解放武装力量和游击队，在六月二日和三日两天中，在平溪县河诗地区，连续猛烈截击前来“扫荡”的南朝鲜帮凶军，据初步统计，共歼敌一百零八名，并迫使这批南朝鲜帮凶军在三日下午全部撤出这个地区。
    多乐省南部地区游击队，五月二十一日在十四号公路上，同时截击敌人的两个车队和妄图前来打通这条交通线的伪军一个保安连，当场打死打伤敌人五十三名，击毁、击伤军车和装甲车六辆。美萩省丐?县隆庆和厚美两个乡的游击队，在六月一日的战斗中，击落美国直升飞机三架。
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方同塔梅地区和广南、土龙木、边和、广义、平顺等省解放武装力量，最近继续袭击敌人的据点和交通线，接连取得新的战绩。
    同塔梅地区解放武装力量在六月十七日深夜至十八日凌晨，对美安镇附近的两个据点同时发起突然袭击，拔除了这两个据点，共歼灭敌人两个排，缴获全部武器和军用物资。
    在广南省，游击队分别在五月二十九日和六月二日，猛烈截击前来奠盘县和大禄县进行“扫荡”的美伪军，打死打伤美军四十五名、全歼伪军一个排，同时重创伪军另外两个排，缴获一批武器和弹药。
    土龙木省禄宁地区游击队于六月十四日夜间袭击距离禄宁镇二十公里的第十三号公路上的一个哨所，歼灭敌人二十名，缴获全部武器。
    边和省东帅县和福平县的游击队，在五月二十六日到二十八日三天中，主动袭击前来进行搜索的敌人，打死敌人三十四名。六月二日，广义省平山县游击队，用地雷炸毁美国侵略军的军车一辆，全歼车上的三十二名美国侵略军。
    平顺省解放武装力量，六月二日炸毁了芒曼—良山路上的帅油铁桥，炸毁敌人一个火车头和两节车厢。
    据新华社河内二十六日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方迪石、平定两省解放武装力量，最近在袭击敌军据点和反“扫荡”战斗中，又取得新的战果，共歼敌三百多名。
    迪石省解放武装力量于六月十四日夜间猛烈袭击迪石市的美国“顾问”宿舍和伪政权办公处，打死打伤敌人一百三十四名（其中有美国侵略者七名），另外还烧毁汽油库一座。
    平定省地方部队和游击队，六月四日在符吉县城东北十五公里的地方猛烈截击前往“扫荡”的两营伪军，把敌人打得丢盔弃甲，仅在吉庆乡就打死打伤敌人一百五十名。
