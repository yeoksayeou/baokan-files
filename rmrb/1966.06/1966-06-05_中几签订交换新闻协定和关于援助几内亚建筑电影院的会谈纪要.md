Title: China and Guinea Sign News Exchange Agreement and Memorandum of Talks on Aiding Guinea in Building a Cinema
Author:
Date: 1966-06-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, June 2nd (电) – China and Guinea signed here on the 2nd an agreement on the exchange of news between Xinhua News Agency and the Guinean News Agency, and a China-Guinea memorandum of talks on the plan for China to aid Guinea in building a cinema.
    Chinese Ambassador to Guinea Chai Zemin and Tibou Tounkara, High Commissioner in charge of Information and Tourism Affairs of the Guinean Presidential Office, signed the agreement and the memorandum of talks on behalf of their respective countries.
    Tibou Tounkara and Chai Zemin spoke at the ceremony successively, expressing their hope that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries will be further developed.

### 中几签订交换新闻协定和关于援助几内亚建筑电影院的会谈纪要

1966-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    中几签订交换新闻协定和关于援助几内亚建筑电影院的会谈纪要
    新华社科纳克里二日电　中国和几内亚二日在这里签订了一项关于新华通讯社和几内亚新闻社交换新闻的协定和一个关于中国援助几内亚建筑一座电影院的计划的中国—几内亚会谈纪要。
    中国驻几内亚大使柴泽民和几内亚总统府负责新闻和游览事务的高级专员提布·通卡拉分别代表两国在协定和会谈纪要上签字。
    提布·通卡拉和柴泽民先后在仪式上讲了话，并且表示希望两国之间的友好合作关系将进一步得到发展。
