Title: Pakistani Ambassador to China, Roza, Leaves Post for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: Roza, Ambassador of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan to the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane this afternoon to return home after being recalled from his post.

### 巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查奉调离任回国

1966-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查奉调离任回国
    新华社四日讯　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国驻中华人民共和国大使罗查奉调离任，今天下午乘飞机离开北京回国。
