Title: Another Group of Foreign Writer Representatives Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: Another group of foreign writer representatives arriving in Beijing to attend the Asian-African Writers' Emergency Meeting arrived in Beijing on the night of the 22nd. They are: Makai and Richard Darrow, representatives from Azania (South Africa); Antonio Cubillo, representative from the Canary Islands; Yusuf Izzidin, representative from Iraq; Ali Wunlil, representative from Morocco; João Monggwanbu and Quebuzza, representatives from Mozambique; Souley Amadou, representative from Niger, etc. Chinese writers including Ba Jin welcomed them at the airport.

### 又一批外国作家代表到京

1966-06-24
第5版()
专栏：

    又一批外国作家代表到京
    新华社二十三日讯　又一批来北京参加亚非作家紧急会议的外国作家代表，于二十二日夜间到达北京。他们是：阿扎尼亚（南非）代表马凯和理查德·达罗，加那利群岛代表安东尼奥·库比洛，伊拉克代表优素福·伊兹丁，摩洛哥代表阿里·温利勒，莫三鼻给代表若奥·蒙格万布和奎布扎，尼日尔代表苏利·阿马杜等。到机场欢迎的中国作家有巴金等。
