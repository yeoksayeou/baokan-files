Title: South Vietnamese Kon Tum Province Liberation Army Fiercely Attacks US Military Camp, Annihilating Five Platoons; Vinh Long and Thu Dau Mot Provinces Liberation Armed Forces Shoot Down Dozens of Enemy Aircraft, Eliminating Over One Hundred US Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1966-06-10
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 9th June—According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, in the five days up to June 7th, the Liberation Armed Forces of Kon Tum Province, South Vietnam, continuously attacked enemy troops in the Dak To area, completely annihilating five platoons of American aggressors, two companies and two platoons of puppet troops. In addition, the Liberation Armed Forces also shot down four enemy aircraft and destroyed four 105mm howitzers and nine military trucks.
    On the morning of June 3rd, the American aggressors dispatched twenty-five helicopters to airlift a large number of puppet troops to the Dak To area, eight kilometers southeast of the Dak To district town, in an attempt to rescue enemy troops besieged by local guerrillas in Do Mo Long. As soon as this enemy reinforcement landed, the Liberation Armed Forces dealt them a fierce blow, wiping out two companies of puppet troops, severely damaging another unit, and shooting down four enemy aircraft.
    In order to rescue the beaten puppet troops, the American aggressors hurriedly dispatched a unit of the US 101st Airborne Division to land in the Dak To area. Early in the morning of June 7th, the Liberation Armed Forces fiercely attacked this US military field camp. After three hours of fighting, the Liberation Armed Forces annihilated five platoons of American aggressors and two platoons of puppet troops, destroying four 105mm howitzers and nine military trucks in one fell swoop.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th June—According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, in the eleven days up to June 4th, the Liberation Armed Forces of Vinh Long Province and Thu Dau Mot Province, South Vietnam, attacked three enemy airfields, destroyed dozens of enemy aircraft, and killed or wounded approximately 280 US and puppet troops.
    On the morning of May 29th, the Liberation Armed Forces of Vinh Long Province bombarded the Vinh Long City Airport with heavy artillery and mortars, destroying twenty-six enemy aircraft and eliminating 146 enemy soldiers, including 100 American aggressor troops.
    On May 25th, the Thu Dau Mot Province Liberation Armed Forces launched their third attack since January 30th on Phu Loi Airport—a stronghold of the US 1st Infantry Division—killing 32 enemy soldiers and destroying two enemy aircraft.
    On the night of June 4th, the Liberation Armed Forces of Thu Dau Mot Province used recoilless rifles and mortars to bombard an airfield, several military facilities, and an administrative office in the Loc Ninh Defense Sector, turning the airfield and the defense sector into a sea of ​​flames. They also killed or wounded nearly one hundred enemy soldiers, most of whom were American 1st Cavalry Division aggressor troops.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 9th June—According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the South Vietnam Liberation Army and guerrillas are fighting heroically in the rainy season, striking the enemy everywhere, eliminating the enemy, and achieving one new victory after another.
    Guerrillas of Lai Thieu District, Thu Dau Mot Province, ambushed a battalion of US troops in Thuan Giao Township on May 29th, killing or wounding more than 100 American aggressor bandits on the spot.
    The Liberation Army of Quang Ngai Province, on the night of May 27th, fiercely shelled the Chic Son stronghold, which was close to the Chu Lai US military base, completely annihilating a company of US Marines.

### 南越昆嵩省解放军猛袭美军营地歼敌五个排  永隆和土龙木省解放武装力量击毁敌机数十架歼美军一百多人

1966-06-10
第6版()
专栏：

    南越昆嵩省解放军猛袭美军营地歼敌五个排
    永隆和土龙木省解放武装力量击毁敌机数十架歼美军一百多人
    新华社河内九日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方昆嵩省解放武装力量在到六月七日为止的五天里，不断袭击达发地区的敌军，全歼美国侵略者五个排，伪军两个连和两个排。此外，解放武装力量还击落敌机四架、击毁一○五毫米大炮四门和军用卡车九辆。
    六月三日上午，美国侵略者出动二十五架直升飞机把大批伪军空投到离达图县城东南面八公里的达发地区，企图救援在都莫隆被当地游击队包围的敌军。这个敌人的援军刚刚着陆，解放武装力量就予以猛烈打击，歼灭伪军两个连，重创另一支部队，并击落敌机四架。
    美国侵略者为了救援挨揍的伪军，急忙出动美国第一○一空降旅的一支部队在达发地区着陆。六月七日清早，解放武装力量猛烈袭击了这支美军的野战营地。经过三个小时的战斗，解放武装力量一举歼灭美国侵略者五个排和伪军两个排，击毁一○五毫米大炮四门和军用卡车九辆。
    新华社河内八日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方永隆省和土龙木省的解放武装力量在到六月四日为止的十一天内，袭击了敌人三个机场，击毁敌机数十架，打死打伤美伪军约二百八十人。
    五月二十九日清晨，永隆省的解放武装力量用重炮和迫击炮轰击永隆市机场，击毁敌机二十六架，歼灭敌军一百四十六人，其中包括美国侵略军一百人。
    五月二十五日，土龙木省解放武装力量对富利机场——美国第一步兵师的据点——发动自今年一月三十日以来的第三次袭击，打死敌军三十二人，击毁敌机两架。
    六月四日夜间，土龙木省的解放武装力量用无后座力炮和迫击炮轰击禄宁防御分区的一个机场、几个军事设施和行政办事处，使这个机场和防御分区变成一片火海。他们还打死打伤将近一百多敌军，其中大部分是美国第一骑兵师侵略军。
    据新华社河内九日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方解放军和游击队在雨季里英勇作战，到处打击敌人，消灭敌人，取得一个又一个新胜利。
    土龙木省莱眺县游击队，五月二十九日在顺交乡英勇狙击一营美军，当场打死打伤美国侵略强盗一百多名。
    广义省解放军在五月二十七日夜间，猛烈炮击紧靠朱莱美军基地的漆山据点，全歼美国海军陆战队一个连。
