Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Launches Extensive Attacks, Winning Successive Victories
Author:
Date: 1966-06-02
Edition: 4

    South Vietnamese Liberation Army Launches Extensive Attacks, Winning Successive Victories
    Binh Dinh Province Liberation Army Kills Over 600 Enemies in Seven Days of Fighting, the Vast Majority Being U.S. Cavalry Division Troops
    Tay Ninh Province Guerrillas Kill and Wound Over 120 U.S. Aggressors in Five Days of Counter-"Sweeps"
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 1st – According to a supplementary report by the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the Liberation Armed Forces of Binh Dinh Province, South Vietnam, annihilated over 600 enemy troops in counter-sweep operations in the area west of Tam Quan town, Hoai Nhon district, from May 5th to 11th. The vast majority of these were troops from the U.S. 1st Cavalry Division. They also shot down and destroyed 13 helicopters and damaged 3 M-113 amphibious armored vehicles.
    The report stated that after annihilating over 500 enemy troops in the area west of Tam Quan town, Hoai Nhon district, on May 5th and 6th, the Liberation Armed Forces followed up their victory with relentless attacks on enemy artillery positions, forward command posts, and field airfields, inflicting heavy casualties. In the two battles attacking the enemy's artillery positions and field airfields, they destroyed 10 enemy helicopters and 4 artillery pieces.
    In related news, guerrillas in Go Dau district and Ben Cau area of Tay Ninh province, continuously fought back against enemy "sweeps" from May 5th to 9th, eliminating many enemy effectives, including over 120 U.S. aggressors killed and wounded.
    The Liberation Armed Forces of Quang Nam province, in the counter-sweep and interception of enemy reinforcements battles in Que Son commune, Tam Ky district, on May 2nd, killed and wounded over 70 U.S. troops and South Korean mercenary troops, including the complete annihilation of a U.S. Marine Corps platoon. On May 15th, guerrillas in Thang Binh district infiltrated a "strategic hamlet" close to the Tuan Duong outpost and completely annihilated a puppet army platoon.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 1st – According to a report by the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the Liberation Armed Forces of Gia Lai province besieged and ambushed reinforcements in the Po Lai Gilang area, 38 kilometers west of Pleiku city, from May 23rd to 29th, eliminating a large number of enemy troops, including the complete annihilation of a reinforced battalion composed of U.S. troops, puppet troops, and South Korean mercenary troops, and a puppet army company. They also shot down two enemy aircraft and captured a large quantity of weapons and military supplies.
    In the late night of May 23rd, the Liberation Armed Forces launched a fierce attack on the enemy stronghold in the Po Lai Gilang area. To relieve the enemy forces in this stronghold, the U.S.-puppet group airdropped two puppet army companies to the Don Bo Liang area, about 5 kilometers southwest of Po Lai Gilang, on the morning of the 24th. The Liberation Armed Forces immediately launched two successive fierce attacks before the enemy could gain a foothold, completely annihilating one puppet army company and scattering the other. At the same time, they shot down one helicopter and one reconnaissance plane, and captured many weapons.
    On the 27th, the enemy again airdropped a puppet army artillery unit and an infantry battalion to the Ge Long He area, 13 kilometers south of Po Lai Gilang. That night, the Liberation Armed Forces attacked the puppet army's artillery position, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.
    On the 28th, the enemy recklessly airdropped a reinforced battalion composed of U.S. aggressor troops, puppet troops, and South Korean mercenary troops to the Y A Ban valley, 18 kilometers southwest of Po Lai Gilang. That night, the Liberation Army completely annihilated two companies. In the early morning of the 29th, the Liberation Army pursued the retreating enemy and almost completely annihilated this enemy force.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 30th – According to a report by the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the Liberation Armed Forces of Binh Dinh province and My Tho province in South Vietnam recently intercepted the enemy in succession, inflicting heavy casualties on them.
    In two days of fighting on May 17th and 18th, the Liberation Armed Forces of Binh Dinh province killed more than 300 enemy troops, including 99 South Korean mercenary troops and one U.S. Army captain.
    On May 17th, the Liberation Armed Forces of Binh Dinh province launched a surprise attack on five companies of South Korean mercenary troops and puppet army security forces at the Khanh Phu train station in Phu Cat district while they were marching. At the same time, while the enemy troops were in chaos, they bravely charged forward and engaged in bayonet combat with the enemy. After just over an hour of fighting, the Liberation Armed Forces killed 111 enemy troops on the spot.
    At 6 a.m. the next morning, the Liberation Armed Forces intercepted three battalions of puppet troops on the Phu Luong and Phu Tho highlands in Hoai Nhon district, killing nearly 200 enemy troops and destroying and damaging 4 M-113 amphibious armored vehicles.
    On the night of May 25th, the Liberation Armed Forces of My Tho province launched a fierce attack on the My Ho stronghold in My Hoa commune, My An district, completely annihilating the defending enemy, a puppet army battalion and a security platoon, and capturing all weapons and military supplies. Prior to this, the Liberation Armed Forces of this province had attacked this stronghold in the late night of May 22nd to the early morning of May 23rd, annihilating more than 110 enemy troops, including the complete annihilation of a puppet army platoon.
    Since the beginning of this year, the South Vietnamese army and people have completely annihilated 18 full battalions of enemy troops, including 10 full battalions of U.S. aggressor troops.

### 南越解放军广泛出击连战皆捷  平定省解放军七天战斗歼敌六百多绝大部分是美国骑兵师部队  西宁省游击队五天反“扫荡”打死打伤美国侵略军一百二十多人

1966-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    南越解放军广泛出击连战皆捷
    平定省解放军七天战斗歼敌六百多绝大部分是美国骑兵师部队
    西宁省游击队五天反“扫荡”打死打伤美国侵略军一百二十多人
    新华社河内一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社补充报道，越南南方平定省解放武装力量五月五日到十一日在怀仁县三关镇西面地区的反扫荡战斗中，共歼灭敌人六百多名，其中绝大部分是美国第一骑兵师的部队，击落、击毁直升飞机十三架，击伤M—113型两栖装甲车三辆。
    报道说，解放武装力量继五月五日和六日在怀仁县三关镇西面地区歼灭敌人五百多名之后，又乘胜追击，接连对敌人的炮兵阵地、前方指挥所和野战机场发起猛烈袭击，给敌人以重大杀伤。其中在袭击敌军的炮兵阵地和野战机场的两次战斗中，击毁敌人的直升飞机十架，大炮四门。
    另据报道，西宁省鹅油县和槟葵地区的游击队，在五月五日到九日期间，连续反击敌人的“扫荡”，消灭敌人许多有生力量，其中打死打伤美国侵略军一百二十多名。
    广南省解放武装力量五月二日在三岐县岐盛乡的反扫荡和截击敌人援军的战斗中，打死打伤美军和南朝鲜帮凶军七十多名，其中全歼美国海军陆战队一个排。五月十五日，升平县游击队又潜入紧靠巡养哨所的一个“战略村”，全歼伪军一个排。
    新华社河内一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方嘉莱省解放武装力量五月二十三日到二十九日在波来古市西面三十八公里的波来吉浪地区围点打援，消灭大量敌人，其中包括全歼由美军、伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军组成的一个加强营和伪军一个连，打落敌机两架，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    五月二十三日深夜，解放武装力量向波来吉浪地区的敌人据点发起猛攻。为了给这个据点的敌军解围，美伪集团在二十四日早晨用直升飞机向波来吉浪西南面约五公里的敦波亮地区空投了两连伪军，解放武装力量在敌人还没有站稳脚跟的时候立即连续发起两次猛烈的攻击，全歼伪军一个连，把另一个连打得七零八落。同时击落直升飞机和侦察机各一架，缴获许多武器。
    二十七日，敌人再向波来吉浪南面十三公里的格龙和地区空投了伪军的一支炮兵部队和一个步兵营。当天夜里，解放武装力量袭击伪军的炮兵阵地，给敌人造成许多损失。
    二十八日，敌人又冒险向波来吉浪西南面十八公里的伊阿奔河谷空投一个由美国侵略军、伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军组成的加强营。在当天夜里就被解放军全歼了两个连。二十九日凌晨解放军又乘胜追击残敌，几乎全部消灭了这股敌军。
    据新华社河内三十日电 据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方平定省和美萩省解放武装力量最近连续截击敌人，使敌人遭到惨重伤亡。
    平定省解放武装力量在五月十七日和十八日的两天战斗中，打死敌人三百多名，其中包括南朝鲜帮凶军九十九名，美军上尉军官一名。
    五月十七日，平定省解放武装力量在符吉县庆福车站，向正在行进中的五个连的南朝鲜帮凶军和伪军保安队发起突然袭击，同时乘敌人的队伍混作一团的时候，英勇冲向前去同敌人展开白刃战。仅经过一个多小时的战斗，解放武装力量当场打死敌人一百一十一名。
    第二天早晨六时，解放武装力量又在怀仁县富良和富寿高地截击三个营的伪军，打死敌人近二百名，击毁、击伤M—113型两栖装甲车四辆。
    美萩省解放武装力量在五月二十五日夜间猛烈袭击美安县美和乡美虎据点，全歼守敌伪军一个营和一个保安排，缴获全部武器和军用物资。在这之前，这个省的解放武装力量曾于五月二十二日深夜到二十三日凌晨袭击了这个据点，歼灭敌人一百一十多名，其中全歼伪军一个排。
    自从今年年初以来，越南南方军民已经全部消灭敌人十八个整营，其中包括美国侵略军十个整营。
