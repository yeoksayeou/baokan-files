Title: The New Romanian Ambassador to China Calls on Chairman Zhu De
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-02
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 1st: The new Romanian Ambassador to China, Aurel Duma, paid a call on Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, this afternoon. Chairman Zhu De held a friendly conversation with him.

### 罗马尼亚新任驻华大使拜会朱德委员长

1966-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚新任驻华大使拜会朱德委员长
    新华社一日讯　罗马尼亚新任驻华大使奥列尔·杜马，今天下午拜会全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德。朱德委员长同他进行了友好的谈话。
