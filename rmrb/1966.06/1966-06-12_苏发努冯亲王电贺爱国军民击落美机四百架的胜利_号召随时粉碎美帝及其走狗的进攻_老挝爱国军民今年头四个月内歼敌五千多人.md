Title: Prince Souphanouvong Sends Telegram Congratulating Patriotic Army and People on Victory of Shooting Down 400 American Planes, Calls for Readiness to Crush Attacks by U.S. Imperialism and Its Lackeys, Lao Patriotic Army and People Annihilate Over 5,000 Enemies in First Four Months of This Year
Author:
Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 9th. According to a Pathet Lao Radio broadcast on the 9th: Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front and Vice Premier of the Lao Tripartite National Union Government, sent a telegram to the Lao army and people on the 7th, warmly congratulating them on the great victory of shooting down 400 American planes.
    The Supreme Command of the Lao People's Liberation Army issued a communique on the 7th regarding the Lao army and people shooting down 400 American planes, announcing that on May 25th, the army and people of the provinces of Sam Neua, Xieng Khouang, Luang Prabang, and Khammouane shot down four American planes. "Thus, from May 17, 1964, to May 25, 1966, the Lao patriotic army and people have shot down a total of 400 American planes."
    Prince Souphanouvong's telegram said that under the leadership of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, the Lao patriotic army and people fought resolutely and valiantly crushed all the enemy's ground encroachment offensives and air raids.
    The telegram said that the victory of the Lao patriotic army and people in shooting down 400 American planes is a heavy blow to the U.S. imperialist policy of aggression in Laos, and once again a crushing defeat for the so-called "American air superiority."
    The telegram pointed out, "The achievement of shooting down 400 American planes is the joint achievement of all units of the Lao People's Liberation Army and the patriotic neutral armed forces, as well as all ethnic groups from north to south. This achievement embodies revolutionary heroism, demonstrating the progress in tactics and technology of our main forces, local forces, and militia guerrillas, as well as the strength of the people of all ethnic groups in the liberated areas."
    The telegram called on all cadres and soldiers of the Lao People's Liberation Army and the patriotic neutral armed forces, as well as the people of all ethnic groups in Laos, to further heighten their vigilance, fight while actively producing, and be ready at any time to crush the ground encroachment and air offensive plans of U.S. imperialism and its lackeys.
    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 7th. According to a Pathet Lao Radio broadcast: The Lao patriotic army and people have achieved major victories in their counterattacks against the offensives of U.S. imperialism and its lackeys from January to April this year.
    According to statistics from relevant departments of the Supreme Command of the Lao People's Liberation Army, during this period, the Lao patriotic army and people annihilated a total of 5,090 enemy troops, including 2,455 killed, 856 wounded, 403 captured, and 1,376 who surrendered; shot down 133 American planes and damaged hundreds. In addition, they also captured a large number of weapons, ammunition, and military supplies.

### 苏发努冯亲王电贺爱国军民击落美机四百架的胜利  号召随时粉碎美帝及其走狗的进攻  老挝爱国军民今年头四个月内歼敌五千多人

1966-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯亲王电贺爱国军民击落美机四百架的胜利
    号召随时粉碎美帝及其走狗的进攻
    老挝爱国军民今年头四个月内歼敌五千多人
    新华社康开九日电　据巴特寮电台九日广播：老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席、老挝三方民族团结政府副首相苏发努冯亲王七日打电报给老挝全国军民，热烈祝贺打落四百架美国飞机的重大胜利。
    老挝人民解放军最高指挥部七日发表关于老挝军民打落四百架美国飞机的公报，宣布在五月二十五日这一天，桑怒、川圹、琅勃拉邦和甘蒙等省军民打落四架美国飞机。“这样，从一九六四年五月十七日到一九六六年五月二十五日止，老挝爱国军民共打落美国飞机四百架。”
    苏发努冯亲王的电报说，在老挝爱国战线党中央的领导下，老挝爱国军民坚决战斗，英勇粉碎了敌人的所有地面蚕食进攻和空中的袭击。
    电报说，老挝爱国军民打落美国飞机四百架的胜利，是对美帝国主义侵略老挝战争政策的一个沉重打击，使所谓“美国空中优势”再次遭到惨败。
    电报指出，“打落美国飞机四百架的战绩是老挝人民解放军和爱国中立武装力量部队各单位以及从北到南的全国各民族的共同成绩。这一战绩体现了革命的英雄主义，表明了我们的主力部队、地方部队和民兵游击队的战术、技术的进步以及解放区各族人民的强大。”
    电报号召老挝人民解放军和爱国中立武装力量全体干部和战士以及老挝全国各族人民进一步提高警惕，边战斗边积极生产，随时准备着粉碎美帝国主义及其走狗的地面蚕食和空中进攻计划。
    新华社康开七日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝爱国军民今年一月到四月在反击美帝国主义及其走狗的进攻中，取得重大的胜利。
    据老挝人民解放军最高指挥部有关部门统计，在这期间，老挝爱国军民共歼敌五千零九十人，其中打死二千四百五十五人，打伤八百五十六人，俘虏四百零三人，投降一千三百七十六人；击落美国飞机一百三十三架，击伤成百架。此外，还缴获很多武器、弹药和军用物资。
