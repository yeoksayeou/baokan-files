Title: Vietnamese Foreign Ministry and South Vietnam National Liberation Front Strongly Condemn US Troop Increase in South Vietnam; US Imperialism's Expansion of War of Aggression Against Vietnam Will Inevitably Meet Utter Defeat; General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army Strongly Protests US Aircraft Intrusion into Hanoi Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-06-18
Edition: 5

    Hanoi, June 17 (Xinhua) – A spokesman for the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on the 16th, strongly condemning the US government's decision to send another 18,000 US troops to South Vietnam shortly, increasing the current US invading forces from 267,000 to 285,000.

    The statement said that this is a new and extremely serious action by US imperialism aimed at further intensifying its war of aggression against South Vietnam.

    The statement also said that the US troop increase in South Vietnam once again exposes the deceptive nature of its so-called "peace negotiation" rhetoric. No matter how US imperialism justifies this troop increase, it cannot hide its incomparably ugly aggressive conspiracy and actions.

    The statement said that the US government's above-mentioned troop increase decision is an extremely barbaric provocation against the Vietnamese people, the American people, and the peace-loving people of the world, and a blatant violation of the 1954 Geneva Agreements on the Vietnam issue.

    The statement said that the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam strongly condemns and resolutely opposes the US government's above-mentioned actions to intensify and expand the war, and calls on the American people and the peace-loving and justice-upholding people of the world to further strengthen their struggle and demand that the US government must stop its war of aggression against Vietnam. The United States must strictly respect the 1954 Geneva Agreements on the Vietnam issue, and must permanently and unconditionally cease all bombing and other war actions against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The United States must withdraw all troops of the United States and its satellite countries, remove all US military bases in South Vietnam, and allow the people of South Vietnam to resolve their own internal affairs.

    The statement concluded, "If US imperialism stubbornly intensifies and expands its war of aggression at its own risk, then it will inevitably suffer a thorough and shameful defeat in South Vietnam."

    Hanoi, June 16 (Xinhua) – According to the South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency, the Foreign Liaison Committee of the Central Committee of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front issued a statement on the 13th, strongly condemning the US imperialism's war expansion action of deciding to send another 18,000 US aggressor troops to South Vietnam in July.

    The statement called on the governments and peoples of all countries related to the 1954 Geneva Agreements to take effective measures to stop the US imperialism's military adventures in the region. The statement resolutely demanded that US imperialism stop sending troops to South Vietnam, strictly abide by the 1954 Geneva Agreements on the Vietnam issue, recognize the South Vietnam National Liberation Front as the sole true representative of the people of South Vietnam, and withdraw its troops from South Vietnam.

    The statement concluded that the determination of the soldiers and civilians throughout Vietnam is a huge force that can move mountains and fill the seas, and can completely defeat the US imperialist aggressors under any circumstances. Even if US imperialism sends another 300,000 US troops or more to South Vietnam, it cannot escape its strategically passive position and will inevitably suffer even more disastrous and shameful defeat.

    Hanoi, June 17 (Xinhua) – The head of the Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army sent an urgent telegram to the Chairman of the International Commission for Vietnam on the 17th, strongly protesting the intrusion of US aircraft in multiple batches into the airspace of Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on the 16th. The telegram said that the war actions of the United States and its lackeys have crudely violated the sovereignty and territory of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and blatantly violated the 1954 Geneva Agreements on the Vietnam issue and international law. This is an extremely barbarous provocation against world progressive public opinion.

### 越南外交部和南越民族解放阵线强烈谴责美国增兵南越  美帝扩大侵越战争必遭彻底失败  越南人民军总司令部强烈抗议美机侵入河内市上空

1966-06-18
第5版()
专栏：

    越南外交部和南越民族解放阵线强烈谴责美国增兵南越
    美帝扩大侵越战争必遭彻底失败
    越南人民军总司令部强烈抗议美机侵入河内市上空
  据新华社河内十七日电　越南民主共和国外交部发言人十六日发表声明，强烈谴责美国政府决定不久将再向越南南方增派一万八千名美军，使目前的美国侵略军从二十六万七千名增加到二十八万五千名。
    声明说，这是美帝国主义旨在进一步加紧侵略越南南方战争的新的极其严重的行动。
    声明又说，美国增兵南越的行动，再一次暴露了它的所谓“和平谈判”论调的欺骗性。美帝国主义无论怎样为它的这一增兵行动辩解，也丝毫不能掩盖它的无比丑恶的侵略阴谋和行动。
    声明说，美国政府的上述增兵决定，是对越南人民、美国人民和世界爱好和平人民的极其横蛮的挑衅，是明目张胆地违反了一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议。
    声明说，越南民主共和国外交部强烈谴责和坚决反对美国政府上述加强和扩大战争的行动，并且呼吁美国人民和世界爱好和平和主持正义的人民进一步加强斗争，要求美国政府必须停止侵略越南的战争。美国必须严格尊重一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议，必须永远地无条件地停止对越南民主共和国的轰炸和其他一切战争行动。美国必须全部撤走美国及其仆从国的军队，撤除美国在越南南方的所有军事基地，让越南南方人民自己解决自己的内部事务。
    声明最后说，“如果美帝国主义一味冒险地加紧和扩大侵略战争，那末，它必将在越南南方遭到彻底和可耻的失败”。
    据新华社河内十六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会对外联络委员会十三日发表声明，强烈谴责美帝国主义决定七月份再向越南南方增派一万八千名美国侵略军的扩大战争行动。
    声明呼吁同一九五四年日内瓦协议有关的各国政府和人民采取有效的措施，制止美帝国主义在这一地区的军事冒险。声明坚决要求美帝国主义停止向越南南方增派军队，严正履行一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议，承认越南南方民族解放阵线是越南南方人民的唯一真正代表和把军队撤出越南南方。
    声明最后指出，越南全国军民的决心是移山填海的巨大力量，完全可以在任何情况下战胜美帝国主义侵略者，即使美帝国主义再向南越增派三十万美军或者更多，它也不能摆脱战略上的被动处境，并且一定要遭到更加惨重、更加可耻的失败。
    据新华社河内十七日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长十七日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会主席，强烈抗议美国飞机多批于十六日侵入越南民主共和国首都河内市上空。电报说，美国及其走狗的战争行动粗暴地侵犯了越南民主共和国的主权和领土，明目张胆地违反了一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议和国际法。这是对世界进步舆论的极其蛮横的挑衅。
