Title: The Vietnamese Acrobatic Troupe Leaves Wuhan for Beijing After Concluding its Performance Tour in Wuhan

Author:

Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 4

    The Vietnamese Acrobatic Troupe Leaves Wuhan for Beijing After Concluding its Performance Tour in Wuhan
    (Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 11th) The Acrobatic Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam concluded its performance tour in Wuhan and left Wuhan for Beijing by special train this afternoon. The Vietnamese artists performed a total of five shows in Wuhan. Their acrobatic performances, rich in fighting spirit and folk elements, were warmly welcomed by the broad masses of the audience. Newspapers in the Wuhan area published news, photos, sketches, and articles, praising the success of the performances. Wuhan Television also broadcasted the grand occasion of the performances.
    The Vietnamese Acrobatic Troupe arrived in Wuhan by train from Hanoi on May 31st. During their stay in Wuhan, they visited the Wuhan Iron and Steel Company, Wuhan Small Tractor Factory, Huazhong Institute of Technology, and the Wuhan Acrobatic Troupe, and held get-togethers with workers, students, and actors. At the gatherings, both guests and hosts raised their arms and shouted "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live President Ho Chi Minh!"

### 结束在武汉的访问演出  越南杂技团离武汉来北京

1966-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在武汉的访问演出
    越南杂技团离武汉来北京
    新华社武汉十一日电　越南民主共和国杂技团结束了在武汉的访问演出，今天下午乘专车离开武汉前往北京。越南艺术家在武汉共演出五场，他们的富有战斗气息和民间色彩的杂技节目，受到了广大观众的热烈欢迎。武汉地区的报纸纷纷发表消息、照片、速写和文章，赞扬这次演出的成功。武汉电视台也转播了演出的盛况。
    越南杂技团是五月三十一日乘火车由河内到达武汉的。在武汉期间，他们参观了武汉钢铁公司、武汉小型拖拉机厂、华中工学院和武汉杂技团等单位，并和工人、学生、演员举行联欢会，宾主在会上一起振臂高呼“毛主席万岁”“胡志明主席万岁”。
