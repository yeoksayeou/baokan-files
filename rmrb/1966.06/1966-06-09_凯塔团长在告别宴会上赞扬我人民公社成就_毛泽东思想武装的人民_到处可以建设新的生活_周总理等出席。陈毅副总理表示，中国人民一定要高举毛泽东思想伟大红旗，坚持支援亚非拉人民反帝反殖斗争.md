Title: Head of the Keita Delegation Praises the Achievements of Our People's Communes; People Armed with Mao Zedong Thought Can Build New Lives Everywhere; Premier Zhou and Others Attend. Vice Premier Chen Yi States That the Chinese People Must Hold High the Great Red Banner of Mao Zedong Thought and Persist in Supporting the Anti-Imperialist and Anti-Colonial Struggle of the People of Asia, Africa, and Latin America.
Author:
Date: 1966-06-09
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Mamadou Madeira Keita, head of the Malian delegation, member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union-African Democratic Rally of Mali, and Minister of Justice of Mali, held a farewell banquet at the Malian Embassy in China tonight.

    Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, President of the Supreme People's Court Yang Xiufeng, and others, were invited to attend the banquet.

    Minister Madeira Keita and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, visited the Yuegezhuang Shashi峪 (Sand and Stone Gully) Brigade in Zunhua County, Hebei Province, known as "Living Yugong," and the Xipu Brigade of the Jianming Commune, which runs the commune with the spirit of "Poor Sticks," and received a warm welcome and cordial reception from the commune members.

    In his speech at the banquet, Minister Madeira Keita praised the achievements made by these two brigades in various aspects, pointing out that their achievements are the victory of the great Mao Zedong Thought. He expressed his firm belief that people armed with Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, with firm will, can build new lives everywhere.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi also spoke at the banquet, warmly thanking Minister Madeira Keita for praising the great Mao Zedong Thought. He stated that the Chinese people must hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, strive for greater victories in all aspects, and persist in supporting the revolutionary struggles of the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America against imperialism and old and new colonialism.

    In their speeches, Minister Madeira Keita and Vice Premier Chen Yi both warmly wished that the friendship and cooperation between China and Mali and between the two peoples would be strengthened and maintained forever.

    Their speeches aroused bursts of warm applause.

    Also invited to the banquet were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, as well as responsible persons from relevant departments such as Wang Daohan, Li Yingji, Liu Xiwu, and Zhang Tiesheng.

    Malian Ambassador to China Guissé Tidiani and others attended the banquet. (Picture attached)

### 凯塔团长在告别宴会上赞扬我人民公社成就  毛泽东思想武装的人民  到处可以建设新的生活  周总理等出席。陈毅副总理表示，中国人民一定要高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，坚持支援亚非拉人民反帝反殖斗争

1966-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    凯塔团长在告别宴会上赞扬我人民公社成就
    毛泽东思想武装的人民
    到处可以建设新的生活
    周总理等出席。陈毅副总理表示，中国人民一定要高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，坚持支援亚非拉人民反帝反殖斗争
    新华社八日讯　马里代表团团长、马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、司法部长马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔，今晚在马里驻中国大使馆举行告别宴会。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅，最高人民法院院长杨秀峰等，应邀出席了宴会。
    马戴拉·凯塔部长等贵宾今天白天在陈毅副总理陪同下，访问了河北省遵化县被称为“活愚公”的岳各庄沙石峪大队，和用“穷棒子”精神办社的建明公社西铺大队，受到社员们的热烈欢迎和亲切接待。
    马戴拉·凯塔部长在宴会上的讲话中，赞扬了这两个大队在各方面取得的成就，指出他们的成就是伟大的毛泽东思想的胜利。他表示深信，用马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想武装起来的有着坚定意志的人民，到处可以建设起新的生活。
    陈毅副总理也在宴会上讲话，热烈感谢马戴拉·凯塔部长称颂伟大的毛泽东思想。他表示，中国人民一定要高举毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，在各方面争取更大的胜利，坚持支援亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲各国人民反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的革命斗争。
    马戴拉·凯塔部长和陈毅副总理在讲话中，都热情祝愿中马两国和两国人民的友谊和合作日益加强，永远保持下去。
    他们的讲话激起了一阵阵热烈的掌声。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，以及汪道涵、李应吉、刘希文、张铁生等有关方面的负责人。
    马里驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼等出席宴会作陪。（附图片）
