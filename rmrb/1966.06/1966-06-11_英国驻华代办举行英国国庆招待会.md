Title: British Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds British National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-06-11
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 10th. British Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. Hopson, held a reception this afternoon for the British National Day. Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and responsible persons from relevant departments were invited to attend the reception.
    Also invited to attend the reception were the diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in China.

### 英国驻华代办举行英国国庆招待会

1966-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    英国驻华代办举行英国国庆招待会
    新华社十日讯　英国驻中国代办霍普森今天下午为英国国庆举行招待会。外交部副部长罗贵波和有关方面负责人应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有各国驻中国的使节。
