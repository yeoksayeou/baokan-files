Title: Vice Premier Bo Yibo Receives North Korean Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd—Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo received the North Korean delegation headed by Kong Rentae, Vice Minister of the Electrical and Coal Industry Ministry of North Korea, this morning and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Liu Lanbo, Vice Minister of Water Conservancy and Electric Power, Cheng Mingsheng, and others. Kim Jae-suk, Counselor of the North Korean Embassy in China, was also present.
    The North Korean delegation is here to discuss issues related to the production of the Yalu River Sino-Korean Joint Venture Power Station and the construction of new power stations.

### 薄一波副总理接见朝鲜代表团

1966-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    薄一波副总理接见朝鲜代表团
    新华社二十三日讯　国务院副总理薄一波今天上午接见以朝鲜电气煤炭工业省副相孔仁泰为首的朝鲜代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有水利电力部副部长刘澜波、程明升等。朝鲜驻华大使馆参赞金在淑也在座。
    朝鲜代表团是前来我国商谈有关鸭绿江中朝合营电站的生产和新电站的建设问题的。
