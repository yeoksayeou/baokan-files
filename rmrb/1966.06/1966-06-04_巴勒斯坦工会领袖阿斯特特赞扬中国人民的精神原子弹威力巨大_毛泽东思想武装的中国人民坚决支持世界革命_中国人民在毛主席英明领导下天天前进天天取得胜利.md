Title: Palestinian Union Leader Astete Praises the Immense Power of the Chinese People's Spiritual Atomic Bomb; The Chinese People Armed with Mao Zedong Thought Resolutely Support World Revolution; Under the Wise Leadership of Chairman Mao, the Chinese People Advance Daily and Achieve Victory Daily
Author:
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo – Mahmoud Abu Astete, Executive Committee Member of the Palestinian Gaza Trade Union Federation, who visited China a month ago, recently stated that the Chinese people have achieved accomplishments in all aspects under the correct leadership of Chairman Mao. Their efforts to defeat U.S. imperialism have reached the point where they have successfully conducted nuclear explosions, "these nuclear explosions make the people of the world happy to hear and make the imperialists tremble with fear."
    He said that the Chinese people deeply love their leader Chairman Mao.
    Astete and the chairman of the federation, Abdul Rahman Dezia, recently received Xinhua News Agency reporters in Gaza and gave talks.
    Astete said that, however, the power of the spiritual atomic bomb—the firm will of the Chinese people—is greater than the atomic bomb itself. He said that he saw in the Beijing Revolutionary Museum how Chairman Mao and his comrades used simple weapons to defeat enemies with sophisticated weapons. He said that now, Chinese workers and commune members, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, are working diligently, soldiers are vigilantly holding weapons, and every Chinese person resolutely supports the world revolution.
    He praised China's policy of self-reliance. He said that the Palestinian people can learn a lot from this.
    He said: "China is advancing every day, and China is achieving victory every day. The Chinese people, with their determination and firm will, under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, will surely achieve more victories and more progress."
    Dezia pointed out that the Chinese people and the Palestinian people are facing their common enemy—the world's great enemy, U.S. imperialism. "As long as U.S. imperialism exists, there will be no peace in the world. Israel and the Chiang Kai-shek clique are puppets of U.S. imperialism. Without the support of the United States, they cannot exist for a moment. Therefore, we support each other in our liberation cause."
    While praising the revolutionary spirit of the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, the two Palestinian union leaders both said that they cherish the friendship with the Chinese people, and the Chinese people cherish the friendship with the Palestinian and revolutionary people all over the world.

### 巴勒斯坦工会领袖阿斯特特赞扬中国人民的精神原子弹威力巨大  毛泽东思想武装的中国人民坚决支持世界革命  中国人民在毛主席英明领导下天天前进天天取得胜利

1966-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦工会领袖阿斯特特赞扬中国人民的精神原子弹威力巨大
    毛泽东思想武装的中国人民坚决支持世界革命
    中国人民在毛主席英明领导下天天前进天天取得胜利
    新华社开罗电　一个月前访问过中国的巴勒斯坦加沙工会联合会执行委员马哈茂德·阿布·阿斯特特最近发表谈话说，中国人民在毛主席的正确领导下在各个方面取得了成就。他们在为打败美帝国主义所作的努力已经达到了这样的地步：他们已经成功地进行了核爆炸，“这些核爆炸使世界人民听了感到高兴，使帝国主义听了感到胆战心惊。”
    他说，中国人民十分热爱他们的领袖毛主席。
    阿斯特特和这个联合会的主席阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·德齐亚不久前在加沙接见了新华社记者，并且发表了谈话。
    阿斯特特说，然而，精神原子弹——中国人民的坚决意志——的威力比原子弹本身更大。他说，他在北京革命博物馆看到了毛主席和他的战友们是怎样用简陋的武器打败武器精良的敌人的。他说，现在，用毛主席的思想武装起来的中国工人们和公社社员们正在勤奋地劳动，士兵们警惕地拿着武器，每一个中国人都坚决支持世界革命。
    他赞扬中国的自力更生政策。他说，巴勒斯坦人民从这里可学到很多东西。
    他说：“中国天天在前进，中国天天在取得胜利。中国人民以他们的决心和坚定意志，在毛泽东主席的英明领导下，一定会取得更多的胜利，更多的进展。”
    德齐亚指出，中国人民和巴勒斯坦人民正面对着他们的共同敌人——世界大敌美帝国主义。“只要美帝国主义还存在，世界上就不会有和平。以色列和蒋介石集团都是美帝国主义的傀儡。没有美国的支撑，它们是一刻也存在不了的。因此，我们在我们的解放事业中互相支持。”
    这两位巴勒斯坦工会领导人在赞扬在毛泽东主席领导下的中国人民的革命精神的同时，都说，他们珍视同中国人民的友谊，中国人民是珍视同巴勒斯坦和全世界革命人民的友谊的。
