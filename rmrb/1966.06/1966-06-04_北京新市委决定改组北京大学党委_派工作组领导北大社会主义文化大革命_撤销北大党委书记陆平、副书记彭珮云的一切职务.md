Title: Beijing New Municipal Committee Decides to Reorganize Peking University Party Committee, Sends Work Team to Lead Peking University's Socialist Cultural Revolution, Dismisses All Posts of Peking University Party Secretary Lu Ping and Deputy Secretary Peng Peiyun
Author:
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd—The newly reorganized Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has decided: (1) to send a work team headed by Zhang Chengxian to Peking University to lead the Socialist Cultural Revolution; (2) to dismiss all posts of Lu Ping, Secretary of the Peking University Party Committee, and Peng Peiyun, Deputy Secretary, and to reorganize the Peking University Party Committee; (3) During the reorganization of the Peking University Party Committee, the work team will exercise the functions and powers of the Party Committee on its behalf.

### 北京新市委决定改组北京大学党委  派工作组领导北大社会主义文化大革命  撤销北大党委书记陆平、副书记彭珮云的一切职务

1966-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    北京新市委决定改组北京大学党委
    派工作组领导北大社会主义文化大革命
    撤销北大党委书记陆平、副书记彭珮云的一切职务
    新华社三日讯　中共新改组的北京市委决定：（一）派以张承先为首的工作组到北京大学对社会主义文化大革命进行领导；（二）撤销中共北京大学党委书记陆平、副书记彭珮云的一切职务，并对北京大学党委进行改组；（三）在北京大学党委改组期间，由工作组代行党委的职权。
