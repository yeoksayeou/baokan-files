Title: Liao Chengzhi Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-06-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association and Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received this morning the entire delegation of the Third Japanese Peace Committee to China, led by Kaneko Reigaku and Asahina Yasushi, and had breakfast with them.
    Yang Ji, Wang Chuanbin, and others were present.
    Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, and his wife, as well as Japanese friend Kazuo Okumura, were also present.

### 廖承志会长接见日本客人

1966-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    廖承志会长接见日本客人
    新华社四日讯　中日友好协会会长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志今天上午接见了由金子灵学和朝比奈靖司率领的日本和平委员会第三次访华代表团全体成员，并且和他们共进早餐。
    在座的有杨骥、王传斌等。
    在京日本和平人士西园寺公一和夫人以及日本朋友奥村一男也在座。
