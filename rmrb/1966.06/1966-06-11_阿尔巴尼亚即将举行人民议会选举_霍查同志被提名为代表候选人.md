Title: Albania to Hold People's Assembly Elections; Comrade Hoxha Nominated as Candidate
Author:
Date: 1966-06-11
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 9th (Electric Dispatch): According to reports in the local newspapers: Comrade Enver Hoxha, the beloved leader of the Albanian people and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, has been unanimously nominated by his constituency as a candidate for the new People's Assembly representative. According to reports, the No. 200 electoral district, composed of the ninth, tenth, eleventh, and twelfth residential areas of Tirana, held a voter meeting on the 8th. At the meeting, Kelezi, a member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, on behalf of the leading body of the Tirana Democratic Front, nominated Comrade Enver Hoxha, the beloved leader of the Albanian people, as a candidate for the People's Assembly representative. At that moment, the entire venue erupted in prolonged and enthusiastic applause. Participants spoke one after another, warmly supporting the nomination of Comrade Hoxha as a candidate for the People's Assembly representative. In their speeches, they unanimously expressed their heartfelt love and support for Comrade Enver Hoxha and the Albanian Labor Party.
    Starting from June 8th, electoral districts throughout Albania have successively held voter meetings to nominate candidates for the People's Assembly representative.

### 阿尔巴尼亚即将举行人民议会选举  霍查同志被提名为代表候选人

1966-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚即将举行人民议会选举
    霍查同志被提名为代表候选人
    新华社地拉那九日电　据此间报纸报道：阿尔巴尼亚人民敬爱的领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志，已被他所在的选区一致提名为新的人民议会代表候选人。据报道，由地拉那第九、十、十一、十二居民区组成的第二百号选区八日举行了选民大会。在大会上，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员凯莱齐以地拉那民主阵线领导机构的名义，提名阿尔巴尼亚人民敬爱的领袖恩维尔·霍查同志为人民议会代表的候选人。这时，全场爆发出长时间的热烈掌声。与会者纷纷发言，热烈支持提名霍查同志为人民议会代表候选人。他们在发言中一致表达了他们对恩维尔·霍查同志和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的衷心爱戴和支持。
    从六月八日起，阿尔巴尼亚全国各地的选区先后举行选民大会，提名人民议会代表候选人。
