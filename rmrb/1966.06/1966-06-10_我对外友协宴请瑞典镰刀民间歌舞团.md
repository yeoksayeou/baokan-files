Title: The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Hosts a Banquet for the Swedish Sickle Folk Song and Dance Troupe

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 9th -- The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries held a banquet tonight to welcome all members of the Swedish Sickle Folk Song and Dance Troupe.

    Song Yiping, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Petri, the Swedish Ambassador to China, and Bo Walker Bullmark, the head of the song and dance troupe, spoke at the banquet, jointly wishing the friendship between the people of China and Sweden would grow day by day.

    Attending the banquet were Song Changhua, Li Shubin, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 我对外友协宴请瑞典镰刀民间歌舞团

1966-06-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我对外友协宴请瑞典镰刀民间歌舞团
    新华社九日讯　中国人民对外文化友好协会今晚举行宴会，欢迎瑞典镰刀民间歌舞团全体成员。
    对外友协副会长宋一平和瑞典驻中国大使佩特里以及歌舞团团长伯尔·沃克·布尔马克在宴会上讲话，共祝中瑞两国人民之间的友谊与日俱增。
    出席宴会作陪的，有宋常华、栗树彬等有关方面负责人。
