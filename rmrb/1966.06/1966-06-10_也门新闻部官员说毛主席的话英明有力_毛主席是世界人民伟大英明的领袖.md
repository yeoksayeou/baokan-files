Title: Yemen News Department Official Says Chairman Mao's Words are Wise and Powerful; Chairman Mao is the Great and Wise Leader of the People of the World
Author:
Date: 1966-06-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Sana'a - Abdulla Ahmed Sul, the Office Director of the Yemen News Department, who visited China last year, told Xinhua News Agency reporters here on the 5th: "Chairman Mao Zedong is the great and wise leader of the people of the world. He not only leads one-fourth of the world's population (China) in revolution but also leads three-fourths of the world's population in revolution." He also said: "Chairman Mao's words are very wise and powerful. His words are theories of a very high standard."

### 也门新闻部官员说毛主席的话英明有力  毛主席是世界人民伟大英明的领袖

1966-06-10
第5版()
专栏：

    也门新闻部官员说毛主席的话英明有力
    毛主席是世界人民伟大英明的领袖
    新华社萨那电　去年访问过中国的也门新闻部办公厅主任阿卜杜拉·艾哈迈德·苏尔五日在这里对新华社记者说：“毛泽东主席是世界人民的伟大英明的领袖。他不仅领导世界人口中四分之一的人民（中国）进行革命，而且领导世界人口中四分之三的人民进行革命。”他还说：“毛主席的话是十分英明有力的。他的话是水平很高的理论。”
