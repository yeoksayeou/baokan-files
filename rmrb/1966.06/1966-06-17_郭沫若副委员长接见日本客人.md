Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-06-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 15th. Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received this afternoon all members of the Japanese Rinzaishu Delegation to China, led by honorary head Koga Daiko and head Yamada Mumon, and had friendly talks with them.

    Present at the reception were Xiao Xianfa, Ngawang Jigme, Ju Zan, Zhou Shujia, Wang Xiaoyun, and others. Japanese peace figure in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, was also present. The delegation arrived in Beijing on June 12th for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the Buddhist Association of China.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社十五日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，今天下午接见了由名誉团长古川大航、团长山田无文率领的日本临济宗访华代表团全体成员，和他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有萧贤法、阿旺嘉措、巨赞、周叔迦、王晓云等。日本在京和平人士西园寺公一也在座。代表团是应中国对外友协和中国佛教协会的邀请，来我国进行友好访问，于六月十二日到达北京的。
