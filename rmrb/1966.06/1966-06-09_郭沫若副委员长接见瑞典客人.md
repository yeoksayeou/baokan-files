Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives Swedish Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 8th - Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received today the Director of Swedish Radio and Television, Mr. Baelentz, and his wife, as well as the advisor of Swedish Radio and the Director of the Swedish Dance Academy, Mr. Häger, and the Director of the Music Department of Swedish Radio, Mr. Blomdahl, and his wife. Vice Chairman Guo Moruo had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Gu Wenhua, Wang Yuanfang, and Meng Qiyu.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见瑞典客人

1966-06-09
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见瑞典客人
    新华社八日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见了瑞典广播公司电视台台长拜仑茨和夫人，瑞典广播公司顾问、瑞典舞蹈学院院长海格尔，以及瑞典广播公司音乐部主任布隆达尔和夫人。郭沫若副委员长同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有顾文华、王元方、孟启予。
