Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three More American Planes

Author:
Date: 1966-06-21
Edition: 5

    Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three More American Planes
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th -- According to Vietnam News Agency, at one o'clock in the morning on the 17th, American aggressors and their running dogs dispatched commando boats to intrude into the territorial waters of the northern part of Ha Tinh Province in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The military and civilians of Ha Tinh Province sank one of the enemy's commando boats with the very first barrage of artillery fire.
    In addition to the three American planes shot down in Nam Ha Province on the 15th, supplementary reports indicate that two more American planes were shot down in Quang Binh Province and Quang Ninh Province.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th -- According to an announcement: The military and civilians of Nghe An Province in northern Vietnam shot down an intruding American jet fighter today and captured an American pilot alive.

### 越南军民又击落美机三架

1966-06-21
第5版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美机三架
    新华社河内十七日电　据越南通讯社报道，十七日凌晨一时，美国侵略者和他们的走狗派遣别动艇侵入越南民主共和国河静省北部的领海。河静省军民用第一排炮弹就击沉了敌人的别动艇一艘。
    另据补充报道，十五日除在南河省打落美国飞机三架外，还在广平省和广宁省打落美机两架。
    据新华社河内十七日电　据公布：越南北方义安省军民，今天击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架，并活捉美国飞行员。
