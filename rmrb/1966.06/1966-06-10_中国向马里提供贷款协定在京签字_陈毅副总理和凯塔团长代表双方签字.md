Title: China and Mali Sign Loan Agreement in Beijing; Vice Premier Chen Yi and Head of Delegation Keita Sign on Behalf of Their Respective Sides

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-10
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 9th -- The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Mali signed an agreement in Beijing this evening regarding the Chinese government's provision of a loan to the Malian government.

    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and Head of the Malian delegation, member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union-African Democratic Rally (US-RDA) and Minister of Justice Mamadou Madeira Keita, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and responsible persons from relevant departments Li Yingji, Liu Xiwu, etc. On the Malian side were the Ambassador to China Gesse Tidiani and members of the Malian delegation, Oumar Ly.

    After the signing, both sides raised their glasses in warm congratulations. (Attached photo)

### 中国向马里提供贷款协定在京签字  陈毅副总理和凯塔团长代表双方签字

1966-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中国向马里提供贷款协定在京签字
    陈毅副总理和凯塔团长代表双方签字
    新华社九日讯　中华人民共和国政府和马里共和国政府，今晚在北京签订了关于中国政府向马里政府提供贷款的协定。
    国务院副总理陈毅，马里代表团团长、马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、司法部长马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔，分别代表两国政府在协定上签字。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，有关方面负责人李应吉、刘希文等；马里方面有驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼，马里代表团团员乌马尔·利。
    签字以后，双方举杯热烈祝贺。（附图片）
