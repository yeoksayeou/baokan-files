Title: Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah's Visit to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-27
Edition: 4

On June 26, Crown Prince Birendra Shah of Nepal, invited by Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived in Beijing by special plane. The picture shows him being warmly welcomed by over 3,000 people from various circles in the capital at the airport.
