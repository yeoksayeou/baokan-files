Title: While Flying Over Iranian and Turkish Airspace En Route to Romania, Premier Zhou Cables Greetings to Iranian Prime Minister and Turkish Prime Minister Respectively
Author:
Date: 1966-06-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 16th. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent telegrams to Amir-Abbas Hoveida, Prime Minister of the Government of the Kingdom of Iran, and Süleyman Demirel, Prime Minister of the Republic of Turkey, respectively, on the 16th while flying over Iran and Turkey en route by special plane for a friendly visit to Romania. Premier Zhou also received their replies.
    In his telegram to the Iranian Prime Minister, Premier Zhou Enlai also asked him to convey his regards to the King, Queen, and Princess Ashraf, and wished the Iranian people happiness. Prime Minister Hoveida replied with thanks and wished Premier Zhou Enlai a smooth journey.
    In his telegram to Prime Minister Süleyman Demirel, Premier Zhou Enlai conveyed his respects to the President and Prime Minister of Turkey, and asked him to convey his best wishes to the Turkish people. Prime Minister Süleyman replied with thanks and extended his cordial greetings to Premier Zhou.

### 在赴罗马尼亚途中飞越伊土上空时  周总理分别致电伊朗首相和土耳其总理致意

1966-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    在赴罗马尼亚途中飞越伊土上空时
    周总理分别致电伊朗首相和土耳其总理致意
    新华社布加勒斯特十六日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，十六日在乘专机前往罗马尼亚进行友好访问的途中飞越伊朗和土耳其上空时，分别打电报给伊朗王国政府首相阿米尔·阿巴斯·胡韦达和土耳其共和国总理苏莱曼·德米雷尔致意。周总理并收到了他们的复电。
    周恩来总理在给伊朗首相的电报中，并请他代为问候国王、王后和阿什拉夫公主，祝伊朗人民幸福。胡韦达首相复电表示感谢，并祝周恩来总理一路顺风。
    周恩来总理在给苏莱曼·德米雷尔总理的电报中，向土耳其总统和总理致以敬意，并请向土耳其人民转致最良好的祝愿。苏莱曼总理复电表示感谢，并向周总理致以衷心的问候。
