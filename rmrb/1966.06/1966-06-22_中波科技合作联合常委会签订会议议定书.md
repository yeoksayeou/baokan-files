Title: Sino-Polish Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation Signs Meeting Protocol
Author:
Date: 1966-06-22
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 20th. The Sino-Polish Joint Standing Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation held its 13th session in Warsaw from June 7th to 20th and signed the protocol of the 13th session of the committee on the 20th. Signing the protocol on the Chinese side was Jia Linfang, acting chairman of the Chinese group of the committee and Vice Minister of the Coal Industry, and on the Polish side was An. Chekhovich, chairman of the Polish group of the committee and Vice Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Economic and Scientific and Technological Cooperation of the Polish Council of Ministers.
    Wang Guoquan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Ka. Olszewski, First Vice Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Economic and Scientific and Technological Cooperation of the Polish Council of Ministers, and all members of both delegations attended the signing ceremony.

### 中波科技合作联合常委会签订会议议定书

1966-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中波科技合作联合常委会签订会议议定书
    新华社华沙二十日电　中国波兰科学技术合作联合常任委员会于六月七日到二十日在华沙举行了第十三届会议，并且在二十日签订了委员会第十三届会议议定书。在议定书上签字的，中国方面是委员会中国组代理主席、煤炭工业部副部长贾林放，波兰方面是委员会波兰组主席、波兰部长会议对外经济和科学技术合作委员会副主席安·切霍维奇。
    中国驻波兰大使王国权、波兰部长会议对外经济和科学技术合作委员会第一副主席卡·奥尔谢夫斯基以及双方代表团全体成员出席了签字仪式。
