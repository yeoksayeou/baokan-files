Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Leaders of Vietnamese Acrobatic Troupe and Watches Performance
Author:
Date: 1966-06-20
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, June 19th. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received tonight Su Hai, the head of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Acrobatic Troupe, vice heads Wu Yuying and Fan Chunshu, and the main performers, who came to China for a performance tour. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

    Present at the reception were Yang Chun, Han Nianlong, Li Qi, Sun Xiaocun and other responsible persons from relevant departments. Also present were Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and Tran Van Thanh, head of the Permanent Mission in China of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front.

    Xinhua News Agency, June 19th. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi watched the performance of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Acrobatic Troupe tonight.

    The Vietnamese acrobatic troupe artists' wonderful performance once again won warm applause from the audience in the capital. When the performance reflected the heroic Vietnamese people's struggle to repel the US imperialist aggression against Vietnam, the audience shouted, "Down with US imperialism!" "US imperialism get out of Vietnam!"

    At the end of the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi went on stage and warmly shook hands with the performers, congratulating them on their successful performance.

    Also watching the performance tonight were Yang Chun, Han Nianlong, Li Qi, Sun Xiaocun and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

    Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and Tran Van Thanh, head of the Permanent Mission in China of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, also watched the performance.

### 陈毅副总理接见越南杂技团负责人并观看演出

1966-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见越南杂技团负责人并观看演出
    新华社十九日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天晚上接见来我国访问演出的越南民主共和国杂技团团长苏海、副团长吴玉英、范春书和主要演员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有杨纯、韩念龙、李琦、孙晓村等有关方面负责人。越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成也在座。
    新华社十九日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天晚上观看了越南民主共和国杂技团的演出。
    越南杂技团艺术家们的精采演出又一次博得了首都观众的热烈掌声。当演出反映英雄的越南人民反击美帝国主义侵略越南的斗争时，观众高呼“打倒美帝国主义！”“美帝国主义从越南滚出去！”
    演出结束时，陈毅副总理走上舞台和演员们热烈握手，祝贺他们演出成功。
    今晚观看演出的还有杨纯、韩念龙、李琦、孙晓村等有关方面负责人。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成也观看了演出。
