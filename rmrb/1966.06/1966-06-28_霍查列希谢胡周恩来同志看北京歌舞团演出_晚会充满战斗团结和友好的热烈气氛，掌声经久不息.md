Title: Comrade Zhou Enlai and Comrades Hoxha, List, and Shehu Attend Performance by Beijing Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1966-06-28
Edition: 1

The Beijing Song and Dance Troupe, which is visiting Albania as part of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, held a grand performance on the evening of June 26 at the National Opera and Ballet Theater in Tirana. The performance was organized by the Albanian Ministry of Education and Culture and the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. When Comrade Zhou Enlai and the leaders of the Albanian Party and State, Enver Hoxha, Haxhi Lleshi, and Mehmet Shehu, appeared in the box at the theater, the entire audience stood up, and shouts of "Enver-Mao Zedong" and thunderous applause lasted for a long time.

Those who attended the performance tonight included: Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party: Adil Çarçani, Beqir Balluku, Gogo Nushi, Haki Toska, Manush Myftiu, Ramiz Alia, Riadh Marmullaku, Spiro Koleka; Alternate Members of the Political Bureau: Kadri Hazbiu, Koço Theodhosi, Pilo Peristeri, Pirro Marko; Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, Muço Peza; Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the People's Assembly of Tirana, Alush Kelmendi; as well as members of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, members of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and members of the government. Also present were the Albanian Ambassador to China, Vasil Natanaeli, and the Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy, Wang Jinchuan.

During the evening, the Beijing Song and Dance Troupe's performance of the "Long March Suite" received a very warm welcome and praise, especially when the performers sang "Entering Zunyi" and "Great Reunion," a giant portrait of Comrade Mao Zedong appeared on the stage curtain, and the audience below erupted in thunderous applause. The tenor Jia Shijun's performance of "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helm" and the song "Song of Enver Hoxha" sung in Albanian, as well as the group dance "Beijing-Tirana" performed by Chinese actors in traditional costumes of both countries, all received enthusiastic applause from the audience. When the actors performed the dance "Beijing-Tirana," giant portraits of Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Hoxha appeared on the stage backdrop, and the entire audience erupted in thunderous applause, causing the performers to have to take multiple bows.

During the intermission, the director of the National Opera and Ballet Theater of Tirana, composer, and famous Albanian actor Muço Kranti congratulated the director of the Beijing Song and Dance Troupe on the success of the performance, saying: "Your program is a new turning point in proletarian revolutionary art. It is full of revolutionary spirit and high political thought. It is also very successful in terms of performance skills." The dye worker Z. Dakal of the Stalin Textile Combine told a Xinhua News Agency reporter: "This is the first time I have attended such a performance full of revolutionary passion. The programs performed tonight all reflect the fighting spirit of the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party of China in opposing imperialism, opposing revisionism, and building socialism. I particularly like the songs praising Mao Zedong and Enver that were performed tonight, because they are the voice of the Chinese people, the Albanian people, and the people of the whole world."

At the end of the evening, the Albanian leaders presented a basket of flowers to the Chinese artists. Hoxha, Shehu, and Zhou Enlai went on stage together and shook hands warmly with the performers. At this time, the stage and the audience joined in rhythmic applause, shouting "Enver-Mao Zedong." The evening was filled with a warm atmosphere of fighting unity and friendship from beginning to end.

### 霍查列希谢胡周恩来同志看北京歌舞团演出  晚会充满战斗团结和友好的热烈气氛，掌声经久不息

1966-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    霍查列希谢胡周恩来同志看北京歌舞团演出
    晚会充满战斗团结和友好的热烈气氛，掌声经久不息
    新华社地拉那二十六日电　随同中国党政代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚的中国北京歌舞团，二十六日晚在地拉那国家歌剧和芭蕾舞剧院举行盛大演出。演出是由阿尔巴尼亚教育和文化部以及对外文化联络委员会举办的。当周恩来同志和阿尔巴尼亚党和国家领导人恩·霍查、哈·列希、穆·谢胡等同志出现在剧院里的包厢时，全场起立，“恩维尔—毛泽东”的欢呼声和暴风雨般的掌声经久不息。
    观看今晚演出的还有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员：阿·查尔查尼、贝·巴卢库、果·努什、哈·托斯卡、马·穆夫蒂乌、拉·阿利雅、里·马尔科、斯·科列加，党中央政治局候补委员：卡·哈兹比乌、科·蒂奥多西、彼·杜米、皮·皮里斯特里，人民议会主席团副主席穆·佩扎，阿中友协主席、地拉那市人民会议执行委员会主席阿·凯莱齐以及劳动党中央委员、人民议会主席团委员和政府成员等。观看演出的还有阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦·纳塔奈利；还有中国大使馆临时代办王锦川。
    在晚会上，北京歌舞团演出的长征组歌《红军不怕远征难》受到了十分热烈的欢迎和赞扬，当演员们演唱《进遵义》、《大会师》时，舞台幕布上出现了光芒四射的毛泽东同志的巨幅画象，顿时，台下响起了雷鸣般的掌声。男高音贾世骏演唱的《大海航行靠舵手》和用阿尔巴尼亚话演唱的《恩维尔·霍查之歌》，以及中国演员穿着中阿两国民族服装表演的集体舞蹈《北京—地拉那》等节目都受到了观众的热烈欢迎。当演员们在演出《北京—地拉那》舞蹈当中，舞台天幕上出现了毛泽东同志和霍查同志的巨幅画象时，全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声，以致演员们不得不一再谢幕。
    在晚会中间休息时，地拉那国家歌剧和芭蕾舞剧院院长、作曲家、阿尔巴尼亚著名演员穆·克兰提亚向北京歌舞团团长祝贺演出成功，他说：“你们的节目是无产阶级革命艺术的一个新的转折点。它充满了革命精神和高度的政治思想性。在表演技巧上也是十分成功的”。斯大林纺织联合工厂染工佐·达卡莱对新华社记者说：“参加今晚这样充满革命激情的晚会对我来说还是生平头一次。晚会上演出的节目都体现了中国人民在毛主席和中国共产党领导下反对帝国主义、反对修正主义和建设社会主义的战斗精神。我特别喜欢今晚演出的歌颂毛泽东和恩维尔的歌曲，因为它们是中国人民、阿尔巴尼亚人民和全世界人民的心声。”
    晚会结束时，阿尔巴尼亚领导人向中国艺术家赠送了花篮。霍查、谢胡和周恩来等同志一起上台，和演员们热烈握手。这时，台上台下合着有节奏的掌声，高呼“恩维尔—毛泽东”。晚会自始至终充满着战斗团结和友好的热烈气氛。（附图片）
    霍查、列希、谢胡和周恩来等同志观看了北京歌舞团演出。演出结束后，霍查、谢胡等同志和周恩来同志一起上台同演员合影。　新华社记者　杜修贤摄（传真照片）
