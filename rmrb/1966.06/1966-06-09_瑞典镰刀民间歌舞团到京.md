Title: Swedish Sickle Folk Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Swedish Sickle Folk Dance Troupe, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to visit and perform in China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation was led by its leader, Boer Walker Bulmak.
    Greeting them at the airport were Song Changhua, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and others.
    Mr. Segerson, Cultural Attache of the Swedish Embassy in China, was also at the airport to welcome them.

### 瑞典镰刀民间歌舞团到京

1966-06-09
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞典镰刀民间歌舞团到京
    新华社八日讯　应中国人民对外文化友好协会邀请来我国访问演出的瑞典镰刀民间歌舞团一行三十人，在团长伯尔·沃克·布尔马克的率领下，于今日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有对外友协书记处书记宋常华等。
    瑞典驻中国大使馆文化随员谢格森也到机场欢迎。
