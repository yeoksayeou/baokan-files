Title: Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Secretariat Condemns US Arms Supply to Israel; US Imperialism is the Number One Enemy of the Arab People; Syrian Deputy Prime Minister Says Imperialist Schemes Against the Arabs Are Bound to Fail
Author:
Date: 1966-06-01
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 29th: The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association issued a statement on the 29th, strongly condemning the US government's latest supply of a large number of heavy weapons to "Israel." The statement said, "While providing weapons to 'Israel,' the United States, together with Britain, is plotting a reactionary 'Islamic Pact' in the Middle East to suppress the liberation movement in this region. This sharpens and deepens the contradictions between the interests of the Arab people and the interests of US imperialism and its lackeys, arousing the resolute opposition of the Arab people. US imperialism encourages 'Israel' to carry out new aggression on the one hand, and talks glibly about 'peace' on the other, supporting the solution of the Palestine issue with the so-called 'Tashkent spirit.' However, all these conspiracies have been exposed and severely condemned by the people of Arab countries." The statement said, "US imperialism is the number one enemy of the Arab people, and the struggle of the Arab people is part of the struggle of the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America against imperialism headed by the United States."
    The statement said, "The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association firmly believes that the Arab people, united for the cause of the liberation of Palestine and fighting together against imperialism, are invincible. The resolute struggle of the Arab people, as well as the struggles of the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, will surely accelerate the inevitable collapse of imperialist rule around the world. Final victory will surely belong to the Arab people."
    Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, May 28th: Syrian Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Ibrahim Makhus told reporters from the Algerian News Agency on the 28th that any imperialist schemes to use Israel against Arab countries will, as in the past, be doomed to failure.
    He said that the Arab people will spare no effort in their struggle against colonialism and Zionism.

### 亚非记协书记处谴责美国向以色列提供武器  美帝是阿拉伯人民的头号敌人  叙副总理说帝国主义反对阿拉伯的图谋必遭失败

1966-06-01
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处谴责美国向以色列提供武器
    美帝是阿拉伯人民的头号敌人
    叙副总理说帝国主义反对阿拉伯的图谋必遭失败
    据新华社二十九日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处二十九日发表声明，强烈谴责美国政府最近又一次向“以色列”提供大批重型武器。声明说，“美国在向‘以色列’提供武器的同时，同英国一道在中东策划反动的‘伊斯兰条约’，来镇压这个地区的解放运动。这就使阿拉伯人民的利益同美帝国主义及其走狗的利益之间的矛盾尖锐和深刻起来，激起阿拉伯人民的坚决反对。美帝国主义一面鼓励‘以色列’进行新的侵略，一面侈谈‘和平’，支持用所谓‘塔什干精神’解决巴勒斯坦问题。但是，所有这一切阴谋都遭到阿拉伯各国人民的揭露和严厉谴责。”声明说，“美帝国主义是阿拉伯人民的头号敌人，阿拉伯人民的斗争是亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲各国人民反对以美国为首的帝国主义的斗争的一部分。”
    声明说，“亚非新闻工作者协会书记处坚决相信，为巴勒斯坦的解放事业而团结起来和为反对帝国主义而共同斗争的阿拉伯人民，是不可战胜的。阿拉伯人民的坚决斗争以及亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲各国人民的斗争必将加速帝国主义的统治在世界各地的不可避免的垮台。最后胜利一定属于阿拉伯人民。”
    新华社阿尔及尔二十八日电　叙利亚副总理兼外交部长易卜拉欣·马胡斯二十八日向阿尔及利亚新闻社记者发表谈话说，帝国主义任何利用以色列来反对阿拉伯国家的图谋，必将同过去一样，遭到肯定的失败。
    他说，阿拉伯人民将不惜一切力量来进行反对殖民主义和犹太复国主义的斗争。
