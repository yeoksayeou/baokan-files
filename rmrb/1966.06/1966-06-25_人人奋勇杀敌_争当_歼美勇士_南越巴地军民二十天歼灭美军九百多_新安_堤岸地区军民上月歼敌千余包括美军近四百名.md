Title: Everyone Bravely Fights the Enemy, Striving to Become "American-Annihilating Heroes"; Ba Ria People's Armed Forces in South Vietnam Annihilate Over 900 American Troops in Twenty Days; Tay Ninh—Thu Dau Mot Region People's Armed Forces Annihilate Over 1,000 Enemy Troops Last Month, Including Nearly 400 American Troops
Author:
Date: 1966-06-25
Edition: 5

    HANOI, June 23 (Xinhua) – The South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency reports that the People's Armed Forces and civilians of Ba Ria Province, South Vietnam, once again crushed a large-scale "mopping-up" operation launched by the American troops and their accomplice troops from May 16 to June 4, achieving a major victory. According to incomplete statistics, a total of 1,020 enemy troops were annihilated, including 955 American aggressor troops; 15 enemy aircraft were shot down or destroyed, 6 military vehicles of various types were destroyed, and a large number of weapons and military supplies were seized.
    The report said that in order to establish a base for the Australian accomplice troops in the Cau Kieu and Tu Son areas along Highway 2, the American puppet clique dispatched the U.S. 173rd Airborne Brigade, in coordination with the Australian accomplice troops and a battalion of puppet troops, under the cover of an artillery battalion of the New Zealand accomplice troops and hundreds of aircraft and military vehicles, to launch a large-scale "mopping-up" operation in Long Phu Commune, Chau Duc District, Ba Ria Province, from May 16 to June 4. The People's Armed Forces and civilians of Ba Ria Province fully displayed the power of people's war, not only repelling the enemy's attacks, but also continuously and actively attacking the enemy, giving the American bandits an extremely heavy blow.
    In this counter-"mopping-up" campaign, the Liberation Armed Forces fought bravely and strived to become "American-Annihilating Heroes," producing many heroic figures and heroic units with outstanding achievements. According to statistics, a total of seventeen units and seventy-seven soldiers were awarded the glorious titles of "Heroic American-Annihilators" and "American-Annihilating Heroes" respectively.
    Local forces and guerrillas actively cooperated with the main forces, constantly attacking and harassing the enemy, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy.
    HANOI, June 24 (Xinhua) – The South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency reports that the Liberation Armed Forces and guerrillas in the South Vietnam Tay Ninh—Thu Dau Mot area frequently attacked the enemy in May, annihilating a total of 1,121 enemy troops, including 398 American aggressor troops. Under the heavy blows of the Liberation Armed Forces and guerrillas, 680 puppet soldiers deserted out of fear for their lives.
    In addition, the Liberation Armed Forces also destroyed the enemy's military sub-district command post, 16 strongholds and outposts, 4 puppet regime offices, and 6 barracks, destroyed a warehouse storing ten tons of ammunition, and destroyed or damaged 18 enemy military vehicles.
    In May, the Liberation Armed Forces and guerrillas in this area fought many brilliant battles. In the late night of the 26th to the early morning of the 27th, they simultaneously attacked and destroyed the Hiep Hoa stronghold and the various strongholds of the Duc Hue military sub-district command post, annihilating nearly 350 enemy troops in one fell swoop, including 14 American aggressor troops. The guerrillas of Duc Hoa District also crushed the aggressor troops' multiple "mopping-up" operations in May, eliminating 355 American troops.
    HANOI, June 23 (Xinhua) – The South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency reports that the Liberation Armed Forces and guerrillas of Gia Dinh and Quang Ngai provinces, South Vietnam, have recently continuously attacked and intercepted the enemy in the enemy's rear, annihilating over 600 enemy troops, including the complete annihilation of three platoons of puppet troops.
    On the night of May 21, the Gia Dinh Provincial Liberation Army launched a surprise attack on the puppet marine corps training school in Thu Duc District, annihilating 365 enemy troops on the spot, including all the commanders of the two battalions of this marine corps. At the same time, they destroyed an enemy ammunition depot.
    The guerrillas of Go Vap and Hoc Mon districts in this province also frequently attacked in the first ten days of June, killing 111 enemy troops, sinking two enemy landing craft, and destroying a bridge. At the same time, they destroyed three enemy outposts.
    From May 25 to June 7, the local forces and guerrillas of Binh Son, Son Tinh, and Mo Duc districts in Quang Ngai Province successively attacked the enemy's camps and intercepted the U.S. puppet troops who came to "mop up" five times, annihilating a total of 132 enemy troops, including 32 American aggressors, completely annihilating two platoons of puppet troops, and shooting down two enemy aircraft.
    HANOI, June 24 (Xinhua) – The South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency reports: The Liberation Armed Forces of Quang Ngai Province, South Vietnam, fought a brilliant battle of attacking strongholds and ambushing reinforcements south of Quang Ngai City on the late night of the 11th and the 12th. According to preliminary statistics, they annihilated a puppet battalion and a company, including 10 American "advisors," heavily damaged two battalions, destroyed five M-113 amphibious armored vehicles, damaged one, and shot down one enemy aircraft. ### 人人奋勇杀敌  争当“歼美勇士”  南越巴地军民二十天歼灭美军九百多  新安—堤岸地区军民上月歼敌千余包括美军近四百名

1966-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    人人奋勇杀敌  争当“歼美勇士”
    南越巴地军民二十天歼灭美军九百多
    新安—堤岸地区军民上月歼敌千余包括美军近四百名
    据新华社河内二十三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方巴地省军民在五月十六日到六月四日的二十天中，再一次粉碎了美军和帮凶军发动的一次大规模“扫荡”，取得重大胜利。据不完全统计，共歼灭敌军一千零二十名，其中包括美国侵略军九百五十五名；击落和击毁敌机十五架，击毁各种军车六辆，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    报道说，美伪集团为了在第二号公路沿线的求江、土山地区给澳大利亚帮凶军建立基地，于五月十六日到六月四日，出动美国第一七三空降旅，同澳大利亚帮凶军和一营伪军配合，在新西兰帮凶军的一个炮兵营和数以百计的飞机和军车的掩护下，向巴地省珠德县的隆福乡展开大规模“扫荡”。巴地省军民充分发挥人民战争的威力，不仅击退了敌人的各次进攻，而且还连续主动袭击敌人，给予美国强盗以极为沉重的打击。
    解放武装力量在这次反“扫荡”战斗中，人人奋勇杀敌，争当“歼美勇士”，出现了许多战绩卓著的英雄人物和英雄单位。据统计，共有十七个单位和七十七名战士分别获得“英勇歼美”和“歼美勇士”的光荣称号。
    地方部队和游击队积极配合主力部队，不断袭击和骚扰敌人，使敌人遭到惨重伤亡。
    新华社河内二十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方新安—堤岸地区解放武装力量和游击队，五月份频频出击敌人，共歼敌一千一百二十一名，其中包括美国侵略军三百九十八名。在解放武装力量和游击队的沉重打击下，六百八十名伪军因害怕丧命而逃跑。
    此外，解放武装力量还摧毁了敌人一个军事支区的指挥所、十六个据点和哨所、四个伪政权办事处和六座兵营，炸毁一座存放十吨弹药的仓库，击毁、击伤敌军车十八辆。
    五月份，这个地区的解放武装力量和游击队打了许多漂亮仗，他们在二十六日深夜到二十七日凌晨，同时袭击并摧毁了协和据点和德惠军事支区指挥所的各个据点，一举歼灭敌人近三百五十名，其中包括美国侵略军十四名。德和县游击队在五月份也粉碎了侵略军的多次“扫荡”，共消灭美军三百五十五名。
    新华社河内二十三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方嘉定、广义两省解放武装力量和游击队，最近连续在敌人后方袭击和截击敌人，共歼敌六百多名，其中包括全歼伪军三个排。
    嘉定省解放军在五月二十一日夜间，奇袭守德县伪军海军陆战队训练学校，当场歼灭敌人三百六十五名，其中包括这支陆战队两个营的全部指挥官。同时炸毁敌人的一座军火仓库。
    这个省鹅邑、霍门两县的游击队在六月份的头十天中也频频出击，共打死敌人一百一十一名，击沉敌人登陆船两艘，炸毁桥梁一座。同时还摧毁敌人三个哨所。
    从五月二十五日到六月七日，广义省平山、山晶、慕德等县的地方部队和游击队接连五次袭击敌人的营地和截击前来
  “扫荡”的美伪军，共歼灭敌人一百三十二名，其中包括美国侵略者三十二名，全歼伪军两个排，并且击落敌机两架。
    据新华社河内二十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广义省解放武装力量，十一日深夜和十二日，在广义市南面打了一次漂亮的攻点打援战斗。据初步统计，共歼灭伪军一个营和一个连，其中包括美国“顾问”十名，重创两个营，击毁M—113型两栖装甲车五辆，击伤一辆，击落敌机一架。（附图片）
