Title: A Japanese Guest Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency: Mr. Koichiro Fujiwara, a Japanese scientific worker, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the sixth, invited by the Science and Technology Association of the People's Republic of China to visit our country.

    Zhang Jin and other responsible persons from relevant departments went to the airport to greet him.

### 日本一客人到北京

1966-06-10
第5版()
专栏：

    日本一客人到北京
    新华社讯　应中华人民共和国科学技术协会邀请来我国访问的日本科学工作者藤原滉一郎，六日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的，有张进等有关方面负责人。
