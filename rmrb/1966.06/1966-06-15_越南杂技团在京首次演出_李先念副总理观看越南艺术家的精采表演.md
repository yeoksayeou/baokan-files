Title: Vietnamese Acrobatic Troupe Performs in Beijing for the First Time; Vice Premier Li Xiannian Watches the Vietnamese Artists' Wonderful Performance
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-15
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 14th. The acrobatic troupe from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, from the front lines of the anti-American struggle, gave its first performance at the Capital Theater tonight. The Vietnamese artists' performance not only brought wonderful acrobatic art to the Chinese people, but also brought the deep fighting friendship of the Vietnamese people.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian watched tonight's performance.
    The acrobatic troupe performed a total of fifteen programs tonight. The program "Happy Production and Ready to Fight at Any Time" showed the revolutionary optimism of the hardworking Vietnamese people. In the fields beside the fields of green gauze and sunflowers full of fruits, they intensified their martial arts training with hatred for the American robbers, ready to strike the American aggressors at any time. Twelve male and female Vietnamese artists performed splendid vehicle skills with vigor. When they raised a red flag with the ten characters "Long Live Chairman Mao, Long Live President Ho" and drove around the stage to salute the audience, thunderous applause erupted throughout the theater. Vietnamese artists also performed "? Bar Skills", "Shaking Board", "Taming Monkeys", and other programs. Each program was warmly welcomed by the audience.
    Responsible persons from relevant departments such as Zhang Xiruo, Ding Xilin, Yang Chun, Li Qi, Cao Keqiang, and Sun Xiaocun, as well as people from the literary and art circles of the capital, watched the performance.
    Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Tu Binh, Tran Van Thanh, head of the Permanent Mission of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, Pham Ngoc Thuan, head of the Vietnamese cultural delegation, and Vietnamese experts and students in Beijing were invited to watch the performance.
    Diplomatic envoys and cultural officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to watch the performance.
    After the performance, Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Ambassador Tran Tu Binh went onto the stage to shake hands cordially with the Vietnamese artists and congratulate them on their successful performance.

### 越南杂技团在京首次演出  李先念副总理观看越南艺术家的精采表演

1966-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    越南杂技团在京首次演出
    李先念副总理观看越南艺术家的精采表演
    新华社十四日讯　来自反美斗争前线的越南民主共和国杂技团，今晚在首都剧场举行首次演出。越南艺术家的演出不仅给中国人民带来了精采的杂技艺术，而且带来了越南人民深厚的战斗友谊。
    李先念副总理观看了今晚的演出。
    杂技团今晚共演出十五个节目。在《愉快的生产和随时准备战斗》节目中，表现了勤劳的越南人民革命的乐观主义精神，他们在果实累累的青纱帐和向日葵旁的田野里，怀着对美国强盗的仇恨，加紧练武，随时准备痛击美国侵略者。十二个男女越南艺术家精神焕发地表演了精采的车技以后，高举写有“毛主席万岁，胡主席万岁”十个大字的红旗，驱车环绕舞台向观众致意时，全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声。越南艺术家们还表演了《?杠技巧》、《晃板》、《驯猴》等，每一个节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    观看演出的还有张奚若、丁西林、杨纯、李琦、曹克强、孙晓村等有关方面负责人和首都文艺界人士。
    越南驻中国大使陈子平，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成，越南文化代表团团长范玉纯，在北京的越南专家、留学生等应邀观看了演出。
    应邀观看演出的。还有各国驻中国使节和文化官员。
    演出结束后，李先念副总理等和陈子平大使走上舞台，同越南艺术家亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功。
