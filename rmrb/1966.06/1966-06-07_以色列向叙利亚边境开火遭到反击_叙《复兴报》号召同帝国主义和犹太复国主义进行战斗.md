Title: Israel Opens Fire on Syrian Border, Repelled; Syrian Al-Ba'ath Newspaper Calls for Struggle Against Imperialism and Zionism
Author:
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, June 6th: A Syrian military spokesperson announced on the morning of June 6th that on June 5th, Israel used machine guns, heavy weapons, and tanks to open fire on the Syrian border. Syria retaliated, killing one Israeli.

    Al-Ba'ath newspaper said on June 6th that the war to defend national dignity, state sovereignty, and the rights of the people against aggression is just and legitimate. The newspaper said that the people must be mobilized to fight against imperialism, reactionaries, and Zionism.

### 以色列向叙利亚边境开火遭到反击  叙《复兴报》号召同帝国主义和犹太复国主义进行战斗

1966-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    以色列向叙利亚边境开火遭到反击
    叙《复兴报》号召同帝国主义和犹太复国主义进行战斗
    新华社大马士革六日电　叙利亚军方发言人六日上午宣布，以色列五日用机枪、重武器和坦克向叙利亚边境开火。叙利亚进行了还击，打死一名以色列人。
    《复兴报》六日说，保卫民族尊严、国家主权和人民权利的反侵略战争是正义和合法的。报纸说，必须动员人民同帝国主义、反动派和犹太复国主义进行战斗。
