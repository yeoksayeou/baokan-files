Title: China Political Science and Law Society Hosts Banquet for Arab Lawyers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-06-20
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, June 19th. The China Political Science and Law Society held a banquet this evening to welcome the Arab Lawyers Union delegation headed by Mustafa Baradie.
    Wang Jiren, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Society, and Baradie, head of the delegation, spoke at the banquet.
    Present at the banquet as guests were Xing Yimin, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court; Sha Mengbi, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association; Wang Chuanbin, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity; Yang Huanan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the China Political Science and Law Society, and others.
    This delegation was invited by the China Political Science and Law Society to visit our country and arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

### 我政法学会欢宴阿拉伯律师代表团

1966-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

    我政法学会欢宴阿拉伯律师代表团
    新华社十九日讯  中国政治法律学会今天晚上举行宴会欢迎以穆斯塔法·巴拉迪为首的阿拉伯律师联合会代表团。
    中国政治法律学会副会长王吉仁和代表团团长巴拉迪在宴会上讲了话。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的，有最高人民法院副院长邢亦民，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任沙梦弼，中国亚非团结委员会副主席王传斌，中国政治法律学会书记处书记杨化南等。
    这个代表团是应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的，在今天早上乘飞机到达北京的。
