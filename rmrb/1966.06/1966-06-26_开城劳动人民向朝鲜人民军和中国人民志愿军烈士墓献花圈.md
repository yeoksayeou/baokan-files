Title: Kaesong Working People Lay Wreaths at the Tombs of Korean People's Army and Chinese People's Volunteer Army Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 25th (Telegram) – According to the Korean Central News Agency, on June 25th (the 16th anniversary of the Korean People's Fatherland Liberation War), the working people of Kaesong laid wreaths at the tombs of the Korean People's Army and Chinese People's Volunteer Army martyrs at the foot of Songak Mountain in Kaesong.
    Joining the working people of Kaesong in laying wreaths were Major General Park Chung-kuk, Chief Commissioner of the Korean-Chinese side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, other commissioners, and responsible persons from the Kaesong Party and government organs and public organizations.

### 开城劳动人民向朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军烈士墓献花圈

1966-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    开城劳动人民向朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军烈士墓献花圈
    新华社平壤二十五日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，开城劳动人民在二十五日（朝鲜人民祖国解放战争十六周年），向开城松岳山麓下的朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军烈士墓献了花圈。
    同开城劳动人民一道前往献花圈的有：朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员朴重国少将和其他委员，以及开城党、政机关和公众团体的负责人。
