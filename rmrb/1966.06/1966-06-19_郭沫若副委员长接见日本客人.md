Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-06-19
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th -- Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Honorary President of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, received Professor Tokutaro Hirone, Director of the Institute for Materials Research of Tohoku University, Japan, this afternoon, who came to China at the invitation of the Beijing Iron and Steel Institute for a study tour and lectures. They had a cordial and friendly conversation. Li Zhennan and others were present at the meeting.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-06-19
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社十七日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中日友好协会名誉会长郭沫若，今天下午接见应北京钢铁学院邀请来我国参观访问和讲学的日本东北大学金属材料研究所所长广根德太郎教授，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有李振南等。
