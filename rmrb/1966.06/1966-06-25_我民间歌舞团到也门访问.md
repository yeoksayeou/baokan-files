Title: Our Folk Song and Dance Troupe Visits Yemen

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-25
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Sana: The Chinese Folk Song and Dance Troupe, led by Yu Wenhuai, arrived here by plane on the 21st at the invitation of the Yemeni government for a friendly visit to Yemen. The song and dance troupe was welcomed by Yemeni officials, including the Deputy Minister of Information, Abdullah Sur. Li Qiangfen, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy, and Chinese experts in Yemen also welcomed them at the airport.

### 我民间歌舞团到也门访问

1966-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我民间歌舞团到也门访问
    据新华社萨那电　由禹文淮率领的中国民间歌舞团应也门政府的邀请，二十一日乘飞机到达这里，对也门进行友好访问。歌舞团受到新闻部副部长阿卜杜拉·苏尔等也门官员的欢迎。中国大使馆参赞李强奋和中国在也门的专家也到机场欢迎。
