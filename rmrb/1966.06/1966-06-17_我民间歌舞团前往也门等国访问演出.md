Title: Our Folk Song and Dance Troupe Leaves for Performance Tours in Yemen and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1966-06-17
Edition: 4

    Our folk song and dance troupe, consisting of 49 members led by the head of the troupe, Yu Wenhuan, left Beijing on the 15th to visit and perform in Yemen, Zambia, and Syria. Yang Chun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and responsible persons from relevant departments, including Li Wei and Cao Shengong, were at the airport to see them off.
    Also at the airport to see them off was Farah, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Syrian Embassy in China.

### 我民间歌舞团前往也门等国访问演出

1966-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我民间歌舞团前往也门等国访问演出
    新华社十六日讯　中国民间歌舞团一行四十九人，在团长禹文淮率领下十五日离开北京前往也门、赞比亚、叙利亚访问和演出。前往机场送行的有对外文委副主任杨纯及有关方面负责人李伟、曹胜功等。
    到机场送行的，还有叙利亚驻华使馆临时代办法拉赫。
