Title: China and Yugoslavia Sign This Year's Trade Protocol
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    China and Yugoslavia Sign This Year's Trade Protocol
    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 10th – China and Yugoslavia signed the 1966 trade protocol in Belgrade on the 10th.
    Signing the protocol on behalf of both sides were: Kang Maozhao, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia, and Sejčić, Director of the Socialist Countries Department of the Federal Secretariat for Foreign Trade of Yugoslavia.

### 中南签订今年换货议定书

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中南签订今年换货议定书
    新华社贝尔格莱德十日电　中国和南斯拉夫一九六六年换货议定书十日在贝尔格莱德签字。
    代表双方在议定书上签字的是：中国驻南斯拉夫大使馆临时代办康矛召和南斯拉夫联邦对外贸易秘书处社会主义国家司司长塞伊契奇。
