Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Holds Talks with Head of Delegation Keita
Author:
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 6th – Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, held talks this morning in a friendly atmosphere with Mamadou Madeira Keita, head of the Malian delegation, member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union-African Democratic Rally of Mali, and Minister of Justice of Mali.

    Participating in the talks on the Chinese side were: Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Daohan, Vice Chairman of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission; Liu Xiwu, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade, and others.

    Participating in the talks on the Malian side were: Oumar Ly, member of the Malian delegation and member of the National Planning and Economic Leadership Committee of Mali; and Gisse Tidiani, Malian Ambassador to China.

    Prior to the talks, Vice Premier Chen Yi received Minister Mamadou Madeira Keita and members of the Malian delegation. (Attached picture)
    June 6th, Vice Premier Chen Yi received Mamadou Madeira Keita (fourth from the left), member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union-African Democratic Rally of Mali and Minister of Justice, and the Malian delegation he led.
　　　
　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Jingde

### 陈毅副总理同凯塔团长会谈

1966-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理同凯塔团长会谈
    新华社六日讯　国务院副总理陈毅同马里代表团团长、马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、司法部长马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔，今天上午在友好的气氛中举行了会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外经委副主任汪道涵，对外贸易部部长助理刘希文等。
    马里方面参加会谈的，有马里代表团团员、马里全国计划和经济领导委员会委员乌马尔·利，马里驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼。
    会谈以前，陈毅副总理接见了马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔部长和马里代表团的成员。（附图片）
    六月六日，陈毅副总理接见马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、司法部长马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔（左四）和由他率领的马里代表团。　　　
　　新华社记者　王敬德摄
