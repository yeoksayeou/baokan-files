Title: Chinese Red Cross Delegation Arrives in Tanzania
Author:
Date: 1966-06-02
Edition: 5

    New China News Agency, Dar es Salaam, 29th – The Chinese Red Cross delegation, led by Ni Peijun, arrived here on the 28th for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Tanzanian Red Cross. Secretary-General Markja and other leaders of the Tanzanian Red Cross welcomed the delegation at the airport.
    He Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Tanzania, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 我红十字会代表团到达坦桑尼亚

1966-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    我红十字会代表团到达坦桑尼亚
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆二十九日电　由倪裴君率领的中国红十字会代表团应坦桑尼亚红十字会的邀请二十八日到达这里进行友好访问。坦桑尼亚红十字会秘书长马克贾和其他领导人到机场欢迎代表团。
    中国驻坦桑尼亚大使何英也到机场欢迎。
