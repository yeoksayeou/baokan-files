    Humphrey's Lament
    U.S. Vice President Humphrey, in a speech at the U.S. Military Academy, said that a "concrete danger" existing in Asia is: "The doctrines of Marx and Lenin are being put forward as an explanation of Asia's past, as a plan of action for Asia's present, and as a blueprint for Asia's future." Humphrey here revealed the unparalleled fear of U.S. imperialism regarding the enormous influence of Marxism-Leninism in Asia.
    Comrade Mao Zedong said: "There are thousands of truths in Marxism, but in the final analysis, it is one sentence: it is right to rebel." He also pointed out: "Based on this principle, then we resist, then we struggle, then we engage in socialism." In today's Asia, Marxism-Leninism is being grasped by more and more of the masses of people and achieving greater and greater victories. Therefore, there is resistance, struggle, and rebellion everywhere. This is what U.S. imperialism and reactionaries of all countries fear most. Humphrey exclaimed helplessly: "Asia means revolution." He got this right.
    The direction of the Asian revolution is to drive imperialism out of Asia, to establish a completely liberated new Asia without imperialism. The Chinese people have already won the victory of the revolution, and the South Vietnamese people are advancing in the direction of victory. But Humphrey said that "mainland China saw the sad ending of an Asian revolution gone astray," and that "the Indochinese peninsula saw another sad ending of an Asian revolution gone astray." Such futile lamentation can only show that U.S. imperialism wants to reverse the direction of the Asian revolution but cannot. The likes of Humphrey will see one "sad ending of an Asian revolution gone astray" after another. The tragedy of U.S. imperialism lies precisely in this.

### 汉弗莱的哀叹

1966-06-09
第6版()
专栏：短评

    汉弗莱的哀叹
    美国副总统汉弗莱在美国军事学院发表讲话说：在亚洲存在的一个“具体危险”就是：“马克思和列宁的学说被提出来作为对亚洲的过去的解释，作为亚洲现在的行动计划，作为亚洲未来的蓝图。”汉弗莱在这里流露出了美帝国主义对于马克思列宁主义在亚洲的巨大影响的无比恐惧。
    毛泽东同志说过：“马克思主义的道理千条万绪，归根结底，就是一句话：造反有理。”他还指出：“根据这个道理，于是就反抗，就斗争，就干社会主义。”在今天的亚洲，马克思列宁主义正在为越来越多的人民群众所掌握，取得了越来越大的胜利。因此，到处都在反抗，在斗争，在造反。这就是美帝国主义和各国反动派所最害怕的。汉弗莱无可奈何地惊呼：“亚洲意味着革命”。他的这句话算是说对了。
    亚洲革命的方向，就是要把帝国主义从亚洲赶出去，就是要建立一个没有帝国主义的彻底解放的新亚洲。中国人民已经取得了革命的胜利，南越人民正在向着胜利的方向前进。而汉弗莱却说什么“在大陆中国看到一场迷失方向的亚洲革命的可悲结局”，“在印度支那半岛看到了另一场迷失方向的亚洲革命的可悲结局。”这种徒然的哀叹，只能表明，美帝国主义想要扭转亚洲革命的方向而不可得。汉弗莱之流将会看到一场又一场类似这样的“迷失方向的亚洲革命的可悲结局”。美帝国主义的可悲之处也正在这里。
