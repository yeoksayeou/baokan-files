Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Personnel of My Embassy
Author:
Date: 1966-06-28
Edition: 2

    Premier Zhou Enlai met with personnel of my embassy and others

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 26

    Vice Chairman of the Communist Party of China Central Committee and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai met with all staff of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Chinese experts working in Albania, Chinese staff of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company, and Chinese students studying in Albania in the evening of June 26 at the Chinese Embassy in Albania.

    After the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai took a group photo with them.

### 周恩来同志接见我使馆人员等

1966-06-28
第2版()
专栏：

    周恩来同志接见我使馆人员等
    新华社地拉那二十六日电　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来二十六日晚间在中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆接见了大使馆全体工作人员、在阿尔巴尼亚工作的中国专家、中阿轮船股份公司的中国工作人员和在阿尔巴尼亚学习的中国留学生。
    接见后，周恩来同志和他们一起照了相。
