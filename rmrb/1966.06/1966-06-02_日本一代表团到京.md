Title: Japanese Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-02
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Artists Association, a seven-member Japanese Kyoto Arts and Crafts Artists Delegation, led by head Asami Ryuzo, arrived in Beijing today. Liu Kaiqu and others went to the airport to welcome them.

**Named Entities**

中国人民对外文化友好协会     Zhongguo Renmin Duiwai Wenhua Youhao Xiehui (Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries)
中国美术家协会     Zhongguo Meishujia Xiehui (Chinese Artists Association)
日本京都工艺美术家代表团  Ribeng Jingdu Gongyi Meishujia Daibiaotuan (Japanese Kyoto Arts and Crafts Artists Delegation)
浅见隆三     Asami Ryuzo
刘开渠     Liu Kaiqu
北京     Beijing
新华社     Xinhua News Agency

### 日本一代表团到京

1966-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    日本一代表团到京
    新华社三十日讯　应中国人民对外文化友好协会和中国美术家协会邀请，日本京都工艺美术家代表团一行七人在团长浅见隆三率领下，今日抵京。刘开渠等前往机场欢迎。
