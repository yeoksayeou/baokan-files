Title: Azanian Friends Say Mao Zedong Thought is an Invaluable Asset to Freedom Fighters; Influence and Prestige of Mao Zedong Thought Will Remain Forever Young
Author:
Date: 1966-06-09
Edition: 5

    New China News Agency, Dar es Salaam, 7th -- Nkoho, General Treasurer of the Pan-Africanist Congress of Azania (South Africa), and Peter Raboroko, Education Secretary, issued a joint statement here to Xinhua News Agency reporters on the 7th, saying: "Chairman Mao's thought is an inexhaustible source of inspiration to the oppressed people of the world." "Chairman Mao's thought is a significant contribution to the literature of the struggle for human liberation and is an invaluable asset to all freedom fighters."
    The statement said: "The application of Chairman Mao's thought has enabled 650 million Chinese people not only to break the shackles of imperialism and old and new colonialism to achieve liberation, but also to take giant strides in establishing a socialist society in the People's Republic of China, a fact which in itself fully proves the inherent power of Chairman Mao's thought."
    The statement said: "Chairman Mao's thought is clear and sharp and has been grasped by those who are willing and able to use it effectively. It is a powerful weapon against oppression and oppressors." The statement said that Mao Zedong Thought has made unparalleled contributions in political and military strategy. The influence and prestige of Chairman Mao's thought will surely remain forever young.

### 阿扎尼亚朋友说毛泽东思想是自由战士非常宝贵的财富  毛泽东思想影响和威望将永葆青春

1966-06-09
第5版()
专栏：

    阿扎尼亚朋友说毛泽东思想是自由战士非常宝贵的财富
    毛泽东思想影响和威望将永葆青春
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆七日电　阿扎尼亚（南非）泛非主义者大会总司库恩科博和教育书记彼得·拉波罗库七日在这里向新华社记者发表联合声明说：“毛主席思想是鼓舞世界被压迫人民的取之不尽的源泉。”“毛主席思想是对争取人类解放斗争的文献的重大贡献，是所有自由战士的非常宝贵的财富。”
    声明说：“毛主席思想的运用使六亿五千万中国人不仅打破了帝国主义和新老殖民主义的枷锁而获得解放，并且在中华人民共和国建立社会主义社会方面迈出了巨人的步伐，这一事实本身就充分证明了毛主席思想固有的威力。”
    声明说：“毛主席思想是明确而锐利的，已经为愿意和能够有效地使用它的人们所掌握。它是反对压迫和压迫者的有力武器。”声明说，毛泽东思想在政治和军事战略方面作出了无比的贡献。毛主席思想的影响和威望定将永葆青春。
