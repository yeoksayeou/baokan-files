Title: Eighteen Poems of Chairman Mao Translated into Arabic and Published in Syria
Author:
Date: 1966-06-21
Edition: 4

　　Mao Zedong’s Eighteen Poems
　　Translated into Arabic and Published in Syria
　　Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 19th—The Arabic translation of Chairman Mao Zedong's eighteen poems has recently been published in Syria.
　　In the preface, the translator Dr. Mamdouh Haqqi said: "The author of the collection of poems is China's highest political leader, who liberated China from feudalism, imperialist aggression, and exploitation. Mao Zedong is the greatest poet of contemporary China."
　　The translator pointed out: "Chairman Mao's poems have been translated into many languages. Left-wing newspapers praise it, while right-wing newspapers treat it with great fear."
　　The translator said: "When I heard the Chinese reciting these poems in person, the cadence and rhythm made me understand its musical and artistic value."
　　He said: "Syria and China have a long history of connection. The publication of Chairman Mao's poems adds a cornerstone to the cultural exchange between the two countries."
　　This translation is based on the English and French translations of Chairman Mao's poems. (Attached picture)
　　The military and civilians in the Laotian liberated areas eagerly hope to learn the truth from Chairman Mao's writings and use Mao Zedong Thought to guide the revolutionary practice in Laos. The picture shows cadres of the Lao People's Liberation Army diligently reading Chairman Mao's writings at night.
　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Xue Tiejun

### 毛主席诗词十八首  译成阿拉伯文在叙利亚出版

1966-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    毛主席诗词十八首
    译成阿拉伯文在叙利亚出版
    新华社大马士革十九日电　毛泽东主席诗词十八首阿拉伯文译本最近在叙利亚出版。
    译者马姆杜·哈基博士在前言中说：“诗集的作者是中国最高的政治领袖，他使中国摆脱封建主义、帝国主义的侵略和剥削。毛泽东是当代中国最伟大的诗人。”
    译者指出：“毛主席诗词已经译成多种文字，左派报刊赞赏它，右派报刊以十分恐惧的心情对待它。”
    译者说：“当我听到中国人亲口诵读这些诗词的时候，音韵铿锵，使我了解到它的音乐、艺术价值。”
    他说：“叙利亚和中国的联系有很悠久的历史，毛主席诗词的出版给两国文化交流增加了一块基石”。
    这个译本是根据毛主席诗词英文和法文译本转译的。（附图片）
