Vice Premier He Long Meets Tanzanian Guests
Tanzanian Football Team Plays Friendly Match with Chinese Team

According to Xinhua News Agency, June 28th: The Tanzanian national football team played their first friendly match in China tonight at the Workers' Stadium in Beijing, receiving a warm welcome from over 60,000 spectators in the capital. The result of the match was a 10-4 victory for the Chinese team.

Vice Premier He Long watched tonight's match. During the grand welcoming ceremony held before the match, Vice Premier He Long met with the visiting team's leader, the Vice Chairman of the Tanzanian Football Association and the Tanzanian Minister of Rural Development and National Cultural Affairs, Love J. Mwangosy, and walked down to the stands to shake hands with the athletes of both teams and took a group photo with them.

The match was conducted in a friendly atmosphere. The excellent skills and tenacious spirit displayed by the athletes of both teams during the match earned them repeated rounds of enthusiastic applause and cheers from the spectators.

Vice Minister of the State Sports Commission, Rong Gao Tan and Li Meng Hua, and the Tanzanian Chargé d'Affaires to China, Ali Mwinyikoko, also watched the match tonight.

According to Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: Vice Director of the International Department of the State Sports Commission, Guo Lei, held a reception tonight, warmly welcoming the Vice Chairman of the Tanzanian Football Association and the Tanzanian Minister of Rural Development and National Cultural Affairs, Love J. Mwangosy, and the entire national football team led by him.

At the reception filled with friendly sentiments, the hosts and guests spoke, wishing for the further development of friendship between the peoples and athletes of China and Tanzania.

The Vice Chairman of the Chinese Football Association, Li Feng Lou, and Chinese football athletes attended the reception.

The Tanzanian Chargé d'Affaires to China, Ali Mwinyikoko, was also invited to the reception.

On June 28th, the Tanzanian national football team and the Chinese football team held their first friendly match. During the grand welcoming ceremony held before the match, Vice Premier He Long met with the visiting team's leader and athletes.  Xinhua News Agency photographer Zhang Bin.

### 贺龙副总理接见坦桑尼亚客人  坦桑足球队同我足球队进行友谊赛

1966-06-29
第5版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见坦桑尼亚客人
    坦桑足球队同我足球队进行友谊赛
    据新华社二十八日讯　坦桑尼亚国家足球队今晚在北京工人体育场同中国足球队进行访华首场友谊比赛，受到了首都六万多观众的热烈欢迎。比赛结果，中国队以十比四获胜。
    贺龙副总理观看了今晚的比赛。在比赛前举行的隆重的欢迎仪式上，贺龙副总理接见了客队领队坦桑尼亚足球协会副主席、坦桑尼亚乡村发展和国民文化部福利事业负责人爱·约·姆旺戈西，并且走下看台同双方运动员一一握手并在一起照了相。
    比赛是在友好的气氛中进行的。双方运动员在比赛中所表现的良好的球艺和顽强的风格，博得了观众一次又一次的热烈掌声和喝采声。
    国家体委副主任荣高棠和李梦华，坦桑尼亚驻我国大使馆临时代办阿里·姆维尼可可，今晚也观看了比赛。
    新华社二十七日讯　国家体委国际司副司长郭雷今晚举行招待会，热烈欢迎坦桑尼亚足球协会副主席、坦桑尼亚乡村发展和国民文化部福利事业负责人爱·约·姆旺戈西和由他率领的国家足球队全体成员。
    在充满友好情谊的招待会上，宾主讲了话，祝愿中、坦两国人民和运动员之间的友谊得到新的发展。
    中国足球协会副主席李凤楼和我国足球运动员们出席了招待会。
    坦桑尼亚驻我国大使馆临时代办阿里·姆维尼可可也应邀出席了招待会。（附图片）
    六月二十八日，坦桑尼亚国家足球队和中国足球队进行首次友谊比赛，在比赛前举行的隆重欢迎仪式上，贺龙副总理接见客队领队和运动员。　新华社记者　张彬摄
