Title: Our Delegation Lays Wreath at Tirana Martyrs' Cemetery
Author:
Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 24th -- Comrade Zhou Enlai, Head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Premier of the State Council, accompanied by Comrade Mehmet Shehu, Member of the Political Bureau of the Albanian Labor Party Central Committee and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, laid a wreath at the Tirana Martyrs' Cemetery on the morning of the 24th. The ribbon on the wreath read: "Eternal Glory to the Albanian Revolutionary Martyrs!"
    Zhao Yimin and Qiao Guanhua, members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, accompanied Comrade Zhou Enlai in laying the wreath.
    Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, also accompanied Comrade Zhou Enlai in laying the wreath.
    Albanian party and government leaders present at the wreath-laying ceremony included: Adil Çarçani, Maqo Muftiu, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Tirana People's Assembly, Abdyl Këllezi, Minister of Foreign Affairs Nesti Nase, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army D. Boçi, and others. Also present was Albanian Ambassador to China, V. Nathanaili. (Attached photo)
    On the afternoon of June 24th, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Premier of the State Council, paid a visit to the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and the Council of Ministers. During the visit, the hosts and guests engaged in sincere, cordial, and friendly talks. From left: Comrade Shehu, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Comrade Hoxha, and Comrade Lleshi.
                    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian (Fax Photo)

### 我代表团向地拉那烈士墓献花圈

1966-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

    我代表团向地拉那烈士墓献花圈
    新华社地拉那二十四日电　中国党政代表团团长、中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来同志，二十四日上午在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席穆·谢胡同志的陪同下，向地拉那烈士陵墓献花圈。花圈的缎带上写着：“阿尔巴尼亚革命烈士永垂不朽！”
    中国党政代表团团员赵毅敏和乔冠华陪同周恩来同志献了花圈。
    陪同周恩来同志献花圈的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川。
    献花圈时阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人在场的有：阿·查尔查尼、马·穆夫蒂乌，地拉那市人民会议执行委员会主席阿·凯莱齐，外交部长奈·纳赛，阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部副主任迪·波奇等。在场的还有阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦·纳塔奈利。（附图片）
    六月二十四日下午，中国党政代表团团长、中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来同志拜会了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央、人民议会主席团和部长会议。拜会时，宾主进行了十分真挚、亲切和友好的谈话。左起：谢胡同志、周恩来同志、霍查同志和列希同志。
                    新华社记者　杜修贤摄（传真照片）
