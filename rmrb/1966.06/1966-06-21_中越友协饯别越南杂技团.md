Title: Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association Fetes Vietnamese Acrobatic Troupe

Author:
Date: 1966-06-21
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th June—The Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association held a banquet tonight to bid farewell to the acrobatic troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Su Hai.
    Attending the banquet were Sun Xiaocun, Vice-President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, Yang Chun, Li Qi, Cao Keqiang, responsible persons of relevant departments, Guo Zhaoxiang, Deputy Director of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship People's Commune, and figures from the capital's literary and artistic circles.
    Hoang Bac, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and Nguyen Minh Phuong, Deputy Head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, were invited to attend the banquet.
    Sun Xiaocun said at the banquet that the programs you performed reflected the heroic Vietnamese people's determination to defeat US imperialism, and have been highly appraised and praised by the people of the capital. This performance tour has made a new contribution to further enhancing the militant friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people.
    In his speech, Su Hai expressed his heartfelt thanks to Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, and people from all walks of life in Beijing for watching their performances. He said that their visits to the clay sculpture exhibition "Rent Collection Courtyard" and other places had left them with a deep impression. He wished the militant friendship between the Vietnamese and Chinese people would last forever.
    At the banquet, Chinese and Vietnamese literary and art workers sang songs such as "The People of Asia, Africa, and Latin America Want Liberation," "The Vietnamese People Fight Well," and "Liberate South Vietnam."

### 中越友协饯别越南杂技团

1966-06-21
第5版()
专栏：

    中越友协饯别越南杂技团
    新华社二十日讯　中越友好协会今晚举行宴会，欢送由苏海率领的越南民主共和国杂技团。
    出席宴会的，有中越友好协会副会长孙晓村，有关方面负责人杨纯、李琦、曹克强，以及中越友好人民公社副主任郭兆祥和首都文艺界人士。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞黄北，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团副团长阮明芳应邀出席了宴会。
    孙晓村在宴会上说，你们演出的节目，反映了英雄的越南人民打败美帝国主义的决心，受到了首都人民高度的评价和赞扬。这次访问演出对进一步增进中越人民的战斗友谊，作出了新的贡献。
    苏海在讲话中衷心感谢董必武副主席、陈毅、李先念副总理等领导人和北京各界人民观看他们的演出。他说，他们参观了泥塑《收租院》展览以及其它地方，给他们留下了深刻的印象。他祝越中人民的战斗友谊万古长青。
    宴会上中越文艺工作者演唱了《亚非拉人民要解放》、《越南人民打得好》、《解放越南南方》等歌曲。
