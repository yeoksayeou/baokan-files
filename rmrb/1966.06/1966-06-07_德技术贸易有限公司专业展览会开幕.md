Title: German Technical Trading Co., Ltd. Professional Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 6th – A professional exhibition of the German Democratic Republic's Technical Trading Co., Ltd. opened this morning at the Beijing Exhibition Hall.
    Attending the opening ceremony on the Chinese side were Xiao Fangzhou, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and relevant responsible persons Gao Shirong, Chen Boqing, etc.; on the German Democratic Republic side was the German Ambassador to China, Birbach, etc.
    At the opening ceremony, Eckloff, Commercial Counselor of the German Embassy in China, Schumann, head of the exhibition delegation, and Yang Guoguang, Minister of the Exhibition Department of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, spoke successively, wishing that this exhibition would further enhance the friendship between the people of China and Germany and the trade relations between the two countries.
    After the opening ceremony, Head of Delegation Schumann held a reception. On the evening of the 5th, Xiao Fangzhou held a banquet to entertain the German guests.

### 德技术贸易有限公司专业展览会开幕

1966-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    德技术贸易有限公司专业展览会开幕
    新华社六日讯　德意志民主共和国技术贸易有限公司专业展览会今天上午在北京展览馆举行开幕式。
    出席开幕式的，中国方面有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席萧方洲和有关方面负责人高仕融、陈伯清等；德意志民主共和国方面有德驻华大使比尔巴赫等。
    在开幕式上，德驻华使馆商务参赞艾克罗夫、展览团团长舒曼以及中国国际贸易促进委员会来华展览部部长杨国光先后讲了话，他们祝愿这次展出进一步增进中德两国人民之间的友谊和两国的贸易关系。
    开幕式以后，舒曼团长举行招待会。五日晚，萧方洲曾举行宴会招待德国客人。
