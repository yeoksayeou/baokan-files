Title: China and Somalia Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Mogadishu, June 11th - Chinese Ambassador to Somalia, Yang Shouzheng, and Somali Minister of Education, Kenadid Ahmed Yusuf, signed the 1966 implementation plan for the China-Somalia Cultural Cooperation Agreement here this morning on behalf of their respective governments.
    After the signing, the hosts and guests had a cordial and friendly conversation and expressed their common desire to continuously strengthen cultural relations between the two countries.

### 中索签订文化合作协定执行计划

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中索签订文化合作协定执行计划
    新华社摩加迪沙十一日电　中国驻索马里大使杨守正和索马里教育部长克奈迪·艾哈迈德·优素福十一日上午在这里分别代表本国政府在中国—索马里文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划上签了字。
    在签字以后，宾主双方进行了亲切友好的谈话，并且表示了不断加强两国文化关系的共同愿望。
