Title: Premier Phạm Văn Đồng Receives Our Cultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 4

    Hanoi, June 6 (Xinhua) – Phạm Văn Đồng, Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received the entire members of the Chinese Cultural Delegation led by Li Qi-tao, Deputy Director of the Chinese People’s Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, on the afternoon of the 6th, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception was Phạm Hồng, Vice-Chairman of the Vietnamese Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    Chen Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, was also present at the reception.

### 范文同总理接见我文化代表团

1966-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    范文同总理接见我文化代表团
    新华社河内六日电　越南民主共和国政府总理范文同，六日下午接见了由中国对外文化联络委员会副主任李琦涛率领的中国文化代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，越南对外文化联络委员会副主任范鸿。
    接见时中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陈亮也在座。
