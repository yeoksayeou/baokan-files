Title: China and Cuba Sign Trade Protocol and Scientific Cooperation Work Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-06-02
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, May 31—The 1966 trade protocol between China and Cuba was signed in Havana on May 26.
    Wang Youping, head of the Chinese government trade delegation and Chinese Ambassador to Cuba, signed on behalf of the Chinese government. Regueira, head of the Cuban government trade delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, signed on behalf of the Cuban government.
    On the 27th, the 1965-1966 scientific cooperation work plan between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the National Committee of the Cuban Academy of Sciences was also signed in Havana.

### 中古签订贸易议定书和科学合作工作计划

1966-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    中古签订贸易议定书和科学合作工作计划
    新华社哈瓦那三十一日电　中、古两国一九六六年度贸易议定书于五月二十六日在哈瓦那签字。
    代表中国政府签字的是中国政府贸易代表团团长、驻古巴大使王幼平，代表古巴政府签字的是古巴政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长雷格伊拉。
    二十七日，中国科学院和古巴科学院全国委员会一九六五年——一九六六年科学合作工作计划也在哈瓦那签字。
