Title: Nepalese People Warmly Welcome Chinese Photo Exhibition, Praising Chinese Youth's Contributions to Construction
Author:
Date: 1966-06-23
Edition: 6

    Kathmandu, Xinhua News Agency - A Chinese photo exhibition introducing the role of Chinese youth in socialist construction was held in Kathmandu from June 8th to 15th.

    The exhibition was organized by the Chinese youth sports delegation participating in Nepal's Second National Youth Festival.

    The exhibition was warmly welcomed by the Nepalese people, especially the youth.

    A viewer named Subedi wrote: The whole world today sees the tremendous progress China has made in a short period of time after shaking off years of imperialist rule.

    A viewer named Sharma wrote: "The Chinese exhibition provides an outstanding example of the anti-imperialist struggle." Many viewers also praised the contributions of Chinese youth to national construction. They also enthusiastically wrote: "Long live Sino-Nepalese friendship!" "The people of China and Nepal are brothers!" One viewer wrote: "The Nepalese people express their heartfelt gratitude for the progress made by our friendly country China and for China's assistance to Nepal."

### 尼泊尔人民热烈欢迎我图片展览会  称赞中国青年在建设中的贡献

1966-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔人民热烈欢迎我图片展览会
    称赞中国青年在建设中的贡献
    新华社加德满都电　介绍中国青年在社会主义建设中的作用的中国图片展览会六月八日到十五日在加德满都举行。
    展览会是由参加尼泊尔第二届全国青年联欢节的中国青年体育代表团举办的。
    展览会受到尼泊尔人民、特别是青年人的热烈欢迎。
    一位名叫苏贝迪的观众写道：全世界今天都看到中国在摆脱帝国主义的多年统治以后在短短的时间里所取得的巨大进步。
    一位名叫夏尔马的观众写道：“中国展出的这个展览会，提供了一个反帝斗争的杰出范例。”许多观众还称赞中国青年在国家建设中做出的贡献。他们还热情地写道：“尼中友好万岁！”“尼中两国人民是兄弟！”一位观众写道：“尼泊尔人对友好国家中国所取得的进步以及中国给尼泊尔的援助表示衷心的感激。”
