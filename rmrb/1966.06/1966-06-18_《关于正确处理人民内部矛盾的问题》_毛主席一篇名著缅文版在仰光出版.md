Title: Chairman Mao's Work "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People" Published in Burmese in Yangon
Author:
Date: 1966-06-18
Edition: 4

Chairman Mao Zedong's work "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People" has recently been published in Burmese in Yangon.

Prior to this, the first and second volumes of the Burmese edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong, Volume IV" have been published successively.

### 《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》  毛主席一篇名著缅文版在仰光出版

1966-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》
    毛主席一篇名著缅文版在仰光出版
    新华社仰光十七日电　毛泽东主席的著作《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》缅文版最近在这里出版。
    在这以前，《毛泽东选集》第四卷缅文版第一、二册已先后出版。
