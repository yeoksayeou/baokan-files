Title: North Vietnamese Army and People Shot Down Seven US Aircraft

Author:
Date: 1966-06-22
Edition: 5

    Hanoi, June 21st, New China News Agency - According to announcements here, the army and people of Ha Bac, Hai Hung, and Lang Son provinces in North Vietnam, along with the People's Air Force, shot down a total of seven invading American bandit aircraft on June 21st, and captured a group of American pilots alive.
    Among the downed enemy aircraft, two were shot down by the army and people of Ha Bac province, two were shot down by the People's Air Force in close coordination with the army and people of Ha Bac province, two were shot down by the army and people of Hai Hung province, and one was shot down by the army and people of Lang Son province.

### 越南北方军民击落美机七架

1966-06-22
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机七架
    新华社河内二十一日电　据这里公布，越南北方河北、海阳、谅山三省军民和人民空军，二十一日共击落入侵的美国强盗飞机七架，并活捉一批美国飞行员。
    在被击落的敌机中，有两架是河北省军民击落的，有两架是人民空军在河北省军民的紧密配合下击落的，有两架是海阳省军民击落的，还有一架是谅山省军民击落的。
