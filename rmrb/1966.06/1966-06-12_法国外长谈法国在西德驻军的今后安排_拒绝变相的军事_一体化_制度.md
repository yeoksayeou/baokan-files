Title: French Foreign Minister Discusses Future Arrangements for French Troops Stationed in West Germany, Rejects Military "Integration" System in Disguise
Author:
Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 5

    According to a June 9th report from Xinhua, French Foreign Minister De Murville recently stated in a radio broadcast that France cannot accept any arrangement regarding the future mission of French troops in West Germany that attempts to continue the old system of "integration." He made these remarks when discussing the North Atlantic Treaty Organization's Ministerial Council meeting in Brussels. According to the meeting's communique, internal discussions will be held within the North Atlantic Treaty Organization's Permanent Council regarding "the tasks and missions of French forces in Germany, including arrangements for their cooperation and command with NATO forces." The French Foreign Minister said that if the future arrangements are equivalent to restoring the old ways – that is, the "integration" system, or requiring French forces to automatically undertake obligations based on the initiatives of Allied command – that is "unacceptable."

### 法国外长谈法国在西德驻军的今后安排  拒绝变相的军事“一体化”制度

1966-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    法国外长谈法国在西德驻军的今后安排
    拒绝变相的军事“一体化”制度
    据新华社巴黎九日电　法国外交部长德姆维尔最近发表广播讲话说，法国不能接受企图延续“一体化”旧制度的关于法军在西德的今后使命的任何安排。他是在谈到北大西洋公约组织布鲁塞尔部长理事会会议时讲这番话的。根据会议公报，北大西洋公约组织常设理事会内部将举行关于“法军在德国的任务和使命，包括他们同北大西洋公约组织军队合作和指挥的安排”的会谈。法国外交部长说，如果未来的安排等于恢复以前的老办法——即“一体化”制度或者要法军根据盟国司令部的倡议自动承担义务——那是“不能容许的”。
