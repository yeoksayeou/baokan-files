Title: Lao People's Army and Civilians Shoot Down 400 US Planes in Two Years People's Liberation Army Headquarters Calls for Fierce Counterattacks Against Enemy Offensives to Achieve Greater Victories
Author:
Date: 1966-06-10
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Khai, 8th. According to a Pathet Lao Radio broadcast on the 8th: From May 17, 1964, to May 25, 1966, the Lao patriotic army and civilians achieved a major victory by shooting down 400 American bandit planes. On May 25th, patriotic army and civilians in the four provinces of Khammouane, Xieng Khouang, Sam Neua, and Luang Prabang shot down a total of four American bandit planes.
    The Supreme Command of the Lao People's Liberation Army sent a telegram on the 7th to the cadres and soldiers of the Lao national patriotic armed forces, warmly congratulating them on their brilliant victory in shooting down 400 American bandit planes with the cooperation of the people.
    The congratulatory telegram called on the Lao national patriotic armed forces to further enhance their patriotism, concentrate their hatred on the muzzles of their guns, shoot down more American planes, capture more American pilots, and more fiercely counterattack the ground offensives of the United States and its lackeys to achieve greater new victories.

### 老挝军民两年击落美机四百架  解放军指挥部号召猛烈反击敌人进攻争取更大胜利

1966-06-10
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝军民两年击落美机四百架
    解放军指挥部号召猛烈反击敌人进攻争取更大胜利
    新华社康开八日电　据巴特寮电台八日广播：老挝爱国军民自一九六四年五月十七日到一九六六年五月二十五日，取得了打落四百架美国强盗飞机的重大胜利。在五月二十五日这天，老挝甘蒙、川圹、桑怒和琅勃拉邦四省爱国军民共打落美国强盗飞机四架。
    老挝人民解放军最高指挥部七日打电报给老挝全国爱国武装力量干部和战士，热烈祝贺他们在人民协同下打落美国强盗飞机四百架的辉煌胜利。
    贺电号召老挝全国爱国武装力量进一步增强爱国主义精神，把仇恨集中到枪口上，打落更多的美国飞机，俘虏更多的美国飞行员，更猛烈地反击美国及其走狗的地面进攻，争取更大的新胜利。
