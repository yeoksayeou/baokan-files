Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Sudanese Guest
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-10
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, June 9th, Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Dr. Tigani El Mahi, a renowned Sudanese psychiatrist who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with him.
    Present at the reception were Ren Yinglun, Cheng Keru, and others.
    Dr. Tigani El Mahi arrived in Beijing on the 3rd. (Attached picture)

### 郭沫若副委员长接见苏丹客人

1966-06-10
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见苏丹客人
    据新华社九日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见应中非友协邀请来我国访问的苏丹著名精神病专家提加尼·马希博士，和他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有任映仑、程克如等。
    提加尼·马希博士是在三日到达北京的。（附图片）
    六月九日，郭沫若副委员长接见苏丹著名精神病专家提加尼·马希博士。　　　　新华社记者　郑小箴摄
