Title: Guo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Receives Syrian Friends
Author:
Date: 1966-06-14
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 13th – Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Halid Sufi, Director of the Damascus Cultural Center of Syria, and Tamim Abu Hassun, Director of the Latakia Cultural Center, who are visiting our country on a friendly visit, this afternoon. Vice-Chairman Guo Moruo had a friendly conversation with the two friends.
    Present at the reception were Song Changhua, Fu Bingyou, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 郭副委员长接见叙利亚朋友

1966-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    郭副委员长接见叙利亚朋友
    新华社十三日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，今天下午接见了来我国进行友好访问的叙利亚大马士革文化中心主任哈利德·苏菲，以及拉塔基亚文化中心主任塔米姆·阿布·哈松。郭沫若副委员长同两位朋友进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有宋常华、傅兵友等有关方面负责人。
