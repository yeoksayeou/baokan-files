Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Separately Receive Tanzanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi met this afternoon with Paul Bomani, Tanzanian Minister for Economic Affairs and Development Planning, and members of the Tanzanian government economic delegation led by him, as well as Mrs. Paul Bomani and Mrs. Kunabi, Executive Committee Member of the Tanzanian National Women's Federation. Chairman Liu had a cordial and friendly conversation with the distinguished guests.
    Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Li Xiannian and his wife, Lin Jiamei, Deputy Director of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission Yang Lin, and others.
    Tanzanian Ambassador to China Wazir Juma was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with Paul Bomani, Tanzanian Minister for Economic Affairs and Development Planning, and members of the Tanzanian government economic delegation led by him, as well as Mrs. Paul Bomani and Mrs. Kunabi, Executive Committee Member of the Tanzanian National Women's Federation, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Deputy Director of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission Yang Lin, and others.
    Tanzanian Ambassador to China Wazir Juma was also present. (Attached Picture)
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi meets with Paul Bomani (fourth from left in front row), Tanzanian Minister for Economic Affairs and Development Planning, and members of the Tanzanian government economic delegation led by him, as well as Mrs. Bomani (fourth from right in front row) and Mrs. Kunabi, Executive Committee Member of the Tanzanian National Women's Federation (second from right in front row).
　  Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Yuanliu.

### 刘主席周总理分别接见坦桑贵宾

1966-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席周总理分别接见坦桑贵宾
    新华社七日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见坦桑尼亚经济事务和发展计划部长保罗·博马尼，和由他率领的坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团的成员，以及保罗·博马尼的夫人和坦桑尼亚全国妇联执委库纳比夫人。刘主席同贵宾们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有李先念副总理和夫人林佳楣，对外经委副主任杨琳等。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马也在座。
    新华社七日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见坦桑尼亚经济事务和发展计划部长保罗·博马尼和由他率领的坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团的成员，以及保罗·博马尼夫人和坦桑尼亚全国妇联执委库纳比夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有李先念副总理以及对外经委副主任杨琳等。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马也在座。（附图片）

　  新华社记者　吴元柳摄
