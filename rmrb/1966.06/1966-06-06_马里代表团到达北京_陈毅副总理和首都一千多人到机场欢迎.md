Title: Malian Delegation Arrives in Beijing Chen Yi and Over a Thousand People from the Capital Welcome at the Airport
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-06
Edition: 4

    Malian Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Vice Premier Chen Yi and Over a Thousand People from the Capital Welcome at the Airport
    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Malian delegation, led by Mamadou Madeira Keita, member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union-African Democratic Rally (US-RDA) and Minister of Justice of Mali, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight for a friendly visit to our country at our invitation.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council and more than 1,500 people from all walks of life in the capital warmly welcomed the distinguished Malian guests at the airport.
    When the distinguished Malian guests met the welcoming crowd, gongs and drums sounded at the airport, and people waved bouquets, shouting slogans such as "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Malian people!" "Long live the solidarity of the Asian and African peoples!" Young women presented flowers to the distinguished Malian guests.
    Also at the airport to welcome them were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, as well as responsible persons from the Foreign Economic Relations Commission, the Ministry of Foreign Trade, the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, and Beijing Municipality, including Wang Daohan, Liu Xiwen, Zhang Tiesheng, and Wang Kunlun.
    Malian Ambassador to China, Guissé Tidiani, and embassy officials, as well as Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Guinean Embassy in China, Keita Atanase, also greeted them at the airport.
    When the Malian delegation arrived in Guangzhou by train this afternoon, Lin Liming, Acting Governor of Guangdong Province, and Luo Peiyuan, Acting Mayor of Guangzhou, warmly welcomed them at the station. (Attached picture)
    Minister Mamadou Madeira Keita and other distinguished Malian guests, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, meet with the capital's crowds who are welcoming them at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Jingde.

### 马里代表团到达北京  陈毅副总理和首都一千多人到机场欢迎

1966-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    马里代表团到达北京
    陈毅副总理和首都一千多人到机场欢迎
    新华社五日讯　由马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、司法部长马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔率领的马里代表团，应邀前来我国进行友好访问，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    国务院副总理陈毅和首都各界一千五百多人，到机场热烈欢迎马里贵宾。
    马里贵宾同欢迎群众见面时，机场上响起锣鼓声，人们挥舞着花束，高呼“中马两国人民友好万岁！”“亚非人民团结万岁！”等口号。女青年向马里贵宾献了花。
    到机场欢迎的，还有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，以及对外经委、对外贸易部、中非友协和北京市的负责人汪道涵、刘希文、张铁生、王昆仑等。
    马里驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼及使馆官员，几内亚驻中国大使馆临时代办凯塔·阿塔纳兹，也到机场迎接。
    马里代表团今天下午乘火车到达广州时，广东省代省长林李明、广州市代市长罗培元等到车站热烈欢迎。（附图片）
    马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔部长等马里贵宾由陈毅副总理陪同，和在机场欢迎的首都群众见面。　　　新华社记者　王敬德摄
