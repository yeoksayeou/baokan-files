Title: Two Cambodian Guests Return Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-24
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd, Mr. Bok Sen An, Director of the Technical Bureau of the Cambodian Sports Bureau and Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the Cambodian National Organizing Committee for the First Asian Games for Emerging Forces, and another person, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country after a five-day friendly visit to China.



**Named Entities**


柬埔寨	Jiǎnpǔzhài (Cambodia)
体育局技术局长	Tǐyù jú jìshù jú zhǎng (Director of the Technical Bureau of the Sports Bureau)
第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会	Dì yī jiè yàzhōu xīnxīng lìliàng yùndònghuì (First Asian Games for Emerging Forces)
柬埔寨全国组织委员会执行局主席	Jiǎnpǔzhài quánguó zǔzhī wěiyuánhuì zhíxíng jú zhǔxí (Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the Cambodian National Organizing Committee)
卜森安	Bǔ Sēn'ān (Bok Sen An)
新华社	Xīnhuá shè (Xinhua News Agency)
北京	Běijīng (Beijing)
中国	Zhōngguó (China)

### 柬埔寨两位客人回国

1966-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨两位客人回国
    据新华社二十三日讯　柬埔寨体育局技术局长、第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会柬埔寨全国组织委员会执行局主席卜森安等二人在我国进行为时五天的友好访问之后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
