Title: Cambodian Troops Repel Invading Thai Forces
Author:
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, July 7th. According to the Cambodian News Agency on the 7th, on June 1st, at dawn, a unit of the Cambodian Oddar Meanchey Province, in the area of ​​Osma, was on a patrol mission along the border, when they repelled a group of invading Thai armed forces. After a brief exchange of fire, the Thais retreated back into Thailand.

### 柬埔寨部队击退入侵的泰军

1966-06-08
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨部队击退入侵的泰军
    新华社金边七日电　据柬埔寨新闻社七日报道，六月一日凌晨，柬埔寨奥多棉藏省奥斯马地方的一支部队在边境执行巡逻任务时，击退一群入侵的泰国武装部队。双方进行了短促的交火以后，泰国人退回泰国境内。
