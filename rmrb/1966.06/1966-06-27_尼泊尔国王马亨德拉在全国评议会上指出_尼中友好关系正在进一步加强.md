Title: Nepal's King Mahendra Says Sino-Nepalese Friendship is Strengthening
Author:
Date: 1966-06-27
Edition: 4

Nepal's King Mahendra pointed out in the National Assembly that Sino-Nepalese friendly relations are further strengthening.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, June 24th: Nepal's King Mahendra said on the 24th at the eighth session of the National Assembly when talking about the relationship between China and Nepal, "My government and the government of the People's Republic of China have recently signed an agreement based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, regarding trade, transportation, and other related matters between the Tibet Autonomous Region of the People's Republic of China and Nepal, which has further strengthened the friendly relations between the two countries."

Mahendra said, this year Nepal's trade with China has exceeded any previous year. He hopes that after the completion of the Kathmandu-Kodari Highway, which is being built with China's help, the trade volume between the two countries will further increase.

When talking about national construction, Mahendra called on the Nepalese people to strengthen the spirit of "self-confidence and self-respect."

### 尼泊尔国王马亨德拉在全国评议会上指出  尼中友好关系正在进一步加强

1966-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王马亨德拉在全国评议会上指出
    尼中友好关系正在进一步加强
    据新华社加德满都二十四日电　尼泊尔国王马亨德拉二十四日在全国评议会第八次会议上谈到中国和尼泊尔的关系时说：“我的政府同中华人民共和国政府最近根据和平共处五项原则，就同中华人民共和国西藏自治区之间的通商、交通和其它有关问题所缔结的协定，进一步加强了两国之间的友好关系。”
    马亨德拉说，今年尼泊尔同中国的贸易额超过了过去任何一年。他希望在中国帮助修建的加德满都—科达里公路完工后，两国的贸易额将进一步增加。
    马亨德拉在谈到国家的建设时，号召尼泊尔人民加强“自信和自尊精神”。
