Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Lin Feng, and Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi Receive the Head of the Asian-African Writers' Delegation, Observers, and Special Guests
Author:

Edition: 3

	Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Lin Feng, and Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi Receive the Head of the Asian-African Writers' Delegation, Observers, and Special Guests
	Xinhua News Agency, June 27th - Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, Vice Chairmen Lin Feng and Liu Ningyi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, today morning received the heads and deputy heads of delegations from 47 countries and regions attending the Emergency Conference of Asian-African Writers, chief observers of international organizations, and special invited guests. They had a warm and friendly conversation.

	Those who were received included: Representative of the Permanent Secretariat of Asian-African Writers, Manuwila; Angola's Viriato da Cruz; Azania (South Africa)'s Morrison; Basutoland's Ntsi Mohale; Bechuanaland's Dikobe Motumise; Cambodia's Zheng Huan; Cameroon's Fei Ze Ma Se Er; Canary Islands' Antonio Kubilo; Ceylon's Jialalate; China's Guo Moruo, Xu Guangping, Ba Jin, Liu Baiyu; Comoros Islands' Abudu Bakari Buwana; Congo (Brazzaville)'s Claude Ndala; Congo (Kinshasa)'s Jibuwei Kangshengding; Ghana's Aierma; Guinea's Segu Kamara; Indonesia's Agang Wei Xipai; Iran's Laming; Iraq's Youxu Fu Yiziding; Japan's Bai Shi Fan, Yi Tian Yi Xian; Jordan's Ma Jin Aihemaide; Democratic People's Republic of Korea's Cui Ronghua, Jiang Xiangwei; Laos' Wuda Ma Zhu Lamani, Zhalun Weng Sangang; Malaya's Shalama; Madagascar's Andeliamanawodatia; Mali's Mamadu Guoluoguo; Morocco's Ali Wenli'er; Mozambique's Ruo Ao Mengguanbu, Abusalong Bahulu; Nepal's Xidi Chalan Shileisita; Niger's Suliyi Amadu; North Kalimantan's Wen Mingquan; Palestine's Abudu Ke Limu Kalimi; Philippines' Amadu Erhan De Si; "Portuguese" Guinea and Cape Verde Islands' Mario Fensike; Sierra Leone's Nansi Guangte Fu Ren; "French" Somaliland's Muhammad Dahang; Somalia's Abudu La Zizhi Nu'er Xi; Southwest Africa's Charles Koraisha; Sudan's Aosiman Hasa Aihemaide; Swaziland's Wudewandewei; Syria's Zhadate Likabi; Tanzania's Yehaiya Muhammadu Hasani; Thailand's Gula Jiali; Turkey's Niechaide; Uganda's Benmusa; Democratic Republic of Vietnam's Chun Chang; National Liberation Front of South Vietnam's Chen Tingyun; Yemen's Saleh Dahang; Zimbabwe's Yiliasi Lusike;

	Three international organizations' observers: Secretary-General of the Asian-African Journalists' Association, Chaduo; Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Liaison Committee, Xiyuan Si Gongyi, Louis Alley, Weili Haliancha; Secretary of the Permanent Secretariat of the Asian-African Lawyers' Conference, Cheng Tian Liangxiong;

	Special invited guests: Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Chen Wencheng; Representative of the Patriotic Front of Thailand abroad, Pa Long Juluan; Chief of the Palestine Liberation Organization Office in Beijing, Jialabu; American writer Strong, etc.

	Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Ziyu, Liu Xiao, Xiao Wangdong, Yuan Ziqin, Chutu Nan, Wang Kunlun, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 陈毅副总理、林枫副委员长、刘宁一副委员长接见亚非作家代表团长、观察员和特邀来宾

1966-06-28
第3版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理、林枫副委员长、刘宁一副委员长接见亚非作家代表团长、观察员和特邀来宾
    新华社二十七日讯　国务院副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长林枫、刘宁一，今天上午接见了出席亚非作家紧急会议的四十七个国家和地区的代表团团长、副团长，和国际组织的首席观察员以及特邀来宾，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    被接见的有：亚非作家常设局秘书长代表曼努维拉，安哥拉的维里亚托·达克鲁斯，阿扎尼亚（南非）的摩里逊，巴苏陀兰的恩齐·莫哈莱，贝专纳的迪科贝·翁图梅兹，柬埔寨的郑璜，喀麦隆的费则·马赛尔，加那利群岛的安东尼奥·库比洛，锡兰的贾亚拉特，中国的郭沫若、许广平、巴金、刘白羽，科摩罗群岛的阿布杜·巴卡里·布瓦纳，刚果（布）的克洛德·恩达拉，刚果（利）的基布韦·康士坦丁，加纳的艾尔马，几内亚的塞古·卡马拉，印度尼西亚的阿甘姆·维斯比，伊朗的拉明，伊拉克的优素福·伊兹丁，日本的白石凡、依田义贤，约旦的马津·艾哈迈德，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的崔荣华、姜相渭，老挝的乌达马·朱拉马尼、扎伦·翁桑昂，马来亚的沙尔玛，马尔加什的安得里亚马纳沃达特亚，马里的马马杜·戈洛戈，摩洛哥的阿里·温利勒，莫三鼻给的若奥·蒙格万布、阿布萨隆·巴胡尔，尼泊尔的西迪·查兰·什雷斯塔，尼日尔的苏利·阿马杜，北加里曼丹的文铭权，巴勒斯坦的阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡尔米，菲律宾的阿马多·埃尔南德斯，“葡属”几内亚和佛得角群岛的马里奥·丰塞卡，塞拉勒窝内的南西·格兰特夫人，索马里海岸（“法属”）的穆罕默德·达汉，索马里的阿卜杜拉齐兹·努尔·赫尔西，西南非洲的查尔斯·考赖萨，苏丹的奥斯曼·哈桑·艾哈迈德，斯威士兰的乌德旺德韦，叙利亚的扎达特·里卡比，坦桑尼亚的叶海雅·穆罕默德·哈桑尼，泰国的古腊·柿巴立，土耳其的聂乍德，乌干达的本穆萨，越南民主共和国的春长，越南南方民族解放阵线的陈庭云，也门的萨利赫·达汉，津巴布韦的伊利亚斯·鲁西克；
    三个国际组织的观察员：亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长西园寺公一、路易·艾黎、维利·哈里安查，亚非法律工作者会议常设书记处书记成田良雄；
    特邀来宾：越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成，泰国爱国阵线驻国外代表帕荣·朱拉暖，巴勒斯坦解放组织驻北京办事处主任加尔布，美国作家斯特朗等。
    接见时在场的，有廖承志、张奚若、刘晓、萧望东、袁子钦、楚图南、王昆仑等各有关方面的负责人。
