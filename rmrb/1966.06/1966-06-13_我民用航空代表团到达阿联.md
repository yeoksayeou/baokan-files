Title: Our Civil Aviation Delegation Arrives in the UAR
Author:
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    According to a report from Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 11: The Chinese Civil Aviation Delegation, led by Shen Tu, Deputy Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, arrived here from Baghdad on the 11th for a friendly visit to the UAR.  Greeting the delegation at the airport was Ahmed Seif, Secretary of State of the Ministry of Defense of the UAR and Director of the Civil Aviation Administration. He Gongkai, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the UAR, also welcomed them at the airport.
    The Chinese Civil Aviation Delegation visited Iraq from the 8th to the 11th.

### 我民用航空代表团到达阿联

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我民用航空代表团到达阿联
    据新华社开罗十一日电　由中国民用航空总局副局长沈图率领的中国民用航空代表团，十一日从巴格达到达这里，对阿联进行友好访问。到机场欢迎代表团的有阿联国防部国务秘书兼民航局局长艾哈迈德·赛夫。中国驻阿联大使馆参赞何功楷也到机场欢迎。
    中国民用航空代表团曾在八日到十一日在伊拉克进行访问。
