Title: Danish Ambassador Hosts Reception to Celebrate Danish Constitution Day
Author:
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 4

Danish Ambassador to China, Mr. O. B. Oldenberg, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the Danish Constitution Day.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Yang Mingxuan, Deputy Foreign Minister Wang Bingnan, and other officials from relevant departments were invited to attend the reception. Ambassador Oldenberg and Vice Premier Chen Yi both delivered speeches at the reception, expressing their wishes for further development of friendly relations between China and Denmark. Envoys from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 为庆祝丹麦宪法日  丹麦驻华大使举行招待会

1966-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    为庆祝丹麦宪法日
    丹麦驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社三日讯　丹麦驻中国大使奥尔登伯格今天下午举行招待会，庆祝丹麦宪法日。
    陈毅副总理，杨明轩副委员长，外交部副部长王炳南以及其他有关方面的负责人，应邀出席了招待会。奥尔登伯格大使和陈毅副总理在招待会上先后致词，共祝中丹两国的友好关系进一步发展。各国驻中国的使节也出席了招待会。
