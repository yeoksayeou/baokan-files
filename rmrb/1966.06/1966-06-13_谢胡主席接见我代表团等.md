Title: Chairman Shehu Receives Our Delegation and Others
Author:
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 11th – Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Shehu, received here on the 11th the Chinese Supply and Marketing Cooperative Delegation led by Cheng Hongyi, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, and the Chinese Track and Field Team led by Liu Dexiu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Track and Field Association.
    Present at the reception were Albanian Minister of Trade Njesila, Chairman of the Central Union of Albanian Consumer Cooperatives Fuga, Secretary-General of the Council of Ministers Rusa, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Malile, Director of the Sports Department of the Ministry of Education and Culture Doda, and others.
    Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, was also present at the reception.
    The reception took place in an extremely cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 谢胡主席接见我代表团等

1966-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    谢胡主席接见我代表团等
    新华社地拉那十一日电阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡十一日在这里接见了由中华全国供销合作社总社副主任程宏毅率领的中国供销合作社代表团和由中国田径协会副主席刘德修率领的中国田径队。
    接见时在座的有阿尔巴尼亚贸易部长恩杰拉，阿尔巴尼亚消费合作社中央联盟主席弗加，部长会议秘书长鲁沙，外交部副部长马利列，文教部体育司司长多达等。
    接见时，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚使馆临时代办王锦川也在座。
    接见是在十分亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
