Title: U.S. Imperialism and Its Accomplices Hold Provocative Exercises in the South China Sea; France and Pakistan Publicly Announce Refusal to Participate
Author:
Date: 1966-06-10
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch from Manila: U.S. imperialism and its accomplice nations conducted large-scale provocative naval exercises in the South China Sea for nineteen days, from May 19 to June 6.

    These exercises were a military threat activity carried out by U.S. imperialism in collusion with British imperialism and other accomplices, aimed at China and Vietnam, to strengthen preparations for expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam. Britain also provided Hong Kong as a base, allowing a large number of U.S. aggressor ships participating in the exercises to assemble in Hong Kong waters.

    The six member countries participating in this naval exercise, codenamed "Sea Imp," were: the United States, Britain, Australia, New Zealand, the Philippines, and Thailand, with Britain and the United States serving as the chief and deputy commanders, respectively. A total of more than 10,000 military personnel, 45 ships, and more than 100 aircraft participated in the exercises. The first stage of the exercises involved naval airstrikes, surface attacks, and anti-submarine warfare tactics in the South China Sea. The second stage, starting from May 28, involved maritime transport exercises from Manila to the Gulf of Siam. France and Pakistan publicly announced their non-participation.

### 美帝及帮凶在南中国海举行挑衅演习  法国和巴基斯坦公开宣布拒绝参加

1966-06-10
第6版()
专栏：

    美帝及帮凶在南中国海举行挑衅演习
    法国和巴基斯坦公开宣布拒绝参加
    新华社九日讯　马尼拉消息：美帝国主义和它的帮凶国家从五月十九日开始到六月六日在南中国海举行了为期十九天的大规模挑衅性海军演习。
    这次演习是美帝国主义勾结英帝国主义和其他帮凶，为加强准备扩大侵略越南战争，针对中国和越南进行的军事威胁活动。英国还提供香港作为基地，使参加演习的大批美国侵略军的舰艇集结在香港海面。
    参加这次以“海上顽童”为代号的海军演习的六个成员国是：美国、英国、澳大利亚、新西兰、菲律宾和泰国，由英国和美国分任正、副指挥。参加演习的军事人员共有一万多人，舰艇四十五艘，飞机一百多架。演习的第一阶段是在南中国海进行海上空袭，海面攻击，反潜艇战术等。第二阶段由五月二十八日起进行从马尼拉到暹罗湾的海上运输演习。法国和巴基斯坦公开宣布不参加。
