Title: Vietnamese Government Decides to Withdraw Embassy in Ghana
Author:
Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 5

    The Vietnamese Government Decides to Withdraw Embassy in Ghana

    (Xinhua News Agency, Accra, June 10) Liu Guixin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Ghana, and other embassy personnel left here by plane on the morning of the 10th after the embassy was closed.

    Before departing, the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a communiqué stating, "The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has decided to withdraw its permanent diplomatic mission from Accra, effective June 1966." The communiqué stated, "The withdrawal of this permanent diplomatic mission does not affect the friendly relations between the peoples of Vietnam and Ghana."

    Prior to this, the Ghanaian coup authorities had already withdrawn their embassy in Vietnam.

### 越南政府决定撤回驻加纳使馆

1966-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    越南政府决定撤回驻加纳使馆
    据新华社阿克拉十日电　越南民主共和国驻加纳大使馆临时代办刘贵新和大使馆其他人员在大使馆关闭以后，于十日上午乘飞机离开这里。
    在动身之前，越南民主共和国大使馆发表公报说，“越南民主共和国政府决定自一九六六年六月起，从阿克拉撤回它的常驻外交使团”。公报说，“撤回这个常驻外交使团并不影响越南和加纳两国人民的友好关系。”
    在这以前，加纳政变当局已经撤回它驻越南的大使馆。
