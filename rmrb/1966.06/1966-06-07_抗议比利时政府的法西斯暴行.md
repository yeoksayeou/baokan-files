    Protest Against the Fascist Atrocities of the Belgian Government
    By Our Staff Commentator
    The Chinese people strongly condemn the Belgian ruling authorities for their brutal suppression of the anti-American demonstrations by the Belgian people, and angrily protest the Belgian ruling authorities' brazen arrest of Comrade Grippa, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, and other patriots.
    Due to the rapid development of contradictions between the United States and France, the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, an instrument of aggression controlled by U.S. imperialism, is on the verge of disintegration. De Gaulle has publicly announced that the headquarters of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization will be kicked out of France. In order to prevent this aggressive group from collapsing, the Johnson administration plans to move its headquarters from Paris to Brussels. This puts Belgium in danger of being placed under U.S. imperialist military occupation. The Belgian people naturally cannot agree to this. Their struggle is entirely natural. The Belgian government authorities have dispatched large numbers of military police to bloody suppress the demonstrating masses and arrest leading comrades of the Belgian Communist Party. This fascist atrocity shows that the Belgian government is willing to betray national interests and further turn its country into an appendage of U.S. imperialism.
    In the Belgian people's anti-American patriotic struggle, the Belgian Communist Party bravely stands at the forefront. The Belgian Communist Party, headed by Comrade Grippa, is a Marxist-Leninist party. It is the vanguard of the Belgian proletariat and the defender of the Belgian national interests. The Belgian Communist Party has consistently fought to safeguard the interests of the Belgian proletariat and working people and to oppose the aggressive policies of U.S. imperialism, setting a shining example in the communist movement of Western European capitalist countries. Comrade Grippa and others have once again demonstrated the revolutionary spirit of communist fighters in this struggle.
    The Belgian people's demonstrations against the North Atlantic Treaty Organization show that the broad masses of the people in Western European capitalist countries are increasingly awakening. The anti-American revolutionary storm is not only sweeping through Asia, Africa, and Latin America, but is also rising in the Western European region controlled by U.S. imperialism. This trend cannot be stopped by bayonets and prisons.
    The Belgian authorities must immediately release Comrade Grippa and other arrested patriots. If the Belgian authorities succumb to the will of U.S. imperialism and establish a North Atlantic Treaty Organization nest in Belgium, they will be punished by history.

### 抗议比利时政府的法西斯暴行
本报评论员
1966-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    抗议比利时政府的法西斯暴行
    本报评论员
    中国人民强烈谴责比利时统治当局残暴镇压比利时人民的反美示威，愤怒抗议比利时统治当局悍然逮捕比利时共产党中央委员会书记格里巴等同志和其他爱国者。
    由于美法矛盾的急剧发展，美帝国主义控制下的侵略工具北大西洋集团濒于瓦解。戴高乐公开宣布，要把北大西洋集团的总部从法国轰出去。约翰逊政府为了要使这个侵略集团免于崩溃，打算把它的总部从巴黎搬到布鲁塞尔。这就使比利时面临着被置于美帝国主义军事占领之下的危险。比利时人民当然不能答应。他们奋起反对，这是十分自然的。比利时政府当局竟然出动大批军警，血腥镇压示威群众，逮捕比利时共产党的领导同志。这种法西斯暴行表明，比利时政府不惜出卖民族利益，进一步把自己的国家变成为美帝国主义的附庸。
    在比利时人民的反美爱国斗争中，比利时共产党英勇地站在最前列。以格里巴同志为首的比利时共产党，是马克思列宁主义的政党。它是比利时无产阶级的先锋队，也是比利时民族利益的捍卫者。比利时共产党一贯为维护比利时无产阶级和劳动人民的利益、反对美帝国主义的侵略政策而坚决斗争，在西欧资本主义国家的共产主义运动中，树立了一面光辉的旗帜。格里巴等同志，在这次斗争中，再一次表现了共产主义战士的革命气概。
    比利时人民反对北大西洋集团的示威表明，西欧资本主义国家的广大人民日益觉醒。反美革命风暴不仅席卷亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲，而且也在美帝国主义控制下的西欧地区兴起。这种趋势，决不是用刺刀和监牢所能阻挡的。
    比利时当局必须立即释放格里巴等同志和其他被捕的爱国者。比利时当局，如果屈从美帝国主义的意志，在比利时建立北大西洋集团巢穴，必将受到历史的惩罚。
