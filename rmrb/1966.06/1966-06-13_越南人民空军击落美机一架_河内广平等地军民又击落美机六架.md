Title: Vietnamese People's Air Force Shoots Down One U.S. Plane, Military and Civilians in Hanoi, Quang Binh, and Other Places Shoot Down Six More U.S. Planes
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-13
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 12th - It was announced here on the 12th that the Vietnamese People's Air Force shot down an invading U.S. jet fighter over the eastern region of Hebei Province on the afternoon of the 12th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 12th - It was announced here that at 10:30 AM on the 12th, U.S. bandit planes brazenly invaded the airspace over Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The highly vigilant military and civilians of Hanoi immediately shot down a U.S. jet fighter with the first round of intense artillery fire. This is the fifth U.S. plane shot down by the military and civilians of Hanoi.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 11th - It was announced here on the 11th that the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in northern Vietnam shot down a total of three U.S. planes on the 9th (one of which was previously announced).
    Two of these three U.S. bandit planes were shot down while they were attempting to rescue the U.S. pilots from the first plane that had been shot down.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 10th - It was announced here on the 10th that the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in northern Vietnam shot down a U.S. jet fighter on the morning of the 9th. Additionally, according to supplementary information, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province also shot down a U.S. plane on May 30th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th - According to the Vietnam News Agency, the military and civilians of Quang Ninh Province in northern Vietnam shot down an invading U.S. bandit plane on the 8th.

### 越南人民空军击落美机一架  河内广平等地军民又击落美机六架

1966-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

    越南人民空军击落美机一架
    河内广平等地军民又击落美机六架
    新华社河内十二日电　据这里十二日公布：越南人民空军十二日下午在河北省东部地区上空，击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
    新华社河内十二日电　据这里公布：十二日上午十时三十分，美国强盗飞机悍然侵犯越南民主共和国首都河内市上空。具有高度警惕的河内市军民，当即以第一轮猛烈的炮火，击落美国喷气式飞机一架。这是河内市军民击落的第五架美国飞机。
    新华社河内十一日电　据这里十一日公布，越南北方广平省军民九日共击落美国飞机三架（其中一架前已公布）。
    这三架美国强盗飞机，有两架是在它们抢救被击落的第一架飞机上的美国飞行员时被击落的。
    新华社河内十日电　据这里十日公布：越南北方广平省军民九日上午击落美国喷气式飞机一架。另据补充消息，五月三十日，义安省军民还击落美国飞机一架。
    新华社河内八日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南北方广宁省军民八日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
