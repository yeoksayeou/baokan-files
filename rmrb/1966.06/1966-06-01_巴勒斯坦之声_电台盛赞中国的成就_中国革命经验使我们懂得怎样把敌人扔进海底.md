Title: "Voice of Palestine" Radio Praises China's Achievements: The Experience of the Chinese Revolution Teaches Us How to Throw the Enemy into the Sea

Author: 
Date: 1966-06-01
Edition: 4

    "Voice of Palestine" Radio Praises China's Achievements
    The Experience of the Chinese Revolution Teaches Us How to Throw the Enemy into the Sea
    (Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 30th) "Voice of Palestine" Radio published a commentary on the 30th, stating: "The experience of the Chinese revolution teaches us that by relying on the people and organizing them, we can throw the enemy into the sea."
    The commentary said that in 1949, "The Chinese People's Army drove American imperialism and its running dog Chiang Kai-shek into the Pacific Ocean. The people of the world congratulated the Chinese people on their victory and were happy about it." The commentary said that the achievements made by the Chinese people "have given us the confidence that we can do the same."
    Speaking of the friendship between the Chinese and Palestinian people, the commentary said: The Palestinian people "have met a friend who is fighting the same enemy, and that friend is the Chinese people. The friendly feelings of the Chinese people quickly turned into action... Millions of Chinese people have expressed their support and solidarity with our cause."

### “巴勒斯坦之声”电台盛赞中国的成就  中国革命经验使我们懂得怎样把敌人扔进海底

1966-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    “巴勒斯坦之声”电台盛赞中国的成就
    中国革命经验使我们懂得怎样把敌人扔进海底
    据新华社开罗三十日电　“巴勒斯坦之声”电台三十日发表评论说：“中国革命的经验使我们懂得，依靠人民，并把他们组织起来，我们就能够把敌人扔到大海里去。”
    评论说，一九四九年，“中国人民军队把美帝国主义及其走狗蒋介石赶进了太平洋。全世界人民都祝贺中国人民的胜利，并对此感到高兴。”评论说，中国人民所取得的成就，“使我们产生了也能这样做的信心。”
    评论谈到中国人民和巴勒斯坦人民之间的友谊时说：巴勒斯坦人民“遇到了和同一个敌人斗争的朋友，这个朋友就是中国人民。中国人民的友好感情很快化为行动……千百万中国人民表示支持和声援我们的事业。”
