Title: Two Syrian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-11
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Khalid Sufi, director of the Damascus Cultural Center of Syria, and Tamim Abu Hassoun, director of the Latakia Cultural Center, arrived in Beijing on the 8th for a friendly visit to our country.
    Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were Song Changhua, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and others.

### 叙利亚两位客人到京

1966-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    叙利亚两位客人到京
    新华社九日讯　应中国人民对外文化友好协会邀请来我国进行友好访问的叙利亚大马士革文化中心主任哈利德·苏菲和拉塔基亚文化中心主任塔米姆·阿布·哈松八日到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中国人民对外文化友好协会书记处书记宋常华等。
