Title: "Malaya Proclamation" Congratulates on Our Nuclear Test; World Progressive People Cheer China's New Achievements
Author:
Date: 1966-06-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 31st - The monthly magazine "Malaya Proclamation," published in London, released a commentary in its May issue, warmly hailing the success of China's third nuclear explosion. The commentary emphasized that China's nuclear explosion was to safeguard world peace and defend China from aggression by U.S. imperialism. The commentary said that while all progressive people around the world are delighted with China's new achievements, only imperialists and revisionists are slandering China, attempting to deceive some people into joining their anti-China movement.
    The commentary exposed the hypocrisy of the U.S.-British-Soviet Moscow Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty and their heavily promoted "prevention of nuclear proliferation." The commentary pointed out that the United States and Soviet revisionists are using these excuses to conduct nuclear tests time and time again, stockpiling nuclear weapons batch after batch, while wanting to prohibit others—especially those who have suffered and may suffer from their aggression—from designing and testing nuclear weapons for legitimate defense.
    The commentary called on all progressive people around the world to see through the nuclear tricks played by U.S. imperialism and its accomplices, and not to be intimidated by their "nuclear deterrence strategy."

### 《马来亚箴言》祝贺我核试验  世界进步人民欢呼中国新成就

1966-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    《马来亚箴言》祝贺我核试验
    世界进步人民欢呼中国新成就
    新华社伦敦三十一日电　在伦敦出版的《马来亚箴言》月刊五月号发表评论，热烈欢呼中国第三次核爆炸成功。评论强调指出，中国进行核爆炸是为了维护世界和平和保卫中国免受美帝国主义的侵略。评论说，当全世界所有进步人民都对中国的新成就感到欢欣鼓舞的时候，只有帝国主义者和修正主义者在诽谤中国，企图欺骗一些人参加他们的反华运动。
    评论揭露了美英苏莫斯科部分禁止核试验条约和它们大肆宣传的“防止核扩散”的虚伪的本质。评论指出，美国和苏联修正主义者用这些借口来一次又一次地进行核试验，一批又一批地储存核武器，同时却想禁止别人——特别是遭受和可能遭受他们侵略的人——设计和试验核武器来进行正当的防御。
    评论要求全世界进步人民看清美帝国主义及其同伙者玩弄的核诡计，不要被他们的“核威慑战略”所吓倒。
