Title: Pakistan's New Ambassador to China Calls on Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1966-06-15
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 14th -- Sultan Muhammad Khan, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan to the People's Republic of China, paid a courtesy call on Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi this morning to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Vice Premier Chen Yi had a friendly conversation with him.

### 巴基斯坦新任驻华大使  拜会陈毅副总理兼外长

1966-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦新任驻华大使
    拜会陈毅副总理兼外长
    新华社十四日讯　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗今天上午拜会陈毅副总理兼外交部长，商谈递交国书事宜。陈毅副总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
