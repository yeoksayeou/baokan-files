Title: Swedish Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Celebrate Swedish Flag Day
Author:
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 4

    To Celebrate Swedish Flag Day
    Swedish Ambassador to China Holds Reception
    Xinhua News Agency, June 6th. The Ambassador of the Kingdom of Sweden to China, Mr. Lennart Petri, held a reception at the embassy this afternoon to celebrate Swedish Flag Day.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, Vice Chairman Lin Feng of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, and responsible persons from relevant departments were invited to attend the reception.
    Ambassador Petri and Vice Premier Chen Yi proposed toasts at the reception successively. They wished for further development of the friendly relations between China and Sweden.
    Heads of diplomatic missions from various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 为庆祝瑞典国旗日  瑞典驻中国大使举行招待会

1966-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    为庆祝瑞典国旗日
    瑞典驻中国大使举行招待会
    新华社六日讯　瑞典王国驻中国大使佩特里今天下午在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝瑞典国旗日。
    国务院副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长林枫，外交部副部长王炳南，以及有关方面负责人应邀出席了招待会。
    佩特里大使和陈毅副总理在招待会上先后祝酒。他们祝愿中瑞两国的友好关系进一步发展。
    各国驻中国的使节也应邀出席了招待会。
