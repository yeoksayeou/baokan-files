Title: Joint Performance of Chinese Revolutionary Songs and Dances by Chinese and Iraqi Artists; Songs Praising Mao Zedong Thought Warmly Welcomed
Author:
Date: 1966-06-06
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, June 3rd – The Iraqi National Song and Dance Troupe and the four Chinese artists of the Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation jointly performed Chinese revolutionary songs and national dances here on the evening of the 2nd, receiving a warm welcome from more than five hundred spectators. The performance was organized by the Iraqi Ministry of Culture and National Guidance.

    The song "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman," sung by a Chinese female soloist in praise of Mao Zedong Thought, received prolonged applause from the audience.

    The "Shepherdess Dance" and "Sowing Dance," which are rich in national color and vividly depict the life of the Chinese working people, left a deep impression on the audience.

    When the Chinese soloist sang the famous Iraqi folk song "Under the Date Palm Tree," which she had just learned from Iraqi friends a few days ago, the audience cheered for her repeatedly.

### 中国和伊拉克艺术家联合演出中国革命歌舞  歌颂毛泽东思想歌曲受到热烈欢迎

1966-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    中国和伊拉克艺术家联合演出中国革命歌舞
    歌颂毛泽东思想歌曲受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社巴格达三日电　伊拉克民族歌舞团和中国政府文化友好代表团的四位中国艺术家二日晚上在这里联合演出中国革命歌曲和民族舞蹈，受到五百多观众的热烈欢迎。这次演出是由伊拉克文化和国家指导部举办的。
    一位中国女独唱家演唱的歌颂毛泽东思想的歌曲《大海航行靠舵手》，受到了观众长时间的鼓掌欢迎。
    富有民族色采并且生动地表现了中国劳动人民生活的“牧羊女舞”和“播种舞”，给观众留下了深刻的印象。
    当中国独唱家演唱她在几天以前刚刚从伊拉克朋友那里学会的伊拉克著名民歌《在椰枣树下》的时候，观众一再向她欢呼。
