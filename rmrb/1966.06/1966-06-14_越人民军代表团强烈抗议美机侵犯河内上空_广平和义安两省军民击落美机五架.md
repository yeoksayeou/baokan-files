Title: Vietnamese People's Army Delegation Strongly Protests U.S. Aircraft Intrusion Over Hanoi; Military and Civilians of Quang Binh and Nghe An Provinces Shot Down Five U.S. Aircraft

Author:
Date: 1966-06-14
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 13th – The Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Army sent an urgent telegram to the International Commission in Vietnam on the 13th, strongly protesting against U.S. aircraft continuously intruding over the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hanoi, on the 11th and 12th, and once again barbarously bombing many residential areas and economic facilities in Nam Dinh City and other provinces.
    The telegram pointed out that these new crimes of U.S. imperialism once again prove that it is still deliberately intensifying the war against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. U.S. imperialism must bear full responsibility for the consequences arising therefrom.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 13th – According to an announcement here on the 13th: The military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in northern Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. jet fighter on the 13th and captured a U.S. pilot alive.
    Prior to this, the military and civilians of this province shot down two U.S. aircraft on the 11th.
    The military and civilians of Nghe An Province also shot down two U.S. aircraft on the 10th.

### 越人民军代表团强烈抗议美机侵犯河内上空  广平和义安两省军民击落美机五架

1966-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    越人民军代表团强烈抗议美机侵犯河内上空
    广平和义安两省军民击落美机五架
    新华社河内十三日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团十三日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议美国飞机在十一日和十二日连续侵犯越南民主共和国首都河内上空，并且再次野蛮轰炸南定市以及其它一些省的许多居民区和经济设施。
    电报指出，美帝国主义这些新的罪行再一次证明，它仍然蓄意加强对越南民主共和国进行的战争。美帝国主义必须对由此而引起的后果承担全部责任。
    新华社河内十三日电　据这里十三日公布：越南北方广平省军民十三日击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架，并活捉美国飞行员。
    在这以前，这个省的军民在十一日击落了美国飞机两架。
    义安省军民在十日也击落了美国飞机两架。
