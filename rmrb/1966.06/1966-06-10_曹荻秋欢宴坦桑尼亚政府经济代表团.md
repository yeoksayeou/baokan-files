Title: Cao Diqiu Hosts a Banquet for Tanzanian Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-06-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 9th. Shanghai Mayor Cao Diqiu and his wife, Shi Bin, held a banquet today to warmly welcome Tanzanian Minister of Economic Affairs and Development Planning, Paul Bomani, and his wife, and the Tanzanian Government Economic Delegation led by him.
    Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Waziri Juma, and Executive Committee Member of the Tanzanian National Women's Federation, Mrs. Kunabi, were invited to attend the banquet.
    At the banquet, Cao Diqiu and Bomani spoke successively. Cao Diqiu said that the Tanzanian people, disregarding all kinds of pressure from imperialism, have recently put forward the resounding slogan of "self-reliance and building the country through diligence and thrift." The people of Shanghai greatly admire this revolutionary spirit of the Tanzanian people. The people of Shanghai, like all the people of China, firmly support the struggle of the people of all African countries against imperialism and new and old colonialism.
    In his speech, Bomani warmly praised the great achievements made by the Chinese people and the people of Shanghai in economic construction and other fields. He said that these achievements give the Tanzanian people the confidence to build their country along the path of self-reliance. The Tanzanian people and the Chinese people will always advance shoulder to shoulder. The Tanzanian people will surely achieve victory in implementing self-reliance, developing the national economy, and in the struggle against imperialism.
    Also present at the banquet were Yang Lin, Deputy Director of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission, Shi Ying, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    The Tanzanian Government Economic Delegation arrived in Shanghai by plane from Beijing on the afternoon of the 8th.

### 曹荻秋欢宴坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团

1966-06-10
第5版()
专栏：

    曹荻秋欢宴坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团
    新华社上海九日电　上海市市长曹荻秋和夫人石斌今天举行宴会，热烈欢迎坦桑尼亚经济事务和发展计划部长保罗·博马尼和夫人，以及由他率领的坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马，坦桑尼亚全国妇联执委库纳比夫人应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会上，曹荻秋和博马尼先后讲话。曹荻秋说，坦桑尼亚人民不顾帝国主义的种种压力，最近又提出了“自力更生、勤俭建国”的响亮口号。上海人民十分钦佩坦桑尼亚人民这种革命精神。上海人民和全中国人民一样，坚决支持非洲各国人民反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争。
    博马尼在讲话中热烈赞扬中国人民和上海人民在经济建设等方面取得的巨大成就。他说，这些成就使坦桑尼亚人民有信心按照自力更生的道路建设自己的国家。坦桑尼亚人民和中国人民将永远肩并肩地前进。坦桑尼亚人民在执行自力更生，发展民族经济方面和在反对帝国主义的斗争中，一定会取得胜利。
    出席宴会作陪的还有对外经委副主任杨琳，上海市副市长石英和其他有关方面的负责人。
    坦桑尼亚政府经济代表团是在八日下午乘飞机由北京到达上海的。
