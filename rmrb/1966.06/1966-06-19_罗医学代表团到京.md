Title: Romanian Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-19
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th June: A Romanian medical delegation of five, led by Maria Micil, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China under the 1966-1967 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Those who met the delegation at the airport included Wang Zhaoyuan, Lin Shixiao, Cheng Keru, and other responsible persons from relevant departments of the Ministry of Health.
    Betrescu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, also went to meet them.

### 罗医学代表团到京

1966-06-19
第6版()
专栏：

    罗医学代表团到京
    新华社十七日讯　根据中罗文化合作协定一九六六——一九六七年执行计划来我国访问的罗马尼亚医学代表团一行五人，在团长玛丽亚·米西尔率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的，有卫生部有关部门负责人王肇元、林士笑、程克如等。
    罗马尼亚驻华使馆临时代办贝特勒斯库也前往迎接。
