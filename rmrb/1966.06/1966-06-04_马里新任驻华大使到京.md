Title: New Malian Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd: The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Mali to the People's Republic of China, Gissé Tidiani, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 马里新任驻华大使到京

1966-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    马里新任驻华大使到京
    新华社三日讯　马里共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使吉塞·提迪亚尼，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
