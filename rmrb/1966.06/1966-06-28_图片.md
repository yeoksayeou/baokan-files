Title: Chen Yi Hosts Grand Banquet for Delegates of the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers
Author: Liu Dong'ao
Date: 1966-06-28
Edition: 4

On June 27th, Vice Premier Chen Yi held a grand banquet to warmly welcome all representatives and observers attending the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers. The photo shows Vice Premier Chen Yi delivering a speech at the banquet.
