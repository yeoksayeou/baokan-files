Title: Guizhou Province Actively Plants Medicinal Crops
Author:
Date: 1966-06-07
Edition: 3

    Guizhou Province Actively Plants Medicinal Crops
    Guizhou, one of China's famous medicinal material producing areas, is actively planting various medicinal crops.
    Since the beginning of spring, the province has planted more than 10,800 mu of various medicinal crops. Among them, there are more than 4.9 million *Eucommia ulmoides* trees and more than 150,000 *Magnolia officinalis* trees. The planting area has increased significantly compared with the same period last year.
    More than 90% of the Chinese medicinal materials produced in Guizhou are wild. In order to increase the production of high-quality Chinese medicinal materials in a planned way, all regions have attached great importance to promoting the experience of artificial cultivation of wild medicinal crops this year.



**Named Entities**

贵州省   Guizhou Sheng (Guizhou Province)
杜仲   Dùzhòng (*Eucommia ulmoides*, hardy rubber tree)
厚朴   Hòupò (*Magnolia officinalis*, magnolia bark)
中国   Zhōngguó (China)

### 贵州省积极种植药用作物

1966-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

    贵州省积极种植药用作物
    我国药材著名产地之一的贵州，正在积极种植各种药用作物。
    入春以来，这个省已经种植各种药用作物一万零八百多亩。其中，杜仲有四百九十多万棵，厚朴十五万多棵。种植面积都比去年同期增加很多。
    贵州出产的中药材，百分之九十以上是野生的。为了有计划地增产优质中药材，今年各地很重视推广野生药用作物人工栽培的经验。
