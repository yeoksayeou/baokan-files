Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds Tea Party to Welcome Tanzanian Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, July 7th -- The All-China Women's Federation held a tea party today to warmly welcome Mrs. Paul Bomani, wife of the Tanzanian Minister of Economic Affairs and Development Planning, and Mrs. Kunabi, executive member of the All-Tanzania Women's Federation.
    Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, delivered a speech at the tea party. She said that the Chinese and Tanzanian peoples have a profound friendship. Chinese women resolutely support the Tanzanian people and women in their struggle against imperialism.
    In her speech, Mrs. Paul Bomani said that the friendship between the Tanzanian and Chinese peoples and women is very sincere. She hoped that this visit would further promote understanding and enhance friendship between the women of the two countries.
    Also present at the tea party were Shen Zijiou, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, Fang Qing, Deputy Director of the Beijing Women's Federation, as well as other women leaders and female advanced producers.

### 我妇联茶会欢迎坦桑尼亚客人

1966-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇联茶会欢迎坦桑尼亚客人
    新华社七日讯　全国妇联今天举行茶会，热烈欢迎坦桑尼亚经济事务和发展计划部长保罗·博马尼的夫人和坦桑尼亚全国妇联执委库纳比夫人。
    全国妇联副主席许广平在茶会上致词。她说，中国人民和坦桑尼亚人民有着深厚的友谊。中国妇女坚决支持坦桑尼亚人民和妇女反对帝国主义的斗争。
    保罗·博马尼夫人在致词中说，坦桑尼亚和中国两国人民和妇女之间的友谊是十分真诚的。她希望，通过这次访问，进一步促进两国妇女的了解，增进友谊。
    出席茶会的还有全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九、北京市妇联副主任方青以及其他妇女界负责人和女先进生产者。
