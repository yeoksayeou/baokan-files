Title: Comrade Zhou Enlai Speaks at the Durres Mass Rally
Author:
Date: 1966-06-28
Edition: 2

	Dear Comrade Muhammad Shehu, dear Comrade Rita Marko, dear Comrade Tomin Yako, and dear comrades and friends,

	On my third visit to your country of the Eagle, I have the opportunity to come to the heroic and beautiful city of Durres in Albania, and I am very happy. The entire city has turned out, and the streets are filled with people, welcoming us in a grand and enthusiastic manner. We are deeply moved. On behalf of the entire delegation of the Chinese Party and Government, I express our heartfelt thanks to you.

	Durres City has a glorious revolutionary tradition. The people of Durres wrote a brilliant chapter in the struggle against fascist aggression and for national liberation. After liberation, as Albania's gateway to the sea, you have played an important role in Albania's socialist construction. Durres is also famous as the "Port of Sino-Albanian Friendship." Comrade Shehu has often said: Between China and Albania, there is a bridge of friendship spanning the ocean, with one pillar in Guangzhou and the other in Durres. You have made valuable contributions to the development of maritime transportation between our two countries, enhancing the friendship and cooperation between China and Albania. Here, on behalf of the Chinese people, I express my highest respect to you.

	Every time we visit your country, we have different and fresh feelings.

	Under the correct leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha and the Albanian Labor Party, the Albanian people have relied on their own efforts and struggled hard, overcoming various difficulties, and achieved brilliant results in socialist construction. Recently, you have launched a vigorous mass revolutionary movement, and the whole country presents a new and vigorous atmosphere. We believe that the series of revolutionary measures you have taken will become a great material force, pushing your socialist cause to new heights.

	The Albanian Labor Party and the Albanian people continue to raise the banner of opposing imperialism led by the United States and the reactionaries of various countries, firmly supporting the revolutionary struggles of the oppressed peoples and nations of the world. You have raised the banner of opposing modern revisionism centered on the Soviet Communist Party leadership even higher, making important contributions to defending the purity of Marxism-Leninism. Your international status is higher, and your international influence is greater. You are respected and praised by the revolutionary people of the world.

	Although the two countries of China and Albania are thousands of miles apart, a common ideal and a common struggle bind us closely together. Our two parties, two countries, and two peoples have formed an eternal and unbreakable combat unity and revolutionary friendship in the struggle against imperialism led by the United States and the reactionaries of various countries, in the struggle against modern revisionism centered on the Soviet Communist Party leadership, and in the cause of socialist revolution and socialist construction in our two countries. We are class brothers of the proletariat. Our unity and friendship are based on Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, and they are tested. We will always stand together, fight together, hand in hand, and shoulder to shoulder, to strive for the victory of the world proletarian revolutionary cause.

	Long live the great unity of the two parties, two countries, and two peoples of China and Albania!
	Long live the heroic Albanian people!
	Long live the strong People's Republic of Albania!
	Long live the glorious Albanian Labor Party!
	Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha, the beloved leader of the Albanian people and the close friend of the Chinese people!
	Long live the invincible Marxism-Leninism!

### 周恩来同志在都拉斯群众大会上讲话  阿尔巴尼亚高举反帝反修的旗帜受到世界革命人民的尊敬和赞扬

1966-06-28
第2版()
专栏：

    周恩来同志在都拉斯群众大会上讲话
    阿尔巴尼亚高举反帝反修的旗帜受到世界革命人民的尊敬和赞扬亲爱的穆罕默德·谢胡同志，亲爱的里塔·马尔科同志，亲爱的托宁·亚科瓦同志，亲爱的同志们，朋友们：
    在我第三次访问你们山鹰之国的时候，有机会来到英雄的、美丽的阿尔巴尼亚名城都拉斯，感到非常高兴。你们全城出动，万人空巷，盛大热烈地欢迎我们，我们深为感动。我代表中国党政代表团全体同志，向你们表示衷心的感谢。
    都拉斯市具有光荣的革命传统。都拉斯人民在反抗法西斯侵略、争取民族解放的战争中，写下了光辉的篇章。解放以后，你们作为阿尔巴尼亚的海上门户，在阿尔巴尼亚的社会主义建设事业中，占有重要的地位。都拉斯又以“中阿友谊之港”著称。谢胡同志多次讲过：中阿之间有一座横架海洋的友谊大桥，它有两个桥墩，一个在广州，一个在都拉斯。你们为发展中阿两国的海上交通，增进中阿两国的友谊和合作，作出了宝贵的贡献。在这里，我以中国人民的名义，向你们致以崇高的敬意。
    我们每一次访问你们的国家，都有不同的、新鲜的感觉。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的正确领导下，自力更生、艰苦奋斗，克服了种种困难，在社会主义建设事业中取得了辉煌的成就。最近，你们掀起了一个轰轰烈烈的群众性革命化运动，全国出现了一片生气勃勃、干劲冲天的新气象。我们相信，你们采取的一系列革命化措施，必将变成巨大的物质力量，把你们的社会主义事业推向新的高涨。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚人民继续高举反对以美国为首的帝国主义和各国反动派的旗帜，坚决支持全世界被压迫人民和被压迫民族的革命斗争。你们更高地举起反对以苏共领导集团为中心的现代修正主义的旗帜，为保卫马克思列宁主义的纯洁性作出了重要的贡献。你们的国际地位更高了，你们的国际影响更大了。你们受到全世界革命人民的尊敬和赞扬。
    中阿两国虽然相距万里，但是，共同的理想和共同的斗争把我们紧紧地联系起来。我们两党、两国和两国人民，在反对以美国为首的帝国主义和各国反动派的斗争中，在反对以苏共领导集团为中心的现代修正主义的斗争中，在两国社会主义革命和社会主义建设的事业中，结成了永恒的、牢不可破的战斗团结和革命友谊。我们是无产阶级的阶级兄弟。我们的团结和友谊是建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义基础上的，是经得起考验的。我们将永远团结在一起，战斗在一起，手携手，肩并肩，为争取全世界无产阶级革命事业的胜利，奋勇前进。
    中阿两党、两国和两国人民的伟大团结万岁！
    英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民万岁！
    坚强的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国万岁！
    光荣的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党万岁！
    阿尔巴尼亚人民的敬爱领袖、中国人民的亲密朋友恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！
    战无不胜的马克思列宁主义万岁！
