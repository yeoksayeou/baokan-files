Title: Malian Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Home

Author:

Date: 1966-06-17
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 15th: The Malian delegation led by Mamadou Madeira Keita, member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union Party of Mali and Minister of Justice of Mali, concluded its friendly visit to our country and left Shanghai by plane tonight for home.
    Yang Lin, Deputy Director of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission, and Lai Yali, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, saw the distinguished guests off at the airport.
    Zhang Chengzong, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, and responsible persons from relevant departments also saw them off at the airport. Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished guests.
    Guisse Tidiani, the Malian Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 马里代表团离上海回国

1966-06-17
第5版()
专栏：

    马里代表团离上海回国
    据新华社上海十五日电　由马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、马里司法部长马马杜·马戴拉·凯塔率领的马里代表团，结束了在我国的友好访问，今晚乘飞机离开上海回国。
    对外经委副主任杨琳、外交部礼宾司司长赖亚力到机场欢送贵宾。
    到机场欢送的，还有上海市副市长张承宗以及各有关方面负责人。少先队员向贵宾们献了花。
    马里驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼也到机场送行。
