Title: Under Strong Public Protest in Belgium, Belgian Authorities Forced to Release Comrade Grippa and Others
Author:
Date: 1966-06-08
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, June 6th -- Under strong public protest in Belgium, Brussels police authorities were forced to release Comrade Grippa, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, and others who were illegally arrested during the demonstration on the 5th, on the evening of the 6th.
    Comrade Grippa and others participated in a large-scale demonstration of more than 1,500 people in Belgium on the 5th, strongly protesting against the American conspiracy to move the institutions of the North Atlantic group from Paris to Brussels, and demanding the removal of all military bases established by the United States and other countries in Belgium. The police carried out brutal suppression of this demonstration and arrested many people.

### 在比利时公众的强烈抗议下  比利时当局被迫释放格里巴同志等

1966-06-08
第5版()
专栏：

    在比利时公众的强烈抗议下
    比利时当局被迫释放格里巴同志等
    新华社布鲁塞尔六日电　在比利时公众的强烈抗议下，布鲁塞尔警察当局被迫在六日晚释放了在五日示威游行中被非法逮捕的比利时共产党中央委员会书记格里巴同志以及其他被逮捕的人。
    格里巴同志等五日参加了比利时公众一千五百多人举行的声势浩大的示威游行，强烈抗议美国阴谋把北大西洋集团的机构从巴黎迁到布鲁塞尔，并且要求撤除美国和其他国家在比利时设立的一切军事基地。警察对这次示威游行进行了残暴的镇压，并且逮捕了很多人。
