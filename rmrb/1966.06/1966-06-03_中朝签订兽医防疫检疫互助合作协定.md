Title: China and North Korea Sign Veterinary Epidemic Prevention and Quarantine Mutual Assistance and Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1966-06-03
Edition: 5

    China and North Korea Sign Veterinary Epidemic Prevention and Quarantine Mutual Assistance and Cooperation Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 1st -- The Veterinary Epidemic Prevention and Quarantine Mutual Assistance and Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea was signed in Pyongyang on June 1, 1966.
    Jiao Ruoyu, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and Zheng Youhao, Vice Chairman of the Korean Agricultural Commission, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.
    The agreement stipulates that both sides should strengthen their close cooperation in veterinary epidemic prevention and quarantine.

### 中朝签订兽医防疫检疫互助合作协定

1966-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    中朝签订兽医防疫检疫互助合作协定
    新华社平壤一日电　中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府兽医防疫检疫互助合作协定已在一九六六年六月一日在平壤签字。
    中国驻朝鲜大使焦若愚和朝鲜农业委员会副委员长郑有镐分别代表两国政府在协定上签了字。
    协定规定双方要加强在兽医防疫、检疫方面的相互密切协作。
