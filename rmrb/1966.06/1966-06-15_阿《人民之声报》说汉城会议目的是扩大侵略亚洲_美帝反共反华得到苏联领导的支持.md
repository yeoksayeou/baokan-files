Title: Albanian *People's Voice* Says Seoul Conference Aimed at Expanding Aggression in Asia; U.S. Imperialism's Anti-Communist and Anti-China Actions Supported by Soviet Leaders
Author:
Date: 1966-06-15
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 12th: The Albanian newspaper *People's Voice* published an article on the 12th stating that the so-called "Ministerial Conference for the Asian and Pacific Region" was carefully planned by the United States. The article said that U.S. imperialism, through aggressive military treaties such as the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, and the ANZUS Security Treaty, as well as utilizing the United Nations with the cooperation of the Khrushchev revisionists, plans and carries out aggression and enslaves the people of various countries. The Seoul conference is merely another step in the desperate struggle and new adventures of the American aggressors.
    The article pointed out that the political and strategic goals of U.S. imperialism in Asia, as well as its attempt to establish new aggressive blocs, lie in: opposing communism, attacking the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China, opposing the progressive and liberation movements on the Asian continent, making Asians fight Asians, and realizing American domination of the world. The Seoul conference planned by the United States is clearly marked with anti-China, anti-Vietnam, and anti-peace sentiments, and its purpose is to further expand the aggression of the U.S. imperialists in the Asian region. The article condemned the Khrushchev revisionists for colluding with the U.S. imperialists to oppose the People's Republic of China and other socialist countries in Asia. The article pointed out that the Khrushchev revisionists are playing a shameless role in the U.S. imperialists' plans of aggression against the People's Republic of China and other Asian countries. Their verbal “opposition” to the Seoul conference is merely a smokescreen used to deceive people.

### 阿《人民之声报》说汉城会议目的是扩大侵略亚洲  美帝反共反华得到苏联领导的支持

1966-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    阿《人民之声报》说汉城会议目的是扩大侵略亚洲
    美帝反共反华得到苏联领导的支持
    据新华社地拉那十二日电　阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》十二日发表文章说，所谓“亚洲和太平洋地区部长会议”是美国精心策划的。文章说，美帝国主义通过北大西洋公约组织、东南亚条约组织和美澳新“安全条约”等侵略性军事条约，同时在赫鲁晓夫修正主义者的合作下利用联合国策划和进行侵略，奴役各国人民。汉城会议只不过是美国侵略者进行绝望挣扎和进行新的冒险的又一步骤。
    文章指出，美帝国主义在亚洲的政治和战略目的以及企图建立新的侵略集团的目的在于：反对共产主义，进攻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和中华人民共和国，反对亚洲大陆的进步运动和解放运动，使亚洲人打亚洲人，实现美国对世界的统治。美国策划的汉城会议明显地打上了反华、反对越南、反对和平的标记，其目的在于进一步扩大美帝国主义者对亚洲地区的侵略。文章谴责赫鲁晓夫修正主义者与美帝国主义者互相勾结，反对中华人民共和国和亚洲其它社会主义国家。文章指出，赫鲁晓夫修正主义者在美帝国主义者反对中华人民共和国和亚洲其它国家的侵略计划中扮演着无耻的角色。他们在口头上也“反对”汉城会议，但是这只不过是他们用以骗人的烟幕。
