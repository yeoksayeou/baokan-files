Title: Spring Thunder Promoting the Cultural Revolution at Peking University
Author: Zheng Yusen, Chai Zhenjiang, Fan Yongtian, Wang Qingkuan, Ma Shufen
Date: 1966-06-05
Edition: 2

    On June 2nd, the vast cadres and masses of the Yuegezhuang People's Commune in Fangshan County saw in the *People's Daily* the big-character poster put up by Nie Yuanzi and six other comrades from Peking University, exposing a major conspiracy by the "Three-Family Village" anti-Party, anti-socialist gang and dragging the anti-Party, anti-socialist black gang elements Lu Ping and Peng Peiyun out of the gutter. Your big-character poster is a sharp steel knife that has stabbed straight into another vital spot of this black gang, destroying their important stronghold at Peking University. Your big-character poster represents the will of the proletarian revolutionaries at Peking University. Like a spring thunder, it pushed the cultural revolution movement at Peking University to a climax, creating a vigorous and revolutionary new face for Peking University. Chairman Mao teaches us that thorough materialists are fearless, "Dare to unhorse the emperor." The seven of you are good comrades taught by Chairman Mao, strong proletarian fighters, unafraid of violence, unafraid of tigers and wolves, daring to revolt, daring to struggle. No matter if they are Party committee secretaries, presidents, or "authorities," or "scholars," as long as they are anti-Party, anti-people, and anti-socialist, they are the enemies of our workers, peasants, and revolutionary intellectuals. All revolutionaries must stand up and resolutely struggle against them. The vast cadres and commune members of our commune greatly admire your noble revolutionary spirit. Your actions represent the wishes of our vast poor and lower-middle peasants. We firmly support your revolutionary actions. All cadres and masses of our Yuegezhuang Commune stand with the proletarian revolutionaries of Peking University, holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, carrying hoes, and wielding pens, resolutely fighting the "Three-Family Village" black gang to the end.
    The new Beijing Municipal Committee has already decided to dismiss Lu Ping and Peng Peiyun from their posts and has sent a work team to lead the cultural revolution movement at Peking University. We firmly support this decisive and wise decision of the new Beijing Municipal Committee. Revolutionary Peking University must stride forward on the broad road of the socialist cultural revolution.
    Yuegezhuang Commune, Fangshan County, Beijing Municipality
    Zheng Yusen, Chai Zhenjiang, Fan Yongtian, Wang Qingkuan, Ma Shufen

### 推动北大文化革命的春雷
郑玉森　柴振江　范永田  王庆宽　马淑芬
1966-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

    推动北大文化革命的春雷
    六月二日，我们房山县岳各庄人民公社广大干部和群众，在《人民日报》上看到了北京大学聂元梓等七位同志贴出的大字报，揭穿了“三家村”黑帮分子反党反社会主义的一个大阴谋，把反党反社会主义的黑帮分子陆平、彭珮云从阴沟里揪出来了。你们的这张大字报是一把锐利的钢刀，一下子捅入了这伙黑帮分子的又一个心房，把他们在北京大学的重要据点捣毁了。你们这张大字报代表了北京大学的无产阶级革命派的意志，春雷一声，把北京大学的文化革命运动推向了高潮，给北京大学开创了一个生气蓬勃的革命新面貌。毛主席教导我们，彻底的唯物主义者是无所畏惧的，“舍得一身剐，敢把皇帝拉下马”。你们七位同志是毛主席教导出来的好同志，是无产阶级的坚强战士，不畏强暴，不怕虎狼，敢于革命，敢于斗争，不管他们是什么党委书记，什么校长，还是什么“权威”，什么“学者”，只要他们反党反人民反社会主义，他们就是我们工人、农民和革命知识分子的敌人。一切革命者都要挺身而出，坚决同他们进行斗争。我们公社的广大干部和社员群众对你们这种高尚的革命精神非常敬佩，你们的行动代表了我们广大贫下中农的心愿。我们坚决支持你们的革命行动。我们岳各庄公社全体干部和群众，和北大的无产阶级革命派站在一起，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，扛起锄头，拿起笔杆，坚决和“三家村”黑帮分子斗争到底。
    新的北京市委已经决定撤销陆平、彭珮云的职务，派去工作组领导北京大学的文化革命运动，我们坚决拥护新的北京市委这一果断而英明的决定。革命的北京大学一定要在社会主义文化大革命的大道上阔步前进。
    北京市房山县岳各庄公社
    郑玉森　柴振江　范永田  王庆宽　马淑芬
