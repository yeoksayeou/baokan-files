Title: Japanese Writer Representative Nakajima Kenzō Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-21
Edition: 5

    Japanese writer representative Nakajima Kenzō Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Nakajima Kenzō, the Japanese representative to the Asian-African Writers' Emergency Meeting, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity and President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, as well as responsible persons from relevant departments, including Chu Tunan, Liu Baiyu, and Ba Jin, were at the airport to welcome them.
    Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also at the airport to greet them.

### 日本作家代表中岛健藏到京

1966-06-21
第5版()
专栏：

    日本作家代表中岛健藏到京
    新华社二十日讯　参加亚非作家紧急会议的日本代表中岛健藏和他的夫人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    中国亚非团结委员会主席、中日友好协会会长廖承志，以及有关方面负责人楚图南、刘白羽、巴金等到机场欢迎。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到机场迎接。
