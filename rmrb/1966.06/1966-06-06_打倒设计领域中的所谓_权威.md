Title: Down with the So-Called "Authorities" in the Design Field

Author:

Date: 1966-06-06
Edition: 3

    We resolutely support the wise decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to reorganize the Beijing Municipal Party Committee. This decision smashed the den of the "Three-Family Village" black gang and exposed the conspiracy of the former Beijing Municipal Party Committee to implement a revisionist line in a vain attempt to restore capitalism. We warmly hail the new victory of Mao Zedong Thought!

    In the realm of design ideology, there has long existed a black line against the Party and against socialism. Bourgeois "experts" and "authorities" have usurped leadership over some design positions. Design work has long been restricted by Soviet revisionist frameworks and influenced by bourgeois ideology. There exists a serious and sharp struggle between two ideologies and two roads. In this severe class struggle, we are determined to, together with the broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers across the country, hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, thoroughly destroy the black line in the design ideology field, and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!
                        The Deyang Working Group of the Eighth Academy of the First Ministry of Machine Building

### 打倒设计领域中的所谓“权威”

1966-06-06
第3版()
专栏：

    打倒设计领域中的所谓“权威”
    我们坚决拥护中共中央改组北京市委的英明决定，这一决定捣毁了“三家村”黑店的老巢，揭穿了前北京市委推行修正主义路线妄图实现资本主义复辟的阴谋。我们热烈欢呼毛泽东思想的新胜利！
    在设计思想领域中，长期来也同样存在一条反党反社会主义的黑线，资产阶级“专家”“权威”篡夺了一些设计阵地的领导权，设计工作长期受苏联修正主义框框束缚，受资产阶级思想影响，存在着严重的尖锐的两种思想两条道路的斗争，在这场严峻的阶级斗争中，我们坚决和全国广大工农兵群众一道，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，彻底摧毁设计思想领域中的黑线，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！
                        一机部八院德阳工作组
