	Albanian Water Power Delegation Arrives in Beijing

	New China News Agency, June 26th

	At the invitation of our country, the Albanian Water Power Delegation, led by its head, Deputy Minister of Construction and Engineering of Albania, Sha Li An Guo Ni, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Vice Minister of Water Resources and Electric Power, Cheng Ming Sheng, and Commissioner of the Foreign Economic Liaison Commission, Chang Yan Qing, and other relevant department heads. Four members of the delegation who arrived earlier and the Charge d'Affaires of the Albanian Embassy in China, Ba Ba Ni, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 阿尔巴尼亚水电代表团到京

1966-06-27
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚水电代表团到京
    新华社二十六日讯　应邀前来我国的阿尔巴尼亚水电代表团，由团长、阿尔巴尼亚建筑工程部副部长沙立·安果尼率领，于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。去机场欢迎的，有水利电力部副部长程明升、对外经济联络委员会委员常彦卿等有关部门负责人。先期到京的代表团的四名团员和阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办巴巴尼等也前往机场迎接。
