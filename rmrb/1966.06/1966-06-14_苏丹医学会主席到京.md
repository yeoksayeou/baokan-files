Title: Chairman of the Sudanese Medical Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-06-14
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 12th - At the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association, Dr. Mohammed Hamad Satti, Chairman of the Sudanese Medical Association and Director of the Medical Research Institute of the Sudanese Ministry of Health, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 11th for a visit to China.



**Named Entities**

苏丹医学会 Sudanese Medical Association
中华医学会 Chinese Medical Association
苏丹卫生部 Sudanese Ministry of Health
穆罕默德·哈马德·萨提 Mohammed Hamad Satti
北京 Beijing
新华社 Xinhua News Agency

### 苏丹医学会主席到京

1966-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏丹医学会主席到京
    新华社十二日讯　应中华医学会邀请来我国访问的苏丹医学会主席、苏丹卫生部医学研究所所长穆罕默德·哈马德·萨提医师，已在十一日晚乘飞机到达北京。
