Title: Our Scientific and Technological Delegation Leaves Beijing for Germany and Romania
Author:
Date: 1966-06-26
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, June 25th. The Chinese Scientific and Technological Delegation, participating in the 10th session of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical Science Cooperation and the 11th session of the Sino-Romanian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Committee, left Beijing by plane today, led by Zhou Wenlong, head of the delegation and Vice Minister of the Petroleum Industry.



**Named Entities**

中国    China

### 我科学技术代表团离京赴德、罗

1966-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    我科学技术代表团离京赴德、罗
    新华社二十五日讯　参加中德技术和技术科学合作常任委员会第十届会议和中罗科学技术合作委员会第十一届会议的中国科学技术代表团，在团长、石油工业部副部长周文龙率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京。
