Title: The President of Mali Receives the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires and Others
Author:
Date: 1968-02-08
Edition: 6

The President of the Republic of Mali and Chairman of the National Committee for the Defense of the Malian Revolution, Modibo Keita, received the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires, Liu Helin, and Chinese agricultural technicians working in Mali on the afternoon of the 6th, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

During the conversation, Modibo Keita expressed satisfaction with the results of China's technical assistance in the field of agriculture to Mali. He hoped that the two countries' technical cooperation in agriculture would achieve new developments.

### 马里总统接见我临时代办等

1968-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

   马里总统接见我临时代办等
　 新华社巴马科六日电　马里共和国总统、马里保卫革命全国委员会主席莫迪博·凯塔六日下午接见了中国驻马里大使馆临时代办刘和林和在马里工作的中国农业技术工作人员，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    在谈话中，莫迪博·凯塔对中国在农业方面向马里提供的技术援助的效果表示满意。他希望两国在农业方面的技术合作得到新的发展。
