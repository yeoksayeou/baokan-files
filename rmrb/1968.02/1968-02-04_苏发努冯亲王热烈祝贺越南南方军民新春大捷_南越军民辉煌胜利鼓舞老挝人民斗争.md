Title: Prince Souphanouvong Warmly Congratulates the Vietnamese People and Army in the South on Their Spring Victory
Author:
Date: 1968-02-04
Edition: 5

	Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, sent a telegram on February 1 to Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, warmly congratulating the Vietnamese people and army in the South on their spring victory.

	The telegram said: The heroic actions taken by the Vietnamese people and army in the South to punish the American aggressors and their lackeys have further pushed the enemy into a difficult situation in their aggressive war. These actions have created extremely favorable conditions for the people of South Vietnam to achieve greater victories in their struggle to liberate South Vietnam.

	The telegram said: The brilliant victories won by the Vietnamese people and army in the South are a great inspiration to the Lao people who are defeating the American imperialists and their Lao lackeys.

	The telegram wished the heroic Vietnamese people and army in the South, under the leadership of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, to achieve even greater victories in their struggle to completely liberate the South and reunify the country.

### 苏发努冯亲王热烈祝贺越南南方军民新春大捷  南越军民辉煌胜利鼓舞老挝人民斗争

1968-02-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯亲王热烈祝贺越南南方军民新春大捷
    南越军民辉煌胜利鼓舞老挝人民斗争
    新华社康开三日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席苏发努冯亲王一日打电报给越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团阮友寿主席，热烈祝贺越南南方军民取得新春大捷。
    电报说：越南南方军民为了给美国侵略者及其走狗以应有的惩罚而采取的英勇行动，使得敌人在侵略战争中进一步陷入困境。这些行动为越南南方人民在解放越南南方的战斗中取得更大的胜利创造了极为有利的条件。
    电报说：越南南方军民所取得的辉煌胜利，对正在打败美帝及其老挝走狗的老挝人民是巨大的鼓舞。
    电报祝愿英雄的越南南方军民在越南南方民族解放阵线领导下，为彻底解放南方，重新统一祖国而赢得更加伟大的胜利。
