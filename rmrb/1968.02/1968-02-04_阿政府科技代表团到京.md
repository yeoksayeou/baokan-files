Title: Albanian Government Science and Technology Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-02-04
Edition: 6

The Albanian government science and technology delegation, which came to China to participate in the 12th session of the Sino-Albanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. The delegation is led by Prokop Murra, the head of the delegation, the chairman of the Albanian side of the Sino-Albanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation, and the deputy chairman of the State Planning Commission of the People's Republic of Albania.

The welcoming party at the airport included Sun Xiaofeng, the chairman of the Chinese side of the Sino-Albanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation and deputy minister of the Petroleum Industry, as well as all members of the Chinese side.

Albanian Ambassador to China Nataneli and others were also at the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 阿政府科技代表团到京

1968-02-04
第6版()
专栏：

    阿政府科技代表团到京
    新华社三日讯　前来我国参加中阿科学技术合作联合委员会第十二届会议的阿尔巴尼亚政府科学技术代表团，在团长、中阿科学技术合作联合委员会阿方组主席、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国家计划委员会副主席普罗科普·穆拉的率领下，于今天上午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中阿科学技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、石油部副部长孙晓风以及中国组的全体成员。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使纳塔奈利等也到机场迎接。
