Title: Stalin: Speech at the First Congress of Shock Brigades of the USSR Collective Farms
Author: 斯大林: (Stalin)
Date: 1968-02-06
Edition: 4

    Youth is our future, our hope. Youth should take over from us old people. Youth should carry our banner to the final victory.

    Stalin: Speech at the First Congress of Shock Brigades of the USSR Collective Farms
