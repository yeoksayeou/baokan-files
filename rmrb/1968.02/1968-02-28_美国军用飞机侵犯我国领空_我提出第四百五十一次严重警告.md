Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Violates China's Airspace; 451st Serious Warning Issued
Author:
Date: 1968-02-28
Edition: 6

U.S. military aircraft violated China's airspace.

On February 27, at 8:40 AM to 8:46 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above the territorial waters east of Nan'ao Island, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 11:17 AM to 11:21 AM, another U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issued the 451st serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵犯我国领空  我提出第四百五十一次严重警告

1968-02-28
第6版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵犯我国领空
    我提出第四百五十一次严重警告
    新华社二十七日讯　二月二十七日八时四十分至八时四十六分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省南澳岛以东地区领海上空。同日十一时十七分至十一时二十一分，又有美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百五十一次严重警告。
