Title: The Song of the Linemen
Author: Gù Shùnzhāng
Date: 1968-02-01
Edition: 4

We stand on the ground,
Erecting power poles;
We soar in the clouds,
Sweating profusely...

Soaring through the clouds, diving into the sea,
To the countryside, up the mountains...
Wherever the Party points,
We display our skills there!

Thunder roars for a thousand miles,
Lightning flashes for ten thousand miles,
Under the blue sky,
We watch the world's changing tides...

The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Makes all reactionaries tremble with fear,
A handful of capitalist roaders within the Party,
Are defeated, lying flat on the ground...

The joyous news of great unity,
Flies to Beijing;
Chairman Mao's voice,
Warms every comrade's heart...

Happiness-filled power poles,
Stretch out in all directions;
Magical golden wires,
Connect factories, villages, and mines...

We bear the mission of the Cultural Revolution,
Flying from one cloud to another;
With the revolutionary spirit of rebellion,
We leap from one power pole to another...

Let Mao Zedong Thought,
Conquer the entire universe;
Let the revolutionary call,
Echo through rivers and mountains...

We stand on the ground,
Determined!
We soar in the clouds,
Overcoming all difficulties...

### 架线工之歌
顾顺章
1968-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

  架线工之歌
  北京电子管厂　顾顺章
  我们在地面，
  竖起电杆；
  我们在云端，
  挥洒热汗……
  腾云，入海，
  下乡，上山……
  党指向那里，
  就在那里把本领施展！
  千里雷声，
  万里电闪，
  我们头顶蓝天，
  注视世界风云变幻……
  无产阶级文化大革命，
  使一切反动派心惊胆战，
  党内一小撮走资派，
  被杀得个人仰马翻！……
  大联合的喜讯，
  飞往北京；
  毛主席的声音，
  温暖每个同志的心坎……
  幸福的电杆，
  向四面八方伸展；
  神奇的金线，
  连接工厂、农村、矿山……
  我们肩负文化大革命的使命，
  从这个云头飞向那个云头；
  我们满怀革命造反派的豪情，
  从这根电杆跨上那根电杆……
  让毛泽东思想，
  占领整个宇宙；
  让革命的呼声，
  响彻万水千山……
  我们在地面，
  下定决心！
  我们在云端，
  排除万难！……
