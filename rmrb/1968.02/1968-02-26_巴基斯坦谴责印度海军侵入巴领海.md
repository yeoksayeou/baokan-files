Title: Pakistan Condemns Indian Navy's Infringement of Pakistani Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1968-02-26
Edition: 6

Pakistan condemns Indian Navy's infringement of Pakistani territorial waters

Pakistan's Minister of Engineering, Food, and Agriculture, Shamso Doda, stated on the 23rd in Karachi: The Indian Navy recently invaded Pakistani territorial waters, illegally seizing 22 Pakistani trawlers and detaining 400 Pakistani fishermen.

He said: The Pakistani government has sufficient evidence that the Indian Navy invaded Pakistani territorial waters and seized the trawlers. The Pakistani government will negotiate with the Indian government to immediately release the trawlers and fishermen illegally detained by the Indian Navy.

### 巴基斯坦谴责印度海军侵入巴领海

1968-02-26
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    巴基斯坦谴责印度海军侵入巴领海
    巴基斯坦工程、粮食和农业部长沙姆索多哈二十三日在卡拉奇说：印度海军最近侵入巴基斯坦领海，非法劫走了巴基斯坦的二十二艘拖网渔船，并且扣留了四百名巴基斯坦渔民。
    他说：巴基斯坦政府掌握了充分的证据，说明印度海军侵入巴基斯坦领海劫走拖网渔船。巴基斯坦政府要同印度政府交涉，要它立即释放被印度海军非法扣留的拖网渔船和渔民。
