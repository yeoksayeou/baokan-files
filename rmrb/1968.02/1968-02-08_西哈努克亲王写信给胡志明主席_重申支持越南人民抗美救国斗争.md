Title: Prince Sihanouk Writes to Chairman Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1968-02-08
Edition: 5

	Prince Sihanouk of Cambodia recently wrote a letter to Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, reaffirming his support for the Vietnamese people's struggle against American imperialism and for national liberation.

	The letter states: "The patriotic struggle you are leading with the greatest determination and courage is being watched with deep admiration by all Khmer people who cherish independence the most. Therefore, I want to reaffirm our fraternal support and wish you victory in your war, which is also ours, and the war of all peoples against imperialism and neo-colonialism."

### 西哈努克亲王写信给胡志明主席  重申支持越南人民抗美救国斗争

1968-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王写信给胡志明主席
    重申支持越南人民抗美救国斗争
    新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社三日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王最近写信给越南民主共和国主席胡志明，重申支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争。
    信中说：“你以最大的决心和勇气领导的这场爱国斗争，受到最珍视独立的所有高棉人怀着强烈敬佩心情的注视。因此，我要向你重申我们的兄弟般的支持，并祝你们在你们的、也是我们的、也是反对帝国主义和新殖民主义统治的各国人民的战争中取得胜利。”
