Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-02-28
Edition: 6

The anti-aircraft forces of Hanoi, the capital of North Vietnam, shot down one invading U.S. jet fighter on February 24 while repelling U.S. air pirates. Earlier, on February 23, the military and civilians of Ninh Binh and Bac Ninh provinces each shot down one invading U.S. pirate aircraft.

### 越南北方军民击落美机三架

1968-02-28
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机三架
    新华社河内二十四日电　据这里报道，越南北方河内市防空部队，二十四日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。在这以前，义安省和河北省军民，二十三日也曾击落入侵的美国强盗飞机各一架。
