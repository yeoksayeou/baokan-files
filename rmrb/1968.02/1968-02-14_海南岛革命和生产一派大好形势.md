Title: A Great Situation of Revolution and Production in Hainan Island
Author:
Date: 1968-02-14
Edition: 4

Hainan Island, standing at the forefront of the South China Sea, is experiencing a great situation of revolution and production under the guidance of the great leader Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line. From the Wuzhishan area to the coastal plains, Chairman Mao's latest instructions have deeply penetrated the hearts of the people. Revolutionary unity is rapidly developing, and spring plowing and rice transplanting are being carried out with great enthusiasm. The eastern part of Hainan Island, which traditionally has an early rice transplanting season, has already completed 70% of the transplanting area in some regions.

### 海南岛革命和生产一派大好形势

1968-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    海南岛革命和生产一派大好形势
    屹立在祖国南海前哨的海南岛，在伟大领袖毛主席的无产阶级革命路线指引下，革命和生产呈现一派大好形势。从五指山区到沿海平原，毛主席的最新指示深入人心，革命大联合迅速发展，春耕插秧搞得热气腾腾。历来插秧季节较早的海南岛东部一线，有些地区已完成插秧面积百分之七十。
