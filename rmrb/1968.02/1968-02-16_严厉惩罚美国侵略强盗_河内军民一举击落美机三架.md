Title: Severe Punishment for American Aggressors: Hanoi Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three U.S. Planes
Author:
Date: 1968-02-16
Edition: 5

The heroic military and civilians of Hanoi severely punished the invading American aggressors with fierce artillery fire this afternoon, shooting down three enemy jet planes in one go.

At 3:45 PM on the 14th, American aggressors dispatched planes to bomb the residential areas in the northern suburbs of Hanoi and fired missiles at various locations within the city. The military and civilians of Hanoi immediately retaliated with a fierce counterattack, celebrating the seventh anniversary of the establishment of the armed forces of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam by shooting down three enemy planes.

On the same day, the alert military and civilians of Phu Tho Province shot down one invading American aggressor plane. The military and civilians of Nghe An Province also shot down two enemy planes.

On the 12th, the military and civilians of Lao Cai Province shot down one enemy plane.

### 严厉惩罚美国侵略强盗  河内军民一举击落美机三架

1968-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    严厉惩罚美国侵略强盗
    河内军民一举击落美机三架
    新华社河内十四日电　据这里今晚公布：英雄的河内市军民十四日下午用猛烈的炮火给予来犯的美国强盗以严厉的惩罚，一举击落敌人喷气式飞机三架。
    十四日下午三时四十五分，美国强盗派遣飞机轰炸河内市北郊居民区，并向市区的许多地方发射导弹。河内市军民立即给予迎头痛击，用击落三架敌机的成绩庆祝越南南方人民解放武装力量统一日七周年。
    在同一天，保持高度警惕的富寿省军民，击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。义安省军民也击落敌机两架。
    十二日谅山省军民击落敌机一架。
