Title: Under the Call of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, Patriotic Soldiers of the Saigon Puppet Army Revolt
Author:
Date: 1968-02-04
Edition: 5

Under the call of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, patriotic soldiers of the Saigon puppet army have been revolting in large numbers. The revolting soldiers have expressed their determination to fight to the end against the U.S.-Saigon puppet clique.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, February 3rd – According to the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, under the call of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, patriotic soldiers of the Saigon puppet army have recently been revolting in large numbers and turning to the side of the people.

	The report said, on January 31st, patriotic soldiers of the 3rd Battalion of the 33rd Regiment of the 21st Division of the puppet army in Soc Trang City, Soc Trang Province, seized the enemy's ammunition depot and carried all the weapons to surrender to the people. They cooperated with the people's liberation armed forces and the people of Soc Trang City to occupy one street after another, eliminating many puppet police and thugs, causing the enemy in the city to be in chaos and panic.

	Another report said, 103 patriotic soldiers of the 1st Division of the Saigon puppet army recently also resolutely left the enemy's ranks and surrendered to the people. They issued a statement on January 20th, expressing their determination to participate in the anti-American national salvation struggle under the glorious banner of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

	The statement said: We, 103 people, are extremely happy to have escaped from the claws of the American bandits and the puppet clique of Nguyen Van Thieu and Nguyen Cao Ky, and to have gained the affection and care of our fellow countrymen. They expressed their determination to fight to the death with the American invaders and the puppet clique of Nguyen Van Thieu and Nguyen Cao Ky.

### 在越南南方民族解放阵线的号召下  南越伪军爱国士兵纷纷起义  起义士兵表示决心与美伪集团斗争到底

1968-02-04
第5版()
专栏：

    在越南南方民族解放阵线的号召下
    南越伪军爱国士兵纷纷起义
    起义士兵表示决心与美伪集团斗争到底
    新华社河内三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，在越南南方民族解放阵线的号召下，南越伪军中的爱国士兵，最近纷纷起义，弃暗投明。
    报道说，一月三十一日，驻扎在朔庄省朔庄市的伪军第二十一师第三十三团第三营爱国士兵占领了敌人的弹药库，并携带全部武器向人民投诚。他们同人民解放武装力量和朔庄市人民配合，占领了一个又一个街区，消灭许多伪警察和恶棍，使市区内的敌人乱成一团，惊恐万状。
    另据报道，越南南方伪军第一师的一百零三名爱国士兵，最近也毅然脱离敌人队伍，向人民投诚，并在一月二十日发表声明，表示要在越南南方民族解放阵线的光荣旗帜下参加抗美救国战斗。
    声明说：我们一百零三个人无比高兴地逃脱了美国强盗和阮文绍—阮高其傀儡集团的魔爪，获得了同胞们的骨肉情谊和爱护。他们表示决心与美国佬和阮文绍—阮高其傀儡集团不共戴天，斗争到底。
