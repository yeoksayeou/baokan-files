Title: To Celebrate the Founding Anniversary of the Korean People's Army, the Military Attaché of the DPRK Embassy in China Holds a Press Conference
Author:
Date: 1968-02-07
Edition: 6

To celebrate the 20th anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army, Colonel Kim Hak, the military attaché of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's embassy in China, held a press conference today. Colonel Kim Hak introduced the history and achievements of the Korean People's Army at the conference.

### 为庆祝朝鲜人民军建军节  朝鲜驻华使馆武官举行记者招待会

1968-02-07
第6版()
专栏：

    为庆祝朝鲜人民军建军节
    朝鲜驻华使馆武官举行记者招待会
    新华社五日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆武官金鹤大校，为庆祝朝鲜人民军建军二十周年，今天举行记者招待会。金鹤大校在招待会上介绍了朝鲜人民军的历史和成就。
