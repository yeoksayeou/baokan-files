Title: Dazhai Brigade of Dazhai Production Brigade, Xiyang County, Shanxi Province, Carries Out Mao Zedong Thought Study Classes
Author:
Date: 1968-02-05
Edition: 4

The Dazhai Brigade of Dazhai Production Brigade, Xiyang County, Shanxi Province, is taking "Fight Selfishness and Criticize Revisionism" as the guiding principle. They are earnestly conducting Mao Zedong Thought study classes, combining the organization of study classes with mass study movements. They are closely following Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, determined to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.
