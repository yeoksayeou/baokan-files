Title: Cambodia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs Sends Protest Notes to the United States and the South Vietnamese Puppet Regime
Author:
Date: 1968-02-05
Edition: 6

Cambodia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs sends protest notes to the United States and the South Vietnamese puppet regime
Strongest protest against the U.S.-Saigon troops' attack on Cambodian outpost
Vietnam's Ministry of Foreign Affairs condemns the U.S.-Saigon clique's aggression against Cambodia

	PHNOM PENH, Feb. 3 (New China News Agency) -- According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on Feb. 3, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Cambodia sent protest notes to the U.S. government and the South Vietnamese puppet regime on Feb. 1, strongly protesting the armed attack by U.S. aggressor troops and South Vietnamese puppet troops on the Cambodian border outpost of Peamontaya on Jan. 18. The protest notes said:

	"The Royal Government strongly protests the armed attack by the U.S.-South Vietnamese armed forces and demands that the U.S. government immediately stop such actions."

	HANOI, Feb. 3 (New China News Agency) -- The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on Jan. 28, strongly condemning the savage attack by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops on the Cambodian outpost of Peamontaya in the province of Prey Veng on Jan. 18.

	The statement pointed out that this was a blatant act of aggression by the United States, violating Cambodia's sovereignty and territorial integrity and trampling on the most basic principles of international law.

	The statement said: The Vietnamese people firmly support the struggle of the fraternal Cambodian people to defend Cambodia's independence, sovereignty, peace, neutrality, and territorial integrity.

	The statement expressed the conviction that the just struggle of the Cambodian people will certainly achieve brilliant victories; all the cunning plots and evil deeds of U.S. imperialism will certainly meet with miserable failure.

### 柬埔寨外交部向美国和南越傀儡送交抗议书  最强烈抗议美伪军袭击柬哨所  越南外交部谴责美伪集团侵柬罪行

1968-02-05
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨外交部向美国和南越傀儡送交抗议书
    最强烈抗议美伪军袭击柬哨所
    越南外交部谴责美伪集团侵柬罪行
    新华社金边三日电  据柬埔寨新闻社三日报道，柬埔寨王国政府外交部二月一日分别向美国政府和南越伪政权送交抗议书，最强烈抗议美国侵略军和南越伪军在一月十八日袭击柬埔寨边境的佩昂蒙特亚哨所。抗议书说：
  “王国政府对美国—南越武装部队的武装袭击提出最强烈的抗议，并要求美国政府立即制止这种行动。”
    据新华社河内电　越南民主共和国外交部一月二十八日发表声明，严厉谴责美国—南越伪军一月十八日野蛮袭击柬埔寨王国波罗勉省的佩昂蒙特亚哨所的滔天罪行。
    声明指出，这是美国侵犯柬埔寨主权和领土完整，践踏国际法起码准则的极其明目张胆的侵略行动。
    声明说：越南人民坚决支持兄弟的柬埔寨人民为保卫柬埔寨的独立、主权、和平、中立和领土完整而进行的斗争。
    声明表示坚信：柬埔寨人民的正义斗争，必定取得辉煌的胜利；美帝国主义的一切狡猾阴谋和罪恶行动，必定遭到惨重的失败。
