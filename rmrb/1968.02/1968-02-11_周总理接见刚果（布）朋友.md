Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Friends from Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1968-02-11
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou today met with friends from Congo (Brazzaville), including George Bosina and eight others, and had a very cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Vice Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Shen Guang was present at the meeting.

The Congolese (Brazzaville) Ambassador to China, Bazinga, was also present. (Photo attached)

Premier Zhou poses for a group photo with George Bosina (front row, second from the left) and eight other friends from Congo (Brazzaville). Photo by Xinhua News Agency.

### 周总理接见刚果（布）朋友

1968-02-11
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见刚果（布）朋友
    新华社十日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了刚果（布）朋友鲍西纳·乔治等九人，同他们进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，邮电部副部长申光在座。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使巴赞加也在座。（附图片）
    周恩来总理同刚果（布）朋友鲍西纳·乔治（前左四）等九人合影。新华社记者摄
