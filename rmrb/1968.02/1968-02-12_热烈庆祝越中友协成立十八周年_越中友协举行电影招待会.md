Title: Celebrating the 18th Anniversary of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1968-02-12
Edition: 6

The Vietnam-China Friendship Association held a film screening reception on the evening of the 10th to celebrate the 18th anniversary of its establishment.

Huang Guoyue, President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Wu Guowei, Deputy Director of the Vietnam Foreign Cultural Affairs Commission, and Nguyen Thi Lieu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Vietnam Fatherland Front Central Committee, attended the reception. Liu Weizhao, Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, was also invited to attend the reception.

Huang Guoyue spoke at the reception. He said that the 18th anniversary of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association comes at a time when the people of South Vietnam have achieved great victories with the sincere support of the Chinese people. He expressed deep gratitude on behalf of the Vietnamese people to the beloved Chairman Mao, the Communist Party of China, the Government of China, and the people of China.

Liu Weizhao also spoke. He enthusiastically praised the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam, warmly congratulated the people of South Vietnam on their brilliant victories since the New Year, and expressed that the Chinese people firmly support the Vietnamese people's anti-American national salvation war until final victory.

### 热烈庆祝越中友协成立十八周年  越中友协举行电影招待会

1968-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    热烈庆祝越中友协成立十八周年
    越中友协举行电影招待会
    据新华社河内十一日电　越中友协十日晚上举行电影招待会，热烈庆祝越中友协成立十八周年。
    越中友协会长黄国越、越南对外文委副主任武国威、越南祖国战线中央委员会副秘书长阮氏榴等出席了招待会。中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陆维钊也应邀出席了招待会。
    黄国越在招待会上讲了话。他说，越中友协成立十八周年，是在越南南方人民在中国人民真诚的支持下，取得伟大胜利的时候到来的。他代表越南人民向敬爱的毛主席、中国共产党、中国政府和中国人民表示深切的谢意。
    陆维钊代办也讲了话。他热情赞扬中越两国人民的战斗友谊，热烈祝贺越南南方人民新春以来取得的辉煌胜利，并且表示，中国人民坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争，直到取得最后胜利。
