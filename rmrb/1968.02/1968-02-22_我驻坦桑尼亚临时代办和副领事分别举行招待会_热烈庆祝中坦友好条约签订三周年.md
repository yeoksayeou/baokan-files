Title: Chinese Charge d'Affaires and Vice Consul Hold Receptions to Celebrate the Third Anniversary of the Sino-Tanzanian Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1968-02-22
Edition: 6

Chinese Charge d'Affaires and Vice Consul Hold Receptions to Celebrate the Third Anniversary of the Sino-Tanzanian Friendship Treaty

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam, February 21—Chinese Charge d'Affaires to Tanzania, Zhou Boqing, and Vice Consul to Zanzibar, Liu Qingyou, held receptions on the evening of the 20th in Dar es Salaam and Zanzibar respectively to celebrate the third anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Tanzanian Friendship Treaty. Over 400 Tanzanian dignitaries from various sectors attended the reception in Dar es Salaam. Among them were Tanzania's Minister of Lands, Settlement, and Water Development, Babu, Mayor of Dar es Salaam, Shambuso, leaders of Tanzanian mass organizations, representatives of the working people, African freedom fighters stationed in Dar es Salaam, and diplomats from some friendly countries in Tanzania.

	At the reception, Tanzanian friends enthusiastically hailed the new developments in the traditional friendship between the two countries since the signing of the Sino-Tanzanian Friendship Treaty three years ago. They also congratulated the Chinese people on their victories and achievements in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and socialist construction under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao.

	The exhibition of Chairman Mao's works, as well as Mao badges and portraits, received a warm welcome at the reception. The exhibition table was surrounded by guests. When guests received "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and Mao badges, they were very happy, and many excitedly shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!"

	Over 200 guests from Zanzibar celebrated the third anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Tanzanian Friendship Treaty with Chinese diplomats and technical personnel at the Chinese Consulate on the evening of the 20th. Ministers from Zanzibar and members of the Revolutionary Council attended the reception.

### 我驻坦桑尼亚临时代办和副领事分别举行招待会  热烈庆祝中坦友好条约签订三周年

1968-02-22
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻坦桑尼亚临时代办和副领事分别举行招待会
    热烈庆祝中坦友好条约签订三周年
    据新华社达累斯萨拉姆二十一日电　中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆临时代办周伯萍和驻桑给巴尔领事馆副领事刘庆有二十日晚分别在达累斯萨拉姆和桑给巴尔举行招待会，热烈庆祝中国和坦桑尼亚友好条约签订三周年。坦桑尼亚各界人士四百多人出席了在达累斯萨拉姆举行的招待会。他们当中有坦桑尼亚土地、定居和水利开发部长巴布，达累斯萨拉姆市市长沙姆布索，坦桑尼亚群众组织的负责人，劳动人民的代表以及在达累斯萨拉姆的非洲自由战士和驻坦桑尼亚的一些友好国家的外交官员。
    在招待会上，坦桑尼亚朋友欢呼中坦两国之间的传统友谊在中坦友好条约签订以来三年中所取得的新发展，并祝贺中国人民在伟大领袖毛主席的英明领导下，在无产阶级文化大革命和社会主义建设中所取得的胜利和成就。
    在招待会上陈列的毛主席著作以及毛主席像章和毛主席像片，都受到热烈的欢迎。
    陈列桌的周围挤满了客人。客人们在得到《毛主席语录》和毛主席像章时都非常高兴，许多人激动地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”
    二百多位桑给巴尔客人，二十日晚同中国的外交人员、技术工作人员一起在中国领事馆热烈庆祝中国和坦桑尼亚友好条约签订三周年。桑给巴尔的部长们和革命委员会的委员们出席了招待会。
