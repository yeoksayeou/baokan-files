Title: Chinese Photo Exhibition Opens in Khartoum
Author:
Date: 1968-02-08
Edition: 6

A Chinese photo exhibition hosted by the Sudan-China Friendship Association opened on February 1 in Khartoum. Many people from various sectors attended the opening ceremony.

Abdul Majid Abu Hasab, Minister of Information and Social Affairs, presided over the opening ceremony. He expressed the hope that the Sudan-China Friendship Association would continue to progress, thereby strengthening the ties between the Sudanese and Chinese peoples.

Abdul Wahab Zein Abidin, Chairman of the Sudan-China Friendship Association, in his speech, congratulated the Chinese people on their great achievements under the leadership of the great leader and supreme commander Chairman Mao Zedong in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and in other fields.

### 我图片展览在喀土穆开幕

1968-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

    我图片展览在喀土穆开幕
    新华社喀土穆电　由苏丹—中国友好协会举办的中国图片展览会二月一日在这里开幕。许多各界人士出席了开幕式。
    新闻和社会事务部长阿卜杜勒·马吉德·阿布·哈萨布主持了开幕式。他表示希望苏丹—中国友好协会继续进步，以增进苏丹人民和中国人民之间的联系。
    苏丹—中国友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·瓦哈卜·宰因·阿卜丁在讲话中祝贺中国人民在伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅毛泽东主席领导下展开的无产阶级文化大革命中以及在其他各个方面所取得的伟大成就。
