Title: China's Ministry of Education Donates School Supplies to Mauritania
Author:
Date: 1968-02-29
Edition: 2

	China's Ministry of Education Donates School Supplies to Mauritania

	Xinhua News Agency, Nouakchott — On February 22, Wei Yongqing, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Mauritania, on behalf of the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China, presented a batch of school supplies to the Ministry of National Education of Mauritania.

	Wei Yongqing said while presenting these school supplies, "These supplies are a token of the Chinese people's friendship towards the Mauritanian people." He expressed his belief that the friendship between the peoples of China and Mauritania will continue to strengthen.

	Mohamedna Ould Hattar, Secretary-General of Mauritania's Ministry of National Education, in his speech, expressed gratitude to the Chinese government and people. He said, "We hope that our two countries will continue to cooperate."

### 我教育部赠送毛里塔尼亚学校用品

1968-02-29
第2版()
专栏：

    我教育部赠送毛里塔尼亚学校用品
    新华社努瓦克肖特电　中国驻毛里塔尼亚大使馆临时代办卫永清，代表中华人民共和国教育部，于二十二日，把一批学校用品赠送给毛里塔尼亚国民教育部。
    卫永清在赠送这些学校用品时说：“这些用品是中国人民对毛里塔尼亚人民友好的表示。”他表示深信，中国和毛里塔尼亚两国人民之间的友谊将不断加强。
    毛里塔尼亚国民教育部秘书长穆罕默德纳·乌尔德·哈塔里在讲话中对中国政府和人民表示谢意。他说：我们希望我们两国将不断进行合作。
