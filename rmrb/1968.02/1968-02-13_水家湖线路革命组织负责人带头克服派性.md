Title: Leaders of the Waterjiahu Line Revolutionary Organization Take the Lead in Overcoming Factionalism
Author:
Date: 1968-02-13
Edition: 2

The leaders of the revolutionary mass organizations of the Waterjiahu Line of the Bangbu Railway Bureau, with the enthusiastic help of the People's Liberation Army, are actively studying and applying Chairman Mao's latest instructions. They are taking the lead in overcoming bourgeois and petty-bourgeois factionalism, strengthening proletarian party spirit, and leading the masses forward along the course charted by Chairman Mao. At present, revolutionary mass organizations have been established in all 17 stations and line departments along the line, and revolutionary great alliances are becoming more and more consolidated. Production is booming. At present, the leaders of the revolutionary mass organizations of the Waterjiahu Line are organizing Mao Zedong Thought study classes with the masses to fully implement Chairman Mao's latest instructions. They are preparing to launch another "people's war" to wipe out factionalism, further consolidate and develop the revolutionary great alliance, and promote the revolutionary three-in-one combination.

### 水家湖线路革命组织负责人带头克服派性

1968-02-13
第2版()
专栏：

    水家湖线路革命组织负责人带头克服派性
    蚌埠铁路分局水家湖线路革命群众组织的负责人，在解放军的热情帮助下，活学活用毛主席的最新指示，带头克服资产阶级、小资产阶级派性，增强无产阶级党性，带领群众沿着毛主席指引的航向奋勇前进。现在全线十七个火车站和线路机关都成立了无产阶级革命派大联合委员会，革命大联合越来越巩固，生产搞得热气腾腾。目前，水家湖线路革命群众组织的负责人正在和群众一起，大办毛泽东思想学习班，全面落实毛主席的最新指示。他们准备再打一场围歼派性的“人民战争”，进一步巩固和发展革命的大联合，促进革命的三结合。
　（据新华社讯）
