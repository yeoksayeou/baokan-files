Title: China's Medical Team Arrives in Mali
Author:
Date: 1968-02-17
Edition: 6

	According to the agreement signed by the Chinese and Malian governments in December last year, a medical team dispatched by the Chinese government arrived in Bamako on the 13th. This team of 19 Chinese medical personnel will assist Mali in its health work.

	Those who went to the airport to welcome the team included Daouda Keita, Director of the Health Bureau of the Malian Ministry of Health and Social Affairs, and Liu Heliang, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Mali.

### 我国医疗队到马里

1968-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    我国医疗队到马里
    新华社巴马科电　根据中国和马里政府去年十二月签订的议定书，由中国政府派遣的一个医疗队十三日到达巴马科。这个由十九人组成的中国医疗队将帮助马里在卫生方面的工作。
    前往机场迎接的有马里卫生和社会事务部卫生局局长达乌达·凯塔和中国驻马里大使馆临时代办刘和林。
