Title: Hanoi Celebrates the Seventh Anniversary of the Establishment of the Armed Forces of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-02-17
Edition: 5

The Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to North Vietnam, the Ministry of Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Hanoi City Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front jointly held a rally on the evening of the 15th to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the establishment of the armed forces of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

Members of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front Nguyen Thi Thien, head of the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to North Vietnam Nguyen Van Tien, and Major General Xie Chunqiu of the Vietnam People's Army, as well as representatives of various strata of people in Hanoi, attended the rally.

In her opening speech, Nguyen Thi Thien said: Over the past seven years, the armed forces of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, under the wise leadership of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, with the full support and assistance of the people of South Vietnam, have continuously carried forward revolutionary heroism, waged a great and creative people's war, and achieved greater victories and strength. We, in the rear of the motherland, warmly congratulate the armed forces of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam on their rapid growth and brilliant achievements! Their great victories have opened up a very favorable new situation for the national struggle against American imperialism.

Nguyen Van Tien said at the meeting: Over the past seven years, the armed forces of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, closely united with the people, have developed the people's war in South Vietnam to a high level, pushing the enemy into a difficult situation from all sides. He said: The great victories of the South Vietnamese people and armed forces over the past seven years and the brilliant victories at the beginning of this spring are inseparable from the full support of 17 million compatriots in the North and Chairman Ho Chi Minh's concern for the South Vietnamese people and armed forces, and are inseparable from the support of brothers and friends from the five continents.

Major General Xie Chunqiu, on behalf of all cadres and soldiers of the armed forces of North Vietnam, warmly congratulated the heroic South Vietnamese people and armed forces on their brilliant victories in the new year, and pointed out: This victory proves that the armed forces of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam have become an invincible army, and they will surely defeat the American aggressors. The cadres and soldiers of the armed forces of North Vietnam promise to the compatriots in the North and South and the comrades of the armed forces of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam: We will strive to learn from the glorious examples of revolutionary heroism of the South Vietnamese people and armed forces, learn from their valuable experience, actively train, improve our combat effectiveness, and wholeheartedly fulfill the glorious task of the rear for the front.

The meeting adopted a resolution, warmly congratulating the armed forces of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam on their rapid growth over the past seven years, and warmly welcoming the great and comprehensive victories achieved by the South Vietnamese people and armed forces since the new year.

The resolution said: The 17 million compatriots and soldiers in the North are determined to do everything to achieve a complete victory over the American aggressors, and to work hand in hand with the 14 million compatriots and soldiers in the South to resolutely complete the glorious historical task of liberating the South, building and defending the North, and ultimately reunifying the country. The resolution concluded: We are confident that our people, adhering to the invincible people's war line, with a powerful and invincible people's armed forces, and with the sympathy and support of progressive humanity, will surely achieve a complete victory.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 10th, the Vietnam People's Army Daily published an editorial on the 10th, warmly applauding the broad masses of peasants in South Vietnam rising up to fight against the American aggressors and their lackeys. The editorial pointed out that the peasants in South Vietnam will certainly advance and strike the enemy more fiercely to achieve greater victories in the struggle against American imperialism.

The editorial pointed out that the broad masses of peasants in South Vietnam, with their stormy struggle and brilliant achievements, have foiled the "pacification" plot of the American aggressors and their lackeys in the countryside.

The editorial said that the current situation is very favorable for the peasants in South Vietnam to rise up more vigorously and strike the enemy more fiercely, resolutely driving the enemy out of the countryside, and strongly supporting the struggle of the urban people.

The editorial expressed confidence: The heroic peasants in South Vietnam, together with the urban people, will push the revolution to comprehensive development, achieve greater victories, and lead the struggle against American imperialism to complete victory.

### 河内集会热烈庆祝越南南方人民解放武装力量统一日七周年  南方军民打开抗美救国战争新局面  越南人民决心坚持人民战争彻底战胜美国强盗

1968-02-17
第5版()
专栏：

    河内集会热烈庆祝越南南方人民解放武装力量统一日七周年
    南方军民打开抗美救国战争新局面
    越南人民决心坚持人民战争彻底战胜美国强盗
    新华社河内十六日电　越南祖国战线中央委员会、越南南方民族解放阵线常驻越南北方代表团、越南民主共和国国防部和越南祖国战线河内市委员会，十五日晚上联合举行集会，热烈庆祝越南南方人民解放武装力量统一日七周年。
    越南劳动党中央委员会委员、越南祖国战线中央委员会主席团委员阮氏十，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻越南北方代表团团长阮文进，越南人民军代表谢春秋少将，以及河内各阶层人民代表出席了集会。
    阮氏十在致开幕词时说：七年来，南方人民解放武装力量在越南南方民族解放阵线的英明领导下，在南方人民全心全意的关怀和帮助下，不断发扬革命英雄主义，进行了一场伟大的、富于创造性的人民战争，越战越胜，越战越强。我们在祖国的大后方，热烈祝贺南方人民解放武装力量的飞跃成长和显赫战功！他们所取得的伟大战绩，为全国人民的抗美救国事业开创了一个十分有利的新局面。
    阮文进在会上说：七年来，人民解放武装力量同人民紧密团结，将南方的人民战争发展到高度水平，从各个方面把敌人推向困窘的境地。他说：南方军民七年来的巨大胜利和今年初春的辉煌胜利，是同一千七百万北方同胞的全心全力的支援和胡志明主席对南方军民的关怀分不开的，是同五大洲兄弟和朋友的支持分不开的。
    谢春秋代表越南北方人民武装力量全体干部和战士，热烈祝贺英雄的南方军民的新春显赫战功，并且指出：这一战功证明南方人民解放武装力量已经成为一支百战百胜的军队，它必定能彻底战胜美国侵略强盗。北方人民武装力量的干部和战士向南北两方同胞和南方人民解放武装力量的同志们保证：我们将努力学习南方军民的革命英雄主义光辉榜样，学习他们的宝贵经验，积极锻炼，提高战斗力，全心全力地完成大后方对大前线的光荣任务。
    大会通过一项决议，热烈祝贺越南南方人民解放武装力量七年来的迅速成长，热烈欢呼越南南方军民新春以来取得的巨大和全面的胜利。
    决议说：一千七百万北方同胞和战士，决心做到一切为了彻底战胜美国侵略强盗，一心一意地同一千四百万骨肉的南方同胞和战士并肩一道，坚决完成解放南方，建设和保卫北方，进而统一祖国的光荣历史任务。决议最后说：我们坚信，我国人民坚持无敌的人民战争路线，有一支百战百胜的强大的人民武装力量，又得到进步人类的同情和支持，必定能取得彻底的胜利。
    据新华社河内十日电　越南《人民军队报》十日发表社论，热烈欢呼越南南方广大农村人民奋起痛打美国强盗及其走狗。社论指出，南方农村人民必将乘胜前进，更猛烈地打击敌人，以夺取抗美救国战争的更大胜利。
    社论指出，越南南方农村人民暴风骤雨般地奋起斗争和取得的辉煌战功，使美国强盗及其走狗“绥靖”农村的阴谋破产了。
    社论说，目前形势很有利于南方农村人民更加蓬勃地奋起斗争，更猛烈地打击敌人，坚决地把敌人从农村全部赶出去，有力地支援城市人民的战斗。
    社论表示深信：英雄的南方农村人民，必将同城市人民一起把革命推向全面的发展，夺取更加巨大的胜利，把抗美救国战争引向彻底胜利。
