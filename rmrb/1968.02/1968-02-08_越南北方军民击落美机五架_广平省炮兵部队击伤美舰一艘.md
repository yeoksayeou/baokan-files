Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five U.S. Aircraft; Artillery Unit in Quảng Bình Province Damages One U.S. Warship
Author:
Date: 1968-02-08
Edition: 6

The heroic military and civilians of North Vietnam shot down five U.S. aircraft. The artillery unit in Quảng Bình Province damaged one U.S. warship.

	According to reports from here, at 4 PM on the 7th, a U.S. spy plane brazenly invaded the airspace over Hanoi. The heroic military and civilians of Hanoi immediately launched a powerful counterattack, shooting down the enemy aircraft on the spot.

	According to another report, the North Vietnamese People's Air Force shot down one invading U.S. aircraft over the skies of Phú Thọ Province on the 5th. The artillery unit in Quảng Bình Province hit and set fire to an invading U.S. warship on the 4th.

	According to reports from here, the military and civilians of North Vietnam shot down three U.S. aircraft.

	The military and civilians of Quảng Bình Province shot down one enemy aircraft on the 3rd. The military and civilians of Nghệ An Province shot down one enemy aircraft on the 1st.

	The female militia of Nghệ An Province also shot down one enemy aircraft on January 28.

### 越南北方军民击落美机五架  广平省炮兵部队击伤美舰一艘

1968-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机五架
    广平省炮兵部队击伤美舰一艘
    新华社河内七日电　据这里报道，七日下午四时，美国强盗侦察机一架，悍然侵入河内市上空。英雄的河内市军民立即给予有力反击，当场击落了这架敌机。
    另据报道，越南北方人民空军，五日在宣光省上空击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。广平省炮兵部队四日把一艘入侵的美国军舰击中起火。
    新华社河内五日电　据这里公布，越南北方军民击落美机三架。
    广平省军民三日击落敌机一架。义安省军民一日击落敌机一架。
    义安省的女民兵在一月二十八日也击落敌机一架。
