Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Invades Our Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-02-01
Edition: 6

On January 31, at 11:55 AM to 12:32 PM, a U.S. military aircraft twice invaded the airspace over the territorial waters north of Changshan County, Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 12:05 PM to 12:14 PM, another U.S. military aircraft twice invaded the airspace over the territorial waters west of Changshan County, Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issued the 450th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空  我提出第四百五十次严重警告

1968-02-01
第6版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我国领空
    我提出第四百五十次严重警告
    新华社三十一日讯　一月三十一日十一时五十五分至十二时三十二分，美国军用飞机一架，两次侵入我广东省海南岛昌感县以北领海上空。同日十二时零五分至十二时十四分美国军用飞机又一架，两次侵入我广东省海南岛昌感县以西领海上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百五十次严重警告。
