Title: Persist in Revolutionizing in Spare Time
Author:
Date: 1968-02-16
Edition: 2

We have an active amateur propaganda troupe in our rural area. They adhere to the principles of revolutionizing in spare time and economizing for revolution. They use the spare time during the day and evenings to rehearse and perform small-scale cultural programs, actively promoting Mao Zedong Thought, advancing the ideological revolution among the commune members, and boosting their enthusiasm for production. They have received enthusiastic praise and support.

However, some amateur propaganda troupes occupy daytime production time and a large number of labor forces to rehearse large-scale performances. For example, one propaganda troupe mobilized fifteen laborers to rehearse a grand opera. During the performance, it went on until late at night, affecting the commune members' rest and the next day's production.

I believe that rural amateur cultural propaganda troupes should adhere to the principle of revolutionizing in spare time, engage in small-scale amateur propaganda activities, seize the revolution, promote production, and not affect agricultural production, especially during the current busy spring season.

River South, Changyuan County, Henan Province, a reader

### 要坚持业余闹革命

1968-02-16
第2版()
专栏：革命群众来信

    要坚持业余闹革命
    我们这里农村的业余宣传队，搞得很活跃。他们坚持业余闹革命、节约闹革命的原则，利用生产的空闲时间和晚上，排练和演出一些小型的文艺节目，积极宣传毛泽东思想，促进了社员们的思想革命化，鼓舞了社员们的生产热情。他们受到了热情称赞和支持。
    但是，有些业余宣传队，占用白天的生产时间和很多劳动力，去排演大型节目。例如，有一个宣传队，就调动了十五个劳动力去排演大戏。演出时，一搞就是大半夜，耽误了社员的休息，影响了第二天的生产。
    我认为，农村业余文艺宣传队，应当坚持业余闹革命的原则，搞小型业余的宣传活动，抓革命，促生产，而不能影响农业生产，特别是在当前春忙时节。
            河南长垣县一读者
