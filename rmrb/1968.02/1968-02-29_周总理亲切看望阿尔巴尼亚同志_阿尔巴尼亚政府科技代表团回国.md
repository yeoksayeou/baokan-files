Title: Premier Zhou Visits Albanian Comrades
Author:
Date: 1968-02-29
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou En-lai of the State Council visited the residence of Albanian comrades who are currently in China on February 28. He met with the entire delegation of the Albanian Government Science and Technology Delegation led by Prokop Murra, Deputy Chairman of the Albanian State Planning Commission. He also met with Albanian agricultural experts Enver Babameto and Aqif Bashaj, and Mihajl Ilija, Chief Engineer of the Albanian Oil Refinery. Zhou had a very cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Accompanying Premier Zhou on the visit were Sun Hsiao-feng, Vice Minister of the Petroleum Industry, and Albanian Ambassador to China, Natanaeli.

New China News Agency reported on February 28 that the Albanian Government Science and Technology Delegation, led by Prokop Murra, Deputy Chairman of the Albanian State Planning Commission, concluded their visit to China and departed from Beijing by plane today.

Sun Hsiao-feng, Vice Minister of the Petroleum Industry, and Albanian Ambassador to China, Natanaeli, were among those who saw them off at the airport.

### 周总理亲切看望阿尔巴尼亚同志  阿尔巴尼亚政府科技代表团回国

1968-02-29
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理亲切看望阿尔巴尼亚同志
    阿尔巴尼亚政府科技代表团回国
    新华社二十八日讯　国务院总理周恩来，今天前往正在中国访问的阿尔巴尼亚同志的住所，看望了由阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会副主席普罗科普·穆拉率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府科技代表团全体成员，阿尔巴尼亚农业专家恩维尔·巴巴麦托、艾契累姆·巴夏，阿尔巴尼亚炼油厂总工程师米哈依尔·伊利亚，同他们进行了极为亲切友好的谈话。
    陪同周总理前往看望的有石油部副部长孙晓风。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利也在座。
    新华社二十八日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会副主席普罗科普·穆拉率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府科技代表团，结束了在中国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    石油部副部长孙晓风，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利等，到机场送行。
