Title: Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs Issues Note of Protest
Author:
Date: 1968-02-26
Edition: 6

Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs Issues Note of Protest
Strongly Condemns U.S. Aggressor Troops and South Vietnamese Puppet Army for Attacking Cambodian Border

New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 23rd — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 23rd, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued notes to the U.S. government and the South Vietnamese puppet regime on February 19th, strongly condemning the aggressive acts of U.S. aggressor troops and the South Vietnamese puppet army on February 10th, when they once again launched a savage attack on Cambodian border residents. The notes stated: The Royal Government of Cambodia firmly demands that the U.S. government and the South Vietnamese puppet regime immediately cease these aggressive actions.

### 柬埔寨外交部发出照会  最强烈抗议美侵略军和南越伪军袭击柬边境

1968-02-26
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨外交部发出照会
    最强烈抗议美侵略军和南越伪军袭击柬边境
    新华社金边二十三日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二十三日报道：柬埔寨外交部二月十九日分别照会美国政府和南越伪政权，最强烈抗议美国侵略军和南越伪军二月十日又疯狂袭击柬埔寨边境居民的侵略罪行。照会说：柬埔寨王国政府坚决要求美国政府和南越伪政权立即停止这种侵略行径。
