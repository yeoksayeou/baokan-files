Title: New Zealand, Sweden, and Portugal People's Protest Marches Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-02-26
Edition: 5

A group of New Zealanders held a demonstration in Auckland on the 24th to protest against the arrival of the U.S. warships "Richard" and "Edwards" in Auckland, and to protest against U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam. The demonstrators held up placards reading, "We support the struggle of the (South Vietnamese) National Liberation Front for freedom!" "Stop U.S. aggression in Vietnam!" and so on.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 21st, thousands of Swedes held a torchlight procession in Stockholm to protest against U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam.

The demonstrators carried portraits of the beloved leader of the Vietnamese people, Chairman Ho Chi Minh, the national flag of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the flag of the South Vietnamese National Liberation Front. They shouted, "U.S. imperialism out of Vietnam!" "Johnson, murderer!" "The South Vietnamese National Liberation Front will surely win!" "Long live the South Vietnamese National Liberation Front!" After the demonstration, a mass rally was held in the city center.

In the Portuguese capital of Lisbon, 500 young people, despite the suppression of the Portuguese reactionary authorities, held a demonstration in front of the U.S. embassy on the 21st to protest against U.S. aggression in Vietnam. The demonstrators held up placards denouncing U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam and shouted slogans in support of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the South Vietnamese National Liberation Front.

### 新西兰瑞典葡萄牙人民示威游行  抗议美帝侵略越南

1968-02-26
第5版()
专栏：

    新西兰瑞典葡萄牙人民示威游行
    抗议美帝侵略越南
    新华社惠灵顿二十五日电　一批新西兰群众二十四日在奥克兰举行示威，抗议美国军舰“理查德号”和“爱德华兹号”抵达奥克兰，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南。示威群众高举着标语牌，上面写着：“我们支持（越南南方）民族解放阵线争取自由的斗争！”“制止美国对越南的侵略！”等。
    据新华社二十二日讯　数千名瑞典人二十一日晚在斯德哥尔摩举行火炬游行，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南。
    示威者举着越南人民敬爱的领袖胡志明主席的画像、越南民主共和国的国旗和越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜。他们高呼：“美帝国主义滚出越南！”“约翰逊杀人犯！”“越南南方民族解放阵线必胜！”“越南南方民族解放阵线万岁！”示威后在市中心举行了群众集会。
    在葡萄牙首都里斯本，五百名青年不顾葡萄牙反动当局的镇压，二十一日在美国大使馆前举行反对美国侵略越南的示威。示威者举着谴责美帝国主义侵略越南标语牌，并高呼支持越南民主共和国和越南南方民族解放阵线的口号。
