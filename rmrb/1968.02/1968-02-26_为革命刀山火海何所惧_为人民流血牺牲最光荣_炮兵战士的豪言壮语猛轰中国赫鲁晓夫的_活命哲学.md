Title: For the Revolution, Fear Not the Knives and Fire Mountains; For the People, Sacrifice is the Greatest Honor - The Cannon Soldiers' Bold Words Strongly Criticize the "Survival Philosophy" of the Chinese Khrushchev

Author:

Date: 1968-02-26

Edition: 4

Some say: Life is the most precious thing. In my view: The most precious thing in life is a high level of proletarian awareness, a vigorous proletarian revolutionary spirit, and a lofty proletarian moral character.
		Certain Division Political Instructor, Peng Zuxun

Leave safety to the class brothers, leave danger to oneself. It is better to sacrifice one's own life to ensure the survival of the people.
		Certain Division, Third Platoon

To die for the revolutionary cause on the battlefield is the greatest honor and happiness of my life.
		Certain Division Soldier, Guan Shihe

Born for the revolution, live for the revolution; as long as the heart beats, fight for the revolution.
		Certain Division Squad Leader, Liu Tongli

Born for the people, loyal and devoted. Die for the people, dedicate oneself completely. I am willing to be tempered and grow in the midst of fierce battles, not willing to spend my life in peaceful days. I am willing to shed blood and sacrifice for the people of the world, not willing to be a coward who fears death. To defend our iron fortress and for the liberation of the people of the world, I am willing to sacrifice my life.
		Certain Division Soldier, Jia Zhanjiang

For the revolution, one should try to avoid unnecessary sacrifices. But when the revolution requires sacrifice, we should be fearless, face death bravely, and willingly give up our lives.
		Certain Division Section Chief, Zheng Ruodu

A heart full of red, fear not the shattering of the body! Forever loyal to Chairman Mao, unshakable in the face of calamity.
		Certain Division Soldier, Liu Jianlie

Fear not the rain of bullets, dedicate oneself to the public.
		Certain Division, Second Platoon

Live standing up, die standing up. Stand like a steel giant on one's own battlefield.
		Certain Division, Anti-Aircraft Gun Second Platoon

The enemy's artillery fire is limited, but the power of Mao Zedong Thought is boundless.
		Certain Division Squad Leader, Li Shuheng

The enemy's artillery fire can damage our flesh and bones, can take away our lives, but it can never shatter or take away the revolutionary soldier's red heart loyal to Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought, nor can it ever shatter the revolutionary soldier's unyielding iron will.
		Certain Division Soldier, Xu Shuhua

Shedding blood is nothing, only for the suffering people. Pain is trivial, a life for the people!
		Certain Division Soldier, Zhang Yuhe

In times of crisis, stand up, knowing that sacrifice is inevitable, go with a smile.
		Certain Division Soldier, Zhu Kuiyuan

For communism, fear not sacrifice. The victory of the people's revolution is won by the sacrifice of thousands and millions. To bear the sacrifice for victory is the greatest pride and joy of communists.
		Certain Division Deputy Squad Leader, Li Shaoming

The rolling river flows east, a red sun shines in the heart, in the deep bamboo grove, radio waves flash, in the vast sky, bind the dragon. In the smoke of war, tempered with red courage, with a heart for the world, plant a great loyalty. Willingly shed hot blood for the people, exchange for a world of red.
		Certain Division, Radar Second Station

### 为革命刀山火海何所惧  为人民流血牺牲最光荣——炮兵战士的豪言壮语猛轰中国赫鲁晓夫的“活命哲学”

1968-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    为革命刀山火海何所惧  为人民流血牺牲最光荣
    ——炮兵战士的豪言壮语猛轰中国赫鲁晓夫的“活命哲学”
    有人说：人生最宝贵的是生命。依我看：人生最宝贵的是高度的无产阶级觉悟，旺盛的无产阶级革命斗志，崇高的无产阶级道德品质。
                          某部教导员　彭祖循
    把安全留给阶级兄弟，把危险留给自己。宁可牺牲自己的生命，也要换取人民的生存。
                                  某部三连
    在战场上为革命利益而死，是我一生最大的光荣和幸福。
                            某部战士　关士和
    生为革命生，死为革命死；只要心在跳，拚命干革命。
                            某部班长　刘同立
    生为人民生，忠心耿耿。死为人民死，鞠躬尽瘁。我愿在炮火纷飞的战斗中锻炼成长，不愿在平静的日子里度过一生。我愿为世界人民流血牺牲，不愿做贪生怕死的可怜虫。为了保卫我们铁打的江山，为了世界人民的解放，牺牲生命也心甘情愿。
                                某部战士　贾占刚
    为了革命，应当尽量减少那些不必要的牺牲。但是，当革命需要牺牲自己的时候，我们就义无反顾，视死如归，毫不犹豫地献出自己的生命。
                          某部股长　郑若杜
    一颗红心怀天下，粉身碎骨何所惜！永远忠于毛主席，天崩地裂志不移。
                          某部战士　刘建列
    枪林弹雨无所惧，舍身为公献忠心。
                                    某部二连
    生要站着生，死要站着死。要象钢铸铁打的巨人，屹立在自己的战斗岗位上。
                              某部高机二连
    敌人炮火的威力是有限的，毛泽东思想的威力是无限的。
                            某部班长　李书恒
    敌人的炮火能损伤我们的肌骨，能夺去我们的生命，但它永远炸不碎、夺不走革命战士忠于毛主席和毛泽东思想的一颗红心，永远也炸不垮革命战士无坚不摧的钢铁意志。
                          某部战士　徐树合
    流血不要紧，只为受苦人，伤痛只等闲，一生为人民！
                            某部战士　张玉和
    危急关头，挺身而出，为了人民，明知要牺牲，也要含笑而去。
                          某部战士　朱魁元
    为了共产主义不怕牺牲。人民革命的胜利，是要千百万人的牺牲去换来的。为了胜利而承担牺牲，是我们共产党人最大的骄傲和愉快。
                          某部副班长　李绍明
    滚滚江水永向东，一轮红日照心中，竹林深处电波闪，万里长空缚苍龙。战火硝烟赤胆炼，胸怀世界树大忠。甘将热血献人民，换得全球红彤彤。
                              某部雷达二站
