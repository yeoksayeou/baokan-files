Title: Soldiers and Farmers Together Prepare for the Spring Plowing
Author: Jiang Zhi Shun
Date: 1968-02-26
Edition: 3

    With the help of the People's Liberation Army's agricultural support troops, the Fourteen Flatlands People's Commune in Dongfeng County, Jilin Province, has launched a high tide of preparation for spring farming. The image shows soldiers from a certain unit of the Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team and commune members transporting fertilizer to the fields.
