	Okinawan People Protest Against U.S. B-52 Aircraft Deployment
	Over a thousand two hundred Japanese workers, youth, and students in Okinawa held a protest rally in front of the U.S. military base in Kadena on the 9th, strongly demanding that the recently arrived U.S. B-52 bombers immediately leave.

	After the rally, participants chanted slogans such as "Immediately return Okinawa!" and "U.S. imperialism, get out of Vietnam!" They also held a demonstration march around the U.S. military base.

	Jordanian Forces Retaliate Against New Israeli Provocations
	On the 11th, a Jordanian military spokesman in Amman announced that Israeli aggressor forces stationed on the West Bank of the Jordan River carried out new provocations against Jordan from 10 AM to 6 PM on the 11th, shelling Jordanian outposts on the east bank of the Jordan River along the ceasefire line. The Jordanian military responded with counterattacks, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

	He said that on the 11th, the enemy's seven observation posts and two military vehicles were destroyed, five tanks and three artillery pieces were hit, and a considerable number of soldiers were killed or wounded.

	According to a report from Cairo newspapers on the 9th, quoting a Jordanian military spokesman, the Jordanian military inflicted heavy losses on the enemy during an exchange of fire with Israeli aggressor forces on the 8th.

### 简明新闻

1968-02-16
第6版()
专栏：

    简明新闻
    冲绳人民反对美军Ｂ—５２飞机进驻
    冲绳的日本工人、青年、学生等一千二百多人，九日晨在嘉手纳美军基地前举行抗议集会，强烈要求最近进入冲绳的美军Ｂ—５２型轰炸机立即滚出去。
    与会者在大会结束后，高呼“立即归还冲绳！”“美帝国主义从越南滚出去！”等口号，他们还在美军基地周围举行了示威游行。
    约旦军队反击以色列侵略军新挑衅
    约旦军事发言人十一日在阿曼宣布，驻在约旦河西岸的以色列侵略军从十一日上午十时到下午六时向约旦进行新的挑衅，炮击在停火线沿线地区约旦河东岸的约旦哨所。约旦军队给予还击，使敌人受到了重大损失。
    他说，在十一日，敌人的七个观察哨和两辆军用车被摧毁，五辆坦克和三门大炮被击中，还死伤了相当数目的士兵。
    据九日开罗报纸援引约旦军事发言人的谈话说，约旦军队于八日在同以色列侵略军交火中，使敌人蒙受重大的损失。　（据新华社讯）
