Title: New Dutch Charge d'Affaires Presents Credentials to Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1968-02-13
Edition: 6

The new Dutch Charge d'Affaires to China, Mr. Doukeson, presented his letter of appointment to Vice Premier Chen Yi, who is also the Minister of Foreign Affairs, this morning.

### 荷兰新任驻华代办  向陈毅副总理递交委任书

1968-02-13

专栏：

    荷兰新任驻华代办
