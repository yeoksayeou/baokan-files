Title: Congratulations on the Seventh Anniversary of the Establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1968-02-03
Edition: 1

You have fought well! Under extremely difficult conditions, relying on your own strength, you have fought the most ferocious U.S. imperialism to the point where it is at its wits' end and in a sorry state. This is a great victory. The Chinese people salute you.
