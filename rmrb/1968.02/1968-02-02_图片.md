Title: Lanzhou Chemical Industry Company Holds "Three-in-One" Mao Zedong Thought Study Class
Author:
Date: 1968-02-02
Edition: 3

Lanzhou Chemical Industry Company, with the enthusiastic help of the People's Liberation Army's 8046 Unit, organized a "three-in-one" Mao Zedong Thought study class. The picture shows Comrade Wang Ke, former Party Secretary of the Installation Department, sharing his experiences in criticizing and repudiating revisionism during the "three-in-one" Mao Zedong Thought study class.
