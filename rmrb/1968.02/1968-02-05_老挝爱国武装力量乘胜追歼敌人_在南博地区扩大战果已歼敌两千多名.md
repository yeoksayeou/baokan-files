Title: Lao Patriotic Armed Forces Pursue and Annihilate the Enemy, Expanding Victories in the Nambak Region with Over 2,000 Enemy Casualties

Author:

Date: 1968-02-05

Edition: 6

Lao Patriotic Armed Forces Pursue and Annihilate the Enemy

In the Nambak Region, Victories Expand with Over 2,000 Enemy Casualties

KANGPHENG, Feb. 3 (Xinhua) — According to a report from the Pathet Lao News Agency, the Lao Patriotic Armed Forces continued to pursue and search for remnants of the enemy in the Nambak region. On January 22 and 23, they annihilated 170 enemy soldiers, the majority of whom surrendered to the patriotic forces with their weapons.

After the liberation of Nambak by the patriotic forces, large numbers of enemy troops fled into the jungle. With the assistance of local ethnic minorities, the patriotic forces relentlessly pursued and searched for the remnants. Preliminary reports indicate that the patriotic military and people have annihilated over 2,000 enemy soldiers in the Nambak region. Currently, the patriotic forces are continuing their pursuit and annihilation of the remaining enemy troops.

KANGPHENG, Feb. 3 (Xinhua) — According to a broadcast from the Pathet Lao radio station, in January, the patriotic armed forces in the Sam Neua Province of Laos bravely repelled the enemy's "eating away" attacks, achieving significant victories. According to incomplete statistics, in 22 days of battle, they annihilated 249 enemy soldiers, including 124 killed, 28 wounded, 34 captured, and 63 who surrendered to the people.

During these battles, the patriotic armed forces seized over a hundred pieces of weaponry, including cannons, mortars, and rocket launchers, as well as a large amount of military equipment. They also liberated 700 residents.

### 老挝爱国武装力量乘胜追歼敌人  在南博地区扩大战果已歼敌两千多名

1968-02-05
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国武装力量乘胜追歼敌人
    在南博地区扩大战果已歼敌两千多名
    新华社康开三日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝爱国武装力量在南博地区继续追击和搜索残敌，在一月二十二日和二十三日又歼灭敌人一百七十名，其中大多数敌人携带武器向爱国武装力量投降。
    在爱国武装力量解放南博后，大量敌军逃窜到丛林中。爱国武装力量在当地各族人民的协助下，紧紧追击和搜索残敌。据初步消息，爱国军民在南博地区已歼敌两千多人。现在，爱国武装力量追歼残余敌军的战斗仍在继续进行中。
    新华社康开三日电　据巴特寮电台广播，老挝桑怒省的爱国武装力量在一月间，英勇击退了敌人的“蚕食”进攻，取得重大战果。据不完全统计，在二十二天的战斗中，歼灭敌军二百四十九名，其中打死一百二十四名、打伤二十八名、俘虏三十四名、向人民投诚六十三名。
    在这些战斗中，爱国武装力量缴获了上百件包括大炮、迫击炮、火箭筒在内的武器，以及大量军用装备。他们还解放了七百名居民。
