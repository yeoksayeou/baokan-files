Title: A Family of Poor Peasants in Shiyouzi Brigade Studies Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1968-02-25
Edition: 3

A family of poor peasants, the Zhao family, from Shiyouzi Brigade, Shiyouzi People's Commune, Chongli County, Hebei Province, has established a Mao Zedong Thought study class with the help of the People's Liberation Army. This is a scene of them studying in the class.

Photo provided by *Zhangjiakou Daily*.
