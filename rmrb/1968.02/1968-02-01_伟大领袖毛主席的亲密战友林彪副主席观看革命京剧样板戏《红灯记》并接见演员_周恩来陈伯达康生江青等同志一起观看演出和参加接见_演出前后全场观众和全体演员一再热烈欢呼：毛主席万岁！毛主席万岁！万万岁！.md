Title: Vice Chairman Lin Biao, Close Comrade-in-Arms of Chairman Mao, Watches the Revolutionary Model Opera "The Red Lantern" and Meets the Performers
Author:
Date: 1968-02-01
Edition: 1

Vice Chairman Lin Biao, close comrade-in-arms of our great leader Chairman Mao, along with Premier Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, Yao Wenyuan, Xie Fuzhi, Yang Chengwu, Ye Qun, Wu Faxian, Qiu Huizuo, Zhang Xiuzhen, Li Tianhuan, and Liu Jinping, watched the revolutionary model opera "The Red Lantern" performed by the China Peking Opera Theater on the evening of January 31.

When Vice Chairman Lin, Premier Zhou, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, Yao Wenyuan, and other central leaders entered the auditorium, the entire audience was so excited that they raised their red "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and enthusiastically shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years, ten thousand years!"

Senior officials from the various headquarters of the People's Liberation Army, various military branches, and various military regions stationed in Beijing were also invited to watch the performance.

After the performance, Vice Chairman Lin, Premier Zhou, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, Yao Wenyuan, Xie Fuzhi, Yang Chengwu, Ye Qun, Wu Faxian, and other comrades met with all the performers of "The Red Lantern." At this time, both on stage and off, everyone sang "The Great Helmsman Steers Us Across the Sea" and once again shouted enthusiastically for a long time, "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years, ten thousand years!"

		Lin Biao, Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and other comrades went on stage to meet with all the performers after the performance and posed for a group photo.

### 伟大领袖毛主席的亲密战友林彪副主席观看革命京剧样板戏《红灯记》并接见演员  周恩来陈伯达康生江青等同志一起观看演出和参加接见  演出前后全场观众和全体演员一再热烈欢呼：毛主席万岁！毛主席万岁！万万岁！

1968-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    伟大领袖毛主席的亲密战友林彪副主席观看革命京剧样板戏《红灯记》并接见演员
    周恩来陈伯达康生江青等同志一起观看演出和参加接见
    演出前后全场观众和全体演员一再热烈欢呼：毛主席万岁！毛主席万岁！万万岁！
    新华社三十一日讯　我们伟大领袖毛主席的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及周恩来总理、陈伯达、康生、江青、姚文元、谢富治、杨成武、叶群、吴法宪、邱会作、张秀川、李天焕、刘锦平等同志，在一月三十一日晚，观看了中国京剧院演出的革命京剧样板戏《红灯记》。
    当林副主席、周总理、陈伯达、康生、江青、姚文元等中央领导同志进入会场时，全场观众激动得高举红彤彤的《毛主席语录》连连热情欢呼：“毛主席万岁！毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
    中国人民解放军各总部、各军兵种以及在京的各军区负责同志也应邀观看了演出。
    演出结束后，林副主席、周总理、陈伯达、康生、江青、姚文元、谢富治、杨成武、叶群、吴法宪等同志接见了演出《红灯记》的全体演员。这时，台上台下放声高唱《大海航行靠舵手》，再一次长时间热烈欢呼：“毛主席万岁！毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
   （附图片）
   林彪副主席以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、江青等同志，在演出结束后走上舞台接见全体演员，并和他们合影。                                                            
                       《解放军报》记者摄（新华社发）
