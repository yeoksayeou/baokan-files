Title: President of Congo (Brazzaville) Receives Chinese Chargé d'Affaires
Author:
Date: 1968-02-26
Edition: 6

According to Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville. On the 17th, President Massamba-Debat of Congo (Brazzaville) received Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Li Chenguang at the Chinese Embassy in Congo (Brazzaville) and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

During the meeting, President Massamba-Debat praised China's assistance to African countries and the work of Chinese technical personnel in African countries.

### 刚果（布）总统接见我临时代办

1968-02-26
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）总统接见我临时代办
    据新华社布拉柴维尔电　刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴十七日接见了中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光，并同他进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    接见时，马桑巴—代巴总统赞扬了中国向非洲国家提供的援助和在非洲国家的中国技术工作人员的工作。
