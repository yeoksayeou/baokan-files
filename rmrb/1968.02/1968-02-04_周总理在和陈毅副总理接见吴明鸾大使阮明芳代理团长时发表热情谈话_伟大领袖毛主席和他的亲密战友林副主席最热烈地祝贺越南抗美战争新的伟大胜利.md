Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yee Greet Ambassador Vu Mingluan and Deputy Head of Delegation Nguyen Minh Phuong
Author:
Date: 1968-02-04
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi held talks with Ambassador Vu Mingluan and Deputy Head of Delegation Nguyen Minh Phuong, expressing warm sentiments. Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin warmly congratulate the new great victories of the Vietnamese people in their war against American aggression.

Premier Zhou, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group, extended the warmest congratulations to the people of North and South Vietnam. He said: The Vietnamese people have shown unyielding willpower, high organizational discipline, and a fearless spirit of self-sacrifice in their struggle. The Chinese people deeply admire this and are determined to learn from you. We firmly believe that the Vietnamese people, by persevering in a protracted war, will surely defeat the American aggressors and their lackeys. The seven hundred million Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, will resolutely support the Vietnamese people in carrying the war of resistance against American aggression to the end.

Xinhua News Agency, February 3rd: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi today received Ambassador Vu Mingluan of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, engaging in a very cordial conversation.

Ambassador Vu Mingluan, in his conversation, reported to Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi on the new great victories achieved by the entire Vietnamese people in the war of resistance against American aggression, and expressed the confidence and firm resolve of the Vietnamese people in striving for final victory.

Premier Zhou, on behalf of our great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group, extended the warmest congratulations to the entire Vietnamese people for their great victories, under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party, in mobilizing the entire nation, both front and rear, in the war of resistance against American aggression.

Premier Zhou reiterated in his conversation that the seven hundred million Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought and tempered by the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, will resolutely support the Vietnamese people in carrying the war of resistance against American aggression to the end.

Present at the reception were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Affairs Bureau Cao Keqiang, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Dai Ping.

Xinhua News Agency, February 3rd: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi today received Deputy Head of Delegation Nguyen Minh Phuong of the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China, engaging in a very cordial conversation.

Deputy Head of Delegation Nguyen Minh Phuong reported to Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi on the recent broad attacks launched by the people of South Vietnam against the American aggressors and their lackeys, achieving brilliant victories.

Premier Zhou, on behalf of our great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group, extended the warmest congratulations to the people of South Vietnam for their great victories under the leadership of the National Liberation Front.

Premier Zhou praised, saying: The recent powerful attacks launched by the people of South Vietnam against the enemy have the characteristics of combining military and civilian efforts, urban and rural areas, and internal and external forces, bringing the Vietnamese people's war to a high level. The Vietnamese people have shown unyielding willpower, high organizational discipline, and a fearless spirit of self-sacrifice in their struggle. The Chinese people deeply admire this and are determined to learn from you.

Premier Zhou pointed out that the final victory of the Vietnamese people's war of resistance against American aggression is increasingly approaching, but before achieving the final victory, there is still a difficult road ahead, because the enemy will surely make a desperate struggle. Our great leader Chairman Mao said in a telegram to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho: "Persistence is victory." We firmly believe that the Vietnamese people, by persevering in a protracted war, will surely defeat the American aggressors and their lackeys, achieving final victory.

Present at the reception were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Affairs Bureau Cao Keqiang, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Dai Ping.

### 周总理在和陈毅副总理接见吴明鸾大使阮明芳代理团长时发表热情谈话  伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席最热烈地祝贺越南抗美战争新的伟大胜利

1968-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

   周总理在和陈毅副总理接见吴明鸾大使阮明芳代理团长时发表热情谈话
   伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席最热烈地祝贺越南抗美战争新的伟大胜利
    周总理同时代表中共中央、国务院、中央军委、中央文革，向越南北方和南方军民表示最热烈祝贺。他说：越南人民在战斗中表现出的坚强意志，高度的组织性和纪律性，不怕牺牲、赴汤蹈火的大无畏精神，中国人民从内心里感到钦佩，并且一定要向你们学习。我们坚信，越南人民坚持持久战争，一定能够打败美国侵略者及其走狗。用毛泽东思想武装的七亿中国人民，誓作越南人民的坚强后盾，坚决支持越南人民把抗美救国战争进行到底。
    新华社三日讯　国务院总理周恩来和副总理陈毅，今天接见了越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾，同他进行了极为亲切的谈话。
    吴明鸾大使在谈话中，向周恩来总理和陈毅副总理，介绍了全体越南人民在抗美救国战争中取得的新的伟大胜利，并且表达了越南人民争取最后胜利的信心和坚强决心。
    周总理代表我们伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，代表中共中央、国务院、中央军委、中央文革，最热烈地祝贺全体越南人民在越南劳动党的领导下，举国上下，前方后方，一齐动员起来，在抗美救国战争中取得的伟大胜利。
    周总理在谈话中重申，用毛泽东思想武装起来的、经过无产阶级文化大革命锻炼的七亿中国人民，誓作越南人民的坚强后盾，坚决支持越南人民把抗美救国战争进行到底。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长韩念龙，第二亚洲司副司长曹克强，礼宾司副司长戴平。
    新华社三日讯　国务院总理周恩来和副总理陈毅，今天接见了越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳，同他进行了极为亲切的谈话。
    阮明芳代理团长向周恩来总理和陈毅副总理，介绍了越南南方军民最近向美国侵略者及其走狗发动广泛袭击，取得辉煌胜利的情况。
    周恩来总理代表我们伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，代表中共中央、国务院、中央军委、中央文革，最热烈地祝贺越南南方军民在民族解放阵线领导下最近所取得的伟大胜利。
    周总理赞扬说，越南南方人民最近向敌人发动的强大袭击，具有军民结合、城乡结合、内外结合的特点，把越南人民战争发展到一个高度水平。越南人民在战斗中表现出的坚强意志，高度的组织性和纪律性，和不怕牺牲、赴汤蹈火的大无畏精神，中国人民从内心里感到钦佩，并且一定要向你们学习。
    周总理指出，越南人民抗美救国战争的最后胜利越来越接近，但在取得最后胜利之前，还有一段艰苦的路程，因为敌人总是要进行垂死挣扎的。我们伟大领袖毛主席在给阮友寿主席的贺电中说：“坚持下去就是胜利。”我们坚信，越南人民坚持持久战争，一定能够打败美国侵略者及其走狗，取得最后的胜利。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长韩念龙，第二亚洲司副司长曹克强，礼宾司副司长戴平。
