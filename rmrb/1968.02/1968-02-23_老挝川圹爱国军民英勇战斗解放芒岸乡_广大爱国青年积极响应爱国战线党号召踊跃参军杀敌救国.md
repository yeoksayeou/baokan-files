Title: Patriotic Military and People of Xieng Khouang Liberate Mang Xai Village
Author:
Date: 1968-02-23
Edition: 6

Patriotic military and people of Xieng Khouang province in Laos bravely fought and liberated Mang Xai village. Broad masses of patriotic youth actively responded to the call of the Lao Patriotic Front and enthusiastically joined the army to fight the enemy and save the country.

KANGO, Feb. 21 (New China News Agency) According to the Pathet Lao News Agency, the patriotic military and people of Mang Xai village in Xieng Khouang province, Laos, bravely attacked the enemy stationed in the village on February 3, achieving brilliant results and completely liberating the village.

According to the report, the patriotic military and people of Mang Xai village killed 46, wounded 38, captured and surrendered 54, and annihilated the command of two enemy battalions in this battle. They also seized seven cannons of different calibers, ten light and heavy machine guns, and a large number of other weapons and military supplies.

Another report said that the Supreme Command of the Lao People's Liberation Army sent a telegram on the 15th to all patriotic military and people of Mang Xai village in Xieng Khouang province, warmly commending their brilliant victory in the brave battle. The telegram called on the military and people of Mang Xai village to further heighten their vigilance, quickly consolidate the victories already won, actively and proactively attack the enemy, annihilate more enemies, and win greater new victories.

The Voice of Pathet Lao also published a commentary, hailing the new victory of the military and people of Xieng Khouang province.

KANGO, Feb. 22 (New China News Agency) According to the Pathet Lao News Agency, the broad masses of patriotic youth in Laos actively responded to the call of the Lao Patriotic Front to resist the Americans and save the country, enthusiastically joining the army to fight the enemy. According to the report, tens of thousands of male and female youths joined the army last year, directly taking up arms to fight the enemy and save the country. The number of people joining the militia and guerrilla forces in 1967 increased by about 50 percent compared to 1966. In Xieng Khouang province and Muang Xai province, several thousand male and female youths joined the militia and guerrilla forces last year. They bravely fought and smashed the enemy's many "eating away" attacks and "sweeping" operations.

In Vientiane province, hundreds of male and female youths also joined the guerrilla forces. Last year, they bravely counterattacked the enemy's large-scale "sweeping" operation against the eastern part of the province by ten enemy battalions, annihilating more than 350 enemies in one battle.

In Saravane province and Xekong province, 1,356 male and female youths voluntarily enlisted to fight the enemy last year.

KANGO, Feb. 20 (New China News Agency) According to the Pathet Lao News Agency, the patriotic military and people of Sam Neua province in Laos bravely counterattacked the invading American air pirates on February 14, shooting down one American pirate plane.

KANGO, Feb. 19 (New China News Agency) According to the Pathet Lao News Agency, the brave patriotic military and people of Xieng Khouang and Sam Neua provinces in Laos severely punished the American air pirates who were madly bombing the 6th and 7th highways on February 9 and 14, shooting down two American planes in one go.

	Soldiers of the Lao People's Liberation Army are actively training, preparing to go to the front line.

### 老挝川圹爱国军民英勇战斗解放芒岸乡  广大爱国青年积极响应爱国战线党号召踊跃参军杀敌救国

1968-02-23
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝川圹爱国军民英勇战斗解放芒岸乡
    广大爱国青年积极响应爱国战线党号召踊跃参军杀敌救国
    新华社康开二十一日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道：老挝川圹省芒岸乡爱国军民，二月三日英勇袭击驻扎在这个乡的敌人，取得了辉煌战果，并完全解放了这个乡。
    据报道，芒岸乡爱国军民在这次战斗中共消灭和瓦解敌人一百三十八名，其中包括打死四十六名，打伤三十八名，俘虏和迫降五十四名，并歼灭敌军两个营的指挥部，缴获各种不同口径大炮七门、轻重机枪十挺及其它大批武器和军用物资。
    另据报道，老挝人民解放军最高指挥部十五日打电报给川圹省芒岸乡全体爱国军民，热烈表彰他们英勇战斗所取得的辉煌胜利。电报号召芒岸乡军民进一步提高警惕，迅速巩固已经取得的胜利，积极、主动地进攻敌人，消灭更多的敌人，夺取更大的新胜利。
    巴特寮电台也发表评论，欢呼川圹省芒岸乡军民的新胜利。
    新华社康开二十二日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝广大爱国青年积极响应老挝爱国战线党的抗美救国号召，踊跃参军杀敌。据报道，在去年一年中，有成万名男女青年参军，直接拿起武器，杀敌救国。一九六七年参加民兵游击队的人数比一九六六年增加百分之五十左右。在川圹省和芒塞省，去年一年里有几千名男女青年参加民兵游击队。他们英勇战斗，击溃和粉碎了敌人的多次“蚕食”进攻和“扫荡”。
    在万象省，也有数以百计的男女青年参加游击队，去年，他们英勇反击了十营敌军对这个省东部发动的一次大规模“扫荡”，一举歼敌三百五十多名。
    在巴色省和会晒省，去年有一千三百五十六名男女青年自愿报名参军杀敌。
    新华社康开二十日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道：老挝桑怒省爱国军民，二月十四日英勇反击入侵的美国空中强盗，击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社康开十九日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道：英勇的老挝川圹和桑怒省爱国军民，在二月九日和十四日，严惩对第六、第七号公路进行疯狂轰炸的美国空中强盗，一举击落美国飞机两架。
