Title: Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Exert the Might of People's War, Eliminating Over 15,900 Enemies in One Year
Author:
Date: 1968-02-03
Edition: 6

The heroic Lao patriotic military and civilians have exerted the invincible might of people's war. In 1967, they launched fierce attacks on the U.S. aggressors and their lackeys on various battlefields across the country, achieving brilliant victories. According to statistics, the Lao patriotic military and civilians engaged in 1,859 battles with the enemy in the past year, eliminating and dissolving 15,967 enemies. Among them, 8,281 were killed, 4,395 were wounded, 454 were captured, 1,659 surrendered, and 1,178 defected to the people's side.

During this period, the Lao patriotic military and civilians also shot down and destroyed 287 enemy aircraft, seized and destroyed 48 military vehicles, sank 82 motorboats and motorized boats, blew up 7 bridges, seized 3,303 various types of weapons, including 122 different caliber cannons and 248 light and heavy machine guns. They also seized hundreds of tons of ammunition, 178 wireless communication devices, and a large amount of military supplies.

### 老挝爱国军民发挥人民战争威力  一年歼敌一万五千九百多名

1968-02-03
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民发挥人民战争威力
    一年歼敌一万五千九百多名
    新华社康开二日电  据巴特寮通讯社报道：英勇的老挝爱国军民发挥人民战争的无敌威力，一九六七年在全国各个战场上接连地猛烈打击美国强盗及其走狗，取得辉煌胜利。据统计，老挝爱国军民在过去一年中同敌人进行了一千八百五十九次战斗，共消灭和瓦解敌人一万五千九百六十七名，其中打死八千二百八十一名、打伤四千三百九十五名、俘虏四百五十四名、迫降一千六百五十九名、向人民投诚一千一百七十八名。
    老挝爱国军民在这期间还击落击毁敌机二百八十七架，缴获和击毁军车四十八辆，击沉汽艇和机动艇八十二艘，炸毁桥梁七座，缴获各种武器三千三百零三件，其中包括各种不同口径的大炮一百二十二门、轻重机枪二百四十八挺，还缴获弹药成百吨，无线电收发报机一百七十八部，以及大批军用物资。
