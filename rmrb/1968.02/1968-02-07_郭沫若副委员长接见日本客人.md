Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1968-02-07
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo received a delegation of Japanese Social Party parliamentarians led by Matsumoto Yasukiyo on the afternoon of the 5th. The delegation consisted of six people. Guo Moruo had a friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1968-02-07
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社五日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，五日下午接见了由松本康行率领的日本社会党国会议员秘书学习团一行六人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
