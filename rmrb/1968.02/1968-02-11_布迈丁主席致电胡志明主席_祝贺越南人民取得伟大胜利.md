Title: Boumedienne Chairman Congratulates Ho Chi Minh Chairman on Great Victory of Vietnamese People
Author:
Date: 1968-02-11
Edition: 5

	Boumedienne Chairman Wires Ho Chi Minh Chairman
	To Congratulate Vietnamese People on Great Victory

	Xinhua News Agency, Algiers: On February 7, Houari Boumedienne, Chairman of the Algerian National Revolutionary Council and Prime Minister of the Algerian Government, sent a telegram to Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, congratulating the Vietnamese people on their great victories recently achieved in the struggle against U.S. imperialism and its lackeys. Boumedienne also expressed that Algeria is determined to support the Vietnamese people's just struggle until final victory.

	In the telegram, Boumedienne said: "At this moment when the heroic Vietnamese people have achieved brilliant victories in their struggle against the U.S. aggressors and their puppets, I, on behalf of the Algerian people, the Algerian government, and myself, assure you that we stand completely united with you and will always support you unremittingly."

	The telegram said: "Recent developments have shown that the peoples of various countries fighting against aggression are invincible, and imperialism is bound to fail."

	The telegram said: "We have always believed that your just cause will triumph. The Algerian people will always stand by your side until the final victory. I am confident that the final victory is not far off."

### 布迈丁主席致电胡志明主席  祝贺越南人民取得伟大胜利

1968-02-11
第5版()
专栏：

    布迈丁主席致电胡志明主席
    祝贺越南人民取得伟大胜利
    新华社阿尔及尔电　阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会主席、政府总理胡阿里·布迈丁七日打电报给越南民主共和国主席胡志明，祝贺越南人民最近在反对美帝及其走狗的斗争中所取得的伟大胜利，并且表示，阿尔及利亚决心支持越南人民的正义斗争直到取得最后胜利。
    布迈丁主席在电报中说：“在英雄的越南人民在反对美国侵略者及其傀儡的斗争中取得辉煌胜利的时候，我以阿尔及利亚人民、阿尔及利亚政府和我个人的名义向您保证，我们完全同你们团结在一起，始终不懈地支持你们。”
    电报说：“最近的事态发展说明，进行反侵略斗争的各国人民是不可战胜的，帝国主义必败。”
    电报说：“我们一直相信你们的正义事业会取得胜利，阿尔及利亚人民将始终站在你们一边，直到最后胜利。我确信，最后胜利已经为期不远了。”
