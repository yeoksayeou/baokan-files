Title: China and South Yemen Sign Agreement to Establish Diplomatic Relations and Agree to Develop Friendly Cooperation Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1968-02-03
Edition: 1

	China and South Yemen Sign Agreement to Establish Diplomatic Relations
	Agree to Develop Friendly Cooperation Between the Two Countries

	Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: The plenipotentiary representative of the People's Republic of China, Huang Hua, and the plenipotentiary representative of the government of the People's Republic of South Yemen, Muhammad Hadi Awad, signed an agreement on January 31st in Cairo to establish formal diplomatic relations between the two countries. Both sides agreed to issue a joint communiqué, the full text of which is as follows:

	Joint Communiqué

	The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the People's Republic of South Yemen, in accordance with the interests and wishes of the peoples of both countries, have decided to establish formal diplomatic relations and exchange ambassadors.

	The Government of the People's Republic of China has already recognized the People's Republic of South Yemen and its sovereignty over its entire territory and islands. The Government of the People's Republic of South Yemen recognizes the Government of the People's Republic of China as the sole legal government representing the entire Chinese people.

	The two governments agree to develop friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries in accordance with the principles of mutual respect for sovereignty and territorial integrity, non-aggression, non-interference in internal affairs, equality and mutual benefit, and peaceful coexistence.

	February 2, 1968

### 我国和南也门签订建交协议  一致同意发展两国之间的友好合作关系

1968-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和南也门签订建交协议
    一致同意发展两国之间的友好合作关系
    新华社二日讯　中华人民共和国全权代表黄华和南也门人民共和国政府全权代表穆罕默德·哈迪·阿瓦德，于一月三十一日在开罗签订了关于两国建立正式外交关系的协议。双方并商定发表新闻公报，全文如下：
    新闻公报
    中华人民共和国政府和南也门人民共和国政府根据各自国家人民的利益和愿望决定建立正式外交关系并互换大使级外交代表。
    中华人民共和国政府业已承认南也门人民共和国和它对其全部领土和岛屿的主权。南也门人民共和国政府承认中华人民共和国政府是代表全体中国人民的唯一合法政府。
    两国政府一致同意遵照互相尊重主权和领土完整、互不侵犯、互不干涉内政、平等互利和和平共处的原则发展两国之间的友好合作关系。
                                       一九六八年二月二日
