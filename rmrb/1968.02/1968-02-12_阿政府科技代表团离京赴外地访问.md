Title: The Albanian Government Science and Technology Delegation Leaves Beijing for a Visit to Other Places
Author:
Date: 1968-02-12
Edition: 6

By Xinhua News Agency, February 10th

An Albanian government science and technology delegation, led by Prokop Murra, Vice-Chairman of the Albanian State Planning Commission, left Beijing today for a visit to other places, accompanied by Sun Xiaofeng, Vice-Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Petroleum.

During their stay in Beijing, the Albanian science and technology delegation participated in the first meeting of the 12th session of the Sino-Albanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation. Both sides held talks on scientific and technological cooperation projects between the two countries.

### 阿政府科技代表团离京赴外地访问

1968-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    阿政府科技代表团离京赴外地访问
    据新华社十日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会副主席普罗科普·穆拉率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府科学技术代表团，在我石油部副部长孙晓风的陪同下，今天离开北京去外地参观访问。
    阿尔巴尼亚科学技术代表团在京期间，参加了中阿科学技术合作联合委员会第十二届会议的第一次会议，双方并就两国的科学技术合作项目进行了会谈。
