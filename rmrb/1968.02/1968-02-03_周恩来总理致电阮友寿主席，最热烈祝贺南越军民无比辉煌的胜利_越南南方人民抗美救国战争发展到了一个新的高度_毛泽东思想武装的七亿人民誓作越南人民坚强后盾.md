Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Cables Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho, Extending the Warmest Congratulations on the Magnificent Victory of the South Vietnamese Military and People
Author: 
Date: 1968-02-03
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai cabled Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, extending the warmest congratulations on the magnificent victories achieved by the heroic South Vietnamese armed forces and people in their recent overwhelming attacks on the enemy.

The following is the full text of the cable:

To Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam:

Recently, the South Vietnamese armed forces, together with the broad masses of the people, have launched overwhelming attacks on more than fifty enemy-occupied towns and cities, including Saigon, Da Nang, Hue, and other major cities, provincial capitals, and military strongholds, achieving magnificent victories. The news of these victories has brought great joy and inspiration to the entire Chinese people. On behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government, I extend the warmest congratulations to you, to the heroic South Vietnamese armed forces, and to the people of South Vietnam.

Your victories mark a new height in the South Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation. Your victories once again prove the immense power of people's war.

The days of the American aggressors in Vietnam are numbered. The more desperate their situation, the more frenzied their struggles will be. But the more frenzied the enemy becomes, the more it will temper the South Vietnamese people's resolute will to fight and win. Our great leader Chairman Mao has said, "Persist to the end and victory will be yours." We are firmly convinced that the heroic Vietnamese people will continue to strive forward, overcome all difficulties, and ultimately defeat the American aggressors, achieving final victory.

The seven hundred million Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, will stand firmly behind you!

Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
February 2, 1968

周恩来
1968-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理致电阮友寿主席，最热烈祝贺南越军民无比辉煌的胜利
    越南南方人民抗美救国战争发展到了一个新的高度
    毛泽东思想武装的七亿人民誓作越南人民坚强后盾
    新华社二日讯　周恩来总理二日打电报给越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团阮友寿主席，最热烈地祝贺英雄的越南南方人民解放武装力量和越南南方人民最近以排山倒海之势，向敌人发动的袭击中取得的无比辉煌的胜利。电报全文如下：越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团阮友寿主席：
    最近，越南南方人民解放武装力量同广大人民群众一道，以排山倒海之势，出奇制胜，同时向敌占区五十多个城镇，包括西贡、岘港、顺化等大城市、省会和军事据点，发动袭击，取得了无比辉煌的胜利。捷报传来，全中国人民无不为之欢欣鼓舞。我代表中国人民和中国政府，向你，向英雄的越南南方人民解放武装力量和越南南方人民，表示最热烈的祝贺。
    你们的这次胜利，标志着越南南方人民的抗美救国战争发展到了一个新的高度。你们的这次胜利，再一次证明了人民战争的无比强大的威力。
    美国侵略者在越南的日子不长了，惟其不长，它必然还会进行更加疯狂的垂死挣扎。但是，敌人越是疯狂，正好更加锻炼越南人民敢于斗争、敢于胜利的坚强意志。我们的伟大领袖毛主席说，“坚持下去就是胜利。”我们深信，英雄的越南人民，一定能够再接再厉，排除万难，奋勇前进，彻底打败美国侵略者，取得最后胜利。
    用毛泽东思想武装起来的七亿中国人民，誓作你们的坚强后盾！
                          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                  一九六八年二月二日
