Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi Meet with Vietnamese Diplomats
Author:
Date: 1968-02-04
Edition: 2

	Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Vu Minh Luan (fourth from the left). This is a group photo taken during the meeting. Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter.

	Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Deputy Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Nguyen Minh Phuong (third from the left). This is a group photo taken during the meeting. Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
