Title: People Around the World Look to Beijing, the Center of World Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-02-09
Edition: 5

Today, hundreds of millions of people around the world are looking to Beijing, the center of the world revolution. Hundreds of millions of red hearts are turned towards Chairman Mao, the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time. The image shows Tanzanian friends, with hearts full of longing and boundless respect, paying tribute to the great leader Chairman Mao's statue.
