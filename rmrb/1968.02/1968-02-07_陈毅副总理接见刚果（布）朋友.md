Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Friends from Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1968-02-07
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi today met with Congolese (Brazzaville) friends including George Bawasina and eight others who are visiting our country. He had a warm and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Vice Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Shen Guang. Congolese (Brazzaville) Ambassador to China Bazanga was also present.

### 陈毅副总理接见刚果（布）朋友

1968-02-07
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见刚果（布）朋友
    新华社六日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见正在我国访问的刚果（布）朋友鲍西纳·乔治等九人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，邮电部副部长申光在座。
    刚果（布）驻我国大使巴赞加也在座。
