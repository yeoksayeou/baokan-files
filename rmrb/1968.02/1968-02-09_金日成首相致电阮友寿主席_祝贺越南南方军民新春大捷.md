Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Cables Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho
Author:
Date: 1968-02-09
Edition: 3

	Premier Kim Il-sung Cables Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho
	To Congratulate the People and Soldiers of South Vietnam on Their Spring Offensive Victories

	P'yongyang, February 8 (New China News Agency) — Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea sent a cable on February 5 to Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, to congratulate the people and soldiers of South Vietnam on their Spring Offensive victories.

	The cable said: "The armed forces of the people of South Vietnam have recently launched surprise attacks on almost all cities, including Saigon, and on military bases in the enemy-occupied areas of South Vietnam, inflicting heavy blows on U.S. imperialism and the traitorous clique of Nguyen Van Thieu and Nguyen Cao Ky, and winning brilliant victories."

	The cable said: "The new feats of arms achieved by the armed forces of the people of South Vietnam and the patriotic people have great military and political significance for hastening the final victory of the struggle to resist the United States and save the nation."

	The cable said: "This victory has brought boundless joy to the revolutionary people of the whole world and has dealt a fatal blow to the U.S. aggressors and their lackeys."

### 金日成首相致电阮友寿主席  祝贺越南南方军民新春大捷

1968-02-09
第3版()
专栏：

    金日成首相致电阮友寿主席
    祝贺越南南方军民新春大捷
    新华社平壤八日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首相金日成五日打电报给越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿，祝贺越南南方军民新春大捷。
    电报说：“越南南方人民解放武装力量最近对包括西贡在内的几乎所有城市和南越敌占区的敌人军事基地发动了突袭，给予美帝国主义和阮文绍—阮高其卖国集团以沉重的打击，取得辉煌的胜利。”
    电报说：“越南南方人民解放武装力量和爱国人民建立的新战功对于加速抗美救国斗争的最后胜利具有巨大的军事和政治意义。”
    电报说：“这个胜利使全世界革命人民无限高兴，给予美帝侵略者及其走狗以致命的打击。”
