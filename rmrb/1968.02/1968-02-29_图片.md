Title: Famous National Agricultural Labor Model and Deputy Director of the Hebei Revolutionary Committee, Gao Yang County's Wugong Commune Wugong Brigade Leader, Geng Changsuo, Leads by Example in Spring Plowing
Author:
Date: 1968-02-29
Edition: 3

Famous national agricultural labor model, deputy director of the Hebei Revolutionary Committee, and brigade leader of Wugong Brigade in Wugong Commune, Gao Yang County, Geng Changsuo, follows Chairman Mao's teachings. During the busy spring plowing season, he leads by example, studying and working alongside the commune members. Together, they have thrown themselves into the front lines of spring plowing production, determined to achieve new feats for the people in spring plowing production. This is Geng Changsuo (front left) working with commune members to transport fertilizer.
