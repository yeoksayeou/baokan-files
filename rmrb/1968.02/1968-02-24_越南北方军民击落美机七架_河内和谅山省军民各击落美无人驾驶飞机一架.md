Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Seven U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-02-24
Edition: 5

The military and civilians of North Vietnam have shot down seven U.S. aircraft. The military and civilians of Hanoi and Lang Son Province each shot down one U.S. unmanned reconnaissance aircraft.

According to reports from here, on the 18th, the military and civilians of Hanoi, in their counterattack against American air pirates, shot down one U.S. unmanned reconnaissance aircraft.

Prior to this, on the 16th, the military and civilians of Lang Son Province also shot down one U.S. unmanned reconnaissance aircraft. On the 17th, the military and civilians of Phu Tho Province shot down one invading U.S. jet aircraft.

According to reports from here, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down four U.S. pirate aircraft on the 11th, 14th, and 15th.

It is reported that two of the U.S. pirate aircraft were shot down by militia fighters in Quang Ninh County and Dong Hai City on the 11th and 15th, respectively.

### 越南北方军民击落美机七架  河内和谅山省军民各击落美无人驾驶飞机一架

1968-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机七架
    河内和谅山省军民各击落美无人驾驶飞机一架
    新华社河内电　据这里报道，河内市军民，十八日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落美国无人驾驶侦察机一架。
    在这以前，谅山省军民，十六日也曾击落一架美国无人驾驶侦察机。十七日，富寿省军民击落了入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
    新华社河内电　据这里公布，越南北方广平省军民在十一、十四和十五日三天击落美国强盗飞机四架。
    据报道，其中两架美国强盗飞机是先后在十一日和十五日由广宁县和洞海市的民兵战士击落的。
