Title: Agreement on Sino-Albanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-02-27
Edition: 6

The agreement on the 12th session of the Sino-Albanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation was signed in Beijing today.

Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen attended the signing ceremony.

On behalf of China, the agreement was signed by Sun Xiaofeng, Vice Minister of the Petroleum Industry and head of the Chinese government's scientific and technological delegation. On behalf of Albania, the agreement was signed by Prokop Murra, Vice Chairman of the Albanian State Planning Commission and head of the Albanian government's scientific and technological delegation.

Those present at the signing ceremony included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, Albanian Ambassador to China Natanaili, and all members of the Chinese and Albanian government scientific and technological delegations.

Before the signing, Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen met with Prokop Murra and all members of the Albanian government's scientific and technological delegation, engaging in a very cordial conversation.

### 中阿科技合作会议议定书在京签字  聂荣臻副总理出席并接见阿政府科技代表团

1968-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

    中阿科技合作会议议定书在京签字
    聂荣臻副总理出席并接见阿政府科技代表团
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国科学技术合作联合委员会第十二届会议议定书今天在北京签字。
    国务院副总理聂荣臻出席了签字仪式。
    在议定书上签字的，中国方面是中国政府科技代表团团长、石油部副部长孙晓风；阿尔巴尼亚方面是阿尔巴尼亚政府科技代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会副主席普罗科普·穆拉。
    参加签字仪式的，还有外交部副部长乔冠华，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利，以及中国和阿尔巴尼亚的政府科技代表团全体团员。
    签字以前，聂荣臻副总理接见了普罗科普·穆拉团长和阿尔巴尼亚政府科技代表团全体团员，同他们进行了极为亲切的谈话。
