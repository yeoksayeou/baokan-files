Title: The Revolutionary and Production Situation in the Rural Areas of Yanji County, Jilin Province, is Excellent
Author: Zhu Xu Shi Guangfu
Date: 1968-02-22
Edition: 3

The vast rural areas of Yanji County, Jilin Province, where revolutionary committees have already been established, are experiencing a thriving revolutionary and production situation. Preparations for spring farming are in full swing. The image shows soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army working alongside the poor and lower-middle peasants of the Tongfo Public Commune's Tongxin Brigade to transport fertilizer.
