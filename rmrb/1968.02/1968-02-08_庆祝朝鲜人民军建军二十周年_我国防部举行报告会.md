Title: Celebrating the 20th Anniversary of the Founding of the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1968-02-08
Edition: 6

The Ministry of National Defense held a report meeting this afternoon to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army.

Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Wang Xinting, Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department Zhang Xianyue, and responsible officials from various military branches, as well as representatives from the PLA's army, navy, and air force totaling over 800 people, attended the meeting.

The acting charge d'affaires of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's embassy in China, Kim Jaesuk, and military attache Kim Heokdae, along with deputy military attache Kim Byungho, were invited to attend the meeting.

The meeting was presided over by Deputy Director of the Office of the Ministry of National Defense Pan Zhenwu. Kim Heokdae was invited to give a report at the meeting.

A film was shown after the meeting.

### 庆祝朝鲜人民军建军二十周年  我国防部举行报告会

1968-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜人民军建军二十周年
    我国防部举行报告会
    新华社七日讯　国防部今天下午举行报告会，庆祝朝鲜人民军建军二十周年。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭、总后勤部副部长张贤约和各军种兵种负责人，以及中国人民解放军陆海空三军指战员代表八百多人出席了报告会。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑和武官金鹤大校、副武官金炳浩少校应邀出席了报告会。
    报告会由国防部办公厅副主任潘振武主持。金鹤大校应邀在会上作了报告。
    会后放映了电影。
