Title: The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is Developing in an Excellent Situation
Author:
Date: 1968-02-27
Edition: 1

Under the brilliant guidance of Chairman Mao's latest instructions, the entire province of Hunan is vigorously promoting Mao Zedong Thought study classes, actively learning from and applying Chairman Mao's works, and carrying out the great mass movement to thoroughly implement Chairman Mao's latest instructions on an unprecedented scale. The situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is excellent.

This is a photo of the residents of Changsha City's Qing Shui Tang Street participating in a Mao Zedong Thought study class, sharing their experiences in criticizing and repudiating revisionism and combating selfishness.
