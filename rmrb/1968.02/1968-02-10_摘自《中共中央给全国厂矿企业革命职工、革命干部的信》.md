Title: Message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to Revolutionary Workers and Cadres in All Factories and Mines Across the Country
Author:
Date: 1968-02-10
Edition: 4

You should adhere to the regulations of the Party Central Committee and maintain the eight-hour workday. Outside of the eight-hour workday, you should continue to engage in the Cultural Revolution. It is strictly forbidden to leave your production or work post without authorization during working hours. You must struggle against phenomena such as absenteeism and shirking responsibilities.

### 摘自《中共中央给全国厂矿企业革命职工、革命干部的信》

1968-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    你们应该按照党中央的规定，坚持八小时工作制，在八小时工作制以外的时间，坚持文化革命。不许在生产时间内，擅自离开生产和工作岗位。要同一些无故旷工、敷衍了事的不良现象作斗争。
       摘自《中共中央给全国厂矿企业革命职工、革命干部的信》
