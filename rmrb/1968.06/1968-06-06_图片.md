Title: Workers of the Daxinggou Forestry Bureau Automobile Team Criticize the Counter-Revolutionary Revisionist Line in the Forestry System
Author: Wáng Lèqún
Date: 1968-06-06
Edition: 4

Workers of the Daxinggou Forestry Bureau Automobile Team in Jilin Province are seizing their rest time to fiercely criticize the counter-revolutionary revisionist line of China's Khrushchev in the forestry system.
