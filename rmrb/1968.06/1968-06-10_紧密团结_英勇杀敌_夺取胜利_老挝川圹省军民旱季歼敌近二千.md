Title: United and Brave, Seize Victory – Nearly 2,000 Enemies Annihilated in the Dry Season in Laos' Xieng Khouang Province
Author:
Date: 1968-06-10
Edition: 6

United and brave, seize victory
Nearly 2,000 enemies annihilated in the dry season in Laos' Xieng Khouang Province

According to Xinhua News Agency, Kengtung, June 8th – According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station: In the six months of the 1967-1968 dry season, the people's liberation army and patriotic neutral forces of Xieng Khouang Province, along with various ethnic groups, united closely, fought bravely, and achieved great victories.

The broadcast said that during this period, the patriotic military and civilians of Xieng Khouang Province engaged in 138 battles, annihilating nearly 1,900 enemy soldiers, shooting down 10 enemy aircraft, and seizing 218 various weapons. Additionally, 416 enemy soldiers surrendered with their weapons.

The broadcast said that at the same time, 128 villages with a total of more than 12,000 residents were liberated, and approximately 740 people left the enemy-occupied areas to settle in the liberated zones.

The broadcast said that during this period, the patriotic armed forces reclaimed the vast regions of Muang Xieng, Tathom, and Tavane, and destroyed the nests of American-sponsored bandits in Xieng Sane, Naxay, Boun Oum, Muang Te, and Muang Phou, further expanding and consolidating the Xieng Khouang-Charl Plain liberated zone.

The patriotic armed forces also launched lightning attacks deep into enemy-occupied areas, raiding the headquarters of the 23rd Camp of the pseudo-army and the logistical base in Nong Sour.

According to another report from the Pathet Lao News Agency: On May 29th, the patriotic military and civilians of Kongsedong County, Saravane Province, killed 82 enemies and wounded many more in a counter-"sweeping" battle, seizing a large amount of weapons, ammunition, and military supplies.

Previously, the military and civilians of this province intercepted the enemy on April 9th, 20th, and May 11th as they moved from Nam Bou to the Li Xan area, killing 59 enemies and wounding many more.

On April 28th, the guerrilla forces and people of Dong Xay, Udomxay Province, annihilated 82 enemies in an anti-bandit battle.

### 紧密团结　英勇杀敌　夺取胜利  老挝川圹省军民旱季歼敌近二千

1968-06-10
第6版()
专栏：

    紧密团结　英勇杀敌　夺取胜利
    老挝川圹省军民旱季歼敌近二千
    据新华社康开八日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝川圹省人民解放军和爱国中立力量以及各族人民，在一九六七——一九六八年旱季中的六个月内，紧密团结，英勇杀敌，取得了巨大胜利。
    广播说，在这期间，川圹省爱国军民进行了一百三十八次战斗，共歼灭敌人一千九百多名，击落敌机十架，缴获各种武器二百一十八件。此外，还有四百一十六名敌军携械投诚。
    广播说，与此同时，有一万二千多名居民的一百二十八个村庄获得了解放，另有约七百四十人离开了敌占区，到解放区安家落户。
    广播说，在这期间，爱国武装力量收复了芒银、塔通和塔文等广大地区，并捣毁了美国豢养的匪徒在川佐、纳西亚、奔翁、芒特和芒波的巢穴，从而进一步扩大和巩固了川圹—查尔平原解放区。
    爱国武装力量还深入到敌人占领的地区发动闪电攻势，袭击了伪军第二十三营的营部和农绥的后勤基地。
    另据巴特寮通讯社报道：沙拉湾省孔塞东县爱国军民五月二十九日在反“扫荡”战斗中打死敌人八十二名，打伤多名，并缴获大批武器、弹药和军用物资。
    在这以前，这个省军民在四月九日、二十日和五月十一日，截击从农布开往寮岸地区的敌人，共打死敌人五十九名，打伤多名。
    乌多姆塞省东舍地区游击队和人民四月二十八日在剿匪战斗中，歼敌八十二名。
