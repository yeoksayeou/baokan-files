Title: China's Ministry of National Defense Hosts Banquet to See Off the Malian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-06-20
Edition: 5

The Ministry of National Defense held a banquet on June 15 in Shanghai to warmly see off the Malian military delegation led by Mamadou Diakité, the Malian Republic's minister-level representative responsible for defense and security.

Vice Minister of National Defense Xiao Jingguang and Mamadou Diakité, the delegation leader, both spoke at the banquet, expressing their wish for the deep friendship between the peoples and armed forces of China and Mali to continue to develop.

Comrade Xiao Jingguang said in his speech that the Chinese people, following the teachings of our great leader Chairman Mao, have always regarded supporting the Malian people's struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism, and supporting the Malian people's self-reliant efforts to build their country, as our internationalist duty. In the just struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism, the Chinese people will always be the most loyal and reliable friends of the Malian people.

Mamadou Diakité said at the banquet that the unforgettable 15 days spent in your beautiful country have made me feel the sincere friendship of the Chinese people towards the Malian people. He praised the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by our great leader Chairman Mao.

He said, "I sincerely wish this unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution a complete victory, and wish Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!"

Also invited to the banquet were Ms. Msa Kuribali, Chargé d'Affaires of the Malian Embassy in China.

Senior officials of the Shanghai garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Shiying, Liu Haotian, Zhou Jianping, and members of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee, Feng Guozhu, attended the banquet as hosts.

New China News Agency, Shanghai, June 19 — The Malian military delegation led by Mamadou Diakité, the Malian Republic's minister-level representative responsible for defense and security, concluded their friendly visit to our country. They departed Shanghai by plane on the afternoon of the 15th, carrying with them the deep friendship of the Chinese people towards the Malian people.

Vice Minister of National Defense Xiao Jingguang, senior officials of the PLA Navy, Air Force, and Army in Shanghai, Li Shiying, Liu Haotian, Zhou Jianping, and members of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee, Feng Guozhu, as well as over 500 soldiers and Red Guards, went to the airport to see off the Malian guests.

Ms. Msa Kuribali, Chargé d'Affaires of the Malian Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

During their stay in Shanghai, the Malian military delegation visited and inspected the East Sea Fleet of the Navy and the Malu People's Commune in the suburbs, and were warmly welcomed by the soldiers and revolutionary masses of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 我国防部设宴欢送马里军事代表团  迪亚基特团长祝愿中国文化大革命取得完全胜利

1968-06-20
第5版()
专栏：

    我国防部设宴欢送马里军事代表团
    迪亚基特团长祝愿中国文化大革命取得完全胜利
    新华社上海十九日电　国防部十五日在上海举行宴会，热烈欢送由马里共和国负责国防和安全的部长级代表马马杜·迪亚基特率领的马里军事代表团。
    我国国防部副部长萧劲光和马马杜·迪亚基特团长先后在宴会上讲话，共祝中马两国人民和两国军队之间的深厚友谊不断发展。
    萧劲光同志在讲话中说，中国人民遵循伟大领袖毛主席的教导，一向把支持马里人民反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争，把支持马里人民在自力更生的基础上建设祖国的斗争，看作是自己义不容辞的国际主义义务。在反对帝国主义、新老殖民主义的正义斗争中，中国人民永远是马里人民最忠实、最可靠的朋友。
    马马杜·迪亚基特团长在宴会上说，在你们美丽的国家度过了难忘的十五天，我感到了中国人民对马里人民的真诚友谊。他赞扬由我们的伟大领袖毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命。
    他说，我热诚地祝愿这场史无前例的无产阶级文化大革命取得完全的胜利，祝愿毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！
    应邀出席宴会的，还有马里驻中国大使馆临时代办姆萨·库里巴利。
    中国人民解放军驻上海部队负责人李世焱、刘浩天、周建平和上海市革命委员会领导成员冯国柱等出席宴会作陪。
    新华社上海十九日电　由马里共和国负责国防和安全的部长级代表马马杜·迪亚基特率领的马里军事代表团，结束了在我国的友好访问，满载着中国人民对马里人民的深厚友谊，十五日下午乘飞机离开上海回国。
    我国国防部副部长萧劲光、中国人民解放军驻上海陆海空三军负责人李世焱、刘浩天、周建平等和上海市革命委员会领导成员冯国柱，以及人民解放军指战员、红卫兵小将五百多人到机场热烈欢送马里客人。
    马里驻中国大使馆临时代办姆萨·库里巴利也到机场送行。
    马里军事代表团在上海期间参观访问了海军东海舰队和市郊马陆人民公社，受到中国人民解放军指战员和革命群众的热烈欢迎。
