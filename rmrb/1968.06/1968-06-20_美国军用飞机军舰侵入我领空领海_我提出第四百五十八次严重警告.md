Title: U.S. Military Aircraft and Warships Intrude into Our Airspace and Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1968-06-20
Edition: 5

U.S. military aircraft and warships intrude into our airspace and territorial waters
We issue the 458th serious warning

	New China News Agency, June 19th

	On June 15th, from 10:05 to 10:45, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 7:48 to 11:36, one U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters of the restricted zones around Dangan Island and Beijian Island in Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft and warships, the spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issues the 458th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机军舰侵入我领空领海  我提出第四百五十八次严重警告

1968-06-20
第5版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机军舰侵入我领空领海
    我提出第四百五十八次严重警告
    新华社十九日讯　六月十五日十时零五分至十时四十五分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。同日七时四十八分至十一时三十六分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省担杆岛、北尖岛禁区领海。对于美国军用飞机和军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百五十八次严重警告。
