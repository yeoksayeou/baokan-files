Title: Significant Increase in Sea Salt Production in Liaoning's Salt Regions This Year
Author:
Date: 1968-06-09
Edition: 2

The revolutionary workers and cadres in Liaoning's salt regions, with the help of the People's Liberation Army's Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams, have resolutely implemented Chairman Mao's great line of "grasp revolution, promote production." They have been advancing revolution and production without pause. From January to April this year, sea salt production increased by 70.8 percent compared to the same period last year. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 辽宁省盐区今年海盐生产有很大增长

1968-06-09
第2版()
专栏：

    辽宁省盐区今年海盐生产有很大增长
    辽宁盐区的广大革命职工、革命干部，在解放军毛泽东思想宣传队的帮助下，坚决贯彻毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，不停顿地把革命和生产推向前进。今年一至四月份，海盐生产比去年同期增长百分之七十点八。（据新华社讯）
