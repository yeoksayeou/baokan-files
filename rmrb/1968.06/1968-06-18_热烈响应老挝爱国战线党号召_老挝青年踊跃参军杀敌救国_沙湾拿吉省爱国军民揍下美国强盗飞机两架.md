Title: Enthusiastically Responding to the Call of the Lao Patriotic Front, Lao Youth Eagerly Join the Army to Fight the Enemy and Save the Nation; Patriotic Military and Civilians in Savannakhet Province Shoot Down Two American Aggressor Aircraft

Author:

Date: 1968-06-18

Edition: 6

Young men and women from various provinces in southern Laos have actively responded to the call of the Lao Patriotic Front, eagerly joining the Lao People's Liberation Army to fight the enemy and save the nation.

According to incomplete statistics, in the past three months, nearly a thousand young people from various provinces in southern Laos have joined the army. Among them, more than three hundred are from Xam Neua Province. Some of these young people have traveled over seventy or eighty kilometers through mountains and rivers to find the People's Liberation Army and apply for enlistment.

During this period, thousands of young women have also joined the militia and guerrilla units, fighting against the enemy and defending their homes.

According to another report, in the past six months from last winter to this spring, hundreds of young people from Luan Namtha Province have also joined the Lao People's Liberation Army.

According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, the patriotic military and civilians of Savannakhet Province shot down two American aggressor aircraft on May 20 and June 1.

### 热烈响应老挝爱国战线党号召  老挝青年踊跃参军杀敌救国  沙湾拿吉省爱国军民揍下美国强盗飞机两架

1968-06-18
第6版()
专栏：

    热烈响应老挝爱国战线党号召
    老挝青年踊跃参军杀敌救国
    沙湾拿吉省爱国军民揍下美国强盗飞机两架
    新华社康开十七日电  据巴特寮通讯社报道：老挝南方各省男女青年，积极响应老挝爱国战线党的号召，踊跃参加老挝人民解放军，杀敌救国。
    据不完全统计，近三个月来，老挝南方各省有近一千名青年参军，其中甘蒙省就有三百多名。这些青年中，有的人爬山涉水七、八十公里，找人民解放军申请入伍。
    在这期间，还有成千名女青年加入民兵游击队，打击敌人，保卫家园。
    另据报道，在去冬今春的六个月中，琅南塔省也有数以百计的青年参加老挝人民解放军。
    新华社康开十七日电据巴特寮电台广播，老挝沙湾拿吉省爱国军民五月二十日和六月一日共击落美国强盗飞机两架。
