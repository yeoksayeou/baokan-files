Title: Dongfeng Mine Focuses on Revolution to Boost Coal Production
Author:
Date: 1968-06-18
Edition: 2

The revolutionary workers of Dongfeng Coal Mine in Hunjiang, Jilin Province, have been deeply and persistently engaged in revolutionary criticism. By promoting revolution to drive production, they have facilitated the development of production. The entire mine has completed and exceeded the coal production task for the first five months of this year ahead of schedule.

Currently, the revolutionary and production situation at this mine is excellent. The broad revolutionary workers are determined to raise high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, to grasp class struggle firmly, to carry out revolutionary criticism deeply and persistently, to further develop the excellent situation, and to achieve even greater victories in revolution and production.

### 东风矿狠抓革命带动煤炭增产

1968-06-18
第2版()
专栏：

    东风矿狠抓革命带动煤炭增产
    吉林省浑江东风煤矿的革命职工，深入持久地开展革命大批判，以革命带动生产，促进了生产的发展。全矿提前超额完成了今年头五个月的煤炭生产任务。
    目前，这个矿的革命、生产形势一派大好，广大革命职工决心更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，狠抓阶级斗争，深入持久地开展革命大批判，进一步发展大好形势，夺取革命、生产的更大胜利。
