Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down 12 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-06-16
Edition: 5

The military and civilians of North Vietnam have shot down 12 U.S. aircraft.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi, it was announced here that on the morning of the 9th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down another invading U.S. jet fighter-bomber. On the 6th and 8th, the military and civilians of Nghe An, Quang Binh, and Quang Tri provinces and the Vinh Linh area had shot down four U.S. aggressor aircraft in succession.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi, it was announced here that the military and civilians of North Vietnam have recently shot down seven more invading U.S. aggressor aircraft in combat.

On the 9th, the military and civilians of the Vinh Linh area and Quang Binh Province shot down two U.S. aircraft. Before this, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province also shot down a U.S. F-105 fighter on the 6th.

The military and civilians of the Vinh Linh area and Quang Binh Province shot down two U.S. F-105 fighters on the 8th and 10th. On the 11th, the militia of Cam Chau County in Quang Binh Province not only shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft but also captured a live U.S. air pirate.

### 越南北方军民击落美机十二架

1968-06-16
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机十二架
    据新华社河内电　据这里公布：越南北方广平省军民九日上午又打落一架入侵的美国喷气式战斗轰炸机。在六日和八日，义安、河静、广平三省和永灵区的军民曾先后打落四架美国强盗飞机。
    据新华社河内电　据这里公布：越南北方军民最近在战斗中又打落七架入侵的美国强盗飞机。
    九日，永灵区和广平省军民打落两架美国飞机。在这以前，广平省军民还在六日打落一架美国Ｆ—105战斗机。
    永灵区和广平省军民在八日和十日共打落两架美国Ｆ—105战斗机。十一日，河静省锦川县民兵不仅打落了两架入侵的美国强盗飞机，并且活捉了一名美国飞贼。
