Title: Liaoyuan Mining Bureau Completes May Plan Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1968-06-14
Edition: 3

Under the guidance of Chairman Mao's latest series of instructions, and inspired by the joyous news of Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin receiving the representatives of the coal mine revolution and production promotion conference, the revolutionary movement in the Liaoyuan Mining Bureau of Jilin Province is thriving, and production is booming. In May, the entire bureau completed the national coal production plan two days ahead of schedule and exceeded the quota.

### 辽源矿务局提前完成五月计划

1968-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

    辽源矿务局提前完成五月计划
    在毛主席一系列最新指示的指引下，在毛主席和林副主席接见煤矿抓革命、促生产会议代表的喜讯鼓舞下，吉林省辽源矿务局革命搞得轰轰烈烈，生产搞得热气腾腾。五月份全局提前两天超额完成了国家规定的煤炭生产计划。
