Title: Iraqi Workers, Students, and the Masses Stage Anti-American Demonstration in Baghdad
Author:
Date: 1968-06-25
Edition: 5

Iraqi workers, students, and the broad masses of the people, carrying large banners that read "Imperialism and Zionism are the common enemies of the Kurdish and Arab peoples," and shouting slogans such as "Down with American imperialism!" "Against 'political solutions'!" and "Persist in armed struggle!" held an anti-American demonstration in Baghdad. The demonstration was photographed by a Xinhua News Agency reporter.
