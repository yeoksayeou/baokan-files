Title: Heavy Artillery Strikes Base, Inflicting Heavy Losses on U.S. Troops in Sweeping Operations, Achieving Great Victory
Author:
Date: 1968-06-13
Edition: 6

The people's armed forces in the South Vietnamese province of Quang Tri have, in eight days, annihilated more than a thousand U.S. troops.

According to a report from the Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi, the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency reported: The people's liberation armed forces in the South Vietnamese province of Quang Tri, on May 30 and 31, launched fierce attacks on the enemy in the areas of Duc Lap, Kon Tum, and Khe Sanh, annihilating more than 800 enemy troops in two days.

The report said that on the afternoon of May 30, the people's liberation armed forces in Quang Tri Province ambushed a battalion of U.S. invading troops four kilometers east of Duc Lap. After several hours of intense battle, the liberation army killed and wounded more than 200 enemy troops, forcing the remaining enemy to flee in disarray.

On the same day, the liberation army's artillery in the Duc Lap area repeatedly shelled the enemy bases at Bai Son, Kon Tum, Tan Son Nhat, and Quan An, killing and wounding more than 100 enemy troops.

On the same day, the people's liberation armed forces in the Khe Sanh area ambushed a company of U.S. troops between Hill 689 and Hill 522, annihilating more than 100 enemy troops.

On the morning of May 31, two companies of U.S. troops advanced from Bai Son towards the southeast of Kon Tum. The liberation army in the Kon Tum area launched a fierce ambush, killing and wounding more than 100 enemy troops. At 4 p.m., a group of enemy troops daringly moved out from Kon Tum. The liberation army's artillery opened fire, killing and wounding more than 30 enemy troops. At 4:30 p.m., the enemy dispatched eight helicopters to airlift reinforcements. The liberation army's artillery, undaunted, continued to fight, annihilating another 30 enemy troops.

At 2:45 a.m. on May 31, the liberation army in the Khe Sanh area launched a multi-pronged attack on the enemy stronghold west of Chi Village. After 40 minutes of battle, the liberation army almost completely annihilated a U.S. company and severely damaged another. At the same time, the liberation army's artillery fiercely shelled the U.S. command post and artillery positions near Chi Village, preventing them from supporting the enemy in Chi Village. The liberation army's artillery also shelled the enemy stronghold at Dak To, causing many warehouses to catch fire and burn.

At 10 a.m., the enemy dispatched two companies of U.S. troops and some tanks to Chi Village. The liberation army soldiers immediately ambushed the enemy, annihilating 70 and seizing a batch of weapons.

According to a report from the Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi, the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency reported: The people's liberation armed forces in the northern region of Quang Tri Province, from June 4 to 6, killed and wounded more than 200 U.S. troops and shot down seven enemy aircraft.

The people's liberation armed forces' artillery, on June 4, fiercely shelled the U.S. troops conducting sweeps from the Kon Tum area, killing and wounding more than 100 enemy troops. On the afternoon of June 5, the people's liberation armed forces ambushed a company of U.S. troops advancing from Hoang to the southeast of Da Nang in the Khe Sanh area, annihilating 30 enemy troops. The enemy hastily dispatched aircraft for cover, which were immediately shot down by two of our forces.

On the morning of June 6, the people's liberation armed forces again ambushed a company of U.S. troops north of Ba Chuc, annihilating 70 enemy troops. On the same day, the people's liberation armed forces' artillery also fiercely attacked the enemy strongholds at Ba Chuc and Dak To, killing and wounding more than 100 enemy troops.

The enemy hastily dispatched helicopters for cover. The various anti-aircraft artillery units of the people's liberation armed forces immediately launched fierce attacks, shooting down five enemy aircraft.

Previously, from June 2 to 4, the people's liberation armed forces in the northern region of Quang Tri Province also annihilated more than 110 U.S. invading troops in the Khe Sanh area.

### 猛烈炮轰基地  痛打“扫荡”的美军  取得巨大战果  南越广治省人民武装八天歼美军一千多

1968-06-13
第6版()
专栏：

    猛烈炮轰基地  痛打“扫荡”的美军  取得巨大战果
    南越广治省人民武装八天歼美军一千多
    据新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广治省人民解放武装力量，五月三十日和三十一日，在育庙、昆天地区和溪山战场猛烈打击敌人，两天歼敌八百多名。
    报道说，五月三十日下午，广治省人民解放武装力量在育庙东面四公里的地方截击一营美国侵略军，经过几个小时激战，解放军打死、打伤敌人二百多名，残敌仓惶逃窜。
    这一天，在育庙地区的解放军炮兵，多次炮击拜山、昆天、卵石坡、关昂等敌军基地，打死、打伤敌人一百多名。
    同一天，溪山地区的人民解放武装力量，在六八九高地和五二二高地之间伏击一连美军，歼敌百余名。
    三十一日上午，两连美军从拜山向昆天东南进犯。昆天地区的解放军又猛烈截击敌人，打死、打伤敌人一百多名。下午四时，一股敌军冒险从昆天出动，解放军炮兵向敌人开火，打死、打伤敌人三十多名。四时三十分，敌人出动八架直升飞机空投增援部队，解放军炮兵再接再厉，又消灭敌人三十名。
    五月三十一日凌晨二时四十五分，溪山地区的解放军，分成多路猛烈袭击吉村西面的敌军据点。经过四十分钟的战斗，解放军几乎全歼美军一个连，重创另一个连。与此同时，解放军炮兵猛烈炮击吉村附近的美军的营指挥所和炮兵阵地，使他们无法支援吉村。解放军炮兵同时还炮击达根敌军据点，使基地内许多仓库起火燃烧。
    上午十点钟，敌人出动两连美军和一些坦克前往吉村。解放军战士们立即截击敌人，消灭七十名，缴获一批武器。
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：广治省北部地区人民解放武装力量在六月四日到六日的战斗中，打死打伤美军二百多名，击落敌机七架。
    人民解放武装力量炮兵，四日在昆天地区猛烈轰击从据点出来“扫荡”的美军，打死打伤敌人一百多名。五日下午，人民解放武装力量在溪山地区截击从霍国开往达贯东南的一连美军，消灭敌人三十名。敌人慌忙出动飞机掩护，当场被击落两架。
    六日上午，人民解放武装力量又在巴庄北面截击一连美军，消灭敌人七十名。同一天，人民解放武装力量炮兵还猛烈袭击了敌人在巴庄和达根据点，打死打伤敌人一百多名。
    敌人急忙出动直升飞机掩护，人民解放武装力量的各个猎机组立即予以猛烈打击，击落敌机五架。
    在这以前，广治省北部地区人民解放武装力量从二日到四日还在溪山地区消灭美国侵略军一百一十多名。
