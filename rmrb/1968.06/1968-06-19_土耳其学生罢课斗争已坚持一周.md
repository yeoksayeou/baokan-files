Title: Turkish Student Strike Continues for One Week
Author:
Date: 1968-06-19
Edition: 6

Turkish students' strike movement, involving more than 60,000 students, continues to develop. After occupying several major universities in Istanbul, students have now taken control of the Technical University, which has around 15,000 students. Meanwhile, students from two other schools, the Academy of Fine Arts, and the Florence Nightingale Nursing School have joined the struggle. Nursing school students have already occupied their campus. The Turkish student strike has now lasted for one week.

### 土耳其学生罢课斗争已坚持一周

1968-06-19
第6版()
专栏：

    土耳其学生罢课斗争已坚持一周
    本报讯　土耳其六万多学生罢课运动仍在继续发展。伊斯坦布尔学生在占领了这个城市的几所主要大学后，又占领了有一万五千人左右的技术大学。同时，又有两所学校、美术学院和弗洛伦斯·南丁格尔护士学校学生加入斗争行列。护士学校学生已占领了学校。土耳其学生罢课斗争已经坚持了一周。
