Title: Czechoslovakian Ambassador to China Leaves Post
Author:
Date: 1968-06-28
Edition: 6

The Ambassador of the Czechoslovakian Socialist Republic to China, Křesťan, has left his post and departed Beijing this morning to return to his home country.

### 捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使奉调离任

1968-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使奉调离任
    新华社二十七日讯　捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国驻中国大使克日斯特克奉调离任，今天上午离开北京回国。
