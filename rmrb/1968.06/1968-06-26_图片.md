Title: Female Workers at Ford's British Plant Strike for Equal Pay
Author:
Date: 1968-06-26
Edition: 6

	Female workers at the Ford Motor Company's plant in London are on strike, holding up signs demanding an end to gender-based wage discrimination and refusing to yield to the management. They are resolutely continuing their strike struggle.
