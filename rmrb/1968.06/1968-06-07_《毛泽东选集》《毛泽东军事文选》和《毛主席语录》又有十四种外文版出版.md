Title: The Selected Works of Mao Zedong, The Selected Military Works of Mao Zedong, and The Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong Have 14 More Foreign Language Editions Published

Author:
Date: 1968-06-07
Edition: 1

The Selected Works of Mao Zedong, The Selected Military Works of Mao Zedong, and The Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong have 14 more foreign language editions published.

New China News Agency, June 5th: The Selected Works of Mao Zedong, The Selected Military Works of Mao Zedong, and The Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong have recently had 14 more foreign language editions published.

The Thai, Vietnamese, Japanese, Arabic, Spanish, and German editions of Volume 1 of The Selected Works of Mao Zedong, the Vietnamese and Japanese editions of Volume 2, and the French edition of Volume 3 have all been published by the Foreign Languages Press.

The Thai, Indonesian, and Spanish editions of The Selected Military Works of Mao Zedong have also been published.

The Lao and Korean editions of The Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong have also been recently published by the Foreign Languages Press.

The Bengali and Tamil editions of The Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong will be published soon.

### 《毛泽东选集》《毛泽东军事文选》和《毛主席语录》又有十四种外文版出版

1968-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    《毛泽东选集》《毛泽东军事文选》和《毛主席语录》又有十四种外文版出版
    新华社五日讯　《毛泽东选集》、《毛泽东军事文选》和《毛主席语录》最近又有十四种外文版出版。
    《毛泽东选集》第一卷的泰、越南、日本、阿拉伯、西班牙、德文版，第二卷的越南、日本文版，第三卷的法文版已经由外文出版社出版。
    《毛泽东军事文选》的泰、印度尼西亚和西班牙文版也已出版。
    《毛主席语录》的老挝文版和朝鲜文版也在最近由外文出版社出版。
    《毛主席语录》的孟加拉文版和泰米尔文版不久即将出版。
