Title: Tanzanian President Nyerere Arrives in Beijing for State Visit
Author:
Date: 1968-06-18
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, President Julius K. Nyerere of the United Republic of Tanzania will arrive in Beijing today for a state visit.

BEIJING, June 17 (Xinhua) -- President Julius K. Nyerere of the United Republic of Tanzania, at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, will arrive in Beijing on June 18 for a state visit. The revolutionary masses of the capital, who are striving for a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, are preparing to give a warm and enthusiastic welcome to the Tanzanian guests.

DAR ES SALAAM, June 17 (Xinhua) -- President Julius K. Nyerere of the United Republic of Tanzania, at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, departed from Dar es Salaam this afternoon for a state visit to China. Accompanying President Nyerere on his visit to China are his wife and other members of his entourage. They were given a grand send-off at the airport.

This is President Nyerere's second visit to China. Since President Nyerere and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China visited each other three years ago, the friendly cooperative relations between Tanzania and China have been further strengthened and developed.

First Vice President Karume and Second Vice President Kawawa of Tanzania saw President Nyerere off at the airport.

Today, Dar es Salaam Airport was filled with a festive atmosphere. Hundreds of people gathered in the airport lounge and corridors to see off President Nyerere and his entourage, wishing them a successful visit and asking President Nyerere to convey the friendly greetings of the Tanzanian people to the Chinese people. Members of the Tanzanian National Dance Troupe performed traditional dances to the beat of drums and the sound of joyful clapping.

Those who saw President Nyerere off at the airport also included Speaker of the National Assembly Adam Sapie Mkwawa, government ministers, Secretary-General of the Tanganyika African National Union Msekwa, Secretary-General of the Afro-Shirazi Party Sabit Komba, and leaders of the youth, workers, and women's organizations of the Tanganyika African National Union, as well as officials of the Tanzanian People's Defense Force and the police force.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Tanzania, Zhou Boqing, members of the Chinese Embassy, and representatives of Chinese engineering and technical personnel working in Tanzania were also at the airport to see them off. Diplomats from other countries were also present at the airport to see them off.

### 应中华人民共和国政府邀请前来进行国事访问  坦桑尼亚联合共和国总统尼雷尔今日到京

1968-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    应中华人民共和国政府邀请前来进行国事访问
    坦桑尼亚联合共和国总统尼雷尔今日到京
    新华社十七日讯　坦桑尼亚联合共和国总统朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔，应中华人民共和国政府邀请，前来进行国事访问，将在十八日到达北京。正在夺取无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利的首都革命群众，准备隆重、热烈地欢迎坦桑尼亚贵宾。
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆十七日电　坦桑尼亚联合共和国总统朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔，应中华人民共和国政府邀请，十七日下午离开达累斯萨拉姆前往中国进行国事访问。同尼雷尔总统一起访问中国的有他的夫人及其他随行人员。他们在机场受到盛大的欢送。
    这是尼雷尔总统第二次访问中国。自从尼雷尔总统和中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来三年前互相访问以来，坦桑尼亚和中国之间的友好合作关系得到了进一步加强和发展。
    坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆和第二副总统卡瓦瓦在机场为尼雷尔总统送行。
    今天，达累斯萨拉姆机场充满着节日气氛。拥挤在机场休息室和过道的数百送行的人祝尼雷尔总统及其随行人员访问成功，并请尼雷尔总统向中国人民转达坦桑尼亚人民的友好祝愿。坦桑尼亚民族舞蹈团团员在击鼓声中和欢乐的拍手声中表演了民族舞蹈。
    在机场送行的还有国民议会议长亚当·萨皮·姆克瓦瓦，政府的部长们，坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟全国执行书记姆塞克瓦，非洲—设拉子党总书记萨比特·孔布以及坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟青年团、工人和妇女组织的领导人，坦桑尼亚人民国防军负责人以及警察部队的负责官员。
    中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆临时代办周伯萍、中国大使馆人员以及在坦桑尼亚工作的中国工程技术人员的代表也到机场送行。在机场送行的还有外交使节。（附图片）
