Title: Defeated Vietnam Invader Westmoreland in Japan
Author:
Date: 1968-06-22
Edition: 5

The former U.S. commander in Vietnam, Westmoreland, held activities in Japan on his way back to the United States after being recalled.

According to reports, Westmoreland held a series of secret talks with Japanese reactionary government Prime Minister Sato, Foreign Minister Miki, and the Director of the Defense Agency Masuda. When discussing the U.S. war in Vietnam, he had to admit that the U.S. could not possibly win this war of aggression.

### 侵越败将威斯特摩兰到日本活动

1968-06-22
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    侵越败将威斯特摩兰到日本活动
    前侵越美军败将威斯特摩兰在被撤换回国途中，十八、十九日到日本进行活动。
    据报道，威斯特摩兰同日本反动政府首相佐藤、外长三木、“防卫厅”长官增田进行了一系列的秘密会谈。在谈到美国侵越战争时，他不得不承认美国“不可能”打赢这场侵略战争。
