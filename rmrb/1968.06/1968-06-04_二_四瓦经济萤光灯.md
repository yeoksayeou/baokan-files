Title: 2-4 Watt Economy Fluorescent Lamps
Author:
Date: 1968-06-04
Edition: 2

The revolutionary workers of the Wuxi Engineering Division of the Jiangsu Provincial Industrial Equipment Installation Company have fully demonstrated their revolutionary spirit of daring to think, speak, and act, and daring to innovate. They have successfully developed a new product, the 2-4 Watt Economy Fluorescent Lamp. This type of fluorescent lamp is suitable for both household low-light illumination and rural use. It not only has a wide range of applications but also saves electricity.

### 二——四瓦经济萤光灯

1968-06-04
第2版()
专栏：

    二——四瓦经济萤光灯
    江苏省工业设备安装公司无锡工程处的革命职工，充分发挥敢想、敢说、敢干、敢于创造的革命精神，试制成功新产品二—四瓦经济萤光灯。这种萤光灯既适合家庭微光照明，又适用于农村，不但用途广，而且节省电。
