Title: Celebrating Danish Constitution Day: Danish Ambassador Hosts Reception
Author:
Date: 1968-06-04
Edition: 6

Danish Ambassador to China, O. A. Oldenberg, held a reception today to celebrate the Constitution Day of the Kingdom of Denmark.

Relevant officials from the Chinese government and the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Luo Guibao and Lin Haiyun, were invited to attend the reception.

Ambassadors from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝丹麦宪法日  丹麦驻华大使举行招待会

1968-06-04
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝丹麦宪法日
    丹麦驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社三日讯　丹麦驻中国大使奥尔登伯格今天举行招待会，庆祝丹麦王国宪法日。
    政府有关部门、北京市革命委员会的负责人罗贵波、林海云等，应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国使节也出席了招待会。
