Title: Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five Enemy Aircraft

Author:

Date: 1968-06-16

Edition: 5

Lao patriotic military and civilians shot down five enemy aircraft.

New China News Agency, Khamkeut: According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, on May 26 and June 1, the patriotic military and civilians of Savannakhet Province in Laos fought back against American air pirates and shot down a total of four enemy aircraft.

New China News Agency, Khamkeut: According to a report by the Pathet Lao News Agency, on May 19, the patriotic military and civilians of Savannakhet Province bravely intercepted a transport plane heading to the enemy stronghold in Hongsa County to provide supplies and shot down one aircraft on the spot.

### 老挝爱国军民击落敌机五架

1968-06-16
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民击落敌机五架
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台广播，老挝沙湾拿吉省爱国军民五月二十六日和六月一日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，共击落敌机四架。
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮通讯社报道：老挝沙耶武里省爱国军民，五月十九日英勇截击前往洪沙县敌军据点接济的运输机，当场击落一架。
