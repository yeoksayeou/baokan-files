Title: Chinese Medical Team Arrives in Conakry
Author:
Date: 1968-06-13
Edition: 6

	Chinese Medical Team Arrives in Conakry

	Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, June 10 — According to the agreement signed by the Chinese and Guinean governments in December last year, a medical team dispatched by the Chinese government arrived in Conakry by plane today.

	This medical team, consisting of 34 people, is the first Chinese medical team sent to Guinea. It will follow the teachings of the great leader Chairman Mao, serving the Guinean people wholeheartedly.

	Those who welcomed the Chinese medical team at the airport included Madame Loffo Kamara, State Secretary for Social Affairs of Guinea, and Mr. Kourama Baba, Director of Health Affairs of the State Secretariat for Social Affairs, as well as Lin Bingshan, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Guinea.

	Madame Loffo Kamara, in her conversation with the Chinese personnel at the airport, enthusiastically praised the effective and close cooperation between the Chinese and Guinean peoples. She also expressed her heartfelt gratitude for the consistent and selfless aid provided by the Chinese people to the Guinean people. She sincerely wished the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, good health.

### 我国医疗队到达科纳克里

1968-06-13
第6版()
专栏：

    我国医疗队到达科纳克里
    新华社科纳克里十日电　根据中国和几内亚政府去年十二月签订的议定书，由中国政府派遣的一个医疗队十日乘飞机到达科纳克里。
    这个由三十四人组成的医疗队是中国派到几内亚的第一个医疗队，它将遵循伟大领袖毛主席的教导，全心全意地为几内亚人民服务。
    到机场欢迎中国医疗队的有几内亚社会事务国务秘书洛福·卡马拉夫人和几内亚社会事务国务秘书处卫生事务总督察员库鲁马·巴巴及中国驻几内亚大使馆临时代办林秉南。
    洛福·卡马拉夫人在机场同中国工作人员谈话时，热情赞扬中国人民和几内亚人民之间卓有成效和紧密的合作，并且表示衷心感谢中国人民给予几内亚人民一贯的、无私的援助。她衷心祝愿中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席身体健康。
