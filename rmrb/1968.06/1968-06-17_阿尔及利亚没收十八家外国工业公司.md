Title: Algeria Nationalizes Eighteen Foreign Industrial Companies
Author:
Date: 1968-06-17
Edition: 6

Algeria has decided to nationalize eighteen foreign industrial companies. The Algerian government announced on the 14th that it would take over these companies.

The seized foreign companies are involved in cement, machinery, chemicals, and food industries. Among them, one is owned by British-Dutch capital, while the rest are subsidiaries of French capital companies. These companies have an annual turnover of over three billion dinars and employ nearly three thousand workers.

### 阿尔及利亚没收十八家外国工业公司

1968-06-17
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    阿尔及利亚没收十八家外国工业公司
    阿尔及利亚政府十四日决定把十八家外国工业公司收归国有。
    这些被没收的外国公司分布在水泥、机械、化学和食品等工业部门，其中，除一家属于英国—荷兰资本外，其余都是法国资本总公司的分公司。这些公司每年的营业额在三亿第纳尔以上，雇佣工人近三千人。
