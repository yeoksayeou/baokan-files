Title: Closing Announcement for the Memorial Exhibition of Norman Bethune
Author:
Date: 1968-06-17
Edition: 6

The "Memorial Exhibition of Norman Bethune" has been on display for half a year and will close on June 21. This is to announce the closure.

	Beijing Military Region Memorial Exhibition of Norman Bethune
