Title: U.S. Ships and Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-06-03
Edition: 6

On June 1st from 10:00 to 14:25, a U.S. military vessel intruded into our territorial waters near the northeastern region of Pingtan Island, Fujian Province. On the same day, from 11:03 to 11:41, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. On June 2nd from 09:33 to 14:16, another U.S. military vessel intruded into our territorial waters near the northeastern region of Pingtan Island, Fujian Province. On the same day, from 13:40 to 13:57, another U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. In response to these military provocations by U.S. military vessels and aircraft, the spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, has issued the 457th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我国领海领空  我提出第四百五十七次严重警告

1968-06-03
第6版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我国领海领空
    我提出第四百五十七次严重警告
    新华社二日讯　六月一日十时至十四时二十五分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省平潭岛东北地区领海。同日十一时零三分至十一时四十一分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。六月二日九时三十三分至十四时十六分，又有美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省平潭岛东北地区领海。同日十三时四十分至十三时五十七分，又有美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百五十七次严重警告。
