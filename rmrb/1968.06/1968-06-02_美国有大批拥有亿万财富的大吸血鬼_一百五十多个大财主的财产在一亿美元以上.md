Title: The United States Has a Large Number of Billionaire Bloodsuckers
Author:
Date: 1968-06-02
Edition: 5

The latest issue of the American magazine "Fortune" published an article titled "America's Billionaires." It reveals that due to the intensified exploitation of the working people by the monopoly bourgeoisie in the United States, the rich are becoming richer, and there is now a large number of billionaire bloodsuckers.

The article states: In today's America, "having a few million dollars is no longer a big deal. Today, the term 'millionaire' is rarely used."

This bourgeois publication, after four months of investigation, compiled an incomplete statistic, revealing that there are at least 153 people in the United States whose actual wealth exceeds $100 million, making them billionaires. Among them, some people's wealth has exceeded $1 billion.

### 美国有大批拥有亿万财富的大吸血鬼  一百五十多个大财主的财产在一亿美元以上

1968-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    美国有大批拥有亿万财富的大吸血鬼
    一百五十多个大财主的财产在一亿美元以上
    本报讯　最近一期美国《幸福》杂志刊载一篇题为《美国的亿万富翁》的文章，它透露在美国由于垄断资产阶级对劳动人民残酷剥削的加强，富者愈富，目前有着一大批拥有亿万财富的大吸血鬼。
    这篇文章写道：在今天的美国，“光是拥有几百万家财已经不再享有什么巨大声望。今天已经难得使用‘百万富翁’这个字眼”。
    这家资产阶级的刊物经过四个月的调查，作出不完全的统计，现在美国至少有一百五十三人的实际财产在一亿美元以上，使他们成为亿万富翁，而其中有些人的财产已超过十亿美元。
