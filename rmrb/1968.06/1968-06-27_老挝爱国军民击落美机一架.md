Title: Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Shoot Down One U.S. Plane
Author:
Date: 1968-06-27
Edition: 6

Lao patriotic military and civilians shoot down one U.S. plane

New China News Agency, Khamkeut, June 26 — According to a report from the Pathet Lao News Agency, on June 19, the patriotic military and civilians of Khamkeut City in Khammouane Province, Laos, shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft and damaged another.

### 老挝爱国军民击落美机一架

1968-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民击落美机一架
    新华社康开二十六日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝川圹省康开市爱国军民，十九日击落美国强盗飞机一架，击伤另一架。
