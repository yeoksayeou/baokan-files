Title: Guinea Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-06-10
Edition: 6

A trade delegation from the Republic of Guinea, led by the Director of the Domestic Trade Bureau of the Ministry of Trade, Transportation, and Posts and Telecommunications, Fofana Segou, arrived in Beijing by plane on June 8th. The delegation consists of three members.

### 几内亚贸易代表团到京

1968-06-10
第6版()
专栏：

    几内亚贸易代表团到京
    新华社九日讯　以几内亚共和国贸易、交通、邮电部内贸局局长福法纳·塞古为首的几内亚贸易代表团一行三人，八日乘飞机到达北京。
