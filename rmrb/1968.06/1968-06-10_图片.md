Title: Revolutionary Committee of Huadian People's Commune in Jilin Province Conducts Mao Zedong Thought Study Classes in the Fields
Author: Liú Yuánshùn
Date: 1968-06-10
Edition: 4

Cadres from the Revolutionary Committee of Huadian People's Commune in Jilin Province, together with poor and lower-middle peasants, have set up Mao Zedong Thought study classes in the fields. In the photograph, the deputy head of the Revolutionary Committee, Jin Yùzhū, is seen discussing experiences in criticizing and repudiating selfishness and revisionism with the poor and lower-middle peasants.
