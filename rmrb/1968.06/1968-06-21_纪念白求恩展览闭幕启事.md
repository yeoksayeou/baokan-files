Title: Notice of the Closing of the Exhibition Commemorating Dr. Norman Bethune
Author:
Date: 1968-06-21
Edition: 5

The exhibition "Commemorating Dr. Norman Bethune" has been on display for half a year. It is now scheduled to close on June 21. This is to announce the closure.

Beijing Military Region Exhibition Commemorating Dr. Norman Bethune
