Title: Cambodian Minister of Public Health Receives Chinese Technical Personnel
Author:
Date: 1968-06-29
Edition: 6

Cambodian Minister of Public Health Madame Dipouman held a reception on June 24 to welcome Chinese technical personnel who came to Cambodia to study the construction of a medical college and a hospital, both of which are being built with Chinese aid.

Chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia, Zheng Siyong, and relevant Cambodian officials attended the reception.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

This group of Chinese technical personnel left Phnom Penh for China on June 25.

### 柬公共卫生大臣接见我技术人员

1968-06-29
第6版()
专栏：

    柬公共卫生大臣接见我技术人员
    新华社金边二十七日电　柬埔寨公共卫生大臣狄普曼夫人，二十四日举行招待会，招待前来柬埔寨研究由中国援助建设的一所医学院和一所医院建设工程的中国技术人员。
    中国驻柬埔寨大使馆临时代办郑斯雄以及柬埔寨有关人士出席了招待会。
    招待会是在友好的气氛中进行的。
    这一批中国技术人员已于二十五日离开金边回国。
