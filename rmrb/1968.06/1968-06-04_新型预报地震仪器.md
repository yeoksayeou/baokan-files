Title: New Type of Earthquake Prediction Instrument
Author:
Date: 1968-06-04
Edition: 2

Workers at the Wuxi Instrument and Meter Equipment Factory, despite poor equipment conditions and insufficient technical expertise, have resolved to overcome all difficulties. After three months of hard work, they have successfully developed a new type of earthquake prediction instrument. This achievement provides a material foundation for the advancement of earthquake prediction work in our country.

### 新型预报地震仪器

1968-06-04
第2版()
专栏：

    新型预报地震仪器
    无锡仪器仪表设备厂的革命职工，在设备条件差，技术力量不足的情况下，下定决心，排除万难，经过三个月的努力，终于试制成功新型预报地震仪器，为我国地震预报工作的开展，提供了物质基础。
