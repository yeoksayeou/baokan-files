Title: Vice Premier Bista Visits Jinan and Receives Warm Welcome from Revolutionary Masses
Author:
Date: 1968-06-01
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Bista of Nepal Visits Jinan and Receives Warm Welcome from Revolutionary Masses

New China News Agency, Jinan, May 31st - The Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Nepal, Kirti Nidhi Bista, along with his entourage, concluded their two-day visit to Jinan. Accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and Nepalese Ambassador to China Subba, they departed Jinan by special plane this morning for a visit to Nanjing.

Deputy Director of the Shandong Provincial Revolutionary Committee, Han Jinhai, and other leaders from Shandong Province and Jinan City were at the airport to see off the Nepalese guests.

Vice Premier Bista and his delegation arrived in Jinan on the 29th. That evening, the Shandong Provincial Revolutionary Committee and the Jinan Municipal Revolutionary Committee held a banquet to warmly welcome the Nepalese friends. Deputy Director of the Shandong Provincial Revolutionary Committee, Han Jinhai, and Vice Premier Bista both spoke at the banquet, praising the deep friendship and friendly cooperation between the peoples of China and Nepal. Han Jinhai said that the development of Sino-Nepalese friendship is a contribution to the unity and anti-imperialist struggle of the peoples of Asia and Africa, and is an example of the implementation of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. He expressed that we will always unswervingly contribute our efforts to the development of friendship between the peoples of China and Nepal and the friendly neighborly relations between the two countries.

Vice Premier Bista said in his speech that during this visit to China, we were received by your great leader Chairman Mao, who also had a friendly conversation with us, and we are very moved. He said, China and Nepal are friendly neighboring countries. The Nepalese people are very grateful for China's assistance, and the Nepalese people have deep feelings for the Chinese people. He said, I believe that in the future, our friendly relations between the two countries will be further strengthened and developed.

During their visit to Jinan, the Nepalese friends visited factories, toured the city and famous sights, and everywhere they went, they received a warm welcome from the revolutionary masses.

### 比斯塔副首相在济南参观访问  受到广大革命群众的热烈欢迎

1968-06-01
第6版()
专栏：

    比斯塔副首相在济南参观访问
    受到广大革命群众的热烈欢迎
    新华社济南三十一日电　尼泊尔王国副首相兼外交大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔及其随行人员，在济南进行了两天的参观访问后，在外交部副部长韩念龙和尼泊尔驻中国大使苏巴陪同下，今天上午乘专机离开济南前往南京参观访问。
    山东省革命委员会副主任韩金海，以及山东省和济南市各方面的负责人到机场欢送尼泊尔客人。
    比斯塔副首相等尼泊尔客人是在二十九日到达济南的。当天晚上，山东省革命委员会和济南市革命委员会设宴热烈欢迎尼泊尔朋友。山东省革委会副主任韩金海和比斯塔副首相在宴会上先后讲话，赞扬中尼两国人民的深厚友谊和友好合作关系。韩金海说，中尼友好关系的发展是对亚非人民团结反帝斗争的贡献，是执行和平共处五项原则的范例。他表示，我们将始终不渝地为发展中尼两国人民的友谊和两国的友好睦邻关系而贡献自己的力量。
    比斯塔副首相在讲话中说，我们这次到中国访问，受到你们伟大领袖毛主席的接见，毛主席并和我们作了友好的谈话，我们很感动。他说，中尼两国是友好的邻邦。尼泊尔人民非常感谢中国的援助，尼泊尔人民对中国人民是怀有深厚感情的。他说，我相信，今后我们两国的友好关系，将会更进一步加强和发展。
    尼泊尔朋友在济南访问期间，参观了工厂，游览了市容和名胜，所到之处，受到革命群众的热烈欢迎。
