Mozambique Guerrillas Inflict Heavy Casualties on Portuguese Colonial Forces

In a series of attacks and ambushes, a batch of enemy forces were annihilated.

	NEW DELHI, June 8 (Xinhua) — The Mozambique guerrillas carried out a series of attacks and ambushes in the northern province of Delgado in April, killing more than 30 Portuguese colonial troops and destroying seven enemy vehicles.

	According to a bulletin issued by the Mozambique guerrillas recently, on April 3 and 26, the guerrillas attacked three strategic villages of the Portuguese colonial forces in Canhacate, Darumba, and Enanconda, killing six enemy soldiers and seizing a large number of weapons and military supplies, liberating many Mozambican people in these villages.

	On April 24, the guerrillas ambushed and killed three Portuguese colonial troops in Nyankoma.

	On April 9, 21, and 28, the guerrillas destroyed seven enemy vehicles in ambushes and landmine attacks, and killed more than 20 enemy soldiers.

### 莫三鼻给游击队痛打葡萄牙殖民军  在一系列袭击和伏击战中歼灭一批敌人

1968-06-08
第6版()
专栏：

    莫三鼻给游击队痛打葡萄牙殖民军
    在一系列袭击和伏击战中歼灭一批敌人
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆电　莫三鼻给游击队在今年四月份在北部的德尔加多角省进行的一系列袭击和伏击中，打死了葡萄牙殖民军三十多名，还击毁了敌车七辆。
    据莫三鼻给游击队不久前在这里发表的公报说，四月三日和二十六日，游击队袭击了在坎雅塔蒂、达鲁姆巴和埃南孔达的葡萄牙殖民军的三个战略村，打死了六名敌人，缴获了大批武器和军用物资，使这些村子的许多莫三鼻给群众获得自由。
    四月二十四日，游击队在尼扬科马的一次伏击中，打死葡萄牙殖民军三人。
    四月九日、二十一日和二十八日，游击队在伏击战或地雷战中，摧毁敌车七辆，还打死敌人二十多名。
