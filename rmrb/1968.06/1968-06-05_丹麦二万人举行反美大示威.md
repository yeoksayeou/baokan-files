Title: 20,000 Danes Hold Anti-American Protest
Author:
Date: 1968-06-05
Edition: 6

Approximately 20,000 Danish people held a large-scale demonstration in the capital of Copenhagen on June 3 to protest against American imperialism's aggression in Vietnam.

The demonstrators, despite police attempts to block them, held an anti-American, anti-Vietnam War protest rally in front of the U.S. Embassy in Copenhagen. After the rally, many demonstrators rushed to the harbor and threw a large number of stones and bottles at two U.S. military ships docked there. They also demonstrated in front of the offices of the American Esso Oil Company and an American travel agency, smashing many of the travel agency's windows with stones and bottles.

When the demonstrators passed by the Thai Embassy in Copenhagen, they smashed some of the embassy's windows with stones and painted large slogans reading "American Lackey" on the walls.

### 丹麦二万人举行反美大示威

1968-06-05
第6版()
专栏：

    丹麦二万人举行反美大示威
    据新华社四日讯　哥本哈根消息：大约二万名丹麦群众三日在首都哥本哈根举行大规模的示威游行，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南。
    示威群众不顾警察的阻拦，在美国驻哥本哈根大使馆前，举行了反美侵越的抗议大会。在大会结束后，许多示威群众冲向港口，对停泊在这里的两艘美国军舰投掷了大量的石头和瓶子。他们还到美国德士古石油公司的办事处和美国旅行社门前举行示威，并用石头、瓶子打碎了旅行社的许多玻璃窗。
    当示威群众经过泰国驻哥本哈根的使馆时，用石头砸碎了这个使馆的一些玻璃窗，并刷上“美国走狗”的大标语。
