	Quotations from Chairman Mao
		Destruction is followed by construction. Destruction means criticism, means revolution. Destruction must be carried out by reasoning, and reasoning is construction. With destruction in the forefront, construction is also included.

### 毛主席语录

1968-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    不破不立。破，就是批判，就是革命。破，就要讲道理，讲道理就是立，破字当头，立也就在其中了。
