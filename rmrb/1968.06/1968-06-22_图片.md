Title: Lyon Students Build Barricades to Resist Police Violence
Author:
Date: 1968-06-22
Edition: 6

Progressive students in Lyon, France's second-largest city, fearlessly constructed barricades on the streets, ready to defend themselves against the savage suppression by reactionary police at any moment.
