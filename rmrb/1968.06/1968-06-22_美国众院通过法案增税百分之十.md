Title: U.S. House of Representatives Passes Bill to Increase Taxes by Ten Percent
Author:
Date: 1968-06-22
Edition: 5

The U.S. House of Representatives passed a bill on the 20th to increase taxes by ten percent. This is a measure taken by American imperialism to reduce the international trade deficit, ease the dollar crisis, and intensify the exploitation of the working people. According to reports, after the bill is passed by both houses of the U.S. Congress, the Johnson administration will increase tax revenue by $1.52 billion in the next fiscal year starting from July 1.

### 美国众院通过法案增税百分之十

1968-06-22
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    美国众院通过法案增税百分之十
    美国众议院二十日通过了增税百分之十的法案。这是美帝国主义为了减少国际收支逆差、缓和美元危机、加紧搜刮劳动人民而采取的一项措施。据报道，在美国国会两院通过以后，这项新法案将使约翰逊政府在从今年七月一日开始的下一财政年度内增加一百五十二亿美元的税收。
