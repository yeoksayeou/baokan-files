Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-06-22
Edition: 1

Imperialism is most afraid of the awakening of the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, and of the awakening of the people of all countries in the world. We must unite and drive U.S. imperialism out of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, and send it back to where it came from.

### 毛主席语录

1968-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    帝国主义最怕的是亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民觉悟，怕世界各国人民的觉悟。我们要团结起来把美帝国主义从亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲赶回它的老家去。
