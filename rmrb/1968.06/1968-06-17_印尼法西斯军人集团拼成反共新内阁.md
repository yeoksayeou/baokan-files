Title: Indonesian Fascist Military Clique Forms New Anti-Communist Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1968-06-17
Edition: 6

The fascist military regime in Indonesia, led by Suharto, has recently formed a new anti-communist cabinet. Key departments such as defense, foreign affairs, and internal affairs remain under the control of a small group of right-wing generals and reactionary politicians led by Suharto. To seek more U.S. "aid," Suharto appointed the notorious right-wing figure Sumitro as the new trade minister.

According to a recent Associated Press report, Suharto will soon send Sumitro to Washington to elaborate on Indonesia's intentions and to seek more U.S. aid.

### 印尼法西斯军人集团拼成反共新内阁

1968-06-17
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    印尼法西斯军人集团拼成反共新内阁
    印度尼西亚法西斯军人政权头目苏哈托最近又拼凑了一个新的反共内阁。国防、外交、内政等重要部门仍然操纵在以苏哈托为首的一小撮右派将领和反动政客的手中。为了乞讨更多的美“援”，苏哈托任命臭名昭彰的老右派头子苏米特罗为新内阁中的贸易部长。
    据美联社最近透露，不久苏哈托将派苏米特罗“到华盛顿去详细阐明印尼的目的，以争取更多的美援”。　（据新华社讯）
