Title: Notice of the Closing of the Exhibition Commemorating Dr. Norman Bethune
Author:
Date: 1968-06-18
Edition: 6

The exhibition commemorating Dr. Norman Bethune has been on display for half a year. It will close on June 21. This is to announce the closure.

Beijing Military Region Exhibition Commemorating Dr. Norman Bethune
