Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Aircraft Violate Cambodian Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-06-12
Edition: 6

	U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Aircraft Violate Cambodian Airspace
	Cambodian Government Lodges Strong Protest

	According to a report by Xinhua News Agency in Phnom Penh, the Cambodian News Agency reported: The Royal Government of Cambodia issued a statement on June 6 condemning the latest act of aggression by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet aircraft against Cambodia.

	The statement said that on the night of May 30 to the early morning of May 31, a U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet "Dakota" military aircraft invaded the airspace of Cambodia's Takeo Province and strafed a Cambodian military outpost, injuring two soldiers.

	According to a report by Xinhua News Agency in Phnom Penh, the Cambodian News Agency reported: The Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent separate notes to the Saigon puppet regime and the U.S. government on June 4, strongly protesting the act of aggression by the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet army on June 2, when three helicopters were dispatched to strafe the military defense facilities of Cambodia's Kampong Cham Province, injuring two Cambodian soldiers.

### 美国—南越傀儡飞机侵犯柬领空  柬埔寨政府提出强烈抗议

1968-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越傀儡飞机侵犯柬领空
    柬埔寨政府提出强烈抗议
    据新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道：柬埔寨王国政府六日发表声明，谴责美国—南越傀儡飞机侵犯柬埔寨的新的侵略罪行。
    声明说，五月三十日深夜到三十一日凌晨，一架美国—南越傀儡的“达科他”式军用飞机侵入柬埔寨干丹省领空，并对柬埔寨军事哨所进行扫射，这个哨所的两名士兵受重伤。
    据新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道：柬埔寨外交部四日分别照会西贡傀儡当局和美国政府，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军二日派遣三架直升飞机对柬埔寨柴桢省武装部队的军事防御设施进行扫射，打伤两名柬埔寨军人的侵略罪行。
