Title: Danish Ambassador to China Leaves Post
Author:
Date: 1968-06-06
Edition: 6

Danish Ambassador to China, Mr. Oldenberg, has been recalled and left his post. He departed Beijing this morning by airplane.

### 丹麦驻中国大使奉调离任

1968-06-06
第6版()
专栏：

    丹麦驻中国大使奉调离任
    新华社五日讯　丹麦王国驻中国大使奥尔登伯格奉调离任，今天上午乘飞机离开北京。
