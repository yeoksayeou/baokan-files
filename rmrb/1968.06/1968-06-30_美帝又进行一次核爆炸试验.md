Title: The U.S. Imperialists Conduct Another Nuclear Explosion Test
Author:
Date: 1968-06-30
Edition: 5

	Washington, June 29 (Xinhua) -- The U.S. imperialists, in collusion with the Soviet revisionist renegade clique, have concocted the so-called "Non-Proliferation Treaty" to prevent other countries from developing nuclear weapons, while they themselves are hastening the development of nuclear weapons.

	According to an announcement made by the U.S. Atomic Energy Commission on June 28, the U.S. conducted another nuclear explosion test at the Nevada nuclear weapons test site. The explosive force of this test was equivalent to 200,000 tons of TNT.

	This is the 18th nuclear explosion test conducted by the U.S. imperialists this year, averaging three tests per month.

	The actions of the U.S. imperialists show that the so-called "Non-Proliferation Treaty" they have concocted in collusion with the Soviet revisionist renegade clique is merely a grand conspiracy and a big swindle aimed at preventing other countries from developing nuclear weapons, while allowing themselves to accelerate the development of nuclear weapons and to threaten and blackmail other countries with nuclear weapons.

### 美帝又进行一次核爆炸试验

1968-06-30
第5版()
专栏：

    美帝又进行一次核爆炸试验
    新华社二十九日讯　华盛顿消息：美帝国主义勾结苏联现代修正主义叛徒集团，炮制所谓“防止核扩散”条约，禁止别国发展核武器，而它自己却在加紧发展核武器。
    据美国原子能委员会二十八日宣布，美国在内华达州核武器试验场，又进行了一次核爆炸试验，这次爆炸的力量等于二十万吨黄色炸药。
    这是今年到现在为止，美帝国主义进行的第十八次核爆炸试验，平均每月进行三次。
    美帝国主义的行动说明，它勾结苏修叛徒集团炮制的所谓“防止核扩散”条约，目的不过是要禁止别国发展核武器，而让它们自己放手加速发展核武器，对他国进行核威胁和核讹诈的一个大阴谋、大骗局。
