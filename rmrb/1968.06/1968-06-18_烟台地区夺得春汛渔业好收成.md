Title: Yantai Region Achieves Bountiful Spring Tide Fishery Harvest
Author:
Date: 1968-06-18
Edition: 2

The fishermen and revolutionary cadres of the coastal people's communes in Yantai Region, Shandong Province, armed with the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, have conducted in-depth revolutionary criticism and struggle, continuously promoting the revolutionization of people's thinking, and achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest in spring tide fishery production. By the end of May, the total catch in the region had increased by 33.3 percent compared to the same period last year.

### 烟台地区夺得春汛渔业好收成

1968-06-18
第2版()
专栏：

    烟台地区夺得春汛渔业好收成
    山东省烟台地区沿海各人民公社的广大渔民和革命干部，以战无不胜的毛泽东思想为武器，深入开展革命大批判，不断促进人的思想革命化，夺得了春汛渔业生产空前大丰收。截止五月底，全区捕捞任务比去年同期增长了百分之三十三点三。
                                        （据新华社讯）
