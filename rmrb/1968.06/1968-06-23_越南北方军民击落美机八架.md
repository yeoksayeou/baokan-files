Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Eight U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-06-23
Edition: 5

The military and civilians of North Vietnam have shot down eight U.S. aircraft.

	According to reports from here: The military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down four invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on the 14th, 17th, and 20th.

	The military and civilians of Hon Giao Island and Nghe An Province shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft each on the 17th and 19th.

	It was announced here today: The military and civilians of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft in battle on the evening of the 18th.

### 越南北方军民击落美机八架

1968-06-23
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机八架
    新华社河内二十二日电　据此间公布：越南北方广平省军民在十四日、十七日和二十日共击落入侵的美国强盗飞机四架。
    昏果岛和义安省军民在十七日和十九日分别击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社河内十九日电　据这里今天宣布：越南北方义安省军民，十八日晚间在战斗中击落两架入侵的美国强盗飞机。
