Title: Icelandic Youth Protest Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-06-02
Edition: 5

A group of young people in Reykjavik, the capital of Iceland, held a demonstration on May 26 to protest against U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam.

On the morning of May 26, a naval fleet of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization arrived at the port of Reykjavik for a "visit." Despite police attempts to stop them, the demonstrators rushed onto one of the warships and hoisted a red flag and the national flag of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on it. This action was to express their protest against U.S. policy in Vietnam and to show their support for the Vietnamese people who are bravely fighting against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.

### 冰岛青年示威抗议美帝侵略越南  不顾警察阻拦冲上北大西洋集团的军舰插上红旗

1968-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    冰岛青年示威抗议美帝侵略越南
    不顾警察阻拦冲上北大西洋集团的军舰插上红旗
    新华社讯　雷克雅未克消息：冰岛首都雷克雅未克的一批青年五月二十六日举行示威，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南。
    二十六日早晨，北大西洋侵略集团的一支海军舰队到雷克雅未克港口进行“访问”，示威青年不顾警察阻拦，冲上其中的一艘军舰，并在那艘军舰上插上一面红旗和一面越南民主共和国的国旗，以表示他们对美国侵越政策的抗议和对正在英勇地进行抗美救国斗争的越南人民的声援。
