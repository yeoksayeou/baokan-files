Title: Workers and Students Unite! Down with Capitalism!
Author:
Date: 1968-06-08
Edition: 5

Workers and students in Sweden unite! Down with capitalism!
Swedish workers and students demonstrate in support of the French people's just struggle
Some demonstrators hold up portraits of Chairman Mao, wave the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and shout "Long live Chairman Mao!"

NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY STOCKHOLM, June 7 - Over a thousand Swedish workers and students held a demonstration and rally in Stockholm last night in support of the just struggle of French workers and students.

The demonstrators carried red flags, banners, and placards. The placards read: "Down with capitalism!" "Support the struggle of the French people!" "Stand with the French workers and students!" "Workers and students unite!" "Long live the Paris Commune!" and other slogans.

As the demonstrators marched through the streets, they shouted, "Smash capitalism!" "Power to the workers!" and other slogans. Some demonstrators held up portraits of Chairman Mao, waved the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and shouted "Long live Chairman Mao!" As the marchers passed through the streets, many people stood at their windows waving and greeting the demonstrators.

The chairman of the Swedish Marxist-Leninist Communist League, B. Gustafsson, and other leaders also participated in the demonstration. Among the marchers were also foreigners residing in Sweden.

After the march, a rally was held. The meeting began with the singing of "The Internationale." Speakers at the meeting expressed their firm support for the struggle of French workers and students and condemned the fascist atrocities committed by French police against the fighting French workers and students.

The demonstrators also distributed leaflets. The leaflets stated that the struggle of the French people is just and that they have set an example for workers and students in all countries. The leaflets pointed out that workers and students must unite to fight against monopoly capital.

### “工人和学生联合起来！”“打倒资本主义！”  瑞典工人学生示威支持法国人民正义斗争  有的示威者高举毛主席画像，挥动《毛主席语录》，高呼“毛主席万岁！”

1968-06-08
第5版()
专栏：

    “工人和学生联合起来！”“打倒资本主义！”
    瑞典工人学生示威支持法国人民正义斗争
    有的示威者高举毛主席画像，挥动《毛主席语录》，高呼“毛主席万岁！”
    新华社斯德哥尔摩七日电  一千多名瑞典工人和学生六日晚上在斯德哥尔摩举行示威和集会，支持法国工人和学生的正义斗争。
    示威者高举着红旗、横幅标语和标语牌。标语牌上写着：“打倒资本主义！”“支持法国人民的斗争！”“和法国工人学生团结在一起！”“工人和学生联合起来！”“巴黎公社万岁！”等口号。
    示威者在大街上游行时高呼：“粉碎资本主义！”“工人要权力！”等口号。有的示威者高举毛主席画像，挥动《毛主席语录》，高呼“毛主席万岁！”当游行队伍在大街上行进时，许多人站在窗口向示威者招手致意。
    瑞典马列主义者共产主义联盟主席布·古斯塔夫逊和其他领导人也参加了这一示威游行。在游行队伍中还有居住在瑞典的外国人。
    游行以后，举行了集会。会议开始时与会者高唱《国际歌》。在会上发言的人表示坚决支持法国工人和学生的斗争，谴责法国警察对战斗的法国工人和学生的法西斯暴行。
    示威者还散发了传单。传单说，法国人民的斗争是正义的，他们为各国的工人和学生树立了榜样。传单指出，工人和学生必须联合起来同垄断资本进行斗争。
