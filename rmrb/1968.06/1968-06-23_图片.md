Title: Students Gather in Rio de Janeiro to Protest Killings
Author:
Date: 1968-06-23
Edition: 6

Students gathered on the streets of Rio de Janeiro, the capital of Brazil, to angrily protest the atrocity of the U.S. imperialists and the Brazilian reactionaries shooting students.
