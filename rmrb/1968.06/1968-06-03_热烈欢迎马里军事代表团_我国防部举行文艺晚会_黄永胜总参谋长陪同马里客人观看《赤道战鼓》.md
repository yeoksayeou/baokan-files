Title: Warmly Welcoming the Malian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-06-03
Edition: 6

The Ministry of National Defense held an evening of artistic performances to warmly welcome the Malian military delegation led by Malian Republic's Minister-level Representative for Defense and Security, Mamadou Diaguitte.

China's People's Liberation Army Chief of the General Staff, Huang Yongsheng, accompanied the Malian guests to watch the play "Drums of the Equator," which reflects the armed struggle of the Congolese (Kinshasa) people. The Chargé d'Affaires of the Malian Embassy in China, Mussa Koulibaly, and embassy staff, as well as Malian friends in Beijing, also attended the performance. After the show, Mamadou Diaguitte and Huang Yongsheng, along with others, went on stage to shake hands with the performers, congratulating them on the successful performance.

### 热烈欢迎马里军事代表团  我国防部举行文艺晚会  黄永胜总参谋长陪同马里客人观看《赤道战鼓》

1968-06-03
第6版()
专栏：

    热烈欢迎马里军事代表团
    我国防部举行文艺晚会
    黄永胜总参谋长陪同马里客人观看《赤道战鼓》
    新华社二日讯　国防部今晚举行文艺晚会，热烈欢迎由马里共和国负责国防和安全的部长级代表马马杜·迪亚基特率领的马里军事代表团。
    中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜陪同马里客人观看了反映刚果（金）人民武装斗争的话剧《赤道战鼓》。马里驻中国大使馆临时代办姆萨·库里巴利和使馆人员，以及在北京的马里朋友也出席了晚会。演出结束后，马马杜·迪亚基特团长和黄永胜总参谋长等走上舞台，同演员们热烈握手，祝贺演出成功。
