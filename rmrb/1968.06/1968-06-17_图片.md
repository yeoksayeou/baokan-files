Title: Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Seventh Wharf of Shanghai Port, Yu Xingzhou, Carries Out Mao Zedong's Instructions
Author:
Date: 1968-06-17
Edition: 2

Yu Xingzhou (right), the chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Seventh Wharf of Shanghai Port, always remembers Chairman Mao's instruction to "be both an official and a common person." He frequently goes to the production frontline to participate in labor, maintaining close ties with the masses. He has been praised by the revolutionary masses.

	Xinhua News Agency Photographer
