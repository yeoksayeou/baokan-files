Title: Old Scabs, New Betrayals—Denouncing the French Revisionist Traitor Clique
Author:
Date: 1968-06-08
Edition: 2

The single-volume edition of the important commentary by the editorial staff of the People's Daily, "Old Scabs, New Betrayals—Denouncing the French Revisionist Traitor Clique," has been published by the People's Publishing House. Starting from June 9, it will be available at the Beijing Xinhua Bookstore and will soon be distributed to Xinhua Bookstores nationwide.

### 《老工贼，新叛卖——斥法修叛徒集团》单行本出版

1968-06-08
第2版()
专栏：

    《老工贼，新叛卖——斥法修叛徒集团》单行本出版
    新华社七日讯　《人民日报》评论员重要文章《老工贼，新叛卖——斥法修叛徒集团》已由人民出版社出版单行本，六月九日起开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
