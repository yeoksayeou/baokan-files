Title: Pakistani Defense Minister Condemns India's Military Build-Up Threatening Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1968-06-28
Edition: 6

Pakistani Defense Minister Afzal Rahman Khan on June 24 condemned India for massively expanding its military capabilities with the help of certain major powers. He said this poses a serious threat to Pakistan.

In his speech to the National Assembly on defense issues, he stated that these major powers, which are assisting India in strengthening its military forces, have not considered the impact of such actions on Pakistan's national security. Pakistan is fully aware of India's military buildup and is taking all possible measures to be prepared for any unforeseen events.

He said that the security, sovereignty, and integrity of Pakistan are the primary and fundamental goals of the Pakistani government. India still views Pakistan as its number one enemy and is rapidly expanding its military with large-scale military aid from major powers.

He said that we cannot ignore the impact of India's military buildup on Pakistan's security. We are fully aware of the dangers we face.

Afzal Rahman Khan said that Pakistan's brave and self-sacrificing armed forces, with the support of the determined and resolute people of the nation, will certainly foil and crush any malicious plots by the enemy.

### 巴国防部长谴责印度扩充军备威胁巴基斯坦  巴基斯坦人民决心粉碎敌人任何阴谋

1968-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    巴国防部长谴责印度扩充军备威胁巴基斯坦
    巴基斯坦人民决心粉碎敌人任何阴谋
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十六日电　巴基斯坦国防部长阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗二十四日在国民议会谴责印度在某些大国的帮助下大规模地扩充军备。他说，这是对巴基斯坦的严重威胁。
    他在国民议会上就国防问题发表讲话时说，某些帮助印度扩充军事力量的大国，并没有考虑到这种作法对巴基斯坦国家安全产生的影响。巴基斯坦完全意识到印度在扩充军备，并正在采取一切可能的措施，随时准备应付不测事件的发生。
    他说，巴基斯坦的安全、主权和完整是巴基斯坦政府首要的、根本的目标。印度仍旧把巴基斯坦看成是它的头号敌人；并正在大国的大规模军事援助下，迅速地扩充军备。
    他说，我们对印度扩充军备对巴基斯坦安全产生的影响决不能等闲视之。我们完全意识到我们所面临的危险。
    阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗说，巴基斯坦英勇的、勇于献身的武装部队在坚定的、有决心的全国人民支持下，定将挫败和粉碎任何敌人的险恶阴谋。
