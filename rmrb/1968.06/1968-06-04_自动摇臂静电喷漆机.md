Title: Automatic Swing Arm Electrostatic Spray Painting Machine
Author:
Date: 1968-06-04
Edition: 2

Workers of the Hangzhou Transportation Machinery Repair Factory, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, have successfully developed an automatic swing arm electrostatic spray painting machine after more than a year of effort. This electrostatic spray painting machine is characterized by flexible operation, ease of use, high stability, high efficiency, and an attractive appearance. It can spray paint large workpieces up to four meters in height and is also suitable for smaller workpieces. The work efficiency is more than three times higher than manual spray painting.

### 自动摇臂静电喷漆机

1968-06-04
第2版()
专栏：

    自动摇臂静电喷漆机
    杭州交通机械修配厂革命职工，用毛泽东思想武装自己的头脑，经过一年多的努力，试制成功自动摇臂静电喷漆机。这种静电喷漆机，具有操纵灵活，使用方便，稳定性强，利用率高，外形美观等特点。能喷涂高达四米的大型工件，也能适用于较小体积的工件，工效比过去手工喷漆提高三倍以上。
