Vice Premier Bista Says China's Aid Strengthens Nepal-China Friendship

China is Nepal's Good Friend

KATHMANDU, June 12 (Xinhua) -- Nepalese Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Kirtinidhi Bista said on June 9 at a reception held by the Nepal-China Friendship Association to welcome his return from a visit to China: "Our friendship with the great friendly neighbor China is constantly strengthening."

Bista said: "Our friendly visit to China was very satisfying."

Bista said, "During our visit, Chairman Mao received us." Bista said, "I am very satisfied with the talks between Chairman Mao and other Chinese leaders with us." Bista said, "China has provided us with aid in a very warm-hearted manner, further strengthening our existing friendly relations." He said, "China is Nepal's good friend."

Bista also said, "China has achieved tremendous success in production." "The hard work of the Chinese people has particularly moved us."

Purna Bahadur, President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, delivered a welcome speech at the beginning of the reception.

Those present at the reception included Foreign Secretary Yadu Nath Khanal, Chairman of the Public Works Commission Keshab Bahadur, other government officials, National Assembly members, and people from all walks of life. The Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal, Tu Guo Wei, and the head of the Chinese technical group currently working in Nepal also attended the reception.

### 比斯塔副首相说中国的援助加强了尼中友好关系  中国是尼泊尔的好朋友

1968-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

    比斯塔副首相说中国的援助加强了尼中友好关系
    中国是尼泊尔的好朋友
    新华社加德满都十二日电　尼泊尔王国副首相兼外交大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔九日在尼中友好协会为他访问中国归来而举行的招待会上说：“我们同伟大的友好邻邦中国的友谊正在不断加强。”
    比斯塔说：“我们对中国进行的友好访问是令人满意的。”
    比斯塔说，在我们访问期间，“承蒙毛主席接见了我们。”比斯塔说：“我对毛主席和其他中国领导人同我们的谈话感到十分满意。”比斯塔说：“中国十分热情地向我们提供援助，进一步加强我们之间现有的友好关系。”他说：“中国是尼泊尔的好朋友。”
    比斯塔还说：“中国在生产方面取得了巨大的成就。”“中国人民的辛勤劳动使我们特别感动。”
    尼中友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔在招待会开始时致欢迎词。
    出席招待会的有外交秘书雅杜·纳特·卡纳尔、公共事业委员会主席凯谢尔·巴哈杜尔、其他政府官员、全国评议会议员和各界人士。中国驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维和目前在尼泊尔工作的中国技术人员小组的负责人也出席了招待会。
