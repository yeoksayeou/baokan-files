Title: "The Enemy Incites a Strike, We Will Increase Production"
Author:
Date: 1968-06-20
Edition: 2

"When the enemy incites a strike, we will increase production." The revolutionary workers of the Kailuan Coal Mine, with hearts burning with loyalty to defend Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line, are working tirelessly underground to mine coal. They say: "Actions speak louder than words. Every extra ton of coal we produce is an extra bit of loyalty to Chairman Mao!"
