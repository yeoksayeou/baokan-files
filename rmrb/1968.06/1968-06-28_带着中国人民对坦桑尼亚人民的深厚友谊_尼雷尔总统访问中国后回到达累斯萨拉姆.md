Title: Carrying the Deep Friendship of the Chinese People for the Tanzanian People, President Nyerere Returns to Dar es Salaam After Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1968-06-28
Edition: 6

Tanzania's President Julius K. Nyerere and his wife, along with other Tanzanian friends, returned to Dar es Salaam on June 26th after a friendly visit to China, carrying with them the deep friendship of the Chinese people for the Tanzanian people.

On June 26th, Dar es Salaam Airport was filled with a festive atmosphere. Banners hung at the airport read, "African liberation is our goal" and "Long live the friendship between the peoples of Tanzania and China." Hundreds of people from all walks of life gathered there to warmly welcome President Nyerere and to enthusiastically cheer his significant achievements from his visit to China and the new developments in Tanzanian-Chinese friendship.

When President Nyerere and his wife, along with the accompanying personnel, stepped off the plane, First Vice President Karume and Second Vice President Kawawa, as well as the Chinese Charge d'Affaires to Tanzania, Zhou Boqing, went forward to warmly welcome them. At this moment, the welcoming crowd cheered.

Among the welcoming crowd were government ministers, leaders of the Tanganyika African National Union and the African-Settler Party, members of parliament, leaders of mass organizations, and representatives of various African liberation movements stationed in Dar es Salaam.

Staff from the Chinese Embassy in Tanzania and representatives of Chinese engineering and technical personnel and medical teams working in Tanzania also went to the airport to welcome them.

The New China News Agency, Rawalpindi, June 26th: After visiting China, President Julius K. Nyerere of the United Republic of Tanzania and his wife, along with other accompanying personnel, stopped briefly in Karachi, Pakistan, on their way back to their country on June 26th before departing from Karachi by special plane.

President Nyerere told Pakistani journalists at Karachi Airport that his visit further strengthened the unity among Asian and African countries.

Those who went to the airport to see off President Nyerere included the Director of the Protocol Department of the Pakistani Foreign Ministry, Tiyabji, and others.

The Chinese Charge d'Affaires to Pakistan, Xu Ying, also went to the airport to see off the delegation.

Also present at the airport to see off the delegation were the Chinese Foreign Ministry Protocol Department official who accompanied President Nyerere from China to Karachi and the Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko.

### 带着中国人民对坦桑尼亚人民的深厚友谊  尼雷尔总统访问中国后回到达累斯萨拉姆

1968-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    带着中国人民对坦桑尼亚人民的深厚友谊
    尼雷尔总统访问中国后回到达累斯萨拉姆
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆二十六日电　坦桑尼亚联合共和国总统朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔和夫人以及其他坦桑尼亚朋友在对中国进行了友好访问以后，带着中国人民对坦桑尼亚人民的深厚友谊，二十六日下午回到了这里。
    二十六日，达累斯萨拉姆机场上洋溢着节日的气氛。机场上挂着横幅标语，上面写着：“非洲解放是我们的目标”、“坦中两国人民的友谊万岁”。数百名各阶层人士聚集在那里，热烈欢迎尼雷尔总统，并热烈欢呼他访问中国所取得的巨大成就和坦中两国友谊的新发展。
    当尼雷尔总统和夫人以及随行人员走下飞机时，第一副总统卡鲁姆和第二副总统卡瓦瓦以及中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆临时代办周伯萍迎上前去热烈欢迎他们。这时，欢迎的人群欢呼起来。
    在欢迎的人群中，有政府的部长们，坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟和非洲—设拉子党的负责人，议员们，群众组织的负责人以及驻达累斯萨拉姆的各非洲解放运动的代表。
    中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆的工作人员以及在坦桑尼亚工作的中国工程技术人员和医疗队的代表也到机场欢迎。
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十六日电　坦桑尼亚联合共和国总统朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔和夫人以及其他随行人员，在访问了中国以后，二十六日在回国途中在巴基斯坦的卡拉奇稍事停留之后，乘专机离开卡拉奇。
    尼雷尔总统在卡拉奇机场，向巴基斯坦记者谈到他对中国的访问时说，他的访问进一步加强了亚非国家之间的团结。
    前往机场迎送尼雷尔总统的有巴基斯坦外交部礼宾司司长特雅布吉等。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英也到机场迎送。
    在机场欢送的还有，陪同尼雷尔总统从中国到卡拉奇的中国外交部礼宾司负责人和坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科。
