Title: Paris Students Burn Reactionary Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1968-06-19
Edition: 6

Some reactionary newspapers in Paris have been spreading lies and slandering the Paris student movement. Angry students have retaliated by throwing these reactionary newspapers on the ground and setting them on fire.
