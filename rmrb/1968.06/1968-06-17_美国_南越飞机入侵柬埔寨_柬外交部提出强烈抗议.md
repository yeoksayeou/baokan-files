Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Aircraft Invade Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1968-06-17
Edition: 6

	U.S.-South Vietnamese Aircraft Invade Cambodia
	Cambodian Foreign Ministry Lodges Strong Protest

	BEIJING, June 14 — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry on June 11 sent separate notes to the Saigon puppet regime and the U.S. government, strongly protesting the new act of aggression by U.S.-South Vietnamese aircraft that invaded the province of Pursat on June 1 and 2, killing two Cambodian civilians and injuring three others.

	The notes demand that the Saigon puppet regime and the U.S. government cease such criminal actions and compensate for the damages.

### 美国—南越飞机入侵柬埔寨  柬外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-06-17
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越飞机入侵柬埔寨
    柬外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边十四日电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨外交部十一日分别照会西贡傀儡当局和美国政府，强烈抗议美国—南越飞机六月一日和二日入侵磅湛省，打死柬埔寨人两名，打伤三名的新的侵略罪行。
    照会要求西贡傀儡当局和美国政府停止这种罪恶行径，并赔偿损失。
