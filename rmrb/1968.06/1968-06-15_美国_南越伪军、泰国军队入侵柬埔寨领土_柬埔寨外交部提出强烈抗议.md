Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Troops and Thai Army Invade Cambodian Territory
Author:
Date: 1968-06-15
Edition: 5

U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops and Thai army invade Cambodian territory
Cambodian Foreign Ministry lodges strong protest

	NEW DELHI, June 11 (Xinhua) - According to a report from the Cambodian News Agency: The Cambodian Foreign Ministry sent letters of protest to the Saigon puppet regime and the U.S. government on June 7 and 8, strongly condemning the recent two instances of U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops invading Cambodian territory in the province of Takeo, resulting in the death of one Cambodian and the injury of three others. The letters demanded that the Saigon puppet regime and the U.S. government cease such criminal acts and compensate for the damages incurred.

	NEW DELHI, June 12 (Xinhua) - According to a report from the Cambodian News Agency: The Cambodian Foreign Ministry sent a letter of protest to the Thai puppet regime on June 6, strongly condemning the Thai army's brazen invasion of Cambodian territory in the province of Battambang on May 30, resulting in the death of 16 Cambodian soldiers and the injury of two others. The letter demanded that the Thai puppet regime stop such acts of aggression and warned that if similar criminal acts were to occur again, the Thai regime would be held fully responsible for the consequences.

### 美国—南越伪军、泰国军队入侵柬埔寨领土  柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军、泰国军队入侵柬埔寨领土
    柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边十一日电  据柬埔寨新闻社报道：柬埔寨外交部六月七日和八日分别照会西贡傀儡当局和美国政府，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军最近两次入侵柬埔寨干丹省，打死一名柬埔寨人、打伤三人的罪行。照会强烈要求西贡傀儡当局和美国政府停止这类罪恶行径，并要求它们赔偿损失。
    新华社金边十二日电  据柬埔寨新闻社报道：柬埔寨外交部六日照会泰国傀儡当局，强烈地抗议泰国军队五月三十日悍然侵入柬埔寨马德望省，打死十六名柬埔寨军人，打伤二人的罪行。照会要求泰国傀儡当局制止这种侵略行径，并警告说，如果再发生类似罪恶行径，其后果将完全由泰国当局负责。
