Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-06-13
Edition: 1

All factories, schools, departments, and enterprises must, under the principle of revolution, achieve great unity according to systems, industries, and classes. This will facilitate the establishment of the revolutionary tripartite alliance, the carrying out of the great criticism and the struggle-criticism-transformation in each unit, and the promotion of revolution, production, work, and preparedness for war.

### 毛主席语录

1968-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    各工厂、各学校、各部门、各企业单位，都必须在革命的原则下，按照系统，按照行业，按照班级，实现革命的大联合，以利于促进革命三结合的建立，以利大批判和各单位斗批改的进行，以利于抓革命、促生产、促工作、促战备。
