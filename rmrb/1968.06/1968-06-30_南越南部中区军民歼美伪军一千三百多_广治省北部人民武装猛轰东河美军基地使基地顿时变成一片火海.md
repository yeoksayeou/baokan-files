Title: South Vietnam's Central Region Military and Civilians Annihilate Over 1,300 American and Puppet Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1968-06-30
Edition: 5

South Vietnam's Central Region military and civilians annihilated over 1,300 American and puppet soldiers in the first ten days of June. They launched attacks on nine enemy-occupied towns and military outposts, and ambushed enemy forces on transportation routes and around their bases. They killed and wounded 1,395 American and puppet soldiers, shot down 21 aircraft, sank or damaged 16 ships, destroyed 3 cannons, and numerous other military equipment.

The military and civilians of Binh Dinh and My Tho provinces made significant contributions to these victories. On June 10, the People's Liberation Armed Forces of Binh Dinh Province annihilated 250 American soldiers in Ong Chon River and My Thuan Commune, sinking or damaging 14 warships. On June 1, 3, and 8, the People's Liberation Armed Forces of My Tho Province repelled enemy "sweeping" operations in Chau Thanh District and Hoai Nhon District, annihilating over 700 American soldiers and shooting down 20 helicopters.

According to another report from the Liberation News Agency, on the night of June 16, the People's Liberation Armed Forces of My Tho Province launched a fierce artillery barrage and attack on many targets within My Tho City. Preliminary reports indicate that in just 40 minutes of battle, the People's Liberation Armed Forces annihilated a puppet army battalion and severely damaged another artillery battalion.

That same night, the People's Liberation Armed Forces also shelled the Phu Cat American base and American and puppet military positions along Highway 4, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

According to a report from the Liberation News Agency in Hanoi on June 26, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in the northern region of Quang Tri Province in South Vietnam launched a fierce artillery barrage on the Dong Ha American base and the Vung Chua port from June 19 to 22, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

Preliminary reports indicate that hundreds of American invaders were annihilated, dozens of ammunition depots exploded, millions of gallons of gasoline were burned, and many barracks were destroyed. The enemy's airfield was completely paralyzed.

In the afternoon of June 20, the People's Liberation Armed Forces turned the enemy's Dong Ha base into a sea of fire. The flames rose over a thousand meters high, quickly spreading from the eastern area to the western area, and from the southern area to the northern area. The intense explosions shook the entire region. The fire burned until the morning of June 21 and had not yet been extinguished. In this barrage, the People's Liberation Armed Forces destroyed six large enemy ammunition depots, burned six oil storage tanks containing millions of gallons of gasoline, destroyed many barracks, annihilated hundreds of American invaders, paralyzed the Dong Ha airfield, and shot down one transport aircraft. The Dong Ha base's power plant was also damaged.

According to a report from the Liberation News Agency in Hanoi on June 26, the military and civilians of Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, and other provinces in South Vietnam have recently achieved new victories by continuously striking the enemy.

From June 10 to 13, the military and civilians of Quang Nam Province continuously attacked and ambushed the enemy, annihilating 204 American invaders, shooting down four aircraft, destroying nine military vehicles, burning down two warehouses, and seizing a batch of weapons and military supplies.

On June 11, the local forces and guerrilla units of Son Ha District in Quang Ngai Province bravely ambushed the enemy during a "sweeping" operation. After a short but fierce battle, they almost completely annihilated a puppet army company. On June 18, the People's Liberation Armed Forces of Phu Yen Province ambushed the enemy twice, annihilating two puppet army battalions and five platoons, and shooting down one enemy aircraft.

From June 10 to 21, the People's Liberation Armed Forces of Cam Lo and Duong Lang Districts in Quang Tri Province annihilated over 340 American and puppet soldiers, including over 280 American invaders; destroyed 10 transport vehicles, four tanks, and armored cars.

### 南越南部中区军民歼美伪军一千三百多  广治省北部人民武装猛轰东河美军基地使基地顿时变成一片火海

1968-06-30
第5版()
专栏：

    南越南部中区军民歼美伪军一千三百多 
    广治省北部人民武装猛轰东河美军基地使基地顿时变成一片火海
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方南部中区军民六月上旬向敌人盘踞的九个城镇和军事支区发动进攻，并且在交通线和敌人基地周围截击敌军，共打死打伤美伪军一千三百九十五名，击落飞机二十一架，击沉、击伤船只十六艘，击毁大炮三门和其它许多作战工具。
    槟椥和美萩两省军民对取得上述胜利作出了巨大贡献。槟椥省人民解放武装力量十日在翁宗河和美盛乡一举消灭美军二百五十名，击沉击伤战船十四艘。美萩省人民解放武装力量一日、三日和八日，在周城县和丐礼县粉碎了敌人的“扫荡”，消灭美军七百多名，击落直升飞机二十架。
    另据解放通讯社报道，六月十六日夜间，美萩省人民解放武装力量又向美萩市内的许多目标发动猛烈炮轰和进攻。据初步消息，仅经过四十分钟的战斗，人民解放武装力量就歼灭了伪军一个营和重创另一个炮兵营。
    当天夜里，人民解放武装力量还炮击了平德美军基地和第四号公路沿线的美伪军阵地，使敌人遭到严重损失。
    据新华社河内二十六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广治省北部地区人民解放武装力量，从十九日到二十二日连续四天猛烈炮轰东河美军基地和越门港口，使敌人遭到严重损失。
    据初步消息，有数以百计的美国侵略军被消灭，敌人成十座弹药库发生爆炸，成百万公升汽油被烧毁，许多营房被击毁，敌军机场完全陷于瘫痪。
    人民解放武装力量在二十日下午的轰击中，把敌人的东河基地打得变成一片火海，烈火升高一千多米，迅速地从东区蔓延到西区，从南区蔓延到北区，远离三十公里以外都可以看到烈火。猛烈的爆炸声震动了整个地区。烈火燃烧到二十一日上午仍然没有完全熄灭。在这次轰击中，人民解放武装力量击毁敌人的大型弹药库六座，烧毁贮有成百万公升汽油的贮油池六个，击毁许多营房，消灭美国侵略军成百名，使东河机场陷于瘫痪，并击落运输机一架。东河基地的发电厂也遭到破坏。
    新华社河内二十六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广南、广义等省军民最近接连打击敌人，取得了新的战果。
    广南省军民从六月十日到十三日不断袭击和截击敌人，共消灭美国侵略军二百零四名，击落飞机四架，击毁各种军车九辆，烧毁仓库两座，缴获武器和军用物资一批。
    广义省山晶县地方部队和游击队六月十一日英勇截击进行“扫荡”的敌人，经过短时间的激战，几乎全歼伪军一个连。平顺省人民解放武装力量，在十八日两次截击敌人，歼灭伪军两个连、五个排，击落敌机一架。
    广治省甘露和犹灵县人民解放武装力量从六月十日到二十一日消灭美伪军三百四十多名，其中有美国侵略军二百八十多名；击毁运输车十辆、坦克和装甲车四辆。
