Title: Busy Scene at Tai'erzhuang Grain Procurement Station in Shandong's Zaozhuang
Author:
Date: 1968-06-30
Edition: 3

The Tai'erzhuang Grain Procurement Station in Zaozhuang, Shandong Province, is bustling with activity. Various communes and production teams are enthusiastically selling surplus grain to the state.
