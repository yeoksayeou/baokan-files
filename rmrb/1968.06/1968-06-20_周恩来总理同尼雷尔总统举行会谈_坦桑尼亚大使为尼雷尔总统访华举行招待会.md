Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with President Nyerere
Author:
Date: 1968-06-20
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai holds talks with President Nyerere

Tanzanian Ambassador hosts reception for President Nyerere's visit to China

BEIJING, June 19 (Xinhua) -- Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council held talks with President Julius K. Nyerere of the United Republic of Tanzania today.

The talks were conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

Present at the talks from the Tanzanian side were: R. N. Sijohn, A. M. Bok Mwanuka, C. Y. Mgonja, Paul Mwaluko, Paul Sogigwa, Brigadier Salakikya, Shaidi, Mzena, Chand S. Ali, Colonel Chacha, Regarulira, Major Mwakipesi, Msolomi, Butiku.

From the Chinese side were: Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Huang Yongsheng, Wu Faxian, Wen Yucheng, Zhao Qimin, Ji Pengfei, Xie Huaiyi, Qian Zhengying, and others.

BEIJING, June 19 (Xinhua) -- In honor of President Julius K. Nyerere of the United Republic of Tanzania's visit to China, Tanzanian Ambassador Paul Mwaluko held a reception tonight.

President Nyerere and his wife, along with other accompanying personnel, attended the reception.

Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, Chief of Staff of the People's Liberation Army Huang Yongsheng, Deputy Chief of Staff Wu Faxian, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, and relevant government departments, People's Liberation Army, Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, and other relevant officials such as Su Yu, Wen Yucheng, Zhao Qimin, Lin Haiyun, Ji Pengfei, Xie Huaiyi, Guo Lu, Qian Zhengying, Ding Guanyi, Ding Xilin, as well as representatives from Beijing's revolutionary mass organizations, were invited to attend the reception.

At the reception, filled with a friendly atmosphere, Ambassador Mwaluko and Vice Premier Chen Yi toasted. Ambassador Mwaluko proposed a toast to the fighting friendship and unity between the 700 million people of China and Tanzania, to the development and prosperity of China, to the victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China, to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people and the most respected friend of the Tanzanian people, and to the health of President Nyerere and his wife!

Vice Premier Chen Yi, in his toast, said that President Nyerere has been in China for two days. Through the visit and talks, the guests will have a deeper understanding of our situation. This understanding will help to strengthen the friendship between our two countries. He wished the Tanzanian guests a successful visit and proposed a toast to the friendship between China and Tanzania and to the health of President Nyerere!

At the beginning of the reception, performers from the Tanzanian Acrobatics Learning Team sang Tanzanian and Chinese songs, which were warmly received.

### 周恩来总理同尼雷尔总统举行会谈  坦桑尼亚大使为尼雷尔总统访华举行招待会

1968-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理同尼雷尔总统举行会谈
    坦桑尼亚大使为尼雷尔总统访华举行招待会
    新华社十九日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天同坦桑尼亚联合共和国总统朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔举行会谈。
    会谈在诚挚友好的气氛中进行。
    参加会谈的，坦桑尼亚方面有：拉·南·西乔纳、艾·姆·博克·穆南卡、切·约·姆贡贾、保罗·姆瓦卢科、保罗·索齐格瓦、萨拉基基亚准将、沙伊迪、姆泽纳、昌德·斯·阿里、查查中校、雷加鲁利拉、姆瓦基佩西少校、姆索洛米、布蒂库。
    中国方面有：陈毅、李先念、黄永胜、吴法宪、温玉成、赵启民、姬鹏飞、谢怀德、钱正英等。
    新华社十九日讯　为坦桑尼亚联合共和国总统朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔访问中国，坦桑尼亚驻中国大使保罗·姆瓦卢科今天晚上举行招待会。
    尼雷尔总统和夫人及其他随行人员出席了招待会。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅、李先念，中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜，副总参谋长吴法宪，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，以及政府有关部门、中国人民解放军、北京市革命委员会和有关方面的负责人粟裕、温玉成、赵启民、林海云、姬鹏飞、谢怀德、郭鲁、钱正英、丁国钰、丁西林，北京市革命群众组织的代表，应邀出席了招待会。
    在充满友好气氛的招待会上，姆瓦卢科大使和陈毅副总理先后祝酒。姆瓦卢科大使提议，为七亿中国人民和坦桑尼亚人民的战斗友谊和团结，为中国的发展和繁荣，为中国无产阶级文化大革命的胜利，为中国人民的伟大领袖、坦桑尼亚人民的最敬爱的朋友毛泽东主席的健康，为尼雷尔总统和夫人的健康干杯！
    陈毅副总理在祝酒中说，尼雷尔总统来中国访问已经有两天了。通过访问，通过会谈，贵宾们对我们的情况将有进一步的了解。这种了解，对增进我们两国之间的友谊会有帮助的。他祝坦桑尼亚贵宾访问成功，并提议为中国和坦桑尼亚两国的友谊，为尼雷尔总统的健康干杯！
    招待会开始时，坦桑尼亚杂技学习团的演员们演唱了坦桑尼亚和中国歌曲，受到热烈欢迎。
