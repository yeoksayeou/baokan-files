Title: Albanian Deputy Premier of the State Council Receives Chinese Transportation Delegation; Minutes of the Seventh Meeting of the Management Committee of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company Signed
Author:
Date: 1968-06-13
Edition: 6

Albanian Deputy Premier of the State Council Adil Charçani received the Chinese transportation delegation headed by Li Tingquan, Charge d’Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, on June 11.

Present at the reception were Albanian Minister of Communications Kirko, Deputy Foreign Minister Maliqi, and Deputy Minister of Communications Leka.

During the reception, Comrade Charçani had a very cordial and friendly conversation with the members of the Chinese delegation.

The minutes of the seventh meeting of the Management Committee of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company were signed in Tirana on June 11.

Albanian Deputy Minister of Communications Lambi Leka and the head of the Chinese transportation delegation, Li Tingquan, signed the minutes.

Attending the signing ceremony were Albanian Minister of Communications Kirko, Deputy Foreign Minister Maliqi, Deputy Minister of Trade Kati, and the Albanian manager of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company, Klosi.

All members of the Chinese transportation delegation also attended the signing ceremony.

That evening, Albanian Minister of Communications Kirko held a farewell banquet for the Chinese transportation delegation.

### 阿部长会议副主席接见我交通代表团  中阿轮船股份公司第七次管理委员会会议议定书签订

1968-06-13
第6版()
专栏：

    阿部长会议副主席接见我交通代表团
    中阿轮船股份公司第七次管理委员会会议议定书签订
    新华社地拉那十二日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席阿迪尔·查尔查尼十一日接见以中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃为首的中国交通代表团。
    接见时在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚交通部长基尔科、外交部副部长马利列和交通部副部长莱卡等。
    接见时，查尔查尼同志同中国代表团成员进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
    新华社地拉那十二日电　中阿轮船股份公司第七次管理委员会会议议定书十一日在地拉那签订。
    阿尔巴尼亚交通部副部长兰比·莱卡和中国交通代表团团长李庭荃分别在议定书上签了字。
    出席签字仪式的有：阿尔巴尼亚交通部部长基尔科、阿尔巴尼亚外交部副部长马利列、贸易部副部长卡蒂、中阿轮船股份公司阿尔巴尼亚方面经理克洛西。
    中国交通代表团全体成员也参加了签字仪式。
    当天晚上，阿尔巴尼亚交通部部长基尔科为中国交通代表团举行了送别宴会。
