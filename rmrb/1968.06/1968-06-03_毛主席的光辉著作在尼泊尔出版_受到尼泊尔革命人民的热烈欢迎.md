Title: Chairman Mao's Brilliant Works Published in Nepal
Author:
Date: 1968-06-03
Edition: 5

The brilliant works of Chairman Mao, the great leader of the proletariat, "Talks at the Yenan Forum on Literature and Art" has been translated into the Newari language and recently published as a separate volume in Kathmandu. It has been warmly welcomed by the revolutionary people of Nepal.

This great work was translated by Purna Bahadur, the president of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, and published by this association.

Another brilliant work by Chairman Mao, "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," has also been translated into Newari and published as a separate volume in Bakhtapur.

### 毛主席的光辉著作在尼泊尔出版  受到尼泊尔革命人民的热烈欢迎

1968-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席的光辉著作在尼泊尔出版
    受到尼泊尔革命人民的热烈欢迎
    新华社加德满都电　无产阶级的伟大导师毛主席的光辉著作《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》已经译成尼瓦尔文，最近在加德满都出版了单行本，受到尼泊尔革命人民的热烈欢迎。
    这篇伟大著作是由尼中友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔翻译的，并且由这个协会出版。
    另一篇毛主席的光辉著作《论人民民主专政》也译成了尼瓦尔文，在巴克塔普尔出版了单行本。
