Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-06-26
Edition: 5

The military and civilians of North Vietnam shot down three U.S. aircraft on June 23, 1968.

According to a statement released in Hanoi today, the military and civilians of Hanoi shot down one invading U.S. unmanned reconnaissance aircraft on June 23. On June 21 and 22, the military and civilians of Ha Tinh and Nghe An provinces shot down two U.S. aggressor aircraft.

### 越南北方军民击落美机三架

1968-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机三架
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里今天宣布：河内市军民二十三日打落了一架入侵的美国无人驾驶侦察机。在二十一日和二十二日，河静省和义安省军民曾打落两架美国强盗飞机。
