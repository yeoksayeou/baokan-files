	To make literature and art become a very good component of the entire revolutionary machine, serving as a powerful weapon to unite the people, educate the people, attack the enemy, and annihilate the enemy, helping the people to unite as one and fight against the enemy.

### 毛主席语录

1968-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    要使文艺很好地成为整个革命机器的一个组成部分，作为团结人民、教育人民、打击敌人、消灭敌人的有力的武器，帮助人民同心同德地和敌人作斗争。
