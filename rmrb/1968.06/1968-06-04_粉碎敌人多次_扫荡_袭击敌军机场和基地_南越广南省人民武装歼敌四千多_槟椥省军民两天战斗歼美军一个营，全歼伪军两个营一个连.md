Title: Defeating Enemy "Sweeping Operations" and Attacking Enemy Airfields and Bases
Author:
Date: 1968-06-04
Edition: 6

The people's armed forces in Quang Nam Province, South Vietnam, launched continuous attacks against the enemy from May 4 to 24, achieving a great victory by annihilating more than 4,000 enemy troops and mercenaries.

From May 4 to 19, the military and civilians of Dien Ban County bravely fought, repeatedly intercepting the enemy's "sweeping operations." They annihilated more than 1,200 enemy troops and mercenaries, including 870 American invaders, shot down nine enemy planes, and destroyed sixteen military vehicles.

The military and civilians in the southern part of Quang Nam Province, from May 5 to 24, fought continuously for twenty days in the Hoang Sa area. They annihilated more than 1,000 enemy troops, almost all of whom were American invaders, and shot down forty-seven enemy planes. The guerrilla forces and local troops in this area were very active. In their multiple battles from May 5 to 12, in addition to annihilating nearly 600 enemy troops and mercenaries, they also destroyed twelve enemy vehicles, shot down five planes, blew up four bridges, one power plant, one police station, and dozens of barracks, demolished five "strategic hamlets," and seized a large number of weapons.

From May 5 to 24, the military and civilians in the Cau Binh River area of Quang Nam Province also won continuous victories. They foiled the enemy's multiple "sweeping operations," killing, injuring, and capturing more than 1,400 enemy troops, including more than 1,100 American invaders. They destroyed sixteen tanks and armored vehicles, shot down three planes, and seized a large number of weapons, ammunition, and military supplies.

The people's armed forces and the self-defense forces of Hue City in the northern part of Quang Nam Province cooperated closely and launched two consecutive attacks on the enemy in Hue City in early May, annihilating 400 enemy troops and mercenaries.

On May 20, 21, and 24, the military and civilians of Quang Nam Province launched consecutive attacks on the enemy's airfields, logistics bases, and artillery positions in Da Nang and Qui Nhon, destroying twenty-five enemy planes of various types and many combat tools.

According to a report from the New China News Agency in Hanoi, the military and civilians of Binh Dinh Province recently foiled the enemy's multiple "sweeping operations" near Binh Dinh City, achieving brilliant results.

According to statistics, in just two days of battle on May 7 and 8, the people's armed forces annihilated an American invasion camp, completely annihilated two camps and one company of puppet troops, killing and injuring more than 1,200 enemy troops, including more than 600 American troops; they sank and damaged twelve enemy ships.

### 粉碎敌人多次“扫荡”  袭击敌军机场和基地  南越广南省人民武装歼敌四千多  槟椥省军民两天战斗歼美军一个营，全歼伪军两个营一个连

1968-06-04
第6版()
专栏：

    粉碎敌人多次“扫荡”  袭击敌军机场和基地
    南越广南省人民武装歼敌四千多
    槟椥省军民两天战斗歼美军一个营，全歼伪军两个营一个连
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广南省人民解放武装力量，在五月四日到二十四日期间向敌人发动连续进攻，取得了消灭美伪军和帮凶军四千多名的巨大胜利。
    从五月四日到十九日，奠盘县军民英勇战斗，多次截击出动“扫荡”的敌人，共消灭美伪军和帮凶军一千二百多名，其中包括美国侵略军八百七十多名，击落敌机九架，击毁军车十六辆。
    广南省南部军民，从五月五日到二十四日，在横山地区进行的连续二十天战斗中，共消灭敌人一千多名，几乎全部是美国侵略军，并且击落敌机四十七架。这个地区的游击队和地方部队十分活跃。他们在五月五日到十二日期间的多次战斗中，除消灭美伪军近六百名外，还击毁敌人军车十二辆，击落敌机五架，炸毁桥梁四座、发电厂一座、伪警察局一所和营房数十座，捣毁敌人“战略村”五个，并且缴获了许多武器。
    在五月五日到二十四日期间，广南省军民在秋盆河地区也连打胜仗，他们粉碎了敌人的多次“扫荡”，共打死、打伤和俘虏敌人一千四百多名，其中包括美国侵略军一千一百多名，击毁坦克、装甲车十六辆，击落飞机三架，同时缴获了许多武器弹药和军用物资。
    广南省北部人民解放武装力量和会安市武装自卫队密切配合，在五月上旬曾连续两次袭击了会安市的敌人，吃掉了美伪军四百名。
    在五月二十日、二十一日和二十四日，广南省军民又接连袭击了岘港、朱莱等敌军机场、后勤基地和炮兵阵地，共击毁敌人各种类型飞机二十五架和许多作战工具。
    据新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方槟椥省军民不久以前在槟椥市附近，粉碎了敌人发动的多次“扫荡”，取得辉煌战果。
    据统计，仅在五月七日和八日两天的战斗中，人民解放武装力量就歼灭了美国侵略军一个营，全歼伪军两个营和一个连，共打死打伤敌人一千二百多名，其中美军有六百多名；击沉击伤敌船十二艘。
