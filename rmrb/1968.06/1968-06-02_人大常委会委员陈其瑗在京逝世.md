Title: NPC Standing Committee Member Chen Qiyuan Passes Away in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-06-02
Edition: 4

National People's Congress Standing Committee Member, CPPCC National Committee Member, and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Interior Chen Qiyuan passed away in Beijing on May 30 due to illness. He was 81 years old.

### 人大常委会委员陈其瑗在京逝世

1968-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    人大常委会委员陈其瑗在京逝世
    新华社一日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、政协全国委员会委员、内务部副部长陈其瑗同志，因病于五月三十日在北京逝世，终年八十一岁。
