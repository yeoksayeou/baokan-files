Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Airplanes Invade Cambodian Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-06-25
Edition: 5

	U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet airplanes invade Cambodian airspace
	Cambodian Foreign Ministry lodges strong protest

	Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, June 23rd — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency: On the 19th, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry sent a note to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, strongly protesting the deliberate intrusion of U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet airplanes into Cambodian airspace over the provinces of Takeo, Kampong Speu, and Kampot on May 28th, 29th, 30th, 31st, and June 1st, a total of nine times.

	The note demands that the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime cease such actions.

### 美国—南越伪军飞机侵犯柬领空  柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军飞机侵犯柬领空
    柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边二十三日电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道：柬埔寨外交部十九日照会美国政府和西贡傀儡当局，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军的飞机在五月二十八日、二十九日、三十日、三十一日和六月一日连续九次蓄意侵犯柬埔寨柴桢省领空。
    照会要求美国政府和西贡傀儡当局停止这种行径。
