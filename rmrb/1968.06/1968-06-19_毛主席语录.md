Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-06-19
Edition: 1

The Chinese people regard the victories of the anti-imperialist struggles of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America as their own victories. They offer enthusiastic support and sympathy for all their anti-imperialist and anti-colonialist struggles.

### 毛主席语录

1968-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    中国人民把亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民的反帝国主义斗争的胜利看作是自己的胜利，并对他们的一切反帝国主义、反殖民主义的斗争给以热烈的同情和支持。
