Title: Nepalese Vice Premier Kirtinidhi Bista Leaves Shanghai for Guangzhou to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1968-06-02
Edition: 4

	Nepalese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kirtinidhi Bista and his entourage concluded their friendly visit to China and left Shanghai this afternoon for Guangzhou to return home.

	Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and leaders of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee went to the airport to see them off. Nepalese Ambassador to China Subba also went to the airport to see them off.

	Vice Premier Bista arrived in Shanghai this afternoon by special plane from Nanjing. During his stay in Nanjing, leaders of the Jiangsu Provincial and Nanjing Municipal Revolutionary Committees held a banquet to welcome Vice Premier Bista and his entourage. The Nepalese friends also visited the Nanjing Chemical Plant and the Jiangning County Fertilizer Plant, where they were warmly welcomed by the revolutionary workers of the two plants.

	The following is the full text of the joint communiqué issued by both sides after Nepalese Vice Premier Kirtinidhi Bista concluded his friendly visit to China:

	At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, His Excellency Kirtinidhi Bista, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, paid a friendly visit to the People's Republic of China from May 23 to June 1, 1968. He was warmly welcomed and cordially entertained by the Government and people of China.

	Vice Premier Bista visited Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Jinan, and toured factories, people's communes, construction projects, schools, and historical sites. The great achievements of the Chinese people in all fields left a deep impression on the Vice Premier.

	Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people, and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, received Vice Premier Bista and his entourage and had a cordial and friendly talk with them. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China had a meeting and talk with Vice Premier Bista and his entourage.

	Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China held talks with Vice Premier Bista in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

	The two sides reviewed the relations between the two countries and expressed satisfaction with the fruitful development of the friendly relations between China and Nepal in recent years. These relations are based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. The two sides expressed their determination to work together to further strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries.

	The two sides agreed that mutual visits by leaders of the two countries contribute positively to promoting mutual understanding and strengthening friendship.

	The two sides held talks on the issue of developing friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries. Vice Premier Bista of the Kingdom of Nepal expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Government of the People's Republic of China for its generous assistance to the development of Nepal. The two sides expressed satisfaction with the signing of the trade agreement to further promote trade and commerce between the two countries. The two sides agreed that economic cooperation between China and Nepal should be continuously consolidated and developed. The Chinese side expressed its willingness to provide continued and increased material and technical assistance to the economic development of Nepal through consultation.

	The two sides also exchanged views on international issues of common concern.

	Vice Premier Bista cordially invited Vice Premier Chen Yi to visit Nepal at his convenience, and the Chinese Government accepted the invitation.

	June 1, 1968

### 尼副首相比斯塔离上海经广州回国  比斯塔副首相结束对中国友好访问后，双方发表联合公报

1968-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    尼副首相比斯塔离上海经广州回国
    比斯塔副首相结束对中国友好访问后，双方发表联合公报
    新华社上海一日电　尼泊尔王国副首相兼外交大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔及其随行人员，结束了在我国的友好访问，今天下午离开上海经广州回国。
    外交部副部长韩念龙，上海市革命委员会领导成员到机场欢送。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使苏巴也到机场送行。
    比斯塔副首相是在今天下午从南京乘专机到达上海的。在南京期间，江苏省和南京市革命委员会负责人设宴热烈欢迎比斯塔副首相及其随行人员。尼泊尔朋友还参观了南京化学厂和江宁县化肥厂，受到这两个厂的革命职工的热烈欢迎。
    新华社一日讯　在尼泊尔王国副首相基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔结束了对中国的友好访问以后，今天双方发表了联合公报。联合公报全文如下：
    应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，尼泊尔王国副首相基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔阁下于一九六八年五月二十三日至六月一日到中华人民共和国进行了友好访问，受到了中国政府和中国人民的热烈欢迎和盛情接待。
    基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔阁下访问了北京、上海、南京和济南，参观了工厂、人民公社、建设工程、学校和名胜古迹等。中国人民在各方面所取得的巨大成就给副首相留下了很深刻的印象。
    中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席接见了比斯塔副首相和他的随行人员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同比斯塔副首相和他的随行人员，进行了会晤和谈话。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅和比斯塔副首相在亲切友好的气氛中举行了会谈。
    双方回顾了两国之间的关系，对于近几年来，中尼两国友好关系不断获得有成果的发展，表示满意。这种关系是建立在互相尊重、和平共处五项原则基础之上的。双方表示将共同努力，进一步加强两国的友好关系。
    双方一致认为，两国领导人的互相访问，对于促进相互了解和加强友谊，是一种积极的贡献。
    双方就发展两国友好合作关系的问题进行了会谈。尼泊尔王国副首相衷心感谢中华人民共和国政府对尼泊尔的发展所给予的慷慨援助。双方对签订进一步促进两国贸易和商业的贸易协定表示满意。双方同意，中尼两国之间的经济合作，应当不断巩固和发展。中国方面表示，愿意通过协商，对尼泊尔的经济发展提供继续增加的物质和技术援助。
    双方还就两国共同关心的国际问题，充分地交换了意见。
    比斯塔副首相阁下极其热情地邀请陈毅副总理在他方便的时候访问尼泊尔，中国政府接受了这一邀请。
               一九六八年六月一日
