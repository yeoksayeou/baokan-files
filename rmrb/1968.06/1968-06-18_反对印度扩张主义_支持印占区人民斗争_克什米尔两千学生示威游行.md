Title: Oppose Indian Expansionism, Support the Struggle of the People in Indian-Occupied Areas
Author:
Date: 1968-06-18
Edition: 6

According to a report by the *Pakistan Times* on the 4th, more than 2,000 students in Muzaffarabad, the capital of the Pakistani-controlled area of Kashmir, recently held a demonstration to oppose the reactionary rule of the authorities in the Indian-occupied area of Kashmir.

During the demonstration, the students chanted slogans opposing Indian expansionism and supporting the struggle of the people in the Indian-occupied areas. They also held a rally.

Abdul Hamid Khan, the Chairman of the Azad Jammu and Kashmir government, addressed the demonstrators, expressing support for the struggle of the people in the Indian-occupied areas and believing that their struggle would be victorious.

According to another report by the Pakistan Press International, the Indian reactionary authorities have recently intensified their suppression of the people in the Indian-occupied area of Kashmir. Students in Srinagar, the capital of the Indian-occupied area, had previously chanted slogans opposing Indian reactionary rule and held demonstrations. The Indian reactionary authorities responded with brutal suppression, mass arrests of local people, and the closure of all schools and colleges in Srinagar.

### 反对印度扩张主义　支持印占区人民斗争  克什米尔两千学生示威游行

1968-06-18
第6版()
专栏：

    反对印度扩张主义　支持印占区人民斗争
    克什米尔两千学生示威游行
    新华社拉瓦尔品第电　据《巴基斯坦时报》四日报道：克什米尔巴基斯坦控制区首府穆扎法拉巴的两千多名学生，不久前举行示威游行，反对克什米尔印度占领区当局的反动统治。
    这些学生在游行中高呼反对印度扩张主义和支持印占区人民斗争的口号。他们还举行了集会。
    查谟和克什米尔自由政府主席阿卜杜勒·哈米德汗对参加示威的人发表讲话，表示支持印占区人民的斗争，并且相信他们的斗争必胜。
    另据巴基斯坦联合通讯社报道，印度反动当局最近加紧镇压克什米尔印占区人民。克什米尔印占区首府斯利那加的学生曾经高呼反对印度反动统治的口号，举行示威游行。印度反动当局对此进行残酷镇压，大规模地逮捕当地人民，并且封闭了斯利那加的全部学校和学院。
