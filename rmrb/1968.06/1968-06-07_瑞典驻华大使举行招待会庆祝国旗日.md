Title: Swedish Ambassador Hosts Reception to Celebrate National Flag Day
Author:
Date: 1968-06-07
Edition: 6

Swedish Ambassador to China, Petter Pettersson, held a reception today to celebrate the National Flag Day of the Kingdom of Sweden.

Relevant officials from the government and the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Luo Guibao, were invited to attend the reception.

Ambassadors from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 瑞典驻华大使举行招待会庆祝国旗日

1968-06-07
第6版()
专栏：

    瑞典驻华大使举行招待会庆祝国旗日
    新华社六日讯　瑞典驻中国大使佩特里今天举行招待会，庆祝瑞典王国国旗日。
    政府有关部门、北京市革命委员会的负责人罗贵波等，应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国使节也出席了招待会。
