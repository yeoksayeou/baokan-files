Title: The U.S. Empire Hurries to Recruit Cannon Fodder at Home
Author:
Date: 1968-06-22
Edition: 5

The U.S. Johnson administration is hurriedly recruiting cannon fodder within the country in an attempt to fill the increasingly heavy casualties in the Vietnam War and make a desperate struggle.

The U.S. Department of Defense announced on the 19th: The U.S. government plans to recruit 18,300 soldiers for the army in August, an increase of 3,300 from July. This brings the total number of recruits for January to August to 245,500. The U.S. Department of Defense claims that the total number of recruits for this year is expected to reach 302,000, an increase of 72,000 from 1967.

### 美帝加紧在国内搜罗侵越炮灰

1968-06-22
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    美帝加紧在国内搜罗侵越炮灰
    美国约翰逊政府在美国国内加紧搜罗炮灰，妄图填补在侵越战争中日益惨重的伤亡，进行垂死挣扎。
    美国国防部十九日宣布：美国政府八月份为陆军征兵一万八千三百人，比七月份增加三千三百人。这就使美国一至八月份的征兵总数达到二十四万五千五百人。美国国防部声称，预计美国今年全年的征兵数额将达到三十万二千人，比一九六七年要多征七万二千人。
