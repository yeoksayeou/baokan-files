Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-06-05
Edition: 1

	The distinction between the Communist Party and the Nationalist Party, the fundamental difference between the proletariat and the bourgeoisie, and the essential difference between the dictatorship of the proletariat and the dictatorship of the bourgeoisie all lie in whether the broad masses of the people are protected or suppressed.

### 毛主席语录

    毛主席语录
