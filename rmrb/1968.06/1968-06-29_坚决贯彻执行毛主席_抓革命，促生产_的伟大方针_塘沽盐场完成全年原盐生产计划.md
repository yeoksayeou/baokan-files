Title: Resolutely Implementing Chairman Mao's Great Directive of "Grasp Revolution, Promote Production"
Author:
Date: 1968-06-29
Edition: 2

The Tanggu Salt Field Revolutionary Committee, with the assistance of the People's Liberation Army's leftist support personnel stationed at the field, has resolutely implemented Chairman Mao's great directive of "grasp revolution, promote production." Both revolution and production have achieved tremendous victories. Now, this salt field has completed and exceeded the state's annual salt production plan for this year half a year ahead of schedule. This is the fastest and best year for completing the salt production task since the liberation of this salt field.

Since the birth of the Tanggu Salt Field Revolutionary Committee at the beginning of this year, it has focused on the fundamental task of vigorously studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought in the midst of numerous tasks. The committee members have gone deep into the various teams scattered across the hundred-mile salt flats, organizing various types of Mao Zedong Thought study classes with the salt workers. Revolutionary workers make monthly plans to study and apply Mao Zedong Thought, hold lectures and sharing sessions in the middle of the month, and summarize experiences at the end of the month. The spiritual outlook of the people has undergone a significant change. The salt workers say: "In our hearts, there is a red sun, with firm will and courage we have strength. We do not believe in the heavens, we do not believe in the earth, we wholeheartedly follow Chairman Mao." The vigorous mass movement of studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought has ensured that Chairman Mao's series of latest instructions are promptly implemented, achieving double victories in revolution and production.

### 坚决贯彻执行毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针  塘沽盐场完成全年原盐生产计划

1968-06-29
第2版()
专栏：

    坚决贯彻执行毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针
    塘沽盐场完成全年原盐生产计划
    据新华社天津电　塘沽盐场革命委员会在驻场人民解放军支左人员的帮助下，坚决贯彻执行毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，革命和生产都取得了巨大胜利。现在，这个盐场已经提前半年多超额完成了今年全年国家原盐生产计划。这是解放以来这个盐场完成原盐生产任务最快、最好的一年。
    塘沽盐场革命委员会在今年初诞生以后，在千头万绪的工作中首先狠抓活学活用毛泽东思想这个根本。革委会委员深入到分散在百里盐滩的各班组里，和盐工们一起大办各种类型的毛泽东思想学习班。广大革命职工月初订活学活用毛泽东思想的计划，月中开讲用会，月底总结经验，人的精神面貌发生了显著变化。盐工们说：“心中有个红太阳，志坚胆壮有力量。不信天，不信地，一心紧跟毛主席。”狠抓活学活用毛泽东思想的群众运动，保证了毛主席的一系列最新指示及时得到落实，取得了革命和生产双胜利。
