Title: Qingdao Cotton Mill No. 3 Achieves New Production Levels
Author:
Date: 1968-06-18
Edition: 2

Qingdao Cotton Mill No. 3's revolutionary workers have focused on class struggle and conducted a sustained and in-depth revolutionary criticism campaign, significantly promoting the ideological transformation of the people. The entire factory has taken on a new look in terms of revolution and production, achieving unprecedented levels in both production and technological innovation in the factory's history. Last year, this factory's cotton yarn and cotton cloth production both completed the state plan ahead of schedule. In the first four months of this year, the factory has exceeded the state plan each month, with quality steadily improving. Since May, production has seen a significant increase. The mass movement of technological innovation and technological revolution is thriving, with new inventions and creations constantly emerging, and new varieties greatly increasing.

### 青岛国棉三厂生产出现新水平

1968-06-18
第2版()
专栏：

    青岛国棉三厂生产出现新水平
    青岛国棉三厂的革命职工，狠抓阶级斗争，持久深入地开展革命大批判，大大促进了人的思想革命化。全厂革命和生产出现一片崭新面貌，生产和技术革新都开创了本厂历史上从未有过的新水平。去年，这个厂的棉纱、棉布产量分别提前完成了国家计划，今年头四个月月月超额完成国家计划，质量稳步上升，五月以来产量又有显著增长。技术革新和技术革命的群众性活动蓬勃开展，新的发明创造不断涌现，新品种大大增加。
