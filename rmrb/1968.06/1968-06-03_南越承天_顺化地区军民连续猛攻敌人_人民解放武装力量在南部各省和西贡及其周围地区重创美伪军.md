Title: People's Liberation Forces in South Vietnam Inflict Heavy Casualties on U.S. and Puppet Forces
Author:
Date: 1968-06-03
Edition: 5

People's Liberation Forces in the Quang Tri-Hue Region Launch Continuous Attacks on the Enemy

People's Liberation Armed Forces in Southern Provinces and Around Saigon Inflict Heavy Casualties on U.S. and Puppet Forces

	HANOI, June 2 (Xinhua) — According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the people and armed forces of the Quang Tri-Hue region in South Vietnam launched continuous attacks on the enemy from May 17 to 23. They annihilated more than 1,900 U.S. aggressors and two companies of puppet troops, destroyed more than 170 vehicles, 26 artillery pieces, and 20 helicopters, and demolished more than 500 barracks.

	On the night of May 20, the People's Liberation Armed Forces simultaneously attacked the U.S. armored cavalry base at Dong Du, the command post of the U.S. 101st Airborne Division, and the Phu Bai base, annihilating more than 1,650 U.S. aggressors, destroying more than 120 vehicles, 26 artillery pieces, and 20 helicopters, and demolishing more than 500 barracks. At the Dong Du U.S. armored cavalry base alone, the People's Liberation Armed Forces annihilated 1,050 U.S. aggressors and destroyed 101 vehicles.

	On the night of May 23, the People's Liberation Armed Forces attacked the enemy's garrison at Gia Trung village southwest of Phu Bai, annihilating two companies of puppet troops and destroying 15 vehicles. Prior to this, from May 17 to 19, the People's Liberation Armed Forces continuously struck the enemy in the areas of Phu Vang County, My Lam, Tra Khuc, and Yen Dai, annihilating 275 U.S. aggressors and destroying 35 tanks and armored vehicles.

	According to another report, on May 28 and 29, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in the northern part of Quang Tri Province ambushed and attacked the enemy in the counties of Duc Lap and U Minh, annihilating nearly 300 U.S. and puppet troops, including nearly 250 U.S. troops.

	HANOI, June 2 (Xinhua) — According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, from May 25 to 29, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Saigon and its surrounding areas annihilated and severely defeated two companies of U.S. and puppet troops, annihilating more than 500 enemy troops and sinking four warships.

	On May 25 and 26, the Liberation Army annihilated 240 puppet marines north of Saigon. On the night of May 27, the Liberation Army annihilated a company of U.S. troops in the An Loc area southwest of Tay Ninh. On the morning of May 28, the Liberation Army fiercely intercepted the puppet marines in the Tra Vinh area, severely defeating a company of puppet troops.

	On May 29, the Liberation Army attacked numerous enemy targets in the fifth and sixth districts of Saigon. The Liberation Army also fiercely bombarded the puppet navy headquarters, annihilating 250 enemy troops and sinking four warships. On the morning of May 30, the Liberation Army continued to bombard various enemy targets in the city center. On May 31, the Liberation Army fiercely attacked the puppet marines and paratroopers in the Phu Tho and Phu Dien areas.

	According to another report, on the morning of May 19, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in the Saigon-Gia Dinh region launched three consecutive attacks on enemy ships at the Dong Trieu River junction, 20 kilometers southeast of Saigon, seriously damaging two U.S. military transport ships of 10,000 tons each and one landing craft. On May 17 and 18, the people and armed forces of Gia Dinh Province intercepted enemy ships in the Co Phuong commune's Mang Luong Canal and intercepted enemy reinforcements in the Long Dat and Do Phu areas, annihilating 310 U.S. aggressors and sinking two warships.

	On the night of May 22, the people and armed forces of Tay Ninh Province attacked the enemy in and around the city of Chi Hoa, annihilating nearly 200 U.S. troops.

	HANOI, June 2 (Xinhua) — According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in the southern provinces of South Vietnam launched fierce attacks on U.S. and puppet troops, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy.

	From May 25 to 28, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Dinh Province launched two consecutive victories in the Dong Son area, 7 kilometers from the city of An Khe, annihilating a U.S. battalion and five companies, and destroying or damaging 42 tanks and armored vehicles.

	From May 3 to 10, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Ba Ria and Long An Provinces launched continuous attacks on the enemy in the provincial capital of Long An, five military outposts, and more than 20 county outposts, annihilating nearly 900 enemy troops, including more than 200 U.S. troops, and annihilating a puppet battalion and six companies.

	On the night of May 25, the Liberation Army in Long An Province attacked a U.S. parking lot in Thap Muoi village, destroying or damaging 25 tanks and armored vehicles and killing or wounding many enemy troops.

	On the morning of May 23, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Vinh Long Province ambushed the enemy in the Ba Trieu area, 4 kilometers west of Vinh Long City, killing or wounding more than 100 enemy troops and seizing a large amount of weapons and military supplies.

	On May 26, the Liberation Army in Tay Ninh Province launched a fierce attack on the U.S. troops stationed in the Dong Xuan area of Chau Duc County, annihilating two U.S. infantry companies.

### 南越承天——顺化地区军民连续猛攻敌人  人民解放武装力量在南部各省和西贡及其周围地区重创美伪军

1968-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    南越承天——顺化地区军民连续猛攻敌人
    人民解放武装力量在南部各省和西贡及其周围地区重创美伪军
    新华社河内二日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方承天—顺化地区军民，在五月十七日到二十三日期间，接连向敌人发动进攻，消灭美国侵略军一千九百多名和伪军两个连，击毁军车一百七十多辆，击毁大炮二十六门和直升飞机二十架，摧毁营房五百多座。
    五月二十日夜晚，人民解放武装力量同时袭击东多美军装甲车团基地、美国第一○一空降师指挥所和芙拜基地等美军据点，消灭美国侵略军一千六百五十多名，击毁军车一百二十多辆、击毁大炮二十六门和直升飞机二十架，摧毁营房五百多座。仅在东多美军装甲团基地，人民解放武装力量就消灭了美国侵略军一千零五十名，击毁军车一百零一辆。
    二十三日夜晚，人民解放武装力量袭击芙拜西南面嘉中村的敌军驻地，全歼伪军两个连，击毁军车十五辆。在这之前，人民解放武装力量在十七日到十九日三天中，接连在富汪县城、美蓝、左沙和延大等地区打击敌人，消灭美国侵略军二百七十五名，击毁坦克和装甲车三十五辆。
    另据报道，广治省北部人民解放武装力量二十八日和二十九日两天在育庙和犹灵两县袭击和伏击敌人，共消灭美伪军近三百名，其中包括美军近二百五十名。
    新华社河内二日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方人民解放武装力量从五月二十五日到二十九日期间，在西贡及其周围地区全歼和重创美伪军两个连，另歼敌五百多名，击沉战船四艘。
    五月二十五日和二十六日，解放军在西贡北面消灭伪海军陆战队二百四十名。二十七日夜间，解放军又在堤岸西南的安乐地区歼灭美军一个连。二十八日早晨，解放军在浙林地区猛烈截击伪海军陆战队，重创伪军一个连。
    二十九日，解放军袭击了西贡市第五区和第六区的许多敌军目标。解放军还猛烈炮轰了伪海军司令部，歼敌二百五十名，击沉战船四艘。三十日上午，解放军继续炮轰市中心的各个敌人目标。三十一日，解放军在富林、富定地区猛烈袭击了伪海军陆战队和伞兵。
    另据报道，西贡—嘉定地区人民解放武装力量五月十九日上午在西贡东南二十公里的东争河三岔口，接连三次袭击敌船，严重击伤敌人万吨军用运输船两艘和登陆艇一艘。嘉定省军民五月十七日和十八日还在协福乡芒路运河上截击敌军船队，并在龙得、多福地区截击前往增援的敌人，共消灭美国侵略军三百一十名，击沉战船两艘。
    堤岸省军民五月二十二日夜间进攻芹若县城及其周围地区的敌人，消灭美军近二百名。
    据新华社河内二日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方南部各省人民解放武装力量猛烈袭击美伪军，给予敌人重大的杀伤。
    边和省人民解放武装力量五月二十五日和二十八日接连在距离新渊县城七公里的同盏地区打了两个胜仗，共歼灭美国侵略军一个营和五个连，击毁击伤坦克和装甲车四十二辆。
    巴地、隆庆两省人民解放武装力量从五月三日到十日接连袭击了隆庆省会春禄市、五个军事支区和各县二十多个据点的敌人，共消灭敌人近九百名，其中包括美军二百多名，全歼伪军一个营和六个连。
    隆安省解放军五月二十五日夜间在上德立村袭击美军的停车场，击毁击伤坦克和装甲车二十五辆，打死打伤了许多敌人。
    金瓯省人民解放武装力量五月二十三日上午在金瓯市西南四公里的巴条地区伏击敌人，打死打伤敌人一百多名，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    土龙木省解放军五月二十六日猛烈袭击驻扎在周城县东站地区的美军，歼灭了美军两个步兵连。
