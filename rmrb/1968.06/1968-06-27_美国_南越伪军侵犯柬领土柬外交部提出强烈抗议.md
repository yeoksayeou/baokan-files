Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Forces Invade Cambodian Territory, Cambodian Foreign Ministry Lodges Strong Protest
Author:
Date: 1968-06-27
Edition: 6

According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry on June 25 sent separate notes to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, strongly protesting the deliberate invasions of Cambodian territory by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces on May 20, 26, and 28. These invasions resulted in the deaths of one Cambodian woman and injuries to two Cambodian civilians.

The Cambodian Foreign Ministry also sent notes on June 21 and 25 to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, strongly protesting the multiple incursions into Cambodian airspace by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet aircraft in May, which resulted in injuries to three Cambodian civilians.

### 美国—南越伪军侵犯柬领土柬外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军侵犯柬领土柬外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边二十六日电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨外交部六月二十五日分别照会美国政府和西贡傀儡当局，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军在五月二十日、二十六日和二十八日四次蓄意侵犯柬埔寨领土，并且打死一名柬埔寨妇女和打伤两名柬埔寨居民的新罪行。
    柬埔寨外交部在二十一日和二十五日还两次分别照会美国政府和西贡傀儡当局，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军飞机在五月份多次侵犯柬埔寨领空，并打伤三名柬埔寨居民的罪行。
