Title: Chinese Transportation Delegation Leaves Tirana to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1968-06-15
Edition: 5

The Chinese Transportation Delegation, after participating in the seventh session of the Management Committee of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Joint Venture Company in Albania, departed Tirana by plane on the 13th to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Albania's Deputy Minister of Transportation, Ramiz Leke, and the Albanian manager of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Joint Venture Company, Gjermal Klosi.

China's Chargé d'Affaires in Albania, Li Tingqian, also went to the airport to see the delegation off.

### 我交通代表团离地拉那回国

1968-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    我交通代表团离地拉那回国
    新华社地拉那十三日电　中国交通代表团，在阿尔巴尼亚参加中阿轮船股份公司第七次管理委员会会议后，已在十三日乘飞机离开地拉那回国。
    到机场欢送的有阿尔巴尼亚交通部副部长兰比·莱卡和中阿轮船股份公司阿尔巴尼亚方面经理格马尔·克洛西。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃也到机场欢送代表团。
