Title: Sudan Forms New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1968-06-12
Edition: 6

	Sudan Forms New Cabinet

	Xinhua News Agency reports from Khartoum: Recently, Ismail al-Azhari was re-elected as the Chairman of the Sudanese Supreme Council, and Ahmed al-Mahgoub was re-elected as the Prime Minister of Sudan.

	On June 2, Sudan formed a new cabinet. Mahgoub serves as both the Prime Minister and the Minister of Defense. Ali Abdel Rahman serves as both the Vice Prime Minister and the Minister of Foreign Affairs. Hassan Awadalla serves as the Minister of the Interior, and Sharif Hussein Hundi serves as the Minister of Finance and Economy.

### 苏丹成立新内阁

1968-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹成立新内阁
    新华社讯　喀土穆消息：最近，伊斯梅尔·阿扎里重新当选为苏丹国家最高委员会主席，艾哈迈德·马哈古卜重新当选为苏丹总理。
    六月二日，苏丹成立了新内阁。马哈古卜任总理兼国防部长，阿里·阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼任副总理兼外交部长，哈桑·阿瓦达拉任内政部长，沙里夫·侯赛因·欣迪任财政经济部长。
