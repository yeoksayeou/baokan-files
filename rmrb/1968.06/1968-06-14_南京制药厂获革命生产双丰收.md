Title: Nanjing Pharmaceutical Factory Achieves Double Harvest in Revolution and Production
Author:
Date: 1968-06-14
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of Nanjing Pharmaceutical Factory, under the guidance of Chairman Mao's latest instructions, have taken class struggle as the key link, vigorously carried out the revolution, and vigorously promoted production, achieving a double harvest in revolution and production. At present, all ten workshops and departments in the entire factory have established revolutionary three-in-one combined leadership teams. In terms of production, both output and quality have significantly increased; the total output value in April increased by 34 percent compared to March, and the output value in May increased by more than 60 percent compared to April. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 南京制药厂获革命生产双丰收

1968-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

    南京制药厂获革命生产双丰收
    南京制药厂广大革命职工在毛主席最新指示指引下，以阶级斗争为纲，狠抓革命，猛促生产，取得革命、生产双丰收。目前，全厂十个车间、部门，已经全部成立了革命三结合的领导班子。在生产方面，产量和质量显著上升；四月份总产值比三月份增长百分之三十四，五月份又比四月份增长了百分之六十以上。（据新华社讯）
