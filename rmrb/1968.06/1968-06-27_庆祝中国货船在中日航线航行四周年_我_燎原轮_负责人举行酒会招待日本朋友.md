Title: Celebrating the Fourth Anniversary of Chinese Cargo Ships on the China-Japan Route
Author:
Date: 1968-06-27
Edition: 5

The Chinese cargo ship "Liaoyuan" docked at Tokyo's Harumi Pier held a reception on the evening of the 24th, representing the China Ocean Shipping Company, to celebrate the fourth anniversary of Chinese cargo ships on the China-Japan route with Japanese friends from various circles.

	Japanese friends invited to the reception included Utsunomiya Tokuma, Miyazaki Seimin, Kenmoto Tomotaro, Itamoto Tokumatsu, Nishikawa Keibun, Matsuoka Yoko, Kawase Ikkan, and more than 70 others.

	The hosts and guests toasted to the long life of the great leader Chairman Mao.

	Utsunomiya Tokuma, Vice President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, said in his speech that Chairman Mao pointed out, "The Japanese people are a great people. They will never allow American imperialism to ride roughshod over them for long." "The Japanese people's desire for independence, democracy, peace, and neutrality will definitely be realized."

	Utsunomiya Tokuma pointed out that the obstacles to improving Sino-Japanese relations are not on the Chinese side but on the Japanese government.

	Miyazaki Seimin, Chairman of the Headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine), praised the great proletarian cultural revolution led by the great leader Chairman Mao, saying it is achieving comprehensive victory.

	He said that the revolutionary people of Japan will unite all possible forces to smash the Sato government's policy of following the United States and opposing China, to promote Sino-Japanese friendship and restore Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations.

	Hagiwara Saiji, Executive Director of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, representative of the Japanese shipping industry, Nakamura Takao, and Chairman of the Tokyo Chinese Association, Kam Man-fong, also spoke at the meeting.

	The political commissar of the Chinese "Liaoyuan," Cao Limin, spoke on behalf of the China Ocean Shipping Company, expressing gratitude to the broad Japanese friends and overseas Chinese in Japan for their enthusiastic assistance and care over the past four years.

	He said that all Chinese crew members are willing to make the greatest efforts to strengthen friendly trade exchanges between the peoples of China and Japan.

### 庆祝中国货船在中日航线航行四周年  我“燎原轮”负责人举行酒会招待日本朋友

1968-06-27
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国货船在中日航线航行四周年
    我“燎原轮”负责人举行酒会招待日本朋友
    据新华社东京二十五日电 正在东京晴海码头停泊的中国货轮“燎原轮”的负责人二十四日晚上，代表中国远洋运输公司，在船上举行酒会，与日本各界朋友共同庆祝中国货船在中日航线航行四周年。
    应邀出席酒会的日本朋友有宇都宫德马、宫崎世民、兼田富太郎、板本德松、西川景文、松冈洋子、川濑一贯等七十多人。
    宾主在酒会上不断举杯敬祝伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆。
    日本国际贸易促进协会副总裁宇都宫德马在讲话中说，毛主席指出：“日本民族是一个伟大的民族。它是绝不会让美帝国主义长期骑在自己头上的。”“日本人民要求独立、民主、和平、中立的愿望，一定能够实现。”
    宇都宫德马指出，改善日中关系的障碍不在中国方面，而在日本政府。
    日中友好协会（正统）总部理事长宫崎世民称赞中国人民在伟大领袖毛主席亲自领导下开展的伟大的无产阶级文化大革命正在取得全面胜利。
    他说，日本革命人民将团结一切可以团结的力量，为粉碎佐藤政府追随美国、敌视中国的政策，为促进日中友好、恢复日中邦交而斗争。
    日本国际贸易促进协会专务理事萩原定司、日本海运界代表上中龙男和东京华侨总会会长甘文芳也在会上讲了话。
    中国“燎原轮”政治委员曹立民在讲话中代表中国远洋运输公司对广大日本朋友和旅日华侨四年来对中国货船的热情协助和关怀表示感谢。
    他表示中国全体船员愿意为加强中日两国人民的友好贸易往来做出最大的努力。
