Premier Zhou Enters into Talks with the Military Delegation from Mali

	BEIJING, June 7 (Xinhua) - Premier Zhou Enlai today received the Minister-level Representative of the Republic of Mali responsible for defense and security, and the head of the Mali Military Delegation, Mamadou Diagouna, along with the entire delegation led by him. He had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

	Present at the reception were Comrades Huang Yongsheng, Xiao Jinguang, and Li Tianyou, among others.

	Moussa Coulibaly, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Malian Embassy in China, was also present.

### 周总理接见马里军事代表团

1968-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见马里军事代表团
    新华社七日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天接见了马里共和国负责国防和安全的部长级代表、马里军事代表团团长马马杜·迪亚基特，和由他率领的马里军事代表团的全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，黄永胜、萧劲光、李天佑等同志在座。
    马里驻中国大使馆临时代办姆萨·库里巴利也在座。
