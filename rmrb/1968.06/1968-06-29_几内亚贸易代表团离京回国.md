Title: Guinea Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1968-06-29
Edition: 6

A trade delegation from the Republic of Guinea, headed by the Director of the Domestic Trade Bureau of the Ministry of Trade, Transportation, and Posts and Telecommunications, Fofana Segou, departed Beijing by plane on the 26th and returned to their home country.

### 几内亚贸易代表团离京回国

1968-06-29
第6版()
专栏：

    几内亚贸易代表团离京回国
    新华社讯　以几内亚共和国贸易、交通、邮电部内贸局局长福法纳·塞古为首的几内亚贸易代表团，二十六日乘飞机离开北京回国。
