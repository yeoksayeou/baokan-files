Title: Xinhua News Agency and Nepal National News Agency Sign Agreement to Exchange News
Author:
Date: 1968-06-15
Edition: 5

	Xinhua News Agency and Nepal National News Agency
	Sign Agreement to Exchange News
	Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, June 13 — The Xinhua News Agency of the People's Republic of China and the Nepal National News Agency signed an agreement today in Kathmandu to exchange news.

	This agreement was signed after friendly consultations based on the principles of friendship, equality, and mutual benefit between the two countries.

	Representatives of the Xinhua News Agency and the Nepal National News Agency signed the agreement.

### 新华社和尼泊尔民族通讯社  签订交换新闻的协议

1968-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    新华社和尼泊尔民族通讯社
    签订交换新闻的协议
    新华社加德满都十三日电　中华人民共和国新华通讯社和尼泊尔王国民族通讯社今天在加德满都签订了交换新闻的协议。
    这项协议，是根据两国的友好关系和平等互利的原则进行友好协商以后签订的。
    新华通讯社和民族通讯社的代表分别在协议上签了字。
