Title: The Red Cross Society of China Offers Condolences and Donates Funds to Aid Iraqi Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1968-06-21
Edition: 5

According to Xinhua News Agency in Baghdad on the 19th, due to recent floods in Iraq, the Red Cross Society of China sent a telegram to the Iraqi Red Crescent Society expressing condolences and donated 30,000 yuan to aid the flood victims in Iraq. On the 15th, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Iraq, Wei Jianye, handed over the condolence telegram and the donation to the President of the Iraqi Red Crescent Society, Aladdin Kayat.

Wei Jianye said during the handover of the condolence telegram and the donation, "The common struggle against the number one enemy of the world's people, American imperialism, has united the Chinese people and the Arab people. He said that the Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, have always supported the just struggle of the Arab people, including the Iraqi people, against the aggression of American imperialism's tool, Israel."

Aladdin Kayat, on behalf of the Red Crescent Society and the Iraqi people, expressed heartfelt thanks to the Red Cross Society of China and the Chinese people for their sympathy and support for the Iraqi people.

### 中国红十字会  慰问并赠款救济伊拉克灾民

1968-06-21
第5版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会
    慰问并赠款救济伊拉克灾民
    据新华社巴格达十九日电　由于伊拉克最近发生水灾，中国红十字会致电伊拉克红新月会表示慰问，并捐赠人民币三万元救济伊拉克水灾灾民。十五日，中国驻伊拉克大使馆临时代办韦建业把慰问电和赠款交给伊拉克红新月会会长阿拉丁·卡亚特。
    韦建业在送交慰问电和赠款时说，反对世界人民的头号敌人美帝国主义的共同斗争，把中国人民和阿拉伯人民连结在一起。他说，以毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民，始终支持包括伊拉克人民在内的阿拉伯人民反对美帝国主义的侵略工具以色列的正义斗争。
    阿拉丁·卡亚特代表红新月会和伊拉克人民，对中国红十字会和中国人民同情和支持伊拉克人民表示衷心的感谢。
