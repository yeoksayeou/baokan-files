Title: China and Sudan Sign 1968 Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-06-22
Edition: 5

The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of the Sudan signed the 1968 Trade Agreement in Khartoum on June 20.

According to the agreement, the trade volume between the two countries in 1968 will increase compared to 1967. The main commodities China will purchase from Sudan include cotton and oilseeds. The main commodities Sudan will purchase from China include textiles, steel, pharmaceuticals, tea, and stationery.

### 中国和苏丹签订今年贸易议定书

1968-06-22
第5版()
专栏：

    中国和苏丹签订今年贸易议定书
    新华社喀土穆二十日电　中华人民共和国政府和苏丹共和国政府二十日在喀土穆签订了一九六八年贸易议定书。
    根据议定书，两国一九六八年贸易额比一九六七年有增加。中国方面向苏丹购买的主要商品是棉花和油料；苏丹方面向中国购买的主要商品有纺织品、钢材、医药、茶和文具等。
