Title: Albania and Congo (Brazzaville) Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1968-06-28
Edition: 6

Albania and Congo (Brazzaville) have decided to establish diplomatic relations.

	NEW YORK, June 27 (Xinhua) -- According to a report by the Albanian News Agency, the Government of the People's Republic of Albania and the Government of the Republic of Congo (Brazzaville) have agreed to further strengthen friendly relations between the two countries. Both sides have reached an agreement to establish diplomatic relations and to exchange ambassadors.

### 阿尔巴尼亚和刚果（布）决定建交

1968-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚和刚果（布）决定建交
    新华社地拉那二十七日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府和刚果（布）共和国政府为进一步加强两国之间的友好关系，双方达成协议，决定在两国之间建立外交关系，并互派大使级外交代表。
