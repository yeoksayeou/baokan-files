Title: Chairman Mao and His Close Comrade-in-Arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao Receive President Nyerere and Other Tanzanian Friends
Author:
Date: 1968-06-22
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao received President Nyerere and other Tanzanian friends.

Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao warmly shook hands with Tanzanian friends and had a friendly conversation. The Tanzanian friends applauded enthusiastically and paid tribute to Chairman Mao.

Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, Zhang Chunqiao, Yao Wenyuan, Huang Yongsheng, and Wu Faxian participated in the reception. Comrades Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Wen Yucheng also participated in the reception.

New China News Agency, June 21st: Our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao today received the President of the United Republic of Tanzania, Julius K. Nyerere, and his wife, along with other Tanzanian friends.

When President Nyerere and his wife arrived at the reception hall, Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Biao warmly shook hands with them and took a group photo with all Tanzanian friends. The Tanzanian friends who were received applauded enthusiastically and paid tribute to Chairman Mao. Chairman Mao also applauded continuously and waved to the Tanzanian friends in greeting.

Subsequently, Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Biao had a friendly conversation with President Nyerere and the Tanzanian officials who accompanied him, including the Deputy Minister of the Office of the Second Vice President, R. N. Sijona; the Deputy Minister of the Office of the President, A. M. Bok Mwanuka; the Deputy Minister in charge of foreign affairs, C. Y. Mgonja; the Ambassador of Tanzania to China, Paul Mwaluko; and the Brigadier General of the Tanzania People's Defense Force, Salakiqia.

The Tanzanian friends who were received also included: Mrs. Mgonja, Isa Mpoto, Namata and his wife, Juma Almasi, Ahmed Mahmoud, Paul Sozigwa, Shaydi, Mzena, Chand S. Ali, Colonel Chacha, Regarulira, Nonoli, Mwakipesi, Major Michael Indada, Marusi, Juma Mwapachu, Regasila, Asha Ngoma, Mrs. Mgaya, Msolomi, Butiku, Ngatwika, Bo M. Klemens, N. Omari, Mrs. Mwemu, Binvimbo, Fari, Kipanga, Kadabaro, and the entire Tanzanian Acrobatics Training Team currently in China.

Those who participated in the reception included leaders from various sectors of the Party and the state: Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, Zhang Chunqiao, Yao Wenyuan, Huang Yongsheng, and Wu Faxian.

Also participating in the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Vice Chief of Staff of the People's Liberation Army, Wen Yucheng.

Our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao received President Julius K. Nyerere of the United Republic of Tanzania and his wife, along with other Tanzanian friends on June 21st.

### 毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席接见尼雷尔总统及其他坦桑尼亚朋友

1968-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席接见尼雷尔总统及其他坦桑尼亚朋友
    同坦桑尼亚朋友热烈握手，并进行了亲切友好的谈话；坦桑尼亚朋友热烈鼓掌，向毛主席致意
    周恩来陈伯达康生江青张春桥姚文元黄永胜吴法宪同志参加接见。参加接见的还有陈毅李先念温玉成同志
    新华社二十一日讯　我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，今天接见了坦桑尼亚联合共和国总统朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔和夫人及其他坦桑尼亚朋友。
    当尼雷尔总统和夫人等来到接见厅时，毛主席和林彪副主席同他们热烈握手，并且同坦桑尼亚全体朋友一起照了像。被接见的坦桑尼亚朋友热烈鼓掌，向毛主席致意。毛主席也不断地鼓掌，向坦桑尼亚朋友招手致意。
    随后，毛主席和林彪副主席同尼雷尔总统以及随同来访的坦桑尼亚第二副总统办公室国务部长拉·南·西乔纳、总统办公室国务部长艾·姆·博克·穆南卡、主管外事的国务部长切·约·姆贡贾、坦桑尼亚驻中国大使保罗·姆瓦卢科、坦桑尼亚人民国防军准将萨拉基基亚，进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    被接见的坦桑尼亚朋友还有：姆贡贾夫人、伊萨·姆波托、纳马塔和夫人、朱马·阿尔马西、艾哈迈德·马哈茂德、保罗·索齐格瓦、沙伊迪、姆泽纳、昌德·斯·阿里、查查中校、雷加鲁利拉、诺诺利、姆瓦基佩西少校、迈克尔·印达达、马鲁西、朱马·姆瓦帕楚、雷加西拉、阿夏·恩戈马夫人、姆加亚、姆索洛米、布蒂库、恩加特威卡、博·姆·克莱门斯、尼·奥马里、姆韦穆女士、宾维姆波、法里、基潘加、卡达巴罗，以及正在中国的坦桑尼亚杂技学习团全体成员。
    参加接见的，有党和国家各方面的负责人周恩来、陈伯达、康生、江青、张春桥、姚文元、黄永胜和吴法宪同志。
    参加接见的，还有国务院副总理陈毅、李先念以及中国人民解放军副总参谋长温玉成。（附图片）
    我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，六月二十一日接见了坦桑尼亚联合共和国总统朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔和夫人及其他坦桑尼亚朋友。
