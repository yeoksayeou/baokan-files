Title: Chile Experiences Worst Drought in a Century
Author:
Date: 1968-06-24
Edition: 5

Chile has experienced the worst drought in a century, surpassing the severity of the 1924 drought. According to reports, the current water shortage in Chile averages 85%, with some regions experiencing a 100% shortage. Due to the severe drought, the Chilean government has acknowledged that the country is facing a "national disaster."

### 智利发生一百年来最大的旱灾

1968-06-24
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    智利发生一百年来最大的旱灾
    智利发生一百年来最大的一次旱灾，比一九二四年的旱灾还要严重。据报道，目前智利全国缺水平均达到百分之八十五，有些地区缺水达到百分之一百。由于严重的干旱，智利官方承认，智利正面临着“全国性的灾难”。
