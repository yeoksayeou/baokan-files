Title: The Friendship Road Cycling Race Begins
Author: Lǐ Jiāhǎo
Date: 1989-08-14
Page: 4

Newswire: Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 13th

The "Friendship Road" cycling race from Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia, to Irkutsk, Soviet Union, began today. The event is hosted by the Mongolian People's Republic. Three teams from the Soviet Union, two teams from Mongolia, and the Chinese national training team, totaling 35 athletes, are participating in the competition. Among them, the Chinese team has 5 athletes.

Today, the first stage of the 60-kilometer race was held. Tomorrow, the athletes will leave Ulaanbaatar and pass through Darchan, Mongolia, and Ulan-Ude, Soviet Union, arriving at the final destination, Irkutsk, on August 23rd. The total distance of the race is over 1,200 kilometers. The "Friendship Road" Ulaanbaatar-Irkutsk cycling race began in 1974 and has been held annually since then. Previously, only Mongolian and Soviet athletes participated. This year marks the first time Chinese athletes are taking part in the race.

### 友谊之路自行车赛开赛
李加好
1989-08-14
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    友谊之路自行车赛开赛
    新华社乌兰巴托8月13日电（记者李加好）由蒙古人民共和国主办的“友谊之路”蒙古乌兰巴托至苏联伊尔库茨克自行车赛今天在此开赛，苏联三支代表队、蒙古两个代表队和中国国家集训队共35名运动员参加比赛。其中中国队参赛的运动员有5人。
    今天举行了第一阶段60公里赛程的比赛，运动员明天将离开乌兰巴托，经过蒙古达尔汗市、苏联乌兰乌德市，于本月23日到达终点伊尔库茨克市，全部赛程为1200多公里。“友谊之路”乌兰巴托—伊尔库茨克自行车赛始于1974年，每年举行一次，以前只有蒙苏运动员参赛。中国运动员是第一次参加这一自行车赛。
