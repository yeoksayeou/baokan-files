Title: New Zealand Prime Minister Lange Resigns
Author:
Date: 1989-08-07
Edition: 3

New Zealand Prime Minister David Lange announced on August 7 in Wellington that he had submitted his resignation to the cabinet.

Lange said at a press conference that the reason for his resignation was that his doctors had advised him that continuing in the role of Prime Minister would be detrimental to his physical and mental health.

### 新西兰总理朗伊提出辞职

1989-08-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    新西兰总理朗伊提出辞职
    新西兰总理朗伊7日在惠灵顿说，他已向内阁提出辞职。
    朗伊在记者招待会上谈到他辞职原因时说，他的医生曾忠告他说，继续担任总理职务将不利于他的身心健康。
