Title: Work of Committees at the International Conference on the Cambodian Issue Concludes
Author: Yang Mu
Date: 1989-08-26
Edition: 3

The work of the committees at the Paris International Conference on the Cambodian Issue concluded tonight. The two co-chairs announced that the ministerial-level meeting before the conference's closure will be held as scheduled on the afternoon of the 28th.

This international conference opened on July 30. Starting from August 2, the four committees under the conference began their work. Three working committees and one special committee discussed specific topics related to the comprehensive political resolution of the Cambodian issue. Above the four committees, a coordination committee was established to synthesize and coordinate the results and disputed issues discussed by the four committees.

Over the past nearly three weeks, the four committees worked intensively, with heated debates and frequent bilateral or multilateral contacts. On the 25th, the three working committees held full-day meetings, while the special committee adjourned. After synthesizing the progress and disputed issues from the various committees, the coordination committee announced the conclusion of the committees' work at midnight that day and will present the results to the ministerial-level meeting for discussion.

### 柬问题国际会议各委员会工作结束  部长级会议将于二十八日举行
杨木
1989-08-26
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    柬问题国际会议各委员会工作结束
    部长级会议将于二十八日举行
    新华社巴黎8月25日电　（记者杨木）柬埔寨问题巴黎国际会议各委员会工作于今天夜间宣告结束。会议两主席宣布，本次国际会议闭幕前的部长级会议将按计划于28日下午举行。
    这次国际会议是7月30日开幕的。从8月2日开始，会议所属的四个委员会开始工作。三个工作委员会及一个特别委员会分别讨论与全面政治解决柬埔寨问题有关的专题。在四个委员会之上设立了一个协调委员会，负责综合并协调四个委员会讨论的结果和争议的问题。
    在过去近三个星期中，四个委员会工作紧张，争论激烈，双边或多边接触频繁。25日，三个工作委员会全天开会，特别委员会休会。协调委员会综合了各委员会工作进展情况及争议的问题后，于当天深夜宣告各委员会工作结束，并将把结果提请部长级会议讨论。
