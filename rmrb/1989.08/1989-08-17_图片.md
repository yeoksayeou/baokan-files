Title: The Manager of the Mudanjiang Commercial Building
Author: Xu Jianzhong
Date: 1989-08-17
Edition: 5

The general manager of the Mudanjiang Commercial Building in Heilongjiang Province and Communist Party member, Shan Chunliang (center), is skilled at using ideological and political work to drive business development. He educates sales staff to "be the masters of the building and the friends of the customers." The photo shows Shan Chunliang encouraging employees who have returned from winning the national "Zhongxing Cup" for large commercial enterprises to remain humble and strive for greater heights.
