Title: Major Traffic Accidents Increased Last Month
Author:
Date: 1989-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 8th.

According to the Ministry of Public Security, major traffic accidents occurred frequently nationwide in July. In the middle and latter half of the month, over 10 days saw 5 incidents of large passenger buses overturning, resulting in 78 deaths and 158 injuries.

Statistics show that in July, a total of 22,339 traffic accidents occurred on roads nationwide, causing 4,314 deaths, 14,149 injuries, and direct economic losses amounting to 31.24 million yuan.

### 上月交通特大恶性事故多

1989-08-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    上月交通特大恶性事故多
    据新华社北京8月8日电　据公安部介绍，7月份全国交通特大恶性事故突出。在中下旬10多天里，发生5起死亡10人以上的大客车翻车事故，共有78人死亡，158人受伤。
    据统计，7月份全国道路共发生交通事故22339起，死亡4314人，受伤14149人，直接经济损失3124万元。
