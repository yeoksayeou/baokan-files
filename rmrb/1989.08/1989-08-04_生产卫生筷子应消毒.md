Title: Disinfection Should Be Carried Out for the Production of Hygienic Chopsticks
Author: Zhao Jingcheng
Date: 1989-08-04
Page: 6

Suggestions and Demands

Disinfection Should Be Carried Out for the Production of Hygienic Chopsticks

Some manufacturers, when producing disposable hygienic chopsticks, do not have any hygiene management system in place. After the chopsticks are produced, they are not disinfected and are directly sent to the catering industry. In reality, some of these hygienic chopsticks have played a role in spreading bacteria.

	Qingdao Sawmill No. 2 Factory, Zhao Jingcheng

### 生产卫生筷子应消毒
赵竞成
1989-08-04
第6版(读者来信)
专栏：建议与要求

    生产卫生筷子应消毒
    有的厂家在生产一次性使用的卫生筷子时，并无卫生管理制度。筷子生产出来后，也不经过卫生消毒，就直接送往饮食行业。实际上，部分卫生筷子起了传播病菌的作用。
                            青岛锯材二厂  赵竞成
