Title: Portugal Experiences Unprecedented Heatwave in a Century
Author:
Date: 1989-08-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

Portugal has experienced an unprecedented heatwave this summer, with sustained dry heat severely disrupting the country's social and economic life. According to the Portuguese National Meteorological Service, the capital Lisbon, which is situated on the Atlantic coast, had an average temperature of 25.1 degrees Celsius in July, the highest since records began in 1856.

### 葡萄牙出现百年未遇高温

1989-08-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    葡萄牙出现百年未遇高温
    葡萄牙今年夏天出现了百年未遇的持续干热，严重干扰了该国社会经济生活。据葡萄牙国家气象台3日宣布，濒临大西洋的首都里斯本7月份平均气温为25.1摄氏度，是1856年有气象资料记载以来最高的。
          （据新华社）
