Title: The World Ocean Circulation Experiment Chinese Committee Established
Author: Jiang Jianke
Date: 1989-08-03
Edition: 2

The World Ocean Circulation Experiment Chinese Committee was established in Beijing today.

The World Ocean Circulation Experiment is a large-scale international collaborative investigation project initiated by the Intergovernmental Oceanographic Commission, the Scientific Committee on Oceanic Research, the World Meteorological Organization, and the International Council for Science. Starting from 1990, the project will conduct a decade-long global satellite observation to further understand the mechanisms of global ocean circulation changes and their impact on global climate.

The World Ocean Circulation Experiment Chinese Committee is composed of units including the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the State Education Commission, the Ministry of Communications, the State Meteorological Administration, and the State Oceanic Administration.

### 世界海洋环流实验中国委员会成立
蒋建科
1989-08-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    世界海洋环流实验中国委员会成立
    本报北京8月3日讯　记者蒋建科报道：世界海洋环流实验中国委员会今天在京成立。
    世界海洋环流实验是政府间海委会、海洋研究科学委员会、世界气象组织、国际科学联盟等国际组织发起的一项大规模国际合作调查计划。从1990年开始，进行为期10年的全球卫星观测，以进一步了解全球海洋环流变化及其对全球气候影响的机制。
    世界海洋环流实验中国委员会由中国科学院、国家教委、交通部、国家气象局、国家海洋局等单位组成。
