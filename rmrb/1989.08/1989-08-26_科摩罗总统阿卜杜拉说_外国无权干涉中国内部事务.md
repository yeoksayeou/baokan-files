Title: Comoros President Abdulla Says Foreign Countries Have No Right to Interfere in China's Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1989-08-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 26th.

Moroni, August 25th - Comoros President Abdulla stated today that China is a sovereign nation, and matters that occur in China are China's internal affairs. Foreign countries have no right to interfere.

Abdulla made these remarks after listening to the Chinese Ambassador to Comoros, Liu Qingyou, who briefed him on China's views on the international situation, China's foreign policy, and the situation in Beijing following the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riots. Abdulla said he is a friend of China, and the two countries respect each other and do not interfere in each other's internal affairs. He emphasized that the friendly cooperative relationship between the two countries should be further consolidated and developed.

On the same day, Comoros Minister of Foreign Affairs, Cooperation, and Foreign Trade, Kaffi, reaffirmed the Comoros government's desire to develop friendly relations with China during a meeting with Ambassador Liu Qingyou. Kaffi stated, "China and Comoros are friendly countries. We support you and will always stand by your side."

### 科摩罗总统阿卜杜拉说  外国无权干涉中国内部事务

1989-08-26
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    科摩罗总统阿卜杜拉说
    外国无权干涉中国内部事务
    新华社北京8月26日电　莫罗尼消息：科摩罗总统阿卜杜拉25日在莫罗尼说，中国是一个主权国家，中国发生的事情是中国的内部事务，外国无权干涉。
    阿卜杜拉是在听取中国驻科摩罗大使刘庆有介绍中国对国际形势的看法和中国的对外政策以及北京平息反革命暴乱情况后说这番话的。他说，他是中国的朋友，科中两国相互尊重，互不干涉内政，我们之间的友好合作关系要继续巩固和发展。
    同一天，科摩罗外交、合作和外贸部长卡菲在会见刘庆有大使时重申了科摩罗政府发展科中友好关系的愿望。他说，科中是友好的国家，我们支持你们，永远站在你们一边。
