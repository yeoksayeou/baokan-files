Title: Some Members of the Hong Kong Basic Law Consultative Committee Return to Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1989-08-17
Edition: 2

Some members of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region Basic Law Consultative Committee, including Deputy Chairman Lo Tak-shing, Kwok Chiu-hwa, and Kwok Chi-kwan, as well as part of the Executive Committee, a total of 9 people, ended their two-day visit to Beijing today and returned to Hong Kong by plane this afternoon.

During their stay in Beijing, Qi Pengfei, Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office and Chairman of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region Basic Law Drafting Committee, met and hosted a dinner for Lo Tak-shing and his delegation.

Vice Directors Li Hou and Lu Ping of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office, along with some mainland drafting committee members, held a discussion with the consultative committee members. They exchanged views on the work of soliciting opinions on the draft Basic Law in Hong Kong.

### 香港基本法咨委会部分委员返港

1989-08-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    香港基本法咨委会部分委员返港
    新华社北京8月17日电　香港特别行政区基本法咨询委员会副主任委员罗德丞、高苕华、郭志权及部分执行委员一行9人今天结束了为期两天的北京访问，于下午乘飞机返回香港。
    在京期间，国务院港澳办公室主任、香港特别行政区基本法起草委员会主任委员姬鹏飞会见并宴请了罗德丞一行。
    国务院港澳办公室副主任李后、鲁平及部分内地起草委员同咨询委员会的朋友们进行了座谈，就基本法（草案）在香港征求意见的工作交换了意见。
