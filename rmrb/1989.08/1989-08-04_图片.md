Title: Colombia's National Art Restoration Center
Author: Liu Zhongyang
Date: 1989-08-04
Edition: 7

The Colombia National Art Restoration Center was established in 1974 and is the first art "clinic" in Latin America. Over the past 15 years, it has restored more than 1,000 precious artworks. In 1981, the center also established a school for the protection, restoration, and museum studies, training professional personnel. The image shows staff members restoring an ancient masterpiece.

	New China News Agency reporter Liu Zhongyang
