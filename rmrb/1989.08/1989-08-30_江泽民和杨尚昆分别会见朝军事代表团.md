Title: Jiang Zemin and Yang Shangkun Meet with North Korean Military Delegation
Author: Yang Zi Di
Date: 1989-08-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 30th.

General Secretary of the Communist Party of China, Jiang Zemin, this afternoon in Zhongnanhai met with the North Korean military delegation led by Marshal Choi Kwang, a candidate member of the Political Bureau of the Workers' Party of Korea and Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army.

Jiang Zemin told Choi Kwang that Chairman Kim Il-sung, the Workers' Party of Korea, and the government firmly supported China in suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion. He expressed his gratitude for this.

He said that the lessons from the recent turmoil and rebellion in China are that we must resolutely oppose bourgeois liberalization in the future.

He said that the Chinese and North Korean peoples have a fraternal friendship forged in blood. China will continue to develop this friendship in the future.

Choi Kwang conveyed the greetings of Chairman Kim Il-sung and Kim Jong-il to Jiang Zemin. He once again conveyed the congratulations and support of the Workers' Party of Korea, the government, and Chairman Kim Il-sung for China's victory in suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion.

Jiang Zemin also asked Choi Kwang to convey his greetings to Chairman Kim Il-sung and Comrade Kim Jong-il.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 30th.

President Yang Shangkun this evening met and hosted a banquet for the North Korean military delegation led by Marshal Choi Kwang, a candidate member of the Political Bureau of the Workers' Party of Korea and Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

Vice Secretary of the Central Military Commission Hong Xuezhi, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army Xiong Guangkai, Deputy Chief of the General Staff Xu Huizhi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhu Qizhen, and North Korean Ambassador to China Zhu Changjun attended the meeting and banquet.

### 江泽民和杨尚昆分别会见朝军事代表团
杨子迪
1989-08-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民和杨尚昆分别会见朝军事代表团
    新华社北京8月30日电　（记者杨子迪）中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在中南海会见了由朝鲜劳动党政治局候补委员、朝鲜人民军总参谋长崔光大将率领的朝鲜军事代表团。
    江泽民对崔光大将说，金日成主席、朝鲜劳动党和政府对中国平息反革命暴乱给予了坚决支持。他对此表示感谢。
    他说，前一段时间的动乱和暴乱给中国的教训就是：今后要坚决反对资产阶级自由化。
    他说，中朝两国人民有着鲜血凝成的战斗友谊。中国今后要继续发展这种友谊。
    崔光大将向江泽民转达了金日成主席和金正日对他的问候。他再次转达了朝鲜劳动党、政府和金日成主席对中国平息反革命暴乱所取得的胜利表示祝贺和支持。
    江泽民也请崔光大将转达他对金日成主席和金正日同志的问候。
    新华社北京8月30日电　国家主席杨尚昆今晚在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请由朝鲜劳动党政治局候补委员、朝鲜人民军总参谋长崔光大将率领的朝鲜军事代表团。
    中央军委副秘书长洪学智、中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田、副总参谋长徐惠滋、外交部副部长朱启祯及朝鲜驻华大使朱昌骏参加了会见和晚宴。
