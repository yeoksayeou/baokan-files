Title: Anhui Dissolves 21 Provincial Companies
Author: Gongs Shubin
Date: 1989-08-06
Edition: 4

This year, Anhui has legally dissolved 21 companies established by provincial-level bureaus and departments, and all party and government officials (including retired officials) who held concurrent positions or jobs in these companies have withdrawn.

Among the dissolved companies, those with illegal business operations have been or are being legally investigated and handled.

### 安徽撤销二十一家省办公司
龚舒斌
1989-08-06
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    安徽撤销二十一家省办公司
    本报讯　今年以来，安徽依法撤销了二十一家省直厅局兴办的流通性公司，在这些公司中兼职、任职的党政机关干部（包括离退休干部）已全部退出。
    在被撤销的公司中有违法经营问题的，有的已依法作了处理，有的正在调查处理中。
                          （龚舒斌）
