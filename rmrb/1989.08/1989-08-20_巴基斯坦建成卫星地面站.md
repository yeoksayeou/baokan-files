Title: Pakistan Completes Satellite Ground Station
Author:
Date: 1989-08-20
Page: 3

Pakistan has completed its satellite ground station. On the 17th, Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto presided over the inauguration ceremony. An official from the Pakistan Space and Upper Atmosphere Research Commission revealed that Pakistan's first artificial satellite is ready. If negotiations regarding its launch are successful, it will be sent into space.

### 巴基斯坦建成卫星地面站

1989-08-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    巴基斯坦建成卫星地面站
    巴基斯坦总理贝·布托17日主持了巴卫星地面站的启用仪式。一位巴外层空间研究委员会官员透露，巴第一颗人造卫星已经准备好，如果有关发射的谈判成功即可将它送入太空。
