Title: Hezbollah Claims Attack on French Warship, EEC Delegation Visits Lebanon, Soviet Envoy in Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1989-08-25
Edition: 3

Hezbollah Claims Attack on French Warship

BEIRUT, Aug. 24 (Xinhua) -- The Iranian-backed Hezbollah today said it would attack French warships near the Lebanese coast.

Hezbollah leader Abbas Musawi told reporters after meeting 150 Shiite Muslim leaders in Sur that, "Our battle will shock everyone."

Yesterday, Shiite militia leader Berri said that if French warships approached Lebanese waters, Muslim forces would shell them. He said, "We do not want to fight with France, but we must defend our dignity and homeland."

EEC Delegation Visits Lebanon

PARIS, Aug. 24 (Xinhua) -- The French Foreign Ministry announced that an EEC delegation left Paris today for Lebanon to assess the needs of the residents and provide humanitarian aid to Lebanon.

The delegation, led by French Ambassador to Lebanon Rene Ara, includes eight representatives from Spain, Italy, Belgium, Greece, and the European Commission.

The delegation will contact both sides of the conflict in Lebanon to provide aid to all Lebanese people.

Soviet Envoy in Lebanon

BEIRUT, Aug. 24 (Xinhua) -- Soviet envoy, Deputy Foreign Minister for Middle East Affairs Tarasov, is currently visiting Beirut, seeking ways to end the current fighting in Lebanon.

### 黎真主党声言袭击法国军舰  欧共体派团赴黎考察  苏联特使访问黎巴嫩

1989-08-25
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    黎真主党声言袭击法国军舰
    欧共体派团赴黎考察  苏联特使访问黎巴嫩
    新华社贝鲁特8月24日电  伊朗支持的真主党今天说，它将袭击靠近黎巴嫩海岸的法国军舰。
    真主党领导人阿巴斯·穆萨维在苏尔会见150名什叶派穆斯林领导人后对记者说，“我们的战斗将使所有的人感到震惊。”
    昨天，什叶派民兵领导人贝里说，如果法国军舰靠近黎巴嫩海面，穆斯林部队将予以炮击。他说，“我们不愿同法国发生冲突，但是我们必须保卫我们的尊严和祖国。”
    新华社巴黎8月24日电  法国外交部宣布，欧洲共同体的一个代表团24日离开巴黎，前往黎巴嫩考察居民需要，以便向黎巴嫩提供人道主义援助。
    代表团由法国驻黎巴嫩大使勒内·阿拉率领，由来自西班牙、意大利、比利时、希腊及欧洲委员会的8名代表组成。
    代表团将与黎交战双方接触，以便向所有的黎巴嫩人提供援助。
    新华社贝鲁特8月24日电  苏联特使、外交部中东司司长塔拉索夫目前正在贝鲁特访问，寻求结束当前黎巴嫩战火的途径。
