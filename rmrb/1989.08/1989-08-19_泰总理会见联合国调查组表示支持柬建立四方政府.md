Title: Thai Prime Minister Meets UN Investigation Team, Expresses Support for the Establishment of a Quadripartite Government in Cambodia
Author: Liuaicheng
Date: 1989-08-19
Edition: 3

    Thai Prime Minister Chatchai Chunhawan met today with a United Nations investigation team led by Major General Martin Weidegat in Bangkok. He stated that Thailand will continue to support the peaceful resolution of the Cambodian issue. He hopes that the four factions in Cambodia will discuss and resolve issues on an equal footing. Regardless, the four factions are all Cambodians, and their status should be equal. Thailand and ASEAN support a quadripartite government led by Prince Sihanouk.

    Major General Martin Weidegat briefly introduced the activities of the United Nations investigation team over the past ten days in Cambodia and along the Thai-Cambodian border to Prime Minister Chatchai.

    He said that an important aspect of their investigation in Cambodia this time is to understand the road and transportation infrastructure in Cambodia.

### 泰总理会见联合国调查组表示支持柬建立四方政府
刘爱成
1989-08-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    泰总理会见联合国调查组表示支持柬建立四方政府
    本报曼谷8月18日电　记者刘爱成报道，泰国总理差猜·春哈旺今天在这里会见由马丁·韦德塞特中将率领的联合国赴柬埔寨实地调查组时说，泰国将继续支持柬问题的和平解决。希望柬四方在平起平坐的基础上共同讨论和解决问题。不管怎么说，柬四方都是柬埔寨人，四方的地位应相等。泰国和东盟支持西哈努克亲王为领导的四方联合政府。
    马丁·韦德塞特中将向差猜总理简单介绍了联合国调查组十多天来在柬埔寨及泰柬边境的活动情况。
    他说，他们这次赴柬调查的一个重要内容是了解柬埔寨的道路及交通基础设施情况。
