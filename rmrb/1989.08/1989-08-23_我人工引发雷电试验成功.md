Title: China Successfully Conducts Artificial Lightning Strike Experiment
Author: Chen Huiming
Date: 1989-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, August 23rd

Researchers from the Lanzhou Institute of Plateau Atmospheric Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, recently successfully conducted two artificial lightning strike experiments using rocket-triggered lightning technology.

Lightning is a frequent natural discharge phenomenon in the atmosphere that occurs over long distances and with large currents. Lightning can destroy power facilities and electronic instruments, cause fires, and the recent explosion of oil tanks in Shandong's Huangdao due to a lightning strike is a case in point.

Artificial lightning strike refers to the use of certain devices and facilities to induce lightning in an environment with thunderstorm electricity, thereby making the occurrence of lightning controllable in time and space, achieving the goals of lightning protection, avoidance, and damage reduction. The Lanzhou Institute of Plateau Atmospheric Physics, in collaboration with other research institutions, developed a type of lightning-triggering rocket with a fine steel wire trailing from its tail. During the rocket's ascent, the fine steel wire will induce lightning.

On July 12th and July 18th, researchers conducted two lightning-triggering experiments near Lanzhou. When the rocket reached a height of 400 meters above the ground, it triggered intense lightning.

### 我人工引发雷电试验成功
陈惠明
1989-08-23
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    我人工引发雷电试验成功
    据新华社兰州8月23日电　（记者陈惠明）中国科学院兰州高原大气物理研究所的科研人员，最近成功地运用火箭导线技术进行了两次人工引发雷电试验。
    雷电是大气层中发生频繁的一种超长距离、超大流量的自然放电现象。雷电会击毁电力设施、电子仪器，引起火灾，山东黄岛油罐前不久因雷击爆炸就是一例。
    人工引发雷电是指在存在雷暴电的环境下，利用一定的装置和设施诱发雷电，从而使雷电的发生由不可控制变成在时间和空间上可以控制，达到防雷、避雷、减轻损害的目的。兰州高原大气物理研究所与其它科研单位合作，研制出一种尾部拖带细钢丝的引雷火箭。在火箭升空过程中，细钢丝会引发雷电。
    7月12日和7月18日，科研人员在兰州附近进行了两次引雷试验，当火箭升空到离地面400米的高度时，引发了强烈的雷电。
