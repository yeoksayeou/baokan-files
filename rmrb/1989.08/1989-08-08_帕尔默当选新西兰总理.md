Title: Palmer Elected New Zealand Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-08-08
Edition: 1

New Zealand's former deputy prime minister Geoffrey Palmer was elected prime minister at a special meeting of the governing Labour Party's parliamentary decision-making committee on August 8.

Former Minister of Health and Housing Helen Clark was elected as New Zealand's deputy prime minister.

Palmer and Clark are also the chairman and deputy chairman of the Labour Party, respectively.

New Zealand's former prime minister David Lange announced his resignation yesterday.

### 帕尔默当选新西兰总理

1989-08-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    帕尔默当选新西兰总理
    新华社惠灵顿8月8日电　新西兰前副总理杰弗里·帕尔默8日在执政的工党议会决策委员会特别会议上当选为总理。
    前卫生和住房部部长海伦·克拉克当选为新西兰副总理。
    帕尔默和克拉克也分别是工党的主席和副主席。
    新西兰前总理戴卫·朗伊昨天宣布辞职。
