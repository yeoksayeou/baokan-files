Title: Raising Northern Fish in the South
Author: Wang Dong
Date: 1989-08-11
Edition: 2

Rainbow trout is a cold-water fish species.

To develop the cold-water resources of the south, Jian De County in Zhejiang Province has successfully raised rainbow trout in net cages in the cold-water area of the Xin'anjiang Hydropower Station. This year, it is expected to produce 35,000 kilograms of fish.
