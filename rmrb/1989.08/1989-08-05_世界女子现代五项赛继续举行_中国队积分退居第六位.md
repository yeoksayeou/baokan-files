Title: The Ninth World Women's Modern Pentathlon Championship Continues; China's Team Drops to Sixth Place
Author:
Date: 1989-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, August 4th

The ninth World Women's Modern Pentathlon Championship continued today with swimming and shooting events. West Germany surged ahead with a total of 9011 points, temporarily leading the competition. China's team, with 8809 points, dropped to sixth place.

In the morning's swimming event, Hungary's Ilona Kovacs completed the 200-meter course in 2 minutes and 16.73 seconds, securing first place. China's Liu Yan finished in 2 minutes and 22.97 seconds, placing tenth. In the afternoon's shooting event, China's Zhang Limin ranked 14th. The shooting event's first place was taken by Hungary's Csilla Temesi.

The team standings after the two events are as follows: Hungary (8994 points) is in second place, France (8994 points) is in third, Poland (8975 points) is in fourth, and the United States (8848 points) is in fifth.

After three events, the individual leader is the Soviet Union's Tatyana Chernetskaya with 3162 points. France's Caroline Delemer is in second place with 3128 points, and Hungary's Ilona Kovacs is in third with 3105 points. China's Liu Yan is in ninth place with 3019 points.

### 世界女子现代五项赛继续举行  中国队积分退居第六位

1989-08-05
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    世界女子现代五项赛继续举行
    中国队积分退居第六位
    新华社维也纳8月4日电  第九届世界女子现代五项锦标赛今天进行了游泳和射击两个项目的比赛，联邦德国后来居上，以积9011分暂时领先。中国队积分8809分，退居第六位。
    在今天上午举行的游泳比赛中，匈牙利队的伊伦·科瓦奇以2分16秒73的成绩游完200米全程，获得了第一名。中国队的刘燕以2分22秒97获得第十名。在下午的射击比赛中，中国的张丽敏获得第十四名。射击赛第一名被匈牙利选手泰梅西·奇里斯塔夺得。
    团体总分居第二位的是匈牙利队（8994分），法国队（8994分）、波兰队（8975分）、美国队（8848分）分列第三至第五位。
    在已进行的3个项目比赛中，个人成绩最好的是苏联的塔季阿娜·切尔涅茨卡娅，她的积分是3162分。暂列第二名是法国的卡罗利娜·德莱默尔，积3128分。匈牙利的伊伦·科瓦奇列第三位，积3105分。中国的刘燕名列第九，积3019分。
