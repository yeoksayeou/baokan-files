Title: UN Secretary-General Calls for Israel to Revoke Deportation Order
Author:
Date: 1989-08-29
Edition: 3

The United Nations Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar issued a statement on August 28, calling on the Israeli occupying authorities to fulfill their obligations under the Fourth Geneva Convention on the protection of civilians in occupied territories and to revoke the order to deport five Palestinians. Pérez de Cuéllar fervently hopes that all Palestinians who have been deported by the Israeli occupying authorities under similar circumstances will be allowed to return to their homes and reunite with their families.

### 联合国秘书长发表声明  呼吁以色列撤销驱逐令

1989-08-29
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    联合国秘书长发表声明  呼吁以色列撤销驱逐令
    联合国秘书长德奎利亚尔28日发表声明，呼吁以色列占领当局，遵守关于保护占领区平民的《第4日内瓦公约》规定的义务，撤销驱逐5名巴勒斯坦人出境的命令。德奎利亚尔热烈希望所有在类似情况下被以色列占领当局驱逐出境的巴勒斯坦人都被允许返回家园与家人团聚。　（本报专电）
