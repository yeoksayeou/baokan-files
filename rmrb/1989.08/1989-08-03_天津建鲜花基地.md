Title: Tianjin Establishes Fresh Flower Base
Author: Liang Xiaqin
Date: 1989-08-03
Edition: 2

After two years of construction, Tianjin's fresh flower base with an annual production capacity of 100,000 stems has been completed.

This flower base is jointly invested by the Ministry of Agriculture and Tianjin Municipality to meet the needs of Tianjin's joint ventures, hotels, and the tourism industry. During this year's National Day holiday, they will release 18,000 stems of fresh flowers to the market.

### 天津建鲜花基地
梁小琴
1989-08-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    天津建鲜花基地
    本报天津8月3日电  记者梁小琴报道：经过两年的建设，年产10万枝鲜花的天津市鲜花基地日前建成。
    这个鲜花基地由农业部和天津市共同投资兴建，以满足天津市三资企业、合资饭店和旅游业的需要。今年国庆期间，他们将向市场投放1.8万株鲜花。
