Title: Results of the "Youth Hot Topics" Essay Contest Announced
Author:
Date: 1989-08-10
Edition: 5

The Communist Youth League Central Committee, Huabei Pharmaceutical Factory, and this newspaper jointly organized the "Youth Hot Topics" essay contest, which concluded on June 26.

This essay contest received extensive support and attention from young people across the country. Over four months, more than 4,000 submissions were received. We would like to express our heartfelt thanks to all participants!

After evaluation by the judges, 16 outstanding works were selected, including three first prizes, five second prizes, and eight third prizes. The winning essays are as follows:

First Prizes:

* "The 'Soft Bone Disease' of Contemporary Youth" (1989.4.3; Author: Wang Yaping, Qingdao Municipal Bureau of Light Industry)
* "Confusion Over Spiritual Nourishment" (1989.5.29; Author: Wang Dantiger, a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in Dandong, Liaoning)
* "Facing the Dropped Coin..." (1989.4.10; Author: Wang Xiaolei, a ninth-grade female student at the Affiliated Middle School of Inner Mongolia Normal University)

Second Prizes:

* "Beauty Belonging Only to Women" (1989.6.5; Author: Xia Yan, Journalism Department, Nanjing Political Academy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army)
* "What Will Happen in the Future?" (1989.6.5; Author: Pei Haisheng, a second-year student at Shayan County Middle School, Jiangsu Province)
* "Can We Change Our Way of Living?" (1989.5.5; Author: Liu Yansong, Nanzhong County Hospital of Traditional Chinese Medicine, Shaanxi Province)
* "On the 'Spirit of Lei Feng'" (1989.5.5; Author: Wang Chaohai, Political Department of Xinjiang Military Region)
* "The 'Soldier' Talks About Being Poor" (1989.5.15; Author: Yan Zebo, Political Department of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in Anyang, Henan)

Third Prizes:

* "Hope or Worry About Dropping Out to Start a Business?" (1989.3.24; Author: Zhao Aijing, Taihang Instrument Factory, Taiyuan)
* "Opportunity" (1989.4.28; Author: Hou Mingzhu, Party History Working Committee of Suhong County, Jiangsu)
* "Three Hotspots in College Graduates' Job Selection" (1989.3.3; Authors: Su Li and Wang Jianzhong, Research Office of the Communist Youth League Central Committee)
* "Following What?" (1989.4.28; Author: Peng Yonghui, a policeman at Mailbox 75 in Guiyang, Hunan)
* "Come Back, 'Comrade'!" (1989.4.17; Author: Tianran, Office of the County Committee of Zherong County, Fujian)
* "Can We Have Our Own Time?" (1989.5.5; Author: Zhang Lihong, Huabei Pharmaceutical Factory)
* "Possessing Oneself" (1989.5.5; Author: Ben Xiangjun, Zhoujiazhuang Village, Shima Bay, Suburban District, Taian City, Shandong)
* "Confusion Over the 'Leap' in Professional Titles" (1989.3.17; Author: Yang Shusen, Textile Factory of Shayan County, Jiangsu)

People's Daily Legal Department
August 10, 1989

### 《青年热门话题》征文评选揭晓

1989-08-10
第5版(国内专页（政治·法律）)
专栏：

    《青年热门话题》征文评选揭晓
    共青团中央、华北制药厂与本报联合主办的《青年热门话题》有奖征文活动，已于6月26日结束。
    这次征文活动，得到了全国各界青年的广泛支持和关注，4个月中，共收到来稿4000多件，在此，对应征者表示衷心感谢！
    经评委评定，共评出优秀作品16篇，其中一等奖三篇，二等奖五篇，三等奖八篇。它们是：
    一等奖：
    《当代青年的“软骨病”》（1989．4.3；作者，青岛市二轻办王亚平。）
    《对精神食粮的惶惑》（1989.5．29；作者，辽宁丹东驻军某部王殿虎。）
    《面对遗落的硬币……》（1989．4.10；作者，内蒙古师大附中初三女学生王小蕾。）
    二等奖：
    《只属于女人的美》。（1989.6．5；作者，解放军南京政治学院新闻系夏燕。）
    《将来怎么办？》（1989.6.5；作者，江苏省射阳县中学初二学生裴海生。）
    《“换个活法”行吗？》（1989.5．5；作者，陕西省南郑县中医院刘岩松。）
    《关于“雷锋精神”》（1989.5.5；作者，新疆军区政治部王超海。）
    《“当兵的”说“穷”》（1989.5．15；作者，河南安阳驻军某部政治处严泽波。）
    三等奖：
  《弃学办实业希望？忧虑？》（1989．3.24；作者，太原市太行仪表厂赵爱晶。）
    《机会》（1989．4.28；作者，江苏泗洪县党史工作委员会侯明铎。）
    《大学生择业的三个热点》（1989．3.3；作者，共青团中央研究室苏丽、王建中。）
    《跟着什么走？》（1989.4.28；作者，湖南桂阳75信箱驻警彭勇辉。）
    《归来吧，“同志”！》（1989.4．17；作者，福建省柘荣县委办公室天然。）
    《能有自己的时间吗？》（1989．5.5；作者，华北制药厂张丽虹。）
    《拥有自己》（1989.5.5；作者，山东省泰安市郊区仕马湾乡周家庄村本祥俊。）
    《职称“跃进”的困惑》（1989．3.17；作者，江苏省射阳县纺织厂杨树森。）
