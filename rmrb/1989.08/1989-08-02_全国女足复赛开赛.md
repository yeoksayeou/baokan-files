Title: National Women's Football League Semi-Finals Begin
Author: Yuán Wénzhōng Yuán Hóngbīn
Date: 1989-08-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, August 1st

The 1989 National Women's Football Championship semi-finals in the Jinan zone began tonight with a match here. The result was the Shandong Qingdao Red Star Appliance Factory team defeating the Guangxi Beihai Guinan Beverage Factory team 3-2.

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, August 1st

The semi-final matches for the National Women's Football Championship in the Dalian zone started this afternoon in Lushun.

Teams participating in the Dalian zone include those from Changchun, Yanbian, Taiyuan, and Dalian. In today's first round of matches, Changchun and Dalian teams defeated Yanbian and Taiyuan teams 4-0 and 3-0, respectively.

### 全国女足复赛开赛
袁文忠  元洪斌
1989-08-02
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    全国女足复赛开赛
    新华社济南8月1日电　（记者袁文忠）1989年全国女子足球锦标赛复赛济南赛区今晚在这里进行了首场比赛，结果山东青岛红星电器总厂队以3∶2战胜广西北海桂南饮料厂队。
    新华社大连8月1日电　（通讯员元洪斌）全国女子足球锦标赛复赛大连赛区的比赛今天下午在旅顺开赛。
    参加大连赛区比赛的有长春、延边、太原、大连4支球队。在今天进行的第一轮比赛中，长春队和大连队分别以4∶0与3∶0战胜延边队和太原队。
