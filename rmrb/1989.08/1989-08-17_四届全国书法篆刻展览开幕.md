Title: The Fourth National Calligraphy and Seal Cutting Exhibition Opens
Author: Lǐ Guāng Rú
Date: 1989-08-17
Edition: 4

A large number of visitors were drawn to the China Art Museum today to view the Fourth National Calligraphy and Seal Cutting Exhibition. The 594 works on display include traditional styles such as seal script, cursive script, clerical script, and regular script, as well as many innovative pieces. This exhibition also awarded 50 winning works.

### 四届全国书法篆刻展览开幕
李光茹
1989-08-17
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    四届全国书法篆刻展览开幕
    据新华社北京8月17日电　（记者李光茹）众多的参观者今天被吸引到中国美术馆，争相观看第四届全国书法篆刻作品展览。呈现在观众面前的594件作品，既有传统的真、草、隶、篆等书体，也有不少创新之作。这次展览还评出获奖作品50幅。
