Title: Ink Finger Painting Exhibition in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-08-27
Edition: 2

River North painter Zhang Zichun's ink finger painting works will be exhibited from August 23 to 28 at the Chinese Painting Research Institute in Beijing.

Zhang Zichun, who is 61 years old this year, is one of the few painters in the country who are proficient in using fingers instead of brushes for Chinese painting. His finger paintings, with a free and unrestrained style, have gained fame both at home and abroad.

### 指墨作品在京展出

1989-08-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    指墨作品在京展出
    本报讯　河北画家张子春的指墨作品，8月23日至28日在北京中国画研究院展出。
    今年61岁的张子春，是国内为数不多的擅长以指代笔的国画家之一。他的指画，风神洒落，远播海内外。
