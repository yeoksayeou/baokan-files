Jiang Zemin Meets with North Korean Guests, Both Sides Emphasize Developing Sino-North Korean Friendship

New China News Agency, Beijing, August 11 — CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin warmly received Kim Yong-chun, member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and head of the International Department of the Party Central Committee, this afternoon at Zhongnanhai.

Jiang Zemin said, "The Communist Party of China and the Workers' Party of Korea, and the two countries, have forged a deep friendship through long-term struggle. Developing friendly relations between China and North Korea is a fixed policy of our party and government. China will, as always, support the Korean people in their struggle for national independence and peaceful reunification under the leadership of Chairman Kim Il-sung."

Jiang Zemin also introduced to the guest the political and economic situation in China, especially the main spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee and the recent full meeting of the Politburo.

Kim Yong-chun expressed during the meeting that the Workers' Party of Korea and the government firmly support the measures taken by the Chinese party and government to stop the turmoil and quell the riots. He praised the achievements made by the Chinese people in socialist construction since the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee. He reiterated that the Workers' Party of Korea will make unremitting efforts to consolidate and develop the friendship between the two countries.

Kim Yong-chun conveyed the warm greetings from Kim Il-sung and Kim Jong-il to the Chinese party and government leaders. Jiang Zemin also asked him to convey greetings from the Chinese leaders to Kim Il-sung and Kim Jong-il.

Department Head of the International Liaison Department Zhu Liang was present at the meeting.

The delegation, invited to visit, arrived in Beijing on August 4 and had visited Shanghai and Dalian. The North Korean guests are scheduled to leave Beijing tomorrow to return home.

### 江泽民昨会见朝鲜客人  宾主强调发展中朝友谊

1989-08-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民昨会见朝鲜客人  宾主强调发展中朝友谊
    新华社北京8月11日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在中南海亲切会见了朝鲜劳动党中央委员、党中央国际部长金容淳。
    江泽民说，“中朝两党、两国在长期的斗争中结下了深厚的友谊，发展中朝友好关系是我们党和政府的既定政策。中国将一如既往，支持朝鲜人民在金日成主席领导下争取自主和平统一祖国的斗争。”
    江泽民还向客人介绍了中国的政治、经济情况，特别是十三届四中全会和不久前举行的政治局全体会议的主要精神。
    金容淳在会见中表示，朝鲜劳动党和政府坚决支持中国党和政府为制止动乱和平息暴乱所采取的措施。他赞扬中国人民自十一届三中全会以来在社会主义建设中取得的成就。他重申，朝鲜劳动党将为巩固和发展朝中友谊做出不断的努力。
    金容淳向江泽民转达了金日成和金正日对中国党政领导人的亲切问候。江泽民也请他转达中国领导人对金日成、金正日的问候。
    中联部部长朱良会见时在座。
    金容淳一行应邀于8月4日抵达北京后，曾访问了上海、大连。朝鲜客人定于明天离开北京回国。
