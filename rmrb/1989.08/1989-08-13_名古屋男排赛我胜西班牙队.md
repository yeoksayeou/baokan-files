Title: China Men's Volleyball Team Defeats Spain in Nagoya
Author: Jiang Zhanguo
Date: 1989-08-13
Edition: 4

On the second day of the Nagoya International Men's Volleyball Tournament, the Chinese team defeated Spain with a score of 3:0.

The Chinese team played exceptionally well today, winning the first set 15:3 and then securing the next two sets with scores of 15:10 each.

In another match today, the Japanese team defeated India with a score of 3:1 (12:15, 15:8, 15:5, 15:8).

On the 11th, the Chinese team lost to India with a score of 2:3. Tomorrow, the Chinese team will face their final opponent, the Japanese team.

### 名古屋男排赛我胜西班牙队
姜占国
1989-08-13
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    名古屋男排赛我胜西班牙队
    新华社东京8月12日电　（记者姜占国）名古屋消息：在名古屋国际男子排球赛第二天的比赛中，中国队以3∶0战胜西班牙队。
    中国队今天打得很顺手，先以15∶3拿下第一局，接着以两个15∶10结束了比赛。
    今天另一场比赛的结果是日本队以3∶1（12∶15、15∶8、15∶5、15∶8）战胜印度队。
    在11日的首场比赛中，中国队以2∶3负于印度队。明天，中国队将与最后一个对手日本队交锋。
