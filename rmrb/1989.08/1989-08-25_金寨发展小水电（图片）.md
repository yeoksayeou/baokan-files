Title: Jinzhai Develops Small Hydropower
Author: ZHENG Guangyin
Date: 1989-08-25
Edition: 2

Jinzhai County in Anhui Province is one of the 100 pilot counties in China for rural electrification. Currently, the county has completed the construction of 87 small hydropower stations with a total installed capacity of 16,500 kilowatts. The electrification rate for townships and districts has reached 100%.

### 金寨发展小水电（图片）
郑光银
1989-08-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    金寨发展小水电
    安徽省金寨县，是我国100个农村电气化试点县之一。目前全县已建成小水电站87座，总装机达16500千瓦，区乡通电率达100％。　
      郑光银摄
