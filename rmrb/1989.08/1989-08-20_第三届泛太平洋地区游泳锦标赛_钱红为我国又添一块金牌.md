Title: China Adds Another Gold Medal at the Third Pan-Pacific Swimming Championships
Author: Jiang Zhan Guo
Date: 1989-08-20
Edition: 3

On the third day of the Third Pan-Pacific Swimming Championships, Chinese athletes secured one gold, one silver, and one bronze medal. The powerful American team continued to lead, winning 16 out of the 26 completed events.

In the women's 100-meter butterfly final, Qian Hong and Wang Xiaohong finished first and second with times of 1:00.45 and 1:00.81, respectively. The Chinese women's relay team, consisting of Zhuang Yong, Sun Chunli, Yang Wenyi, and Qian Hong, finished third in the 4x100-meter freestyle relay with a time of 3:47.97. In the men's 100-meter butterfly final, Shen Jianqiang finished fourth with a time of 54.47 seconds. So far, Chinese athletes have won a total of 2 gold, 4 silver, and 4 bronze medals.

Japanese swimmer Takako Kosaka set a new Asian record of 2:14.53 in the women's 200-meter backstroke preliminaries.

### 第三届泛太平洋地区游泳锦标赛  钱红为我国又添一块金牌
姜占国
1989-08-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    第三届泛太平洋地区游泳锦标赛
    钱红为我国又添一块金牌
    新华社东京8月19日电  （记者姜占国）我国选手在第三届泛太平洋地区游泳锦标赛第三天的比赛中，又获金、银、铜牌各一枚。实力雄厚的美国队继续独占鳌头，在已结束的26个项目中，共获得16枚金牌。
    在女子100米蝶泳决赛中，钱红和王晓红分别以1分0秒45和1分0秒81的成绩，分获冠、亚军。由庄泳、孙春莅、杨文意和钱红组成的中国女队以3分47秒97的成绩，获4×100米自由泳接力第三名。男选手沈坚强在男子100米蝶泳决赛中，以54秒47夺得第四名。到今天为止，我国运动员共获2枚金牌、4枚银牌和4枚铜牌。日本高濑千香子在女子200米仰泳预赛中，创造了2分14秒53的亚洲新纪录。
