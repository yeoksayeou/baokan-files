Title: The National Calligraphy and Painting Exhibition to Open
Author:
Date: 1989-08-14
Edition: 4

The "National Calligraphy and Painting Exhibition to Celebrate the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation" has been fully prepared and will open on September 1 at the Military Museum of the Chinese People's Revolution in Beijing.

This exhibition, organized by the Beijing Qingyun Calligraphy and Painting Association and the People's Daily Overseas Edition, among other units, received nearly 12,000 entries. Various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions enthusiastically submitted works, and Taiwan, Hong Kong, and other regions also sent in their entries. After evaluation by renowned calligraphers and painters, 5 first-prize works, 27 second-prize works, and 80 third-prize works were selected in both calligraphy and painting categories. The organizers have chosen nearly 400 outstanding calligraphy and painting works to be displayed in the exhibition.

### 《全国书画大联展》将展出

1989-08-14
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    《全国书画大联展》将展出
    新华社北京8月14日电　《庆祝建国40周年全国书画大联展》已筹备就绪，将于9月1日在北京中国人民革命军事博物馆展出。
    由北京卿云诗书画联谊社和人民日报海外版等单位主办的这届书画展览，收到参赛作品近12000件。各省、市、自治区踊跃投稿参赛，台湾、香港等地也寄送了作品。经过书画名家评选，评出一等奖书画各5件、二等奖书画各27件、三等奖书画各80件。主办单位从中遴选出近400幅优秀书画作品参加展出。
