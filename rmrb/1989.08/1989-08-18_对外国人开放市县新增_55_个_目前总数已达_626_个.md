Title: 55 More Cities and Counties Opened to Foreigners, Total Now 626
Author:
Date: 1989-08-18
Edition: 1

The Public Security Bureau's spokesperson announced today that, with the approval of the State Council, 55 more cities and counties in China have been designated as open areas for foreigners. This brings the total number of cities and counties open to foreigners to 626.

According to Article 20 of Chapter 4 of the "Law of the People's Republic of China on the Control of Entry and Exit of Aliens," foreigners holding valid visas or residence permits can travel to these areas without needing to apply for additional travel documents.

The cities and counties opened this time are:

	Qinghai Province: Guide County, Hainan County, Hualong Hui Autonomous County, Xunhua Salar Autonomous County, Dulan County, Wulan County, Maduo County, Jianzha County, Tongren County;
	Lake North Province: Yangxin County, Tongshan County, Tongcheng County, Chongyang County, Luotian County, Xishui County, Yingshan County;
	Fujian Province: Ningde City, Xiapu County, Fuding County, Zherong County, Shouning County, Pingnan County, Zhouning County, Wuping County;
	Sichuan Province: Dachang City, Daxian County, Mingshan County, Liangping County, Wenchuan County, Lixian County;
	Shaanxi Province: Yulin City, Dingbian County, Jingbian County, Hengshan County, Ankang City, Shiquan County, Hanyin County, Ningqian County, Ziyang County, Xunyang County, Pingli County, Baihe County, Zhenping County, Langao County, Shangzhou City, Danfeng County, Zhashui County, Ningqiang County, Weinan City, Huaxian County, Ganquan County, Anse County, Zhidan County, Zichang County, Yanchang County.

### 对外国人开放市县新增55个  目前总数已达626个

1989-08-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    对外国人开放市县新增55个
    目前总数已达626个
    新华社北京8月18日电  公安部新闻发言人今天宣布，经国务院批准，我国又有55个市县被列入对外国人开放地区。至此，我国已有626个市县对外国人开放。
    根据《中华人民共和国外国人入境出境管理法》第四章第二十条规定，外国人持有效的签证或者居留证件，即可前往这些地区旅行，无须申办旅行证件。
    此次对外国人开放的市、县是：
    青海省：贵德县、乐都县、化隆回族自治县、循化撒拉族自治县、都兰县、乌兰县、玛多县、尖扎县、同仁县；湖北省：阳新县、通山县、通城县、崇阳县、罗田县、浠水县、英山县；福建省：宁德市、霞浦县、福鼎县、柘荣县、寿宁县、屏南县、周宁县、武平县；四川省：达县市、达县、名山县、梁平县、汶川县、理县；陕西省：榆林市、定边县、靖边县、横山县、安康市、石泉县、汉阴县、宁陕县、紫阳县、旬阳县、平利县、白河县、镇坪县、岚皋县、商州市、丹凤县、柞水县、宁强县、渭南市、华县、甘泉县、安塞县、志丹县、子长县、延长县。
