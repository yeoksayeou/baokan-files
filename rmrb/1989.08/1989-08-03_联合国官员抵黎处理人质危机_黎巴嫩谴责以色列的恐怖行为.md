Title: UN Official Arrives in Lebanon to Handle Hostage Crisis; Lebanon Condemns Israel's Terrorist Acts
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-08-03
Edition: 3

UN Deputy Secretary-General Arrives in Southern Lebanon to Discuss Hostage Crisis
New York, August 2 (Xinhua) — UN Deputy Secretary-General Marrack Goulding arrived in southern Lebanon today to consult with officials of the UN Interim Force in Lebanon (UNIFIL) on resolving the hostage crisis.

Goulding, who is in charge of UN peacekeeping operations, was dispatched by UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar to retrieve the body of American officer William Higgins, who was killed by a Muslim organization in Lebanon.

Goulding said earlier, while passing through Israel, that he would investigate the Higgins case and seek ways to secure the release of other hostages.

Beirut, August 2 (Xinhua) — The Lebanese government today condemned Israel's abduction of Hezbollah leader Abdul Karim Obeid last week as a "new terrorist act."

This was stated in a letter by Lebanon's deputy permanent representative to the UN, Shakki Shukairi, to UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar.

Shukairi pointed out that Israel's actions blatantly violated Lebanon's sovereignty and territorial integrity, and contravened human rights and international law.

The Lebanese government holds Israel responsible for the lives of the three abducted individuals and demands their immediate release.

### 联合国官员抵黎处理人质危机  黎巴嫩谴责以色列的恐怖行为
刘开宸
1989-08-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联合国官员抵黎处理人质危机  黎巴嫩谴责以色列的恐怖行为
    新华社贝鲁特8月2日电　联合国副秘书长戈尔丁2日抵达南黎巴嫩，同那里的联合国维持和平部队官员就解决人质危机进行磋商。
    负责联合国维持和平部队事务的副秘书长马拉克·戈尔丁此次是受联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔的派遣，去找回被黎巴嫩穆斯林组织绞死的美国军官希金斯的遗体。
    戈尔丁早些时候在途经以色列时说，他将查询希金斯事件和寻求如何使其他人质获释的办法。
    本报联合国8月2日电　记者刘开宸报道：黎巴嫩政府今天谴责以色列在上周劫持黎巴嫩真主党领导人阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·奥贝德是“新的恐怖主义行为”。
    这是黎巴嫩常驻联合国代理代表肖基·舒韦里大使在给联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔的一封信中提出的。
    舒韦里指出，以色列的罪行公然侵犯了黎巴嫩主权和领土完整，违反了人权和国际法。
    黎巴嫩政府指出，以色列对三名被劫持人员的生命负责并要求立即释放他们。
