Title: Iran Responds Positively to U.S. Proposal; Israeli Authorities Reject Partial Hostage Exchange
Author:
Date: 1989-08-08
Edition: 3

Iran Responds Positively to U.S. Proposal; Israeli Authorities Reject Partial Hostage Exchange

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, August 8th - The *Tehran Times* reported today that Iran has responded positively to the U.S. proposal for the release of Western hostages in Lebanon.

According to the London *Sunday Express*, President Bush has proposed that if Western hostages in Lebanon are released, the U.S. government will unfreeze Iranian assets in the United States.

The *Tehran Times* quoted an official close to President Rafsanjani as saying, "The U.S. should take the first step. If Iran is satisfied that its assets will be unfrozen, the second step will be taken by Iran."

After the 1980 Islamic Revolution in Iran, the U.S. froze $11.1 billion in Iranian assets.

The Iranian official said, "We can influence the release of hostages in Lebanon, but we cannot guarantee that Western hostages will definitely be released. Iran's influence on various factions in Lebanon is limited."

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 7th - According to Israeli radio today, Israel has rejected the release of Hezbollah leader Obeid as a condition for the exchange of American hostage Joseph Ciccippio.

Israeli Foreign Ministry spokesman Alon Liel said, "If Israelis are not released, then any deal is impossible."

Defense Minister Rabin said on the 6th, "If it is only for the 16 Western hostages in Lebanon, then Israel will not agree to any deal. Israel's first consideration is for Israelis themselves."

The above statements from Israeli officials came after the Lebanese Shiite "Revolutionary Justice Organization" proposed conditions for the release of Ciccippio. These conditions demand that Israel release the 150 Lebanese and 300 Palestinians it is detaining, and allow 55 expelled Palestinians to return home.

Hezbollah leader Hussein Musawi threatened on the 6th that if Obeid is not released, they will kill an Israeli prisoner.

Foreign Ministry spokesman Liel said that Israel believes Iran and Syria are responsible for the safety of the detainees, while Hezbollah and its backers will be responsible for the safety of the hostages.

### 伊朗对美国建议作出积极响应  以色列当局拒绝部分交换人质

1989-08-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    伊朗对美国建议作出积极响应  以色列当局拒绝部分交换人质
    新华社德黑兰8月8日电  《德黑兰时报》今天报道，伊朗对美国提出的释放在黎巴嫩的西方人质的建议作出了积极的响应。
    据伦敦《星期日快报》报道，布什总统已建议，如果西方在黎巴嫩的人质得到释放，美国政府将解冻伊朗在美国的资财。
    《德黑兰时报》援引一位接近拉夫桑贾尼总统的官员的话说，“美国应当迈出第一步。假如伊朗对它的资财肯定得到解冻表示满意，第二步就会由伊朗迈出。”
    1980年伊朗发生伊斯兰革命以后，美国冻结了伊朗价值111亿美元的资财。
    这位伊朗官员说，“我们可以对释放在黎巴嫩的人质问题施加影响，但不能保证西方人质一定获释。伊朗对黎巴嫩各派的影响是有限的。”
    新华社开罗8月7日电　据以色列电台今天广播，以色列拒绝释放黎巴嫩真主党领导人奥贝德，作为交换美国人质约瑟夫·西西皮奥的条件。
    以色列外交部发言人阿朗·利尔说，“假如以色列人得不到释放，那么任何交易都是不可能实现的。”
    国防部长拉宾6日声称，“假如只是为了在黎巴嫩的16名西方人质，那么以色列是不会同意任何交易的。以色列首先考虑的是以色列人自己。”
    以色列官方的上述言论，是在黎巴嫩什叶派“革命正义组织”提出释放西西皮奥条件后的一天内发表的。这些条件要求以色列释放它扣押的150名黎巴嫩人和300名巴勒斯坦人，并允许55名被驱逐出境的巴勒斯坦人重返家园。
    真主党领袖侯赛因·穆萨维6日发出威胁，假如奥贝德得不到获释，他们就将杀死一名以色列俘虏。
    以外交部发言人利尔说，以色列认为伊朗和叙利亚应对被监禁者的生命负有责任，而真主党及其幕后操纵国将对人质的安全负有责任。
