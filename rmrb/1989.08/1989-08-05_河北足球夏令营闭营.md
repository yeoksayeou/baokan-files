Title: Hebei Football Summer Camp Concludes
Author: Dàqiáng
Date: 1989-08-05
Edition: 4

    The seven-day 1989 Hebei Province Youth Football Summer Camp training session concluded on August 4 in the mountain city of Zhangjiakou. Nearly one hundred coaches, athletes, and researchers from twelve cities and prefectures across the province, as well as the provincial sports school and the provincial sports team, participated in the summer camp.

### 河北足球夏令营闭营
达强
1989-08-05
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    河北足球夏令营闭营
    历时七天的一九八九年河北省少年足球夏令营集训，八月四日在山城张家口结束。全省十二个地市及省体校、省体工大队近百名教练员、运动员和科研工作者参加了夏令营。
                  （达强）
