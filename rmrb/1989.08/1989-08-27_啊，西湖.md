Title: Ah, West Lake
Author: Wáng Ēnyǔ
Date: 1989-08-27
Page: 8

Ah, West Lake,
With your natural essence,
Your unparalleled beauty,
Your timeless poetic charm,
You have captivated travelers from all over the land.
They have poured their affection for you
Into verses,
Into paintings,
Into a sense of pride,
Into countless dreams that linger in the mind…

Though you generously bestow
All your beauty upon visitors,
You never remember any face or name.
Is this indifference?
Or is it the breadth and grandeur of your spirit?
Ah, West Lake!

Yet every visitor carries you deeply in their heart,
Just one encounter,
And they love you for a lifetime,
Cherishing the memories,
Showing them off to others,
Often delighting in reminiscing.
Is this infatuation?
A longing for beauty?
Or something else?
I cannot say for sure.
Ah, West Lake!

### 啊，西湖
王恩宇
1989-08-27
第8版(副刊)
专栏：大地

    啊，西湖
    王恩宇
    你以天然的质，
    绝美姿色，
    千古不衰的诗韵，
    吸引了天下游人。
    他们把对你的眷恋，
    凝成诗行，
    凝成画卷，
    凝成一种骄傲，
    凝成无数耐人寻味的梦……
    尽管你慷慨无私
    把所有的美都给了来者，
    却从不记住任何一张面孔、一个名字。
    这是一种冷漠？
    还是气度和胸怀的博大？
    啊，西湖！
    然而，每一位来者却把你深深印在心上，
    只一次相见，
    便爱你终生，
    并把因此构成的回忆，
    时时翻阅，
    向人炫耀，
    频频欣然回味。
    这是一种痴情？
    是对美的渴慕？
    还是什么？
    我难以说清。
    啊，西湖！
