Title: East German Youth Discover Ancient Germanic Script
Author:
Date: 1989-08-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 2nd.

East German youth archaeologists recently discovered the oldest known Germanic script—the Bergheide script—in the Bergheide region. This script is believed to have formed around 700 BC, making it at least 500 years older than the previously known oldest Germanic script, the Runic script.

A team of 17 young men and women accidentally discovered a site with a diameter of 11 meters and four entrances. Upon excavation, they found a 2,500-year-old ceramic amulet and a ceramic pot from the same period. The pot had inscriptions on it. Archaeologists have named this script the Bergheide script.

### 民德发现日尔曼最古文字

1989-08-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    民德发现日尔曼最古文字
    新华社柏林8月2日电　民主德国青年考古工作者最近在贝格海德地区发现了日尔曼族最古老的文字——贝格海德文字。它大约在公元前700年时就已形成，比迄今发现的日尔曼族最古老的文字鲁内文字至少早500年。
    这批由17名男女青年组成的考古队无意中发现了一块直径11米，有4个入口的遗址。经挖掘，发现了一只2500年前的陶制护身符和一只同年代的陶罐。陶罐上面刻着文字。考古学家们把这种文字称之为贝格海德文字。
