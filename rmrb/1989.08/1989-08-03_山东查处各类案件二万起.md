Title: Shandong Investigates and Handles Over 20,000 Cases
Author: Jia Jianzhou
Date: 1989-08-03
Edition: 4

	Shandong Investigates and Handles Over 20,000 Cases
	Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, August 3, 1989

	Reporter Jia Jianzhou reports: Recently, Ma Jianchun, Secretary of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, pointed out that at present, efforts should be concentrated on investigating and handling major and important cases, severely punishing a group of criminals who have caused great public outrage, and enhancing the confidence and courage of the masses in fighting against corrupt practices.

	In the first half of this year, the discipline inspection system of Shandong Province investigated and handled 5,200 cases of disciplinary violations by Party members, disciplining 4,620 Party members; various levels of supervisory organs investigated and handled 2,300 cases of administrative violations, with 437 people receiving administrative penalties; the industrial and commercial system investigated and handled over 13,000 cases of various types, with fines and confiscations totaling over 16 million yuan, including 23 major cases involving over 10 million yuan each.

### 山东查处各类案件二万起
贾建舟
1989-08-03
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    山东查处各类案件二万起
    本报济南电　记者贾建舟报道：中共山东省委书记姜春云日前指出，当前要集中力量查处一些大案、要案，严惩一批民愤极大的犯罪分子，增强群众同腐败行为作斗争的信心和勇气。
    今年上半年，山东省纪检系统共查处党员违纪案件5200余起，处分党员4620人；各级监察机关共查处行政违纪案件2300余起，有437人受到了政纪处分；工商系统共查处各类案件1.3万余起，罚没款额1600余万元，其中10万元以上的大案23起。
