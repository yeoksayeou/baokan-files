Title: Global Market Trends: London Copper Prices Rise, Tin Prices Fall; Singapore Rubber Prices Drop

Author:

Date: 1989-08-15

Edition: 3

	London: On August 14, the price of copper on the London Metal Exchange continued to rise, closing at 1,711 pounds per ton for delivery in three months, the highest level in three and a half months.

	On the same day, the price of special high-grade zinc for delivery in three months remained high at 1,660 dollars per ton. Due to seasonal demand decline, the closing price of tin fell from 9,050 dollars per ton in the previous trading day to 8,700 dollars per ton.

	Singapore: On August 14, the price of smoked sheet rubber on the Singapore rubber market fell.

	At the market opening, the price of smoked sheet rubber for September delivery was 179 Singapore cents per kilogram (equivalent to 91.8 US cents). Due to lack of buyer enthusiasm, the rubber price fell. At the market close, the price of smoked sheet rubber for September delivery dropped to 177.50 Singapore cents per kilogram (equivalent to 91.0 US cents).

### 世界市场行情  伦敦铜价升锡价降  新加坡橡胶降价

1989-08-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    世界市场行情
    伦敦铜价升锡价降
    新加坡橡胶降价
    新华社北京8月15日电　伦敦消息：8月14日伦敦五金交易所的铜价继续回升，收市时，3个月后交货的铜价每吨达1711英镑，为3个半月来的最高水平。
    当天，3个月后交货的特级锌仍保持在每吨1660美元的较高价格水平上。锡由于季节性需求下降，收盘价从上一个交易日的每吨9050美元下降到每吨8700美元。
    新华社北京8月15日电　新加坡消息：8月14日新加坡橡胶市场上烟胶片价格下降。
    市场开盘时，9月份交货的烟胶片价格为每千克179新分（合91.8美分）。由于购买者不踊跃，胶价出现下跌。市场收盘时，9月份交货的烟胶片价格降至每千克177.50新分（合91.0美分）。
