Title: Coal Enterprises in Handan County Pool Resources to Support Agriculture
Author: Lián Jié, Wú Jiànguó
Date: 1989-08-04
Edition: 2

	Coal enterprises at various levels in Handan County, Hebei Province, have recently pooled one million yuan to support agricultural production, earning praise from the public.

	The western part of Handan County is a hilly area with abundant coal resources underground. In recent years, encouraged by the Party's policy of enriching the people, many farmers have become wealthy through working in coal mines. Of the one million yuan, 820,000 yuan has already been handed over to the county's financial department.

### 邯郸县煤炭企业集资支援农业
廉洁  吴建国
1989-08-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    邯郸县煤炭企业集资支援农业
    据新华社石家庄八月四日电  （通讯员廉洁、吴建国）河北省邯郸县各级各类煤炭企业顾全大局，最近集资一百万元支援农业生产，受到人们的称赞。
    邯郸县西部是丘陵地带，地下有丰富的煤炭资源。近年来，在党的富民政策的鼓励下，不少农民通过务工由穷变富。目前，这一百万元中已有八十二万元上交县财政。
