Title: Coal Mine Collapse in Chile
Author:
Date: 1989-07-31
Edition: 3

Due to continuous heavy rain, the Curanilahue coal mine located 700 kilometers south of Chile's capital Santiago collapsed on the night of the 29th. Out of the 25 miners working underground, 21 died and 4 survived.

### 智利发生矿井坍塌事故

1989-07-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    智利发生矿井坍塌事故
    由于连绵不断的暴雨，位于智利首都圣地亚哥以南700公里处的库拉尼拉韦煤矿29日晚发生矿井坍塌，正在井下采煤的25名矿工中有21人死亡，4人生还。
