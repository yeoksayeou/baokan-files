Title: Angolan Government Resumes Talks with UNITA
Author:
Date: 1989-08-11
Page: 3

The third round of talks between the Angolan government and the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola (UNITA), aimed at achieving a domestic peace plan, continued on the 9th in the outskirts of Kinshasa, the capital of Zaire.

### 安政府同安盟恢复会谈

1989-08-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    安政府同安盟恢复会谈
    安哥拉政府与反政府的争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟（安盟）就实现国内和平计划的第3轮会谈，9日在扎伊尔首都金沙萨郊区继续进行。
