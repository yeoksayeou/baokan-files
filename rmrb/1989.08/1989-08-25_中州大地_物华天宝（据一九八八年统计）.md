Author:
Date: 1989-08-25
Edition: 4

The Central Plains: A Land of Abundance
(According to 1988 statistics)

	Molybdenum, natural soda, and blue asbestos reserves rank first in the country;
	Refractory clay, perlite, and natural oilstone reserves rank second in the country;
	White tungsten ore, bauxite, and natural gas reserves rank third in the country;
	Annual coal production ranks second in the country;
	Annual cigarette production ranks second in the country;
	Annual small tractor production ranks third in the country;
	Annual flat glass production ranks second in the country;
	Annual rolling bearing production ranks first in the country.

### 中州大地  物华天宝（据一九八八年统计）

1989-08-25

专栏：

    中州大地  物华天宝

    耐火粘土、珍珠岩、天然油石保有储量居全国第二位；
    白钨矿、铝土矿、天然气保有储量居全国第三位；
    原煤年产量居全国第二位；
    卷烟年产量居全国第二位；
    小型拖拉机年产量居全国第三位；
    平板玻璃年产量居全国第二位；
    滚动轴承年产量居全国第一位。
