Title: Couple in California Uses Artificial Insemination to Have Three Children
Author:
Date: 1989-08-28
Edition: 7

The couple, Terry and Jane Morrell, from Manhattan, California, are holding their three children, who were all conceived through artificial insemination within a 21-month period. The eldest, Cooper, was born on February 15, 1987. The twins, Christina and Molly McNair, were born on November 29, 1988. These three children were fertilized at the same time but were implanted at different intervals using a new medical technique, which resulted in their birthdates being spaced apart.
