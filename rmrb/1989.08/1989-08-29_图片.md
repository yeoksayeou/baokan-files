Title: The Sky Dream Fantasy
Author: NIU Ming Shan
Date: 1989-08-29
Edition: 8

The Air Force Drama Troupe has recently completed the production of a three-part television series titled "Sky Dream Fantasy" (Scriptwriter: Li Dongcai, Director: Wang Xuexin). The series depicts the spiritual struggle of three generations of an air force family as they face the blue sky.
