Title: West German Citizen Fires at East German Border; East Germany Protests to West German Government
Author: Xǔ Hóngzhì
Date: 1989-08-19
Page: 3

People's Daily, Berlin, August 19th. According to local newspapers, on the night of August 18th, the residential area of the Elsterwerda district in the Democratic German border was fired upon from the West German side.

According to reports, two men fired approximately 50 shots from the West German side towards the residential houses and church in Walhausen.

The report states that the Democratic German representative office in Bonn has strongly protested to the West German Federal Chancellery over this serious border provocation incident.

### 西德公民向民德边境开枪  民德向西德政府提出抗议
许宏治
1989-08-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西德公民向民德边境开枪  民德向西德政府提出抗议
    本报柏林8月19日电　记者许宏治报道：据此间报纸今日报道，8月18日凌晨，民主德国边境埃尔福特专区的居民区受到来自西德一侧的枪击。
    据报道，两名男子从边界西德一侧向瓦尔豪森的居民住宅和教堂开了约50枪。
    报道说，民主德国驻波恩代表处已向西德联邦总理府就这一严重的边界挑衅事件提出强烈抗议。
