Title: Free Exercise
Author: Sun Xuelin
Date: 1989-08-06
Edition: 8

From the deep blue eyes of Rodin,
Completing one beautiful pose after another,
Like the resurrected Venus leaping into the pool,
Red flames, green flames,
Yellow flames, purple flames,
A wreath of fire spirits,
Dipped in the sweat of the training room,
On the green carpet like a lawn,
Write a string of spring language.

### 自由体操
孙学林
1989-08-06
第8版(副刊)
专栏：大地

    自由体操
    孙学林
    从罗丹深蓝的眼睛里走来
    完成一个个美丽的造型
    像复活的维纳斯跃入泳池
    红的火焰　绿的火焰
    黄的火焰　紫的火焰
    火的精灵的花环
    蘸着练功房里的汗水
    在绿如草坪的绒毯上
    写一串春天的语言
