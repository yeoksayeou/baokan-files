Title: Partial Drought Areas Receive Rainfall
Author:
Date: 1989-08-16
Edition: 2

	Central Meteorological Observatory reports that under the influence of weak cold air, from 6 a.m. on the 15th to 6 a.m. today, central and southern Gansu, eastern Qinghai, Shaanxi, central and northern Shanxi, western Hebei, eastern Inner Mongolia, and Beijing received 3 to 15 millimeters of rain, with some areas receiving 20 to 40 millimeters. In particular, central and northern Shanxi and parts of Guanzhong in Shaanxi received 40 to 80 millimeters of rain. This rainfall is beneficial for alleviating the drought.

### 部分旱区降雨

1989-08-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    部分旱区降雨
    新华社北京8月16日电　中央气象台消息，受弱冷空气影响，15日6时至今晨6时，甘肃中部南部、青海东部、陕西、山西中部和北部、河北西部、内蒙古东部以及北京等地降雨3至15毫米、部分地区降雨20至40毫米。其中，山西中部和北部及陕西关中的部分地区降雨量达40至80毫米。这次降雨，对缓解旱情有利。
