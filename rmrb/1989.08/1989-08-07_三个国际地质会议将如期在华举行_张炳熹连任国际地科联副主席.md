Title: Three International Geological Conferences to be Held in China as Scheduled
Author: Zeng Mianhua
Date: 1989-08-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 7th.

    Three international geological conferences scheduled to be held in China will proceed as planned. The 28th International Geological Congress, held recently in Washington, D.C., confirmed that the 1990 Mineralogical Congress, the 1991 Quaternary Geological Congress, and the 1994 Ore Genesis Congress will all take place in China as originally scheduled.

    Professor Zhang Bingxi, Chairman of the Science and Technology Advisory Committee of China's Ministry of Geology and Mineral Resources, was re-elected as a vice-chairman of the International Union of Geological Sciences at this congress through a competitive election.

### 三个国际地质会议将如期在华举行  张炳熹连任国际地科联副主席
曾绵华
1989-08-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    三个国际地质会议将如期在华举行
    张炳熹连任国际地科联副主席
    本报讯　前不久在美国华盛顿举行的第28届国际地质大会确定，原定在中国召开的一些大型国际会议——1990年的矿物学大会，1991年的第四纪地质大会和1994年的矿床成因大会，都将如期举行。
    我国地矿部科学顾问委员会主任张炳熹教授在这次大会通过差额选举，连任国际地科联副主席。（曾绵华）
