Title: Ningbo's Local Deep-Sea Fleet Makes Its Maiden Voyage to Hong Kong
Author: Guo Shuochuan
Date: 1989-08-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Ningbo, August 2nd.

Ningbo's local deep-sea transportation fleet's first cargo ship set sail for Hong Kong on July 31st.

Ningbo, a port city situated on the eastern coast of China, has gradually developed into an important international transshipment port since the late 1970s.

To take advantage of this advantage and develop an export-oriented economy, Ningbo City has recently organized a local deep-sea fleet.

### 宁波地方远洋船队首航香港
郭说传
1989-08-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    宁波地方远洋船队首航香港
    新华社宁波8月2日电　（记者郭说传）宁波市地方远洋运输船队第一艘货轮，7月31日首航香港。
    地处东海之滨的港城宁波，从70年代末开始逐步发展成为重要的国际中转港口。
    为了利用这个优势，发展外向型经济，宁波市最近组织了一支地方远洋船队。
