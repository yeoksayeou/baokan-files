Title: The World University Games are about to open; the Chinese University Sports Delegation departs Beijing
Author: Qǔ Běilín
Date: 1989-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 18th.

The World University Games are about to open, and the Chinese University Sports Delegation departs Beijing.

The Chinese University Sports Delegation today departed Beijing for Duisburg, West Germany, to participate in the 15th World University Games, which begin on the 22nd.

The Chinese University Sports Delegation, led by Liu Xing, consists of 105 members, including 69 athletes from track and field, fencing, rowing, and men's basketball teams. This university games event features only these four main events.

China began participating in the World University Games in 1981, and this is the fifth time the country has competed in this world-class event. In the previous four World University Games, the Chinese delegation achieved its best results in the 14th games in 1987, winning a total of 11 gold medals, 9 silver medals, and 5 bronze medals.

### 世界大学生运动会即将开幕  中国大学生体育代表团离京
曲北林
1989-08-18
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    世界大学生运动会即将开幕
    中国大学生体育代表团离京
    新华社北京8月18日电（记者曲北林）中国大学生体育代表团今天离开北京前往联邦德国杜伊斯堡参加22日开始的第十五届世界大学生运动会。
    以刘兴为团长的中国大学生代表团，全团105人，其中运动员69人，来自田径、击剑、赛艇和男子篮球队。这次大学生运动会只设上述四个大项的比赛。
    中国自1981年起开始参加世界大学生运动会，这一次是第五次出征该项世界性大赛。在前四届大学生运动会上，中国代表团在1987年的第十四届赛会上成绩最好，共夺得11枚金牌、9枚银牌和5枚铜牌。
