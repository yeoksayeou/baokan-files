Title: Opening of the Minority Nationalities Population Seminar
Author: Zhū Dōngjú, Yuán Xīn
Date: 1989-08-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, August 22nd

Fifty experts, scholars, and cadres engaged in the teaching and research of minority nationalities' population gathered in Urumqi today as members of the first "China Minority Nationalities Population Seminar."

Over the next twenty days, they will jointly explore topics including methods and questionnaire design for population surveys of minority nationalities; theories and policies on the development of minority nationalities' population; the research objectives, methods, and tasks of population studies; marriage, family, and population issues specific to minority nationalities; and comprehensive studies on the status of minority nationalities' population.

### 少数民族人口研讨班开学
朱冬菊  原新
1989-08-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    少数民族人口研讨班开学
    新华社乌鲁木齐八月二十二日电　（记者朱冬菊、通讯员原新）五十名从事民族人口的教学和研究工作的专家、学者和干部，作为首届“中国少数民族人口研讨班”成员今天聚集在乌鲁木齐。
    他们将在今后二十天的时间内共同探讨少数民族人口调查研究方法和调查问卷设计；少数民族人口理论，少数民族人口发展政策；民族人口学研究对象、方法和任务；少数民族的婚姻、家庭和人口；以及少数民族人口地位调查和综合研究等课题。
