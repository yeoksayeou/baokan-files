Title: The Capital's Stage Comes Alive Again
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Edition: 2

The curtain rose on the play "Youthful Emperor" at the Minzu Culture Palace tonight, marking the beginning of the evaluation performances for new dramatic and musical works created in Beijing between 1986 and 1989. This event serves as a prelude to the Second National Art Festival.

Over the next half month, 16 professional performing arts troupes in Beijing will stage 20 outstanding dramatic and musical works, bringing the once quiet capital's stage back to life. It is reported that many of the works in this evaluation performance have shown innovation. For instance, the plays "Youthful Emperor" and "The Morning Bell Awakens the Dream" have been selected to participate in the Second National Art Festival.

### 首都舞台再次活跃起来  20台新剧（节）目开始评奖演出

1989-08-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    首都舞台再次活跃起来
    20台新剧（节）目开始评奖演出
    据新华社北京8月21日电　今晚在民族文化宫礼堂上演的曲剧《少年天子》，拉开了北京1986—1989年新创作剧（节）目评奖演出的序幕。这一活动是第二届中国艺术节的前奏。
    在未来的半个多月中，北京的16个专业艺术表演团体将演出20台优秀剧（节）目，使一度冷清的首都舞台再次活跃起来。据悉：这次评奖演出中不少剧目有所创新，像曲剧《少年天子》、京剧《晨钟惊梦》已被选中参加第二届中国艺术节。
