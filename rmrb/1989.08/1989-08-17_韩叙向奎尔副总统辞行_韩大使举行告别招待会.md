Title: Chinese Ambassador Han Xu Bids Farewell to Vice President Quayle and Holds Farewell Reception
Author: Zhang Qixun
Date: 1989-08-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, August 16th.

Chinese Ambassador to the United States Han Xu, who is soon to leave his post, paid a farewell call on U.S. Vice President Dan Quayle today.

During the meeting, both sides exchanged views on Sino-U.S. relations and expressed the hope to maintain and develop bilateral ties.

Present at the meeting were Assistant Secretary of State Solomon and other U.S. officials.

In other news, Chinese Ambassador to the United States Han Xu, who is soon to leave his post, held a farewell reception at the embassy on August 16th.

Attending the reception were U.S. Deputy Secretary of State Lawrence Eagleburger, Assistant Secretary of State Solomon, former Secretary of State Henry Kissinger, former Secretary of State Alexander Haig, former Congressional leaders Mike Mansfield and Hugh Scott, as well as representatives from various sectors of American society, envoys from many countries to the United States, and overseas Chinese and Chinese Americans, totaling over a thousand people. The reception was held in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 韩叙向奎尔副总统辞行  韩大使举行告别招待会
张启昕
1989-08-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    韩叙向奎尔副总统辞行
    韩大使举行告别招待会
    本报华盛顿8月16日电　记者张启昕报道：即将离任的中国驻美国大使韩叙今天拜会美国副总统丹·奎尔，向他辞行。
    在会见中，双方就中美关系交换了意见，并表示希望保持和发展两国关系。
    会见时在座的有助理国务卿索洛蒙及其他美国官员。
    又讯　即将离任的中国驻美国大使韩叙16日在大使馆举行告别招待会。
    出席招待会的有美国副国务卿塞林、助理国务卿索洛蒙、前国务卿基辛格博士、前国务卿黑格将军、前国会领袖曼斯菲尔德和斯科特以及美国各界人士、许多国家驻美使节和华侨、美籍华人等上千人。招待会是在友好、热烈的气氛中进行的。
