Title: Lin Suqing Vows to Return to Seoul

Author: Xú Bǎokāng

Date: 1989-08-12

Edition: 3

    Lin Suqing, a representative of the "National University Student Representative Council" of South Korea, announced at a press conference held in Pyongyang this afternoon that he is determined to cross the military demarcation line at Panmunjom through the Demilitarized Zone on August 15 to return to his hometown of Seoul, even if it means making sacrifices, in order to achieve the great cause of Korean unification.

    According to reports, the South Korean authorities and the U.S. side of the Military Armistice Commission have so far refused to allow Lin Suqing to cross the military demarcation line at Panmunjom to return to his hometown.

### 林秀卿立誓返回汉城
徐宝康
1989-08-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    林秀卿立誓返回汉城
    本报平壤8月12日电　记者徐宝康报道：南朝鲜“全国大学生代表协议会”代表林秀卿今天下午在平壤举行的记者招待会上表示，为了实现朝鲜的统一大业，宁可作出牺牲也要在8月15日通过停战村板门店的军事分界线，返回故乡汉城。
    据报道，南朝鲜当局和军停会美国方面至今仍拒绝让林秀卿通过板门店军事分界线返回故乡。
