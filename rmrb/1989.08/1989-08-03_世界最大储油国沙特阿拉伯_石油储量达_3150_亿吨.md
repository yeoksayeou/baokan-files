Title: Saudi Arabia, the world's largest oil-reserve country, has 315 billion tons of oil reserves
Author: Lü Zhixing
Date: 1989-08-03
Page: 3

Our correspondent Lü Zhixing reports from Damascus: According to a report from Riyadh, the Saudi American Oil Company's annual report announced that Saudi Arabia's oil reserves can reach 31.5 billion tons, and natural gas reserves can reach 715 billion cubic meters.

This report is based on six years of research on the development of Saudi Arabia's existing oil wells and a comprehensive survey of Saudi Arabia.

Saudi Arabia's confirmed oil reserves are 25.4989 billion tons, including Saudi Arabia's share in the neutral zone with Kuwait. Saudi Arabia is the country with the largest oil reserves in the world, accounting for about one-quarter of the world's reserves.

### 世界最大储油国沙特阿拉伯  石油储量达3150亿吨
吕志星
1989-08-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    世界最大储油国沙特阿拉伯
    石油储量达3150亿吨
    本报大马士革8月2日电  记者吕志星报道：利雅得消息，据沙特美国石油公司的年度报告宣布，沙特的石油储量可达3150亿吨，天然气储量可达71500亿立方米。
    这个报告是根据6年来对沙特现有油井发展的研究和对沙特进行全面勘察后作出的。
    沙特已核实的石油储量为2549.89亿吨，其中包括沙特同科威特交接的中立区内的沙特部分，沙特是世界上拥有石油储量最大的国家，约占世界储量的1／4左右。
