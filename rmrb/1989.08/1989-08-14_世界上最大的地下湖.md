Title: The Largest Underground Lake in the World
Author: Chen Yan
Date: 1989-08-14
Page: 7

Some scholars, while investigating the caves and fissures in the northern part of Namibia, accidentally discovered the largest underground lake in the world. The area often experiences warm air currents, and the local residents call it "Dragon's Nose."

The lake is located 59 meters below the surface in a limestone rock layer. The lake covers an area of 1.9 hectares, making it the largest in the world. The deepest part of the lake is approximately 200 meters. The water is extremely clear and transparent, with a temperature of 24 degrees Celsius.

### 世界上最大的地下湖
陈延
1989-08-14
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    世界上最大的地下湖
    一些学者在考察纳米比亚北部的溶洞裂缝时，无意中发现了世界上最大的地下湖。那里常常有暖空气吹来，当地居民叫它
  “龙鼻子”。
    该湖位于地下59米深的白云石岩层处。湖的面积为1.9公顷，居世界之首。湖最深处约200米。湖水极为清澈透明，水温为24摄氏度。
          （陈延）
