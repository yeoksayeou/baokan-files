Title: Silhouette of Jingbo Lake
Author: Wang Fengmei Xu Jianzhong
Date: 1989-08-21
Page: 5

In the northern frontier of our motherland, in the suburbs of Mudanjiang City, Heilongjiang Province, there is a dazzling gem, known worldwide as the famous summer resort, Jingbo Lake. In the midsummer, the water level of Jingbo Lake rises, making the Bridal Veil Falls particularly spectacular, a sight rarely seen in the past 25 years, attracting numerous domestic and foreign tourists.

	① Underground forest, heaven within heaven.
	② Korean women carrying watermelons on their heads, singing and dancing, enjoying a joyful trip at Jingbo Lake.
	③ Tourists from China and abroad together.
	④ Scenic view of the Bridal Veil Falls.
