Title: The Fifth Congress of Fatah Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-08-04
Edition: 3

The fifth congress of Fatah, the main Palestinian guerrilla organization, opened today in Tunis. Over 1,000 delegates attended the meeting. Palestinian President Yasser Arafat and Tunisian President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali attended the opening ceremony.

In his opening speech, Arafat said that Fatah has opened a new chapter in the Palestinian national struggle over the past 25 years. The national unity among various factions of the Palestinian revolution is a victory for the Palestinian people, and the Palestinian uprising shows the people's determination to continue making sacrifices until they achieve self-determination and establish an independent state.

He emphasized that the Palestinian uprising is irreversible, and the independence of the Palestinian people is a condition for peace in the Middle East.

Arafat pointed out that the PLO has proposed suggestions for achieving a just peace, which is based on the inalienable national rights of the Palestinian people, international law, and various forms of dialogue between the PLO and other parties.

Ben Ali reiterated in his opening speech that Tunisia fully supports the Palestinian leadership headed by President Arafat and unconditionally supports any choice made by the Palestinian people in their struggle for national liberation and the establishment of an independent state.

This congress will discuss the strategies for the Palestinian people's struggle against Israeli occupation.

### 巴勒斯坦法塔赫五大开幕  讨论反对以色列占领的斗争策略

1989-08-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦法塔赫五大开幕
    讨论反对以色列占领的斗争策略
    新华社突尼斯8月3日电  巴勒斯坦的主要游击队组织——“法塔赫”第5次代表大会今天在这里开幕，1000名代表出席了会议。巴勒斯坦总统阿拉法特和突尼斯总统本·阿里出席了开幕式。
    阿拉法特在开幕词中说，“法塔赫”25年来开辟了巴勒斯坦民族斗争的新阶段。巴勒斯坦革命各派之间的民族团结是巴勒斯坦人民的一个胜利，巴勒斯坦起义表明了巴勒斯坦人民决心继续作出各种牺牲，直至实现自决权和建立一个独立国家。
    他强调，巴勒斯坦起义是不可逆转的，巴勒斯坦人民的独立是实现中东地区和平的条件。
    阿拉法特指出，巴解组织为实现公正和平提出了建议，而这种和平是建立在巴勒斯坦人民不可剥夺的民族权利、国际法以及巴解组织同其他各方的各种形式的对话基础上的。
    本·阿里在开幕式上重申，突尼斯完全支持以阿拉法特总统为首的巴勒斯坦领导机构，无条件支持巴勒斯坦人民在解放祖国和建立独立国家道路上作出的任何选择。
    这次代表大会将讨论巴勒斯坦人民反对以色列占领的斗争策略。
