Title: The Yellow River Experiences a Second Flood Peak
Author: Yuán Jiànjūn
Date: 1989-08-23
Edition: 2

From August 17 to 18, the middle reaches of the Yellow River experienced moderate to heavy rainfall, while the Wei River basin in Shaanxi Province received heavy to torrential rain. At 8:00 AM on August 20, the Huaxian Station on the Wei River recorded a flood peak of 2,920 cubic meters per second. At 12:18 PM, the Guyuan Station on the main stream recorded a flood flow of 2,360 cubic meters per second. After several floodwaters converged, they passed through the Huayankou Station in downstream Henan at 11:00 AM on August 21, with a maximum flow of 5,160 cubic meters per second. This was the second flood to occur in the lower reaches of the Yellow River this year.

### 黄河出现第二次洪峰
袁建军
1989-08-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    黄河出现第二次洪峰
    据新华社北京8月23日电  （通讯员袁建军）8月17日至18日，黄河中游地区普降中到大雨，陕西渭河流域降大到暴雨。20日8时，渭河华县站出现2920立方米每秒的洪峰；12时18分，干流府谷站洪峰流量为2360立方米每秒。几股洪水汇流后，于21日11时通过下游河南花园口站，最大流量5160立方米每秒，为今年黄河下游出现的第二次洪水。
