Title: Chinese Foreign Minister Arrives in Zambia
Author:
Date: 1989-08-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Lusaka, August 7th.

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen arrived here this afternoon, beginning a three-day official visit to Zambia.

At the airport, Qian stated that the purpose of his visit is to develop Sino-Zambian friendship, enhance mutual understanding, and strengthen cooperation. He hopes that this visit will "promote the all-weather development of friendship and cooperation between the peoples of China and Zambia."

Zambia is the fifth stop on Qian's six-nation tour of Africa. He has already visited Botswana, Lesotho, Zimbabwe, and Angola.

### 钱外长抵达赞比亚

1989-08-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    钱外长抵达赞比亚
    新华社卢萨卡8月7日电　中国外交部长钱其琛于今天下午抵达这里，开始对赞比亚为期3天的正式访问。
    钱外长在机场发表声明指出，他的访问宗旨是发展中赞友谊，增进相互理解，加强合作。他希望此次访问将“推动中赞两国人民的友谊和合作的全天候发展”。
    赞比亚是钱外长非洲六国之行的第五站。他已访问了博茨瓦纳、莱索托、津巴布韦和安哥拉。
