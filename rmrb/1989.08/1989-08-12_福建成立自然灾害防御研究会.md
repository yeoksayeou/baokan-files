Title: Fujian Establishes Natural Disaster Defense Research Society
Author: Chen Rongran, Wang Fanfan
Date: 1989-08-12
Edition: 2

Fujian Establishes Natural Disaster Defense Research Society

According to Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, August 11th — (Correspondent Chen Rongran, Reporter Wang Fanfan) The "Fujian Province Natural Disaster Defense Research Committee," which aims to specialize in the study of the laws governing natural disasters in Fujian Province and their defense measures, was established today in Fuzhou.

This is the first provincial-level natural disaster defense research institution to appear on the mainland. This research institution is sponsored by the Fujian Provincial Science and Technology Commission, and its members come from various departments within the province, including meteorology, seismology, agriculture, forestry, and higher education institutions.

### 福建成立自然灾害防御研究会
陈荣让  王凡凡
1989-08-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    福建成立自然灾害防御研究会
    据新华社福州8月11日电　（通讯员陈荣让、记者王凡凡）旨在对福建省自然灾害活动规律及其防御办法作专门研究的“福建省自然灾害防御研究委员会”，今天在福州成立。
    这是目前大陆上出现的首家省级自然灾害防御研究机构。这家研究机构由福建省科学技术委员会主办，其成员来自省内气象、地震、农业、林业、高等院校等多个部门。
