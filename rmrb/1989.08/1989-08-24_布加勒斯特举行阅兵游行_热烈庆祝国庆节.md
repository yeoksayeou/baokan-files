Title: Bucharest Holds Military Parade and March
Author:
Date: 1989-08-24
Edition: 3

Bucharest held a military parade and march today to enthusiastically celebrate the 45th anniversary of Romania's social and national liberation, anti-fascist, and anti-imperialist revolution.

The parade began at 8:30 AM. Various branches and units of the armed forces marched past the reviewing stand set up in the city center, accepting the inspection of Romania's party and government leaders led by Nicolae Ceaușescu, General Secretary of the Romanian Communist Party and President of the Republic. The Patriotic Guard and Youth Military Training Squads, composed of people from all walks of life and young people, also participated in the parade. Millions of people took part in the grand celebration march. Today, marches were also held in the capital cities of Romania's 40 counties.

### 布加勒斯特举行阅兵游行  热烈庆祝国庆节

1989-08-24
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    布加勒斯特举行阅兵游行
    热烈庆祝国庆节
    新华社布加勒斯特8月23日电　布加勒斯特市今天举行阅兵式和群众游行热烈庆祝罗马尼亚社会和民族解放、反法西斯和反帝革命45周年。
    阅兵式在上午8时半开始。武装部队各军、兵种的队伍经过设在市中心的主席台，接受以罗共总书记、共和国总统尼·齐奥塞斯库为首的罗党政领导人的检阅。由各界群众和青年组成的爱国卫队、青年军训队也列队参加了阅兵式。有数十万群众参加了规模盛大的庆祝游行。今天罗马尼亚40个县的首府也举行了群众游行。
