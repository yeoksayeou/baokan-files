Title: Research Series on Wartime Literature in the Nationalist-Controlled Areas
Author: Chuan Xuan
Date: 1989-08-25
Edition: 8

The Sichuan Education Press has recently published the "Research Series on Wartime Literature in the Nationalist-Controlled Areas," which provides a comprehensive introduction and analysis of the literary landscape of that period.

This series consists of two parts: historical materials and research. The historical materials section includes novels, dramas, poems, essays, and literary theory. The research section, which focuses on history, covers the history of literary development, the history of theoretical development, the history of literary movements, and research on novels, dramas, poems, and essays.

### 《国统区抗战文学研究丛书》
川宣
1989-08-25
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    《国统区抗战文学研究丛书》
    四川教育出版社新近推出的《国统区抗战文学研究丛书》从多方面介绍和研究了这一时期的文学面貌。
    这套丛书有史料和研究两个系列。史料系列包括了小说、戏剧、诗歌、散文、文艺理论方面的资料；研究系列以史为主，包括文学发展史、理论发展史、文学运动史以及小说、戏剧、诗歌、散文等方面的研究著作。　（川宣）
