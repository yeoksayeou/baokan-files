Title: A Pure Zhangjiajie
Author: Chagan
Date: 1989-08-28
Edition: 8

	From the azure gaps in the clouds,
	I catch a glimpse first,
	Yet she has already gazed at me with her pure eyes.
	Hastily, I brush off the dust of the world,
	Lowering my head, too late in realization.
	In such a place,
	Desire is like a spring,
	Fantasies leap into Jinbian Stream,
	Growing into a cluster of reeds.
	Her sacred touch, in this world,
	Is the only one.
	Am I worthy?
	Golden Rooster, oh Golden Rooster,
	Flying to and fro among the tree shadows,
	Have you ever thought
	That in the vast valley,
	My thoughts are
	A feeble stroke,
	Like a lonely palm wind,
	How can I bear to pluck
	This leaf, this branch?
	My destination is unfit for defilement,
	Only by weaving a pure dream into you,
	The "treasure chest in the clouds,"
	I whisper softly,
	Farewell, Zhangjiajie!

### 很纯的张家界
查干
1989-08-28
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    很纯的张家界
    查干
    从琉璃色的云缝里
    先睹为快
    她却已凝视我以很纯的眸子
    急忙弹去积年的红尘
    低垂我憬悟太晚的头颅
    如此境地
    欲念是泉水
    痴想跃入金鞭溪
    长成一丛菖蒲
    她圣洁的爱抚  世间是
    唯一的
    我配吗？
    金鸡呀金鸡
    树影间你飞翔来去
    可曾想到
    空旷的山谷里
    我的思絮是
    无力的一笔
    似椰风  踽踽
    怎忍掐去
    这一叶一枝
    我的归处不堪污浊
    只有把一缕清梦叠入你
    云间的“天书宝匣”
    悄悄道一声
    珍重  张家界！
