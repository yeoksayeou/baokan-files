Title: The People's Daily Literature Department and Novel Selection Magazine Jointly Announce the 1987–1988 Excellent Novella and Short Story Awards
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Page: 6

The People's Daily Literature Department and Novel Selection Magazine, in collaboration with eight enterprises, have jointly organized the 1987–1988 Excellent Novella and Short Story Awards. The evaluation committee has recently decided on the winners, selecting 8 novellas and 11 short stories.

The novellas that received the Excellent Novella Award are (listed in order of publication):

* "White Horse" (Wang Xingquan, published in *October* 1987, Issue 1)
* "Troubled Life" (Chi Li, published in *Shanghai Literature* 1987, Issue 8)
* "Scenery" (Fang Fang, published in *Contemporary Writers* 1987, Issue 5)
* "Hesitant Departure" (Liu Qi, published in *Kunlun* 1987, Issue 6)
* "The Difference Between Winter and Summer" (Miao Changshui, published in *Liberation Army Literature* 1988, Issue 4)
* "Lazy to Divorce" (Chen Rong, published in *Liberation Army Literature* 1988, Issue 6)
* "Tianqiao" (Li Xiao, published in *Youth Literature* 1988, Issue 8)
* "Chasing the Moon Tower" (Ye Zhaoyan, published in *Zhongshan* 1988, Issue 5)

The short stories that received the Excellent Short Story Award are (listed in order of publication):

* "Sweet Iron, Foul Iron" (Yang Yongming, published in *Shanghai Literature* 1987, Issue 3)
* "The Cow Trader's Path" (Yan Ning, published in *People's Literature* 1987, Issue 3)
* "Bohegou Past and Present" (Ma Wen, published in *People's Literature* 1987, Issue 4)
* "Small Clinic" (Zhou Daxin, published in *Hebei Literature* 1987, Issue 4)
* "Pride" (Lu Wenfu, published in *People's Literature* 1987, Issue 5)
* "Autumn Lament of the Horse" (Xie Youyin, published in *Shanghai Literature* 1987, Issue 5)
* "Accompanying Music" (Zhu Chunyu, published in *Chinese Writers* 1987, Issue 3)
* "Tower Platform" (Liu Zhenyun, published in *People's Literature* 1987, Issue 7)
* "The Horse Cart" (Chen Shixu, published in *October* 1987, Issue 4)
* "Shouting Meeting" (Bai Yuan, published in *Youth Literature* 1988, Issue 12)
* "Six Essays on the Year's End" (A Cheng, published in *Beijing Literature* 1988, Issue 12)

This award ceremony was sponsored by Anhui Quanjiao Diesel Engine Factory, Dandong Chemical Fiber Company, Zhongyuan Oilfield Downhole Operation Company, Beijing Yanshan Petrochemical Company, Fujian Yunxiao General's Mountain Mineral Water Development Company, Dandong Dongqi Electrical Group, and Guizhou Yaxi Wine Factory.

### 人民日报文艺部和《小说选刊》杂志社举办  1987年——1988年优秀中短篇小说奖揭晓

1989-08-21
第6版(文艺评论)
专栏：

    人民日报文艺部和《小说选刊》杂志社举办
    1987年——1988年优秀中短篇小说奖揭晓
    由人民日报文艺部和《小说选刊》杂志社联合8家企业举办的1987年—1988年优秀中短篇小说评奖，近日通过评委议决，共评出中篇小说8部、短篇小说11部。
    获得这次优秀中篇小说奖的作品是（以发表时间为序）：
    《白马》（王星泉，载《十月》1987年1期）；
    《烦恼人生》（池莉，载《上海文学》1987年8期）；
    《风景》（方方，载《当代作家》1987年5期）；
    《去意徊徨》（刘琦，载《昆仑》1987年6期）；
    《冬天和夏天的区别》（苗长水，载《解放军文艺》1988年4期）；
    《懒得离婚》（谌容），载《解放军文艺》1988年6期）；
    《天桥》（李晓，载《青年文学》1988年8期）；
    《追月楼》（叶兆言，载《钟山》1988年5期）。
    获得优秀短篇小说奖的作品是（以发表时间为序）：
    《甜的铁、腥的铁》（杨咏鸣，载《上海文学》1987年3期）；
    《牛贩子山道》（雁宁，载《人民文学》1987年3期）；
    《葫芦沟今昔》（马烽，载《人民文学》1987年4期）；
    《小诊所》（周大新，载《河北文学》1987年4期）；
    《清高》（陆文夫，载《人民文学》1987年5期）；
    《马嘶秋诉》（谢友鄞，载《上海文学》1987年5期）；
    《陪乐》（朱春雨，载《中国作家》1987年3期）；
    《塔铺》（刘震云，载《人民文学》1987年7期）；
    《马车》（陈世旭，载《十月》1987年4期）；
    《喊会》（柏原，载《青年文学》1988年12期）；
    《年关六赋》（阿成，载《北京文学》1988年12期）。
    这次评奖得到安徽全椒柴油机总厂、丹东化学纤维公司、中原油田井下作业公司、北京燕山石化公司、福建云霄将军山矿泉水开发公司、丹东东齐电器集团、贵州鸭溪窖酒厂的赞助。
