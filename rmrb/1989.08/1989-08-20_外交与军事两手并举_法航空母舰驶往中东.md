Title: Diplomacy and Military Action in Tandem: French Aircraft Carrier Heads to the Middle East
Author: Zhang Qihua
Date: 1989-08-20
Edition: 3

French aircraft carrier "Foch" set sail yesterday evening from the port of Toulon, escorted by the destroyer "Cassard," heading towards the waters off Lebanon. This move indicates that France is adopting a policy of combining diplomacy with military action in response to the Lebanese crisis.

The "Foch" aircraft carrier will join the destroyer "Descartes," which arrived a few days earlier, along with other naval vessels, forming a "sea and air force capable of dealing with any unforeseen events." French officials stated that France is strengthening its military presence in the eastern Mediterranean to be ready to rescue French citizens there and carry out humanitarian missions. Some commentators believe that "France is prepared to use military force as a backup to its diplomacy."

On the day the "Foch" set sail, a French envoy returned to Paris after meeting with leaders of the conflicting parties in Beirut. France also proposed an emergency meeting of the European Community to provide humanitarian aid to Lebanon. Prior to this, France had sent five envoys to Syria, Morocco, Algeria, the Soviet Union, and the United States to seek a solution to the Lebanese crisis through extensive consultations.

According to today's "Sunday Journal," Lebanese Christian leader Oron expressed his hope that "France will intervene militarily in Lebanon." However, another Lebanese faction leader, Hussein, said he was surprised by France's dispatch of warships to the waters off Lebanon, believing that this move would only strengthen the intransigence of one of the opposing sides.

### 外交与军事两手并举  法航空母舰驶往中东
张启华
1989-08-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    外交与军事两手并举
    法航空母舰驶往中东
    本报巴黎8月20日电　记者张启华报道：法国航空母舰“福煦”号昨天傍晚离开土伦港，在“卡萨尔”号驱逐舰护卫下，向黎巴嫩海域进发。此举表明，法国对黎巴嫩危机采取了外交与军事并举的方针。
    “福煦”号航空母舰将同几天前先期到达的“迪凯斯纳”号驱逐舰，以及其它军舰会合在一起，“组成一支可以应付任何不测事件的海空力量”。法国官方说，法国增强它在东地中海的军事力量，是为了随时营救那里的法国侨民，执行人道使命。有的舆论认为，“法国准备以武力作为其外交之后盾”。
    在“福煦”号出发当天，法国一名特使在贝鲁特会晤冲突双方领导人后已返回巴黎。法国还倡议欧洲共同体召开紧急会议，向黎巴嫩提供人道援助。在这之前，法国已有5名特使分赴叙利亚、摩洛哥、阿尔及利亚、苏联、美国等游说，目的是通过广泛磋商，寻找解决黎巴嫩危机的办法。
    据今天《星期日报》报道，黎巴嫩基督教派领导人奥恩表示，他“希望法国对黎巴嫩进行军事干预”。但黎巴嫩另一教派、穆斯林派领导人胡斯说，他对法国派出军舰到黎巴嫩海域活动表示惊讶，他认为此举只能加强对立一方的不调和态度。
