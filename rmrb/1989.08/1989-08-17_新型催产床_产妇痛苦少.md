Title: New Type of Labor Induction Bed Reduces Maternal Pain
Author: Aixiao
Date: 1989-08-17
Edition: 2

A new type of labor induction bed, developed by 70-year-old inventor Chen Zhi, has passed product appraisal. The unique patented technology, known as the "painless labor induction bed," underwent clinical observation and follow-up for over three years, achieving a 93% effective pain relief rate.

The product consists of various electronic components and specially designed micro-element panels, divided into three parts: the main unit, mattress, and abdominal cover. It is installed on the waiting and delivery beds.

### 新型催产床  产妇痛苦少
艾笑
1989-08-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    新型催产床  产妇痛苦少
    本报讯　记者艾笑报道：七旬发明家陈植推出的独特专利技术——“镇痛催产床”，8月初通过产品鉴定。
    该产品由多种电子元件和特制的微量元素板组成，分主机、床垫和腹罩3部分，装置在待产床和产床上。3年多来通过临床观察和随访，镇痛有效率达93％。
