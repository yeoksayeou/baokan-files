Title: Announcement of the Second National Excellent Youth Readings Selection
Author: Lǐ Āndá
Date: 1989-08-04
Edition: 4

    The announcement of the winners of the second national excellent youth readings selection was recently made in Changchun. A total of 7 first prizes and 36 second prizes were awarded.

    This selection was organized by the Chinese Publishers Association.

### 青年读物评选揭晓
李安达
1989-08-04
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    青年读物评选揭晓
    本报讯　第二届全国优秀青年读物评选最近在长春揭晓，共评出一等奖7种，二等奖36种。
    这次评选是由中国出版工作者协会主办的。
              （李安达）
