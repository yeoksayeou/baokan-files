Title: The Seven-Party Alliance Provisional Government Plans to Establish a Foreign Ministry in Islamabad
Author:
Date: 1989-08-11
Edition: 3

The provisional government of the seven-party alliance of Afghan Mujahideen is currently planning to establish a foreign ministry in Islamabad, the capital of Pakistan.

According to local observers, the seven-party alliance provisional government of the Afghan Mujahideen wants to move the temporary office of the foreign ministry, originally set up in the northwestern border city of Peshawar, to Islamabad to facilitate contact with Western diplomats.

The Pakistani government has not yet made any comments on this matter, but it is estimated that it will not express opposition, as several Mujahideen organizations already have offices in Islamabad.

### 阿七党联盟临时政府筹划在伊斯兰堡设外交部

1989-08-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿七党联盟临时政府筹划在伊斯兰堡设外交部
    新华社伊斯兰堡8月10日电　阿富汗游击队七党联盟临时政府目前正筹划在巴基斯坦首都伊斯兰堡设立外交部。
    据此间观察家们认为，阿游击队七党联盟临时政府想将原来设在巴西北边境城市白沙瓦的外交部临时办事处改设在伊斯兰堡是为了便于接近西方外交官。
    巴政府目前还未就此事发表任何评论，但估计不会表示反对，因为已有几个阿游击队组织在伊斯兰堡设有办事处。
