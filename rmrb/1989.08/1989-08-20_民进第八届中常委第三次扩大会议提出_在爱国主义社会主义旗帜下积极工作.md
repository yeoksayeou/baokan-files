Title: The Third Enlarged Meeting of the Eighth Central Committee of the China Democratic Promotion Association Proposes to Actively Work Under the Banner of Patriotism and Socialism
Author:
Date: 1989-08-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 19th.

The third enlarged meeting of the Eighth Central Committee of the China Democratic Promotion Association (CDPA) concluded today. It was decided that the second plenary session of the Eighth CDPA Central Committee will be held in Beijing this December.

The resolution passed by the meeting stated that the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, held at a critical moment in China's history, has significant current and historical importance for stabilizing the national situation, continuing the line, principles, and policies of the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee, and achieving the strategic goals of China's social development. The resolution calls on all levels of CDPA organizations and all members to understand the situation, raise awareness, boost spirits, strengthen confidence, and work actively under the guidance of the banners of patriotism and socialism to contribute to the completion of the four major tasks proposed by the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee.

The meeting also decided to establish the Board of Directors for the Respect for Teachers and Education Fund. CDPA Chairman Rè Jiéqióng will serve as the chairman of this board.

At the closing ceremony, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Zhào Pǔchū gave a speech.

### 民进第八届中常委第三次扩大会议提出  在爱国主义社会主义旗帜下积极工作

1989-08-20
第2版(要闻)
专栏：

    民进第八届中常委第三次扩大会议提出
    在爱国主义社会主义旗帜下积极工作
    据新华社北京8月19日电  今天结束的中国民主促进会第八届中央常务委员会第三次扩大会议决定，民进八届二中全会于今年12月在北京召开。
    会议通过的民进第八届中常委第三次扩大会议决议认为，在我国历史发展的关键时刻召开的中共十三届四中全会，对进一步稳定全国局势，继续贯彻中共十一届三中全会以来的路线、方针、政策，实现我国社会发展的战略目标，具有重大的现实意义和深远的历史意义。决议号召民进各级组织和全体会员认清形势，提高认识，振奋精神，增强信心，在爱国主义和社会主义两面旗帜的指引下，积极工作，为完成中共十三届四中全会提出的四件大事作出贡献。
    这次会议还决定成立尊师重教基金理事会。民进中央主席雷洁琼担任这个理事会的理事长。
    全国政协副主席赵朴初在闭幕会上讲了话。
