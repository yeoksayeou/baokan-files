Title: American Athletes Set World Record in 4x200m Relay
Author:
Date: 1989-08-24
Page: 3

People's Daily, Bonn, August 24th - The renowned American sprinter Carl Lewis, along with three teammates, collaborated to break the world record in the 4x200m relay. Yesterday, at a sports meet in Koblenz, West Germany, they achieved a time of 1 minute 19.38 seconds, surpassing the previous record of 1 minute 20.26 seconds. The four American athletes averaged a time of 19.9 seconds per leg, with Lewis, who ran the final leg, completing his segment in 19.35 seconds. The other team members were Dan Everett, Ray Barrel, and F. Hurd.

### 美选手创4×200米接力世界纪录

1989-08-24
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    美选手创4×200米接力世界纪录
    本报波恩8月24日电　美国著名短跑运动员卡尔·刘易斯和其他三名队友合作，昨天在西德科布伦茨的运动会上以1分19秒38的成绩打破1分20秒26的4×200接力的世界纪录。四名美国运动员平均成绩是19秒9，跑最后一棒的刘易斯成绩是19秒35。其他三人是丹·埃佛勒特、雷·巴雷尔和弗·赫德。
