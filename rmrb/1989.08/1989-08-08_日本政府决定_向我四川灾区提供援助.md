Title: The Japanese Government Decides to Provide Aid to Sichuan Disaster Area
Author:
Date: 1989-08-08
Edition: 3

The Japanese government today formally decided to provide approximately $2 million in emergency aid and some relief supplies to China due to the flooding in Sichuan Province and other areas.

### 日本政府决定  向我四川灾区提供援助

1989-08-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日本政府决定  向我四川灾区提供援助
    本报东京8月6日电　日本政府今天正式决定，因四川省等地遭到洪水危害，向中国提供约200万美元的紧急援助和部分救灾物资。
