Title: Moonlit Mountain Village
Author:
Date: 1989-08-26
Edition: 7

The artwork "Moonlit Mountain Village" is a Soviet woodcut. It depicts a tranquil and serene scene of a small village nestled in the mountains under the soft glow of the moon. The artist has skillfully captured the essence of rural life, highlighting the simplicity and beauty of nature. The village houses are scattered across the landscape, their silhouettes standing out against the night sky. The moonlight casts a gentle light on the scene, creating a peaceful and idyllic atmosphere. The artwork is a testament to the artist's ability to convey a sense of tranquility and harmony with nature.
