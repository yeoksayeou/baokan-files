Title: U.S. Military Conducts Drills in Panama City; Panamanian Defense Forces Detain Three U.S. Soldiers
Author: Zhǔ Mǎn Tíng
Date: 1989-08-16
Edition: 3

Yesterday, the U.S. Marine Corps stationed in Panama conducted a military drill in the center of Panama City to defend the embassy and the ambassador's residence. The U.S. Embassy simultaneously issued a statement accusing the Panamanian authorities of actions that threatened the safety and operation of the Panama Canal and U.S. citizens.

In yesterday's drill, the U.S. military deployed 40 Marines and 7 helicopters.

The Panamanian government believes that the U.S. military's unilateral and uncoordinated military drills violate the Canal Treaty. The Panamanian government also suspects that the United States has intentions of military intervention.

The Panamanian opposition party, the Democratic Alliance of Citizens, also demanded that the United States respect Panama's sovereignty, while criticizing General Noriega for encouraging U.S. interventionism. The party's statement said it firmly rejects any attempt, overt or covert, to deny the sovereignty and legal rights of the Republic.

The U.S. Embassy in Panama issued a statement saying, "U.S. forces stationed in Panama will continue necessary drills to verify preparations for defending the canal and ensuring its operation." The statement instead accused the Panamanian government of violating the Canal Treaty and repeatedly infringing upon the rights enjoyed by the United States under the treaty in recent years.

The New China News Agency reported from Panama City on August 15 that the Panamanian Defense Forces detained three U.S. soldiers in Colon City on August 15 for insulting Panamanian soldiers.

According to local newspapers, U.S. Army Sergeant Clay, Lavalliere, and Private First Class Farris drove a military vehicle into Colon City. Upon being inspected by the Panamanian Defense Forces as required, they began cursing. The Panamanian Defense Forces detained the three for several hours before releasing them. Recently, U.S. forces stationed in the Panama Canal Zone have repeatedly clashed with the Panamanian Defense Forces. The U.S. military has unjustly detained Panamanian Defense Forces personnel on multiple occasions.

### 美军在巴拿马首都演习  巴拿马国防军扣押三名美军士兵
朱满庭
1989-08-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美军在巴拿马首都演习
    巴拿马国防军扣押三名美军士兵
    本报加拉加斯8月16日电  记者朱满庭报道：昨天，驻巴拿马美国海军陆战队在巴拿马首都市中心进行了一场保卫大使馆和大使官邸的军事演习，美国大使馆同时发表声明指责巴拿马当局的行动威胁着美国公民和运河的安全与运营。
    在昨天的演习中，美军出动了40名海军陆战队员和7架直升机。
    巴拿马政府认为，美军进行单方面的、未经与巴拿马当局协调的军事演习是对运河条约的破坏。巴拿马政府还认为，美国有进行军事干涉的意图。
    巴拿马反对派政党公民反对派民主联盟昨天也要求美国尊重巴拿马主权，同时指责诺列加将军鼓励了美国的干涉主义。这个党发表的公报说，坚决拒绝一切公开或隐蔽的否认共和国国家主权和合法权利的企图。
    美国驻巴拿马大使馆在一份公报中说，“美国驻巴拿马部队还要进行必要的演习，检验为保卫运河和保障运河运营而做的准备。”公报中反而指责巴拿马政府破坏了运河条约，在近几年不断侵犯美国在条约规定中享受的权益。
    新华社巴拿马城8月15日电　巴拿马国防军15日在科隆城扣押了3名辱骂巴拿马官兵的美国士兵。
    据此间报纸报道，美国陆军中士克雷、拉沃拉伊和二等兵法里斯驾驶一辆军车进入科隆城，在按规定接受巴拿马国防军检查时破口大骂。巴国防军将3人扣押几小时后予以释放。最近，驻扎在巴拿马运河区的美国军队不断与巴拿马国防军发生冲突。美军曾多次无理扣押巴拿马国防军官兵。
