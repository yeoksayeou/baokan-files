Title: Qian Qichen Concludes African Visit and Returns to China
Author:
Date: 1989-08-14
Edition: 2

Qian Qichen, the Foreign Minister, returned to Beijing by plane today after completing his visits to six countries in Southern Africa.

Qian began his visits to Botswana, Zimbabwe, Angola, Zambia, Mozambique, and Lesotho on July 26.

During his visits, he also attended the international conference on the Cambodian issue held in Paris, where he met with foreign ministers from 11 countries including the United States, Britain, France, Canada, Japan, and Australia. He exchanged views with them on bilateral relations and the Cambodian issue.

At the airport, Qian was greeted by Mokhesi, the Ambassador of Lesotho to China, and diplomatic officials from the embassies of Zimbabwe, Zambia, and Mozambique in China.

### 钱其琛结束非洲之行回国

1989-08-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    钱其琛结束非洲之行回国
    新华社北京8月14日电　钱其琛外长在结束了对南部非洲6国的访问之后，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    钱外长是上月26日开始对博茨瓦纳、津巴布韦、安哥拉、赞比亚、莫桑比克和莱索托访问的。
    在访问期间，他还参加了在巴黎举行的柬埔寨问题国际会议，会见了包括美国、英国、法国、加拿大、日本、澳大利亚等11个国家的外长，并与他们就双边关系和柬埔寨问题交换了意见。
    在机场迎接钱外长的有莱索托驻华大使莫凯西和津巴布韦、赞比亚、莫桑比克驻华使馆的外交官员。
