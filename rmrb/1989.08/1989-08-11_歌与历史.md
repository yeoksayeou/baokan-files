Title: Song and History
Author: Tang Feng
Date: 1989-08-11
Edition: 8

Song
When I am very melancholy, I will involuntarily sing a song;
When I am very excited, I will also long to hear a song!
Song!
Blue, white, red;
…various melodies fill my vision;
I hear all the trees singing of spring in winter;
My dark shadow sings of sunlight or the days of birth, love, and strength in the grip of illness.
Song!
In this vast world, singing is a way of living. Every person who loves life is a profound singer.

Do you remember those days without songs, Mother?
I realize that in those days without songs, there was a heavy song of the soul, but all the singers feared or could not find the emotional breakthrough. That song, thus, settled like stones in the depths of every soul’s memory.

Later, no one sang that song,
Perhaps because the souls of the horses broke out of the swamp of life and the fence of death…

Wall·History·People
All life grows outside the walls.
Every door will not easily be pushed open. Those tendrils climbing the walls silently survive and die in the silent seasons. Can they prove a truth?
We converse through the walls—
The riverbed dries up and then the tide surges; birds fly out and eventually return to the nest; walls are torn down and new walls are built; history, after great hardship, still wants to return to its original appearance; the geese’s cries approach the autumn sounds, but our shared years are still so young and moving… Can you cross those invisible walls?
Who has the courage to smash that Christmas door?
Ah, people! Only by climbing over the walls and entering the light of history can you prove your value in existence…

### 歌与历史
汤锋
1989-08-11
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    歌与历史
    汤锋
    歌
    很忧郁时会不由自主地高唱一支歌；
    很激动时同样会渴盼听到一支歌呵！
    歌！
    蓝色的白色的红色的；
    ……各色旋律弥漫在我的视野里；
    我听到所有的树木在冬天歌唱春天；
    我黑黑的影子在病魔中歌唱阳光或者是诞生爱与力量的日子。
    歌呵！
    在这个大千世界，歌唱的方式是生存的方式，每一个热爱生活的人都是深沉的歌手。
    还记得那段无歌的岁月吗？我的母亲！
    我颖悟到，无歌的岁月里蕴藏着一支沉重的心灵之歌，但所有的歌手都惟恐抑或觅不到情感的突破口，那支歌，就这样石子般沉淀在每一叶灵魂的记忆深处。
    后来再没有人为这支歌而歌唱，
    或许是因为灵魂的僵马冲出了生之沼泽死之栅栏的缘故……
    墙·历史·人
    所有的生命都生长在墙之外。
    每一扇门都不会轻易被你推开。那些沿墙而上的触须在无声的季节中，悄然生存又自我死亡，它能证实一种真理吗？
    隔着墙我们对话——
    河床干涸之后又暴涨潮汐；鸟儿飞出之后早晚终要归巢；墙拆掉之后又会有新墙砌立；历史在大难之后尚要恢复本来面貌；雁声逼近秋声但我们共同的岁月却依旧那么年轻那么动人……你越得过那些无形的墙吗？
    谁有这个胆量去砸碎那扇圣诞的门？
    呵，人！只有爬过墙去走进历史光轮你才方可证实你生存的价值……
