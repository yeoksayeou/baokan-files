Title: Xinjiang Begins Copper and Nickel Production
Author: Yālìkùn
Date: 1989-08-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, August 27th.

    Xinjiang Begins Copper and Nickel Production

    According to the Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, August 27th (Reporter: Yālìkùn) the Kālātōngkè Copper-Nickel Mine in the Altay Mountains of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region officially began production on August 25th. The history of Xinjiang not producing copper and nickel has come to an end.

### 新疆开始产铜镍
亚力坤
1989-08-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    新疆开始产铜镍
    据新华社乌鲁木齐8月27日电　（记者亚力坤）新疆维吾尔自治区阿勒泰山脉的喀拉通克铜镍矿8月25日正式投产，新疆不产铜镍的历史宣告结束。
