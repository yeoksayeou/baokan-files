Title: Xie Xuexian and Delegation Arrive in Beijing at Invitation
Author: Li Da Chuan
Date: 1989-08-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 22nd.

The first vice-chairman of the "Chinese Unification Alliance" in Taiwan and the executive director of the Young China Party, Xie Xuexian, along with a delegation of four, arrived in Beijing by airplane this afternoon. They were invited by Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Cheng Siyuan, for a three-day visit.

Vice Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Sha Li, and Deputy Director of the Office of the Patriotic Unity Association, Wang Kebin, were among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 谢学贤一行昨应邀抵京
李大川
1989-08-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    谢学贤一行昨应邀抵京
    据新华社北京8月22日电  （记者李大川）台湾“中国统一联盟”第一副主席、青年中国党执行长谢学贤一行4人，应全国政协副主席程思远邀请，今天下午乘飞机抵达北京进行为期三天的访问。
    全国政协副秘书长沙里、祖国统一联谊委员会办公室副主任王克斌等到机场迎接。
