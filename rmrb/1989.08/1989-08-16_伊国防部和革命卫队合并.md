Title: Iran's Defense Ministry and Revolutionary Guards Merge
Author:
Date: 1989-08-16
Page: 3

The Iranian Parliament on August 15th approved the merger of the Defense Ministry and the Islamic Revolutionary Guards Corps during the first session after the summer break. Iranian President and Parliament Speaker Ali Khamenei signed the merger bill proposed by the previous cabinet. Tehran Radio reported that the new department will be named the "Defense and Logistics Department for the Armed Forces." This department will oversee the logistics of Iran's conventional armed forces and the 250,000-strong Revolutionary Guards.

### 伊国防部和革命卫队合并

1989-08-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    伊国防部和革命卫队合并
    伊朗议会15日批准国防部和伊斯兰革命卫队在暑期后的第一次会上合并。主持会议的伊朗总统兼议长拉夫桑贾尼签署了由上届内阁提出的这一合并议案。德黑兰电台广播说，新部的名称为：国防和武装力量后勤部。这个部将主管伊朗常规武装力量和25万革命卫队的后勤事务。
