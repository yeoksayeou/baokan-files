Title: "Yufukang" Effective in Treating Rheumatoid Arthritis
Author: Wangquanzheng
Date: 1989-08-18
Edition: 2

    "Yufukang" Effective in Treating Rheumatoid Arthritis

    Our reporter learned that "Yufukang," a new drug for treating rheumatoid arthritis developed by young physician Shi Yushan of the Beijing Military Area General Hospital, recently won the 1989 Military Science and Technology Progress Second-Class Award. The China Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine, after repeated verification, has proven that this drug has strong anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and immune-boosting effects.

### “钰复康”治类风湿病疗效好
王圈正
1989-08-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    “钰复康”治类风湿病疗效好
    本报讯　北京军区总医院青年医师石玉山研制的治疗类风湿病新药“钰复康”，最近荣获1989年度军队科学技术进步二等奖。中国中医研究院经过反复验证，证明这种药有较强的抗炎、镇痛、提高免疫力等作用。　（王圈正）
