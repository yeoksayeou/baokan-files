Title: Shenyang Hosts Youth Calligraphy Exhibition from Eight Cities
Author: Zhang Feifei
Date: 1989-08-15
Edition: 4

The youth calligraphy exhibition from eight cities across the nation opened today in Shenyang. Nearly 140 works selected from Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Harbin, Guangzhou, Wuhan, and Chengdu are on display at this five-day exhibition. The exhibited works are diverse in form and style, with running script being bold and unrestrained, seal script being deep and steady, and regular script being elegant and refined, all of which are captivating.

### 沈阳举行八市青年书法展
张非非
1989-08-15
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    沈阳举行八市青年书法展
    据新华社沈阳八月十五日电　（记者张非非）全国八城市青年书法展览今日在沈阳开幕。选自北京、上海、天津、沈阳、哈尔滨、广州、武汉、成都八个城市的近一百四十幅作品，参加了这次为期五天的展览。参展作品形式多姿，流派各异，草书挥洒豪放，篆书深沉持重，楷书娟秀清丽，令人注目。
