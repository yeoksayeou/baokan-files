Title: U.S. Arms Sales to Third World Countries Surge
Author:
Date: 1989-08-03
Edition: 3

    In 1988, U.S. arms sales to third world countries surged, nearly matching the sales volume of the Soviet Union. According to the annual report by the U.S. Congressional Research Service, last year, U.S. arms trade increased by 66%, while the Soviet Union's decreased by 47%; the U.S. arms sales to third world countries reached $9.2 billion, nearly matching the Soviet Union's $9.9 billion in sales.

### 美销往第三世界军火剧增

1989-08-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美销往第三世界军火剧增
    1988年，美国销往第三世界的军火剧增，接近苏联的销售额。据美国国会研究处一年一度的报告，去年，美国的军火贸易上升66％，苏联下降47％；美国在第三世界的军火销售额达92亿美元，接近苏联99亿美元的销售额。
          （本报专电）
