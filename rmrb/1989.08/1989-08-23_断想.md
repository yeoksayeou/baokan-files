Title: Random Thoughts
Author: Guo Jiongqin
Date: 1989-08-23
Edition: 8

	Random Thoughts
	Guo Jiongqin

	I
	I use my soul to feel the generous gifts that nature bestows upon humanity.
	I remember to use love to view our world.

	II
	I matured in the fairy tale world created by my mother's words; I grew up in the natural world of my father's works; one day, in life, I found myself, and thus, I strive, beginning my own pursuits and creations.

	III
	When people start to think, she is so rich. At this moment, her heart overflows with warmth and deep love; but it can also harbor storms and tempests; sometimes, it is as tranquil as a windless harbor.

### 断想
郭琼芹
1989-08-23
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    断想
    郭琼芹
    一
    我用心灵来感受大自然对于人类慷慨的赐予。
    我记住，多用爱心来看待我们的世界。
    二
    我在母亲的口头创作的童话世界里成熟了；我在父亲的作品的大自然中成长了；有一天，我在生活中发现了我自己，于是，我奋斗，开始自己的追求和创造。
    三
    当人们开始思想时，她是那么富有。这时候，她的心中洋溢着温存和深刻的爱；但也会蕴藏着狂风暴雨；有时却又安谧得犹如无风的港湾。
