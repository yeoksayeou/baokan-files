Title: The State Council Appoints and Dismisses a Batch of National Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-08-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 1st.

The State Council recently appointed and dismissed a batch of national officials.

Tang Shubei was appointed as Deputy Director of the State Council's Taiwan Affairs Office, Song Muwen as Director of the Press and Publication Administration, Wong Fubai as Consul General in New York (Ambassadorial rank), and Tan Xingju as Consul General in Manchester.

Liu Mingjiu was relieved of his position as Deputy Minister of the Supervision Department, Du Daozheng of his position as Director of the Press and Publication Administration, Wong Fubai of his position as Consul General in Manchester, Tang Xingbo of his position as Consul General in New York (Ambassadorial rank), and Peng Yaoming of his position as Consul General in Tijuana.

### 国务院任免一批国家工作人员

1989-08-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国务院任免一批国家工作人员
    新华社北京8月1日电　国务院最近任免一批国家工作人员。
    任命唐树备为国务院台湾事务办公室副主任，宋木文为新闻出版署署长，翁福培为驻纽约总领事（大使衔），谭兴举为驻曼彻斯特总领事。
    免去刘鸣九的监察部副部长职务，杜导正的新闻出版署署长职务，翁福培的驻曼彻斯特总领事职务，汤兴伯的驻纽约总领事（大使衔）职务，彭耀明的驻蒂华纳总领事职务。
