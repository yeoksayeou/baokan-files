Title: Bangladesh Reorganizes Cabinet, Kazi Zafar Ahmed Takes Over as Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-08-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Dhaka, August 13th.

Bangladesh reorganized its cabinet yesterday, appointing a new vice president and replacing the prime minister. The new vice president is Prime Minister Moudud Ahmed. Moudud's position as prime minister will be taken over by Deputy Prime Minister and Information Minister Kazi Zafar Ahmed.

According to the Bangladesh News Agency, President Ershad earlier dismissed Vice President Nurul Islam, but the report did not provide further details.

### 孟加拉国改组内阁  卡齐·艾哈迈德接任总理

1989-08-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    孟加拉国改组内阁
    卡齐·艾哈迈德接任总理
    新华社达卡8月13日电　孟加拉国总统艾尔沙德昨天对内阁进行了改组，任命一位新的副总统并更换了总理。
    新任命的副总统是穆杜德·艾哈迈德总理。穆杜德的总理职务由副总理兼新闻部长卡齐·扎法尔·艾哈迈德接替。
    据孟加拉国国家通讯社报道，艾尔沙德在早些时候解除了原副总统努尔·伊斯拉姆的职务，但报道未作进一步解释。
