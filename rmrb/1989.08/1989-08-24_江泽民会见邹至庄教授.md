Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with Professor Zhu Zizhuang
Author:
Date: 1989-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 24th.

CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin this afternoon met with Professor Zhu Zizhuang of the economics department at Princeton University in the United States in Zhongnanhai. They exchanged views on China's economic education and economic reforms.

### 江泽民会见邹至庄教授

1989-08-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见邹至庄教授
    新华社北京8月24日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在中南海会见美国普林斯顿大学经济学教授邹至庄，同他就中国经济学教育和中国经济改革等问题交换了意见。
