Title: Italian Athlete Wins First Gold Medal at the World University Games
Author: Wáng Xīngpíng
Date: 1989-08-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Duisburg, August 22nd

Italian athlete Biancareddi won the first gold medal of the 15th World University Games today in the women's foil individual final held here. She defeated Susanna of Hungary with a score of 8:0.

Susanna and another Italian athlete, Trini, won the silver and bronze medals, respectively.

The five Chinese women's foil athletes who participated in this World University Games all lost today and none advanced to the top eight.

After all Chinese athletes passed the first round, except Wang Huifen who was eliminated in the second round, E Jie, Sun Hongyun, Zhu Yan, and Xiao Aihua all advanced to the top 24. Subsequently, E Jie and Sun Hongyun both advanced to the top 16, but both lost in the match to advance to the top 8.

The athletes who advanced to the top eight in the women's foil competition included three Italians, two Soviets, and one each from France, Hungary, and Poland.

The world champion team from the host country, West Germany, surprisingly lost all their matches, dashing their hopes of winning the first gold medal at this World University Games.

### 意选手获大学生运动会首枚金牌  我女花选手无一人进入前八名
王兴平
1989-08-23
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    意选手获大学生运动会首枚金牌
    我女花选手无一人进入前八名
    新华社杜伊斯堡8月22日电　（记者王兴平）意大利选手比安凯迪今天在这里举行的第15届世界大学生运动会女子花剑个人决赛中，以8∶0大胜匈牙利的苏珊娜，夺得本届运动会的第一枚金牌。
    苏珊娜和另一名意大利选手特里妮分获银牌和铜牌。
    参加本届世界大学生运动会女子花剑个人赛的五名中国选手今天先后失利，无一人打入前八名。
    中国选手在全部通过第一轮后，除王会凤在第二轮中被淘汰外，鄂杰、孙洪云、朱艳和肖爱华都进入了前24名。接着，鄂杰和孙洪云又闯进了前16名，但在争夺进入前8名的比赛中双双败北。
    进入女花前八名的有3名意大利选手、两名苏联选手和法国、匈牙利和波兰各一名选手。
    夺取女花金牌呼声最高的世界冠军联邦德国队，出人意料地全军覆没，使东道国夺取本届世界大学生运动会第一枚金牌的希望落空。
