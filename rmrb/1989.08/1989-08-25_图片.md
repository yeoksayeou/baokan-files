Title: A New Modern Refining Plant - Luoyang Refinery
Author: Geng Zexian
Date: 1989-08-25
Page: 4

A brand-new modern refinery, the Luoyang Refinery, has risen from the wilderness and is now capable of processing 300 million tons of crude oil annually. It has the capacity to handle 500 million tons of crude oil per year. The image shows a corner of the liquefied petroleum gas storage area of the refinery.
