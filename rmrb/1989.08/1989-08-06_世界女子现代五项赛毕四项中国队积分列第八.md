Title: Chinese Women's Modern Pentathlon Team Ranks Eighth After Four Events
Author:
Date: 1989-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, August 5th.

The ninth World Women's Modern Pentathlon Championships held its fourth event today: a two-kilometer cross-country run. Yosie Nowood of the United States finished the course in 6 minutes and 55 seconds, securing the top spot.

In today's cross-country run, the top three teams were the Soviet Union, Poland, and the United Kingdom. The Chinese team finished in 12th place. Among the Chinese athletes, Zhang Limin placed 24th with a time of 7 minutes and 38 seconds, Liu Yan finished 34th with 7 minutes and 52 seconds, and Wang Lili came in 43rd with 8 minutes and 6 seconds.

So far, the team with the highest cumulative score is Poland (12,240 points), followed by the Soviet Union (12,066 points), Hungary (12,044 points), the United States (11,978 points), France (11,834 points), and West Germany (11,701 points). The Chinese team has accumulated 11,612 points, placing them in 8th position.

Individual overall rankings:
1. Yosie Nowood (United States) - 4,245 points
2. Tatyana Chernetskaya (Soviet Union) - 4,207 points
3. Caroline Demerle (France) - 4,188 points

### 世界女子现代五项赛毕四项中国队积分列第八

1989-08-06
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    世界女子现代五项赛毕四项中国队积分列第八
    新华社维也纳8月5日电  第九届世界女子现代五项锦标赛，今天进行了第四个项目——两公里越野跑的比赛。美国队的诺伍德·约西以6分55秒的成绩最先跑完全程。
    在今天越野跑比赛中获得前3名的分别是苏联队、波兰队和英国队。中国队名列第12。中国的张丽敏获得第24名，成绩是7分38秒，刘燕获得第34名，7分52秒；王莉莉第43名，8分06秒。
    到目前为止，团体积分最高的是波兰队，（12240分），其次是苏联队（12066分），匈牙利队（12044分），美国队（11978分），法国队（11834分），西德队（11701）。中国队积分11612，居第8位。
    个人总成绩：
    第一：诺伍德·约西（美国）4245分
    第二：塔季阿娜·切尔涅茨卡娅（苏联）4207分
    第三：卡罗利娜·德莱默尔（法国）4188分
