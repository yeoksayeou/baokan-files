Title: ASEAN Leaders Gather in Brunei for Consultations on the Cambodian Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-08-03
Edition: 3

ASEAN leaders gathered in Brunei for consultations on the Cambodian issue

	New Delhi, August 2 (Xinhua) - Brunei: Leaders from five ASEAN countries arrived in Brunei's capital, Bandar Seri Begawan, on August 1 and 2 to attend the coming-of-age ceremony of Brunei Sultan Hassanal Bolkiah's eldest son, Billah.

	The five leaders are Philippine President Corazon Aquino, Indonesian President Suharto, Thai Prime Minister Chatichai Choonhavan, Singapore Prime Minister Lee Kuan Yew, and Malaysian Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamad.

	This is the first time they have met since the ASEAN summit in Manila in December 1987. Today, Lee Kuan Yew and Corazon Aquino's wife held talks with Brunei Sultan Hassanal Bolkiah, focusing on the Cambodian issue. Other leaders are expected to hold a series of talks on August 3 and 4.

	ASEAN supports a comprehensive political solution to the Cambodian issue and advocates for an international organization under the auspices of the United Nations to supervise the withdrawal of Vietnamese troops and the establishment of a provisional joint government in Cambodia led by Prince Sihanouk.

	This meeting coincides with the international conference on the Cambodian issue, which ASEAN foreign ministers are attending. Therefore, this gathering of ASEAN leaders is particularly noteworthy.

### 东盟领导人在文莱聚会  将就柬埔寨问题继续磋商

1989-08-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    东盟领导人在文莱聚会
    将就柬埔寨问题继续磋商
    新华社北京8月2日电　文莱消息：东南亚国家联盟5国领导人1日和2日先后抵达文莱首都斯里巴加湾港，参加文莱苏丹哈桑纳尔·博尔基亚长子比拉的成人仪式。
    这5位领导人是菲律宾总统科·阿基诺、印度尼西亚总统苏哈托、泰国总理差猜·春哈旺、新加坡总理李光耀及马来西亚总理马哈蒂尔·穆罕默德。
    这是自1987年12月在马尼拉举行东盟首脑会议以来，他们第一次聚会。今天李光耀和阿基诺夫人分别同文莱苏丹哈桑纳尔·博尔基亚举行了会谈，会谈的主题是柬埔寨问题。预计其他领导人将于3日和4日举行一系列会谈。
    东盟支持全面地政治解决柬埔寨问题，主张由联合国主持的国际机构来监督越南撤军，建立由西哈努克亲王为首的柬埔寨四方临时联合政府。
    这次会晤正是柬问题国际会议举行之际，东盟外长们参加了会议。因此，东盟领导人这次聚会颇引人注目。
