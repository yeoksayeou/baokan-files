Title: The 11th China-Japan High School Go Tournament Concludes
Author: Jiang Zhanguo
Date: 1989-08-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 11th.

The Chinese high school Go team today defeated the All-Japan high school Go team with a score of 6:0, concluding the 11th China-Japan High School Go Tournament with a total score of 20 wins and 4 losses.

### 第十一届中日高中围棋赛结束
姜占国
1989-08-12
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    第十一届中日高中围棋赛结束
    新华社东京8月11日电（记者姜占国）中国高中围棋代表队今天以6∶0大胜全日本高中围棋代表队，从而以20胜4负的总成绩结束了第十一届中日高中围棋赛。
