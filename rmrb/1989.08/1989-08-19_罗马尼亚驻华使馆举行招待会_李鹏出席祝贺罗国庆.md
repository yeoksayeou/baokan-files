Title: Romanian Embassy in China Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1989-08-19
Edition: 2

The Romanian Embassy in China held a reception to celebrate the 45th anniversary of the founding of the Socialist Republic of Romania. Premier Li Peng attended the event.

Li Peng, on behalf of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese government, and the Chinese people, extended greetings to Romanian Ambassador to China, Angelo Miculescu, and wished the Romanian people happiness and prosperity, and the country prosperity and prosperity.

Miculescu said that there is a long-standing and unbreakable friendship between the two parties, the two countries, and the peoples of Romania and China.

He told Li Peng that the Romanian Party, government, and people have always supported and will continue to support China's struggle to defend socialism.

Vice Chairman of the National People's Congress Habib Chen, State Councilor and Minister of Machine Building and Electronics Zou Jiahua, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Qian Zhengying, Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen, and Director of the International Liaison Department of the Communist Party of China Zhu Liang attended the reception.

More than 100 ambassadors from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 罗马尼亚驻华使馆举行招待会  李鹏出席祝贺罗国庆

1989-08-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    罗马尼亚驻华使馆举行招待会
    李鹏出席祝贺罗国庆
    新华社北京8月19日电　国务院总理李鹏今天出席了罗马尼亚驻华使馆为庆祝罗马尼亚社会主义共和国建国45周年而举行的招待会。
    李鹏代表中国共产党、中国政府和中国人民向罗马尼亚驻华大使安杰洛·米库列斯库表示节日的祝贺，并举杯祝愿罗马尼亚人民幸福快乐，国家繁荣昌盛。
    米库列斯库说，罗中两党、两国和两国人民之间存在着长期的、牢不可破的友谊。
    他对李鹏说，罗马尼亚党、政府和人民过去、现在和将来都坚决支持中国捍卫社会主义的斗争。
    全国人大副委员长习仲勋、国务委员兼机电部长邹家华、全国政协副主席钱正英、外交部长钱其琛、中共中央对外联络部部长朱良等出席了招待会。
    各国驻华使节100多人也出席了招待会。
