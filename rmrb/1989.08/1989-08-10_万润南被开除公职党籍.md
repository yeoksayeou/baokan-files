Title: Wan Runnan Expelled from Public Office and Party Membership
Author:
Date: 1989-08-10
Edition: 1

    Wan Runnan, former chairman and CEO of the Beijing Four Communications Group, was involved in planning, inciting, and directly participating in political turmoil and counter-revolutionary riots. After fleeing the country, he continued to engage in anti-party and anti-socialist activities. The board of directors of the Beijing Four Communications Group and the board of directors of the Beijing Four Communications New Technology Industry Stock Co., Ltd. have revoked all his positions in the group and its subsidiaries and expelled him from the group. The Haidian District People's Government has also decided to dismiss Wan Runnan from his public office. Previously, the Haidian District Committee of the Communist Party of China had expelled Wan Runnan from the party.

### 万润南被开除公职党籍

1989-08-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    万润南被开除公职党籍
    新华社北京八月十日电  四通集团公司原董事长、总裁万润南因策划、煽动、直接参与政治动乱和反革命暴乱后出逃叛国，继续进行反党反社会主义的罪恶活动。北京四通集团公司、四通新技术产业股份有限公司董事会已撤销了他在集团公司及所属公司的一切职务，并开除出四通集团公司。海淀区人民政府也做出决定，开除万润南的公职。此前，中共北京市海淀区委开除了万润南的党籍。
