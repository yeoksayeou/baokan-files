Title: PLA Civilian Staff to Wear Insignias Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1989-07-31
Edition: 2

Starting from August 1, the People's Liberation Army will begin issuing insignias to its civilian staff.

According to regulations from the General Staff Department and the General Logistics Department, civilian staff will wear the insignias during work hours. The insignia is made of aluminum, rectangular in shape, measuring 7 centimeters in length and 2 centimeters in width. The front features the August 1 Military Emblem, along with the red, black, and sky-blue symbols of the army, navy, and air force, respectively, and the word "Civilian." The back side is inscribed with "People's Liberation Army Civilian Staff."

### 我军文职干部今起佩带胸章

1989-07-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    我军文职干部今起佩带胸章
    新华社北京7月31日电　人民解放军文职干部从8月1日起陆续配发胸章。
    按照总参谋部、总后勤部的规定，文职干部上班时间将佩带文职干部胸章。胸章系铝质，呈长条形，长7厘米、宽2厘米，正面图案由八一军徽、陆海空军的正红、黑、天蓝色标及“文职”字样组成，背面有“中国人民解放军文职干部”字样。
