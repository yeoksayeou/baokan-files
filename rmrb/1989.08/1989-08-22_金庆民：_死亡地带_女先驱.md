Title: Jin Qingmin: Female Pioneer of the "Death Zone"
Author: Xiaoyi
Date: 1989-08-22
Edition: 4

The highest peak in Antarctica, Vinson Massif, is a vast kingdom of ice and snow, known to humans as the "death zone." Jin Qingmin, a deputy researcher at the Nanjing Institute of Geology and Mineral Resources, bravely accepted the challenge of death. Every day, she toiled on a 400-meter-high slope, covering a round trip of 8 kilometers, working continuously for over ten hours. On the fourth day, Jin Qingmin discovered an iron ore outcrop on a ridge. To trace and confirm the "iron-bearing zone," she fell from a cliff multiple times. Finally, the "iron-bearing zone" was found! She took out a small five-star red flag and firmly planted it on the ore body, proclaiming to the world: "This treasure is discovered by the great Chinese people!"

This scientific achievement secured China's first international voice in geological research in the heart of Antarctica.

### 金庆民：“死亡地带”女先驱
晓义
1989-08-22
第4版(国庆专版)
专栏：科教三女杰

    金庆民：“死亡地带”女先驱
    南极最高峰文森峰地域是一望无际的冰雪王国，被人类称为“死亡地带”。南京地质矿产研究所副研究员金庆民，毅然接受死神的挑战，每天，在400米比高上下艰难地活动，来回行程8公里，连续工作十几个小时。第四天，金庆民在一道山脊上发现了铁矿露头。为追索和确定“含铁带”，她多次从陡崖上摔下来。“含铁带”终于被她找到了！她拿出一面微型五星红旗，牢牢地插在矿体上，让它向全世界宣告：“这一宝藏，是伟大的中国人民发现的！” 这一科考成就，为我国首先在南极腹地的地学研究上争得了国际发言权。　晓义（附图片）
