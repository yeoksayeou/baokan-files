Title: East Germany Values Basic Scientific Research
Author:
Date: 1989-07-31
Edition: 3

East Germany places great importance on basic scientific research, believing it to be crucial for the development of new technologies, new products, and new techniques. It is also seen as an essential precondition for a country's scientific and technological progress to keep pace with the world's advanced standards.

East Germany is fully aware that today's basic scientific research lays the groundwork for tomorrow's applied research and the widespread application of new technologies, new techniques, and new products. Often, today's laboratory technologies become tomorrow's production methods.

Since the early 1980s, East Germany has integrated the development of small-batch prototype production with practical applications, thereby promoting scientific and technological progress.

### 民德重视基础科学研究

1989-07-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    民德重视基础科学研究
    据新华社柏林7月30日电　民主德国十分重视基础科学研究，认为它既是发展新工艺、新技术、新产品的关键，又是一个国家的科技进步同世界先进水平保持同步发展的不可缺少的前提。
    民主德国清醒地意识到，今天的基础科学研究，是明天应用科研和新技术、新工艺、新产品及其广泛运用的前提；今天的实验室技术，往往就是明天的生产手段。
    民主德国自80年代初以来，把研制小批量样机生产和实地运用结合在一起，对科技进步起到推动作用。
