Title: Successful Implementation of Rice-Mushroom Intercropping
Author: Dù Ruòyuán  Lǐ Róngkuān
Date: 1989-08-30
Page: 2

A new technology with promising prospects for high agricultural efficiency, "rice-mushroom intercropping," has been successfully tested in Hengyang County, Hunan Province.

"Rice-mushroom intercropping" involves utilizing the advantageous space in rice fields to cultivate mushrooms, achieving a dual harvest of rice and mushrooms. According to calculations, early-season rice can yield 450 kilograms per mu (approximately 0.067 hectares), and mushroom harvesting can reach over 2,400 kilograms per mu. The total income per mu can reach 5,100 yuan.

### 稻菌套种获成功
杜若原　李荣宽
1989-08-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    稻菌套种获成功
    本报长沙八月三十日电　一项显示农业高效益可喜前景的新技术——“稻菌套种”试验在湖南省衡阳县取得成功。
    “稻菌套种”是利用稻田有利空间栽培菌类，促使稻菌双收。据测算，早稻一季亩产稻谷四百五十公斤，摘菇二千四百多公斤，每亩收入可达五千一百元。
            （杜若原　李荣宽）
