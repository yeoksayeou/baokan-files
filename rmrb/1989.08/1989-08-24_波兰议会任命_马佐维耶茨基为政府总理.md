Title: Polish Parliament Appoints Mazowiecki as Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-08-24
Edition: 3

Polish Parliament today appointed Tadeusz Mazowiecki as the Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

He received this appointment during the 6th session of the 10th Polish Parliament. According to a live broadcast by Polish Television, out of the 460 seats in the Polish Parliament, 423 members attended today's meeting. There were 378 votes in favor, 4 against, and 41 abstentions.

Mazowiecki is 62 years old, a Catholic, and has been the chief advisor to Lech Wałęsa, the chairman of the Solidarity Trade Union, since August 1980. He is also the editor-in-chief of the Solidarity Trade Union's publication, "Solidarity Weekly." He was nominated as the Chairman of the Council of Ministers on the basis of a coalition between Solidarity Trade Union, the United Peasants' Party, and the Democratic Party. The Polish United Workers' Party, which holds a minority position in the Parliament, has expressed agreement to participate in the government led by a prime minister representing Solidarity Trade Union.

### 波兰议会任命  马佐维耶茨基为政府总理

1989-08-24
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    波兰议会任命
    马佐维耶茨基为政府总理
    新华社华沙8月24日电  波兰议会今天任命塔德乌什·马佐维耶茨基为波兰部长会议主席。
    他是在波兰第10届议会第6次会议上得到这项任命的。据波兰电视台实况报道，在总共有460议席的波兰议会中，出席今天会议的有423名议员。投赞成票的378人，反对的4人，弃权的41人。
    马佐维耶茨基现年62岁，天主教徒，1980年8月起成为团结工会主席瓦文萨的主要顾问，并且是团结工会刊物《团结周刊》的主编。他是在团结工会联合统一农民党和民主党的基础上，被提名为部长会议主席的。在议会中处于少数地位的波兰统一工人党已表示同意参加由团结工会代表任总理的政府。
