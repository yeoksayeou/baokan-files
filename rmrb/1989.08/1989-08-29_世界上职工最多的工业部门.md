Title: The Industry with the Most Workers in the World
Author:
Date: 1989-08-29
Edition: 7

Recent statistics provided by the International Labour Office show that the global food and beverage industry currently employs 30 million workers, making it the industry with the highest number of employees.

A research report released by the International Labour Office on the 22nd stated that among these 30 million workers, the majority are employed by multinational corporations.

The report predicts that from now until the year 2000, the top 100 companies in the global food and beverage industry will control nearly 75% of the global food and beverage market.

### 世界上职工最多的工业部门

1989-08-29
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    世界上职工最多的工业部门
    最近国际劳工局提供的统计数字显示，目前，全世界食品和饮料工业共雇用职工三千万名，是就业人数最多的工业部门。
    国际劳工局二十二日发表的一份研究报告说，在这三千万职工中，由跨国公司雇用的占大多数。
    报告预计，从现在起到二○○○年，世界食品与饮料工业部门中前一百家大公司将占有全球食品与饮料市场的近百分之七十五。
          （新华社）
