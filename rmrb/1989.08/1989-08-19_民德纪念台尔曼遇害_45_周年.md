Title: East Germany Commemorates the 45th Anniversary of Thälmann's Assassination
Author: Xǔ Hóngzhì
Date: 1989-08-19
Edition: 3

Yesterday marked the 45th anniversary of the assassination of Ernst Thälmann, former chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Germany. People's of the German Democratic Republic held various activities to commemorate this outstanding figure of the German and international workers' movement and unyielding anti-fascist warrior.

The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany laid wreaths at the Thälmann memorial in the Friedrichsfelde Socialist Memorial Cemetery in Berlin and at the site of Thälmann's assassination in the Buchenwald concentration camp. Workers from all walks of life, members of the Free German Youth League, and young pioneers of the Thälmann Pioneers all came to pay their respects to Thälmann with bouquets of fresh flowers.

Thälmann was shot by German fascists in the Buchenwald concentration camp on August 18, 1944.

### 民德纪念台尔曼遇害45周年
许宏治
1989-08-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    民德纪念台尔曼遇害45周年
    本报柏林8月19日电　记者许宏治报道：昨天是前德国共产党中央委员会主席恩斯特·台尔曼遇害45周年。民主德国人民纷纷举行活动，悼念这位德国和国际工人运动的卓越活动家和反法西斯的不屈战士。
    德国统一社会党中央委员会向柏林弗里德里希斯费尔德社会主义者纪念墓地的台尔曼纪念墓碑和布痕瓦尔德集中营旧址的台尔曼遇害地敬献了花圈。各界劳动群众、自由德国青年联盟盟员、台尔曼少先队队员们纷纷前来向台尔曼献上一束束鲜花。
    台尔曼是1944年8月18日在布痕瓦尔德集中营被德国法西斯枪杀的。
