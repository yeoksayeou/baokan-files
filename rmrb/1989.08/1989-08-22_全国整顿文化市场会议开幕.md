Title: National Conference on Rectifying the Cultural Market Opens
Author: Shao Jianwu
Date: 1989-08-22
Edition: 1

The national conference on cleaning up and rectifying the cultural market opened today in Beijing.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 22nd (Reporter Shao Jianwu): The national conference on cleaning up and rectifying the cultural market opened today in Beijing.

Vice Minister of Culture Gao Zhanxiang, in his speech, outlined the tasks for this conference: Based on the experience gained so far, the conference will focus on how cultural departments, as the administrative bodies of the cultural market, can both take on the responsibility of cleaning up and rectifying the social cultural market and further enrich the cultural life of the masses, promote socialist arts and culture, and organize and arrange cultural activities for the 40th anniversary of the founding of the nation. The conference will also implement a campaign of inspections and rectifications, and streamline the management system to provide a good social and cultural environment for reform and opening up.

### 全国整顿文化市场会议开幕
邵建武
1989-08-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    全国整顿文化市场会议开幕
    据新华社北京八月二十二日电  （记者邵建武）全国清理整顿文化市场工作会议今天在北京开幕。
    文化部副部长高占祥在讲话中提出了这次会议的任务：在总结前段工作经验的基础上，着重研究作为文化市场主管部门的文化部门，如何既担负起清理整顿社会文化市场的职责，又进一步活跃群众文化生活，繁荣社会主义文艺，安排和组织好国庆四十周年文化活动；既实施突击检查整顿，又理顺管理体制，为改革开放提供一个良好的社会文化环境。
