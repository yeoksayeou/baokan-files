Title: "The Heroic Chronicles of Anshan Steel with the Readers of the Capital"
Author: Zhou Qing
Date: 1989-08-17
Page: 4

The book "The Heroic Chronicles of Anshan Steel," which records the heroic and exemplary figures of Anshan Steel in various periods, was presented to the readers of the capital today. Leaders from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Propaganda Department, the Ministry of Metallurgy, and the State Press and Publication Administration praised the book at a symposium, saying it was published at an opportune time and is well done. Published by the Liaoning People's Publishing House, "The Heroic Chronicles of Anshan Steel" collects the main deeds and photographs of 200 heroic and exemplary figures.

### 《鞍钢英雄谱》与首都读者见面
周庆
1989-08-17
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    《鞍钢英雄谱》与首都读者见面
    本报北京8月17日讯　记者周庆报道：以人物名录形式记载鞍钢各个时期英雄模范人物的《鞍钢英雄谱》，今日与首都读者见面。总工会、中宣部、冶金部、新闻出版署等部门负责同志在座谈中赞扬这本书出得及时、出得好。由辽宁出版社出版的《鞍钢英雄谱》共收集了200名英雄模范的主要事迹和照片。
