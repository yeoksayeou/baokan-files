Xinhua News Agency

Friendly News Briefs

● A delegation from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, at the invitation of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Lao People's Democratic Republic, visited Laos from August 19 to 24.

During the visit, the delegations from both countries held talks in a sincere and friendly atmosphere on the signing of the "Sino-Lao Consular Treaty" and reached a basic consensus on all the clauses of the treaty.

Lao Acting Foreign Minister Thongvoua Keokanphithoun and Deputy Foreign Minister Souban Xithilavong met with the Chinese delegation.

● The minutes of the 5th meeting of the Sino-Czechoslovak Economic, Trade, and Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee were signed in Prague on August 24.

Huang Zicheng, Chairman of the Chinese side and Minister of Energy of China, and Obzina, Chairman of the Czechoslovak side and Deputy Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Government, signed the minutes on behalf of their respective governments.

The minutes stated that both sides were satisfied with the smooth development of economic and trade cooperation and scientific and technological exchanges between the two countries. They also expressed their willingness to continue supporting various new forms of economic and trade cooperation to further promote economic cooperation between the two countries.

● The Bulgaria-China Friendship Association was established in Sofia on August 24.

At the founding ceremony, Professor Ivan Iliyev, a member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and an academician of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, was elected as the chairman of the Bulgaria-China Friendship Association.

In his speech after the election, Iliyev said that the Bulgarian people are eager to learn about China's ancient culture, history, and achievements in socialist construction. He expressed that as the chairman of the Bulgaria-China Friendship Association, he would do his utmost to expand the Bulgarian people's understanding of China and further consolidate and strengthen the friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

### 友好简讯

1989-08-25
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：友好简讯

    友好简讯
    ●中华人民共和国外交部领事代表团应老挝人民民主共和国外交部的邀请从8月19日至24日对老挝进行了访问。
    访问期间，双方领事代表团在诚挚、友好的气氛中就签订《中老领事条约》举行了会谈，并就条约的全部条款基本达成一致。
    老挝代理外长通沃瓦·凯坎皮吞和副外长苏班·西提拉会见了中国代表团。
    ●中捷经济、贸易、科技合作委员会第5次会议纪要24日在布拉格签署。
    混委会中方主席中国能源部长黄毅诚和捷方主席、捷政府副总理奥布齐纳分别代表本国政府在纪要上签字。
    会议纪要指出，双方对两国经济贸易和科技合作的顺利发展感到满意，并表示将继续支持各种新的经济贸易合作方式，以进一步发展两国经济合作。
    ●保加利亚—中国友好协会24日在索非亚成立。
    在成立大会上，保共中央委员、科学院通讯院士伊万·伊利耶夫教授当选为保中友好协会主席。
    伊利耶夫在当选后发表讲话说，保加利亚人民渴望了解中国的古老文化、历史和在当前社会主义建设中取得的成就。他表示，作为保中友协主席，将尽最大的努力扩大保加利亚人民对中国的了解，进一步巩固与加强两国人民之间的友谊。
              （据新华社）
