Title: Friendly News Briefs
Author:
Date: 1989-08-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency,

    ● China's newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen, Lin Zhen, presented his credentials to the Chairman of the Supreme People's Council of the Democratic Yemen, Ali Attaas, on August 16.

    ● On August 17, the 29th session of the China-Romania Science and Technology Cooperation Committee was signed in Bucharest. The protocol stipulates 60 new science and technology cooperation projects for the two countries.

    The protocol stipulates that China and Romania will strengthen scientific and technological cooperation in the fields of chemical engineering and petrochemicals, mechanical manufacturing, metallurgy, non-ferrous metals, light industry, medicine, and agriculture. Among the 60 cooperation projects, 19 are joint research projects undertaken by both sides.

    ● A replica set of ancient Chinese bronze bells, the Bianzhong, arrived in Yokohama, Japan, on August 17. The set was purchased by Showa Women's University in Tokyo for 7 million yen. The university is renowned for its collection of bells from various countries around the world.

### 友好简讯

1989-08-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    友好简讯
    ●中国新任驻也门民主人民共和国特命全权大使林真16日向民主也门最高人民委员会主席团主席阿塔斯递交了国书。
    ●17日在布加勒斯特签署的中国和罗马尼亚科技合作委员会第二十九届会议议定书，为两国规定了60个新的科技合作项目。
    议定书规定，中罗两国将加强在化工和石油化工、机械制造、冶金、有色金属、轻工、医药、农业等方面的科技合作。在60个合作项目中有19个是双方共同承担的科研项目。
    ●一套中国古代乐器青铜编钟复制品17日从中国运抵日本横滨港。这是东京昭和女子大学花700万日元从中国购进的。这所学校以专门搜集世界各国铃铛而著名。　（据新华社）
