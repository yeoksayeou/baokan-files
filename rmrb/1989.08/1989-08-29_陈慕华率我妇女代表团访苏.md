Title: Chen Mu Hua Leads Chinese Women's Delegation to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-08-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 28th.

The Chinese Women's Delegation, led by Chen Mu Hua, Chairwoman of the All-China Women's Federation and Vice-Chairwoman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, concluded its 11-day visit to the Soviet Union and departed from Moscow by airplane on the 28th to return home.

During the visit, Yevgeny Primakov, Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Lyudmila Biryukova, met with the Chinese Women's Delegation. Both sides introduced the situation of their respective countries regarding reforms and other issues.

In talks with Soviet women's leaders, both sides expressed the desire to further develop relations between the two countries on the basis of normalizing Sino-Soviet relations.

The delegation also visited factories, enterprises, and farms in Moscow, Leningrad, and Georgia.

### 陈慕华率我妇女代表团访苏

1989-08-29
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    陈慕华率我妇女代表团访苏
    新华社莫斯科8月28日电　由中国妇联主席、人大常委会副委员长陈慕华率领的中国妇女代表团结束了对苏联为期11天的访问，28日乘飞机离开莫斯科回国。
    访问期间，苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席普里马科夫和部长会议副主席比留科娃会见了中国妇女代表团。宾主就改革等问题介绍了各自国家的情况。
    在与苏联妇女界负责人的会谈中，双方表示要在中苏关系正常化的基础上进一步发展相互之间的关系。
    代表团还在莫斯科、列宁格勒和格鲁吉亚参观了工厂、企业和农庄。
