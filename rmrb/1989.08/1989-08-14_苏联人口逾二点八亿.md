Title: Soviet Union's Population Exceeds 280 Million
Author:
Date: 1989-08-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 13th.

The first phase of the 1989 Soviet Union population census indicates that as of January 12, 1989, the Soviet Union's population has reached 286,717,000.

Over the past decade, the Soviet Union's population (including temporary foreign residents) has increased by 24,300,000.

Urban residents make up 66% of the Soviet Union's population, while rural residents account for only 34%. Over the past 10 years, the rural population has decreased by 900,000.

The Soviet Union's population ranks third in the world, following China and India.

### 苏联人口逾二点八亿

1989-08-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联人口逾二点八亿
    新华社莫斯科8月13日电　苏联1989年人口普查第一阶段调查结果表明，截至1989年1月12日止，苏联人口已达2.86717亿人。
    10年来苏联人口（包括临时居住在苏联的外国人）增加了2430万人。
    苏联居民中，城市居民占66％，农村居民仅占34％，10年内农村人口减少了90万。
    苏联人口居世界第三，仅次于中国和印度。
