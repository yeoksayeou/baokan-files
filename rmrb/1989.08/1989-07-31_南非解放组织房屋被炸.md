Title: South African Liberation Organization's House Bombed
Author:
Date: 1989-07-31
Edition: 3

A timed bomb exploded on the morning of July 30 in a house belonging to the African National Congress in the northeastern part of Lusaka. Officials from the ANC stated that the bomb was planted by South African agents.

### 南非解放组织房屋被炸

1989-07-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    南非解放组织房屋被炸
    一枚定时炸弹7月30日晨在卢萨卡市东北部一所属于南非非洲人国民大会的房子外爆炸。非国大官员说，这枚炸弹是南非特务放的。
