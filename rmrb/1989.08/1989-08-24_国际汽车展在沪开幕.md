Title: International Auto Show Opens in Shanghai
Author: Liushi'an
Date: 1989-08-24
Page: 2

The Shanghai International Automobile Industry Exhibition opened today at the exhibition center in Shanghai. Over a hundred companies from 17 countries and regions gathered in Shanghai to showcase advanced automobiles, automotive technologies, and related equipment and parts from around the world.

The exhibition, organized by the Hong Kong Yasheng Exhibition Services Limited, is the largest automotive exhibition held in China this year.

### 国际汽车展在沪开幕
刘士安
1989-08-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国际汽车展在沪开幕
    本报上海8月24日电　记者刘士安报道：上海国际汽车工业展览会今天在此间展览中心开幕，来自17个国家和地区的百家厂商荟萃上海，展示了世界上先进的各类汽车、汽车工业技术及有关的设备、零部件等。
    由香港雅式展览服务有限公司主办的这届展览会，是今年国内最具规模的汽车展览。
