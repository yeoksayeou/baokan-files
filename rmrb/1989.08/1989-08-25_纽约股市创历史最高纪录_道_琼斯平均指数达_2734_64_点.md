Title: New York Stock Market Hits Historic High  Dow Jones Average Reaches 2734.64 Points
Author: Zhang Liang
Date: 1989-08-25
Page: 3

Our reporter Zhang Liang in Washington reports: The Dow Jones Industrial Average today surged by 56.53 points to reach 2734.64 points, finally breaking the historical high of 2722.42 points set on August 25, 1987.

Local stock market experts analyze that the Dow Jones Index has risen to such heights due to several factors. First, there is a widespread prediction that the U.S. economy will experience a "soft landing." Second, corporate mergers and acquisitions are on the rise, which has driven up stock prices. Additionally, foreign investors are actively purchasing U.S. stocks. Furthermore, interest rates and the two major "twin deficits" (the federal government's budget deficit and the trade deficit) have both decreased.

### 纽约股市创历史最高纪录  道·琼斯平均指数达2734.64点
张亮
1989-08-25
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    纽约股市创历史最高纪录  道·琼斯平均指数达2734.64点
    本报华盛顿8月24日电　记者张亮报道：道·琼斯30种工业股票平均价格指数今天猛升56.53点，达2734.64点，终于突破1987年8月25日所创的历史最高纪录（2722.42点）。
    此间股市专家分析说，道·琼斯指数之所以上升到如此高度是基于如下几个因素。首先，人们普遍预测美国经济会“软着陆”。其次，企业兼并有增无已，结果抬高了股票价格。此外，外国投资者争购美国股票。再则，利率及两大“孪生赤字”（联邦政府财政赤字和外贸赤字）都有所下降。
