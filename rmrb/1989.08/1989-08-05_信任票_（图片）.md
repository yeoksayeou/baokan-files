    Vote of Confidence
    (July 28, 1989, People's Daily, Liu Yongguo illustration)

The image depicts a scene where a group of farmers are holding up a large ballot that reads "Vote of Confidence." The farmers are standing in a field, and the background shows a rural landscape with houses and trees. The illustration conveys a sense of unity and support among the farmers, emphasizing their collective trust and confidence in their leaders or in the policies being implemented. The artwork is a visual representation of the farmers' endorsement and backing, symbolizing their faith in the progress and development of their community.
