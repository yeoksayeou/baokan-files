Title: Bush Pardons Hammer
Author:
Date: 1989-08-16
Page: 3

The White House confirmed on August 15 that President Bush has decided to pardon American businessman and oil tycoon Armand Hammer, who was convicted for illegally providing campaign funds to former President Nixon. In 1976, Hammer was fined $3,000 after being accused by the special prosecutor of the "Watergate" affair of providing $54,000 in illegal campaign funds to Nixon.

### 布什总统赦免哈默

1989-08-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    布什总统赦免哈默
    美国白宫15日证实，布什总统已决定赦免因向前总统尼克松非法提供竞选经费而被判有罪的美国企业家、石油巨富哈默。1976年，哈默因“水门事件”特别检察官指控他非法向尼克松提供54000美元的竞选经费而被判罚3000美元。
