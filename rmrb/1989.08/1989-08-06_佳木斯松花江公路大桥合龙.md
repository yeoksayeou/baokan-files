Title: The Jiamusi Songhua River Highway Bridge is Completed
Author: Liu Yan
Date: 1989-08-06
Edition: 2

The largest highway bridge in terms of span in Northeast China, the Jiamusi Songhua River Highway Bridge, has been completed after four years of construction. The final piece connecting the main bridge and the approach bridge was installed today, marking the official completion and connection of the entire bridge. This bridge is located on the lower reaches of the Songhua River and is a key project during the country's Seventh Five-Year Plan. The bridge is 1,396 meters long, with the main bridge spanning nine sections totaling 910 meters, and the largest span measuring 120 meters. The bridge is expected to be completed by the end of September, one year ahead of schedule.

### 佳木斯松花江公路大桥合龙
刘衍
1989-08-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    佳木斯松花江公路大桥合龙
    本报佳木斯8月6日电　东北地区桥孔跨度最大的公路大桥——佳木斯松花江公路大桥，经过四年建设，最后一片连接主桥和引桥的挂架于今日落成，全桥正式合龙贯通。这座大桥坐落在松花江下游，是国家七五期间重点项目。桥长1396米，其中主桥九孔长度910米，最大跨度为120米。大桥预计9月下旬竣工，比计划提前一年。（刘衍）
