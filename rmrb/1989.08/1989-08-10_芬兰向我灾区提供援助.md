Title: Finland Provides Aid to China's Disaster-Affected Areas
Author:
Date: 1989-08-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 9th.

Finland's Red Cross has decided to provide 300,000 Finnish Marks (approximately $70,000) to aid the victims of floods and mudslides in China.

It is understood that this donation mainly comes from the emergency relief funds of Finland's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, with a smaller portion provided by the Finnish Red Cross.

In July, many provinces in China suffered from heavy rains, resulting in the destruction of vast areas of farmland. Currently, more than 300,000 people urgently need emergency assistance. The International Red Cross has appealed for 11 million Finnish Marks (approximately $2.5 million) in relief for China.

### 芬兰向我灾区提供援助

1989-08-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    芬兰向我灾区提供援助
    新华社赫尔辛基8月9日电　芬兰红十字会决定向中国提供30万芬兰马克（约合7万美元），以援助遭受洪水和泥石流侵害的地区的灾民。
    据悉，这笔捐款主要是由芬兰外交部的紧急救援款提供的，还有一少部分由芬兰红十字会提供。
    7月份中国许多省份暴雨成灾，大片农田被毁，目前有30余万人迫切需要紧急援助。国际红十字会已呼吁向中国提供1100万芬马克（约合250万美元）的救援。
