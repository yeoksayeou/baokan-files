Author: Wang Jingren
Date: 1989-08-25
Edition: 5

New Semester, New Atmosphere
— A Glimpse of Capital Universities

	Left Top: Renmin University of China started classes on August 14. After the school's Party Committee conducted a mobilization for ideological education on the current situation, teachers and students gathered in groups to study relevant documents from the central authorities. They analyzed the origins, nature, and harm of the turmoil and riots, raising their political awareness. This is a discussion between teachers and students from the 1987 class of the Chinese Revolutionary History major.

	Left Bottom: Students from the Health Management major at Beijing Medical University are concentrating on their physiology practical course in the laboratory.

	Right Bottom: The library at Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics is almost full, with students diligently preparing for their courses.

### 新学期新气象（图片）
王景仁
1989-08-25
第5版(国内专页（教科文）)
专栏：

    新学期新气象
    ——首都高校剪影
    左上：中国人民大学于8月14日开学。在校党委对全校进行形势教育动员以后，师生们分组集中学习中央有关文件，认清动乱、暴乱的根源、性质和危害，提高思想认识。这是中国革命史专业87级本科生的学生们正和老师一起讨论。
    左下：北京医科大学卫生事业管理专业的同学们正在实验室专心地上生理实习课。
    右下：在北京舰空航天大学的图书馆内几乎座无虚席，同学们都在认真地准备本专业的课程。
                  本报记者　王景仁摄
