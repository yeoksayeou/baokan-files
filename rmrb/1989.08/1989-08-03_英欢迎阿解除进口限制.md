Title: UK Welcomes Argentina's Removal of Import Restrictions
Author:
Date: 1989-08-03
Edition: 3

The British government on August 2 expressed that Argentina's removal of restrictions on British imports is a "beneficial action."

A spokesperson for the British Foreign Office stated that Britain is looking forward to the implementation of this decision.

However, the Foreign Office refused to comment on whether this positive move by Argentina would lead to a restoration of relations between the two countries.

### 英欢迎阿解除进口限制

1989-08-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英欢迎阿解除进口限制
    英国政府2日表示，阿根廷解除对英国进口的限制是一个“有益的行动”。
    英国外交部发言人说，英国期待着这一决定付之实施。
    然而，外交部拒绝说明，阿根廷的这一积极行动是否将导致两国间关系的恢复。
