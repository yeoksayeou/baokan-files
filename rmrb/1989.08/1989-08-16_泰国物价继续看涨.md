Title: Thailand's Prices Continue to Rise
Author:
Date: 1989-08-16
Edition: 3

Thailand's official figures show that the country's inflation rate for the first half of this year was 4.4%, and it is estimated that the annual rate will reach 5%, higher than last year's 3.8%. Financial experts here point out that, looking at the various factors affecting prices, the situation in the second half of the year will be severe.

### 泰国物价继续看涨

1989-08-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    泰国物价继续看涨
    泰国官方发表的数字表明，今年上半年泰国通货膨胀率为4.4％，估计今年全年将达5％，高于去年的3.8％。此间金融界人士指出，纵观影响物价的各种因素，下半年的形势将是严峻的。　（据新华社）
