Title: An Asteroid Approaches Earth, No Collision Risk, Public Need Not Worry
Author:
Date: 1989-08-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 22nd August

American scientists have discovered that an asteroid is approaching Earth, but there is no risk of collision.

Eleanor Helin, a planetary expert at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, said that the asteroid, named 1989PB, is 25 million miles away from Earth and will be one of the largest celestial bodies to come closest to Earth this century.

Eleanor discovered the asteroid from photographs taken at the Palomar Observatory northeast of San Diego. Amateur astronomers will be able to see the asteroid in the direction of the constellation Cassiopeia when it is closest to Earth.

### 一颗小行星飞近地球  无碰撞危险世人免忧

1989-08-23
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    一颗小行星飞近地球  无碰撞危险世人免忧
    新华社华盛顿8月22日电　美国科学家发现一颗小行星将要飞近地球，但无碰撞危险。
    美国宇航局喷气推进实验室的行星专家埃莉诺·赫林说，这颗命名为1989PB的小行星距地球2500万英里，将是本世纪最接近地球的比较大的天体之一。
    埃莉诺是在圣地亚哥东北部帕洛马天文台从望远镜拍摄的照片上发现这颗小行星的。业余天文爱好者在1989PB距地球最近时，用望远镜在仙后座方向就能看到这颗小行星。
