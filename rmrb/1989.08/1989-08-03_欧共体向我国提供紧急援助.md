Title: The European Community Provides Emergency Aid to China
Author: Wei Wei
Date: 1989-08-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, August 2nd. Reported by Wei Wei: The European Community Commission decided on August 2nd that, in light of the recent floods in Sichuan Province, China, it will provide approximately 500,000 European Currency Units (about $550,000) in emergency aid to China.

This aid will be implemented by the "Doctors Without Borders" organization.

### 欧共体向我国提供紧急援助
魏崴
1989-08-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    欧共体向我国提供紧急援助
    本报布鲁塞尔8月2日电　记者魏崴报道：欧洲共同体委员会8月2日决定，鉴于中国四川省最近发生水灾，向中国提供约50万欧洲货币单位（约合55万美元）的紧急援助。
    这项援助将由“无国界医生”组织负责实施。
