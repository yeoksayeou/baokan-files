Title: Soviet Athletes Break Two World Records in Model Car Racing
Author:
Date: 1989-08-06
Page: 4

Sports Column

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 5th.

At the European Model Car Championship held in Minsk, the capital of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic, Soviet athletes broke two world records.

A microcar with a cylinder capacity of 1.5 cubic centimeters, driven by A. Kartuzikov, reached a speed of 247.184 kilometers per hour, breaking the record of 243.440 kilometers per hour set by Bulgarian athlete M. Leshkov.

In the competition for model cars with a cylinder capacity of 2.5 cubic centimeters, A. Grebionkin's small car reached a speed of 269.469 kilometers per hour, surpassing the record of 269.138 kilometers per hour set by Swede H. Lundqvist.

### 苏联选手破两项汽车模型赛世界纪录

1989-08-06
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    苏联选手破两项汽车模型赛世界纪录
    新华社莫斯科8月5日电　在苏联白俄罗斯加盟共和国首都明斯克举行的欧洲汽车模型锦标赛上，苏联选手打破两项世界纪录。
    由阿·卡尔图济科夫操纵的汽缸容积为1.5立方厘米的微型汽车时速达247.184公里，刷新了保加利亚运动员马·列什科夫保持的243.440公里的纪录。
    在汽缸容积为2.5立方厘米的汽车模型角逐中，阿·格列比翁金操纵的小汽车时速达到269.469公里，超过了瑞典人哈·隆基斯特创造的269.138公里的纪录。
