Title: The Soviet Union Will Launch a Carrier Rocket
Author:
Date: 1989-08-09
Edition: 3

The Soviet Union will launch a carrier rocket in the Pacific region between August 11 and 21 this year.

According to a TASS announcement, the designated area is a quadrilateral formed by the coordinates: 41 degrees 23 minutes north latitude and 174 degrees 26 minutes west longitude, 42 degrees 03 minutes north latitude and 172 degrees 54 minutes west longitude, 34 degrees 54 minutes north latitude and 168 degrees 04 minutes west longitude, and 34 degrees 14 minutes north latitude and 169 degrees 36 minutes west longitude.

Additionally, there is a circular area centered at 0 degrees 15 minutes south latitude and 153 degrees 20 minutes west longitude, with a radius of 30 nautical miles.

TASS stated that the Soviet government has requested that all governments notify relevant agencies to prevent ships and aircraft from entering the aforementioned areas and airspace between 6 AM and 7 PM local time to ensure safety.

### 苏将发射运载火箭

1989-08-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏将发射运载火箭
    新华社莫斯科8月8日电　苏联将于今年8月11日至21日在太平洋地区发射运载火箭。
    据塔斯社宣布，该地区为：由北纬41度23分和西经174度26分，北纬42度03分和西经172度54分，北纬34度54分和西经168度04分，北纬34度14分和西经169度36分四个坐标构成的方形区。
    以南纬0度15分和西经153度20分为中心，半径30海里的圆形区。
    塔斯社说，苏联政府请求各国政府通知有关机构，不让船舶和飞机在当地时间6时至19时进入上述地区及空域，以保证安全。
