Title: Hunan Holds Gathering to Commemorate 40th Anniversary of Peaceful Liberation
Author: Liu Zhiquan  Zhu Zhenji
Date: 1989-08-03
Edition: 4

    Hunan Holds Gathering to Commemorate 40th Anniversary of Peaceful Liberation

    Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, August 3 (Reporters Liu Zhiquan, Correspondent Zhu Zhenji) On August 4, 40 years ago, the famous patriotic generals of the Kuomintang, General Peng Xian and General Chen Mingren, led a rebellion in Changsha. This afternoon, more than 200 leaders from the Communist Party, government, military, and various democratic parties in Hunan Province, along with representatives from various sectors, gathered to commemorate this rebellion and the 40th anniversary of Hunan's peaceful liberation.

    In today's commemorative meeting, the Secretary of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party, Xiong Qingquan, said that we must continue to strengthen cooperation and joint efforts with various democratic parties and patriotic figures from all walks of life to jointly revitalize Hunan.

### 湖南集会纪念和平解放四十周年
刘志权  朱振基
1989-08-03
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    湖南集会纪念和平解放四十周年
    新华社长沙8月3日电　（记者刘志权、通讯员朱振基）40年前的8月4日，国民党著名爱国将领程潜、陈明仁将军在长沙领衔通电起义。今天下午，湖南省党政军、民主党派负责人及各界人士200多人隆重集会，纪念这次起义及湖南和平解放四十周年。
    中共湖南省委书记熊清泉在今天的纪念会上说，要继续加强与各民主党派和各界爱国人士的合作共事，共同振兴湖南。
