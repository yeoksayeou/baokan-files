Title: The European Youth Women's Basketball Tournament Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-08-13
Edition: 4

The eighth European Youth Women's Basketball Championship concluded today in Timisoara, a city in western Romania. In the final match, the Czechoslovakian team narrowly defeated the Romanian team with a score of 58 to 57, securing the championship. The Romanian team took the silver medal, while the Soviet Union team finished in third place.

### 欧洲少年女篮赛结束

1989-08-13
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    欧洲少年女篮赛结束
    新华社布加勒斯特八月十二日电　第八届欧洲少年女子篮球锦标赛今天在罗马尼亚西部的蒂米什瓦拉市结束，捷克斯洛伐克队在决赛中以五十八比五十七险胜罗马尼亚队，荣获冠军。罗马尼亚队获亚军，苏联队列第三位。
