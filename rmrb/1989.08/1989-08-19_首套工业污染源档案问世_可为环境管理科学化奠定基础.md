Title: The First Set of Industrial Pollution Source Archives Emerges
Author: Xie Lianhui
Date: 1989-08-19
Page: 1

People's Daily, Beijing, August 18th - Reporter Xie Lianhui reports: How can we assess China's industrial pollution situation? Today, the "National Industrial Pollution Survey, Evaluation, and Research" passed national appraisal. Based on nearly 30 million pieces of basic industrial pollution survey data, 1,949 industrial pollution source textual archives and 153 computer databases have been established nationwide. This forms the first complete and systematic set of industrial pollution source archives and maps of industrial pollution sources nationwide, changing the long-standing situation of unclear industrial pollution data in China. This lays a solid foundation for the scientific and quantitative management of the environment.

The national industrial pollution source survey was jointly organized by the State Environmental Protection Administration, the State Economic Commission, the State Science and Technology Commission, the State Statistics Bureau, and the Ministry of Finance. Over 850,000 people participated in this nationwide, large-scale industrial pollution source survey for the first time. Experts who participated in the appraisal believe that this achievement is in line with national conditions and has high academic and practical value.

China is a major energy-producing and energy-consuming country, ranking third in the world in both total production and consumption. The main pollutants nationwide include soot, sulfur dioxide, nitrogen oxides, carbon monoxide, and five other types; 17 provinces and cities have severe pollution, including Jiangsu, Liaoning, Sichuan, and Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, and Chongqing; the main polluting industries include the power, steam, and hot water supply industry, building materials and non-metallic mineral products industry, chemical industry, and five other industries.

According to recent statistics from 10 provinces and cities for some enterprises, the comprehensive utilization of "three wastes" has achieved economic benefits of tens of millions of yuan.

### 首套工业污染源档案问世  可为环境管理科学化奠定基础
谢联辉
1989-08-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    首套工业污染源档案问世
    可为环境管理科学化奠定基础
    本报北京8月18日讯　记者谢联辉报道：如何评价我国工业污染情况？今天通过国家成果鉴定的《全国工业污染调查评价与研究》，经过对近3000多万个工业污染基础调查数据研究，在全国各地建立起1949个工业污染源文字档案和153个计算机数据库，形成了全国第一套完整、系统的工业污染源档案资料，绘制了全国工业污染源图集，改变了我国长期以来工业污染底数不清的状况，为环境管理科学化和定量化奠定了良好的基础。
    全国工业污染源调查，是由国家环保局、国家经委、国家科委、国家统计局和财政部联合部署，有85万多人参加的全国性首次大规模工业污染源调查。参加鉴定的专家认为，这项成果符合国情，具有较高的学术价值和适用价值。
    我国是能源生产大国又是能源消费大国，生产总量和消费总量均居世界第三位。全国主要污染物有烟尘、二氧化硫、氮氧化物、一氧化碳等8种；污染严重的省市17个，其中有江苏、辽宁、四川等和北京、天津、上海、重庆等；主要污染行业有电力蒸汽热水产供业、建材非金属矿制品业、化学工业等8个行业。
    据前不久对10个省市的部分企业统计，开展“三废”综合利用，已取得十几亿元的经济效益。
