Title: Song Jian Meets Nayibeg
Author:
Date: 1989-08-24
Edition: 2

	Song Jian Meets Nayibeg

	Beijing, August 24 - State Councilor and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission Song Jian met with Nayibeg, Secretary-General of the International Organizing Committee of the World Mining Congress, this morning at the Great Hall of the People.

	Song Jian briefed Nayibeg on the situation in China and expressed confidence in the successful holding of the 14th World Mining Congress in Beijing next May. Nayibeg expressed satisfaction with the preparations made by the relevant Chinese authorities for the 14th Mining Congress.

### 宋健会见纳依贝格

1989-08-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    宋健会见纳依贝格
    本报北京8月24日讯　国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健今天上午在人民大会堂会见了世界采矿大会国际组委会秘书长纳依贝格。
    宋健向纳依贝格介绍了中国的形势，表示对明年5月在北京成功地召开第十四届世界采矿大会充满信心。纳依贝格对中国有关方面为十四届采矿大会所做的准备表示满意。
