Title: African Cotton Harvest Bountiful
Author:
Date: 1989-08-28
Edition: 3

    According to a report by Morocco's _Maghreb_ newspaper on August 23, 10 French-speaking countries in West and Central Africa have achieved a record-breaking cotton harvest this year, producing 1.25 million tons of seed cotton.

    The report states that the cotton planting area in these 10 countries has doubled compared to 30 years ago, reaching over 1 million hectares. The yield per unit area has also increased from 400 kilograms per hectare in 1970 to the current 1,000 kilograms per hectare.

    Cotton production plays a crucial role in the national economies of countries such as Mali, Burkina Faso, and Chad, accounting for 40-60% of their foreign trade exports.

### 非洲棉花丰收

1989-08-28
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    非洲棉花丰收
    新华社拉巴特电  据摩洛哥《马格里布报》8月23日报道，西非和中非10个法语国家本年度棉花获得创纪录丰收，共产籽棉125万吨。
    据报道，这10国的棉花种植面积比30年前翻了一番，达100余万公顷。单位面积产量也从1970年的每公顷400公斤提高到目前的1000公斤。
    马里、布基纳法索和乍得等国的棉花生产在国民经济中具有重要地位，占这些国家外贸出口的40—60％。
