Title: A Batch of Anti-Corruption and Promoting Integrity Regulations are Being Expedited
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Edition: 1

China's anti-corruption efforts will gradually move towards legalization and institutionalization. A series of laws and regulations aimed at combating and preventing corruption, and promoting integrity, are currently in the process of being formulated.

It is reported that the Supervision Department is expediting the formulation of a series of regulations, including: "Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Supervision," "Provisional Regulations on Administrative Sanctions for Dereliction of Duty and Malfeasance by State Administrative Personnel," "Provisional Regulations on the Declaration of Assets and Income by State Administrative Personnel," and "Provisional Regulations on the Implementation of Administrative Sanctions for Bribery and Corruption by State Administrative Personnel."

### 一批肃贪倡廉法规抓紧制定

1989-08-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

   一批肃贪倡廉法规抓紧制定
    我国廉政建设将逐步走上法律化、制度化轨道，一批惩治和防止腐败、促进廉政建设的法律、法规正在制定之中。
    据介绍，监察部正在抓紧制定的一系列法规包括：《中华人民共和国监察条例》、《国家行政机关工作人员失职渎职行政处分暂行规定》、《国家行政机关工作人员申报财产收入的暂行规定》、《国家行政机关工作人员贪污贿赂行政处分暂行规定实施细则》等。
