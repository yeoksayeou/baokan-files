Title: Argentina and Britain Begin Direct Talks
Author: Lǐ Zhìmíng Liú Kāichéng
Date: 1989-08-17
Edition: 3

Argentina and Britain Begin Direct Talks
UN Secretary-General Welcomes the Move

BUENOS AIRES, Aug. 16 (Xinhua) -- The Argentine Foreign Ministry issued a news release at noon today, announcing that Argentina's special envoy Lucio Garcia del Solar began talks in New York today with Britain's permanent representative to the United Nations, Crispin Tickell.

The release said that according to the agreement reached by both sides, the purpose of the talks is to establish conditions and a schedule for direct negotiations between Argentina and Britain, without prejudice to the positions of both sides on the sovereignty of the Malvinas Islands, South Georgia, and the South Sandwich Islands.

UNITED NATIONS, Aug. 16 (Xinhua) -- UN Secretary-General Javier Perez de Cuellar today welcomed the resumption of talks between British and Argentine representatives in New York.

Perez de Cuellar noted that the resumption of talks between the two countries is "an encouraging development."

### 阿根廷英国开始直接会晤  联合国秘书长表示欢迎
李志明  刘开宸
1989-08-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿根廷英国开始直接会晤
    联合国秘书长表示欢迎
    本报布宜诺斯艾利斯8月16日电　记者李志明报道：阿根廷外交部今天中午12时发布一项新闻公报，宣布阿根廷政府特别代表卢西奥·加西亚·德尔索拉尔今天开始在纽约与英国常驻联合国代表克里斯平·蒂克尔举行会晤。
    公报说，根据双方达成的协议，会晤的目的是建立阿英直接谈判的条件和日程，而不损害各方对马尔维纳斯群岛、南乔治亚和南三维治群岛主权的立场。
    本报联合国8月16日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天对英国和阿根廷代表在纽约恢复谈判一事表示欢迎。
    德奎利亚尔指出，两国恢复谈判是“令人鼓舞的发展”。
