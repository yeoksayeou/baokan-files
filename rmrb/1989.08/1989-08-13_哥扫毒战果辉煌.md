Title: Colombia's Anti-Drug Campaign Achieves Stunning Results
Author:
Date: 1989-08-13
Edition: 3

    Colombia's police recently launched a large-scale anti-drug operation in the country's Santander province, achieving stunning results. According to the Colombian police, anti-drug officers have seized and destroyed eight large drug processing plants in Santander province in recent days, confiscating 1,200 kilograms of pure cocaine, and arresting fourteen drug traffickers. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 哥扫毒战果辉煌

1989-08-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    哥扫毒战果辉煌
    哥伦比亚警方近日在该国的桑坦德尔省开展大规模扫毒行动，战果辉煌。据哥警方十二日声称，扫毒警察近几天在桑坦德尔省查获并捣毁大型毒品加工厂八座，缴获纯可卡因一千二百公斤，还抓获十四名毒品贩子。　（据新华社）
