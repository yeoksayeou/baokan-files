Title: Air France Plane Hijacked, Hijacking Incident Resolved
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-08-24
Edition: 3

An Air France plane was hijacked on its way from Paris to Algiers.

Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, August 23 — The hijacking of an Air France plane flying from Paris to Algiers was resolved this afternoon at Houari Boumediene Airport here.

After the plane landed, the hijacker immediately ordered the passengers to show their passports and then released all the Algerian passengers on board. The hijacker was a 30-year-old Algerian businessman residing in France named Bendada. Half an hour after releasing the Algerian passengers, he expressed his willingness to end the hijacking incident. Algerian authorities arrested him.

The hijacker later told the press that he wanted to draw international attention to the need to end violence in Lebanon and Palestine, to convene an international conference on the Lebanese issue, to demand that the PLO engage in dialogue with Israel, and to release all hostages and prisoners in the Middle East.

### 法航一客机被劫持  劫机事件已获解决
林皎明
1989-08-24
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    法航一客机被劫持  劫机事件已获解决
    本报开罗8月23日电　记者林皎明报道：法国一架“空中客车300”客机在由巴黎飞往阿尔及尔途中被劫持。
    新华社阿尔及尔8月23日电　劫持法国航空公司从巴黎飞往阿尔及尔的大型客机的事件今天下午在这里的胡阿里·布迈丁国际机场已获解决。
    当这架客机在机场降落后，劫机者立即命令乘客出示护照，然后释放了机上所有阿尔及利亚乘客。劫机者是一名旅居法国的阿尔及利亚商人，现年30岁，名叫本达达。在释放阿尔及利亚乘客后半小时，他表示愿意结束劫机事件。阿尔及利亚当局将他逮捕。
    劫机者事后向报界说，他想提请国际舆论注意，必须结束在黎巴嫩和巴勒斯坦的暴力行动、召开关于黎巴嫩问题的国际会议、要求巴解组织同以色列进行对话以及释放在中东的所有人质和被监禁者。
