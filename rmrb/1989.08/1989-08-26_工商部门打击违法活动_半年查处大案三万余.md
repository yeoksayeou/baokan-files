Title: Commerce Authorities Crack Down on Illegal Activities
Author: Pan Gang
Date: 1989-08-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 26th.

The State Administration for Industry and Commerce (SAIC) Vice Minister Gan Guoping announced today that in the first half of this year, industrial and commercial administrative authorities nationwide investigated and handled over 380,000 cases of speculative and illegal activities. Among these, 38,300 were major cases, an increase of more than 74% compared to the same period last year. Additionally, 22,400 major cases of activities with the nature of speculation and profiteering were handled, an increase of 65.6%. The total amount of fines and confiscated funds remitted to the treasury amounted to over 370 million yuan, an increase of 131.4%.

### 工商部门打击违法活动  半年查处大案三万余
潘岗
1989-08-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    工商部门打击违法活动
    半年查处大案三万余
    本报北京8月26日讯　记者潘岗报道：国家工商局副局长甘国屏今天宣布，今年上半年，全国工商行政管理机关共查处投机违法违章案件逾38万件，其中大案3.83万件，比去年同期增加74％以上；查处属于投机倒把性质的大案2.24万件，增加65.6％。上缴财政的罚没款总金额3.7亿多元，增加131.4％。
