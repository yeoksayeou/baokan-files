Title: China's Youth Women's Volleyball Team Loses to Japan
Author:
Date: 1989-08-09
Page: 4

Sports

Xinhua News Agency, Lima, August 8th.

China's youth women's volleyball team lost to Japan 1-3 on August 8th in Lima. The set scores were 15-1, 7-15, 7-15, and 8-15. As a result, they missed the opportunity to enter the top four in the fifth World Youth Women's Volleyball Championship.

### 我青年女排负于日本队

1989-08-09
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    我青年女排负于日本队
    新华社利马八月八日电  中国青年女排八日在利马以一比三负于日本队，四局的比分是，十五比一，七比十五，七比十五，八比十五，失去了进入第五届世界青年女排锦标赛前四名的资格。
