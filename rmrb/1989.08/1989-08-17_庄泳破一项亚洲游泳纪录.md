Title: Zhang Yong Breaks an Asian Swimming Record
Author: Jiang Zhan Guo
Date: 1989-08-17
Edition: 4

Chinese swimmer Zhang Yong set a new Asian record in the women's 200-meter freestyle preliminary heats today at the third Pan Pacific Swimming Championships held here.

Zhang clocked 2 minutes 1.61 seconds to break the previous Asian record of 2:01.72 set by Japanese swimmer Ryoka Yano in July 1983.

The third Pan Pacific Swimming Championships opened today, with athletes from 17 countries and regions competing in the events.

### 庄泳破一项亚洲游泳纪录
姜占国
1989-08-17
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    庄泳破一项亚洲游泳纪录
    新华社东京8月17日电（记者姜占国）中国选手庄泳今天上午在这里举行的第三届泛太平洋游泳赛女子200米自由泳预赛中，以2分1秒61的成绩创一项亚洲纪录。
    原2分1秒72的亚洲纪录是日本选手梁濑香织于1983年7月创造的。
    第三届泛太平洋游泳赛今天拉开战幕，来自17个国家和地区的选手将参加角逐。
