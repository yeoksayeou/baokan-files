Title: Shanghai Hosts International Children's Grand Gala
Author: Zhou Jie Rong
Date: 1989-08-16
Edition: 2

Shanghai hosted the "89 Shanghai International Children's Grand Gala" today. The event was jointly organized by the Shanghai Municipal People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, the China Welfare Institute, and the Shanghai Soong Ching Ling Foundation.

Children's art troupes from five countries, including Czechoslovakia, Japan, Thailand, the Soviet Union, and China, participated in the gala.

The "89 Shanghai International Children's Grand Gala" is a grand event themed around "Peace, Friendship, and Health," featuring a variety of activities.

### 上海举办国际少儿大联欢
周解蓉
1989-08-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    上海举办国际少儿大联欢
    新华社上海8月15日电　（记者周解蓉）由上海市人民对外友好协会、中国福利会、上海宋庆龄基金会等联合举办的“89上海国际少年儿童大联欢”今天在上海开幕。
    参加大联欢的有捷克斯洛伐克、日本、泰国、苏联和中国等5个国家的11个少儿文艺团体。
    “89上海国际少年儿童大联欢”是以“和平、友谊、健康”为宗旨的大联欢活动，形式多种多样。
