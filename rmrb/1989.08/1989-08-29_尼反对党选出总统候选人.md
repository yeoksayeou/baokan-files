Title: Nicaragua's Opposition Party Selects Presidential Candidate

Author:

Date: 1989-08-29

Edition: 3

Nicaragua's largest opposition party, the Democratic Conservative Party, selected Eduardo Molina Paraiso and Milton Rovelto as its candidates for president and vice president, respectively, for next year's February elections on the 27th.

### 尼反对党选出总统候选人

1989-08-29
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    尼反对党选出总统候选人
    尼加拉瓜第一大反对党民主保守党27日推选爱德华多·莫利纳·帕拉西奥斯和米尔顿·罗夫莱托为该党参加明年2月大选的正、副总统候选人。
