Title: The First China Folk Art Festival to Be Held in September
Author:
Date: 1989-08-07
Edition: 4

The First China Folk Art Festival will be held from September 20th to 24th in Dalian City.

It is reported that during the festival, folk art troupes from Guizhou, Xinjiang, Yunnan, Sichuan, Shaanxi, Shanxi, and Hebei will perform wonderful programs. Some renowned folk singers and Peking Opera performers from across the country will also participate in the performances. The festival will showcase over 3,000 kinds of folk artworks. The First Folk Art Festival is jointly organized by the China Folk Artists Association and the Dalian Economic and Technological Development Zone Management Committee.

### 首届中国民间艺术节九月举行

1989-08-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    首届中国民间艺术节九月举行
    本报北京八月七日讯  首届中国民间艺术节将于九月二十日至二十四日在大连市举行。
    据悉：民间艺术节期间，贵州、新疆、云南、四川、陕西、山西、河北等地的民间艺术团体将演出精彩节目，一些全国知名的民歌手和京剧表演艺术家也将参加演出；艺术节上展销的民间艺术品达三千余种。首届民间艺术节由中国民间文艺家协会和大连市经济技术开发区管理委员会联合主办。
