Title: I Won the Silver Medal in the World Youth Greco-Roman Wrestling Championship
Author: Zou Guochang
Date: 1989-08-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, August 6th.

The 1989 World Youth Greco-Roman Wrestling Championship concluded today here. Chinese athletes won one silver medal and two fifth places, with the team ranking sixth overall. Soviet athletes performed the best, winning 5 out of the 10 weight categories.

The competition began on the 4th and featured 136 athletes from 28 countries.

Despite the large number of participants and strong competition, China's four young athletes still achieved good results. Sheng Zeda won the silver medal in the 52 kg category, while Jiang Wei and Su Yilafu secured fifth places in the 48 kg and 57 kg categories, respectively. Wang Hai finished tenth in the 62 kg category.

### 我获世界青年古典式摔跤赛一项亚军
邹国昌
1989-08-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    我获世界青年古典式摔跤赛一项亚军
    新华社布达佩斯8月6日电  （记者邹国昌）1989年世界青年古典式摔跤冠军赛今天在这里结束。中国选手获一项亚军、两个第五名，团体总分列第六位。苏联选手成绩最佳，共获10个级别中的5项冠军。
    这次比赛是4日开始的，有28个国家的136名运动员参赛。
    这次比赛参赛人数最多、强手云集，但中国4名年轻选手仍然取得了较好的成绩。盛泽田过关斩将，获得52公斤级的亚军，蒋伟和苏伊拉夫分别获得48公斤级和57公斤级的第五名。王海获62公斤级第十名。
