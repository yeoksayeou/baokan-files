Title: United Nations Investigation Team to Meet Khmer Resistance Representatives in Bangkok
Author:
Date: 1989-08-11
Edition: 3

Thai Foreign Minister Siddhi Savetsila said today that the United Nations investigation team will meet with representatives of the Khmer resistance forces in Bangkok, not in the Thai-Cambodian border area, next week.

The 15-member United Nations investigation team, led by Major General Martti Ahtisaari, left Bangkok on August 7. This team, dispatched by the Paris International Conference on Cambodia, will spend about a week in Cambodia to gather intelligence for the ministerial-level meeting of the Paris International Conference scheduled for the end of August.

### 联合国调查组即将在曼谷会见柬埔寨抵抗力量代表

1989-08-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联合国调查组即将在曼谷会见柬埔寨抵抗力量代表
    新华社曼谷8月10日电  泰国外长西提·沙卫西拉今天在此间说，联合国赴柬埔寨实地调查组下周将在曼谷，而不是泰柬边界地区，会见柬埔寨抵抗力量代表。
    由马丁·韦德塞特中将率领的联合国赴柬埔寨实地调查组一行15人是7日离开曼谷。这个由关于柬埔寨问题的巴黎国际会议派遣的调查组将在柬埔寨境内活动一周左右，为8月底召开的巴黎国际会议部长级会议收集情报。
