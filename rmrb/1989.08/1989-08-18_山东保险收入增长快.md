Title: Rapid Growth in Shandong's Insurance Revenue
Author: Sūn Niànjù
Date: 1989-08-18
Edition: 2

    Shandong's insurance industry has maintained a rapid development momentum this year. By the end of July, the total revenue from domestic and foreign business reached 580 million yuan, an increase of 150 million yuan compared to the same period last year, representing a growth of 34.99%. This completes 76.39% of the annual plan. Among these, the revenue from domestic property insurance and short-term personal insurance both ranked first among all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions nationwide.

    While the insurance business has developed rapidly, its economic compensation function has also been fully utilized. In the first half of the year, the province handled 45,361 property insurance claims and made 33,971 personal insurance payouts, totaling over 100 million yuan in compensation.

### 山东保险收入增长快
孙念聚
1989-08-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    山东保险收入增长快
    本报讯　今年以来，山东省保险事业保持了迅速发展的好势头。截止7月底，国内外业务总收入达5.8亿元，比去年同期增收1.5亿元，增长34.99％；完成年计划的76.39％。其中国内财产险和短期人身险的业务收入均居全国各省、市、自治区的首位。
    在保险业务迅速发展的同时，经济补偿作用也得到了充分的发挥。上半年，全省共处理财产险赔案45361件，人身险给付33971人次，共支付赔款1亿多元。　（孙念聚）
