Title: Neptune May Have Sixteen Satellites
Author:
Date: 1989-08-12
Edition: 3

Neptune may have 14 to 16 satellites.

According to a report by the *Daily Telegraph*, Dr. Mason of the British Astronomical Association said that Neptune may have 14 to 16 satellites.

Astronomers discovered two of Neptune's satellites in 1846 and 1949, respectively. Since July, four more satellites have been discovered as the U.S. "Voyager 2" spacecraft approached Neptune. "Voyager 2" has been traveling in space for 12 years, covering a distance of 7 billion kilometers since its departure from Earth. It will fly by Neptune on August 24-25.

### 海王星可能有十六个卫星

1989-08-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    海王星可能有十六个卫星
    新华社伦敦8月11日电　据《每日电讯报》报道，英国天文协会的梅森博士说，海王星可能有14—16个卫星。
    天文学家分别于1846年和1949年发现海王星的两个卫星。当美国的“旅行者”2号宇宙飞船飞近海王星时，从7月份以来又新发现4个卫星。“旅行者”2号宇宙飞船离开地球后，在空间已飞行12年共70亿公里，8月24—25日将飞过海王星。
