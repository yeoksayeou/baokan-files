Title: Zhang Jingfu Meets with Canadian Chinese Peng Anna
Author:
Date: 1989-08-13
Edition: 2

Zhang Jingfu, chairman of the China International Talent Exchange Foundation, met with Peng Anna, president of the Canadian Teck Cominco Group and a Canadian citizen of Chinese descent, this morning in the Great Hall of the People. Zhang praised Peng for her efforts in promoting economic, trade, technological, and talent exchanges and cooperation between Canada and China.

Peng has been in China since July 18, during which time she visited the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and held business talks with the China National Gold Group, the China International Trust and Investment Corporation, and the China Great Wall Industry Corporation.

### 张劲夫会见加籍华人彭安娜

1989-08-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    张劲夫会见加籍华人彭安娜
    新华社北京8月13日电　中国国际人才交流协会主席张劲夫今天上午在人民大会堂会见加拿大泰加国际集团总裁、加籍华人彭安娜，赞扬她为促进加拿大同中国在经济、贸易、技术和人才的交流与合作方面所做的努力。
    彭安娜自7月18日来华后，曾到内蒙古自治区参观访问，并同中国黄金总公司、中国国际信托投资公司、中国长城工业总公司举行了业务会谈。
