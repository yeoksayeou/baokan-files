Title: Taiwan Cracks Down on Underground Investment Companies
Author:
Date: 1989-08-14
Edition: 2

Taiwan Cracks Down on Underground Investment Companies

New China News Agency, Hong Kong, August 14th: Taipei News: Recently, the "Ministry of Justice Investigation Bureau" in Taiwan, in coordination with the "Joint Task Force for Handling Illegal Fund-Raising Companies," took decisive action against 8 underground investment companies. A total of 10 suspects have been transferred to the Taipei and Kaohsiung District Prosecutors' Offices for further investigation.

This is the first time that Taiwan has taken legal action against illegal companies since the revised Banking Law came into effect on July 19th.

### 台取缔地下投资公司

1989-08-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    台取缔地下投资公司
    新华社香港8月14日电　台北消息：台湾“法务部调查局”最近配合“处理违法吸收资金公司联合专案小组”的决定，一举对8家地下投资公司展开查缉，共有10名被告被移送台北、高雄地检处侦办。
    这是台湾新修订的银行法7月19日生效之后，首次对违法公司采取法律行动。
