Title: The Asian Handball Championship to be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-08-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 9th.

The fifth Asian Men's Handball Championship and the second Asian Women's Handball Championship will be held in Beijing from August 20th to 31st. Currently, 11 countries and regions have registered 11 men's teams and 5 women's teams to participate.

According to the organizing committee of the championships, the 11 men's teams that have registered are Japan, India, South Korea, Iran, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Palestine, Hong Kong, Chinese Taipei, and China. The 5 women's teams are Japan, South Korea, Hong Kong, Chinese Taipei, and China.

The President of the Asian Olympic Council and the Asian Handball Federation, Prince Fahad of Kuwait, as well as officials from the Asian Handball Federation and the International Handball Federation, will be present to provide guidance.

### 亚洲手球锦标赛将在京举行  11个国家和地区将派队参赛

1989-08-09
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    亚洲手球锦标赛将在京举行
    11个国家和地区将派队参赛
    新华社北京8月9日电　第五届亚洲男子手球锦标赛和第二届亚洲女子手球锦标赛，将于8月20日至31日在北京举行。目前报名参赛的有11个国家和地区的11支男队和5支女队。
    据锦标赛组委会介绍，报名参赛的11支男队是，日本、印度、南朝鲜、伊朗、沙特阿拉伯、卡塔尔、科威特、巴勒斯坦、香港、中国台北和中国；5支女队是：日本、南朝鲜、香港、中国台北和中国。
    亚奥理事会主席兼亚洲手球联合会主席法赫德亲王（科威特）以及亚手联和国际手联的负责人，届时将莅临指导。
