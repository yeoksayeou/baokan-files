Title: The Second World War Academic Discussion Conference Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-08-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 17th.

The Second World War Academic Discussion Conference, held by China's historical community, concluded today in Beijing. The conference marked the 50th anniversary of the full-scale outbreak of the Second World War.

Over 50 experts and scholars participated in the three-day discussion, focusing on four key areas: the origins of the Second World War, the pre-war preparations of the Axis powers (Germany, Italy, and Japan), the defensive development strategies of the anti-aggression countries before the war, and the possibilities of preventing or delaying the Second World War.

### 第二次大战学术讨论会结束

1989-08-17
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    第二次大战学术讨论会结束
    据新华社北京8月17日电　我国史学界举行的第二次世界大战全面爆发50周年学术讨论会今天在北京结束。
    50多位专家学者在为期3天的讨论中，就第二次世界大战的起源、德意日轴心国的战前准备、反侵略国战前的国防发展战略、第二次世界大战防止或推迟的可能性等四个方面进行了广泛探讨。
