Title: Wang Zhen Meets with Tsuruoka Tokuma
Author: Yang Guoqing
Date: 1989-08-19
Edition: 2

Wang Zhen, Vice Chairman of the State and Honorary President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, met today with Tsuruoka Tokuma, a long-time friend of the Chinese people and President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, and his entourage at the Great Hall of the People.

Wang Zhen extended his heartfelt congratulations to Tsuruoka Tokuma on being awarded the honorary title of "Honorary Doctor of Law" by Peking University. He said: Peking University is a renowned institution of higher learning in China, where the famous educator Cai Yuanpei once served as president. Since liberation, Peking University has produced a large number of talents for the country. He said, China's education has achieved great success, but there are also some problems. We are currently studying improvement measures.

Wang Zhen asked Tsuruoka Tokuma to convey his greetings to old friends in Japan such as Saionji Koinchi upon his return, and to inform them that the current situation in China is stable and will be even more stable in the future. He welcomed friends to come to China to see for themselves.

Tsuruoka Tokuma said that China and Japan have a long history and should always maintain friendly relations. He said: "Our family also practices friendship from generation to generation, so this time I brought my son and grandson along."

Saionji Kazumasa, Vice President of the Tokyo Metropolitan Japan-China Friendship Association, said that during the visit, he saw that the campus of Peking University was very peaceful, and he felt happy and reassured.

After the meeting, the host and guests had lunch together.

Kang Keqing, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman of the Soong Ching Ling Foundation, met with Tsuruoka Tokuma and his entourage this morning.

In the evening, Tsuruoka Tokuma held a banquet at the Beijing Hotel to express his gratitude to Peking University for conferring upon him the honorary doctorate, and to commemorate the publication and distribution of the Chinese version of his new book "World Peace and Disarmament" in Beijing.

### 王震会见宇都宫德马  祝贺荣获北大名誉博士称号
杨国强
1989-08-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    王震会见宇都宫德马
    祝贺荣获北大名誉博士称号
    新华社北京8月19日电（记者杨国强）国家副主席、中日友协名誉会长王震今天在人民大会堂会见了中国人民的老朋友、日中友协会长宇都宫德马一行。
    王震对宇都宫德马荣获“北京大学名誉法学博士”称号表示衷心的祝贺。他说：北大是中国著名的学府，著名教育家蔡元培曾在这里担任过校长。解放以来，北大为国家输送了大批人才。他说，中国的教育取得了很大的成绩，但也存在一些问题。我们正在研究改进措施。
    王震请宇都宫德马回国后向西园寺公一等日本老朋友问好，并转告他们，中国目前的局势很稳定，以后还会更加稳定。欢迎朋友们到中国来亲眼看一看。
    宇都宫德马说，中日两国有着悠久的历史，应该永远友好下去。他说：“我们家里也搞世世代代友好，所以这次我把儿子、孙子也带来了。”
    随同来访的东京都日中友协副会长西园寺一晃说，访问中看到北大校园很平静，他感到高兴和放心。
    会见后，宾主共进午餐。
    全国政协副主席、宋庆龄基金会主席康克清，今天上午会见了宇都宫德马一行。
    晚上，宇都宫德马在北京饭店举行宴会，感谢北大授予他名誉博士学位，并纪念他的新著《世界和平与裁军》中文版在北京出版发行。
