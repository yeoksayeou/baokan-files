Title: More German Women Entering Technical Professions
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Edition: 7

In recent years, an increasing number of women in the Federal Republic of Germany have been entering technical professions.

The above image shows Diana Schilling, the first female train driver in the state of Baden-Württemberg in the Federal Republic of Germany, at work.
