Title: Chinese Civil Affairs Official Meets Japanese Envoy
Author: Jiǎ Zhāoquán
Date: 1989-08-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 9th.

    Chinese Civil Affairs Official Meets Japanese Envoy
    Expresses Gratitude for Japanese Aid to Sichuan Disaster Victims

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 9th. — Reporter Jiǎ Zhāoquán reports: Today in the afternoon, Zhang Dejiang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Civil Affairs, met with Hisashi Kubo, Japanese Envoy to China, to express gratitude for Japan's aid to disaster victims in Sichuan and other regions.

    This year, severe floods have struck Sichuan, Anhui, Jiangxi, and other provinces in China, causing significant loss of life and property. To assist the victims in these areas, the Japanese government has donated relief supplies worth $440,000 and $1.5 million in cash. Vice Minister Zhang Dejiang expressed his thanks to the Japanese government and people for their assistance.

    It is reported that part of the Japanese aid has already arrived in Beijing and will soon be transported to the disaster-stricken areas.

### 民政部负责人会见日本驻华公使  感谢日方援助四川等地灾民
贾昭全
1989-08-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    民政部负责人会见日本驻华公使
    感谢日方援助四川等地灾民
    本报北京8月9日讯　记者贾昭全报道：民政部副部长张德江今天下午会见日本驻华公使久保日穰，感谢日本对我国四川等地灾民的援助。
    今年，我国四川、安徽、江西等地相继发生了严重水灾，给人民生命财产造成严重损失。为援助这些地方灾民，日方捐赠了价值44万美元的物资和150万美元现款。对此，民政部副部长张德江向日本政府和人民表示感谢。
    据悉，日本援助的部分物资已运抵北京，不日将送往灾区。
