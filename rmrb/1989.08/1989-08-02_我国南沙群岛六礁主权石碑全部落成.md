Title: Completion of Sovereignty Monuments on Six Reefs in China's Nansha Islands
Author:
Date: 1989-08-02
Edition: 2

The sovereignty monuments on six reefs in China's Nansha Islands—Yongshu, Zhongye, Huayang, Nanhun, Zhubei, and Dongmen—were all completed today.

These monuments were produced under the supervision of the South Sea Fleet of the Chinese Navy. The stones used are high-grade natural granite. The inscriptions are carved in a horizontal format, with the top line reading "Nansha Islands of the People's Republic of China" and the middle line bearing the name of the reef. The largest monument, on Yongshu Reef, stands 50 centimeters high and 80 centimeters wide on its front face.

These six monuments, as symbols of the territorial sovereignty of the People's Republic of China, will stand forever on the sacred Nansha Islands.

### 我国南沙群岛六礁主权石碑全部落成

1989-08-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    我国南沙群岛六礁主权石碑全部落成
    新华社南沙8月2日电  我国南沙群岛永署、赤瓜、华阳、南薰、渚碧、东门等6礁的主权石碑今天全部落成。
    这些石碑是由海军南海舰队监制的。碑石采用高级天然花岗岩，碑铭横幅镌刻，上边一排刻“中华人民共和国南沙群岛”，中央刻礁名。其中最大的永署礁碑正面高50厘米、宽80厘米。
    这6块石碑，作为中华人民共和国领土主权的标志，将永远矗立在神圣的南沙群岛。
