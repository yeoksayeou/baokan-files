Title: Chinese Foreign Minister Meets with Domestic and Foreign Journalists in Paris
Author:
Date: 1989-08-02
Page: 1

Today, the third page of this newspaper published a detailed report on Foreign Minister Qian's meeting with domestic and foreign journalists in Paris on August 2. Foreign Minister Qian answered questions from journalists regarding the international conference on the Cambodian issue, the prospects for resolving the Cambodian problem, and China's domestic and foreign policies.

### 钱外长在巴黎会见中外记者

1989-08-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    钱外长在巴黎会见中外记者
    本报讯　今天本报第三版详细刊登了钱外长2日在巴黎会见中外记者的消息。钱外长就柬埔寨问题国际会议、解决柬问题的前景以及中国内政外交问题回答了记者提问。
