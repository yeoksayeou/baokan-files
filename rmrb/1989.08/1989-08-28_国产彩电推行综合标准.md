Title: Domestic Color TVs to Implement Comprehensive Standards
Author: Liu Litiān, Xǔ Zhèngzhōng
Date: 1989-08-28
Edition: 2

Domestic Color TVs to Implement Comprehensive Standards

	BEIJING, Aug. 28 (Xinhua) -- Reporters Liu Litiān and Xǔ Zhèngzhōng report: China's color TV production now has a unified standard for technical supervision and quality inspection -- the comprehensive standard for color TVs. Approved by the State Bureau of Technical Supervision and the Ministry of Machinery and Electronics Industry, this comprehensive standard for color TV production inspection is now being fully implemented.

	The standardization of color TVs is a crucial technical foundation for the domestic production of color TVs and for forming a scale economy in China's color TV industry. China's comprehensive standard for color TVs includes 344 standards, with 173 national standards and 171 industry (ministry) standards. It stipulates that the electrical, optical, audio, and color performance of color TVs must meet 47 technical indicators, as well as various technical requirements for safety, reliability, electromagnetic compatibility, and environmental adaptability. The reliability indicator for the minimum average time without failure is set at 15,000 hours for qualified products and 20,000 hours for high-quality products.

	Experts believe that the implementation of the comprehensive standard for color TVs signifies that the performance and quality of domestic color TVs have reached the advanced level of similar products worldwide.

### 国产彩电推行综合标准
刘丽天  许正中
1989-08-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国产彩电推行综合标准
    本报北京8月28日讯　记者刘丽天、许正中报道：我国彩电生产的技术监督和质量检测有了统一标准——彩电综合标准。经国家技术监督局和机械电子工业部批准，这一彩电生产检测的综合标准已开始全面推行实施。
    彩电综合标准化是彩电国产化和使我国彩电工业形成规模经济的重要技术基础工作。我国的彩电综合标准包括344个标准，其中国家标准173个，行业（部）标准171个。它规定了彩电的电、光、声、色性能必须达到的47项技术指标，以及安全性、可靠性、电磁兼容性、环境适应性等必须达到的各项技术要求。其中可靠性指标平均无故障时间的下限值，合格品要求在15000小时以上，优质品要求在20000小时以上。
    专家认为，彩电综合标准的实施，标志着国产彩电的性能质量已达到同类产品的世界先进水平。
