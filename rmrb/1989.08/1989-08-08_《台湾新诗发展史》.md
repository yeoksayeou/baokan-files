Title: History of the Development of New Poetry in Taiwan
Author: Gu Jitang
Date: 1989-08-08
Edition: 8

The book "History of the Development of New Poetry in Taiwan" by Gu Jitang was recently published by People's Literature Publishing House. This is the first time a book on the history of new poetry in Taiwan has been published on the mainland. The book is divided into three parts: the upper part—The Birth and Growth Period of New Poetry in Taiwan; the middle part—The Westernization Period of New Poetry in Taiwan; and the lower part—The Return Period of New Poetry in Taiwan.
