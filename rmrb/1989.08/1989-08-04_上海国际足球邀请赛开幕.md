Title: The Shanghai International Football Invitational Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-08-04
Page: 4

The 1989 Shanghai Shengli Cup International City Football Invitational Tournament began on the evening of August 3rd, amidst wind and rain, at Shanghai's Hongkou Stadium. The Shanghai Shen Zhou team defeated the Shanghai Honey Flower team; the Japanese Yokohama team and the Soviet Leningrad team ended in a draw.

### 上海国际足球邀请赛开幕

1989-08-04
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    上海国际足球邀请赛开幕
    一九八九年上海上菱杯国际城市足球邀请赛三日晚冒着风雨在上海虹口体育场拉开战幕。上海神州队胜上海蜂花队；日本横滨队和苏联列宁格勒队握手言和。
