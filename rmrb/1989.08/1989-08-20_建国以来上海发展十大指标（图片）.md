Edition: 4

    Ten Major Indicators of Shanghai's Development Since the Founding of the Nation

    Shanghai Statistics Bureau provided the data; Hong Guangwen illustrated.

    1. **Population Growth**:
        - 1949: 5,366,000
        - 1988: 12,346,000

    2. **Industrial Output Value**:
        - 1949: 1.2 billion yuan
        - 1988: 129.4 billion yuan

    3. **Gross Domestic Product (GDP)**:
        - 1952: 2.5 billion yuan
        - 1988: 71.2 billion yuan

    4. **Per Capita GDP**:
        - 1952: 623 yuan
        - 1988: 5,770 yuan

    5. **Total Retail Sales of Social Consumer Goods**:
        - 1949: 1.2 billion yuan
        - 1988: 46.4 billion yuan

    6. **Per Capita Disposable Income of Urban Residents**:
        - 1956: 636 yuan
        - 1988: 2,233 yuan

    7. **Per Capita Net Income of Rural Residents**:
        - 1956: 119 yuan
        - 1988: 750 yuan

    8. **Length of Roads**:
        - 1949: 1,013 kilometers
        - 1988: 4,800 kilometers

    9. **Number of University Students**:
        - 1949: 10,000
        - 1988: 140,000

    10. **Number of Hospital Beds**:
        - 1949: 26,000
        - 1988: 76,000
