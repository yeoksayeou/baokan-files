Title: The Study of "Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping" to be Published
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Page: 2

The book "The Study of 'Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping'" written by comrades from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's Literature Research Office who participated in the editing of "Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping" is soon to be published by the China Overseas Publishing House. The authors, based on first-hand materials they possess, have provided detailed introductions to the historical background and main content of the key articles in the selected works. They have also, in connection with current realities, elaborated on some important thoughts.

### 《学习〈邓小平文选〉》将出版

1989-08-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    《学习〈邓小平文选〉》将出版
    本报讯　中共中央文献研究室参加《邓小平文选》编辑的同志组织撰写的《学习〈邓小平文选〉》一书，即将由中国华侨出版公司出版。这本书的作者根据掌握的第一手材料，对文选中重点文章的历史背景和主要内容作了详细的介绍，并联系当前实际，对一些重要思想进行了阐发。
