Title: Central Military Commission Confers "Guardians of the Republic" Honorific Title on An Weiping, Yu Ronglu, Zang Lizhi, Shen Yuntian, and Yu Aijun
Author:
Date: 1989-08-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 2nd.

The Central Military Commission conferred the honorific title of "Guardians of the Republic" on Comrade An Weiping and martyrs Yu Ronglu and Zang Lizhi. A naming ceremony was held in Beijing.

More than 1,000 officers and soldiers stood in solemn silence as the national anthem played, paying tribute to martyrs Yu Ronglu and Zang Lizhi.

Deputy Commander of a certain unit of the martial law troops, Zhu Dunfa, read out the order signed by Military Commission Chairman Deng Xiaoping, conferring the "Guardians of the Republic" honorific title on Comrade An Weiping and martyrs Yu Ronglu and Zang Lizhi. Unit leaders presented medals, certificates, and awards to Comrade An Weiping and the families of martyrs Yu Ronglu and Zang Lizhi.

Deputy Political Commissar Li Wenqing of a certain unit of the martial law troops, "Guardian of the Republic" An Weiping, and Platoon Leader Xu Guangfu of the "Sharpshooter Fourth Platoon" spoke on behalf of the grassroots troops. Wang Jiapei, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, also addressed the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 2nd.

A naming ceremony was held today by a certain unit of the martial law troops in the capital, conferring the "Guardians of the Republic" honorific title on Shen Yuntian and Yu Aijun.

Deputy Commander Gu Hui of a certain unit of the martial law troops in the capital read out the order from the Central Military Commission and presented honor certificates, badges, and awards to Shen Yuntian and Yu Aijun.

Shen Yuntian spoke at the meeting, expressing his determination to achieve complete victory in suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion, and to contribute more to safeguarding the Party Central Committee, the socialist motherland, and the peaceful labor of the people.

Deputy Political Commissar Cao Shengsheng of a certain unit of the martial law troops in the capital addressed the meeting, calling on all officers and soldiers to learn from Shen Yuntian and Yu Aijun.

Meng Zhiyuan, a member of the Standing Committee of the Beijing Municipal Committee and Secretary of the Discipline Inspection Commission, also spoke at the meeting.

The naming ceremony also read out the decision of the unit's Party Committee to learn from "Guardians of the Republic" Shen Yuntian and Yu Aijun.

### 中央军委授予安卫平于荣禄藏立杰  沈运田余爱军“共和国卫士”荣誉称号  戒严部队隆重举行命名大会

1989-08-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中央军委授予安卫平于荣禄藏立杰
    沈运田余爱军“共和国卫士”荣誉称号
    戒严部队隆重举行命名大会
    据新华社北京8月2日电　中央军委授予安卫平同志和于荣禄、藏立杰烈士“共和国卫士”荣誉称号的命名大会在京隆重举行。
    在雄壮的国歌声中，1000多名官兵起立向于荣禄、藏立杰烈士默哀。
    戒严部队某部副司令员朱敦法在会上宣读了由军委主席邓小平签署的授予安卫平同志和于荣禄、藏立杰烈士“共和国卫士”荣誉称号的命令。部队负责同志向安卫平同志及于荣禄、藏立杰烈士的亲属颁发了奖章、奖状和证书。
    戒严部队某部副政委李文卿、“共和国卫士”安卫平、“神枪手四连”指导员许广福代表基层官兵分别在会上讲话。中共北京市委副书记汪家镠也在会上讲了话。
    据新华社北京8月2日电　首都戒严部队某部今天隆重举行中央军委授予沈运田、余爱军“共和国卫士”荣誉称号命名大会。
    首都戒严部队某部副司令员固辉在会上宣读了中央军委的命令，并向沈运田和余爱军颁发了荣誉证书、证章和奖品。
    沈运田在大会上讲话，表示要为夺取平息反革命暴乱的彻底胜利再立新功，为保卫党中央、保卫社会主义祖国、保卫人民群众的和平劳动多作贡献。
    首都戒严部队某部副政委曹芃生在大会上讲话，号召全体官兵向沈运田、余爱军学习。
    北京市委常委、纪委书记孟志元也在会上讲话。
    命名大会还宣读了部队党委关于向“共和国卫士”沈运田、余爱军同志学习的决定。
