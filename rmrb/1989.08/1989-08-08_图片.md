Title: Students and Teachers Visit Oil Field in Sichuan
Author: Ma Yueping
Date: 1989-08-08
Page: 5

    Students and teachers from the Sichuan Provincial Petroleum Society's Summer Science Camp, Sichuan Mid-Subdivision, braved the scorching sun to visit the drilling and gas extraction sites at the Moxi Gas Field. The dedication and sense of responsibility of the oil workers in contributing to the nation's oil supply deeply moved the students and teachers.
