Title: French Foreign Minister Writes to Lebanese Acting Prime Minister Hoping for Peace in Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1989-08-19
Edition: 3

French Foreign Minister Dumas writes to the acting Prime Minister of Lebanon's Muslim government, Salim al-Hoss, expressing France's hope for peace in Lebanon.

The French Foreign Ministry released this letter on August 18, which was handed over by Alain de Charette, the French Minister of State for French-speaking countries, who is currently visiting Lebanon.

In the letter, Dumas reaffirmed France's support for the mission given to the Tripartite Committee by the Casablanca Arab Summit, believing that Arab countries should continue their efforts to restore security in Lebanon and ensure its revival and rebirth on the basis of "unity, territorial integrity, and sovereignty."

It was also reported that Dumas sent a telegram to Iraqi Foreign Minister Aziz on August 18, expressing France's strong hope that the international community and the Arab world would work together to end the suffering of the Lebanese people and pave the way for an effective political solution to the Lebanese problem.

### 法外长致信黎代总理希望黎巴嫩恢复和平

1989-08-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    法外长致信黎代总理希望黎巴嫩恢复和平
    新华社巴黎8月18日电  法国外长迪马写信给黎巴嫩穆斯林派政府代总理萨利姆·胡斯，表示法国希望黎巴嫩恢复和平。
    法国外交部18日公布的这封信是由目前正在黎巴嫩访问的法国外交部负责法语国家部长级代表阿兰·德科转交的。
    迪马在这封信中重申法国支持卡萨布兰卡阿拉伯首脑会议赋予三国委员会的使命，认为阿拉伯国家应该继续努力，以恢复黎巴嫩的安全和保证它在“统一、领土完整和主权”的基础上实现复兴和新生。
    另据报道，迪马18日还致电伊拉克外长阿齐兹，表示法国强烈希望国际社会和阿拉伯世界协同努力，以结束黎巴嫩人民的痛苦和开拓有效寻求政治解决黎巴嫩问题的道路。
