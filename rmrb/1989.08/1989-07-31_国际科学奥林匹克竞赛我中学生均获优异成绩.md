Title: Chinese Students Achieve Excellent Results in International Science Olympiads
Author: Cheng Lei
Date: 1989-07-31
Edition: 2

Chinese students participating in the International Science Olympiads have all achieved excellent results. The 18 members of the Chinese team won a total of 7 gold medals, 6 silver medals, and 5 bronze medals. Among them, the 6 members of the mathematics team won 4 gold medals and 2 silver medals, ranking first in the overall score among the 50 participating countries.

China began participating in the International Science Olympiads for high school students in 1985. From May to July this year, China sent four teams to participate in the 30th International Mathematics Olympiad, the 20th International Physics Olympiad, the 21st International Chemistry Olympiad, and the inaugural International Informatics Olympiad. The 5 members of the physics team won 4 silver medals and 1 bronze medal, ranking second in the overall score among the 30 participating countries. The 4 members of the chemistry team won 3 gold medals and 1 bronze medal. Zhou Pei also received the Best Theory Competition Special Award. The 3 members of the informatics team won 3 bronze medals, ranking second in the overall score among the 13 participating countries.

The State Education Commission, the Chinese Association for Science and Technology, and the National Natural Science Foundation of China held an awards ceremony at the Beijing Science Hall this afternoon. Deputy Minister of the State Education Commission Liu Bin, on behalf of Comrade Li Tieying, encouraged the award-winning students to continue climbing the heights of science.

### 国际科学奥林匹克竞赛我中学生均获优异成绩
程蕾
1989-07-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国际科学奥林匹克竞赛我中学生均获优异成绩
    本报北京7月31日讯　我国选派参加国际科学奥林匹克竞赛的18名中学生队员全部获奖，共获金牌7枚、银牌6枚、铜牌5枚。其中，数学代表队的6名选手共获4枚金牌、2枚银牌，在全部50个参赛国中名列总分第一。
    我国是从1985年起陆续参加国际中学生科学奥林匹克竞赛的。今年5月至7月，我国先后派出4个中学生代表队，分别参加第30届国际数学、第20届国际物理、第21届国际化学和首届国际信息学奥林匹克竞赛。物理队的5名选手获得4枚银牌和1枚铜牌，在30个参赛国中名列总分第二。化学队的4名选手，获得3枚金牌和1枚铜牌，周沛同学还获得最佳理论竞赛特别奖。3名参加信息学竞赛的队员，获得3枚铜牌，在13个参赛国中名列总分第二。
    国家教委、中国科协和国家自然科学基金委员会今天下午在北京科学会堂举行了颁奖仪式。国家教委副主任柳斌受李铁映同志的委托，勉励获奖学生继续攀登科学高峰。
                            （程蕾）
