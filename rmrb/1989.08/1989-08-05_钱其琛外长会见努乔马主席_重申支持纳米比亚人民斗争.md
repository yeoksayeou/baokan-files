Title: Foreign Minister Qian Qichen Meets with Chairman Nujoma, Reaffirms Support for the Struggle of the Namibian People
Author: Qián Qíchéng Li Jingwèi
Date: 1989-08-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Harare, August 4th. People's Daily reporters Zhang Jinglei and Li Jingwei report:

Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met with SWAPO Chairman Sam Nujoma this evening and stated that the Chinese government supports the struggle of the people of Southern Africa, particularly the Namibian people, for independence.

Qian Qichen said that after a long struggle, the process of Namibian independence has finally begun. However, the South African authorities will use various means to obstruct Namibian independence and sabotage the independent elections in Namibia. To ensure the smooth progress of Namibian independence, it is necessary to insist on the implementation of Resolution 435 and expose the destructive actions of the South African authorities.

Qian Qichen expressed admiration for the long and arduous struggle waged by SWAPO for Namibian independence.

Nujoma briefed Qian Qichen on the current political and military situation in Namibia. He said that due to the blatant violation of UN Security Council Resolution 435 by the South African regime, the process of Namibian independence faces a severe situation. He hoped that the UN Security Council would send more observer teams to Namibia to ensure that free and fair elections are held in Namibia.

Nujoma thanked the Chinese government and people for their long-standing diplomatic, moral, and material support for SWAPO's struggle.

### 钱其琛外长会见努乔马主席  重申支持纳米比亚人民斗争
张敬雷  李景卫
1989-08-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    钱其琛外长会见努乔马主席  重申支持纳米比亚人民斗争
    本报哈拉雷8月4日电  新华社记者张敬雷、本报记者李景卫报道：钱其琛外长今晚在这里会见西南非洲人民组织主席努乔马时说，中国政府支持南部非洲人民、特别是纳米比亚人民争取独立的斗争。
    钱其琛说，经过长期斗争之后，纳米比亚独立进程总算开始了。但是，南非当局还会使用各种手段，阻挠纳米比亚的独立进程和破坏纳米比亚的独立选举。为了保障纳米比亚独立进程顺利进行，要坚持实施435号决议，揭露南非当局的破坏行径。
    钱其琛对西南非洲人民组织为争取纳米比亚独立所进行的长期艰苦斗争表示钦佩。
    努乔马向钱其琛通报了纳米比亚当前的政治形势和军事形势。他说，由于南非政权赤裸裸地违背安理会435号决议，纳米比亚的独立进程面临着严峻的形势。他希望联合国安理会能向纳米比亚增派观察小组，以保证在纳米比亚举行公正、自由的选举。
    努乔马感谢中国政府和人民在西南非洲人民组织的长期斗争中一直给予外交上、道义上和物质上的支持。
