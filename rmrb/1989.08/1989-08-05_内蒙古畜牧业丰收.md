Title: Inner Mongolia's Livestock Industry Bounteous Harvest
Author:
Date: 1989-08-05
Edition: 2

A reporter from the Inner Mongolia Bureau of Statistics learned that this year, the number of livestock in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region reached 47.5 million head, an increase of 13.1% compared to the same period last year, and a 12% increase over the highest historical year, 1982. The number of counties with over 1 million head of livestock has increased from 8 last year to 17 this year.

### 内蒙古畜牧业丰收

1989-08-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    内蒙古畜牧业丰收
    本报呼和浩特8月5日电  记者从内蒙古统计局获悉：今年内蒙古自治区牲畜头数达到4750万头，比去年同期增加13.1％，比历史上的最高年份——1982年增长12％。牲畜头数超过百万的旗县，已经从去年8个发展到今年17个。
