Title: Sudan Recalls Ambassador to Iran
Author:
Date: 1989-08-29
Edition: 3

Sudan Recalls Ambassador to Iran

The Sudanese government announced on August 27, following a cabinet meeting, that it would recall its ambassador to Iran to discuss how to protest comments made by Iranian television regarding the political situation in Sudan. Iranian television recently aired a commentary stating that the recent revolution in Sudan, which overthrew the previous government, was a revolution of unclear significance.

### 苏丹召回驻伊朗大使

1989-08-29
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    苏丹召回驻伊朗大使
    苏丹政府27日在一次内阁会议后发表的新闻公报中，宣布召回苏丹驻伊朗的大使，以商讨如何抗议伊朗电视台有关苏丹政局的评论。伊朗电视台最近发表评论，认为苏丹最近进行的推翻前政府的革命是一场意义不明确的革命。
