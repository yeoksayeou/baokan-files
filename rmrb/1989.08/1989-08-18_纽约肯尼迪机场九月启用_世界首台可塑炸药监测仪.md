Title: World's First Plastic Explosive Detector to be Deployed at New York's Kennedy Airport in September
Author: Cang Lide
Date: 1989-08-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, August 16th

The world's first plastic explosive detector will be put into use at New York's Kennedy International Airport on September 1st. This 10-ton machine arrived at the airport on the 15th.

According to reports, this $1.1 million device, officially named the "Thermal Neutron Analysis Machine," measures approximately 4 meters in length, 1.8 meters in width, and 2.4 meters in height. When luggage is exposed to low-energy neutrons, plastic explosives absorb the neutrons and emit gamma rays, triggering an automatic alarm. Currently, X-ray scanners widely in use cannot detect plastic explosives, which appear transparent and colorless. The Pan Am Flight 103 explosion over Lockerbie, Scotland, is believed to have been caused by plastic explosives hidden in a tape recorder.

The U.S. Federal Aviation Administration has already ordered six of these thermal neutron detectors, with plans to install them at airports in New York, Miami, Detroit, San Francisco, as well as London and Frankfurt by the end of the year. By 1992, a total of 400 such security devices will be needed for all international flights in the United States. The price of each device is expected to drop to $500,000.

### 纽约肯尼迪机场九月启用  世界首台可塑炸药监测仪
仓立德
1989-08-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    纽约肯尼迪机场九月启用  世界首台可塑炸药监测仪
    本报华盛顿8月16日电　记者仓立德报道：美国纽约肯尼迪国际机场将于今年9月1日启用一台代表反恐怖活动最新技术的可塑炸药监测仪，这台重达10吨的机器已于15日运抵该机场。
    据报道，这台造价110万美元的设备全名为“热能中子分析仪”，长、宽、高各约为4米、1.8米、2.4米。当行李置于低能中子时，可塑炸药可吸收中子并放射伽马射线，此时机器便自动发出警报。在目前普遍使用的X射线监测仪中，可塑炸药呈无色透明状态，难以发现。在英国洛克比上空爆炸的泛美103航班据信是装在收录机中的可塑炸药引起的。
    目前，美国联邦航空管理局已定购了6台热能中子监测仪，预计年底前将分别安置于纽约、迈阿密、底特律、旧金山以及伦敦和法兰克福机场。至1992年全美国际航线共需400台此类安全检查装置，每台装置的价格可望下降至50万美元。
