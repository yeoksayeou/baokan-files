Title: The Soviet Union Halts Military Aid to Nicaragua
Author: Guo Weicheng
Date: 1989-08-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tegucigalpa, August 4th.

The Soviet Union has halted military aid to Nicaragua, supporting the peace process in Central America. This was announced by Alexander Mokanu, Deputy Chairman of the Supreme Soviet, during his visit to Tegucigalpa on August 3rd.

The Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation arrived in Honduras on August 2nd and met with Honduran President José Simón Azcona del Hoyo on the same day.

### 苏联停止向尼加拉瓜供应军火
郭伟成
1989-08-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联停止向尼加拉瓜供应军火
    本报洪都拉斯8月4日电　记者郭伟成报道：苏联最高苏维埃副主席亚历山大·莫卡努3日在特古西加尔巴宣布，苏联支持中美洲和平进程，已经停止对尼加拉瓜的军火供应。
    苏联最高苏维埃代表团2日到达洪都拉斯访问，并于昨天会见了洪都拉斯总统阿斯科纳。
