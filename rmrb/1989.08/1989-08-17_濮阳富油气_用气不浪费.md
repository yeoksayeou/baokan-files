Title: Puyeang Rich in Oil and Gas, Using Gas Without Waste
Author: Feng Qiuchang
Date: 1989-08-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, August 16th.

Located in the heart of the Zhongyuan Oilfield, the city of Puyang in Henan Province has implemented scientific management for users and residents using natural gas, updated and renovated equipment, and saved a significant amount of energy and funds.

In April 1988, after the establishment of the Municipal Gas Company, they required users to treat gas as precious and to turn it off when not in use. They also implemented scientific and standardized management for industrial and residential gas use, effectively preventing gas leaks and accidents. According to statistics, after the technical renovation, the city can save 3.2 million cubic meters of natural gas for the state annually.

### 濮阳富油气　用气不浪费
冯秋昌
1989-08-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    濮阳富油气　用气不浪费
    新华社郑州8月16日电（通讯员冯秋昌）地处中原油田气海的河南省濮阳市，对用户和居民使用天然气实行科学管理，更新改造设备，节约了大量能源和资金。
    1988年4月，市煤气公司成立后，要求用户惜气如金，用气随开，不用随关。他们还对工业和居民用气实行科学化、正规化管理，有效地防止了天然气泄漏造成的浪费和事故。据统计，技改后，全市一年可为国家节省天然气320万立方米。
