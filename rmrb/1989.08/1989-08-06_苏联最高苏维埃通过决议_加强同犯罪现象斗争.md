Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Passes Resolution to Strengthen Fight Against Crime
Author:
Date: 1989-08-06
Edition: 3

The Soviet Supreme Soviet on August 4 passed a resolution titled "Resolutely Strengthening the Fight Against Crime."

The resolution stipulates that temporary committees to combat crime will be established at the all-Union, republic, autonomous republic, territory, and regional levels. Leaders from the procuracy, interior ministry, KGB, courts, and other departments, as well as people's deputies, will participate in the work of these committees.

The resolution suggests that law enforcement officials, in urgent situations involving criminal activities, may take extraordinary measures, including the use of weapons.

### 苏联最高苏维埃通过决议  加强同犯罪现象斗争

1989-08-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃通过决议
    加强同犯罪现象斗争
    新华社莫斯科8月4日电　据塔斯社报道，苏联最高苏维埃4日通过了“坚决加强同犯罪现象斗争”的决议。
    决议规定，在全苏、加盟共和国、自治共和国、边疆区和州一级成立同犯罪现象斗争临时委员会。检察、内务、克格勃、法院等部门的领导人以及人民代表参加委员会的工作。
    决议建议，护法工作者在与刑事犯罪斗争的紧急情况下，可采取包括使用武器在内的非常措施。
