Author:
Date: 1989-08-24
Edition: 3

Lebanon Faces Internal and External Troubles; International Community Closely Monitoring

	NEW DELHI, Aug. 24 (Xinhua) -- The President of the United Arab Emirates, Sheikh Zayed, has proposed an emergency summit of Arab leaders to discuss the deployment of Arab peacekeeping forces in Lebanon.

	Sheikh Zayed made the proposal in an interview with a London-based magazine, the contents of which were released by the UAE on August 23.

	The UAE president suggested that the Arab peacekeeping forces should be composed of troops from Egypt, Algeria, and Morocco, but he did not mention the 35,000 Syrian troops already stationed in Lebanon.

	PARIS, Aug. 24 (Xinhua) -- French President Francois Mitterrand said that France will not allow any other country to dictate its actions in Lebanon.

	Mitterrand made the statement during a cabinet meeting yesterday. He said that France's actions in Lebanon cannot be dictated by any country or group. France's response to the Lebanese crisis is primarily based on international law principles, namely the protection of its citizens, and France is extending a helping hand to all suffering Lebanese people. Mitterrand also said that France will continue to consult closely with the Arab Three-Party Committee and the European Community countries to find a solution to the Lebanese crisis.

	WASHINGTON, Aug. 23 (Xinhua) -- U.S. President George H.W. Bush said today that he will not criticize the French naval operations in the eastern Mediterranean.

	Bush, who is vacationing in Kennebunkport, Maine, expressed understanding of France's military actions because France has long-term interests in Lebanon.

	PARIS, Aug. 23 (Xinhua) -- The 12 member states of the European Community today issued a joint statement in Paris, urgently calling on all parties in Lebanon to cease all shooting and bombing and to implement an immediate and comprehensive ceasefire.

	In addition, the 12 EC countries urged the Arab Three-Party Committee, composed of Morocco, Saudi Arabia, and Algeria, to resume its peace mediation mission to achieve Lebanese national reconciliation, restore Lebanese sovereignty, and implement a permanent ceasefire.

	UNITED NATIONS, Aug. 23 (Xinhua) -- A spokesman for the United Nations said today that UN Secretary-General Javier Perez de Cuellar will send a special envoy to the Middle East this week to assist in finding a solution to the Lebanese crisis.

	The special envoy, Jean-Claude Aime, chief of the Secretary-General's office, will begin his visit to Morocco, Saudi Arabia, and Algeria on August 25 to discuss the situation in Lebanon with the Arab League's Three-Party Committee.

### 黎巴嫩局势内忧外患  国际社会正严重关注

1989-08-24
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    黎巴嫩局势内忧外患  国际社会正严重关注
    新华社北京8月24日电　阿布扎比消息：阿拉伯联合酋长国总统扎耶德建议召开一次紧急阿拉伯首脑会议，讨论向黎巴嫩派遣阿拉伯维持和平部队问题。
    扎耶德总统是在接受伦敦一家杂志采访时提出这项建议的。阿联酋23日公布了这次采访内容。
    扎耶德总统建议阿拉伯维持和平部队由埃及、阿尔及利亚和摩洛哥组成，但他没有提及部署在黎巴嫩的3.5万叙利亚军队问题。
    本报巴黎8月24日电　记者张启华报道：密特朗总统说，法国不让别人来支配它在黎巴嫩的行动。
    密特朗总统是在昨天政府内阁会议上做这一表示的。他说，法国在黎巴嫩的行动，不能由任何国家、任何集团支配。法国对黎巴嫩危机做出的反应，首先是根据国际法原则做出的，即保护它的侨民，法国是向所有遭受苦难的黎巴嫩人伸出援助之手。密特朗总统在讲话中还表示，法国将继续同阿拉伯三方委员会以及欧共体国家保持密切磋商，以寻找解决黎巴嫩危机的办法。
    新华社华盛顿8月23日电　布什总统今天表示，他不会对法国军舰在地中海东部的行动提出批评。
    正在缅因州肯纳邦克波特度假的布什对法国的军事行动表示理解，因为法国在黎巴嫩有着长期的利益。
    新华社巴黎8月23日电　欧洲共同体12个成员国今天在巴黎发表一项联合声明，紧急呼吁黎巴嫩交战各方停止一切射击和轰炸，尽快实行全面停火。
    此外，欧洲共同体12国还敦请由摩洛哥、沙特阿拉伯和阿尔及利亚组成的阿拉伯三国委员会恢复其和平调解使命，以实现黎巴嫩民族和解、恢复黎巴嫩主权和实现永久性停火。
    新华社联合国8月23日电　联合国一位发言人今天说，联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔将于本星期派遣一名特使前往中东以协助寻找解决黎巴嫩危机的办法。
    被任命为特使的联合国秘书长办公厅主任让—克劳德·艾梅将于8月25日起访问摩洛哥、沙特阿拉伯和阿尔及利亚，同阿拉伯联盟三方委员会商讨黎巴嫩局势。
