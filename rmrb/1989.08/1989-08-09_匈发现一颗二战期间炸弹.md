Title: Bomb from World War II Discovered in Hungary
Author:
Date: 1989-08-09
Edition: 3

A 250-kilogram bomb from World War II was discovered in the city of Győr, Mosonmagyaróvár, along the Danube River in northwestern Hungary, according to a report from the Hungarian News Agency on August 8.

The bomb was dropped during World War II. Between 1944 and 1945, Győr experienced 13 major air raids and several smaller ones. Hungarian technical personnel have transported the bomb to a designated location and will soon detonate it.

### 匈发现一颗二战期间炸弹

1989-08-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    匈发现一颗二战期间炸弹
    据匈通社8日报道，在匈牙利西北部哲尔城莫肖尼—多瑙河畔发现一颗重250公斤的炸弹。
    这颗炸弹是二次大战期间投下的。在1944—1945年期间，哲尔城曾遭到13次大型空袭和多次小型空袭。匈牙利技术人员已把这颗炸弹运往指定地点，并将很快引爆。
