Title: Changsha Huanghua Airport Opens
Author: Wu Xinghua
Date: 1989-08-30
Edition: 2

Changsha Huanghua Airport Opens

Our reporter Wu Xinghua reports from Changsha: Changsha Huanghua Airport has been completed. Today, an inaugural flight ceremony was held at the airport, with the first flight to Beijing taking off from Huanghua Airport. This airport is located in the eastern suburbs of Changsha and can accommodate the takeoff and landing of MP-80 type or similar aircraft. The terminal building covers an area of 6,600 square meters and can accommodate 400 passengers during peak times. Currently, there are 23 routes open from Changsha to Beijing, Guangzhou, Nanjing, and other cities.

Construction of Changsha Huanghua Airport began on June 26, 1986, with a total investment of approximately 130 million yuan.

### 长沙黄花机场启用
吴兴华
1989-08-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    长沙黄花机场启用
    本报长沙8月29日电　记者吴兴华报道：长沙黄花机场建成。今天下午在机场举行隆重的首航典礼，飞往北京首班飞机从黄花机场起飞。这座机场座落在长沙市东郊，可供MP—80型或同类飞机起降，候机楼面积6600平方米，高峰时客流量可容纳400人。目前，开通长沙至北京、广州、南京等地23条航线。
    长沙黄花机场从1986年6月26日正式破土动工，共投资约1.3亿元。
