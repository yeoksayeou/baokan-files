Title: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Delegation from Democratic Yemen
Author:
Date: 1989-08-25
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun met with a delegation from the Democratic Yemen in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon. The delegation was led by Mohammad Dubba, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Construction.

Tian Jiyun expressed satisfaction with the development of economic cooperation between the two countries in recent years. He said, "China and the Democratic Yemen are both Third World countries. We should help each other in economic construction. Although China's aid to Yemen is not much, we strive to ensure good results."

Dubba said, "China's aid is sincere."

Both sides agreed that friendship and cooperation between the two countries should continue to develop.

### 田纪云会见民主也门客人

1989-08-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    田纪云会见民主也门客人
    新华社北京8月25日电  国务院副总理田纪云今天下午在人民大会堂会见了以建设住房部副部长穆罕默德·杜巴为团长的民主也门经贸代表团。
    田纪云对近年来两国经济合作的发展表示满意。他说，中国和民主也门同属第三世界国家，在经济建设上相互帮助，中国对也门的援助虽然不多，但力求效益良好。
    杜巴说，中国的援助是真诚的。
    双方一致表示希望两国之间的友谊和合作得到不断发展。
