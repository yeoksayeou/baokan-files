Title: Bi Zhong Breaks Two Asian Hammer Throw Records
Author:
Date: 1989-08-07
Edition: 4

National hammer throw training team member Bi Zhong on August 3rd, during a match between the national team and the Jiangxi team in Jinggangshan City, achieved excellent results of 76.02 meters and 77.04 meters, breaking the national record twice and surpassing the Asian record of 75.94 meters for the hammer throw twice.

Bi Zhong achieved these two excellent results in his fifth and sixth attempts. The Asian record for this event was set by Japanese athlete Murakami Shigeo on July 15, 1984.

### 毕忠两超链球亚洲纪录
从文
1989-08-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    毕忠两超链球亚洲纪录
    新华社南昌8月5日电  （通讯员从文）国家链球集训队队员毕忠8月3日在井冈山市举行的国家队与江西队一场对抗赛中，以76.02米和77.04米的好成绩，两次打破全国纪录和两次超过75.94米的链球亚洲纪录。
    毕忠是在第5次、第6次试投中掷出这两个好成绩的。这个项目的亚洲纪录是日本选手室伏重信在1984年7月15日创造的。
