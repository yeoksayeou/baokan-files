Title: West Germany's Hanover Opens Training Center for Disabled in Computer Technology
Author:
Date: 1989-08-20
Page: 7

A training center for the disabled in electronic computing has recently opened in Hanover, West Germany. The center focuses on helping disabled individuals master skills in sales, advertising, management, and other technical areas to facilitate their employment.
