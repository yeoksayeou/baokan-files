Title: Ariane Rocket Launches Again, World's First Astrometric Satellite Enters Orbit
Author: Zhang Qihua
Date: 1989-08-09
Page: 3

The People's Daily, Paris, August 9th

Today at 1:29 AM Paris time, the Ariane 4 rocket was successfully launched as scheduled from the Kourou Rocket Center in French Guiana. It successfully placed two satellites, each weighing 3,260 kilograms, into space. One of these is the world's first astrometric satellite.

This astrometric satellite is named after the ancient Greek astronomer Hipparchus and is known as the "Cosmic Surveyor." It can transmit over 240,000 pieces of information per second back to Earth, helping scientists determine the positions of 120,000 stars.

The other satellite that entered space is a television broadcasting satellite from West Germany. Through this satellite, more than 300 million viewers in Europe will be able to directly watch West German television.

This is the 33rd successful launch of the Ariane rocket. Starting from this launch, until December of next year, Arianespace will use the Ariane 4 rocket for all launches. Currently, the company has received orders to launch 35 satellites, with a total value of $2.3 billion.

### 阿丽亚娜火箭再次升空  世界首颗天体卫星进入轨道
张启华
1989-08-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿丽亚娜火箭再次升空
    世界首颗天体卫星进入轨道
    本报巴黎8月9日电  记者张启华报道：今天凌晨巴黎时间1时29分，阿丽亚娜4号火箭按预定计划在法属圭亚那的库鲁火箭中心发射成功（见下图），顺利地把2颗重达3260公斤的卫星送入太空，其中一颗是世界上第一个天体测量卫星。
    这颗天体测量卫星以古希腊天文学家伊帕尔科斯命名，被称为“宇宙测量员”，他每秒钟可向地球传回2.4万多个信息资料，帮助科学家测出12万个星球的位置。
    同时进入太空的另一个卫星是联邦德国的电视转播卫星，通过它，欧洲3亿多观众将可以直接收看西德的电视。
    这是阿丽亚娜火箭第33次发射成功，从这一次开始，直到明年12月，阿丽亚娜宇航公司将全部使用4号火箭发射。目前该公司已收到发射35颗卫星的订单，总价23亿美元。（附图片）
