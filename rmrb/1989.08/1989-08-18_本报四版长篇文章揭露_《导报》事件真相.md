Title: The People's Daily Exposes the Truth Behind the "World Economic Herald" Incident
Author:
Date: 1989-08-18
Page: 1

Today, the fourth page of this newspaper published a long article titled "The Truth Behind the 'World Economic Herald' Incident" by Qin Hua Shi. The article exposes the fact that the "World Economic Herald" actively advocated for bourgeois liberalization and created public opinion for the political turmoil that evolved from student protests to riots and eventually to the counter-revolutionary rebellion in Beijing.

### 本报四版长篇文章揭露  《导报》事件真相

1989-08-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    本报四版长篇文章揭露
    《导报》事件真相
    本报讯　今日本报第四版刊登署名忻华实的题为“《世界经济导报》事件真相”的长篇文章，揭露了《世界经济导报》积极鼓吹资产阶级自由化，为这场由学潮到动乱、进而发展成为北京反革命暴乱的政治风波造了一番舆论的真相。
