Title: Nicaragua Announces Suspension of Conscription
Author:
Date: 1989-08-04
Edition: 3

Nicaraguan President Daniel Ortega announced on August 3 during a multi-party dialogue with representatives of 21 political parties that the government will temporarily suspend conscription from September of this year until February of next year, during the election period, to ensure that young people can participate in the elections.

### 尼加拉瓜宣布暂停征兵

1989-08-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    尼加拉瓜宣布暂停征兵
    尼加拉瓜总统奥尔特加3日在同21个政党的代表举行多边对话时宣布，政府从今年9月至明年2月大选期间，暂时停止征兵工作，以保证青年能参加大选。
