Title: The Unyielding Integrity of Li Cunde
Author: Xun Fengming
Date: 1989-08-06
Edition: 4

Li Cunde, the director of the city administration for industry and commerce in Mengcheng County, Anhui Province, has been dedicated to his work since retiring from the military in 1965. Over the past 20 years, he has remained steadfast in his principles, ensuring that he neither indulges in greed, nor shirks his duties, nor engages in corruption. He has upheld and promoted the fine traditions of the people's army through his clean governance. He has been repeatedly recognized as an advanced worker.

The image shows Li Cunde (first from the left) explaining industrial and commercial regulations to individual business owners.
