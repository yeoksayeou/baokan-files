	Foreign Affairs Brief
	▲ On the afternoon of August 7, Wang Renzhong, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, met and had a banquet with a delegation of Romanian party workers led by Radu Berunescu, the economic secretary of the Brasov County Committee of the Romanian Communist Party, at the Great Hall of the People.
	The delegation, which arrived in China on July 24, conducted a study tour on China's economic management and modernization of production.
	▲ On the afternoon of August 7, Song Jian, State Councilor and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission, met and had a banquet with a delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea government science and technology delegation led by Li Zifang, Chairman of the State Science and Technology Commission, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse. Both sides exchanged views on further developing scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries and signed a memorandum of the delegation's visit to China.
	▲ On the afternoon of August 7, General Chi Haotian, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, met with a delegation of Romanian military officers on leave led by Major General K. Kodelescu at the Great Hall of the People. The hosts and guests had a friendly and cordial conversation.
	Chi Haotian highly praised the deep friendship between the two parties, the two armies, and the peoples of China and Romania. He said that this friendship can withstand the test of time.
	Both sides expressed their desire to further develop friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries' armies.

### 外事简讯

1989-08-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲中共中央书记处候补书记温家宝7日下午在人民大会堂会见并宴请由罗马尼亚共产党布拉索夫县委经济书记拉杜·伯乌内斯库率领的罗马尼亚党的工作者代表团。
    代表团自7月24日来华后，就中国的经济管理、生产现代化等问题进行了考察访问。
    ▲国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健7日下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请了由国家科学技术委员会委员长李子方率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府科技代表团。双方就进一步发展两国科技合作交换了意见，并签署了代表团访华纪要。
    ▲中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田上将7日下午在人民大会堂会见由科·科德列斯库少将率领的罗马尼亚军官休假团，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    迟浩田高度评价了中罗两党、两军和两国人民之间的深厚友谊。他说，这种友谊是经得起考验的。
    双方表示要进一步发展两国军队之间的友好合作关系。
                              　（新华社）
