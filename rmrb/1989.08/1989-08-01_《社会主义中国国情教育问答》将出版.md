Title: "Socialist China National Conditions Education Q&A" to be Published
Author: Zhu Xingtang
Date: 1989-08-01
Edition: 4

The People's Daily reports that the Party History Publishing House will soon publish the book "Socialist China National Conditions Education Q&A." This book is co-authored by cadres from the Organization Department, the Propaganda Department, the Central Archives Research Office, the Central Party School, and the State Council Research Office.

### 《社会主义中国国情教育问答》将出版
朱兴堂
1989-08-01
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    《社会主义中国国情教育问答》将出版
    本报讯　中共党史资料出版社即将出版《社会主义中国国情教育问答》一书。本书由中组部、中宣部、中央文献研究室、中央党校、国务院研究室等单位干部撰写。
                                  （朱兴堂）
