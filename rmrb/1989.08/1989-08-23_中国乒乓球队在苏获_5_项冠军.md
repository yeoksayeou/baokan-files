Title: The Chinese Table Tennis Team Wins 5 Titles in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-08-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 22nd.

The 8-day "Olympic Panorama" Magazine Cup International Table Tennis Tournament concluded on August 21st in the Soviet Far Eastern city of Khabarovsk. The Chinese team won a total of 5 titles, including the women's team, men's and women's singles, and men's and women's doubles.

According to today's "Soviet Sports News," except for the mixed doubles, the finals of the four other events were all between Chinese players. In the women's team final, the Chinese team defeated the North Korean team 3-0.

The Soviet team's players defeated the North Korean team and the Chinese players 5-2 and 2-0 in the men's team and mixed doubles finals, respectively, winning two titles.

The biggest upset in this tournament was that the Chinese team was eliminated by the North Korean team in the men's team event and did not advance to the final.

### 中国乒乓球队在苏获5项冠军

1989-08-23
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    中国乒乓球队在苏获5项冠军
    新华社莫斯科8月22日电　为期8天的《奥林匹克全景》杂志杯国际乒乓球赛21日在苏联远东城市哈巴罗夫斯克结束，中国队共夺得女子团体、男女单打、男女双打5项冠军。
  据《苏联体育报》今天报道，除混双以外，4个单项的决赛都在中国选手之间进行。在女子团体决赛中，中国队以3∶0战胜朝鲜队。
    苏联队选手在男子团体和混合双打决赛中以5∶2和2∶0的成绩分别击败了朝鲜队和中国选手，夺得两项冠军。
    这次比赛爆出的最大冷门是中国队在男子团体赛中被朝鲜队淘汰，未能进入决赛。
