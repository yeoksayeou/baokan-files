Title: Chinese Athletes Win Two More Medals at the University Games
Author: Jiang Jian Guo
Date: 1989-08-28
Edition: 3

Chinese athletes won two more medals at the University Games.

Our reporter Jiang Jian Guo reports from Duisburg on August 28: The Chinese men's rowing team achieved a significant breakthrough here yesterday. Fang Shengqiang, Shen Hongfei, Xie Yifan, and Xie Jianmin won the gold medal in the men's lightweight four without coxswain event. This is the first medal China has won in a world-class rowing competition.

Women's high jumper Jin Ling won the bronze medal with a jump of 1.88 meters in the final. The gold medal went to Romania's Galina Astafei (1.91 meters), and the silver medal to Silvia Costa.

So far, our country has won a total of 3 gold medals, 2 silver medals, and 4 bronze medals.

### 大学生运动会我选手又获两块奖牌
江建国
1989-08-28
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    大学生运动会我选手又获两块奖牌
    本报杜伊斯堡8月28日电　记者江建国报道：中国男子赛艇队昨天在这里实现了重大突破，方善强、沈鸿飞、谢一凡和谢建敏在男子轻量级4人双桨无舵项目决赛中获冠军，这是中国男子赛艇在世界级比赛中获得的第一块奖牌。
    女子跳高运动员金玲在决赛中以1.88米的成绩获得铜牌。这个项目的冠军是罗马尼亚的加莉娜·阿斯塔菲（1.91米），亚军是西尔维娅·戈斯塔。
    到目前为止，我国总计获3枚金牌，2枚银牌和4枚铜牌。
