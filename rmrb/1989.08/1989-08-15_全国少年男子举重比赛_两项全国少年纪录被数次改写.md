Title: Two National Youth Weightlifting Records Broken Multiple Times at the National Youth Men's Weightlifting Competition
Author: Hu Hao
Date: 1989-08-15
Edition: 4

The National Youth Men's Weightlifting Competition concluded today. In yesterday's and today's competitions, three athletes broke two national youth records.

In today's 100kg+ category, Wang Qiang from Liaoning team set a new national youth record with a 125.5kg snatch in his third attempt, surpassing the previous record of 125kg. His teammate Yang Lei performed even better, surpassing Wang Qiang's record with a 127.5kg snatch in his second attempt. Subsequently, Yang Lei broke the record again with a 130kg snatch in his third attempt.

Yesterday, Xu Tiehong from Liaoning team broke the national youth record in the 82.5kg category with a 130.5kg snatch, surpassing the previous record of 130kg.

After the competition, the national youth training team, composed of outstanding athletes selected from various teams, will undergo systematic and rigorous training in Changchun City.

### 全国少年男子举重比赛  两项全国少年纪录被数次改写
胡浩
1989-08-15
第4版(综合)
专栏：体坛新人

    全国少年男子举重比赛
    两项全国少年纪录被数次改写
    新华社长春8月14日电（记者胡浩）全国少年男子举重比赛今天结束。在昨天和今天的比赛中，有3人打破2项全国少年纪录。
    在今天进行的100公斤以上级比赛中，辽宁队的王强在第三次试举中以125.5公斤的成绩刷新了该级别125公斤的抓举全国少年纪录。而其队友杨雷力胜一筹，在第二次抓举试举中便以127.5公斤超过王强的成绩。接着，他在第三次试举中以130公斤再破该级别抓举全国少年纪录。
    昨日辽宁队的徐铁丰在82.5公斤级比赛中，以130.5公斤的抓举成绩打破130公斤的全国少年纪录。
    此次赛会结束后，由各队选拔出的优秀运动员组成的国家少年集训队将在长春市进行系统的严格训练。
