Title: Household Savings Agency Gains Popularity
Author: Wei Dianlu
Date: 1989-08-01
Edition: 2

Household Savings Agency Gains Popularity

According to Xinhua News Agency, Shijiazhuang, August 1st - (Correspondent Wei Dianlu) The Industrial and Commercial Bank of China in Huanghua County, Hebei Province, was the first in the country to establish a household savings agency. Savings deposits have grown rapidly. So far, the 25 household savings agencies in this county have attracted savings deposits totaling 4 million yuan.

The household savings agency essentially brings the bank to the fishermen's doorstep, allowing them to deposit and withdraw money at any time, which is very convenient.

### 家庭储蓄代办所受欢迎
韦殿禄
1989-08-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    家庭储蓄代办所受欢迎
    据新华社石家庄8月1日电　（通讯员韦殿禄）河北省黄骅县工商银行率先在全国开办家庭储蓄代办所，储蓄存款增长迅速。到目前为止，这个县25个家庭储蓄代办所吸收的储蓄存款余额已达400万元。
    家庭储蓄代办所实际上是把银行开到了渔民家门口，渔民随时可以存钱取款，极为方便。
