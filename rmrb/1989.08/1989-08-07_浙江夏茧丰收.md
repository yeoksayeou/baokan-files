Title: Abundant Summer Cocoons in Zhejiang
Author: Yu Yun Da
Date: 1989-08-07
Edition: 2

Zhejiang province has seen a bumper harvest of summer cocoons this year, with the procurement volume more than doubling compared to last year.

The province's total summer cocoon rearing reached 274,000 frames, a 7% increase over the same period last year. The total summer cocoon production is expected to reach 160,000 piculs, a 25% increase over last year. So far, the province has procured 103,000 piculs of fresh summer cocoons, a 127% increase over the same period last year. The average procurement price is 443 yuan per picul, which is basically the same as last year.

### 浙江夏茧丰收
虞云达
1989-08-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    浙江夏茧丰收
    新华社杭州8月7日电　（记者虞云达）浙江省今年夏茧喜获丰收，收购量比去年增长一倍以上。
    全省今年夏茧饲养量达27.4万张，比去年同期增加7％，夏茧总产量预计达16万担，比去年增产二成半。到目前为止，全省已收购夏鲜茧10.3万担，比去年同期猛增1.27倍。平均收购价为每担443元，与去年基本持平。
