Title: International Medical Imaging Equipment Exhibition Opens
Author: Mei Hongru
Date: 1989-08-23
Page: 2

The international exhibition of medical imaging treatment and diagnostic equipment opened in Beijing on the 21st.

More than 30 companies and manufacturers from the United States, Switzerland, West Germany, Sweden, Denmark, Japan, and the Hong Kong region participated in the exhibition, showcasing their advanced technologies and products. During the exhibition period, over 120 foreign businesspeople and medical experts will engage in extensive and in-depth trade negotiations and technical exchanges with their Chinese counterparts.

### 国际医用影像设备展览会开幕
梅洪如
1989-08-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国际医用影像设备展览会开幕
    本报讯　国际医用影像治疗及检查设备展览会二十一日在京开幕。
    来自美国、瑞士、联邦德国、瑞典、丹麦、日本和香港地区的三十多个公司和厂家到会展示了他们的先进技术和产品。展出期间，一百二十多位外籍企业家和医疗界专家，将与中国同行进行广泛、深入的贸易洽谈和技术交流。　（梅洪如）
