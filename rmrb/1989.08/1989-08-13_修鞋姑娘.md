Title: The Shoe Repair Girl
Author:
Date: 1989-08-13
Page: 8

The shoe repair girl
Her crutches have taken root in the street
Growing into a youthful aspiration
A vibrant flower
Blooming in the sunlight
Under the sun umbrella
The girl who once dreamed of being a ballerina
Who once wanted to dance on stage
And spin herself into countless rounds of applause
Now sits in the embrace of the street
In the admiration of many
Forging a new life lesson
With her hammer
She drives the nails firmly into the tough leather sole
Turning those lost dreams
Into a passionate exclamation mark
She hammers her days into solidity
So that others can tread more paths
And dance more beautiful dances

### 修鞋姑娘

1989-08-13
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    修鞋姑娘
    她的双拐在大街生了根
    生长出一个青春的憧憬
    一朵艳丽的花
    盛开在阳光下
    太阳伞底下
    那个曾经想当芭蕾舞演员
    曾经想在舞台上
    把自己旋成无数掌声的姑娘
    此刻她正在大街的怀抱里
    坐在许多人敬慕的目光里
    锤炼着一个崭新的生活课题
    她用钉锤
    把鞋钉狠狠砸进坚韧的皮掌
    把那些失落的幻想
    敲打成一片激昂的感叹号
    她把日子敲打得结结实实
    好让别人跋涉更多的路
    旋转更美的舞姿
