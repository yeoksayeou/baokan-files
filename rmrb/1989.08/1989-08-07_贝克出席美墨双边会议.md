Title: Baker Attends U.S.-Mexico Bilateral Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-08-07
Page: 3

U.S. Secretary of State Baker led a high-level American delegation to leave Washington on the 6th and head to Mexico to attend the U.S.-Mexico Binational Commission meeting held on the 7th. The meeting will discuss issues such as foreign debt and drug trafficking.

### 贝克出席美墨双边会议

1989-08-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    贝克出席美墨双边会议
    美国国务卿贝克6日率领美国一个高级代表团离开华盛顿前往墨西哥参加于7日举行的美墨双边委员会会议，讨论 外债和毒品走私等问题。
