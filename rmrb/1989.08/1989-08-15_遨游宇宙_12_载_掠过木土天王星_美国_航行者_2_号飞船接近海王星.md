Title: Voyaging Through Space for 12 Years, Passing Jupiter, Saturn, and Uranus: The U.S. "Voyager 2" Spacecraft Approaches Neptune
Author: Cang Lide
Date: 1989-08-15
Edition: 3

	Voyaging Through Space for 12 Years, Passing Jupiter, Saturn, and Uranus: The U.S. "Voyager 2" Spacecraft Approaches Neptune
	People's Daily, Washington, August 14th, Reporter Cang Lide: After 12 years of flight and a journey of 7.2 billion kilometers, the U.S. "Voyager 2" spacecraft is scheduled to arrive near Neptune in the solar system at midnight on the 24th of this month, and is expected to send back the first images and data of this planet to Earth.

	"Voyager 2" was launched from Florida on August 20, 1977. Since then, relying on the gravitational pull of various planets, the spacecraft flew by Jupiter on July 9, 1979, Saturn on August 25, 1981, and Uranus on January 24, 1986, transmitting back many valuable images and data. It is expected that this time "Voyager 2" will enter Neptune's elliptical atmosphere, reaching a point approximately 4,800 kilometers from the planet, and then approach Neptune's moons. After that, the spacecraft's main mission will be completed, but it will continue its interstellar flight, with a possible lifespan of millions of years. Neptune was discovered in 1846. Its orbital period around the Sun is 165 years, and astronomers have yet to accurately determine its complete orbital trajectory.

	"Voyager 2" weighs approximately 670 kilograms and is composed of 65,000 parts. It carries a 3.65-meter diameter circular antenna and cameras, ultraviolet spectrometers, infrared spectrometers, and other equipment. Currently, the ground control center's communication with the spacecraft takes 8 hours for a round trip.

### 遨游宇宙12载　掠过木土天王星  美国“航行者2”号飞船接近海王星
仓立德
1989-08-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    遨游宇宙12载　掠过木土天王星
    美国“航行者2”号飞船接近海王星
    本报华盛顿8月14日电　记者仓立德报道：经过12年飞行和72亿公里行程，美国“航行者2”号宇宙飞船将于本月24日午夜时分按期抵达太阳系海王星附近，并预计向地球首次发回有关这颗行星的图片资料。
    “航行者2”号是1977年8月20日从佛罗里达州发射进入太空的。此后依靠各个行星的引力，这艘飞船于1979年7月9日飞过木星，1981年8月25日飞过土星，1986年1月24日飞过天王星，并传回许多宝贵的图片及资料。预计此次“航行者2”号将飞入海王星的椭圆形大气层中，到达距离这颗行星约4800公里处，然后接近海王星的卫星。此后这艘飞船的主要使命便告结束，但仍将继续星际飞行，其寿命可能达上百万年。海王星是1846年被发现的，它环绕太阳的运行周期为165年，天文学家至今未能精确测定它的完整运行轨迹。
    “航行者2”号自重约670公斤，由6.5万个零件组成，携带一具直径为3.65米的圆形天线及摄像机、紫外分光仪、红外分光仪等设备。目前，地面控制中心与这艘飞船的联系，每往返一次需8个小时。
