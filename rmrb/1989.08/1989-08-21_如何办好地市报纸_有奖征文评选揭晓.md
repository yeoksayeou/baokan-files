Title: The Award Ceremony for the "How to Run a County-Level Newspaper" Essay Contest is Announced
Author: Zhou Wenhua
Date: 1989-08-21
Edition: 2

The award ceremony for the "How to Run a County-Level Newspaper" Bei Yue Cup essay contest, jointly organized by the Shanxi Province Yanbei Daily, the China County-Level Newspaper Research Association, and the editorial department of *Journalism Front*, was held on August 14 in Datong, Shanxi Province. The first-place prize was left vacant, with five second-place prizes and fifteen third-place prizes awarded.

### “如何办好地市报纸”有奖征文评选揭晓
周文华
1989-08-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    “如何办好地市报纸”有奖征文评选揭晓
    本报讯　记者周文华报道：由山西省雁北日报社、中国地市报研究会和《新闻战线》编辑部联合举办的“如何办好地市报纸”北岳杯有奖征文，8月14日在山西大同市评选揭晓。其中一等奖作品空缺，评出二等奖作品5篇，三等奖作品15篇。
