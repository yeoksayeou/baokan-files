Title: Beirut Resumes Shelling
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 21st

According to news from Beirut today, the shelling between Syrian forces stationed in Lebanon and Christian militias resumed after a brief hiatus, last night at midnight. The exchange of fire resulted in 3 deaths and 8 injuries.

Reports indicate that the shelling began when Syrian forces claimed that a ship loaded with weapons attempted to dock at a port controlled by Christian militias and started shelling the northern coast of Beirut. The Christian militias retaliated by continuously shelling Muslim positions in the western part of Beirut.

### 贝鲁特重开炮战

1989-08-21
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    贝鲁特重开炮战
    新华社开罗8月21日电　据今天来自贝鲁特的消息说，驻黎巴嫩叙利亚部队和黎基督教武装之间的炮战在经过短暂间歇后，昨天午夜再次重新开火，造成3人死亡，8人受伤。
  据报道，这次炮战是在叙军声称，装有武器的船只企图驶入基督教武装控制的港口卸货，并炮轰贝鲁特北部海岸后爆发的。基督教武装连续轰击了贝鲁特西区的穆斯林阵地。
