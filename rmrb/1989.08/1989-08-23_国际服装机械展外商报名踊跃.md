Title: International Garment Machinery Exhibition Sees Eager Overseas Sign-Ups
Author: Lù Guǎnghóng Yán Lì
Date: 1989-08-23
Edition: 2

The upcoming China International Garment Machinery Exhibition, scheduled for early September, has garnered a wide international response. As of now, over thirty companies from Japan, West Germany, France, Switzerland, Hong Kong, and other countries and regions have signed up to participate. Among these are several major manufacturers of garment machinery and equipment.

### 国际服装机械展外商报名踊跃
陆广宏　阎俐
1989-08-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国际服装机械展外商报名踊跃
    本报讯　将于九月初举行的中国国际服装机械展览会，在国际上受到了广泛响应。到目前为止，已有日本、西德、法国、瑞士、香港等国家和地区的三十多家公司报名参展，其中不少是服装机械设备的主要生产厂家。　（陆广宏　阎俐）
