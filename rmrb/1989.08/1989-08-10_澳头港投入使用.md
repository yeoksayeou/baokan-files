Title: Aotou Port Comes into Operation
Author:
Date: 1989-08-10
Edition: 2

Aotou Port in Huizhou County, Guangdong Province, has been completed after five years of construction and officially began operations on August 8.

Aotou Port is located in the southern part of Huizhou County, 47 nautical miles from Hong Kong. It is a natural harbor with excellent geographical conditions and serves as a comprehensive port for both passenger and cargo operations with external regions.

### 澳头港投入使用

1989-08-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    澳头港投入使用
    据新华社广州电广东惠阳县澳头港口岸经过5年建设已顺利竣工，8日正式投入使用。
    澳头港位于惠阳县南部，距香港47海里，是地理条件优越的天然良港，属客、货运综合对外口岸。
