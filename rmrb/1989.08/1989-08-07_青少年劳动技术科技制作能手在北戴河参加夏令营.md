Title: Youths Skilled in Labor Technology and Scientific Creation Attend Summer Camp in Beidaihe
Author: Lü Xiang Tao
Date: 1989-08-07
Edition: 2

A summer camp for the national youth labor technology and scientific creation competition was held today at the Beidaihe Youth Activity Base. The event is jointly organized by six units, including the State Education Commission, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, the China Association for Science and Technology, and the Central Television Station.

This one-week summer camp is the final event of the national youth labor technology and scientific creation competition.

The honorary chairman of this competition, Bo Yibo, and consultants Zhou Peiyuan and Lin Zongtang, expressed their approval and support for this summer camp activity.

### 青少年劳动技术科技制作能手在北戴河参加夏令营
卢向韬
1989-08-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    青少年劳动技术科技制作能手在北戴河参加夏令营
    本报北戴河八月七日电　由国家教委、团中央、中国科协、中央电视台等六单位联合主办的全国青少年劳动技术科技制作竞赛夏令营，今天在北戴河青少年活动基地举行。
    这次为期一周的夏令营，是全国青少年劳动技术科技制作竞赛的最后一项活动日程。
    这次竞赛的名誉主席薄一波，顾问周培源、林宗棠，对这次夏令营活动表示赞赏和支持。     （卢向韬）
