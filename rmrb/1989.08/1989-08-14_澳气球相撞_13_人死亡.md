Title: Hot Air Balloons Collide in Australia, 13 Dead
Author:
Date: 1989-08-14
Page: 3

Two hot air balloons collided in the sky above the central Australian tourist city of Alice Springs on the morning of the 13th, resulting in the deaths of 13 people. This incident is one of the largest air tragedies in Australia in over 20 years.

### 澳气球相撞13人死亡

1989-08-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    澳气球相撞13人死亡
    两个热气球13日上午在澳大利亚中部旅游城市艾利斯斯普林斯上空相撞，13人死亡，成为澳洲20多年来最大的空中悲剧之一。
