Title: Grassland Livestock Industry in Its Second Development Peak in 40 Years, Full of Hope
Author: Chen Zhijiang
Date: 1989-08-20
Edition: 1

The vice-minister of the Ministry of Agriculture, Liu Jiang, after inspecting the grassland livestock industry in Hebei, Inner Mongolia, Gansu, and Qinghai provinces, believes that the grassland livestock industry involving more than 260 counties and 39 million people in China has the potential for significant development.

Liu Jiang, in analyzing the historical development of the pastoral economy, said that in the past 40 years, China's grassland livestock industry has experienced two development peaks: once from 1960 to 1965, and again from 1985 to the present. Similar to the previous peak, the current development opportunity for the grassland livestock industry is related to the current stagnant situation in national agriculture and the tight supply of meat products. He believes that under the condition of already scarce grain supply, developing grain-intensive livestock industry and relying on pig farming to solve the meat supply problem in China is unreasonable. Now, vigorously developing grass-fed livestock industry is an inevitable trend.

In the face of this good opportunity for the grassland livestock industry, it is crucial to guide the development of the pastoral economy centered on livestock industry. The pastoral areas are home to multiple ethnic minorities, including Tibetans, Mongols, Uyghurs, and Kazakhs, and the economic development of these areas is closely related to social stability and ethnic unity. Liu Jiang said that if guided properly, the pastoral economy will develop healthily; otherwise, the opportunity will be missed.

Liu Jiang believes that the modernization pilot projects should be used to drive the development of the pastoral economy.

### 处于四十年来第二次发展高峰  草原牧业大有希望  农业部负责人强调引导好牧区经济
陈志强
1989-08-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    处于四十年来第二次发展高峰
    草原牧业大有希望
    农业部负责人强调引导好牧区经济
    新华社西宁8月20日电　（记者陈志强）农业部副部长刘江在考察河北、内蒙古、甘肃、青海等省区的草原畜牧业后认为，涉及260多个县、3900多万人口的我国草原畜牧业可望获得大发展。
    刘江在分析牧区经济发展历史时说，40年来我国牧区草原畜牧业有过两次发展高峰，一次是1960年到1965年，一次是1985年到现在。同上一次发展高峰一样，这次草原畜牧业发展机遇的来临与目前全国农业徘徊局面以及肉食品供应紧张有关。他认为，在粮食本来就很紧缺的条件下，发展高耗粮畜牧业，把养猪作为畜产品主体来解决我国肉食供应是不合理的。现在大力发展草食畜牧业已是大势所趋。
    在草原畜牧业面临的这个良好机遇面前，如何引导以畜牧业为中心的牧区经济发展是至关重要的。牧区是藏族、蒙古族、维吾尔族、哈萨克族等多民族聚居区，经济的发展关系到社会安定和民族团结。刘江说，引导得好，牧区经济就会健康发展，否则就会丧失良机。
    刘江认为应该通过现代化试点带动牧区经济发展。
