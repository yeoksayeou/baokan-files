Title: China University Men's Basketball Team Loses to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1989-08-25
Page: 3

Column: Sports

The Chinese men's university basketball team lost to Yugoslavia last night in the final 24 seconds of the game, missing out on the opportunity to advance in the group stage with a close score of 101:105. The Chinese team led 54:48 at halftime. With 24 seconds remaining in the second half, the score was tied at 101. Yugoslavia, known for their experience, scored two consecutive points in the final 24 seconds.

### 我大学生男篮负于南斯拉夫队

1989-08-25
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    我大学生男篮负于南斯拉夫队
    本报杜伊斯堡8月25日电　中国男子大学生篮球队昨天晚上在对南斯拉夫的比赛最后24秒中连输两球，从而以101∶105的接近比分失去小组出线机会。中国队上半场以54∶48领先。下半场离结束还有24秒钟时，双方打成101平。南斯拉夫队不愧经验丰富，在24秒钟内连进两球。
