Title: Hebei Province Honors Outstanding Technological Personnel
Author:
Date: 1989-08-07
Edition: 1

Hebei Province has recently established the Governor's Special Award to reward those who have made outstanding contributions in the work of revitalizing the province through science and technology. This year, the award, amounting to 100,000 yuan, will be specifically given to individuals who have achieved technological breakthroughs in projects that have won first-class provincial awards or second-class national awards and above. These individuals must have solved key technical issues and achieved significant economic and social benefits in revitalizing Hebei Province's economy.

### 河北重奖有功科技人员

1989-08-07
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    河北重奖有功科技人员
    新华社石家庄8月7日电　河北省最近设立省长特别奖，奖励那些在科技兴省工作中贡献突出的科技人员。这次定为一万元的省长特别奖，将专门奖励那些具有获得省级一等奖、国家级二等奖以上的科技成果，并在成果中解决了技术关键，在振兴河北省经济中取得显著经济和社会效益的个人。
