Title: Mozambican President Meets Chinese Foreign Minister; Minister Ends Visit to Africa
Author: Yáng Zhìgāng
Date: 1989-08-12
Edition: 3

Mozambican President Meets Chinese Foreign Minister; Minister Ends Visit to Africa

MAPUTO, Aug. 11 (Xinhua) -- Mozambican President Joaquim Chissano today met with Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, who is on a visit to Mozambique.

Chissano briefed Qian on the latest developments in Mozambique and the broader southern African region, and discussed the efforts made by the Mozambican government to achieve peace both domestically and regionally.

Qian congratulated the Mozambican Liberation Front on the achievements of its fifth national congress and expressed support for the "correct line and policies" adopted by the congress to strive for peace and the reconstruction of the economy, which has been devastated by war.

Both sides expressed satisfaction with the development of friendly relations between Mozambique and China.

MAPUTO, Aug. 11 (Xinhua) -- Mozambican National Assembly President Marcelino dos Santos today morning cordially met with visiting Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen. Dos Santos and Qian exchanged views on bilateral relations between Mozambique and China, and also discussed the regional situation in southern Africa.

Qian visited the Mozambican Revolution Museum this morning. Yesterday, he toured the port of Maputo, a dam in the suburbs, and a Chinese aid project in Maputo City.

MAPUTO, Aug. 11 (Xinhua) -- Mozambique and China today signed an agreement here for the construction of the Mozambican Parliament Building.

According to the agreement, China will provide the necessary financial and technical assistance to Mozambique.

Chinese Vice Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Lü Xuejin and Mozambican Deputy Minister of Industry and Energy Fernandes signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

MAPUTO, Aug. 12 (Xinhua) -- Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen today concluded his six-nation tour of southern Africa and departed for home.

Mozambican Foreign Minister Machel and others saw Qian off at the airport. Chinese Ambassador to Mozambique Xiao Sijin and other foreign diplomats also came to the airport to bid farewell.

### 莫桑比克总统会见钱外长  钱外长结束访非之行回国
杨志刚
1989-08-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    莫桑比克总统会见钱外长  钱外长结束访非之行回国
    新华社马普托8月11日电  莫桑比克总统希萨诺今天会见了正在这里访问的中国外交部长钱其琛。
    希萨诺向钱外长简要介绍了莫桑比克国内和整个南部非洲地区形势的最新发展，并介绍了莫政府为争取国内和地区和平所作出的努力。
    钱外长祝贺莫桑比克解放阵线党第五次代表大会取得的成就，并表示支持该代表大会为争取和平、复兴遭受战争破坏的经济而制订的“正确的路线和方针”。
    双方对莫中两国友好关系的发展表示满意。
    新华社马普托8月11日电  莫桑比克人民议会议长马塞利诺·多斯桑托斯今天上午在这里亲切地会见了来访的中国外交部长钱其琛。多斯桑托斯和钱外长就莫中两国关系问题进行了会谈，他们还就南部非洲的地区形势交换了意见。
    钱外长今天上午参观了莫桑比克革命博物馆，昨天参观了马普托港和郊区的一座水坝以及马普托市的中国经援项目。
    新华社马普托8月11日电  莫桑比克和中国今天在这里签订了一项建造莫桑比克议会宫的协议。
    根据协议，中国将向莫提供必要的财政和技术援助。
    正在这里访问的中国经贸部副部长吕学俭和莫桑比克工业、能源部副部长费尔南德斯分别代表本国政府签署了这项协议。
    新华社马普托8月12日电（记者杨志刚）中国外交部长钱其琛今天结束南部非洲六国之行，离开这里回国。
    莫桑比克外长莫孔比等人前往机场为钱外长送行。中国驻莫桑比克大使肖思晋以及其他一些外国使节也前往机场送行。
