Title: Thai Charoen Pokphand Group Expands Investment in China
Author: Ma Shengrong
Date: 1989-08-28
Edition: 3

The Thai Charoen Pokphand Group (CP Group) will continue to expand its investment in China and strengthen economic cooperation with China.

CP Group is a subsidiary of Charoen Pokphand Company, headquartered in Hong Kong. Li Shaozhu, vice president of Charoen Pokphand Company, said that CP Group plans to build four new feed enterprises and one integrated poultry farming enterprise in China. He said that the recent turmoil in China has not affected the group's business in China. Since the group started investing in China in 1982, its business has expanded by an average of 20% per year.

Li Shaozhu said, "The main problem affecting our business expansion is the lack of qualified management personnel." "The Chinese market is huge, and the prospects for investment in China are very good."

### 泰国正大公司扩大对华投资
马胜荣
1989-08-28
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    泰国正大公司扩大对华投资
    新华社曼谷8月28日电  （记者马胜荣）泰国著名的正大公司（卜蜂集团）将继续扩大在中国的投资业务，加强同中国的经济合作。
    卜蜂集团是总部设在香港的正大公司的分公司。正大公司副总裁李绍祝说，卜蜂集团计划在中国新建4个饲料企业和一个养鸡联合企业。他说，最近中国发生的动乱完全没有影响该集团在中国的业务。该集团从1982年到中国投资以来，业务每年平均扩大20％左右。
    李绍祝说：“目前影响我们扩大业务的主要问题是缺少合格的管理人员。”“中国的市场巨大，在中国投资前景很好。”
