Title: Shao Huaze Hosts Dinner for Ovchinnikov
Author:
Date: 1989-08-24
Edition: 2

The editor-in-chief of the People's Daily, Shao Huaze, held a dinner this evening to welcome the political observer from the Soviet Union's Pravda, Ovchinnikov, on his visit to China. The acting charge d'affaires and counselor with the rank of minister, Fidorotov, from the Soviet Union's embassy in China, was also invited to attend.

Ovchinnikov arrived in Beijing this morning at the invitation of the People's Daily.

### 邵华泽宴请奥夫钦尼科夫

1989-08-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    邵华泽宴请奥夫钦尼科夫
    本报北京8月24日讯　《人民日报》总编辑邵华泽今晚举行宴会，欢迎苏联《真理报》政治观察家奥夫钦尼科夫访华。苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办、公使衔参赞菲多托夫应邀出席。
    奥夫钦尼科夫是应《人民日报》社邀请于今天上午到京的。
