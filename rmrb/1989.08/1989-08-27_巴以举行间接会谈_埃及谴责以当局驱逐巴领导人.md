Title: Palestinians and Israelis Hold Indirect Talks; Egypt Condemns Israeli Expulsion of Palestinian Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-08-27
Edition: 3

Palestinians and Israelis Hold Indirect Talks

Egypt Condemns Israeli Expulsion of Palestinian Leaders

According to a report by the New China News Agency in Tunis on August 26, the Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) Executive Committee member Abu Mazen (Mahmoud Abbas) confirmed that the Palestinian Central Committee, including Israeli Defense Minister Rabin, is currently in "indirect contact" regarding the election plan for the occupied territories.

Abu Mazen, in a statement to the United Arab Emirates' "Unity" newspaper, confirmed that Israeli officials proposed that the PLO should nominate candidates from the occupied territories to negotiate with Israel. He said that the Palestinian side completely rejected this idea. The PLO is prepared to form a delegation consisting of Palestinians from inside and outside the occupied territories to directly negotiate with Israel on the issue of organizing elections.

According to a report by the New China News Agency in Cairo on August 26, Egyptian Foreign Minister Boutros Boutros-Ghali condemned Israel's recent expulsion of four Palestinian leaders from the West Bank.

Boutros-Ghali pointed out that the actions of the Israeli authorities violate international law and the principles and regulations of the Geneva Conventions regarding the treatment of residents in occupied territories.

On August 24, the Israeli Supreme Court approved the decision to expel four Palestinian activists from the West Bank, accusing them of being leaders of the 20-month Palestinian uprising in the occupied territories. Since the outbreak of the Palestinian uprising in December 1987, the Israeli authorities have expelled 59 Palestinians from the occupied territories.

### 巴以举行间接会谈  埃及谴责以当局驱逐巴领导人

1989-08-27
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    巴以举行间接会谈
    埃及谴责以当局驱逐巴领导人
    据新华社突尼斯8月26日电　据突尼斯非洲通讯社报道，巴解组织执委会委员阿布·马赞（马哈茂德·阿巴斯）今天证实，巴勒斯坦中央同包括国防部长拉宾在内的以色列负责人，目前正就在被占领土的选举方案进行“间接接触”。
    阿布·马赞在对阿联酋《团结报》发表的声明中确认，以色列负责人提出，巴解组织应该向被占领土居民提出候选人，以便同以色列进行谈判。他说，巴勒斯坦方面完全拒绝了这种想法。巴解组织准备组成一个由被占领土内外巴勒斯坦人参加的代表团，就组织选举问题同以色列直接谈判。
    据新华社开罗8月26日电  埃及外交国务部长加利26日对中东通讯社发表谈话，谴责以色列最近从被占领土约旦河西岸驱逐4名巴勒斯坦领导人。
    加利指出，以色列当局的行径违背了国际法和日内瓦公约有关对待被占领土居民的规定和原则。
    8月24日，以色列最高法庭批准了从约旦河西岸驱逐4名巴勒斯坦积极分子的决定，指控他们是历时20个月的被占领土巴勒斯坦起义的领导人。自1987年12月巴勒斯坦起义爆发以来，以色列当局已从被占领土驱逐了59名巴勒斯坦人。
