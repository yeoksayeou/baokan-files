Title: Multiple Chinese Track and Field Athletes Selected for the Asian Team
Author:
Date: 1989-08-23
Page: 3

According to a report from the *China Sports News* today, 24 Chinese track and field athletes have been selected for the Asian team. They will participate in the fifth World Cup Athletics Championships to be held in Barcelona this September.

The Chinese athletes selected are:

Men's team: 110-meter hurdles Li Teng, 4x400-meter relay Zhao Cunlin, 5,000-meter Wang Helin, high jump Liu Yunpeng, pole vault Liang Xueren, long jump Chen Zunrong, triple jump Chen Yanping, shot put Ma Yongfeng, discus throw Hu Tao, hammer throw Bi Zhong.

Women's team: 100-meter hurdles Liu Huajin, 400-meter hurdles Chen Dongmei, 800-meter Sun Sumei (also 4x400-meter relay), 1,500-meter Feng Yanbo, 3,000-meter Wang Yongmei, 4x100-meter relay Zhang Caihua, Zhang Xiaojun, Liu Shaomei, Ge Weidong, high jump Jin Ling, long jump Xiong Qiying, shot put Huang Zihong, discus throw Hou Xuemei, javelin throw Zhang Li.

Currently, the Chinese athletes participating in the World Cup Athletics Championships are actively preparing and striving to perform at their best in the competition.

### 我多名田径选手入选亚洲队

1989-08-23
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    我多名田径选手入选亚洲队
    本报讯　据《中国体育报》今天报道，中国有24名田径选手入选亚洲队，他们将参加今年9月在巴塞罗那举行的第五届世界杯田径赛。
    这些中国运动员是：
    男子：110米栏李彤，4×400米接力赵存林，五千米王禾林，跳高刘云鹏，撑竿跳高梁学仁，跳远陈尊荣，三级跳远陈燕平，铅球马永峰，铁饼胡涛，链球毕忠。
    女子：100米栏刘华金，400米栏陈冬梅，800米孙素梅（兼4×400米接力），一千五百米冯艳波，三千米王咏梅，4×100米接力张彩华、张小琼、刘绍梅、葛卫东，跳高金玲，跳远熊其英，铅球黄志红，铁饼侯雪梅，标枪张丽。
    目前，参加世界杯田径赛的中国运动员正在积极准备，力争在比赛中发挥出最好水平。
