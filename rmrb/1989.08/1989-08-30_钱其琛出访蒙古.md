Title: Qian Qichen Visits Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1989-08-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 30th.

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, at the invitation of the Mongolian People's Republic government, departed Beijing by train today for a formal friendly visit to the Mongolian People's Republic. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhu Qizhen and Mongolian Charge d'Affaires to China Baolede attended the station to see him off.

### 钱其琛出访蒙古

1989-08-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    钱其琛出访蒙古
    新华社北京八月三十日电  中国外交部长钱其琛应蒙古人民共和国政府的邀请，今天乘火车离开北京前往蒙古人民共和国进行正式友好访问。外交部副部长朱启祯和蒙古驻华使馆临时代办包勒德等前往车站送行。
