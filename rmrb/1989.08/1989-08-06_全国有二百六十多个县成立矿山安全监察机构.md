Title: Over 260 Counties Establish Mine Safety Supervision Agencies
Author: Zhao Mingliang
Date: 1989-08-06
Edition: 1

	Over 260 counties (cities, districts) across the country have been approved to establish mine safety supervision agencies, with nearly a thousand supervisors assigned. This indicates that a safety guarantee system for township and town mines in China is taking shape.

	Currently, over 200 counties (cities, districts) are in the process of setting up these agencies.

### 全国有二百六十多个县成立矿山安全监察机构
赵明亮
1989-08-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    全国有二百六十多个县成立矿山安全监察机构
    据新华社北京八月五日电　（记者赵明亮）从有关部门获悉，目前全国已有二百六十六个县（市、区）经批准成立了矿山安全监察机构，配备了近千名监察人员。这表明我国乡镇矿山安全保障体系正在形成。
    目前尚有二百多个县（市、区）正在筹建这一机构。
