Title: UK Lags Behind in Waste Recycling in Europe, Newspaper Urges Government to Formulate Recycling Plan
Author: Wáng Guóruì
Date: 1989-08-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London

In terms of waste recycling, the UK is the worst-performing country in Europe.

The UK generates 20 million tons of waste annually. Environmental workers say that at least 80% of this waste can be recycled. However, the UK's recycling rate is very low: only 16% of glass is recycled (Germany, Denmark, the Netherlands, and Switzerland all achieve 50%), less than one-third of paper is recycled, and only 8% of metal cans are recycled (less than half the European average).

Effective waste recycling can reduce the need for landfill sites, decrease environmental pollution, and save energy.

Recycling one ton of aluminum cans saves 95% of the energy compared to producing one ton of aluminum from bauxite.

Recycling glass saves 25% of energy, paper saves 50%, and plastic saves 80%.

To improve the UK's poor recycling record, _The Daily Telegraph_ and "Friends of the Earth" have launched a nationwide recycling campaign. They are calling on the British government to include regulations in this autumn's "Green Bill," requiring local authorities to develop recycling plans.

### 英国废品回收工作在欧洲最差  英报促政府制订废品回收计划
王国瑞
1989-08-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英国废品回收工作在欧洲最差
    英报促政府制订废品回收计划
    据新华社伦敦电　（记者王国瑞）在废品回收工作方面，英国是欧洲国家中做得最差的一个。
    英国每年产生2000万吨垃圾。环境工作者说，其中至少有80％的垃圾可以回收。但是，英国的废品回收率很低：回收玻璃16％（联邦德国、丹麦、荷兰和瑞士都达50％），回收废纸不到1／3，回收金属罐头盒8％（不到欧洲平均数的一半）。
    废品回收工作做得好，可以减少倾倒垃圾的场地，减少环境污染，节约能源。
    用回收的铝罐头盒炼一吨铝，比用铝矾土炼一吨铝可节约能源95％。
    玻璃回收节能25％，纸回收节能50％，塑料回收节能80％。
    为了改变英国在废品回收方面的落后状况，《每日电讯报》和“地球之友”已发起在全英国开展废品回收运动，他们要求英国政府在今秋的“绿色法案”中作出规定，要求地方当局制订回收计划。
