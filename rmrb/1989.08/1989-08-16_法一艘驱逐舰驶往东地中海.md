Title: A French Destroyer Heads to the Eastern Mediterranean
Author:
Date: 1989-08-16
Page: 3

People's Daily, Paris, August 16th

According to local newspapers, amid the deteriorating situation in Lebanon, France has decided to dispatch a missile destroyer to the waters off Lebanon to carry out missions.

The "Dekayssena" destroyer set sail from the Toulon naval base yesterday morning. The government issued a statement saying that this decision is to prepare for the evacuation of French nationals living in Lebanon. The French ambassador to Beirut has been shot at multiple times.

### 法一艘驱逐舰驶往东地中海

1989-08-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    法一艘驱逐舰驶往东地中海
    本报巴黎8月16日电　据此间报纸报道：在黎巴嫩局势急剧恶化情况下，法国决定派出一艘导弹驱逐舰前往黎巴嫩海域执行任务。
    被派出的“迪凯斯纳”号驱逐舰是昨天早晨离开土伦军港的。政府在一项公告中说，这一决定是为了准备营救生活在黎巴嫩的法国人。法国驻贝鲁特大使已多次受到枪击。
