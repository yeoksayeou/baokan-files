Title: Automated Wastewater Treatment System Developed
Author:
Date: 1989-08-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 14th.

The first domestically developed automated control system for treating electroplating wastewater, which ensures that the treated water meets the allowable discharge standards, was successfully tested and approved in Beijing today.

Experts have praised the electroplating wastewater treatment system, designed and manufactured by the Military Equipment and Radar Repair Factory of the Logistics Department of the Beijing Military Region. The system is noted for its low cost and minimal land requirements, making it highly suitable for widespread application.

### 自控废水处理系统制成
新庆
1989-08-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    自控废水处理系统制成
    本报北京8月14日讯　国内首创、通过自动控制使电镀废水达到允许排放标准的废水处理自动控制装置及其相关设施、技术，今天在北京通过技术鉴定。
    专家认为，由北空后勤部军械雷达修理厂设计试制的这一套电镀废水处理系统，费用低，占地少，有推广价值。　（新庆）
