Title: Japan's Defense Budget to Increase Again Next Year, Exceeding 400 Billion Yen for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1989-08-20
Edition: 3

Japan's defense budget for the next fiscal year will increase again, reaching 4.1688 trillion yen (approximately $29.777 billion), a 6.35% increase from the current fiscal year. This will be the first time Japan's defense budget has exceeded 4 trillion yen.

According to the Japanese Defense Agency's announcement on the 19th, the next fiscal year is the final year of the mid-term defense force expansion plan (from the 1986 fiscal year to the 1990 fiscal year), and the agency's policy is to complete the plan as scheduled. The Ground Self-Defense Force will introduce 30 new tanks and 36 SSM-1 surface-to-ship missiles to modernize its combat brigades and enhance its anti-tank and anti-sea invasion firepower. The Maritime Self-Defense Force will introduce 2 Aegis-equipped ships, 1 submarine, and 3 missile boats to improve its anti-submarine, anti-aircraft, and anti-torpedo capabilities. The Air Self-Defense Force will introduce 11 F-15 fighter jets and 1 Patriot surface-to-air missile battery.

### 日本明年度防卫费继续增加  总额将首次突破四万亿日元

1989-08-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    日本明年度防卫费继续增加  总额将首次突破四万亿日元
    据新华社东京8月20日电　据日本防卫厅19日宣布，明年度的防卫预算额将比本年度增加6.35％，达4.1688万亿日元（约合297.77亿美元）。这将是日本防卫费首次突破4万亿日元大关。
    明年度是中期防卫力量扩充计划（1986年度至1990年度）最后一年，防卫厅的方针是要按计划完成。陆上自卫队为了提高作战师的现代化和反坦克、反海上进攻火力，将引进新坦克30辆、88式地对舰导弹SSMI36枚。海上自卫队为了提高防潜、防空、防鱼雷能力，将引进宙斯盾舰2艘、潜水艇1艘、导弹艇3艘。航空自卫队将引进F15狙击战斗机11架，爱国者地对空导弹1群等。
