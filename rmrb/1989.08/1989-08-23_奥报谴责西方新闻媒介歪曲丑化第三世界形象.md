Title: Austrian Newspaper Condemns Western Media for Distorting and Vilifying the Image of the Third World
Author:
Date: 1989-08-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 23rd.

An article titled "The Third World and the Media" was published in the Austrian newspaper *Salzburg News* on August 22nd. The article condemns certain Western media outlets for distorting and vilifying the image of Third World countries.

The article states that Western news agencies have extensively reported on the situations in Third World countries, but most of these reports are false and unrealistic, presenting a distorted picture to readers. The article points out that some Western journalists, driven by their biases against Third World countries, focus on the negative aspects of these nations, reporting extensively on topics such as war, crises, disasters, and famines. To enhance the impact, they even resort to exaggeration and sensationalism, while ignoring the progress and development in these countries. The article says that if Western media genuinely want to do good for Third World countries, they should provide insightful analyses of the pressing issues these countries face and offer practical suggestions, rather than merely seeking sensational stories to entertain Western readers and audiences.

The article concludes by stating that today's five major Western news agencies virtually monopolize the global news market. Under these circumstances, Third World countries have every reason to doubt whether their interests are being treated fairly. The article suggests that Third World countries should strengthen their own media industries to counterbalance Western news agencies and accurately report the real situations in the Third World to the global audience.

### 奥报谴责西方新闻媒介歪曲丑化第三世界形象

1989-08-23
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    奥报谴责西方新闻媒介歪曲丑化第三世界形象
    新华社北京8月23日电  奥地利《萨尔茨堡新闻》8月22日发表一篇题为《第三世界和新闻媒介》的署名文章，谴责某些西方新闻媒介歪曲丑化第三世界国家形象的作法。
    文章说，西方新闻机构对第三世界国家情况进行了大量报道，但其中大多数都是虚假的，不真实的，读者从这些报道中只能得到一幅“歪曲的画面”。文章指出，某些西方记者出于对第三世界国家的偏见，把视线集中在这些国家的阴暗面上，连篇累牍地进行大量报道，战争、危机、灾难、饥荒成了他们报道的主题，为增强效果，甚至不惜采取渲染夸大的手法，而对第三世界国家的进步与发展却视而不见。文章说，如果西方新闻媒介真想为第三世界国家做些好事，就应该对这些国家急待解决的一些问题进行中肯的分析，并提出切实可行的建议，而不要仅猎取一些耸人听闻的东西去取悦西方读者和听众。
    文章最后说，今天的5大西方通讯社几乎垄断了世界新闻市场，在这种情况下，第三世界国家完全有理由怀疑自己的利益是否受到了公正对待。文章建议第三世界国家加强自己的新闻事业，以此来与西方新闻机构抗衡，真实地向世界报道第三世界的情况。
