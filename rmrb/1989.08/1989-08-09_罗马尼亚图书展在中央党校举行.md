Title: Romanian Book Exhibition Held at the Central Party School
Author:
Date: 1989-08-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th August

Approximately 1,000 sets of Romanian books began their exhibition today at the Central Party School of the Communist Party of China in Beijing. This book exhibition is held to celebrate the 45th anniversary of Romania's liberation.

Romanian Ambassador to China, Angelo Mikulescu, and Vice-President of the Central Party School, Xue Ju, both spoke at the opening ceremony. The exhibition will conclude on the 12th, with some of the books to be donated to the Central Party School Library.

### 罗马尼亚图书展在中央党校举行

1989-08-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    罗马尼亚图书展在中央党校举行
    新华社北京8月9日电　约1000套罗马尼亚图书今天起在北京中共中央党校开始展出。这次图书展是为了庆祝罗马尼亚解放45周年举办的。
    罗马尼亚驻华大使安杰洛·米库列斯库和中央党校副校长薛驹分别在开幕式上讲了话。图书展将于12日结束，部分图书将赠于中央党校图书馆。
