Title: Czechoslovakia Owes Western Countries $6.7 Billion
Author:
Date: 1989-08-14
Edition: 3

According to the Czech newspaper *Lidová demokracie*, Czechoslovakia currently owes Western developed countries approximately $6.7 billion. During the current five-year plan, the debt owed to Czechoslovakia by socialist countries and the trade surplus with these countries will amount to 350-400 billion foreign exchange korunas (approximately 10 foreign exchange korunas for 1 dollar).

### 捷欠西方外债67亿美元

1989-08-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    捷欠西方外债67亿美元
    据捷《人民民主报》12日透露，捷克斯洛伐克目前欠西方发达国家的外债约为67亿美元。在本5年计划中，社会主义国家欠捷的债务和捷同这些国家的外贸顺差将达350—400亿外汇克朗（约10外汇克朗为1美元）。
            （据新华社）
