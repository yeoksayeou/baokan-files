Zambian President Kunda Hosts Dinner for Minister Qian

Zambian and Chinese Foreign Ministers Hope for Peaceful Resolution of Southern African Issues

Minister Qian Meets ANC Secretary-General in Lusaka

LUSAKA, Aug. 8 (Xinhua) — Zambian President Kenneth Kaunda held a working dinner today to welcome Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen and the delegation he leads.

In his welcome speech, Kaunda noted that China plays a very important role in international affairs. He thanked the Chinese government for the substantial aid it has provided to Zambia in its struggle for independence and in developing its national economy.

Qian praised Zambia's important role in supporting the struggle for independence and liberation in this region. He said the Chinese government will continue to make efforts to strengthen the extensive bilateral cooperation between China and Zambia.

When discussing China's domestic situation, Qian pointed out that some in the West believe that the Chinese government's actions to suppress counter-revolutionary riots will weaken China's role in international affairs. In fact, he said, this prediction is completely wrong.

He said the Chinese government will adhere to an independent and peaceful foreign policy, and strengthen solidarity with the people of the Third World, including Africa.

LUSAKA, Aug. 8 (Xinhua) — Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, who is visiting Zambia, and Zambian Defense Minister and Acting Foreign Minister F. Hapunda today expressed the hope that the issues in Southern Africa can be resolved through dialogue and consultation. In the evening, Hapunda hosted a welcome dinner for Qian and his delegation.

LUSAKA, Aug. 8 (Xinhua) — Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, who is visiting here, met today with Alfred Nzo, the Secretary-General of the African National Congress of South Africa.

Nzo briefed Qian on the latest developments in the political situation in Southern Africa, particularly in South Africa. He accused the South African authorities of continuing to implement apartheid policies in South Africa.

Qian agreed with Nzo's analysis of the situation in South Africa and expressed China's continued support for the South African people's just struggle against apartheid.

### 卡翁达总统设宴欢迎钱外长  中赞外长希望和平解决南部非洲问题  钱外长在卢萨卡会见南非国大总书记
郝章印  姜显明
1989-08-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    卡翁达总统设宴欢迎钱外长
    中赞外长希望和平解决南部非洲问题
    钱外长在卢萨卡会见南非国大总书记
    据新华社卢萨卡8月8日电  （记者郝章印、姜显明）赞比亚总统卡翁达今天举行工作晚餐，欢迎中国外交部长钱其琛和由他率领的中国代表团。
    卡翁达在欢迎词中指出，中国在国际事务中发挥了非常重要的作用。
    赞中双边关系非常密切，他感谢中国政府在赞比亚争取独立和发展国民经济中所给予的巨大援助。
    钱外长赞扬了赞比亚在支持这一地区独立解放斗争中的重要作用，并表示中国政府将继续作出努力，加强中赞两国广泛的双边合作。
    钱外长在谈到中国的国内形势时指出，西方国家有人认为中国政府镇压反革命暴乱的行动将削弱中国在国际事务中的作用，而事实上，这种预测是完全错误的。
    他说，中国政府将坚持独立自主的和平外交政策，加强同包括非洲在内的第三世界国家人民的团结。
    据新华社卢萨卡8月8日电　正在赞比亚访问的中国外长钱其琛和赞比亚国防部长兼代理外长弗·哈蓬达今天在这里一致表示，希望南部非洲问题通过对话和协商途径和平解决。晚上，哈蓬达为钱外长一行举行了欢迎宴会。
    新华社卢萨卡8月8日电  正在这里访问的中国外交部长钱其琛今天下午和南非非洲人国民大会总书记艾尔弗雷德·恩佐举行了会晤。
    恩佐向钱外长介绍了南部非洲、尤其是南非的政治形势的最新发展。他指责南非当局继续在南非实行种族隔离政策。
    钱外长同意恩佐对南非局势的分析，并表示中国继续支持南非人民反对种族隔离的正义斗争。
