Title: Chengdu Takes Measures to Advance Housing Reform
Author: Lǐ Nánlǐng Chéng Jiàn
Date: 1989-08-23
Edition: 2

Chengdu is preparing to steadily advance housing system reform. The next steps in the work will be carried out on the basis of improving existing policies, following these methods: Continuing the rent reform of "raising rent and issuing vouchers," adhering to the principle of "reforming one when one is mature," and approving the rent reform plans of units whose conditions are mature; continuing to sell public housing to employees, implementing a combination of renting and selling, and preferential sales of housing.

### 成都采取措施推进住房改革
李南岭  成建
1989-08-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    成都采取措施推进住房改革
    新华社成都8月23日电　（记者李南岭、成建）成都市准备继续稳步推进住房制度改革。下一步工作将在完善现有政策的基础上，按照以下几种办法进行：继续实行提“租发券”的租金改革，坚持“成熟一个，改革一个”的原则，审批条件成熟单位的租金改革方案；继续向职工出售公房，实行租卖结合，优惠售房等。
