Title: Support Rate for Japan's New Cabinet at 46.5%
Author:
Date: 1989-08-17
Edition: 3

According to a recent nationwide public opinion poll conducted by Kyodo News, the approval rating of Japan's newly formed Miyazawa Cabinet has surpassed that of the previous cabinet, reaching 46.5%. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 日新内阁支持率46.5％

1989-08-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日新内阁支持率46.5％
    据共同社最近发表的一项全国舆论调查，日本新成立的海部内阁国民支持率超过上届内阁，达46.5％。（据新华社）
