Title: Japanese Youth Friendship Camping Team Arrives in Xinjiang
Author: ZHU Dongju
Date: 1989-08-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, July 31st. (Reporter: Zhu Dongju) A delegation of 60 Japanese youth from the Fukuoka Youth Friendship Exchange Camping Team arrived here today and were warmly welcomed by Tiemu'er Da'wamaiti, Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and Yusufo·Aisha, Mayor of Urumqi City. Over the next eight days, the camping team will venture into the Tian Shan Mountains, visit the scenic high-altitude lake Tianchi, climb Bogeda Peak, and engage in cultural exchanges with the Kazakh herders and children of the Tian Shan pastures. They will also hold festivities to deepen understanding and foster friendship.

### 日青少年友好野营队抵新疆
朱冬菊
1989-08-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    日青少年友好野营队抵新疆
    新华社乌鲁木齐七月三十一日电　（记者朱冬菊）日本福冈青少年友好交流野营队一行六十人，今天在这里受到新疆维吾尔自治区主席铁木尔·达瓦买提和乌鲁木齐市市长玉素甫·艾沙的欢迎。在以后的八天里，野营队将深入天山，观看游览风光秀丽的高山湖泊天池，攀登博格达峰、跟天山牧场的哈萨克牧民和儿童联欢，进行文化交流，增进了解和友谊。
