Title: Qian Xuesen on Winning the Award: All Achievements Belong to the Party and the Collective
Author:
Date: 1989-08-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 7, 1989

Qian Xuesen expressed his thoughts on winning the award at a ceremony held by the Commission of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defense and the Chinese Association for Science and Technology on August 3 to congratulate him on receiving the "Rockefeller Award." He said, "All achievements belong to the Party and the collective" (the full text is on the fourth page of this newspaper).
