Title: Vice Premier Ji Pengfei Meets with Macau Business Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-08-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 23rd.

Vice Premier Ji Pengfei today met and hosted a banquet for a delegation from Macau's business community led by Vice Chairman and General Manager of the Macau Chinese General Chamber of Commerce and Macau Tai Fung Bank, Mr. Ho Tuck On, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse this afternoon.

Ji Pengfei reiterated during the meeting that the Chinese government adheres to the "one country, two systems" policy and will faithfully implement the Sino-Portuguese Joint Declaration, making unremitting efforts for the smooth transition and stable development of Macau.

Vice Directors Li Hou and Lu Ping of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macau Affairs Office attended the meeting and the banquet. The Macau business delegation arrived in Beijing on August 21st for a visit. During their stay in Beijing, the delegation held a symposium with the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, visited Sino-foreign joint ventures, and inspected the facilities and venues under construction for the Asian Games.

### 姬鹏飞会见澳门工商界代表团

1989-08-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    姬鹏飞会见澳门工商界代表团
    新华社北京8月23日电　国务院港澳办公室主任姬鹏飞今天下午在钓鱼台宾馆会见并宴请了以澳门中华总商会副会长、澳门大丰银行总经理何厚铧为团长的澳门工商界代表团。
    姬鹏飞在会见时重申，中国政府坚持“一国两制”方针，认真执行中葡联合声明，为澳门的平稳过渡和稳定发展作不懈努力。
    国务院港澳办公室副主任李后、鲁平参加了会见并出席了宴会。澳门工商界代表团是21日到北京参观访问的。在京期间，代表团同全国工商联举行了座谈，参观了中外合资企业和正在兴建的亚运会工程、设施。
