Title: Ethiopia Searches for Missing Plane
Author:
Date: 1989-08-10
Edition: 3

An Ethiopian plane went missing on August 7th. After two days of search efforts, no trace of the plane has been found. Among the passengers on the plane were 16 Americans, including one member of Congress. They were en route to a refugee camp housing approximately 60,000 Sudanese refugees when they disappeared.

### 埃塞俄比亚搜寻失踪飞机

1989-08-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    埃塞俄比亚搜寻失踪飞机
    埃塞俄比亚一架飞机7日失踪，搜寻工作进行两天仍未发现这架飞机的踪迹。乘坐这架飞机的有包括1名国会议员在内的16名美国人。他们是去一个安置大约6万名苏丹难民营途中失踪的。
            （据新华社）
