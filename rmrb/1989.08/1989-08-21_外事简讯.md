Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Page: 2

    Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office, Chi Pengfei, hosted a reception and dinner for the Hong Kong Youth Industrial and Commercial Entrepreneurs' Visit to Beijing delegation on the evening of August 21 at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

    Chi Pengfei stated that China's policy of "one country, two systems" in Hong Kong remains unchanged, and the interests of the mainland and Hong Kong are aligned.

    On the afternoon of August 21, Li Guixian, State Councilor and Governor of the People's Bank of China, met with a delegation led by the Chairman of the Board of Directors of the First City Bank of Texas, USA, and the Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Sino-American Joint Venture ABC International Limited, Abbott, at the Great Hall of the People. Li Guixian briefed the guests on the situation of China's financial system reforms and industrial and agricultural construction.

### 外事简讯

1989-08-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务院港澳办公室主任姬鹏飞8月21日晚在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请香港青年工商企业家访京团。
    姬鹏飞说，中国在香港实行“一国两制”的政策不变，大陆和香港的利益是一致的。
    ▲国务委员兼中国人民银行行长李贵鲜8月21日下午在人民大会堂会见了美国德克萨斯第一城市银行公司董事长、中美合营ABC国际有限公司董事长阿博特一行。李贵鲜向客人们介绍了中国金融体制改革的情况和工农业建设情况。
