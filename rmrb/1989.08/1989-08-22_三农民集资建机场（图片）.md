Title: Three Peasants Pool Resources to Build an Airport
Author: Zhang Hai Long
Date: 1989-08-22
Edition: 2

An airport for general aviation, funded by three peasants—Zhang Guanglu, Song Chunxiang, and Tang Qunxi—with an investment of 1.5 million yuan, has been completed in Lingbao County, Henan Province. The inaugural trial flight ceremony was held on August 11.
