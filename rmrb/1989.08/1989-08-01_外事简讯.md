Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-08-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

	State Councilor Li Tieying met with a delegation of Indian journalists led by senior editor Kewal Varma of the "Sunday Weekly" in the afternoon of August 1st at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse. He also answered questions raised by the Indian guests. Song Muwen, Director of the State Press and Publication Administration, was present at the meeting.

	Nuo Zhifu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, met with a delegation from the Senegalese National Union of Workers led by Umar Sane, the first deputy secretary-general, in Beijing on the evening of August 1st.

	The delegation from the Senegalese National Union of Workers arrived in Beijing on July 31st at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for a friendly visit to China.

	Zhu Liang, Minister of the International Liaison Department, met with and hosted a dinner for a delegation of the Workers' Party of Korea led by Kim Jin-yul, Deputy Director of the Heavy Industry Department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, in Beijing on the evening of August 1st.

	The Thai Social Welfare Delegation concluded their visit to China on August 1st and departed from Guangzhou for their home country. The Thai delegation visited Beijing, Taiyuan, Nanjing, and Guangzhou.

### 外事简讯

1989-08-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员李铁映1日下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见由《星期日周刊》高级编辑凯瓦尔·瓦尔马率领的印度新闻代表团，并回答了印度客人们提出的问题。国家新闻出版署署长宋木文会见时在座。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福1日晚在北京会见了以塞内加尔全国工人联合会第一副总书记乌玛尔·萨内为首的塞工联代表团。
    塞工联代表团是应全总邀请于7月31日到达北京，对中国进行友好访问的。
    ▲中联部部长朱良1日晚在北京会见并宴请了以朝鲜劳动党中央重工业部副部长金镇玉为团长的朝鲜劳动党党的工作者代表团。
    ▲泰国社会福利代表团于1日结束访华，离开广州回国。泰国代表团访问了北京、太原、南京和广州。
            （新华社）
