Title: Peking University Confers Honorary Doctorate in Law on Tsutomu Hato
Author: Yan Dong
Date: 1989-08-17
Page: 2

People's Daily, Beijing, August 17th

Today in the afternoon, Peking University President Ding Shisun conferred the "Honorary Doctorate in Law" from Peking University to the renowned Japanese politician and friend of the Chinese people, Tsutomu Hato.

Mr. Tsutomu Hato not only cares deeply about the future and destiny of Japan and the cause of Sino-Japanese friendship but also about the future and destiny of all humanity. He has dedicated his life to the struggle against nuclear war and military expansion. His book "World Peace and Disarmament" has been officially published in Chinese by Peking University Press.

### 北大授予宇都宫德马“名誉法学博士”证书
雁冬
1989-08-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    北大授予宇都宫德马“名誉法学博士”证书
    本报北京8月17日讯　记者雁冬报道：今天下午，北京大学丁石孙校长授予日本著名政治家、中国人民的好朋友宇都宫德马“北京大学名誉法学博士”证书。
    宇都宫德马先生不仅关心日本的前途和命运，关心日中友好事业，而且关心全人类的前途和命运，把反对核战争、反对扩军作为毕生为之奋斗的事业。他的《世界和平与裁军》中文版已由北京大学出版社正式出版。
