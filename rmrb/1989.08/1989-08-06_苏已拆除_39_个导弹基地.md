Title: Soviet Union Has Demolished 39 Missile Bases
Author:
Date: 1989-08-06
Edition: 3

The Soviet Union has demolished 39 missile bases, according to a report in a Moscow newspaper on August 5. Another missile base will be demolished in September this year. The demolition of missile bases by the Soviet Union is in accordance with the Intermediate-Range Nuclear Forces (INF) Treaty approved by the Soviet Union and the United States in June of last year.

### 苏已拆除39个导弹基地

1989-08-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏已拆除39个导弹基地
    据莫斯科报纸5日报道，苏联已拆除导弹基地39个。今年9月将拆除另一个导弹基地。苏联拆除导弹基地是根据苏美去年6月批准的中导条约进行的。
