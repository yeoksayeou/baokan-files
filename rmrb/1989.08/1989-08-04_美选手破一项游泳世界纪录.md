Title: American Swimmer Breaks a Swimming World Record

Author:

Date: 1989-08-04

Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 4th.

American swimmer Mike Barroman set a new world record in the men's 200-meter breaststroke on August 3rd during the preliminary rounds of the U.S. National Swimming Championships held in Los Angeles. He achieved a time of 2 minutes and 12.90 seconds.

The previous world record for this event was 2 minutes and 13.34 seconds, set by Canadian athlete Victor Davis on August 2, 1984, at the Los Angeles Olympics. This is the first swimming world record to be broken since the 1988 Seoul Olympics.

### 美选手破一项游泳世界纪录

1989-08-04
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    美选手破一项游泳世界纪录
    新华社北京8月4日电  据外电报道，美国游泳运动员迈克·巴罗曼3日在洛杉矶举行的美国全国游泳锦标赛的预赛中，以2分12秒90的成绩创造了男子200米蛙泳世界纪录。
    该项目原世界纪录为2分13秒34，是加拿大选手维克托·戴维斯1984年8月2日在洛杉矶奥运会上创造的。这是自去年9月汉城奥运会以来刷新的第一个游泳世界纪录。
