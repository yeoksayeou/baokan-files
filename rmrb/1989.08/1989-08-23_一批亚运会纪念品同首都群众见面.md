Title: A Batch of Asian Games Souvenirs Meet with the People of the Capital
Author: Xiaogao
Date: 1989-08-23
Edition: 3

A batch of souvenirs from the 11th Asian Games met with the people of the capital today. These exquisite products from all over the country reflect the workers' concern for the Asian Games and showcase the wisdom of the nation, leaving people amazed. The Executive Vice Chairman of the Asian Games Organizing Committee, Zhang Baifu, and many other senior comrades visited enthusiastically to view the exhibits.

### 一批亚运会纪念品同首都群众见面
晓高
1989-08-23
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    一批亚运会纪念品同首都群众见面
    本报北京8月23日讯　今天，第十一届亚运会标志纪念品，在龙乡饭店第一次同首都群众见面。这些来自全国各地的精美制品，表现了工人们对亚运会的关心，展示出民族的智慧，使人们大开眼界。亚运会组委会常务副主席张百发和许多老同志兴致勃勃地前来观看。　（晓高）
