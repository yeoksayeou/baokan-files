Title: Lebanese Factions Disagree on French Military Intervention
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, August 20th.

French aircraft carrier "Foch" heads towards Lebanese waters, Lebanese factions hold different views on French military intervention. Lebanese Christian faction Prime Minister General Michel Aoun expressed hope for French military intervention in Lebanon on the 20th.

Lebanese pro-Syrian newspapers accused France of "using humanitarian aid as a cover for military adventurism" on the 19th.

Additionally, the pro-Iranian Hezbollah warned France not to embark on a new "Crusade," stating that such military action "will definitely fail."

Waleed Junblatt, leader of the Lebanese Progressive Socialist Party, considered Syria's most reliable ally, said on the 19th that if France intervenes in Lebanon, it will bear the responsibility for dividing Lebanon.

Xinhua News Agency, Madrid, August 20th.

French President Mitterrand stated at a press conference in Madrid on the 20th that France will not intervene militarily in Lebanon and will continue diplomatic efforts to ensure that the ceasefire in that country is respected.

Mitterrand made these remarks after an informal meeting with Spanish Prime Minister Gonzalez. Mitterrand's words ruled out the possibility of French military intervention in Lebanon.

Xinhua News Agency, Kuwait, August 20th.

Kuwait's most influential newspaper, "Al-Anbaa," today called on the French government not to interfere in Lebanese affairs and urged European countries to work together to resolve the Lebanese crisis.

### 黎各派对法军事干预有分歧  密特朗说法不对黎巴嫩进行军事干涉  科《舆论报》要求法国不干涉黎事务

1989-08-21
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    黎各派对法军事干预有分歧
    密特朗说法不对黎巴嫩进行军事干涉
    科《舆论报》要求法国不干涉黎事务
    新华社巴黎8月20日电  法国航空母舰“福煦”号驶向黎巴嫩海域后，黎各派对法的军事干预持不同立场。黎巴嫩基督教派政府总理米歇尔·奥恩将军20日表示希望法国军事干预黎巴嫩。
    黎巴嫩亲叙利亚派报纸19日指责法国“以人道主义援助为名，为军事冒险开辟道路”。
    另外，亲伊朗的真主党警告法国不得进行一次新的“十字军东征”，如果进行这样的军事行动，它“肯定会失败”。
    被认为是叙利亚最可靠的同盟者的黎巴嫩社会进步党领袖瓦利德·琼布拉特19日说，如果法国干涉黎巴嫩，它将承担分裂黎巴嫩的责任。
    新华社马德里8月20日电  法国总统密特朗20日在马德里举行的一次记者招待会上说，法国不对黎巴嫩进行军事干涉，继续外交行动，以使在那个国家的停火得到尊重。
    密特朗是在同西班牙首相冈萨雷斯举行一次非正式会议之后发表这番谈话的。密特朗的话排除了法国对黎巴嫩进行军事干涉的可能性。
    新华社科威特8月20日电  科威特最有影响的《舆论报》今天要求法国政府不要干涉黎巴嫩事务并且呼吁欧洲国家协同努力解决黎巴嫩危机。
