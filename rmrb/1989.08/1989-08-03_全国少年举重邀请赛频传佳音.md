Title: National Youth Weightlifting Invitational Tournament Frequently Reports Good News
Author: Xiao Ciyi
Date: 1989-08-03
Edition: 4

The "Jingzhou Cup" National Youth Women's Weightlifting Invitational Tournament in the 48kg category saw frequent outstanding performances, with 11 athletes surpassing the national best record.

In the snatch event, Hebei athlete Qu Xiaodong took the lead with a 63kg lift, surpassing the national best record of 62.5kg. Subsequently, Hunan athletes Zhang Yonghong and He Lifeng, along with Guangxi athlete Qin Meirong, each lifted 65kg, surpassing Qu Xiaodong's new record. Finally, Qu Xiaodong sealed the victory with a 67.5kg lift, setting a new national best record and securing first place.

The clean and jerk event was even more intense. After Hebei athlete Qu Xiaodong, Hunan athlete Shi Minghua, Guangxi athlete Qin Meirong, Hubei athlete Wang Yulan, Hunan athlete He Lifeng, and Shenyang athlete Wang Bingbing each lifted 80kg to tie the best record for this event, Hunan athletes Shi Minghua and Zhang Yonghong both lifted 85kg, breaking the original national best record. Shortly after, Shi Minghua made a great effort to lift 87.5kg, setting a new best record.

Hunan athlete Zhang Yonghong achieved a total of 150kg, surpassing the national best record of 142.5kg, and won first place; Guangxi athlete Qin Meirong and Hunan athlete He Lifeng achieved totals of 145kg and 140kg respectively, securing second and third places.

### 全国少年举重邀请赛频传佳音
肖赐轶
1989-08-03
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    全国少年举重邀请赛频传佳音
    新华社武汉8月2日电  （通讯员肖赐轶）“荆州杯”全国少年女子举重邀请赛48公斤级比赛佳绩频传，有11人次超过全国最好成绩。
    河北选手屈晓东在抓举比赛中先声夺人，开始就以63公斤的成绩，超过62.5公斤的全国最好成绩。接着，湖南选手张永红、何利文和广西选手覃美荣先后举起65公斤，超过屈晓东的最新成绩。最后，屈晓东一举定音，以67.5公斤的成绩写下了新的全国最好成绩，荣获第一名。
    挺举比赛更为激烈。继河北选手屈晓东、湖南选手石明华、广西选手覃美荣、湖北选手王玉兰、湖南选手何利文、沈阳选手王兵兵先后以80公斤的成绩平这个项目的最好成绩之后，湖南选手石明华、张永红又均以85公斤将原全国最好成绩改写。不多时，石明华又奋力举起87.5公斤，写下一项最好新成绩。
    湖南选手张永红以150公斤的总成绩超过142.5公斤的全国最好成绩，获得第一名；广西选手覃美荣、湖南选手何利文分别以145公斤和140公斤的总成绩，获得第二和第三名。
