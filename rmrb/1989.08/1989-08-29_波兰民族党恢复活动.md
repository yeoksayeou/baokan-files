Title: Polish National Party Resumes Activities
Author:
Date: 1989-08-29
Edition: 3

According to a report by the Polish News Agency on the 27th, nationalists from all over Poland gathered in Warsaw on the 26th. They decided to resume the public activities of the Polish National Party and elected a provisional executive committee and a party chairman. The attendees also decided to hold a national party congress next year to formulate the party's platform and constitution. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 波兰民族党恢复活动

1989-08-29
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    波兰民族党恢复活动
    据波通社27日报道，波兰全国“民族主义运动人士”26日在华沙聚会，决定恢复波兰民族党的公开活动，并选出了该党的临时总理事会和党的主席。与会者还决定明年召开民族党代表大会，以制订该党的党纲和党章。（据新华社）
