Title: The Prince and Princess of Wales Will Visit Hong Kong as Scheduled
Author:
Date: 1989-08-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, 25th August.

A British royal spokesman announced today that Prince Charles and Princess Diana will visit Hong Kong as scheduled in November.

This will be the first visit to Hong Kong by Prince Charles and Diana. They will also visit Indonesia.

The royal spokesman said the visit is scheduled from November 3rd to 10th.

### 英王子王妃将如期访香港

1989-08-26
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    英王子王妃将如期访香港
    新华社伦敦8月25日电　英国皇家发言人今天宣布，查尔斯王子和王妃黛安娜11月份将如期访问香港。
    这是查尔斯王子和黛安娜首次访问香港。他们同时还将访问印度尼西亚。
    皇家发言人说，访问定于11月3日至10日。
