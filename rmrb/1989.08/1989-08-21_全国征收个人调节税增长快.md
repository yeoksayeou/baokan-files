Title: Nationwide Collection of Personal Adjustment Taxes Sees Rapid Growth
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Edition: 1

In the first seven months of this year, tax authorities at all levels across the country have made significant progress in actively promoting the trial implementation of personal taxable income declaration, strengthening the control of tax sources, plugging loopholes, and seriously investigating and dealing with tax evasion cases. By the end of July, the cumulative amount of personal income adjustment tax collected nationwide reached 23.6 million yuan, which is 324.9% of the same period last year.

### 全国征收个人调节税增长快

1989-08-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    全国征收个人调节税增长快
    今年前7个月，全国各级税务部门在积极开展个人应税收入申报试点工作，加强税源的源泉控制，堵塞漏洞，并严肃查处偷漏税案件方面取得了显著成效。截至7月底，全国累计征收个人收入调节税2.36亿元，为去年同期的324.9％。
                                        （据新华社）
