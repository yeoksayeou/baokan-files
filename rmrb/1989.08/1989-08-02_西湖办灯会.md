Title: West Lake Lantern Festival
Author: Cao Yinkang
Date: 1989-08-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, August 2nd

The "Mid-Summer Dream Garden" lantern festival at the West Lake Forest Resort has hung 12 sets of large-scale craft lanterns and nearly 100 flower lanterns, attracting nearly 10,000 visitors to admire them.

The West Lake Forest Resort is a scenic spot on the northern shore of West Lake. The area is lush with trees, cool and pleasant, and features novel and interesting recreational activities such as tents, hammocks, and barbecues. The nighttime lantern festival that began last month has made the resort vibrant and colorful.

### 西湖办灯会
曹银康
1989-08-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    西湖办灯会
    新华社杭州8月2日电  （记者曹银康）杭州西湖密林度假村的“仲夏梦园”灯会，挂出大型工艺彩灯12组，花灯近百只，吸引近万人观赏。
    西湖密林度假村是西湖北线的一个游览点，那里林木幽深、凉爽宜人，辟有新颖、多趣的帐篷、吊床、野炊等游乐项目。上月末开始的这个夜间灯会把度假村打扮得色彩斑斓。
