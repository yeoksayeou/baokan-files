Title: Zhang Xian and Tibetan Women Together
Author: Wáng Zhèn Xué
Date: 1989-08-09
Page: 6

In May 1956, Zhang Xian, the wife of Marshal Chen Yi, posed for a group photo with Tibetan women in the Shigatse region of the Tibet Autonomous Region. The photo was taken by Wang Zhenxue.
