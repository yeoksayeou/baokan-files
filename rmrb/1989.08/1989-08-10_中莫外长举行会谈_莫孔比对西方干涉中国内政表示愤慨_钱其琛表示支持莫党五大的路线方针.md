Title: Chinese and Mozambican Foreign Ministers Hold Talks
Author: Yang Zhi Gang
Date: 1989-08-10
Edition: 3

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen held talks with Mozambican Foreign Minister Joaquim Chissano in Maputo today. The two sides exchanged views on the international situation, particularly the situation in Southern Africa, and briefed each other on their domestic situations.

After listening to Qian's account of the domestic situation in China, especially the suppression of the counter-revolutionary rebellion, Chissano expressed understanding of China's actions and indignation at Western countries' interference in China's internal affairs.

Qian pointed out that over the past year, as the international situation gradually eased, the people of Southern Africa, through active struggle and efforts, have made the situation in Southern Africa show a positive trend. With the efforts and support of all parties, Mozambique has begun its peace process, although it will encounter some difficulties and even setbacks, the overall trend of peace in Southern Africa is irreversible.

Qian believed that under this situation, the issue of South Africa should be put on the agenda. He stated that the Chinese government firmly supports the South African people's just struggle against racism and the apartheid system.

Chissano agreed with Qian's analysis of the situation in Southern Africa. He pointed out that although there are still some difficulties in solving various problems in Southern Africa, the overall trend is developing towards peace.

Chissano briefed Qian on the two major tasks set by the Fifth Congress of the Mozambique Liberation Front (FRELIMO) to achieve domestic peace and economic development.

Qian expressed that the Chinese party and government support the line and policies set by the Fifth Congress of FRELIMO. He firmly believes that under the guidance of the line of the Fifth Congress of FRELIMO, the Mozambican people, united as one, will be able to overcome the difficulties on the road ahead and achieve new successes in the process of realizing domestic peace and implementing the economic recovery plan.

Qian arrived in Maputo this morning after visiting Zambia.

### 中莫外长举行会谈  莫孔比对西方干涉中国内政表示愤慨  钱其琛表示支持莫党五大的路线方针
杨志刚
1989-08-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中莫外长举行会谈
    莫孔比对西方干涉中国内政表示愤慨
    钱其琛表示支持莫党五大的路线方针
    新华社马普托8月9日电　（记者杨志刚）正在这里访问的中国外长钱其琛同莫桑比克外长莫孔比今天在这里举行会谈，双方相互通报了各自国内的情况，并就国际形势特别是南部非洲形势交换了意见。
    在听取钱外长关于中国国内形势、特别是平息反革命暴乱的情况介绍以后，莫外长对中国国内平息反革命暴乱表示理解，对西方国家干涉中国内政表示愤慨。
    钱外长指出，一年多来，在国际形势逐步转向缓和的背景下，南部非洲地区人民经过积极的斗争和努力，使南部非洲局势呈现出积极发展的趋势。莫桑比克在各方努力和支持下已经开始和平进程，虽然会遇到一些困难，甚至出现一些曲折，但南部非洲地区走向和平的总趋势不可逆转。
    钱外长认为，在这种形势下，南非问题的解决应当提到日程上来。他表示，中国政府坚决支持南非人民反对种族主义、种族隔离制度的正义斗争。
    莫孔比外长对钱外长关于南部非洲形势的分析表示赞同。他指出，尽管南部非洲地区各个方面问题的解决尚有一些困难，但总的趋势是朝着和平方向发展的。
    莫孔比外长着重向钱外长通报了莫桑比克解放阵线党“五大”制订的实现国内和平和发展经济的两大任务。
    钱外长表示，中国党和政府支持莫解放阵线党“五大”制订的路线方针，坚信莫人民在莫党“五大”路线的指引下，团结一致，同心同德，定能克服前进道路上的困难，在实现国内和平和落实经济恢复计划的进程中不断取得新的成就。
    钱其琛外长是在访问赞比亚后于9日上午抵达马普托的。
