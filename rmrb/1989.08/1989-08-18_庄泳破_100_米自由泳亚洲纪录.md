Title: Zhang Yong Breaks 100m Freestyle Asian Record
Author: Jiang Zhanguo
Date: 1989-08-18
Edition: 4

Chinese athlete Zhang Yong today broke the Asian record for the women's 100-meter freestyle during the preliminary heats of the third Pan Pacific Swimming Championships held here. She achieved a time of 55.38 seconds, surpassing her own previous record of 55.47 seconds.

Zhang Yong's new record also broke the championship record of 55.86 seconds set at the Pan Pacific Swimming Championships.

### 庄泳破100米自由泳亚洲纪录
姜占国
1989-08-18
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    庄泳破100米自由泳亚洲纪录
    新华社东京8月18日电（记者姜占国）中国运动员庄泳今天在这里举行的第三届泛太平洋游泳锦标赛女子100米自由泳预赛中，以55秒38的成绩打破了由她自己保持的55秒47的亚洲纪录。
    庄泳创造的这个成绩还打破了泛太平洋游泳锦标赛55秒86的大会纪录。
