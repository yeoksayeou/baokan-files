Title: Train Collision in Portugal
Author:
Date: 1989-08-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency,

Two trains collided in the central Portuguese city of Fatima on the 17th, injuring more than 20 people and destroying the locomotive.

### 葡萄牙发生火车相撞事故

1989-08-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    葡萄牙发生火车相撞事故
    葡萄牙中部城市法蒂玛17日发生两列火车相撞事故，20多人受伤，火车头报废。
