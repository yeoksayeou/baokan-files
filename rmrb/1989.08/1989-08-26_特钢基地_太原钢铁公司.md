Title: Special Steel Base – Taiyuan Iron and Steel Company
Author:
Date: 1989-08-26
Page: 4

Enterprise Stars

    What was once an unassuming small steel mill has now transformed into a massive steel company, becoming the largest special steel production base in China.

    In 1950, Taiyuan Iron and Steel Company (Taiyuan Steel) first conducted trials for silicon steel smelting, a process that was uncharted territory in China at the time. In September 1952, it successfully produced China's first batch of stainless steel. In 1954, it successfully manufactured China's first hot-rolled silicon steel sheet. Today, Taiyuan Steel produces 16 million tons of steel annually, offering 474 steel grades and over 5,300 steel product varieties. Among these, the production of stainless steel leads the nation and has entered the ranks of the world's top 20 steel producers with an annual output of 10 million tons.

### 特钢基地——太原钢铁公司

1989-08-26
第4版(国庆专版)
专栏：企业明星

    特钢基地——太原钢铁公司
    原来一座很不起眼的小钢铁厂，如今已变成规模宏大的钢铁公司，变成我国最大的特殊钢生产基地。
    1950年，太钢首先进行当时在我国还是空白的硅钢冶炼试验，1952年9月，成功地试炼出我国第一炉不锈钢。1954年，成功地试制出我国第一张热轧矽钢片。如今，太钢年产钢160万吨，有474个钢号、5300多个钢材品种，其中，不锈钢产量居全国之首，并跨入世界20家10万吨不锈钢生产厂家行列。
