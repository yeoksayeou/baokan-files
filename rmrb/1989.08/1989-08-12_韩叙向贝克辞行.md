Title: Han Xu Bids Farewell to Baker
Author:
Date: 1989-08-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, August 11th.

Outgoing Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the United States, Han Xu, met with U.S. Secretary of State James Baker today to bid him farewell. During the meeting, both sides expressed their desire to maintain and develop Sino-American relations.

### 韩叙向贝克辞行

1989-08-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    韩叙向贝克辞行
    本报华盛顿8月11日电　即将离任的中华人民共和国驻美国大使韩叙今天拜会美国国务卿贝克，向他辞行。在会见中，双方都表示了维护和发展中美关系的愿望。
