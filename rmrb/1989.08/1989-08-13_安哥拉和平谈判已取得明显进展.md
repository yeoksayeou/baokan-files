Title: Significant Progress Made in Angola Peace Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-08-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kinshasa, August 11th.

Significant progress has been made in the third round of talks between the Angolan government and the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola (UNITA), which are aimed at achieving a peace plan for Angola. This was announced in a news release issued today after four days of negotiations here, mediated by Zairean President Mobutu Sese Seko.

The news release stated that the two sides discussed, in a calm atmosphere, the practical measures proposed by Zaire for the implementation of the ceasefire agreement.

### 安哥拉和平谈判已取得明显进展

1989-08-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    安哥拉和平谈判已取得明显进展
    新华社金沙萨8月11日电  在这里举行的安哥拉政府同反政府的争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟（安盟），就实现安国内和平计划的第3轮会谈取得了“明显的进展”。
    这是在扎伊尔总统蒙博托的斡旋下，安双方经过4天的谈判后，今天在这里发表的一项新闻公报中宣布的。
    公报说，双方在“平静的气氛中”，就扎伊尔提出的关于执行停火协议实际措施的建议进行了讨论。
