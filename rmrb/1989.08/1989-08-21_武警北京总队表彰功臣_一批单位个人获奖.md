Title: Beijing Garrison of the People's Armed Police Honors Heroes
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Page: 2

This morning, the Beijing Garrison of the People's Armed Police held a grand ceremony to honor the heroes and models who fought in the struggle to stop the turmoil, suppress the counter-revolutionary riot, and defend the capital.

Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee Li Ximing, State Councilor and Mayor of Beijing Chen Xitong, as well as leaders from the Beijing Military Region, the General Headquarters of the People's Armed Police, the Beijing Garrison Command, and the Ministry of Public Security attended the meeting. They presented medals and certificates to representatives of over 40 heroic units and more than 700 heroic officers and soldiers.

During this turmoil and counter-revolutionary riot, the Beijing Garrison of the People's Armed Police stood firm, held high their banner, fought side by side with the martial law troops, and engaged in a life-and-death struggle with the counter-revolutionary rioters, defending the socialist republic with their blood and lives. The Beijing Garrison also successfully completed the mission of protecting the party and state leadership organs, broadcasting and television, telegraph communications, railways and airports, and diplomatic missions in the capital.

### 武警北京总队表彰功臣  一批单位个人获奖
周德伟　翟永太
1989-08-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    武警北京总队表彰功臣
    一批单位个人获奖
    本报北京8月21日讯　今天上午，中国人民武装警察部队北京总队隆重表彰在制止动乱、平息反革命暴乱、保卫首都斗争中的功臣模范。
    中共中央政治局委员、北京市委书记李锡铭，国务委员、北京市市长陈希同，以及北京军区、武警总部、北京卫戍区等党政军及公安部负责同志出席大会，并向40多个功臣单位和700多名功臣官兵的代表颁发了奖章和奖状。
    武警北京总队在这次动乱和反革命暴乱中立场坚定，旗帜鲜明，与戒严部队并肩战斗，和反革命暴徒展开殊死搏斗，用鲜血和生命捍卫了社会主义共和国。北京总队还圆满完成了保卫党和国家首脑机关、广播电视、电报通信、铁路机场、驻华使馆等首都重要部门的任务。
                  （周德伟　翟永太）
