Title: Yen Falls Further Against the Dollar
Author:
Date: 1989-08-20
Edition: 3

On the 18th, in the Tokyo foreign exchange market, the yen exchange rate dropped to 143.58 yen to the dollar at one point.

This was the lowest rate for the yen since July 3rd.

Regarding the future trend of the yen, the Tokyo financial sector believes that the yen's price will continue to fall.

However, due to the possibility of further reductions in U.S. bank interest rates, the yen's decline will not be too significant.

### 日元对美元汇价再跌

1989-08-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    日元对美元汇价再跌
    在18日的东京外汇市场上，日元汇率一度跌至1美元兑换143.58日元。
    这是7月3日以来日元的最低行情。
    关于日元今后的行情，东京金融界的看法是，日元价格将继续下跌。
    但是由于美国银行利息可能还会降低，因此日元下跌的幅度不会太大。
