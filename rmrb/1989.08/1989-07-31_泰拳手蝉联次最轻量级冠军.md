Title: Thai Boxer Defends Featherweight Title
Author:
Date: 1989-07-31
Edition: 4

Thai boxer Khao Sae successfully defended his World Boxing Association featherweight title today in Bangkok by fending off a strong challenge from Colombian contender Alberto Castaneda.

This was Khao Sae's 11th successful title defense.

### 泰拳手蝉联次最轻量级冠军

1989-07-31
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    泰拳手蝉联次最轻量级冠军
    新华社曼谷七月二十九日电　泰国拳手克奥赛今天在这里击退了哥伦比亚选手阿尔贝托·卡斯塔德强有力的挑战，蝉联世界拳击协会次最轻量级冠军。
    这是克奥赛第十一次卫冕成功。
