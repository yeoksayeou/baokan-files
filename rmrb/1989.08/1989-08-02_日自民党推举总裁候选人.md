Title: LDP Selects New Presidential Candidate
Author:
Date: 1989-08-02
Page: 3

    The Liberal Democratic Party (LDP) of Japan has nominated Kaifu Toshiki as the candidate for the new party president to succeed Uno. This is the first candidate nominated since Uno announced his resignation.

    The 58-year-old Kaifu Toshiki has previously served as the Vice-Chief Cabinet Secretary and Minister of Education, and belongs to the Kobun faction within the LDP.

    (Special Wire)

### 日自民党推举总裁候选人

1989-08-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日自民党推举总裁候选人
    海部俊树2日被自民党内河本派推举出来，作为宇野后任的自民党新总裁候选人。这是从宇野表示辞职后推举出的第一个候选人。
    58岁的海部俊树曾担任过内阁官房副长官和文相，在自民党内属河本派。
            （本报专电）
