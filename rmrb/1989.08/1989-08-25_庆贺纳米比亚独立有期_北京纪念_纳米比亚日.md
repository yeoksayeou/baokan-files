Title: Celebrating the Imminent Independence of Namibia
Author:
Date: 1989-08-25
Edition: 2

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Africa Friendship Association held a reception at the Great Hall of the People this evening to commemorate "Namibia Day." Vice Premier Wu Xueqian attended the reception.

Liu Gengyin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, said in his speech: "This year is a year of historic turning points in Southern Africa. Today, with joyful hearts, we welcome the beginning of the much-anticipated process of Namibia's independence, celebrate the end of Namibia's long-suffering under South Africa's occupation, and continue to support and champion the just struggle of the resolute Namibian people for national independence, so that Namibia can achieve independence as scheduled."

He said, "We sincerely hope that all relevant parties, especially the South African authorities, will keep their promises and faithfully implement all relevant resolutions and agreements. We call on the international community to remain vigilant, continue to pressure the South African authorities, and continue to support the struggle of the Namibian people."

Liberian Ambassador and Acting Chairman of the African Diplomatic Corps John Ricks said in his speech that Namibia is the last region on the African continent still under colonial rule, which has special significance for the African continent. The President of the China-Africa Friendship Association, Gong Dafen, and diplomatic envoys from African countries to China attended the reception.

### 庆贺纳米比亚独立有期  北京纪念“纳米比亚日”

1989-08-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    庆贺纳米比亚独立有期
    北京纪念“纳米比亚日”
    据新华社北京8月25日电  中国人民对外友好协会和中国非洲人民友好协会今晚在人民大会堂举行招待会，纪念“纳米比亚日”。国务院副总理吴学谦出席了招待会。
    对外友协副会长刘庚寅在讲话中说：“今年是南部非洲发生历史性转折的一年。今天，我们怀着欣喜的心情，欢迎举世关注的纳米比亚独立进程的开始，庆贺饱尝南非长期霸占之害的纳米比亚终于独立有期，继续支持和声援刚毅不屈的纳米比亚人民争取民族独立的正义斗争，让纳米比亚如期实现独立。”
    他说，我们衷心希望有关各方，特别是南非当局，信守诺言，认真履行各项有关决议和协议。我们呼吁国际社会保持警惕，继续对南非当局施加压力，继续声援和支持纳米比亚人民的斗争。
    利比里亚驻华大使、代理非洲使团团长约翰·里克斯在讲话中说，纳米比亚是非洲土地上迄今仍处在殖民统治下的最后一个地区，对非洲大陆来说具有特殊的意义。中非友协会长宫达非以及非洲国家驻华外交使节出席了招待会。
