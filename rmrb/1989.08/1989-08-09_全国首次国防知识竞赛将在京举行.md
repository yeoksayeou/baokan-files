Title: The First National Defense Knowledge Competition to be Held in Beijing
Author: Xú Jīngyuè
Date: 1989-08-09
Edition: 4

The first national defense knowledge competition will be held in Beijing on the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of New China. The recently published seventh issue of "Military Knowledge" has printed the competition questions, which cover topics such as ancient and modern domestic and foreign defense theories, defense science and technology, famous battles, and military figures. The competition will award first, second, and third prizes, as well as excellent prizes. The final round and the award ceremony will take place in Beijing before and after National Day.

### 全国首次国防知识竞赛将在京举行
徐京跃
1989-08-09
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    全国首次国防知识竞赛将在京举行
    据新华社北京八月九日电  （记者徐京跃）全国首次有关国防知识的竞赛，将在新中国成立四十周年之际举行。新近发行的《军事知识》第七期刊登了竞赛试题，内容包括古今中外的国防理论、国防科技、著名战例、军事人物等。竞赛设一、二、三等奖和优秀奖，国庆节前后在北京举行决赛和发奖仪式。
