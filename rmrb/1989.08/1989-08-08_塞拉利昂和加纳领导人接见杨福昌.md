Title: Leaders of Sierra Leone and Ghana Meet with Yang Fuchang
Author: Chen Cailin
Date: 1989-08-08
Edition: 3

Sierra Leone and Ghana leaders meet with Yang Fuchang

New China News Agency, Beijing, August 8th: The news from Freetown is that Sierra Leone Acting President Milah S. Sheriff met with Chinese Assistant Foreign Minister Yang Fuchang on August 2nd.

Sheriff said to Yang Fuchang, "Sierra Leone and China have good diplomatic relations and have consistent views on many international issues such as the independence of Namibia and African debt." He also thanked China for its economic aid to Sierra Leone.

According to New China News Agency, Accra, August 8th: (Reporter Chen Cailin) Ghana's National Provisional Defense Council Chairman Jerry Rawlings said today when talking about the domestic situation in China, "We are glad that the Chinese government has taken actions to restore normal order and hope that the Chinese people will not be influenced by external forces and walk their own path."

Rawlings made these remarks when he met with Chinese Assistant Foreign Minister Yang Fuchang, who is currently visiting here. He hopes that the close friendly cooperative relationship between Ghana and China will continue to develop and welcomes more Chinese people to come to Ghana to participate in Ghana's economic development work.

Yang Fuchang and his delegation of four arrived here on the evening of the 6th.

### 塞拉利昂和加纳领导人接见杨福昌
陈才林
1989-08-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    塞拉利昂和加纳领导人接见杨福昌
    新华社北京8月8日电　弗里敦消息：塞拉利昂代总统米劳·谢里夫8月2日接见了中国外交部长助理杨福昌。
    谢里夫对杨福昌说，塞中两国有着良好的外交关系，并在纳米比亚独立和非洲债务等许多国际问题上与中国有着一致的看法。他还感谢中国对塞拉利昂的经济援助。
    据新华社阿克拉8月8日电　（记者陈才林）加纳全国临时保卫委员会主席罗林斯今天在这里谈到中国国内形势时说：“我们感到庆幸的是，中国政府已经采取行动恢复了正常秩序，希望中国人民不要受外界的影响，走自己的路。”
    罗林斯是在接见正在这里访问的中国外交部长助理杨福昌时说这番话的。他希望加纳和中国之间的密切友好的合作关系继续发展，并欢迎更多的中国人来加纳参与加纳的经济发展工作。
    杨福昌一行4人是6日晚抵达这里的。
