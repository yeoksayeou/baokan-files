Title: Nepal and India Resume High-Level Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-08-27
Edition: 3

Nepal and India Resume High-Level Talks
Rajiv Gandhi to Meet Birendra

KATHMANDU, Aug. 25 (Xinhua) -- Indian Foreign Minister Narasimha Rao arrived here today to resume high-level talks between India and Nepal aimed at breaking the deadlock in bilateral relations.

Rao told reporters at the airport that the ties between India and Nepal are permanent and strong. He said, "Some things that should not have happened have happened" between the two countries.

In addition to holding talks with Nepal's Foreign Minister Surya Bahadur Thapa, Rao will also meet with King Birendra.

BEIJING, Aug. 26 (Xinhua) -- Indian Foreign Minister Rao, after concluding his two-day visit to Nepal, announced today that Indian Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi and Nepal's King Birendra will meet in Belgrade on September 4 to seek improvements in the stalled Nepal-India relations.

### 尼泊尔印度恢复高级会谈  拉·甘地将会晤比兰德拉

1989-08-27
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    尼泊尔印度恢复高级会谈
    拉·甘地将会晤比兰德拉
    据新华社加德满都8月25日电　印度外交部长拉奥今天抵达这里，恢复印度与尼泊尔高级官员为寻求打破两国关系僵局所举行的会谈。
    拉奥在机场对新闻记者说，连接印度和尼泊尔的纽带是永久的和牢固的。他说，两国间“发生了一些不该发生的事情”。
    拉奥除同尼泊尔外交大臣乌帕德亚亚举行会谈外，还将拜会国王比兰德拉。
    据新华社北京8月26日电  综合本社驻新德里、加德满都记者报道：印度外长拉奥今天在结束对尼泊尔的两天访问后宣布，印度总理拉·甘地与尼泊尔国王比兰德拉将于9月4日在贝尔格莱德举行会晤，以寻求改善陷于僵局的尼印关系。
