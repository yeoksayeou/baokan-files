Title: Sino-Belgian Joint Production of Rolling Mills
Author: Yan Jiasen
Date: 1989-08-10
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 10th

    The Baosteel Group and the Belgian Rolling Mill Manufacturing Company recently signed a joint venture agreement. The two sides will cooperate to produce cold rolling mills, forming an advanced large-scale rolling mill production base in southern China.

    Cold rolling mills are key components for the cold rolling mill of Baosteel, which started production last year. If relying on imports, hundreds of millions of dollars would be needed annually. After the establishment of this company, in addition to meeting the needs of the mill, part of the products will be sold to the international market. After the cold rolling mills of Baosteel are domestically produced, hundreds of millions of dollars can be saved annually.

### 中比联合生产轧辊
严家森
1989-08-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    中比联合生产轧辊
    本报上海8月10日电　宝钢集团与比利时轧辊制造公司最近签订合资协议。中比将合作制造冷轧辊，在我国南方形成一个先进的大型轧辊生产基地。
    冷轧辊是去年投产的宝钢冷轧厂的主要生产备件，若依靠进口，每年需花费几百万美元。这一公司成立后，除满足本厂需要，一部分产品将销往国际市场。宝钢冷轧辊实现国产化后，每年可节约数百万美元。（严家森）
