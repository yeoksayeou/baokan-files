Title: 1988 Small Statistics: Shanghai Enterprises' Contributions
Author:
Date: 1989-08-20
Page: 4

The top 10 enterprises in Shanghai by realized profit and tax revenue (accounting for nearly 1/4 of the total profit and tax revenue of all industrial enterprises in the city):

1. Shanghai Petrochemical Company, 11.78 billion yuan;
2. Shanghai Tobacco Company, 11.48 billion yuan;
3. Shanghai Gaoqiao Petrochemical Company, 8.24 billion yuan;
4. Shanghai Electric Power Industry Bureau, 6.57 billion yuan;
5. Shanghai Baoshan Iron & Steel Company, 4.58 billion yuan;
6. Shanghai Third Iron & Steel Plant, 3.79 billion yuan;
7. Shanghai Fifth Iron & Steel Plant, 2.67 billion yuan;
8. Shanghai Volkswagen Automobile Company, 1.97 billion yuan;
9. Shanghai Bicycle Factory, 1.70 billion yuan;
10. Shanghai Third Bicycle Factory, 1.57 billion yuan.

These 10 enterprises collectively realized a total profit and tax revenue of 54.35 billion yuan, accounting for nearly 1/4 (23.9%) of the total profit and tax revenue of all industrial enterprises in the city.

The top 10 enterprises by per capita profit realization (selected from 350 enterprises that contributed over 100 million yuan in profit and tax):

1. Shanghai Lihua Limited Company, 32.26 million yuan;
2. Shanghai Cigarette Factory, 19.04 million yuan;
3. Shanghai Volkswagen Automobile Company, 9.19 million yuan;
4. Pujiang Beverage Factory of China Grain and Oil Food Import and Export Corporation, 8.77 million yuan;
5. Drum Factory of China Petrochemical Sales Company, East China Branch, 7.72 million yuan;
6. Shanghai Huasheng Chemical Factory, 7.03 million yuan;
7. Shanghai Household Chemical Factory, 6.78 million yuan;
8. Shanghai Ocean Fishery Company, 5.64 million yuan;
9. Shanghai Aluminum Wire Factory, 5.53 million yuan;
10. Shanghai Daily Fragrance Factory, 5.53 million yuan.

### 1988年小统计  上海企业的奉献

1989-08-20
第4版(国庆专版)
专栏：

    1988年小统计
    上海企业的奉献
    实现利润、税金最多的10家企业（占全市工业企业实现利税总额的近1／4）
    1、上海石油化工总厂，11.78亿元；2、上海烟草公司，11.48亿元；3、上海高桥石油化工公司，8.24亿元；4、上海电力工业局，6.57亿元；5、上海宝山钢铁总厂，4.58亿元；6、上海第三钢铁厂，3.79亿元；7、上海第五钢铁厂，2.67亿元；8、上海大众有限公司，1.97亿元；9、上海自行车厂，1.70亿元；10、上海自行车三厂，1.57亿元。
