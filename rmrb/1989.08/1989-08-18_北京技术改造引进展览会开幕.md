Title: Beijing Technology Upgrading and Introduction Exhibition Opens
Author: Yan Shigu
Date: 1989-08-18
Page: 2

Column: News from All Directions

The "Beijing Municipal Industrial Technology Upgrading and Technology Introduction 10-Year Achievements Exhibition" opened today at the Beijing Agricultural Exhibition Hall. The exhibition, which will run for 10 days, showcases the significant achievements made in Beijing's technology upgrading efforts through a rich collection of photographs, actual equipment, and models.

According to statistics, the total industrial output value of the city in 1988 was 1.4 times that of 10 years ago.

### 北京技术改造引进展览会开幕
颜世贵
1989-08-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    北京技术改造引进展览会开幕
    本报北京8月18日讯　记者颜世贵报道：为期10天的“北京市工业技术改造技术引进10年成果展览”，今天在北京农业展览馆开幕。它以丰富的图片资料和一些实物、模型，展示了北京市在技术改造方面取得的巨大成就。据统计，1988年同10年前比，全市工业总产值增长了1.4倍。
