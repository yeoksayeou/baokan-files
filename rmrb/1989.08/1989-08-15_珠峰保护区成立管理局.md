Title: Establishment of the Management Bureau for the Mount Everest Nature Reserve
Author: Wang Guangming
Date: 1989-08-15
Edition: 2

The Mount Everest Nature Reserve Management Bureau was recently established in Xigazê, Tibet. Experts from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the American Institute for High Altitude Research attended the inauguration ceremony.

The nature reserve covers an area of approximately 20,000 square kilometers with a population of nearly 30,000. It is centered around Mount Everest and includes most of the regions of Dingri, Dingjie, Nyalam, and Gyangzê counties. Within the reserve, there are the world's largest and most extensive virgin forests with the greatest elevation difference.

### 珠峰保护区成立管理局
王广明
1989-08-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    珠峰保护区成立管理局
    本报日喀则八月十五日电　珠穆朗玛峰自然保护区管理局，近日在西藏日喀则成立。中国科学院和来自美国高山研究所的专家出席了成立大会。
    这个自然保护区面积约二万平方公里，人口近三万。它以珠峰为中心，包括定日、定结、聂拉木、吉隆四县大部地区，区内有世界上高差最大、面积广阔的原始森林。
          （王广明）
