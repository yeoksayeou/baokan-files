Title: Foreign Investment in Beijing Shifts to Industrial Sector
Author: Xia Junsheng
Date: 1989-08-22
Edition: 1

By the end of June this year, Beijing had approved 317 "three-in-one" enterprises engaged in industrial production, accounting for 64% of the total number of foreign-invested enterprises in the city. This figure indicates that the focus of foreign investment in Beijing has shifted from non-production sectors such as tourism and services to industrial production sectors.

### 外商在京投资转向工业行业
夏俊生
1989-08-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    外商在京投资转向工业行业
    据新华社北京八月二十二日电  （记者夏俊生）截至今年六月底，北京市已批准工业生产型“三资”企业三百一十七家，占全市外商投资企业总数的百分之六十四。这一数字标志着外商在北京市的投资热点已由旅游服务业等非生产性行业转向工业生产性行业。
