Title: The Publication of "Zhou Enlai's Theory and Practice of the United Front"
Author:
Date: 1989-08-05
Edition: 4

The book "Zhou Enlai's Theory and Practice of the United Front" has recently been published by Anhui People's Publishing House.

This work comprehensively and systematically introduces Zhou Enlai's contributions to establishing, consolidating, and developing the united front under the leadership of the Communist Party of China. The book primarily elucidates three key points: Zhou Enlai was a model for united front work after the founding of the Communist Party of China; Zhou Enlai scientifically applied the basic principles of Marxism-Leninism, enriching and developing the Communist Party of China's theory of the united front; Zhou Enlai's theory and practice of the united front have significant guiding importance for conducting united front work in the new era.

### 《周恩来统一战线的理论与实践》问世

1989-08-05
第4版(综合)
专栏：

   《周恩来统一战线的理论与实践》问世
    新华社电　《周恩来统一战线的理论与实践》一书，最近由安徽人民出版社出版。　　这部作品全面系统地介绍了周恩来同志在建立、巩固和发　展中国共产党领导的统一战线上所作的贡献。全书主要阐明了三个重点问题：周恩来同志是中国共产党建立以后从事统一战线工作的模范；周恩来同志科学地运用马列主义的基本原理，丰富和发展了中国共产党的统一战线理论；周恩来统一战线的理论与实践对开展新时期的统一战线工作有重大指导意义。
