Title: Defective Color Tubes Can Be Revitalized
Author: Xú Yàozhōng
Date: 1989-08-23
Edition: 2

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 23rd, (reporter Xu Yaozhong) The reporter learned from the Ministry of Machinery and Electronics' 12th Research Institute that the newly established Sanxin Electronics Limited Company in Shenzhen has recently restored 5,000 defective color display tubes to their original condition. These tubes have been re-installed by the manufacturers into new color televisions.

Experts say that this indicates China has successfully mastered the technology to revitalize defective color tubes, which will bring significant social and economic benefits to the country.

Experts further explain that the prospects for color tube regeneration are very broad. It is understood that by the end of the "Eighth Five-Year Plan" period, China's color tube production capacity will reach more than 12 million units. If 240,000 defective color tubes can be repaired annually, it will yield benefits of more than 240 million yuan.

### 废损彩管可以起死回生
徐耀中
1989-08-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    废损彩管可以起死回生
    据新华社北京8月23日电　（记者徐耀中）记者从机电部第12研究所获悉，这个所在深圳兴办的三新电子有限公司新近把5000只报废的彩色显像管修复如新，并被生产厂家装上了新的彩电。
    专家称，这表明我国已成功地掌握了使废损彩管起死回生的新技术，它将给我国带来良好的社会效益和经济效益。
    专家介绍评价说，彩管再生工作的前景十分广阔。据了解，到“八五”期间，我国彩管生产能力将达到1200万只以上，如一年能从中修复24万多只报废彩管，即可获益2.4亿元以上。
