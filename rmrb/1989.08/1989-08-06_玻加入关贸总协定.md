Title: Bolivia Joins the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade
Author:
Date: 1989-08-06
Page: 3

The signing ceremony for Bolivia's accession to the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT) was held on August 4 in Geneva. Bolivia became the 97th signatory country to the agreement. Once the Bolivian Parliament ratifies this agreement, it will come into effect 30 days later.

### 玻加入关贸总协定

1989-08-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    玻加入关贸总协定
    玻利维亚加入关税及贸易总协定组织的协议书签字仪式4日在日内瓦举行。
    玻利维亚为该组织的第97个协议签署国。一旦玻利维亚议会批准这项协议书，协议书将在30天后生效。
