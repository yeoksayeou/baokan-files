Title: The Third Pan-Pacific Swimming Championships Conclude
Author: Jiang Zhan Guo
Date: 1989-08-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 21st.

On the final day of the Third Pan-Pacific Swimming Championships, Chinese athletes secured one gold medal and two silver medals. The powerful U.S. team performed exceptionally well, winning 8 gold medals and setting 4 new world records, adding significant color to the competition.

Chinese athlete Lin Li won the gold medal in the women's 200-meter individual medley with a time of 2:14.69, breaking her own Asian record. Yang Wenyi took the silver medal in the women's 50-meter freestyle with a time of 25.95 seconds. The Chinese team of Yang Wenyi, Huang Xiaomin, Qian Hong, and Zhuang Yong secured the second place in the women's 4x100-meter medley relay with a time of 4:11.54.

On August 20th, the U.S. team dominated the final 10 events, winning 8 gold medals and setting 4 new world records. The remaining gold medal was won by an Australian athlete.

The Third Pan-Pacific Swimming Championships began on August 17th and featured athletes from 17 countries and regions. In the 36 events, the U.S. team won 25 gold medals. China, Australia, and Canada each won 3 gold medals, tying for the second place. Chinese athletes also won 6 silver medals and 4 bronze medals. Japan and Suriname each won one gold medal.

The organizing committee announced that the Fourth Pan-Pacific Swimming Championships will be held in Edmonton, Canada, in August 1991.

### 第三届泛太平洋游泳赛结束  我选手共获奖牌三金六银四铜
姜占国
1989-08-21
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    第三届泛太平洋游泳赛结束
    我选手共获奖牌三金六银四铜
    新华社东京8月21日电　（记者姜占国）在本届泛太平洋地区游泳锦标赛最后一天比赛中，我国运动员获一项冠军，两项亚军。实力雄厚的美国队发挥极佳，获8项冠军，并有4人打破4项世界纪录，为本届比赛增色不少。
    我国运动员林莉在女子200米个人混合泳决赛中以2分14秒69获得冠军，打破了由她本人保持的亚洲纪录。杨文意在女子50米自由泳决赛中以25秒95获得亚军。杨文意、黄晓敏、钱红和庄泳获女子4×100米混合泳第二名，成绩是4分11秒54。
    在20日进行的最后10个项目比赛中，美国队获8块金牌，其中有4人打破4项世界纪录。另一块金牌被澳大利亚运动员夺得。
    第三届泛太平洋地区游泳锦标赛是从本月17日开始在此地举行的，17个国家和地区的运动员参加了比赛。在总计36个项目的比赛中，美国队获25项冠军；中国、澳大利亚和加拿大队均获3项冠军，金牌数并列第二，我国运动员还获6项亚军和4项第三名；日本和苏里南各获一项冠军。
    大会组委会宣布，第四届泛太平洋地区游泳锦标赛将于1991年8月在加拿大的埃德蒙顿举行。
