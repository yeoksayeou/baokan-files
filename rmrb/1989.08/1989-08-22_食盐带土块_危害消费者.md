Title: Salt Containing Soil Clumps Harms Consumers
Author: Qiū Tiānshòu  Zhāng Lǐgēn
Date: 1989-08-22
Edition: 6

From January to May 1989, our county imported 725 tons of regenerated salt from the salt fields in Ge'ermu, Ha'erga, and Keke in Qinghai Province. The soil content in this salt reached 6%. Such a large amount of soil mixed in the salt is difficult to pick out completely, seriously endangering the health of the broad masses of consumers.

    食盐带土块  危害消费者
    1989年1至5月，我县从青海格尔木、哈尔盖、柯柯等盐场调运来的725吨再生盐中泥土含量达6％。这么多泥土掺杂在盐中，难以挑捡干净，严重危害广大消费者的身体健康。
