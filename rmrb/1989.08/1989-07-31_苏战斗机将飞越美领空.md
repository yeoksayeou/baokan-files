Title: Soviet Fighter Jets to Fly Over U.S. Airspace
Author:
Date: 1989-07-31
Page: 3

Two Soviet MiG-29 fighter jets participating in the Canadian Aviation Exhibition will fly over U.S. airspace in August, escorted by U.S. fighter jets. This escorted flight is the first of its kind in North American aviation history, indicating a further strengthening of cooperation between the two superpowers, the United States and the Soviet Union.

### 苏战斗机将飞越美领空

1989-07-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏战斗机将飞越美领空
    两架参加加拿大航空展览的苏联米格—29战斗机将于8月在美国战斗机的护航下，飞越美国领空。这一护航飞行在北美航空史上是第一次，表明了美苏两个超级大国相互间合作的进一步加强。
