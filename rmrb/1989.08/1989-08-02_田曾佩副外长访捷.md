Title: Vice Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei Visits Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1989-08-02
Edition: 3

Vice Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei of China visited Czechoslovakia from July 30 to August 2. During his visit, he held talks with Deputy Foreign Minister Nechra of Czechoslovakia on bilateral relations and international issues. First Deputy Foreign Minister Sadovsky of Czechoslovakia also met with Tian Zengpei.

### 田曾佩副外长访捷

1989-08-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    田曾佩副外长访捷
    新华社布拉格8月2日电　中国外交部副部长田曾佩于7月30日至8月2日对捷克斯洛伐克进行了访问。
    访问期间，他同捷外交部副部长内赫拉就双边关系和国际问题举行了会谈。捷外交部第一副部长萨多夫斯基也会见了田曾佩。
