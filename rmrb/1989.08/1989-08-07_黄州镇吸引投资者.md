Title: Huangzhou Town Attracts Investors
Author: Fang Zhengjun  Chen Jinlin
Date: 1989-08-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, August 7th.

One of the "Five Small Special Economic Zones in Eastern Hubei" approved by the Hubei Provincial Government, Huangzhou Town in Huanggang County, is further improving its investment environment. Among them, three companies from Hong Kong have respectively established Fuliheng Limited Company, Longteng Industry, and Changjiang Joint Venture Rolling Steel Company.

### 黄州镇吸引投资者
方政军  陈锦林
1989-08-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    黄州镇吸引投资者
    新华社武汉8月7日电  （记者方政军、通讯员陈锦林）湖北省政府批准的经济开发“鄂东五小特区”之一黄冈县黄州镇，进一步改善投资环境。其中，香港三家公司分别建立了富利恒有限公司、龙腾实业和长江联营轧钢公司。
