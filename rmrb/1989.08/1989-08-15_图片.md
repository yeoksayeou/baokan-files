Title: Members of the U.S. House Armed Services Committee Visit the Soviet "Buran" Space Shuttle
Author:
Date: 1989-08-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency (Telephoto)

On August 14th, members of the U.S. House Armed Services Committee delegation visited the Soviet "Buran" space shuttle at the Baikonur Cosmodrome.

The delegation was greeted by Soviet officials and given a detailed tour of the space shuttle and its facilities. The visit is part of a broader effort to enhance cooperation and understanding between the two superpowers in the field of space exploration. The delegation expressed interest in the technological advancements and operational capabilities of the Soviet space program, noting the significance of such exchanges in fostering international collaboration.
