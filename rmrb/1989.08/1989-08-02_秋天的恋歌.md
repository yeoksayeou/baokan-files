Title: Autumn's Love Song
Author: Lai Jianmei
Date: 1989-08-02
Page: 8

In the rustling of yellow leaves,
My love flies away, piece by piece.
Autumn sounds sweep past my eyes,
With nameless blossoms leaving their branches.
A song of autumn rain weaving through the phoenix trees,
Sings softly of the distant summer.
The season of the red osmanthus,
Is no longer a burning anticipation.
The Snow Princess will emerge gracefully from the end of autumn,
This was your longing and mine.
Dreaming of a winter filled with peach blossom snow,
I stand alone in the vast, cold snowfield, carving my steadfastness.
You let the cold, stern winter test your sincerity.
After a flock of geese fly by,
Spring quietly arrives.

### 秋天的恋歌
赖建梅
1989-08-02
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    秋天的恋歌
    赖建梅
    在黄叶簌簌的纷响中
    我的爱一片片飘飞
    秋声从眼前掠过
    有莫名的花蕊离别枝头
    一支梧桐秋雨斜织的歌
    绵密地唱着悠远的夏天
    桕树转红的季节
    已不再成为焦灼的等待
    雪公主将从秋末盈盈地走出
    这曾是你的也是我的渴盼
    幻想桃花雪飘满的冬天
    我在旷凉的雪野久伫塑我的坚
     贞
    你让寒冷严峻考验你的真诚
    等一排雁鸣过后
    春天悄悄来临
