Title: Chinese Football Team Arrives in Kunming for High-Altitude Training
Author: Yáng Yǒng
Date: 1989-08-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, August 26th

The Chinese football team, in preparation for the second round of the World Cup qualifying matches in Asia, arrived in Kunming on August 25th. They will undergo a three-week high-altitude training session at the Haikou Training Base.

The team that traveled to Kunming for this training is the same squad that participated in the first round of matches. During their stay in Kunming, the Chinese football team will focus on physical and mental conditioning, as well as counter-pressing tactics, in preparation for the second round of matches against the other five teams. Their goal is to advance to the finals in Rome.

### 中国足球队抵昆明进行高原集训
杨嵘
1989-08-26
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    中国足球队抵昆明进行高原集训
    新华社昆明8月26日电　（通讯员杨嵘）中国足球队为备战世界杯赛亚洲区预选赛第二阶段比赛，25日已到达昆明，将在海埂训练基地进行为期3周的高原集训。
    这次赴昆明集训的中国队是参加第一阶段比赛的原班人马。在昆明训练期间，中国足球队将针对参加第二阶段比赛的另5个队的打法，着重于身体和心理素质及反紧逼战术等方面进行训练，力争进军罗马。
