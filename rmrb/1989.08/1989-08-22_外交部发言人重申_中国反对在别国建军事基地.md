Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Reiterates China's Opposition to Building Military Bases in Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-08-22
Edition: 1

The spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs reiterated today that China's principled stance is to oppose any country establishing military bases in other countries.

The spokesperson made this statement in response to a reporter's question about China's comments on the Singapore government's proposal to allow U.S. warships to have a base in Singapore.

He said, "We hope that the relevant countries will act with caution in handling the issue of U.S. military bases in Southeast Asia."

### 外交部发言人重申  中国反对在别国建军事基地

1989-08-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人重申
    中国反对在别国建军事基地
    新华社北京8月22日电　外交部发言人今天重申，中国的原则立场是反对任何国家在别国建立军事基地。
    发言人是在有记者问及中国政府对新加坡提出让美国军舰在新加坡拥有基地有何评论时作此回答的。
    他说，“我们希望有关国家在处理美国在东南亚军事基地问题上谨慎从事。”
