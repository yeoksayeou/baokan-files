Title: Mozambican Rebel Forces Halt Attacks on Nacala Corridor
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Edition: 3

Mozambican rebel forces have ceased attacks on the Nacala Corridor, which connects Mozambique to landlocked countries in Central Africa. On August 19, Afonso Dhlakama, the chairman of the Mozambican National Resistance (RENAMO), pledged that his rebel forces would stop attacking the Nacala Corridor.

According to local newspaper reports on August 20, Afonso made this promise after peace talks mediated by the Kenyan government with Mozambican church leaders. He stated that this is the first step towards achieving peace in Mozambique.

### 莫桑比克反政府武装停止袭击纳卡拉走廊

1989-08-21
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    莫桑比克反政府武装停止袭击纳卡拉走廊
    反政府的莫桑比克全国抵抗运动主席阿方索·德拉卡马19日许诺，他的反政府武装将停止袭击莫陆地联结中非国家的纳卡拉走廊。
    据当地报纸20日报道，阿方索是在肯尼亚政府主持下与莫桑比克教会领导人进行和平谈判后作出上述许诺的。他说，这是为实现莫桑比克和平进程所采取的第一个步骤。
