Title: Jiang Zemin Meets with Zhao Xicheng
Author:
Date: 1989-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 26th.

Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Communist Party of China, met with Mr. Zhao Xicheng, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Fuhua Shipping Company of New York, USA, this morning in Zhongnanhai. They had a friendly and cordial conversation that lasted for one hour.

Present at the meeting was Hu Chuanzhi, General Manager of the China State Shipbuilding Corporation.

### 江泽民会见赵锡成

1989-08-26
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民会见赵锡成
    新华社北京8月26日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天上午在中南海会见美国纽约福茂航运公司董事长赵锡成先生，同他进行了1小时的亲切友好的交谈。
    会见时，中国船舶工业总公司总经理胡传治在座。
