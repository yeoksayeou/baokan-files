Title: The Central Committee Plenum of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-08-01
Edition: 3

The two-day-long 25th Plenum of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia concluded on July 31. In his closing remarks, Chairman of the Central Committee Presidency of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Pancheski, emphasized that all measures and actions must be taken to stop nationalism. The meeting decided that the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia will be reduced from the current 165 members to 129 members.

### 南共联盟中央全会结束

1989-08-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    南共联盟中央全会结束
    为期两天的南共联盟25中全会于7月31日结束。南共联盟中央主席团主席潘切夫斯基在会议结束时强调指出，必须采取一切措施和行动制止民族主义。会议决定，今后南共联盟中央委员会将由目前的165人减至129人。
