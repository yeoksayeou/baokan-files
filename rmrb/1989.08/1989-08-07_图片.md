Title: Finnish Primary and Secondary Schools Offer Nature Lessons in Natural Environments
Author:
Date: 1989-08-07
Page: 7

In addition to teaching natural science courses in natural environments, Finnish primary and secondary schools also encourage students to organize their own teaching activities. This approach aims to cultivate students' abilities for independent activity and critical thinking. The image shows Finnish primary school students taking a biology class outdoors.

Xinhua News Agency
