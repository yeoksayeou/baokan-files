Title: Beirut Shelling Escalates, 18 More Dead
Author:
Date: 1989-08-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, August 5th.

Beirut security officials said on August 5th that Syrian forces stationed in Lebanon and Christian militias engaged in intense artillery exchanges in Beirut the previous night, resulting in at least 18 deaths and 82 injuries.

This round of shelling was the most severe since Syrian forces and Christian militias clashed on March 8th. The total death toll has now reached 534, with 2,266 injured.

The fighting primarily occurred in the western part of Beirut and its southern suburbs. A Syrian military ammunition depot was destroyed.

### 贝鲁特炮战升级  又有18人死亡

1989-08-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    贝鲁特炮战升级
    又有18人死亡
    新华社贝鲁特8月5日电　据贝鲁特治安人士5日说，叙利亚驻黎巴嫩部队同基督教武装昨晚在贝鲁特相互猛烈炮击，造成至少18人死亡、82人受伤。
    这次炮击是叙利亚部队同基督教武装自3月8日炮击以来最严重的一次。这次炮击使死亡总人数达534人，伤亡人数达2266人。
    炮战主要是在贝鲁特西区和南郊进行的。叙利亚军队的一座弹药库被摧毁。
