Title: Beijing Garrison Holds Meeting to Commend the Suppression of the Riots
Author: guō jiā xiǎng xián
Date: 1989-08-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 7th.

The Beijing Garrison today held a meeting to summarize and commend the efforts in stopping the turmoil and suppressing the counter-revolutionary riots. Members of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China Central Committee and Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee Li Xixian, and Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, He Haoqian, spoke at the meeting.

Li Xixian stated at the meeting that the Beijing Garrison has been steadfast and resolute throughout the entire process of stopping the turmoil and suppressing the counter-revolutionary riots. The facts have once again proven that this guard unit is reliable and trustworthy, and is absolutely loyal to the Party and the people. It is indeed the loyal guardian of the capital.

He Haoqian emphasized at the meeting that the stability of the entire country depends on the capital, and the stability of the capital ensures the stability of the country. The garrison troops must fully recognize the important mission and great historical responsibility of guarding the capital and protecting the Party Central Committee. In terms of military discipline and rapid response, they must set an example for the entire army.

During the meeting, representatives from outstanding units and individuals of the garrison and local militia departments introduced their advanced deeds in stopping the turmoil and suppressing the riots.

Chen Xitong, Hong Xuezhi, Liu Huaqing, Guo Linxiang, and others attended the meeting.

### 北京卫戍区召开平暴表彰会
郭嘉　响贤
1989-08-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    北京卫戍区召开平暴表彰会
    本报北京8月7日讯　北京卫戍区今天召开了制止动乱、平息暴乱总结表彰大会。中共中央政治局委员、北京市委书记李锡铭和人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田在会上讲了话。
    李锡铭在会上说，北京卫戍区在制止动乱、平息反革命暴乱的全过程中，始终旗帜鲜明，立场坚定。事实再次证明这支警卫部队是经得起考验的，是无限忠于党和人民的，不愧是首都的忠诚卫士。
    迟浩田在会上强调，全国安定看首都，首都稳定天下安。卫戍部队要充分认识肩负的保卫首都、保卫党中央的重要使命和重大的历史责任。在军容风纪、快速反应上，要为全军作出好样子。
    会上，卫戍区和地方人武部门的优秀单位和个人代表介绍了他们制乱平暴的先进事迹。
    陈希同、洪学智、刘华清、郭林祥等出席了大会。
        （郭嘉　响贤）
