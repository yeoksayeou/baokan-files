Title: Xinjiang to Hold the Second National Minority Games
Author: Jiang Zhiwu
Date: 1989-08-27
Edition: 3

Relevant departments in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region are currently busy preparing for the second Xinjiang National Minority Traditional Sports Games. The sports extravaganza, scheduled to take place from September 22 to 28 in Artux at the foot of the Pamir Plateau, will feature six competitive events: horse racing, goat pulling, traditional wrestling, archery, swing, and martial arts. Additionally, there will be six performance events: Dawazi (tightrope walking), camel racing, and ram fighting.

### 新疆将举行第二届民运会
姜志武
1989-08-27
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    新疆将举行第二届民运会
    新华社乌鲁木齐八月二十六日电  （记者姜志武）新疆维吾尔自治区有关部门目前正在加紧筹备新疆第二届少数民族传统体育运动会。将于九月二十二日至二十八日在帕米尔高原脚下的阿图什举行的这次体育盛会，共设速度赛马、刁羊、民族式摔跤、射箭、秋千和武术六个比赛项目，以及达瓦孜（踩大绳）、赛骆驼、斗羊等六个表演项目。
