Title: Athletes from 13 Asian Countries and Regions Compete in Singapore
Author: Lǐ Yǒngmíng
Date: 1989-08-13
Edition: 4

Athletes from 13 Asian countries and regions are competing in Singapore.

Chinese athletes win two gold medals on the first day

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, August 12 (Reporter Li Yongming) - The Hunan Athletics Team from China today, on the first day of the 51st Singapore Open Athletics Championships, won 2 gold medals and 1 bronze medal, and broke one championship record.

Hunan athlete Xiang Mǐyuǎn in the triple jump competition achieved a gold medal with a result of 16.09 meters, breaking the 15.97 meters record set by Malaysian athlete Sidik Shahak last year. Another Hunan athlete, Zhang Liánbiāo, won the men's javelin gold medal with a result of 68.54 meters. Female athlete Zhou Qīng won the bronze medal in the women's 400 meters.

Athletes from 13 Asian countries and regions will compete for 55 gold medals over the two-day event. The Hunan Athletics Team, consisting of 7 athletes, will participate in 11 events.

Thai athletes performed exceptionally well, winning a total of 3 gold medals, 2 silver medals, and 3 bronze medals. The Japanese and South Korean teams each won 2 gold medals, while the teams from Hong Kong, Malaysia, Brunei, and India each won 1 gold medal.

### 亚洲13个国家和地区田径选手角逐新加坡  中国运动员第一天获两枚金牌
李永明
1989-08-13
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    亚洲13个国家和地区田径选手角逐新加坡
    中国运动员第一天获两枚金牌
    新华社新加坡8月12日电（记者李永明）中国湖南田径队今天在第51届新加坡田径公开赛的第一天比赛中，夺得2枚金牌，1枚铜牌，并破一项大会纪录。
    湖南选手向米远在三级跳远比赛中，以16.09米的成绩获得金牌，并打破了马来西亚运动员西迪克·沙哈克去年创下的15.97米的大会纪录。湖南另一名选手张连标以68.54米的成绩获男子标枪金牌。女运动员周青获女子400米铜牌。
    来自亚洲13个国家和地区的运动员将在为期两天的比赛中角逐55枚金牌。湖南田径队由7名选手组成，他们将参加11项比赛。
    泰国选手表现突出，共夺得3枚金牌、2枚银牌和3枚铜牌，日本队和南朝鲜队各获2枚金牌，香港、马来西亚、文莱和印度队各获1枚金牌。
