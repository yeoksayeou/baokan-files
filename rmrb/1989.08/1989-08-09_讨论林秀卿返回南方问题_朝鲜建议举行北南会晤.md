Title: Discussing the Issue of Lin Su-kyung's Return to the South
Author: Xú Bǎo Kāng
Date: 1989-08-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8 August

The Committee for the Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland and the Central Committee of the Red Cross Society of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today separately telephoned the "Unification Board" and the Red Cross of South Korea to suggest holding a meeting between the two sides. The purpose of this meeting is to discuss the issue of ensuring the safe return of Lin Su-kyung, a representative of the "Pan-National Alliance for the Great Unity of Korea," through Panmunjom on August 15.

The Director of the Secretariat of the Committee for the Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland and the Chairman of the Central Committee of the Red Cross Society separately proposed in the telephone calls that each side send three representatives to hold a North-South working-level meeting at the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission building in Panmunjom at 10 a.m. on August 14 and 11 a.m. on August 11, respectively. They also expressed the hope that the South would respond positively as soon as possible.

### 讨论林秀卿返回南方问题  朝鲜建议举行北南会晤
徐宝康
1989-08-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    讨论林秀卿返回南方问题
    朝鲜建议举行北南会晤
    本报平壤8月8日电　记者徐宝康报道：朝鲜祖国和平统一委员会和朝鲜红十字中央委员会今天分别打电话给南朝鲜“统一院”和红十字会，建议举行双方工作会晤，以便商讨南朝鲜“全大协”代表林秀卿8月15日安全通过板门店返回南方问题。
    朝鲜祖国和平统一委员会书记局局长和红十字中央委员会委员长在电话中分别具体建议，双方各派3名代表于8月14日上午10时和8月11日上午10时，在板门店中立国监察委员会会址举行北南工作会晤，并希望南方尽早给予积极答复。
