Title: Widening the Gap: Implementing a New Wage System Based on Performance
Author:
Date: 1989-08-28
Edition: 3

The Bulgarian government has decided to expand the use of a new wage system in the material production sector starting from September 1 of this year.

Bulgaria's new wage system simplifies the original multi-tiered classification wage system into a three-category, three-level basic wage system. The goal is to reasonably widen the wage gap and make the differences between wage levels more apparent.

According to reports, the new wage system adopted by Bulgaria grants companies and enterprises the authority to reasonably adjust internal wages without exceeding the total wage fund. The new wage system ensures that diligent and hardworking workers and experts receive guaranteed position-based wages, while significantly increasing the wages of technical personnel who bear major responsibilities during the technological revolution.

### 拉开档次　按劳付酬  保将推行新工资制

1989-08-28
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    拉开档次　按劳付酬
    保将推行新工资制
    新华社索非亚电　保加利亚政府决定，从今年9月1日起，在物质生产部门扩大运用新工资制。
    保加利亚的新工资制把原有的多级别的类别工资制精简合并成三类三级基本工资制，目的在于合理拉开工资差距，使同类工资之间的档次差别能得到明显的体现。
    据报道，保加利亚采用的新工资制，赋予公司和企业在不突破工资基金总额的条件下合理调整内部工资的权力。新工资制既能保证忠于职守、勤奋工作的工人和专家获得有保障的职务工资，又能明显提高在科技革命过程中担负重大责任的技术人员的工资。
