Title: International Symposium on Exploration Geophysics Opens
Author: Jiang Jianke
Date: 1989-08-23
Edition: 2

The "International Symposium on Exploration Geophysics Beijing 1989," jointly organized by the China Exploration Geophysical Society and the Society of Exploration Geophysicists of the United States, opened on August 23 at the Beijing Library. Leaders such as Yan Jici and Zhu Xun attended the opening ceremony.

More than 500 representatives from 18 countries and regions, including China, the United States, the Soviet Union, Japan, and France, participated in the conference. During the conference, an exhibition was held simultaneously, featuring 38 foreign and 8 domestic booths showcasing over a hundred of the world's most advanced exploration geophysical instruments, models, and videos.

### 勘探地球物理国际讨论会开幕
蒋建科
1989-08-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    勘探地球物理国际讨论会开幕
    本报讯　由中国勘探地球物理联合会和美国勘探地球物理协会联合召开的“勘探地球物理北京八九国际讨论会”，二十三日在北京图书馆开幕，严济慈、朱训等领导同志出席开幕式。
    来自中国、美国、苏联、日本、法国等十八个国家和地区的五百多名代表参加会议。会议期间同时举办展览会，三十八个外商展台和八个国内展台共展出上百种世界上最先进的勘探地球物理仪器实物、模型和录像。　（蒋建科）
