Title: Rural Joy
Author: Lǐ Bǎoróng, Wáng Zhī
Date: 1989-08-03
Page: 8

Poetry and Painting Pairing

    Rural Joy
    Photography by Lǐ Bǎoróng
    Poetry by Wáng Zhī

    Lift up the fresh days
    Lift up the days sweet as a Hami melon
    A small courtyard, a wisp of dawn light rising...
    Lift up the green
    Lift up the hands, rough as tree trunks
    That little sun!
    The straw hats, just off work, are cooling off in the green shade
    The threads of the chair convey emotions
    Weave a picture of rural joy!
    Like a jingling camel bell
    Like a fragrant boutonniere
    On the ancient Silk Road...

### 农家乐
李宝荣  王之
1989-08-03
第8版(副刊)
专栏：诗画配

    农家乐
    李宝荣　摄影
    王之　配诗
    把新鲜的日子举起来
    把哈密瓜一样甜的日子举起来
    小院，升起一缕霞光……
    把翠绿举起来
    把树干一样皴裂的手举起来
    那一颗小小的太阳！
    刚下工的草帽在绿茵乘凉
    椅子上的经线和纬线传递情感
    织就一幅农家的喜悦！
    像一枚叮当响的驼铃
    像一枚芳香的胸花
    在古老的丝绸之路上……
