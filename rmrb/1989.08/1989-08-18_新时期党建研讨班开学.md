Title: New Period Party Building Seminar Begins
Author: He Ping, Guan Dao Liang
Date: 1989-08-18
Edition: 2

The "New Period Party Building Seminar" jointly organized by the magazines *Qiushi*, *Dangjian*, *Dangjian Yanjiu*, and the Beijing Municipal Party School of the Communist Party of China began today.

The seminar has invited leaders and experts from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and relevant departments in Beijing to systematically teach party-building knowledge. The seminar will last for 12 days. The teaching method combines classroom instruction with correspondence courses. Over 700 party cadres from various parts of the country are participating in the classroom instruction. Nearly 20,000 people have signed up for the correspondence course.

### 新时期党建研讨班开学
何平  管道亮
1989-08-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    新时期党建研讨班开学
    新华社北京8月18日电　（通讯员何平、管道亮）由《求是》、《党建》、《党建研究》杂志社和中共北京市委党校联合举办的“新时期党的建设研讨班”今天开学。
    这个研讨班聘请中共中央及北京有关部门的领导和专家教授，系统讲授党建知识。研讨班为期12天。研讨班采取面授、函授相结合的教学形式。参加这次面授班的有来自全国各地党务部门的领导同志共700多人。报名参加函授的近2万人。
