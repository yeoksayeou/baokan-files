Title: China's Team Achieves Mediocre Results at the World Canoeing Championships
Author: Wang Jinbo
Date: 1989-08-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 27th.

The 22nd World Canoeing Championships concluded today in the Bulgarian city of Plovdiv after the final five 10,000-meter events.

In the afternoon's finals for men's single and double canoe and kayak 10,000 meters, and men's four-person kayak 10,000 meters, Soviet athletes secured 2 gold medals, 1 silver medal, and 2 bronze medals. Hungarian athletes won 1 gold medal and 2 silver medals, while Czechoslovakian athletes took home 1 gold medal and 1 bronze medal. Danish athletes earned 1 gold medal, and French and British athletes each won 1 silver medal. Polish and Portuguese athletes each secured 1 bronze medal. Chinese athlete Xue Bing participated in the men's single kayak 10,000-meter race, finishing in 19th place. Chinese athletes Kuang Rizhong and Wang Xinqiang placed 8th in the men's double canoe 10,000-meter final.

### 世界皮划艇锦标赛我队成绩平平
王金柏
1989-08-28
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    世界皮划艇锦标赛我队成绩平平
    新华社索非亚8月27日电　（记者王金柏）第22届世界皮划艇锦标赛今天进行了最后5个10000米项目决赛后，在保加利亚的普罗夫迪夫市结束。
    在今天下午进行的男子单人和双人皮、划艇10000米和男子四人皮艇10000米共5个项目的决赛中，苏联选手夺得2枚金牌、1枚银牌和2枚铜牌，匈牙利选手夺得1枚金牌和2枚银牌，捷克斯洛伐克选手夺得1枚金牌和1枚铜牌，丹麦选手获1枚金牌，法国和英国选手各获1枚银牌，波兰和葡萄牙选手各获1枚铜牌。中国选手薛冰参加了男子单人皮艇10000米比赛，名列第19；中国选手邝日忠和王新强在双人划艇10000米决赛中获得第8名。
