Title: Color-Printed Glass: Vibrant and Multicolored
Author: Liao Yongmin
Date: 1989-08-05
Edition: 2

The Tianhe District Special Crafts Factory in Guangzhou has recently invented a technology for color-printed glass—bichromatic offset printing double-sided transparent color plate glass technology.

Color-printed glass uses special printing technology and techniques to print natural landscapes, oil paintings, decorative patterns, and other images onto glass with over 95% precision. The colors are natural and lifelike, with a strong sense of depth. Additionally, it features double-sided transparency and consistent color and image effects. This type of glass can withstand washing with water, gasoline, and weak acids and will not fade for at least 10 years.

### 彩印玻璃　五色斑斓
廖勇民
1989-08-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    彩印玻璃　五色斑斓
    新华社广州8月5日电　（记者廖勇民）广州市天河区特种工艺厂最近发明了彩印玻璃技术——胶版摄影印刷双面透视彩色平板玻璃技术。
    彩印玻璃，是采用特种印刷技术与工艺，以95％以上的高精度，将摄影的自然景物、油画、装饰图案等，直接印制在玻璃上，不但色彩天然、逼真，还富有立体感，而且具有双面透视、色彩与图像相一致的特殊效果。这种玻璃能经受水、汽油和弱酸等的洗擦，10年不退色。
