Title: Beijing International Television Week to be Held at the End of the Month
Author: Li Mingguang
Date: 1989-08-06
Edition: 4

    Beijing International Television Week to be Held at the End of the Month

    According to a report by our reporter Li Mingguang, to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, Beijing Television will host the "Beijing International Television Week" from August 31 to September 6. Eighteen television stations from 14 countries and regions, including Japan, the Soviet Union, Poland, Mexico, Pakistan, Egypt, and the German Democratic Republic, will participate in this television week event. During this period, 15 television dramas (34 episodes) provided by the participating television stations, as well as more than 50 documentary films, animated films, and music films, will be screened.

### 北京国际电视周月底举行
李明光
1989-08-06
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    北京国际电视周月底举行
    本报讯　记者李明光报道：为庆祝中华人民共和国成立40周年，北京电视台将于8月31日至9月6日举行“北京国际电视周”。日本、苏联、波兰、墨西哥、巴基斯坦、埃及、民主德国等14个国家和地区的18家电视台参加本次电视周活动。其间将展播参展电视台提供的15部（34集）电视剧，50余部纪录片、动画片、音乐片等。
