Author: 
Date: 1989-08-26
Edition: 1

Wan Li Meets with Thai Parliament Delegation
Hopes to Strengthen Exchanges Between the Two Countries and Their Parliaments

BEIJING, Aug. 26 (Xinhua) -- Wan Li, chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met today with a delegation from the Military Committee of the House of Representatives of the Thai Parliament, led by Chairman Admiral Sirichai Sirilankha.

Wan Li said, "You are the largest foreign delegation that the NPC has received since the suppression of the riots in China." He thanked the Thai government for its very friendly attitude towards China's suppression of the riots. He said that China and Thailand are friendly neighboring countries. The two countries have comprehensive cooperation and exchanges in the fields of politics, economy, trade, science and technology, and the military. He expressed the hope of further strengthening exchanges between the two countries and their parliaments.

Sirichai reaffirmed that Thailand's policy towards China in all aspects would not change. He said that the members of his delegation had seen with their own eyes that the situation in Beijing had returned to normal and was stable.

Wan Li emphasized that China's policy of reform and opening up, independent and peaceful foreign policy, and strengthening friendly cooperation with Third World countries would not change.

### 万里会见泰国国会代表团  希望加强两国和两国议会间交往

1989-08-26
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    万里会见泰国国会代表团
    希望加强两国和两国议会间交往
    新华社北京8月26日电　全国人大常委会委员长万里今天在人民大会堂会见由主席西里·西里朗西海军上将率领的泰王国国会下议院军事委员会代表团。
    万里说，“你们是中国平息暴乱后，全国人大接待的最大的外国代表团。”他感谢泰国政府对中国的平暴所采取的非常友好态度。他说，中泰两国是友好邻邦。两国在政治、经济、贸易、科技、军事等领域有着全面的合作和交流。他表示希望进一步加强两国和两国议会间的交往。
    西里重申了泰国对中国在各个方面的政策不变。他说，他的代表团成员亲眼看到北京的局势已恢复正常，是稳定的。
    万里强调，中国改革开放、独立自主的和平外交政策以及加强同第三世界国家友好合作关系的政策不会改变。
