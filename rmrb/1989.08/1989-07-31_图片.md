Title: Abundant Peach Harvest in Hebei Province
Author: Zhang Yingjie
Date: 1989-07-31
Edition: 2

    This year, Mancheng County in Hebei Province has seen a bumper harvest of peaches, bringing joy to the farmers. While ensuring the local demand is met, they are actively supporting the supply to markets in Beijing, Tianjin, and other areas.

    This is a large batch of fresh peaches from Xiaomabang Village being packed and shipped out.
