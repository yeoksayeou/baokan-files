Title: Private and Foreign Joint Venture Emerges in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1989-08-06
Edition: 2

The first private and foreign joint venture enterprise in Shenyang, the Qianjin Steel Pipe Fittings Co., Ltd., recently commenced operations. This company is a joint venture between the private enterprise Shenyang Liaodong Steel Pipe Fittings Factory and the Hong Kong-based Qianjin Enterprise Co., Ltd. The total investment is 8 million yuan, with an annual production capacity of 7,500 tons of steel pipe fittings, generating a production value of 7 million US dollars. All products will be exported.

### 沈阳出现私人与外商合资企业

1989-08-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    沈阳出现私人与外商合资企业
    新华社沈阳8月6日电　（记者张非非）沈阳市第一家由私营企业与外商合资的企业千金钢制弯头有限公司最近开工。这个公司由私营企业沈阳辽东钢制弯头厂和香港千金——企业有限公司合资兴办，总投资800万元，计划年产钢制弯头7500吨，产值700万美元，产品全部外销。
