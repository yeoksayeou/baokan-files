Title: Wu Xueqian Meets with Iranian Heavy Industry Department Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-07-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 31st.

Vice Premier Wu Xueqian met with a delegation from the Iranian Ministry of Heavy Industry led by Deputy Minister Moshen M. Alizadeh this morning in the Zhongnanhai Purple Light Pavilion.

Wu Xueqian, on behalf of the Chinese government, welcomed the delegation's visit. He said that further developing economic, trade, and industrial cooperation between China and Iran is in the common interest of the peoples of both countries.

This afternoon, Vice Premier and Minister of the Ministry of Machine-Building and Electronics Industry Zhou Jiahuai met with Alizadeh and his delegation at the Great Hall of the People. Both sides expressed their willingness to further develop industrial and technological cooperation between China and Iran.

Following the meeting, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Machine-Building and Electronics Industry Zhao Mingsheng and Alizadeh signed a Memorandum of Understanding for the visit of the Iranian Ministry of Heavy Industry delegation to China. According to the memorandum, both sides agreed to take possible steps, based on the principles of equality, mutual benefit, and friendly cooperation, to establish a long-term and stable industrial cooperation relationship. Both sides agreed to cooperate in the production and technology transfer of power station equipment, sugar manufacturing, papermaking, machine tools, and engineering machinery manufacturing.

Zhou Jiahuai attended the signing ceremony.

The delegation arrived in Beijing on July 20 at the invitation of the Ministry of Machine-Building and Electronics Industry. During their stay in China, the delegation visited factories and enterprises in Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou, learned about the development of China's industrial technology, and discussed and implemented cooperation projects in the machinery and electronics industries with relevant departments.

### 吴学谦会见伊朗重工业部代表团

1989-07-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    吴学谦会见伊朗重工业部代表团
    新华社北京7月31日电　国务院副总理吴学谦今天上午在中南海紫光阁会见由副部长莫森·麦·阿里扎德率领的伊朗重工业部代表团。
    吴学谦代表中国政府对代表团的来访表示欢迎。他说，进一步发展中伊两国间的经济、贸易、工业等领域的合作关系，符合两国人民的共同利益。
    今天下午，国务委员兼机械电子工业部部长邹家华在人民大会堂会见了阿里扎德一行。双方表示将进一步发展中伊两国工业、技术合作。
    会见后，机械电子工业部副部长赵明生和阿里扎德签署了伊朗重工业部代表团访问中国谅解备忘录。根据备忘录，双方同意在平等互利、友好求实的原则下，采取可能的步骤，建立长期稳固的工业合作关系。双方同意在电站设备、制糖、造纸、机床、工程机械制造等方面进行合作生产和技术转让。
    邹家华出席了签字仪式。
    代表团是应机械电子工业部的邀请于7月20日抵京的。在华期间，代表团参观访问了北京、上海、广州等地的工厂企业，了解了中国工业技术发展情况，并同有关部门探讨和落实了两国机电工业合作项目。
