Title: Qu Yuanzhi Introduces Inorganic Medium Heat Pipe Conduction Technology
Author: Sun Yi Zhang Boling
Date: 1989-08-13
Page: 2

A piece of cutting-edge research, valued at 1.4 million yuan, was freely donated by its inventor to a domestic unit for production. This world-leading technology in heat conduction—high-efficiency heat pipe conduction technology using inorganic media and components—was developed by Qu Yuanzhi, a 53-year-old researcher at the Tianjin "Jinbao Research Institute," after 10 years of research. It is cost-effective and significantly energy-efficient.

### 渠玉芝推出无机介质热管传导技术
孙仪　张伯岭
1989-08-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    渠玉芝推出无机介质热管传导技术
    本报讯　一项有人出140万元科技转让费还买不到的最新科研成果，被它的发明者无偿奉献给国内一家单位开发生产。这项世界热导尖端——无机介质高效热管传导技术及元件，是由天津市“津宝科研所”53岁的研究员渠玉芝经过10年研制成功的。它成本低廉，节能效果显著。　（孙仪　张伯岭）
