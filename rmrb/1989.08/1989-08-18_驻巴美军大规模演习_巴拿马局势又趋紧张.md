Title: U.S. Military in Panama Conducts Large-Scale Drills; Situation in Panama Tenses Again
Author: Zhū Mǎn Tíng
Date: 1989-08-18
Edition: 3

The U.S. Southern Command at its base in Panama conducted another large-scale military drill yesterday. The exercise involved approximately 12,000 personnel from the Army, Navy, and Air Force of nine U.S. bases along the canal zone, lasting six hours.

The U.S. Southern Command issued a statement saying that this joint exercise was to test the U.S. military's rapid response capability to protect American citizens and the Panama Canal, and it is the most important drill of the year. The U.S. military's drill has made the situation in Panama even more tense.

### 驻巴美军大规模演习  巴拿马局势又趋紧张
朱满庭
1989-08-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    驻巴美军大规模演习  巴拿马局势又趋紧张
    本报加拉加斯8月18日电  记者朱满庭报道：美国驻巴拿马基地的南方部队司令部昨天再度进行大规模军事演习。演习中，运河区沿岸的9个美军基地的陆海空三军各兵种约1.2万人参加，历时6个小时。
    美国南方司令部发表公报说，这次联合演习是为了检验美国部队保卫美国公民和巴拿马运河的快速反应能力，是一年之中最重要的演习。美军这次演习使巴局势更趋紧张。
