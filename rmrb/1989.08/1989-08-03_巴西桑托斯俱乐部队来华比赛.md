Title: Brazilian Santos Club Team to Visit China for Matches
Author: Wāng Dàzhāo
Date: 1989-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 3rd.

The Chinese Football Association today announced the schedule for the Brazilian Santos Club football team's visit to China.

The visiting team will arrive in Beijing on August 4th, play their first match against Tianjin Team on August 6th in Tianjin, face the Chinese Olympic Team on August 9th in Beijing, challenge the Chinese National Team on August 12th in Dalian, take on Liaoning Team on August 15th in Shenyang, play against Shanghai Team on August 18th in Shanghai, meet Hubei Team on August 21st in Wuhan, and conclude their tour with a match against Guangdong Team on August 24th in Guangzhou.

### 巴西桑托斯俱乐部队来华比赛
汪大昭
1989-08-03
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    巴西桑托斯俱乐部队来华比赛
    本报北京8月3日讯　记者汪大昭报道：中国足球协会今天上午在这里公布了巴西桑托斯俱乐部足球队访华比赛的日程。
    客队将于8月4日抵京，8月6日在天津与天津队首战，8月9日在北京对中国奥林匹克队，8月12日在大连对中国国家队，8月15日在沈阳对辽宁队，8月18日在上海对上海队，8月21日在武汉对湖北队，8月24日在广州对广东队。
