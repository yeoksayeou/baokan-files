Title: Thai and Lao Military Leaders Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-08-03
Edition: 3

The acting supreme commander of the Thai armed forces and army commander-in-chief Chavalit Yongchaiyudh held talks with the visiting chief of the general staff of the Lao People's Army, Sisavath Keobounphanh, on August 2 at the Thai Army Conference Hall. The two sides discussed issues related to further developing cooperation between the two countries.

### 泰老军方领导人会谈

1989-08-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    泰老军方领导人会谈
    泰国武装部队代理最高司令、陆军总司令差瓦立·永猜裕同正在曼谷访问的老挝人民军总参谋长西沙瓦·乔本潘2日在泰国陆军会议厅举行会谈，双方讨论了进一步发展两国合作关系问题。
