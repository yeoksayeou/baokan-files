Title: U.S. Crime Rate Rises in 1988
Author:
Date: 1989-08-07
Edition: 3

The Federal Bureau of Investigation in the United States released a report on August 6 stating that in 1988, the United States recorded 1.56 million violent crimes, an increase of 5.5% compared to the previous year, setting a new historical high. The report indicated that among these 1.56 million violent crimes, there were 20,675 homicides and 92,486 rapes. Compared to 1987, these figures increased by 2.9% and 1.5%, respectively.

### 1988年美国犯罪率上升

1989-08-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    1988年美国犯罪率上升
    美国联邦调查局6日公布的一份报告说，美国1988年共发生156万起暴力案件，比去年增加了5.5％，创历史最高纪录。报告说，在这156万起暴力案中，凶杀案占20675起，强奸案占92486起，与1987年相比，分别增加了2.9％和1.5％。 
          （据新华社）
