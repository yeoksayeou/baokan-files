Title: 31 Teams Compete in Guilin for the Mr. Olympia Cup
Author: Fan Guozhong, Heng Zhi Cheng
Date: 1989-08-10
Edition: 4

The seventh Mr. Olympia Cup National Bodybuilding Championship concluded in Guilin on August 9. The China Coal Industry Sports Association team won the team overall championship and took home the Mr. Olympia Cup.

The teams that finished in second to sixth place in the overall standings were: Shanghai Dalmei Team, Qingdao Team, Yunnan Team, Shandong Beiji Star Watch Group Team, and Jiangsu Team.

A total of 31 bodybuilding teams from across the country, with 243 athletes, participated in this competition.

The women's champions were: 52 kg class, Zhang Ping from Shanghai; 57 kg class, Chen Shaohua from Zhejiang; 57 kg and above class, Zhao Qiaoling from Dalian.

The men's champions were: 60 kg class, Qu Zhijin from Qingdao Team; 65 kg class, Shi Wenlin from Yunnan Team; 70 kg class, Wang Lijin from Coal Industry Sports Association Team; 75 kg class, Zhang Huiming from Coal Industry Sports Association Team; 80 kg class, Yang Xinmin from Shandong Team; 85 kg class, Yi Kangming from Anhui Team; 90 kg class, He Yushan from Yunnan Team; 95 kg class, Huang Bo from Shandong Team.

The mixed pair champions were Zhang Ping and Zhou Hongsheng from Shanghai Team.

In the group posing and women's pair performance, the China Coal Industry Sports Association Team and Zhejiang Team's Chen Shaohua and Tong Jianhua won first place, respectively.

### 三十一支健美队在桂林角逐  中国煤矿体协队捧走力士杯
范国忠  衡志诚
1989-08-10
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    三十一支健美队在桂林角逐
    中国煤矿体协队捧走力士杯
    新华社南宁8月10日电　（通讯员范国忠、记者衡志诚）中国煤矿体协队荣获第七届力士杯全国健美大赛团体总分冠军，捧走了力士杯。
    获得团体总分2至6名的队依次是：上海达尔美队、青岛队、云南队、山东北极星钟表集团队、江苏队。
    本次大赛于9日在桂林闭幕，有国内31支健美代表队的243名选手参赛。
    获得女子冠军的选手分别是：52公斤级，上海张萍；57公斤级，浙江陈少华；57公斤以上级，大连赵巧玲。
    获得男子冠军的分别是，60公斤级，青岛队曲智进；65公斤级，云南队石文林；70公斤级，煤矿体协队王力劲；75公斤级，煤矿体协队张惠明；80公斤级，山东队杨新民；85公斤级，安徽队易康明；90公斤级，云南队何玉珊；95公斤级，山东队黄波。
    获得混合双人冠军的是上海队张萍、周洪生。
    在集体造型和女子双人表演赛中，中国煤矿体协队和浙江队陈少华、童建华分别获得第一名。
