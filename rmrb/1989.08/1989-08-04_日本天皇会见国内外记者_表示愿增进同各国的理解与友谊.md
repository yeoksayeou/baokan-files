Title: Japanese Emperor Meets Domestic and Foreign Journalists, Expresses Desire to Enhance Understanding and Friendship with Other Countries
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-08-04
Page: 3

People's Daily, Tokyo, August 4th. Correspondent Yu Qing reports: This afternoon, Japanese Emperor Akihito and Empress Michiko met with domestic and foreign journalists for the first time at the Imperial Palace, answering questions on domestic issues and international relations.

When asked about the significance of international friendship and a visit to China, Emperor Akihito said, "In today's world, all countries can only benefit humanity by being members of the international community. I am willing to enhance understanding and friendship with the people of all countries. Regarding the visit to China, it is a matter decided by the government. If there is an opportunity to visit, I will make a meaningful visit to promote understanding and friendship."

When asked about Emperor Showa's war responsibility, the imperial system, and freedom of speech, Emperor Akihito did not directly address the issue of war responsibility. He said, "In that past war, many people at home and abroad lost their lives or suffered hardships, which I deeply regret. Japan has taken the path of peace under the new constitution and hopes for world peace to come soon. Protecting freedom of speech is the foundation of democracy and is very important. Freedom of speech also includes discussing the emperor's war responsibility and the imperial system."

### 日本天皇会见国内外记者  表示愿增进同各国的理解与友谊
于青
1989-08-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日本天皇会见国内外记者
    表示愿增进同各国的理解与友谊
    本报东京8月4日电　记者于青报道：今天下午，日本明仁天皇和皇后美智子在皇宫首次会见国内外记者，就国内问题和国际关系问题回答了记者的提问。
    明仁天皇在回答国际友好的意义和访问中国的问题时说，现在世界上的所有国家，只有作为国际社会的一员，才能造福人类。我愿意增进同各国人们的理解和友谊。关于访问中国的问题，是由政府来决定的。如果有访问的机会，我愿为增进理解和友好的发展进行有意义的访问。
    在被问及昭和天皇的战争责任、天皇制和言论自由的问题时，明仁天皇没有直接回答战争责任问题。他说，在过去那场战争中，国内外许多人丧生或遭受苦难，对此实感痛心。日本在新宪法下，走上了和平的道路，希望世界和平早日到来。保障言论自由是民主主义的基础，十分重要。言论自由也包括谈论天皇战争责任和天皇制问题。
