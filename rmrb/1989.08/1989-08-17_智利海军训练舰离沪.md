Title: Chilean Navy Training Ship Leaves Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1989-08-17
Edition: 2

The Chilean Navy training ship "Esmeralda" concluded its six-day training visit to Shanghai and departed on the 15th. Rear Admiral Wu Shengli, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Shanghai Naval Base, and others went to the Yangtze River Naval Wharf to see it off.

### 智利海军训练舰离沪

1989-08-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    智利海军训练舰离沪
    据新华社北京电　智利海军“埃斯梅拉达”号训练舰结束了对上海的6天训练访问，于15日离开上海。海军上海基地副参谋长吴胜利大校等前往扬子江海军码头送行。
