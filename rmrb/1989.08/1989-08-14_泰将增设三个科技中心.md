Title: Thailand to Establish Three New Technology Centers
Author:
Date: 1989-08-14
Edition: 3

Thailand plans to establish three new technology centers to promote scientific research and economic development in the country.

According to officials from Thailand's Ministry of Science, Technology, and Energy, the funds needed to build these three national technology centers will be allocated by the government. The three centers will focus on marine science, information technology, and space technology, respectively.

To better serve the new technology centers, Thailand will send 800 scientific workers to the United States for further studies.

### 泰将增设三个科技中心

1989-08-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    泰将增设三个科技中心
    新华社曼谷8月12日电　泰国政府计划再建立3个科技中心，以促进泰国的科学研究和经济发展。
    据泰国科技和能源部官员透露，建立这3个全国性科技中心所需经费将由政府拨款。3个科技中心将分别致力于研究海洋科学、信息技术和空间技术。
    为了更好地为新科技中心服务，泰国将派遣800名科学工作者去美国进修。
