Title: The Chinese Association Research Society Established
Author: Jia Zhaoguan
Date: 1989-08-28
Edition: 2

	The Chinese Association Research Society Established

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 28th: Reporter Jia Zhaoguan reports: There are more than 1,000 nationally recognized associations and over 100,000 local associations in our country. This was announced by Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Honorary President of the Chinese Association Research Society, Lei Jieqiong, at the founding meeting of the Chinese Association Research Society today.

	The Chinese Association Research Society, which aims to promote the healthy development of associations in our country and to advance the construction of socialist spiritual and material civilization, has the following tasks: to conduct academic research on associations; to organize the exchange of experiences in association activities; to connect with compatriots from Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and overseas, as well as with friendly organizations and individuals abroad, to develop international friendly exchanges of the same nature; and to undertake other tasks entrusted by the Party organization and the government.

	At the founding meeting of the society, Lei Jieqiong, Vice Chairman, renowned jurist Zhang Youyu, and Vice Minister of Civil Affairs Fan Baojun were appointed as honorary presidents. Shi Huibai, an advisor to the Ministry of Civil Affairs, was elected as the president.

### 中国社团研究会成立
贾昭全
1989-08-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    中国社团研究会成立
    本报北京8月28日讯　记者贾昭全报道：我国现有业经确认的全国性社团1000多个，地方性社团10万多个。这是全国人大常委会副委员长、中国社团研究会名誉会长雷洁琼今天在中国社团研究会成立大会上讲话时宣布的。
    旨在推动我国社团健康发展、促进社会主义两个文明建设的中国社团研究会，其任务是，开展关于社团的学术研究；组织交流社团活动经验；联系港、澳、台、侨胞和国外友好团体、人士，发展国际相同性质的民间友好交往；承办党组织和政府委托的其他任务。
    在研究会成立大会上，雷洁琼副委员长、著名法学家张友渔、民政部副部长范宝俊担任研究会名誉会长。民政部顾问史怀璧当选为会长。
