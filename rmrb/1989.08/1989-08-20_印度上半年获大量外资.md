Title: India Receives Large Amount of Foreign Investment in First Half of the Year
Author:
Date: 1989-08-20
Edition: 3

India's official figures released on the 17th indicate that India received a total of $124 million in foreign direct investment in the first half of this year.

In the first half of this year, India approved a total of 335 cooperative projects, including 102 projects related to finance. India's main investors come from the Federal Republic of Germany, the United States, and Japan.

	(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 印度上半年获大量外资

1989-08-20
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

    印度上半年获大量外资
    印度官方17日公布的数字表明，今年上半年印度共获得1.24亿美元的外国直接投资。
    今年上半年印度共批准了335个合作项目，其中包括102个与金融有关的项目。印度的主要投资者来自联邦德国、美国和日本。
            （据新华社）
