Title: Someone in Xinyu is Selling Harmful Techniques
Author: Dong Zihai
Date: 1989-08-05
Page: 8

    Take a look at the technical information offered by a private information service provider in Xinyu City, Jiangxi:
    1. The secret principle and formula for a master key, send 20 yuan for the materials fee, and you can obtain it.
    2. "One Hundred and Eight Secret Techniques for Harming Others in Secret."
    3. "The Technique to Make Someone Bedridden."
    4. "The Technique to Make Someone Go Blind."
    5. "The Technique to Make Someone Mute."
    6. "The Technique to Make Someone Deaf."
    7. "The Technique to Make Someone Stupid."
    8. "The Technique to Make Someone's Five Viscera Shatter."
    9. "The Technique to Make Someone's Seven Orifices Bleed."

    All of the above techniques (2–9) are 10 yuan each.

    How can such harmful techniques be considered technical information and spread in society?

    (July 25, *Legal Daily*)

### 新余有人出卖害人术
董自海
1989-08-05
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

    新余有人出卖害人术
    请看江西新余市某个体信息专业户办的技术信息：
    一、万能钥匙的原理和配制绝密技术，寄二十元资料费，可得到。
    二、《暗中害人一百零八种绝招》。
    三、《使他卧床不起法》。
    四、《使他双目失明法》。
    五、《使他哑吧法》。
    六、《使他耳聋法》。
    七、《使他愚蠢法》。
    八、《使他五脏俱裂法》。
    九、《使他七窍流血法》。
    以上二—九种技术均十元一份。
    这样的害人术怎么能算技术信息，向社会传播呢？
      （七月二十五日《法制日报》  董自海文）
