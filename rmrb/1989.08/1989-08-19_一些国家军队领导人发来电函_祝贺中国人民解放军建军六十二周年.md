Title: Military Leaders from Several Countries Send Congratulatory Messages on the 62nd Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1989-08-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 19th.

Recently, military leaders from several countries sent telegrams and letters to General Qin Jiwei, Minister of National Defense, General Zhang Zhen, Chief of the General Staff, and General Yang Baibing, Director of the General Political Department, to congratulate the Chinese People's Liberation Army on its 62nd anniversary.

The following sent congratulatory telegrams and letters:

* General Chawalit Yongchaiyudh, Acting Supreme Commander of the Thai Armed Forces;
* General Mirza Aslam Beg, Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff Committee, Pakistan;
* General Hussain Muhammad Ershad, President and Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces, Bangladesh (retired);
* General Atiqur Rahman, Chief of Army Staff, Bangladesh;
* Rear Admiral Sultan Ahmed, Chief of Naval Staff, Bangladesh;
* Colonel Saleh Obeid Ahmed, Minister of Defense, Democratic Yemen;
* Colonel Haseem Kassem Tah, First Deputy Minister of Defense and Chief of Staff, Democratic Yemen;
* Major Hussein Ali Hassan, Director of the Political Department of the Armed Forces, Democratic Yemen;
* General Saw Maung, Minister of Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, Burma;
* General Abdel Halim Abu Ghazala, Minister of Defense, Egypt;
* General Lutfi Lubbad, Chief of the General Staff, Egyptian Armed Forces;
* Marshal O Jin U, Minister of the People's Armed Forces, Democratic People's Republic of Korea;
* General Choi Kwang, Chief of the General Staff, Korean People's Army;
* Marshal Dmitry Yazov, Minister of Defense, Soviet Union;
* General Veljko Kadijević, Minister of National Defense, Yugoslavia;
* General T. Hapunda, Minister of Defense, Zambia;
* General Sachchhat Shrestha, Chief of the Army Staff, Royal Nepalese Army;
* General Ion Coman, Chief of the General Staff, Socialist Republic of Romania;
* General Ion Coman, Secretary of the Supreme Political Council of the Romanian Armed Forces;
* Admiral R.H. Tahiliani, Chairman of the Chiefs of Staff Committee, Indian Armed Forces;
* General Son Sen, Commander-in-Chief of the National Army of Democratic Kampuchea;
* General József Paczinkó, Chief of the General Staff, Hungarian People's Army;
* General Lajos Keresztes, Director of the Political Department, Hungarian People's Army;
* General Fritz Streletz, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of the General Staff, German Democratic Republic;
* General Bruno Behrend, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Director of the Political Department, German Democratic Republic;
* General Heinz Kessler, Minister of National Defense, German Democratic Republic;
* General Milan Vácslavik, Minister of National Defense, Czechoslovakia.
