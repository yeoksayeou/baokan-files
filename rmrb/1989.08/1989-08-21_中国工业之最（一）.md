Title: China's Industrial Leaders (Part 1)
Author:
Date: 1989-08-21
Edition: 5

In the 40 years since the founding of the People's Republic of China, the country has undertaken massive economic construction. A large number of major construction projects have been completed and put into operation, enhancing the nation's economic strength. Do you want to know about the largest and most advanced enterprises, and the largest and most advanced projects? This newspaper will introduce China's industrial leaders in eight sectors to our readers. — Editor

Coal Industry

	• Largest mining area: Datong Coal Mining Bureau in Shanxi Province. Ongoing expansion. The mining area has coal reserves of 37.5 billion tons and a production capacity of 23.5 million tons per year.
	• Largest coal mines: Xinglongzhuang Mine in Yanzhou Mining Area, Panji No. 1 Mine in Huainan Mining Area, Xiqu Mine in Gujiao Mining Area, and Baodian Mine in Yanzhou Mining Area. They were completed and put into operation in December 1981, December 1983, December 1984, and June 1986, respectively. Each has a production capacity of 3 million tons per year.
	• Largest open-pit coal mine: Antaibao Open-Pit Mine in Pinglu Area. Completed and put into operation in September 1987. Production capacity of 15 million tons per year.
	• Largest coal washing plants: Xinglongzhuang Mine Coal Washing Plant in Yanzhou Mining Area, Yangquan No. 1 Mine Coal Washing Plant in Yangquan Mining Area, Panji No. 1 Mine Coal Washing Plant in Huainan Mining Area, and Xiqu Coal Washing Plant in Gujiao Mining Area. They were completed and put into operation in December 1984, December 1986, August 1986, and November 1987, respectively. Each has a washing capacity of 3 million tons per year.

### 中国工业之最（一）

1989-08-21
第5版(国内专页（经济）)
专栏：

    中国工业之最（一）
    中华人民共和国诞生40年来，进行了巨大规模的经济建设。大批重大建设工程竣工投产，增强了国家经济实力。你想知道最大、最新的企业，最大、最新的工程吗？本报将分八个行业向读者介绍中国工业之最。　——编者
    煤炭工业
    △最大的矿区：山西大同矿务局。继续扩建。矿区煤炭保有储量375亿吨，产煤能力2350万吨／年。
    △最大的煤井：兖州矿区兴隆庄矿井、淮南矿区潘集一号矿井、古交矿区西曲矿井、兖州矿区鲍店矿井。它们分别于1981年12月、1983年12月、1984年12月、1986年6月建成投产。产煤能力各300万吨／年。
    △最大的露天煤矿：平朔矿区安太堡露天矿。1987年9月建成投产。产煤能力1500万吨／年。
    △最大的洗煤厂：兖州矿区兴隆庄矿井洗煤厂、阳泉矿区阳泉一矿洗煤厂、淮南矿区潘集一号矿井洗煤厂、古交矿区西曲洗煤厂。它们分别于1984年12月、1986年12月、1986年8月、1987年11月建成投产。洗煤能力各300万吨／年。
