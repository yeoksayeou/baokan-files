Title: On the Theory of Lifestyle
Author: Wáng Yùbō, Wáng Yǎlín, Wáng Ruìshēng
Date: 1989-08-06
Page: 6

The issue of lifestyle has gradually gained attention from China's theoretical circles in recent years, sparking heated discussions in various publications. The entire society is highly concerned about this topic. This book employs a methodology that combines historical materialism and sociology to study lifestyle, addressing how humans survive, reproduce, and develop through various lifestyles. It explores the concept of lifestyle; uses comparative analysis to examine the formation, development, and similarities and differences of lifestyles across different historical social forms. The book also discusses the impact of technological revolutions and social reforms on lifestyle changes and their interrelationships.

In recent years, significant changes have occurred in China's political life, economic life, and cultural life. Therefore, a systematic study of lifestyle issues holds important theoretical and practical significance.

### 《生活方式论》
王玉波  王雅林　王锐生
1989-08-06
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：

   《生活方式论》
    王玉波  王雅林　王锐生著
    上海人民出版社出版
    生活方式问题，近年来逐渐受到了我国理论界的重视，在一些报刊上展开了热烈的讨论，整个社会都十分关注。本书运用历史唯物主义和社会学相结合的方法研究生活方式，回答了人是以怎样的生活方式生存繁衍和发展的，探索了生活方式范畴；运用比较法分析了有史以来各种不同社会形态的生活方式形成、发展规律及相似处和本质区别，探讨了技术革命与社会改革对生活方式变革的影响及相互关系等。近几年来，我国政治生活、经济生活、文化生活发生了很大变化，系统研究生活方式问题具有重要的理论意义和实践意义。
