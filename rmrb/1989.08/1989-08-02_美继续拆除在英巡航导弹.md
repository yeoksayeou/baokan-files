Title: The United States Continues to Dismantle Cruise Missiles in the UK
Author:
Date: 1989-08-02
Edition: 3

The United States began dismantling cruise missiles deployed at the Royal Air Force base in Greenham Common, England, on August 1. The dismantling of the cruise missiles is being carried out in accordance with the Intermediate-Range Nuclear Forces (INF) Treaty between the United States and the Soviet Union. Of the 96 missiles deployed at the base, 16 were transported to the United States on August 1. The entire dismantling process will be completed within two years.

### 美继续拆除在英巡航导弹

1989-08-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美继续拆除在英巡航导弹
    美国1日开始拆除部署在英国格林汉康芒空军基地的巡航导弹。拆除巡航导弹是按照美苏中导条约的规定进行的。部署在该基地的96枚导弹中，16枚1日运往美国。全部拆除工作将在两年之内完成。
            （据新华社）
