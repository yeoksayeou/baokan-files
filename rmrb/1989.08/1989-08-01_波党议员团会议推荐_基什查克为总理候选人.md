Title: Polish United Workers' Party Deputies Meeting Recommends Kiszczak as Prime Minister Candidate
Author:
Date: 1989-08-01
Edition: 3

The newly elected First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party, Rakowski, recommended Central Committee Politburo member and Interior Minister Kiszczak as the candidate for Prime Minister.

Rakowski made this recommendation at the July 31 meeting of the Polish United Workers' Party deputies on behalf of the Party Central Committee. After being elected as the Party's First Secretary on July 28, he resigned from his position as Prime Minister.

General Kiszczak was born in October 1925. He joined the Party in 1945. During the Hitler occupation, he participated in the resistance movement. In 1945, he joined the Polish People's Army and served as the Deputy Chief of the General Staff. Since 1981, he has been the Interior Minister.

### 波党议员团会议推荐  基什查克为总理候选人

1989-08-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    波党议员团会议推荐
    基什查克为总理候选人
    新华社华沙7月31日电  新当选的波兰统一工人党中央第一书记拉科夫斯基推荐中央政治局委员、政府内务部长基什查克为政府总理候选人。
    拉科夫斯基是在31日召开的波兰统一工人党议员团会议上代表党中央提出这一人选的。他本人7月28日当选党中央第一书记后，辞去了总理职务。
    基什查克上将生于1925年10月，1945年入党。希特勒占领期间参加过抵抗运动，1945年加入波兰人民军，曾任波军副总参谋长。从1981年起，任内务部长。
