Title: Zhang Jingfu Meets with American Agricultural Experts
Author:
Date: 1989-08-23
Edition: 2

Zhang Jingfu, Chairman of the China International Talent Exchange Foundation, met and hosted a dinner for a delegation of American agricultural experts led by Jerome Haylington, Chairman of the U.S. International Basic Economic Cooperation Research Organization, on August 22 in Beijing.

After the meeting, Haylington, on behalf of the U.S. International Basic Economic Cooperation Research Organization, signed a letter of intent with the China International Talent Exchange Foundation.

### 张劲夫会见美国农业专家团

1989-08-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    张劲夫会见美国农业专家团
    本报讯　中国国际人才交流协会主席张劲夫22日在北京会见并宴请了由美国国际基础经济合作研究组织主席杰罗姆·海灵顿先生率领的农业专家团。
    会见结束后，海灵顿先生代表美国国际基础经济合作研究组织，与中国国际人才交流协会签署了合作意向书。
