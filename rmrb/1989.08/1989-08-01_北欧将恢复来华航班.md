Title: Scandinavian Airlines to Resume Flights to China
Author:
Date: 1989-08-01
Edition: 3

    Scandinavian Airlines to Resume Flights to China
    Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, July 31

    According to a report by the Swedish newspaper *Dagens Nyheter* today, Scandinavian Airlines has decided to resume flights to China starting from August 22.

    The report states that Scandinavian Airlines and several other airlines from Nordic countries are also preparing to resume flights to China in August and September. The Danish airline C. A. O. has indicated that it will resume its first flight to China on October 22.

    Scandinavian Airlines and airlines from Nordic countries had canceled their flights to China during the turmoil in Beijing.

### 北欧将恢复来华航班

1989-08-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    北欧将恢复来华航班
    新华社斯德哥尔摩7月31日电　据瑞典《每日新闻报》今天报道，斯堪的纳维亚航空公司决定从8月22日起恢复飞往中国的航班。
    据报道，斯堪的纳维亚和北欧的其他几家航空公司也准备在8、9月份恢复飞往中国的航班。丹麦切勒堡航空公司则表示等到10月22日再恢复飞往中国的首次班机。
    斯堪的纳维亚和北欧国家的航空公司是在北京发生暴乱期间取消飞往中国的航班的。
