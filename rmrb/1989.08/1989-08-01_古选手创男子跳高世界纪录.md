Title: Cuban Athlete Sets New Men's High Jump World Record
Author:
Date: 1989-08-01
Edition: 4

Cuban athlete Javier Sotomayor set a new men's high jump world record on July 29 in the Caribbean Athletics Championships held in San Juan. He cleared a height of 2.44 meters.

The 21-year-old, 1.93-meter-tall Sotomayor had previously set a world record of 2.43 meters on September 8, 1988, in Salamanca, Spain.

The world's number one high jumper, Javier Sotomayor.

### 古选手创男子跳高世界纪录

1989-08-01
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    古选手创男子跳高世界纪录
    新华社北京7月31日电　据外电报道，古巴选手哈维尔·索托马约尔29日在圣胡安举行的加勒比田径锦标赛上，跃过2米44的横竿，创造了新的男子跳高世界纪录。
    现年21岁、身高1米93的索托马约尔去年9月8日曾在西班牙的萨拉曼卡创造了2米43的世界纪录。
