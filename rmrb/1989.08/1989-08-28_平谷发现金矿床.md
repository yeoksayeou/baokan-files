Title: Gold Deposit Discovered in Pinggu
Author: Ji Huichang
Date: 1989-08-28
Page: 2

From the Four Corners

A medium-sized gold deposit has been discovered at the site of the old Wan Zhuang gold mine in Pinggu County, Beijing, which had been shut down and closed for ten years. This discovery has given new life to the old mine. Recently, a gold ore processing plant with a daily capacity of fifty tons has been completed and put into trial production.

### 平谷发现金矿床
纪怀昌
1989-08-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    平谷发现金矿床
    本报讯　北京市平谷县在停产闭坑十年之久的万庄金矿旧址，发现一个中型金矿床，从而使这座老矿获得新生。最近，一座日处理五十吨金矿床的选矿厂建成试产。　（纪怀昌）
