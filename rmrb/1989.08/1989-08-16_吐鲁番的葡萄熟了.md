Title: The Grapes of Turpan Are Ripe
Author: Hādī'ěr Mǎmùtí
Date: 1989-08-16
Edition: 2

The grapes in Turpan, Xinjiang, are ripe again this year. According to Xinhua News Agency, the total production of grapes in Turpan is expected to reach over 110,000 tons, an increase of over 20,000 tons compared to last year. This marks the 10th consecutive year of bumper harvests.

Currently, Uighur farmers are busy harvesting grapes, either transporting fresh grapes to the market or sending them to drying rooms to make raisins.

Compared to 1979, the grape cultivation area in Turpan has expanded from 50,000 mu to 184,200 mu, and the total production has increased from 23,800 tons to over 110,000 tons. The yield per mu has also risen from 545 kilograms to 1,063 kilograms.

### 吐鲁番的葡萄熟了
哈迪尔·马木提
1989-08-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    吐鲁番的葡萄熟了
    据新华社乌鲁木齐8月16日电（记者哈迪尔·马木提）新疆吐鲁番葡萄今年又获大丰收，总产量预计可达11万多吨，比去年增产2万多吨，是连续第10个丰收年。
    眼下，维吾尔族果农们正在忙着采摘葡萄，或是把鲜葡萄拉运上市，或是送到荫房晾制成葡萄干。
    与1979年相比，吐鲁番今年葡萄面积由5万亩扩大到18.42万亩，总产量由2.38万吨增加到11万多吨，单产由545公斤提高到1063公斤。
