Title: The First Western China Commodities Fair Concludes
Author: Liu Kenjia, Qi Baodong
Date: 1989-08-25
Edition: 2

The first Western China Commodities Fair, which lasted for five days, concluded yesterday in Tianwater, Gansu. Representatives from 32 cities, prefectures, and districts, as well as 12 ministries and commissions including commerce, supply and marketing, materials, and light industry, and large enterprises from 15 provinces and autonomous regions including Shaanxi, Gansu, Sichuan, Ningxia, Qinghai, Xinjiang, Yunnan, and Anhui, participated in this grand event. A total of 1.3 million representatives from over a thousand businesses and enterprises attended the fair.

### 首届西部商品交易会结束
刘肯嘉　漆保东
1989-08-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    首届西部商品交易会结束
    本报甘肃天水8月25日电　为时5天的首届中国西部商品交易会，昨天在这里结束。来自陕、甘、川、宁、青、新、云、皖等15个省区的32个地、州、市和商业、供销、物资、轻工等12个厅、局及大型企业的46个商贸代表团及千余家商业、企业的1.3万多名代表，参加了这次盛会。（刘肯嘉　漆保东）
