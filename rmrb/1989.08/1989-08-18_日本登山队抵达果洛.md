Title: Japanese Mountaineering Team Arrives in Guoluo
Author: Chén Zhìqiáng
Date: 1989-08-18
Edition: 4

Japanese Mountaineering Team Arrives in Guoluo

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, August 16th - (Reporter Chen Zhìqiáng) The Japanese Animaqing Mountaineering Team arrived today in the snowy plateau of the Guoluo Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture in Qinghai Province, known as the "Holy Mountain and Holy Water." They have begun their acclimatization training. The Japanese team's mission this time is to climb the famous holy mountain, the Nyainqêntanglha Feng, in Guoluo.

### 日本登山队抵达果洛
陈志强
1989-08-18
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    日本登山队抵达果洛
    新华社西宁八月十六日电　（记者陈志强）日本阿尼玛卿登山队今天抵达“神山神水”的雪域高原青海省果洛藏族自治州，开始适应性训练。日本登山队此行将攀登果洛著名神山年保叶什贡峰。
