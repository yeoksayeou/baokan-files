Title: "The Mother of the Soldiers" Rong Guanxiu Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-08-13
Edition: 2

"The Mother of the Soldiers" Rong Guanxiu passed away today at 7:20 AM in Shijiazhuang due to illness. She was 93 years old.

Rong Guanxiu was from Xiapansong Village, Ping Mountain County, Hebei Province. She joined the Communist Party of China in 1938. She actively participated in the anti-Japanese war support work, risking her life to protect the soldiers and rescue the wounded. On February 19, 1944, the Jin-Cha-Ji Border Region Government and the Jin-Cha-Ji Military Region conferred upon her the honorable title of "The Mother of the Soldiers." She participated in the First National Political Consultative Conference as a representative of the North China Region and attended the "Founding Ceremony." Rong Guanxiu was elected as a representative to the First through Fifth National People's Congresses, a representative to the Third and Fourth National Women's Congresses, and an executive committee member of the Fourth National Women's Congress. She was also awarded the title of National "March 8th Red Banner Pacesetter."

During her illness, leaders from Hebei Province, Shijiazhuang Region, Ping Mountain County, and relevant departments, as well as leaders from the Hebei Military Region and Shijiazhuang Military Sub-district, visited her at the Shijiazhuang Peace Hospital to show their respect for this patriotic and military-supporting model.

### “子弟兵的母亲”戎冠秀逝世

1989-08-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    “子弟兵的母亲”戎冠秀逝世
    新华社石家庄8月12日电　“子弟兵的母亲”戎冠秀同志，因病医治无效，于今天上午7时20分在石家庄逝世，享年93岁。
    戎冠秀同志是河北省平山县下盘松村人，1938年加入中国共产党。她积极参加抗日支前工作，冒着生命危险掩护子弟兵、救护伤病员。1944年2月19日，晋察冀边区政府、晋察冀军区授予她“子弟兵的母亲”的光荣称号。她做为华北区人民代表参加了全国第一次政治协商会议，出席了“开国大典”。戎冠秀先后当选为第一至第五届全国人大代表，全国妇联第三、四届代表大会代表，全国妇联第四届代表大会执委会委员。她还曾被评为全国“三八”红旗手标兵。
    戎冠秀病重期间，河北省、石家庄地区、平山县主要领导、有关部门的负责同志及河北省军区、石家庄军分区等领导同志先后到石家庄和平医院看望这位爱国拥军模范。
