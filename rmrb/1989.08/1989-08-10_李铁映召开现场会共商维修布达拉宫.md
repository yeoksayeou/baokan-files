Title: Li Tieying Holds On-Site Meeting to Discuss the Repair of the Potala Palace
Author: Liu Wei, Zhang Chunbao, Chai Huaji
Date: 1989-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, August 9th - Xinhua News Agency reporters Liu Wei, Zhang Chunbao, and Chai Huaji report: Li Tieying, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and State Councilor, held an on-site office meeting in Lhasa today with relevant departments of the Autonomous Region Government to discuss the repair of the Potala Palace.

After listening to the opinions and suggestions from various parties, Li Tieying said, the Potala Palace is a world-renowned palace-style temple architectural complex, a treasure of the historical and cultural heritage of the Chinese nation, and a sacred place of Tibetan Buddhism. Repairing the Potala Palace well is of great significance for implementing the Party's policies on united front, ethnic, and religious work, for protecting the country's historical relics, promoting national culture, and developing the tourism industry. The Central Committee of the Party and the State Council attach great importance to this matter. This task must be done well and cannot be done poorly.

### 李铁映召开现场会共商维修布达拉宫
刘伟  张春宝  柴怀吉
1989-08-10
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    李铁映召开现场会共商维修布达拉宫
    本报拉萨8月9日电  本报记者刘伟、新华社记者张春宝、柴怀吉报道：正在西藏考察的中共中央政治局委员、国务委员李铁映，今天在拉萨同自治区人民政府有关部门联合召开现场办公会，共商维修布达拉宫事宜。
    李铁映听取了各方面人士的意见和建议后说，布达拉宫是举世闻名的宫殿式寺庙古建筑群，是中华民族历史文化遗产中的瑰宝，也是藏传佛教的圣地。维修好布达拉宫，对于贯彻执行党的统战、民族、宗教政策，对于保护祖国历史文物、弘扬祖国文化，发展旅游事业等，都有重要意义。党中央、国务院对此非常重视，这件事只能办好，决不能办坏。
