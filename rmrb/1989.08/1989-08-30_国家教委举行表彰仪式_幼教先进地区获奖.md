Title: Ministry of Education Holds Award Ceremony
Author: Wáng Nàméi
Date: 1989-08-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 30th.

Today morning, the Ministry of Education held a commendation ceremony for the national advanced counties, cities, and districts in early childhood education. A total of 85 counties, cities, and districts were awarded the title of National Advanced County (City, District) in Early Childhood Education. Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Chen Mu Hua and Lei Jieqiong attended the ceremony.

Since the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, China's early childhood education has developed steadily and healthily under the leadership of party committees and governments at all levels. According to statistics from 1988, there were 171,000 kindergartens of various types nationwide, with 18.545 million children in attendance, an increase of 135% compared to 1978. The 85 counties, cities, and districts that were commended this time are the advanced examples that have emerged in the development and reform of early childhood education over the past decade.

### 国家教委举行表彰仪式  幼教先进地区获奖
王娜梅
1989-08-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国家教委举行表彰仪式
    幼教先进地区获奖
    本报北京8月30日讯　记者王娜梅报道：今天上午国家教委举行了全国幼儿教育先进县、市、区表彰仪式，85个县、市、区，光荣地获得全国幼儿教育先进县（市区）称号。全国人大常委会副委员长陈慕华、雷洁琼参加了表彰仪式。
    党的十一届三中全会以来，我国幼儿教育事业在各级党委和政府领导下，得到稳步、健康发展。据1988年统计，全国有各类幼儿园17.1万所，在园幼儿1854.5万人，比1978年增长了135％。这次表彰的85个县、市、区，就是近十年来幼儿教育发展、改革中涌现出来的先进典型。
