Title: Soviet Male Athlete Breaks World Record in Finswimming
Author:
Date: 1989-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 12th.

According to a report by TASS, at the 13th European Finswimming Championships held in Moscow, 22-year-old Soviet athlete Yakovlev set a new world record in the 400-meter finswimming event with a time of 2 minutes 57.27 seconds.

The previous record was 2 minutes 57.76 seconds, set by Soviet athlete Semenov in February of this year.

The championships began on August 10th at the Moscow Olympic Water Sports Center and featured 126 athletes from 16 countries. All events will conclude on August 13th.

### 苏男选手破蹼泳世界纪录

1989-08-13
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    苏男选手破蹼泳世界纪录
    新华社莫斯科8月12日电　据塔斯社报道，在此间举行的第十三届欧洲速度蹼泳锦标赛上，22岁的苏联运动员雅科夫列夫以2分57秒27的成绩刷新了400米蹼泳世界纪录。
    原纪录为2分57秒76，是苏联运动员谢苗诺夫今年2月创造的。
    这届锦标赛是10日开始在莫斯科奥林匹克水上运动中心举行的，来自16个国家的126名运动员参加了这一比赛。全部比赛将于13日结束。
