Title: Lhasa Holds First Tibetan Calligraphy Competition and Exhibition
Author: Duoji Zhandui
Date: 1989-08-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, August 6th

The first Tibetan Calligraphy Competition and Exhibition of the Tibet Autonomous Region opened this afternoon in Lhasa. Vice Chairman Apho Angjang was present to cut the ribbon and inscribed a calligraphy piece for the exhibition.

Five hundred and fifty-eight authors from Tibet, Qinghai, Sichuan, Gansu, Beijing, and Zhengzhou participated in this national Tibetan calligraphy competition. The participants included calligraphy enthusiasts and renowned calligraphers from various ethnic groups such as Tibetan, Han, Hui, Manchu, Tujia, and Mongolian.

The works were categorized into six types of Tibetan calligraphy styles: "Cotun," "Bichu," "Wujian," "Zhuzha," "Qiu," and comprehensive styles. Additionally, there were works in Sanskrit and Mongolian scripts.

### 拉萨举行首届藏文书法大赛展览
多吉占堆
1989-08-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    拉萨举行首届藏文书法大赛展览
    新华社拉萨八月六日电  （记者多吉占堆）西藏自治区首届藏文书法大赛展览今天下午在拉萨开幕，阿沛·阿旺晋美副委员长为展览剪彩，并为展览题了词。
    来自西藏、青海、四川、甘肃、北京、郑州等地的五百五十八位作者参加了这次全国性的藏文书法比赛。作者包括藏、汉、回、满、土家、蒙古族中的各界书法爱好者和著名书法家。
    作品分“粗顿”、“比稠”、“乌坚”、“主杂”、“秋”和综合等六种藏文书体，同时也有梵文、蒙文等书体。
