Title: Soviet Team Achieves Best Results in World Wrestling Championships Greco-Roman Style
Author: Shi Guangyao
Date: 1989-08-29
Edition: 3

The World Wrestling Championships Greco-Roman style concluded on August 27 in Martigny, Switzerland. In the 10 events, the Soviet team won 5 gold medals. Following their 3 gold medals on August 26, the Soviet team secured two more championships on August 27. In the 48 kg final, the Soviet wrestler Kuchelenko defeated Norway's Renningen to claim the title. Another Soviet wrestler, Mazidov, won the gold medal in the 62 kg category.

### 世界摔跤锦标赛古典式比赛苏队成绩最佳
施光耀
1989-08-29
第3版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

    世界摔跤锦标赛古典式比赛苏队成绩最佳
    新华社日内瓦8月27日电　（记者施光耀）世界摔跤锦标赛古典式摔跤赛27日在瑞士的马尔提尼结束。在全部10项比赛中，苏联队获5枚金牌。苏联队继26日获得3枚金牌后，27日又获得两项冠军。在48公斤级决赛中，苏联的克乌切连克战胜挪威的伦尼根，获得冠军，另一名苏联选手马济多夫赢得62公斤级的金牌。
