Title: The Ninth World Women's Modern Pentathlon Championship Concludes: Chinese Athlete Wins Equestrian Event Crown
Author:
Date: 1989-08-07
Edition: 4

The Ninth World Women's Modern Pentathlon Championship concluded today. The Polish team secured the team championship with a total score of 15,062 points. Lori Nowood of the United States topped the individual standings with 5,315 points. Liu Yan of the Chinese team finished eighth individually with 5,059 points, and the Chinese team placed 12th in the team standings with 13,641 points.

The event, held in Vienna Neustadt, was the largest in the history of the championships, attracting 60 athletes from 22 countries and regions.

In the final event of the day, the equestrian competition, Liu Yan from the Chinese team remained calm and composed, demonstrating excellent technical skills to achieve a perfect score of 1,100 points, tying for the top spot with American athlete Alata King. The third place in the individual equestrian event went to Snaets of the Netherlands with 1,094 points, while 11 athletes tied for fourth place.

The top three teams in the equestrian event were Finland, the Netherlands, and Austria.

The United States (15,128 points), Italy (14,845 points), West Germany (14,821 points), Hungary (14,686 points), and Czechoslovakia (14,373 points) secured the second to sixth places in the team standings.

### 第九届世界女子现代五项赛结束  中国选手获马术赛冠军

1989-08-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    第九届世界女子现代五项赛结束
    中国选手获马术赛冠军
    新华社维也纳8月6日电　第九届世界女子现代五项锦标赛今天结束。波兰队以总分15062分的成绩获得团体冠军。美国队的诺伍德·洛里以5315分获得个人总分第一。中国队的刘燕以总分5059分的成绩获个人第八名。中国队还以13641分名列团体第12位。
    在维也纳新城举办的这次比赛是历届规模最大的一次比赛。来自22个国家和地区的60名运动员参加了比赛。
    在今天进行的最后一个项目——马术比赛中，中国队的刘燕沉着、冷静，很好地发挥了技术水平，以1100分满分的成绩同美国选手阿拉塔·金并列这个项目的冠军。
    获马术个人第三名的是荷兰的斯娜特斯，成绩为1094分，并列第四的共有11名选手。
    马术团体前三名分别是芬兰队、荷兰队和奥地利队。
    美国（15128分）、意大利（14845分）、联邦德国（14821分）、匈牙利（14686分）、捷克斯洛伐克（14373分）分获团体总分第二名至第六名。
