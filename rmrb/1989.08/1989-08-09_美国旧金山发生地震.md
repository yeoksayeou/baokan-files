Title: Earthquake Strikes San Francisco
Author:
Date: 1989-08-09
Edition: 3

An earthquake measuring 5.2 on the Richter scale struck the San Francisco area in the early hours of August 9, resulting in one fatality. The quake occurred at 1:13 AM and lasted for 10 seconds. Several aftershocks followed within the next few hours, with a total of five recorded.

### 美国旧金山发生地震

1989-08-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国旧金山发生地震
    美国旧金山地区8日凌晨发生里氏5.2级地震，造成一人死亡。地震发生于凌晨1点13分，持续10秒钟，其后数小时内，又连续发生了5次余震。（据新华社）
