Title: East Germany Actively Assists African Countries in Training Technical Workers
Author:
Date: 1989-08-14
Edition: 7

East Germany is actively helping African countries train technical workers. The picture shows engineering and technical personnel from Karl Marx City in East Germany teaching technical skills to trainees from Mozambique.
