Title: Mother of the Soldiers and Banner of Patriotism and Military Support: Funeral of Rong Guanxiu Held
Author: Yè Bīng Nán
Date: 1989-08-23
Edition: 2

The funeral of Rong Guanxiu, known as the "Mother of the Soldiers," was held this morning in Shijiazhuang. Ma Yibo, Peng Zhen, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, Liu Lan Tao, Xiao Ke, Kang Keqing, and others sent wreaths.

Rong Guanxiu was an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China and a banner of patriotism and military support. She was born in 1896 in Xiapansong Village, Ping Mountain County, Hebei Province, and joined the Communist Party of China in 1938. She actively participated in the anti-Japanese war support work, risking her life to protect the soldiers and rescue the wounded. On February 19, 1944, the Jin-Cha-Ji Border Region Government and the Jin-Cha-Ji Military Region conferred upon her the honorable title of "Mother of the Soldiers." Rong Guanxiu passed away in Shijiazhuang on August 12, 1989, at the age of 93.

Xing Chongzhi, Yue Qifeng, and other provincial party and government leaders of Hebei, as well as more than 800 people from various sectors of Shijiazhuang, attended the funeral. Vice Minister of Civil Affairs Fan Baojun, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the PLA Zhou Wenyuan, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing Military Region Shen Yiyang, and other leaders also came from Beijing to pay their respects to Rong Guanxiu's body. The Central Office of the Communist Party of China, the Office of the Central Advisory Commission, the Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Ministry of National Defense, the Ministry of Civil Affairs, the three headquarters of the PLA, the Beijing Military Region, the All-China Women's Federation, and various large organizations in Hebei Province sent wreaths.

### 子弟兵的母亲　爱国拥军的旗帜  戎冠秀遗体告别仪式举行
叶冰男
1989-08-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    子弟兵的母亲　爱国拥军的旗帜
    戎冠秀遗体告别仪式举行
    新华社石家庄8月22日电　（记者叶冰男）“子弟兵的母亲”戎冠秀同志遗体告别仪式，今天上午在石家庄市隆重举行。
    姚依林、彭真、徐向前、聂荣臻、薄一波、刘澜涛、肖克、康克清等送了花圈。
    戎冠秀同志是中国共产党优秀党员、是爱国拥军的一面旗帜。1896年她出生于河北省平山县下盘松村，1938年加入中国共产党。她积极参加抗日支前工作，冒着生命危险掩护子弟兵、救护伤病员。1944年2月19日，晋察冀边区政府、晋察冀军区授予她“子弟兵的母亲”的光荣称号。1989年8月12日，戎冠秀同志因病在石家庄逝世，享年93岁。
    邢崇智、岳岐峰等河北省党政领导同志及石家庄市各界800多人参加了遗体告别仪式。民政部副部长范宝俊、解放军总政治部副主任周文元、北京军区副参谋长沈贻焱等领导同志也专程从北京赶来向戎冠秀的遗体告别。中共中央办公厅、中央顾问委员会办公厅、全国人大常委会办公厅、国防部、民政部、解放军三总部、北京军区、全国妇联和河北省各大机关送了花圈。
