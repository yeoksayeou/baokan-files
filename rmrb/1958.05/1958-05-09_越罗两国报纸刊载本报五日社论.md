Title: Vietnamese and Romanian Newspapers Publish This Newspaper's May 5th Editorial
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to Xinhua News Agency, the "Nhan Dan" newspaper, the central organ of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the "Capital Daily," the organ of the Hanoi Municipal Party Committee, published excerpts on May 8th from the "People's Daily" May 5th editorial, "Modern Revisionism Must Be Criticized."

The Romanian "Scînteia" newspaper published the full text of the "People's Daily" May 5th editorial on May 8th.
