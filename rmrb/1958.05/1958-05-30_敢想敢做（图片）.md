Author: Tong Fan
Date: 1958-05-30

Column: Glorious Labor, Glorious People

    Dare to Think, Dare to Do
    Zhao Xuecai, an inspector at the Xi'an Agricultural Machinery Factory in Shaanxi Province, was originally a forging worker. After the Party put forward the slogan of technological revolution, he proposed more than ten technological innovation suggestions, which increased work efficiency by one to twelve times.
    This is a tool improved by Zhao Xuecai for a four-row cotton seeder. Photo by Tong Fan
