Title: Finnish President Kekkonen Visits Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-05-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 22nd: Finnish Republic President Kekkonen arrived in Moscow on the 22nd. He is visiting the Soviet Union in return for an invitation from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Chairman Voroshilov.

Accompanying the President were Foreign Minister Hunninen, Minister of Commerce and Industry Kivikoski, Second Minister of Transportation and Public Works Antikainen, Member of Parliament Dr. Virolainen, Chief of the General Staff Lieutenant General Veliäinen, and others.

Welcoming Kekkonen at the airport were Voroshilov, Kirichenko, Kozlov, Mikoyan, Khrushchev, ministers of various ministries, marshals, and others.

Voroshilov and Kekkonen spoke at the airport.

Voroshilov said he believes that this visit will further develop friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Finland. Soviet-Finnish relations are an example of peaceful coexistence between countries with different social systems.

In his reply, Kekkonen emphasized that his visit to the Soviet Union would help strengthen the friendship and trust between the Finnish and Soviet people.

### 芬总统吉科宁访苏

1958-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    芬总统吉科宁访苏
    据新华社22日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：芬兰共和国总统吉科宁22日到达莫斯科。他是应苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫的邀请到苏联进行回拜访问的。
    随着总统同来的有外交部长胡尼宁、工商部长基维凯斯、交通和公共工程部第二部长安蒂卡伊宁、议员维洛莱宁博士、总参谋部参谋长韦利亚宁中将等。
    到机场欢迎吉科宁的有伏罗希洛夫、基里钦科、科兹洛夫、米高扬、赫鲁晓夫，各部部长，元帅等。
    伏罗希洛夫和吉科宁在机场上讲了话。
    伏罗希洛夫说，他相信，这次访问将进一步发展苏芬友好关系。苏芬关系是不同社会制度的国家和平共处的榜样。
    吉科宁在答词中强调指出，他这次访问苏联将有助于加强芬苏人民之间的友谊和信任。
