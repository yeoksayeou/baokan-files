Title: Czechoslovak, Bulgarian and Albanian Newspapers Publish Our Newspaper's May 5th Editorial
Author:
Date: 1958-05-08
Page: 5
　
　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to Xinhua News Agency reports: Bulgarian and Albanian newspapers published in full on the 6th and 7th respectively the "People's Daily" editorial of May 5th, "Modern Revisionism Must Be Criticized." Czechoslovakian "Red Rights Daily" and "Slovak Truth Daily" also published detailed summaries of the "People's Daily" editorial of May 5th on the 6th.

### 捷保阿三国报纸刊载本报五日社论

1958-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    捷保阿三国报纸刊载本报五日社论
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：保加利亚和阿尔巴尼亚的报纸分别在6日和7日全文刊载了5日“人民日报”社论“现代修正主义必须批判”。捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”和“斯洛伐克真理报”6日也分别刊载了5日“人民日报”社论的详细摘要。
