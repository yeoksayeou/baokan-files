Title: Pedal-Powered Flywheel Waterwheel (Picture)
Author: Lü Xiangyou
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 2

    Pedal-Powered Flywheel Waterwheel
    This was made by peasants in Jingxian County, Hebei Province, using bicycle parts and waterwheel pipe chains. It pumps a lot of water, and it is easy and labor-saving to operate while sitting on it. The cost is 83 yuan.

### 脚踏飞轮水车（图片）
吕相友
1958-05-07

专栏：

    脚踏飞轮水车
    这是河北景县农民利用自行车零件和水车管链制成，出水多，人坐在上面操作，轻便省力。造价八十三元。
