Title: Damascus Newspapers Enthusiastically Welcome TASS Statement Warning the United States Not to Interfere in Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 19th. Damascus newspapers today enthusiastically welcomed the TASS statement warning the United States not to interfere in Lebanon's internal affairs. All newspapers published the full text or summaries of the statement prominently on their front pages.

　　“Victory” emphasized: “The Soviet Union warns the United States and Britain not to interfere in Lebanon’s internal affairs. Such interference creates the danger of a new war breaking out in the Middle East. Any interference by imperialism in Lebanon’s internal affairs is a serious threat to Lebanon’s national independence.”

　　“Al-Nour” stated in its headline: “The Soviet Union gives a serious warning to the United States regarding interference in Lebanese affairs. Imperialists choose Lebanon as a target for dangerous provocations, wanting to establish imperialist rule in this country and suppress the national liberation movement in the Middle East.”

　　“Al-Barada” wrote: “Western interference in Lebanon's internal affairs will have serious consequences for the cause of peace in the Middle East.”

　　“Al-Ayyam” pointed out in its headline: “Imperialists attempt to seriously strike the national liberation movement in the Middle East by interfering in Lebanon’s internal affairs.”

### 对塔斯社警告美国不要干涉黎巴嫩的声明  大马士革报纸热烈欢迎

1958-05-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　对塔斯社警告美国不要干涉黎巴嫩的声明
　　大马士革报纸热烈欢迎
    新华社大马士革19日电　大马士革各报今天热烈欢迎塔斯社就美国干涉黎巴嫩内政发表的声明。各报都在第一版显著地位刊载了这个声明的全文或摘要。
    “胜利报”强调指出：“苏联警告美国和英国，不要干涉黎巴嫩的内政。这种干涉造成在中东爆发新战争的危险。帝国主义对黎巴嫩内政的任何干涉，都是对黎巴嫩国家独立的严重威胁。”
    “光明报”在标题中说：“苏联对美国干涉黎巴嫩事务给予严重警告。帝国主义者选择黎巴嫩作为危险挑衅的目标，是想在这个国家建立帝国主义统治和镇压中东的民族解放运动”。
    “巴拉达报”写道：“西方干涉黎巴嫩内政会给中东和平事业带来严重后果。”
    “旷野报”在标题中指出：“帝国主义者企图用干涉黎巴嫩内政的办法来给中东民族解放运动以严重打击。”
