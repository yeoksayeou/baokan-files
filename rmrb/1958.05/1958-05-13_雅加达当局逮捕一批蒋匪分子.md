Title: Jakarta Authorities Arrest a Group of Chiang Kai-shek Elements
Author:
Date: 1958-05-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 12th: The Jakarta "People's Daily's" weekly "Sports and Film News" reported on the 11th that twelve of the sixteen leading figures of the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Jakarta have been arrested recently by the authorities, and the remaining four have fled.

Among those arrested were Wang Yalu, manager of the Da Tung Bank, Li Guoyuan, deputy manager, Li Jianmin, principal of Zhongshan Middle School, Ma Shuli, director of the "China Business Daily," and Rao Boji, a major embezzler.

The newspaper said that one of the four Chiang Kai-shek clique members who escaped has fled to Singapore. This Chiang Kai-shek element received a "loan" of 100 million rupiahs from Sjafruddin when Sjafruddin was still the president of the Bank of Indonesia.

According to Antara News Agency, Indonesian authorities closed down an organization of the Chiang Kai-shek Kuomintang in Surabaya and the "Qingguang Daily," run by Chiang Kai-shek clique members, on the 7th.

### 雅加达当局逮捕一批蒋匪分子

1958-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    雅加达当局逮捕一批蒋匪分子
    据新华社雅加达12日电　雅加达“人民日报”的星期刊“体育和电影消息”11日报道说，在雅加达的蒋介石集团分子的十六名首脑人物中有十二人已经在最近被当局逮捕，其余四人已经逃走。
    被捕的这些蒋介石集团分子中有大东银行经理王亚禄、副经理李国元、中山中学校长李剑民、“中华商报”社长马树礼以及大贪污犯饶博基。
    这家报纸说，逃脱的四名蒋介石集团分子中有一名已经逃到新加坡。这个蒋介石集团分子当沙弗鲁丁还担任印度尼西亚银行总裁时，曾经从沙弗鲁丁那里得到了一亿盾的“贷款”。
    另据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚当局曾经在7日封闭了泗水的蒋介石国民党的一个机构和蒋介石集团分子办的“青光日报”。
