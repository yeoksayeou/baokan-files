Title: Soviet and Czechoslovak Newspapers Publish Comrade Shaoqi's Report
Author:
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 6

　　Soviet and Czechoslovak Newspapers
　　Publish Comrade Shaoqi's Report
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 29th: "Pravda" today published the full text of the Work Report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress, delivered by Comrade Liu Shaoqi, across three pages.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 29th: Today's "Red Right" newspaper published a lengthy summary of the Work Report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress, delivered by Comrade Liu Shaoqi, under the title "China Enters a New Stage of Socialist Construction."
　　Other newspapers also published summaries of Comrade Liu Shaoqi's report.

### 苏捷报纸刊载少奇同志报告

1958-05-30
第6版()
专栏：

    苏捷报纸
    刊载少奇同志报告
    新华社莫斯科29日电　“真理报”今天以三个版面的篇幅全文登载了刘少奇同志所作的中国共产党中央委员会向第八届全国代表大会第二次会议的工作报告。
    据新华社布拉格29日电　今天的“红色权利报”以“中国进入社会主义建设的新阶段”为题刊载了刘少奇同志所作的中国共产党中央委员会向第八届全国代表大会第二次会议的工作报告的长篇摘要。
    其他报纸也刊登了刘少奇同志的报告的摘要。
