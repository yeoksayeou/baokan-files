Title: Korean Guests Visit Northeast China

Author:
Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Xu Zhenshook, accompanied by Wang Yifu, Deputy Minister of Internal Affairs, left Beijing by train tonight to visit various places in Northeast China and to meet Korean war orphans who are being raised by the Chinese people there.

Those who saw them off at the station included Chen Shutong, Liao Chengzhi, Xie Juezai, Zhang Xiruo, Li Dequan, Li Youwen, Li Yanlu, Wang Ziyi, Chen Qiyuan, Xia Yan, Zhang Tianyun, Le Songsheng, and others, as well as the Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yonghao.

### 朝鲜贵宾去东北参观访问

1958-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜贵宾去东北参观访问
    新华社9日讯　以许贞琡为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团由内务部副部长王一夫陪同，在今天晚间乘火车离开北京去东北各地参观访问，并且会见生活在那里的由中国人民抚育的朝鲜战灾孤儿。
    到车站送行的，有陈叔通、廖承志、谢觉哉、张奚若、李德全、栗又文、李延禄、王子宜、陈其瑗、夏衍、张天云、乐松生等，以及朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。
