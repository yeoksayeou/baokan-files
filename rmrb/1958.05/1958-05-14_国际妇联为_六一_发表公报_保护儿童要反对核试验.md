Title: International Women's Federation Issues Communiqué for "June 1st": Protecting Children Requires Opposing Nuclear Testing
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-14
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 13th. According to a report from the German News Agency: The Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation issued a communiqué on the 10th in anticipation of the upcoming "June 1st" International Children's Day, stating that the threat to children is growing due to the continued nuclear weapons testing.

　　The communiqué stated that the Fourth Congress of the Women's International Democratic Federation is scheduled to open in Vienna on June 1st of this year. Women all over the world are launching vigorous struggles against the danger of atomic war. The communiqué pointed out that in order to protect children and give them a safe, healthy, and happy future, it is extremely important to wage a decisive struggle to win the cessation of nuclear weapons testing.

### 国际妇联为“六一”发表公报  保护儿童要反对核试验

1958-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    国际妇联为“六一”发表公报
　　保护儿童要反对核试验
    新华社13日讯　据德意志通讯社柏林讯：国际民主妇女联合会书记处10日为迎接即将到来的“六一”国际儿童节发表公报说，由于核武器试验的继续进行，儿童所遭到的威胁正日益增长。
    公报说，国际民主妇女联合会第四届代表大会定今年6月1日在维也纳开幕。全世界各地的妇女正在展开有力的斗争反对原子战争的危险。公报指出，为了保护儿童并且给他们以安全、健康和幸福前途，进行决定性的斗争来争取停止核武器试验是十分重要的。
