Title: On the Eve of May Day, Chairman Mao Visits Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 30th — On the eve of today's May Day, Chairman Mao visited the Tangxia Agricultural Cooperative in the suburbs of Guangzhou in the afternoon, and in the evening he visited the Guangdong Province Improved Agricultural Tools Exhibition currently being held in Guangzhou.

Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee of the Communist Party of China, accompanied Chairman Mao on the visit. When visiting the Tangxia Agricultural Cooperative, Zhu Guang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangzhou Municipal Party Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Guangzhou, also accompanied him.

The members of the Tangxia Agricultural Cooperative, men, women, old and young, greeted the beloved Chairman Mao with cheers, laughter, and applause. Chairman Mao, with a smile on his face, waved and nodded frequently under the banyan tree in the village center to thank and greet everyone.

In the office of the Tangxia Cooperative, Chairman Mao asked the names and ages of the cadres of the agricultural cooperative one by one, and carefully inquired about the situation of the early rice, sideline occupations, and farmers' lives this year. Afterwards, Chairman Mao went to the fields with the cooperative's cadres to understand the production situation on the spot, visited the cadres' experimental fields, and expressed satisfaction with the work of this agricultural cooperative.

In the evening, Chairman Mao visited the four pavilions of cultivation, water conservancy, aquatic products, and soil fertilizer at the Guangdong Province Improved Agricultural Tools Exhibition, highly praising the inventions and innovations of the masses. (Attached picture)

### 五一劳动节前夕  毛主席在广州参观访问

1958-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    五一劳动节前夕
    毛主席在广州参观访问
    新华社广州30日电　毛主席在今天——“五一”劳动节前夕的下午，在广州市访问了郊区的棠下农业社，晚上又参观了正在广州举办的广东省改良农具展览会。
    陪同毛主席参观访问的有中共广东省委第一书记陶铸；到棠下农业社访问时，中共广州市委书记处书记、广州市长朱光也陪同前往。
    棠下农业社的男女老幼社员们，以欢呼声、笑声、掌声来迎接亲爱的毛主席。毛主席在村头街心的榕树下，满脸笑容地频频招手点头向大家答谢、问好。
    在棠下社的办公室里，毛主席逐一询问了这个农业社的干部的名字、年纪，又仔细地询问了今年早稻、副业和农民生活的情形。随后，毛主席同社的干部到田间实地了解生产情况，参观了社干部的试验田，并且对这个农业社的工作表示满意。
    晚上，毛主席又在广东省改良农具展览会参观了耕耘、水利、水产和土壤肥料四个馆，极为称赞群众的发明创造和革新精神。（附图片）
