Title: Mongolia and the German Democratic Republic Victoriously Complete First Quarter Production Plans
Author:
Date: 1958-05-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 4th - The Mongolian State Statistical Bureau issued a communique on the first quarter production situation, stating that Mongolia has completed 106.1% of its first quarter industrial production plan, with many state-owned and cooperative factories overfulfilling their plans.
    The communique, published on the 2nd, pointed out that compared with the same quarter of 1957, the output of major industrial products in the first quarter of this year increased by 15.9% for coal, 18.4% for electricity, 10.7% for bricks, and 19.5% for felt.
    The communique stated that the construction sector overfulfilled the first quarter basic construction plan by 9.6%, the transportation sector fulfilled the passenger transport plan by 131.3%, and the freight transport plan by 92.4%.
    Mongolia's pastoral areas are now carrying out the work of receiving newborn livestock. The communique pointed out that, according to statistics, the survival rate of the five types of newborn livestock that have been received reached 96.5%.
    The communique also stated that the completed retail sales plan for the first quarter increased by 10.6% compared to the same quarter of 1957.
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th - According to a Deutsche Presse-Agentur report on the 3rd, the Central State Statistical Bureau of the German Democratic Republic announced that the execution of the national economic plan for the first quarter of 1958 in the German Democratic Republic was good. Compared with the same period last year, the production of various departments of the state-owned economy in the first quarter of this year had a large increase: coal and electricity 6%, mining and casting 9%, chemical industry 9%, heavy machinery manufacturing 11%, general machinery manufacturing 11%, light industry 9%, food industry 24%.
    Compared with the same period last year, the production of semi-state-owned industrial enterprises increased by 21%; the production of private factories increased by 10.6%.
    The total number of production workers in socialist industrial enterprises increased by more than 8% compared to the same period last year; hourly productivity increased by more than 14%. The average wage of production workers increased by 2%.
    The total investment of state-owned industry in the first quarter reached 1.9 billion marks, an increase of 21% compared with the same period last year.
    The total trade volume of foreign trade and trade with West Germany increased by 20% compared with the first quarter of last year. The report said that in order to welcome the convocation of the Fifth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, a total of more than two million workers are currently engaged in the socialist great competition in various departments of the national economy.

### 蒙古和民主德国  胜利完成第一季度生产计划

1958-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古和民主德国
    胜利完成第一季度生产计划
    新华社乌兰巴托4日电　蒙古国家统计局发表第一季度生产情况的公报说，蒙古已经完成了第一季度工业生产计划的106.1％，有许多国营和合作社营工厂超额完成了计划。
    2日发表的这篇公报指出，今年第一季度的主要工业产品产量和1957年同一季度相比，煤增加了15.9％、电力增加了18.4％、砖增加10.7％、毡子增加19.5％。
    公报说，建筑部门超额完成第一季度基本建设计划的9.6％，运输部门完成客运计划的131.3％，完成货运计划的92.4％。
    蒙古牧区现在正进行接产幼畜的工作。公报指出，根据统计，已经接产的五种牲畜的幼畜成活率达到96.5％。
    公报还说，第一季度完成的货物零售计划比1957年同一季度增加了10.6％。
    据新华社4日讯　据德意志通讯社3日报道，民主德国中央国家统计局宣布，民主德国1958年度国民经济计划第一季度的执行情况良好。同去年同期相比，今年第一季度内国营经济各部门的生产有了很大增长：煤和电力6％，采矿和铸造9％，化学工业9％，重型机器制造业11％，一般机器制造业11％，轻工业9％，食品工业24％。
    同去年同期相比，半国营工业企业的生产增长了21％；私营工厂的生产增长了10.6％。
    社会主义工业企业中的生产工人总数比去年同期增加了8％以上；每小时的生产率增长了14％以上。生产工人的平均工资提高了2％。
    国营工业在第一季度内的投资总额达到十九亿马克，比去年同期增加21％。
    对外贸易和对西德贸易的总贸易额比去年第一季度增长了20％。报告说，为了迎接德国统一社会党第五次代表大会的召开，在国民经济各部门中，目前总共有两百多万工人投入了社会主义大竞赛。
