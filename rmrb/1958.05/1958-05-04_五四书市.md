    "May Fourth Book Fair"
    To commemorate the "May Fourth" Youth Day, Xinhua Bookstore is holding a "Book Fair" at the Workers' Cultural Palace, selling literary books and periodicals such as "Song of Youth", and inviting the author of this book, Comrade Yang Mo, to meet with readers.

### “五四书市”

1958-05-04
第4版()
专栏：看看听听玩玩

    “五四书市”
    为了纪念“五四”青年节，新华书店在劳动人民文化宫举办“书市”，出售“青春之歌”等文艺书刊，并请本书作者杨沫同志和读者见面。
