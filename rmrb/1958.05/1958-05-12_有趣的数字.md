    Interesting Numbers
    The number of capillaries in a cross-section of human muscle is astonishing, averaging over a thousand per square millimeter. If all the capillaries in the body were connected end to end, their length could circle the Earth two and a half times.

### 有趣的数字

1958-05-12
第8版()
专栏：

    有趣的数字
    人的肌肉横断面上，毛细管的数目多得惊人，平均每分毫米内就有一千个以上。如果把身体上所有的毛细管一头对一头地接起来，长度可绕行地球两圈半。
