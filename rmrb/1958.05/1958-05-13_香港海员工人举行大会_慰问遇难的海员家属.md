Title: Hong Kong Maritime Workers Hold General Meeting to Console Families of Deceased Seamen
Author:
Date: 1958-05-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 12th—More than one thousand Hong Kong maritime workers and their families held a gathering on the evening of the 11th to express their condolences to the families of the crew members who were victims of the bombing of two Hong Kong merchant ships.

　　At the consolation meeting, Hong Kong maritime workers and their families unanimously condemned the inhuman crimes of the US-Chiang elements. Representatives of the Hong Kong maritime workers pointed out in their condolence speeches that the United States and the Chiang Kai-shek clique must bear full responsibility for this incident.

　　Chen Yao-cai, chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions, read out the condolence letter from the council of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions at the meeting. The letter stated: "For the safety of workers' lives, for safeguarding the workers' professional lives, and for the interests of shipping and trade, we strongly protest the atrocities of the US-Chiang elements. The US-Chiang elements must immediately stop their murderous atrocities and must compensate for all loss of life and property."

### 香港海员工人举行大会  慰问遇难的海员家属

1958-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    香港海员工人举行大会
　　慰问遇难的海员家属
    据新华社香港12日电　香港海员工人和他们的家属一千多人11日晚上举行集会，慰问香港两艘遇炸商船受害船员的家属。
    在慰问大会上，香港海员工人和他们的家属一致指责美蒋分子这种惨无人道的罪行。香港海员工人代表在会上致慰问词的时候指出，美国和蒋介石集团必须对这一事件负起全部责任。
    港九工会联合会会长陈耀材在大会上宣读了港九工会联合会理事会的慰问信。这封信说：“为了工人生命安全，为了维护工人的职业生活，为了航运和贸易利益，我们严重抗议美蒋分子的暴行，美蒋分子必须立即停止杀人暴行并必须赔偿一切生命财产损失。”
