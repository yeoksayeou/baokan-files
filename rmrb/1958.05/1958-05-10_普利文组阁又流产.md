Title: Pleven's Cabinet Formation Fails Again
Author:
Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 9th: Pleven, who was commissioned for the second time to form a new French cabinet, announced his failure to form a cabinet again on the 8th, because the candidates for cabinet members could not get the consent of the ruling group.

### 普利文组阁又流产

1958-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    普利文组阁又流产
    据新华社9日讯　巴黎消息：第二次受命组织法国新内阁的普利文，由于阁员人选得不到统治集团内部的同意，已在8日再度宣布组阁失败。
