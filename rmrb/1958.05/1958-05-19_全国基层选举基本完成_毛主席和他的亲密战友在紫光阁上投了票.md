Title: National Basic-Level Elections Basically Completed, Chairman Mao and His Close Comrades-in-Arms Voted at Ziguang Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1958-05-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The national basic-level elections have been basically completed.
　　Chairman Mao Zedong and his close comrades-in-arms Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, and other comrades, today voted together with the voters of the Zhongnanhai electoral district in Xidan District, Beijing, to elect representatives to the Third People's Congress of Xidan District.
　　According to the number stipulated by the Election Law, the Zhongnanhai electoral district should elect one representative. Before the election, after extensive deliberation and discussion among the voters in this electoral district, Yang Fangzhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, was nominated as a candidate for representative. The polling station was set up in the Ziguang Pavilion, surrounded by green trees on the edge of Zhongnanhai. A portrait of Chairman Mao and the national flag were hung in the polling station. The front of the ballot box was filled with flowers. From early morning, voters came here one after another to solemnly exercise their right to vote.
　　Chairman Mao Zedong and comrades such as Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De voted separately in the afternoon and evening. Chairman Mao Zedong arrived at the Ziguang Pavilion polling station at 9 o'clock tonight, presented his voter certificate at the verification ticket office, collected and filled out the ballot, and then, together with other voters, put the ballot into the ballot box in order.
　　After voting, Chairman Mao Zedong and others shook hands with Du Ruo, Chairman of the Xidan District Election Committee and Secretary of the Xidan District Committee of the Communist Party of China, who was here to observe the progress of the voting.
　　Also participating in the voting at Ziguang Pavilion today were Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Peng Dehuai, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Lu Dingyi, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Bo Yibo, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, as well as many senior cadres of the Central Government, leaders of democratic parties, and staff members of the central organs.

### 全国基层选举基本完成  毛主席和他的亲密战友在紫光阁上投了票

1958-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国基层选举基本完成
　　毛主席和他的亲密战友在紫光阁上投了票
    新华社18日讯　毛泽东主席和他的亲密战友刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、邓小平等同志，今天和北京市西单区中南海选区的选民们一起投票，选举西单区第三届人民代表大会的代表。
    根据选举法规定的人数，中南海选区应选出一个代表。在选举以前，经过这个选区选民广泛的酝酿和讨论，提出国务院副秘书长杨放之为代表候选人。投票站设在中南海边绿树环抱的紫光阁，投票站里悬挂着毛主席像和国旗。投票箱的前面摆满了鲜花。从清晨起，选民们就陆续来到这里庄严地行使自己的选举权利。
    毛泽东主席和刘少奇、周恩来、朱德等同志在下午和晚上分别进行了投票。毛泽东主席在今晚九时来到了紫光阁投票站，向验证发票处交验了选民证，领取和填写了选票，然后同其他选民们一起依次把选票投进投票箱。
    投票以后，毛泽东主席等同在这里观察投票进行情况的西单区选举委员会主席、中共西单区委员会书记杜若握手。
    今天在紫光阁参加投票的，还有林伯渠、董必武、彭德怀、陈毅、李富春、李先念、陆定一、陈伯达、康生、薄一波、李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培，以及中央的许多高级干部、民主党派的负责人、中央机关的工作人员。
