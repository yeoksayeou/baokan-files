    Amazing Facts
    △ Pinks are afraid to "hear" noise. If you put them near a radio, they will wither soon.
    △ Some small birds lay relatively large eggs, and the shape of these eggs is mostly pointed at one end. The little mother sits on the pointed end and incubates several eggs at the same time.

### 奇妙的事实

1958-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    奇妙的事实
    △石竹怕“听”吵音。如果将它放在收音机附近，不久就会枯萎。
    △有些小鸟，身体很小而生的蛋却比较大，这种蛋的形状多半是一头尖，小母亲就坐在尖的那一头，同时孵好几个蛋。
