Title: Wooden Boat Power Station (Picture)
Author: Zhang Qicai
Date: 1958-05-03
Page: 3

Column: Blossoms of Construction

    Wooden Boat Power Station
    The Qunying Agricultural Cooperative in Chengjiao Township, Cangxi County, Sichuan Province, built a wooden boat power station on the river滩 of the Jialing River. This power station has a generating capacity of 500 kilowatts, which can provide lighting electricity for more than 150 cooperative member households in the entire cooperative. The above picture shows the exterior of the wooden boat power station. The picture on the right shows Han Yuanxuan, the cooperative's power generation technician, monitoring the distribution panel. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Qicai photographed it.

### 木船发电站（图片）
张齐才
1958-05-03
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    木船发电站
    四川苍溪县城郊乡群英农业社在嘉陵江的河滩上修建了一个木船发电站。这个电站的发电能力有五百瓩，可供全社一百五十多户社员的照明用电。上图是木船发电站外貌。右图是社里的发电技术员韩远宣在看管配电盘。　　新华社记者　张齐才摄
