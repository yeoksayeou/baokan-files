Title: Hungarian Supreme Court Sentences a Group of Counter-Revolutionaries
Author:
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 18th. The People's Court of the Hungarian Supreme Court sentenced a group of counter-revolutionary criminals on the 17th. Among them, Bécsi Géza and three others were sentenced to death and have been executed, and twelve people were sentenced to prison terms of varying lengths.

During the Hungarian counter-revolutionary events in October 1956, this group of counter-revolutionaries directly participated in armed riots and had connections with Western spies. Among these people, some were the heads of counter-revolutionary organizations, and they continued to carry out counter-revolutionary activities until February last year.

### 匈牙利最高法院  判处一批反革命分子

1958-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利最高法院
    判处一批反革命分子
    新华社布达佩斯18日电　匈牙利最高法院人民法庭17日判处了一批反革命罪犯。其中，贝契·盖佐等四人被判处死刑并已执行，有十二人分别被判处刑期不等的有期徒刑。
    在1956年10月匈牙利反革命事件期间，这一批反革命分子曾经直接参加了武装暴乱，并且同西方间谍分子有联系。在这些人当中，有的还是反革命组织的头子，他们直到去年2月还在继续进行反革命活动。
