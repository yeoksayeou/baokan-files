Title: One-Third of London Dockworkers Involved in Wave of Strike Struggles
Author:
Date: 1958-05-24
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, London, May 23rd: The London dockworkers' strike, which began on the 15th, is expanding. As of this morning, the number of striking workers has increased to around 10,000, accounting for one-third of all London dockworkers.
    It is reported that approximately seventy cargo ships are currently docked in various London ports without being unloaded.

### 伦敦三分之一码头工人卷入罢工斗争浪潮

1958-05-24
第6版()
专栏：

    伦敦三分之一码头工人卷入罢工斗争浪潮
    据新华社23日讯　伦敦消息：15日开始的伦敦码头工人的罢工正在扩大。到今天上午为止，罢工工人已经增加到一万人左右，占全部伦敦码头工人的三分之一。
    据报道：目前在伦敦各港口上，已经有大约七十只货船停泊在那里没有人卸货。
