Title: Salutations! To China's Machine Manufacturing Plants! (And Another Poem)
Author: Hong Yang
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 8

    Salutations! To China's Machine Manufacturing Plants!
    (And Another Poem)
    Hong Yang
    I dedicate my most resounding song,
    To these machine manufacturing plants,
    Using the highest note of the high notes,
    To sing of these rows of factory buildings!
    Look at the factory logos on the gantry cranes;
    Shanghai, Shenyang, Jinan…
    Look at the long lines;
    Lathes, milling machines, planers…
    Machines made in China!
    China's machine manufacturing plants!
    Ah! The most resounding song in the world,
    Cannot fully express my ardent heart.
    Standing before the majestic gantry crane,
    A solemn vow rises in my heart:
    To set this beloved machine in motion,
    Producing millions of new factories!
    Millions of machine manufacturing plants,
    Millions of lathes spinning rapidly!
    This is the jubilant heart of the motherland!
    This is the strength of socialism!
    Salutations! To machines made in China!
    Salutations! To China's machine manufacturing plants!
    The Old Master
    The old master comes to the new factory,
    Humming and singing incessantly,
    You ask him why he is so happy,
    He just smiles, refusing to speak.
    Sometimes he becomes silent again,
    Every wrinkle deep in thought,
    You ask him what he is thinking?
    He just shakes his head, refusing to speak.
    Everyone loves this new factory,
    Affectionately calling it: "The Heart of the Motherland".
    Who loves it most ardently, most deeply?
    Our old master!
    Less than a month has passed,
    The old master's name is on the red榜 (red announcement board);
    “Increased production efficiency fivefold!”
    He put the answer in his hands.

### 敬礼！中国的机器制造厂！（外一首）
洪洋
1958-05-01
第8版()
专栏：

    敬礼！中国的机器制造厂！
    （外一首）
    洪洋
    我把最嘹亮的歌声，
    献给这机器制造厂，
    用高音中的最高音，
    歌唱这一座座厂房！
    看那龙门上的厂标；
    上海、沈阳、济南……。
    看那长长的行列；
    车床、铣床、刨床……。
    中国制造的机器！
    中国的机器制造厂！
    呵！世上最嘹亮的歌声，
    也难唱出我的热肠。
    站在巍峨的龙门架前，
    心里升起了庄严的誓言：
    要拨动这心爱的机器，
    生产出千百万座新工厂！
    千万座机器制造厂，
    千万部车床飞速旋转！
    这便是祖国欢跳的心脏！
    这便是社会主义的力量！
    敬礼！中国制造的机器！
    敬礼！中国的机器制造厂！
    老师傅
    老师傅来到新工厂，
    嘴里不停地哼哼唱唱，
    你问他为何这般高兴，
    他光笑，不肯把话讲。
    有时他又变得沉默，
    每根皱纹都在思索，
    你问他想些什么？
    他光摇头，不肯把话讲。
    人人都爱这新工厂，
    亲切地唤它：“祖国的心脏”。
    谁爱得最热烈、最深沉？
    我们的老师傅！
    时光过去不到一月，
    老师傅的名字挂上红榜；
    “提高生产效率五倍！”
    他把答案放在了双手上。
