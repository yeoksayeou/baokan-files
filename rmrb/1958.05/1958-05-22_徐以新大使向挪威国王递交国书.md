Title: Ambassador Xu Yixin Presents Credentials to the King of Norway
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Oslo, May 21st: China's new ambassador to Norway, Xu Yixin, presented his credentials to King Olav V of Norway on May 20th.

### 徐以新大使向挪威国王递交国书

1958-05-22
第6版()
专栏：

    徐以新大使
    向挪威国王递交国书
    新华社21日讯　奥斯陆消息：中国新任驻挪威大使徐以新5月20日向挪威国王奥拉夫五世递交了国书。
