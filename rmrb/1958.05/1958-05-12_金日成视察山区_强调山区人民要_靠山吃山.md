Title: Kim Il-sung Inspects Mountainous Areas, Emphasizes that People in Mountainous Areas Should "Live Off the Mountains"
Author:
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th: According to Pyongyang newspapers today, Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of North Korea, inspected work in Hyesan City, Ryanggang Province, in the northeast mountainous region of Korea, on the 10th.

Kim Il-sung inspected factories, lumberyards, agricultural producers' cooperatives, and economic departments in Hyesan City and Pot'aebong and other places in the mountainous area.

When speaking to the masses in Hyesan City, Kim Il-sung emphasized that the people in mountainous areas should "live off the mountains." He emphasized the need to develop forestry and, in agriculture, to operate a diversified economy, develop cash crops, animal husbandry, beekeeping, and sericulture to raise the living standards of farmers in mountainous areas to the level of middle peasants. He also emphasized the need to strengthen health work in rural areas.

Premier Kim Il-sung was warmly welcomed by forestry workers and farmers in the deep mountains and dense forests. In Pot'aebong, Premier Kim Il-sung met with old comrades-in-arms who participated in the Battle of Pot'aebong and visited the battle site.

### 金日成视察山区  强调山区人民要“靠山吃山”

1958-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　金日成视察山区
    强调山区人民要“靠山吃山”
    据新华社平壤11日电　据平壤报纸今天报道，朝鲜内阁首相金日成10日到朝鲜的东北部山区两江道惠山市视察工作。
    金日成视察了山区里的工厂、木材场、农业生产合作社和惠山市、普天堡等地的经济部门。
    金日成在对惠山市的群众讲话时强调，山区人民要“靠山吃山”，他强调要发展林业，在农业方面要经营多种经济，发展经济作物、畜牧业、养蜂和养蚕业，把山区农民的生活提高到中农水平。他还强调，要加强农村中的卫生工作。
    金日成首相受到深山密林中的林业工人和农民们的热烈欢迎。在普天堡，金日成首相会见了当年一同参加普天堡战斗的老战友，并且访问了当年的战斗遗迹。
