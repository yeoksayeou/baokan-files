Title: Soviet National Symphony Orchestra Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Soviet National Symphony Orchestra, a group of over one hundred people led by Anosov, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic and the orchestra's director, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. This symphony orchestra is one of the three largest and most famous symphony orchestras in the Soviet Union. They are visiting China for performances at the invitation of the Chinese People’s Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, according to the 1958 executive program of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
　　Ivanov, People's Artist of the Soviet Union, who serves as the artistic director and chief conductor of the orchestra, also arrived in Beijing on the same flight today.
　　The Soviet National Symphony Orchestra will visit and perform in Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Harbin for a month and a half.

### 苏联国家交响乐团到北京

1958-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联国家交响乐团到北京
    据新华社15日讯　苏联国家交响乐团一行一百多人，由俄罗斯联邦苏维埃社会主义共和国功勋艺术家、团长阿诺索夫率领，已在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。这个交响乐团是全苏联三个最大的著名交响乐团中的一个。他们这次是根据中苏文化合作协定1958年执行计划应我国对外文化联络委员会的邀请来中国访问演出的。
    在这个乐团中担任艺术指导和总指挥的苏联人民演员伊万诺夫，也在今天同机到京。
    苏联国家交响乐团将在北京、上海、杭州和哈尔滨等地访问演出一个半月。
