Title: French Oncology Expert Lacassagne Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Professor Lacassagne, a renowned French oncology expert invited by the Chinese Medical Association, and Professor Jamet, a radiology expert, along with two others, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 29th for a visit and lectures in our country.
