Title: Joliot-Curie Visits Moscow
Author:
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th: TASS, Moscow reported: The distinguished French scientist and Chairman of the World Peace Council, Professor Joliot-Curie, arrived in Moscow on the 10th.

Joliot-Curie is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. He will learn about the work of many research institutes of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and many other scientific institutions.

### 约里奥—居里到莫斯科访问

1958-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    约里奥—居里到莫斯科访问
    据新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：法国杰出的科学家、世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里教授10日到达莫斯科。
    约里奥—居里是应苏联科学院的邀请来苏联访问的。他将了解苏联科学院许多研究所以及其他许多科学机构的工作情况。
