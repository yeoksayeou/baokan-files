Title: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Ministry of Culture, and the Chinese Musicians Association Host a Banquet in Honor of the USSR State Symphony Orchestra
Author:
Date: 1958-05-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 27th - The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Ministry of Culture, and the Chinese Musicians Association jointly held a banquet tonight in honor of all the artists of the USSR State Symphony Orchestra.

Shen Yanbing, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Minister of Culture, delivered a speech at the beginning of the banquet. He said that the visit and performance of the USSR State Symphony Orchestra, which enjoys a high reputation in the Soviet Union and internationally, is an important event in the friendship and cultural exchange between the Chinese and Soviet people. The Chinese people and music lovers feel extremely pleased and fortunate to be able to hear the orchestra's wonderful performances. Shen Yanbing said that the USSR State Symphony Orchestra's outstanding ability to quickly reflect major domestic and international events through musical art, its excellent style of connecting with the masses, and its rich experience in serving the broad masses of working people are examples for the Chinese music community to learn from. Their visit and performances will powerfully promote the great leap forward in China's music scene.

Anosov, the head of the orchestra, then spoke. He said that all the members of the orchestra are very grateful for the warm hospitality of the Chinese people, especially when they went deep into the production units to perform directly for Chinese workers and peasants and received an extremely enthusiastic welcome, all the Soviet artists were deeply moved. He also said that all the unforgettable and profound impressions the orchestra members have seen in China will greatly enrich their artistic creation. The orchestra's conductor, Soviet People's Artist Ivanov, also said at the banquet that all Soviet musicians are extremely excited that their performances can touch the hearts of the Chinese people. He also warmly welcomed Chinese symphony orchestras to visit and perform in the Soviet Union.

Li Dequan, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Vice Minister of Culture, Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, Ma Sicong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, and the famous music conductor Li Guoquan, also spoke at the banquet, congratulating the orchestra on its artistic achievements and the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people.

Antonov, the Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in China, was invited to attend the banquet, which was attended by three hundred people. Also present were Shao Lizi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, as well as heads of relevant people's organizations and famous figures from the capital's literary and art circles.

During the banquet, Soviet and Chinese artists performed many musical and acrobatic programs.

This afternoon, the USSR State Symphony Orchestra performed for more than 1,000 farmers at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Cooperative and was warmly welcomed by the farmers.

### 中苏友协、文化部、中国音协三单位  设宴招待苏联国家交响乐团

1958-05-28
第3版()
专栏：

    中苏友协、文化部、中国音协三单位
    设宴招待苏联国家交响乐团
    新华社27日讯　中苏友好协会总会、文化部和中国音乐家协会今晚联合举行宴会，招待苏联国家交响乐团的全体艺术家。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长、文化部部长沈雁冰在宴会开始时首先致词，他说，在苏联和国际上都负有盛誉的苏联国家交响乐团这次前来我国访问演出，是中苏人民友谊和文化交流上的重要事件。中国人民和音乐爱好者都以能够听到乐团的精采演奏感到莫大的愉快和幸运。沈雁冰说，苏联国家交响乐团用音乐艺术迅速反映国内外重大事件的卓越能力，联系群众的优良作风和为广大劳动人民服务的丰富经验，是中国音乐界学习的榜样。他们的访问演出将有力地促进我国音乐界的大跃进。
    阿诺索夫团长接着讲话。他说，全体乐团人员都十分感谢中国人民的热情款待，特别是当他们深入到生产单位直接为中国工人农民演出而受到无比热情的欢迎时，所有苏联艺术家都深深感动。他还说，乐团人员在中国所看到的一切难忘的深刻印象，将大大丰富他们的艺术创作。乐团总指挥、苏联人民演员伊万诺夫也在宴会上说，所有苏联音乐家都为自己的演奏能够打动中国人民的心而异常兴奋。他并且热情地欢迎中国的交响乐团能够到苏联去访问演出。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全，中苏友好协会总会秘书长、文化部副部长钱俊瑞，对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，中国音乐家协会副主席马思聪和著名音乐指挥黎国荃，也先后在宴会上致词，祝贺乐团的艺术成就和中苏人民的伟大友谊。
    苏联驻中国临时代办安东诺夫应邀出席了今晚有三百人参加的宴会。出席作陪的，还有中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子，文化部副部长夏衍，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥以及有关人民团体的负责人和首都文艺界著名人士。
    宴会上，苏联和中国的艺术家们演出了许多音乐和杂技节目。
    今天下午，苏联国家交响乐团到中苏友好农业合作社为一千多农民演出，受到了农民们的热情欢迎。
