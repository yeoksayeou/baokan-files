Title: Wheelbarrow Sprayer (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 2

    Wheelbarrow Sprayer
    Created by Guangmang Agricultural Cooperative, Jindian Township, Linfen County, Shanxi.
    Suitable for spraying 1059 pesticide in cotton fields.
    Operated by one person, it can spray thirty to forty mu per day.
    This is a modification of a single-tube sprayer. Pushing it allows for automatic pressurization and spraying, eliminating the need to hold it by hand, ensuring safe spraying.
    The modification cost is fifteen yuan per unit.

### 手推车喷雾器（图片）

1958-05-09
第2版()
专栏：

    手推车喷雾器
    山西临汾县金殿乡光芒农业社创造。
    适于在棉田喷洒1059农药用。
    一人操作，每日可喷洒三十至四十亩。
    这是利用单管喷雾器改装的，推着走可以自动加压喷雾，不用手拿，保证喷药安全。
    每部改装费十五元。
