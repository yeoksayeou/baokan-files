Title: Chairman Mao Cables Congratulations on Norway's National Day
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a cable to King Olav V of the Kingdom of Norway, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Norway. The text of the cable is as follows:
His Majesty King Olav V of the Kingdom of Norway:

On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Norway, I would like to extend warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the Norwegian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the friendly relations between China and Norway further development, and wish Your Majesty good health, and the Kingdom of Norway prosperity and its people happiness.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, May 16, 1958, Beijing

### 毛主席电贺挪威国庆

1958-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席电贺挪威国庆
    新华社16日讯　毛泽东主席致电挪威王国国王奥拉夫五世，祝贺挪威王国国庆。电文如下：挪威王国国王奥拉夫五世陛下：
    当挪威王国国庆的时候，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向陛下和挪威人民致热烈的祝贺。祝中、挪两国之间的友好关系进一步的发展，祝陛下身体健康，挪威国家繁荣、人民幸福。
          中华人民共和国主席毛泽东　一九五八年五月十六日北京
