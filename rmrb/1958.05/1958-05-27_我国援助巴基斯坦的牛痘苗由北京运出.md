Title: China's Aid of Cowpox Vaccine to Pakistan Shipped from Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 26th. The Red Cross Society of China announced today that the first batch of 1.26 million doses of cowpox vaccine donated by the Red Cross Society of China to the Red Cross Society of Pakistan was airlifted from Beijing to Dhaka, the capital of East Pakistan, today.
　　The person in charge of the Red Cross Society of China said that in order to help Pakistan eliminate the smallpox and cholera prevalent in the east, the Red Cross Society of China decided to donate 3 million doses of cowpox vaccine and 1,000 liters of cholera vaccine to the Red Cross Society of Pakistan. The cholera vaccine and another 1.74 million doses of cowpox vaccine will be shipped successively from Beijing, Shanghai, and Wuhan.

### 我国援助巴基斯坦的牛痘苗由北京运出

1958-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我国援助巴基斯坦的牛痘苗由北京运出
    新华社26日讯　中国红十字会今天宣布，中国红十字会捐给巴基斯坦红十字会的第一批牛痘苗一百二十六万人份已经在今天由北京空运东巴基斯坦首府达卡。
    中国红十字会负责人说，为了援助巴基斯坦扑灭在东巴流行的天花和霍乱，中国红十字会曾经决定捐给巴基斯坦红十字会三百万人份的牛痘苗和一千公升霍乱菌苗。霍乱菌苗和另外一百七十四万人份牛痘苗将自北京、上海和武汉三地陆续启运。
