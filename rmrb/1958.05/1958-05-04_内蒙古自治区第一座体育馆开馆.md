Title: The First Gymnasium in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Opens

Author:
Date: 1958-05-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, 3rd: The first gymnasium in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region opened on the evening of May 1st.

This is a large gymnasium built in Hohhot, with relatively complete facilities. The gymnasium has a basketball court, volleyball court, tennis court, table tennis room, weightlifting room, wrestling room, boxing room, athlete rest rooms, and shower rooms. Various gymnastics and equipment activities can also be carried out in the gymnasium.

The gymnasium has a building area of more than 4,400 square meters and can accommodate more than 2,400 athletes and people.

### 内蒙古自治区第一座体育馆开馆

1958-05-04
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区第一座体育馆开馆
    据新华社呼和浩特3日电  内蒙古自治区第一座体育馆1日晚开馆。
    这是建设在呼和浩特的大型体育馆，设备比较完善，馆内设有篮球场、排球场、网球场、乒乓球室、举重室、摔跤室、拳击室、运动员休息室和淋浴室等。在馆内还能进行各种体操和器械活动。
    这座体育馆建筑面积四千四百多平方公尺，能容纳运动员和群众二千四百多人。
