Author: Sun Zhongjing
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 3

Dredging the Jialing River Channel

Work has begun on dredging the 263-kilometer-long section of the Jialing River's middle reaches (Nanchong to Hechuan). Dredging this section of the waterway requires blasting more than 100 dangerous shoals in the river and also dredging the narrow river channels. After dredging, with the river channel deepened and widened, wooden boats of up to 50 tons and tugboats of 200 horsepower will be able to navigate unhindered year-round. This is a photograph of the surveying staff of the Sichuan Provincial Department of Transportation's Navigation Engineering Team conducting surveys at Jinbaitan on the Nanchong-Hechuan section.

### 整治嘉陵江航道（图片）
孙忠靖
1958-05-09
第3版()
专栏：

    整治嘉陵江航道
    整治嘉陵江中游（南充至合川间）全长二百六十三公里航道的工作已经开始了。整治这段航道需要炸掉江中一百多处险滩，还要淘挖狭窄的河漕。经过整治，河道加深加宽后，五十吨以下的木船和二百马力的拖轮都能够终年航行无阻了。这是四川省交通厅航道工程队的测量工作人员正在南充至合川段的金白滩进行测量。
    新华社记者　孙忠靖摄
