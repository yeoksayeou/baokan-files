Title: East German Ambassador to China Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Mr. Wangdel, held a reception in Beijing tonight to celebrate the 140th anniversary of the birth of Karl Marx.

    Those attending the reception included Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice-Minister Liu Zhiming, and Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei. Ambassadors and diplomatic officials from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 民主德国驻华大使举行酒会

1958-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国驻华大使举行酒会
    新华社5日讯　德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔，今晚在北京举行酒会庆祝卡尔·马克思诞辰一百四十周年。
    出席酒会的有文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长刘芝明、外交部副部长姬鹏飞等人。各国驻华使节和外交官员也出席了酒会。
