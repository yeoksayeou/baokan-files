Title: The Work Report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress

Author: 
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 1

The general line of the Party for socialist construction is the application and development of the Party's mass line in the cause of socialist construction. To fully combine centralized leadership with decentralized management, to fully combine the strength of the central authorities with the strength of the localities, the strength of the state with the strength of the masses, to fully combine large-scale undertakings with medium and small-scale undertakings, and to fully combine improvement work with popularization work, all of this applies not only to industry, but also to other economic and cultural undertakings, and to the entire cause of the technological revolution and the cultural revolution.
     ——“The Work Report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress”

### “中国共产党中央委员会向第八届全国代表大会第二次会议的工作报告”

1958-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    党的社会主义建设的总路线是党的群众路线在社会主义建设事业中的应用和发展。把集中领导和分散经营充分地结合起来，把中央的力量和地方的力量、国家的力量和群众的力量充分地结合起来，把大型的事业和中小型的事业、提高的工作和普及的工作充分地结合起来，这一切，不但适用于工业，而且适用于其他经济事业和文化事业，适用于技术革命和文化革命的整个事业。
     ——“中国共产党中央委员会向第八届全国代表大会第二次会议的工作报告”
