Title: Party and State Leaders Receive "May Day" Parade Representatives
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, May 4th: Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and other Party and state leaders including Chen Yun, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Ye Jianying, Bao Erhan, Su Yu, Xiao Ke, and Xiao Hua, received this afternoon over 300 representatives, including those from more than 30 fraternal ethnic groups, who participated in the "May Day" celebrations, as well as representatives from the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

### 党和国家领导人接见“五一”观礼代表

1958-05-05
第2版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人接见“五一”观礼代表
    新华社4日讯　毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、周恩来总理、以及党和国家的领导人陈云、林彪、邓小平、陈毅、乌兰夫、李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通、叶剑英、包尔汉、粟裕、萧克、萧华等，在今天下午先后接见了参加“五一”节观礼的包括三十多个兄弟民族的三百多位代表，以及中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军的代表。
