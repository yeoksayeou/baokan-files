Title: The US Dares to Test Missiles in Taiwan, Seriously Provoking Our Country and the People of Asia

Author:

Date: 1958-05-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd Dispatch: According to a United Press International report from Taipei, the U.S. Air Force test-launched "Matador" missiles in Taiwan on the 2nd. This is the first time the United States has tested this type of missile in the Far East. The test launch was conducted in secret. U.S. Commander in Taiwan, Doyle, observed the test launch.

The United States announced on May 7 last year that it would deploy "Matador" missile units to Taiwan. Over the past year, the United States' plot to turn Taiwan into an atomic war base has been met with strong protests and condemnations from the Chinese people and the people of Asia.

This time, the United States openly test-fired "Matador" missiles in Taiwan, our country's territory it occupies, is another serious act of provocation against the Chinese people and the people of Asia, engaging in atomic war blackmail and escalating international tensions.

### 美国竟在台湾试发导弹  对我国和亚洲人民进行严重挑衅

1958-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    美国竟在台湾试发导弹
    对我国和亚洲人民进行严重挑衅
    新华社2日讯　据合众社台北消息，美国空军2日在台湾试验发射了“斗牛士式”导弹。这是美国在远东第一次试验发射这种导弹。试验发射在秘密中进行。侵台美军司令道伊尔参观了这次试验发射。
    美国在去年5月7日宣布把“斗牛士式”导弹部队派驻台湾。一年来，美国把台湾变为原子战争基地的阴谋，已经受到中国人民和亚洲人民的强烈抗议和谴责。
    这次美国在它霸占下的我国领土台湾公然试验发射“斗牛士式”导弹，是它又一次对中国人民和亚洲人民进行原子战争讹诈、加剧国际紧张局势的严重的挑衅行为。
