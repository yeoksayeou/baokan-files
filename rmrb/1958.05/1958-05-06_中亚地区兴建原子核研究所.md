Soviet Science and Technology Briefs

    Nuclear Research Institute to be Built in Central Asia
    A science city is being built in the picturesque Kiblas area on the outskirts of Tashkent, the capital of the Uzbek Republic. This is Central Asia's first atomic center—the Institute of Nuclear Physics of the Uzbek Academy of Sciences. Its basic task is to conduct basic theoretical research in nuclear physics, such as studying the properties of elementary particles, studying how to apply atomic rays and radioactive isotopes in science and technology and in industrial and agricultural production, training skilled personnel in atomic power engineering, and assisting other scientific institutions in Central Asia in the utilization of atomic energy.

### 中亚地区兴建原子核研究所

1958-05-06
第5版()
专栏：苏联科技简讯

    中亚地区兴建原子核研究所
    在乌兹别克共和国首都塔什干郊区风景优美的基勃拉斯地区，一座科学城正在兴建起来，这是中亚细亚地区的第一个原子中心——乌兹别克科学院原子核物理研究所。它的基本任务是进行原子核物理学方面的基本理论的研究，如研究基本粒子的特性，研究如何在科学技术中，在工农业生产中应用原子射线和放射性同位素，培养原子动力工程方面熟练干部，协助中亚细亚地区其他科学机构利用原子能等。
