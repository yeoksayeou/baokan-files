Title: The Soviet Union Again Calls on Britain to Stop Hydrogen Bomb Tests; The British Government Must Be Held Responsible for the Consequences of Continuing This Dangerous Step
Author:
Date: 1958-05-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 14th: TASS reported on the 14th that the Soviet government sent a note to the British government on the 12th, expressing deep regret that Britain, despite the Soviet Union having ceased nuclear weapons testing, still conducted a hydrogen bomb explosion on April 28th of this year in the Christmas Island area of the Pacific. It also stated that the British government must bear serious responsibility for the possible consequences of its dangerous step.

The Soviet government's note pointed out that the British government's recent hydrogen bomb explosion created serious obstacles to resolving the issue of universal cessation of nuclear weapons testing. The British government's arbitrary designation of vast areas within international shipping and aviation routes in the Pacific's Christmas Island region as danger zones for nuclear weapons testing directly violates international law principles regarding the freedom of the high seas. Testing nuclear weapons in the aforementioned area threatens the lives and health of residents on nearby islands, obstructs freedom of navigation and fishing activities, and also infringes upon the rights of other countries. The Soviet government cannot agree to such actions by the British government.

The Soviet government once again urgently calls on the British government to reconsider its attitude, cease testing atomic and hydrogen weapons, as a practical measure towards stopping the atomic arms race and eliminating the threat of atomic war.

### 苏联再次呼吁英国停试氢弹  英政府应对继续试验这种危险步骤的后果负责

1958-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联再次呼吁英国停试氢弹
    英政府应对继续试验这种危险步骤的后果负责
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科14日讯：苏联政府12日照会英国政府，对它在苏联已经停止核武器试验的情况下仍然于今年4月28日在太平洋圣诞岛地区进行氢弹爆炸一事表示深感遗憾，并且声明：英国政府应对它的这一危险步骤所可能产生的后果负严重责任。
    苏联政府的照会指出，英国政府最近进行的氢弹爆炸在解决普遍停止核武器试验的问题上制造了严重障碍。英国政府在太平洋圣诞岛地区的国际航海和航空线上任意划定广阔地区作为危险区来进行核武器试验，这一做法也直接违反了有关公海自由的国际法原则。在上述地区试验核武器威胁附近岛屿居民的生命和健康，妨碍了航海自由和捕鱼工作，也损害了其他国家的权利。苏联政府不能同意英国政府的这种行为。
    苏联政府再次向英国政府发出迫切的呼吁，要求英国政府考虑自己的态度，停止试验原子武器和氢武器，以作为走向停止原子军备竞赛消除原子战争威胁的实际措施。
