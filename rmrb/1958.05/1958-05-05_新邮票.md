    New Stamps
    Today, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications is issuing a set of two commemorative stamps for the 140th anniversary of Marx's birth. The eight-fen stamp features a half-length portrait of Marx in 1867; the twenty-two-fen stamp depicts Marx giving a report at the German Workers' Educational Association in London. ### 新邮票

1958-05-05
第8版()
专栏：

    新邮票
    今天邮电部发行马克思诞生一百四十周年纪念邮票一套，共两张。面值八分的是马克思1867年半身像；面值二十二分的是马克思正在伦敦德国工人教育协会上作报告。（附图片）
