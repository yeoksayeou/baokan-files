Title: Vietnamese National Assembly Delegation to Visit Eastern Europe
Author:
Date: 1958-05-13
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: A delegation from the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, Ton Duc Thang, is about to visit some socialist countries in Eastern Europe.

### 越国会代表团将访东欧

1958-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　越国会代表团将访东欧
    据新华社讯　越南民主共和国国会代表团在国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜率领下，即将访问东欧一些社会主义国家。
