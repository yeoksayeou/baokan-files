Title: Do Not Kill Ruzbeh! Ruzbeh's Wife Calls on UN Secretary-General to Intervene! Public Figures in Various Countries Demand the Iranian Government Rescind the Verdict

Author: 
Date: 1958-05-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th: The wife of the famous Iranian patriot Khosrow Ruzbeh, who is currently studying in the German Democratic Republic, has once again appealed to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, requesting him to urge the Iranian authorities to rescind the death sentence against Ruzbeh.

The news of the Iranian authorities' decision to execute Ruzbeh has aroused the anger of the teachers and students of the foreign student class at Karl Marx University in Leipzig. In their letter to the Shah of Iran, all 300 teachers and students in the class demanded the immediate cancellation of the death sentence against Ruzbeh and his immediate release.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: People's organizations and prominent figures in Albania and Romania strongly condemned the Iranian authorities' decision to execute Ruzbeh and demanded the cancellation of this verdict.

Xinhua News Agency, May 7th: (TASS, Paris, 6th) According to "L'Humanité," the Iranian Embassy in Paris, the president of the Tehran Military Court, and the heads of the Iranian government have received many telegrams and letters demanding amnesty for Ruzbeh, the famous Iranian social activist sentenced to death. The newspaper reported that staff at Paris Orly Airport are collecting signatures to demand amnesty for Ruzbeh.

### 不准杀害鲁兹贝赫  鲁兹贝赫夫人呼吁联合国秘书长出面干涉  各国公众要伊朗政府取消判决

1958-05-08
第6版()
专栏：

    不准杀害鲁兹贝赫
    鲁兹贝赫夫人呼吁联合国秘书长出面干涉
    各国公众要伊朗政府取消判决
    据新华社柏林7日电　目前在德意志民主共和国学习的伊朗著名爱国者霍斯劳·鲁兹贝赫的妻子再次向联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德发出呼吁，要求他促使伊朗当局取消对鲁兹贝赫的死刑判决。
    伊朗当局决定处死鲁兹贝赫的消息，引起来比锡卡尔·马克思大学外国留学生班师生的愤怒。全班三百多名师生在他们写给伊朗国王的信中，要求立即取消对鲁兹贝赫的死刑判决，并且立即把他释放。
    据新华社7日讯　阿尔巴尼亚、罗马尼亚的人民团体、知名人士强烈谴责伊朗当局决定处死鲁兹贝赫，并且要求取消这一判决。
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社巴黎6日讯：据“人道报”报道，伊朗驻巴黎大使馆、德黑兰军事法庭庭长和伊朗政府首脑收到了许多电报和信，要求赦免被判处死刑的伊朗著名社会活动家鲁兹贝赫。据报纸报道，巴黎奥利机场的工作人员正在为要求赦免鲁兹贝赫而征集签名。
