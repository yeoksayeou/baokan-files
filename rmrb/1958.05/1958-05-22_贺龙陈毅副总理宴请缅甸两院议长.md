Title: He Long and Chen Yi Hold Banquet for Burmese Speakers of Parliament
Author:
Date: 1958-05-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi held a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the Speaker of the Burmese Federal Parliament's House of Nationalities, Sao Hkun Hkio, and his wife, the Speaker of the House of Representatives, Bo Mu Aung, and his wife, as well as their entourage.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu attended the banquet. Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy in China, U Ba Maung, was also invited to attend the banquet.

The distinguished guests were also accompanied today by Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Member Gao Chongmin, and Deputy Secretary-General Liu Guanyi, etc., to visit the Ming Tombs Reservoir construction site, the Great Wall, and the Summer Palace.

### 贺龙陈毅副总理宴请缅甸两院议长

1958-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙陈毅副总理宴请缅甸两院议长
    新华社21日讯　国务院副总理贺龙和陈毅今天晚间举行宴会，欢送缅甸联邦国会民族院议长萧恢塔和夫人、代表院议长波木昂和夫人以及他们的随行人员。
    外交部副部长章汉夫出席了宴会。缅甸驻中国大使馆临时代办吴巴茂也应邀出席了宴会。
    贵宾们今天还由全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、陈叔通，委员高崇民和副秘书长刘贯一等陪同，参观了十三陵水库工地，游览了长城和颐和园。
