Title: Soap Water for Pain Relief
Author: Fan Longfei
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 8

Why

    Soap Water for Pain Relief
    When you are stung by a bee, applying some soap water can alleviate the pain. The pain from a bee sting is caused by the bee injecting its formic acid into the skin. Soap water is alkaline, and it can neutralize the formic acid, gradually reducing the amount of formic acid in the skin, and the feeling of pain will disappear accordingly.
        (Fan Longfei)

### 肥皂水止痛
范龙飞
1958-05-20
第8版()
专栏：为什么

    肥皂水止痛
    当你被蜂刺后，如果擦上一些肥皂水，就可以减轻痛楚。因为蜂刺后所以感到痛，是由于蜂把它的蚁酸注入人的皮肤里而引起的。肥皂水是碱性，它能与蚁酸中和，使皮肤里的蚁酸逐渐减少，痛的感觉也就随着消失。
        （范龙飞）
