Title: Joint Institute for Nuclear Research Achieves Fruitful Results: Building World's Only Bubble Chamber to Search for New Particles; Largest Nuclear Machine Provides Abundant Materials
Author:
Date: 1958-05-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: According to TASS, the Academic Committee meeting of the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research continued on the 21st in Dubna, near Moscow. Professor Wang Ganchang, the Chinese scientist leading the construction of the propane bubble chamber, presented a report on the structure and parameters of this giant bubble chamber at the meeting. Reports on research using the synchrophasotron were given by Skrzypczak of Poland, Lyubimova and Bogachev of the Soviet Union, and Wang Shufen of China.

In several reports on the synchrophasotron, it was pointed out that this huge, ten-billion-electron-volt nuclear machine, which recently began operation, has already provided physicists with abundant materials that will help to deeply explore the secrets of the structure of matter. Currently, no other laboratory in the world can conduct such high-energy particle experiments. The reports mentioned some significant materials that have been obtained, which explain the properties of "elementary" particles and their interactions with high-energy protons.

The propane bubble chamber, a unique research device, is used to study the interaction of high-energy particles with hydrogen and helium nuclei. Scientific personnel from the Soviet Union, China, and Vietnam participated in the construction of this bubble chamber. One of the tasks of the research using this bubble chamber is to search for a new particle that no one has yet discovered—the anti-hyperon. On the same day, Professor Richter also reported on the construction of a giant laboratory in the German Democratic Republic that processes special photographic emulsions.

### 联合原子核研究所成果丰硕  建造世界唯一气泡室寻找新粒子  最大原子核机器提供了丰富材料

1958-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    联合原子核研究所成果丰硕
    建造世界唯一气泡室寻找新粒子  最大原子核机器提供了丰富材料
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社讯：联合原子核研究所学术委员会会议21日继续在莫斯科近郊的杜布纳举行。领导建造丙烷气泡室的中国科学家王淦昌教授在会上作了关于这个巨型气泡室的结构和参数的报告。波兰的斯克日普恰克、苏联的柳比莫娃和鲍加乔夫、中国的王树芬做了关于用同步稳相加速器进行的研究的报告。
    在关于同步稳相加速器的一些报告中指出，这个前不久开始使用的、一百亿电子伏特的巨大的原子核机器，已经向物理学家提供了有助于深入探索物质结构秘密的丰富材料。现在世界上还没有其它任何一个实验室能进行这样高能量的粒子试验。报告中谈到了已经获得的一些说明“基本”粒子性质和它们同高能质子相互作用的有意义的材料。
    丙烷气泡室，这个独一无二的研究装置，是用来研究高能粒子同氢核和氦核相互作用的。参加制造这个气泡室的有苏联、中国和越南的一些科学工作人员。用这个气泡室进行研究的任务之一，就是寻找一种任何人还未发现的新粒子——反超子。这一天，李赫特尔教授还报告了民主德国一所加工特种照相乳胶的巨型实验室的建造情况。
