Title: Jinan Ironworks Blast Furnace Completed - First Batch of Molten Iron Flows

Author: 
Date: 1958-05-04
Page: 2

Jinan Ironworks Blast Furnace Completed
    First Batch of Molten Iron Flows

    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 2nd. The first blast furnace of the Jinan Ironworks in Shandong Province officially went into production on May 1st, and the first batch of molten iron flowed out. This blast furnace can produce 100 tons of pig iron for casting every day. The Jinan Ironworks began construction in October last year. (Attached picture)
    The No. 3 blast furnace of the Jinan Ironworks under construction. Photo by Sun Jing, reporter of Xinhua News Agency

### 济南铁厂高炉完工  流出了第一炉铁水

1958-05-04
第2版()
专栏：

    济南铁厂高炉完工
    流出了第一炉铁水
    据新华社济南2日电  山东省济南铁厂第一座高炉5月1日正式投入生产，流出了第一炉铁水。这座高炉每天可以生产铸造生铁一百吨。济南铁厂是去年10月开工兴建的。（附图片）
