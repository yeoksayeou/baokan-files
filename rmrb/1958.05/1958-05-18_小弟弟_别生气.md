Title: Little Brother, Don't Be Angry
Author: Shen Pengnian
Date: 1958-05-18
Page: 4

　　Little Brother, Don't Be Angry
    (Children's Doggerel)
    Shen Pengnian
　　Little brother, don't be angry,
　　Big brother will explain the reasoning to you.
　　Mom's attending an election meeting,
　　To discuss important state matters.
　　Discussing important matters requires careful thought,
　　How could she bring you and me along!
　　If you're angry, grow up quickly,
　　Grow up and go to the factory,
　　Go to the factory, operate machines,
　　You'll go to the election meeting too!
　　If you're angry, grow up quickly,
　　Grow up and go to the farm,
　　Go to the farm to drive a tractor,
　　You'll go to the election meeting too!
　　If you're angry, grow up quickly,
　　Grow up and defend the country,
　　Join the People's Liberation Army, fly airplanes,
　　You'll go to the election meeting too!
　　Little brother, don't be angry,
　　Big brother will explain the reasoning to you.
　　Is the reasoning I've given correct,
　　Please give your opinions too!

### 小弟弟　别生气
沈彭年
1958-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　小弟弟　别生气
    （儿童小快板）
    沈彭年
　　小弟弟，别生气，
　　哥哥给你讲道理。
　　妈妈开的选举会，
　　商量国家大事体。
　　商量大事要仔细，
　　怎么能带我和你！
　　你要生气你快长，
　　长大以后进工厂，
　　进工厂，开机器，
　　选举大会你也去！
　　你要生气你快长，
　　长大以后到农场，
　　农场去开拖拉机，
　　选举大会你也去！
　　你要生气你快长，
　　长大以后保国防，
　　解放军，开飞机，
　　选举大会你也去！
　　小弟弟，别生气，
　　哥哥给你讲道理。
　　道理讲得对不对，
　　请你也把意见提！
