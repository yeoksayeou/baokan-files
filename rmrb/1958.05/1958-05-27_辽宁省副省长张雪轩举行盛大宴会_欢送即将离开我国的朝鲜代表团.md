Title: Liaoning Province Vice Governor Zhang Xuexuan Hosts Grand Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Departing North Korean Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-05-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, May 26th: Liaoning Province Vice Governor Zhang Xuexuan hosted a grand farewell banquet tonight for the North Korean People's Delegation visiting China, who are soon to depart.

Vice Minister of Internal Affairs Wang Yifu, who accompanied the delegation on their visits throughout the country, was invited to attend the banquet.

Also invited to the banquet were the heads of the North Korean Consulate General in Shenyang and the North Korean Children's Education Office, as well as the presidents of North Korean primary schools and junior technical colleges in the three northeastern provinces.

After the banquet began, Zhang Xuexuan first represented the Liaoning Provincial People's Committee and the people of the entire province in bidding farewell to the friendly envoys of the heroic North Korean people, and once again expressed heartfelt thanks to the distinguished guests for coming to China for a friendly visit despite their hard work. He expressed that they would make the greatest effort to do a good job in the repatriation of war orphans, so that they could return to their homeland smoothly to participate in socialist construction.

Head of Delegation Xu Zhenshu gave a speech in which she sincerely thanked the Chinese people for their warm hospitality and friendly sentiments during the delegation's visit to China, and congratulated them on the brilliant achievements of the Chinese people in building socialism, which they had witnessed during their visit. She went on to say that the delegation had seen the children of Korean patriotic martyrs and war orphans being nurtured and educated in the embrace of the Chinese people. The Chinese people have sincerely and wholeheartedly cared for them for six years, day after day, in order to help them master advanced production techniques. The delegation was deeply moved by this noble undertaking of the Chinese people. She stated that she would convey the profound friendship of the Chinese people to the North Korean people.

Wang Yifu also spoke at the banquet, thanking the delegation on behalf of the State Council for the friendship and encouragement they had brought to the Chinese people.

After the banquet, the guests and hosts watched a musical and dance performance together.

This morning, Vice Minister of Internal Affairs Wang Yifu and Vice Governor of Liaoning Province Zhang Xuexuan exchanged views with the delegation in a friendly atmosphere on issues such as the phased return of war orphans to North Korea and the temporary stay of some orphans in China for internships and visits.

### 辽宁省副省长张雪轩举行盛大宴会  欢送即将离开我国的朝鲜代表团

1958-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    辽宁省副省长张雪轩举行盛大宴会
    欢送即将离开我国的朝鲜代表团
    据新华社沈阳26日电　辽宁省副省长张雪轩今晚为朝鲜人民访华代表团即将离开中国举行盛大欢送宴会。
    陪同代表团到各地参观的内务部副部长王一夫应邀参加了宴会。
    应邀参加宴会的还有朝鲜驻沈阳总领事馆和朝鲜儿童教育处的负责人以及在东北三省的朝鲜初等学院初级技术学院的院长。
    宴会开始后，张雪轩首先代表辽宁省人民委员会和全省人民欢送英雄的朝鲜人民的友好使者，并对贵宾们不辞辛苦地来到我国作友好访问再一次表示衷心的感谢。他表示一定要尽最大努力作好战灾孤儿的回国工作，使他们顺利地回到祖国参加社会主义建设。
    许贞琡团长在讲话中衷心感谢中国人民在代表团访问中国期间的热情款待和友好情谊，并且对在参观中看到的中国人民建设社会主义的辉煌成就表示祝贺。她接着说，代表团看到了在中国人民怀抱里受到抚养和教育的朝鲜爱国烈士的子女和战灾孤儿。中国人民为了帮助他们掌握先进的生产技术，六年如一日地诚心诚意地关怀着他们。中国人民的这种高贵业迹使代表团受到了极大的感动。她表示一定要把中国人民对朝鲜人民的深厚友谊转达给朝鲜人民。
    王一夫也在宴会上讲了话，他代表国务院感谢代表团给中国人民带来的友情和鼓舞。
    宴会后，宾主一起观看了音乐歌舞节目。
    今天上午，内务部副部长王一夫和辽宁省副省长张雪轩同代表团在友好的气氛中就战灾孤儿分批回朝鲜和部分孤儿暂时留在中国实习参观等问题交换了意见。
