Title: "Yellow River" Off-Road Vehicle
Author: Gao Wenxian
Date: 1958-05-03
Page: 3

New Product

    "Yellow River" Off-Road Vehicle
    Three automobile repair factories in Jinan collaborated to produce a small off-road vehicle named "Yellow River." This vehicle has a top speed of 90 kilometers per hour, a load capacity of 1,000 kilograms, and can tow a trailer with a total weight of 800 kilograms. It can travel on uncultivated land and is suitable for rural areas and field exploration. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Gao Wenxian (with picture)

### “黄河牌”越野汽车
高文献
1958-05-03
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    “黄河牌”越野汽车
    济南三个汽车修理工厂协作制成一辆名叫“黄河牌”小型越野汽车。这种汽车的最高时速为九十公里，载重量一千公斤，还能拖带总量八百公斤的挂车，能在荒野地面上行驶，适宜于农村以及野外勘探使用。　　新华社记者　高文献摄（附图片）
