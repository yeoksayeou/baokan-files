Title: Fan Wheel Duster (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 2

    Fan Wheel Duster
    Invented by Wang Buquan, Shoulu Township, Dingxiang County, Shanxi Province
    This is made using the principle of a windmill; it is labor-saving and easy to operate.
    One person can operate it and dust two mu of seedling fields per hour.
