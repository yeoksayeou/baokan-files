Title: Everyone Waves and Cheers to Send the Third Great Sputnik into Space
Author: Zang Kejia
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 8

Everyone Waves and Cheers
　　——Sending the Third Great Sputnik into Space
　　Zang Kejia
　　You have flown into the sky again,
　　Riding the strong east wind,
　　Everyone waves and cheers to you,
　　Like seeing off a friend on a long journey.
　　You are a robust traveler,
　　With rapid steps and great ambition,
　　Bearing the glorious mission of peace-loving people,
　　To travel in outer space.
　　When flying close to the moon,
　　Say hello for us:
　　Guests from the human world will visit soon,
　　Please ask Chang'e to tidy up the Guanghan Palace.
　　If you meet that unlucky "explorer" on the way,
　　I don't think he'll be in a good mood,
　　A shorty standing below a red giant,
　t make him blush, he will blush anyway.
　　The Soviet satellite is strong and big,
　　America has a "Vanguard",
　　Invisible to the naked eye,
　　Shrinking and weak,
　　Congenitally deficient and small in body.
　　The Soviet satellite is big and strong,
　　Its footsteps resound in the air,
　　Most people are happy to hear it,
　　A few people are frightened to hear it.
            Morning of May 16th

### 人人向你招手，欢呼——送第三颗大卫星上天
臧克家
1958-05-17
第8版()
专栏：

　　人人向你招手，欢呼
　　——送第三颗大卫星上天
　　臧克家
　　你又飞上了天空，
　　乘着强烈的东风，
　　人人向你招手，欢呼，
　　像送一个朋友远行。
　　你是一个强健的旅行家，
　　脚步神速气魄大，
　　负着和平人民的光荣使命，
　　到宇宙空间去旅行。
　　飞近月球的时候，
　　替我们招呼一声：
　　人间的客人不久要来访，
　　请嫦娥打扫一下广寒宫。
　　路上遇见那个倒霉的“探险者”，
　　我想他不会有好的心情，
　　红色巨人下站个矮子，
　　不叫他脸红他也会脸红。
　　苏联的卫星壮又大
　　美国有个“先锋号”，
　　人的肉眼看不到，
　　萎萎缩缩虚弱症，
　　先天不足身体小。
　　苏联的卫星大又壮，
　　凌空脚步发声响，
　　多数人听了心欢喜，
　　少数人听了心发慌。
            5月16日早
