Title: Albania and the United Arab Republic Issue Joint Communiqué, Decide to Strengthen Cultural Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1958-05-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 13th - After talks here between visiting Minister of State Guidance of the United Arab Republic, Radwan, and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Education and Culture of Albania, Mufti, a joint communiqué was issued stating that, out of a shared desire to further strengthen the friendship and cultural cooperation between the two countries, Albania and the United Arab Republic will further strengthen cooperation in science, education, literature, art, sports, and press and publication on the basis of mutual respect.

Both sides decided to exchange workers and students in science, art, and education, and agreed to sign a cultural cooperation agreement between the two countries in the near future.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 13th - In order to further strengthen the friendly relations between the two governments, Albania and the United Arab Republic have decided to upgrade their respective legations to embassies.

The Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly appointed Ulvi Lulo on May 13th as Albania's first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the United Arab Republic.

### 阿尔巴尼亚和阿联发表会谈公报  决定加强两国文化合作

1958-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚和阿联发表会谈公报
    决定加强两国文化合作
    新华社地拉那13日电　正在阿尔巴尼亚访问的阿拉伯联合共和国国家指导部部长拉德温和阿尔巴尼亚部长会议第一副主席、教育和文化部部长穆夫蒂乌在这里会谈后发表公报指出，阿尔巴尼亚和阿拉伯联合共和国出于进一步加强两国的友谊和文化合作的共同愿望，将在互相尊重的基础上进一步加强两国在科学、教育、文学、艺术、体育和新闻出版等方面的合作。
    双方决定互派科学、艺术、教育等方面的工作者和留学生，并且同意在最近期间签订两国文化合作协定。
    新华社地拉那13日电　阿尔巴尼亚和阿拉伯联合共和国两国政府为了进一步加强两国的友好关系，决定将彼此的公使馆升格为大使馆。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团已经在13日任命乌耳维·卢勒为阿尔巴尼亚首任驻阿拉伯联合共和国特命全权大使。
