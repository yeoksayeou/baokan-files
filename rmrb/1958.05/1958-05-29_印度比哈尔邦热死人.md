Title: Deaths Due to Heat in Bihar, India
Author:
Date: 1958-05-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 27th: According to the Press Trust of India, temperatures in some parts of Bihar, India, have recently reached as high as 112 to 114 degrees Fahrenheit, and at least twenty-four people have died of heatstroke in the state.

### 印度比哈尔邦热死人

1958-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    印度比哈尔邦热死人
    据新华社新德里27日电  据印度报业托辣斯报道，印度比哈尔邦的一些地方最近气温高达华氏一百一十二度至一百一十四度，在这个邦里至少已经热死了二十四个人。
