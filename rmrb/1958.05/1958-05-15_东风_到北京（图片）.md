Author: Jì Liánbō
Date: 1958-05-15
Page: 1

　　"Dongfeng" Arrives in Beijing
　　China's first domestically produced car, the "Dongfeng" brand sedan, arrived in Beijing on May 14th as a gift to the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China. Xinhua News Agency reporter Ji Lianbo.
