    Amazing Facts
    The banyan tree in southern China is a unique plant. Many aerial roots protrude from its branches and trunks. These aerial roots often grow downwards, and when they touch the ground, they become another trunk. The largest banyan tree can have more than a thousand main trunks. In India, there is a banyan tree under which seven thousand people can stand.

### 奇妙的事实

1958-05-15
第8版()
专栏：

    奇妙的事实
    我国南方的榕树是一种奇特的植物，从树枝树干上都会突出许多气根，气根往往从上向下，碰到地面就成另一树干。最大的榕树，一株可以长达一千多根主干，印度有一株榕树下面可以立七千人。
