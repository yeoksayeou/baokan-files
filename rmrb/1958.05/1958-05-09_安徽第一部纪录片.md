Title: Anhui's First Documentary

Author: 
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 1

    Anhui's First Documentary
    After the establishment of the preparatory office of the Anhui Film Studio, the first film, "Anhui News" No. 1, has been completed. This documentary was first screened in Hefei on May 1st. At present, the preparatory office is actively preparing for the construction of the factory, preparing to start shooting feature films and stage art films next year. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 安徽第一部纪录片

1958-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    安徽第一部纪录片
    安徽省电影制片厂筹备处成立后，已摄制完成第一部影片——“安徽新闻”第一号。这部纪录片已在5月1日首先在合肥市放映。现在，筹备处积极筹备建厂，准备明年开始拍摄故事片和舞台艺术片。　（新华社）
