Title: Powered Water Dragon (Picture)
Author: Lü Xiangyou
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 2

    Powered Water Dragon
  　This waterwheel is driven by a gas engine and has a very large discharge capacity of 351 tons per hour. When using gas engines, the Hongxing Cooperative in Ninghe County, Hebei Province, creatively used brick and adobe to build the gas furnace wall, changing the previously used method of using steel plates for the furnace wall.

### 动力水龙（图片）
吕相友
1958-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

    动力水龙
  　这种水车是利用煤气机带动，出水量很大，每小时三百五十一吨。河北宁河县红星社在利用煤气机时，创造性地使用了砖石土坯来砌煤气炉壁，改变了一向沿用的使用钢板作炉壁的办法。
