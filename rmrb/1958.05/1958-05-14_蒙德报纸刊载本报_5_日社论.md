Title: Mongolian Newspapers Publish This Newspaper's May 5th Editorial
Author:
Date: 1958-05-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Mongolian "Unen" (Truth) and the East German "Neues Deutschland" (New Germany) respectively published excerpts and the full text of this newspaper's May 5th editorial on the 8th and 13th.
