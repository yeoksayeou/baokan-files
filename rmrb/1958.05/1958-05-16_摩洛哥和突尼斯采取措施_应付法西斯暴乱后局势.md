Title: Morocco and Tunisia Take Measures to Deal with the Situation After the Fascist Riot
Author:
Date: 1958-05-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Rabat Dispatch: The Moroccan government held an emergency meeting yesterday to study the situation in Algeria. The situation in Algeria is very chaotic because French far-right elements and some colonial soldiers launched riots on the 13th.

After the meeting, the Moroccan government announced that it had decided to take "emergency measures" and "certain diplomatic steps."

Sources said that the meeting decided to close the Moroccan-Algerian border and cut off all telecommunications between the two countries.

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: According to TASS Paris: According to Paris Radio reports, the fascist riots in Algeria have caused concern in Tunisia. The Tunisian government is watching the development of the situation. Instructions on taking security measures have been distributed to the governors of various provinces. It was announced in Tunis on the morning of the 14th that any telephone contact between Algeria and Tunisia has been interrupted.

### 摩洛哥和突尼斯采取措施  应付法西斯暴乱后局势

1958-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　摩洛哥和突尼斯采取措施
　　应付法西斯暴乱后局势
    新华社15日讯　拉巴特消息：摩洛哥政府昨天举行紧急会议来研究阿尔及利亚的局势。由于法国极右翼分子和部分殖民军人在13日发动了暴乱，阿尔及利亚的局势非常混乱。
    会后摩洛哥政府宣布，它已经决定采取“紧急措施”和“某些外交步骤”。
    有消息说，会议决定封锁摩洛哥—阿尔及利亚边界，并且断绝了两国之间的一切电讯来往。
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：据巴黎电台报道，阿尔及利亚的法西斯暴乱在突尼斯引起了忧虑。突尼斯政府正在注视着局势的发展。关于采取安全措施的指令已经分发给各省省长。据14日早晨在突尼斯宣布，阿尔及利亚和突尼斯之间的任何电话联系已经中断。
