Title: Indonesian Communist Party Second Deputy General Secretary Djoto Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd—Djoto, Second Deputy General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, and Shama, a staff worker from the Propaganda and Education Department of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee; and Li Qixin, a responsible staff member of the central organs of the CCP.

### 印度尼西亚共产党第二副总书记约多到京

1958-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党第二副总书记约多到京
    新华社22日讯　印度尼西亚共产党第二副总书记约多和印度尼西亚共产党中央宣传鼓动部工作人员沙玛乘飞机于今天下午到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中共中央总书记邓小平，中央书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员刘宁一，中共中央机关负责工作人员李启新等。
