Title: A Poem and a Painting
Author: Zuo Hai, Yu Jiming, Luo Yongliang, Guo Zhenhua
Date: 1958-05-13
Page: 8

　　A Poem and a Painting
　　Picking up manure every morning is commonplace,
　　Coming home from school, the basket is full again.
　　Born and raised in a farmer's family, retaining true colors,
　　A good lad in the Young Pioneers.
        Zuo Hai, Poem
　　    Yu Jiming, Luo Yongliang, Guo Zhenhua, Painting

　　一诗一画
