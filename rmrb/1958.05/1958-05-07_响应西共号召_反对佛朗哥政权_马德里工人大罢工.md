Title: Responding to the Call of the Spanish Communist Party, Workers in Madrid Strike Against the Franco Regime
Author:
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Madrid dispatch: Responding to the call to hold a "National Reconciliation Day," tens of thousands of industrial workers in the Spanish capital staged a political strike and slowdown yesterday against the Franco dictatorship.

The slogan of "National Reconciliation" was put forward by the Spanish Communist Party last autumn, calling on all forces opposed to Franco, regardless of political affiliation and religious belief, to unite and fight to overthrow the dictatorship.

### 响应西共号召　反对佛朗哥政权  马德里工人大罢工

1958-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    响应西共号召　反对佛朗哥政权
    马德里工人大罢工
    新华社6日讯　马德里消息：西班牙首都成千成万的工业工人响应举行“全国和解日”的号召，在昨天举行了反对佛朗哥独裁统治的政治性罢工和怠工。
    “全国和解”的口号是西班牙共产党在去年秋天提出的，它号召反对佛朗哥的一切力量，不分政治倾向和宗教信仰，团结起来，为推翻独裁政权而斗争。
