Author:
Date: 1958-05-22

	Construction Flowers

	Capital's First Pair of New Shafts
	Xinhua News Agency, 21st - The first pair of shafts above the quota in Beijing's first Five-Year Plan, the Dattai Shafts of the Jingxi Mining Bureau, officially went into production on the 20th. This pair of shafts was designed by our country itself, and most of the mining equipment was supplied domestically. After acceptance, the engineering quality fully meets the design requirements. The production process of the mine is fully mechanized from hoisting to mineral processing and loading. After being put into production, it can produce 200,000 tons of coal for the country this year.

### 首都第一对新竖井

1958-05-22
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    首都第一对新竖井
    新华社21日讯　北京第一个五年计划期间动工兴建的第一对限额以上的竖井——京西矿务局大台竖井，20日正式投入生产。这对竖井是由我国自行设计的，矿井设备大部分由国内供应。经过验收，工程质量完全符合设计要求。矿井的生产过程从提升到选矿、装车全部机械化。投入生产后，今年就可以给国家生产二十万吨煤。
