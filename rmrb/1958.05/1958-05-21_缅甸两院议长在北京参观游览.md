Title: Burmese Speakers of Both Houses of Parliament Visit and Tour Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The Chairman of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Burmese Federal Parliament, Sao Hkun Hkio, and his wife, the Chairman of the Chamber of Deputies, Bo Khin Maung, and his wife, along with their entourage, today visited the Temple of Heaven Park and the Palace Museum, and also visited the state-owned Beijing Electronic Tube Factory.

Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and members Gao Chongmin, Deputy Secretary-General Liu Guanyi and others accompanied the distinguished guests on their visits and tours.

In the evening, all the distinguished guests attended a soiree held by Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing, and watched acrobatics and Peking opera performances at the soiree.

### 缅甸两院议长在北京参观游览

1958-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸两院议长在北京参观游览
    据新华社20日讯　缅甸联邦国会民族院议长萧恢塔和夫人、代表院议长波木昂和夫人以及他们的随员，今天先后游览了天坛公园和故宫博物院，并参观了国营北京电子管厂。
    人大常委会副委员长李济深、委员高崇民、副秘书长刘贯一等陪同贵宾们游览和参观。
    晚间，全体贵宾们出席人大常委会副委员长、北京市市长彭真举行的晚会，观看了晚会上演出的杂技和京剧。
