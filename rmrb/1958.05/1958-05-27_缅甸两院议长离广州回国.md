Title: Burmese Speakers of the Two Houses of Parliament Depart Guangzhou to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1958-05-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 26th. The Speaker of the House of Nationalities of the Burmese Federal Parliament, Sao Hkun Hkio, and his wife, the Speaker of the House of Representatives, Bo Khin Maung, and his wife, along with their entourage, concluded their visit to our country today and left Guangzhou for their home country via Hong Kong.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Wei Jinfei, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhang Ruiquan, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, and people from all walks of life in the province and city. Liu Guanyi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, who accompanied them on visits to various places, also accompanied the distinguished guests to Shenzhen.

After visiting Beijing, the Speakers of the two houses of the Burmese Federal Parliament and their wives successively visited the Yellow River Diversion Project near Zhengzhou, the Wuhan Iron and Steel United Enterprise and the Yangtze River Bridge in Wuhan. The distinguished guests also visited Shaoshan Chong, Xiangtan County, Hunan, the hometown of our leader Chairman Mao.

The distinguished guests arrived in Guangzhou by special plane on the 25th. They were welcomed by Wei Jinfei, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhang Ruiquan, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, and others. Afterwards, they visited the China Export Commodities Fair and the Guangzhou Paper Mill, and also carefully visited the Guangdong Provincial Agricultural Implement Reform Promotion and Appraisal Exhibition.

On the evening of the 25th, Wei Jinfei, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, hosted a banquet at the Yingbin Hotel to bid farewell to the distinguished guests.

### 缅甸两院议长离广州回国

1958-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸两院议长离广州回国
    新华社广州26日电　缅甸联邦国会民族院议长萧恢塔和夫人、代表院议长波木昂和夫人以及他们的随行人员，今天结束在我国的访问，离开广州取道香港回国。
    到车站欢送的有广东省副省长魏今非，广州市副市长张瑞权和省、市各界人士。陪他们到各地参观访问的全国人民代表大会常委会副秘书长刘贯一还陪送贵宾到深圳。
    缅甸联邦国会两院议长和他们的夫人，访问北京后，先后参观了郑州附近的引黄济卫工程，武汉市的武汉钢铁联合企业和长江大桥。贵宾们还访问了我国领袖毛主席的故乡——湖南湘潭县韶山冲。
    贵宾们是25日乘专机到广州的。受到广东省副省长魏今非，广州市副市长张瑞权等人的欢迎。以后参观了我国出口商品陈列馆、广州造纸厂，还仔细地参观了广东省农具改革推广评比展览会。
    25日晚上，广东省副省长魏今非曾在迎宾馆设宴欢送贵宾。
