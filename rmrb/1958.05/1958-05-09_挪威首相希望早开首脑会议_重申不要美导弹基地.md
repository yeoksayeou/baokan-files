Title: Norwegian Prime Minister Hopes for Early Summit, Reaffirms No US Missile Bases
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 8th: Norwegian Prime Minister Gerhardsen said upon arriving in New York on the 7th that Norway hopes a summit meeting of major powers will be held soon. He said, "The Norwegian people hope to hold such a meeting."
	Gerhardsen reiterated his opposition to the establishment of US atomic rocket bases in Norway. In response to a reporter's question about the establishment of US atomic rocket bases in Europe, he said that Norway strictly adheres to a policy of non-involvement. He said, "Today's world situation makes it unnecessary for a small country like Norway, which borders the Soviet Union, to possess such weapons."
	Gerhardsen is in the United States to attend the centennial celebration of the state of Minnesota. This state is the center of immigration for Nordic countries in the United States.

### 挪威首相希望早开首脑会议  重申不要美导弹基地

1958-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    挪威首相希望早开首脑会议
    重申不要美导弹基地
    据新华社8日讯　纽约消息：挪威首相基哈德森7日在到达纽约时说，挪威希望早日召开大国最高级会议。他说，“挪威人民希望举行这种会议。”
    基哈德森重申反对在挪威建立美国原子火箭基地。他在回答新闻记者关于在欧洲建立美国原子火箭基地的问题时说，挪威严格遵守不介入的政策。他说，“今天的世界局势使得像挪威这样同苏联接壤的小国，没有必要拥有这种武器。”
    基哈德森是到美国参加明尼苏达州建州一百周年纪念的。这个州是北欧国家在美国的移民的中心。
