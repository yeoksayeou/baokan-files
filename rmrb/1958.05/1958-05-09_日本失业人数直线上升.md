Title: Japan's Unemployment Rate Rises Sharply
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 6

    Japan's Unemployment Rate Rises Sharply
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 8th – News: Amidst the economic crisis, Japan's unemployment rate is increasing dramatically. According to the announcement today by the Statistics Bureau of the Japanese Prime Minister's Office, by the end of March, the number of fully unemployed people had reached 850,000, an increase of 250,000 from the end of February. This figure is the highest record of unemployment in Japan since March 1956.
    Japanese authorities admit that the forced production cuts in textile factories, mines, and various manufacturing industries have resulted in mass layoffs everywhere. It seems the unemployment rate will continue to rise sharply.

### 日本失业人数直线上升

1958-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    日本失业人数直线上升
    新华社8日讯　东京消息：在经济危机声中，日本失业人数剧烈增加。据日本总理府统计局今天宣布，到3月底为止，完全失业的人数已经达到八十五万人，比2月底增加了二十五万人。这个数字是1956年3月以来日本失业人数的最高纪录。
    日本当局承认，它对纺织工厂、矿山和各种制造工业强行削减生产的结果，造成到处大批解雇工人的情况。看来失业人数还要直线上升。
