    "The Path of Modern Agricultural Construction in Our Country"
    "Young Communist Leaguer" Series, Volume 4, "The Path of Modern Agricultural Construction in Our Country," has been published. This collection introduces the achievements and experiences of agricultural construction in our country over the past few years, and explains the basic policies and important measures related to the current agricultural construction in our country. Important articles include Deng Zihui's "On Contradictions Among the People in Rural Areas and the Guiding Principles and Methods for Correctly Handling Contradictions," Chen Zhengren's "Riding the Wind and Waves, Striving to Achieve the National Agricultural Development Program Ahead of Schedule," and Wang Guangwei and Fang Weizhong's "Developing Industry and Agriculture Simultaneously on the Basis of Prioritizing the Development of Heavy Industry," etc. (Xiang)

### “我国现代农业建设的道路”

1958-05-05
第8版()
专栏：新书架

    “我国现代农业建设的道路”
    “青年共产主义者”丛刊第四集“我国现代农业建设的道路”已经出版了。这一集丛刊介绍了我国农业建设几年来的成就和经验，讲解了我国当前农业建设的基本政策和有关的重要措施。重要文章有邓子恢的“论农村人民内部矛盾和正确处理矛盾的方针办法”，陈正人的“乘风破浪，为争取提前实现全国农业发展纲要而斗争”，王光伟、房维中的“在优先发展重工业的基础上发展工业和发展农业同时并举”等。　　　　　　　　（翔）
