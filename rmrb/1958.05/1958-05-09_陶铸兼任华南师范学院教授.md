Title: Tao Zhu Concurrently Appointed as Professor at South China Normal College
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 8th: Tao Zhu, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, has accepted the invitation of South China Normal College to concurrently serve as a professor in the college's Marxism-Leninism Teaching and Research Office. Qu Mengjue, Secretary of the Provincial Party Committee, has previously lectured on political courses at Sun Yat-sen University and other schools, and will continue to make time to lecture on political courses at various universities in the future.

### 陶铸兼任华南师范学院教授

1958-05-09
第7版()
专栏：

    陶铸兼任华南师范学院教授
    新华社广州8日电　中共中央委员、中共广东省委第一书记陶铸，接受华南师范学院的聘请，兼任这个学院的马列主义教研室教授。省委书记区梦觉曾为中山大学等校讲授政治课，今后也将抽空到各高等学校讲政治课。
