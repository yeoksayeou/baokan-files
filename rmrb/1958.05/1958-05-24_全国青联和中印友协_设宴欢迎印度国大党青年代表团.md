Title: All-China Youth Federation and Sino-Indian Friendship Association Host Banquet to Welcome Indian National Congress Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: The All-China Youth Federation and the Sino-Indian Friendship Association jointly held a banquet this evening to welcome the Indian National Congress Youth Delegation.

    Liu Xiyuan, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, delivered speeches at the banquet, welcoming the young friends from India.

### 全国青联和中印友协  设宴欢迎印度国大党青年代表团

1958-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    全国青联和中印友协
    设宴欢迎印度国大党青年代表团
    据新华社23日讯　中华全国青年联合会、中印友好协会今天晚间联合举行宴会欢迎印度国大党青年代表团。
    全国青联主席刘西元、中印友好协会会长丁西林先后在会上致词，欢迎来自印度的青年朋友。
