Title: Small Iron Plant (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 3

    Small Iron Plant
    This is an iron smelting plant with an annual output of 5,000 tons of pig iron. The volume of the blast furnace is 13 cubic meters, and the investment in building the plant is about 110,000 yuan, which is suitable for county-level operation. Its main production equipment includes a blast furnace, a blower, a boiler, and water pumps. If there is no power supply locally, steam engines or boiler-camel machines can be used for transmission.

### 小铁厂（图片）

1958-05-06
第3版()
专栏：

    小铁厂
    这是一座年产生铁五千吨的炼铁厂，高炉容积是十三立方公尺，建厂投资约十一万元，适于县办。它的主要生产设备有高炉、鼓风机、锅炉和水泵等。如当地无电源时，可采用蒸汽机或锅驼机传动。
