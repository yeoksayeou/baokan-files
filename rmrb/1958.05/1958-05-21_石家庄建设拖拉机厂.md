Construction Flower:

    Shijiazhuang Construction Tractor Factory
    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 20th - A medium-sized universal tractor manufacturing plant in Shijiazhuang, Hebei Province, has recently started construction. Once completed, it will have an annual output of 20,000 twenty-five horsepower tractors.
    This factory will adhere to the principles of "more, faster, better, and more economical" in its construction and production, and will be partially put into production this year, and will trial-produce seventy to eighty tractors.

### 石家庄建设拖拉机厂

1958-05-21
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    石家庄建设拖拉机厂
    新华社天津20日电　河北省石家庄市举办的一座中型万能拖拉机制造厂在最近开工兴建。它建成以后，能年产二十五马力的拖拉机二万台。
    这座工厂将根据多快好省的原则，一边建设一边生产，今年就可以部分投入生产，并且试制生产拖拉机七十到八十台。
