Title: Soviet Weightlifting Team Achieves Complete Victory, Defeats American Opponents Three Times
Author:
Date: 1958-05-19
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 18th: The Soviet weightlifting team, currently visiting the United States, defeated the strong American weightlifting team for the third time on the evening of the 17th in New York, with a score of four to three, victoriously concluding their visit to the United States. The Soviet team first won in Chicago with a score of six to one, and then won in Detroit with a score of four to three.
    The Soviet athletes who achieved victory were Medvedev in the heavyweight class, Lomakin in the light heavyweight class, Bushuyev in the lightweight class, and Vorobyev in the middleweight class.

### 苏联举重队全胜  三次击败美国对手

1958-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联举重队全胜
　　三次击败美国对手
    据新华社18日讯　纽约消息：正在美国访问的苏联举重队，17日晚上在纽约以四比三的比数第三次击败强有力的美国举重队，胜利地结束了在美国的访问。苏联选手第一次在芝加哥以六比一取胜，第二次在底特律以四比三取胜。
    苏联选手获得胜利的是重量级的梅德维杰夫、轻重量级的洛马金、轻量级的布舒耶夫和次重量级的沃罗比耶夫。
