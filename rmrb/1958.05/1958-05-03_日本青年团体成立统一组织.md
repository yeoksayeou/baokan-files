Title: Japanese Youth Groups Establish Unified Organization
Author:
Date: 1958-05-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd. Japan's six youth and student organizations established the "All-Japan Youth and Student Joint Struggle Conference" in Tokyo on April 30th.
　　
The organizations participating in this group are the All-Japan Federation of Student Self-Government Associations, the Japan Trade Union General Council Youth Women's Council, the All-Japan Youth Women's Conference, the Japan Farmers Union Youth Department Preparatory Committee, the Japan Socialist Party Youth Department, and the Japan Democratic Youth League.

### 日本青年团体成立统一组织

1958-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    日本青年团体成立统一组织
    据新华社2日讯　东京消息：日本六个青年和学生的组织4月30日在东京成立“全日本青年学生联合斗争会议”。
    参加这个组织的有全日本学生自治会总联合会、日本工会总评议会青年妇女协议会、全日本青年妇女会议、日本农民工会青年部筹备会、日本社会党青年部和日本民主青年同盟。
