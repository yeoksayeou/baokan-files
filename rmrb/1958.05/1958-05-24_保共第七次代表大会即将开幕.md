Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Seventh Congress to Open Soon
Author:
Date: 1958-05-24
Page: 5

Bulgarian Communist Party Seventh Congress to Open Soon

    According to the resolution of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party will open on June 2 this year. The Congress has the following four items on the agenda: 1. Summary report of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, to be delivered by Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party; 2. Report of the Inspection Committee of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, to be delivered by Katrandzhiev, Chairman of the Central Inspection Committee; 3. The Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party's instructions on the Third Five-Year Plan (1958-1962) for the Development of the National Economy, to be delivered by Yugov, Chairman of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers; 4. Election of the leading bodies of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party.
    Since the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party in 1954, the Bulgarian people, under the leadership of the Communist Party, have achieved many significant achievements on the political, economic, and cultural fronts, especially in accordance with the spirit of the resolutions of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party in 1956. Many important measures have been taken and brilliant results have been achieved in improving the Party and state's leadership of industry and agriculture, further developing socialist democracy, giving play to the creativity of the masses, streamlining state institutions, and bringing the leadership of the central ministries closer to production units. Now, the Bulgarian people are welcoming the Party's Seventh Congress with even greater labor enthusiasm.

### 保共第七次代表大会即将开幕

1958-05-24
第5版()
专栏：兄弟党的生活

    保共第七次代表大会即将开幕
    根据保加利亚共产党中央委员会的决议，保共第七次代表大会将于今年6月2日开幕。代表大会有下列四项议程：一、保共中央的总结报告，报告人是保共中央第一书记日夫科夫；二、保共中央检查委员会的报告，报告人是中央检查委员会主席卡特兰吉耶夫；三、保共第七次代表大会关于发展国民经济的第三个五年计划（1958—1962年）的指示，报告人是保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫；四、选举保共中央领导机构。
    在1954年保共第六次代表大会以后，保加利亚人民在共产党领导下，在政治、经济和文化战线上，都获得了许多重大成就，特别是根据1956年保共中央四月全会决议的精神，在改进党和国家对工农业的领导、进一步发展社会主义民主、发挥人民群众的创造性、精简国家机构、使中央各部领导接近生产单位等方面，采取了许多重要措施，取得了辉煌的成果。现在，保加利亚人民正以更高的劳动热忱迎接党的第七次代表大会。
