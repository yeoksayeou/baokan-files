Title: Wind Turbine (Picture)
Author: Shu Shen
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 3

　　Wind Turbine
　　In the darkness, the wind turbine generates electricity, illuminating the surrounding fields. This is a type of wind turbine suitable for rural power generation, successfully trial-produced by the Zhangjiakou City Farm Implement Factory in Hebei Province in early May. It has a simple structure, is easy to operate, and has a wide range of uses. In a level 4 wind, it can generate three thousand kilowatts of electricity and can also drive various agricultural machinery.

　　Photo by Shu Shen (Xinhua News Agency)

### 风动机（图片）
舒慎
1958-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　风动机
　　黑夜里，风动机发出了电流，照耀着四野。这是河北省张家口市农具厂5月初试制成功的一种适合于农村发电用的风动机。它的构造简单，容易操作，用途很广泛。它在四级风的情况下，可以发出三千瓩电力，同时还可以带动各种农业机械。
                                 舒慎摄（新华社稿）
