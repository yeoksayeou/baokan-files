Title: General Strike Erupts in Baghdad Protesting the Formation of the Arab Federation, Opposing Foreign Interference in Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 20th - According to the "Al-Sharq" newspaper, a general strike erupted in Baghdad, the capital of Iraq, on the 18th, accompanied by mass demonstrations. Demonstrators protested the formation of the Iraq-Jordan Federation, opposed the Baghdad Pact, and supported the Lebanese people's struggle for independence, opposing foreign interference in Lebanon's internal affairs.

### 巴格达爆发总罢工  抗议成立伊约联邦　反对外国干涉黎巴嫩

1958-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    巴格达爆发总罢工
    抗议成立伊约联邦　反对外国干涉黎巴嫩
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社贝鲁特讯：据“东方报”报道，伊拉克首都巴格达在18日爆发了总罢工，并且举行了群众性示威游行。示威群众抗议成立伊拉克—约旦联邦，反对巴格达条约，支持黎巴嫩人民为争取独立，反对外国干涉黎巴嫩内政而斗争。
