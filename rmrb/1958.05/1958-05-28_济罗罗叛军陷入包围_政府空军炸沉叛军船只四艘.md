Title: Jirolo Rebels Encircled; Government Air Force Sinks Four Rebel Ships
Author:
Date: 1958-05-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 27th - Indonesian Army Information Head Piengaadi announced on the evening of the 26th that the Indonesian government forces had encircled the rebels occupying Jirolo, the capital of Halmahera Island in the northern Maluku Islands.

During the mopping-up operation in Jirolo, the Indonesian government forces seized a batch of rebel equipment, including one mortar and two machine guns.

Piengaadi also said that the Indonesian Air Force sank four rebel ships in Parigi, Central Sulawesi, on May 10th. According to news received by the Indonesian Army Headquarters, thirty rebels were killed in the air raid.

### 济罗罗叛军陷入包围  政府空军炸沉叛军船只四艘

1958-05-28
第6版()
专栏：

    济罗罗叛军陷入包围
    政府空军炸沉叛军船只四艘
    新华社雅加达27日电　印度尼西亚陆军新闻处处长皮恩加迪26日晚上宣布，印度尼西亚政府军在马鲁古群岛北部的哈马黑拉岛已经包围了窃踞着首府济罗罗的叛军。
    印度尼西亚政府军在济罗罗的扫荡战中，缴获了一批叛军的装备，其中有迫击炮一门和机关枪两挺。
    皮恩加迪还说，印度尼西亚空军5月10日在中苏拉威西的巴里吉曾炸沉了叛军船只四艘。据印度尼西亚陆军总部接到的消息，在这次空袭中，有叛军三十人被炸死。
