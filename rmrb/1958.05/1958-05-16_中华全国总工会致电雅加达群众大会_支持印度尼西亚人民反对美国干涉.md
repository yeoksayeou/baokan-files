Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Jakarta Mass Meeting, Supporting Indonesian People's Opposition to US Interference
Author:
Date: 1958-05-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The "Mass Movement Against Foreign Interference" in Jakarta will hold a general meeting on the 16th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today sent a telegram to the meeting expressing its support.

    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions' telegram said: "The Indonesian government and people resolutely oppose US imperialism's interference in Indonesia's internal affairs and firmly continue the struggle to resolve the rebel groups. This is a just action to safeguard national independence and defend world peace. The Chinese workers and people believe that with the Indonesian people's persistent and unremitting national unity, the Indonesian people's just struggle will certainly achieve final victory."

### 中华全国总工会致电雅加达群众大会  支持印度尼西亚人民反对美国干涉

1958-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会致电雅加达群众大会
　　支持印度尼西亚人民反对美国干涉
    新华社15日讯　雅加达“反对外国干涉群众运动”将在16日举行大会。中华全国总工会今天致电大会表示支持。
    中华全国总工会的电报说：“印度尼西亚政府和人民坚决反对美帝国主义干涉印度尼西亚内政，坚持进行解决叛乱集团的斗争，这是维护民族独立、保卫世界和平的正义举动。中国工人和人民相信，在印度尼西亚始终不懈地坚持全民的团结之下，印度尼西亚人民的正义斗争是一定会得到最后胜利的。”
