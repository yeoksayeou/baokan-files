Title: Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception to Commemorate the Queen of the Netherlands' Birthday
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Dutch Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. C. H. A. Plug, held a reception in Beijing tonight to commemorate the birthday of Queen Juliana of the Netherlands.

    Our country's Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Luo Guibo, was invited to attend the reception and gave a speech at the reception, congratulating the Queen of the Netherlands on her health, and congratulating the Dutch people on their prosperity and world peace.

    Also invited to attend were Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, and many others from our country, as well as heads of diplomatic missions from many countries.

### 荷兰代办举行酒会  纪念荷兰女王的诞辰

1958-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    荷兰代办举行酒会
    纪念荷兰女王的诞辰
    新华社30日讯　荷兰驻中国代办处代办司灵博今晚在北京举行酒会，纪念荷兰女王朱丽安娜的诞辰日。
    我国外交部副部长罗贵波应邀出席酒会，并在酒会上致词祝贺荷兰女王的健康，祝贺荷兰人民的繁荣和世界和平。
    应邀出席的还有我国对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，文化部部长沈雁冰等多人，以及许多国家的驻华使节。
