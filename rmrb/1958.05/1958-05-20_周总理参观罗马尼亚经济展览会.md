Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits Romanian Economic Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Premier Zhou Enlai visited the Romanian Economic Exhibition this evening.

Premier Zhou Enlai carefully viewed the machinery manufacturing, light industry, petroleum, chemical, plastics, food, and other industrial products displayed at the exhibition, and wrote an inscription for the exhibition to celebrate the opening of the Romanian Economic Exhibition. Romanian Ambassador to China, Mr. Rudenko, and members of the Romanian government delegation currently visiting China accompanied the visit today.

### 周总理参观罗马尼亚经济展览会

1958-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理参观罗马尼亚经济展览会
    新华社19日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间参观了罗马尼亚经济展览会。
    周恩来总理详细参观了展览会展出的机器制造业、轻工业、石油、化学、塑料、食品等工业的产品，并为展览会题词，庆贺罗马尼亚经济展览会的展出。罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科和正在中国访问的罗马尼亚政府代表团团员等今天陪同参观。
