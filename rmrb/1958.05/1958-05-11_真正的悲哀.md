Title: The Real Sadness
Author:
Date: 1958-05-11
Page: 3

    Notes
    
    The British magazine "The Economist" published an editorial acknowledging a cold fact: the productivity of socialist countries is growing faster than that of capitalist countries. Especially this year, the situation is even worse for capitalist countries. Socialist countries will continue to develop, while the best capitalist countries can do is maintain the status quo.

    From this, the magazine sensed the real sadness of the capitalist world. Therefore, it "appealed to the United States," pointing out that the most prominent political fact today is not the hydrogen bomb, but the increasingly rapid economic development of socialist countries, while capitalist countries cannot even maintain the status quo.

    But what effect can this appeal have on the United States, which is mired in economic crisis? Due to the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system, the East wind prevails over the West wind. This is the general trend, and it cannot be changed by Britain or the United States.

### 真正的悲哀

1958-05-11
第3版()
专栏：札记

    真正的悲哀
    英国的杂志“经济学家”发表了一篇社论，承认了一个冷酷的事实：社会主义国家的生产力比资本主义国家增长得快。尤其是今年，对于资本主义国家来说，情况更为不妙，社会主义国家还会继续发展，而资本主义国家中，最好的顶多是维持现状。
    这家杂志从这里感到了资本主义世界真正的悲哀。为此“向美国呼吁”，指出今天最突出的政治事实不是氢弹，而是社会主义国家经济越来越发展，资本主义国家连现状也维持不住。
    可是，这个呼吁对于陷在经济危机之中的美国，又能起什么作用呢？由于社会主义制度无比的优越性，东风压倒西风，这是大势所趋，已经不是英国，也不是美国所能改变的了。
