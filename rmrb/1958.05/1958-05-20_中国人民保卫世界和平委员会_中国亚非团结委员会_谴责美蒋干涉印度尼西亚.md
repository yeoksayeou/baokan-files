Title: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee Condemns US-Chiang Kai-shek Interference in Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee today sent telegrams to the Indonesian Peace Committee and the Indonesian Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, saying that the US imperialism and the Chiang Kai-shek clique's blatant support for the criminal acts of the Indonesian rebels seriously threatens peace in Asia and the world.

The telegram said: "The Chinese people are deeply concerned and firmly support the Indonesian people's struggle to safeguard national sovereignty, independence, and oppose imperialist interference. The US imperialism, colluding with its followers and using the Chiang Kai-shek clique to blatantly support the criminal acts of Indonesian rebels, is not only a gross interference in Indonesia's internal affairs, violating Indonesia's independence and sovereignty, but also a direct provocation against the Bandung Conference and the Cairo Conference. Moreover, it is deliberately intensifying international tensions and seriously threatening peace in Asia and the world. This cannot but arouse great indignation and universal protest from the peace-loving people of Asian and African countries and the world. We firmly believe that the Indonesian people's just struggle against the interference of US imperialism and in defense of independence and peace, with the support of peace-loving people all over the world, will surely achieve final victory, and the shameful conspiracy of the US interventionists and their followers will be completely defeated."

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会　中国亚非团结委员会  谴责美蒋干涉印度尼西亚

1958-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会　中国亚非团结委员会
    谴责美蒋干涉印度尼西亚
    新华社19日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国亚非团结委员会今天打电报给印度尼西亚和平委员会和印度尼西亚亚非团结委员会说，美帝国主义和蒋介石集团明目张胆地支持印度尼西亚叛乱分子的罪恶行为，严重地威胁了亚洲和世界的和平。
    电报说：“中国人民深切关怀并坚决支持印度尼西亚人民为维护国家主权、独立和反对帝国主义干涉而进行的斗争。美帝国主义纠集它的追随者并利用蒋介石集团明目张胆地支持印度尼西亚叛乱分子的罪恶行为，不但是粗暴地干涉印度尼西亚的内政，侵犯了印度尼西亚的独立主权，也是对万隆会议和开罗会议的直接挑衅，而且是蓄意加剧国际紧张局势，严重的威胁了亚洲及世界和平，这不能不引起我们亚非各国以及全世界爱好和平人民的极大愤慨和普遍抗议。我们深信，印度尼西亚人民为反对美国帝国主义的干涉和保卫独立和和平的正义斗争，在全世界爱好和平的人民的声援下，一定会取得最后的胜利，美国干涉者及其追随者的可耻阴谋必将遭到彻底失败。”
