    Looking at Pictures of the Great Leap Forward
    The "Great Leap Forward Photography Exhibition," sponsored by the China Photographic Society, is being held at the Art Exhibition Hall in Shuaifu Garden. These one hundred or so works are all moving scenes recorded by photographers during the nationwide Great Leap Forward.

### 看大跃进的图片

1958-05-25
第4版()
专栏：看看听听玩玩

    看大跃进的图片
    中国摄影学会主办的“大跃进摄影展览”，正在帅府园美术展览馆举行。这一百多幅作品都是摄影工作者在全民大跃进中纪录下来的一些动人的场景。
