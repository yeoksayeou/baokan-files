    Joint Exhibition of Works by Four Artists
    The Chinese Artists Association is exhibiting the “Joint Exhibition of Wang Henei, Xiao Shufang, Li Hu, and Zong Qixiang’s Chinese Paintings, Watercolor Paintings, and Sculptures” at the Art Exhibition Hall from May 2nd.
    Xiao Shufang, Li Hu, and Zong Qixiang's works mainly reflect the new face of socialist construction and portraits of advanced workers in the form of Chinese paintings and watercolor paintings. They have also made new attempts in terms of expression methods. Wang Henei's small sculptures mostly feature animals as subjects and are able to capture the dynamics and characteristics of various animals.
    This exhibition will be moved to the Summer Palace on the 20th, and will later tour Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Xi'an, Shenyang, and other places.

### 四位美术家作品联合展览

1958-05-04
第4版()
专栏：看看听听玩玩

    四位美术家作品联合展览
    中国美术家协会从5月2日起在美术展览馆展出“王合内、萧淑芳、李斛、宗其香国画、水彩画、雕塑作品联合展览”。
    萧淑芳、李斛、宗其香的作品主要是以国画、水彩画形式反映社会主义建设中的新面貌，和先进工作者像等等。在表现方法上他们也作了新的尝试。王合内的小型雕塑作品。多以动物为题材，能掌握住各种动物的动态和特征。
    这次展览，将于20日移到颐和园展出，以后还要到上海、武汉、广州、重庆、西安、沈阳等地巡回展出。
