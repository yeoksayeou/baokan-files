Title: Independence Party of Morocco Forms New Cabinet; Bela Frej Appointed Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1958-05-14
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 13th - A new cabinet led by Bela Frej, Secretary-General of the Independence Party of Morocco, was sworn in yesterday.

　　The new cabinet consists of ten ministers, eight of whom are from the Independence Party, and the remaining two are non-party individuals who are pro-Independence. Bela Frej serves as Prime Minister and Foreign Minister.

　　During the inauguration ceremony of the new cabinet, King Mohammed V of Morocco stated that the main objectives of Moroccan foreign policy are: to recover territories seized by other countries; to resolve the issue of foreign troops stationed in the country; and to strengthen fraternal ties with other Arab countries, especially to establish a North African alliance.

　　The territories Morocco seeks to reclaim are mainly Spanish Sahara and French West Africa's Mauritania. Currently, the foreign troops stationed in Morocco include 30,000 French troops, 35,000 Spanish troops, and 15,000 American troops. As for the North African alliance, its main member countries include Morocco, Tunisia, and Algeria.

### 摩洛哥独立党组成新阁  贝拉弗里杰担任首相

1958-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　摩洛哥独立党组成新阁
    贝拉弗里杰担任首相
    据新华社13日讯　拉巴特消息：由摩洛哥独立党总书记贝拉弗里杰领导的新内阁在昨天就职。
    新内阁由十个大臣组成，其中八人是独立党人，其余两人是亲独立党的无党派人士。贝拉弗里杰任首相兼外交大臣。
    摩洛哥国王穆罕默德五世在举行新阁授权仪式时说，摩洛哥外交政策的主要目标是：收复被别国夺取的领土；解决外国驻军问题；加强同其他阿拉伯国家的兄弟联系，特别是建立北非国家联盟。
    摩洛哥要求收回的领土主要是西（班牙）属撒哈拉和法属西非洲的毛里塔尼亚。目前驻在摩洛哥的外国军队，有法军三万人，西班牙军三万五千人，美军一万五千人。至于北非国家联盟，其主要成员国除摩洛哥外，还包括突尼斯和阿尔及利亚。
