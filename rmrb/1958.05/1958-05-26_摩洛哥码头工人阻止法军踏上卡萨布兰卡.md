Title: Moroccan Dockworkers Prevent French Troops from Landing in Casablanca
Author:
Date: 1958-05-26
Page: 6

    Moroccan Dockworkers
    Prevent French Troops from Landing in Casablanca
    Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca, May 24th: Moroccan dockworkers prevented 910 French troops from landing in Casablanca on the 23rd. These French troops arrived from Marseille, France, aboard the ship "Koutoubia."
    The action of the Casablanca dockworkers is consistent with the demands of the people throughout the country. The Moroccan people are demanding the withdrawal of the 30,000 French troops stationed in their country.

### 摩洛哥码头工人阻止法军踏上卡萨布兰卡

1958-05-26
第6版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥码头工人
    阻止法军踏上卡萨布兰卡
    据新华社24日讯　卡萨布兰卡消息：摩洛哥的码头工人在23日阻止了九百一十名法军在卡萨布兰卡登陆。这批法军是乘“库图巴号”轮船从法国马赛开来的。
    卡萨布兰卡码头工人的行动是和全国人民的要求一致的。摩洛哥人民正在要求驻在他们国家的三万名法国军队撤出去。
