Title: Voroshilov's Visit to Yugoslavia Postponed
Author:
Date: 1958-05-13
Page: 5

　　Voroshilov's Visit to Yugoslavia Postponed
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th: TASS reported from Moscow on the 11th that Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, had postponed his visit to Yugoslavia, which was originally scheduled for May of this year.

### 伏罗希洛夫访南延期

1958-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　伏罗希洛夫访南延期
    新华社12日讯　塔斯社莫斯科11日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫原定将在今年5月对南斯拉夫进行的访问，现已延期。
