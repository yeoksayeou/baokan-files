Title: UAR Vice President Accuses Imperialism of Interfering in Lebanon; Syrian Region Establishes Organization to Support the Struggle of the Lebanese People
Author:
Date: 1958-05-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 22nd: United Arab Republic Vice President Houlani told reporters from the Syrian News Agency on the 21st that the imperialist attacks and interference in Lebanon are directed at the United Arab Republic and all Arabs, as well as their freedom and independence.

He said that this action might not be limited to Lebanon, but could expand to the entire Arab world, even threatening world peace.

Houlani pointed out that today, all promises and accusations cannot conceal the crimes committed by imperialism against the Arab people and world peace.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 22nd: After a meeting of mass organizations and representatives from all walks of life in the Syrian region, numbering about forty, an organization to support Lebanon was established here on the afternoon of the 21st. Its purpose is to carry out work to support the struggle of the Lebanese people morally and financially.

The organization has committees responsible for publicity, fundraising, relief, and other matters, as well as an executive body.

According to the Syrian News Agency, following the expulsion of 1,800 Syrians on May 20th, the Lebanese authorities expelled another 1,500 Syrians on the 21st.

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: TASS, Cairo, May 21st: According to the "People's Newspaper," the movement of Jordanian people supporting the Lebanese people's struggle for freedom and independence is expanding.

The newspaper said that leaflets distributed by Jordanian patriots stated, "The Lebanese people's struggle for their rights is also a struggle against the imperialist lackeys and their accomplices throughout the Arab East."

### 阿联副总统指责帝国主义干涉黎巴嫩  叙利亚地区成立支援黎巴嫩人民斗争组织

1958-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联副总统指责帝国主义干涉黎巴嫩
    叙利亚地区成立支援黎巴嫩人民斗争组织
    据新华社大马士革22日电　阿拉伯联合共和国副总统胡拉尼21日对叙利亚通讯社记者说，帝国主义对黎巴嫩的攻击和干涉是针对阿拉伯联合共和国和全体阿拉伯人以及他们的自由和独立的。
    他说，这种行动不可能只限在黎巴嫩，而将可能扩大到全阿拉伯世界，以至威胁世界和平。
    胡拉尼指出，在今天，一切诺言和指责都不能掩盖帝国主义对阿拉伯人和世界和平所犯的罪行。
    新华社大马士革22日电　叙利亚地区的群众团体和各界的约四十名代表举行会议以后，21日下午在这里成立了支援黎巴嫩组织。它的宗旨是进行在道义上和财政上支援黎巴嫩人民的斗争的工作。
    这个组织设有负责宣传、募捐、救济等事宜的委员会以及一个执行机构。
    据叙利亚通讯社报道，黎巴嫩当局继5月20日驱逐了一千八百名叙利亚人以后，在21日又驱逐了一千五百名叙利亚人。
    新华社22日讯　塔斯社开罗21日讯：据“人民报”报道，约旦人民支持黎巴嫩人民争取自由和独立斗争的运动正在扩大。
    报纸说，约旦爱国者散发的传单说，“黎巴嫩人民争取自己的权利的斗争，也是反对在整个阿拉伯东方的帝国主义走狗及其帮凶的斗争”。
