Title: Inner Mongolia Discovers Crystal Mine
Author:
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 3
Exploration Good News

    Inner Mongolia Discovers Crystal Mine
    A crystal mine has been newly discovered in Kouhezi Township, Kulun Banner, Jerim League, Inner Mongolia. The exposed part is on the mountainside, and the mine is two chi wide. The Banner is now preparing to start mining.

### 内蒙古发现水晶矿

1958-05-20
第3版()
专栏：勘探喜讯

    内蒙古发现水晶矿
    内蒙古哲里木盟库伦旗扣何子乡新发现一处水晶矿，露头部分是在大山的半坡，矿宽二市尺。现在，这个旗准备着手开采。
