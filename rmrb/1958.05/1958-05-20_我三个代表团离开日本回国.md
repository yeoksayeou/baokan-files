Title: Three Chinese Delegations Leave Japan for Home

Author:

Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 19th: The Chinese Legal Workers Delegation, the Chinese Song and Dance Ensemble, and the Chinese Metals and Minerals Import and Export Corporation Delegation and the Chinese Minerals Corporation Delegation, who were visiting Japan, set sail for home today.

### 我三个代表团离开日本回国

1958-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　我三个代表团离开日本回国
    新华社19日讯　东京消息：在日本访问的中国法律工作者代表团、中国歌舞团和中国五金进出口公司和中国矿产公司代表团，今天乘船启程回国。
