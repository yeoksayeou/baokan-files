Sulfur
    Sulfur is a non-metallic element, pale yellow in color, solid, and can be processed into powder. Sulfur is the main raw material for preparing the pesticide "lime sulfur mixture." "Lime sulfur mixture" can prevent and control diseases and insect pests such as wheat stem rust, cotton red spider mites, and citrus rust mites.

### 硫磺

1958-05-22
第2版()
专栏：小常识

    硫磺
    硫磺是一种非金属元素，色淡黄，固体，可加工成粉。硫磺是配制农药“石灰硫磺合剂”的主要原料。“石灰硫磺合剂”可以防治小麦秆锈病、棉花红蜘蛛和柑桔锈壁虱等病虫害。
