Title: Mobile Medical Ship
Author:
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 7

　　Mobile Medical Ship
    (Reported by our newspaper) The Xinsheng United Clinic in Yushan Town, Kunshan County, Jiangsu Province, in order to support agricultural production, recently joined forces with the China National Medicines Corporation to establish a mobile medical ship, which sailed to the Shangmingdian water network area in Chenmu Township, more than fifty li away from the town and extremely inconvenient to reach, to treat farmers. The ship has internal medicine, surgery, traditional Chinese medicine, Western medicine, obstetrics and gynecology, trauma, acupuncture, etc., and is equipped with traditional Chinese medicine and Western medicine. After seeing the patients, they immediately dispense medicine; they also vigorously carry out health education to improve the public's health knowledge, which is very popular among the masses.

### 流动医疗船

1958-05-17
第7版()
专栏：

　　流动医疗船
    本报讯　江苏昆山县玉山镇新生联合诊所，为了支援农业生产，最近和国药总店联合建立了一只流动医疗船，开往交通极不方便、离城五十余里的陈墓乡尚明甸水网地区，为农民治病。船上有内外科、中西医、妇产、伤科、针灸等，并备有中药和西药。他们替病人看完病，马上就配药；还大力开展卫生宣传，提高群众卫生知识，很受群众欢迎。
