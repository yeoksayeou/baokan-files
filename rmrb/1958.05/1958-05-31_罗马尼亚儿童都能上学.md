Title: All Children in Romania Can Attend School
Author:
Date: 1958-05-31
Page: 5

All children in Romania can receive education in the era of the people's government. A four-year compulsory education system is implemented throughout the country, and a seven-year compulsory education system is implemented in cities, towns, and workers' districts.

The national school network is constantly expanding, and the number of students is increasing daily. For example, the number of middle schools has doubled compared to 1938, and the number of students has almost more than doubled.

In the era of the people's government, the number of kindergartens has more than tripled, and more than one hundred children's palaces and children's homes, many libraries and theaters, etc., have been opened.

### 罗马尼亚儿童都能上学

1958-05-31
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚儿童都能上学
    在人民政权的年代里，罗马尼亚所有的儿童都可以受到教育。各地实行了四年制的义务教育，而在城市、市镇和工人区实行了七年制的义务教育。
    全国的学校网在不断扩大，学生人数在日益增加。例如，中学的数目比1938年增加了一倍，学生人数差不多增加了两倍以上。
    在人民政权的年代里，幼儿园的数目增加了三倍以上，并且开办了一百多所少年宫和少年之家、很多图书馆和剧院等等。
