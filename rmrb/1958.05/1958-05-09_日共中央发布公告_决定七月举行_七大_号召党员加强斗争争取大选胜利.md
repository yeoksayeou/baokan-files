Title: CCP Central Committee Issues Announcement, Decides to Hold "Seventh Congress" in July, Calling on Party Members to Strengthen Struggle to Win General Election Victory
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 8th. TASS, Tokyo, 8th: "Akahata" published an announcement from the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party stating that, based on the resolutions of the 17th and 18th Enlarged Meetings of the Central Committee, the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party has decided to hold its Seventh National Congress in Tokyo from July 21st for a period of ten days.

The announcement said that in the process of preparing for the Congress, the Japanese Communist Party strengthened its ties with the masses of the people. The announcement from the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party stated that the sharp development of the general election struggle shows that the working class, farmers, urban residents of all strata, especially young people and women, place increasing hope in the Party.

The Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party calls on all Communist Party members to stand at the forefront of the people's struggle, strive for victory in the general election, and on this basis, further unite the Party ranks before the Congress.

### 日共中央发布公告  决定七月举行“七大”  号召党员加强斗争争取大选胜利

1958-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    日共中央发布公告
    决定七月举行“七大”
    号召党员加强斗争争取大选胜利
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社东京8日讯：“赤旗报”发表日本共产党中央委员会公告说，日本共产党中央委员会根据第十七次、十八次中央委员会扩大会议的决议，决定从7月21日起在东京举行为期十天的第七次全国代表大会。
    公告说，在代表大会的筹备过程中，日本共产党加强了同人民群众的联系。日本共产党中央委员会的公告说，大选斗争的尖锐发展表明，工人阶级、农民、各阶层的城市居民，特别是青年和妇女，对党寄予越来越大的希望。
    日本共产党中央委员会号召全体共产党员站在人民斗争的前列，争取大选的胜利，并且在这个基础上使党的队伍在代表大会前夕更加团结。
