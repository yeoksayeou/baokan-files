Foreign Press Clippings

    Official and Wealth

    Two American millionaires will compete for the position of Governor of New York State this fall.

    The Democratic Party may nominate Governor Harriman again as its candidate. He owns $75 million in assets. The most likely candidate to be nominated by the Republican Party is Nelson A. Rockefeller, whose assets are worth $100 million.
        ——British "Daily Express" (with picture)
    (Illustration by Ying Tao)

### 官与财

1958-05-05
第5版()
专栏：外国报刊拾零

    官与财
    两个美国大富翁，将在今年秋天争夺纽约州州长的职位。
    民主党可能再度以哈里曼州长为竞选人。他拥有七千五百万美元财产。最有希望被共和党提名为竞选人的是纳尔逊·阿·洛克菲勒，他的财产值一亿美元。
        ——英国“每日快报”（附图片）
