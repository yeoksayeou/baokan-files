Title: British Amalgamated Engineering Union Opposes Production of Nuclear Weapons
Author:
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, London, May 10th -- According to TASS, London, May 9th: The annual conference of the British Amalgamated Engineering Union, one of Britain's most influential trade unions, currently being held in Great Yarmouth, passed a resolution demanding a ban on the production and testing of nuclear weapons. The resolution also firmly protested the establishment of American rocket weapon bases in Britain.

The delegates attending the conference entrusted the union's executive committee to request the Trades Union Congress and the Labour Party to strive for the convening of a high-level meeting.

### 英混合机械工业工会  反对生产核武器

1958-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　英混合机械工业工会
    反对生产核武器
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社伦敦9日讯：正在大雅穆斯举行的英国最有影响的工会之一英国混合机械工业工会年会通过一项决议，要求禁止生产和试验核武器。决议还坚决抗议在英国建立美国火箭武器基地。
    参加年会的代表委托工会执行委员会向英国职工大会和工党提出要求，要求它们争取召开高级会议。
