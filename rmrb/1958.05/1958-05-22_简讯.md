Title: Brief News

Author: 
Date: 1958-05-22
Page: 6

    Brief News
    △The China Traditional Opera and Dance Drama Theater left Paris for Lyon on the 20th.
    △Representatives of the Indian and Bulgarian governments decided in New Delhi on the 20th to extend the trade agreement between the two countries for another year without changing the commodity list.
    △The International Cartoonists Conference held in Paris from May 2nd to 5th decided to establish the International Cartoonists Association.
    △Women's organizations in the United Arab Republic are preparing to participate in the Fourth Congress of the Women's International Democratic Federation to be held in Vienna in June this year.

### 简讯

1958-05-22
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    △中国戏曲歌舞团20日下午离巴黎前往里昂。
    △印度和保加利亚两国政府的代表20日在新德里决定把两国贸易协定中的货单不加更改地延长一年。
    △5月2日到5日在巴黎举行的国际漫画家代表大会决定建立国际漫画家协会。
    △阿联各妇女团体正筹备参加国际民主妇女联合会将在今年6月在维也纳召开的第四届代表大会。
