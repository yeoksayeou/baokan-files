Title: China, Czechoslovakia Expand Exchange of Daily Necessities
Author:
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th - A protocol on the exchange of daily necessities between China and Czechoslovakia was signed here on the afternoon of the 7th. This agreement was reached during talks between the delegation of the First Ministry of Commerce of China and representatives of the Ministry of Trade of Czechoslovakia.

### 中捷扩大交换日用品

1958-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    中捷扩大交换日用品
    据新华社布拉格7日电　中捷日用品交换议定书7日下午在这里签字。这项协议是中国第一商业部代表团和捷克斯洛伐克贸易部代表在商谈中达成的。
