Title: Prominent Poet Neruda Elected President of the Chilean Writers' Association
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 6

Santiago dispatch: According to the Chilean "El Siglo" newspaper, the newly elected leadership of the Chilean Writers' Association appointed world-renowned poet Pablo Neruda as the President of the Chilean Writers' Association on April 14. The members of the new leadership also include: Vice-President David Cruz Ocampo and Secretary-General Matilde Ladrón de Guevara, among others.

### 著名诗人聂鲁达当选智利作协主席

1958-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    著名诗人聂鲁达当选智利作协主席
    圣地亚哥讯：据智利“世纪报”报道，智利作家协会新选出的领导机构在4月14日任命世界著名诗人巴勃罗·聂鲁达为智利作家协会主席。新领导机构的成员还有：副主席达维德·克鲁斯·奥坎波和总书记马蒂耳戴·拉德朗·戴·格瓦拉等人。
