Title: European Socialist Countries Sign Agreement to Open Regular Sea Routes
Author:
Date: 1958-05-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 3rd - Following the suggestion of the Economic Mutual Assistance Committee's (member states: the Soviet Union and Eastern European People's Democratic countries) transportation issue working group, a meeting was held in Tirana from April 28th to 30th to study the opening of regular sea routes between Albanian ports and the Soviet, Bulgarian, and Romanian ports on the Black Sea, as well as Polish ports.
    Representatives from relevant organizations in Albania, Bulgaria, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia participated in the meeting.
    A relevant agreement was signed after the meeting.

### 欧洲社会主义国家签订协定  开辟定期海运航线

1958-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    欧洲社会主义国家签订协定
    开辟定期海运航线
    新华社地拉那3日电　根据经济互助委员会参加国（苏联和东欧人民民主国家）运输问题工作组的建议，4月28日到30日在地拉那举行会谈，研究了阿尔巴尼亚各港口和苏联、保加利亚与罗马尼亚在黑海的港口以及波兰各港口之间开辟定期海运航线问题。
    参加会谈的有阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联和捷克斯洛伐克等有关组织的代表。
    会谈后签订了有关的协定。
