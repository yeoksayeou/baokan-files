Title: Andreyev Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, today sent a telegram to Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, to congratulate them on International Labor Day, May 1st. The telegram reads as follows:
To President Soong Ching Ling of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Secretary-General Qian Junrui of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association,

Dear Comrades!

I sincerely thank you for the festive greetings to the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association.

On the occasion of International Labor Day, May 1st, on behalf of all members of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, I extend warm congratulations to you, and through you to all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and to the fraternal Chinese people. I wholeheartedly wish the Chinese people further achievements in the cause of building socialism and consolidating world peace.

Long live the eternal friendship and fraternal cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and China!

Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Andreyev
Moscow, April 30, 1958

### 安德烈耶夫电贺宋庆龄

1958-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    安德烈耶夫电贺宋庆龄
    新华社30日讯　苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫今天致电中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、秘书长钱俊瑞，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。电文如下：中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞亲爱的同志们！
    衷心地感谢你们对苏中友好协会的节日的祝贺。
    值此“五一”国际劳动节之际，谨代表苏中友好协会全体会员，向你们，并通过你们向中苏友好协会的全体会员、向兄弟的中国人民致以热烈的祝贺。全心地祝中国人民在建设社会主义和巩固世界和平的事业中获得进一步的成就。
    苏中两国人民永恒的友谊和兄弟合作万岁！
                          苏中友好协会理事会主席　安德烈耶夫
                                      1958年4月30日于莫斯科
