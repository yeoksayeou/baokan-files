Title: Full Moon - Celebrating the Third Artificial Satellite
Author: Deng Tuo
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 8

　　Full Moon
　　——Celebrating the Third Artificial Satellite
    Deng Tuo
　　Last night, the immortals gathered in the Moon Palace,
　　A great star came to the world.
　　The tune "Gu Xi" was played,
　　Xian'e rose to dance,
　　Startling the Three Terraces.
　　Taking the heavens as the earth,
　　Turning the universe upside down,
　　Only this extraordinary talent can do it.
　　Peaceful competition,
　　The East Wind is triumphant,
　　Smiling broadly.

### 人月圆——庆贺第三个人造卫星
邓拓
1958-05-17
第8版()
专栏：

　　人月圆
　　——庆贺第三个人造卫星
    邓拓
　　月宫昨夜群仙会，
　　世上大星来。
　　曲弹姑洗，
　　纤阿起舞，
　　惊动三台。
　　以天为地，
　　乾坤倒转，
　　惟此奇才。
　　和平竞赛，
　　东风得意，
　　笑逐颜开。
