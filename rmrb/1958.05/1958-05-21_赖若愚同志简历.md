Title: Comrade Lai Ruoyu's Biography
Author:
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 1

    Comrade Lai Ruoyu was born in Wutai County, Shanxi Province, on January 1, 1910. He joined the Communist Party of China in 1929. In 1930, he was arrested and imprisoned in Beijing. After being released from prison in 1932, he continued to engage in revolutionary activities. After the start of the War of Resistance Against Japan, he worked in the Taihang area of Shanxi Province, serving successively as Secretary of the CPC Prefectural Committee, Minister of the Organization Department of the District Party Committee, and Secretary of the District Party Committee. In 1949, he served as Secretary of the CPC Taiyuan Municipal Committee, Shanxi Province, and later as Secretary of the CPC Shanxi Provincial Committee and Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government. In 1952, he served as Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. ### 赖若愚同志简历

1958-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    赖若愚同志简历
    赖若愚同志，山西五台县人，1910年1月1日生，1929年加入中国共产党，1930年在北京被捕入狱，1932年出狱后继续进行革命活动。抗日战争开始后，在山西省太行地区工作，历任中共地委书记，区党委组织部部长，区党委书记等职，1949年任中共山西省太原市委书记，后任中共山西省委书记，山西省人民政府主席等职，1952年任中华全国总工会秘书长，1953年中国工会第七次全国代表大会上当选为中华全国总工会主席，1954年中华人民共和国第一届人民代表大会上当选为常务委员会委员，1956年中共第八次全国代表大会上当选为中共中央委员，1957年中国工会第八次全国代表大会继续当选为中华全国总工会主席。
