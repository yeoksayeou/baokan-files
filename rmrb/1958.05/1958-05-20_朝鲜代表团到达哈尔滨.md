Title: Korean Delegation Arrives in Harbin
Author:
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 19th—The Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Xu Zhenshu and deputy head Kim Cheon-hae, arrived in Harbin at 6:20 tonight, accompanied by Wang Yifu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs. Also accompanying the delegation were Li Deji, acting head of the Korean Consulate in Changchun, and Kim Yong-am, Deputy Director of the Korean War Orphan Education Office in Shenyang.

After the distinguished guests were escorted off the train by Wang Yifu, they were warmly welcomed by more than 400 people, including Vice Governor of Heilongjiang Province Yu Tianfang, Wang Qingzheng, Secretary-General of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China Li Jianbai, Vice Mayor of Harbin Wang Huacheng, Secretaries of the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China Lin Xiaoxia and Zheng Yiping, and Lieutenant General Xie Youfa, Political Commissar of the Harbin Garrison. One hundred and thirty Korean war orphans from Qiqihar Secondary School who are interning at the Harbin Rolling Stock Repair Factory also went to the station to welcome relatives from their motherland.

In the evening, Vice Governors of Heilongjiang Province Yu Tianfang and Wang Qingzheng and others accompanied the distinguished guests to dinner.

Xinhua News Agency, Jilin, 19th—The Korean People's Delegation to China left Jilin City by car this morning to visit Harbin City, Heilongjiang Province. Deputy Minister of Internal Affairs Wang Yifu accompanied them. Li Deji, head of the Korean Consulate in Changchun, also traveled in the same car.

Those who went to the station to see off the distinguished guests included: Zhou Jiewen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Jilin Provincial People's Committee, Zhang Wenhai, Mayor of Jilin City, Li Shoushan, Secretary of the Jilin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Xie Zhengrong, Major General in charge of the Jilin Garrison, and Korean war orphans, totaling more than 2,000 people.

### 朝鲜代表团到达哈尔滨

1958-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜代表团到达哈尔滨
    新华社哈尔滨19日电　以许贞琡团长、金天海副团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团，在我国内务部副部长王一夫陪同下今晚六时二十分来到哈尔滨。和代表团同来的还有朝鲜驻长春领事馆代理馆务李德基和沈阳朝鲜战灾孤儿教育处副处长金龙岩。
    贵宾们由王一夫陪同走下火车后，受到了黑龙江省副省长于天放、王清正，中共黑龙江省委秘书长李剑白，哈尔滨市副市长王化成，中共哈尔滨市委书记林肖硖、郑依平，驻哈尔滨部队政治委员谢有法中将等四百多人的热烈欢迎。在哈尔滨机车车辆修理工厂实习的齐齐哈尔中等学院的一百三十名朝鲜战灾孤儿也到车站迎接来自自己祖国的亲人。
    晚上，黑龙江省副省长于天放、王清正等人陪同贵宾共进晚餐。
    新华社吉林19日电　朝鲜人民访华代表团在今天上午乘车离开吉林市，前往黑龙江省哈尔滨市访问。内务部副部长王一夫陪同前往。同车前往的还有朝鲜驻长春领事馆负责人李德基。
    到车站欢送贵宾的有：吉林省人民委员会副秘书长周介文，吉林市市长张文海，中共吉林市委书记李守善，驻吉林部队负责人谢正荣少将和朝鲜战灾孤儿等共两千多人。
