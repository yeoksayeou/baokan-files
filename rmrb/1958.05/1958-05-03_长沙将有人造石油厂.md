Title: Changsha to Have a Synthetic Oil Plant
Author:
Date: 1958-05-03
Page: 3

Construction Blooms

    Changsha to Have a Synthetic Oil Plant
    The Changsha No. 1 Synthetic Oil Factory recently started construction. The factory's main products will be gasoline, diesel, machine oil, and motor oil. It will also produce by-products such as pyrolysis benzene, toluene, and refined naphthalene. It will use rosin from pine trees as raw material; rosin is produced everywhere in the mountainous areas of Hunan. This factory will be completed in July of this year; after completion, it will be able to produce about 12,000 tons of synthetic oil annually. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 长沙将有人造石油厂

1958-05-03
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    长沙将有人造石油厂
    长沙市第一人造石油工厂最近开工兴建。这个厂的主要产品是汽油、柴油、机械油、车用机油等。另外还生产副产品热解苯、甲苯和精萘等。它将采用松树产的松脂作原料；松脂在湖南山区各处都产。这个厂将在今年7月建成；建成后每年能产人造石油一万二千吨左右。　　（据新华社讯）
