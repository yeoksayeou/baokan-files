Title: Guo Moruo Receives Delegation from the Hungarian Academy of Sciences
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received Ferenc Erdei, head of the delegation from the Hungarian Academy of Sciences and Secretary-General of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, and delegation members Imre Szabó and Tivadar Siklós this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Xie Xinhe, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Wang Tuo, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Affairs.

Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet last night to welcome the delegation from the Hungarian Academy of Sciences.

### 郭沫若接见匈牙利科学院代表团

1958-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见匈牙利科学院代表团
    新华社30日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今天下午接见了匈牙利科学院代表团团长、匈牙利科学院秘书长埃尔德·费伦茨和代表团团员萨波·伊姆雷、西克洛什·提瓦达。
    接见时在座的，有中国科学院副秘书长谢鑫鹤和对外联络局局长王拓。
    中国科学院副院长竺可桢昨晚曾设宴欢迎匈牙利科学院代表团。
