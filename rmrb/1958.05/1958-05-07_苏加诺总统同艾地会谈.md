Title: President Sukarno Meets with Aidit
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 5th: Indonesian President Sukarno received a delegation from the Indonesian Communist Party, consisting of Aidit, Lukman, and Sudisman, at Bogor Palace at noon on the 5th.

The delegation presented the resolutions of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party to President Sukarno. During this harmonious meeting, the representatives of the Indonesian Communist Party explained the aforementioned resolutions. Regarding international issues, the representatives mentioned the economic recession in the United States and its potential impact on the Indonesian economy. Regarding domestic issues, the representatives emphatically discussed eliminating rebel groups, developing a united front of all democratic forces, and also emphasized the fundamental tasks of opposing American-instigated aggression and defending the existing democratic system.

### 苏加诺总统同艾地会谈

1958-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统同艾地会谈
    新华社雅加达5日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺5日中午在茂物接见了由艾地、鲁克曼和苏蒂斯曼三人组成的印度尼西亚共产党代表团。
    代表团把印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会第六次全体会议的决议转交给苏加诺总统。在这次融洽的会谈中，印度尼西亚共产党的代表解释了上述决议。关于国际问题，代表们提到了美国的经济衰退和它对印度尼西亚经济可能发生的影响。关于国内问题，代表们强调地谈到消灭叛乱集团，发展一切民主力量的统一战线，也强调了反对美国唆使的侵略和保卫现有民主制度的基本任务。
