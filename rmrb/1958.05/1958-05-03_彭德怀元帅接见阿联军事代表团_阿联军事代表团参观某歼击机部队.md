Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai Receives Military Delegation from the United Arab Republic; Military Delegation from the United Arab Republic Visits a Certain Fighter Jet Unit
Author:
Date: 1958-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd May – Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai received this afternoon the head of the Military Goodwill Delegation of the United Arab Republic visiting China, Lieutenant General Mohammed Ibrahim, delegation members Lieutenant General Salem, Brigadier General Karim, and delegation member, Military Attaché of the United Arab Republic to China, Colonel Montaki, and presented gifts to the distinguished guests.

Present at the reception were: Vice Minister of National Defense General Tan Zheng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff General Zhang Zongxun, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, and China's Military Attaché to the United Arab Republic, Senior Colonel Li Dazhong.

Minister Peng Dehuai and the distinguished guests held a long and cordial conversation. Head of Delegation Ibrahim said to Minister Peng Dehuai: "Congratulations to the Chinese People's Liberation Army for its high level, and thank you for your thoughtful care. Your doors are open to us everywhere." On behalf of the Minister of Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the United Arab Republic, Marshal Amer, Head of Delegation Ibrahim presented gifts to Minister Peng Dehuai and invited the Chinese People's Liberation Army to send a military delegation to the United Arab Republic for a visit at an appropriate time. After accepting the gifts presented by Marshal Amer, Minister Peng Dehuai extended greetings to the Armed Forces of the United Arab Republic on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Minister Peng Dehuai said: "Our friendship is long-term and sincere, and it is built on the common foundation of opposing imperialist aggression and safeguarding our own national security."

At the end of the reception, Minister Peng Dehuai presented gifts to the distinguished guests and took a group photo with them.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd May – The Military Goodwill Delegation of the United Arab Republic, accompanied by Air Force Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, and China's Military Attaché to the United Arab Republic, Senior Colonel Li Dazhong, visited a certain fighter jet unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force this morning.

### 彭德怀元帅接见阿联军事代表团  阿联军事代表团参观某歼击机部队

1958-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅接见阿联军事代表团
    阿联军事代表团参观某歼击机部队
    新华社2日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅今天下午接见阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团团长穆罕默德·易卜拉欣中将、团员萨勒姆中将、卡里姆准将和团员、阿拉伯联合共和国驻我国武官蒙大奇上校，并向贵宾们赠送了礼品。
    接见时在座的有：国防部副部长谭政大将，副总参谋长张宗逊上将，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，国防部外事处处长朱开印大校，我国驻阿拉伯联合共和国武官李大中上校。
    彭德怀部长和贵宾们进行了长时间的亲切的交谈。易卜拉欣团长向彭德怀部长说：“祝贺中国人民解放军有很高的水平，感谢你们对我们的周到照顾，你们的门到处都是对我们开着的。”易卜拉欣团长代表阿拉伯联合共和国国防部长、武装部队总司令阿密尔元帅向彭德怀部长赠送了礼品，并邀请中国人民解放军在适当时机派遣军事代表团到阿拉伯联合共和国进行参观访问。彭德怀部长接受了阿密尔元帅赠送的礼品后，代表中国人民解放军向阿拉伯联合共和国武装部队致意。彭德怀部长说：“我们的友谊是长期的真诚的，是建筑在反对帝国主义侵略、保卫自己国家安全的共同基础上。”
    接见结束时，彭德怀部长向贵宾们分别赠送了礼品，并和贵宾们一起合影留念。
    据新华社2日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团今天上午由常乾坤空军中将、朱开印大校和我国驻阿拉伯联合共和国武官李大中上校等陪同参观了中国人民解放军空军某歼击机部队。
