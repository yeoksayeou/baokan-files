Title: Chongqing, Xi'an and Other Cities Hold Rallies in Commemoration
Author:
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th—Chongqing, Xi'an, Harbin, Shenyang, and other cities held commemorative meetings and report meetings on the 4th and 5th to commemorate the 140th anniversary of Marx's birth.

    Xin Yizhi, Secretary of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Wang Rong, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zheng Yiping, Secretary of the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhang Peng of the Marxist-Leninist Teaching and Research Office of the First Intermediate Party School of the CPC Central Committee; and Wang Huachun of the Marxist-Leninist Teaching and Research Office of Shenyang Normal University delivered reports respectively. They pointed out that to commemorate Marx is to better study Marxism-Leninism. They also pointed out that the Program of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia is a thorough anti-Marxist revisionism, and called on everyone to defend Marxist-Leninist theory and oppose revisionism.

### 重庆、西安等城市集会纪念

1958-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    重庆、西安等城市集会纪念
    据新华社5日讯　重庆、西安、哈尔滨、沈阳等城市分别在4日、5日举行纪念会和报告会，纪念马克思诞生一百四十周年。
    中共重庆市委书记辛易之、中共陕西省委宣传部副部长王荣、中共哈尔滨市委书记郑依平、中共中央第一中级党校马列主义教研室张鹏和沈阳师范学院马列主义教研室王化春分别作了报告。他们指出，纪念马克思，就要更好地学习马列主义。并指出，南共纲领是彻头彻尾的反马克思主义的修正主义，号召大家保卫马列主义理论，反对修正主义。
