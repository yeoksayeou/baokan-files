Title: A Symbol of Sino-Czech Friendship: Beijing Film Processing Factory Begins Trial Operation
Author:
Date: 1958-05-03
Page: 3

    Construction Flowers

    A Symbol of Sino-Czech Friendship
    Beijing Film Processing Factory Begins Trial Operation
    The newly built modern Beijing Film Processing Factory, through the efforts of construction and installation personnel and with the assistance of Czechoslovak experts, has been basically completed five months ahead of schedule and held a trial operation on April 30.
    Czechoslovak Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, Mr. Bušiak, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, and Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture Wang Lanshi, and others attended the trial operation ceremony at the factory.
    After the factory is officially put into production in the second half of this year, it can wash and print about 60 million meters of film annually (an ordinary feature film is about 2,500 to 2,700 meters long), and the quality of washing and printing can also be higher than the current level in our country.
  (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 中捷友谊的一个标志  北京电影洗印厂试运转

1958-05-03
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    中捷友谊的一个标志
    北京电影洗印厂试运转
    新建的现代化的北京电影洗印厂，经过建筑、安装人员的努力和捷克斯洛伐克专家的帮助，已提前五个月基本修建完成，4月30日举行了试运转。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中华人民共和国大使布希尼亚克，文化部副部长夏衍，文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西等都到厂出席了试运转仪式。
    这个厂在今年下半年正式投入生产后，每年可以洗印六千万公尺左右的影片（普通一部故事片大约是二千五百公尺到二千七百公尺长），洗印的质量也可以比我国现在的水平高。
  （据新华社讯）
