Title: Yugoslav Parliamentarian and University President Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 29th — According to the 1958 execution plan of the Sino-Yugoslav Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Professor of the Faculty of Law, Doctor of Law, and member of the Federal Chamber of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, Marks Šnuderl of the University of Ljubljana, and the President of the University of Sarajevo, Čamo Edhem, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit and study tour in China.

### 南斯拉夫一议员和一大学校长到京

1958-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫一议员和一大学校长到京
    新华社29日讯　根据中南文化合作协定1958年执行计划来中国访问考察的南斯拉夫卢布尔雅那大学法学系教授、法学博士、联邦国民议会联邦院议员马克斯·什努德尔和南萨拉热窝大学校长查莫·埃德罕穆两人，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。
