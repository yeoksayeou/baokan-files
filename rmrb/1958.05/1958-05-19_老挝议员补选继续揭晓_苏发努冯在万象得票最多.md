Title: Lao By-election Results Continue to be Announced; Souphanouvong Receives Most Votes in Vientiane
Author:
Date: 1958-05-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th. Vientiane Radio announced today that the parliamentary elections in Vientiane Province have been fully revealed. Prince Souphanouvong, leader of the Lao Patriotic Front, was elected as a member of the Lao National Assembly with the highest number of votes (over 37,000 votes). Bouthdi, of the Lao Committee for the Support of Peace and Neutrality Policy, was also elected with over 21,000 votes.

According to regulations, only two members of parliament were to be elected from Vientiane Province in this by-election. Candidates from the National Party and the Independent Party all failed to be elected.

In Luang Prabang Province, with the results tallied from 239 out of 289 polling stations, the three candidates from the Lao Patriotic Front, Madame Khampheng Boupha, Prince Chao Souk, and Phoumi, continue to lead.

### 老挝议员补选继续揭晓  苏发努冯在万象得票最多

1958-05-19
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝议员补选继续揭晓
　　苏发努冯在万象得票最多
    新华社河内17日电　据万象电台今天广播，万象省的议员选举投票已经全部揭晓，老挝爱国党领袖苏发努冯亲王以最多票数（三万七千多票）当选为老挝国民议会议员。老挝拥护和平中立政策委员会的布迪也以两万一千多票当选。
    按规定，在这次补充选举中万象省只选出议员两名。国家党和独立党等的候选人全都落选。
    在琅勃拉邦省，二百八十九个选举站中的二百三十九个站开票结果，老挝爱国党的三名候选人坎平·布法夫人、昭·苏克亲王和富米继续领先。
