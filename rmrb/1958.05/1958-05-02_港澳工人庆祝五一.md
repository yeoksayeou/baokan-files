Title: Hong Kong and Macau Workers Celebrate May Day
Author:
Date: 1958-05-02
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, May 1st. Workers in Hong Kong and Macau gathered to celebrate May Day.

The Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions held a tea party for representatives of various trade unions last night to celebrate the working class's own festival. Various industrial trade unions in Hong Kong and Kowloon have also held celebratory activities such as gatherings. Workers from all walks of life in Hong Kong and Kowloon will jointly hold a May Day celebration gathering on May 3rd. Workers from all walks of life in Macau have also held various celebration activities in the past few days. The Macau Federation of Trade Unions held a celebration party tonight and held a photo exhibition of the Great Leap Forward in industry and agriculture in the motherland.

### 港澳工人庆祝五一

1958-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

    港澳工人庆祝五一
    新华社香港1日电　香港和澳门的工人纷纷集会庆祝“五一”。
    港九工会联合会昨晚举行各工会代表茶叙会，庆祝工人阶级自己的节日。港九各业工会也先后分别举行了联欢会等庆祝活动。港九各业工人将在5月3日联合举行庆祝“五一”的联欢大会。澳门各行各业工人也在这几天举行了各种庆祝活动。澳门工联总会在今天晚上举行了庆祝晚会，并举办了祖国工农业大跃进图片展览。
