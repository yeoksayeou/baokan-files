Title: Burmese Journalists Delegation Leaves Beijing for Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1958-05-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Burmese Journalists Delegation, invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit China, left Beijing for Hanoi today, led by head of the delegation U Ba Than, after a visit to China of more than thirty days.

Deng Tuo, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, held a banquet on the evening of the 6th to bid farewell to the Burmese Journalists Delegation.

### 缅甸新闻工作者代表团离京去河内

1958-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸新闻工作者代表团离京去河内
    据新华社7日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会的邀请，来中国访问的缅甸新闻工作者代表团，在中国进行了三十多天的访问之后，今天在团长吴巴丹的率领下，离开北京前往河内。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会会长邓拓，曾在6日晚举行宴会，为缅甸新闻工作者代表团送行。
