Title: Morocco Demands Immediate Withdrawal of French Troops from Eastern Border
Author:
Date: 1958-05-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, May 21st: A spokesman for the Moroccan Foreign Ministry announced yesterday that Morocco has formally requested the immediate withdrawal of French troops stationed in eastern Morocco near the Algerian border.
    
    Reports say that the above request was made in a note from Morocco to France. The note has been delivered to the French Ambassador to Morocco. It is said that the Moroccan authorities are particularly concerned about the withdrawal of French troops in view of the military riots instigated by French right-wing extremists in Algeria.

### 摩洛哥要求法军立即撤出东部边境

1958-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥要求法军
    立即撤出东部边境
    据新华社21日讯　拉巴特消息：摩洛哥外交部发言人昨天宣布，摩洛哥已经正式要求驻扎在摩洛哥东部靠近阿尔及利亚边境的法国军队立即撤退。
    有消息说，上述要求是在摩洛哥给法国的一份照会中提出的。这份照会已经交给了法国驻摩洛哥大使。据说，摩洛哥当局鉴于法国极右分子在阿尔及利亚策动军事暴乱，对法军撤退问题特别关心。
