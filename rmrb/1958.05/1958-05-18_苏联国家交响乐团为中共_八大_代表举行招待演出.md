Title: Soviet National Symphony Orchestra Holds Reception Performance for Delegates to the Eighth National Congress of the CPC
Author:
Date: 1958-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 17th: The Soviet National Symphony Orchestra gave a special reception performance tonight at the Tianqiao Theater for delegates attending the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China. The orchestra will officially perform tomorrow. At the end of each program tonight, the delegates applauded warmly for a long time, and the Soviet artists had to take bows many times.

At the end of the entire performance, Chen Yi, He Long, and others, accompanied by Antonov, the Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in China, stepped onto the stage to present flower baskets to the Soviet artists and warmly applaud their successful performance.

Tonight, the orchestra's director Anosov and chief conductor, Soviet People's Artist Ivanov, conducted two sets of programs respectively. In addition to the overture to Glinka's opera "Ruslan and Lyudmila," the founder of Russian classical music, and Tchaikovsky's "1812" Overture, the program also included five symphonies composed by Chinese composers Ma Sicong, He Luting, and Shi Yongkang.

### 苏联国家交响乐团为中共“八大”代表举行招待演出

1958-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联国家交响乐团为中共“八大”代表举行招待演出
    新华社17日讯　苏联国家交响乐团今晚在天桥剧场专门为出席中共第八届全国代表大会第二次会议的代表们举行了一次招待演出。乐团将在明天正式公演。在今晚每一个节目演出完毕时，代表们都长时间的热烈鼓掌，苏联艺术家不得不多次谢幕。
    整个演出结束时，陈毅、贺龙等由苏联驻华临时代办安东诺夫陪同步上舞台，向苏联艺术家们赠送花篮，热烈鼓掌祝贺他们的演出成功。
    今晚乐团团长阿诺索夫和总指挥、苏联人民演员伊万诺夫分别指挥演出了两组节目。节目中除包括俄罗斯古典音乐奠基人格林卡的歌剧“芦斯兰与柳德米拉”序曲和柴可夫斯基的“一八一二”序曲外，还有中国作曲家马思聪、贺绿汀和施咏康所作的五个交响曲。
