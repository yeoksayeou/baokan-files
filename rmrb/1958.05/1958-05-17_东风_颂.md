Title: Ode to "Dongfeng"
Author: Yiding
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 8

　　Ode to "Dongfeng"
　　Yiding
　　On the afternoon of May 15th, the delegates of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China watched the test drive of China's first car produced by the First Automobile Works – the "Dongfeng" brand passenger car – outside Huairen Hall. A impromptu poem to express my joy.
　　A small new car, a wonder to all,
　　Test drive outside Huairen Hall,
　　A hundred flowers bloom in the beautiful spring light,
　　Joyfully watching the "Dongfeng's" first blossom.

### “东风”颂
一丁
1958-05-17
第8版()
专栏：

　　“东风”颂
　　一丁
    五月十五日下午，中共“八大”代表在怀仁堂外看第一汽车厂出品的我国第一辆小汽车——“东风”牌客车试车。口占一绝志喜。
    小小新车众所奇，
    怀仁堂外试奔驰，
    百花齐放春光好，
    喜看“东风”第一枝。
