Title: Unwilling to Promote European Peace: US Rejects Rapacki Plan
Author:
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 5

    Unwilling to Promote European Peace
    US Rejects Rapacki Plan
    Xinhua News Agency, May 5th dispatch from Washington: After holding it up for two months and twenty days, the United States government released on the 4th a reply to the Polish government, clearly indicating its rejection of Poland's proposal to establish a nuclear-weapon-free zone in Central Europe to promote European peace.
    In a memorandum delivered to the United States on February 14th of this year, the Polish government elaborated on its plan to establish a nuclear-weapon-free zone in Central Europe.
    This famous Rapacki Plan, since it was proposed last October, has won the hearts of the people of Western European countries. The Belgian government formally expressed its agreement to accept the plan, and the governments of other countries have expressed keen interest in it.

### 无意促进欧洲和平  美国拒绝腊帕茨基计划

1958-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    无意促进欧洲和平
    美国拒绝腊帕茨基计划
    据新华社5日讯　华盛顿消息：在搁置了两个月零二十天以后，美国政府在4日公布了它给波兰政府的一分复照，明白表示拒绝波兰关于建立中欧无原子武器区以促进欧洲和平的建议。
    波兰政府在今年2月14日交给美国的一分备忘录里详细地阐述了它的建立中欧无原子武器区的计划。
    这一个著名的腊帕茨基计划自从去年10月提出以后，深得西欧国家的人心。比利时政府曾经正式表示它同意接受这个计划，而另外一些国家的政府曾经对这个计划表示过浓厚的兴趣。
