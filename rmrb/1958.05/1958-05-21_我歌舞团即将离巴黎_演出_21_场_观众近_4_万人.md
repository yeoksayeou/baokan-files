Title: Our Song and Dance Troupe About to Leave Paris: 21 Performances, Nearly 40,000 Viewers

Author:

Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 20th: Wu Han, head of the Chinese Opera and Dance Troupe, held a reception at the George V Hotel on the evening of the 19th, prior to his departure from Paris. Many prominent figures from Parisian art, culture, science, politics, economics, and the press attended the reception. In a brief speech, Wu Han said that the Chinese Opera and Dance Troupe's visit to Paris was to strengthen the friendship between the Chinese and French peoples. He thanked the Parisian public for their warm reception and the French friends for their valuable assistance during the performances.

The Chinese Opera and Dance Troupe came here to participate in the Paris International Theatre Festival and held twenty-one performances in Paris, with a total audience of thirty-eight thousand people.

### 我歌舞团即将离巴黎  演出21场　观众近4万人

1958-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    我歌舞团即将离巴黎
    演出21场　观众近4万人
    据新华社巴黎20日电　中国戏曲歌舞团团长吴晗，在离开巴黎之前19日晚在乔治五世大饭店举行招待会。巴黎艺术、文化、科学、政治、经济界和报界的许多著名人士参加了这次招待会。吴晗在简短的讲话中说：中国戏曲歌舞团的访问巴黎，是为了加强中法两国人民的友谊。他感谢巴黎公众的热烈接待和法国朋友们在演出期间所给予的可贵协助。
    中国戏曲歌舞团是来这里参加巴黎国际戏剧节的，先后在巴黎举行了二十一次演出，观众共有三万八千人。
