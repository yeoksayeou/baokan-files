Title: Sprinkler Tank (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 2

    Sprinkler Tank
    Created by the People's Committee of Pingyi County, Shandong Province.
    Suitable for watering and spraying pesticides in nurseries and vegetable gardens.
    One person carries two sprinkler tanks, one in front and one behind, with a wooden pole (or shoulder pole), walking and spraying along the edges of the nursery beds, capable of watering two beds.

### 喷水箱（图片）

1958-05-09
第2版()
专栏：

    喷水箱
    山东省平邑县人民委员会创造。
    适合于苗圃和菜园灌水喷药用。
    一个人用木杠（或扁担）前后各担一个喷水箱，在苗圃埂上边走边喷，可浇二个床面。
