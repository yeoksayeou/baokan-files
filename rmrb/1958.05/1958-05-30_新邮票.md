New Stamps
    To commemorate International Children's Day on June 1st, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications will issue a set of special "Children" stamps, with a total of four designs, all with a face value of 8 fen. The designs depict the life of children as infants, pre-schoolers, beginners in school, and those who have joined the Young Pioneers, showing how children in socialist society are growing up happily in the infinite care of the Party and the government and in a peaceful environment. This set of stamps is multi-colored, with vivid and lovely images, and rich in national characteristics.

### 新邮票

1958-05-30
第8版()
专栏：

    新邮票
    为了纪念“六一”国际儿童节，邮电部将发行“儿童”特种邮票一套，共四个图案，面值均为8分。图案通过了婴儿、学龄前、初学及已参加少先队的儿童生活情形，表现出社会主义社会的孩子们，在党和政府的无限关怀下及和平环境中幸福地成长着。这套邮票多彩套印，形像生动可爱，富有民族色彩。
