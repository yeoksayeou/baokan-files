    Did you know?
    △The length of time food stays in the stomach varies. For example, pork has a strong resistance to gastric juices, so it stays in the stomach for four hours. However, drinking a glass of boiled water only takes two minutes to reach the duodenum.

### 你知道吗？

1958-05-22
第8版()
专栏：

    你知道吗？
    △食物在胃里停留时间各有不同，如猪肉对于胃液有顽强抵抗力，因此在胃中要停留四小时，而如饮一杯开水，只需二分钟就达到十二指肠。
