Title: Khrushchev Receives Chen Yun and Li Fuchun
Author:
Date: 1958-05-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 26th: Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, received Chen Yun, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Ye Jizhuang, member of the CPC Central Committee and Minister of Foreign Trade, in Moscow on the 26th and held talks with them.
    Andropov, a member of the Central Committee of the CPSU, was present at the talks.
    The talks were conducted in a sincere and comradely atmosphere.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见陈云李富春

1958-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见陈云李富春
    新华社莫斯科26日电　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，26日在莫斯科接见了中国共产党中央委员会副主席、国务院副总理陈云，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李富春，中共中央委员、对外贸易部长叶季壮，并且同他们进行了谈话。
    谈话时在座的有苏共中央部长安德罗波夫。
    谈话在诚恳的、同志式的气氛中进行。
