Title: Lebanese Parties Alliance Refutes Government's False Accusation Against the UAR
Author:
Date: 1958-05-27
Page: 6

    Lebanese Parties Alliance
    Refutes Government's False Accusation Against the UAR
    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 26th - The Middle East News Agency reported that the Lebanese Parties Alliance sent a telegram to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on the 25th, condemning the Lebanese pro-Western government's "accusation" against the United Arab Republic submitted to the UN Security Council. The telegram stated that the government's action was intended to transform Lebanese internal affairs into an international issue. The telegram expressed hope that the UN Security Council would reject the case.

### 黎巴嫩各党派联盟  驳斥政府对阿联的诬告

1958-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩各党派联盟
    驳斥政府对阿联的诬告
    据新华社大马士革26日电　中东通讯社报道，黎巴嫩各党派联盟在25日打电报给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，谴责黎巴嫩亲西方政府向联合国安全理事会提出的指责阿拉伯联合共和国的“控诉”。电报说，政府的这个行动是想把黎巴嫩内政事件变为国际问题。电报表示希望联合国安全理事会拒绝受理。
