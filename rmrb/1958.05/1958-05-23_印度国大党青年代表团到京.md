Title: Indian National Congress Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-23
Page: 4

    Indian National Congress Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd – The Indian National Congress Youth Delegation, invited by the All-China Youth Federation to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by Ravindra Varma, Chairman of the Youth Department of the All-India Congress Committee.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Liu Xiyuan, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Wu Xueqian, Vice Chairman; Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; and Guan Shixiong, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Youth Federation.

### 印度国大党青年代表团到京

1958-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度国大党青年代表团到京
    新华社22日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的印度国大党青年代表团一行六人，在团长、印度国大党全国委员会青年部主席拉文德拉·瓦尔马率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    去机场欢迎的，有中华全国青年联合会主席刘西元、副主席吴学谦，中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立和北京市青联副主席关世雄等。
