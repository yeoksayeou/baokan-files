Title: Heilongjiang Transfers Combine Harvesters to Support Wheat Harvesting in Henan
Author:
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 4

    Heilongjiang Transfers Combine Harvesters
    to Support Wheat Harvesting in Henan
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) To help Henan harvest wheat, Heilongjiang Province has transferred 75 combine harvesters and many machine operators to Henan. Ten of the best machines and excellent machine operators, selected from the state-owned Youyi Farm, arrived in the Henan countryside on the 26th. These personnel and machines will be assigned to work in Xinxiang, Shangqiu, Xuchang, Xinyang and other four regions where wheat is concentrated and there are few people and a lot of land.

### 黑龙江调出联合收割机  支援河南麦收

1958-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    黑龙江调出联合收割机
    支援河南麦收
    据新华社讯　为了帮助河南收麦，黑龙江省调拨七十五台联合收割机和许多机务人员前来河南。由国营友谊农场挑选出的十台最好的机器和优秀的机务人员，26日已领先到达了河南农村。这些人员、机具将要安排到新乡、商丘、许昌、信阳等四个专区小麦集中、人少地多的地方工作。
