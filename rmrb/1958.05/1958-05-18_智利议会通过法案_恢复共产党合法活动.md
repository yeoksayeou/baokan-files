Title: Chilean Parliament Passes Law Restoring Legal Activities of the Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1958-05-18
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, May 16th, TASS: According to the "Siglo" newspaper, the Chilean Parliament's Senate and Chamber of Deputies have passed a law to abolish the clauses in the so-called "Law for the Defense of Democracy" that prohibit the legal activities of the Communist Party.

    The resolution passed by the Senate and Chamber of Deputies, which is awaiting the approval of the Chilean President, is a great victory for progressive forces.

### 智利议会通过法案  恢复共产党合法活动

1958-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

    智利议会通过法案
    恢复共产党合法活动
    据新华社17日讯　塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯16日讯：据“世纪报”报道，智利议会参众两院通过了一项法案，决定废除所谓“保护民主法”中的禁止共产党合法活动的条款。
    参众两院通过的、尚待智利总统批准的这一决议是进步势力的一个巨大胜利。
