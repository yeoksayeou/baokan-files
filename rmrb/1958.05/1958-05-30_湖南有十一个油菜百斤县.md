Author:
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 4

Small Statistics

Hunan Has Eleven Counties with 100 Jin Per Mu of Rapeseed
Hunan Province has seen eleven counties with a rapeseed yield of 100 jin per mu this year. These eleven counties are: Xupu, Qidong, Lingling, Chenxi, Qianyang, Huitong, Dayong, Shaodong, Dongkou, Shuangfeng, and Xinshao.

Hunan Province also has 119 townships and 1,434 cooperatives with rapeseed yields above 100 jin per mu this year; 23 townships and 260 cooperatives with rapeseed yields of 150 jin to 200 jin per mu; and 5 townships and 74 cooperatives with rapeseed yields of 201 jin to 250 jin per mu. In addition, there are six cooperatives with rapeseed yields of 250 jin to 300 jin per mu, and one cooperative with a yield of over 300 jin per mu.

### 湖南有十一个油菜百斤县

1958-05-30
第4版()
专栏：小统计

    湖南有十一个油菜百斤县
    湖南省今年出现了十一个油菜籽亩产一百斤的县，这十一个县是：溆浦、祁东、零陵、辰溪、黔阳、会同、大庸、邵东、洞口、双峰、新邵。
    湖南省今年还有一百一十九个乡、一千四百三十四个社，亩产油菜籽百斤以上；有二十三个乡、二百六十个社，亩产菜籽一百五十斤到二百斤；有五个乡、七十四个社，亩产菜籽二百零一斤到二百五十斤。此外还有六个社亩产菜籽二百五十斤到三百斤，有一个社亩产三百斤以上。
