Title: Vietnamese National Assembly Delegation Leaves to Visit Eastern European Brother Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 5

　　Visiting Eastern European Brother Countries
    Vietnamese National Assembly Delegation Leaves
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th - The National Assembly delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam left Hanoi this morning to visit some socialist countries in Eastern Europe. The five-member delegation is led by Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly.

### 访问东欧兄弟国家  越南国会代表团启程

1958-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

　　访问东欧兄弟国家
    越南国会代表团启程
    据新华社河内16日电　越南民主共和国国会代表团今天上午离开河内前往东欧的一些社会主义国家访问。这个五人代表团由越南国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜率领。
