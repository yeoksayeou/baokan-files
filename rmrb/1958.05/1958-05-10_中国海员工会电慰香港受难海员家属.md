Title: Chinese Seamen's Union Sends Condolences to Families of Hong Kong Seamen in Distress
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Liu Dachao, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union, today sent a telegram to the Hong Kong Seamen's Union, expressing condolences to the families of the Hong Kong seamen in distress.

The telegram said: "American aircraft assisted the Indonesian rebel group in indiscriminately bombing Hong Kong merchant ships berthed in Indonesia, killing and injuring our fellow seamen. This inhuman and bandit-like atrocity cannot but arouse the great indignation of seamen and all workers across the country.

Please convey the deep condolences of the nation's seamen to the families of our fellow seamen in distress."

### 中国海员工会电慰香港受难海员家属

1958-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    中国海员工会电慰香港受难海员家属
    新华社9日讯　中国海员工会全国委员会主席刘达潮今天打电报给香港海员工会，对香港受难海员家属表示慰问。
    电报说：“美国飞机协助印度尼西亚叛乱集团滥炸停泊在印度尼西亚的香港商船，炸死、炸伤我海员同胞。这一惨无人道的强盗暴行，不能不引起全国海员和全体职工的极大愤慨。
    请向受难海员同胞的家属转达全国海员对他们的深切慰问。”
