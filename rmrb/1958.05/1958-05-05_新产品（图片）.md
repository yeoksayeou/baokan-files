Title: New Products (Photos)
Author: Huo Shenglian, Xu Zhicheng, An Kang
Date: 1958-05-05
Page: 4

    New Products
    The "Chengfeng" brand automobile manufactured by the Shanghai Municipal Transportation Bureau's Automotive Repair System is capable of carrying a load of one and a half tons. Photo by Huo Shenglian (Xinhua News Agency draft)
    The water sprinkler truck trial-produced by the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant. Its water carrying capacity is 3.5 tons.
          Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Zhicheng photo
    The machine repair workshop of the Beijing Agricultural Machinery Plant has successfully trial-produced an artificial rainmaking machine. Its range diameter reaches 140 meters, and calculated at 100 tons of water per mu, it can irrigate 135 mu of land in ten hours. Xinhua News Agency reporter An Kang photo
    The "Zhonghua" brand hand-operated mobile pump manufactured by the Shanghai Safety Fire Equipment Factory can discharge 66 tons of water per hour. Photo by Huo Shenglian (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 新产品（图片）
霍生联  胥志成  安康
1958-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

    新产品
    上海市交通运输局汽车修理系统制成的“乘风”牌汽车，能够载重一吨半。霍生联摄（新华社稿）
    第一汽车制造厂试制成的洒水车。它的载水量有三点五吨。
          新华社记者　胥志成摄
    北京农业机械厂机修车间试制成功一台人工降雨机。它的射程直径达一百四十公尺，按每亩地浇水一百吨计算，十个钟头可浇地一百三十五亩。　新华社记者　安康摄
    上海安全消防器材厂制成的中华牌手抬式机动泵，每小时能出水六十六吨。　霍生联摄（新华社稿）
