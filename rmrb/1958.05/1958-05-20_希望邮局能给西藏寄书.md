Title: Hoping the Post Office Can Send Books to Tibet
Author: Dang Xiong Cheng Wanjun
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 3

　　Hoping the Post Office Can Send Books to Tibet
　　I work in Tibet. In recent years, what we have felt to be the greatest inconvenience in Tibet is the lack of spiritual food. At present, no Xinhua Bookstore branches have been established in the Tibetan region. Comrades working here have to ask friends in the mainland to buy books and send them here. However, the postal and telecommunications departments in the mainland refuse to accept printed matter sent to the Tibetan region. This is really inconvenient for comrades working in Tibet.
　　It is hoped that the postal and telecommunications departments, in conjunction with the rectification and study movement, will break with conventions and quickly resolve this problem.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Tibet, Dangxiong, Cheng Wanjun

### 希望邮局能给西藏寄书
当雄成万钧
1958-05-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　希望邮局能给西藏寄书
    我是在西藏工作的。几年来，我们在西藏感到最大的不便就是缺少精神食粮。目前西藏地区还没有建立新华书店的分支店。在这里工作的同志，要买书就得托内地的朋友代买寄来。可是内地邮电部门却不肯收寄西藏地区的印刷品。这对在西藏工作的同志实在不方便。
    希望邮电部门，结合整风学习，打破常规，迅速解决这个问题。
              西藏　当雄成万钧
