Title: National Leaders Watch Fireworks and Receive Foreign Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-05-02
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 1st May: Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and other national leaders of our country watched the colorful festive fireworks together with guests from many countries on the Tiananmen Rostrum on the evening of May Day, as well as the grand celebration of hundreds of thousands of people on Tiananmen Square.

This evening on the Tiananmen Rostrum, Vice Chairman Zhu, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou also separately received the heads of delegations from various countries and prominent figures from various countries who were invited to attend the May Day celebrations and observation, and held conversations with them.

There were wonderful artistic performances and a grand ball on the Tiananmen Rostrum, where our national leaders and guests spent this carnival night together.

### 国家领导人观看焰火接见外宾

1958-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

    国家领导人观看焰火接见外宾
    新华社1日讯　朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理和我国的其他领导人员，五一节晚间在天安门城楼上同来自许多国家的客人们一起观看了多彩的节日焰火和天安门广场上几十万人的大联欢。
    朱副主席、刘委员长和周总理今晚在天安门城楼上还分别接见了应邀参加五一节观礼的各国代表团的负责人和各国著名人士，同他们进行了交谈。
    天安门城楼上有精彩的艺术表演和盛大的舞会，我国国家领导人和客人们一起度过了这个狂欢之夜。
