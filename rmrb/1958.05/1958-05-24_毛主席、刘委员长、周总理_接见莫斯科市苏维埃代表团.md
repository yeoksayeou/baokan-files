Title: Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, Premier Zhou Receive Moscow City Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-05-24
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai received all members of the Moscow City Workers' Representative Soviet delegation, headed by Bobrovnikov, this evening.

    Present at the reception were Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Zeng Yongquan, Feng Jiping, and Antonov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China. (Attached picture)

    The image below shows the scene of the reception. The second person from the left is the delegation leader, Bobrovnikov. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo.

### 毛主席、刘委员长、周总理  接见莫斯科市苏维埃代表团

1958-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席、刘委员长、周总理
    接见莫斯科市苏维埃代表团
    新华社23日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理，今天晚间接见了以波布罗夫尼科夫为首的莫斯科市劳动者代表苏维埃代表团的全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有彭真、陈毅、曾涌泉、冯基平，以及苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办安东诺夫。（附图片）
