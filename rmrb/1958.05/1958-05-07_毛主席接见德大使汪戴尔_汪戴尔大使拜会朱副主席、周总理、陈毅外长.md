Title: Chairman Mao Receives GDR Ambassador Wang Diel; Ambassador Wang Diel Pays Courtesy Calls on Vice Chairman Zhu, Premier Zhou, and Foreign Minister Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th May - Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Wang Diel, the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, this evening.

Present at the reception were Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Yutian, Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, 6th May - After his arrival in China, Wang Diel, the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, paid courtesy calls on Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, and Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 毛主席接见德大使汪戴尔  汪戴尔大使拜会朱副主席、周总理、陈毅外长

1958-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见德大使汪戴尔
    汪戴尔大使拜会朱副主席、周总理、陈毅外长
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东今天晚上接见了德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔。
    接见时在座的有外交部副部长张闻天，社会主义国家司司长王雨田。
    新华社6日讯　德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔到任后，曾分别拜会了中华人民共和国副主席朱德，国务院总理周恩来，副总理兼外交部长陈毅。
