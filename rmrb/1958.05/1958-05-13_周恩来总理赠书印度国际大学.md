Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Donates Books to Visva-Bharati University, India
Author:
Date: 1958-05-13
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 12th: According to a report by the Press Trust of India on the 11th, the batch of Chinese books donated by Premier Zhou Enlai to Visva-Bharati University in Shantiniketan, West Bengal, India, has been transported to the location. This university was founded by the great Indian poet Tagore.

　　These books were promised to the Chinese College of Visva-Bharati University by Premier Zhou Enlai during his visit to Shantiniketan at the end of January last year.

　　The six boxes of books donated by Premier Zhou Enlai contain more than 10,000 volumes, including precious classical books, modern works, important documents, and periodicals.

### 周恩来总理赠书印度国际大学

1958-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理赠书印度国际大学
    新华社新德里12日电　据印度报业托辣斯11日报道：周恩来总理赠送给印度西孟加拉邦山提尼克坦国际大学的一批中国书籍已经运到当地。这所大学是印度大诗人泰戈尔创办的。
    这些书籍是去年1月底周恩来总理访问山提尼克坦时答应赠送给国际大学的中国学院的。
    周恩来总理赠送的六箱书籍共一万多册，其中包括珍贵的古典书籍、现代著作、重要文件和期刊杂志等。
