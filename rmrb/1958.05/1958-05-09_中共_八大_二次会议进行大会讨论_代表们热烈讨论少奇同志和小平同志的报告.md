Title: The Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the CPC Holds Plenary Discussions. Representatives Enthusiastically Discuss Comrades Shaoqi and Xiaoping's Reports.
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th dispatch – The Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China continued this afternoon.

Today’s session focused on discussions of Comrade Liu Shaoqi’s report on the work of the Central Committee and Comrade Deng Xiaoping’s report on the Moscow meetings of communist and workers' parties of various countries.

During the group meetings on the 6th and 7th, the representatives enthusiastically discussed the reports by Comrades Liu Shaoqi and Deng Xiaoping.

### 中共“八大”二次会议进行大会讨论  代表们热烈讨论少奇同志和小平同志的报告

1958-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中共“八大”二次会议进行大会讨论
    代表们热烈讨论少奇同志和小平同志的报告
    新华社8日讯　中国共产党第八届全国代表大会第二次会议今日下午继续举行。
    今天的大会就刘少奇同志关于中央委员会的工作报告和邓小平同志关于各国共产党和工人党的莫斯科会议的报告，进行了讨论。
    在6日和7日两天的小组会议中，代表们热烈地讨论了刘少奇同志和邓小平同志的报告。
