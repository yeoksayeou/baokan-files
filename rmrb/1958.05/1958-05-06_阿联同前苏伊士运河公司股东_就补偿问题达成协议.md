Title: Arab United Republic and Former Suez Canal Company Shareholders Reach Agreement on Compensation Issue
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rome: Representatives of the United Arab Republic and representatives of the former Suez Canal Company shareholders reached an agreement in Rome on April 29 regarding the compensation issue for the former Suez Canal Company shareholders.

The agreement stipulates that the United Arab Republic will compensate the former Suez Canal Company shareholders a total of 28.3 million Egyptian pounds, to be paid in seven installments over six years.

### 阿联同前苏伊士运河公司股东  就补偿问题达成协议

1958-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联同前苏伊士运河公司股东
    就补偿问题达成协议
    据新华社讯　罗马消息：阿拉伯联合共和国代表同前苏伊士运河公司股东代表4月29日在罗马就对前苏伊士运河公司股东的补偿问题达成了协议。
    协议规定，阿拉伯联合共和国将总共补偿前苏伊士运河公司股东二千八百三十万埃镑，在六年内分七期偿付。
