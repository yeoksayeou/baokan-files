Title: Central Sumatra Rebel Stronghold Falls; Government Troops Recapture Bukittinggi
Author:
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 5th: According to an announcement by the Indonesian Army authorities this morning, government troops recaptured Bukittinggi, the location of the "government" of the Central Sumatra rebel group, on the morning of May 4th. Government troops have begun to mop up the remaining rebels in the area.

### 中苏门答腊叛军老巢完蛋  政府军收复武吉丁宜

1958-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏门答腊叛军老巢完蛋
    政府军收复武吉丁宜
    新华社雅加达5日电　据印度尼西亚陆军当局今天上午宣布，政府军在5月4日上午收复了中苏门答腊叛乱集团“政府”所在地武吉丁宜。政府军已经开始扫荡当地的残余叛军。
