Title:  Friendlier Exchanges Between the People of China and Vietnam Made Easier: Beilun River Friendship Bridge Opens to Traffic
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 29th: The Beilun River Friendship Bridge, a friendship bridge connecting Dongxing City (under the jurisdiction of Hepu Special District, Guangdong Province) in China to Mong Cai City in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, has recently been completed and a traffic opening ceremony was held on the morning of May 25th.

Huang Maocheng, Vice Chairman of the Administrative Committee of Hai Ninh Province, Vietnam, and Chen Rutang, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, spoke at the meeting on behalf of their respective governments. They congratulated the bridge construction workers of China and Vietnam for completing the bridge construction task ahead of schedule, and pointed out that the completion of the Beilun River Friendship Bridge will play a significant role in promoting political, economic, and cultural exchanges and enhancing friendship between the people of China and Vietnam. After their speeches, Du Min, Chairman of the Administrative Committee of Hai Ninh Province, Vietnam, and Chen Rutang, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, cut the ribbon. Ten brightly decorated cars slowly drove across the bridge from both ends amidst cheers from the people of China and Vietnam. Afterwards, the masses participating in the ceremony held a grand celebratory parade.

The bridge is 124.8 meters long and 10 meters wide, and was jointly invested in by the governments of China and Vietnam. Construction began in November last year.

### 中越两国人民的友好往来更加便利  北仑河友谊大桥落成通车

1958-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

    中越两国人民的友好往来更加便利
    北仑河友谊大桥落成通车
    据新华社广州29日电　一座由中国的东兴市（属广东省合浦专区）通往越南民主共和国芒街市的友谊大桥——北仑河友谊大桥，已于最近建成，并在5月25日上午举行了通车典礼。
    越南海宁省行政委员会副主席黄茂成和广东省副省长陈汝棠，先后代表两国政府在大会上讲了话。他们祝贺中越两国的建桥职工提前完成了建桥任务，并且指出北仑河友谊大桥的建成，对中越两国人民政治、经济、文化的交流和友谊的增强将起重大作用。他们讲话以后，越南海宁省行政委员会主席杜敏和广东省副省长陈汝棠剪彩，十辆装饰得五彩缤纷的汽车，在中越两国人民的欢呼声中，分别从两端徐徐开过大桥。接着，参加典礼的群众举行了盛大的庆祝游行。
    这座大桥全长一百二十四点八公尺，宽十公尺，是由中越两国政府共同投资，在去年11月开始兴建的。
