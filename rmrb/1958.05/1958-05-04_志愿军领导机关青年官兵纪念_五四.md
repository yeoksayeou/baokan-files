Title: Volunteer Army Leading Organ Youth Officers and Soldiers Commemorate "May Fourth"
Author:
Date: 1958-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd. The vast numbers of young officers and soldiers of the leading organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army solemnly gathered today to commemorate the "May Fourth" Youth Day.

　　At nine o'clock in the morning, the youth from various units of the Volunteer Army leading organ entered the colorfully flagged assembly square to the sound of gongs and drums. They each carried their outstanding achievements report cards, honor rolls, and leap forward resolutions from training, work, and study to report their good news to the meeting. The honor roll of a certain squad of a certain unit was plastered with photos of many soldiers who had excellent training results and had made contributions to cherishing weapons. For three or four years, eight squads of this squad had never damaged a single weapon. There was also a report of good news from a certain automobile company breaking the savings target by 70% and driving safely for 50,000 kilometers without accidents in four months, and a leap forward resolution of "one nothing, three fast, more fast, six good" from the Communist Youth League branch of a certain automobile company.

　　Ding Laifu, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Volunteer Army, spoke at the meeting. He encouraged the young people to inherit and carry forward the revolutionary spirit of the May Fourth Movement, to dare to think and speak, to be able to drill and work, to be courageous to create, to dare to innovate, and to dare to struggle against right-leaning conservative ideas and all backward phenomena; to struggle against various difficulties on the road of advancing to socialism, and to build the motherland into a strong socialist country.

　　Kim Ki-soo, head of the Korean youth delegation, also spoke at the meeting. He also presented the Volunteer Army leadership organ with a brocade banner and a model Democratic Youth League member medal on behalf of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League.

　　After the meeting, the young people launched various competitions such as singing, ball games, and shooting. In the evening, they sang and danced with the Korean youth in the駐地.

### 志愿军领导机关青年官兵纪念“五四”

1958-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军领导机关青年官兵纪念“五四”
    据新华社平壤3日电　中国人民志愿军领导机关广大青年官兵，今天隆重集会纪念“五四”青年节。
    上午九时，志愿军领导机关各单位的青年敲锣打鼓整队进入彩旗飘扬的大会广场。他们各自抬着自己在训练、工作和学习中的优秀成绩报喜牌、光荣榜和跃进决心书向大会报喜。某部一小队的光荣榜上贴着许多训练成绩优秀和爱护武器有功战士的照片，三、四年来，这个小队有八个班从来未损坏一件武器。这里还有某部汽车连突破节约指标70％、四个月无事故安全行车五万公里的报喜牌和某部汽车连共青团支部“一无、三快、多快、六好”的跃进决心书。
    志愿军政治部副主任丁莱夫少将在会上讲话。他勉励青年们继承和发扬五四的革命精神，要敢想敢说、能钻能干、勇于创造、敢于革新、勇于向右倾保守思想及一切落后现象作斗争；向社会主义前进道路上各种困难作斗争，把祖国建成为富强的社会主义国家。
    朝鲜青年代表团团长金基洙也在会上讲了话。他还代表朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会赠给志愿军领导机关锦旗和模范民青盟员奖章。
    会后，青年们展开了歌诵、球类、射击等各种比赛。晚上，他们又和驻地朝鲜青年一起歌舞联欢。
