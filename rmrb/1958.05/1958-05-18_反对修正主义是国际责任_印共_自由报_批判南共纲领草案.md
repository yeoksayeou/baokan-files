Title: Opposing Revisionism is an International Responsibility: Indian Communist "Freedom Daily" Criticizes Draft Program of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia

Author:

Date: 1958-05-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 14th: The Indian Communist newspaper "Freedom Daily," published in Calcutta, issued an editorial on the 13th entitled "Against Revisionism," calling for a "relentless ideological struggle" against the revisionism of the Draft Program of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia.

The newspaper supported the "People's Daily" editorial published in Beijing on May 5th, entitled "Modern Revisionism Must Be Criticized," and pointed out that the Draft Program of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia exposes the characteristics of modern revisionists, and this program opposes the basic principles of Marxism-Leninism.

The newspaper condemned the program for making a "distorted analysis" of the international situation, insisting that the Soviet Union pursues a "position of strength" policy. The newspaper said that another example of the program abandoning the basic principles of Marxism-Leninism is that it puts forward the theory that in capitalist societies, the state transcends two opposing classes. The newspaper questioned: "Has any bourgeois state ever opposed the interests of the bourgeoisie? Can any facts be cited to prove that the bourgeois state has opposed monopoly capital?" It said there are no such facts, because this simply will not happen.

The newspaper said that resolutely opposing this attempt to revise Marxism-Leninism is our international responsibility today. No Marxist can remain silent on this issue.

### 反对修正主义是国际责任  印共“自由报”批判南共纲领草案

1958-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　反对修正主义是国际责任
    印共“自由报”批判南共纲领草案
    新华社新德里14日电　在加尔各答出版的印度共产党报纸“自由报”在13日发表了题为“反对修正主义”的社论，号召对南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟纲领的修正主义进行“无情的思想斗争”。
    这家报纸支持北京“人民日报”5月5日发表的题为“现代修正主义必须批判”的社论，并且指出，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟纲领暴露了现代修正主义者的特点，这个纲领反对马克思列宁主义的基本原则。
    这家报纸谴责这个纲领对国际局势进行“歪曲的分析”，硬说苏联实行“实力地位”政策。报纸说，这个纲领抛弃马克思列宁主义基本原则的另一个例子是，它提出了在资本主义社会中国家超于两个敌对阶级之上的理论。这家报纸质问道：“有哪一个资产阶级国家曾经反对过资产阶级的利益？难道能够举出任何事实来证明资产阶级国家反对过垄断资本吗？它说，没有这样的事实。因为根本不会发生这种事情。”
    这家报纸说，坚决反对这种修改马克思列宁主义的企图是我们今天的国际责任。没有一个马克思主义者能够在这个问题上保持沉默。
