Title: Korean People's Delegation to China Passing Through Andong, Soon to Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, 3rd. The Korean People's Delegation to China, led by Head of Delegation Xu Zhengyu and Deputy Head of Delegation Jin Tianhai, a party of seven, arrived in our border city of Andong by train at 11:10 this evening.

Duan Yongjie, Secretary of the CPC Andong Municipal Committee and Mayor of Andong, Deputy Mayor Li Tefu, Liu Peng, Vice Chairman of the Andong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and more than 600 representatives from all walks of life went to the station to welcome them. Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Director of the Organization Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Si Zhenya, Deputy Director of the Department of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, who had come specially from Beijing to greet the distinguished guests, were also present.

After the distinguished guests got off the train at Andong Station, they received flowers presented by young women of Andong. In the lamplight, the welcomers waved bright bouquets and shouted: "Salute to the heroic Korean people!" "Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Korean people!"

Accompanied by Wang Mingyuan and Si Zhenya, the entire Korean People's Delegation to China took a train to Beijing that night.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团  过安东即将来京

1958-05-04
第2版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团
    过安东即将来京
    新华社安东3日电　朝鲜人民访华代表团在团长许贞琡、副团长金天海率领下，一行七人，今晚十一点十分，乘火车到达我国边境城市安东。
    中共安东市委书记、安东市市长段永杰，副市长李特夫，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会安东市分会副主席刘鹏和各界人民代表共六百多人，前往车站欢迎。前往欢迎的，还有特地从北京赶来迎接贵宾的中国人民保卫世界和平委员会组织部副部长王明远和内务部副司长司枕亚。
    贵宾们在安东车站下车后，接受了安东市青年妇女的献花。欢迎的人们在灯光下挥舞着鲜艳的花束，高呼：“向英雄的朝鲜人民致敬！”“中朝人民牢不可破的友谊万岁！”
    朝鲜人民访华代表团全体人员，由王明远、司枕亚陪同，当晚乘火车前往北京。
