Title: Launch Competition to Welcome National Day: North Korea Launches Technical Innovation Movement
Author:
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 29th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Korean workers are enthusiastically launching a production increase competition to welcome the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. In this movement, workers are collectively displaying initiative and creativity, forming a mass technical innovation movement, while at the same time practicing strict economy and striving to break through conservation targets.

In the past three months, Korean workers have implemented more than 7,000 technical innovations, greatly exceeding the original quotas.

In the coal mining industry, as a result of most of the joint shock brigades participating in the high-speed tunneling movement, coal production in April this year exceeded the state plan by 25,000 tons. In the cement industry, the production of each kiln has been increased from 100 days of uninterrupted operation to 150 days of uninterrupted operation. In the textile industry, due to the widespread competition among workers in the same trade, production in April exceeded the state production plan by 445,000 meters.

### 开展竞赛　迎接国庆  朝鲜掀起技术革新运动

1958-05-30
第6版()
专栏：

    开展竞赛　迎接国庆
    朝鲜掀起技术革新运动
    据新华社平壤29日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社消息：朝鲜工人为迎接朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立十周年，正在热烈展开一个增产竞赛运动。在这个运动中，工人们集体地发挥积极性和创造性，形成了一个群众性的技术革新运动，同时厉行节约，并且努力突破节约指标。
    在过去三个月中，朝鲜工人已经实行了七千多项技术革新，从而使生产大大超过了原来的定额。
    在煤矿业方面，由于大多数联合突击队参加高速度挖坑道运动的结果，今年4月份煤的生产超过国家计划二万五千吨。在水泥工业方面，各个窑的生产，已经从一百天不间断提高为一百五十天不间断。在纺织工业方面，由于普遍展开了同工种工人之间的竞赛，4月份的生产超过了国家生产计划四十四万五千公尺。
