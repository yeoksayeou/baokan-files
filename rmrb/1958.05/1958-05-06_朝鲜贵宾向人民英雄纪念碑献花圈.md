Title: Korean Guests Lay Wreath at the Monument to the People's Heroes
Author:
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Xu Zhengyu, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, Kim Chun-hae, deputy head, and all members of the delegation, accompanied by Wang Yifu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, Yue Songsheng, Vice Mayor of Beijing, and Li Yonghao, Korean Ambassador to China, went to Tiananmen Square this morning to lay a wreath at the Monument to the People's Heroes.

### 朝鲜贵宾向人民英雄纪念碑献花圈

1958-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜贵宾向人民英雄纪念碑献花圈
    据新华社5日讯　朝鲜人民访华代表团团长许贞琡、副团长金天海和全体团员，由内务部副部长王一夫、北京市副市长乐松生和朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐陪同，今天上午前往天安门广场向人民英雄纪念碑敬献了花圈。
