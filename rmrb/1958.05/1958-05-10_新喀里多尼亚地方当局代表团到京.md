Title: Delegation of Local Authorities from New Caledonia Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th - At the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the delegation of local authorities from New Caledonia (an archipelago in the Southwest Pacific) arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. The delegation was led by its head, Mr. Rabauld. Ji Chao-ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and others greeted them at the airport.

### 新喀里多尼亚地方当局代表团到京

1958-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    新喀里多尼亚地方当局代表团到京
    新华社9日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请的新喀里多尼亚（西太平洋中的一个群岛）地方当局代表团一行三人，在团长拉鲍尔德率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎等到机场迎接。
