Title: The Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China Opens: Comrade Shaoqi Elucidates the Party's General Line for Socialist Construction; Comrade Xiaoping Criticizes Modern Revisionism Represented by the Yugoslav Communist League Program
Author:
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. The Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China was held in Beijing at 3:00 p.m. today.

After Comrade Mao Zedong announced the opening of the meeting, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Party, delivered a work report. The report covered the current situation, the Party's general line for socialist construction, and future tasks. His report continued until 6:00 p.m.

Following this, Comrade Deng Xiaoping delivered a report on the Moscow meeting of the Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries. In his report, he explained the great significance of the Moscow meeting and the Moscow Declaration, and criticized modern revisionism represented by the program of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia.

Today's meeting ended at 7:50 p.m. Group discussions will be held from the 6th. (Attached picture)

Right picture: Comrade Liu Shaoqi giving a report at the meeting. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong photographed.

### 中共“八大”第二次会议开幕  少奇同志阐明了党的社会主义建设的总路线  小平同志批判了以南共纲领为代表的现代修正主义

1958-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中共“八大”第二次会议开幕
    少奇同志阐明了党的社会主义建设的总路线
    小平同志批判了以南共纲领为代表的现代修正主义
    新华社5日讯　中国共产党第八届全国代表大会第二次会议今天下午三时在北京举行。
    会议在由毛泽东同志宣布开幕后，就由刘少奇同志代表党中央委员会作工作报告。报告的内容是：目前形势，党的社会主义建设的总路线和今后的各项任务。他的报告继续到下午六时。
    接着，邓小平同志作了关于各国共产党和工人党的莫斯科会议的报告。他在报告中说明了莫斯科会议和莫斯科宣言的重大意义，并且对以南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟的纲领为代表的现代修正主义作了批判。
    今天的会议在下午七时五十分结束。会议自6日起进行小组讨论。（附图片）
