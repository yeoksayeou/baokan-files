Title: Straw Hats Turn Yellow After Prolonged Use
Author: Mingxun
Date: 1958-05-18
Page: 4

　　Straw Hats Turn Yellow After Prolonged Use

　　New straw hats are a beautiful off-white color. But after prolonged use, they gradually turn yellow.

　　This is because straw hats are bleached white using sulfur dioxide. Before bleaching, straw hats are also yellow, because the straw braids contain yellow organic pigments. When bleaching with sulfur dioxide, the sulfur dioxide combines with the organic pigments to form another, more complex, colorless compound, which fades the original yellow color of the straw hat. However, the colorless compound formed in this way is very unstable. Over time, it gradually decomposes and turns back into yellow organic pigments, so it appears yellow again.

　　　　　　　　　　（Mingxun）

### 草帽用久了会变黄
铭勋
1958-05-18
第4版()
专栏：为什么

　　草帽用久了会变黄
    新草帽是白黄色的，很漂亮。可是用久了就会逐渐变成黄色。
    原来草帽是用二氧化硫来漂成白色的。在未漂白前，草帽也是黄色，因为草帽鞭中含有黄色的有机色质。用二氧化硫漂白时，就是使二氧化硫与有机色质化合而生成了另一种更复杂的无色化合物，使草帽原有的黄色褪掉。但是这样生成的无色化合物很不稳定，时间一久，它就逐渐分解，又变成黄色的有机色质，所以看起来又是黄色的了。
                      （铭勋）
