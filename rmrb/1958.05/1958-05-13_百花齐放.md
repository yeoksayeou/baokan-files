Title: Let a Hundred Flowers Bloom
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1958-05-13
Page: 8

　　Let a Hundred Herbs Bloom
　　Guo Moruo
    39 Morning Glory
　　Early in the morning, we sound the trumpets:
　　"The sun is out, quickly amplify your enthusiasm!"
　　Ten thousand trumpets play in unison, even thunder is silenced,
　　Waking up six hundred million people like a stampede of horses.
　　Overturning mountains and seas, don't be afraid of collapsing the sky,
　　People have the ability to mend the sky like Nuwa.
　　Since the world belongs to the working people,
　　Hurry and build a socialist China!
    40 Moonflower
　　We and the morning glory have quite a few things in common,
　　But morning glories bloom in the morning, we bloom at night.
　　Milky white flowers emit a light fragrance,
　　The leaves are fertile and moist, the vines climb well, able to reach the eaves.
　　Scientists graft us onto sweet potatoes,
　　The resulting sweet potatoes are as big as pumpkins, and plentiful and sweet.
　　Sweet potatoes are praised for their abundant yield,
　　If we marry, we guarantee more production than ever before.
    41 Ten Mile Fragrance
　　We are white, single-petaled wild roses,
　　Our fragrance can reach ten miles in the wind.
　　Only growing wild in the countryside of Sichuan and Yunnan,
　　Like the peasants of the past, we are not valued.
　　Why not transplant us to other provinces?
　　Even Sichuan and Yunnan are unwilling to plant us in gardens?
　　If cultivated in large quantities and used to make perfume,
　　Wouldn't that be economical and extremely precious?
    42　Ten Sisters
　　We are a very common type of rose,
　　A cluster of ten flowers, hence the name Ten Sisters.
　　This signifies that unity is strength,
　　Ten fingers are more capable than one.
　　Sometimes quantitative change can lead to qualitative change,
　　Small but many flowers can increase grandeur.
　　We try our best to bloom profusely,
　　So much so that you'll worry about the flower rack being crushed.
    43  Monthly Rose
　　Compared to the Ten Sisters, we work alone,
　　Our flower shape is large but cannot form a cluster.
　　Even if we can bloom every month and season,
　　The lack of fragrance is a major flaw.
　　If we were grafted onto the Ten Sisters,
　　Wouldn't it be stronger if the flower shape was large and clustered?
　　And if we were grafted onto the Ten Mile Fragrance,
　　Wouldn't we be fragrant for ten miles and incomparably beautiful?
    44  Rose
　　We fully sympathize with the monthly rose's suggestion,
　　We only have a little more fragrance than the monthly rose.
　　Who can graft us onto the Ten Sisters?
　　We don't want to be conservative, we truly want to leap forward.
　　If our flowers could become an entire frame or two,
　　Instead of just one or two pots as they are now;
　　Blooming more, blooming faster, blooming better, and blooming economically,
　　That would be a socialist revolution for us.

### 百花齐放
郭沫若
1958-05-13
第8版()
专栏：

　　百药齐放
　　郭沫若
    39 牵牛花
　　一大清早我们就奏起喇叭：
　　“太阳出来了，快把干劲放大！”
　　万只喇叭齐奏，雷霆都喑哑，
　　吹起六亿人民如奔腾万马。
　　倒海排山，不要怕把天弄垮，
　　人们有补天能力就像女娲。
　　天下既是劳动人民的天下，
　　赶快建成社会主义的中华！
    40月光花
　　我们和牵牛花有不少相同之点，
　　但牵牛花开在早晨，我们在晚间。
　　乳白色的花朵放出些香味清淡，
　　叶够肥润，藤善攀缘，可直上屋檐。
　　科学家把我们嫁接在白薯上面，
　　结出的白薯大如倭瓜，又多又甜。
　　白薯本以产量丰富而受人称赞，
　　咱们结了婚，保证着多产再多产。
    41十里香
　　我们是白色的单瓣野蔷薇，
　　我们的香气随风可达十里。
　　只野生在四川、云南的乡间，
　　像往日的农民不受人重视。
　　为什么不移植到别的省份，
　　连川滇也不肯种在庭园里？
　　如果栽培多，用来制造香水，
　　岂不是经济而又非常名贵？
    42　十姊妹
　　我们是一种最普通的蔷薇，
　　一簇十朵花，故叫作十姊妹。
　　这可意味着团结就是力量，
　　十个指头比一个指头有为。
　　有时候量变可以引起质变，
　　花小而多便可以增加壮美。
　　我们是尽可能地开出繁花，
　　使你会耽心，要把花架压碎。
    43  月季花
　　比起十姊妹来我们是在单干，
　　我们的花型虽大却不能成团。
　　即使月月季季都能开出花来，
　　毫无香气也是个最大的缺点。
　　假使把我们嫁接在十姊妹上，
　　花型既大而又成团不是更强？
　　又假使把我们嫁接上十里香，
　　岂不会香闻十里而艳丽无双？
    44  玫瑰花
　　月季花的建议，我们是全表同情，
　　我们比月季花就只多一点香韵。
　　谁能够把我们嫁接上十姊妹呢？
　　我们不想保守，真愿意跃步前进。
　　我们的花如果能成为一架两架，
　　而不是像目前这样的一盆两盆；
　　开得多，开得快，开得好，又开得省，
　　那在我们也就是社会主义革命。
