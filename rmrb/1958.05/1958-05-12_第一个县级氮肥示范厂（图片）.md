Title: The First County-Level Nitrogen Fertilizer Demonstration Plant (Picture)
Author: Xia Daoling
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 1

The First County-Level Nitrogen Fertilizer Demonstration Plant

Our country's first county-level nitrogen fertilizer demonstration plant officially started production in Shanghai on May 10th. Right: Representatives from more than 200 units across the country visit the gas production section. Lower Right: Vice Minister of Chemical Industry Hou Debang (left), Shanghai Vice Mayor Xu Jianguo (center), and Soviet phosphate fertilizer expert Zhuchkov watch the first batch of nitrogen fertilizer produced by the plant together. Lower Left: The first batch of nitrogen fertilizer. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xia Daoling photographed (faxed photo)

### 第一个县级氮肥示范厂（图片）
夏道陵
1958-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　第一个县级氮肥示范厂
　　我国第一个县级氮肥示范工厂五月十日在上海正式开工生产。右：来自全国各地二百多个单位的代表参观造气工段。下右：化工部副部长侯德榜（左）、上海市副市长许建国（中）、苏联磷肥专家茹契柯夫一起观看这个厂生产的第一批氮肥。下左：第一批氮肥。新华社记者　夏道陵摄（传真照片）
