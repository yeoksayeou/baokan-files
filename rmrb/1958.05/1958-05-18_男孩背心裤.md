Boy's Vest and Pants

　　These two children's outfits are designed for boys aged three to five. The smaller one on the left (A) is made of light-colored drill or poplin. The vest has an opening in the back, trimmed with white lace, with two small white buttons on the waist, and some embroidery on the front chest.

　　The one on the right (B) has a vest made of white cloth or white poplin, and the trim and pants are made of small plaid cloth. The pants have an opening on the right side of the waist, with a total of six small buttons front and back, and a small embroidery on the lower right corner of the vest.

### 男孩背心裤

1958-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　男孩背心裤
    这两件童装，是为三岁到五岁的男孩子设计的。左边较小的一件（甲），是用浅色的士林布或府绸作的。背心后面有开口，周围镶白花边，腰上钉两粒小白扣，前胸再绣一点花。
    右边一件（乙）是用白布或白府绸作背心，用小格布作边和裤子。裤腰右边开口，前后共钉六粒小扣，背心的右下角绣一点花。
