Title: Korean People's Delegation to China in Shenyang: Awards Medals and Orders to Ninety-Nine People Including Li Qing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 11th. The Korean People's Delegation to China, led by Head of Delegation Xu Zhenshu and Deputy Head of Delegation Jin Tianhai, entrusted by the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, awarded the Second and Third Class National Flag Orders and Merit Medals to ninety-nine people including Li Qing, Deputy Director of the Department of Civil Affairs of Liaoning Province, at an award ceremony held in Shenyang City tonight.

    The award ceremony was held at the welcoming rally for the Korean People's Delegation to China by people from all walks of life in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City.

    Deputy Head of Delegation Jin Tianhai read out the decree of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea on awarding orders to citizens of the People's Republic of China who had made contributions to the protection and fostering of Korean war orphans and the children of patriotic martyrs. Then, Head of Delegation Xu Zhenshu and others pinned the Second Class National Flag Order on the chests of seven people including Li Qing, Deputy Director of the Liaoning Provincial Department of Civil Affairs; Li Huaqing, Director of the Health Department; Gong Xiaguang, Mayor of Yingkou City; Tian Shaobo, Mayor of Liaoyang City; Wu Xuewen, Mayor of Qinhuangdao City, Hebei Province; Duan Yongjie, Mayor of Andong City; and Chen Fan, a doctor at the Xiongyuecheng Korean Elementary School in Gaiping County.

    Thirty-three people, including Wang Daoju, Director of the Yingkou Textile Factory, were awarded the Third Class National Flag Order, and fifty-nine people, including Zhang Gexun, Deputy Section Chief of the Social Affairs Section of the Department of Civil Affairs, were awarded the Merit Medal.

    In his speech, Head of Delegation Xu Zhenshu congratulated the recipients of the medals and orders for their contributions to the cause of strengthening Korean-Chinese friendship. She said: These orphans you have fostered are the crystallization of your blood and sweat. They will live forever in the hearts of the Korean people as a bond and symbol of friendship between the people of Korea and China.

    Li Qing, Deputy Director of the Department of Civil Affairs of Liaoning Province, delivered a thank-you speech on behalf of the recipients of the medals and orders, thanking the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea for awarding them such a lofty honor.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团在沈阳  授予李青等九十九人勋章和奖章

1958-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团在沈阳
    授予李青等九十九人勋章和奖章
    新华社沈阳11日电　以许贞琡团长、金天海副团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团，受朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会的委托，今晚在沈阳市举行的授勋仪式上把二级、三级国旗勋章和功劳章授予辽宁省民政厅副厅长李青等九十九人。
    授勋仪式是在辽宁省及沈阳市各界人民欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团大会上举行的。
    金天海副团长在授勋仪式上宣读了朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会关于授予在保护和抚育朝鲜战灾孤儿和爱国烈士子女工作中有功的中华人民共和国公民勋章的政令。接着，许贞琡团长等把二级国旗勋章戴在李青、李华清（卫生厅厅长），宫霞光（营口市市长）、田少伯（辽阳市市长）、武学文（河北省秦皇岛市市长）、段永杰（安东市市长）和陈帆（盖平县熊岳城朝鲜初等学院医生）等七人的胸前。
    荣获三级国旗勋章的有营口纺织厂厂长王道举等三十三人，荣获功劳章的有民政厅社会科副科长张格勋等五十九人。
    许贞琡团长在致词中祝贺荣获勋章、奖章的人在加强朝中友谊的事业中建立的功勋。她说：你们抚育的这些孤儿就是你们的血汗结晶，他们将作为朝中两国人民友谊的纽带和标志，永远活在朝鲜人民的心里。
    辽宁省民政厅副厅长李青代表荣获勋章奖章的人员致谢词，他感谢朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会授予他们这样崇高的荣誉。
