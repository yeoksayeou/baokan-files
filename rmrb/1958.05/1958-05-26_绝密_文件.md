    "Top Secret" Document
    Mrs. F. L. Whipple, wife of the director of the Smithsonian Astrophysical Observatory (USA), said that her husband (the famous Harvard astronomer) had written a scientific paper in 1946 and sent it to a similar research institution in California. Later, he needed to use some materials from this paper, but the research institution replied: "This paper has been classified as top secret material because we do not consider you safe and reliable, so we cannot allow you to read this document."
      ——American "St. Louis Post-Dispatch" (with picture)

### “绝密”文件

1958-05-26
第6版()
专栏：外国报刊拾零

    “绝密”文件
    （美国）斯密逊天体物理学研究所所长的妻子弗·勒·威普尔夫人说，他的丈夫（哈佛大学著名的天文学家）在1946年，曾经写了一篇科学论文送给加利福尼亚州的一个类似性质的研究机构。以后，他需要利用这篇论文中的某些材料，但是这所研究机构却答复说：“这篇论文已被列为绝密材料，因为我们不认为你安全可靠，所以不能允许你阅读这份文件。”
      ——美国“圣路易邮报”（附图片）
