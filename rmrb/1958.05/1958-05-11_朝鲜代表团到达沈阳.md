Title: Korean Delegation Arrives in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1958-05-11
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 10th. The Korean People's Delegation to China, accompanied by Wang Yifu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, arrived in Shenyang by train at 11:35 this morning.
　　Zhang Xuexuan, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, Yu Ping, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Ning Wu, Chairman of the Liaoning Provincial Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, greeted them at the station.
　　Kim Yong-bin, Consul General of North Korea in Shenyang, Vice-Consuls An Gwi-mo and Cao Ying-hyun, Kim Yong-yan, Deputy Director of the Korean Children's Education Department, and representatives of Korean residents in Shenyang also welcomed them at the station.
　　At 3:30 p.m., Kim Cheon-hae, Deputy Head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, and all members of the delegation, accompanied by Zhang Panxin, Director of the Liaoning Provincial Foreign Affairs Office, paid a visit to Zhang Xuexuan, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province. During the visit, Kim Cheon-hae, on behalf of the Korean Workers' Party, the Korean government, and the Korean people, expressed his gratitude to the people of Liaoning Province for their affection in raising Korean war orphans. After the visit, Zhang Xuexuan, Liu Baotian, and others accompanied the delegation to the Beiling Martyrs Cemetery to lay wreaths at the tombs of the martyrs of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. After laying flowers, they visited the portraits and relics of martyrs Huang Jiguang, Qiu Shaoyun, and Sun Zhanyuan, which were displayed next to the martyrs' cemetery.
　　In the evening, Zhang Xuexuan, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, hosted a banquet to welcome the Korean People's Delegation to China.

### 朝鲜代表团到达沈阳

1958-05-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜代表团到达沈阳
    新华社沈阳10日电　朝鲜人民访华代表团在内务部副部长王一夫陪同下，今天上午十一时三十五分乘车到达沈阳。
    辽宁省副省长张雪轩、中共辽宁省委书记俞屏、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会辽宁省分会主席宁武和沈阳市市长刘宝田等到车站欢迎。
    朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬、副领事安龟模、曹英铉和朝鲜儿童教育处副处长金龙颜以及在沈阳的朝鲜侨民代表等也到车站欢迎。
    下午三时三十分，朝鲜人民访华代表团副团长金天海和全体团员由辽宁省外事处处长张盘新陪同，拜会了辽宁省副省长张雪轩。金天海在拜会时代表朝鲜劳动党、朝鲜政府和人民感谢辽宁省人民抚育朝鲜战灾孤儿的情谊。拜会以后，张雪轩、刘宝田等陪同代表团到北陵烈士陵园向中国人民志愿军烈士墓敬献花圈，献花以后又参谒了陈列在烈士陵旁的黄继光、丘少云、孙占元等烈士的遗像和遗物。
    晚间，辽宁省副省长张雪轩设宴欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团。
