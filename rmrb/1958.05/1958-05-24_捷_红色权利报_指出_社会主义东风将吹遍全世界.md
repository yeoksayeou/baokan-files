Title: Czech "Red Right Daily" Points Out: The Socialist East Wind Will Blow All Over the World
Author:
Date: 1958-05-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 23rd. The Czechoslovakian "Red Right Daily" published a commentary today entitled "The East Wind Prevails Over the West Wind," saying that the moment when the socialist east wind will blow all over the world is coming.

The commentary emphasized that nothing proves the correctness of this slogan more clearly than the political events of paramount importance at present.

The commentary pointed out the tremendous achievements of Soviet science and technology as demonstrated by the launch of the Soviet Union's third satellite. The commentary also pointed out that the flames of anger against American domination, interference, and subversive activities have recently suddenly ignited in several key areas of world politics.

The commentary believes that Nixon's trip to Latin America demonstrated the crushing bankruptcy of American policy. The commentary also mentioned the American intervention in Lebanon and the victory of the Lao Patriotic Party in the by-elections despite American intimidation and massive propaganda.

### 捷“红色权利报”指出  社会主义东风将吹遍全世界

1958-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    捷“红色权利报”指出
    社会主义东风将吹遍全世界
    新华社布拉格23日电　捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”今天以“东风压倒西风”为题发表评论说，社会主义的东风吹遍全世界的时刻就要到来了。
    评论强调指出，没有任何东西能比目前具有头等重要意义的政治事件更加清楚地证明这个口号的正确性。
    这篇评论指出了苏联发射第三个卫星一事所表明的苏联科学和技术的巨大成就。评论同时还指出，反对美国的统治、干涉和颠覆活动的怒火最近已经在世界政治的几个关键性地方突然燃烧起来。
    评论认为，尼克松的拉丁美洲之行表明了美国政策的惨重的破产。评论还谈到美国干涉黎巴嫩以及尽管美国进行恐吓和大规模宣传而老挝的爱国党仍在补缺选举中得胜的事情。
