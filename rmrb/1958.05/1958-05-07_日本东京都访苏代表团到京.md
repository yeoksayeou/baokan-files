Title: Japanese Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Japanese Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly delegation, headed by Kentaro Nagase, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, after a tour of the Soviet Union.

### 日本东京都访苏代表团到京

1958-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    日本东京都访苏代表团到京
    新华社5日讯　以长濑健太郎为首的日本东京都议会访苏代表团一行八人，在苏联参观访问后，应中国人民外交学会的邀请来我国访问，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。
