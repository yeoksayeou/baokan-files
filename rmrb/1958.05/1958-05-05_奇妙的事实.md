    Strange Facts
    Pinks are afraid of "hearing" noisy sounds. If you put it near a radio, it will wither soon.
    Lily of the valley should not be mixed in a vase with other flowers. Because it can secrete toxins in the water, causing other flowers to be poisoned and die.

### 奇妙的事实

1958-05-05

专栏：

    奇妙的事实
    石竹怕“听”吵音。如果将它放在收音机附近，不久就会枯萎。
    铃兰不可以和他花混放一瓶。因为它能把毒质分泌在水中，使其他花卉中毒死亡。
