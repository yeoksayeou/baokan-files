Title: Member States of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance Communist and Workers' Parties Representatives Meeting Opens; Warsaw Pact Member States' Summit-Level Political Consultative Committee Meeting to be Held in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th May: The meeting of representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of the member states of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance opened in Moscow on May 20th.
    Participating in the representatives meeting were: Hoxha, Shehu, Koleka, Njele, Shtulla, and Hasco, representatives of the Albanian Labour Party; Zhivkov, Yugov, R. Damyanov, Panchevski, Hristozov, and Lukanov, representatives of the Bulgarian Communist Party; Kádár, Apro, Revesz, and Kish, representatives of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party; Ulbricht, Grotewohl, H. Rau, Stoph, Leuschner, and Wenzer, representatives of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany; Gomułka, Cyrankiewicz, Jędrychowski, and Jaroszewicz, representatives of the Polish United Workers' Party; Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chivu Stoica, Bodnăraş, Salajan, Borilă, Avram Bunaciu, and G. Marin, representatives of the Romanian Workers' Party; Khrushchev, Mikoyan, Kirichenko, Kozlov, Kuzmin, Kabanov, and Khrunichev, representatives of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union; Novotný, Široký, Dzuranský, Šimůnek, and Lomský, representatives of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia.
    Invited to participate in the representatives meeting were: Nguyen Duy Trinh, Nguyen Van Tran, and Dang Viet Chau, representatives of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Chen Yun, Li Fuchun, and Ye Jizhuang, representatives of the Chinese Communist Party; Kim Il, Jung Il-ryong, and Li Li-ok, representatives of the Korean Workers' Party; Damba and Tsende, representatives of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th May: Announcement Concerning the Convening of a Meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty Member States.
    The governments of the Warsaw Treaty member states have decided to convene a meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty member states at the level of heads of government in Moscow on May 24th, 1958. The ministers of foreign affairs and ministers of defense of the contracting states will also participate in the meeting.

### 八国经济互助委员会成员国  共产党工人党代表会议开幕  华沙条约缔约国首脑级政协会议将在莫斯科召开

1958-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    八国经济互助委员会成员国  共产党工人党代表会议开幕
    华沙条约缔约国首脑级政协会议将在莫斯科召开
    新华社莫斯科20日电  5月20日八国经济互助委员会成员国共产党、工人党代表会议在莫斯科开幕。
    参加代表会议的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表霍查、谢胡、科列加、恩杰拉、什图拉、哈斯科同志，保加利亚共产党代表日夫科夫、于哥夫、拉·达米扬诺夫、潘切夫斯基、赫里斯托佐夫、卢卡诺夫同志，匈牙利社会主义工人党代表卡达尔、奥普罗、雷维斯、基什同志，德国统一社会党代表乌布利希、格罗提渥、海·劳、斯多夫、洛伊施纳、文策尔同志，波兰统一工人党代表哥穆尔卡、西伦凯维兹、英德里霍夫斯基、雅罗谢维奇同志，罗马尼亚工人党代表乔治乌—德治、基伏·斯托伊卡、波德纳拉希、萨拉扬、伯尔德拉德亚努、阿夫拉姆·布纳丘、格·马林同志，苏联共产党代表赫鲁晓夫、米高扬、基里钦科、科兹洛夫、库兹明、卡巴诺夫、赫鲁尼切夫同志，捷克斯洛伐克共产党代表诺沃提尼、西罗基、杜兰斯基、西穆?克、洛姆斯基同志。
    应邀参加代表会议的还有越南劳动党代表阮维祯、阮文珍、邓曰州同志，中国共产党代表陈云、李富春、叶季壮同志，朝鲜劳动党代表金一、郑一龙、李理玉同志，蒙古人民革命党代表达姆巴、曾德同志。
    新华社莫斯科20日电  关于召开华沙条约缔约国政治协商委员会会议的公报。
    华沙条约缔约国政府决定于1958年5月24日在莫斯科召开华沙条约缔约国政府首脑级的政治协商委员会会议，缔约各国的外交部长和国防部长也参加会议。
