Title: Picture: Mutual Understanding
Author: Xiang Zhi
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 7

    The junior high school run by the Daili Agricultural Cooperative in Putian County, Fujian Province, promptly offered courses on small-seedling close planting for high yield and small-seedling close planting techniques during the busy rice transplanting season. Teachers also led students to participate in the cooperative's rice transplanting. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xiang Zhi took this picture.
