Title: Sixty Years of Peking University (Photos)
Author: Fu Jun
Date: 1958-05-04
Page: 2

    Sixty Years of Peking University
    Since liberation, Peking University has developed rapidly, with newly built school buildings three times larger than the old Yenching University. It now has more than 8,000 students. The lower left picture shows the newly built teaching building.
    The lower right picture shows Chairman Mao's studio when he worked at Peking University.
    Upper left picture: After liberation, Peking University moved to the old site of Yenching University. The picture shows the beautiful Weiming Lake on the current Peking University campus.
    Upper right picture: Peking University is also a big family of international friendship. Currently, there are more than 250 international students from 22 countries studying at Peking University.
                                Xinhua News Agency reporter Fu Jun photographed

### 北京大学六十年（图片）
傅军
1958-05-04
第2版()
专栏：

    北京大学六十年
    解放后，北京大学发展很快，新建的校舍比旧燕京大学大三倍。现有学生八千多人。下左图是新建的教学大楼。
    下右图是毛主席在北京大学工作时的工作室。
    右左图　解放后，北京大学迁至燕京大学旧址。图为现在北京大学校园内的美丽的未名湖。
    右右图　北京大学也是国际友爱的大家庭，现在北京大学学习的有来自二十二个国家的留学生共二百五十多人。
                                新华社记者　傅军摄
