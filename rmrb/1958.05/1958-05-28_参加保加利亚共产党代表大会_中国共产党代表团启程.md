Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Departs to Attend Bulgarian Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-05-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. At the invitation of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has decided to send a delegation headed by Comrade Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, to attend the 7th Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party to be held on June 2nd. The delegation also includes Comrade An Ziwen, member of the CPC Central Committee, and Comrade Zhu Qiwu, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria.

Comrade Dong Biwu and others left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 27th, and will transfer in Moscow to Sofia.

### 参加保加利亚共产党代表大会  中国共产党代表团启程

1958-05-28
第3版()
专栏：

    参加保加利亚共产党代表大会
    中国共产党代表团启程
    新华社27日讯　中国共产党中央委员会应保加利亚共产党中央委员会的邀请，决定派遣以中共中央政治局委员董必武同志为首的代表团，出席6月2日举行的保加利亚共产党第七次代表大会。这个代表团的成员还有中共中央委员安子文同志和中国驻保加利亚大使朱其文同志。
    董必武等同志已经在27日上午乘飞机离开北京，经莫斯科转赴索非亚。
