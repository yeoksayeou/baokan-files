Title: Korean Youth Delegation Visits Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers; Seven Art Troupes Begin Farewell and慰问 Performances

Author:
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 5th: The Korean youth delegation that came to visit the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) set off for Pyongyang on the afternoon of the 4th. Before leaving, the delegation, accompanied by Major General Ding Laifu, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the CPV, laid a wreath at the Cemetery of CPV Martyrs.

During their visit, the Korean youth delegation attended the CPV leadership organs' commemoration of the "May Fourth" Youth Day and held discussions with young officers and soldiers of the CPV leadership organs.

Heroic models from the delegation were also invited to give reports to young officers and soldiers of the CPV artillery command organs. Reports by combat hero Lieutenant Li Juhe and female labor hero Yan Zhengzi were warmly welcomed.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 5th: To bid farewell to the Chinese People's Volunteers, the Central Committee of the Korean People has decided to dispatch seven large art troupes in May to perform farewell and慰问 performances for various units of the CPV. Among them, the 152-member Korean National Choe Seung-hee Dance Theater has already given its first performance of the large-scale dance drama "The Legend of Okran Pool" at the CPV leadership organs. This play is a national dance drama created by Choe Seung-hee based on Korean folklore.

### 朝鲜青年代表团访问志愿军总部  七艺术团体开始送别慰问演出

1958-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜青年代表团访问志愿军总部
    七艺术团体开始送别慰问演出
    据新华社平壤5日电　来中国人民志愿军总部访问的朝鲜青年代表团，4日下午启程回平壤。临行前，代表团在志愿军政治部副主任丁莱夫少将陪同下，到志愿军烈士陵园献了花圈。
    朝鲜青年代表团在访问期间，出席了志愿军领导机关的纪念“五四”青年节大会，同志愿军领导机关的青年官兵举行了座谈。
    代表团中的英雄模范人物还应邀向志愿军炮兵指挥机关的青年官兵作了报告。战斗英雄李聚和少尉和女劳动英雄严正子的报告，受到热烈欢迎。
    据新华社平壤5日电　朝鲜人民欢送中国人民志愿军中央委员会决定在5月份派出七个大型艺术团，分别到志愿军各部队作送别慰问演出。其中朝鲜国立崔承喜舞蹈剧场一百五十二人，已在志愿军领导机关首次演出了大型舞剧“玉兰池的传说”。这个剧是崔承喜根据朝鲜民间传说创作的民族舞剧。
