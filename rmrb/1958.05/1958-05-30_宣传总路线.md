Title: Propaganda General Line
Author:
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: "Resolution of the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China on the Work Report of the Central Committee." Documents such as Comrade Liu Shaoqi's "Work Report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress" and "Resolution of the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China on the Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties in Moscow" have been published in Chinese, Mongolian, and Korean by the People's Publishing House and the Ethnic Publishing House, respectively.

The Ethnic Publishing House also published the Tibetan, Uyghur, and Kazakh versions of the "Eighth National Congress" Second Session documents on the 29th.

The People's Daily Publishing House has also published for readers in various regions the loose-leaf selections of "Resolution of the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China on the Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties in Moscow," "Resolution of the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China on the National Agricultural Development Program from 1956 to 1967," and Tan Zhenlin's "Explanation on the National Agricultural Development Program (Second Revised Draft) from 1956 to 1967."

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 29th: The publishing departments of Shaanxi Province are publishing various books related to the propaganda of the general line of socialist construction.

The Provincial People's Publishing House is about to publish the Work Report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Selections for Studying the Party's General Line for Socialist Construction, and Selections for Studying the Great Leap Forward in Industrial and Agricultural Production, totaling more than 600,000 copies. The series of advanced experiences of Shaanxi Province farmers in building a new socialist countryside in a faster, better, and more economical way, edited by this publishing house, is also being printed. Theoretical books focusing on elaborating the general line of socialist construction and the two types of contradictions will also be published successively this year.

### 宣传总路线

1958-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

    宣传总路线
    首都大量出版发行  八大二次会议文件
    新华社28日讯　“中国共产党第八届全国代表大会第二次会议关于中央委员会的工作报告的决议”。刘少奇同志关于“中国共产党中央委员会向第八届全国代表大会第二次会议的工作报告”和“中国共产党第八届全国代表大会第二次会议关于在莫斯科举行的各国共产党和工人党代表会议的决议”等文件，已由人民出版社、民族出版社分别用汉文、蒙文、朝鲜文出版。
    民族出版社29日还出版藏文版、维吾尔文版和哈萨克文版的“八大”二次会议文件。
    人民日报出版社也为各地读者出版了“中国共产党第八届全国代表大会第二次会议关于在莫斯科举行的各国共产党和工人党代表会议的决议”、“中国共产党第八届全国代表大会第二次会议关于1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要的决议”和谭震林所作“关于1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要（第二次修正草案）的说明”的活叶文选。
    陕西出版各种图书
    新华社西安29日电　陕西省出版部门正在出版有关宣传社会主义建设总路线的各种书籍。
    省人民出版社即将出版中共中央委员会的工作报告、党的社会主义建设总路线学习文选和工农业生产大跃进学习文选等书，共六十多万册。这个出版社编辑的陕西省农民多快好省地建设社会主义新农村的先进经验丛书，也正在付印。有关阐述社会主义建设总路线和两类矛盾为中心的理论书籍，今年也将陆续出版。
