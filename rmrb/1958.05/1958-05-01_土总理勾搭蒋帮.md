Title: Turkish Prime Minister Colludes with Chiang Gang
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Turkish Prime Minister Menderes has been active in Taiwan these past few days. Before going to Taiwan, Menderes visited Japan and South Korea.

Menderes held secret talks with Chiang Kai-shek in Taiwan. According to United Press International citing well-informed Turkish sources, Menderes told Chiang Kai-shek that a "Northeast Asia Treaty Organization" should be established as the "northern link in the anti-communist chain" to encircle the Soviet Union and China.

### 土总理勾搭蒋帮

1958-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    土总理勾搭蒋帮
    据新华社30日讯　土耳其总理曼德列斯这几天正在台湾活动。在到台湾以前，曼德列斯曾经访问了日本和南朝鲜。
    曼德列斯在台湾和蒋介石举行了秘密会谈。据合众社引消息灵通的土耳其人士的谈话说，曼德列斯向蒋介石表示，应该成立“东北亚条约组织”作为包围苏联和中国的“反共锁链中的北部环节”。
