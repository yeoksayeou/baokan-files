Agricultural Proverbs on Pest Control

　　Catching one moth increases the yield by one basket.

　　Eliminate rice borers, and the granaries will be full of rice.

　　People fear tuberculosis, rice fears stem borers.
　　(Yibin, Sichuan)

### 治虫农谚

1958-05-09
第2版()
专栏：

    治虫农谚
    捕捉一个蛾，产量加一箩。
    螟虫除光，谷米满仓。
    人怕肺痨病，稻怕钻心虫。
    （四川宜宾）
