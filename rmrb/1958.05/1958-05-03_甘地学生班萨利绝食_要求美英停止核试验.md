Title: Gandhi's Student Bansali on Hunger Strike Demanding US and UK Stop Nuclear Tests
Author:
Date: 1958-05-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 1st: Acharya Bansali, a close student and assistant of India's Mahatma Gandhi, has been on a hunger strike since April 1st, demanding that major powers abandon nuclear weapons. The All-India Peace Council sent a delegation to visit Bansali in Takli Village, Mumbai Province, on April 28th. He told the delegation that he "greatly admired the Soviet government's decision to stop nuclear weapons testing and hoped that Britain and the United States would follow suit."

Bansali also expressed "great indignation at the continued conduct of nuclear tests." He also expressed his determination to continue intermittent hunger strikes until such tests cease and the major powers no longer use nuclear weapons as instruments of war.

### 甘地学生班萨利绝食  要求美英停止核试验

1958-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    甘地学生班萨利绝食
    要求美英停止核试验
    据新华社新德里1日电　印度圣雄甘地的亲密的学生和助手阿查雅·班萨利从4月1日以来一直在绝食，要求各大国放弃核武器。全印和平理事会曾经在4月28日派代表团到孟买邦塔克利村访问班萨利。他向代表团表示，他“很钦佩苏联政府关于停止核武器试验的决定，并且希望英国和美国也跟着采取这个步骤”。
    班萨利还对目前还有“继续进行核试验一事表示非常愤慨”。他并且表示决心继续间歇地举行绝食，一直到这种试验停止进行和大国不再以核武器作为战争工具时为止。
