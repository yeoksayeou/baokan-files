Title: Omani Nationalists Annihilate Ninety British Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1958-05-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 25th: The Omani Imam's office in Damascus announced today that Omani nationalists recently engaged in fierce fighting with British colonial troops in the Wadi Tai region east of Nizwa. In this battle, Omani nationalists killed ninety British soldiers, while the Omani side suffered fifty losses.

### 阿曼民族主义者  歼灭英军九十人

1958-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    阿曼民族主义者
    歼灭英军九十人
    据新华社大马士革25日电　阿曼教长驻大马士革办事处今天宣布，阿曼民族主义者最近在尼兹瓦以东的塔伊山谷同英国殖民军队发生激战。在这场战斗中，阿曼民族主义者打死英军九十人，阿曼方面损失五十人。
