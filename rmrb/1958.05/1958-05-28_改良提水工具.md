"Improved Water Lifting Tools"

The book "Improved Water Lifting Tools," edited by the Bureau of Farmland Water Conservancy of the Ministry of Agriculture, introduces the advanced water lifting tools created and improved by the masses in Hebei, Henan, Shandong, Jiangsu, Hunan, Hubei, Jiangxi, Sichuan and other provinces. Their common characteristics are: simple to manufacture, using local materials, convenient to use, low cost, and loved by the masses. In addition to simple text descriptions for each tool, this book will be of great help in solving the difficulties of insufficient water lifting tools. This book has recently been published by the Water Conservancy and Electric Power Publishing House. (Li)

### “改良提水工具”

1958-05-28
第7版()
专栏：出版消息

    “改良提水工具”
    农业部农田水利局编辑的“改良提水工具”一书，介绍了河北、河南、山东、江苏、湖南、湖北、江西、四川等省群众创造和改良的先进提水工具。它们共同的特点是：制造简单，就地取材，使用方便，造价低廉，群众爱用。本书对每一种工具除了用浅显文字说明以外，对解决提水工具不足的困难，会有很大帮助。这本书最近已由水利电力出版社出版。　（力）
