Title: Seven Minority Nationalities Visiting Delegations Leave Beijing to Visit Various Places
Author:
Date: 1958-05-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Seven minority nationalities visiting delegations from the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and Guangdong, Yunnan, Guizhou, Gansu, Sichuan, and Hunan provinces left Beijing on the 15th for Tianjin, Changchun, and other places to visit after attending the May Day celebrations.
    During their stay in Beijing, the visiting delegations were received by Chairman Mao and other central leaders and participated in symposiums hosted by the Nationalities Affairs Commission. During the symposiums, the members of various ethnic groups spoke enthusiastically about the moving scenes of the great leap forward in industry and agriculture in the ethnic regions, exchanged advanced experiences, and listened to a report by Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, on the great leap forward in industry, agriculture, commerce, and the elimination of the four pests and hygiene in the capital. In addition, they also visited the Ming Tombs Reservoir project, the National Agricultural Implements Exhibition, the Central Institute for Nationalities, and factories in the capital, and toured scenic spots and historical sites.

### 七个少数民族参观团离京赴各地参观

1958-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　七个少数民族参观团离京赴各地参观
    新华社15日讯　来自广西僮族自治区和广东、云南、贵州、甘肃、四川、湖南省的七个少数民族参观团，在北京参加五一节观礼后，15日分赴天津、长春等地参观。
    参观团在京期间，受到了毛主席和其他中央负责同志的接见，并分别参加了民族事务委员会所主持的座谈会。各族团员在座谈中畅述了民族地区工农业大跃进的动人情景，交流了先进经验，听取了北京市副市长张友渔介绍首都工、农、商业及除四害讲卫生等大跃进情况的报告。此外，他们还参观了十三陵水库工程、全国农具展览会、中央民族学院和首都的工厂，游览了名胜古迹。
