Title: Kuznetsov Visits Dulles
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 20th: According to TASS, Washington, May 20th: Kuznetsov, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, who arrived in Washington from Latin America, paid a courtesy visit to U.S. Secretary of State Dulles on the 19th and held a half-hour talk with him. Kuznetsov held a banquet, which was attended by Murphy, Deputy Assistant Secretary of State.

    库兹?佐夫访杜勒斯
