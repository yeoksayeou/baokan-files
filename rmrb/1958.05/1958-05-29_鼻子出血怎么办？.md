Title: What to do about a nosebleed?
Author: Jun Yan
Date: 1958-05-29
Page: 8

What to do about a nosebleed?

    The most common cause of nosebleeds is dryness of the nasal mucosa, which causes the capillaries to rupture. Therefore, attention should be paid to nasal hygiene. A bump on the nose, hitting something, or a tumor in the nasal cavity can all cause nosebleeds. In addition, nosebleeds can also be caused by diseases in other parts of the body, such as typhoid fever, kala-azar, leukemia, blood diseases, high blood pressure, and high fever.

    When you have a nosebleed, you should first calm down and not panic, because being too nervous often makes it difficult to stop the bleeding. If only one nostril is bleeding, you can press that nostril shut with your finger. If both nostrils are bleeding, pinch both nostrils shut and breathe through your mouth temporarily. This will often stop the bleeding after ten minutes or so. In addition, you can also pack the nasal cavity with sterile cotton and apply a cold compress to the nose with a cold towel. Generally speaking, if the bleeding is not too severe, these methods can achieve the purpose of stopping the bleeding.

    If these methods still do not stop the bleeding, you will have to go to the hospital. At the hospital, some drugs that constrict the blood vessels in the nasal cavity can be used to stop the bleeding. If the bleeding area is small, cauterization can be used to stop the bleeding. If the bleeding is severe and the bleeding site cannot be found for a while, then sterile petroleum jelly gauze packing can be used to stop the bleeding.
                                            (Jun Yan)

### 鼻子出血怎么办？

1958-05-29
第8版()
专栏：小常识

    鼻子出血怎么办？
    鼻子流血的最常见原因，是由于鼻腔里的粘膜干燥，毛细血管破裂而引起的。因此应该注意鼻腔的卫生。鼻子碰了，或者撞在什么东西上，以及鼻腔长瘤子，都会流鼻血。此外，鼻出血也可能是身体其他部分的疾病引起的，像伤寒、黑热病、白血病、血液病、高血压、发高烧等等。
    当鼻子流血的时候，首先应该安静，不要惊慌，因为精神太紧张，往往不容易停止出血。如果是一个鼻孔流血，可以用手指压紧这个鼻孔。如果两个鼻孔都流血，就把两个鼻孔统统捏紧，暂用嘴来呼吸。这样过个十多分钟，往往能止住流血。此外，也可以用消毒棉花填塞在鼻腔里，再用冷毛巾放在鼻子上做冷敷。一般说，如果流血不太冲，用这些办法，都可以达到止血的目的。
    如果这些办法还不能止住流血，就只好到医院去了。在医院里，可以用一些使鼻腔血管收缩的药来止血。出血的地方小，就用药物烧灼的办法来止住。出血冲，而又一时找不到出血的部位，那就用消毒的油纱条填塞鼻腔法来止住。
                        （峻巗）
