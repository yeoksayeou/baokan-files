Title: New Film Genre "Around the World"
Author: Du Yiping
Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 8

Column: Stage and Screen

    New Film Genre "Around the World"
    "Around the World" is a new type of film magazine added by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio in 1958. The subject matter is wide-ranging, including major political events in various countries, advanced science and technology, as well as the cultural life and customs of people from different countries.
    One issue of this film genre will be released every month. The first and second issues have now been completed and will soon meet the audience. (Du Yiping)

### 新片种“在世界各地”
杜一平
1958-05-10
第8版()
专栏：舞台与银幕

    新片种“在世界各地”
    “在世界各地”是中央新闻纪录电影制片厂在1958年新增添的一种电影杂志片。取材范围广泛，包括世界各国的重大政治事件，先进的科学技术，以及各国人民的文化生活和风俗习惯等。
    这个片种每月出一号。第一、二号现在已制作完成，即将与观众见面。　（杜一平）
