Title: International Broadcasting Organization Asian Member Nations' Folk Music Broadcasting Conference Concludes
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The International Broadcasting Organization Asian Member Nations' Folk Music Broadcasting Conference, which opened in Beijing on April 24, closed today. During the conference, representatives and observers from participating countries gave reports on folk music broadcasting in their respective countries, appreciated 172 representative and excellent folk songs and musical pieces from the twelve participating countries, and discussed and exchanged experiences of various countries in disseminating folk music through broadcasting.

The conference held a closing ceremony today, presided over by Wen Jize, Vice Chairman of the Council of the International Broadcasting Organization. Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the People's Republic of China, attended and delivered a speech. Choi Heon, representative of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Subali, observer of Indonesia, Hayashida Nobuo, observer of Japan, and Badran, observer of the United Arab Republic, spoke at the meeting.

The conference communiqué was adopted at today's meeting.

### 国际广播组织亚洲会员国民间音乐广播会议闭幕

1958-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    国际广播组织亚洲会员国民间音乐广播会议闭幕
    据新华社30日讯　4月24日在北京召开的国际广播组织亚洲会员国民间音乐广播会议，今天闭幕。会议过程中，与会各国代表和观察员分别作了有关各国民间音乐广播的报告，欣赏了与会的十二个国家有代表性的优秀的民间歌曲和乐曲一百七十二首，并讨论和交流了各国通过广播传播民间音乐的经验。
    会议在今天举行了闭幕式。闭幕式由国际广播组织理事会副主席温济泽主持。中华人民共和国对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林出席致词，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表崔宪、印度尼西亚观察员苏巴礼、日本观察员林信夫、阿拉伯联合共和国观察员巴德朗在会上讲了话。
    在今天的会议上通过了会议公报。
