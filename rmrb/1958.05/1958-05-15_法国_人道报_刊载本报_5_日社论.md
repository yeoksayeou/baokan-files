Title: French "L'Humanité" Publishes Our Newspaper's Editorial of the 5th

Author:
Date: 1958-05-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The French Communist Party's newspaper "L'Humanité" prominently published a summary of the "People's Daily" editorial of May 5th, "Modern Revisionism Must Be Criticized," on page seven on May 6th.

### 法国“人道报”刊载本报5日社论

1958-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

    法国“人道报”刊载本报5日社论
    新华社14日讯　法国共产党机关报“人道报”5月6日在第七版上以显著地位摘要刊载了“人民日报”5月5日的社论：“现代修正主义必须批判”。
