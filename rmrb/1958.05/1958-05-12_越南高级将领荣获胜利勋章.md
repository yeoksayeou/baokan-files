Title: Vietnamese Senior Generals Awarded Victory Medals
Author:
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi – On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the Dien Bien Phu Victory (May 7), Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Council of Ministers decided to award the Victory Medal, First Class, to senior and mid-level generals and commanders of the Vietnamese People's Army.

　　Senior generals awarded the medal include Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, Van Tien Dung, Chief of General Staff, Nguyen Chi Thanh, Director of the General Political Department, and many others.

### 越南高级将领荣获胜利勋章

1958-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南高级将领荣获胜利勋章
    据新华社河内电　越南民主共和国胡志明主席和部长会议在奠边府大捷四周年（5月7日）的时候，决定以一级胜利勋章授给越南人民军的高、中级将领和指挥员。
    荣获勋章的高级将领中有越南人民军总司令武元甲、总参谋长文进勇、政治总局主任阮志清等多人。
