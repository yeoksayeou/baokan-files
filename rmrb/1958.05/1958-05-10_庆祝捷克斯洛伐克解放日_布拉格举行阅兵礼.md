Title: Prague Holds Military Parade to Celebrate Czechoslovak Liberation Day
Author:
Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 9th: A grand military parade celebrating the 13th anniversary of Czechoslovakia's liberation was held this morning in Letná Park, the capital. The entire city was freshly decorated, with red flags fluttering, adding even more splendor to the spring-dressed Prague. At 10:00 AM, Minister of National Defense, General Lomský, inspected the army, border guards, public security forces, and people's militia gathered in the square and delivered a speech to the inspected troops and residents of the capital. In his speech, Lomský reviewed the tremendous achievements Czechoslovakia has made since its liberation and its efforts to safeguard peace. He called for further strengthening of national defense to crush the militarists' and revanchists' conspiracy to prepare for an atomic war, and emphasized that the unity of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union is the most reliable guarantee for Czechoslovakia's peaceful construction and national security.

Afterward, troops from various branches marched past the podium in a review formation, accepting the inspection of Czechoslovak party and government leaders Novotný, Široký, and others. In the evening, salutes were fired, fireworks soared, and millions of people sang and danced to celebrate the holiday. President Novotný also held a grand National Day reception.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放日  布拉格举行阅兵礼

1958-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放日
    布拉格举行阅兵礼
    新华社布拉格9日电　捷克斯洛伐克庆祝解放十三周年的盛大阅兵典礼今天上午在首都勒提尼广场举行。这一天全城装饰一新，红旗飘飘，使春装俏丽的布拉格益增风采。上午十时国防部长洛姆斯基上将检阅了聚集在广场的陆军、边防军、公安部队和人民纠察队，并且向受检阅的部队和首都居民发表了演说。洛姆斯基在讲话中回顾了捷克斯洛伐克解放以来获得的巨大成就和它为维护和平所作的努力。他号召进一步加强国防力量以粉碎军国主义者和复仇主义者准备原子战争的阴谋，并且强调指出，以苏联为首的社会主义国家的团结是捷克斯洛伐克和平建设和国家安全的最可靠的保证。
    各兵种部队随后以分列式走过主席台，接受了捷克斯洛伐克党政府领导人诺沃提尼、西罗基等的检阅。晚间礼炮齐鸣，熖火飞翔，千万群众载歌载舞欢庆了节日。诺沃提尼总统并且举行了盛大的国庆招待会。
