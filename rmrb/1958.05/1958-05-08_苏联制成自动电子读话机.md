Title: Soviet Union Manufactures Automatic Electronic Reading Machine
Author:
Date: 1958-05-08
Page: 7

Science News

    Soviet Union Manufactures Automatic Electronic Reading Machine

    In the Soviet Union, an average telegraph operator has to send 70,000 symbols and press the telegraph key 70,000 times per shift, and also check whether each letter is sent correctly. At such a high speed of work, the reaction of the human visual organs and other parts gradually becomes dull, and mistakes will naturally occur.

    In order to solve this problem, a group of young scientists from the Television Teaching and Research Office of the Odessa Telecommunications Institute in the Soviet Union has created an automatic electronic reading machine for typing and printing documents after two years of creative exploration.

    The main task of this machine is to identify letters. That is, let the beam emitted by the television-type cathode ray tube scan each letter in sequence. When the light beam falls on a black line or white paper, the intensity of the reflected light changes, and the current in the photoelectric tube circuit also changes accordingly. According to the number and time of electrical pulses, the analysis part of the reading machine can distinguish this or that letter, and turn on the relay carrying the signal of the letter at the output end. The number of these relays is equal to the letters and numbers on the alphabet, a total of forty. Another special device converts the signal into an electrical code, and the telegraph machine connected to the automatic electronic reading device automatically transmits the translation to the designated place. This machine will be used in printing plants to automate typesetting.
                            (Science)

### 苏联制成自动电子读话机

1958-05-08
第7版()
专栏：科学新闻

    苏联制成自动电子读话机
    在苏联一个电报员平均每班要发送七万个符号、七万次地按动发报机的键，并且还要检验每一个字母拍发得正确与否。在这样高速度的工作中，人的视官和其它部分的反应渐渐变钝，自然就会出错。
    为了解决这个问题，苏联敖德萨电讯学院电视教研室的一组年青的科学工作者两年来经过了创造性的探究，制成了一种打字和印刷文件用的自动电子读话机。
    这种机器的主要任务是辨别字母。即让电视型阴极射线管发出光束，光束按次扫过每一字母。当光束落在黑线或白纸上时，反射光的强度改变，光电管电路中的电流也相应起变化。根据电脉冲数量和时间的不同，读话机的分析部分就可以区别出这个或那个字母，并在输出端上开启载有该字母信号的继电器。这种继电器的数量同字母表上的字母和数字相等，共有四十个。另有特制的装置变信号为电码，接在自动电子读话装置上的电报机把译文自动传到指定的地方。这种机器将用在印刷厂，使排字自动化。
                            （科）
