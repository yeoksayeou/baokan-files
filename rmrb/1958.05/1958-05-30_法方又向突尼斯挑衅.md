Title: French Side Provokes Tunisia Again
Author:
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 29th—The Tunisian government accused French forces on the 28th of having violated Tunisian territory three times in the past twenty-four hours.

The Tunisian Government Information Department pointed out that on the morning of the 27th, a French patrol had intruded into Tunisian territory from Algeria near Ain Draham. French helicopters also twice intruded into Tunisian airspace to transport munitions to French military strongholds in southern Tunisia.

### 法方又向突尼斯挑衅

1958-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    法方又向突尼斯挑衅
    新华社29日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯政府28日指责法军在过去二十四小时内曾经三次侵犯突尼斯的领土。
    突尼斯政府情报部指出，在27日上午法国一支巡逻队曾经在艾恩德拉姆附近从阿尔及利亚侵入突尼斯境内。法国的直升飞机也曾两次侵入突尼斯领空向突尼斯南部法军据点运送军火。
