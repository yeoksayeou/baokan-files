Title: Anhui Nitrogen Fertilizer Plant Starts Construction
Author:
Date: 1958-05-03
Page: 3

Column: Blossoms of Construction

    The first nitrogen fertilizer factory in Anhui Province, the Anhui Nitrogen Fertilizer Plant, officially started construction in Hefei on April 28th. This nitrogen fertilizer plant will produce 44,000 tons of calcium cyanamide annually. It will be built in two phases. The first phase, with a production capacity of 22,000 tons of calcium cyanamide per year, will be completed and put into production in early November of this year. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 安徽氮肥厂动工

1958-05-03
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    安徽氮肥厂动工
    安徽省第一座生产氮素化学肥料的工厂——安徽氮肥厂于4月28日在合肥正式开工兴建。这座氮肥厂年产四万四千吨石灰氮。它将分两期建成，第一期年产二万二千吨石灰氮的厂房，将在今年11月初建成投入生产。　　（据新华社讯）
