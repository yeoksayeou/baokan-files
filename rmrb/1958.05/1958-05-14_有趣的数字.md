    Interesting Numbers
    The human body contains approximately fifty different types of cells, totaling about one hundred quadrillion. Among them, the most numerous are the brain's nervous system cells, numbering around two hundred quadrillion, followed by blood cells, with fifteen quadrillion.

### 有趣的数字

1958-05-14

专栏：

    有趣的数字
    人体内的全部细胞，约有五十种之多，总数则约有一千万万万个，其中最多的是脑神经系的，约有二十万万万个，血液有十五万万万个。
