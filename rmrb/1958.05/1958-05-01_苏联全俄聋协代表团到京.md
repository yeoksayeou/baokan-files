Title: Soviet All-Russia Society of the Deaf Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. A delegation from the Soviet All-Russia Society of the Deaf arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese Association for the Welfare of the Deaf and Mute. The delegation is led by P.V. Subenkin, Chairman of the Central Council of the All-Russia Society of the Deaf, and will be visiting China for approximately half a month.

### 苏联全俄聋协代表团到京

1958-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联全俄聋协代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　苏联全俄罗斯聋人协会代表团，应中国聋哑人福利会邀请，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团由全俄聋协中央理事会主席保·苏卞金率领，将在中国进行为时大约半个月的访问。
