Title: French University Professors Gather to Condemn Continued Western Nuclear Testing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th, TASS, Paris, 5th: French university professors and teachers held a meeting in Paris to discuss the dangers of nuclear weapons testing.
    The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution condemning the continued testing of atomic weapons and demanding that France not prepare to produce atomic bombs. The resolution advocated that the nation's money and resources be used for scientific research into the peaceful uses of atomic energy.

### 法国大学教授集会  谴责西方继续核试验

1958-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    法国大学教授集会
    谴责西方继续核试验
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社巴黎5日讯：法国各大学的教授和教师在巴黎举行会议，讨论试验核武器的危险性。
    会上一致通过决议，谴责继续试验原子武器的行动，并且要求法国不准备生产原子弹。决议主张把国家的钱财和资源用于和平利用原子能方面的科学研究工作。
