Title: Hydrogen Bomb Testing Must Not Be Allowed
Author:
Date: 1958-05-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 1st: Over six hundred prominent British scientists have appealed to the British Prime Minister, demanding that the British government immediately take measures to stop nuclear weapons testing. Many famous British scientists have signed the appeal, including sixty-nine Fellows of the Royal Society and ninety-three professors from British universities.

Lord Bertrand Russell, chairman of the "Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament" organization that initiated the appeal, said in a letter attached to the appeal to Macmillan, "I would like to add that I personally believe that it is unacceptable for Britain to continue its current series of [nuclear] tests, disregarding the fact that the Soviet government has already stopped (nuclear) testing. There is a precious opportunity to reduce the nuclear danger we all face. I hope that you and your government will accept not only the opinions of many prominent scientists, but also the opinions of millions of ordinary people, and order an immediate cessation of the current series of tests being conducted on Christmas Island."

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 1st: British Prime Minister Macmillan today rejected the appeal from prominent British scientists calling for an immediate halt to hydrogen bomb testing. In a letter to Russell, Macmillan acknowledged that the demand to end British hydrogen bomb testing was a matter of "concern to all the British people," but said he doubted "whether it would be wise to stop British testing now."

### 不能容许试验氢弹  英六百多著名科学家向政府提出要求  麦克米伦竟无理拒绝科学家们的呼吁

1958-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    不能容许试验氢弹
    英六百多著名科学家向政府提出要求
    麦克米伦竟无理拒绝科学家们的呼吁
    据新华社伦敦1日电　六百一十八名英国著名科学家向英国首相发出呼吁，要求英国政府立刻采取措施停止试验核武器。在呼吁书上签名的有英国许多著名科学家，其中有六十九位是皇家学会的会员，九十三位是英国各大学的教授。
    发起这项呼吁的“争取裁减核军备运动”组织主席、哲学家罗素在随呼吁书附给麦克米伦的一封信中说，“我愿附加说明一点，我个人认为，英国不顾苏联政府已经停止（核）试验，而仍然进行它目前的一系列（核）试验，这是不能容许的。目前存在着减少我们大家面临的原子核危险的宝贵机会。我希望你和你的政府将接受不仅是许多著名科学家的意见，而且是千百万普通人民的意见，并且下令立即停止目前在圣诞岛进行的一系列试验。”
    据新华社伦敦1日电　英国首相麦克米伦今天拒绝了英国著名科学家的关于要求立刻停止氢弹试验的呼吁。麦克米伦在给罗素的信中虽然一方面承认要求结束英国的氢弹试验是“英国全体人民都关心”的问题，但是另一方面又说，他对“现在就停止英国的试验是否明智”表示怀疑。
