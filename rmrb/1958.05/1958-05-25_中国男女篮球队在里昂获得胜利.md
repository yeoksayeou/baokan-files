Title: Chinese Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Win in Lyon
Author:
Date: 1958-05-25
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 24th: The Chinese men's and women's basketball teams both won in their matches against the mixed men's and women's team from Lyon on the 22nd in Lyon. The men's team won 101 to 68, and the women's team won 68 to 38.

Among the Lyon men's team, two were national team members and three were national youth team members. The Lyon mixed women's team is also one of the strongest women's teams in France.

### 中国男女篮球队在里昂获得胜利

1958-05-25
第2版()
专栏：

    中国男女篮球队在里昂获得胜利
    据新华社巴黎24日电　中国男女篮球队22日在里昂同里昂的男、女混合队交锋中均获胜利。男子队以一百零一比六十八获胜，女子队以六十八比三十八获胜。
    里昂男子队中有两名是国家队员，有三名是国家青年队的队员。里昂女子混合队也是法国女子强队之一。
