Title: Soviet Embassy in China Holds Reception for Soviet National Symphony Orchestra's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 29th - The Soviet Embassy in China held a reception this evening for the Soviet National Symphony Orchestra's visit and performance in China.

    Li Dequan, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, and Ma Sicong, Vice Chairman, were invited to attend the reception.

    Counsellor Sudarikov said in his speech at the reception that the Soviet National Symphony Orchestra has performed for more than 10,000 listeners in Beijing and received the most sincere and comradely warm welcome from the Chinese people. This is another manifestation of the friendship between the two peoples. Anosov, the head of the delegation, said at the reception that they were satisfied in all aspects of artistic creation during their visit and performance. The friendship between Chinese and Soviet musicians and peoples is demonstrated at all times. They are also extremely happy to be able to go deep into production units and perform directly for Chinese workers, peasants and the broad masses of working people.

    Qu Wu, on behalf of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Ministry of Culture, and the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, thanked the Soviet artists for their wonderful performances at the reception. He said that the Soviet musicians have contributed to China's socialist Great Leap Forward and provided a model for Chinese musicians to learn from.

    The friendly reception lasted for two hours.

### 为苏联国家交响乐团访华  苏联驻华大使馆举行招待会

1958-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

    为苏联国家交响乐团访华
    苏联驻华大使馆举行招待会
    新华社29日讯　苏联驻华大使馆今晚为苏联国家交响乐团到中国访问演出举行招待会。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全，文化部副部长夏衍、对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，中国音乐家协会主席吕骥、副主席马思聪等应邀出席了招待会。
    苏达利柯夫参赞在招待会上致词说，苏联国家交响乐团在北京已为一万多听众演出，受到中国人民最诚恳的同志式的热烈欢迎，这是两国人民友谊的又一次表现。阿诺索夫团长在招待会上说，他们在访问演出的期间，在艺术创作等各方面都得到了满足。中苏两国音乐家和人民间的友谊，随时都表现出来。他们并且为了特别能深入生产单位，直接为中国工人、农民和广大劳动人民演出，而感到异常高兴。
    屈武代表中苏友协、文化部和对外文委在招待会上感谢苏联艺术家的精彩演出。他说，苏联音乐家为中国的社会主义大跃进作出了贡献，并且为中国音乐家提供了学习的榜样。
    充满友谊的招待会进行了两个小时。
