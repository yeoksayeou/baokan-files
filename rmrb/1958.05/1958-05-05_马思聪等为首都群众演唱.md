Title: Ma Sicong and Others Perform for the Capital's Masses
Author:
Date: 1958-05-05
Page: 7

    Ma Sicong and the soprano Liu Shufang, among others, spent a pleasant evening with the masses who were at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace and enjoying the park last night. In the crowd, Ma Sicong performed a violin solo, playing his own works "Nostalgia" and "Dance of the Frontier." Liu Shufang sang songs such as "Jasmine Flowers Planted Along the Wall" and "The Oranges are Ripe." The audience repeatedly welcomed their performances with warm applause. People also enjoyed performances by pianists and pipa players.
    At the Cultural Palace, at the "Book Fair" commemorating the "May Fourth" Youth Day, which concludes today, writers Guo Moruo, Ye Shengtao, Lao She, Yang Mo, poet Zang Kejia, and others met with readers from the 2nd to the 4th and signed books for them as souvenirs.

### 马思聪等为首都群众演唱

1958-05-05
第7版()
专栏：

    马思聪等为首都群众演唱
    本报讯　昨晚，音乐家马思聪和女高音歌唱家刘淑芳等到北京市劳动人民文化宫和游园的群众共同度过了一个愉快的夜晚。在人群中，马思聪表演了小提琴独奏。演奏了他自己的作品“思乡曲”和“塞外舞曲”。刘淑芳演唱了“?子花儿顺墙栽”、“桔子熟了”等歌曲。听众们一再用热烈的掌声欢迎他们的表演。人们还欣赏了钢琴家和琵琶演奏家的表演。
    在文化宫里，今天结束的纪念“五四”青年节的“书市”上，作家郭沫若、叶圣陶、老舍、杨沫，诗人臧克家等曾分别在2日至4日和读者见了面，并且为读者在书上签名留念。
