Foreign Press Clippings
    Such College Students
    The Extra-Curricular Education Auxiliary Center of Indiana State University (USA) issued the following question to the school's students: What impact do you think Soviet artificial satellites will have on the future of the United States?
    10% of the university's freshman and sophomore students replied that they did not know that the Soviet Union had launched artificial satellites.
          ——American "New York Times"

    American "Tradition"
    Princess Hannelore, the American wife of Prince Alexander Hohenlohe, opened a nightclub in the Austrian Alps that caters exclusively to the wealthy.
    Six weeks ago, she bought a black girl in the "souk" of (African) Somaliland to serve her, for a fair price of only $200. She told reporters: "The reason I bought a female slave is because I am from Georgia in the (American) South, where we raise slaves."
          ——British "Daily Mail"

### 外国报刊拾零

1958-05-19
第6版()
专栏：外国报刊拾零

　　外国报刊拾零
    如此大学生
    （美国）印第安纳州立大学的课外教育辅助中心，向该校的学生发出以下一道问题：你认为苏联人造卫星对美国的前途将会产生什么影响？
    10％的大学一、二年级学生答复说，他们并不知道苏联已经发射过人造卫星。
          ——美国“纽约时报”
    美国“传统”
    亚历山大·荷汉罗希亲王的美国妻子汉尼琪儿王妃，在奥地利的阿尔卑斯山开了一家专门招待大富豪的夜总会。
    六个星期前，她在（非洲）索马里兰的沙发利买了一个黑人女孩来服侍自己，价钱很公道，只花了二百美元。她对记者说：“我购买女奴的原因，是因为我是（美国）南部乔治亚州的人，我们那里是养奴隶的。”
          ——英国“每日邮报”
