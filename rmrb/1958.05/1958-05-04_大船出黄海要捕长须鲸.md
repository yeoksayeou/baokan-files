Title: Large Ship Sets Sail for Yellow Sea to Hunt Fin Whales
Author: Gu Tiefeng
Date: 1958-05-04
Page: 2

    Large Ship Sets Sail for Yellow Sea to Hunt Fin Whales
    (Our dispatch) Our country's first fin whale hunting ship set sail on the 18th, heading rapidly towards the northern Yellow Sea.

    This fin whale hunting ship was specially modified from a 250-horsepower motor vessel. It has many modern facilities, including communication equipment, automated winches, and more.

    In the past, the Luda Aquatic Products Company could only catch small whales weighing three to four tons. Now, it can catch fin whales that are ten times larger than small whales.
                    (Gu Tiefeng)

### 大船出黄海要捕长须鲸
顾铁风
1958-05-04
第2版()
专栏：

    大船出黄海要捕长须鲸
    本报讯　我国第一艘捕长须鲸的捕鲸船已在18日出海，向黄海北部快速进发。
    这艘捕长须鲸的捕鲸船，是由一艘二百五十马力机轮专门改装的，船上有许多现代化设备，有通讯联系设备，有自动化的卷扬机等等。
    旅大水产公司过去只能捕三、四吨重的小鲸，现在就可捕获比小鲸大十倍的长须鲸了。
                    （顾铁风）
