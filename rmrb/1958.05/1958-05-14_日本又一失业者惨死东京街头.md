Title: Another Unemployed Person Dies Tragically on the Streets of Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1958-05-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 13th – TASS reports: The body of unemployed person Hideo Suzuki was discovered on the streets of Tokyo on the morning of the 12th. According to the "Yomiuri Shimbun," he died of exhaustion.

Such news is commonplace in Japanese newspapers. Freezing to death, starving to death, suicide due to difficult living conditions – this is the fate of many working people in Japan. According to the "Yomiuri Shimbun," some people in Japan committed suicide every day last year.

### 日本又一失业者惨死东京街头

1958-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    日本又一失业者惨死东京街头
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社东京讯：12日上午在东京街头发现了失业者铃木秀雄的尸体。据“读卖日本新闻”报道，精疲力尽是他致死的原因。
    这类消息在日本报纸上已经司空见惯。冻死、饿死、因生活艰难而自杀——这就是日本许多劳动人民的命运。据“读卖日本新闻”报道，日本去年每天都有一些人自杀。
