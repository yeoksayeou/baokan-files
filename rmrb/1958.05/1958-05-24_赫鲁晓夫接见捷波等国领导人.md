Title: Khrushchev Receives Leaders from Czechoslovakia, Poland, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-05-24
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 23rd: According to TASS, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and President of Czechoslovakia, Novotny, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Gomulka, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Zhivkov, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Yugov, and the First Deputy Chairman, R. Damyanov, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Ulbricht, and the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Damba, and the First Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers, Tsend, on the 21st and 22nd respectively.

    Khrushchev held talks with the party and government leaders of these countries separately. The talks were conducted in a sincere and comradely atmosphere.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见捷波等国领导人

1958-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见捷波等国领导人
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在21日和22日分别接见了捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、捷克斯洛伐克总统诺沃提尼，波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡，保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫、保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫和第一副主席拉·达米扬诺夫，德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希和德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥等，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴和蒙古部长会议第一副主席曾德。
    赫鲁晓夫分别同这些国家的党政领导人作了交谈。谈话是在真诚的、同志式的气氛中进行的。
