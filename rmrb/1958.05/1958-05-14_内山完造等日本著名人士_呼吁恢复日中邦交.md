Title: Kanzō Uchiyama and Other Prominent Japanese Figures Call for the Restoration of Sino-Japanese Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1958-05-14
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch from Tokyo: Seven prominent Japanese figures today jointly issued a statement calling on the Japanese people to strive for the restoration of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations.
    The statement pointed out that the recent incidents of insulting the Chinese national flag and the complete cessation of Sino-Japanese trade caused by the sabotage of the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement have cast a shadow over Sino-Japanese relations. The statement demanded a reversal of this situation.
    The statement was signed by prominent figures in Japanese society such as Kanzō Uchiyama, Tadashi Obata, Yasaburo Shimonaka, Hiroshi Suekawa, Kazuo Suzuki, Kenzo Nakajima, and Raicho Hiratsuka. They decided to widely distribute this statement to all sectors of Japanese society.

### 内山完造等日本著名人士  呼吁恢复日中邦交

1958-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    内山完造等日本著名人士
　　呼吁恢复日中邦交
    新华社13日讯　东京消息：七位日本著名人士今天联合发表声明，呼吁日本人民为争取恢复日中邦交而努力。
    声明指出，最近先后发生的侮辱中国国旗事件和由于第四次日中贸易协定遭到破坏而引起的日中贸易的全面停顿，在日中关系方面投下了暗影。声明要求扭转这种状态。
    在声明上签字的有内山完造、小畑忠良、下中弥三郎、末川博、铃木一雄、中岛健藏和平冢雷鸟等日本社会名流。他们决定要把这项声明广泛发给日本各界。
