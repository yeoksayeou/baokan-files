Title: Huang Zhiyong and Li Menglin Set New Records in 5,000-meter Race and Discus Throw
Author:
Date: 1958-05-05
Page: 7
　
　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Chinese People's Liberation Army track and field athletes Huang Zhiyong and Li Menglin broke the national records for the men's 5,000-meter race and the men's discus throw, respectively, on the 3rd during a demonstration match at the Peking University 60th Anniversary Track and Field Weightlifting Games.

　　Huang Zhiyong completed the 5,000-meter race in 15 minutes and 3.4 seconds, breaking the national record of 15 minutes and 9.6 seconds set by Fu Shenghai last year. This is the third time he has broken the national 5,000-meter record this year.

　　Li Menglin threw the discus to a distance of 45.85 meters, breaking the national record of 45.20 meters set by Li Bingcheng last year.

### 黄志勇  李孟林  创五千公尺长跑和铁饼新纪录

1958-05-05
第7版()
专栏：

    黄志勇  李孟林
    创五千公尺长跑和铁饼新纪录
    据新华社讯　中国人民解放军田径选手黄志勇、李孟林，3日在参加北京大学六十周年校庆田径举重运动大会的表演赛时，分别打破了男子五千公尺长跑和男子铁饼的全国纪录。
    黄志勇用十五分三秒四的时间跑完五千公尺全程，刷新了傅生海在去年创造的十五分九秒六的全国纪录。这是他今年第三次打破五千公尺全国纪录。
    李孟林把铁饼掷到四十五点八五公尺的远处，打破了李秉诚在去年创造的四十五点二○公尺的全国纪录。
