Title: Capital's Circles Publicly Commemorate Comrade Lai Ruoyu. Trade Unions and People's Organizations from Various Countries Send Condolence Messages

Author:

Date: 1958-05-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd. Three thousand representatives from all circles in the capital solemnly held a public memorial service today at Zhongshan Hall to mourn the passing of Comrade Lai Ruoyu.

Hanging high in the mourning hall was a portrait of Comrade Lai Ruoyu. Wreaths sent by party and state leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, and others were placed on both sides. At precisely 10:00 AM, the public memorial ceremony began, presided over by Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping, Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Lin Biao, Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Xiannian, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, Dong Biwu, Zhang Dingcheng, and Zhang Zhizhong. At this moment, the band played funeral music. Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, presented a wreath to the portrait of Comrade Lai Ruoyu, and the entire audience observed a moment of silence.

Li Xuefeng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, then delivered a eulogy. He said: "Comrade Lai Ruoyu was an outstanding member of our Party and a revolutionary fighter who had been tested for a long time." After recounting Comrade Lai Ruoyu's glorious achievements in devoting himself to the revolution, Li Xuefeng emphasized that Comrade Lai Ruoyu possessed a high degree of initiative and enthusiasm for the revolutionary cause of the Party and the working class. He had the fine qualities of humility, prudence, diligence, and simplicity of the Party and the working class. He was good at uniting comrades and closely contacting the masses, was amiable and approachable, and could also seriously uphold principled struggle. He was always full of revolutionary optimism in any period. Comrade Lai Ruoyu also constantly strived to study Marxism-Leninism and Chairman Mao's works, paid great attention to the integration of theory and practice, was good at discovering new things, and supported the creative activities of the masses. These are all models for us Communists, revolutionary cadres, and workers to learn from.

Also participating in today's public memorial ceremony were responsible persons from various departments of the CPC Central Committee and some representatives attending the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the CPC, responsible persons from various departments of state organs, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and various industrial trade unions, various people's organizations and democratic parties, Comrade Lai Ruoyu's friends, family members, and representatives from workers and all circles in the capital.

After the public memorial ceremony, Comrade Lai Ruoyu's ashes were moved to the Babaoshan Cemetery.

Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has received a large number of telegrams from the World Federation of Trade Unions, trade union organizations from various countries, people's organizations, and trade union workers condoling the death of Comrade Lai Ruoyu. These condolence messages stated that Comrade Lai Ruoyu dedicated his life to the cause of the Chinese working class and to the construction of socialism in the motherland. He worked tirelessly in his position as Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, devoting all his energy to the development of the Chinese trade union movement and to strengthening the cause of proletarian internationalism. The death of Comrade Lai Ruoyu is a major loss to the Chinese trade union movement and the international trade union movement.

Organizations that sent telegrams to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to express condolences on the death of Comrade Lai Ruoyu include the World Federation of Trade Unions, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union, the editorial board of the Soviet Labor Newspaper, the International Transport Workers' Union , the Arab Trade Union International Confederation, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Bulgaria, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, the Egyptian Trade Union Federation, the Free German Trade Union Federation, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Hungary, the All-India Trade Union Congress, the All-Indonesia Central Trade Union Federation, the Plantation Workers' Union of the Republic of Indonesia, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Iran, the Italian General Confederation of Labor, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the National Metalworkers' Union of Japan, the Central Conference for Joint Struggle of Metalworkers' Unions of Japan, the Japan Teachers' Union, the Kyoto Teachers' Union, the Japan Coal Miners' Union, the All-Japan Beer Brewery Workers' Union, the All-Japan Automobile Transport Workers' Union, the Japan National Relief Association, the Matsukawa Incident Countermeasures Committee, the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Poland, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Romania, the Singapore Trades Union Congress, the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia, and trade union delegations from the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, North Korea, Hungary, Poland, Indonesia, and Ceylon visiting China, and so on.

Also sending condolence messages to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions was S. Sujono, Minister of Labor of the Republic of Indonesia, and others. (Photo attached)

After the public memorial ceremony, Comrade Lai Ruoyu's ashes were moved to the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery.

Xin Hua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian, Photo

### 首都各界公祭赖若愚同志  各国工会和人民团体来电吊唁

1958-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界公祭赖若愚同志
    各国工会和人民团体来电吊唁
    新华社23日讯　首都各界代表三千人今天上午在中山堂隆重举行公祭，追悼赖若愚同志的逝世。
    灵堂内高悬着赖若愚同志的遗像。党和国家的领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云等人送的花圈陈放在两旁。十时正，公祭仪式由刘少奇、周恩来、邓小平、林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通、林彪、彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李先念、聂荣臻、薄一波、董必武、张鼎丞、张治中等主持开始。这时，乐队奏起哀乐，中国共产党中央委员会副主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇向赖若愚同志的遗像敬献花圈，全场默哀。
    中共中央书记处书记李雪峰接着致悼词。他说：“赖若愚同志是我们党的优秀党员，是久经考验的革命战士。”李雪峰在追述了赖若愚同志献身革命的光荣业迹后着重指出，赖若愚同志对党和工人阶级的革命事业具有高度的积极性和主动性。他具有党和工人阶级的谦虚谨慎、勤俭朴素的优良作风。他善于团结同志和密切联系群众，和蔼可亲，平易近人，又能严肃地坚持原则斗争。他在任何时期都充满革命的乐观主义精神。赖若愚同志并且经常努力学习马克思列宁主义和毛主席的著作，很注意理论和实际的联系，善于发现新鲜事物，支持群众的创造活动。这些都是我们共产党人，革命干部和工人群众学习的榜样。
    参加今天的公祭仪式的，还有中共中央各部门的负责人和出席中共八大二次会议的部分代表，国家机关各部门、全国总工会和各产业工会、各人民团体和民主党派的负责人，赖若愚同志的生前友好和家属，以及首都职工和各界代表。
    公祭后，赖若愚同志的骨灰随即移灵至八宝山公墓。
    新华社23日讯　中华全国总工会接到世界工会联合会、各国工会组织、人民团体、工会工作者吊唁赖若愚同志逝世的大批电报。这些唁电表示赖若愚同志为中国工人阶级的事业，为祖国建设社会主义献出了自己的一生，他在中华全国总工会主席的岗位上不倦地劳动，把自己的全部精力用于发展中国工会运动、用于加强无产阶级国际主义的事业。赖若愚同志的逝世是中国工会运动和国际工会运动的重大损失。
    致电中华全国总工会吊唁赖若愚同志逝世的有世界工会联合会、全苏工会中央理事会、苏联劳动报编委会、运输工人工会国际、阿拉伯工会国际联合会、阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会、保加利亚工会中央理事会、捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会、埃及工会联合会、自由德国工会联合会、匈牙利工会中央理事会、全印工会大会、全印度尼西亚中央职工会、印度尼西亚共和国农园工会、伊朗统一工会中央理事会、意大利总工会、日本工会总评议会、日本全国金属工会、日本五金工会联合斗争中央会议、日本教职员工会、京都教职员工会、日本煤矿工会、全日本啤酒工会、日本全国汽车运输工会、日本国民救援会、松川事件对策委员会、日中友好协会、蒙古人民共和国工会中央理事会、波兰工会中央理事会、罗马尼亚工会中央理事会、新加坡职工联合总会、越南总工会、南斯拉夫工会中央理事会和在中国访问的苏联、保加利亚、朝鲜、匈牙利、波兰、印度尼西亚、锡兰工会代表团等。
