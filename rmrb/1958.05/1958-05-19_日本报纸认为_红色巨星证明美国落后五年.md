Title: Japanese Newspapers Believe Red Giant Proves America is Five Years Behind
Author:
Date: 1958-05-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 17th (TASS dispatch): Japanese newspapers today published many reports introducing the Soviet Union's third artificial satellite. Commentaries in the newspapers emphasized that with the launch of the third satellite, the Soviet Union is even further ahead of the United States. Some newspapers pointed out that this gap is at least five years.

### 日本报纸认为  红色巨星证明美国落后五年

1958-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    日本报纸认为
    红色巨星证明美国落后五年
    据新华社18日讯　据塔斯社东京17日讯：日本报纸今天发表了许多材料，介绍苏联第三颗人造卫星。各报在评论中强调指出，随着第三颗卫星的发射，苏联更远远地走在美国的前面了。一些报纸指出，这一距离至少有五年。
