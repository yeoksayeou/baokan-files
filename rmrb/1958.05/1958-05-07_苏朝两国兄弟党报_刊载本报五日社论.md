Title: Soviet and Korean Brotherly Party Newspapers Publish This Newspaper's Editorial of the 5th

Author:

Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th. Today's "Pravda" published the full text of the Chinese "People's Daily" editorial of the 5th: "Modern Revisionism Must Be Criticized".
	Soviet newspapers today also carried news of the opening of the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th. Today, the Korean "Rodong Sinmun" published the full text of the Chinese "People's Daily" editorial of May 5th entitled "Modern Revisionism Must Be Criticized" on page four.

### 苏朝两国兄弟党报  刊载本报五日社论

1958-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏朝两国兄弟党报
