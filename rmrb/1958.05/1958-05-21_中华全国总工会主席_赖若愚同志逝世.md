Title: Comrade Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 20th: Obituary

Comrade Lai Ruoyu, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Member of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and Member of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, passed away at Beijing Hospital at 7:05 PM on May 20, 1958, due to liver cancer. It is now decided that the body will lie in state at Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. On the afternoon of May 22nd, people from all walks of life will pay their respects, and a public memorial service will be held at 10:00 AM on May 23rd. After the public memorial service, the body will be moved to Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery. This is the obituary.

                                  Funeral Committee for Comrade Lai Ruoyu
                                          May 20, 1958 (Attached image)

### 中华全国总工会主席  赖若愚同志逝世

1958-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会主席
    赖若愚同志逝世
    新华社20日讯　讣告
    中国共产党中央委员会委员，中华全国总工会主席，中华人民共和国第一届人民代表大会常务委员会委员，中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会委员赖若愚同志患肝癌症，于1958年5月20日下午七时零五分在北京医院逝世。现决定停灵于中山公园中山堂，5月22日下午各界吊唁，5月23日上午十时举行公祭，公祭后移灵八宝山。特此讣告。
                                  赖若愚同志治丧委员会
                                          1958年5月20日（附图片）
