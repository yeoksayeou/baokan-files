Title: The First Yellow River Irrigation (Picture)
Author: Yuan Ruxun, Altai
Date: 1958-05-25
Page: 3

Column: Blossoms of Construction

    The First Yellow River Irrigation
    On May 17th, Dengkou County, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, in the Yellow River Houtao area, introduced Yellow River water for irrigation for the first time. This is the Three Rivers Weir being used for water diversion for irrigation. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ruxun, Altai photographed)
