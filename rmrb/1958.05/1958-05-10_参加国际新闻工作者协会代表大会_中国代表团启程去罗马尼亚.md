Title: Chinese Delegation Departs for Romania to Attend the International Organization of Journalists Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Chinese delegation attending the Fourth Congress of the International Organization of Journalists departed for Romania today.

    The head of the delegation is Jin Zhonghua, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, and the members are Wang Yi, Li Bingquan, and Yuan Shuipai. Yuan Shuipai had previously left for Romania on the 6th to participate in the International Feature Writers Meeting.

### 参加国际新闻工作者协会代表大会  中国代表团启程去罗马尼亚

1958-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    参加国际新闻工作者协会代表大会
    中国代表团启程去罗马尼亚
    新华社9日讯　参加国际新闻工作者协会第四次代表大会的中国代表团，今天启程去罗马尼亚。
    代表团团长是中国新闻工作者协会副会长金仲华，团员是王揖、李炳泉和袁水拍。袁水拍已先期在6日去罗马尼亚参加国际特写记者会见。
