Title: Togo Unification Committee Leader Olympio Appointed New Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1958-05-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 17th Dispatch: The Legislative Assembly of the French-administered Togo unanimously passed a resolution on the 16th authorizing Sylvanus Olympio, leader of the Togo Unification Committee, to serve as the new Prime Minister of French-administered Togo.

The Togo Unification Committee, which advocates for Togo's independence from French colonial rule, achieved a significant victory in the legislative assembly elections held in late April.

### 多哥统一委员会领袖奥林匹欧担任新总理

1958-05-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　多哥统一委员会领袖
    奥林匹欧担任新总理
    据新华社17日讯　多哥法国托管区立法议会16日一致通过授权多哥统一委员会领袖西凡虞·奥林匹欧担任多哥法托管区新总理。
    主张多哥摆脱法国殖民统治而独立的多哥统一委员会，在4月下旬举行的立法议会选举中取得了重大胜利。
