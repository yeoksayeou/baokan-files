Title: Ibrahim Cables Thanks to Peng Dehuai
Author:
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Lieutenant General Mohammed Ibrahim, head of the military friendship delegation from the United Arab Republic visiting China, sent a thank you telegram to Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the State Council, on his way back to his country from Beijing. The text is as follows:

Marshal Peng Dehuai:

As we leave Beijing, we would like to express our deep gratitude for the very pleasant time we have had in your great country. We will always remember this visit. We believe that this visit has demonstrated the strong and sincere friendship between our two countries. We wish you personally all success in the future and the Chinese people happiness and prosperity.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Leader General Ibrahim, May 6th

### 易卜拉欣电谢彭德怀

1958-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    易卜拉欣电谢彭德怀
    新华社6日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团团长穆罕默德·易卜拉欣中将在离开北京返国途中，拍给国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅的感谢电，文如下：彭德怀元帅：
    当我们离开北京之际，我们要为在你们伟大的国家中过得十分愉快而表示深切的谢意，我们将永远记忆着这一次访问。我们相信这次访问证明了我们两国之间巩固的真诚的友谊，祝你个人将来一切成功，祝中国人民幸福和繁荣。
                                团长　易卜拉欣将军　5月6日
