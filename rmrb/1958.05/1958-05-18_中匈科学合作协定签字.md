Title: Sino-Hungarian Scientific Cooperation Agreement Signed
Author:
Date: 1958-05-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 17th. A scientific cooperation agreement between the Chinese and Hungarian Academies of Sciences was signed in Beijing on the afternoon of May 17th.  Representing the two Academies of Sciences were Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Erdey Ferenc, head of the delegation from the Hungarian Academy of Sciences.

Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Nogradi, the Hungarian Ambassador to China, attended the signing ceremony.

Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet on the evening of May 17th to bid farewell to the delegation from the Hungarian Academy of Sciences. The delegation will leave Beijing on the 18th to return home, while Academician Janossy Lajos will continue his visit to China.

### 中匈科学合作协定签字

1958-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈科学合作协定签字
    新华社17日讯　中匈两国科学院科学合作协定17日下午在北京签字。代表两国科学院签字的是中国科学院副院长竺可桢和匈牙利科学院代表团团长埃尔德·费伦茨。
    中国科学院院长郭沫若和匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第出席了签字仪式。
    中国科学院副院长竺可桢17日晚设宴欢送匈牙利科学院代表团。代表团将于18日离京回国，团员雅诺西·拉洛斯院士继续在中国访问。
