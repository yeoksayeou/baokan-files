Title: Self-Priming Pressure Sprayer (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 2

	Self-Priming Pressure Sprayer
	Manufactured by Jiangmen Handicraft Industry Union, Guangdong Province.
	Suitable for spraying pesticides on crops such as grains, sugarcane, vegetables, and jute. Operated by two people, it can spray 14 to 17 mu per day. Its spray height can reach 11 meters and its width is one meter.

### 自由吸水压力喷雾器（图片）

1958-05-09
第2版()
专栏：

    自由吸水压力喷雾器
    广东省江门市手工业联社制造。
    适于禾谷类、甘蔗、疏菜、黄麻等作物的喷药治虫用。由二人操作，每日可喷十四至十七亩。它的喷射高度可达十一公尺，幅宽为一公尺。
