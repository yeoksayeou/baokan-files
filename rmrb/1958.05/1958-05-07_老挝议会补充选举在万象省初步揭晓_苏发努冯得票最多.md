Title: Lao Parliamentary By-Election in Vientiane Province Initially Revealed: Souphanouvong Receives the Most Votes
Author:
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 6

    Lao Parliamentary By-Election in Vientiane Province Initially Revealed
    Souphanouvong Receives the Most Votes
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th - According to a broadcast by Vientiane Radio this morning, voting for the Lao National Assembly by-election began at 7:00 AM on the 4th and ended at 6:00 PM that day.
    According to the vote count results from Vientiane Province as of the 6th, Prince Souphanouvong, the candidate of the Lao Patriotic Party, led with 6,730 votes among the twelve candidates in Vientiane Province. Vientiane Province has a total of 80,730 voters, and is stipulated to elect two members of parliament. In Luang Prabang Province, Prince Chao Souk, the candidate of the Patriotic Party, led with 4,006 votes.
    It is reported that Phoui Sananikone, a member of parliament from the United Party in Attopeu Province in southern Laos, accused that at least thirteen people had been killed during the election campaign. He said that fifty people had been arrested in Attopeu Province alone.

### 老挝议会补充选举在万象省初步揭晓  苏发努冯得票最多

1958-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝议会补充选举在万象省初步揭晓
    苏发努冯得票最多
    新华社河内6日电　据万象电台今晨广播，老挝国民议会补充选举的投票在4日上午七时开始进行，到当天下午六时结束。
    据万象省截至6日为止的开票结果，在万象省的十二名候选人中，老挝爱国党的候选人苏发努冯亲王以六千七百三十票领先。万象省共有选民八万零七百三十人，按规定可选出议员两名。在琅勃拉邦省，爱国党的候选人昭·苏克亲王以四千零六票领先。
    据报道，老挝南部阿速坡省的联合党议员福耳塞纳指责说，在选举前的竞选运动中，至少已经有十三人被杀害。他说，单在阿速坡省就有五十人被捕。
