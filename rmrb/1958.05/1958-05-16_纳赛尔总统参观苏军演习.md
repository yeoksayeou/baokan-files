Title: President Nasser Visits Soviet Military Exercises
Author:
Date: 1958-05-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 15th: According to TASS, President Nasser of the United Arab Republic and other national leaders of the United Arab Republic visited the summer camp of the Taman Mechanized Guards Division on the 14th, visited the shooting range, and watched overall combat exercises using various modern weapons. The distinguished guests reviewed the coordinated offensive exercises of the air force, tanks, and army, and watched modern military equipment.
    President Nasser said when leaving the troops that the movements of the various services were very coordinated, the command was excellent, and the weapons were powerful.

### 纳赛尔总统参观苏军演习

1958-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　纳赛尔总统参观苏军演习
    据新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔和阿拉伯联合共和国其他国家领导人14日访问了塔曼机械化近卫师夏令营，参观了打靶场，观看了用各种现代化武器进行的总体战作战演习。贵宾们检阅了空军、坦克、陆军的协同进攻演习，观看了现代化的军事装备。
    纳赛尔总统在离开部队时说，各兵种的动作非常协调，指挥高明，武器威力强大。
