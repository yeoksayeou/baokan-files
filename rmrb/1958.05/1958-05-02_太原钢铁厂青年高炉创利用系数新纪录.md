Title: Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant's Youth Blast Furnace Sets New Record for Utilization Coefficient
Author:
Date: 1958-05-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, May 1st. The youth blast furnace of the Ironmaking Department of Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant set a new record for blast furnace utilization coefficient on April 30th. This time, the utilization coefficient was 0.536, a decrease of 0.005 compared to the national record set by this blast furnace on April 13th. The daily output exceeded the state plan by 4%, and the quality of pig iron reached 100%.

### 太原钢铁厂青年高炉创利用系数新纪录

1958-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

    太原钢铁厂青年高炉创利用系数新纪录
    据新华社太原1日电　太原钢铁厂炼铁部青年高炉，4月30日又创高炉利用系数新纪录。这次的利用系数是零点五三六，比这个高炉4月13日创造的全国新纪录降低了零点零零五，日产量超过国家计划4％，生铁质量达到100％。
