Title: Yemeni Army Deals Heavy Blow to Invaders
Author:
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 11th: Yemeni Deputy Foreign Minister Hassan Ibrahim received a telegram from Taiz on the evening of May 10th stating that the Yemeni army and the People's Anti-Aggression Army, fighting side by side, were sufficient to repel the British army's attack on the Yemeni border city of Kataba. The British army suffered heavy losses during the four-day offensive.

### 也门军队痛歼侵略者

1958-05-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　也门军队痛歼侵略者
    据新华社开罗11日电　也门外交副大臣哈桑·易卜拉欣10日晚上收到塔兹发来的电报说，也门军队和人民抗敌军并肩作战，足以击退英国军队对也门边境城市卡塔巴的进攻。英国军队在四天的进攻中遭受到重大的损失。
