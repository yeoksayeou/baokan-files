Title: China-Albania Cultural Cooperation Agreement 1958-1959 Implementation Plan Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 2

    China-Albania Cultural Cooperation Agreement
    1958-1959 Implementation Plan Signed in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th -- The 1958-1959 implementation plan of the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Albania was signed in Beijing on the afternoon of the 29th.
    Qu Wu, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Prifti, the Albanian Ambassador to China, signed the implementation plan on behalf of their respective countries.
    At the signing ceremony, Qu Wu and Prifti delivered speeches, congratulating the continuous development of the friendship between China and Albania and the cultural relations between the two countries.
    After the signing, Qu Wu also held a reception to celebrate.

### 中阿文化合作协定  1958—1959年执行计划在北京签订

1958-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    中阿文化合作协定
    1958—1959年执行计划在北京签订
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国文化合作协定1958——1959年执行计划，29日下午在北京签订。
    我国对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使巴利里，分别代表两国在执行计划上签字。
    在签字仪式上，屈武和巴利里先后讲了话，他们祝中阿两国友谊和两国文化关系不断发展。
    签字以后，屈武还举行了酒会，表示庆祝。
