Title: Democratic German Table Tennis Team to Play Beijing Team Today; Korean Weightlifting Team to Meet "August 1st" Team Tomorrow

Author:

Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 7

Sports News

    Democratic German Table Tennis Team to Play Beijing Team Today
    Korean Weightlifting Team to Meet "August 1st" Team Tomorrow
    (Our Newspaper) The eleven-member table tennis team from the German Democratic Republic, invited to visit our country, arrived in Beijing on the 8th. In Beijing, they will have two friendly matches with the Beijing Table Tennis Team tonight and tomorrow afternoon.

    The Democratic German table tennis team that came to our country this time has seven male and female players, all of whom are well-known table tennis athletes in their country.

    Also, the eight-member Korean weightlifting team led by Kim Yeon-ik, the International Minister of the Sports Guidance Committee of the Korean Cabinet, arrived in Beijing on the 8th. They are here in our country for a friendly competition by invitation.

    The Korean weightlifting team has brought a total of six athletes this time (one in each weight class except heavyweight), including four Master Athletes and two First-Class Athletes.

    The first team to face the Korean team is the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Weightlifting Team, and the competition will be held on the 11th. Zhao Qingkui, our country's famous light heavyweight athlete, and Xu Honglin, the featherweight athlete, will also perform in the competition on the 11th.

### 民主德国乒乓球队今战北京队  朝鲜举重队明日遇“八一”队

1958-05-10
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    民主德国乒乓球队今战北京队
    朝鲜举重队明日遇“八一”队
    本报讯　应邀来我国访问的德意志民主共和国乒乓球队一行十一人于8日到达北京。在北京，他们将在今日晚上和明日下午同北京市乒乓球队进行两场友谊比赛。
    这次前来我国的民主德国乒乓球队共有男女队员七人，他们都是国内著名的乒乓球运动员。
    又讯　由朝鲜内阁体育指导委员会国际部长金连亿率领的朝鲜举重队一行八人于8日到北京。他们这次是应邀来我国作访问比赛的。
    朝鲜举重队这次共来六名运动员（除重量级外，每级一人），其中有四名运动健将，两名一级运动员。
    第一个迎战朝鲜队的是中国人民解放军“八一”举重队，比赛时间是11日。我国著名轻重量级运动员赵庆奎和次轻量级运动员徐鸿林也将在11日的比赛中作表演。
