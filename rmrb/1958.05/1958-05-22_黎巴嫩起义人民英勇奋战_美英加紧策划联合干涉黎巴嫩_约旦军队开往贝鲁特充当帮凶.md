Title: Lebanese Insurgent People Fight Heroically; U.S. and Britain Intensify Plot for Joint Intervention in Lebanon; Jordanian Troops Head to Beirut as Accomplices

Author:
Date: 1958-05-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 21st: Lebanese insurgents engaged in fierce fighting with government-controlled armed forces in several regions on the 20th. The insurgents valiantly repelled government troops equipped with new American weapons.

Residents of Beirut and many other cities continue to persist in strikes and market boycotts. The supply of food and other daily necessities in Beirut has become difficult.

Damascus newspapers published accounts from eyewitnesses arriving from Lebanon. They said that several American planes landed at Beirut airport. American officers and soldiers arrived in Lebanon on these planes.

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: TASS, Cairo, 20th: According to newspaper reports, a contingent of Jordanian troops is heading from the Jordanian capital Amman to Beirut to suppress the Lebanese insurgents.

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The U.S. and British governments are engaged in frequent consultations, plotting intervention in Lebanon. According to a report by the Washington correspondent of the London "Daily Telegraph and Morning Post," the United States and Britain have reached an agreement to take joint action in Lebanon, including deploying troops, "if certain circumstances arise."

### 黎巴嫩起义人民英勇奋战  美英加紧策划联合干涉黎巴嫩  约旦军队开往贝鲁特充当帮凶

1958-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩起义人民英勇奋战
    美英加紧策划联合干涉黎巴嫩  约旦军队开往贝鲁特充当帮凶
    据新华社大马士革21日电　黎巴嫩起义人民同政府控制的武装力量20日在好几个地区进行了激烈的战斗。起义者奋勇击退了用新式美国武器装备起来的政府部队。
    贝鲁特和其他许多城市的居民继续坚持罢工、罢市。贝鲁特的粮食和其他日用品的供应已经发生困难。
    大马士革报纸发表了从黎巴嫩来的目击者的谈话。他们说，有数架美国飞机在贝鲁特机场着陆。有美国的军官和士兵乘这些飞机到达黎巴嫩。
    据新华社21日讯　美英两国政府正在进行频繁的商谈，策划干涉黎巴嫩的行动。据伦敦“每日电讯与晨邮报”驻华盛顿记者报道，美国和英国已经达成协议，将对黎巴嫩采取联合行动，其中包括出动军队，“如果发生某种情况的话”。
    据新华社21日讯　塔斯社开罗20日讯：据报纸消息，有一批约旦军队正在从约旦首都安曼开往贝鲁特去镇压黎巴嫩起义人民。
