Title: Jinping Phosphate Beneficiation Plant Completed
Author: Cui Guangyi
Date: 1958-05-14
Page: 3

Construction Blossoms

    Jinping Phosphate Beneficiation Plant Completed
    China's first modern phosphate beneficiation plant—the Jinping Phosphate Mine Beneficiation Plant in Lianyungang City, Jiangsu Province, was completed two months ahead of schedule in April. The plant has four main workshops with an annual output of 300,000 tons of concentrate. This year, the trial production will be 50,000 tons of concentrate. (Cui Guangyi)

### 锦屏建成选矿厂
崔广义
1958-05-14
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

　　锦屏建成选矿厂
    我国第一座现代化的磷矿选矿厂——江苏省新海连市锦屏磷矿选矿厂，已经提前两个月在4月份建成了。这个厂共分四个主要车间年产精矿三十万吨，今年试产精矿五万吨。　（崔广义）
