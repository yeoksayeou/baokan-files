Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams of Congratulations on Afghanistan's National Day; Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Sends Telegram of Congratulations to Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister

Author:

Date: 1958-05-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th: Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram on May 25th to His Majesty Mohammed Zahir Shah, King of Afghanistan, congratulating him on the 40th anniversary of the independence of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Majesty Mohammed Zahir Shah, King of Afghanistan:

    On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the independence of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the Afghan people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Kingdom of Afghanistan prosperity and strength, and the Afghan people happiness and prosperity.
                                            Mao Zedong
                                            Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                                         May 25, 1958

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram on May 25th to His Royal Highness Sardar Mohammed Daoud Khan, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, congratulating him on the 40th National Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Royal Highness Sardar Mohammed Daoud Khan, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan:

    On the occasion of the 40th National Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Royal Highness and the Government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

    I am pleased to see that with the increasing economic and cultural contacts between China and Afghanistan, especially through the mutual visits of leaders of the two countries, the mutual understanding and friendly relations between our two countries have been enhanced. I wish Your Royal Highness and the Afghan people new achievements in the cause of national construction. I wish the traditional friendship between China and Afghanistan to develop further on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.
                                       Zhou Enlai
                                       Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                                     May 25, 1958

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th: Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council, sent a telegram on May 25th to His Royal Highness Sardar Mohammed Naim Khan, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, congratulating him on the 40th National Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Royal Highness Sardar Mohammed Naim Khan, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan:

    On the occasion of the 40th National Day of your country, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Royal Highness. I wish your country greater contributions to the cause of promoting friendship and unity among Asian and African countries and safeguarding world peace.
                                            Chen Yi
    Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                                          May 25, 1958

### 毛主席周总理电贺阿富汗王国国庆  陈毅副总理兼外长致电阿副首相兼外交大臣祝贺

1958-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    毛主席周总理电贺阿富汗王国国庆
    陈毅副总理兼外长致电阿副首相兼外交大臣祝贺
    新华社26日讯　毛泽东主席25日致电阿富汗王国国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿，祝贺阿富汗王国独立四十周年。电文如下：阿富汗王国国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿陛下：
    欣逢阿富汗王国独立四十周年纪念之际，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向陛下和阿富汗人民表示诚挚的祝贺。祝阿富汗王国繁荣富强，阿富汗人民幸福兴旺。
                          中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                              1958年5月25日
    新华社26日讯　周恩来总理25日致电阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德汗，祝贺阿富汗王国四十周年国庆。电文如下：阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德汗殿下：
    欣逢阿富汗王国四十周年国庆之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并且以我个人的名义向殿下和阿富汗王国政府表示热诚的祝贺。
    我高兴地看到，随着中阿两国在经济和文化方面日益增多的接触，特别是通过两国领导人员的相互访问，我们两国间的相互了解和友好关系有了增进。祝殿下和阿富汗人民在国家建设事业中获得新的成就。祝中阿两国间的传统友谊在和平共处五项原则的基础上获得更进一步的发展。
                        中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                            1958年5月25日
    新华社26日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅25日致电阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗，祝贺阿富汗王国四十周年国庆。电文如下：阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗殿下：
    值此贵国四十周年国庆之际，我谨向殿下表示热烈的祝贺。祝贵国在促进亚非各国友好团结和维护世界和平的事业中作出更大的贡献。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部部长　陈　毅
                                              1958年5月25日
