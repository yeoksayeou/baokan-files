Title: Nanping Paper Mill Put Into Production
Author: Chen Fupei
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 3

Construction Flowers

    Nanping Paper Mill Put Into Production

    (Our report) The Nanping Paper Mill in Fujian Province, with a daily output of 100 tons of newsprint, was put into production on May 1.

    This paper mill is one of the three major newsprint mills in the country. After the completion of the second phase of the project, the daily output will reach 300 tons. (Chen Fupei) (Attached picture)

### 南平造纸厂投入生产
陈福培
1958-05-21
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    南平造纸厂投入生产
    本报讯　日产百吨新闻纸的福建省南平造纸厂，已在5月1日投入生产。
    这个纸厂是目前全国三大新闻纸厂之一。第二期工程建成后，日产量可达三百吨。　　（陈福培）（附图片）
