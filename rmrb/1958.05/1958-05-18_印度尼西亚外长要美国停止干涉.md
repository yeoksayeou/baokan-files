Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Demands the United States Stop Interfering
Author:
Date: 1958-05-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 17th: Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio summoned the United States Ambassador Howard P. Jones again this morning for a meeting lasting over an hour.

Subandrio told reporters afterwards that the content of his meeting with Jones was still about foreign interference in Indonesia. He said he had made it clear to Jones that he hoped the United States would use its influence to quickly stop foreign interference in Indonesia. The Indonesian government cannot wait indefinitely. He also told Jones, "Given the strong reaction of the Indonesian people against foreign interference in Indonesia's internal affairs, the government will find it difficult to control."

### 印度尼西亚外长要美国停止干涉

1958-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚外长要美国停止干涉
    新华社雅加达17日电　印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约今天早晨再次召见美国大使霍华德·琼斯，同他进行了一个多小时的会谈。
    苏班德里约事后对报界说，他同琼斯会谈的内容仍然是关于外国干涉印度尼西亚的问题。他说，他曾经明白地向琼斯表示，他希望美国运用它的影响，迅速停止外国对印度尼西亚的干涉。印度尼西亚政府不能无限期地等待下去。他还对琼斯说，“鉴于印度尼西亚人民反对外国干涉印度尼西亚内部事务的反应已经这样强烈，政府将难于加以控制。”
