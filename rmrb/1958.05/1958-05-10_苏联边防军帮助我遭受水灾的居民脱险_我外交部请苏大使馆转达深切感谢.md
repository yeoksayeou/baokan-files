Title: Soviet Border Guards Help Residents Escape Flood; Our Foreign Ministry Asks Soviet Embassy to Convey Deep Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 9th. On May 2nd of this year, when Emuer Township, Huma County, Heilongjiang Province, China, suffered a flood due to ice jams blocking the river channel during the spring thaw in the upper reaches of the Heilong River, Soviet border guards, under the personal command of Ivanov, the Second Secretary of the Amur Oblast Committee of the CPSU, rescued all the disaster-stricken people of the township from danger. The Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, therefore, today sent a note to the Soviet Embassy in China, asking it to convey its deep gratitude to the Party, government, border troops, and residents of the Amur Oblast of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联边防军帮助我遭受水灾的居民脱险  我外交部请苏大使馆转达深切感谢

1958-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联边防军帮助我遭受水灾的居民脱险
    我外交部请苏大使馆转达深切感谢
    新华社9日讯　今年5月2日当我国黑龙江省呼玛县额木尔乡因为黑龙江上游解冻时冰排堵塞河道而发生水灾时，苏联边防军在苏共阿穆尔州委第二书记依万诺夫亲自指挥下，将全乡灾民抢救脱离险境。我国外交部为此在今天照会苏联驻华大使馆，请其转达对苏联阿穆尔州党、政、边防部队和居民的深切感谢。
