Title: Soviet Barter Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. A Soviet barter delegation of three, headed by Comrade Golchak, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Cultural Goods of the Soviet Ministry of Commerce, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 26th. Specific discussions on barter business between China and the Soviet Union in 1958 will be held in Beijing. On the morning of the 27th, Wu Xuezhi, Deputy Minister of the First Ministry of Commerce, met with all members of the Soviet barter delegation and exchanged views on the method of this barter work and the expansion of related barter business.

### 苏联易货代表团到北京

1958-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联易货代表团到北京
    新华社28日讯　以苏联商业部文化用品总管理局副局长高尔察克同志为首的苏联易货代表团一行三人，于26日下午乘飞机到达北京。1958年中、苏两国易货业务即将在北京进行具体商谈。27日上午第一商业部副部长吴雪之接见了苏联易货代表团全体人员，双方就此次易货工作进行的方式以及有关易货业务的扩展等问题，交换了意见。
