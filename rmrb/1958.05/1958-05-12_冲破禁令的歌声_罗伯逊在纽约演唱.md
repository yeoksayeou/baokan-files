Title: Songs Breaking Through the Ban: Robeson Performs in New York
Author:
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, May 10th: World-renowned black singer Paul Robeson gave a concert at Carnegie Hall in New York last night, Robeson's first performance in New York in ten years. The audience warmly welcomed this gifted bass-baritone singer. Robeson sang many black songs and songs from other countries. He also sang Chinese folk songs in Chinese. The concert was very successful.

### 冲破禁令的歌声  罗伯逊在纽约演唱

1958-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    冲破禁令的歌声
    罗伯逊在纽约演唱
    据新华社10日讯　纽约消息：世界闻名的黑人歌唱家保罗·罗伯逊9日晚上在纽约卡内基音乐厅举行音乐会，这是罗伯逊十年来第一次在纽约演出。听众热烈欢迎这位天才的男低声歌唱家。罗伯逊演唱了许多黑人歌曲及其他国家的歌曲。他还用中文演唱了中国的民间歌曲。音乐会进行得非常成功。
