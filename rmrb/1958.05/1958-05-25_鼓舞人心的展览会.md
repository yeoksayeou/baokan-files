    Inspiring Exhibition
    Today, the "Baoji-Chengdu Railway, Yingtan-Xiamen Railway, Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Exhibition" sponsored by the Ministry of Railways opened at the Beijing Natural History Museum in Tianqiao. In addition to seeing some inspiring exhibits, visitors can also watch documentary films about these projects for free.

### 鼓舞人心的展览会

1958-05-25
第4版()
专栏：看看听听玩玩

    鼓舞人心的展览会
    今天，在天桥北京自然博物馆里，铁道部主办的“宝成铁路，鹰厦铁路，武汉长江大桥工程展览会”开幕了。观众除了可以看到一些鼓舞人心的展品以外，还可以免费看到有关这些工程的纪录影片。
