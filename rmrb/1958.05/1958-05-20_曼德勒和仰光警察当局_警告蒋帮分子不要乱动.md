Title: Mandalay and Yangon Police Authorities Warn Chiang Gang Elements Not to Act Rashly
Author:
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 19th - According to the "Burma New Light of Burma" report on May 17th, Mandalay Police Chief Wu Ba Dan warned local Chiang Kai-shek group elements that if they continue to distribute leaflets supporting the Taiwan Chiang Kai-shek group, they will be subject to legal sanctions.

　　Wu Ba Dan summoned 25 ringleaders of the Chiang Kai-shek group in Mandalay on May 15 to talk about this. He also ordered them to stop teaching "political classes" in the schools they controlled.

　　It is reported that Yangon Police Chief Wu Qin Mao Mao had issued a warning to local Chiang Kai-shek group elements on May 7, ordering them to stop contacting the Kuomintang remnant troops on the Burmese border.

### 曼德勒和仰光警察当局  警告蒋帮分子不要乱动

1958-05-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　曼德勒和仰光警察当局
　　警告蒋帮分子不要乱动
    新华社仰光19日电　据“缅甸新光报”5月17日报道，曼德勒警察局长吴巴丹警告当地的蒋介石集团分子说，如果他们继续散发关于支持台湾蒋介石集团的传单，他们将受到法律的制裁。
    吴巴丹是5月15日把曼德勒的蒋介石集团分子25名首脑召去谈这番话的。他还勒令他们停止在他们控制的学校里教“政治课”。
    据报道，仰光警察厅长吴钦貌貌曾经在5月7日对当地的蒋介石集团分子发出警告，要他们停止同缅甸边境上国民党残余军队的联系。
