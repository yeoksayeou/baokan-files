Publishing News

    "Technical Exchange Reference Materials"
    In order to better disseminate and exchange advanced experiences, the Shanghai Industrial Production Exhibition for Comparing Advanced Techniques, Learning from the Advanced, Achieving More, Faster, Better, and More Economically has decided to compile "Technical Exchange Reference Materials" to be published in loose-leaf selected form. This set of materials will include approximately three hundred types, covering heavy industry, the chemical industry, light industry, and the textile industry, and is currently being published successively by the Science and Technology Publishing House. (Qian)

### “技术交流参考资料”

1958-05-28
第7版()
专栏：出版消息

    “技术交流参考资料”
    上海市工业生产比先进比多快好省展览会为了更好地传播和交流先进经验，决定编辑“技术交流参考资料”，以活叶文选形式出版。这套资料约有三百种，包括重工业、化学工业、轻工业及纺织工业等方面，目前正由科学技术出版社陆续出版。　（乾）
