Title: IJA Conference Issues Call: Journalism Serves Peace
Author:
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency Report: The Fourth Congress of the International Organization of Journalists, held in Bucharest, closed on the morning of the 18th. The meeting elected a new leadership, and adopted five resolutions on peace, on the work of the International Organization of Journalists, etc. It also passed a letter expressing support for journalists working in Algeria, South Korea, South Vietnam, and other persecuted environments.

In the resolution on peace, the delegates called on honest journalists to shout "No War." The meeting elected Hermann (France) as the President of the International Organization of Journalists, De. Kraminov (Soviet Union), Deng Tuo (China), Hoffman (Poland), Leduc (Mexico), Mauri Ryume (Finland) as Vice Presidents, and elected Knobloch (Czechoslovakia) as Secretary-General, and reserved a vice-presidential position for an African country.

In the two days before the closing of the Congress (16th and 18th), the meeting discussed the reports heard at the meeting on the first day. Jin Zhonghua, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, spoke at the meeting on the 17th about the current situation of the socialist Great Leap Forward in China and the current tasks of Chinese journalists.

### 国际记协会议发出呼吁  新闻工作为和平服务

1958-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    国际记协会议发出呼吁
　　新闻工作为和平服务
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：在布加勒斯特举行的国际新闻工作者协会第四次代表大会18日上午闭幕。会议选举了新的领导机构，通过了关于和平、关于国际新闻工作者协会的工作等五个决议，还通过了一封对在阿尔及利亚、南朝鲜、南越及其它在受迫害的环境中工作的记者表示支持的信。
    在关于和平的决议中，代表们呼吁正直的新闻工作者高呼“不要战争”。会议选举赫尔曼（法国）为国际新闻工作者协会的主席，德·克拉米诺夫（苏联）、邓拓（中国）、霍夫曼（波兰）、勒杜克（墨西哥）、毛里·吕厄梅（芬兰）为副主席，选举克诺布洛赫（捷克斯洛伐克）为秘书长，并保留一副主席位置由非洲国家担任。
    在代表大会闭幕的前两天（16日、18日），会议曾就第一天会议上听取的各项报告进行了讨论。中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长金仲华在17日的会议上发言时谈到了目前中国的社会主义大跃进的情况和中国新闻工作者的当前任务。
