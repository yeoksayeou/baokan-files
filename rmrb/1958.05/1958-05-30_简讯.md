Title: Brief News

Author:
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 6

Brief News
    △ The Chinese Women's Delegation led by Zhang Yun arrived in Vienna on the 28th. The delegation is attending the Fourth Congress of the Women's International Democratic Federation, which opens on June 1st.
    △ The Chinese Opera and Dance Troupe, led by Wu Han, left Lyon on the afternoon of the 27th and traveled by land to Marseille. The troupe arrived in Marseille that evening and is preparing for its first performance in Marseille on the 29th.

### 简讯

1958-05-30
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    △由章蕴率领的中国妇女代表团28日到达维也纳。代表团是来参加6月1日开幕的国际民主妇联第四届代表大会的。
    △由吴晗率领的中国戏曲歌舞团于27日下午离开里昂由陆路前往马赛。歌舞团当晚到达马赛，准备在29日进行在马赛的第一次演出。
