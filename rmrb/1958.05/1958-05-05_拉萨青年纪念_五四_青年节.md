Title: Lhasa Youth Commemorate "May Fourth" Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1958-05-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 4th -- Over 1,200 Tibetan and Han Chinese Communist Youth League members and youths in Lhasa gathered today to commemorate "May Fourth" Youth Day.

Xia Zha Gaden Bianjue, Chairman of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Federation, introduced the course of the "May Fourth" Movement at the meeting and called on young people to carry forward the "May Fourth" revolutionary spirit and dedicate all their strength to building a new Tibet.

Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, attended the meeting on behalf of the Tibet Working Committee to congratulate the young people.

### 拉萨青年纪念“五四”青年节

1958-05-05
第2版()
专栏：

    拉萨青年纪念“五四”青年节
    新华社拉萨4日电　拉萨市藏、汉族共青团员和青年一千二百多人，今天集会纪念“五四”青年节。
    拉萨市爱国青年联谊会主任委员夏扎·噶登班觉在会上介绍了“五四”运动的经过，号召青年们发扬“五四”革命精神，献出自己的一切力量，建设新西藏。
    中共西藏工委副书记周仁山代表西藏工委到会向青年们祝贺。
