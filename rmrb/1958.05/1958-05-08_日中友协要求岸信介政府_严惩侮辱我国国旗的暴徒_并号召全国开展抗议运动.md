Title: China-Japan Friendship Association Demands the Kishi Nobusuke Government Severely Punish Thugs Who Insulted the Chinese National Flag, and Calls on the Whole Country to Launch a Protest Movement
Author:
Date: 1958-05-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 7th: The Japan-China Friendship Association held an emergency meeting of its standing board of directors today, and passed a resolution demanding that the Kishi Nobusuke government, in accordance with Article 92 of the Japanese Criminal Law, severely punish the two thugs who insulted the Chinese national flag in Nagasaki, and investigate the background of this incident to thoroughly pursue responsibility. The resolution also demanded that the Japanese government resolutely stop the Taiwan Chiang Kai-shek clique from any interference in Japan's internal affairs, in order to guarantee the dignity and safety of the Chinese national flag.

The Japan-China Friendship Association will send representatives to the Japanese government tomorrow to submit this request.

The meeting also decided that the Japan-China Friendship Association will notify its branches in various regions to launch a nationwide protest movement, and call on relevant groups to jointly hold various protest rallies throughout the country.

### 日中友协要求岸信介政府  严惩侮辱我国国旗的暴徒  并号召全国开展抗议运动

1958-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    日中友协要求岸信介政府
    严惩侮辱我国国旗的暴徒
    并号召全国开展抗议运动
    据新华社7日讯　东京消息：日中友好协会今天召开常任理事会紧急会议，通过了一项决议，要求岸信介政府根据日本刑法第九十二条的规定，严厉处罚在长崎发生的侮辱中国国旗的两个暴徒，并且查明这个事件的幕后关系，彻底追究责任。决议还要求日本政府坚决制止台湾蒋介石集团对日本内政的任何干涉，以保障中国国旗的尊严和安全。
    日中友好协会将在明天派遣代表把这项要求送交日本政府。
    会议还决定，日中友好协会将通知各地的分会展开全国规模的抗议运动，同时呼吁有关团体在全国各地联合举行各种抗议集会。
