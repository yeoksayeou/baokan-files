Title: Indonesian Minister of Information States Government Troops Will Continue to Quell Rebellion, Air Force News Agency Says Government Air Force Has Gained Superiority
Author:
Date: 1958-05-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 22nd: Indonesian Minister of Information Sudibjo stated on the evening of the 21st that the Indonesian government will continue to sweep away the rebels in eastern Indonesia. He said that the government's military operations against the rebel groups in eastern Indonesia are proceeding well according to plan. The government will continue to do its best to quell the rebellion there in order to liberate the areas controlled by the "Charter of Overall Struggle" rebel group in a short period of time.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 22nd: The Indonesian Air Force News Agency issued a statement on the evening of the 21st stating that the government air force destroyed eight rebel aircraft during the air raids on the airports of Mepanget, Tasuka, Jirolo, and Morotai on the 15th. The Air Force News Agency said that this shows that the government air force has gained superiority.

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: Manila dispatch: The Indonesian Embassy in the Philippines issued a statement on the 17th, denouncing the lie of Philippine Defense Minister Vargas that Indonesia accepted Soviet military assistance. The statement believes that Vargas insists on this lie in order to achieve the purpose of harming the relationship between Indonesia and the Philippines, these two neighboring countries.

### 印度尼西亚情报部长声明  政府军将继续平定叛乱  空军新闻处说政府空军已占优势

1958-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚情报部长声明
    政府军将继续平定叛乱
    空军新闻处说政府空军已占优势
    新华社雅加达22日电　印度尼西亚情报部长苏迪比约21日晚上表明，印度尼西亚政府将继续扫荡东印度尼西亚方面的叛军。他说，政府对东印度尼西亚的叛乱集团采取的军事行动按照计划进行得很好。政府将继续尽力平定那里的叛乱，以便短期内解放“全面斗争约章”叛乱集团所控制的地区。
    新华社雅加达22日电　印度尼西亚空军新闻处21日晚上发表声明说，政府空军在15日空袭梅彭格特、塔苏卡、济罗罗和摩罗泰等机场的时候，炸毁了叛军飞机八架。空军新闻处说，这表明政府空军已经占优势。
    据新华社22日讯　马尼拉消息：印度尼西亚驻菲律宾大使馆在17日发表声明，斥责菲律宾国防部长瓦加斯诬指印度尼西亚接受苏联军事援助的谎言。声明认为，瓦加斯坚持这种谎言，是为了要达到损害印度尼西亚和菲律宾这两个近邻国家的关系的目的。
