Title: Head of the North Korean Consulate in Changchun Holds Banquet for Visit of North Korean Delegation to Changchun
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-16
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, May 15th: Li Deji, the head of the Consulate of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Changchun, held a banquet this evening for the visit of the North Korean People's Delegation to China to Changchun.

    Xu Shou-xuan, Deputy Governor of Jilin Province, Zhao Tianye, Deputy Secretary-General of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Major General Zhong Mingbiao, Deputy Commander of the Jilin Military Region, Shen Yagang, Secretary of the Changchun Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Li Chengkun, Deputy Mayor of Changchun, were invited to attend the banquet. Leading party and government officials from cities and counties in Jilin Province where there are North Korean orphanages, as well as some individuals who have been awarded medals and decorations, were also invited to attend the banquet.

    Wang Yifu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, also attended the banquet.

    After Consul Li Deji delivered a welcoming speech, delegation leader Xu Zhenshu spoke. She once again expressed her deep gratitude to the Chinese people for supporting the Korean people's resistance against aggression and for assisting in the upbringing of Korean war orphans. Xu Shou-xuan, Deputy Governor of Jilin Province, also spoke at the banquet.

### 朝鲜驻长春领事馆负责人  为朝鲜代表团访问长春举行宴会

1958-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻长春领事馆负责人
　　为朝鲜代表团访问长春举行宴会
    新华社长春15日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻长春领事馆负责人李德基今天晚上为朝鲜人民访华代表团来长春访问举行宴会。
    吉林省副省长徐寿轩，中共吉林省委副秘书长赵天野，吉林军区副司令员锺明彪少将，中共长春市委书记沈亚纲、长春市副市长李承锟等应邀出席了宴会。吉林省设有朝鲜孤儿院的地方的市、县党、政领导人以及部分荣获勋章、奖章的人员也应邀出席宴会。
    内务部副部长王一夫也出席了宴会。
    在领事馆负责人李德基致欢迎词之后，代表团团长许贞琡讲了话。她再一次对中国人民支援朝鲜人民反抗侵略并协助抚育朝鲜战灾孤儿表示深切的感谢。吉林省副省长徐寿轩也在宴会上讲了话。
