Title: Comparing Mechanical Energy
Author:
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 5

　　Comparing Mechanical Energy
　　First Red Star　100
　　Third Red Star　1671
　　“Explorer”　18.2
　　“Vanguard”　2.1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: TASS reports: If the mechanical energy of the Soviet Union's first artificial satellite is set at 100, then the mechanical energy of the Soviet Union's third artificial satellite is equal to 1,671, while the total mechanical energy of the American artificial satellites "Explorer 1" and "Explorer 3" is only 18.2, and "Vanguard" is only 2.1. This was said by Soviet interstellar transportation expert Sternfeld. He said that the energy of the Soviet Union's first satellite is equivalent to the energy possessed by ten trains weighing 1,150 tons and traveling at a speed of 80 kilometers per hour. The mechanical energy of the Soviet Union's second artificial satellite has already reached 6.33 times that of the first.

### 比比机械能

1958-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　比比机械能
    第一个红星　100
    第三个红星　1671
    “探险者”　18.2
    “先锋号”　2.1
    据新华社19日讯　据塔斯社讯：如果把苏联第一个人造卫星的机械能定为一百，那末苏联第三个人造卫星的机械能就等于一千六百七十一，而美国人造卫星“探险者一号”和“探险者三号”的全部机械能只是十八点二，“先锋号”才只有二点一。这是苏联星际交通专家斯特恩菲尔德说的。他说，苏联第一个卫星的能量等于十列重一千一百五十吨和行速每小时八十公里的火车所具有的能量。苏联第二个人造卫星的机械能就已经达到了它的六点三三倍。
