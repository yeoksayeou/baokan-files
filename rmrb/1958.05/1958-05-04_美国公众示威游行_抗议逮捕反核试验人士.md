Title: US Public Demonstrations Protest Arrests of Anti-Nuclear Testing Activists
Author:
Date: 1958-05-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd, Washington dispatch: The American "Non-violent Action Against Nuclear Weapons" organization held a demonstration in front of the Atomic Energy Commission on the 2nd to protest the authorities' unreasonable arrest of four peace activists who went to Eniwetok Atoll to protest American nuclear weapons testing.

### 美国公众示威游行  抗议逮捕反核试验人士

1958-05-04
第3版()
专栏：

    美国公众示威游行
    抗议逮捕反核试验人士
    据新华社3日讯　华盛顿消息：美国“非暴力反对核武器和平组织”2日在原子能委员会门前组织示威游行，抗议当局无理逮捕四个前往恩纳威托克岛抗议美国核武器试验的和平人士。
