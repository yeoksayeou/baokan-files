Title: Japanese Tokyo Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 29th – The Japanese Tokyo Delegation, headed by Yamazaki Shichijiro, consisting of twenty-four members, arrived in Beijing today. Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of Beijing, and heads of major mass organizations in Beijing went to the airport to welcome them.

　　This delegation is visiting China at the invitation of Peng Zhen, the Mayor of Beijing.

### 日本东京都各界访华代表团到京

1958-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    日本东京都各界访华代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　以山崎七次郎为首的日本东京都各界访华代表团一行二十四人今天到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有北京市副市长张友渔和北京市各主要群众团体的负责人。
    这个代表团是应北京市市长彭真的邀请来我国访问的。
