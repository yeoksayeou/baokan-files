Title: Japanese Organizations to Jointly Hold National Convention Demanding the Kishi Government Resolve the Incident of Insulting Our National Flag
Author:
Date: 1958-05-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th: Seventeen Japanese trade, friendship, peace, labor union, and women's organizations will jointly hold a national convention in Tokyo on May 17th to demand that the Japanese government resolve the incident of thugs insulting the Chinese national flag in Nagasaki, implement the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement, and address fishery issues between Japan and China. The organizations participating in the national convention will include the "Manufacturers' Council for Full Implementation of the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement," the "National Movement Implementation Committee for Full Implementation of the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement," the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, the Japan-China Import and Export Association, the Japan-China Friendship Association, the National Council for the Normalization of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), the Japan-China Fisheries Council, the Federation of Japanese Women's Organizations, and others.

The preparatory committee for the national convention also plans to distribute 200,000 leaflets throughout the country, urging various organizations and the Japanese people in various regions to actively assist the national convention.

### 要岸政府解决侮辱我国旗事件  日本各团体将联合举行国民大会

1958-05-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　要岸政府解决侮辱我国旗事件
    日本各团体将联合举行国民大会
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：日本十七个贸易、友好、和平、工会和妇女团体将在5月17日在东京联合举行国民大会，要求日本政府解决在长崎发生的暴徒侮辱中国国旗事件、实施第四次日中贸易协定以及日中两国的渔业问题。参加国民大会的团体将有，“完全实施第四次日中贸易协定厂商协议会”、“完全实施第四次日中贸易协定国民运动实行委员会”、日本国际贸易促进协会、日中输出入协会、日中友好协会、日本恢复日中邦交国民会议、日本工会总评议会、日中渔业协议会、全日本妇女团体联合会等。
    国民大会筹备委员会还准备向全国各地分发二十万张传单，吁请各团体和各地日本人民对国民大会给予积极协助。
