Title: Protesting Kishida Nobusuke's Tolerance of Thugs Insulting Our National Flag, Nagasaki Citizens Rally and Demonstrate
Author:
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 5

　　Protesting Kishida Nobusuke's Tolerance of Thugs Insulting Our National Flag
　　Nagasaki Citizens Rally and Demonstrate
    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th. According to TASS Tokyo, May 14th: Nagasaki citizens held a mass rally to protest the Kishida Nobusuke government's tolerance of thugs insulting the national flag of the People's Republic of China.
    The mass rally was organized by the Nagasaki branch of the Japan-China Friendship Association. Representatives from neighboring prefectures of Nagasaki also participated in the rally.
    The assembly passed a resolution demanding that the Kishida Nobusuke government take measures to resolve the incident of thugs tearing down the Chinese national flag.
    After the mass rally, the participants, holding the Japanese and Chinese national flags, marched towards the Chiang Kai-shek clique's "Consulate" in Nagasaki City. According to the "Akahata" newspaper, when the demonstrators passed in front of the "Consulate," they shouted: "Chiang Kai-shek elements get out of Japan!"

### 抗议岸信介纵容暴徒侮辱我国国旗  长崎市民集会示威

1958-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    抗议岸信介纵容暴徒侮辱我国国旗
　　长崎市民集会示威
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社东京14日讯：长崎市民举行了群众大会，抗议岸信介政府纵容暴徒侮辱中华人民共和国国旗。
    这次群众大会是由日中友好协会长崎分会主办的，长崎邻近各县的代表也参加了群众大会。
    大会通过一项决议，要求岸信介政府采取措施解决暴徒扯下中国国旗的事件。
    群众大会结束后，到会者举着日本国旗和中华人民共和国国旗前往蒋介石集团驻长崎市“领事馆”。据“赤旗报”报道，示威游行者在经过“领事馆”门前时高呼：“蒋介石分子滚出日本！”
