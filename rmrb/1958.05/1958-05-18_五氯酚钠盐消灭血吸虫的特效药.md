Sodium Pentachlorophenate: A Special Medicine for Eliminating Schistosomiasis

A highly effective medicine for eliminating schistosomiasis—sodium pentachlorophenate—has recently been successfully trial-produced at the Shenyang Chemical Plant. It is a grayish-white powder. According to tests by relevant departments, spraying a solution of it in water on areas where schistosomiasis grows can kill more than 90% of the schistosomiasis within twenty-eight hours. This medicine has a higher kill rate than "Benarsen" and other medicines used in large quantities in the past in our country to kill schistosomiasis.

This medicine has begun mass production at the Shenyang Chemical Plant in May, and it is estimated that 25 tons will be supplied to various regions before the end of June.

### 五氯酚钠盐消灭血吸虫的特效药

1958-05-18
第2版()
专栏：新产品

    五氯酚钠盐消灭血吸虫的特效药
    一种消灭血吸虫效用很高的药品——五氯酚钠盐最近在沈阳化工厂试制成功，它是一种灰白色的粉末。经有关部门试验，用水把它溶解喷射到生长血吸虫的地方，二十八小时内就能杀死90％以上的血吸虫。这种药品比我国过去大量使用的杀血吸虫的“苯砷”等药品，有更高的杀死率。
    这种药品5月份已开始在沈阳化工厂大量生产，预计6月底以前就有二十五吨供给各地。
                      （新华社）
