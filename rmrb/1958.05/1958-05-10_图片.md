    From May 1st to 3rd, the China-Vietnam Youth Solidarity and Friendship Meeting was held in Hekou, China and Lao Cai, Vietnam.
    During the three days, youths of various ethnic groups from the two countries had extensive and close contact; and held cultural performances and sports competitions, and exchanged experiences in industrial and agricultural production.
    Right picture: Young women of ethnic minorities from the two countries gather together happily.
    Bottom picture: During the get-together in Lao Cai, Vietnam, young people from the two countries gave wonderful cultural performances.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Chuanguo photographed

专栏：

    5月1日至3日，在中国河口和越南老街举行了中国云南省越南老街省青年团结联欢大会。
    三天里，两国各民族青年进行了广泛的亲密的接触；并举行了文艺会演和体育比赛，交流工农业生产经验。
