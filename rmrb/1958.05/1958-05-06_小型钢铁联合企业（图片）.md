Author:
Date: 1958-05-06

    Small-Scale Iron and Steel Complex
    This is an integrated iron and steel enterprise with an annual output of 30,000 tons of steel, including ironmaking, steelmaking, and rolling workshops, as well as corresponding public facilities. The total investment is about 2.3 million yuan, suitable for operation by special districts. If the local power supply is insufficient, steam engines or locomobiles can be used for power.

### 小型钢铁联合企业（图片）

1958-05-06
第3版()
专栏：

    小型钢铁联合企业
    这是一个年产三万吨钢的联合企业，包括有炼铁、炼钢、轧钢等车间和相应的公用设施，总投资约二百三十万元，适合于专区举办。如当地电源不足，可以采用蒸汽机或锅驼机传动。
