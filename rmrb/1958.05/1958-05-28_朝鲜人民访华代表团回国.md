Title: Korean People's Delegation Returns Home After Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1958-05-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, May 27th. The Korean People's Delegation, which arrived in our country on May 3rd for a friendly visit, has concluded its tour and visit activities in China. It left Shenyang by train at 2:25 this morning via Andong to return home.

Zhang Panxin, Secretary-General and Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Liaoning Provincial People's Committee, and Kim Yong-bin, Korean Consul General in Shenyang, accompanied the delegation to Andong by the same train.

The distinguished guests were warmly seen off at the station by Zhang Xuexuan, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province, Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, responsible persons from the provincial and municipal Party, government, military, and people's organizations, and 600 people from all walks of life.

Wang Yifu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, heads of the Korean Consulate General in Shenyang and the Korean Children's Education Office, and heads of Korean Children's Academies in the three northeastern provinces, also came to the station to see them off.

A warm send-off ceremony was held at the station. Zhang Xuexuan delivered a farewell speech, expressing his heartfelt gratitude to the delegation for bringing the profound friendship of the Korean people to the Chinese people, and wished the delegation a safe journey. In her farewell speech, delegation leader Xu Zhenshook repeatedly stated that upon returning home, she would certainly convey the deep friendship of the Chinese people towards the Korean people and the achievements of China's peaceful construction to the Korean people. Finally, she shouted in Chinese: "Dear comrades, I wish you happiness, goodbye!"

At 7:42 AM, the distinguished guests arrived in Andong, where they were warmly welcomed by Duan Yongjie, Secretary of the Andong Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Mayor of Andong, and Major General He Jixiang, the leading officer of the local garrison, and many others. Subsequently, Duan Yongjie held a luncheon to see off the distinguished guests.

At 9:20 AM, the distinguished guests left Andong by train to return to Korea.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团回国

1958-05-28
第3版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团回国
    新华社沈阳27日电　5月3日来到我国进行友好访问的朝鲜人民访华代表团，已结束在中国的参观访问活动，今天上午二点二十五分乘车离开沈阳经安东回国。
    辽宁省人民委员会秘书长兼外事处处长张盘新和朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬，同车陪送到安东。
    贵宾们在车站上受到辽宁省副省长张雪轩、沈阳市市长刘宝田和省市党、政、军、人民团体负责人以及各界人民六百人的热情欢送。
    内务部副部长王一夫、朝鲜驻沈阳总领事馆和朝鲜儿童教育处的负责人以及在东北三省的朝鲜儿童学院的负责人，也到车站欢送。
    在车站上举行了热烈的欢送仪式。张雪轩致欢送词，衷心地感谢代表团给中国人民带来的朝鲜人民的深厚友谊，他祝代表团一路平安。许贞琡团长在致告别词中，一再表示回国后一定把中国人民对朝鲜人民的深情厚谊和中国和平建设的成就，转达给朝鲜人民。最后她用中国话高呼：“亲爱的同志们，祝你们幸福，再见吧！”
    七时四十二分，贵宾们到达安东时，受到中共安东市委书记、安东市市长段永杰和当地驻军首长贺吉祥少将等多人的热情欢迎。随后，段永杰举行便宴欢送贵宾。
    九时二十分，贵宾们乘车离开安东回朝鲜。
