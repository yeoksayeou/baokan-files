　　A Poem and a Painting
    Gao Mi　Poem  Yu Jiming, Luo Yongliang, Guo Zhenhua　Painting
　　Going to the countryside is just like being at home, the loving mother cares day and night.
　　Today I go to the high mountains to explore for mines, before leaving she carefully mends my winter clothes.
