Title: "New York Times" Science Commentator Points Out: Red Giant Star Opens New Stage in Studying Interstellar Space
Author:
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 5

　　"New York Times" science commentator Lawrence commented on the Soviet Union's launch of its third satellite, saying: "The Soviet Union's giant Earth satellite has opened a new stage in the study of interstellar space. The Soviet scientists' launch of the third satellite has made the day of launching interstellar rockets to the moon even closer. This event once again strikingly proves that the United States has allowed Russia to seize a huge advantage in launching rockets."

### “纽约时报”科学评论员指出  红色巨星开辟研究星际空间新阶段

1958-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    “纽约时报”科学评论员指出
    红色巨星开辟研究星际空间新阶段
    据新华社讯　“纽约时报”科学评论员劳伦斯就苏联发射第三个卫星发表评论说：“苏联巨型的地球卫星打开了研究星际空间的新阶段。苏联科学家发射第三个卫星就使发射星际火箭到月球上去的日子更加临近了。这个事件再一次惊人地证明了，美国让俄国在发射火箭方面夺取了巨大的优势”。
