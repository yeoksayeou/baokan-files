Title: A Hundred Flowers Blooming in Agricultural Machinery
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1958-05-22
Page: 2

Agricultural Machinery: A Hundred Flowers Blooming

　　Guo Moruo

　　Hundreds of millions of native experts across the country have cultivated countless exotic flowers of agricultural machinery; these exotic flowers bear rich fruits, building socialist China quickly, well, and economically. Scientists should come here to learn and take native experts as our teachers. Grafting modern science onto indigenous technology, like grafting a moonflower onto a sweet potato stem, will improve the quality of science, and catching up with the world's advanced level will be no problem.

　　Written after visiting the Agricultural Tool Exhibition on May 12, 1958

### 农业机械的百花齐放
郭沫若
1958-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

    农业机械的百花齐放
  　郭沫若全国亿万的土专家，开出了农业机械的无数奇花；这些奇花结出的丰盛的果实，多快好省地建设着社会主义的中华。科学家们应该到这里来学习，拜土专家们作我们的老师。把近代科学嫁接上土生的技术，犹如把月光花嫁接上番薯的梗子，那就会提高科学的品质，赶上世界先进水平不成问题。
    1958年5月12日参观农具展览会后题
