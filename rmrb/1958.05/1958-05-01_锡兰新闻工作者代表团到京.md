Title: Ceylonese Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 29th — The five-member delegation of Ceylonese journalists invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit China arrived in Beijing by train this morning.

The delegation arrived in Guangzhou from Hong Kong on the 25th. The members of the delegation are: S. P. Amarasingam, M. J. Tissainayagam, M. S. Jaimon, M. L. Ratra Kindra, and L. Ratnapala.

### 锡兰新闻工作者代表团到京

1958-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    锡兰新闻工作者代表团到京
    据新华社29日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来中国访问的锡兰新闻工作者代表团一行五人，今天早上乘火车到达北京。
    代表团是在25日从香港到达广州的。代表团的成员是：萨·庞·阿玛拉辛甘、玛·贾·提赛纳雅甘、莫·赛·贾蒙、默·拉·拉特拉金德拉和拉·拉特纳帕拉。
