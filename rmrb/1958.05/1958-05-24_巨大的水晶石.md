Title: Huge Quartz Crystals

Author: 
Date: 1958-05-24
Page: 3

Farmers in Shigemen Village, Fugong County, Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture, Yunnan Province, have unearthed three large quartz crystals. The largest weighs over 200 jin, while the other two weigh over 100 jin and 38 jin respectively.

Quartz is not only used as a precious gemstone for carving beautiful handicrafts and ornaments, but also as an important material for manufacturing radio oscillators, radar detectors, and precision optical instruments. Large, high-quality crystals are especially valuable. These three crystals are all very valuable; the one weighing 38 jin is worth more than 1,000 yuan.

### 巨大的水晶石

1958-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

    巨大的水晶石
    云南省怒江傈僳族自治州福贡县石格门村农民，挖到了三颗大水晶石，最大的一颗重二百多市斤，另外两颗重一百多市斤和三十八市斤。
    水晶不仅被当做贵重的宝石，用以雕刻美丽的工艺品和装饰品，而且可以作制造无线电振荡器、雷达探测器和精密光学仪器的重要材料。体大质高的水晶尤为珍贵。这三块水晶价值都很高，重三十八斤的那颗就值一千多元。
