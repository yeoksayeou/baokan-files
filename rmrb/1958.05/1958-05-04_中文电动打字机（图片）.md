Title: Chinese Electric Typewriter (Picture)
Author: Tian Ming
Date: 1958-05-04
Page: 2

New Product

    Chinese Electric Typewriter
    The "Chinese Electric Typewriter" has been successfully trial-produced at the Tianjin Red Star Typewriter Factory and put into production. This typewriter has four electric switches above the type tray for typing, horizontal tray, vertical tray, and typing symbols. The cost of this typewriter is 50% lower than ordinary Chinese typewriters, while the efficiency is increased by 30%.
        Xinhua News Agency Reporter Tian Ming Photo

### 中文电动打字机（图片）
田明
1958-05-04
第2版()
专栏：新产品

    中文电动打字机
    “中文电动打字机”在天津红星打字机厂试制成功，并投入生产。这台打字机字盘上方有打字、横盒、纵盒和打符号的四个电门。这种打字机的成本比普通的中文打字机还低50％，而效率却提高了30％。
        新华社记者　田明摄
