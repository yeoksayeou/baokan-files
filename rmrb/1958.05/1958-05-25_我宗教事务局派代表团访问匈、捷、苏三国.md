Title: Religious Affairs Bureau Sends Delegation to Visit Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-05-25
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency News:

    At the invitation of the governments of Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and the Soviet Union, the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council of our country has sent a delegation headed by Director He Chengxiang to make friendly visits to Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and the Soviet Union. The delegation departed from Beijing on the 23rd.

### 我宗教事务局派代表团访问匈、捷、苏三国

1958-05-25
第3版()
专栏：

    我宗教事务局派代表团访问匈、捷、苏三国
    新华社讯　应匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、苏联等国政府的邀请，我国国务院宗教事务局派出以局长何成湘为首的代表团，前往匈、捷、苏三国作友好访问。这个代表团已在23日由北京启程。
