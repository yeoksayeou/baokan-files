Title: Premier Zhou Receives Korean Visiting Delegation; Korean Guests Visit Minister Xie Juezai
Author:
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 1

    Zhou Enlai Receives Korean Delegation
    Korean Guests Visit Minister Xie Juezai
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th -- Premier Zhou Enlai received the Korean People's Visiting Delegation, headed by Xu Zhenshook and deputy head Kim Cheon-hae, along with all members of the delegation, on the morning of the 6th.
    Present at the meeting were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xie Juezai, Minister of Internal Affairs, Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Wang Yutian, Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Li Yongho, Korean Ambassador to China.
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th -- The Korean People's Visiting Delegation, headed by Xu Zhenshook and deputy head Kim Cheon-hae, along with all members of the delegation, paid a visit to Minister of Internal Affairs Xie Juezai this afternoon.
    During the visit, the hosts introduced the situation of our country nurturing Korean war-stricken orphans to the guests.
    Present at the visit were Wang Ziyi, Chen Qiyuan, and Wang Yifu, Vice Ministers of Internal Affairs, and Li Yongho, the Korean Ambassador to China.

### 周总理接见朝鲜访华团  朝鲜贵宾拜会谢觉哉部长

1958-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见朝鲜访华团
    朝鲜贵宾拜会谢觉哉部长
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理在6日上午接见了朝鲜人民访华代表团团长许贞琡、副团长金天海和代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，内务部部长谢觉哉，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，外交部社会主义国家司司长王雨田，以及朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。
    新华社6日讯　朝鲜人民访华代表团团长许贞琡、副团长金天海和代表团全体人员，在今天下午拜会了内务部部长谢觉哉。
    拜会的时候，主人向客人们介绍了我国抚育朝鲜战灾孤儿的情况。
    拜会时在座的，有内务部副部长王子宜、陈其瑗、王一夫和朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐。
