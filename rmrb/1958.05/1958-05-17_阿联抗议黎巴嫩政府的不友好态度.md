Title: UAR Protests Lebanese Government's Unfriendly Attitude
Author:
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 16th: According to news published in local newspapers today, the United Arab Republic's ambassador to Lebanon, Galib, delivered a note to the Lebanese Foreign Ministry on the 15th, protesting the recent unfriendly attitude shown by Lebanon towards the United Arab Republic.

The note stated that the Lebanese government recently arrested many expatriates from the United Arab Republic, provoked an employee of the United Arab Republic embassy in Beirut, and searched an embassy vehicle.

The note demanded that the Lebanese government immediately investigate these incidents.

### 阿联抗议黎巴嫩政府的不友好态度

1958-05-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿联抗议黎巴嫩政府的不友好态度
    新华社大马士革16日电　据这里报纸今天登载的消息，阿拉伯联合共和国驻黎巴嫩大使加利布在15日交给黎巴嫩外交部一个照会，就黎巴嫩最近表现的对阿拉伯联合共和国的不友好态度提出抗议。
    照会说，黎巴嫩政府最近逮捕了许多阿拉伯联合共和国的侨民，向贝鲁特的阿拉伯联合共和国大使馆的一名雇员寻衅，并且搜查了大使馆的一辆汽车。
    照会要求黎巴嫩政府立即对这些事件进行调查。
