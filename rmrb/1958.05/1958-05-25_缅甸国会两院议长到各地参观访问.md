Title: Burmese Parliamentary Speakers Visit Various Places
Author:
Date: 1958-05-25
Page: 3
	
The Speakers of the two Houses of the Burmese Parliament Visit Various Places

(By this newspaper) The Speaker of the House of Nationalities of the Union of Burma, Sao Hkun Hkio, and his wife, the Speaker of the House of Representatives, Bo Mu Aung, and his wife, along with their entourage, left Beijing by special plane on the 22nd to visit various places.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Chen Shutong, and members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and heads of the All-China Women's Federation, the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee.

U Ba Maung, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy in China, also saw them off at the airport.

On the morning of the 22nd, the distinguished guests visited the project to divert the Yellow River to irrigate the Wei River in Zhengzhou. On the 23rd, they arrived in Wuhan, where they visited the Yangtze River Bridge in the morning and the Wuhan Iron and Steel Company, which is under construction, in the afternoon. On the morning of the 24th, they arrived in Changsha and visited Chairman Mao's hometown, Shaoshan Chong, in Xiangtan County.

### 缅甸国会两院议长到各地参观访问

1958-05-25
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸国会两院议长到各地参观访问
    本报讯　缅甸联邦国会民族院议长萧恢塔和夫人、代表院议长波木昂和夫人以及他们的随行人员，在22日乘专机离开北京，到各地参观访问。
    在机场欢送他们的有人大常委会副委员长林伯渠、李济深、陈叔通和委员，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国务院秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长章汉夫以及全国妇联、中缅友好协会、北京市人民委员会的负责人。
    缅甸驻中国大使馆临时代办吴巴茂也到机场欢送。
    22日上午，贵宾们到郑州参观了引黄济卫工程。23日到武汉，上午参观了长江大桥，下午参观了正在建设中的武汉钢铁公司。24日上午到长沙，并到湘潭县去访问了毛主席的故乡韶山冲。
