Title: Secretary-General of the Korean-Chinese Side Strongly Protests US Provocations
Author:
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th - According to the Korean Central News Agency from Kaesong, at today's meeting of the Secretaries-General of the Military Armistice Commission, the Secretary-General of the Korean-Chinese side lodged a strong protest with the US side regarding the recent series of provocative and hostile acts carried out by the US. The Secretary-General of the Korean-Chinese side pointed out at the meeting that, despite repeated protests from the Korean-Chinese side, the US side continues to violate the Armistice Agreement by dispatching military aircraft to intrude into the airspace of the Korean-Chinese side. The Secretary-General of the Korean-Chinese side lodged a strong protest with the US side regarding this and demanded that the US side severely punish the personnel involved and guarantee that such incidents will not occur again.

The Secretary-General of the Korean-Chinese side also pointed out at the meeting the despicable provocative acts carried out by the US security personnel against the security personnel of the Korean-Chinese side within the Panmunjom Joint Security Area. The Secretary-General of the Korean-Chinese side pointed out that the Korean-Chinese side resolutely opposes and absolutely cannot tolerate such vile insults and provocative acts by the US side. The US side will be fully responsible for all the consequences arising from such actions by the US side.

The Secretary-General of the Korean-Chinese side also pointed out at the meeting the US personnel’s continued act of setting fires in the demilitarized zone, disregarding the warnings from the Korean-Chinese side. The Secretary-General of the Korean-Chinese side demanded that the US side severely punish the US personnel who set the fires and pointed out that the US side will bear full responsibility for all the consequences resulting from this.

### 朝中方面秘书长  强硬抗议美方挑衅

1958-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    朝中方面秘书长
    强硬抗议美方挑衅
    据新华社平壤20日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯，在今天举行的军事停战委员会秘书长会议上，朝中方面秘书长就美方最近进行的一系列的挑衅敌对行为向美方提出强硬抗议。朝中方面秘书长在会上指出，美方不顾朝中方面多次的抗议，违反停战协定继续派遣军用飞机侵入朝中方面地区上空。朝中方面秘书长对此向美方提出强硬抗议，并且要求美方严惩有关人员，保证不再发生这类事件。
    朝中方面秘书长在会上还指出了板门店共同警卫区内的美方警卫人员对朝中方面的警卫人员所进行的卑劣的挑衅行为。朝中方面秘书长指出，朝中方面坚决反对并且绝不能容许美方这种卑鄙的污辱和挑衅行为。由美方的这种行为所引起的一切后果全部由美方负责。
    朝中方面秘书长在会上还指出美方人员不顾朝中方面的警告，继续在非军事区内放火的行为。朝中方面秘书长要求美方严惩放火的美方人员，并且指出，由此招致的一切后果由美方负全部责任。
