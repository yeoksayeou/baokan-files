    Summer Planting · Autumn Planting

    The sowing that takes place in most provinces of the Yellow River and Huai River basins from late May to late June is abbreviated as "Summer Planting" or "Summer Sowing." The main summer-planted crops are sweet potatoes, corn, peanuts, sesame, soybeans, millet, etc. Currently, the sowing being carried out in Hubei, Henan, Shaanxi, Shandong, and other provinces at the same time as or after harvesting wheat is summer planting.

    Autumn Planting (Sowing) is called Winter Planting (Sowing) in southern regions. Generally speaking, the main autumn-planted (winter-planted) crops are: winter wheat, rapeseed, broad beans, peas, and other overwintering crops. The time for autumn planting (winter planting) of wheat varies from north to south, generally from mid-September (Hebei Province) to late November (Guangdong Province). In the provinces of the Yangtze River basin, the time for winter planting of rapeseed is generally from early to late October.

### 夏播·秋播

1958-05-29
第4版()
专栏：名词解释

    夏播·秋播
    黄、淮流域的大部省份在5月下旬到6月下旬进行的播种，简称“夏播”或“夏种”。夏播的作物主要是甘薯、玉米、花生、芝麻、大豆、谷子等。目前湖北、河南、陕西、山东等省在收麦的同时或收麦以后进行的播种，就是夏播。
    秋播（种），南方地区叫冬播（种）。就大部地区说，秋播（冬播）的庄稼主要是：冬小麦、油菜、蚕豆、豌豆等越冬作物。秋播（冬播）小麦的时间北早南迟，一般是9月中旬（河北省）—11月下旬（广东省）。在长江流域各省，冬播油菜的时间，一般是10月上旬到下旬。
