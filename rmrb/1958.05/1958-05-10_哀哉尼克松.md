Title: Alas, Nixon
Author: He Jun
Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 6

Alas, Nixon

    The Vice President of the United States,
    With the grand name of Nixon,
    Went to Latin America on a “trip,”
    To flaunt his power in the "backyard." (Note)
    First to Uruguay,
    Then to Argentina...
    "American crooks, get out!"
    Shouts of booing everywhere.
    The day before yesterday in Peru,
    "Visiting" university students,
    The students first gave him the cold shoulder,
    Then threw stones.
    An aide lost his teeth,
    The president's neck swelled,
    Bodyguards and police dared not move,
    His imposing manner vanished without a trace!
    Crisis looms at home,
    The "backyard" is in chaos,
    A leaky boat running into a headwind,
    Alas, Nixon!

    Note: Americans call Latin America America's "backyard."

### 哀哉尼克松
禾君
1958-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    哀哉尼克松
    禾君
    美国副总统，
    大名尼克松，
    拉丁美洲去“旅行”，
    “后院”逞威风。（注）
    先到乌拉圭，
    又到阿根廷……
    “美国骗子滚回去！”
    到处嘘骂声。
    前天在秘鲁，
    “访问”大学生，
    学生先给闭门羹，
    然后石头扔。
    副官牙齿落，
    总统脖子肿，
    保镖警察不敢动，
    威风影无踪！
    家中危机凶，
    “后院”乱哄哄，
    船破又遇顶头风，
    哀哉尼克松！
    注：美国人把拉丁美洲叫做美国的“后院”。
