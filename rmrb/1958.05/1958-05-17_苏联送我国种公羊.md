Title: Soviet Union Sends Breeding Rams to China
Author:
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 2

　　The Soviet Ministry of Agriculture recently gifted China four Merino breeding rams to assist in the improvement of Chinese livestock breeds. This is a fine-wool sheep breed. The heaviest of these four rams weighs 142 kilograms; the highest wool yield reaches 13.85 kilograms, and the wool length is 8.5 centimeters, which is longer than the wool length of China's current Xinjiang fine-wool sheep.

　　These four rams will remain in places such as the Nanshan Breeding Sheep Farm in Urumqi, Xinjiang, to conduct experimental crossbreeding with Xinjiang fine-wool sheep in order to further improve the quality of Xinjiang fine-wool sheep. (Ji)

### 苏联送我国种公羊

1958-05-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏联送我国种公羊
    苏联农业部为了帮助我国改良畜种，最近赠送给我国四只美利奴种公羊。这是一种毛用细毛羊。这四只羊的体重最重的有一百四十二公斤；毛产量最高的达到十三点八五公斤，毛长度八点五公分。比我国目前新疆细毛羊的毛长度高。
    这四只羊将留在新疆乌鲁木齐南山种羊场等地，和新疆细毛羊进行试验性的杂交，以便进一步提高新疆细毛羊的品质。　（基）
