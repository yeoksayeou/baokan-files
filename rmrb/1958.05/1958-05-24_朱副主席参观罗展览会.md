Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Visits Romanian Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1958-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: Vice Chairman Zhu De visited the Romanian Economic Exhibition this morning.

Accompanied by Bashkirev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, and Abraham, head of the Romanian Economic Exhibition Delegation, Vice Chairman Zhu De first visited agricultural, handicraft, and industrial products such as food and textiles. Then, in another hall, he visited products from the petroleum, plastics, and chemical industries, as well as various machines, and watched Romanian workers operate various precision machine tools. After leaving this hall, Vice Chairman Zhu De visited various agricultural machines on the square. After the visit, Vice Chairman Zhu De spoke with Bashkirev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, and others, praising the leap-forward development of Romanian socialist construction and the brilliant achievements made in socialist construction.

The exhibition of the Romanian Economic Exhibition has been warmly welcomed by Chinese audiences. Since the opening of the exhibition on April 28, nearly 400,000 people have visited it. During this period, the exhibition also received letters from industrial departments in Xinjiang, Liaoning, Hebei, and other places, requesting information and pictures of advanced products from the exhibition in order to learn from Romania's experience in manufacturing advanced products.

### 朱副主席参观罗展览会

1958-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    朱副主席参观罗展览会
    新华社23日讯　朱德副主席今天上午参观了罗马尼亚经济展览会。
    朱德副主席在罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办巴什基洛和罗马尼亚经济展览会展览团团长阿布拉罕等陪同下，首先参观了农业、手工业和食品、纺织等工业的产品。接着在另一个馆内，参观了石油、塑料、化学等工业的产品以及各种各样的机器，并看了罗马尼亚工人操作各种精密机床的表演。走出这个馆，朱德副主席到广场上参观了各种农业机械。朱德副主席参观后和罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办巴什基洛等交谈时，盛赞罗马尼亚社会主义建设的飞跃发展和在社会主义建设中取得的辉煌成就。
    罗马尼亚经济展览会的展出，受到中国观众的热烈欢迎。从展览会在4月28日开幕以来，参观展览会的已有近四十万人。在这个时期内，展览会还收到了新疆、辽宁、河北等地工业部门的来信，向展览会要先进产品的资料和图片，好学习罗马尼亚制造先进产品的经验。
