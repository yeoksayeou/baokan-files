Title: People from All Circles in the Capital Mourn the Passing of Lai Ruoyu; Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party Send Telegrams of Condolence
Author:
Date: 1958-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd. More than 10,000 representatives from all circles in the capital went to Zhongshan Park this afternoon to mourn the passing of Comrade Lai Ruoyu.

Today, those who went to pay their respects at Comrade Lai Ruoyu's memorial hall in Zhongshan Hall included Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liao Chengzhi, member of the CPC Central Committee, as well as heads of central and Beijing municipal organs, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and various industrial trade unions, people's organizations and democratic parties, and worker representatives from many factories and enterprises in Beijing.

Diplomatic envoys or officials from the embassies of the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, North Korea, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, Democratic Germany, Albania, Vietnam, and Yugoslavia in China also paid their respects this afternoon.

Also among those who paid their respects were trade union delegations and representatives from the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Hungary, India, Indonesia, Japan, and other countries in Beijing, as well as other foreign guests in Beijing.

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd. The Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on the 21st, expressing condolences on the death of Lai Ruoyu.

The telegram read: "We have learned with deep sorrow of the passing of Comrade Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and our dear comrade, who dedicated his life to the cause of the working class. We express our fraternal sympathy and most sincere condolences to the Chinese trade unions and workers for this extremely painful loss."

### 首都各界吊唁赖若愚逝世  世界工联书记处、越南劳动党中央致电吊唁

1958-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    首都各界吊唁赖若愚逝世
    世界工联书记处、越南劳动党中央致电吊唁
    新华社22日讯　首都各界代表一万多人今天下午陆续前往中山公园，吊唁赖若愚同志的逝世。
    今天前往设在中山堂内的赖若愚同志灵堂吊唁的，有中国共产党中央政治局委员董必武，中共中央委员廖承志，以及中央和北京市各机关、全国总工会和各个产业工会、人民团体和民主党派的负责人，还有北京市许多工厂企业的职工代表。
    苏联、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古、民主德国、阿尔巴尼亚、越南和南斯拉夫等国家的驻华使节或外交官员，今天下午也前往吊唁。
    前往吊唁的，还有在北京的苏联、保加利亚、匈牙利、印度、印度尼西亚、日本等国家的工会代表团和代表，以及其他在京外宾。
    新华社22日讯　世界工会联合会书记处21日致电中华全国总工会，吊唁赖若愚的逝世。
    唁电说：“我们沉痛地获悉中华全国总工会主席、我们亲爱的赖若愚同志逝世的消息，赖若愚同志将他的一生贡献于工人阶级的事业。我们对中国工会和工人这个极痛苦的损失表示兄弟般的同情和最诚恳的哀悼。”
    新华社22日讯　越南劳动党中央委员会21日致电中共中央吊唁赖若愚同志的逝世。
    唁电说：“惊闻中国共产党中央委员会委员、中华全国总工会主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、全国政治协商会议委员、亲爱的赖若愚同志逝世的消息，我们感到非常悲痛。
    “越南劳动党中央委员会谨向你们和赖若愚同志的家属表示吊唁。”
