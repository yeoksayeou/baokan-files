Title: Blast Furnace Utilization Coefficient of 0.488: Shijiazhuang Iron and Steel Plant's Youth Furnace Surpasses Benxi Iron and Steel Plant
Author:
Date: 1958-05-23
Page: 3

    Blast Furnace Utilization Coefficient of 0.488
    Shijiazhuang Iron and Steel Plant's Youth Furnace Surpasses Benxi Iron and Steel Plant
    (Our Dispatch) Shijiazhuang Iron and Steel Plant, due to measures such as increasing the blast temperature to 1064 degrees Celsius, increasing smelting intensity, reducing coke ash content, and increasing the iron content of sinter, the plant's Youth Blast Furnace created a national record of 0.488 for blast furnace utilization coefficient (the average number of cubic meters of furnace volume occupied per ton of iron produced per day) on May 20. This surpassed the national record of 0.493 set by the Youth Blast Furnace of Benxi Iron and Steel Company on May 16. (Hua Yinxiao)
    (Our Dispatch) On May 16, the utilization coefficient of the Youth Blast Furnace of the No. 1 Ironmaking Plant of Benxi Iron and Steel Company decreased to 0.493, a further decrease of 0.008 compared to the 0.501 achieved on May 14. On the same day, the overall utilization coefficient of the plant also decreased to 0.513, surpassing the record of 0.532 set by the Youth Furnace of Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant.

### 高炉利用系数0.488  石钢青年炉赶过本溪炼铁厂

1958-05-23
第3版()
专栏：

    高炉利用系数0.488
    石钢青年炉赶过本溪炼铁厂
    本报讯　石景山钢铁厂由于把风温提高到摄氏一○六四度，以及增加冶炼强度、降低焦炭灰分和提高烧结矿含铁量等各项措施，这个厂青年高炉于5月20日创造高炉利用系数0.488（每昼夜炼一吨铁，所占用的炉体容积平均立方公尺数）的全国最高纪录。超过5月16日本溪钢铁公司炼铁厂青年高炉0.493的全国纪录。　（华荫孝）
    本报讯　5月16日，本溪钢铁公司第一炼铁厂青年高炉利用系数降低到0.493比5月14日所创造的0.501又降低了0.008。这天，全厂利用系数也降低到0.513，超过太原钢铁厂青年炉所创0.532纪录。
