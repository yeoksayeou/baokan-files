Author: Wang Yousheng
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 7

	The "Tianjin Rice Research Institute" has been established in Jinghai County, Hebei Province.
	The establishment of the institute has received strong support from units such as the State Council Science Planning Commission, the Central Ministry of Agriculture, and the Academy of Agricultural Sciences. The Tianjin Special District has also allocated 20,000 mu of paddy fields as the institute's experimental fields. In order not to miss the farming season, the institute's staff has already begun experimental work such as raising seedlings of excellent rice varieties from home and abroad. The picture shows the staff observing the growth of seedlings of excellent domestic rice varieties. (Photo by Wang Yousheng)

    “天津稻作研究所”在河北静海县建立。
    研究所的建立，得到了国务院科学规划委员会、中央农业部和农业科学院等单位的大力支援，天津专区还拨了两万亩水稻田作为研究所的试验田。为了不误农时，目前研究所的工作人员已开始了国内、外优良水稻品种的育秧等试验工作。图为工作人员在观察国内优良水稻品种的秧苗生长情况  王佑生摄
