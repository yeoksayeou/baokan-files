Title: United Arab Republic Military Delegation Departs for Home, Carrying Letters from Our National Leaders to President Nasser and Marshal Amer

Author:

Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The military goodwill delegation from the United Arab Republic departed from Beijing today by special plane to return home.

Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Senior Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army including General Xu Guangda, General Li Da, General Zhang Zongxun, General Xiao Hua, General Yang Chengwu, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang of the Air Force, Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan of the Navy, Lieutenant General Zhang Lingbin, and Lieutenant General Gan Weihan, and more than 300 officers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as Colonel Li Dazhong, our military attache to the United Arab Republic, went to the airport to see the distinguished guests off.

Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the United Arab Republic in China, Shebini, and officials from the embassy also went to the airport to see them off.

When the delegation arrived at the airport, the military band played farewell music. Minister Peng Dehuai accompanied the head of the delegation, Muhammad Ibrahim, to review the honor guard. Subsequently, Head Ibrahim delivered a farewell speech, saying that your warm welcome made us feel at home; your friendly attitude opened all doors for us; all of these are signs of sincere friendship and brotherly affection. He then said, I am deeply convinced that everyone in our republic is eager to see you, and they will welcome you in Cairo and Damascus.

Minister Peng Dehuai delivered a speech. He, on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, thanked the delegation for bringing the sincere friendship of the people and armed forces of the United Arab Republic. He said, I would like to take this opportunity to convey the profound friendship and best wishes of the Chinese people and armed forces to General Ibrahim and all members of the delegation, and through you to the people and armed forces of the United Arab Republic, and wish you more brilliant victories in the struggle to safeguard national independence.

Head Ibrahim and all members of the delegation shook hands with the hosts and military attaches of various countries to China who came to see them off.

Before the delegation left Beijing to return home, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, handed over letters written by Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai to President Nasser respectively, and a letter written by Marshal Peng Dehuai to Marshal Amer, Minister of Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the United Arab Republic, to Head Ibrahim, asking him to take them to President Nasser and Marshal Amer respectively.

### 阿联军事代表团启程回国  并带去我国家领导人给纳赛尔总统和阿密尔元帅的信件

1958-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    阿联军事代表团启程回国
    并带去我国家领导人给纳赛尔总统和阿密尔元帅的信件
    新华社6日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团今天上午乘专机离开北京返国。
    国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，外交部副部长章汉夫，许光达大将、李达上将、张宗逊上将、萧华上将、杨成武上将、萧向荣中将、王秉璋空军中将、周希汉海军中将、张令彬中将、甘渭汉中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和陆海空军军官三百多人，以及我国驻阿联武官李大中上校，都到机场欢送贵宾。
    阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国临时代办谢宾尼和大使馆官员，也到机场欢送。
    代表团到达机场时，军乐队奏起欢送乐曲，彭德怀部长陪同穆罕默德·易卜拉欣团长检阅了仪仗队。随后，易卜拉欣团长致告别词说，你们对我们的热情欢迎，使我们感到像在家里一样；你们友好的态度把一切门都替我们打开了；所有这些，都是真诚友谊和兄弟情义的标志。他接着说，我深信我们共和国的每一个人都迫切地想看到你们，而且他们将会在开罗和大马士革欢迎你们。
    彭德怀部长致词。他代表中国人民解放军全体官兵感谢代表团带来了阿拉伯联合共和国人民和武装部队的真诚友谊。他说，我愿借此机会，向易卜拉欣将军和代表团全体团员，并通过你们向阿拉伯联合共和国的人民和武装部队，传达中国人民和武装部队的深厚友谊和良好祝愿，祝你们在维护民族独立的斗争中取得更辉煌的胜利。
    易卜拉欣团长和全体团员同前来欢送的主人们和各国驻华武官一一握手告别。
    在代表团离开北京返国前夕，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将把毛泽东主席和周恩来总理分别写给纳赛尔总统的信，和彭德怀元帅写给阿拉伯联合共和国国防部长、武装部队总司令阿密尔元帅的信交给易卜拉欣团长，请他分别带给纳赛尔总统和阿密尔元帅。
