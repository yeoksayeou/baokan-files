Title: Yemeni Chargé d'Affaires in Cairo Expresses Gratitude for Soviet Support of Yemen
Author:
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 20th: The Yemeni Chargé d'Affaires in Cairo, Shami, visited the Soviet Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, Kiselev, on May 19th to express his gratitude for the support of Yemen's struggle against British aggression in the recent joint statement issued in Moscow by the leaders of the Soviet Union and the United Arab Republic.

### 也门驻开罗代办表示感谢苏联支持也门

1958-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    也门驻开罗代办
    表示感谢苏联支持也门
    新华社开罗20日电　也门驻开罗的代办沙米在5月19日访问了苏联驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使基谢廖夫，向他表示感谢最近在莫斯科发表的苏联和阿拉伯联合共和国的领导人的联合声明中支持也门为反对英国的侵略而作的斗争。
