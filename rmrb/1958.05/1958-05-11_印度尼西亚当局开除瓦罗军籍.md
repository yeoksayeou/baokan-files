Title: Indonesian Authorities Expel Warouw from Military Service
Author:
Date: 1958-05-11
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 10th. Indonesian Army spokesman Piengaadi announced on the evening of the 9th that Warouw, the former military attaché of the Indonesian Embassy in China, had been expelled from military service. This action was taken in accordance with an order issued by President Sukarno on May 6.

Antara News Agency reported that previously, Warouw had been removed from his post for deserting without permission. Later reports indicated that he had actively participated in the activities of the "Permesta" rebellion group in North Sulawesi. He had been "appointed" as the "Deputy Prime Minister" of the rebel "government". Reports indicate that he was recently in Manado, North Sulawesi.

### 印度尼西亚当局开除瓦罗军籍

1958-05-11
第3版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚当局开除瓦罗军籍
    新华社雅加达10日电　印度尼西亚陆军发言人皮恩加迪9日晚上宣布，前印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆武官瓦罗已经被开除军籍。这个行动是根据苏加诺总统5月6日的一项命令采取的。
    安塔拉通讯社报道，在这以前，由于瓦罗未经允许擅离职守，他已经被撤职。后来有消息说，他积极参加北苏拉威西“全面斗争约章”叛乱集团的活动。他曾经被“任命”为叛乱“政府”的“副总理”。有消息说，他最近在北苏拉威西的万鸦老。
