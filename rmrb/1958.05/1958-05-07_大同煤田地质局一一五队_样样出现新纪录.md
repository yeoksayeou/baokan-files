Title: Datong Coalfield Geological Bureau's Team 115: Everything Shows New Records

Author:

Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 3

On the Leap Forward List, Who is Leading Today?

    Datong Coalfield Geological Bureau's Team 115
    Everything Shows New Records
    (Our dispatch) In April, Datong Coalfield Geological Bureau's Team 115 set a new national record of 860.5 meters of core drilling, more than double the state plan. The team led by Fang Shifu also set a new record of 1,476.02 meters drilled per drilling rig per month; the topographic survey team set a new record of 1.9 square kilometers surveyed per day in relatively difficult areas (Category 4 areas), completing 200.7% of the plan. The steady installation team, which is specifically responsible for dismantling, transporting, and installing drilling rigs and towers, set a new record of dismantling and installing three drilling towers per day.

### 大同煤田地质局一一五队  样样出现新纪录

1958-05-07
第3版()
专栏：跃进榜上，今日谁家领先？

    大同煤田地质局一一五队
    样样出现新纪录
    本报讯　4月份，大同煤田地质局一一五队，创造了岩心探钻八百六十点五公尺的全国新纪录，比国家计划提高二倍以上。这个队的方士福领导的小组还创造了每台钻机每月钻进一千四百七十六点零二公尺的最高纪录；地形测量组在比较困难的地区（四类地区）创造了每日测量一点九平方公里的新纪录，完成计划200.7％。专门负责拆卸、运输、安装钻机、钻塔的稳装小组，创造了日拆装三台钻塔的新纪录。
