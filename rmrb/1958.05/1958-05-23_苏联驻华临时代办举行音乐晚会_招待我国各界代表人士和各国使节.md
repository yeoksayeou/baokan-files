Title: Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in China Holds Musical Soirée to Entertain Representatives from Various Circles in Our Country and Foreign Envoys

Author:
Date: 1958-05-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: The Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in China, Antonov, held a musical soirée tonight, featuring a brilliant performance by the Soviet State Symphony Orchestra, to entertain representatives from various circles in our country and foreign envoys to China.
    Each piece performed by the Soviet musicians tonight was met with enthusiastic applause from the thousand-odd audience members. At the end of the entire performance, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, along with Guo Moruo, Li Jishen, Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Bao Erhan, Zhang Xiruo, Shi Liang, Qian Junrui, and Polish Ambassador to China Kirylok, accompanied by the Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in China, Antonov, stepped onto the stage to warmly shake hands with the Soviet musicians and thank them for their wonderful performance.

### 苏联驻华临时代办举行音乐晚会  招待我国各界代表人士和各国使节

1958-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联驻华临时代办举行音乐晚会
    招待我国各界代表人士和各国使节
    据新华社22日讯　苏联驻中国临时代办安东诺夫今晚举行音乐晚会，由苏联国家交响乐团演出精采节目，招待我国各界代表人士和各国驻华使节。
    苏联音乐家们今晚演出的每一个节目，都博得了一千几百名听众的热烈鼓掌。整个演出结束时，陈毅副总理兼外交部长，以及郭沫若、李济深、吴玉章、沈钧儒、黄炎培、陈叔通、包尔汉、张奚若、史良、钱俊瑞和波兰驻华大使基里洛克等人，由苏联驻中国临时代办安东诺夫陪同步上舞台，同苏联音乐家们热烈握手，感谢他们的精彩演出。
