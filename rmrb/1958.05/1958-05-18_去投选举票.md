Title: Going to Vote
Author: Jiashu
Date: 1958-05-18
Page: 4

　　Going to Vote
    Jiashu
　　The gongs and drums are loud and lively,
　　Red lanterns shine at the polling station.
　　The red lanterns attract tens of thousands,
　　Tens of thousands of people laugh happily.
　　Red lanterns shine, tens of thousands laugh,
　　Hurry to cast your ballot.
　　Don't forget your voter ID,
　　You need it to elect representatives.
　　Arrive at the polling station,
　　Receive a ballot.
　　Holding the glorious ballot in your hand,
　　Fill it out carefully, thinking it over.
　　Choose the best of the best,
　　Elect good representatives.

### 去投选举票
嘉树
1958-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　去投选举票
    嘉树
　　敲锣打鼓好热闹，
　　选举站上红灯照。
　　红灯吸引人千万，
　　千百万人心欢笑。
　　红灯照，万人笑，
　　快快去投选举票。
　　选民证，别忘了，
　　有它才能选代表。
　　到了选举站，
　　领张选举票。
　　光荣的选票手中拿，
　　细心地填写多思考。
　　好里再挑好，
　　选出好代表。
