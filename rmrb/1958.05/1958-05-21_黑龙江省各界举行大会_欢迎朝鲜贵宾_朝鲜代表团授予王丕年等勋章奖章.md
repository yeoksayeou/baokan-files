Title: Heilongjiang Province Holds Grand Meeting to Welcome Distinguished Guests from Korea; Korean Delegation Awards Medals and Orders to Wang Pinian and Others

Author:
Date: 1958-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, May 20th: Heilongjiang Province and Harbin City held a grand welcoming rally at the Harbin Workers' Cultural Palace today, attended by 1,700 people from all walks of life, to welcome the Korean People's Delegation visiting China.

	Yu Tianfang, Deputy Governor of Heilongjiang Province, said in his welcoming speech that since October 1952, Heilongjiang Province has received 7,134 Korean war orphans, who have now grown up and acquired certain cultural knowledge, and can serve the construction of their own country. The Chinese people regard the cultivation and education of Korean war orphans as a glorious internationalist duty.

	Head of the delegation, Xu Zhenshu, spoke amidst warm applause. She conveyed the fraternal greetings of the Korean people to all the people of Heilongjiang Province and Harbin City, and thanked the people of Heilongjiang Province for their contributions in raising Korean war orphans. After her speech, Xu Zhenshu presented a banner to the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Committee.

	After the meeting, the Harbin Song and Dance Troupe performed song and dance programs for the distinguished guests.

	Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, May 20th: The Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Xu Zhenshu and Deputy Head Kim Chun-hae, was entrusted by the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to award orders and medals to 99 Chinese personnel, including Wang Pinian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Committee and Director of the Department of Civil Affairs, for their contributions to the nurturing of Korean war orphans.

	Deputy Head Kim Chun-hae read out the decree of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on awarding orders to citizens of the People's Republic of China who have made contributions to the protection and nurturing of Korean war orphans and children of martyrs. Xu Zhenshu pinned the Second Class National Flag Order on the chests of six people: Wang Pinian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Committee and Director of the Department of Civil Affairs; Luo Shu, Director of the Department of Health; Gao Heng, Mayor of Qiqihar City; Xia Jun, Mayor of Mudanjiang City; Cao Guojun, Director of the Mudanjiang Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory; and Liu Pengfei, County Head of Shuangcheng County. Thirty-three people, including Shang Cheng, Director of the Harbin Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory, were awarded the Third Class National Flag Order. Fifty-eight people, including Hong Fuwan, Deputy Section Chief of the Social Section of the Heilongjiang Provincial Department of Civil Affairs, were awarded the Medal of Merit.

	Xu Zhenshu delivered a congratulatory speech to the Chinese personnel who received the orders and medals at the awarding ceremony.

	Wang Pinian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Committee and Director of the Department of Civil Affairs, delivered a speech of thanks on behalf of the personnel who received the orders and medals.

### 黑龙江省各界举行大会  欢迎朝鲜贵宾  朝鲜代表团授予王丕年等勋章奖章

1958-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    黑龙江省各界举行大会
    欢迎朝鲜贵宾
    朝鲜代表团授予王丕年等勋章奖章
    据新华社哈尔滨20日电　黑龙江省和哈尔滨市各界一千七百多人，今天在哈尔滨市工人文化宫举行欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团大会。
    黑龙江省副省长于天放在欢迎会上致词说，从1952年10月开始，黑龙江省接待了七千一百三十四名朝鲜战灾孤儿，现在他们已经长大成人，掌握了一定的文化知识，可以为建设自己的祖国服务了。中国人民把培养和教育朝鲜战灾儿童看作是光荣的国际主义的义务。
    许贞琡团长在热烈的掌声中讲话。她向黑龙江省和哈尔滨市全体人民转达了朝鲜人民兄弟般的问候，并且感谢黑龙江省人民在抚育朝鲜战灾孤儿方面的功绩。许贞琡团长讲话后，向黑龙江省人民委员会赠送了一面锦旗。
    会后，由哈尔滨歌舞团为贵宾演出了歌舞节目。
    新华社哈尔滨20日电　以许贞琡团长、金天海副团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团，今天下午受朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会的委托，授予黑龙江省人民委员会副秘书长、民政厅长王丕年等九十九名对抚育朝鲜战灾孤儿有功的中国工作人员以勋章和奖章。
    金天海副团长在授勋仪式上宣读了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会关于授予在保护和抚育朝鲜战灾孤儿和烈士子女工作中有功的中华人民共和国公民勋章的政令。许贞琡团长把二级国旗勋章佩戴在黑龙江省人民委员会副秘书长、民政厅长王丕年，卫生厅长罗恕，齐齐哈尔市长高衡，牡丹江市长夏钧，牡丹江机车车辆修理工厂厂长曹国钧和双城县长刘鹏飞六个人的胸前。荣获三级国旗勋章的有哈尔滨机车车辆修理工厂厂长商诚等三十三人，荣获功劳章的有黑龙江省民政厅社会科副科长洪福万等五十八人。
    许贞琡团长在授勋仪式上向荣获勋章和奖章的中国工作人员致祝词。
    黑龙江省人民委员会副秘书长、民政厅长王丕年代表荣获勋章和奖章的人员致谢词。
