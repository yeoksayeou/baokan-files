Title: American Federation of Scientists Calls on Government to Suspend Nuclear Weapons Testing First
Author:
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 5th: The American Federation of Scientists issued a statement on the 4th, calling on the U.S. government to reach an agreement to halt nuclear testing without waiting for a comprehensive disarmament agreement.
    This organization, with two thousand members, said that it would be better to reach an agreement to halt nuclear testing first, and then try to stop the production of nuclear weapons, because it is more difficult to monitor the production of nuclear weapons, while nuclear weapons tests are detectable. The statement said, "It seems that progress (in solving the disarmament problem) can only be made by starting with smaller steps."

### 美国科学家联合会  要求政府先停试核武器

1958-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    美国科学家联合会
    要求政府先停试核武器
    新华社5日讯  华盛顿消息：美国科学家联合会4日发表声明，要求美国政府达成停止核试验协议，而不必等待全面的裁军协议。
    这个拥有两千个会员的组织说，先达成停止核试验的协议，然后再设法停止核武器的生产，这样作是比较好的，因为监督核武器生产要困难一些，而核武器试验则是可以测知的。声明说，“看来只有从较小的步骤开始才可能取得（解决裁军问题的）进展。”
