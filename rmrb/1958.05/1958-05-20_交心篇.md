Title: Gaining Intimacy with the Party
Author: Ba Ren
Date: 1958-05-20
Page: 8

　　Gaining Intimacy with the Party
　　Ba Ren
　　Express your heart to the Party,
　　Turn selfish desires into public spirit.
　　Public spirit is Party spirit,
　　Wholeheartedly serving the people.
　　Hiding selfish desires and not confessing,
　　Is fearing the sun's bright light.
　　What is hidden in a dark room,
　　That won't breed bacteria over time?
　　If selfish desires can be expressed,
　　Even ugliness can become crystal clear.
　　Dirty pond water flows out of the ditch,
　　Entering the stream, it naturally becomes clean.
　　Transforming selfish desires into public spirit,
　　Depends on constantly opening one's heart.
　　Look at those who are beautiful,
　　They look in the mirror every day.
　　Public spirit is Party spirit,
　　As bright as the sun.
　　Selfish desires are like all things,
　　Flourishing and thriving in their own way.
　　Opening our hearts again and again,
　　Life competes in joy.
　　Wholeheartedly serving the people,
　　The people are wholeheartedly united.
　　The people are wholeheartedly united,
　　Construction advances fiercely together.
　　Long live the Communist Party!
　　The future is splendid and bright!

### 交心篇
巴人
1958-05-20
第8版()
专栏：

　　交心篇
　　巴人
　　向党要交心，
　　私心化公心。
　　公心是党性，
　　一心为人民。
　　私心而不交，
　　为怕太阳明。
　　何物藏暗室，
　　日久不生菌？
　　私心而能交，
　　丑恶亦晶莹。
　　污池水出沟，
　　入溪流自清。
　　私心化公心，
　　端在常交心。
　　试看秀美者，
　　日日对明镜。
　　公心是党性，
　　太阳同光明。
　　私心如万物，
　　欣欣各向荣。
　　交心复交心，
　　生命竞欢腾。
　　一心为人民，
　　人民尽一心。
　　人民尽一心，
　　建设齐猛晋。
　　共产党万岁！
　　前途煊似锦。
