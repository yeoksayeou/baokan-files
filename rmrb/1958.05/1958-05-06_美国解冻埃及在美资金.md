Title: US Unfreezes Egyptian Funds in US

Author:

Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: The United States government announced on April 30th that it would unfreeze Egyptian dollar assets in the United States from May 1st.

    The Treasury Department's announcement stated that this measure was taken based on the advice of the State Department. It said that the U.S. government took this measure because the United Arab Republic government and the former Suez Canal Company recently concluded an agreement.

    The Egyptian funds that the United States announced it was unfreezing actually only amounted to $28 million.

### 美国解冻埃及在美资金

1958-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    美国解冻埃及在美资金
    据新华社2日讯　美国政府4月30日宣布，从5月1日起解冻埃及在美国的美元资产。
    财政部的公报说，这个措施是根据国务院的意见采取的。它说，美国政府所以采取这个措施是由于阿拉伯联合共和国政府和前苏伊士运河公司最近缔结了协议。
    美国宣布解冻的埃及资金实际上只有二千八百万美元。
