Title: Satellite Passes Over China Time
Author:
Date: 1958-05-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 17th. The Purple Mountain Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences announced: On the evenings of the 18th and 19th, the Soviet Union's third artificial Earth satellite will fly over China from northwest to southeast. Around 19:24 on the evening of the 18th, people in the areas of Fuzhou and Xiamen along the southeast coast of China will be able to see the artificial satellite with the naked eye in the northeastern sky; around 21:10, the satellite will fly over the areas of Kunming and Gejiu in southwestern China again. Around 20:06 on the evening of the 19th, people in Changsha, Hengyang, Guilin, Wuzhou, Guangzhou, Shantou and other places in China will be able to see the satellite fly over with the naked eye.

### 卫星经过我国时间

1958-05-18
第2版()
专栏：

    卫星经过我国时间
    新华社南京17日电　中国科学院紫金山天文台发布：18日晚和19日晚，苏联第三个人造地球卫星将由西北向东南飞过我国上空。18日晚十九时二十四分左右，我国东南沿海福州、厦门一带地区在东北方天空用肉眼可以看到人造卫星；二十一时十分左右，卫星再次飞过我国西南昆明、个旧一带地区的上空。19日晚二十时零六分左右，我国长沙、衡阳、桂林、梧州、广州、汕头等地都可以用肉眼看到卫星飞过。
