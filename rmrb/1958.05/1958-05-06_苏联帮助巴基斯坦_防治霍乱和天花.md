Title: The Soviet Union Helps Pakistan Prevent Cholera and Smallpox
Author:
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, May 4th: TASS reports that the Soviet Ambassador to Pakistan, Mr. Shpetiko, met with the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Pakistan, Mr. Hussein, on May 2nd and informed him that, in view of the cholera and smallpox outbreaks in some areas of East and West Pakistan, the Soviet government has decided to provide Pakistan with 1,000 liters of cholera vaccine and 3 million doses of smallpox vaccine free of charge. At the same time, a group of Soviet experts will be sent to Pakistan, bringing with them the necessary medical equipment.

### 苏联帮助巴基斯坦  防治霍乱和天花

1958-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联帮助巴基斯坦
    防治霍乱和天花
    据新华社5日讯  据塔斯社卡拉奇4日讯：苏联驻巴基斯坦大使什佩季科在5月2日拜会了巴基斯坦外交部副部长侯赛尼，并且通知他说：苏联政府鉴于东、西巴基斯坦的一些地区发生了霍乱和天花，决定无偿地援助巴基斯坦一千公升霍乱疫苗和三百万分天花疫苗，同时向巴基斯坦派遣一批苏联专家，这批专家将带来必需的医疗器材。
