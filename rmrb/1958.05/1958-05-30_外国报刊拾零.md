Foreign Press Clippings

　　Satellite Bodhisattva
　　A small St. Christopher medal, a saint who protects travelers, was placed in the Vanguard rocket that launched the satellite as its "sacred guide."

　　A (U.S.) Navy spokesman said that the previous two Vanguard rockets failed to launch because they did not have a holy image. Placing a holy image inside the Vanguard rocket was suggested by the rocket manufacturer and approved by a Navy official.
　　——British "Manchester Guardian"
　　　　You Yun Chang Illustration
　　Bloodthirsty Game
　　The U.S. government recently approved a patent for a toy knife designed by John Shaffer of Florida. A person using the knife only needs to grip the handle, and a pouch on the handle will shoot out a stream of red liquid.
　　… When the tip of the knife touches the opponent's body, the liquid can be released. The patent holder said: "In this way, the user will get extra pleasure from the realistic effect of bleeding." At the same time, the location of the imaginary wound also shows the accuracy of the knife wielder.
　　　　——U.S. "New York Times"

### 外国报刊拾零

1958-05-30
第5版()
专栏：外国报刊拾零

    外国报刊拾零
    卫星菩萨
    运载卫星上天的先锋号火箭中，曾经放了一个克立斯托弗圣徒的小神像牌，作为这颗卫星的“神圣向导”。克立斯托弗圣徒是一位保护旅行者的神。
    一位（美国）海军发言人说，先前两支先锋号火箭因为没有安放神像，所以发射失败了。在先锋火箭内安放神像牌是火箭制造商建议的，并由一位海军官员批准的。
    ——英国“曼彻斯特卫报”
       游允常插图
      嗜血游戏
    美国政府最近批准了佛罗里达州人约翰·沙弗尔设计的玩具小刀的专利权。使用这种小刀的人只须握紧刀柄，柄上的小囊就会射出一股红色液体。
    ……当刀尖触到对方的身上，就可放出液体。这位专利人说：“这样，使用者将从流血的逼真效果上得到格外的乐趣。”同时，假想的伤口的位置也表明了使刀者的准确性。
          ——美国“纽约时报”
