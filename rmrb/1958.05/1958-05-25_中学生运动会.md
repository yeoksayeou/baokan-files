    Middle School Student Sports Meet
    The 1958 Beijing Middle School Student Track and Field Meet, an unprecedented event with more than three thousand three hundred and forty athletes participating, began yesterday at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium and will continue today. A middle school sports meet with so many participants is sure to be exciting.

### 中学生运动会

1958-05-25
第4版()
专栏：看看听听玩玩

    中学生运动会
    规模空前、有三千三百四十多个运动员参加的1958年北京市中等学校学生田径运动会，昨天在先农坛体育场开始举行，今天还要举行一天。一个中等学校运动会有这样多的人参加，一定很精彩。
