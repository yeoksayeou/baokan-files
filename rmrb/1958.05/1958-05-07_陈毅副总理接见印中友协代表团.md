Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Delegation from the India-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th -- Vice Premier Chen Yi received this afternoon the delegation from the India-China Friendship Association, headed by B.P.L. Bedi, Secretary-General of the India-China Friendship Association, along with all members of the delegation.

Present at the reception were Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见印中友协代表团

1958-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见印中友协代表团
    新华社6日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了印中友好协会代表团团长、印中友协秘书长布腊姆·普腊卡什，以及代表团的全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有中印友好协会会长丁西林等人。
