Title: British Planes Intercept Arab Union Planes Multiple Times
Author:
Date: 1958-05-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 25th: An official spokesman for the United Arab Republic announced on May 25th that British fighter planes had recently intercepted United Arab Republic Air Force planes traveling between Cairo and Damascus on three occasions.

On May 22nd, British "Hawker Hunter" jet fighters intercepted a United Arab Republic Air Force transport plane and attempted to force it to land in Cyprus. The other two interceptions occurred on May 19th and 21st. The spokesman said that the United Arab Republic planes ignored these actions and continued their normal flights between Cairo and Damascus.

### 英机多次拦截阿联飞机

1958-05-28
第6版()
专栏：

    英机多次拦截阿联飞机
    据新华社开罗25日电　阿拉伯联合共和国官方发言人25日宣布，英国战斗机最近曾经三次拦截往返于开罗和大马士革之间的阿拉伯联合共和国空军的飞机。
    5月22日，英国的“霍克—猎人式”喷气战斗机拦截了阿拉伯联合共和国空军的运输机，并且企图强迫它在塞浦路斯降陆。另外两次拦截行动发生在5月19日和21日。发言人说，阿拉伯联合共和国飞机不理会这些举动，并且继续在开罗和大马士革之间作正常飞行。
