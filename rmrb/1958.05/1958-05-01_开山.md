Title: Mountain Blasting
Author: Liu Zhensheng
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 8

    Mountain Blasting
    Liu Zhensheng
    Steel drills probe into the mountain's heart,
    Dynamite is buried in the mountain's chest,
    A earth-shattering explosion,
    The sleeping barren mountain,
    Instantly bursts into joyous bloom!

### 开山
刘振声
1958-05-01
第8版()
专栏：

    开山
    刘振声
    钢钻探进山的心脏，
    炸药埋在山的胸膛，
    惊天动地一声巨响，
    沉睡的荒山，
    顿时间心花怒放！
