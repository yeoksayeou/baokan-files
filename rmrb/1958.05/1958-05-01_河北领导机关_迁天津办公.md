Title: Hebei Leadership Organs Move to Tianjin for Office Work
Author:
Date: 1958-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 29th - After Tianjin became the capital of Hebei Province, the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Hebei Provincial People's Committee have moved to Tianjin for office work.

Tonight, the leading Party and government officials of Hebei Province and Tianjin Municipality held a get-together.

### 河北领导机关  迁天津办公

1958-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    河北领导机关
    迁天津办公
    据新华社天津29日电　天津市成为河北省的省会以后，中共河北省委员会和河北省人民委员会已迁来天津市办公。
    今天晚上，河北省同天津市的党政负责人举行了联欢会。
