Title: Huangtankou Hydropower Station Generates Electricity (Photo)
Author: Zhang Shenming
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 3

    Huangtankou Hydropower Station Generates Electricity
    The first 7,500 kilowatt generating unit of the Huangtankou Hydropower Station on the Wuxi River in the upper reaches of the Qiantang River in Zhejiang Province began generating electricity on May 1, fifty days ahead of schedule.
    This is the Huangtankou Hydropower Station's main project, a 140-meter-long and 40-meter-high dam. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhang Shenming photographed.

### 黄坛口水电站发电（图片）
张申明
1958-05-07
第3版()
专栏：

    黄坛口水电站发电
    浙江省钱塘江上游乌溪江上的黄坛口水电站，第一台七千五百瓩的机组已经在5月1日开始通水发电，比原计划提前了五十天。
    这是黄坛口水电站主要工程有一百四十公尺长、四十公尺高的拦河坝。　新华社记者　张申明摄
