Title: US New Posture Towards Indonesia: Agrees to Supply Rice and Arms

Author:

Date: 1958-05-24
Page: 5

    US New Posture Towards Indonesia
    Agrees to Supply Rice and Arms
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 22nd: This morning, the exchange of letters ceremony for the United States supplying Indonesia with 35,000 to 37,000 metric tons of rice was held at the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. This batch of rice is what the United States suddenly agreed to supply to Indonesia. Here, public opinion believes that this is a new posture made by the United States, the purpose of which is to "ease" Indonesia's attitude towards the United States.
    Washington also agreed on the 21st to allow the supply of $500,000 worth of military equipment to the Indonesian police force. However, according to the United Press, officials in Washington said that this measure by the United States "does not mean that the United States will lift the overall ban on providing large quantities of military equipment to Indonesia before the end of the (Indonesian) 'civil war'."

### 美对印度尼西亚的新姿态  同意供给大米和军火

1958-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    美对印度尼西亚的新姿态
    同意供给大米和军火
    据新华社雅加达22日电　22日上午在印度尼西亚外交部举行了美国供给印度尼西亚三万五千到三万七千公吨大米的换文仪式。这批大米是美国突然同意供给印度尼西亚的。这里的舆论认为，这是美国作出的新姿态，其目的在于“缓和”印度尼西亚对美国的态度。
    华盛顿在21日还同意允许把价值五十万美元的军事装备供应印度尼西亚的警察部队。但是，据合众社报道，华盛顿的官员说，美国的这一措施“并不意味着在（印度尼西亚）‘内战’结束前取消美国禁止对印度尼西亚提供大批军火的总的禁令”。
