Title: Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Sends Condolences on the Death of Comrade Lai Ruoyu
Author:
Date: 1958-05-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 21st The Presidium of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Chairman Viktor Grishin, sent a telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions today, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Lai Ruoyu. The telegram reads as follows:
    Dear Comrades of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:
    We are deeply saddened by the unfortunate passing of Comrade Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and our friend. The All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, on behalf of the working people and trade unions of the Soviet Union, expresses our deep condolences and joins you in mourning. Lai Ruoyu dedicated his life to the cause of the Chinese working class and all working people, and to the cause of building socialism in his own country. In his position as Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, he worked tirelessly, devoting all his energy to developing the domestic trade union movement and strengthening the cause of proletarian internationalism. Comrade Lai Ruoyu tirelessly fought to strengthen the unbreakable socialist camp, a reliable bastion of world peace, and actively strengthened world peace. The image of Comrade Lai Ruoyu will forever remain in our hearts.
    Entrusted by the Presidium of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions.
              Chairman V. Grishin
                May 21, 1958
                      Moscow

### 全苏工会中央理事会主席  电唁赖若愚同志

1958-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    全苏工会中央理事会主席
    电唁赖若愚同志
    新华社21日讯　全苏工会中央理事会主席团主席维克多·格里申今天打电报给中华全国总工会，吊唁赖若愚同志。电文如下：中华全国总工会亲爱的同志们：
    中华全国总工会主席，我们的朋友赖若愚同志不幸逝世，全苏工会中央理事会谨代表苏联的劳动人民和工会表示沉痛的吊唁并和你们一起哀悼。赖若愚为中国工人阶级和全体劳动人民的事业，为在自己祖国建成社会主义的事业献出了自己的一生。他在中华全国总工会主席的岗位上不倦地劳动，把自己的全部精力用于发展国内工会运动的事业，用于加强无产阶级国际主义的事业。赖若愚同志以不倦的精力为加强世界和平的可靠堡垒——牢不可破的社会主义阵营而斗争，积极地加强世界和平。赖若愚同志的形象将永远留在我们的心里。
    受全苏工会中央理事会主席团委托。
              主席维·格里申
                1958年5月21日
                      于莫斯科
