Title: Lin Tiqiang Decides to Accelerate Research and Finalize and Promote Rice Transplanters Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1958-05-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 25th: Lin Tiqiang, assistant researcher at the Nanjing Agricultural Mechanization Research Institute and one of the creators of China's first rice transplanter, said excitedly after seeing the news of the closing of the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China: "The Party's National Congress has put forward the important task of technological revolution, which is both an encouragement and a spur to agricultural scientists. In order to support the early realization of the National Program for Agricultural Development, we have moved the original plan to finalize and promote the rice transplanter in 1962 ahead to this autumn." He also said: "We must adopt revolutionary methods to carry out scientific research, requiring everyone to contribute ideas and work together to do a good job in the research and finalization of the rice transplanter. In the future, we will further study and solve the problem of mechanized rice harvesting."

### 林体强决定加速研究  提前定型推广插秧机

1958-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    林体强决定加速研究
    提前定型推广插秧机
    据新华社南京25日电　全国第一部水稻插秧机创造者之一、南京农业机械化研究所助理研究员林体强，看到中共八大二次会议闭幕的消息后，兴奋地说：“党的全国代表大会提出了技术革命的重要任务，这对农业科学工作者是鼓舞也是鞭策。为了支援提前实现农业发展纲要，我们已将原订插秧机到1962年才定型推广的计划提前到今年秋季实现”。他还说：“我们要采取革命的办法进行科学研究，要求人人献计，大家动手，搞好插秧机的研究定型工作，今后将进一步研究解决水稻机械收割的问题”。
