Title: "Prague Spring" International Music Festival Opens
Author:
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 11th: The thirteenth "Prague Spring" International Music Festival opened today in the hall of the ancient Charles University in Czechoslovakia. At the opening ceremony, Janáček's Second String Quartet was performed to commemorate the thirtieth anniversary of the death of this great Czechoslovak composer. At the opening concert, Smetana's "My Country" was traditionally performed.

The music festival will run until June 3rd. Artists from sixteen countries will hold more than fifty concerts and many opera performances during this music festival.

### “布拉格之春”国际音乐节开幕

1958-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    “布拉格之春”国际音乐节开幕
    新华社布拉格11日电　第十三届“布拉格之春”国际音乐节今天在捷克斯洛伐克古老的卡罗利努姆大学的大厅里开幕。在举行开幕式时，演奏了杨纳切克的第二弦乐四重奏，以纪念这位伟大的捷克斯洛伐克作曲家逝世三十周年。在开幕音乐会上，按照传统演奏了斯麦塔那的“我的祖国”。
    音乐节将举行到6月3日。十六个国家的艺术家们将在这次音乐节中举行五十多次音乐会和许多歌剧演出。
