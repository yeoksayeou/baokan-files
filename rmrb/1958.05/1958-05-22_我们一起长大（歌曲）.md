Title: We Grow Up Together (Song)
Author:
Date: 1958-05-22
Page: 8
Weekly Song

    We Grow Up Together

    [Singing Instructions] When teaching this song, inspire the children's imagination so they feel like they are really seeing a verdant, swaying sapling. When singing the lines "Little sapling, let's have a contest...", sing the words "see who" with more emphasis, as if really challenging someone to a competition. When singing the lines "The little sapling smiled slightly...", use a soft (the little sapling is speaking) tone, or a girl can sing it solo. When it comes to "I wear a red scarf", it should be switched to the lively tone of a Young Pioneer.

### 我们一起长大（歌曲）

1958-05-22
第8版()
专栏：每周一歌

    我们一起长大
    〔唱法说明〕教唱这首歌时，要启发孩子们的想像，使他们感到眼前似乎真的出现了一株青翠摇拽的小树苗一样。唱到“小树苗呀咱们来比赛……”这几句时，要像真和谁挑战比赛似地，“看谁”两字唱的要重一些；唱到“小树苗听了微微笑……”这几句时，要用一种轻柔（小树苗讲话）的口气，或由一个女孩独唱也可以。到“我戴上一条红领巾”时，便应转换成少先队员活泼的语气。
