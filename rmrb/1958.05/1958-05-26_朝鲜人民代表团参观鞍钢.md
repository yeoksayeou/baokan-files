Title: Korean People's Delegation Visits Anshan Iron and Steel

Author:
Date: 1958-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 25th: The Korean People's Delegation, headed by Delegation Leader Xu Zhenshu and Deputy Delegation Leader Jin Tianhai, visited six major factories of Anshan Iron and Steel today, including iron smelting, steel smelting, large rolling mill, and seamless steel pipe plants. They received a warm welcome from the workers.

　　After the visit, Delegation Leader Xu Zhenshu said that although we only visited a small part of the Anshan Iron and Steel factories, we are very happy because we have seen the great achievements made by the Chinese workers under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party. This achievement has strengthened the strength of socialism.

　　The Korean People's Delegation arrived in Anshan this morning, accompanied by Wang Yifu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, and Zhang Xuexuan, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province. They left Anshan at 3:00 pm and returned to Shenyang.

### 朝鲜人民代表团参观鞍钢

1958-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民代表团参观鞍钢
    据新华社鞍山25日电　以许贞琡团长、金天海副团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团，今天在鞍山参观了鞍钢炼铁、炼钢、大型轧钢、无缝钢管等六个主要工厂，受到了工人们的热烈欢迎。
    许贞琡团长在参观以后说，虽然我们只参观了鞍钢的一小部分工厂，但是非常高兴，因为我们看到了中国工人在中国共产党的领导下所取得的伟大成就，这种成就增强了社会主义的力量。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团是在我国内务部副部长王一夫、辽宁省副省长张雪轩陪同下，于今天上午到达鞍山的。下午三点离开鞍山返沈阳。
