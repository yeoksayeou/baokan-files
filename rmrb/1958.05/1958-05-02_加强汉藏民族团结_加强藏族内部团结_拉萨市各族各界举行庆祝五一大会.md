Title: Strengthen Han-Tibetan National Unity, Strengthen Tibetan Internal Unity: Lhasa Celebrates May Day with Meeting of All Ethnic Groups

Author:

Date: 1958-05-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 1st: Over 600 people of all ethnic groups and circles in Lhasa held a meeting today to celebrate International Labor Day on May 1st. Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Work Committee, called on everyone at the meeting to closely coordinate with the situation of the great leap forward in national production and construction on the basis of anti-imperialism, patriotism, unity, and progress, to carry out the rectification movement to the end in all organs and units, to implement the Party's various policies in the Tibetan region, and to make better contributions to the cause of the Tibetan people. Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, on behalf of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, warmly congratulated the workers of all ethnic groups and circles in Lhasa on International Labor Day on May 1st. In his speech, he said: "We should have a great leap forward in political thought and national unity. Further consolidate and expand the anti-imperialist and patriotic united front, resolutely overcome great Han chauvinism and local nationalism; further strengthen the unity between the Han and Tibetan nationalities and the internal unity of the Tibetan people, and strive to gradually build a new socialist Tibet."

### 加强汉藏民族团结　加强藏族内部团结  拉萨市各族各界举行庆祝五一大会

1958-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

    加强汉藏民族团结　加强藏族内部团结
    拉萨市各族各界举行庆祝五一大会
    据新华社拉萨1日电　拉萨市各族各界职工六百多人今天举行了庆祝“五一”国际劳动节大会。中共西藏工委副书记周仁山在大会上号召大家在反帝爱国、团结、进步的基础上，与全国生产建设大跃进的形势密切配合，把整风运动在各机关、各单位内进行到底，贯彻执行党在西藏地区的各项政策，为西藏人民的事业作出更好的贡献。阿沛·阿旺晋美代表西藏自治区筹备委员会向拉萨市各族各界职工热烈祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。他在讲话中说：“我们应该在政治思想和民族团结方面来个大跃进。进一步巩固和扩大反帝爱国统一战线，坚决克服大民族主义和地方民族主义；进一步加强汉藏民族间的团结和藏族内部的团结，为逐步建设社会主义的新西藏而奋斗。”
