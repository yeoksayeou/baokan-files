Title: Kishi Nobusuke Threatens Government Personnel Not to Participate in Rallies
Author:
Date: 1958-05-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th: The Kishi Nobusuke government threatened government agency personnel on the 15th, forbidding them from participating in the rally planned for May 17th to protest the government's suppression of trade union violence. This rally is being held by the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan as the second activity celebrating "May Day" Labor Day.

### 岸信介威胁机关人员不准参加集会

1958-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    岸信介威胁机关人员不准参加集会
    据新华社16日讯　东京消息：岸信介政府15日威胁政府机关的人员不准他们参加预定在5月17日举行的抗议政府镇压工会暴行的大会。这次大会是日本工会总评议会作为庆祝“五一”劳动节的第二次活动而举行的。
