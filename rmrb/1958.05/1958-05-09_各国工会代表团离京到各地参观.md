Title: Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries Leave Beijing to Visit Various Places
Author:
Date: 1958-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 8th: Trade union delegations from various countries, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day International Labor Day celebrations, have visited factories, schools, handicraft cooperatives, and agricultural cooperatives in Beijing after the celebrations. They also visited the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and various industrial trade unions, and held some symposiums with relevant units to learn about the work of Chinese trade unions and the ideological and production Great Leap Forward of Chinese workers.
    The trade union delegations from various countries have left in batches to visit Northeast, Central China, East China, and South China.

### 各国工会代表团离京到各地参观

1958-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表团离京到各地参观
    据新华社8日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请前来参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的各国工会代表团，在“五一”观礼后，分别在北京参观了工厂、学校、手工业合作社、农业合作社，访问了中华全国总工会和各产业工会，并且和有关单位举行了一些座谈会，了解了中国工会工作和中国工人的思想、生产大跃进的情况。
    各国工会代表团已分批前往东北、华中、华东、华南参观访问。
