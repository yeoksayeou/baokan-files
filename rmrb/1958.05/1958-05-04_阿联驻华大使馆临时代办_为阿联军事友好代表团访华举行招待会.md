Title: Chargé d'Affaires of the UAR Embassy in China Holds Reception for the UAR Military Friendship Delegation's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1958-05-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the United Arab Republic in China, She Binni and his wife, held a reception this evening for the visit of the Military Friendship Delegation of the United Arab Republic to China.

Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Bao Erhan, President of the Sino-Arab Friendship Association, Senior Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Xiao Hua, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Luo Shunchu of the Navy, Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Zhang Lingbin, and Colonel Li Dazhong, our military attaché to the United Arab Republic, were invited to attend the reception.

Also present at the reception were Lieutenant General Mohammed Ibrahim, head of the Military Friendship Delegation of the United Arab Republic visiting China, and all members of the delegation.

### 阿联驻华大使馆临时代办  为阿联军事友好代表团访华举行招待会

1958-05-04
第2版()
专栏：

    阿联驻华大使馆临时代办
    为阿联军事友好代表团访华举行招待会
    新华社3日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国驻华大使馆临时代办谢宾尼和夫人，今天晚上为阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好代表团访问中国举行招待会。
    国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅，外交部副部长章汉夫，中阿友好协会会长包尔汉，中国人民解放军高级将领萧华上将、萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将、王秉璋空军中将、张令彬中将等和我国驻阿联武官李大中上校应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团团长穆罕默德·易卜拉欣中将和全体团员。
