Title: Soviet, Hungarian, Polish, Albanian and Other Countries' Newspapers Publish News of the CCP's "Eighth National Congress"
Author:
Date: 1958-05-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

Soviet, Hungarian, Polish, Albanian and Other Countries' Newspapers
Publish News of the CCP's "Eighth National Congress"
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Soviet "Pravda," the Albanian "Zeri i Popullit" and "Bashkimi" published the full text on the 30th of the resolution of the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China on the meeting of representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of all countries held in Moscow.
    Soviet "Pravda," Bulgarian "Rabotnichesko Delo," Romanian "Scinteia," "Romania Libera," and "Tineretul Scinteii" have all, in recent days, devoted considerable space to publishing news of the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China. The "Pravda" of the 26th also published news and photos of the Fifth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee.
    The Hungarian "Népszabadság" published the full text of Comrade Liu Shaoqi's report on the work of the Central Committee of the Party at the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China on the entire sixth page on the 30th. The Hungarian "Népakarat" also published an abridged version of the report on the same day. The Romanian "Scinteia" published excerpts from speeches by Comrades Liu Shaoqi and Deng Xiaoping at the meeting.
    The Czechoslovak "Práce" published an abstract of Comrade Tan Zhenlin's explanation of the National Agricultural Development Program from 1956 to 1967 on the 30th.

### 苏匈保罗阿等国报纸刊载中共“八大”消息

1958-05-31
第5版()
专栏：

    苏匈保罗阿等国报纸
    刊载中共“八大”消息
    据新华社讯　苏联“真理报”、阿尔巴尼亚“人民之声报”和“团结报”30日登载了中国共产党第八届全国代表大会第二次会议关于在莫斯科举行的各国共产党和工人党代表会议的决议的全文。
    苏联“真理报”，保加利亚“工人事业报”，罗马尼亚“火花报”、“自由罗马尼亚报”、“青年火花报”。在最近几天都以大量篇幅分别刊载了中国共产党第八届代表大会第二次会议的消息，26日的“真理报”并刊载了举行第八届中央委员会第五次全体会议的消息和照片。
    匈牙利“人民自由报”30日以第六版全版发表了刘少奇同志在中国共产党第八届全国代表大会第二次会议上作的关于党中央委员会工作的报告，匈牙利“人民之声报”同日也摘要刊登了这个报告。罗马尼亚“火花报”在消息中摘要发表了刘少奇、邓小平等同志在会上的发言。
    捷克斯洛伐克“劳动报”30日登载了谭震林同志关于1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要的说明的摘要。
