    On the eve of the "May Day" festival, the Harbin No. 2 Machinery Factory trial-produced a grain combine harvester as a gift to the farmers. This harvester has 55 horsepower and can harvest 15 mu of crops per hour. During the slack season, it can also be used as power for generating electricity and rice milling machinery. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Wei photographed.
