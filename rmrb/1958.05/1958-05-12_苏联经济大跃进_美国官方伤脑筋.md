Title: Soviet Economic Great Leap Forward: American Officials Ponder
Author:
Date: 1958-05-12
Page: 6

　　Soviet Economic Great Leap Forward
　　American Officials Ponder

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 10th dispatch: “New York World-Telegram and the Sun” published a Washington dispatch stating that American officials are paying close attention to Khrushchev’s statement regarding the Soviet Union’s plan to surpass the United States in industrial and agricultural output, because “they know that Khrushchev is not speaking lightly.”

The newspaper wrote that the question of exactly when the Soviet Union will catch up with the United States is debatable, but “one thing is very clear: Russia is accelerating its pace in developing its economy.” After citing many materials comparing the levels of development in Soviet and American production, the newspaper said: “Senior officials in the United States government are becoming uneasy about the speed of Russian economic development.”

### 苏联经济大跃进  美国官方伤脑筋

1958-05-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏联经济大跃进
　　美国官方伤脑筋
    新华社11日讯  据塔斯社纽约10日讯：“纽约世界电讯与太阳报”刊登一条华盛顿消息说，美国官方人士非常注意赫鲁晓夫关于苏联计划在工农业的产量方面超过美国的提法，因为“他们知道赫鲁晓夫并不是信口开河”。
    这家报纸写道，苏联究竟在什么时候赶上美国这个问题可以争论，但是“有一点是很清楚的：在发展经济上俄国正在加快速度”。这家报纸在援引许多关于苏联和美国生产发展水平对比的材料后说：“美国政府的高级人士正在对俄国经济发展的速度感到不安”。
