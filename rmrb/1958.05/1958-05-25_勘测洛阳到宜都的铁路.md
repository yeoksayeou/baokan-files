Title: Surveying the Railway from Luoyang to Yidu
Author:
Date: 1958-05-25
Page: 3

Construction Flowers

　　Surveying the Railway from Luoyang to Yidu
　　Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, May 24th. The railway line between Luoyang in Henan Province and Yidu in Hubei Province is currently being surveyed by the Fourth Design Institute of the Ministry of Railways.

　　This railway is 660 kilometers long, and when completed, it will run parallel to the Beijing-Guangzhou Railway, running through the central part of the country; it will greatly promote the industrial construction of Nanyang, Xiangfan, Jingzhou, Yichang and other regions, as well as the great agricultural development along the route.

　　In order to speed up railway construction, the employees of the Fourth Design Institute of the Ministry of Railways boldly broke with convention and adopted the method of conducting preliminary and detailed surveys simultaneously, with a one-time design, and quickly launched the surveying work.

### 勘测洛阳到宜都的铁路

1958-05-25
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    勘测洛阳到宜都的铁路
    新华社武汉24日电　河南洛阳到湖北宜都之间的铁路线，正由铁道部第四设计院勘测中。
    这条铁路长六百六十公里，建成后将同京广铁路平行，纵贯祖国的中部；对于南阳、襄樊、荆州、宜昌等地区的工业建设和沿线农业大发展将起巨大的促进作用。
    为了加快铁路建设，铁道部第四设计院职工大胆地打破了常规，采取了初测、定测同时并进、一次设计的办法，迅速展开了勘测工作。
