Title: American Industrialist Eaton Points Out: U.S. Has Hundreds of Espionage Agencies, Larger in Scale Than Hitler's Germany
Author:
Date: 1958-05-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th, New York Dispatch: Industrialist Eaton, in a televised speech on the 4th, charged that the United States has a spy network larger in scale than Hitler's Germany.

Eaton said that besides the FBI, the United States has nearly one hundred other espionage agencies, which possess extensive "investigative and police powers." He said that "every department of the" U.S. "government has its own investigators and police forces."

### 美工业家伊顿指出  美国特务机关上百  规模赛过希特勒德国

1958-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    美工业家伊顿指出
    美国特务机关上百
    规模赛过希特勒德国
    新华社5日讯　纽约消息：工业家伊顿在4日发表的一篇电视广播演说里指责美国有着一个比希特勒德国规模更大的特务网。
    伊顿说，美国除了联邦调查局以外，还有另外将近一百个特务机关，它们拥有广泛的“调查和警察权力”。他说，美国“政府每一个部门都有它自己的调查人员和警察部队”。
