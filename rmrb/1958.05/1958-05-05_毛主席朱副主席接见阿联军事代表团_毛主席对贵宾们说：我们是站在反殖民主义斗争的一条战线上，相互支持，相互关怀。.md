Title: Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Zhu Receive the Military Delegation from the United Arab Republic. Chairman Mao said to the distinguished guests: We stand on the same front in the anti-colonialist struggle, supporting and caring for each other.
Author:
Date: 1958-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 4th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the military goodwill delegation from the United Arab Republic, headed by Lieutenant General Ibrahim, this evening and had a conversation lasting more than an hour.

Chairman Mao said to the distinguished guests that the Chinese people are very happy to have the friendship of the United Arab Republic.

Chairman Mao said that people all over the world support you and the people of the Arab countries. We stand on the same front in the anti-colonialist struggle, supporting and caring for each other.

Chairman Mao asked the distinguished guests to convey his greetings to President Nasser and wished him good health.

During the meeting, head of the delegation Ibrahim expressed the delegation's friendly wishes to the Chinese people, thanked the Chinese people for their support of the Arab people's anti-colonialist struggle, and presented President Nasser's letter and gifts to Chairman Mao. Marshal Peng Dehuai, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and Xie Binni, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the United Arab Republic Embassy in China, were present at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, May 4th: Vice Chairman Zhu De received Lieutenant General Ibrahim, head of the military goodwill delegation from the United Arab Republic, and delegation members: Lieutenant General Salem, Brigadier General Karim, and Colonel Montaki, in the afternoon of the 4th. Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong was present at the reception. During the meeting, the distinguished guests presented gifts to Vice Chairman Zhu De on behalf of Field Marshal Amir, Vice President and Minister of Defense of the United Arab Republic. ### 毛主席朱副主席接见阿联军事代表团  毛主席对贵宾们说：我们是站在反殖民主义斗争的一条战线上，相互支持，相互关怀。

1958-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席朱副主席接见阿联军事代表团
    毛主席对贵宾们说：我们是站在反殖民主义斗争的一条战线上，相互支持，相互关怀。
    新华社4日讯　毛泽东主席在今晚接见了以易卜拉欣中将为首的阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团，进行了一个多小时谈话。
    毛主席对贵宾们说，中国人民得到了阿拉伯联合共和国的友谊，感到非常高兴。
    毛主席说，全世界人民都支持你们，支持阿拉伯各国人民。我们是站在反殖民主义斗争的一条战线上，相互支持，相互关怀。
    毛主席请贵宾们把问候带给纳赛尔总统，祝他身体健康。
    在接见的时候，易卜拉欣团长表达了代表团对中国人民的友好祝愿，感谢中国人民对阿拉伯人民反殖民主义斗争的支持，并且把纳赛尔总统给毛主席的信和礼品转给了毛主席。接见时在座的，有彭德怀元帅和萧向荣中将，以及阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办谢宾尼。
    新华社4日讯　朱德副主席在4日下午接见了阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团团长易卜拉欣中将和代表团团员：萨勒姆中将、卡里姆准将、蒙大奇上校。接见时在座的有萧向荣中将。在接见的时候，贵宾们代表阿拉伯联合共和国副总统兼国防部长阿密尔元帅向朱德副主席赠送了礼品。（附图片）
