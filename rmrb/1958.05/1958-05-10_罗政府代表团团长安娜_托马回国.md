Title: Romanian Government Delegation Leader Anna Toma Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-05-10
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Anna Toma, leader of the Romanian government delegation and First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, who came to preside over the Romanian Economic Exhibition, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Other members of the delegation will continue to stay in our country for visits.
    Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Ji Chao-ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and others went to the airport to see her off.
    Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko, held a banquet on the evening of the 8th to bid farewell to delegation leader Anna Toma.

### 罗政府代表团团长安娜·托马回国

1958-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    罗政府代表团团长安娜·托马回国
    新华社9日讯　前来主持罗马尼亚经济展览会的罗马尼亚政府代表团团长、对外贸易部第一副部长安娜·托马，在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。代表团其他人员仍继续留在我国参观访问。
    到机场送行的，有我对外贸易部副部长林海云和中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎等人。
    罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科，8日晚为欢送安娜·托马团长举行了宴会。
