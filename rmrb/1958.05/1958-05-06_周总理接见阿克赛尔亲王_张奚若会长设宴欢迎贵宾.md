Title: Premier Zhou Receives Prince Axel, President Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author: 
Date: 1958-05-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th -- Premier Zhou Enlai received Prince Axel of Denmark and his secretary, Manager Bak, this evening, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, Vice President Qiao Guanhua, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation Rong Gaotang, and Deputy Director of the West European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Song Zhiguang. Danish Ambassador to China, Mr. Gregersen, was also present.

Xinhua News Agency, 5th -- President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Prince Axel of Denmark and his secretary, Manager Bak.

Among those present were Li Dequan, Luo Guibo, Lu Xuzhang, Ji Chao Ding, Dong Shouyi, and others.

Danish Ambassador to China, Mr. Gregersen, also attended the banquet. (Attached photo)

### 周总理接见阿克赛尔亲王  张奚若会长设宴欢迎贵宾

1958-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿克赛尔亲王
    张奚若会长设宴欢迎贵宾
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上接见了应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的丹麦阿克赛尔亲王和秘书巴克经理。
    接见时在座的有副总理兼外交部长陈毅，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪，中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠，外交部西欧司副司长宋之光。接见时，丹麦驻华大使格瑞杰生也在座。
    新华社5日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚设宴欢迎丹麦阿克赛尔亲王和秘书巴克经理。
    出席作陪的有李德全、罗贵波、卢绪章、冀朝鼎、董守义等。
    丹麦驻华大使格瑞杰生也出席了宴会。（附图片）
