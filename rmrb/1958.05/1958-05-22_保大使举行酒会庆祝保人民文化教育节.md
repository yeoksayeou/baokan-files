Title: Bulgarian Ambassador Holds Reception to Celebrate Bulgarian People's Culture and Education Festival
Author:
Date: 1958-05-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 21st: Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Mr. N. Garkov, held a reception this evening to celebrate the Bulgarian People's Culture and Education Festival. After the reception, the Bulgarian film "Haiduk's Oath" was shown.

Those invited to attend included Zhang Xiruo, Ding Xilin, Zheng Zhenduo, Qu Wu, people from cultural and educational circles in the capital, and ambassadors and ministers from various countries to China.

### 保大使举行酒会庆祝保人民文化教育节

1958-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    保大使举行酒会庆祝保人民文化教育节
    新华社21日讯　保加利亚驻中国大使?加尔科夫在今天晚间举行酒会，庆祝保加利亚人民文化教育节。酒会后放映了保加利亚影片“海杜特的誓言”。
    应邀出席的，有张奚若、丁西林、郑振铎、屈武和首都文化教育界人士，以及各国驻中国的使节。
