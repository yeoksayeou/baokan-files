Title: Damascus Mass Rally Supports the Struggle of the Lebanese People
Author:
Date: 1958-05-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 25th: Five thousand Damascus residents held a mass rally last night, hosted by the Committee to Support Lebanon, to express solidarity with and support for the just struggle of the Lebanese people.

    The host of the meeting, Syrian parliamentarian Malki, said in his opening remarks: "We do not want to interfere in Lebanon's internal affairs, but we will never allow any foreign interference."

    Speakers at the meeting unanimously condemned Western interference and refuted the lie that the communists were creating unrest in Lebanon.

    During the conference, participants repeatedly shouted slogans such as "Down with American imperialism," "Down with the Eisenhower Doctrine," "Down with the Baghdad Pact," and "Long live the peace-loving people."

### 大马士革群众集会  支持黎巴嫩人民的斗争

1958-05-26
第6版()
专栏：

    大马士革群众集会
    支持黎巴嫩人民的斗争
    据新华社大马士革25日电　大马士革居民五千人昨天晚上在支援黎巴嫩委员会主持下，举行了一个群众大会，表示声援和支持黎巴嫩人民的正义斗争。
    大会主持人、叙利亚议员马尔基致开会词说：“我们不愿干涉黎巴嫩的内政，可是我们决不允许任何外国人的干涉”。
    在会上发言的人一致谴责西方的干涉和驳斥所谓共产党在黎巴嫩制造暴乱的谎言。
    在大会进行期间，与会者一再高呼：“打倒美帝国主义”，“打倒艾森豪威尔主义”，“打倒巴格达条约”和“爱好和平的人民万岁”等口号。
