Title: President Sukarno Calls for Maintaining International Peace, Eliminating Imperialism, and Achieving National Equality
Author:
Date: 1958-05-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 29th: Indonesian President Sukarno reiterated on the evening of the 28th the importance of maintaining international peace and cooperation. Speaking at Gadjah Mada University in Yogyakarta, he said that the world should be a world of national equality free from imperialism and colonialism.

Sukarno said that the Indonesian Communist Party also advocates that Indonesia should realize the ideal of establishing a unified republic, which was embraced by the 1945 Indonesian independence revolution, and that it also demands the realization of a just and prosperous society and international peace and social justice. He said, however, that not everyone who holds such views is a communist.

Sukarno said that in politics he is a nationalist, in spirit he is a worshiper of God, and in society he is a socialist. He stated that he is neither a social democrat nor a communist.

Sukarno appealed that in this world of intercontinental ballistic missiles and nuclear weapons, international peace and cooperation should be realized.

Sukarno once again stated that he believes Indonesia must practice guided democracy and establish a National Planning Council. He said that his concept has not yet been fully realized. He said that the current Caretaker Cabinet and National Council that have been established are the minimum achievements, and also the highest achievements obtained in the current environment.

### 苏加诺总统呼吁维护国际和平  消灭帝国主义　实现民族平等

1958-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统呼吁维护国际和平
    消灭帝国主义　实现民族平等
    新华社雅加达29日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺28日晚上重申必须维护国际间的和平和合作的主张。他在日惹加查玛达大学发表演说时说，这个世界应当是免除帝国主义和殖民主义的民族平等的世界。
    苏加诺说，印度尼西亚共产党也是主张印度尼西亚应该实现1945年印度尼西亚独立革命所怀抱的建立统一的共和国的理想的，它并且要求实现公正和繁荣的社会以及国际和平和社会正义。他说，但是，并不是所有抱有这种主张的人都是共产党人。
    苏加诺说，在政治方面，他是一个民族主义者，在精神方面，他是一个敬拜真主的人，在社会方面，他是一个社会主义者。他表示，他既不是社会民主主义者，也不是共产主义者。
    苏加诺呼吁说，在这个拥有洲际弹道导弹和核武器的世界里，应该实现国际的和平和合作。
    苏加诺再一次表明，他认为印度尼西亚必须实行有指导的民主，并且建立民族计划委员会。他说，他的概念还没有得到完全实现。他说，目前已经建立的事务内阁和民族委员会是最低限度的成就，同时也是在目前环境下获得的最高成就。
