Title: Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition in Moscow Closes
Author:
Date: 1955-02-03
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 2nd) The People's Republic of China Arts and Crafts Exhibition, which opened in Moscow on November 30th last year, closed on January 31st. During the exhibition period, tens of thousands of spectators visited the exhibition. They expressed great admiration for the exquisite beauty of Chinese handicrafts.

The exhibits at the exhibition will soon be sent to Leningrad, Riga and Kiev for exhibition.

### 我国工艺美术展览会在莫斯科闭幕

1955-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我国工艺美术展览会在莫斯科闭幕
   【新华社莫斯科二日电】去年十一月三十日在莫斯科开幕的中华人民共和国工艺美术展览会，已经在一月三十一日闭幕。在展览期间，有数以万计的观众参观了展览会。他们对于中国工艺品的精致美丽，表示了很大的赞赏。
    展览会上的展览品即将先后送到列宁格勒、里加和基辅展出。
