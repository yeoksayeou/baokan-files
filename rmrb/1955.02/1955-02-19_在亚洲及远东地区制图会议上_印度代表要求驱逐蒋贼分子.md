Title: At the Cartographic Conference of Asia and the Far East, Indian Representative Demands Expulsion of Chiang Kai-shek Bandits
Author:
Date: 1955-02-19
Page: 1

    At the Cartographic Conference of Asia and the Far East
    Indian Representative Demands Expulsion of Chiang Kai-shek Bandits
    [Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 18th] TASS, Delhi: The Cartographic Conference of Asia and the Far East, initiated by the United Nations, opened in Mussoorie, India. At the plenary session on the 16th, Indian representative Gopal moved to expel the Kuomintang representatives. He pointed out that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China is the legal government of China, enjoys the trust of the Chinese people, and fully meets the requirements of the United Nations Charter.

### 在亚洲及远东地区制图会议上  印度代表要求驱逐蒋贼分子

1955-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    在亚洲及远东地区制图会议上
    印度代表要求驱逐蒋贼分子
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社德里讯：由联合国发起的亚洲及远东地区绘制地图的会议在印度穆索里开幕。在十六日的全体会议上，印度代表戈帕尔动议驱逐国民党代表。他指出：中华人民共和国的中央人民政府是中国的合法政府，它得到中国人民的信任，并且完全符合联合国宪章的要求。
