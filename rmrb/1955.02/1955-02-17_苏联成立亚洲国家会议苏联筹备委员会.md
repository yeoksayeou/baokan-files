Title: Soviet Union Establishes Soviet Preparatory Committee for the Asian Countries Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-02-17
Page: 4

    Soviet Union Establishes Soviet Preparatory Committee for the Asian Countries Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 15th] TASS reported from Moscow on the 15th that the Soviet Union has recently established a Soviet Preparatory Committee for the Asian Countries Conference, composed of representatives from all walks of life. The preparatory committee includes: N.S. Tikhonov (writer, chairman of the committee), A.Y. Korneichuk (writer), D.K. Khodzhibayev (Chairman of the Trade Union Council of the Uzbek Republic), and N. Bazhanova (Professor of Kazakh State University), among others.
    The task of the Soviet Preparatory Committee is to widely publicize the purpose of the upcoming Asian Countries Conference to the Soviet people and to send a Soviet delegation to Delhi.

### 苏联成立亚洲国家会议苏联筹备委员会

1955-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联成立亚洲国家会议苏联筹备委员会
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：苏联已经在最近成立了一个由各阶层代表组成的亚洲国家会议苏联筹备委员会。筹备委员会包括：尼·谢·吉洪诺夫（作家，委员会主席），亚·叶·考涅楚克（作家），德·克·霍德日耶夫（乌兹别克共和国工会理事会主席）和纳·巴扎诺娃（哈萨克国立大学教授）等。
    苏联筹备委员会的任务是广泛地向苏联人民宣传即将召开的亚洲国家会议的宗旨，并且派遣一个苏联代表团到德里去。
