Title: Leading Organs of Democratic Parties Call on All Members to Participate in Signature Campaign Against the Use of Atomic Weapons
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 16th: The leading organs of various democratic parties have issued instructions, calling on local organizations and all members to enthusiastically participate in the signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons.
    The Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a meeting on the 15th, adopted a resolution, and sent a telegram to local organizations urging them to actively respond to the call of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, mobilize all members and the masses they are connected with to participate in the signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons and to carry out propaganda work.
    The headquarters of the China Democratic League also held a meeting on the 15th, making a resolution on supporting the "Appeal to the People of the World" and launching a signature campaign. It called on all levels of the Democratic League organizations and all League members to widely promote the signature campaign, and to make every effort to oppose the use of atomic weapons, so that the inhuman attempt of US imperialism to prepare for an atomic war can be completely crushed under the condemnation of the people of the world.
    The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce pointed out in its call to all levels of organizations and all members to actively participate in the signature campaign: Implementing the "Resolution on Launching a Signature Campaign Against the Use of Atomic Weapons" of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Joint Expanded Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace is a major political task for all members at present.
    The headquarters of the China Association for Promoting Democracy issued a notice to its local organizations, requesting all members to enthusiastically respond to the call against the use of atomic weapons, to act quickly under the leadership of the local relevant authorities, not only to sign themselves, but also to widely and deeply carry out propaganda work among the masses they are connected with.
    The Central Executive Bureau of the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party also issued a notice, calling on the entire party to immediately and actively participate in the signature campaign, and to universally spread the just call against the use of atomic weapons to every member and the masses they are connected with through various means, so that everyone can participate in the signature.
    In recent days, the central and Beijing municipal organizations of various democratic parties have held meetings one after another to launch a signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons. The Central Executive Bureau and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party held a symposium on the 14th. At the meeting, many people unanimously stated in their speeches that they would take practical actions to defeat the conspiracy of the US aggressive bloc to launch an atomic war. The headquarters of the China Democratic League held a gathering on the 15th, the Beijing Municipal Branch of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce held a joint expanded meeting on the 14th, and the Beijing Municipal Organization of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a signature rally on the 15th. At these meetings, those present, in addition to those who had already signed elsewhere, signed on the spot against the use of atomic weapons.

### 各民主党派领导机关号召全体成员  参加反对使用原子武器签名运动

1955-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    各民主党派领导机关号召全体成员
    参加反对使用原子武器签名运动
   【新华社十六日讯】各民主党派领导机关分别发出了指示，号召各地所属组织和全体成员热烈参加反对使用原子武器签名运动。
    中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员会在十五日举行了会议，通过决议并电示各地组织积极响应世界和平理事会常委会的号召，动员全体成员和所联系群众参加反对使用原子武器签名运动和进行宣传工作。
    中国民主同盟总部也在十五日召开了会议，作了关于拥护“告全世界人民书”和展开签名运动的决议，并号召民盟各级组织和全体盟员广泛推动签名运动，以一切努力来反对原子武器的使用，使美帝国主义准备进行原子战争的这一灭绝人性的企图，在全世界人民谴责之下被彻底粉碎。
    中国民主建国会总会在号召该会各级组织和全体会员积极参加签名运动时指出：贯彻中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员联席扩大会议的“关于发动反对使用原子武器签名运动的决议”是全体会员当前的重大政治任务。
    中国民主促进会总部向该会各地组织发出通知，要求全体会员热烈响应反对使用原子武器的号召，在当地有关机关的领导下迅速行动起来，不但要自己签名，并要在所联系的群众中广泛深入地展开宣传工作。
    中国农工民主党中央执行局也发出了通知，号召全党立即积极参加签名运动，并通过各种方式把反对使用原子武器的正义号召普遍传到每一个成员和所联系的群众中去，使大家都能参加签名。
    在最近几天，各民主党派中央和北京市级组织纷纷集会，展开了反对使用原子武器签名运动。中国农工民主党中央执行局和北京市委员会在十四日举行了座谈会。会上，许多人在发言里都一致表示要以实际行动击败美国侵略集团准备发动原子战争的阴谋。中国民主同盟总部在十五日举行了集会，中国民主建国会北京市分会和北京市工商业联合会在十四日举行了联席扩大会议，中国国民党革命委员会北京市组织在十五日举行了签名大会，在这些会上，到会者除已在别处签了名的以外，都曾当场签名反对使用原子武器。
