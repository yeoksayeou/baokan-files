Title: Joint Statement by the Communist Parties of Syria and Lebanon Calls on the People to Thwart Imperialist Military Treaties
Author:
Date: 1955-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, February 21st: "Al-Telegraph" published a joint statement by the Communist Parties of Syria and Lebanon, calling on the people of both countries to strengthen their struggle against imperialist military blocs. The United States and Britain are trying to drag Arab countries into these blocs.

The statement, referring to the Iraqi-Turkish military treaty, said: "This sinister alliance, welcomed by Washington and London...is a heinous conspiracy by the imperialists against the people of Arab countries. The direct purpose of this alliance is to politically, economically, and militarily enslave Arab countries, restore foreign occupation of Syria and Lebanon, seize new territories in Syria and Lebanon, and incorporate them into Turkey. Moreover, it means that our country will pursue a policy of hostility and provocation, drafted by Turkey, towards the great socialist country, the loyal friend of the Arabs, and the defender of the national interests of all Arabs, the Soviet Union."

The statement indicated that in such an alliance, Syria and Lebanon "will be transformed into military bases linked to the aggressive Atlantic bloc."

The statement sharply criticized the domestic and foreign policies pursued by Iraqi Prime Minister Nuri al-Said, which violate the interests of the Iraqi and other Arab countries' people. The statement called on workers, peasants, and all upright citizens of both countries to work together to thwart any imperialist military treaties.

### 叙利亚和黎巴嫩两国共产党联合发表告人民书  号召人民挫败帝国主义的军事条约

1955-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚和黎巴嫩两国共产党联合发表告人民书
    号召人民挫败帝国主义的军事条约
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社贝鲁特二十一日讯：“电讯报”刊载了叙利亚和黎巴嫩两国共产党联合发表的告两国人民书，号召他们加强反对帝国主义军事集团的斗争。美国和英国正在设法把阿拉伯国家拉进这种集团中去。
    告人民书在提到伊拉克—土耳其军事条约时说：“这个受到华盛顿和伦敦欢迎的罪恶联盟……是帝国主义者对付阿拉伯各国人民的一个罪大恶极的阴谋。这个联盟的直接的目的是：在政治、经济和军事上奴役阿拉伯国家，恢复外国对叙利亚和黎巴嫩的占领，在叙利亚和黎巴嫩夺取新的地区，并且把它们并入土耳其。而且，这意味着我们的国家将对伟大的社会主义国家、阿拉伯人的忠实朋友和所有阿拉伯人的民族利益的捍卫者苏联实行由土耳其拟订的敌视和挑衅的政策。”
    告人民书表示，在这种联盟中，叙利亚和黎巴嫩“将被变为同侵略性的大西洋集团联结起来的军事基地”。
    告人民书尖锐地抨击伊拉克首相努里·赛义德奉行的违反伊拉克和其他阿拉伯国家人民的利益的对内和对外政策。告人民书号召两国的工人、农民和一切正直的公民共同努力，挫败任何帝国主义的军事条约。
