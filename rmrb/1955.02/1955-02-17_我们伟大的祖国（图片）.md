    Our Great Motherland
    After the beginning of spring, southern China already presents a scene of spring, while in northern China it is still the season when ice and snow cover the earth. This is a scene of young people in Jilin City skiing at the Beishan Ski Resort in Jilin City.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    立春以后，祖国南方已经呈现一片春天的景象，而在祖国的北方却仍然是冰雪笼罩大地的季节。这是吉林市的青年们正在吉林市北山滑雪场上滑雪的场面。
