Title: Ministry of Geology Convenes National Regional Hydrogeology Conference
Author: Chen Mengxiong
Date: 1955-02-24
Page: 2

    Ministry of Geology Convenes National Regional Hydrogeology Conference
    The Ministry of Geology convened the first National Regional Hydrogeology Conference in China's history from the 10th to the 17th of this month and established the Editorial and Review Committee for the Hydrogeological Zoning Map of China. Since groundwater is one of the country's important resources, whether it be for urban water supply, industrial water use, farmland irrigation, or the use of mine groundwater and medicinal mineral springs, it is inseparable from hydrogeological work. However, the current national hydrogeological work is far from meeting this need, so the meeting decided to rationally develop groundwater resources, and the first step is to compile a national regional hydrogeological map.
    Soviet hydrogeological experts Krylov and others gave reports at the meeting, introducing the development process of Soviet hydrogeology, and explained that China has a vast territory and very complex hydrogeological conditions. It is necessary to creatively establish China's own hydrogeological science on the basis of advanced Soviet theories. The experts introduced the advanced Soviet theories of latitudinal zoning and vertical zoning and also put forward preliminary opinions on hydrogeological zoning in China. Various production departments expressed their eager expectations for the compilation and review of the National Hydrogeological Map and unanimously guaranteed to cooperate vigorously and fully supply relevant data under the leadership of the Editorial and Review Committee for the Hydrogeological Zoning Map of China of the Ministry of Geology. (Chen Mengxiong)

### 地质部召开全国区域水文地质会议
陈梦熊
1955-02-24
第2版()
专栏：

    地质部召开全国区域水文地质会议
    地质部于本月十日至十七日，召开了我国有史以来第一次全国区域水文地质会议，成立了中国水文地质分区图编审委员会。由于地下水是国家重要资源之一，不论城市供水，工业用水，农田灌溉用水，以及矿山地下水与可供医疗的矿泉水的利用等，都与水文地质工作分不开，而目前全国水文地质工作却远不能满足这种需要，所以会议决定要合理开发地下水资源，首先是编制全国区域水文地质图。
    苏联水文地质专家克雷洛夫等在会上作了报告，介绍了苏联水文地质的发展过程，并说明中国疆土辽阔，水文地质条件十分复杂，必须在苏联先进理论的基础上，创造性地建立起中国自己的水文地质科学。专家介绍了苏联先进的纬度分带学说与垂直分带学说，又提出了中国水文地质分区的初步意见。各生产部门对于全国水文地质图的编审工作，表示了殷切的期望，并一致保证在地质部全国区域水文地质分区图编审委员会的领导下，大力合作，充分供应有关资料。　（陈梦熊）
