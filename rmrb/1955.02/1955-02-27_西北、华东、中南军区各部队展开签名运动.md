Title: Signature Campaigns Launched by Units of Northwest, East China, and Central-South Military Regions
Author:
Date: 1955-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report:机关、部队、军事学校机关、部队、军事学校Units and military academies of the Northwest Military Region, East China Military Region, and Central-South Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have enthusiastically launched signature campaigns against the use of atomic weapons.

    Units stationed in Lanzhou, Urumqi, Xi'an, Xining, and along the railway lines of the Northwest Military Region have all signed the "Letter to the People of the World." Literary soldier Cui Bawawa said while signing: "The Soviet Union uses atomic energy to create happiness for mankind, while the imperialists are preparing to launch an atomic war. This is absolutely intolerable to peace-loving people all over the world. We are soldiers who defend peace, and we must accelerate the construction of a modernized national defense force and resolutely smash the imperialists' sinister conspiracy to launch an atomic war." Wei Baosuo, a first-class anti-bandit combat hero from a certain unit, said while signing: "Our People's Liberation Army resolutely opposes war and the use of atomic weapons, but we are definitely not afraid of any atomic intimidation or blackmail!" Heroes and model workers from a certain organization and unit of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Urumqi, Xinjiang Province, solemnly held a signature rally on the 22nd, and all attendees signed their names.

    All 341 people's heroes and model workers who attended the commendation meeting of the East China Military Region Headquarters signed the "Letter to the People of the World." The 5th Company of a certain army unit that participated in the Battle of Yijiangshan Island solemnly lined up on February 20 at a signature rally and signed their names on a large "Letter to the People of the World." All commanders and fighters of a certain unit of the East China Navy also signed their names at a signature rally held on the same day. Naval combat hero Chen Lifu said: "Our signatures are a warning to American imperialism: if it dares to launch an atomic war, it will be destroyed!" Leading organs of the Shandong Military Region and various units stationed in Jinan held a grand signature rally on the 21st. Everyone who attended the meeting signed their names. In recent days, directly subordinate units of the Zhejiang Military Region and various service units stationed in Hangzhou have held separate signature rallies, and 100% of the commanders and fighters who attended the meetings signed the "Letter to the People of the World." The People's Liberation Army units guarding Xiamen City on the front lines of Fujian have also enthusiastically launched a signature campaign. Many units, with the exception of a few personnel on duty, have had all their soldiers sign their names. The famous combat hero, battalion commander Cai E of a certain unit, said: "We are defenders of peace and must stop atomic war. No matter how much American imperialism clamors, it will not shake our determination to resolutely defend peace and liberate Taiwan!"

    Units of the Central-South Military Region that began signing had all personnel sign their names except those on duty. Directly subordinate units of the Central-South Military Region and armored forces held separate rallies on February 17. Leaders of the Central-South Military Region also attended the rallies and signed their names along with all the commanders and fighters present. The Guangzhou Municipal organs and units of the Guangdong Provincial Public Security Corps held a signature rally on the 21st, and everyone who attended the meeting signed the "Letter to the People of the World." Zhang Yiqing, a combat hero from a certain unit, said: "We love peace and oppose war, but we will never be intimidated by the warmongers' clamoring and provocations. If the enemy dares to launch a war of aggression and use atomic weapons, we will resolutely wipe them out!"

### 西北、华东、中南军区各部队展开签名运动

1955-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    西北、华东、中南军区各部队展开签名运动
    据新华社讯：中国人民解放军西北军区、华东军区和中南军区的机关、部队、军事学校热烈展开反对使用原子武器签名运动。
    西北军区驻兰州、乌鲁木齐、西安、西宁和铁路沿线的各部队都已在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。文艺战士崔八娃在签名时说：“苏联把原子能用在创造人类的幸福上，而帝国主义却准备发动原子战争。这是全世界爱好和平的人民所绝对不能容忍的。我们是保卫和平的战士，我们一定要加速现代化国防军的建设，坚决粉碎帝国主义发动原子战争的罪恶阴谋。”某部一级剿匪战斗英雄魏保锁在签名时说：“我们人民解放军坚决反对战争，反对使用原子武器，但是我们决不怕任何原子恫吓和讹诈！”驻新疆省乌鲁木齐市的人民解放军某机关、部队的功臣、模范们在二十二日隆重举行了签名大会，到会的人都签了名。
    华东军区出席军区司令部机关庆功大会的三百四十一名人民功臣和模范工作者都在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。参加解放一江山岛战斗的陆军某部第五连在二月二十日举行的签名大会上，全连指挥员、战斗员庄严地列队在大幅的“告全世界人民书”上签了自己的名字。华东海军某部全体指挥员、战斗员也在同一天举行的签名集会上签了名。海军战斗英雄陈立富说：“我们签名正是警告美帝国主义：如果它敢于发动原子战争，毁灭的必定是它自己！”山东军区领导机关和驻济南市各部队在二十一日举行了盛大的签名大会。参加大会的人全部都签了名。浙江军区直属部队和驻杭州市各兵种部队连日来分别举行了签名集会，到会的指挥员、战斗员百分之百都在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。守卫在福建前线厦门市的人民解放军各部队也热烈展开了签名运动，许多单位除了个别出勤人员外，全体军人都签了名。著名的战斗英雄、某部营长蔡萼说：“我们是和平的保卫者，一定要制止原子战争。不管美帝国主义怎样叫嚣，都丝毫动摇不了我们坚决保卫和平、解放台湾的意志和决心！”
    中南军区开始签名的单位除了出勤人员外，全体人员都签了名。中南军区直属部队和装甲兵部队在二月十七日分别举行了大会，中南军区首长也出席了大会并和到会的全体指战员一同签了名。广东省公安总队驻广州市机关、部队，在二十一日召开了签名大会，到会的人都在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。某部战斗英雄张义清说：“我们热爱和平，反对战争，但绝不为战争贩子的叫嚣和挑衅所吓倒。如果敌人胆敢发动侵略战争和使用原子武器，我们坚决把他们消灭干净！”
