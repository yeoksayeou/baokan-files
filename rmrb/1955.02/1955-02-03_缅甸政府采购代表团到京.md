Title: Burmese Government Procurement Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-02-03
Page: 1
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: The first group of thirty-four members of the Burmese Government Procurement Delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Bo Min Gaung, Minister of Public Works and Reconstruction of the Union of Burma, arrived in Beijing by train on February 2nd.
    Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Chen Ming, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau, Shi Zhiang, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and others went to the station to greet them.
    Wu Maung Maung Toe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy in China, Wu Tao, Second Secretary, and others also went to the station to greet them.

### 缅甸政府采购代表团到京

1955-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府采购代表团到京
   【新华社二日讯】缅甸联邦政府采购代表团首批一行三十四人，由团长、缅甸联邦政府公用事业和复建部部长波敏贡率领，在二月二日乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站迎接的有我对外贸易部副部长雷任民、第三局副局长陈明、中国进出口公司副经理石志昂等。
    缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴茂茂铎、二等秘书吴陶等人也前往车站迎接。
