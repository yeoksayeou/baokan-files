Title: Soviet Government Appoints Representative to the Disarmament Commission Subcommittee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-02-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 18th. TASS reported: Andrei Andreyevich Gromyko, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, has been appointed as the Soviet representative to the United Nations Disarmament Commission Subcommittee meeting scheduled to be held in London on February 25th. Yakov Alexandrovich Malik, Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom, has been appointed as the Soviet Deputy Representative to the aforementioned meeting.

### 苏联政府任命出席裁军委员会小组会代表

1955-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联政府任命出席裁军委员会小组会代表
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十八日讯：苏联外交部第一副部长安·安·葛罗米柯已被任命为出席定于二月二十五日在伦敦举行的联合国裁军委员会小组委员会会议的苏联代表。苏联驻英国大使雅·亚·马立克被任命为出席上述会议的苏联副代表。
