Title: Over Two Hundred Overseas Chinese in Japan Return to China
Author:
Date: 1955-02-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, February 15th: The Japanese ship "Kouan Maru," which came to China to pick up Japanese nationals returning home, arrived at Tanggu New Port on the 15th. Two hundred and seventy-seven overseas Chinese from Japan also returned to their motherland on the "Kouan Maru."

　　Dajima Soji, director of the Japanese Red Cross Society's Sanitation Department, who accompanied the Chinese compatriots back home, and Sato Nobukazu, Yamamoto Torao, and Kosugi Toshiaki, representatives of the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japan Peace Liaison Committee, who came to pick up Japanese nationals returning home, arrived in Tianjin at noon on the same day. Thirteen Japanese journalists and sixty-three crew members of the "Kouan Maru" also came to Tianjin with them.

　　In the evening, Li Yunge, chairman of the Tianjin branch of the Chinese Red Cross Society, held a banquet to welcome Dajima Soji and others. Ni Feijun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Red Cross Society Headquarters, and Ji Feng, Director of the International Liaison Department, who came to Tianjin on behalf of the Chinese Red Cross Society Headquarters to welcome Dajima Soji and others, also attended the banquet.

　　The crew members of the "Kouan Maru" participated in a get-together organized by the North China District Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union.

### 我旅日侨胞二百多人回国

1955-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我旅日侨胞二百多人回国
   【新华社天津十五日电】前来中国接日本侨民回国的日本轮船“兴安丸”已在十五日抵达塘沽新港。中国旅日侨民二百七十七人，也乘“兴安丸”返回祖国。
    陪送中国侨民回国的日本红十字会卫生部长大岛宗二和来接日本侨民回国的日本红十字会、日中友好协会、日本和平联络会三团体的代表佐藤信一、山本寅雄、古杉敏秋在当天中午抵达天津。随同他们来天津的还有日本新闻工作者十三人和“兴安丸”船员六十三人。
    当晚，中国红十字会天津市分会会长李允恪举行宴会，欢迎大岛宗二等人。代表中国红十字会总会来天津欢迎大岛宗二等人的中国红十字会总会副秘书长倪斐君和国际联络部部长纪锋等也参加了宴会。
    “兴安丸”的船员们参加了中国海员工会华北区委员会举行的联欢晚会。
