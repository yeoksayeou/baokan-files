Title: The People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic Submits New Proposals to the West German Parliament, Suggesting Immediate Negotiations on Preparing for All-German Elections
Author:
Date: 1955-02-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 21st: According to the German News Agency (ADN), the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic submitted new proposals to the West German Federal Parliament on the 18th, suggesting immediate negotiations on preparing for all-German elections.
    The proposal suggests that the People's Chamber send a delegation to the Federal Parliament on the 24th to explain the People's Chamber's basic views on free and democratic all-German elections, election law, and international supervision. It also invites the Federal Parliament to send a delegation to the People's Chamber to explain the Federal Parliament's views on this election at the next plenary session of the People's Chamber. The proposal states: The delegation from the People's Chamber is also prepared to negotiate the proposals put forward by both sides and other issues related to reunification and guaranteeing peace.
    The proposal states: The People's Chamber has already appointed representatives for the negotiating delegation. Please inform the Federal Parliament when it can begin the suggested negotiations.

### 民主德国人民议院向西德议院提出新建议  建议就准备全德选举问题立即举行谈判

1955-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国人民议院向西德议院提出新建议
    建议就准备全德选举问题立即举行谈判
    【新华社二十二日讯】据德意志通讯社柏林二十一日讯，德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团十八日向西德联邦议院提出了新的建议，建议就准备全德选举问题立即举行谈判。
    这项建议提出由人民议院派遣代表团在二十四日到联邦议院说明人民议院对自由和民主的全德选举、选举法以及国际监督的基本观点，也邀请联邦议院派遣代表团到人民议院来，在人民议院下一次全体会议上说明联邦议院对这一选举的意见。建议说：人民议院的代表团还准备谈判由双方所提出的建议以及有关重新统一和保障和平的其他问题。
    建议说：人民议院已经任命了谈判代表团的代表。请联邦议院通知它什么时候能够开始建议中的谈判。
