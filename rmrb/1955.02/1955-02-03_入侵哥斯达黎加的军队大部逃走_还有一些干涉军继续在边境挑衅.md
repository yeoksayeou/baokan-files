Title: Most of the Troops Invading Costa Rica Fled, Some Interventionist Troops Continue to Provoke at the Border
Author:
Date: 1955-02-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: According to foreign news agencies, most of the interventionist troops who invaded Costa Rica from Nicaragua have fled back to Nicaragua and are being received by the Nicaraguan government.

Some interventionist troops continue to provoke in the border area of Costa Rica. According to the Costa Rican government, on January 28, Costa Rican patrols engaged in another battle with a group of invading elements in the northwestern border area. According to Costa Rican Foreign Minister Mario Esquivel, regular troops of the Nicaraguan government participated in this battle, and Nicaraguan government aircraft crossed the border and invaded the airspace above the area where the battle took place.

### 入侵哥斯达黎加的军队大部逃走  还有一些干涉军继续在边境挑衅

1955-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    入侵哥斯达黎加的军队大部逃走
    还有一些干涉军继续在边境挑衅
    【新华社二日讯】据外国通讯社报道，从尼加拉瓜侵入哥斯达黎加的干涉军队大部已逃回尼加拉瓜，由尼加拉瓜政府加以收容。
    还有一些干涉军队继续在哥斯达黎加边境地区进行挑衅。据哥斯达黎加政府宣布，一月二十八日，哥斯达黎加的巡逻队在西北部边境地区，同一股侵入分子又发生战斗。据哥斯达黎加外交部长马里奥·埃斯基维尔说，在这次战斗中，有尼加拉瓜政府的正规部队参加，同时尼加拉瓜政府的飞机又越过边境，侵入到发生战斗地区的上空。
