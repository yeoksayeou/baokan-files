    Our Great Motherland
    Workers at the state-owned Tianjin Printing and Dyeing Factory are printing newly designed linen fabrics to supply the people with summer clothing. This is a color comparison being done by the staff in the design room, comparing the printed linen with the designed patterns.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营天津印染厂职工正在印制图案新颖的麻纱，准备供应人民夏季的衣料。这是图案室工作人员，把印制成的麻纱和设计的图案作色调上的比较。
