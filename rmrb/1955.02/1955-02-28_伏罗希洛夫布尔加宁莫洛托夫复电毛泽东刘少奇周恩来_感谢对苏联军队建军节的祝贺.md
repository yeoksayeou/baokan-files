Title: Voroshilov, Bulganin, Molotov Reply to Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai: Thank You for Congratulations on Soviet Army Founding Day

Author:

Date: 1955-02-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 27th: Voroshilov (Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR), Bulganin (Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers), and Molotov (Soviet Foreign Minister) replied to Mao Zedong (Chairman of the People's Republic of China), Liu Shaoqi (Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress), and Zhou Enlai (Premier of the State Council and concurrently Foreign Minister), thanking them for their congratulations on the founding anniversary of the Soviet Army. The text of the telegram is as follows:

Beijing, Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and concurrently Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China

Dear Comrades, please accept our sincere thanks for your warm congratulations on the thirty-seventh anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy.

K.Y. Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union Of Soviet Socialist Republics

N.A Bulganin, Chairman Of The Council Of Ministers Of The Union Of Soviet Socialist Republics

V.M. Molotov, Minister Of Foreign Affairs Of The Union Of Soviet Socialist Republics

Moscow, February 26, 1955

### 伏罗希洛夫布尔加宁莫洛托夫复电毛泽东刘少奇周恩来  感谢对苏联军队建军节的祝贺

1955-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫布尔加宁莫洛托夫复电毛泽东刘少奇周恩来
    感谢对苏联军队建军节的祝贺
    【新华社二十七日讯】苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联外交部长莫洛托夫复电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇和国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，感谢他们对苏军建军节的祝贺。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东同志中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志
    当苏联陆海军建军三十七周年的时候，承蒙热烈祝贺，亲爱的同志们，请接受我们深切的谢意。
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团主席  克·叶·伏罗希洛夫
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席  尼·亚·布尔加宁
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部长  维·米·莫洛托夫
                 一九五五年二月二十六日于莫斯科
