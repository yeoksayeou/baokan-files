Title: Establishment of Mr. Zhang Lan's Funeral Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-02-10
Page: 1

　　Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Soong Ching Ling, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Li Jishen
　　Luo Ronghuan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong

　　Luo Ruiqing, Burhan, Ma Xulun, Peng Zemin, Luo Longji, Li Dazhang, Xiong Kewu, Shi Liang, Chen Qiyou, Xu Deheng
　　Hu Yaobang, Long Yun, Liu Wenhui, Deng Xihou, Gao Chongmin, Li Chunqing, Xian Ying
