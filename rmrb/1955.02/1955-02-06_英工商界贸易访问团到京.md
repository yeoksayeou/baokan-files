Title: British Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 5th: The first group of twenty-three members of the British Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation (one of whom had already arrived in Beijing), led by the head of the delegation, Barrow Hepburn, and the Vice Chairman and General Manager of Gale Company, Blot, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 5th.

The British Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation was organized by a liaison committee formed by a group of British manufacturers who visited the Berlin China National Import and Export Corporation representative office last year. It includes representatives from nineteen British industrial and commercial enterprises and the British Council for the Promotion of International Trade. They represent some of the major British industries and import and export companies in automobiles, electrical equipment, machinery, chemicals, medicine, and general merchandise. The delegation will discuss specific trade with the China National Import and Export Corporation and various export corporations in Beijing. That evening, Cao Zhongshu, Acting Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, hosted a banquet to welcome them.

### 英工商界贸易访问团到京

1955-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    英工商界贸易访问团到京
    【新华社五日讯】英国工商界贸易访问团首批一行二十三人（其中一人已先到京），由团长巴罗赫本和盖尔公司副董事长兼总经理布洛特率领，在五日下午到达北京。
    这次来我国的英国工商界贸易访问团，是由去年访问过柏林中国进出口公司代表处的一批英国厂商回英后成立的联络委员会所组织的，包括十九家英国工商界企业和英国国际贸易促进委员会的代表，他们代表英国的汽车、电工器材、机械、化工、医药、百货等一些主要工业和进出口公司。访问团在京将和中国进出口公司、各出口公司商谈具体贸易。当日晚，中国进出口公司代经理曹中枢设宴欢迎。
