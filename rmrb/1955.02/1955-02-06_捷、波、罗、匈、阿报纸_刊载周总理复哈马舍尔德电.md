Title: Czechoslovak, Polish, Romanian, Hungarian, and Albanian Newspapers Publish Premier Zhou's Reply to Hammarskjöld

Author:
Date: 1955-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague: Newspapers in Prague, Warsaw, Bucharest, Budapest, and Tirana all prominently published the telegram from Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on February 3rd.
