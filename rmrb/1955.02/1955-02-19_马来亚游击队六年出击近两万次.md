Title: Malayan Guerrillas Launch Nearly 20,000 Attacks in Six Years
Author:
Date: 1955-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, 18th: According to statistics released by the British authorities in Malaya on the evening of the 15th, since the British authorities promulgated the "Emergency Ordinance" in June 1948, Malayan guerrillas have launched more than 19,500 attacks. In January of this year, the guerrillas launched a total of 75 attacks. In these battles, the guerrillas killed and wounded more than 20 British soldiers and police officers.

### 马来亚游击队六年出击近两万次

1955-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    马来亚游击队六年出击近两万次
    【新华社十八日讯】新加坡消息：据马来亚英国当局十五日晚公布的统计数字，自从英国当局在一九四八年六月颁布“紧急法令”以来，马来亚游击队共出击一万九千五百次以上。在今年一月间，游击队共出击七十五次；在这些战斗中，游击队打死、打伤英国军队和警察二十多人。
