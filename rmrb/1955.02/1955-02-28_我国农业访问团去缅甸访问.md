Title: Chinese Agricultural Delegation to Visit Burma
Author:
Date: 1955-02-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 27th: At the invitation of the Burmese government, China has organized a People's Republic of China Agricultural Delegation to visit Burma. The delegation consists of seven people, led by Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, and left Beijing for Burma on the 27th.

### 我国农业访问团去缅甸访问

1955-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我国农业访问团去缅甸访问
    【新华社二十七日讯】我国应缅甸政府的邀请，组织中华人民共和国农业访问团赴缅甸访问。访问团一行共七人，由中华人民共和国农业部副部长刘瑞龙率领，在二十七日离北京赴缅甸。
