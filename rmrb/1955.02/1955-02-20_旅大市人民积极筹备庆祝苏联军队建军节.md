Title: Lüda City People Actively Prepare to Celebrate the Soviet Army Founding Day
Author:
Date: 1955-02-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, February 19th: The people of Lüda City are joyfully and actively preparing various celebration activities to welcome the upcoming 37th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

    The Soviet Army Command in the Lushunkou area, at the invitation of the Lüda City Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, is preparing to give more than 1,000 reports to people from all walks of life in Lüda City. These report activities began on the 15th.

    The Soviet Army's reports have been warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Lüda City. On the first day, more than 90 report meetings were held, attended by more than 45,000 people, including workers, farmers, fishermen, People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Lüda, government staff, cultural and artistic workers, medical workers, students, and street residents. Many people also held discussion meetings after listening to the reports.

    Preparations for the festival are also actively underway. On the eve of the festival, the city will simultaneously hold grand celebration meetings in areas such as Dalian and Lushun. The city will be decorated with lanterns and colored banners from the 21st to the 26th. People from all walks of life will also hold extensive get-togethers with the Soviet Army and their families before and after the festival. Workers from various factories, government staff, farmers, and street residents will hold gatherings to recall and discuss the deep friendship they have shared with the Soviet Army over the past ten years. At the same time, a "Great Friendship Pictorial Exhibition" will be held in the city, and the Soviet Army will be invited to visit Lüda City's state-owned and locally-owned factories and cultural and welfare facilities.

    In order to commemorate the Soviet Army and eternally cherish the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, Lüda City has begun active planning for the construction of the "Soviet Army Martyrs Memorial Tower" and the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall" and other permanent monuments. Currently, the Lüda City Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has sent staff to collect various stories of friendship between the people of Lüda and the Soviet Army over the past ten years for the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall."

### 旅大市人民积极筹备庆祝苏联军队建军节

1955-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    旅大市人民积极筹备庆祝苏联军队建军节
    【新华社旅大十九日电】旅大市人民正以欢欣的心情积极筹备各种庆祝活动，迎接即将到来的苏联军队建军三十七周年。
    驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部应旅大市中苏友好协会邀请，准备连续给旅大市各界人民作一千多场报告，报告活动已从十五日开始。
    苏军的报告受到旅大市各界人民的热烈欢迎。在开始的第一天，就进行了九十多场报告会，参加报告会的有工人、农民、渔民、人民解放军驻旅大部队和机关工作人员、文化艺术工作者、医务工作者、学生和街道居民等四万五千多人。许多人听过报告后还举行了座谈会。
    庆祝节日的准备工作也正在积极进行。在节日前夕，全市将分别在大连和旅顺等地区同时举行隆重的庆祝大会。全市将从二十一日到二十六日悬灯结彩。各界人民还将在节日前后和苏军及其家属广泛地举行联欢。各工厂的工人、机关工作人员、农民和街道居民等将分别举行集会，回忆、座谈和苏军相处十年来的深厚友谊；同时市内还将举行“伟大友谊画片展览会”，以及邀请苏军参观旅大市国营、地方国营工厂和文化福利设施。
    为了纪念苏军和永志中苏两国人民的友谊，旅大市已开始积极筹划建筑“苏军烈士纪念塔”和“中苏友谊馆”等永久性纪念物。现在，旅大市中苏友好协会已派出工作人员为“中苏友谊馆”搜集旅大人民和苏军相处十年来的各种友谊事迹。
