Author:
Date: 1955-02-04

Our Great Motherland
A unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, braving the severe cold of minus forty degrees, crossed snow-capped mountains over 6,000 meters high to deliver a large amount of grain to the herdsmen of the Guoluo Tibetan Autonomous Region in Qinghai Province, whose transportation was blocked by heavy snow. This is a picture of air force soldiers loading grain onto airplanes.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民解放军空军某部冒着零下四十度的严寒，越过高达六千多公尺的雪山，把大批粮食投给被大雪阻塞了交通的青海省果洛藏族自治区的牧民们。这是空军战士正把粮食装上飞机的情形。
