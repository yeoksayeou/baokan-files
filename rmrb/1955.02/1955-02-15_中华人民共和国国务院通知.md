Date: 1955-02-15
Page: 2

    Notice from the State Council of the People's Republic of China
    According to the resolution of the Fifth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress regarding the Draft Military Service Law of the People's Republic of China, the revised draft is now printed and distributed. People's Committees at all levels should immediately conduct discussions and solicit opinions from the people. The People's Committees of each province, autonomous region, and directly administered municipality are responsible for compiling and organizing the collected opinions and submitting them to the State Council before the end of April of this year, in order to summarize and revise them for submission to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress will further review this draft and submit it to the next session of the National People's Congress for discussion and approval. State Council, February 15, 1955

### 中华人民共和国国务院通知

1955-02-15
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国国务院通知
    根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五次会议关于中华人民共和国兵役法草案的决议，现在将修正了的草案印发，地方各级人民委员会应即进行讨论和征求人民意见，并由各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会负责将讨论和征求的意见集中整理，在本年四月底以前报送国务院，以便汇总修正，提送全国人民代表大会常务委员会。全国人民代表大会常务委员会将对这一草案再加审议，提请全国人民代表大会下一次会议讨论通过。国务院一九五五年二月十五日
