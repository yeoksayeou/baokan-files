Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Speeches on the Issuance of New Renminbi Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1955-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 20th: The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast speeches on the issuance of the new Renminbi from February 21st to March 4th (excluding Saturdays), from 18:45 to 19:00 each day. The program's main content includes popular speeches about the significance of the new currency issuance, exchange procedures, and related issues, as well as radio addresses by Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China, Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, economist Ma Yinchu, Qian Jiaju, and others.

### 中央人民广播电台  今起播送有关发行新人民币的讲话

1955-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台
    今起播送有关发行新人民币的讲话
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民广播电台从二月二十一日起到三月四日（星期六除外），在每天十八点四十五分到十九点的时间内，播送有关发行新人民币的讲话节目。节目主要内容有关于新币发行的意义、兑换手续和有关问题的通俗讲话，还有中国人民银行行长曹菊如、中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通、经济学家马寅初、千家驹等人的广播讲演。
