Title: Ode to Friendship
Author: Muhamad Ahya
Date: 1955-02-01
Page: 3

    Ode to Friendship
    By Muhamad Ahya, Deputy Head of the Indonesian Agricultural Delegation
    The tide rises and falls on the sea,
    In the daytime, the moon's light is unseen;
    But the friendship between our two nations,
    Will forever shine, never fade;
    The surging waves can sink ships,
    The violent torrents can shatter reefs;
    But friendship, cannot be destroyed,
    Even if fierce winds and raging waves engulf the sky.
    Bright flowers will eventually wither,
    Living people will also die;
    But friendly feelings,
    Can be maintained even through ten thousand generations.
    The bright moon illuminates the avenue,
    The brilliant stars, how beautiful;
    The People's Republic of China has boundless victory,
    Today's victory, shall be enjoyed for generations to come!
    The quiet flowing water, the gentle breeze,
    The vast sky is filled with tranquility and peace;
    Friendship, will always be nurtured,
    Constantly deepening, constantly growing.
    The hawk circles and soars in the sky,
    一心想攫食那和平的鸽子;
    永可想念的中华人民共和国呵，
    将在我们眼里远去，但贴近在我们心里。
    The hawk circles and soars in the sky,
    一心想攫食那和平的鸽子;
    Ever memorable People's Republic of China,
    Will fade from our sight, but remain close to our hearts.
    The taste of mandarin fish at the banquet,
    Is so succulent and sweet;
    Let us say thank you at parting,
    And hope we will meet again soon.
    (Translated by Xue Hexian, Proofread by Baren)

### 友谊颂
穆罕默德·阿赫雅
1955-02-01
第3版()
专栏：

    友谊颂
    印度尼西亚农业考察团副团长  穆罕默德·阿赫雅
    海上的潮水涨了又要退，
    白天里，不见月亮的光辉；
    但是，我们两个民族的友谊呵，
    永远灿烂，永不衰退；
    翻腾的海浪，会卷沉船儿，
    猛烈的狂涛，会冲碎礁石；
    但是，友谊呵，不可摧毁，
    哪怕它狂风怒涛卷天来。
    鲜艳的花朵终要凋谢，
    活着的人们也会死去；
    但是，友好的感情呵，
    虽经历万世，也能保持。
    明亮的月亮，照耀着大道，
    灿烂的星星，多么美妙；
    中华人民共和国胜利无疆，
    今日的胜利，世代永享！
    静静的流水，徐徐的和风，
    大空里充满着静谧与和平；
    友谊呵，将永远受到培养，
    不断地深厚，不断地生长。
    鸷鹰在空中盘旋飞翔，
    一心想攫食那和平的鸽子；
    永可想念的中华人民共和国呵，
    将在我们眼里远去，但贴近在我们心里。
    筵席上桂鱼的味道，
    是那么的肥美和香甜；
    让我们离别时说声感谢，
    还期望我们早日得到重见。
    （薛河献译，巴人校）
