Title: China's New Ambassador to Romania, Ke Bonian, Arrives in Bucharest
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th. Bucharest Dispatch: Ke Bonian, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Romanian People's Republic, arrived in Bucharest on the 15th.
    Those who greeted him at the station included Romanian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Rudenko, Bucharest People's Council Chairman Vidrashku, Bucharest Garrison Commander Lengu, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, Li Enqiu, and embassy staff, as well as diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from other countries stationed in Romania.

### 我国新任驻罗大使柯柏年到达布加勒斯特

1955-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我国新任驻罗大使柯柏年到达布加勒斯特
    【新华社布拉格十六日电】布加勒斯特消息：中华人民共和国新任驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使柯柏年，在十五日到达布加勒斯特。
    到车站欢迎的有罗马尼亚外交部副部长鲁登科、布加勒斯特人民会议主席维德拉希库、布加勒斯特市卫戍司令伦古，中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办李恩求和大使馆人员，以及其他国家驻罗马尼亚的外交使节和使馆人员等。
