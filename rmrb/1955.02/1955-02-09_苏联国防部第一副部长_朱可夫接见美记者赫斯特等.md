Title: First Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Zhukov, Receives American Journalists Hearst, et al.
Author:
Date: 1955-02-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 7th: The First Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union, G.K. Zhukov, received American journalists William Randolph Hearst, Kingsbury Smith, and Bob Considine on the 7th and held talks with them.

### 苏联国防部第一副部长  朱可夫接见美记者赫斯特等

1955-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部第一副部长
    朱可夫接见美记者赫斯特等
   【新华社八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日讯：苏联国防部第一副部长、苏联元帅格·康·朱可夫，在七日接见了美国记者威·伦·赫斯特、金斯伯里·史密斯和佛·康尼夫，并且和他们进行了谈话。
