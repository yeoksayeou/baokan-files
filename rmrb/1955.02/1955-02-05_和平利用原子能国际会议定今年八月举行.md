Title: International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Scheduled for August This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-02-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New York, 3rd dispatch from TASS: According to a United Nations Secretariat communiqué, the United Nations Secretary-General has sent invitations to the governments of sixty United Nations member states and twenty-four governments of countries that are not members of the United Nations but are members of some specialized agencies, inviting them to participate in the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy. This conference is scheduled to open on August 8th at the United Nations European headquarters in Geneva and will last for twelve days.
    The United Nations Secretary-General has decided to appoint H.J. Bhabha, President of the Atomic Energy Research Establishment of India, as the chairman of the conference. The Secretary-General will also appoint six vice-chairmen of the conference from candidates from the Soviet Union, the United States, the United Kingdom, France, Canada, and Brazil.

### 和平利用原子能国际会议定今年八月举行

1955-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    和平利用原子能国际会议定今年八月举行
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社纽约三日讯：据联合国秘书处公报，联合国秘书长已向六十个联合国会员国的政府以及二十四个虽非联合国会员国、但是一些专门机构的成员国的政府发出了请柬，邀请它们参加和平利用原子能国际会议。这个会议定于今年八月八日在日内瓦联合国欧洲总部会址开幕，会期是十二天。
    联合国秘书长决定任命印度原子能研究学会会长赫·格·巴巴为这个会议的主席。秘书长还将从苏联、美国、英国、法国、加拿大和巴西的候选人中任命会议的六个副主席。
