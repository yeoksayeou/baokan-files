Title: Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Technical Advisory Committee Established
Author: Lin Gang
Date: 1955-02-04
Page: 2

    Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Technical Advisory Committee Established

    The Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Technical Advisory Committee was established yesterday in Beijing. This technical advisory committee was formed by the Ministry of Railways, in accordance with the former Central People's Government Administration Council's "Resolution on the Construction of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge," and is composed of relevant domestic experts and professors in various fields such as bridge engineering, architecture, railways, highways, steel structures, reinforced concrete, geology, soil, and municipal construction. Its mission is to study and discuss complex technical issues related to the bridge's design and construction, and to conduct research and provide suggestions on the bridge's artistic design. The chairman of the Technical Advisory Committee is Mao Yisheng, and there are twenty-five committee members: Luo Ying, Ji Quan, Zhou Fengjiu, Jin Tao, Wang Zhuting, Wang Du, Tao Shuzeng, Cai Fangyin, Yang Kuanlin, Gu Yisun, Yu Chichang, Sun Lingxi, Li Guohao, Li Xuehai, Zhang Wei, Liu Huixian, Huang Wenxi, Yu Tiaomei, Gu Dezhen, Chen Shihua, Liang Sicheng, Bao Ding, Wang Jiqi, Zhao Zukang, and Li Wenping. (Lin Gang)

### 武汉长江大桥技术顾问委员会成立
林钢
1955-02-04
第2版()
专栏：

    武汉长江大桥技术顾问委员会成立
    武汉长江大桥技术顾问委员会昨天在北京成立。这个技术顾问委员会，是铁道部根据前中央人民政府政务院“关于修建武汉长江大桥的决议”，聘请国内桥梁、建筑、铁路、公路、钢铁结构、钢筋混凝土、地质、土壤、市政建设等各方面的有关专家和教授组成的。它的任务是研究和讨论有关大桥设计和施工中的复杂技术问题，对大桥的美术设计进行研究并提出建议。技术顾问委员会主任委员为茅以升、委员二十五人，为罗英、嵇铨、周凤九、金涛、王竹亭、王度、陶述曾、蔡方荫、杨宽麟、顾宜孙、余炽昌、孙令希、李国豪、李学海、张维、刘恢先、黄文熙、俞调梅、谷德振、陈士骅、梁思成、鲍鼎、汪季琦、赵祖康、李温平。（林钢）
