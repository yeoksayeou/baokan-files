Title: Ceylonese Minister to Burma, Gunasinghe, Hosts Banquet for Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-02-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 18th: Gunasinghe, the Ceylonese Minister to Burma, held a tea party on the afternoon of the 16th to entertain the Chinese cultural delegation visiting Burma. Head of the delegation Zheng Zhenduo and the delegation members attended the tea party, accompanied by Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma.

### 锡兰驻缅甸公使古尼辛哈设宴招待我国文化代表团

1955-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰驻缅甸公使古尼辛哈设宴招待我国文化代表团
    【新华社十八日讯】仰光消息：锡兰驻缅甸公使古尼辛哈在十六日下午举行茶会，招待在缅甸访问的中国文化代表团。郑振铎团长和团员们在中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明陪同下出席了茶会。
