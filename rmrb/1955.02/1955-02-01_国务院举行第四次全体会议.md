Title: The State Council Holds its Fourth Plenary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-02-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st January. The Fourth Plenary Meeting of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the morning of January 31st. Premier Zhou Enlai gave a report at the meeting on the issue of the Soviet Union providing China with scientific, technical and industrial assistance in promoting research into the peaceful uses of atomic energy. The meeting then adopted the "Resolution on the Soviet Union's Proposal to Assist China in Researching the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy". In addition, the meeting approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 国务院举行第四次全体会议

1955-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第四次全体会议
    【新华社三十一日讯】中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第四次会议在一月三十一日上午举行。周恩来总理在会上就关于苏联在促进原子能和平用途的研究方面给予中国以科学、技术和工业上的帮助的问题作了报告。会议接着通过了“关于苏联建议帮助中国研究和平利用原子能问题的决议”。此外，会议通过了各项任免案。
