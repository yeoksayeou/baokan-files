Title: Opening of the Second Session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the USSR

Author:

Date: 1955-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 3rd: TASS Moscow, February 3rd: The Second Session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the USSR opened on February 3rd in the Great Kremlin Palace in Moscow. The Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR convened at 12:00 noon. Attending the meeting were representatives of all the Union Republics and Autonomous Republics, national districts and provinces, as well as guests—representatives of the working people of the capital. Also attending the meeting were diplomatic envoys from various countries and foreign and Soviet journalists.

The meeting was opened by Vilis Lācis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities.

Based on his proposal, the following agenda was unanimously adopted:

I. The State Budget of the USSR for 1955 and the execution of the State Budget for 1953.

II. The international situation and the foreign policy of the Soviet government.

III. Approval of the decrees of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

At the joint meeting of the Soviet of Nationalities and the Soviet of the Union, scheduled to be held at 5:00 p.m., the report on the State Budget of the USSR will be presented.

The Soviet of the Union is scheduled to meet at 3:00 p.m.

### 苏最高苏维埃四届二次会议开幕

1955-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    苏最高苏维埃四届二次会议开幕
   【新华社三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：苏联最高苏维埃第四届第二次会议于二月三日在莫斯科伟大的克里姆林宫开幕。苏联最高苏维埃民族院在正午十二时开会。出席会议的有代表所有的加盟共和国和自治共和国，民族区和州的代表以及来宾——首都劳动人民的代表。出席会议的还有各国使节以及外国和苏联的新闻记者。
    会议由民族院主席维利斯·拉齐斯致开幕词。
    根据他的建议，一致通过了下列议程：
    一、苏联一九五五年度的国家预算和一九五三年度国家预算的执行情况。
    二、国际局势和苏联政府的外交政策。
    三、批准苏联最高苏维埃主席团的命令。
    在定于下午五时开会的民族院和联盟院联席会议上，将提出苏联国家预算报告。
    联盟院定在下午三时举行会议。
