Our Great Motherland
After the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway was opened to traffic, Sichuan Province's transportation industry has greatly developed. The rice produced in Sichuan Province can not only satisfy the consumption of the people in the region, but also be used to supply the needs of people in various places and national economic construction. This is a large amount of grain waiting to be transported at Chongqing Station.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    成渝铁路通车后，四川省的交通运输事业有了很大的发展。四川省盛产的大米，除满足本区人民食用以外，还可以用来供应各地人民及国家经济建设的需要。这是在重庆车站待运的大批粮食。
