Title: Preparing to Attend Asian Countries Conference: Japanese Circles Nominate Delegation Members
Author:
Date: 1955-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 25th: The Japanese Preparatory Committee for the Asian Countries Conference is actively organizing a 47-member Japanese delegation, including leading figures from political, economic, cultural, religious, and labor union circles, to participate in the Asian Countries Conference to be held in New Delhi in April.

According to Kyodo News, on the 23rd, the Japanese Preparatory Committee selected 32 representatives from among those recommended from all over the country, and plans to select all the representatives before mid-March. Among the 32 representatives already selected are: Jiichiro Matsumoto, President of the Japan-China Friendship Association; Kei Hoashi, former member of the House of Representatives; Tomi Kora, member of the House of Councillors; and Shinobu Tabata, former president of Doshisha University.

### 准备出席亚洲国家会议  日本各界推举代表团人选

1955-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    准备出席亚洲国家会议
    日本各界推举代表团人选
    【新华社二十五日讯】东京消息：亚洲国家会议日本筹备委员会正在积极筹组一个包括政治、经济、文化、宗教、工会等各界领导人物在内的共四十七人的日本代表团，准备参加四月在新德里举行的亚洲国家会议。
    据共同社报道，二十三日，日本筹备委员会已从全国各地推荐出来的代表中，选出了三十二个代表，并准备在三月中旬以前选出全体代表。在已经选出的三十二个代表中有：日中友好协会会长松本治一郎、前任众议员帆足计、参议员高良富、前任同志社大学校长田畑忍等。
