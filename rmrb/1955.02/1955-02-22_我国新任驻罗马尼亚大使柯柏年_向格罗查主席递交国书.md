Title: China's New Ambassador to Romania, Ke Bonian, Presents Credentials to Chairman Groza
Author:
Date: 1955-02-22
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, February 21st: Ke Bonian, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Romanian People's Republic, accompanied by Counselor of the Embassy Li Enqiu and others, presented his credentials to Peter Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, on the afternoon of the 19th. Present at the occasion were Romanian Minister of Foreign Affairs Simion Bughici, Secretary of the Presidium of the National Assembly A. Bunaciu, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs D. Ionescu, Acting Director of the Second International Relations Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ion Morozi, and Director of the Office of the Presidium of the National Assembly Ion Vlăbiei.

　　When Ambassador Ke Bonian presented his credentials, he delivered a congratulatory speech to Chairman Groza, who responded with a reply.

　　Ambassador Ke Bonian said in his congratulatory speech: Under the leadership of the glorious Romanian Workers' Party and with the help of the great Soviet Union, the Romanian people have overcome numerous difficulties, consolidated the people's democratic regime, restored and developed the national economy, and made the Romanian People's Republic a front-ranking socialist industrial nation.

　　The fraternal friendship between the peoples of China and Romania has been growing and strengthening over the past five years. Through economic cooperation, mutual assistance in technology and science, and cultural exchange, the friendly relations between the two peoples have greatly developed. As the peoples of both countries share the common ideals of building socialism and the determination to safeguard world peace, there is no doubt that this unbreakable fraternal friendship will continue to develop and consolidate with the advancement of the socialist construction cause of both countries, and make greater contributions to the cause of world peace.

　　Chairman Groza said in his reply: The Romanian people feel heartfelt joy for the achievements made by the glorious Chinese people. The People's Republic of China is playing the role of a great world power with a sense of responsibility, making practical contributions to the cause of international peace and security. The fraternal and sincere friendly relations between the Romanian people and the great Chinese people are growing day by day. The Romanian people believe that the further development of friendship between Romania and China will help consolidate the camp of peace, democracy, and socialism, whose strong pillar is the friendship with the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China.

### 我国新任驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年  向格罗查主席递交国书

1955-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我国新任驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年
    向格罗查主席递交国书
    【新华社二十一日讯】布加勒斯特消息：中华人民共和国新任驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使柯柏年，十九日下午偕大使馆参赞李恩求等，向罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查递交国书。在场的有罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长西·布吉奇，国民议会主席团秘书阿·布纳邱，外交部交际司司长迪·扬涅斯库，外交部第二国际关系司代理司长扬·莫罗齐，国民议会主席团办公厅主任扬·弗拉比耶。
    柯柏年大使递交国书时，向格罗查主席致颂词，格罗查主席致答词。
    柯柏年大使致颂词说：罗马尼亚人民在光荣的罗马尼亚工人党的领导下和伟大苏联的帮助下，克服了重重困难，巩固了人民民主政权，恢复并发展了国民经济，使罗马尼亚人民共和国走进了先进的社会主义工业国的行列。
    五年来中罗两国人民之间的兄弟友谊日益发展和巩固。通过经济上的合作，技术科学上的互助和文化上的交流，两国人民的友好关系有了极大发展。由于两国人民具有建设社会主义的共同理想和维护世界和平的决心，毫无疑问，这种牢不可破的兄弟友谊，必将随着两国社会主义建设事业的推进，不断地获得发展和巩固，并为世界和平事业作出更大的贡献。
    格罗查主席在答词中说：罗马尼亚人民为光荣的中国人民所取得的成就感到衷心的欢欣。中华人民共和国充满责任心地起着世界伟大强国的作用，对国际和平和安全事业做出实际的贡献。罗马尼亚人民和伟大的中国人民之间的兄弟般的真诚友好关系正在日益发展。罗马尼亚人民认为，罗中两国之间的友谊的进一步发展，有助于和平民主和社会主义阵营的巩固，而这一阵营的坚强支柱就是同苏联和中华人民共和国之间的友谊。
