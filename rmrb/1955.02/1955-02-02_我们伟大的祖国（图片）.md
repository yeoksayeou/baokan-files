    Our Great Motherland
    The autonomous regions of all nationalities in our country are systematically and step-by-step helping the minority nationalities to cultivate cadres and develop political, economic and cultural construction undertakings. This is Man Cang (center), a Mongolian combat hero and model Communist Party member, and Chen Lianxiang, an agricultural labor model, studying at the Ulanhot Workers and Peasants Accelerated Secondary School in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. They are borrowing reference books for their studies in the library.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国各民族自治区域正在有计划、有步骤地帮助各少数民族培养干部和发展政治、经济、文化建设事业。这是在内蒙古自治区乌兰浩特工农速成中学学习的蒙族战斗英雄、模范共产党员满仓（中）和农业劳动模范陈莲香。他们正在图书馆借阅学习用的参考书籍。
