Title: Soviet Minister of Health Kovrigina Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-02-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 19th:

    Soviet Minister of Health, Kovrigina, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 19th, at the invitation of the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China.
    Accompanying Minister Kovrigina on her visit to China was Sagatov, Minister of Health of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the People's Republic of China, Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yan Jingqing, Director of the Beijing Municipal Public Health Bureau, and others.
    S.G. Kocho, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the airport.

### 苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜到达北京

1955-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜到达北京
    【新华社十九日讯】应中华人民共和国卫生部邀请来中国访问的苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜，在十九日乘飞机到达北京。
    随同科夫里金娜部长访问中国的，有乌兹别克苏维埃社会主义共和国卫生部长萨加托夫。
    到机场欢迎的有中华人民共和国卫生部长李德全，外交部礼宾司副司长王倬如和北京市公共卫生局局长严镜清等多人。
    苏联驻我国大使馆参赞司高磋和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
