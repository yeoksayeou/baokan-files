Title: Shatter the Sinister Plans of the Atomic War Plotters
Author:
Date: 1955-02-28
Page: 4

    Statement by the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 26th] TASS, Moscow, February 25th: The All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions issued a statement saying that, representing 47 million Soviet workers, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions fully supports and warmly endorses the World Peace Council's appeal to the people of the world against the preparation of atomic war. The Soviet people believe that it is their sacred duty to actively struggle against the threat of a new war and to safeguard peace and the friendship of all nations. The Soviet trade unions warmly support the peaceful foreign policy of the Soviet government, which is waging an unyielding struggle for the reduction of armaments and the complete prohibition of the use of atomic and hydrogen weapons.

    The statement said that in the face of the danger of atomic war, the democratic and progressive forces of the world will unite even more closely under the banner of the struggle for peace, and will counter the aggressive policy of preparing atomic war with a policy of peace and peaceful coexistence of all countries and all nations, and with a policy of implementing friendly international cooperation. The Soviet people fully support the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on February 9, 1955, which pointed out a clear path for consolidating peace and developing friendly relations between countries.

    The statement concluded by calling on the working people of the Soviet Union to unanimously support the World Peace Council's appeal to the people of the world against the preparation of atomic war.
    Statement by the Secretariat of the Board of the Soviet Writers' Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 27th] TASS, Moscow, February 26th: The Secretariat of the Board of the Soviet Writers' Union issued a statement endorsing the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council to the people of the world.

    The statement said that the call of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council against atomic war and demanding the prohibition of atomic weapons fully conforms to the wishes of all the people of the Soviet Union. Soviet writers fully welcome and support this call. Soviet writers appeal to all writers in the world who cherish the interests of their own people to stand in the ranks of active peace fighters and contribute their talents and influence to the noble cause of stopping a new war. The struggle against the threat of atomic war is the direct responsibility of every honest person. At this decisive moment, no one has the right to stand idly by.

    The statement said that Soviet writers firmly believe that the united will of all peace-loving people in the world will shatter the sinister plans of the atomic war plotters. Peace will surely triumph over war.
    People in Various Places in Poland Held Rallies
    [Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 27th] People across Poland have been holding rallies to support the resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, demanding the destruction of existing atomic weapons and the cessation of the production of atomic weapons.

    The Warsaw Committee for the Defense of Peace and the Warsaw Municipal Committee of the Polish National Front jointly held a mass meeting of thousands of people in the sports hall on February 25th. After hearing the report by Jerzy Iwaszkiewicz, Chairman of the Polish Committee for the Defense of Peace, the meeting unanimously adopted a resolution fully supporting the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council against atomic weapons. The meeting also sent telegrams to the citizens of Paris and Berlin, hoping that they would act in concert with the citizens of Warsaw in the struggle against the threat of atomic war and against the revival of German militarism.

    Other major cities in Poland, such as Poznan, Krakow, Lodz, Opole, Olsztyn, and Czestochowa, have also held mass rallies to express their warm support for the resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council. The mass meeting in Krakow decided to send a telegram to the residents of Leipzig, expressing their willingness to jointly fight against the preparation of atomic war.
    A Signature Campaign Widely Unfolded in Various Places in Romania
    [Xinhua News Agency, February 27th] According to the Romanian News Agency on the 26th, the signature campaign in Romania against the use of atomic weapons is unfolding widely in various places. Up to now, more than 850,000 people in the three states of Ploiesti, Hunedoara, and Galati alone have signed the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council to the people of the world.

    Many people actively participated in the work of collecting signatures. There are more than 3,000 signature collection groups in the Ploiesti region, and more than 1,300 women in the Hunedoara region actively collect signatures in various places.

### 粉碎原子战争策划者的罪恶计划

1955-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    粉碎原子战争策划者的罪恶计划
    全苏工会中央理事会的声明
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十五日讯：全苏工会中央理事会发表声明说，全苏工会中央理事会代表四千七百万苏联职工，完全支持并且热烈拥护世界和平理事会关于反对准备原子战争的告全世界人民书。苏联人民认为，为反对新战争威胁、维护和平和各国人民的友谊而进行积极的斗争是自己神圣的责任。苏联工会热烈支持苏联政府的和平外交政策。苏联政府正为裁减军备、完全禁止使用原子武器和氢武器而进行着不屈不挠的斗争。
    声明说，在原子战争危险面前，世界民主和进步的力量将要更加紧密地团结在为和平而斗争的旗帜下，用和平及一切国家和一切民族和平共处的政策，用实行友好的国际合作的政策，来对抗准备原子战争的侵略的方针。苏联人民完全拥护苏联最高苏维埃一九五五年二月九日的宣言，这个宣言指出了巩固和平和发展各国之间的友好关系的明确道路。
    声明最后号召苏联劳动人民一致支持世界和平理事会关于反对准备原子战争的告全世界人民书。
    苏联作家协会委员会书记处的声明
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十六日讯：苏联作家协会委员会书记处发表声明，拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会告全世界人民书。
    声明说，世界和平理事会常务委员会提出的反对原子战争、要求禁止原子武器的号召，完全符合于苏联全体人民的愿望。苏联作家完全欢迎和支持这一号召。苏联作家呼吁世界上所有珍重本国人民的利益的作家站到积极的和平战士的队伍里来，把自己的才能和影响贡献给制止新战争的崇高事业。反对原子战争威胁的斗争是每一个正直的人的直接责任。当此决定性的关头，谁也没有权利袖手旁观。
    声明说，苏联作家坚决相信，全世界一切爱好和平的人的统一意志，一定会粉碎原子战争策划者的罪恶计划。和平一定战胜战争。
    波兰各地人民举行集会
    【新华社华沙二十七日电】波兰全国各地人民纷纷举行集会，拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会的决议，要求销毁现存的原子武器和停止原子武器的生产。
    华沙保卫和平委员会和波兰全国阵线华沙市委员会，二月二十五日在体育馆大厅联合举行了数千人的群众大会。大会在听取了波兰保卫和平委员会主席伊瓦希凯维奇的报告后，一致通过了一项决议，全力支持世界和平理事会常务委员会关于反对原子武器的号召。大会还打电报给巴黎和柏林的市民，希望他们同华沙的市民在反对原子战争威胁和反对复活德国军国主义的斗争中一致行动。
    波兰其他各主要城市如波兹南、克拉科夫、罗兹、奥波耳、奥尔兹丁和彻索夫等都先后举行了群众集会，表示热烈拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会的决议。克拉科夫市的群众大会决定致电来比锡市的居民，表示愿意共同为反对准备原子战争而斗争。
    罗马尼亚各地广泛展开签名运动
    【新华社二十七日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社二十六日讯：罗马尼亚反对使用原子武器的签名运动正在各地广泛展开。截至目前为止，单是普罗什蒂、洪尼多阿拉和加拉齐三个州就有八十五万多人在世界和平理事会常务委员会告全世界人民书上签了名。
    许多人积极参加征集签名的工作。普罗什蒂州有三千多个征集签名的小组，洪尼多阿拉州有一千三百多个妇女积极到各处征集签名。
