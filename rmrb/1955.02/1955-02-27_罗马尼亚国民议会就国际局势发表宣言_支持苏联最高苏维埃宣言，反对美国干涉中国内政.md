Title: Romanian National Assembly Issues Declaration on International Situation, Supports Soviet Supreme Soviet Declaration, Opposes US Interference in China's Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1955-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: According to the Romanian News Agency, the Romanian People's Republic National Assembly held a meeting in Bucharest on the 22nd. After listening to and discussing the report on foreign policy made by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, the meeting unanimously passed a declaration.

The declaration said: The National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic believes that the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union is a document of great international significance. If the foreign policies of all countries could be guided by the international principles put forward in the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the current serious threat to the peace of all peoples could be successfully eliminated.

The declaration continued: The Romanian people believe that the policy of reviving German militarism represents a danger. Due to American interference in China's internal affairs, a dangerous situation has arisen in the Far East, especially on China's Taiwan Island and other islands along the Chinese coast. The fact that the People's Republic of China has not been restored to its rights as a major power in the United Nations is abnormal and intolerable.

The declaration said that the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, aware of its responsibility in safeguarding peace and defending the interests of its people, has decided to take practical actions to strengthen ties with the parliaments of all countries. Any initiative to achieve this lofty goal will receive the enthusiastic support of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic.

### 罗马尼亚国民议会就国际局势发表宣言  支持苏联最高苏维埃宣言，反对美国干涉中国内政

1955-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚国民议会就国际局势发表宣言
    支持苏联最高苏维埃宣言，反对美国干涉中国内政
    【新华社二十三日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会二十二日在布加勒斯特举行了会议。会议在听取和讨论了罗马尼亚部长会议主席乔治乌—德治所作的外交政策的报告后，一致通过了一项宣言。
    宣言说：罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会认为，苏联最高苏维埃的宣言是一个具有极大国际意义的文件。假若各国的外交政策能够以苏联最高苏维埃的宣言中所提出的国际原则为指导，那么就可以胜利地消除目前对各国人民的和平的严重威胁。
    宣言接着说：罗马尼亚人民认为，复活德国军国主义的政策代表着一种危险。由于美国干涉中国的内政，远东地区特别是中国的台湾岛和中国沿海其他岛屿发生了危险的局势。中华人民共和国还没有被恢复它在联合国中作为一个大国的权利，这是一种不正常的和不可容忍的事情。
    宣言说，罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会意识到自己在保卫和平和保卫本国人民利益的工作中的责任，决定采取实际行动来加强同各国议会的联系。任何为实现这个崇高目标的倡议，都将得到罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会的热烈支持。
