Title: The State Council Holds Fifth Plenary Session
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-21
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, February 20th: The Fifth Plenary Session of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the afternoon of the 17th. After hearing a report from Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China, on the issuance of new Renminbi, the meeting approved the "Order on the Issuance of New Renminbi and the Recall of Existing Renminbi." After hearing a report from Liao Chengzhi, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, on the past status of overseas remittances and issues regarding improving future work, the meeting approved the "Order on Resolutely Implementing the Policy of Protecting Overseas Remittances." After hearing a report from Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on condolences to the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, the meeting decided to organize a "Delegation of the People's Republic of China to Extend Condolences to the Soviet Troops Stationed in the Lushunkou Area." The meeting also heard a report from Vice Premier He Long on the issue of streamlining the staffing of state organs and approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 国务院举行第五次全体会议

1955-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第五次全体会议
    【新华社二十日讯】中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第五次会议在十七日下午举行。会议在听了中国人民银行行长曹菊如所作的关于发行新人民币的说明后，通过了“关于发行新的人民币和收回现行的人民币的命令”。会议在听了华侨事务委员会副主任廖承志所作的关于过去侨汇工作状况和改进今后工作问题的报告后，通过了“关于坚决贯彻保护侨汇政策的命令”。会议在听了中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华所作的关于慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军的报告后，决定组织“中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团”。会议还听了贺龙副总理所作的关于整缩国家机关人员编制问题的报告和通过了各项任免案。
