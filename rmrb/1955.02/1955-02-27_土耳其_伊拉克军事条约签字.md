Title: Turkish-Iraqi Military Treaty Signed
Author:
Date: 1955-02-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, February 26th: Turkish Prime Minister Adnan Menderes and Iraqi Prime Minister Nuri al-Said signed the Turkish-Iraqi military treaty in Baghdad on the 24th. With this, Iraq has formally joined the system of military alliances orchestrated by the United States.

### 土耳其—伊拉克军事条约签字

1955-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    土耳其—伊拉克军事条约签字
   【新华社二十六日讯】巴格达消息：土耳其总理曼德列斯和伊拉克首相努里·赛义德二十四日在巴格达签订了土耳其—伊拉克军事条约。至此，伊拉克正式参加了美国策划的军事联盟的体系。
