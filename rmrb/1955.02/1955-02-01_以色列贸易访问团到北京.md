Title: Israeli Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-02-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 31st: An Israeli trade delegation of six people, led by David Hacohen, head of the delegation and Israeli Minister to Burma, arrived in Beijing by plane on January 31st.
    Those who went to the airport to greet them included Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Deputy Director Liu Xiven, and Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.

### 以色列贸易访问团到北京

1955-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    以色列贸易访问团到北京　
    【新华社三十一日讯】以色列贸易访问团一行六人，由团长、以色列驻缅甸公使戴维·哈科汉率领，在一月三十一日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有中华人民共和国对外贸易部第三局局长卢绪章、副局长刘希文、中国进出口公司副经理李应吉等人。
