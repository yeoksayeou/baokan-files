Title: Head of German Trade Delegation, Weiss, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-02-02
Page: 4

    Head of German Trade Delegation, Weiss, Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st] Gerhard Weiss, head of the German Democratic Republic trade delegation and Deputy Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane on February 1 to conduct trade negotiations for the 1955 Sino-German trade agreement.
    Xu Xuehan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of our country, Jia Shi, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau, Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department, and others went to the airport to greet him.
    The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Johannes König, the Commercial Counsellor, Dürrber, and Norbert, the Deputy Head of the German Democratic Republic trade delegation who arrived in our country a few days ago, also went to the airport to greet him.

### 德贸易代表团团长魏斯到京

1955-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    德贸易代表团团长魏斯到京
    【新华社一日讯】前来我国进行一九五五年度中德贸易谈判的德意志民主共和国贸易代表团团长、德意志民主共和国对外与国内贸易部副部长格哈德·魏斯，在二月一日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有我国对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒、第二局副局长贾石、交际处处长杨勉等人。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希、商务参赞杜尔伯及在日前来我国的德意志民主共和国贸易代表团副团长诺贝尔特也前往机场迎接。
