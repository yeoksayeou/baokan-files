Title: Celebrating the Victory of Our Army in Liberating Yijiangshan Island, Over 20,000 Soldiers and Civilians in a Certain Place in Zhejiang Held a Victory Celebration Rally
Author:
Date: 1955-02-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency report: People from all walks of life in a certain area of Zhejiang Province, who actively supported the Chinese People's Liberation Army in the battle to liberate Yijiangshan Island, together with the land and naval forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the local public security forces stationed there, totaling more than 20,000 people, jointly held a victory celebration rally on January 26 to celebrate the liberation of Yijiangshan Island and the major victory of annihilating the Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops on the island.
    The rally began at 9:00 AM. When the troops participating in the battle to liberate Yijiangshan Island appeared, people cheered along the road, and the sound of applause and firecrackers was incessant. After the Young Pioneers presented flowers and saluted the participating troops, the commander of the participating troops presented a flag to the Second Battalion, which had planted a large red flag on the main peak, Highland 203, of Yijiangshan Island, during the battle. During the flag presentation, prolonged applause and shouts of "Salute to the Liberation Army that liberated Yijiangshan Island" rang out in the venue. After receiving the flag, the representative of the Second Battalion stated on the spot that they would not be arrogant or rash, but would study hard, continue to improve their skills, and strive for greater victories. Then, the representatives of the participating troops gave a detailed report at the meeting on the battle achievements and the significance of the victory in liberating Yijiangshan Island. Yu Shukai, a representative of a naval unit that coordinated with the army to liberate Yijiangshan Island, thanked the people for their support on behalf of the participating troops and expressed that they would make persistent efforts to win greater victories to repay the support and love of the people of the motherland. Wang Shuwu, a representative of the local people's government, and Wang Zhizhong, a representative of the workers, also reported at the meeting on the achievements of the local people in supporting the battle to liberate Yijiangshan Island and called on people from all walks of life to heighten their vigilance, work hard in production, continue to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating the coastal islands and Taiwan, and resolutely eliminate the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique. After the victory celebration rally, the troops and people from all walks of life also held a grand get-together.

### 庆祝我军解放一江山岛的胜利  浙江某地军民两万多人举行祝捷大会

1955-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝我军解放一江山岛的胜利
    浙江某地军民两万多人举行祝捷大会
    据新华社讯：在解放一江山岛战斗中积极支援中国人民解放军作战的浙江某地各界人民，和解放一江山岛的中国人民解放军陆、海军部队及驻地公安部队共二万多人，在一月二十六日联合举行祝捷大会，庆祝一江山岛的解放和全歼岛上蒋贼军的重大胜利。
    大会在九时开始。当参加解放一江山岛战斗的部队出现的时候，人们夹道欢呼，鼓掌声和鞭炮声经久不息。少年先锋队员向参战部队献花、致敬后，参战部队的指挥员向在战斗中把一面大红旗插上一江山岛主峰二零三高地的第二营授旗。授旗时，会场上响起了经久不息的掌声和“向解放一江山岛的解放军致敬”的口号声。第二营的代表受旗后，当场表示要不骄不躁，努力学习，继续提高本领，争取更大的胜利。接着，参战部队代表在会上详细地报告了解放一江山岛的战斗事迹和胜利意义。协同陆军解放一江山岛的海军某部代表俞树楷代表参战部队感谢人民的支援，并表示要再接再厉，争取更大的胜利来答谢祖国人民的支援和爱护。当地人民政府代表汪树吾、工人代表王直忠也在会上报告了当地人民在支援解放一江山岛的战斗中的成绩，并号召各界人民提高警惕，努力生产，继续支援人民解放军解放沿海岛屿和台湾，坚决消灭蒋介石卖国集团。祝捷大会结束后，部队和各界人民还举行了盛大的联欢会。
