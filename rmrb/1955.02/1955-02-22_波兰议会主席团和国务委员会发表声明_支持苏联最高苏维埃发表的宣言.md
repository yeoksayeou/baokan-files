Title: Statement by the Presidium of the Polish Parliament and the State Council Supporting the Declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-02-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 20th: The Presidium of the Parliament and the State Council of the Polish People's Republic have issued a statement regarding the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

The statement said that the Presidium of the Parliament and the State Council of the Polish People's Republic welcomes the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union proposing that parliaments of all countries cooperate to maintain and consolidate peace. The Presidium of the Parliament and the State Council of the Polish People's Republic is deeply convinced that this initiative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union is in line with the wishes of the people of all countries and is conducive to the cause of peace.

The statement continued: The parliaments of all countries bear a serious responsibility for the fate of the world. Now, when aggressive military blocs are being pieced together or revived in Europe and other parts of the world, when plans to use atomic weapons confront mankind with new dangers, when the arms race is accelerating, and when a network of military bases aimed at aggression is being expanded around the peace-loving peoples of Europe and Asia, the responsibility of the parliaments of all countries is particularly significant. Cooperation between the parliaments of all countries may help to solve the most important international issues, and may become an important factor in promoting harmonious coexistence among the peoples of all countries, the peaceful settlement of international disputes, and the expansion of international cooperation in the economic and cultural fields. The Parliament of the Polish People's Republic strives to expand friendly cooperation with the people of all countries on the basis of mutual respect for sovereignty, equality, and independence, in order to promote the relaxation of international tensions and the development of peaceful and friendly relations among all countries.

### 波兰议会主席团和国务委员会发表声明  支持苏联最高苏维埃发表的宣言

1955-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

   波兰议会主席团和国务委员会发表声明
    支持苏联最高苏维埃发表的宣言
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社华沙二十日讯：波兰人民共和国议会主席团和国务委员会就苏联最高苏维埃的宣言发表了一项声明。
    声明说，波兰人民共和国议会主席团和国务委员会欢迎苏联最高苏维埃发表的建议各国议会进行合作以维护和巩固和平的宣言。波兰人民共和国议会主席团和国务委员会深信：苏联最高苏维埃的这个倡议，符合于各国人民的愿望，是有利于和平事业的。
    声明接着说：世界各国议会对于世界的命运负有严重的责任。现在，当欧洲和世界其他地区正在拼凑或复活侵略性军事集团，使用原子武器的计划使人类面临着新的危险的时候，当军备竞赛正在加速进行，以侵略为目的的军事基地网正在欧洲和亚洲爱好和平的各国人民周围扩建的时候，各国议会的责任就特别重大了。各国议会间的合作可能有助于解决最重要的国际问题，可能成为使各国人民和睦相处、和平解决国际争端和扩大在经济和文化方面的国际合作的一个重要因素。波兰人民共和国议会力求在互相尊重主权、平等和独立的基础上，扩大同一切国家人民间的友好合作，以便促进国际紧张局势的缓和和一切国家间的和平和友好关系的发展。
