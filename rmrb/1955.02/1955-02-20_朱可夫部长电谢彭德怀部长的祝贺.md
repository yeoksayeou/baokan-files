Title: Minister Zhukov Sends Telegram to Thank Minister Peng Dehuai for Congratulations
Author: Ge·Zhu Ke Fu
Date: 1955-02-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov sent a telegram to Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, thanking him for his congratulations on his appointment as Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China:

    Dear Comrade Minister! I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude to you for sending your congratulations and best wishes on my appointment as Minister of Defense of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

    Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union Marshal of the Soviet Union G. Zhukov
    February 16, 1955

### 朱可夫部长电谢彭德怀部长的祝贺
格·朱可夫
1955-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朱可夫部长电谢彭德怀部长的祝贺
    【新华社十九日讯】苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅电复中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀，感谢对他被任命为苏联国防部长的祝贺。电文如下：中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀同志：
    敬爱的部长同志！当我被任命为苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的国防部长时，承致祝贺和良好的愿望，我谨向您表示衷心的感谢。
    苏联国防部长  苏联元帅  格·朱可夫
    一九五五年二月十六日
