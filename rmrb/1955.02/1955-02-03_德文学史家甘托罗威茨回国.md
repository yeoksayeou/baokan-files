Title: German Literary Historian Kantorowicz Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-02-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: Alfred Kantorowicz, a literary historian from the German Democratic Republic, who came to China for a visit and lectures according to the 1954 execution plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing on the 2nd to return to his country.
    Kantorowicz came to China last November. During his stay in China, he visited Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai and other places. He held many reports and seminars, introducing the literature and writers of the German Democratic Republic to Chinese literary workers.

### 德文学史家甘托罗威茨回国

1955-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    德文学史家甘托罗威茨回国
   【新华社二日讯】根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问、讲学的德意志民主共和国文学史家艾尔弗雷德·甘托罗威茨在二日离开北京回国。
    甘托罗威茨是在去年十一月来我国的。在我国期间曾在北京、南京、上海等地访问。举行了多次报告会、座谈会，向中国的文学工作者介绍了有关德意志民主共和国的文学和作家的情况。
