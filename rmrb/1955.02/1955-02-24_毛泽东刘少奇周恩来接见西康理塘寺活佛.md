Title: Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai Receive Living Buddhas from Litang Monastery in Xikang

Author:

Date: 1955-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi, and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received Losang Tubdain Gyaincain Gyatso Kelsang, the Living Buddha of Litang Monastery in Litang County, Xikang Province, and Ngawang Tenzin Gelek Namgyal Rinchen at Zhongnanhai at 4 p.m. on the 23rd.

Present at the meeting were: Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China Wang Feng.

During the reception, Living Buddha Losang Tubdain, etc., presented brocade banners and hadas to Chairman Mao Zedong and others.

### 毛泽东刘少奇周恩来接见西康理塘寺活佛

1955-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东刘少奇周恩来接见西康理塘寺活佛
    【新华社二十三日讯】　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、国务院总理周恩来二十三日下午四时在中南海接见了西康省理塘县理塘大喇嘛寺活佛罗桑土登·降央克珠和昂旺定吉·阿称·罗赛。
    在座的有：国务院副总理邓小平、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉、国务院秘书长习仲勋、中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任汪锋。
    接见时，罗桑土登活佛等向毛泽东主席等敬献锦旗和哈达。
