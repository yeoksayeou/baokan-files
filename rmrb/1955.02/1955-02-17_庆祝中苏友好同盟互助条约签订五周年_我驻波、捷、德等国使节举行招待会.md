Title: Receptions Held by Chinese Ambassadors to Poland, Czechoslovakia, Germany and Other Countries to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance
Author:
Date: 1955-02-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 16th. Yang Qiliang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, held a reception in Warsaw on the evening of the 14th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

    Among those present at the reception were Rokossovsky, Vice-Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, Marshal of Poland, and Suhansky, Director of the Fifth Department of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Babalin, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in Poland, and diplomatic envoys from various people's democratic countries to Poland also attended the reception.
    The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere and did not end until late at night.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 16th. Cao Ying, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Czechoslovak Republic, held a reception on the 14th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.
    Invited to the meeting were David, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Czechoslovak Republic, Tarakanov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in Czechoslovakia, and all members of the Embassy, as well as many diplomatic envoys from various people's democratic countries to Czechoslovakia.
    The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 15th. Wang Yutian, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, held a reception on the evening of the 14th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.
    Among those present were Hanke, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, heads of various departments of the German Democratic Republic Party and government and various people's organizations, and people from cultural and journalistic circles, totaling more than one hundred people.
    Orlov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, representatives of the Polish, Czechoslovak, and Democratic People's Republic of Korea embassies in Germany, and senior generals of the Soviet forces stationed in Germany also attended the reception. During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the friendship between China and the Soviet Union and the common friendship between the people of China and Germany and the Soviet people, and toasted peace in Asia and Europe.
    The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年  我驻波、捷、德等国使节举行招待会

1955-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年
    我驻波、捷、德等国使节举行招待会
   【新华社华沙十六日电】中华人民共和国驻波兰大使馆临时代办杨琪良十四日晚在华沙举行招待会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年。
    出席招待会的有波兰部长会议副主席兼国防部部长、波兰元帅罗科索夫斯基，波兰外交部第五司司长苏汉斯基等。
    苏联驻波兰大使馆临时代办巴巴林和各人民民主国家驻波兰外交使节也出席了招待会。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行，直到深夜才结束。
   【新华社布拉格十六日电】中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国特命全权大使曹瑛十四日举行招待会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年。
    应邀到会的有捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部长戴维、苏联驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办塔拉卡诺夫和大使馆全体人员，以及各人民民主国家驻捷克斯洛伐克外交使节等多人。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
   【新华社柏林十五日电】中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办王雨田十四日晚举行招待会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年。
    到会的有德意志民主共和国外交部副部长韩克，德意志民主共和国党和政府各部门和各人民团体的负责人，文化界、新闻界人士共百余人。
    苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使馆参赞奥尔洛夫和波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国等驻德使馆代表，驻德苏军部队高级将领等多人也参加了招待会。席间，宾主间频频为中苏友谊和中德两国人民同苏联人民的共同友谊干杯，为亚洲和欧洲和平干杯。
    招待会在热烈友好空气中进行。
