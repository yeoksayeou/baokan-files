Title: Chinese Women's Delegation to Attend International Women's Federation Council Meeting in Geneva
Author:
Date: 1955-02-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The Chinese women's delegation of six (one of whom is already abroad), led by Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, left Beijing by plane on the 1st to attend the Geneva meeting of the Council of the Women's International Democratic Federation.
    The meeting will focus on: 1. The development of the international democratic women's movement since the Copenhagen World Women's Congress and the tasks of the Women's International Democratic Federation in the struggle for peace, friendship between peoples, and the rights of women and children. 2. Preparations for the World Mothers' Congress for the Protection of Children, Against War, in Support of Disarmament and Friendship between Peoples. 3. Supporting the resolutions passed by the recent enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, mobilizing broad masses of women to participate in the signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons, and other issues.

### 出席国际妇联理事会会议  中国妇女代表团去日内瓦

1955-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    出席国际妇联理事会会议
    中国妇女代表团去日内瓦
    【新华社一日讯】出席国际民主妇女联合会理事会日内瓦会议的中国妇女代表团六人（其中一人已在国外），由中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平率领，在一日乘飞机离北京去日内瓦。
    这次会议将着重讨论：一，自哥本哈根世界妇女大会后，国际民主妇女运动的发展以及国际民主妇联在争取和平、人民之间的友谊、妇女和儿童权利的斗争中的任务。二，筹备世界母亲保卫儿童、反对战争、拥护裁军和人民友好大会。三，支持最近召开的世界和平理事会常务委员会扩大会议通过的决议，动员广大妇女参加反对使用原子武器的签名运动等问题。
