Title: General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army Calls on the Entire Army to Participate in Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1955-02-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army recently issued an instruction calling on all military personnel to participate in the signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons. The instruction requires all units to regard this signature campaign as an important current political task. They should universally convene signature rallies based on their garrison locations. Responsible cadres should give reports to all military personnel on opposing the use of atomic weapons and call for signatures to be collected. Military newspapers and clubs should fully cooperate with the propaganda of this movement to educate all military personnel to establish the determination and confidence to oppose the use of atomic weapons, oppose imperialist aggression, liberate Taiwan, and defend the motherland and world peace.

### 解放军总政治部号召全军参加签名运动

1955-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军总政治部号召全军参加签名运动
   【新华社十六日讯】中国人民解放军总政治部最近发出指示，号召全体军人参加反对使用原子武器的签名运动。指示中要求各部队把这次签名运动作为当前重要的政治任务，以驻地为单位普遍召集签名集会，由负责干部向全体军人作关于反对使用原子武器的报告，并号召举行签名。部队报刊和俱乐部，应充分配合这一运动的宣传，以达到教育全体军人树立反对使用原子武器、反对帝国主义侵略、解放台湾和保卫祖国、保卫和平的决心和信心。
