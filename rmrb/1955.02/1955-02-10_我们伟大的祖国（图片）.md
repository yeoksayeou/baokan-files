Our Great Motherland
	The large-scale Guangxi Sugar Factory is under active construction and is expected to be officially put into production in early 1956. After the factory starts production, it will be able to produce more than 360,000 catties of white sugar and more than 15,000 catties of alcohol per day. This is a corner of the Guangxi Sugar Factory construction site.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    规模很大的广西糖厂正在积极兴建，预计将在一九五六年初正式生产。这个厂开始生产后每日可产白糖三十六万多斤，酒精一万五千多斤。这是广西糖厂工地的一角。
