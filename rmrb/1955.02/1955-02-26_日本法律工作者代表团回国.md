Title: Japanese Legal Professionals Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-02-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency: The Japanese delegation of eighteen members, including the head of the delegation, Nagano Kunisuke, the deputy heads Tozawa Tetsuhiko and Okabayashi Tatsuo, who attended the Asian Lawyers Conference at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society, left Beijing on the 22nd and returned home via Guangzhou and Hong Kong. While in Beijing, the delegation met with people from the Chinese legal community and held several discussions on China's legal and judicial systems, as well as education and economic aspects. The delegation also visited the Beijing People's Court, Peking University, and Renmin University of China.

### 日本法律工作者代表团回国

1955-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    日本法律工作者代表团回国
    据新华社讯：应中国政治法律学会的邀请来我国访问的参加亚洲法律工作者会议的日本代表团一行十八人，由团长长野国助、副团长户泽铁彦和冈林辰雄率领，在二十二日离开北京，经广州取道香港回国。代表团在北京时与我国法律界人士见了面，并就我国法律和司法制度以及教育、经济等方面的问题举行数次座谈。代表团还参观了北京市人民法院、北京大学和中国人民大学等处。
