Title: Czechoslovakia's Economic Plan Executed Well Last Year
Author:
Date: 1955-02-05
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th) According to a report from the Czechoslovak News Agency: The Czechoslovak State Statistical Office published a communique on the execution of the national economic plan for 1954.

    The communique stated: The revised gross industrial output plan for 1954 was fulfilled by 102.5%, and the total output increased by 4.4% compared to 1953. Among them, means of production increased by 4.2%, and consumer goods increased by 4.7%. The number of employed people in industry increased by 2%. Labor productivity increased by 2%.

    In 1954, through geological surveys, new coal mines and other raw material deposits were further discovered.

    In 1954, new technologies were further developed and adopted in the machinery manufacturing industry, construction industry, agriculture, and other national economic sectors. About 40,000 rationalization proposals have been implemented in industry.

    In agriculture, the sowing plans for grain and vegetables in 1954 were fulfilled by 101% and 109% respectively. The unit area yield of sugar beets increased by 3.8%. Livestock products increased by about 5%. In this year, agriculture received 8,900 tractors, more than 1,300 various combine harvesters, and many other agricultural machines. By October 1, 1954, the socialist sector in agriculture already accounted for 44% of the country's cultivated land.

    In 1954, both railway passenger transport and road transport overfulfilled the annual plan.

    The communique then discussed the improvement of the material and cultural life of the working people. The communique pointed out: Compared with 1953, the retail trade volume increased by 20%, and personal consumption increased by 16%.

### 捷克斯洛伐克去年经济计划执行情况良好

1955-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克去年经济计划执行情况良好
   【新华社布拉格四日电】据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社消息：捷克斯洛伐克国家统计局发表了一九五四年国民经济计划执行情况的公报。
    公报说：修改了的一九五四年工业总产量计划完成了百分之一百零二点五，总产量比一九五三年增加了百分之四点四。其中生产资料增加了百分之四点二，生活资料增加了百分之四点七。工业中就业人数增加了百分之二。劳动生产率提高了百分之二。
    一九五四年，通过地质调查工作，进一步发现了新的煤矿、和其他的原料蕴藏。
    一九五四年，在机器制造业、建筑业、农业和其他国民经济部门中，进一步发展和采用了新的技术。工业中已经实行了约四万件合理化建议。
    在农业方面，一九五四年谷物和蔬菜播种计划分别完成了百分之一百零一和一百零九。甜菜单位面积产量提高了百分之三点八。牲畜产品增加了约百分之五。这一年，农业得到了八千九百部拖拉机，一千三百多部各种联合机和其他许多农业机器。到一九五四年十月一日，农业中社会主义部分已经占有全国耕地百分之四十四。
    一九五四年铁路客运和公路运输都超额完成了全年计划。
    公报接着谈到了劳动人民物质和文化生活改善的情况。公报指出：和一九五三年相比，零售贸易额增长了百分之二十，个人消费量增加了百分之十六。
