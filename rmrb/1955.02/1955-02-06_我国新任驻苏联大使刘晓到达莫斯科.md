Title: China's New Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-02-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th. Liu Xiao, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic ofChina to the Soviet Union, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 4th.
    Those who welcomed him at the airport included: Kuznetsov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; Butusov, Deputy Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet; Major General Kolesnikov, Commander of the Moscow City Defense; Yudin, the current Soviet Ambassador to the People's Republic of China in Moscow; Kiselyov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ledovsky, Deputy Director of the Far East Department. Diplomatic officials from many countries to the Soviet Union also welcomed him at the airport.
    Wen Ning, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also welcomed him at the airport.

### 我国新任驻苏联大使刘晓到达莫斯科

1955-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我国新任驻苏联大使刘晓到达莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科五日电】中华人民共和国新任驻苏联特命全权大使刘晓，四日乘飞机到达莫斯科。
    到机场欢迎的有：苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫，莫斯科市苏维埃副主席布托索夫，莫斯科市城防司令科列斯尼科夫少将，现在在莫斯科的苏联驻中华人民共和国大使尤金，苏联外交部交际司司长基谢列夫、远东司副司长列多夫斯基。到机场欢迎的还有许多国家驻苏联的外交官员。
    中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办温宁和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
