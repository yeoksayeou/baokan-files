Title: My Olympic Committee Cables the International Olympic Committee to Protest the Invitation of the Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Sports Organization to Participate in the 16th Olympic Games
Author:
Date: 1955-02-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - The Chinese Olympic Committee (All-China Sports Federation) sent a telegram to the International Olympic Committee on the 19th, protesting that the Melbourne Organizing Committee of the 16th International Olympic Games invited the so-called sports organization of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique to participate in the games at the same time as inviting the Chinese Olympic Committee to participate in the 16th Olympic Games. The text of the protest telegram is as follows: To Mr. Mayer, Secretary-General of the International Olympic Committee, and to all members of the International Olympic Committee.

　　The Chinese Olympic Committee (All-China Sports Federation) replied to the Melbourne Organizing Committee of the 16th Olympic Games on December 20, 1954, stating that it accepted the official invitation to participate in the 16th Olympic Games to be held in Melbourne in 1956. However, it is reported that the Melbourne Organizing Committee has also sent an official invitation to the so-called sports organization of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, which has long been spurned by the 600 million Chinese people. We protest this unreasonable practice.

　　The Chinese Olympic Committee (All-China Sports Federation) is the only legal Chinese Olympic organization. The Chinese people and athletes will never recognize the so-called sports organization of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, which has long been unqualified to participate in any international sports activities. Therefore, inviting the so-called sports organization of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique to participate in the 16th Olympic Games not only seriously violates the provisions of the Olympic Charter, but also is not conducive to the normal development of international sports activities.

　　Therefore, we request the International Olympic Committee to revoke the Melbourne Organizing Committee's invitation to the so-called sports organization of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique.

　　Chinese Olympic Committee (All-China Sports Federation)

### 我奥林匹克委员会电国际奥林匹克委员会  抗议邀请蒋匪体育组织参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会

1955-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

    我奥林匹克委员会电国际奥林匹克委员会
    抗议邀请蒋匪体育组织参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会
   【新华社十九日讯】中国奥林匹克委员会（中华全国体育总会）十九日致电国际奥林匹克委员会，抗议第十六届国际奥林匹克运动会墨尔本组织委员会在邀请中国奥林匹克委员会参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会的同时，又邀请蒋介石卖国集团的所谓体育组织参加这个运动会。抗议电文如下：国际奥林匹克委员会秘书长麦耶并转国际奥林匹克委员会全体委员。
    中国奥林匹克委员会（中华全国体育总会）已于一九五四年十二月二十日函复第十六届奥林匹克运动会墨尔本组织委员会，表示接受参加将于一九五六年在墨尔本举行的第十六届奥林匹克运动会的正式邀请。但是，据悉墨尔本组织委员会竟同时将正式邀请书发给早已为六万万中国人民所唾弃的蒋介石卖国集团的所谓体育组织，我们对这一不合理的做法，提出抗议。
    中国奥林匹克委员会（中华全国体育总会）是唯一合法的中国的奥林匹克组织。中国人民和运动员决不承认蒋介石卖国集团的所谓体育组织，它早已没有任何资格参加任何国际体育活动。因此，邀请蒋介石卖国集团的所谓体育组织参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会，不但严重地违反了奥林匹克会章的规定，而且不利于国际体育活动的正常发展。
    为此，我们要求国际奥林匹克委员会撤销墨尔本组织委员会对蒋介石卖国集团的所谓体育组织的邀请。
    中国奥林匹克委员会（中华全国体育总会）
