Title: Strengthen Peace Forces, Crush the Threat of Atomic War: Signature Campaign Against the Use of Atomic Weapons Launched Enthusiastically Across Romania
Author:
Date: 1955-02-18
Page: 4

    Strengthen Peace Forces, Crush the Threat of Atomic War
    Signature Campaign Against the Use of Atomic Weapons Launched Enthusiastically Across Romania

    [Xinhua News Agency, February 17th] According to the Romanian News Agency: A signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons is being enthusiastically launched throughout Romania.

    In the capital, Bucharest, by February 15th, more than 218,000 citizens had signed the World Peace Council Standing Committee's "Appeal to the People of the World." Currently, 1,500 working groups composed of thousands of activists (including workers, intellectuals, and housewives) are going door-to-door collecting signatures.

    Argentine Peace Committee Decides to Strengthen the Movement Against Preparing for Atomic War

    [Xinhua News Agency, February 17th] TASS, Buenos Aires, February 16th: The Argentine Peace Committee held a meeting in Buenos Aires to discuss the resolutions of the World Peace Council Standing Committee and the situation in the Far East caused by the provocative actions of the United States against the People's Republic of China. The Argentine Peace Committee expressed its support for the resolutions of the World Peace Council and decided to strengthen the movement against preparing for atomic war and striving for the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons.

    In a resolution on the situation in the Far East, the Argentine Peace Committee demanded that the United States cease its provocative actions against China and that the People's Republic of China be admitted to the United Nations. The resolution called on the Argentine people to support the People's Republic of China.

    The Argentine Peace Committee also sent a telegram to the French Republic Senate, requesting it to reject the London and Paris agreements for the rearmament of West Germany.

    Uruguayan National Peace Committee Decides to Launch Signature Campaign Against Atomic Weapons

    [Xinhua News Agency, February 17th] According to TASS, Montevideo, February 16th: The Uruguayan National Peace Committee has decided to launch a broad movement to collect signatures for the "Appeal to the People of the World" against atomic weapons. The National Peace Committee also decided to convene a national conference for the defense of peace in April this year to discuss the tasks of the peace movement in the current international situation.

    The Executive Committee of the National Peace Committee also sent a telegram to the French Republic Senate, requesting it to reject the Paris agreements.

    West German Atomic Scientist Otto Hahn Calls on People to Oppose Atomic War

    [Xinhua News Agency] Hamburg dispatch: According to the Associated Press, West German atomic scientist Professor Otto Hahn, in a radio address on scientific issues on the evening of the 13th, called on people to oppose atomic war and demanded negotiations to resolve the problem of peaceful coexistence between countries.

    Professor Otto Hahn said: "No one knows the fatal danger (of atomic war) more clearly than atomic scientists." He asked politicians of all countries to "solve their problems at the conference table," and said: "Agreement must be reached on the peaceful coexistence of all peoples, even if their ideologies are fundamentally different."

    Otto Hahn also called for the application of atomic science to peaceful endeavors. He pointed out the enormous role of the element "cobalt," used in the manufacture of hydrogen bombs, in industry and agriculture.

### 加强和平力量，粉碎原子战争威胁  罗马尼亚各地热烈展开反对使用原子武器签名运动

1955-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    加强和平力量，粉碎原子战争威胁
    罗马尼亚各地热烈展开反对使用原子武器签名运动
    【新华社十七日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社消息：罗马尼亚全国各地正在热烈展开反对使用原子武器的签名运动。
    在首都布加勒斯特，到二月十五日为止，已经有二十一万八千多个市民在世界和平理事会常务委员会“告全世界人民书”上签了名。现在，由几千个积极分子（其中包括工人、知识分子和家庭主妇等）组成的一千五百个工作小组，正在挨家挨户地征集签名。
    阿根廷和平委员会决定加强反对准备原子战争的运动
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯十六日讯：阿根廷和平委员会在布宜诺斯艾利斯举行会议，讨论了世界和平理事会常务委员会的决议和由于美国对中华人民共和国的挑衅行动而造成的远东局势。阿根廷和平委员会表示拥护世界和平理事会的决议，并且决定加强反对准备原子战争、争取禁止原子武器和氢武器的运动。
    阿根廷和平委员会在关于远东局势的决议中，要求制止美国对中国的挑衅行动，并且接纳中华人民共和国进入联合国。决议号召阿根廷人民支持中华人民共和国。
    阿根廷和平委员会并且致电法国共和国参议院，要求它否决重新武装西德的伦敦和巴黎协定。
    乌拉圭全国和平委员会决定发动反对原子武器签名运动
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社蒙得维的亚十六日讯：乌拉圭全国和平委员会决定展开一个广泛的运动，为反对原子武器的“告全世界人民书”征集签名。全国和平委员会还决定在今年四月召开一次全国保卫和平大会，讨论和平运动在目前国际形势下的任务。
    全国和平委员会执行委员会还致电法国共和国参议院，要求否决巴黎协定。
    西德原子科学家奥托·哈恩呼吁人们反对原子战争
    【新华社讯】汉堡消息：据美联社报道，西德原子科学家奥托·哈恩教授十三日晚在就科学问题发表广播演说时，吁请人们反对原子战争，要求谈判解决各国和平共处问题。
    奥托·哈恩教授说：“没有比原子科学家更加清楚地知道（原子战争）致命的危险。”他要求各国政治家“在会议桌上解决他们的问题”，他说：“必须在各国人民和平共处的问题上取得协议，即使他们的观念形态有根本的不同”。
    奥托·哈恩并且要求把原子科学应用于和平事业。他指出制造氢弹的元素“钴”在工业和农业上的巨大作用。
