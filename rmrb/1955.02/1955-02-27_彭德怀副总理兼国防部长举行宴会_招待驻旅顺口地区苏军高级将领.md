Title: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai Holds Banquet to Entertain Senior Soviet Military Officers Stationed in the Lushunkou Area
Author:
Date: 1955-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Luda, February 26th: Peng Dehuai, head of the People's Republic of China delegation to express regards to the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, and Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet in Luda City on the afternoon of the 26th to entertain senior Soviet military officers stationed in the Lushunkou area.

Invited to attend were senior Soviet military officers stationed in the Lushunkou area, and the Soviet Consul General in Dalian, Belyanov, and others.

Attending as guests were the deputy heads of the delegation: Song Qingling, He Long, Guo Moruo, Nie Rongzhen, and all the delegates, as well as the principal responsible persons of the Party and government in Luda City.

More than 200 people attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Nie Rongzhen, the deputy head of the People's Republic of China delegation to express regards to the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, delivered a speech. He first expressed his gratitude to the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area for their great contributions to safeguarding the security of the Soviet Union and China and maintaining peace in the Far East over the past five years. He said: According to the agreement between the Chinese and Soviet governments, the Soviet armed forces will withdraw from the Lushunkou naval base, and you will gradually leave our country soon. After you leave our country, I firmly believe that the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which takes the great Soviet armed forces as a model for learning and has been tempered in long-term combat, will surely accomplish the glorious tasks assigned to it by the Party and the government.

Then, the representatives of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area spoke, expressing their deep gratitude for the solicitude and concern of the Government of the People's Republic of China, and wishing the great friendship between the people and the armies of the Soviet Union and China to continue to develop and consolidate.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of close friendship and warmth.

After the banquet, a dance was held.

### 彭德怀副总理兼国防部长举行宴会  招待驻旅顺口地区苏军高级将领

1955-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀副总理兼国防部长举行宴会
    招待驻旅顺口地区苏军高级将领
    【新华社旅大二十六日电】中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团团长、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，二十六日下午在旅大市设宴招待驻旅顺口地区苏军高级将领。
    应邀出席的，有驻旅顺口地区苏军高级将领，苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫等。
    出席作陪的，有代表团副团长：宋庆龄、贺龙、郭沫若、聂荣臻和全体代表，以及旅大市党政负责人。
    出席宴会的共有二百多人。
    宴会上，中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团副团长、中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻首先致词，他首先对驻旅顺口地区苏军五年来在保卫苏中两国安全、维护远东和平的事业中所作的伟大贡献表示感谢。他说：根据中苏两国政府的协议，苏联武装部队将从旅顺口海军根据地撤离，你们不久将陆续离开我国。在你们离开我国之后，我深信：以伟大苏联武装部队作为学习榜样的和久经战斗锻炼的中国人民解放军，定能完成党和政府所给予它的光荣任务。
    接着，驻旅顺口地区苏军代表讲话，对中华人民共和国政府的慰问和关怀表示深切的感激，并祝苏中两国人民及其军队的伟大友谊继续发展和巩固。
    宴会在亲密友好和热烈的空气中进行。
    宴会后，举行了舞会。
