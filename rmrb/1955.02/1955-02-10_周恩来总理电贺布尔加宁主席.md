Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Telegram to Chairman Bulganin to Congratulate Him
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1955-02-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 9th -- Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Bulganin to congratulate him on his appointment as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. The telegram reads as follows:

Comrade N.A. Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

I am delighted to learn of your appointment as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. Comrade Chairman, please allow me, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, to extend to you my heartfelt and warm congratulations.

I am confident that, under the leadership of the united and rock-solid Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, you will achieve great success in the great cause of the Soviet people building communism and defending world peace.

Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
February 9, 1955

### 周恩来总理电贺布尔加宁主席
周恩来
1955-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理电贺布尔加宁主席
    【新华社九日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来致电布尔加宁祝贺他被任命为苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席。电文如下：
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁同志：
    欣悉您被任命为苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席，主席同志，请您允许我代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向您致衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    我相信，您在团结一致、坚如磐石的苏联共产党中央委员会的领导下，一定会在伟大的苏联人民建设共产主义和保卫世界和平的事业中获得巨大的成就。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
    一九五五年二月九日
