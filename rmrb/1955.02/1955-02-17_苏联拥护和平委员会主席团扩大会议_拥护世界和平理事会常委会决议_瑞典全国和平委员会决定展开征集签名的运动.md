Title: Enlarged Meeting of the Presidium of the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace Endorses Resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council; Swedish National Peace Committee Decides to Launch Signature Collection Campaign
Author:
Date: 1955-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 16th: TASS reports from Moscow on February 15th: The Presidium of the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace recently held an enlarged meeting. Representatives from the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Soviet Academy of Sciences, the Soviet Writers' Union, the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Soviet Slavic Committee, and committees for the defense of peace and other mass organizations from many Soviet republics and regions attended the meeting.

　　The meeting heard a report by Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace, on the results of the work of the enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council held in Vienna in January.

　　The meeting also heard a report by Korneychuk, who spoke about the recent talks in Warsaw between the delegation of the European Conference Against the Arming of West Germany and representatives of various circles of society in the Soviet Union and people's democracies.

　　The meeting adopted a resolution on Tikhonov's report stating that the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace believes that the resolutions adopted by the World Peace Council reflect the aspirations of the people of all countries, who are opposing the threat of a new war, opposing the policy of reviving German militarism, and resolutely demanding the rejection of the London and Paris agreements, the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons, and the destruction of stockpiles of such weapons. The Presidium of the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace fully supports and endorses all the resolutions of the enlarged Vienna meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

　　The resolution continued: The Soviet people are filled with an unwavering determination to uphold the cause of peace. The Soviet Union's peaceful foreign policy has once again been clearly confirmed in the resolutions of the just-concluded session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and in the declaration adopted by the session. The Soviet government reiterated its willingness to conclude without delay an agreement on the prohibition of the use of atomic weapons and the use of atomic energy solely for peaceful purposes. The Soviet Union fully supports the World Peace Council's proposal for the universal destruction of atomic weapon stockpiles by all countries.

　　The resolution concluded: "The Presidium of the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace is deeply convinced that the resolutions of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council will be warmly welcomed and fully supported by all peace-loving people of the Soviet Union. The entire Soviet people will express their support for the World Peace Council's call to strive for the destruction of atomic weapon stockpiles and the prohibition of the manufacture of atomic weapons."

　　The Presidium of the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace also decided to convene an enlarged plenary meeting of the Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace in the first half of March this year, with the participation of representatives from the committees for the defense of peace in various regions, territories, and republics, in order to discuss the launch of the signature campaign for the World Peace Council's Appeal to the People of the World in the Soviet Union and the convocation of the Fifth All-Union Congress of Peace Supporters.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, February 16th: TASS reports from Stockholm on February 15th: The Swedish National Peace Committee discussed the resolutions of the Vienna meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council and fully endorsed the Standing Committee's Appeal to the People of the World calling for all countries to destroy stockpiles of atomic weapons and immediately cease the manufacture of atomic weapons.

　　The Swedish National Peace Committee decided to launch a nationwide signature collection campaign for the Appeal to the People of the World and called on all trade union organizations, political groups, religious organizations, cultural organizations, and sports organizations to support the demand for the prohibition of atomic weapons.

### 苏联拥护和平委员会主席团扩大会议  拥护世界和平理事会常委会决议  瑞典全国和平委员会决定展开征集签名的运动

1955-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联拥护和平委员会主席团扩大会议
    拥护世界和平理事会常委会决议
    瑞典全国和平委员会决定展开征集签名的运动
   【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：苏联拥护和平委员会主席团最近举行了扩大会议。参加会议的有全苏工会中央理事会、苏联科学院、苏联作家协会、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会、苏联青年反法西斯委员会、苏联对外文化协会、苏联斯拉夫委员会，以及苏联许多加盟共和国和州的拥护和平委员会及其他群众团体的代表。
    会议听取了苏联拥护和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫关于一月间在维也纳举行的世界和平理事会常务委员会扩大会议的工作结果的报告。
    会议还听取了考涅楚克的报告，他谈到了欧洲反对武装西德会议代表团同苏联和人民民主国家社会各界代表最近在华沙举行会谈的情况。
    会议就吉洪诺夫的报告通过决议说：苏联拥护和平委员会认为，世界和平理事会所通过的决议反映了各国人民的愿望，他们正在反对新战争威胁，反对复活德国军国主义的政策，坚决要求拒绝伦敦和巴黎协定，禁止原子武器、氢武器和销毁这类武器的储存。苏联拥护和平委员会主席团完全支持和拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会维也纳扩大会议的各项决议。
    决议接着说：苏联人民充满着维护和平事业的坚定不移的决心。苏联的和平外交政策在刚刚闭幕的苏联最高苏维埃会议的决议中，在会议所通过的宣言中再一次得到了明确的证实。苏联政府重申，它愿意毫不拖延地缔结关于禁止使用原子武器和原子能只用于和平目的的协定。苏联完全支持世界和平理事会关于各国一律销毁原子武器的储存的建议。
    决议最后说：“苏联拥护和平委员会主席团深信，世界和平理事会常务委员会的决议将得到苏联全体爱好和平的人民的热烈赞同和充分支持。全体苏联人民将表示拥护世界和平理事会关于争取销毁原子武器储存和禁止制造原子武器的号召。”
    苏联拥护和平委员会主席团还决定，在今年三月上半月召开有各州、边疆区和加盟共和国的拥护和平委员会的代表参加的苏联拥护和平委员会扩大全体会议，以便讨论关于在苏联展开世界和平理事会告全世界人民书的签名运动和召开第五届全苏和平拥护者代表会议的问题。
   【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩十五日讯：瑞典全国和平委员会讨论了世界和平理事会常务委员会维也纳会议的决议，完全拥护常务委员会的要求各国销毁储存的原子武器和立即停止制造原子武器的告全世界人民书。
    瑞典全国和平委员会决定在全国展开一个为告全世界人民书征集签名的运动，并且号召各工会组织、政治团体、宗教组织、文化组织和体育组织拥护禁止原子武器的要求。
