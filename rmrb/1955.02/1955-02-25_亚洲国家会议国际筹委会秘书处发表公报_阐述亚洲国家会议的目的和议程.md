Title: Asian Nations Conference International Preparatory Committee Secretariat Issues Communiqué: Explaining the Purpose and Agenda of the Asian Nations Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-02-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 24th: The Secretariat of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian Nations Conference (formerly translated as the Asian Nations Conference on Easing International Tensions) issued a communiqué on the 22nd, explaining the purpose and agenda of the Asian Nations Conference to be held in early April.

    Regarding the purpose of the Asian Nations Conference, the communiqué stated, "The purpose of the conference will be to provide an opportunity for the exchange of views on common issues concerning Asia. This exchange of views will help the people of Asian countries to reach a further common understanding and close contact in all aspects, thereby helping to ease the current world tensions."

    The communiqué pointed out that the first item on the agenda of the conference will be the discussion of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence jointly announced by the Prime Ministers of China and India. The conference will plan specific steps to enable the people of Asian countries to understand and appreciate these principles, and to have these principles accepted by Asian countries and the rest of the world, so that they may constitute a solid foundation for peace and security.

    The communiqué also stated that the conference will also discuss the issue of banning and controlling weapons of mass destruction, colonialism and foreign interference in the internal affairs of Asian countries, the dangers of establishing military alliances and conventions to divide Asian countries and the establishment of foreign military bases in Asia, the recognition of the legitimate status of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations, the normalization of diplomatic relations between Asian countries, the restoration of the unification of Korea by peaceful means, and racial discrimination. The conference will also discuss the development of trade between Asian countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit, the economic development of Asian countries and the improvement of living standards, as well as issues related to science, society, and culture.

    The communiqué said that the draft on the purpose and agenda of the Asian Nations Conference was unanimously approved at the meeting of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian Nations Conference held in New Delhi from February 9th to 10th. The representatives of China and the Soviet Union arrived in New Delhi after the meeting of the International Preparatory Committee. The representatives of China and the Soviet Union fully supported this draft.

    The communiqué stated that the representatives of Burma, Ceylon, India, Japan, Korea, and Syria who participated in the meeting of the International Preparatory Committee unanimously agreed that there is an urgent need for closer cooperation among Asian countries in political, economic, scientific, and cultural fields. They also believed that Asian problems cannot be permanently resolved without full and active cooperation by Asian countries themselves.

    The representatives believed that the economic and industrial development of Asia can be promoted and the living standards of the broad masses of the Asian people can be improved through greater trade and exchange of technical knowledge.

    The representatives also expressed great concern about the threat of weapons of mass destruction and emphasized the need for the exchange of scientific knowledge and cultural exchanges among Asian countries.

### 亚洲国家会议国际筹委会秘书处发表公报  阐述亚洲国家会议的目的和议程

1955-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲国家会议国际筹委会秘书处发表公报
    阐述亚洲国家会议的目的和议程
    据新华社新德里二十四日电：亚洲国家会议
  （前译为亚洲国家缓和国际紧张局势会议）国际筹备委员会秘书处二十二日发表公报阐述即将在四月初召开的亚洲国家会议的目的和议程。
    公报谈到亚洲国家会议的目的说，“会议的目的将是提供一个就有关亚洲的共同问题交换意见的机会。这种意见交换将有助于使亚洲各国人民在各方面达成进一步的共同谅解和进行密切联系，从而有助于缓和目前世界紧张局势。”
    公报指出：会议的第一项议程将是讨论中印两国总理发表的和平共处的五项原则。会议将计划具体步骤使亚洲各国人民了解和体会这些原则，并使亚洲和世界各国接受这些原则，这样它们就可能构成和平和安全的巩固基础。
    公报又说，会议还将讨论关于禁止和管制大规模毁灭性武器的问题，殖民主义和外国对亚洲各国内政的干涉，建立军事联盟和公约以分裂亚洲国家以及外国在亚洲建立军事基地的危险，承认中华人民共和国在联合国中的合法地位，使亚洲各国之间的外交关系正常化，用和平方法恢复朝鲜的统一，以及种族歧视等。会议还将讨论亚洲国家在平等互利的基础上发展贸易，亚洲国家的经济发展和提高生活水平，以及有关科学、社会和文化方面的问题。
    公报说，二月九日到十日在新德里举行的亚洲国家会议国际筹备委员会会议一致通过的关于亚洲国家会议的目的和议程的草案。中国和苏联的代表是在国际筹备委员会会议以后到达新德里的。中国和苏联两国代表全力支持这个草案。
    公报说，参加国际筹备委员会会议的缅甸、锡兰、印度、日本、朝鲜和叙利亚的代表一致认为，在亚洲国家中间，迫切需要在政治、经济、科学和文化等方面进行更密切的合作。他们并且认为，亚洲国家自己若不进行充分而积极的合作，亚洲问题是不能得到永久性的解决的。
    代表们认为，通过更大量的贸易来往和技术知识的交流，亚洲经济和工业的发展可以得到促进，亚洲广大人民的生活水平可以得到提高。
    代表们对于大规模毁灭性武器的威胁也表示了很大的关注，并强调在亚洲国家间进行科学知识交流和文化交流是必要的。
