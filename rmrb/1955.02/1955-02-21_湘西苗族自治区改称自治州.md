Title: Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region Reorganized as Autonomous Prefecture
Author:
Date: 1955-02-21
Page: 3

    The Second Session of the First People's Congress of Hunan Province, in accordance with the provisions of the Constitution, passed a resolution to change the Hunan Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region to the Hunan Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture, and has submitted it to the State Council for approval. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 湘西苗族自治区改称自治州

1955-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

    湘西苗族自治区改称自治州
    湖南省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议根据宪法的规定，通过了关于改湖南省湘西苗族自治区为湖南省湘西苗族自治州的决议，并已报请国务院批准。　（新华社）
