Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-02-22
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    After the State Council's order regarding the issuance of the new Renminbi and the recall of the current Renminbi was announced, the broad masses of people across the country expressed their warm support. This is a picture of workers in the large parts workshop of the Shanghai Machine Tool Plant excitedly reading the new Renminbi sample notes published in the newspaper.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国务院关于发行新的人民币和收回现行的人民币的命令公布以后，全国广大人民表示热烈拥护。这是上海机床厂大件车间工人兴奋地在看报纸上刊载的新人民币票样。
