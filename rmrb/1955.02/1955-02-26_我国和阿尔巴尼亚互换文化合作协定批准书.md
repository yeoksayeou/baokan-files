Title: China and Albania Exchange Instruments of Ratification of Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana: The ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification of the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Albania was held on February 21st at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania. This agreement was signed in Beijing on October 14, 1954, ratified by the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania on November 6, 1954, and approved by the Chairman of the People's Republic of China on December 22 of the same year.

Representing the Albanian side for the exchange of instruments of ratification was Behar Shtylla, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania, and representing the Chinese side was Xu Yixin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Albania.

The Albanian side attending the ceremony included: Faik Pacrami, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations; Koço Papa Kristo, President of the Academy of Sciences; Vasil Nathanaili, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Kiço Ceu, Deputy Minister of Education and Culture; F. Jakova, Director of the Department of Culture of the Ministry of Education and Culture; D. Ramani, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; M. Kroy, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Chinese side attending the ceremony included Wang Dong, First Secretary of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Albania, Liu Hualun, Third Secretary, and Zhang Wen.

### 我国和阿尔巴尼亚互换文化合作协定批准书

1955-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和阿尔巴尼亚互换文化合作协定批准书
    【新华社讯】地拉那消息：中华人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国文化合作协定的批准书的互换仪式，于二月二十一日在阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部举行。这个协定于一九五四年十月十四日在北京签订，并于一九五四年十一月六日经阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团批准，于同年十二月二十二日经中华人民共和国主席批准。
    代表阿尔巴尼亚方面进行批准书交换的是阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部长贝·什图拉，代表中国方面的是中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚特命全权大使徐以新。
    阿尔巴尼亚方面参加仪式的有：阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席法·帕奇拉米，科学院院长科·帕帕里斯托，外交部副部长瓦·纳塔奈利，教育和文化部副部长基·切乌，教育和文化部文化司司长弗·雅科瓦，外交部新闻司司长德·拉马尼，外交部交际司司长穆·克罗伊。
    中国方面参加仪式的有中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆一等秘书王栋、三等秘书刘化伦和章文。
