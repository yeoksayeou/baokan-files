Title: Our Delegation to Console Soviet Troops Visited Lushunkou

Author:
Date: 1955-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, February 25th: Peng Dehuai, Head of the People's Republic of China Delegation to Console Soviet Troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense; Deputy Heads of the delegation Song Qingling, He Long, Guo Moruo, Nie Rongzhen and representatives of the delegation visited the Lushun Military History Museum, Soviet troop barracks and hospitals, and inspected the Lushunkou harbor by ship on the 25th.

Accompanying them were Soviet Ambassador to China, Academician Yudin, senior Soviet military officers stationed in the Lushunkou area, and Soviet Consul General in Dalian, Belyanov, and others.
	
	Railway Workers in Manzhouli Prepare to Welcome Soviet Troops Returning Home
	
This newspaper reports: Manzhouli railway workers, after hearing the news that the Soviet troops stationed in Lushunkou would return home before May 31st in accordance with the Sino-Soviet Communiqué, decided to decorate the station as beautifully as a festival to welcome the Soviet troops returning home. They have established a preparatory group to actively carry out various preparations.

### 我国慰问苏军代表团在旅顺口参观

1955-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国慰问苏军代表团在旅顺口参观
    【新华社旅大二十五日电】中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团团长、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，副团长宋庆龄、贺龙、郭沫若、聂荣臻和代表团代表，二十五日到旅顺口参观了旅顺军事历史博物馆和苏军营房、医院等处，并乘坐舰艇巡视旅顺口港湾。
    陪同参观的，有苏联驻我国大使尤金院士，驻旅顺口地区苏军的高级将领和苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫等。
  满洲里铁路职工准备欢送苏军回国
    【本报讯】满洲里铁路职工，听到驻旅顺口苏军根据中苏会谈公报，将在五月三十一日以前回国的消息后，决定把车站布置得像节日一样的漂亮，准备欢送苏军回国。他们成立了筹备小组，积极进行各项准备工作。
