Title: National Assembly Standing Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Passes Resolution Supporting the Declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi: The Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a meeting on the 19th. The meeting passed a resolution supporting the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

The resolution stated: "The people and National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam welcome the initiative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to establish direct contact between the parliaments of all countries and to exchange parliamentary delegations for visits. Such mutual visits will help promote understanding among the peoples of all countries, establish friendship between them, and avoid unfounded misunderstandings and hatred. The National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will strive to expand its relations with the parliaments of other countries and welcomes parliamentary delegations from all countries to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam."

### 越南民主共和国国民大会常委会通过决议  支持苏联最高苏维埃发表的宣言

1955-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国国民大会常委会通过决议
    支持苏联最高苏维埃发表的宣言
    【新华社二十二日讯】　据越南通讯社河内讯：越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会在十九日举行会议。会议通过了支持苏联最高苏维埃的宣言的决议。
    决议说：“越南民主共和国的人民和国民大会欢迎苏联最高苏维埃关于各国议会之间建立直接接触和各国互派议会代表团进行访问的创议。这种相互访问将有助于促进各国人民的了解，建立他们之间的友谊和避免毫无根据的误解和仇恨。越南民主共和国国民大会将努力扩大它同其他国家的议会的关系，并且欢迎各国的议会代表团访问越南民主共和国。”
