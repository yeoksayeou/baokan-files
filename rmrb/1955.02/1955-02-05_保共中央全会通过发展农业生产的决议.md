Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Passes Resolution on Developing Agricultural Production
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-05
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 3rd) TASS reports: The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party held a plenary session on January 31st.

    The plenary session heard and discussed the report by Chervenkov on the implementation of the Sixth Party Congress's resolution on developing agriculture.

    The plenary session finally passed a resolution. The resolution pointed out: In order to implement the Sixth Party Congress's resolution, the state has already transported 2,719 tractors (each calculated at 15 horsepower), 569 combine harvesters, and more than 8,000 other agricultural machines to the countryside, and has dispatched 2,350 agronomists and livestock breeders to work at various machine tractor stations. The organization and labor remuneration of agricultural production cooperatives have been greatly improved. The state purchase prices of many agricultural products have also been increased.

    In order to further increase agricultural production, the plenary session formulated many measures, including measures to increase agricultural investment and raise purchase prices. The resolution attaches great importance to how to strengthen Party organizations in rural areas, improve mass political work, and strengthen agricultural work cadres.

### 保共中央全会通过发展农业生产的决议

1955-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    保共中央全会通过发展农业生产的决议
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社索非亚三日讯：保加利亚共产党中央委员会在一月三十一日举行了全体会议。
    全体会议听取并且讨论了契尔文科夫就第六次党代表大会关于发展农业的决议的执行情况所作的报告。
    全体会议最后通过了一项决议。决议指出：为了执行第六次党代表大会的决议，国家已经将两千七百一十九台拖拉机（每台以十五匹马力计算）、五百六十九台联合机和八千多台其他农业机器运到农村去，已经派遣了两千三百五十名农艺师和牲畜饲养家到各机器拖拉机站工作。农业生产合作社的组织和劳动报酬有了大大的改善。许多农产品的国家收购价格也提高了。
    为了进一步提高农业的生产，全体会议拟定了许多措施，其中包括增加农业的投资额和提高收购价格的措施等。决议十分重视如何加强农村中的党组织、改善群众的政治工作和加强农业工作干部的问题。
