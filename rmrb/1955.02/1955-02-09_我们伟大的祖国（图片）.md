Author:
Date: 1955-02-09

    Our Great Motherland
    A self-study group organized by seven graduating elementary school students from South 16th Street, Dongfujia District, Harbin, has established a sound learning system with the help of the local Communist Youth League organization and the school, providing a good self-study model for graduating elementary school students who are studying on their own. This is a scene of the Harbin Cultural Center preparing to show slides to them.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    哈尔滨市东傅家区南十六道街七个高小毕业生组织的一个自学小组，在当地青年团组织和学校的帮助下，建立了健全的学习制度，给自学的高小毕业生提供了良好的自学榜样。这是哈尔滨市文化馆正准备给他们放映幻灯的情形。
