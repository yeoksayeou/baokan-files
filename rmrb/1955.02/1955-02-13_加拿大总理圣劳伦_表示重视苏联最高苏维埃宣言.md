Title: Canadian Prime Minister St. Laurent Expresses Importance of the Soviet Supreme Soviet Declaration
Author:
Date: 1955-02-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 11th (TASS): According to reports from Ottawa, Canadian Prime Minister St. Laurent said that the Canadian government will carefully study the Soviet proposal for meetings between parliamentarians of various countries to discuss peace issues. Commenting on the declaration of the Soviet Supreme Soviet on February 9, 1955, St. Laurent said that every suggestion that could ease international tensions must be utilized.

### 加拿大总理圣劳伦  表示重视苏联最高苏维埃宣言

1955-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    加拿大总理圣劳伦
    表示重视苏联最高苏维埃宣言
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社纽约十一日讯：据来自渥太华的消息称，加拿大总理圣劳伦说，加拿大政府将仔细研究苏联关于各国议员会晤以讨论和平问题的建议。圣劳伦就苏联最高苏维埃一九五五年二月九日的宣言发表评论说，必须利用可以缓和国际紧张局势的每一个建议。
