Title: Khrushchev Receives American Journalists Hearst and Others
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-07
Page: 4

    TASS, Moscow, February 5th. Soviet Communist Party Central Committee First Secretary N.S. Khrushchev received American journalists William Randolph Hearst, Kingsbury Smith, and Frank Conniff on February 5th and had a long conversation with them.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见美记者赫斯特等

1955-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见美记者赫斯特等
   【新华社六日讯】　塔斯社莫斯科五日电：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫在五日接见了美国记者威·伦·赫斯特、金斯伯里·史密斯和佛·康尼夫，并和他们作了一次长时间的谈话。
