Title: Peng Dehuai Sends Telegram to Zhukov Congratulating Him on His Appointment as Soviet Minister of Defense
Author:
Date: 1955-02-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Soviet Marshal Zhukov, congratulating him on his appointment as Minister of Defense. The telegram reads as follows: To Marshal Zhukov, Minister of Defense of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

    I am delighted to learn of your appointment as Minister of Defense of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. Comrade Marshal, I extend to you my warm congratulations and wish you great success in your future work.

    Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, February 10, 1955

### 彭德怀电贺朱可夫被任命为苏联国防部长

1955-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀电贺朱可夫被任命为苏联国防部长
    【新华社十四日讯】中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀致电苏联元帅朱可夫，祝贺他被任命为国防部长。电文如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟国防部长朱可夫元帅：
    欣悉您被任命为苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟国防部长，元帅同志，我谨向您致以热烈的祝贺，并祝您在今后工作中获得巨大的成就。
   中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀　一九五五年二月十日
