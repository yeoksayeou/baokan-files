Title: Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Democratic Republic of Vietnam Launch Signature Campaign Against Atomic Warfare
Author:
Date: 1955-02-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 20th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland of Korea and the Korean National Peace Committee held a joint meeting in Pyongyang on February 18th, and decided to launch a nationwide signature campaign against atomic warfare.
    Han Sol Ya, Chairman of the Korean National Peace Committee, delivered a report at the meeting on measures to implement the declaration and the "Appeal to the People of the World" adopted by the Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council. In the report, Han Sol Ya said that in response to the call of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, the Korean people will launch a broad signature campaign against atomic warfare.
    The representatives attending the meeting held a heated discussion and supported the report.
    The meeting passed a resolution expressing full support for the declaration of the Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council and its "Appeal to the People of the World," and warmly welcoming and supporting its call to launch a signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons. The resolution called on all Korean people to carry out a nationwide signature campaign on the "Appeal to the People of the World." The resolution stated, "We firmly believe that the indestructible and united peace forces of good people around the world can stop the atomic maniacs and defend lasting peace and security."
    A preparatory committee was established at the meeting to collect signatures against atomic warfare and participate in the World Peace Conference. The preparatory committee is chaired by Park Jung-ai, with Han Sol Ya and five others as vice-chairmen, and Han Young-san as secretary-general.
    Subsequently, the meeting adopted the preparatory committee's work plan for implementing the resolutions adopted by the Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.
    After the meeting, Kim Du-bong, Park Jung-ai, and all the participants signed the "Appeal to the People of the World."

    Xinhua News Agency, February 20th: According to Vietnam News Agency, Hanoi, February 19th: The National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front and the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace held a joint meeting on the 17th. The meeting was presided over by Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front.
    The meeting heard a report by Duong Duc Hien, Secretary-General of the Vietnam Democratic Party, on the domestic movement against the aggressive Southeast Asia group's upcoming meeting in Bangkok. The meeting considered the Bangkok meeting to be a serious threat to Southeast Asian countries, especially Indochina countries. The meeting unanimously passed a resolution condemning the aggressive Southeast Asia group and the Bangkok meeting, and strongly protesting the open interference of American imperialists in Vietnam. The meeting also denounced the actions of the authorities in southern Vietnam in undermining democratic freedoms and the Geneva Accords under the direction of the United States.
    The meeting also heard a report by Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, on the world people's movement against the use of atomic weapons. The meeting resolved to launch a nationwide signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons and will establish a committee to lead this movement.
    The meeting also heard a report from the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam that had returned after attending the Asian Lawyers' Conference held in Calcutta, and warmly welcomed the resolution of the Asian Lawyers' Conference on the prohibition of the use of all atomic weapons, hydrogen weapons, and other weapons of mass destruction.
    The meeting also discussed the preparatory work and organizational plan for the National Congress of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front, scheduled for April.

### 朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和越南民主共和国  展开反对原子战争的签名运动

1955-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和越南民主共和国
    展开反对原子战争的签名运动
    【新华社平壤二十日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线和朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会于二月十八日在平壤举行了联席会议，决定展开全国规模的反对原子战争的签名运动。
    朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长韩雪野在会上作了关于采取措施以履行世界和平理事会常务委员会扩大会议所通过的宣言和“告全世界人民书”的报告。韩雪野在报告中说，为了响应世界和平理事会常务委员会的号召，朝鲜人民将发动一个广泛的反对原子战争的签名运动。
    出席会议的代表进行了热烈的讨论，支持这个报告。
    会议通过决议，表示完全支持世界和平理事会常务委员会扩大会议的宣言和它的“告全世界人民书”，并且热烈欢迎和支持它要求展开反对使用原子武器签名运动的号召。决议号召全体朝鲜人民进行一个全国性的在“告全世界人民书”上签名的运动。决议说，我们坚信，全世界善良人们的不可摧毁的和团结一致的和平力量是能够制止原子狂人和保卫持久和平和安全的。
    会上成立了一个为反对原子战争征集签名和参加世界和平大会的筹备委员会。筹备委员会由朴正爱任委员长，韩雪野等五人任副委员长，韩荣山任书记长。
    接着，会议通过了筹备委员会关于执行世界和平理事会常务委员会扩大会议所通过的决议的工作计划。
    会议结束后，金枓奉、朴正爱和所有的与会者都在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。
    【新华社二十日讯】据越南通讯社河内十九日讯：越南国民联合战线全国委员会和越南保卫世界和平委员会十七日举行联席会议。会议由越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜主持。
    会议听取了越南民主党总书记杨德贤关于国内反对侵略性的东南亚集团即将在曼谷举行的会议的运动的报告。会议认为曼谷会议是对东南亚国家、特别是对印度支那国家的严重威胁。会议一致通过决议，谴责侵略性的东南亚集团和曼谷会议，并且强硬抗议美帝国主义者在越南的公开干涉。会议还斥责了越南南部地区当局在美国指使下破坏民主自由和日内瓦协定的行为。
    会议又听取了越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探的关于世界人民反对使用原子武器运动的报告。会议决议发起一个全国性的反对使用原子武器的签名运动，并且将设立一个委员会来领导这个运动。
    会议也听取了参加在加尔各答举行的亚洲法律工作者会议以后归来的越南民主共和国代表团的报告，并且热烈欢迎亚洲法律工作者会议关于禁止使用一切原子武器、氢武器和其他大规模毁灭性武器的决议。
    会议还讨论了关于预定在四月举行的越南国民联合战线全国代表大会的筹备工作和组织计划的报告。
