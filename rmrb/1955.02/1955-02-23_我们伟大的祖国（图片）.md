Our Great Motherland
    Dalian Port, located at the southern tip of the Liaodong Peninsula in our country, is a famous port in China. The harbor is ice-free all year round and has modern machinery and warehouse equipment. This is a crane lifting soybeans from a train onto a ship.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大连港位于我国辽东半岛南端，是我国著名的港口。港湾常年不冻，并有近代化的机械和仓库设备。这是吊车正把大豆从火车上送上轮船。
