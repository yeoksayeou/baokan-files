Title: Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference Holds Plenary Session to Discuss Far East and European Situations
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, 8th - TASS reports: The Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference held a plenary session on the 7th. According to Reuters, the conference discussed the situation in the Far East.
    According to press sources, the Prime Ministers will also discuss the European situation caused by the fall of the Mendès-France government, and the future of arming West Germany in light of the French government's failure.
    According to the aforementioned press sources, the Prime Ministers began discussing the communiqué regarding the outcome of the conference at the meeting on the 7th, and this communiqué will be published after the closing of the meeting on the 8th.

### 英联邦总理会议举行全体会议  讨论远东局势和欧洲局势

1955-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    英联邦总理会议举行全体会议
    讨论远东局势和欧洲局势
   【新华社八日讯】塔斯社伦敦讯：英联邦总理会议在七日举行全体会议。据路透社报道，在会议上讨论了远东局势的问题。
    据新闻界人士消息，总理们还要讨论由于孟戴斯—弗朗斯政府倒台而造成的欧洲局势以及从法国政府的失败来看武装西德的前途等问题。
    从上述新闻界人士获悉，总理们在七日的会议上着手讨论关于会议结果的公报，这个公报将在八日会议闭幕后发表。
