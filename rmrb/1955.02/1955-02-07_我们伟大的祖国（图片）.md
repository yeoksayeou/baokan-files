    Our Great Motherland
    The heroic deeds of the great Chinese workers' movement -- the "February 7th" strike -- inspire the younger generation. On the eve of the 32nd anniversary of the "February 7th" strike, Young Pioneers from the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau Staff and Workers' Children's School visited Lin Maoxiang, a participant in the "February 7th" strike, and listened to him recount the historical events of the "February 7th" strike.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    伟大的中国工人运动——“二七”罢工的英雄事迹，鼓舞着年轻一代的人们。在“二七”三十二周年的前夕，郑州铁路局职工子弟学校的少年先锋队员们，访问了“二七”罢工的参加者林茂湘，听他讲述“二七”罢工斗争的史迹。
