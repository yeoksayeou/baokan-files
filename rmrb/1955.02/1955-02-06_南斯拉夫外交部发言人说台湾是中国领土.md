Title: Yugoslav Foreign Ministry Spokesman Says Taiwan is Chinese Territory
Author:
Date: 1955-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, February 5th: According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency on February 4th from Belgrade: The spokesman for the Yugoslav Foreign Ministry, Branko Draskovic, stated at a press conference on February 4th, when explaining Yugoslavia's position on the Taiwan issue, that Taiwan is part of Chinese national territory.

### 南斯拉夫外交部发言人说台湾是中国领土

1955-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫外交部发言人说台湾是中国领土
    【新华社五日讯】据南斯拉夫通讯社贝尔格莱德四日讯：南斯拉夫外交部发言人布朗科·德拉斯科维奇四日在记者招待会上说明南斯拉夫对台湾问题的态度时说：台湾是中国国家领土的一部分。
