Title: UN Security Council Scheduled to Meet Today
Author:
Date: 1955-02-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, February 12th. TASS reported: The United Nations Secretariat announced that the President of the Security Council, Mr. Béland, has scheduled a meeting of the Security Council for 11:30 AM New York time on February 14th.

### 联合国安理会定今日开会

1955-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国安理会定今日开会
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社纽约十二日讯：联合国秘书处宣布，安全理事会主席贝朗德定于二月十四日纽约时间十一时三十分召开安全理事会会议。
