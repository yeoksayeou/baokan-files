Title: Czechoslovak National Assembly Presidium Passes Resolution Supporting Declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 26th: TASS reported that the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic, at its meeting on February 24th, after listening to and discussing the report on the international situation made by Z. Fierlinger, Chairman of the National Assembly, unanimously passed a resolution.

The resolution stated that the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic warmly welcomes the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The Presidium of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic believes that the current international situation increases the responsibility of parliaments of all countries. Parliaments of all countries, responsible for taking legal action on issues of war and peace, should loudly call for the defense of peace. The way out of the current situation is to establish an effective collective security system; to re-establish a unified and peaceful Germany through free and democratic all-German elections; to eliminate foreign interference in the internal affairs of other countries; and to achieve universal disarmament and a ban on the use of atomic weapons and other weapons of mass destruction. Therefore, the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic warmly welcomes the proposals made by the Soviet government on February 18th regarding the destruction of existing atomic and hydrogen weapons and the limitation of armaments.

The resolution stated: The Presidium of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic fully agrees with the lofty initiative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The Presidium of the National Assembly believes that direct contacts between parliaments of various countries, mutual exchange of delegations, and speeches by parliamentary delegations of one country in the parliaments of other countries will greatly contribute to the efforts of the people of all countries to safeguard the peaceful future of the world.

### 捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席团通过决议  支持苏联最高苏维埃发表的宣言

1955-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席团通过决议
    支持苏联最高苏维埃发表的宣言
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，二月二十四日举行的捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席团会议，在听取并且讨论了国民议会主席兹·费林格在会上所作的关于国际形势的报告后，一致通过了一项决议。
    决议说，捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席团热烈欢迎苏联最高苏维埃的宣言。捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席团认为，目前的国际局势加重了世界各国议会的责任。负责就战争与和平问题采取法律行动的各国议会，应当大声疾呼地来保卫和平。目前形势的出路是，建立有效的集体安全体系；通过自由、民主的全德选举重新建立统一、和平的德国；消除外国对其他国家内政的干涉；普遍裁减军备以及禁止使用原子武器和其他大规模毁灭性武器。因此，捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席团热烈欢迎苏联政府二月十八日提出的关于销毁现存的原子武器、氢武器和限制军备的建议。
    决议说：捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席团完全同意苏联最高苏维埃的崇高倡议。国民议会主席团相信，各国议会之间的直接联系，互派代表团和一国的议会代表团在他国的议会中发表演说，在极大程度上有助于各国人民保障全世界的和平未来的努力。
