Title: Japan's National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations Holds Meeting; Decides to Hold National Congress Demanding the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations with China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-02-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 27th: The Japan National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations held an expanded meeting of its executives in Tokyo on the 24th to discuss the organization's current work policy.

The meeting decided to demand that the Japanese government conduct negotiations with the Soviet Union unconditionally to restore normal relations between the two countries. The meeting also decided to hold a national congress in Tokyo on March 30 to demand the restoration of diplomatic relations with China and the Soviet Union.

### 日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议举行会议  决定举行全国大会要求同中苏恢复邦交

1955-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议举行会议
    决定举行全国大会要求同中苏恢复邦交
    【新华社二十七日讯】东京消息：日本恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议于二十四日在东京举行干部扩大会议，讨论了这个组织当前的工作方针问题。
    会议决定要求日本政府无条件地和苏联进行恢复两国正常关系的谈判。会议还决定要在三月三十日在东京举行要求同中国和苏联恢复邦交的全国大会。
