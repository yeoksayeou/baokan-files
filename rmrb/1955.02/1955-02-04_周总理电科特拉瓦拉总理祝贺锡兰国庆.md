Title: Premier Zhou Sends a Congratulatory Telegram to Prime Minister Kotelawala on Ceylon's National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 3rd:

    Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Ceylon Prime Minister Kotelawala on February 2nd, congratulating him on Ceylon's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

    To His Excellency Prime Minister Kotelawala:

    On the occasion of Ceylon's National Day, I extend my congratulations to you, the government of Ceylon, and the people of Ceylon, and wish that the relations between China and Ceylon will be increasingly enhanced in the cause of maintaining peace in Asia and the world.

                         Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                            February 2, 1955, Beijing

### 周总理电科特拉瓦拉总理祝贺锡兰国庆

1955-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电科特拉瓦拉总理祝贺锡兰国庆
   【新华社三日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来于二月二日致电锡兰总理科特拉瓦拉，祝贺锡兰国庆。电文如下：科特拉瓦拉总理阁下：
    值此锡兰国庆之际，我谨向您、锡兰政府和锡兰人民表示祝贺，并祝中锡两国关系在维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中日益增进。
                         中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
                            一九五五年二月二日北京
