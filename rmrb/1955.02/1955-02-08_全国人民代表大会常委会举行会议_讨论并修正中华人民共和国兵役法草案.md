Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting to Discuss and Revise the Draft of the Military Service Law of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1955-02-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 7th: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its fifth meeting on February 7th.

　　The meeting heard explanations from Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, and Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Committee of the People's Republic of China, and discussed and revised the "Military Service Law of the People's Republic of China (Draft)" proposed by the State Council of the People's Republic of China.

    The meeting also resolved to have the State Council distribute this draft to people's committees at all levels for discussion and to solicit opinions from the people, so that it could be further revised and submitted to the next meeting of the National People's Congress for discussion and approval.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议  讨论并修正中华人民共和国兵役法草案

1955-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    讨论并修正中华人民共和国兵役法草案
　　【新华社七日讯】全国人民代表大会常务委员会在二月七日举行第五次会议。
　　会议听了中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部部长彭德怀和中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻的说明，讨论并修正了中华人民共和国国务院提出的“中华人民共和国兵役法（草案）”。
    会议并决议将这一草案交由国务院发给各级人民委员会讨论和征求人民意见，以便再加修正，提请全国人民代表大会下一次会议讨论通过。
