Title: People of All Countries Determined to Smash the Threat of Atomic War; Belgian Peace League Calls for Mass Movement Against Atomic War

Author:

Date: 1955-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 18th: According to TASS, Brussels, February 17th: The National Committee of the Belgian Peace League adopted a resolution against the London and Paris Agreements and in support of the World Peace Council Standing Committee's "Appeal to the People of the World" from the Vienna meeting. It also adopted a letter to the German people, expressing solidarity with their struggle against the remilitarization of West Germany.

The National Committee's resolution, pointing out the fact that the Belgian Lower House approved the London and Paris Agreements despite the will of the majority of the country's residents, called on all Belgians to intensify their struggle to prevent the Belgian Upper House from ratifying the Paris Agreements.

The resolution stated that the Belgian Peace League will unite with all forces in Belgium fighting for peace in order to successfully launch a mass movement in support of the Vienna meeting's "Appeal to the People of the World" against atomic war.

The letter to the German people, adopted by the National Committee, said: We understand your desire to restore national unity. There can be no lasting peace in Europe without a peaceful solution to the German question. We are pleased to see that the German people are opposing militarism, which threatens the German people themselves and Germany's neighbors. Your struggle is a powerful encouragement to the Belgian people's movement against the arming of West Germany.

Syrian Peace Committee Calls for Signature Collection for "Appeal to the People of the World" in Urban and Rural Areas

Xinhua News Agency, February 18th: According to TASS, Beirut, February 17th: The Syrian Peace Committee recently held an enlarged meeting in Damascus to discuss the World Peace Council Standing Committee's "Appeal to the People of the World" against the preparation of atomic war.

The declaration adopted at the meeting pointed out: The Syrian Peace Committee warmly agrees with the World Peace Council's initiative to launch a broad movement to collect signatures for the "Appeal to the People of the World." The committee calls on all peace advocates in the country to launch a broad movement to collect signatures for the "Appeal to the People of the World" in Syrian cities and villages and among all segments of the population, in order to demonstrate to the world that the Syrian people resolutely demand an immediate ban on weapons of mass destruction.

British Methodist Church President Calls on Believers to Support a Policy of Complete Prohibition of Hydrogen Bombs

Xinhua News Agency, February 18th: TASS, London, February 17th: According to the British News Agency, on the 16th, the President of the British Methodist Church, leader of the National Committee for the Prohibition of Hydrogen Bombs, Reverend Donald Soper, called on all believers to support a policy of complete prohibition of hydrogen bombs.

Soper said: "It is hoped that all mankind will unite and resolutely oppose war and the latest manifestation of the horrors of war."

Indonesian "People's Daily" Calls for Stopping the Madness of the Atomic Maniacs

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta: The Indonesian "People's Daily" commented on the 15th on the Indonesian National Peace Committee's call to launch a signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons, saying that this movement is of particular importance at present, as the United States is taking adventurous actions in the Taiwan region. The commentary said that American warmongers are threatening to turn the world to ashes with atomic bombs, while American newspapers are fabricating false news, making war seem inevitable. The commentary said that if the people of the world realize that their fate is in their own hands, not in the hands of those atomic maniacs, then war is not inevitable. The commentary said that the madness of the atomic maniacs can be stopped, as long as all peace-loving people rise up to frustrate their criminal attempts.

### 各国人民决心粉碎原子战争威胁  比利时和平联盟号召展开反对原子战争的群众运动

1955-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    各国人民决心粉碎原子战争威胁
    比利时和平联盟号召展开反对原子战争的群众运动
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社布鲁塞尔十七日讯：比利时和平联盟全国委员会通过了一项反对伦敦和巴黎协定、支持世界和平理事会常务委员会维也纳会议“告全世界人民书”的决议，还通过了一项告德国人民书，表示声援他们反对西德重新军国主义化的斗争。
    全国委员会的决议在指出比利时下议院不顾全国大多数居民的意志而批准伦敦和巴黎协定的事实后，号召所有比利时人加紧斗争，阻止比利时上议院批准巴黎协定。
    决议表示，比利时和平联盟将和比利时一切为和平而斗争的力量联合起来，以便成功地展开支持维也纳会议关于反对原子战争的告全世界人民书的群众运动。
    全国委员会通过的告德国人民书说：我们理解到你们要恢复国家统一的愿望。不和平解决德国问题，欧洲就不可能有持久和平。我们愉快地看到：德国人民正在反对威胁着德国人民自己和德国邻国的军国主义。你们的斗争对比利时人民反对武装西德运动是强有力的鼓舞。
    叙利亚和委会号召在城乡为“告全世界人民书”征集签名
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社贝鲁特十七日讯：叙利亚和平委员会最近在大马士革举行扩大会议，讨论了世界和平理事会常务委员会反对准备原子战争的“告全世界人民书”。
    会议通过的宣言指出：叙利亚和平委员会热烈地同意世界和平理事会关于发起广泛运动为“告全世界人民书”征集签名的倡议。委员会号召全国所有和平拥护者在叙利亚的城市和农村中、在各个居民阶层中展开一个为“告全世界人民书”征集签名的广泛的运动，以便向全世界表明，叙利亚人民坚决要求立即禁止大规模毁灭性武器。
    英国卫理公会主席号召信徒支持完全禁止氢弹的政策
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社伦敦十七日讯：据英国新闻联合社报道，十六日，英国卫理公会主席、争取禁止氢弹全国委员会领导人、唐纳德·索佩尔牧师号召所有信徒支持完全禁止氢弹的政策。
    索佩尔说：“希望全人类团结起来，坚决反对战争和战争的恐怖的最新的表现。”
    印度尼西亚“人民日报”号召制止原子狂者的疯狂行动
    【新华社雅加达讯】印度尼西亚的“人民日报”十五日评论印度尼西亚全国和平委员会号召开展反对使用原子武器的签名运动说，由于美国正在台湾地区采取冒险行为，这个运动在目前具有特别重要的意义。评论说，美国战争贩子正在威胁要用原子弹把世界化为灰烬，同时美国报纸还在捏造虚假的消息，把战争说得好像是不可避免的。评论说，如果全世界人民认识到他们的命运掌握在他们自己手中，而不是在那些原子狂者手中，那么，战争不是不可避免的。评论说，原子狂者的疯狂行动是能够制止的，只要一切爱好和平的人民都起来挫败他们的罪恶企图。
