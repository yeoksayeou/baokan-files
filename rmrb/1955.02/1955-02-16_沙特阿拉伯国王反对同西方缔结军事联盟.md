Title: Saudi Arabian King Opposes Military Alliances with the West
Author:
Date: 1955-02-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 15th: Saudi Arabian King Ibn Saud issued a statement on the 11th, calling on all Arab countries to take active measures to oppose military alliances with Western countries.

    In the statement, Saud condemned Iraq's position. He said that those who undermine the consistent policies of Arab countries "must bear the responsibility before history and the Arab people for endangering all Arab countries by making them a stepping stone for imperialism." He also said that the Saudi Arabian government "will firmly stand with those Arab countries that abide by the consistent principles of Arab countries and refuse to sign any treaties that violate the interests of Arab countries."

### 沙特阿拉伯国王反对同西方缔结军事联盟

1955-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯国王反对同西方缔结军事联盟
    【新华社十五日讯】开罗消息：沙特阿拉伯国王伊本·沙特十一日发表声明，呼吁所有阿拉伯国家采取积极措施，反对同西方国家缔结军事联盟。
    沙特在声明中谴责伊拉克的立场。他说：那些破坏阿拉伯国家一致政策的人，“要在历史和阿拉伯人民的面前对于使所有阿拉伯国家遭受到成为帝国主义踏板的危险负责”。他又说：沙特阿拉伯政府“将坚定地和那些遵守阿拉伯国家的一致原则，拒绝签订任何违反阿拉伯国家利益的条约的阿拉伯国家站在一起”。
