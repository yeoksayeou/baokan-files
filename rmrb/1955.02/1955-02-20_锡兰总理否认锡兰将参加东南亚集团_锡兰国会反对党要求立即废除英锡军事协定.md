Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Denies Ceylon Will Join Southeast Asia Group; Ceylon Parliament Opposition Party Demands Immediate Abolition of Anglo-Ceylon Military Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-02-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 19th: Bombay dispatch: Ceylon Prime Minister Sir John Kotelawala, on his way home after attending the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference, said in Bombay on the 18th, denying that Ceylon would join the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization. Kotelawala said, "We are outside the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization."

Xinhua News Agency, February 19th: Colombo dispatch: Representatives of the Ceylon parliamentary opposition parties presented a motion on the 17th demanding the immediate abolition of the military agreement signed between Ceylon and Britain, and the immediate removal of all foreign bases and military facilities in Ceylon. The motion also called for a refusal to allow foreign ships, fighter planes, and troops carrying troops to enter or pass through Ceylon's territory and territorial waters.

### 锡兰总理否认锡兰将参加东南亚集团  锡兰国会反对党要求立即废除英锡军事协定

1955-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理否认锡兰将参加东南亚集团
    锡兰国会反对党要求立即废除英锡军事协定
    【新华社十九日讯】孟买消息：锡兰总理科特拉瓦拉在参加英联邦总理会议后回国途中，十八日在孟买发表谈话，否认锡兰将参加东南亚条约组织。科特拉瓦拉说：“我们是在东南亚条约组织外面的。”
    【新华社十九日讯】科伦坡消息：锡兰国会反对党的代表十七日曾经提出一项动议，要求立即废除锡兰同英国签订的军事协定，并且立即撤除外国设在锡兰的一切基地和军事设施。动议还要求拒绝允许外国载运军队的船只、战斗飞机和军队进入或通过锡兰的领土和领海。
