Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Alliance: Staff of the State-Run Friendship Farm Hold Meeting to Celebrate
Author:
Date: 1955-02-16
Page: 2

    Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Alliance
    Staff of the State-Run Friendship Farm Hold Meeting to Celebrate
    The staff of the State-Run Friendship Farm warmly celebrated the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. On the evening of the 14th, hundreds of staff members of the State-Run Friendship Farm, Soviet experts, and representatives of local organizations held a celebration meeting. At the meeting, Wang Caoli, Director of the State-Run Friendship Farm, Nikolenko, Soviet expert and Chief Consultant of the farm director, Liu Xuewen, County Head of Jixian County, Li Zuogui, farm worker, and Zhao Zhendong, farmer of the Xianfeng Agricultural Production Cooperative of Jixian County, all spoke at the meeting. In his speech, Wang Caoli called on all employees to humbly learn from the Soviet experts and make this farm, which symbolizes the profound friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, a success. Liu Xuewen, the head of Jixian County, said in his speech that the people of Jixian County would work hard to support the construction of the farm. (Our newspaper's Fu Liyuan dispatch of the 15th)

### 庆祝中苏同盟条约签订五周年  国营友谊农场职工开会庆祝

1955-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏同盟条约签订五周年
    国营友谊农场职工开会庆祝
    国营友谊农场的职工们热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年。十四日晚间，国营友谊农场的职工和苏联专家以及当地各机关的代表数百人举行了庆祝大会。会上，国营友谊农场场长王操犁、苏联专家、场长总顾问尼科连科，以及集贤县县长刘学文、农场工人李作贵和集贤县先锋农业生产合作社农民赵振东等，都在会上讲话。王操犁在讲话中号召全体职工，虚心地向苏联专家学习，把这个象征着中苏人民深厚友谊的农场办好。集贤县县长刘学文在讲话中表示集贤县的人民将努力支援农场的建设。（本报福利屯十五日电）
