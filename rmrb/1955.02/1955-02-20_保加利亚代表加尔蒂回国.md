Title: Bulgarian Representative Garti Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-02-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Sa. Garti, a member of the Bulgarian delegation who came to China to negotiate the 1955 executive plan for the Sino-Bulgarian cultural cooperation agreement, left Beijing for home on the 19th.
	During its stay in China, the delegation visited schools and scenic spots in Beijing, Wuhan and other places.

### 保加利亚代表加尔蒂回国

1955-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚代表加尔蒂回国
    【新华社十九日讯】来我国谈判中保文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划的保加利亚谈判代表团团员萨·加尔蒂已在十九日离开北京回国。
    代表团在我国期间曾在北京、武汉等地参观了学校，游览了名胜古迹。
