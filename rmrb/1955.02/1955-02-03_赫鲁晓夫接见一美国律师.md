Title: Khrushchev Receives an American Lawyer
Author:
Date: 1955-02-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 1st: N.S. Khrushchev received the American lawyer and journalist, Mr. Ma. MacDuffie, who is currently in Moscow, on February 1st, and held talks with him.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见一美国律师

1955-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见一美国律师
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科一日电：二月一日，尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫接见了目前在莫斯科的美国律师、新闻记者马·麦克达菲，并且和他进行了谈话。
