Title: The Entire Chinese People Resolutely Oppose the Use of Atomic Weapons! (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-02-27
Page: 5

To defend peace and oppose the use of atomic weapons, millions of Chinese workers have signed their names on the "Appeal to the People of the World." They express their determination to oppose wars of aggression and defend peace with practical actions by fulfilling and overfulfilling national plans, and actively supporting the liberation of Taiwan.

    Left Picture: Steelworkers at the Taiyuan Iron and Steel Plant signing. Shanxi Daily manuscript Liang Yi photo
    Right Picture: All the textile workers of Yi Shijuan Group at the State-owned Shanghai No. 2 Cotton Mill are signing. Shanghai Labor Daily manuscript Wang Chaozhen photo

Chinese scientists are signing to oppose the use of atomic weapons. They also advise American scientists, especially physicists: science serves peace, and scientists should become a force for defending peace. You should resolutely refuse to be accomplices of the American aggressive bloc! This is a picture of scientists from three organizations including the Chinese Academy of Sciences signing the "Appeal to the People of the World." Xinhua News Agency manuscript Yu Huiru photo

The vast number of farmers in China have also demonstrated their strong will to defend peace, and have signed the "Appeal to the People of the World" to oppose the use of atomic weapons. This is a picture of rural women in Qinjiagang Township, No. 3 District, Chongqing signing. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Shilin photo

    We oppose war, but we are not afraid of war. We oppose the use of atomic weapons, but we are not afraid of atomic weapons. Every commander and fighter of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has signed the "Appeal to the People of the World." This is a picture of tank soldiers of a certain tank unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army signing. PLA Pictorial manuscript Gao Hong photo

To defend peace and the fruits of human culture and art; to oppose the barbaric crimes of American imperialism. Literary and art workers in our country have all signed the "Appeal to the People of the World." This is a picture of the 95-year-old painter and Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, Qi Baishi, signing. This newspaper Wang Zhiyuan photo

    Children are also signing the "Appeal to the People of the World" to oppose the use of atomic weapons! They understand that only in peaceful days can they study with peace of mind and grow up healthily. This is a picture of students at the No. 1 Central Elementary School in Dongsi District, Beijing signing. Xinhua News Agency manuscript He Bingjie photo

Buddhists, Taoists, Christians, or Catholic priests and nuns… although they have different religious beliefs, their desire to love the motherland and love peace is the same. Religious figures in our country have also signed the "Appeal to the People of the World" to oppose the use of atomic weapons. This is a picture of lamas at the Yonghe Temple in Beijing signing. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Huiru photo

    People of all different occupations have written their names on the "Appeal to the People of the World" to oppose the use of atomic weapons. This is a picture of sailors on the Jiangdong Wheel signing. This newspaper reporter Yuan Yiping photo

Every family in China understands that opposing the use of atomic weapons is for the sake of a peaceful and happy life for all Chinese people and the people of the world. This is a picture of the families of workers in Caoyang New Village, Shanghai signing the "Appeal to the People of the World." Liberation Daily manuscript Bi Pinfu photo

    Thousands upon thousands of workers, farmers, soldiers, women, children, ethnic minorities... people of different occupations and different religious beliefs; hundreds of millions of people in China are signing the "Appeal to the People of the World" to oppose the use of atomic weapons. This is a picture of students from 49 ethnic groups, including Dai, Lahu, Hani, Yi, Miao, and Kawa, at Yunnan Nationalities Institute, who live in the big family of the motherland, signing. Yunnan Daily Lu Huachang photo

### 全中国人民坚决反对使用原子武器！（图片）

1955-02-27
第5版()
专栏：

    全中国人民坚决反对使用原子武器！
    为了保卫和平，反对使用原子武器，千百万中国工人都把自己的名子写在“告全世界人民书”上。他们表示要以完成和超额完成国家计划，积极支援解放台湾的实际行动来反对侵略战争，保卫和平。
    左图：太原炼钢厂的炼钢工人在签名。　山西日报稿　梁　毅摄
    右图：国营上海棉纺二厂裔式娟小组全体纺织工人在签名。　上海劳动报稿　王朝桢摄
    中国的科学家们签名反对使用原子武器，他们并且向美国的科学家们，特别是物理学家们忠告：科学是为和平服务的，科学家应该成为保卫和平的力量，你们应该坚决拒绝作美国侵略集团的帮凶！这是中国科学院等三团体的科学家们在“告全世界人民书”上签名。　新华社稿　喻惠如摄
    全中国广大的农民也表现了自己强大的保卫和平的意志，纷纷在“告全世界人民书”上签名反对使用原子武器。这是重庆市第三区覃家岗乡农村妇女在签名。　新华社记者　刘诗临摄
    我们反对战争，但是我们不怕战争。我们反对使用原子武器，但是我们也不怕原子武器。中国人民解放军每一个指挥员战斗员都在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。这是中国人民解放军坦克部队某部坦克手在签名。解放军画报社稿　高宏摄
    保卫和平、保卫人类文化艺术的成果；反对美帝国主义的野蛮罪行。我国的文学艺术工作者都在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。这是九十五岁的老画家、中国美术家协会主席齐白石在签名。　本报　王志渊摄
    孩子们也在“告全世界人民书”上签名，反对使用原子武器！他们懂得：只有在和平的日子里才能安心地学习健康地成长。这是北京市东四区第一中心小学的学生们在签名。　新华社稿　何秉洁摄
    佛教徒、道教徒、基督教徒或者是天主教的神甫、修女……虽然是有着不同的宗教信仰；可是爱祖国、爱和平的意愿是一致的。我国的宗教界人士也都在“告全世界人民书”上签名，反对使用原子武器。这是北京雍和宫喇嘛在签名。新华社记者　喻惠如摄
    各种不同职业的人都把自己的名字写在“告全世界人民书”上反对使用原子武器。这是江东轮的海员在签名。　本报记者　袁毅平摄
    全中国每一个家庭都懂得：反对使用原子武器是为了我们全中国人民和全世界人民都能过和平幸福生活。这是上海市曹杨新村的工人家属在“告全世界人民书”上签名。　解放日报稿　毕品富摄
    千千万万的工人、农民、解放军、妇女、儿童、少数民族……不同职业，不同宗教信仰的人；全中国成亿的人都在“告全世界人民书”上签名，反对使用原子武器。这是生活在祖国大家庭中的少数民族——云南民族学院的傣族、?颇族、哈尼族、彝族、苗族、佧瓦族等四十九个民族的学员在签名。云南日报　吕华昌摄
