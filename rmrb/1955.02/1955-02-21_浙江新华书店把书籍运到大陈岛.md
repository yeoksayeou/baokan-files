Title: Zhejiang Xinhua Bookstore Transports Books to Dachen Island
Author:
Date: 1955-02-21
Page: 3

    Zhejiang Xinhua Bookstore Transports Books to Dachen Island
    Xinhua Bookstore in Zhejiang Province has transported a large number of books to Dachen Island to supply the People's Liberation Army troops stationed on the island. These books include "On the Party," "How to Be a Good Communist Party Member," and "Strive for the Liberation of Taiwan." There are also literary works such as "Dedicate Everything to the Party," "Defend Yan'an," "How Steel Was Tempered," as well as "Selections of Poems from the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea" and "The Collected Works of Pushkin." In addition, a batch of mathematics, physics, and chemistry textbooks, song collections, and a large number of large-format pictures were also transported. According to the person in charge of the bookstore, they will soon transport the other two batches of books that have been prepared to Yushan Islands and Pishan Island. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 浙江新华书店把书籍运到大陈岛

1955-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

    浙江新华书店把书籍运到大陈岛
    浙江省的新华书店已把大批书籍运往大陈岛，供应进驻岛上的人民解放军部队。运去的这些书籍中有“论党”“怎样做一个共产党员”和“为解放台湾而斗争”等。还有“把一切献给党”“保卫延安”“钢铁是怎样炼成的”以及“抗美援朝诗选”和“普希金文集”等文学作品。此外还运去一批数学、物理、化学课本，歌曲选集和大量的大幅画片等。据书店负责人说：不久以后，他们还要把已经准备好了的另外两批书籍运到渔山列岛和披山岛上去。（新华社）
