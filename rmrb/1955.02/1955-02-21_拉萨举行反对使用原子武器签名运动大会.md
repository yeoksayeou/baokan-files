Title: Lhasa Holds Rally for Signature Campaign Against the Use of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-02-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, February 20th: Representatives from all circles in Lhasa held a rally on the afternoon of the 19th for a signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons. All 259 attendees took the lead in signing the Tibetan version of the "Letter to the People of the World." Those who signed included secular and religious officials from the Tibetan local government, the military, the Panchen Kanpo Conference Hall, the Potala Palace, representatives from the three major monasteries, various people's organizations, and working cadres stationed in Tibet.

At the meeting, upon the proposal of the chairman and with unanimous approval, the Tibetan Local People's Signature Campaign Committee Against the Use of Atomic Weapons was established. The committee is chaired by Xia Su · Jumo Tseren Dorje, the acting Kalon of the Tibetan local government, and the vice chairmen are Taiji · Delerao Deng from the Panchen Kanpo Conference Hall, Kaimo · Suoan Wangdui, the commander of the Tibetan local government's Zha military, the Grand Secretary of the Tibetan local government's Yicang, Grand Lama Ronglangse · Tuden Losang, and Chen Jingbo and Tang Huatao, representatives from various Lhasa organizations.

Xia Su · Jumo Tseren Dorje, the acting Kalon of the Tibetan local government and chairman of the meeting, reported on the criminal conspiracy of the American aggressor clique to prepare for atomic warfare and the appeal of the World Peace Council. Representatives from all walks of life also spoke out against the use of atomic weapons. Duwa Kanpo, a representative of the three major monasteries, said: We, all the monks and nuns of Tibet, warmly respond to the call of the World Peace Council. We will sign to oppose the use of atomic weapons and pray for world peace. The 76-year-old poet Awa Cazhu excitedly shouted at the meeting: Let us all sign the "Letter to the People of the World"! Warn the American warmongers not to go mad, and show our determination and strength to oppose the use of atomic weapons and liberate Taiwan. In his speech, Taiji · Delerao Deng from the Panchen Kanpo Conference Hall, representing the officials and lamas affiliated with Tashilhunpo Monastery in Xigaze, expressed their intention to join the people of Lhasa in launching a signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons.

Also speaking at the meeting were Mrs. Doga · Qimei Banzhong, Chairman of the Patriotic Women's Federation, and representatives from the Patriotic Youth Cultural Association.

Awang Jiayang, Vice Chairman of the Tibetan Autonomous Region People's Government of Xikang Province, who came to express condolences to the road construction workers on the Kangzang and Qingzang highways on behalf of the Xikang Provincial People's Government, and Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, also attended the meeting and spoke.

### 拉萨举行反对使用原子武器签名运动大会

1955-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨举行反对使用原子武器签名运动大会
    【新华社拉萨二十日电】十九日下午，拉萨市各界代表举行反对使用原子武器签名运动大会，出席大会的二百五十九人都领先在藏文的“告全世界人民书”上签了名。这些签名的人包括西藏地方政府、军队、班禅堪布会议厅、布达拉宫的僧俗官员，三大寺、各人民团体代表以及入藏工作干部等。
    会上，经大会主席提议并一致通过成立了西藏地方人民反对使用原子武器签名运动委员会。委员会由西藏地方政府代理噶伦夏苏·举墨策仁多吉任主任委员，班禅堪布会议厅台吉·德来饶登、西藏地方政府札萨藏军司令凯墨·索安旺堆、西藏地方政府译仓大秘书大喇嘛绒朗色·土登洛桑、拉萨各机关代表陈竞波和汤化陶五人为副主任委员。
    大会主席西藏地方政府代理噶伦夏苏·举墨策仁多吉就美国侵略集团准备进行原子战争的罪恶阴谋和世界和平理事会的号召作了报告。各界代表也纷纷发言反对使用原子武器。三大寺代表都瓦堪布说：我们西藏全体僧众热烈响应世界和平理事会的号召。我们要为反对使用原子武器签名，为世界和平祈祷。七十六岁的老诗人阿瓦擦珠在会上激动地高呼：让我们都在“告全世界人民书”上签名！警告美国战争贩子不要发狂，显示我们反对使用原子武器、解放台湾的决心和力量。班禅堪布会议厅台吉·德来饶登发言中代表日喀则札什伦布寺所属的官员和喇嘛表示将和拉萨各界人民一起展开反对使用原子武器的签名运动。
    在会上讲话的还有爱国妇女联谊会主任委员朵噶夫人·齐美班仲和爱国青年文化联谊会的代表。
    代表西康省人民政府前来慰问康藏、青藏公路筑路人员的西康省藏族自治区人民政府副主席阿旺嘉错和中国人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华也出席了会议并讲了话。
