Title: Hungarian People Eagerly Sign Petitions Against Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-02-16
Page: 4

The Hungarian people throughout the country are launching a signature campaign against atomic weapons. Workers, peasants, scientists, artists, and people from all walks of life are eagerly signing the World Peace Council Standing Committee's appeal to the people of the world for the destruction and prohibition of the manufacture of atomic weapons. The signatures of millions of people are a serious blow to the warmongers. (Hungarian National Photo Agency, Xinhua News Agency) (with picture)
　　
　　The thirty members of the family of baker Bo. Suha in Budapest have all signed the appeal to the people of the world for the destruction and prohibition of the manufacture of atomic weapons.
　　
　　I. Kasini, a model worker at the Budapest Small Motor Factory, signed the appeal to the people of the world for the destruction and prohibition of the manufacture of atomic weapons.
　　
　　Workers at the Knishi Cannery in Kecskemét, Hungary, signed the appeal to the people of the world for the destruction and prohibition of the manufacture of atomic weapons.

### 匈牙利人民踊跃签名反对原子武器

1955-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人民踊跃签名反对原子武器
    匈牙利全国各地正展开反对原子武器的签名运动，广大的工人、农民、科学家和艺术工作者，以及各阶层的人民都踊跃在世界和平理事会常务委员会关于销毁和禁止制造原子武器的告全世界人民书上签名。千百万人民的签名就是对战争挑拨者的严重打击。匈牙利国家图片社稿（新华社发）（附图片）
    布达佩斯的面包工人伯·苏哈一家三十口人，都在销毁和禁止制造原子武器的告全世界人民书上签了名。
    布达佩斯小型电机厂模范工人伊·卡西尼在销毁和禁止制造原子武器的告全世界人民书上签名。
    匈牙利凯斯克门特的克尼西罐头制造厂工人在销毁和禁止制造原子武器的告全世界人民书上签名。
