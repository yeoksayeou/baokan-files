Title: Burmese Minister of Information Hosts Farewell Banquet for Chinese Cultural Delegation; Head of Delegation Zheng Zhenduo Holds Farewell Reception in Rangoon
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 18th: Burmese Minister of Information, U Tun Win, Chairman of the Reception Committee for the Chinese Cultural Delegation to the Union of Burma, hosted a farewell banquet on the evening of the 16th to bid farewell to the Chinese Cultural Delegation. Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

Those present included Burmese Minister of Culture, U Win; Minister of Trade, U Ra Shi; Chief Justice of the Supreme Court, U Tin Maung; Speaker of the House of Nationalities of the Parliament, Sao Hkun Hkio; former Burmese Ambassador to China, U Myint Toon; Daw Khin Kyi, wife of Aung San; and Member of Parliament, Bo Mya Sein, among others.

More than 400 people from the Burmese theater, music, and cultural circles, as well as overseas Chinese, also attended the banquet.

U Tun Win said in his speech at the banquet that those who had seen the performances of the Chinese Cultural Delegation had expressed warm praise for their performances. He said that mutual visits by delegations could promote friendship and cultural development between countries and help consolidate peace. He expressed his hope and belief that cultural exchanges between China and Burma would be more vibrant in the future, and that the friendship between the two countries would be further enhanced.

Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, said in his speech that we firmly believe that under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, friendly visits between Chinese and Burmese delegations will further develop and consolidate the friendship between the two countries. This friendship is particularly important today because it is an important force for safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

At the banquet, Daw Khin Kyi, wife of Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, presented a silver shield each to the dance troupe, orchestra, singing troupe, and Peking Opera troupe of the Chinese Cultural Delegation on behalf of Prime Minister U Nu, and presented gold medals to Chinese classical opera actors Li Shaochun, Zhang Meijuan, and suona player Ren Tongxiang. U Tun Win, on behalf of the reception committee, presented the delegation with gifts such as books, oil paintings, sculptures, and ethnic minority costumes of Burma.

Head of Delegation Zheng Zhenduo presented a set of costumes and props for the Lotus Flower Dance to Prime Minister U Nu, which were accepted by Daw Khin Kyi on his behalf.

After the banquet, Burmese music and drama figures and members of the delegation held a get-together. They happily sang songs of Sino-Burmese friendship together.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 18th: Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, held a farewell reception in Rangoon on the evening of the 15th.

Those present at the reception included Burmese Minister of Culture, U Win; Minister of Information, U Tun Win; Chief Justice of the Supreme Court, U Tin Maung; Speaker of the House of Representatives of the Burmese Parliament, Bo Mu Aung; Member of Parliament, Bo Mya Sein; Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association, Sin Po Ho, and members of the association's council; Chairman of the Burma-Soviet Cultural Association, U Aung Hla; Chairman of the Burmese Film and Drama Association, U Chin Rhei; figures from the cultural and artistic circles; and representatives of overseas Chinese.

Also present at the reception were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to Burma.

Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and embassy staff also attended the reception.

A total of more than 500 people attended the reception.

The reception took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 缅甸宣传部长设宴欢送我国文化代表团  郑振铎团长在仰光举行临别招待会

1955-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸宣传部长设宴欢送我国文化代表团
    郑振铎团长在仰光举行临别招待会
    【新华社十八日讯】仰光消息：缅甸联邦政府欢迎中国文化代表团招待委员会主席、宣传部长吴顿温十六日晚举行送别宴会，欢送中国文化代表团。中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和大使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的有缅甸文化部长吴温、贸易部长吴拉实、最高法院首席法官吴登貌、国会民族院议长萧恢塔、缅甸前任驻中国大使吴敏登、昂山夫人和国会议员冒色者等。
    出席宴会的还有缅甸戏剧界、音乐界和文化界人士及华侨等共四百多人。
    吴顿温在宴会上致词说，看过中国文化代表团演出的人都对他们的演出表示热烈的赞扬。他说，互派代表团访问，可以促进国家之间的友好和文化的发展，并且有助于巩固和平。他表示希望并且相信今后中缅两国文化交流将更富有生命力，两国友好将更加增进。
    中国文化代表团团长郑振铎致词说，我们深信在和平共处的五项原则的指导下，中缅两国代表团相互间的友好访问，必将进一步发展和巩固两国间的友谊。这种友谊在今天特别重要，因为它是保卫亚洲和世界和平的重要力量。
    在宴会上，缅甸总理吴努的夫人都妙意代表吴努总理赠送给中国文化代表团舞蹈队、乐队、歌唱队和京剧队银盾各一座，赠送给中国古典剧名演员李少春、张美娟和唢呐演奏员任同祥金质奖章各一枚。吴顿温代表招待委员会向代表团赠送书籍、油画、雕刻和缅甸少数民族服装等礼物。
    郑振铎团长把荷花舞的服装道具一套赠送给吴努总理，由都妙意代表接受。
    宴会结束后，缅甸音乐和戏剧界人士和代表团团员进行联欢。他们一起愉快地唱缅中友好歌。
    【新华社十八日讯】仰光消息：中国文化代表团郑振铎团长十五日晚在仰光举行临别招待会。
    出席招待会的有缅甸联邦政府文化部长吴温，宣传部长吴顿温、最高法院首席法官吴登貌，缅甸联邦国会代表院议长波木昂、国会议员冒色者，缅中友好协会主席信波和协会的理事，缅苏文化协会主席吴昂拉、缅甸电影戏剧协会主席吴阵锐和文化艺术界人士，以及华侨代表等。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻缅甸的外交使节和外交官员。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和大使馆人员也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的共约五百多人。
    招待会在友好的空气中进行。
