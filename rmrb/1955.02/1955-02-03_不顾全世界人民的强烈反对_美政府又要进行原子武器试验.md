Title: Despite the Strong Opposition of People Around the World, the US Government is Going to Conduct Atomic Weapons Tests Again

Author:

Date: 1955-02-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 31st: Regardless of the strong demands of people around the world for the prohibition of atomic weapons, the United States Government Atomic Energy Commission has announced that it will conduct a series of atomic weapons tests in February. In a report submitted to the US Congress on January 29, the commission said that the new series of tests would examine the use of various atomic weapons in war. The Associated Press revealed that the new tests will involve eight to twelve atomic explosions. The US government's choice to conduct atomic weapons tests at this time is clearly intended to intimidate the peace-loving people of the world.

### 不顾全世界人民的强烈反对  美政府又要进行原子武器试验

1955-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    不顾全世界人民的强烈反对
    美政府又要进行原子武器试验
   【新华社三十一日讯】华盛顿消息：不顾全世界人民关于禁止原子武器的强烈要求，美国政府原子能委员会又宣布要在二月份举行一连串的原子武器试验。这个委员会在一月二十九日向美国国会提出的报告中说，新的一连串试验是要试验在战争中使用各种原子武器的问题。合众社透露，新的试验中将有八次到十二次的原子爆炸。美国政府所以选择在这个时机进行原子武器试验，目的显然是为了要恫吓全世界要求和平的人民。
