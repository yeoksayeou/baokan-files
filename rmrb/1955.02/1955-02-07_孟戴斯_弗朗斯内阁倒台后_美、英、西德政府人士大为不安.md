Title: After the Fall of the Mendès France Cabinet, U.S., British, and West German Government Officials Were Greatly Uneasy
Author:
Date: 1955-02-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The fall of the French Mendès France government, which had signed the Paris Agreements, has caused great anxiety among government figures in the United States, Britain, and West Germany. They fear this event may delay the timetable for ratification of the Paris Agreements, or even affect their approval.

United Press International commented on the fall of the Mendès France government:

“(U.S.) officials acknowledge that this failure has cast doubt on the timetable for ratification of the Paris Agreements.” Agence France-Presse also pointed out that Washington figures are concerned that Mendès France’s fall will “delay the Senate's approval of the Paris Agreements.”

Reuters reported that the failure of the Mendès France government
“has greatly shocked official circles in London…” “Current attention is focused on the fate of the Paris Agreements on the rearmament of West Germany—the cornerstone of Western European policy.” The news agency said, "London is concerned that if the Senate insists on amendments or if its vote is in danger of being delayed, the whole plan may collapse for lack of a Mendès France who can get these agreements passed." The United Press International correspondent in London also reported that diplomats in London believe that due to the fall of the Mendès France government, the fate of the "Western European Union" is uncertain.

According to a United Press International correspondent in Bonn, the fall of the French Mendès France government "has shocked the West German government." West German officials expressed concern about the prospect of France ratifying the Paris Agreements. According to the reporter, the fall of the Mendès France government is a setback for Adenauer personally, because Adenauer placed great hope in Mendès France, believing that he had the ability to get the rebellious French parliament to pass the Paris Agreements.

### 孟戴斯—弗朗斯内阁倒台后  美、英、西德政府人士大为不安

1955-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    孟戴斯—弗朗斯内阁倒台后
    美、英、西德政府人士大为不安
    【新华社六日讯】　签订了巴黎协定的法国孟戴斯—弗朗斯政府的倒台，在美国、英国和西德政府人士中引起了很大不安。他们担心这件事可能推迟批准巴黎协定的时间表，甚至会影响巴黎协定的批准。
    合众社在评论孟戴斯—弗朗斯政府垮台时说：
  “（美国）官员们承认，这次失败使得巴黎协定批准的时间表成了问题。”法新社也指出，华盛顿人士担心：孟戴斯—弗朗斯的倒台会“推迟共和国参议院批准巴黎协定”。
    路透社报道说，孟戴斯—弗朗斯政府的失败
  “使伦敦的官方人士大为震惊……”“当前的注意力集中在关于重新武装西德的巴黎协定——西方国家的欧洲政策的基石——的命运上。”这家通讯社说，“伦敦方面担心：如果共和国参议院坚持修正或者它的表决有拖延的危险，整个计划可能因为少了能够使这些协定得到通过的孟戴斯—弗朗斯而垮台。”合众社驻伦敦记者也报道说，伦敦的外交人士认为，由于孟戴斯—弗朗斯政府的垮台，“西欧联盟”的命运就不肯定了。
    又据合众社驻波恩记者报道，法国孟戴斯—弗朗斯政府的垮台使“西德政府感到震惊”。西德官方人士对法国批准巴黎协定的前景表示担心。据这位记者报道，孟戴斯—弗朗斯政府的垮台对于阿登纳个人是一种挫折，因为阿登纳对于孟戴斯—弗朗斯寄予很大希望，认为他有能力使表示反抗的法国议会通过巴黎协定。
