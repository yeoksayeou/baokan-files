Title: Resolution of the State Council of the People's Republic of China on the Soviet Union's Proposal to Assist China in Studying the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-02-01
Page: 1

The Chinese people and the Government of the People's Republic of China warmly welcome the proposal of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union on January 17, 1955, regarding Soviet assistance to the People's Republic of China in the fields of science, technology, and industry to promote research into the peaceful uses of atomic energy.

The Soviet Union has achieved brilliant accomplishments in the peaceful use of atomic energy, creating the world's first atomic power station in 1954. The Chinese people are thrilled by this. Now, the Soviet government has decided to assist the People's Republic of China, the Polish People's Republic, the Czechoslovak Republic, the Romanian People's Republic, and the German Democratic Republic in developing research into the peaceful uses of atomic energy, and is considering expanding the scope of countries it can assist, so that the Soviet Union's outstanding scientific achievements in the peaceful use of atomic energy can widely benefit mankind. The Chinese people and government warmly support the great goal embodied in this noble decision of the Soviet government: to use atomic energy for peaceful purposes on the basis of international cooperation, in order to promote the development of human civilization. This is a shining manifestation of the Soviet Union's peaceful diplomatic policies. This is a further development of the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union. The Chinese people and government express their heartfelt gratitude for this sincere and selfless assistance from the Soviet Union. Due to the long-term oppression of imperialism and feudalism, the development of science and technology in China is lagging behind. However, we are deeply convinced that in liberated China, its scientists, engineers, and workers, with the sincere assistance of the Soviet Union, will surely be able to quickly master the technology of using atomic energy.

Atomic reactors can generate electricity for peaceful service, and also dangerous fissile materials for war service to manufacture atomic weapons. Obviously, only by prohibiting the use of atomic and hydrogen weapons can the efforts to make peaceful use of atomic energy benefit the people of all countries in the world be fully and effectively developed.

At present, the American aggressive bloc not only continues to oppose the prohibition of the use of large-scale destructive atomic and hydrogen weapons, but is also frantically clamoring for and preparing for atomic war. Although they have long lost their monopoly on atomic and hydrogen weapons, they are still attempting to use atomic weapons everywhere as blackmail capital to expand aggression and prepare for war. In the East, the American aggressive bloc is attempting to use the threat of atomic weapons to achieve its goals of seizing foreign territories and preparing for new wars. This will absolutely not intimidate the Chinese people and the people of the East. The Chinese people firmly advocate the prohibition of the use of atomic and hydrogen weapons, and resolutely oppose the criminal policy of preparing for atomic war.

With the help and cooperation of the Soviet Union, the Chinese people will, together with the peace-loving people of the world, unremittingly fight against atomic war, for the prohibition of the use of atomic and hydrogen weapons, and for the promotion of the peaceful use of atomic energy. History proves that all advanced scientific inventions will ultimately lead to the progress of mankind. We firmly believe that the warmongers who attempt to use atomic energy to destroy mankind will only destroy themselves, and the final victory will belong to the peace-loving countries and people who insist on the peaceful use of atomic energy.

### 中华人民共和国国务院  关于苏联建议帮助中国研究和平利用原子能问题的决议

1955-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国国务院
    关于苏联建议帮助中国研究和平利用原子能问题的决议
    （一九五五年一月三十一日国务院全体会议第四次会议通过）
    中国人民和中华人民共和国政府热烈欢迎苏联部长会议在一九五五年一月十七日关于苏联在促进原子能和平用途的研究方面给予中华人民共和国以科学、技术和工业上的帮助的建议。
    苏联在和平利用原子能方面取得了人类历史上的辉煌成就，在一九五四年创立了世界上第一个原子能发电站。中国人民为此而欢欣鼓舞。现在，苏联政府决定帮助中华人民共和国、波兰人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国和德意志民主共和国发展原子能和平用途方面的研究并正在考虑扩大它所能够援助的国家的范围，使苏联在和平利用原子能方面的卓越科学成果得以广泛地为人类造福。中国人民和政府热烈支持苏联政府这一崇高决定所体现的伟大目标：在国际合作的基础上，把原子能用于和平的目的，以促进人类文明的发展。这是苏联和平外交政策的光辉表现。这是中苏伟大友谊的一个新的发展。中国人民和政府对于苏联这一真诚无私的帮助，表示衷心的感谢。由于帝国主义和封建主义的长期压迫，中国的科学和技术的发展是落后的。但是，我们深信，在解放了的中国，它的科学家、工程师和工人们，在苏联的真诚帮助下，一定能够迅速掌握使用原子能的技术。
    原子反应堆能够产生为和平服务的电力，也能够产生为战争服务的供制造原子武器用的危险的分裂物质。很显然，只有禁止使用原子武器和氢武器，才有可能使和平利用原子能为世界各国人民造福的努力获得充分有效的发展。
    目前，美国侵略集团不仅仍然反对禁止使用大规模毁灭性的原子武器和氢武器，而且正在疯狂地叫嚣和准备原子战争。它们虽然早已丧失了原子武器和氢武器的垄断地位，却还到处企图以原子武器为扩大侵略、准备战争的讹诈资本。在东方，美国侵略集团正企图用原子武器的恐吓来达到它霸占外国领土、准备新的战争的目的。这是绝对不能吓倒中国人民和东方人民的。中国人民坚决主张禁止使用原子武器和氢武器，坚决反对准备原子战争的罪恶政策。
    中国人民在苏联的帮助和合作下，将同全世界爱好和平人民一起，为反对原子战争、禁止使用原子武器和氢武器、为促进和平利用原子能而奋斗不懈。历史证明，一切先进的科学发明终将导致人类的进步。我们坚信，企图利用原子能来毁灭人类的好战分子将只能毁灭他们自己，而最后胜利的将是坚持和平利用原子能的爱好和平的国家和人民。
