    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers in various regions are vigorously increasing the production of oil crops to meet the needs of national construction and people's lives. The Cangyuan Agricultural Production Cooperative in Qingpu County, Jiangsu Province, has planted more than 520 mu of rapeseed. Cooperative members are fertilizing the fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农民正在大力增产油料作物，以满足国家建设和人民生活的需要。江苏青浦县仓园农业生产合作社种植了五百二十余亩油菜，社员们正在田间施肥。
