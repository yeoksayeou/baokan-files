Title: Libya Announces Participation in Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-02-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Benghazi, February 11th: Libyan Prime Minister Mustafa Halim stated at a cabinet meeting on the 1st that Libya had accepted the invitation to attend the Asian-African Conference to be held in Indonesia in April. Halim will lead the Libyan delegation to the conference.

### 利比亚宣布将参加亚非会议

1955-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    利比亚宣布将参加亚非会议
    【新华社十一日讯】班加西消息：利比亚首相穆斯塔法·哈利姆一日在内阁会议上说，利比亚已经接受了参加将在四月间在印度尼西亚举行的亚非会议的邀请。哈利姆将率领利比亚代表团参加会议。
