Title: The Burmese Government Believes Taiwan is Territory of the People's Republic of China. Vice-Chairman of the Indonesian Peace Committee Condemns US Interference in China's Internal Affairs.
Author:
Date: 1955-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Rangoon dispatch: According to a report in the Burmese newspaper "People's Daily" on January 30th, citing the Burmese-language "Yangon Daily," a Burmese government spokesman said that the Burmese government believes that Taiwan is the territory of the People's Republic of China, and this position is firm and unwavering at all times. The Burmese government is willing to work towards this end.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 6th: Professor Priyono, Vice-Chairman of the Indonesian National Peace Committee, issued a statement to the press on the 5th, stating that the issue of mainland China and Taiwan should be considered China's own internal affair. Other parties should not interfere.
	Priyono, in criticizing the attitude of the United States, said that the recent demonstrations by the American peace movement against President Eisenhower's decision clearly demonstrate that there are wise and brave people in the United States.
	In his statement, Priyono also emphasized that the People's Republic of China should be allowed to enter the United Nations.

### 缅甸政府认为台湾是中华人民共和国国土  印度尼西亚和平委员会副主席斥责美国干涉中国内政

1955-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府认为台湾是中华人民共和国国土
    印度尼西亚和平委员会副主席斥责美国干涉中国内政
　　【新华社七日讯】仰光消息：据缅甸“人民报”一月三十日引缅文“仰光日报”消息，缅甸政府发言人说，缅甸政府认为台湾是中华人民共和国的国土，这个主张是任何时候都坚定不移的，缅甸政府并愿为这个主张而努力。
　　【新华社雅加达六日电】印度尼西亚全国和平委员会副主席普里约诺教授五日向报界发表声明说，关于中国大陆和台湾的问题应该认为是中国自己的内政问题。其他方面不应该干涉。　　普里约诺在指责美国的态度时说，美国的和平运动最近举行的反对艾森豪威尔总统的决定的示威，清楚地证明了美国是有明智和勇敢的人们的。　　普里约诺在声明中还强调应该让中华人民共和国进入联合国。
