Author:
Date: 1955-02-12
Page: 1

To Welcome the Fifth Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance
People All Over the Country Are Actively Preparing for Celebration Activities
[Our Newspaper] To warmly welcome the arrival of the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, people all over the country are actively preparing various celebration activities.

On the anniversary and in the days before and after, people from all walks of life in Beijing, Shanghai, Shenyang, Harbin, Dalian and other cities will hold celebration rallies and various celebration activities. Workers, cadres of institutions, and students in Beijing will hold friendship gatherings with Soviet experts and Soviet students on the 12th. The Communist Youth League organizations and young people in various units in Shanghai will hold various small-scale celebration activities in combination with the education of young people in communist moral character; many agricultural producers' cooperatives in the suburbs will invite mutual aid teams and individual farmers to hold discussion meetings. Dalian will hold celebration rallies simultaneously in four districts on the eve of the celebration; factories, enterprises, schools and other units with Soviet experts will also hold get-togethers before and after the celebration. Workers and staff of factories and enterprises, farmers in townships, troops stationed in Dalian of the People's Liberation Army and staff of institutions, and students of schools have respectively held get-togethers with the Soviet troops stationed in Lushun. At each get-together, famous combat heroes and model figures from the Soviet army were invited to speak; both sides also performed brilliant cultural programs.

In order to further strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship and learn from the Soviet Union, various places are planning to hold relevant report meetings. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association plans to hold three reports for various units: "Sino-Soviet Friendship and World Peace", "Soviet Economic and Technical Assistance to China" and "Soviet Assistance to China's Agricultural Construction". The first report meeting was held on the evening of the 9th. Some members of the delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and the Chinese Film Workers who visited the Soviet Union will make reports to people from all walks of life on their visit to the Soviet Union before and after the anniversary. Many factories in Shanghai will hold report meetings and story meetings focusing on consolidating Sino-Soviet friendship, learning from the Soviet Union's advanced experience, and supporting the liberation of Taiwan, opposing American war provocations, and defending the Far East and world peace. The Nanjing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Jiangsu Provincial Association for Science and Technology Popularization, the Nanjing Library, the Nanjing People's Broadcasting Station and other units will jointly hold a report meeting on the Soviet Union's use of atomic energy for peaceful construction, reading report meetings on the Soviet novels "Virgin Soil Upturned" and "Our Vital Business", as well as Soviet film evenings and Soviet music appreciation sessions. Medical staff, educational staff, and staff of the Lenin Communist Youth League of the Soviet troops stationed in Dalian were invited to make reports to relevant departments in Dalian. Units in Qiqihar with Soviet experts will also conduct a review of the situation of learning from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and implementing the suggestions of Soviet experts.

Various cities will hold photo exhibitions and show Soviet films to widely introduce the Soviet Union and promote Sino-Soviet friendship to the masses. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will exhibit photos such as "Great Friendship", "The Peaceful and Constructive Labor of the Soviet People" and "The Soviet Union's Republics" in the exhibition rooms and shop windows of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center, the Labor People's Cultural Palace, Beihai, Zhongshan Park, Tianqiao, Wangfujing and other twelve places; it has also prepared dozens of sets of small pictures and distributed them to cultural centers in various districts, some large industrial and mining enterprises, construction sites; it will also supply more than 800 sets of posters on "Soviet Collective Farms" and "To Mechanize Agriculture, We Must Develop Industry" to farmers in the suburbs. In addition, Soviet films on agricultural collectivization have been prepared for farmers in the suburbs and will be screened on a tour after February 14. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center will also organize five Soviet music film concerts. The Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has prepared 18,000 sets of posters of "The Soviet Union's Great Assistance to China's Economic Construction", as well as a lot of written propaganda reference materials on strengthening Sino-Soviet friendship and safeguarding world peace, for propaganda during the celebration. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Gallery in Xinjiekou and Xuanwu Lake in Nanjing and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Room in the Workers' Cultural Palace, as well as the display windows of major companies and stores, are replacing new pictures to promote Sino-Soviet friendship. The Liaoning People's Publishing House has published a large number of pictorials introducing the deeds of Soviet experts in developing the profound friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union through selfless labor in Anshan Iron and Steel, the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, Dalian Shipbuilding Company, and Fuxin Coal Mine. Dalian held photo exhibitions of Sino-Soviet friendship deeds in ten places throughout the city at the same time. From February 1, the Soviet troops stationed in Lushunkou began screening movies for the people of Dalian in four cinemas and nearby rural areas. The Harbin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will hold a large-scale photo exhibition "The Consolidation and Development of Sino-Soviet Friendship in the Past Five Years." Various propaganda materials compiled by the Heilongjiang Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association have been distributed to various places.

### 迎接中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年  全国各地人民热烈筹备庆祝活动

1955-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    迎接中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年
    全国各地人民热烈筹备庆祝活动
    【本报讯】全国各地人民热烈迎接中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年纪念日的到来，正在积极筹备各种庆祝活动。
    纪念日当天和前后，北京、上海、沈阳、哈尔滨、旅大等城市各界人民将举行庆祝大会和各种庆祝活动。北京市工人、机关干部、学生将在十二日和苏联专家、苏联留学生举行友谊联欢会。上海市各单位青年团组织和青年群众，将结合对青年的共产主义道德品质的教育，举行各种小型的庆祝活动；郊区的许多农业生产合作社将邀请互助组和单干农民举行座谈会。旅大全市在庆祝日前夕，将分四个地区同时举行庆祝大会；有苏联专家的工厂、企业、学校等单位，也将在庆祝日前后举行联欢会。各工厂企业的职工、乡镇的农民、人民解放军驻旅大的部队和机关工作人员、学校的学生，已分别和驻旅顺口的苏军进行联欢活动。在每次联欢会上，苏军方面的著名战斗英雄和模范人物，都应邀讲了话；双方还都表演了精采的文艺节目。
    为了进一步加强中苏友谊和向苏联学习，各地都计划举行有关的报告会。北京市中苏友好协会计划为各单位举行“中苏友好与世界和平”、“苏联对我国经济技术的援助”和“苏联对我国农业建设的援助”等三个报告。第一个报告会已在九日晚举行。中苏友好协会、中华全国科学技术普及协会、中国电影工作者等访苏代表团在京部分团员，将在纪念日前后向各界人民作访苏报告。上海市许多工厂将举行以巩固中苏友谊、学习苏联先进经验，结合支援解放台湾、反对美国战争挑衅、保卫远东和世界和平为内容的报告会、故事会。南京市中苏友好协会和江苏省科学技术普及协会、南京图书馆、南京人民广播电台等单位，将联合举办关于苏联将原子能用于和平建设的报告会、苏联小说“被开垦的处女地”和“我们的切身事业”的读书报告会，以及苏联电影晚会和苏联音乐欣赏会等。旅大市苏军部队的医务工作人员、教育工作人员和列宁共产主义青年团的工作人员，应邀向旅大市各有关部门作报告。齐齐哈尔有苏联专家的单位还将检查一次学习苏联先进经验和执行苏联专家建议的情况。
    各城市将举行图片展览会和放映苏联影片，广泛向人民群众介绍苏联和宣传中苏友好。北京市中苏友好协会将在北京市中苏友好协会文化馆、劳动人民文化宫、北海、中山公园、天桥、王府井等十二处的展览室和橱窗中展出“伟大的友谊”、
  “苏联人民和平建设性劳动”、“苏联各加盟共和国”等图片；又准备了几十套小型图片，发给各区文化馆、部分大工矿企业、工地展览；还将供给郊区农民八百多套“苏联的集体农庄”和“要使农业机械化必须发展工业”挂图。此外还为郊区农民准备了有关农业集体化的苏联影片，在二月十四日后下乡巡回放映。北京市中苏友好协会文化馆还将组织五场苏联音乐电影会。上海市中苏友好协会已经准备了一万八千套“苏联对我国经济建设伟大援助”的挂图，以及许多有关加强中苏友好保卫世界和平等文字宣传参考资料，供庆祝时进行宣传之用。南京市新街口和玄武湖的中苏友好画廊和工人文化宫的中苏友好室，以及各大公司、商店的展览橱窗，正在更换宣传中苏友谊的新图片。辽宁人民出版社已经出版了大批画册，介绍苏联专家在我国鞍钢、第一汽车制造厂、大连造船公司和阜新煤矿等地，以忘我劳动发展中苏两国人民深厚友谊的事迹。旅大在全市十个地方同时举办了中苏友谊事迹的图片展览会。驻旅顺口苏军从二月一日开始，在四个电影院和附近农村为旅大市人民放映电影。哈尔滨市中苏友好协会将举办一个“五年来中苏友谊巩固与发展”大型图片展览会。黑龙江省中苏友好协会编制的各种宣传材料已陆续发往各地。
