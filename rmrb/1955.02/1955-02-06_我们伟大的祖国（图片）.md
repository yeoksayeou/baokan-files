Our Great Motherland
	The Young Pioneers of Class 4, Grade 3, of Huixing Girls' Middle School in Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province, learned that the PLA soldiers had planted the horsetail pine seeds they sent on Yijiangshan Island. They are now preparing to send photos, red scarves, and report cards to the soldiers to congratulate them on their greater victories.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    浙江省杭州市惠兴女子中学初三级第四班的少年先锋队员们，听到解放军战士已将她们寄去的马尾松种子种在一江山岛上之后，她们又准备把照片、红领巾和成绩单寄给战士们，祝贺他们取得更大的胜利。
