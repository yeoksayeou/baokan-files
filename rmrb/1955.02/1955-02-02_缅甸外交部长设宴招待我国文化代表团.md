Title: Burmese Foreign Minister Hosts Banquet for Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-02-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 1st: The Foreign Minister of the Union of Burma, Sao Khun Hkio, hosted a banquet on the evening of January 31st in honor of the Chinese Cultural Delegation. Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, and all members of the delegation attended the banquet.

    Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

    Those present as hosts included the Burmese Minister of Information, U Tun Win, senior Burmese government officials, and members of parliament. U Hla Maw, the Burmese Ambassador to China, who is currently in Rangoon, also attended the banquet.

    Diplomatic envoys and officials from the Soviet Union, India, and other countries stationed in Burma also attended the banquet.

    Overseas Chinese representatives, including Xu Simin, were also invited to attend the banquet. More than 300 people attended the banquet.

    The banquet proceeded throughout in a friendly atmosphere.

    That afternoon, delegation leader Zheng Zhenduo and members of the Chinese Cultural Delegation visited Rangoon University and attended a tea party held by Dr. Chan Htoon, Registrar of Rangoon University.

    Prior to this, on January 29th, Zheng Zhenduo and the members of the Chinese Cultural Delegation also held a symposium for the exchange of experiences with prominent Burmese musicians U Ba Than and playwrights and dancers. The symposium was presided over by U Win, the Burmese Minister of Culture.

### 缅甸外交部长设宴招待我国文化代表团

1955-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸外交部长设宴招待我国文化代表团
    【新华社一日讯】仰光消息：缅甸联邦政府外交部长藻昆卓一月三十一日晚设宴招待中国文化代表团。中国文化代表团团长郑振铎和全体团员出席了宴会。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明及使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的有缅甸宣传部长吴顿温，缅甸政府高级官员及国会议员等。目前在仰光的缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有苏联、印度等国驻缅甸的外交使节和官员。
    华侨代表徐四民等也应邀出席了宴会。出席宴会的共有三百余人。
    宴会始终在友好的气氛中进行。
    这一天的下午，郑振铎团长和中国文化代表团团员们曾参观了仰光大学，并且出席了仰光大学教务长阵旺博士举行的茶会。
    在此以前，郑振铎团长和中国文化代表团团员还曾在一月二十九日同缅甸著名的音乐家吴巴丹和戏剧家、舞蹈家们举行了经验交流座谈会。主持座谈会的是缅甸联邦文化部长吴温。
