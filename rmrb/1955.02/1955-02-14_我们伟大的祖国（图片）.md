    Our Great Motherland
    Some workshops of the auxiliary factories of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant have been put into production successively, which will supply the various basic workshops of the plant with the various tools they need, and accumulate experience for gradually shifting to full-scale production. This is a picture of workers studying and checking the rotation of machine tools with the help of Soviet expert Simonov before the start of work.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    第一汽车制造厂辅助工厂部分车间陆续投入生产，将供给该厂各基本车间所需的各种工具，并为逐步转入全面生产积累经验。这是开工前工人们在苏联专家西蒙诺夫帮助下，研究和检查机床转动的情况。
