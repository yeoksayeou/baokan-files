Title: Bainqen Erdini to Visit Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1955-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Bainqen Erdini Qoigyijabu, Vice Chairman of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, departed from Beijing on the afternoon of the 1st to visit various places in Northeast China. Sa Kongliao, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, accompanied Bainqen Erdini to Shenyang.

Accompanying Bainqen Erdini on his visit to Northeast China were: Gagyi Losang Nyima, Ji Jinmei, Ngawang Jinba, as well as Bainqen Erdini's family members and entourage.

Those who saw him off at the station included: Liu Chun and Yang Jingren, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China; Yi Lirong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China; as well as officials from the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing and the Bainqen Erdini's Office in Beijing.

Also at the station to see him off were: Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, and Fan Ming, Deputy Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region.

### 班禅额尔德尼去东北参观

1955-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼去东北参观
    据新华社讯：中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会副主席班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，一日下午从北京出发到东北各地参观。中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任萨空了陪送班禅额尔德尼到沈阳。
  随同班禅额尔德尼到东北参观的有：尕金洛桑尼玛、计晋美、纳旺金巴，以及班禅额尔德尼的家属和随行人员。
    到车站欢送的有：中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任刘春、杨静仁，中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会副秘书长易礼容，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初，以及达赖驻京办事处、班禅驻京办事处官员。
    到车站欢送的还有：中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武、西藏军区副政治委员范明。
