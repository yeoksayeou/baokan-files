Title: Indonesian President Sukarno Opposes Atomic and Hydrogen Bomb Tests
Author:
Date: 1955-02-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 27th: Indonesian President Sukarno said at a mass rally in Yogyakarta on the 25th that the Indonesian people demand a halt to atomic and hydrogen bomb tests. He said that the Indonesian people are making efforts to ensure that these bombs, which endanger humanity, no longer explode. He said that atomic energy should be used to benefit humanity.

### 印度尼西亚总统苏加诺  反对进行原子弹氢弹实验

1955-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总统苏加诺
    反对进行原子弹氢弹实验
      【新华社雅加达二十七日电】印度尼西亚总统苏加诺二十五日在日惹举行的一次群众大会上说，印度尼西亚人民要求停止进行原子弹和氢弹的实验。他说，印度尼西亚人民正在进行努力，使得这种危害人类的炸弹不再爆炸。他说，原子能应该用来为人类谋福利。
