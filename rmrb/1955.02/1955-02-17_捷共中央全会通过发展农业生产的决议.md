Title: CPCz Central Committee Plenary Session Approves Resolution on Developing Agricultural Production
Author:
Date: 1955-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 15th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia held a plenary session in Prague on February 10th and 11th.

The plenary session heard and discussed a report by V. Široký, member of the Political Bureau of the Party Central Committee, on further developing Czechoslovak agriculture and on the Party's work in rural areas. Twenty-two people spoke in the discussion. The First Secretary of the Party Central Committee, A. Novotný, made a concluding speech at the meeting.

The plenary session finally approved Široký's report and adopted a resolution on further implementing the Tenth Party Congress's directives on increasing agricultural production.

### 捷共中央全会通过发展农业生产的决议

1955-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央全会通过发展农业生产的决议
    【新华社布拉格十五日电】捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会二月十日和十一日在布拉格举行了全体会议。
    全体会议听取并且讨论了党中央委员会政治局委员威·西罗基所作的关于进一步发展捷克斯洛伐克农业和关于党在农村中的工作的报告。在讨论中发言的有二十二人。党中央委员会第一书记安·诺沃提尼在会上作了总结发言。
    全体会议最后批准了西罗基的报告，并且通过了一项关于进一步贯彻第十次党代表大会的提高农业生产的指示的决议。
