Title: Chinese Delegation Presents Sino-Soviet Friendship Medals to Soviet Army Officers and Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1955-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, February 23rd: The representatives of the People's Republic of China's delegation to慰问 (express sympathy and appreciation to) the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, including Zhang Naiqi, Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Zongxun, Xiao Jingguang, Liu Yalou, and Qian Junrui, presented Sino-Soviet Friendship Medals to the Soviet Army's land, sea, and air force units stationed in the Lushunkou area in three separate locations on the morning of February 23rd, the 37th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. At each location, the representatives congratulated the Soviet Army on its Army Day and expressed their sympathy and appreciation, and presented the Sino-Soviet Friendship Medals to officers, non-commissioned officers, and soldiers of all ranks in the Soviet Army.

### 我国代表团向苏军军官战士赠中苏友谊章

1955-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表团向苏军军官战士赠中苏友谊章
    【新华社旅大二十三日电】　中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团代表章乃器、朱学范、张宗逊、萧劲光、刘亚楼、钱俊瑞，在苏联建军三十七周年纪念日——二月二十三日上午分三处向驻旅顺口地区苏军陆、海、空军部队赠送中苏友谊章。代表们在各处向苏军祝贺建军节和表示慰问，并向苏军的各级军官、士官和战士赠送中苏友谊章。
