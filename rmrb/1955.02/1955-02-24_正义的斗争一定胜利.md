Title: The Just Struggle Will Surely Triumph
Author: Chen Yuan
Date: 1955-02-24
Page: 2

    The Just Struggle Will Surely Triumph
    Chen Yuan, President of Beijing Normal University
    We Chinese people have always loved peace and firmly opposed war. We love science and want to promote human progress with science and technology, and resolutely oppose the use of scientific achievements to manufacture weapons of murder. We support the peaceful use of atomic energy and international cooperation to benefit mankind more broadly, and resolutely oppose the use of atomic weapons to wage war and cause human suffering.
    We Chinese people have already grasped the destiny of our country and are using all our strength to strengthen our peaceful construction, and are using all our strength to seek a happier and better life for the people. Moreover, with the help of the great Soviet friendship, we will also use atomic energy for peaceful construction. Everything we do is for the purpose of defending international peace and developing the cause of human progress.
    We Chinese people have stood up. We cannot be intimidated, and we are invincible. Therefore, our struggle to liberate our territory of Taiwan cannot be resisted by any force.
    Anyone with reason in the world can distinguish between good and evil, support us, cheer for the achievements of our cause, and understand that our cause is a strong guarantee for promoting world peace.
    However, the American aggressor group is afraid of peace, hates the progress of the Chinese people, opposes our peaceful cause, opposes our liberation struggle, clamors for and manufactures war, and brandishes atomic weapons to challenge the Chinese people and all peace-loving people in the world. This inhumane and evil conspiracy cannot but arouse extreme indignation among the Chinese people and all peace-loving people in the world, and be resolutely opposed!
    We Chinese people will never be intimidated by the threat of war. In order to defend peace, we will never allow the heinous conspiracy of the American aggressor group to succeed. This is the firm will of our 600 million people, united as one. We will solemnly sign our names on the "Appeal to the People of the World" by the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council to express our determination and demonstrate our enormous strength. We also believe that all peace-loving people in the world will further unite, expose the conspiracy of the American aggressor group to prepare for atomic war, enthusiastically respond to the call of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, and fight to defend human peace and progress.
    Our struggle is a just struggle, so our strength is invincible! The just struggle will surely win victory!

### 正义的斗争一定胜利
陈垣
1955-02-24
第2版()
专栏：

    正义的斗争一定胜利
    北京师范大学校长　陈垣
    我们中国人民一贯爱好和平，坚决反对战争；我们热爱科学，要以科学技术促进人类进步，坚决反对以科学成果来制造杀人武器；我们拥护和平利用原子能并实行国际合作，以更广泛的为人类造福，坚决反对用原子武器进行战争，使人类遭受灾难。
    我们中国人民已经掌握了自己国家的命运，正在用一切力量加强我们的和平建设，正在用一切力量谋求人民的更幸福更美好的生活，而且我们也就要在伟大的苏联友谊的帮助下把原子能用于和平建设事业，我们的一切都是为了保卫国际和平和发展人类进步事业而奋斗。
    我们中国人民已经站起来了，我们是吓不倒的，我们是不可战胜的，因此，我们将要解放自己的领土台湾的斗争，也是没有任何力量可以抗拒的。
    全世界任何一个有理性的人，都会分辨出善恶是非，都会支持我们，都会为我们的事业的成就而欢呼，都可以了解：我们的事业是促进世界和平的坚强保障。
    美国侵略集团却害怕和平，仇视中国人民的进步，反对我们的和平事业，反对我们的解放斗争，叫嚣着制造着战争，并挥舞着原子武器向中国人民、向全世界爱好和平的人民挑战。这种灭绝人性的罪恶阴谋，不能不引起中国人民和全世界一切爱好和平的人民极端愤恨，并坚决反对！
    我们中国人民决不会被战争威胁吓倒，为了保卫和平，我们决不容许美国侵略集团的万恶阴谋得逞。这是我们六万万人民万众一心的坚决意志，我们要在世界和平理事会常委会告全世界人民书上，庄严的签上自己的名字，来表示自己的决心，来显示我们巨大的力量。我们也相信，全世界一切爱好和平的人民一定会进一步团结起来，揭穿美国侵略集团准备原子战争的阴谋，热烈响应世界和平理事会常委会的号召，为保卫人类和平和进步而斗争。
    我们是正义的斗争，所以我们的力量是无敌的！正义的斗争是一定能够赢得胜利的！
