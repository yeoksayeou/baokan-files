Title: At the Joint Session of the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Chairman Bulganin Expressed Gratitude for the Trust Placed in Him
Author:
Date: 1955-02-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th (TASS): The Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR continued their joint session on the afternoon of the 9th. Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, N.A. Bulganin, spoke at the meeting, and the representatives responded with warm and sustained applause. N.A. Bulganin thanked the deputies of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR for the honor bestowed upon him—appointing him as the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR. He said that he would do everything in his power to live up to this trust. N.A. Bulganin also spoke about the main current issues in the work of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

### 在苏联最高苏维埃联盟院民族院联席会议上  布尔加宁主席发言感谢对他的信任

1955-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    在苏联最高苏维埃联盟院民族院联席会议上
    布尔加宁主席发言感谢对他的信任
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院九日下午继续举行联席会议。苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁在会上发言，代表们报以热烈的、经久不息的掌声。尼·亚·布尔加宁感谢苏联最高苏维埃代表给予他的荣誉——任命他为苏联部长会议主席。他说，他将尽一切力量不辜负这个信任。尼·亚·布尔加宁还谈到了苏联部长会议工作中当前的主要问题。
