Title: The World's First Nuclear Power Plant (Photo)
Author:
Date: 1955-02-05
Page: 4

    The World's First Nuclear Power Plant
    A view of the world's first nuclear power plant - the Atomic Energy Power Station affiliated with the Soviet Academy of Sciences. This power plant does not generate electricity by using the heat energy produced by burning coal or other fuels, but by using the atomic energy released when uranium atoms are split in an atomic reactor.

    The atomic boiler (atomic reactor) used in the atomic power plant is entirely located underground and surrounded by a thick layer of concrete for protection, so that workers will not be harmed when radioactive rays are emitted during atomic fission. What can be seen in the picture are the protruding parts of the atomic boiler attachments.

    The high heat emitted by the atomic reactor turns the water in the heat exchanger into high-pressure steam. What can be seen in the picture are rows of heat exchangers.

    In the central control room, the staff controls all the equipment of the atomic boiler and the atomic power plant according to the instructions of various instruments, allowing electricity to be sent from here to factories and farms. Provided by the Soviet Academy of Sciences (Xinhua News Agency)

### 世界第一座原子能发电站（图片）

1955-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    世界第一座原子能发电站
    全世界第一座原子能发电站——苏联科学院所属原子能发电站的外景。这个发电站不是利用煤或其他燃料燃烧时所产生的热能来发电，而是利用原子反应堆中铀原子核分裂时所释放的原子能来发电的。
    原子能发电站所用的原子锅炉（原子反应堆），全部设置在地下，周围包着很厚的混凝土保护层，当原子核分裂而发出放射线时，工作人员不致受伤害。在图中所见到的，是原子锅炉附件的突出部分。
    原子反应堆所发出的高热，使热交换器里的水变成高压蒸汽。图中所见到的，就是一排排的热交换器。
    在中央控制室里，工作人员根据各种仪器的指示，控制原子锅炉和原子能发电站所有的设备，让电流从这里送到工厂和农庄。苏联科学院供稿（新华社发）
