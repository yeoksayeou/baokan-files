    Our Great Motherland
    The newly built boiler room of the state-owned Shanghai No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory has begun to deliver steam. The completely mechanized production process of the new boiler not only improves the workers' production efficiency, but also improves the workers' working conditions. This is a corner of the new boiler workshop of the factory.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营上海第一印染厂新建的锅炉间已经开始输送蒸气。新锅炉的全部机械化生产过程，不仅提高了工人的生产效率，而且改善了工人的劳动条件。这是该厂新锅炉车间的一角。
