Title: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai Holds Grand Reception to Entertain Representatives of the Soviet Army Stationed in the Lushunkou Area
Author:
Date: 1955-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, February 24th. Peng Dehuai, Head of the People's Republic of China Delegation to Comfort the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area, and Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, held a grand reception in Lüda City on the afternoon of the 24th to entertain representatives of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area.

Invited to attend were senior generals of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area, as well as representatives of officers, non-commissioned officers, and soldiers at all levels.

Also invited were Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and Belyanov, the Soviet Consul General in Dalian, and others.

Attending as escorts were the Deputy Heads of the Delegation: Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China; and representatives Lin Feng, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Naiqi, Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Zongxun, Xiao Hua, Xiao Jingguang, Liu Yalou, He Wei, Qian Junrui, Lai Ruoyu, Shi Liang, Hu Keshi, Du Zheheng, Guo Shushen, as well as Song Li, Mayor of Lüda City; Hu Zhonghai, Deputy Secretary of the Lüda City Committee of the Communist Party of China; Qiao Chuanjue, President of the Lüda City Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and others.

More than 1,000 people attended the reception.

At the reception, Soong Ching Ling, Deputy Head of the People's Republic of China Delegation to Comfort the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area and President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and representatives of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area delivered speeches (full texts to be published separately).

During the dinner, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to congratulate the great and unbreakable friendship between the peoples and armies of the Soviet Union and China, and to wish good health to the leaders of the parties and governments of the Soviet Union and China.

The reception proceeded in an atmosphere of close friendship.

After the reception, song and dance performances were given by the Xinjiang Military Region Art Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Northeast Song and Dance Troupe, who had come to Lüda City with the delegation.

### 彭德怀副总理兼国防部长举行盛大招待会  招待驻旅顺口地区苏军代表

1955-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀副总理兼国防部长举行盛大招待会
    招待驻旅顺口地区苏军代表
    【新华社旅大二十四日电】中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团团长、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，二十四日下午在旅大市举行盛大招待会，招待驻旅顺口地区苏军代表。
    应邀出席的，有驻旅顺口地区苏军的高级将领以及各级军官、士官和战士代表。
    应邀出席的，还有苏联驻我国大使尤金院士和苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫等。
    出席作陪的，有代表团副团长：中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻和代表林枫、章伯钧、章乃器、朱学范、张宗逊、萧华、萧劲光、刘亚楼、何伟、钱俊瑞、赖若愚、史良、胡克实、杜者蘅、郭述申，以及旅大市市长宋黎，中国共产党旅大市委员会副书记胡忠海，旅大市中苏友好协会会长乔传珏等人。
    出席招待会的，共有一千多人。
    招待会上，中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团副团长、中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄和驻旅顺口地区苏军代表分别讲了话（全文另发）。
    席间，宾主一再举杯祝贺苏中两国人民及其军队的伟大的牢不可破的友谊，祝贺苏中两国的党和政府的领袖的健康。
    招待会在亲密友好的空气中进行。
    招待会后，由随代表团前来旅大市的中国人民解放军新疆军区文工团和东北歌舞团演出了歌舞节目。
