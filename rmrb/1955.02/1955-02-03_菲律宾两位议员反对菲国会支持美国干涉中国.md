Title: Two Philippine Senators Oppose Philippine Congress's Support for U.S. Interference in China
Author:
Date: 1955-02-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Manila: According to newspaper reports, Philippine Senators Recto and Sia stated on January 27 that they opposed the Philippine Congress's resolution to unconditionally support the United States' armed intervention in China to liberate Taiwan. Recto and Sia believe that the Philippines should not be directly drawn into this complex vortex of war.

### 菲律宾两位议员反对菲国会支持美国干涉中国

1955-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    菲律宾两位议员
    反对菲国会支持美国干涉中国
   【新华社讯】马尼拉消息：据报纸报道，菲律宾参议员雷克托和西亚两人，一月二十七日表示反对菲律宾国会通过无条件支持美国武装干涉中国解放台湾的决议。雷克托和西亚认为，菲律宾不应直接被牵入到这一个复杂的战争漩涡中。
