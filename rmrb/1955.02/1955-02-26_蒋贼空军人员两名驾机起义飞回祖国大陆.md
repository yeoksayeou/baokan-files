Title: Two Chiang Kai-shek Air Force Personnel Defect and Fly Back to the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1955-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 25th: Two Chiang Kai-shek Air Force personnel, Liu Ruolong and Song Baorong, defected and flew back to the mainland.

    On the morning of February 23rd, Liu Ruolong and Song Baorong, two Chiang Kai-shek Air Force personnel, defected with a PT-17 type 393 primary trainer aircraft and safely landed at a certain place on the mainland at 6:15 AM. Liu Ruolong is originally from Yangzhou, Jiangsu Province, is 25 years old this year, and was originally a student in the 35th class of the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force Military Academy. Song Baorong is originally from Shanghai, is 24 years old this year, and was originally a student in the 36th class of the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force Military Academy.

    Liu Ruolong and Song Baorong's patriotic action of defecting and flying back to the mainland was warmly welcomed and enthusiastically received by the local people and the People's Liberation Army.

### 蒋贼空军人员两名驾机起义飞回祖国大陆

1955-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼空军人员两名驾机起义飞回祖国大陆
    【新华社二十五日讯】台湾蒋军空军人员刘若龙、宋宝荣驾机起义，飞返祖国大陆。
    二月二十三日上午，台湾蒋军空军人员刘若龙、宋宝荣等二人驾驶PT—17型393号初级教练机一架起义，在六时十五分飞返祖国大陆某地安全着陆。刘若龙原籍江苏省扬州市人，今年二十五岁，原是蒋贼空军军官学校第三十五期学生。宋宝荣原籍上海市人，今年二十四岁，原是蒋贼空军军官学校第三十六期学生。
    刘若龙、宋宝荣驾机起义飞回祖国大陆的爱国行动，受到了当地人民和人民解放军的热烈欢迎和热情接待。
