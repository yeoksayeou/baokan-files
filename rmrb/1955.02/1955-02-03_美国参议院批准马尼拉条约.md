Title: U.S. Senate Approves Manila Treaty
Author:
Date: 1955-02-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, February 2nd: The U.S. Senate approved the so-called "Southeast Asia Collective Defense Treaty," signed in Manila on September 8, 1954, by a vote of eighty-two to one on February 1st.

    Senator William Langer voted against ratifying the treaty. He said that the treaty was very dangerous and could drag the United States into a "bloody war" in Southeast Asia.

### 美国参议院批准马尼拉条约

1955-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    美国参议院批准马尼拉条约
   【新华社二日讯】华盛顿消息：美国参议院二月一日以八十二票对一票批准了一九五四年九月八日在马尼拉签订的所谓“东南亚集体防务条约”。
    参议员威廉·朗杰投票反对批准这个条约。他说，这个条约是有很大危险性的，它可能把美国牵连到东南亚的“流血的战争”中去。
