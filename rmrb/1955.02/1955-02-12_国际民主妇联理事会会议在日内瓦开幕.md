Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Council Meeting Opens in Geneva
Author:
Date: 1955-02-12
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, February 10th: TASS, Geneva, February 10th—The fifth meeting of the Council of the International Democratic Women's Federation opened in Geneva on the 10th. Participating in the meeting were council members and representatives from forty-two countries in Europe, Latin America, Asia, and Africa. The agenda of the council meeting includes issues related to the struggle of women in all countries for their rights and the defense of peace. One of the activities of the meeting will be to hear a report by Ann Minella, Deputy Secretary-General of the International Democratic Women's Federation, "On the development of the women's democratic movement since the World Women's Congress and the tasks of the International Democratic Women's Federation in the struggle for peace, friendship between peoples, and the rights of women and children."

### 国际民主妇联理事会会议在日内瓦开幕

1955-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联理事会会议在日内瓦开幕
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社日内瓦十日讯：国际民主妇女联合会理事会第五次会议十日在日内瓦开幕。参加会议的有理事会理事和欧洲、拉丁美洲、亚洲和非洲的四十二个国家的代表。理事会议程包括同世界各国妇女争取她们的权利和维护和平的斗争有关的问题。会议的活动之一将是听取国际民主妇联副总书记安·米奈拉“关于在世界妇女代表大会以后妇女民主运动的发展和国际民主妇联在争取和平、各国人民之间的友谊、争取妇女和儿童的权利的斗争中的任务”的报告。
