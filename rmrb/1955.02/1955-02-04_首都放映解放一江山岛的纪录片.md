Title: Documentary on the Liberation of Yijiangshan Island to be Screened in the Capital
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-04
Page: 3

The "Newsreel" No. 7, produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, focusing on the liberation of Yijiangshan Island by our army, will be screened in Beijing starting from February 3rd and will be shown throughout the country.

    The film records the fierce bombardment of the Chiang bandit army's positions on Yijiangshan Island by the mixed air force group and naval vessels of the People's Liberation Army, the heroic landing and attack on the enemy by our army's ground forces on Yijiangshan Island, the transportation of large quantities of ammunition and supplies to Yijiangshan Island by our army's logistics units, and the escorting of a large number of Chiang bandit prisoners to our army's rear. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 首都放映解放一江山岛的纪录片

1955-02-04
第3版()
专栏：

    首都放映解放一江山岛的纪录片
    中央新闻纪录电影制片厂摄制的、以纪录我军解放一江山岛为主要内容的“新闻简报”第七号，从二月三日起在北京放映，并将在全国各地放映。
    影片纪录了我人民解放军空军混合机群、海军舰艇猛烈轰击一江山岛蒋贼军阵地和我陆军部队在一江山岛强行登陆并英勇攻击敌人，我军后勤部队将大批弹药和物资运往一江山岛以及大批蒋贼军俘虏被押向我军后方等情况。　（新华社）
