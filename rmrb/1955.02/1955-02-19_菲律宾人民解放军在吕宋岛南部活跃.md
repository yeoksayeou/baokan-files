Title: People's Liberation Army of the Philippines Active in Southern Luzon
Author:
Date: 1955-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 18th—News from Manila: The People's Liberation Army of the Philippines, active in Luzon Island, is continuously conducting guerrilla warfare. The Philippine Army authorities admitted on the 13th that, in the past six months, the People's Liberation Army of the Philippines had 131 engagements with government troops in southern Luzon Island.

### 菲律宾人民解放军在吕宋岛南部活跃

1955-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾人民解放军在吕宋岛南部活跃
    【新华社十八日讯】马尼拉消息：活跃在吕宋岛的菲律宾人民解放军不断地进行游击战。菲律宾陆军当局十三日承认，在过去六个月间，菲律宾人民解放军在吕宋岛南部同政府军队进行了一百三十一次战斗。
