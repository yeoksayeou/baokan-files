Title: Vice Minister Lei Renmin Hosts British Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1955-02-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, held a reception on the 14th to host all members of the British industrial and commercial trade delegation visiting China.
    Those attending the reception included: Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China; Chen Ming, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Cao Zhongshu, Acting Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation; Wen Shizhen, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and managers of various export corporations.
    Also attending the reception was Mr. Youde, Secretary of the British Representative Office in China.

### 雷任民副部长招待英国工商界贸易访问团

1955-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    雷任民副部长招待英国工商界贸易访问团
    【新华社十四日讯】中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民在十四日举行酒会，招待来我国访问的英国工商界贸易访问团全体团员。
    参加酒会的有：中华人民共和国外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华，对外贸易部第三局副局长陈明，中国进出口公司代经理曹中枢，中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长文士桢及各出口公司经理等。
    参加酒会的还有英国驻我国代办处秘书游德。
