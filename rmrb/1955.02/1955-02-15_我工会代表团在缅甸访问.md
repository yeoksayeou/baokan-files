Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Visits Burma
Author:
Date: 1955-02-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Chinese trade union delegation, led by Liu Ziju, which was invited to visit Burma, left Rangoon by plane on the morning of the 9th to visit Lashio, Namtu, Mandalay, Hsipaw and other places, accompanied by Wu Dun, a member of the Executive Committee of the Trade Union Congress (Burma), and others.
    Prior to this, the Chinese trade union delegation visited the Burmese government's Dalla Dockyard on the morning of the 8th and attended a welcome tea party held by the Executive Committee of the Burmese Government Dalla Dockyard Workers' Association. Wu Ong Maung, Vice Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Burmese Government Dalla Dockyard Workers' Association, delivered a speech at the tea party. He said that the Burmese workers warmly support the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and are willing to stand with the world working class to strive for lasting peace and stop atomic warfare.
    In his reply, Delegation Leader Liu Ziju expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome and hospitality of the Burmese workers. He said that the Chinese working class and the people of the whole country are working hard to implement the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. Liu Ziju also congratulated the solidarity and friendship between the trade unions of China and Burma.

### 我工会代表团在缅甸访问

1955-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团在缅甸访问
    据新华社讯：应邀访问缅甸的中国工会代表团团长刘子久等一行五人在工会大会（缅甸）执行委员会委员吴敦等陪同下，九日上午乘飞机离开仰光到腊戍、南渡、曼德勒、梢埠等地访问。
    在此以前，中国工会代表团曾在八日上午参观了缅甸政府都榜船坞，并出席了缅甸政府都榜船坞工人协会执行委员会举行的欢迎茶会。缅甸政府都榜船坞工人协会执行委员会副主席吴翁貌在茶会上致词。他说：缅甸工人热烈拥护和平共处五项原则，并愿与世界工人阶级站在一起争取持久和平、制止原子战争。
    刘子久团长在答词中对缅甸工人的热烈欢迎和招待表示感谢。他说：中国工人阶级和全国人民正为贯彻和平共处五项原则而努力。刘子久团长并祝贺中缅两国工会团结友好。
