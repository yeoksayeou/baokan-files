Title: Peng Dehuai Sends Telegram to Zhukov to Congratulate the Soviet Army on its 37th Anniversary
Author: Peng Dehuai
Date: 1955-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd:

    Peng Dehuai Sends Telegram to Zhukov to Congratulate the Soviet Army on its 37th Anniversary

    To Marshal G.K. Zhukov, Minister of Defense of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:
    On the occasion of the 37th anniversary of the founding of the great Soviet Army, I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations to you, Comrade Marshal, and wish the heroic Soviet Army great achievements in the cause of defending world peace.

                                Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Peng Dehuai
                                February 21, 1955, in Beijing

### 彭德怀电朱可夫祝贺苏军建军三十七周年
彭德怀
1955-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀电朱可夫祝贺苏军建军三十七周年
    【新华社二十二日讯】中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀致电苏联国防部长朱可夫，祝贺苏联军队建军三十七周年。电文如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟国防部长格·康·朱可夫元帅：
    值此伟大的苏联军队建军三十七周年之际，我谨向您，元帅同志，致衷心的祝贺，并祝英勇的苏联军队在保卫世界和平的事业中获得巨大的成就。
                               中华人民共和国国防部长　彭德怀
                                一九五五年二月二十一日于北京
