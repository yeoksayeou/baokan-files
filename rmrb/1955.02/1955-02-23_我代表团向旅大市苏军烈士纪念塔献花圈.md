Title: Our Delegation Lays Wreath at Soviet Martyrs Memorial Tower in Lushun-Dalian

Author:

Date: 1955-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lushun-Dalian, February 22nd: The head of the People's Republic of China delegation to慰问 the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, led the entire delegation in laying a wreath at the Soviet Martyrs Memorial Tower in Lushun-Dalian at 3:00 p.m. on the 22nd. Accompanying them were the delegation's deputy heads, Soong Ching Ling, He Long, Guo Moruo, Nie Rongzhen, and representatives Lin Feng, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Naiqi, Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Zongxun, Xiao Hua, Xiao Jingguang, Liu Yalou, He Wei, Qian Junrui, Lai Ruoyu, Shi Liang, Hu Keshi, Du Zheheng, and Guo Shushen.

    Attending the wreath-laying ceremony were Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, senior generals of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, and Mr. Belyanov, the Soviet Consul General in Dalian, and others.

    Also attending the wreath-laying ceremony were Song Li, the Mayor of Lushun-Dalian, Hu Zhonghai, Deputy Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Lushun-Dalian Committee, and Qiao Chuanjue, President of the Lushun-Dalian Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and others.

### 我代表团向旅大市苏军烈士纪念塔献花圈

1955-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我代表团向旅大市苏军烈士纪念塔献花圈
    【新华社旅大二十二日电】中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团团长、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀率领全体代表，在二十二日下午三时向旅大市苏军烈士纪念塔敬献花圈。随同前往献花圈的，有代表团副团长宋庆龄、贺龙、郭沫若、聂荣臻和代表林枫、章伯钧、章乃器、朱学范、张宗逊、萧华、萧劲光、刘亚楼、何伟、钱俊瑞、赖若愚、史良、胡克实、杜者蘅、郭述申。
    参加献花圈仪式的，有苏联驻我国大使尤金院士，驻旅顺口地区苏军部队的高级将领，苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫等。
    参加献花圈仪式的，还有旅大市市长宋黎，中国共产党旅大市委员会副书记胡忠海，以及旅大市中苏友好协会会长乔传珏等人。
