Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-02-15
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    People from all walks of life in Guangzhou actively respond to the call of the World Peace Council and participate in the signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons. This is a scene of the representatives attending the Second Congress of Guangzhou Trade Unions signing against the use of atomic weapons.

    我们伟大的祖国
