Title: Supply Department Store Established on Yijiangshan Island
Author:
Date: 1955-02-01
Page: 2

The state-owned department store of Haimen District, Zhejiang Province, has established a supply department store on Yijiangshan Island. The first batch of general merchandise and daily necessities, including cigarettes, toothbrushes, soap, towels, socks, toothpaste, fountain pens, as well as various canned foods, fruits, biscuits, etc., has been transported to Yijiangshan Island. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 一江山岛设立百货供应处

1955-02-01
第2版()
专栏：

  　一江山岛设立百货供应处
    浙江省海门区国营百货商店在一江山岛设立供应处。首批百货日用品，包括卷烟、牙刷、肥皂、毛巾、袜子、牙膏、金笔以及各种罐头食物、水果、饼干等已经运到了一江山岛。　　　（新华社）
