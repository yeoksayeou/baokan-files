Title: Molotov Receives British Ambassador to the Soviet Union and Indian Chargé d'Affaires to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-02-06
Page: 1
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 5th: TASS Moscow, February 4th: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov received the British Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Sir William Hayter, on February 4th.
    On the same day, V.M. Molotov also received Mr. P. Gore, the Indian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to the Soviet Union.

### 莫洛托夫接见英驻苏大使和印驻苏代办

1955-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫接见英驻苏大使和印驻苏代办
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科四日讯：苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫在二月四日接见了英国驻苏联大使威廉·海特尔勋爵。
    同一天，维·米·莫洛托夫还接见了印度驻苏联临时代办普·高尔先生。
