Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Premier Zhou's Reply to Hammarskjöld
Author:
Date: 1955-02-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 4th: Moscow newspapers today published in prominent positions the full text of the telegram sent by Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of China, on the 3rd, in reply to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld.
