    Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Alliance
    President Soong Ching Ling's Telegram to Chairman Denisov
   【Xinhua News Agency, 13th】Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, sent a telegram to Denisov, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. The text is as follows: To Chairman Denisov, Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Moscow:
    On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, the entire people of China are celebrating this historically significant anniversary with unparalleled excitement. On behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, I extend warm congratulations to you and, through you, to the great Soviet people.
    The great, selfless, and comprehensive assistance given by the great Soviet government and the Soviet people to our country's construction efforts has greatly encouraged and strengthened the Chinese people's confidence in striving for the victorious construction of socialism. The Chinese people express their heartfelt gratitude and high respect for this friendly assistance from the Soviet government and the Soviet people.
    Over the past five years, the great Sino-Soviet alliance has been developing and consolidating with irresistible force. It has made great contributions to the prosperity of China and the Soviet Union and to the defense of world peace. At a time when the war group headed by US imperialism is creating international tensions, frantically clamoring for atomic war, and seriously threatening world peace, the Sino-Soviet alliance, which signifies an immensely powerful force for peace, will play an even more significant role in the struggle against wars of aggression and in defense of peace in the Far East and the world.
    In order to defend peace in Asia and the world, and to ensure the success of our country's socialist construction, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will make even greater efforts to further consolidate and develop the great friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, strengthen Sino-Soviet cultural exchanges, and promote the earnest study of the advanced experience of Soviet socialist construction by all the Chinese people.
    Long live the great and unbreakable Sino-Soviet alliance! Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, February 11, 1955

### 庆祝中苏同盟条约签订五周年  宋庆龄会长致电杰尼索夫主席

1955-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏同盟条约签订五周年
    宋庆龄会长致电杰尼索夫主席
   【新华社十三日讯】中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄致电苏联对外文化协会理事会主席杰尼索夫庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年。文如下：莫斯科苏联对外文化协会理事会杰尼索夫主席：
    欣逢中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年，全中国人民正以无比兴奋的心情来庆祝这个具有伟大历史意义的纪念日。我谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员向您并通过您向伟大的苏联人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    伟大的苏联政府和苏联人民对于我国建设事业所给予的伟大的、无私的、全面的援助，极大地鼓舞和增强了中国人民为胜利建成社会主义而奋斗的信心。中国人民对于苏联政府和苏联人民这种友好的援助表示衷心的感谢和崇高的敬意。
    五年来，伟大的中苏友好同盟以不可抗拒的力量日益发展和巩固着。它对中苏两国的繁荣和保卫世界和平作出了伟大的贡献。正当目前以美帝国主义为首的战争集团制造国际紧张局势，疯狂叫嚣原子战争，严重威胁世界和平的时候，标志着无比强大的和平力量的中苏友好同盟，在反对侵略战争、保卫远东和世界和平的斗争中，将发生更其重大的作用。
    为了保卫亚洲和世界的和平，为了保证我国社会主义建设的成功，中苏友好协会将以更大的努力为进一步巩固和发展中苏两国人民的伟大友谊，加强中苏文化交流和推动全中国人民认真学习苏联社会主义建设的先进经验而努力。
    伟大的牢不可破的中苏友好同盟万岁！中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄一九五五年二月十一日杰尼索夫主席致电宋庆龄会长
   【新华社十三日讯】苏联对外文化协会理事会主席杰尼索夫致电中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年。文如下：北京中苏友好协会总会宋庆龄会长：
    值此中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年之际，苏联对外文化协会理事会谨向您，并通过您向中苏友好协会，向全中国的兄弟人民致以衷心的祝贺和最美好的愿望。
    祝苏联与中华人民共和国两国人民之间的伟大的友谊和合作万世常新！苏联对外文化协会理事会主席亚·杰尼索夫教授一九五五年二月十日于莫斯科
