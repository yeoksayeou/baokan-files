Title: Groundbreaking Ceremony for the Sino-Soviet Friendship Tower Held in Lushun
Author:
Date: 1955-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, February 23rd: The groundbreaking ceremony for the Sino-Soviet Friendship Tower, which will be built in Lushunkou, Lüda City, was solemnly held at 3 p.m. on the 23rd in the square in front of the Lushun Museum.

    Soviet and Chinese national flags were hung at the entrance of the square. Evergreen pine trees surrounded the square, with colored flags planted in front of the trees. The railings around the base of the Friendship Tower in the center of the square were decorated with red silk. More than 4,000 people participated in the ceremony inside and outside the square, including representatives of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area and representatives from all walks of life in Lüda City.

    Peng Dehuai, head of the People's Republic of China Delegation to慰问 the Soviet Troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, and deputy heads Song Qingling, He Long, Guo Moruo, and Nie Rongzhen attended the groundbreaking ceremony.

    Also attending the groundbreaking ceremony were delegation representatives Lin Feng, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Naiqi, Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Zongxun, Xiao Hua, Xiao Jingguang, Liu Yalou, He Wei, Qian Junrui, Lai Ruoyu, Shi Liang, Hu Keshi, Du Zheheng, and Guo Shushen, as well as Lüda Mayor Song Li, Deputy Secretary of the Lüda Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Hu Zhonghai, and President of the Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Qiao Chuanjue, among others.

    On the Soviet side, those who attended the groundbreaking ceremony included Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, representatives of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, and Soviet Consul General in Dalian Belyanov, etc.

    As the groundbreaking ceremony began, the band played the Soviet and Chinese national anthems.

    At the meeting, Guo Moruo, deputy head of the People's Republic of China Delegation to慰问 the Soviet Troops stationed in the Lushunkou area and chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin delivered speeches. (Full texts to be published separately.) In their speeches, they wished that the Sino-Soviet Friendship Tower built in Lushunkou would shine forever, along with the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, and that the Sino-Soviet Friendship Tower would forever play the role of a lighthouse in maintaining peace in the Far East and the world.

    Amidst music, Head Peng Dehuai, Deputy Heads Song Qingling, He Long, Guo Moruo, Nie Rongzhen, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, representatives of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, and others wielded shovels and broke ground, laying the foundation stone for the Sino-Soviet Friendship Tower.

    Then, twenty children from the Soviet Union and China presented flowers at the foundation stone. At this moment, the people attending the ceremony applauded enthusiastically for the eternal and unbreakable great friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China. The groundbreaking ceremony ended with the music of "Moscow-Beijing."

### 中苏友谊塔奠基典礼在旅顺举行

1955-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友谊塔奠基典礼在旅顺举行
    【新华社旅大二十三日电】　将要修建在旅大市旅顺口的中苏友谊塔的奠基典礼，二十三日下午三时在旅顺博物馆前的广场上隆重举行。
    广场入口处悬挂着苏联和中国国旗。环绕广场的是常青的松树，树前遍插着彩色旗帜，广场中央友谊塔基地四周的栏杆上都饰以红色彩绸。在广场内外参加典礼的共有四千多人，其中有驻旅顺口地区苏军的代表和旅大市各界代表。
    中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团团长彭德怀，副团长宋庆龄、贺龙、郭沫若、聂荣臻参加了奠基典礼。
    参加奠基典礼的还有：代表团代表林枫、章伯钧、章乃器、朱学范、张宗逊、萧华、萧劲光、刘亚楼、何伟、钱俊瑞、赖若愚、史良、胡克实、杜者蘅和郭述申，以及旅大市市长宋黎、中国共产党旅大市委员会副书记胡忠海、旅大市中苏友好协会会长乔传珏等人。
    苏联方面参加奠基典礼的有：苏联驻我国大使尤金，驻旅顺口地区苏军代表，苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫等。
    奠基典礼开始，乐队奏苏联和中国国歌。
    在大会上，中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团副团长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若和苏联驻我国大使尤金分别讲了话。（全文另发）他们在讲话中祝在旅顺口建立的中苏友谊塔随同中苏两国人民的牢不可破的友谊永远光辉灿烂，祝中苏友谊塔在维护远东与世界和平事业中将永远发挥着灯塔的作用。
    彭德怀团长，宋庆龄、贺龙、郭沫若、聂荣臻副团长，苏联驻我国大使尤金，驻旅顺口地区苏军代表等在乐声中执锨铲土，为中苏友谊塔竖立基石。
    接着，苏中两国的二十名儿童在基石前献花。这时，参加典礼的人们为苏中两国人民间永恒的牢不可破的伟大友谊热烈鼓掌。奠基典礼在“莫斯科—北京”的乐声中结束。
