Title: Soviet Representative Gromyko Arrives in London to Attend the United Nations Disarmament Commission Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-02-25
Page: 4

    Attending the United Nations Disarmament Commission Meeting
    Soviet Representative Gromyko Arrives in London
    [Xinhua News Agency, London, February 24th] TASS London, February 23rd: The Soviet representative to the United Nations Disarmament Commission Subcommittee meeting, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Andrei Andreyevich Gromyko, arrived in London on the 23rd. The Subcommittee will begin its work on February 25th.

### 出席联合国裁军委员会会议  苏联代表葛罗米柯到伦敦

1955-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

    出席联合国裁军委员会会议
    苏联代表葛罗米柯到伦敦
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十三日讯：出席联合国裁军委员会小组委员会会议的苏联代表、苏联外交部第一副部长安·安·葛罗米柯二十三日到达伦敦。小组委员会将从二月二十五日起开始工作。
