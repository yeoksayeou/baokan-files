Title: "People's Daily" Extensively Publishes Congratulatory Telegrams and Articles Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty
Author:
Date: 1955-02-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 14th: "Pravda" on the 14th published on its front page the congratulatory telegrams between Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council, and Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Nikolai Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Vyacheslav Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs, to commemorate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. "Pravda" also published an editorial entitled "Great Friendship." The first page of "Pravda" also featured a large photo of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company. The fourth page, under the large headline "Warm Greetings to Our Great Friends and Allies," carried commemorative articles written by people from all walks of life.

### “真理报”大量刊登有关庆祝中苏条约签订五周年的贺电和专文

1955-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    “真理报”大量刊登有关庆祝中苏条约签订五周年的贺电和专文
    【新华社莫斯科十四日电】“真理报”十四日在第一版发表了中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来和苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，部长会议主席布尔加宁，部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫为纪念中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年的来往贺电。“真理报”并发表了题为“伟大的友谊”的社论。“真理报”第一版还刊载了鞍山钢铁公司的大幅图片。第四版在“向我们伟大的朋友和同盟者致热烈的敬礼”大字标题下登载了各界人士所写的纪念文章。
