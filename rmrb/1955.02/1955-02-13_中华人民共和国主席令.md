Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1955-02-13
Page: 2

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China

    The Regulations on the Conferment of Medals and Orders to Meritorious Personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army during the Chinese People's Revolutionary War, passed by the Seventh Session of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on February 12, 1955, are hereby promulgated.

   Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
          February 12, 1955

### 中华人民共和国主席令
毛泽东
1955-02-13
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    中华人民共和国授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员的勋章奖章条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五五年二月十二日第七次会议通过，现予公布。
   中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
          一九五五年二月十二日
