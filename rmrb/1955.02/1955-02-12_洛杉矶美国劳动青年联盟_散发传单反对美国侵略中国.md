Title: Los Angeles American Labor Youth League Distributes Leaflets Against U.S. Aggression Against China
Author:
Date: 1955-02-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 9th: According to the U.S. San Francisco newspaper "People's World" on February 2nd, workers at General Motors, Chrysler, and other automobile factories enthusiastically responded last week to a call in a leaflet distributed by the Los Angeles American Labor Youth League. This leaflet called on American workers and their unions to write to Eisenhower opposing "fighting for Taiwan and other small islands five thousand miles away." The leaflet demanded "No more second Korean War."

The leaflet stated that Taiwan is "Chiang Kai-shek's hideout for plunder and corruption," and said: "If we send troops, we will have to face the resistance of China's 600 million people."

The leaflet asked: "Who will fight, Eisenhower and Nolan, or you?" The leaflet pointed out that "If war breaks out, we American youth of draft age or in the reserves will become cannon fodder."

### 洛杉矶美国劳动青年联盟  散发传单反对美国侵略中国

1955-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    洛杉矶美国劳动青年联盟
    散发传单反对美国侵略中国
   【新华社柏林九日电】据美国旧金山报纸“人民世界报”二月二日报道，通用汽车公司、克莱斯勒和其他汽车工厂的工人们在上星期热烈响应由洛杉矶美国劳动青年联盟散发的一项传单的号召，这项传单号召美国工人和他们的工会写信给艾森豪威尔，反对“为五千英里以外的台湾和其他小岛作战”。传单要求“不再打第二个朝鲜战争”。
    这项传单说，台湾是“蒋介石的掠夺和贪污的藏身之地”，并且说：“如果我们派军队的话，我们将不得不遭到中国的六亿人民的抵抗”。
    这项传单问道：“谁将去打仗，艾森豪威尔和诺兰还是你们？”传单指出，“如果战争爆发的话，我们这些达到应征年龄的或在后备军中的美国青年将成为炮灰”。
