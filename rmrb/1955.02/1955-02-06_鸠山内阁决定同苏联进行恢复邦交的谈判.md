Title: Hatoyama Cabinet Decides to Negotiate with the Soviet Union to Restore Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 5th: According to Kyodo News, the Hatoyama Cabinet decided at a meeting held on the 4th to accept the Soviet Union's recent proposal to exchange views on possible steps to normalize Soviet-Japanese relations.

The Cabinet meeting decided to grant Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu full authority to handle the date and procedure for issuing Japan's reply, as well as the selection of the Japanese delegation. Shigemitsu said after the cabinet meeting: Japan's reply will be sent in a day or two.

### 鸠山内阁决定同苏联进行恢复邦交的谈判

1955-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    鸠山内阁决定同苏联进行恢复邦交的谈判
    【新华社五日讯】东京消息：据共同社报道，鸠山内阁在四日举行的会议上决定接受苏联方面最近提出的就苏日关系正常化的可能步骤交换意见的建议。
    内阁会议决定授予外务相重光葵全权处理发出日本复文的日期、程序以及日本代表团人选的问题。重光葵在内阁会议后说：日本的复文将在一、两天内发出。
