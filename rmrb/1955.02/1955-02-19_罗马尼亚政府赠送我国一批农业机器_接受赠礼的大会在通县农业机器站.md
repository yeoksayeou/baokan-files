Title: Romanian Government Presents Agricultural Machinery to Our Country; Acceptance Ceremony Held at Tong County Agricultural Machinery Station
Author:
Date: 1955-02-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: A grand ceremony for the Government of the People's Republic of China to accept agricultural machinery, including tractors, donated by the Government of the Romanian People's Republic, was held on the 17th at the Tong County Agricultural Machine Tractor Station in Hebei Province. This gift from the Government of the Romanian People's Republic to our government was made during our National Day celebrations last year, and includes two КД—three-five type tractors, two three-bottom plows, four twenty-four row seeders, two sets of tine harrows, and a box of spare parts. Our government has decided to use all of them to equip the Tong County Agricultural Machine Tractor Station.

The Chinese side attending the acceptance ceremony included: Yang Xiandong, Deputy Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China; Li Jingyu, Director of the Agricultural Machinery Management Bureau; Xi Fengzhou, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Agriculture; Wang Chongli, Commissioner of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Sun Dongchuan, Director of the Agricultural Machinery Administration Bureau of the Hebei Provincial Department of Agriculture and Forestry; Xu Ruilin, Secretary of the Tong County Local Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Wang Xian, Commissioner of the Tong County Special Office, and many others.

The Romanian People's Republic side attending the conference included: Guo Zuowen, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China; Economic Counselor Wei Qiangnu; First Secretary Meng Tiannu; Second Secretary Ye Sang and many others.

Also attending the conference were: all staff of the Tong County Agricultural Machine Tractor Station and more than 1,000 nearby farmers.

At the meeting, Ambassador Guo Zuowen presented the gift on behalf of the Government of the Romanian People's Republic, and Deputy Minister Yang Xiandong accepted the gift on behalf of the People's Republic of China. Ambassador Guo Zuowen said in his speech: The Government of the Romanian People's Republic's gift of agricultural machinery to the Government of the People's Republic of China expresses the Romanian people's respect for the achievements of the Chinese people. He said: The powerful Chinese people have risen up to defend their victorious fruits, defend Far Eastern and world peace, and have won the sympathy and support of all peace-loving countries. He wished new and greater achievements to those who are working for the development of Chinese agriculture. In his speech, Deputy Minister Yang Xiandong expressed his gratitude to the Government and people of the Romanian People's Republic. He said: Our country is carrying out the socialist transformation of agriculture, and these machines will certainly play an important demonstration and promotion role. He congratulated the Romanian people on their new and more brilliant achievements in building socialism.

After the meeting, workers from the Tong County Agricultural Machine Tractor Station drove the brand-new КД—three-five type tractors and performed operations in the nearby fields.

### 罗马尼亚政府赠送我国一批农业机器  接受赠礼的大会在通县农业机器站

1955-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚政府赠送我国一批农业机器
    接受赠礼的大会在通县农业机器站举行
    【新华社十八日讯】中华人民共和国政府接受罗马尼亚人民共和国政府赠送拖拉机等农具大会，十七日在河北省通县农业机器拖拉机站隆重举行。这是去年我国国庆时罗马尼亚人民共和国政府对我国政府的赠礼，包括КД—三五型拖拉机二台、三铧犁二台、二十四行播种机四台、钉齿耙二组和备品一箱。我国政府决定全部用来装备通县农业机器拖拉机站。
    参加接受赠礼大会的我国方面有：中华人民共和国农业部副部长杨显东、农业机械管理总局局长李菁玉、农业部办公厅副主任席凤洲、外交部专员王崇理、河北省农林厅农业机械管理局局长孙东川、中共通县地方委员会书记徐瑞林、通县专署专员王宪等多人。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国方面参加大会的有：罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使郭佐文、经济参赞维强努、一等秘书孟天努、二等秘书耶桑等多人。
    参加大会的还有：通县农业机器拖拉机站全体职工和附近农民一千多人。
    会上，郭佐文大使代表罗马尼亚人民共和国政府赠礼，杨显东副部长代表中华人民共和国受礼。郭佐文大使讲话说：罗马尼亚人民共和国政府赠送中华人民共和国政府农业机器，表示了罗马尼亚人民对中国人民的成就所怀的敬意。他说：强大的中国人民奋起保卫他们的胜利果实，保卫远东和世界和平，获得了一切爱好和平的各国人民的同情和支持。他祝正在为中国农业的发展而工作的人们获得新的巨大的成就。杨显东副部长讲话时，对罗马尼亚人民共和国政府和人民表示感谢。他说：在我国正进行农业的社会主义改造，这些机器一定会起重要的示范和推动作用。他祝贺罗马尼亚人民在建设社会主义事业中取得新的更加光辉的成就。
    会后，通县农业机器拖拉机站的工人驾驶着崭新的КД—三五型拖拉机，在附近田野上作了操作表演。
