Title: Indian Conference for Peace and Asian Solidarity Opens; Passes Resolution Demanding Withdrawal of US Troops from Taiwan Area
Author:
Date: 1955-02-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 1st. TASS reported from Delhi on the 1st: According to the Indian Press Trust, the Conference for Peace and Asian Solidarity opened in Bangalore, with approximately 400 representatives attending. The opening ceremony was presided over by J.C. Kumarappa, a member of the World Peace Council.

    The conference passed a resolution stating that the struggle between the People's Republic of China and Chiang Kai-shek is China's internal affair, and no foreign country has the right to interfere. The conference demanded that the United States cease its actual occupation of Taiwan and withdraw its troops from the island and adjacent waters.

    The conference warmly welcomed the convening of the Asian-African Conference and expressed hope that this conference would lead to the unity of the people of participating countries, regardless of any foreign attempts to undermine their unity and freedom. The conference stated that the Five Principles declared by the Prime Ministers of India, China, and Burma are a powerful force for safeguarding peace. The conference also condemned the Southeast Asia Military Alliance.

### 印度争取和平和亚洲团结代表会议开幕  通过决议要求美国军队从台湾地区撤退

1955-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    印度争取和平和亚洲团结代表会议开幕
    通过决议要求美国军队从台湾地区撤退
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社德里一日讯：据印度报业托辣斯报道，争取和平和亚洲团结代表会议在班加罗尔市开幕，参加会议的约有四百名代表。主持代表会议开幕式的是世界和平理事会理事杰·克·库马拉帕。
    代表会议通过决议说，中华人民共和国和蒋介石之间的斗争是中国的内政，任何外国都无权干涉。代表会议要求美国停止对台湾的实际占领，把军队从这个岛屿和邻近海面撤退。
    代表会议热烈欢迎亚非国家会议的召开，并且希望，这次会议能导致与会各国人民的团结，而不顾外国企图破坏他们的团结和自由的一切威胁。代表会议说，印度、中国和缅甸三国总理所宣布的五项原则是保卫和平的强大力量。代表会议还谴责东南亚军事联盟。
