Title: German Socialist Unity Party Central Committee Designates This Year as "Schiller Year"
Author:
Date: 1955-02-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd. The 150th anniversary of the death of the German poet Schiller falls on May 9th this year. The German Democratic Republic has decided to name 1955 "Schiller Year" to commemorate this great patriotic poet and fighter.

The Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party issued a statement on "Schiller Year" on February 1st. The statement pointed out that Schiller was a great spiritual representative of the German bourgeoisie at the turn of the 18th and 19th centuries in its search for democracy, freedom, national unity, and independence. Schiller's works awakened the German people to rise up and fight against political and social oppression and against the division of Germany. The statement called on the German people to deeply study the poet's great patriotic ideals and fight for the democracy and unity of the motherland.

### 德国统一社会党中央定今年为“席勒年”

1955-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党中央
    定今年为“席勒年”
   【新华社柏林三日电】今年五月九日是德国诗人席勒逝世一百五十周年纪念日。德意志民主共和国决定把一九五五年命名为“席勒年”，以纪念这位伟大的爱国诗人和战士。
    德国统一社会党中央委员会在二月一日发表关于“席勒年”的声明，声明指出，席勒是十八和十九世纪之交的德国资产阶级寻求民主、自由、民族统一和独立的一个伟大的精神代表，席勒的作品唤醒了德国人民起来为反对政治和社会的压迫、反对德国的分裂而斗争。声明号召德国人民深入地学习诗人的这种伟大的爱国主义理想，为祖国的民主和统一而斗争。
