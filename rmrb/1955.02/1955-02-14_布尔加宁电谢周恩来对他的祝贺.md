Title: Bulganin Cables Zhou Enlai to Thank Him for His Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1955-02-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 13th—Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Socialist Republics, sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, thanking him for his congratulations on his appointment as Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

I extend my gratitude to you, Comrade Premier, the government and people of the People's Republic of China, for your congratulations on my appointment as Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers.

　　Bulganin
　　Moscow, February 12, 1955

### 布尔加宁电谢周恩来对他的祝贺

1955-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁电谢周恩来对他的祝贺
    【新华社十三日讯】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席布尔加宁电复中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，感谢对他被任命为苏联部长会议主席的祝贺。电文如下：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    当我被任命为苏联部长会议主席时，承致祝贺，谨向您，总理同志，中华人民共和国政府和人民表示感谢。  布尔加宁  一九五五年二月十二日于莫斯科
