Title: PLA Northeast Military Region Leadership Organ Celebrates Soviet Army Day
Author:
Date: 1955-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, February 23rd: The leadership organ of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Northeast Military Region held a cadre meeting on the 22nd to warmly celebrate the 37th anniversary of the founding of the great Soviet Army. Attending the meeting were Zhou Huan, Deputy Political Commissar of the Northeast Military Region, leading members of the political department, heads of various arms of service, and cadre representatives, totaling more than 700 people.

Zhou Huan, Deputy Political Commissar, delivered a report at the celebration meeting. He called on the cadres present and all commanders and fighters to redouble their efforts to study the advanced military science and technology of the great Soviet Army, to learn the Soviet Army's high degree of patriotism and internationalism, and to struggle for the liberation of our territory of Taiwan, and for the defense of the motherland, the defense of the East, and world peace.

From February 14th to 23rd, over the course of ten days, various organs, troops, and military academies under the Northeast Military Region universally carried out various celebration and publicity education activities to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and the 37th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

### 解放军东北军区领导机关庆祝苏联建军节

1955-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军东北军区领导机关庆祝苏联建军节
    【新华社沈阳二十三日电】　中国人民解放军东北军区领导机关在二十二日召开干部大会，热烈庆祝伟大苏军建军三十七周年。出席大会的有东北军区副政治委员周桓、政治部首长和各兵种首长及干部代表等共七百多人。
    周桓副政委在庆祝大会上作了报告，他号召到会干部和全体指战员，要加倍努力学习伟大苏军的先进军事科学和技术，学习苏军的高度爱国主义和国际主义精神，为解放我国领土台湾，为保卫祖国、保卫东方和世界和平而奋斗。
    东北军区所属各机关、部队和军事学校，从二月十四日到二十三日的十天中，为庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年和苏军建军三十七周年，曾普遍进行了各种庆祝和宣传教育活动。
