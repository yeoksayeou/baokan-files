Title: Ministry of Culture and Others to Hold Amateur Music and Dance Performance

Author: 
Date: 1955-02-10
Page: 2

The amateur music and dance performance jointly organized by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the Ministry of Higher Education of the People's Republic of China, and the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China will be held in Beijing starting from February 10th.

The joint notice issued by the Ministry of Culture and other units pointed out that the purpose of holding this performance is to select outstanding programs and performing talents and to promote the development of mass amateur art activities.

The programs in this performance are mainly short, exquisite, and popular national and folk music and dances. Some of them are programs from workers' amateur art activities, and some are excellent programs selected from rural areas and schools. According to the plan, outstanding programs and performing talents from workers' amateur art activities will be selected from the trade union systems of seven cities, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Dalian, and Chongqing, and from the National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union system. Excellent programs from rural art activities will be selected from provinces such as Shaanxi, Hebei, Shanxi, Liaoning, Jilin, Sichuan, Henan, Guangdong, Shandong, Zhejiang, and Anhui. Student art programs will also be selected from secondary schools and above in the aforementioned seven cities, including Beijing, that have a relatively solid foundation in regular artistic activities.

According to the situations of the previous performances held in various places, mass amateur art activities have very rich programs and excellent performing talents. Shandong Province's "Hand Drum Dance" (also known as Ring Drum), "Umbrella Dance," "Small Suona Solo," "Bamboo Flute Duet," the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region's "Lusheng Unity Dance," "Carrying Pole Dance," "Shrimp Catching Dance," "Treasure Gourd" (Zhuang Opera), "Qin Niangmu" (Dong Opera), Anhui's "Liu Hai Teases the Golden Toad," "Sowing Barley," "Fengyang Flower Drum," Guangzhou's "Weaving Girl Threads Flowers," "Playing Horse Lantern," "Money Drum Dance," Dalian's "Butterfly Dance," etc., have all been warmly welcomed by the masses in the performances held in various places.

### 文化部等举办业余音乐舞蹈观摩演出

1955-02-10
第2版()
专栏：

    文化部等举办业余音乐舞蹈观摩演出
    中华人民共和国文化部、中华全国总工会、中国新民主主义青年团、中华人民共和国高等教育部、中华人民共和国教育部联合举办的群众业余音乐、舞蹈观摩演出自二月十日起在北京举行。
    文化部等单位发出的联合通知中指出，举办这次观摩演出的目的是选拔优秀节目和演唱人材，推动群众业余艺术活动的开展。
    这次演出的节目主要是一些短小精彩的、为群众喜爱的民族、民间音乐和舞蹈。其中有的是职工业余艺术活动的节目，也有从农村和学校中选出的优秀节目。根据计划，将从北京、天津、上海、广州、沈阳、旅大、重庆等七个市总工会系统和铁路工会全国委员会系统中选出职工业余艺术活动的优秀节目和演唱人材，从陕西、河北、山西、辽宁、吉林、四川、河南、广东、山东、浙江、安徽等省选出农村艺术活动的优秀节目，还将从前述北京等七个市平常文艺活动较有基础的中等以上学校中选出学生的文艺节目。
    根据各地已经举行会演的情况来看，群众业余艺术活动中，有着很丰富的节目和优秀的演唱人材。山东省的“手鼓舞”（又叫环鼓）、“伞舞”、“小唢呐独奏”、“竹笛二重奏”，桂西僮族自治区的“芦笙团结舞”、“扁担舞”、“捞虾舞”、“宝葫芦”（僮戏）、“秦娘姆”（侗戏），安徽的“刘海戏金蟾”、“种大麦”、“凤阳花鼓”，广州的“织女穿花”、“打马灯”、“钱鼓舞”，大连的“蝴蝶舞”等，都在各地会演中受到了群众热烈欢迎。
