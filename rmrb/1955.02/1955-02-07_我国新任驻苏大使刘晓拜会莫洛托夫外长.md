Title: China's New Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, Calls on Foreign Minister Molotov
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 6th: China's newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, called on Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov on the 5th to discuss matters concerning the presentation of his credentials to the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

### 我国新任驻苏大使刘晓拜会莫洛托夫外长

1955-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我国新任驻苏大使刘晓拜会莫洛托夫外长
    【新华社莫斯科六日电】　我新任驻苏联特命全权大使刘晓五日拜会苏联外交部长莫洛托夫，商谈大使向苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席递交国书事宜。
