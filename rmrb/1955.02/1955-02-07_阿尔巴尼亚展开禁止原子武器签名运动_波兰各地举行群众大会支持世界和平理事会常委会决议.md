Title: Albania Launches Signature Campaign to Ban Atomic Weapons, Mass Rallies Held Across Poland in Support of the Resolutions of the World Peace Council Standing Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-02-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 6th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, the Albanian National Peace Committee held an enlarged meeting in Tirana on the 5th. In addition to the members of the National Peace Committee, representatives from various mass organizations, figures from the scientific and cultural circles, and advanced workers also attended the meeting.

　　The meeting heard and discussed the report made by the Chairman of the National Peace Committee, Mr. Shtylla, on the current international situation and the importance of the resolutions of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council. The speakers unanimously supported these resolutions and expressed their willingness to promote them widely among the masses.

　　The meeting passed a resolution calling on peace committees at all levels and mass organizations to collect signatures for the "Appeal to the People of the World" by the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council. The participants at the meeting signed the "Appeal to the People of the World", and this signature campaign has now begun in Albania.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 6th: According to TASS, mass rallies and gatherings are being held in various parts of Poland, warmly supporting the resolutions of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

　　The workers of the Finder Nitrogen Fertilizer Factory in Chorzów resolutely opposed plans to use atomic weapons at a rally held on the 4th and expressed their gratitude to the Soviet government for its measures to help use atomic energy for peaceful purposes.

　　The Presidium of the Council of the Polish Association of Jurists adopted a resolution at a meeting held in Warsaw. The resolution stated: Polish legal professionals unanimously support the resolutions of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, and we demand that atomic energy be used entirely for peaceful purposes. The declaration of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council is a manifestation of the effort to adhere to the principles of international law, which stipulate that major powers have a special responsibility for eliminating the consequences of war and for consolidating peace and the security of all nations.

　　Mass rallies and gatherings were also held in factories and institutions in Szczecin and other Polish cities in support of the resolutions of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

### 阿尔巴尼亚展开禁止原子武器签名运动  波兰各地举行群众大会支持世界和平理事会常委会决议

1955-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚展开禁止原子武器签名运动
    波兰各地举行群众大会支持世界和平理事会常委会决议
    【新华社六日讯】　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚全国和平委员会五日在地拉那举行了扩大会议。参加会议的除了全国和平委员会的委员外，还有各群众团体的代表、科学文化界人士和先进工作者。
    会议听取并讨论了全国和平委员会主席什图拉所作的关于目前国际形势和世界和平理事会常务委员会各项决议的重要性的报告。发言的人一致拥护这些决议，并且愿意在群众中广为宣传。
    会议通过了一项决议，号召各级和平委员会和群众团体征集世界和平理事会常务委员会告全世界人民书的签名。参加会议的人都在告全世界人民书上签了名，这一签名运动从此开始在阿尔巴尼亚展开。
    【新华社六日讯】　据塔斯社华沙五日讯：波兰各地纷纷举行群众大会和集会，热烈支持世界和平理事会常务委员会的各项决议。
    霍若夫城芬德尔氮肥工厂的职工在四日举行的大会上坚决反对使用原子武器的计划，并对苏联政府帮助把原子能用于和平目的的措施表示感谢。
    波兰法学工作者协会理事会主席团在华沙举行的会议通过了一项决议。决议说：波兰法律工作者一致支持世界和平理事会常务委员会的各项决议，我们要求原子能完全用于和平目的。世界和平理事会常务委员会的宣言是争取遵守国际法原则的表现，这些原则规定大国对消除战争的后果、对巩固和平和各国人民的安全负有特别的责任。
    斯才新和波兰其他城市的工厂和机关也举行了群众大会和集会，支持世界和平理事会常务委员会的决议。
