Title: Commemorating "Day of Struggle Against Colonial System" World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretariat Issues Letter to Youth of the World

Author:

Date: 1955-02-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th: Budapest dispatch: The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, to commemorate the "Day of Struggle Against Colonial System" (February 21), issued a letter to the youth of the world.

The letter to the youth of the world calls on the youth of colonial and dependent countries to strengthen their struggle against the establishment of military blocs and the policy of aggression, against foreign interference in internal affairs, and against the establishment of foreign military bases on the territory of their countries; to strengthen the struggle against persecution and discrimination, to strive for democratic freedoms, and to strive for better living conditions, and to strengthen various actions to defend and develop national culture.

The letter to the youth of the world calls on the youth of the suzerain states to strengthen the struggle against colonialism and to support the struggle of the youth of colonial and dependent countries.

The letter to the youth of the world concludes: Link the activities commemorating February 21 with the preparations for the Fifth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship, and wish February 21 new achievements and ever-wider unity!

### 纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”  世界青联书记处发表告全世界青年书

1955-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”
    世界青联书记处发表告全世界青年书
    【新华社布拉格十八日电】布达佩斯消息：世界民主青年联盟书记处为纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”（二月二十一日），发表了告全世界青年书。
    告全世界青年书号召殖民地和附属国的青年加强他们反对建立军事集团和进行侵略的政策、反对外国干涉内政和反对在他们国家的领土上建立外国军事基地的斗争；加强反对迫害和歧视，争取民主自由，争取获得更好的生活条件的斗争，加强争取保卫和发展民族文化的各种行动。
    告全世界青年书号召宗主国的青年加强反对殖民主义的斗争，并且声援殖民地和附属国青年的斗争。
    告全世界青年书最后说：把纪念二月二十一日的活动同第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的筹备工作联系起来，祝二月二十一日获得新的成就和愈来愈广泛的团结！
