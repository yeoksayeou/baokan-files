Title: German Modern History Scholar, Dr. Bieshevell Returns Home

Author:

Date: 1955-02-18
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, 17th) Dr. Bieshevell, a German Democratic Republic scholar of modern Chinese history who visited our country under the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing for home on the 17th.

After arriving in our country last October, Dr. Bieshevell introduced the situation and problems related to the study of modern German history to our historical workers, and held several discussions.

Dr. Bieshevell also visited Shanghai, Guangzhou, Nanjing and other places.

### 德国近代史学者贝喜发回国

1955-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    德国近代史学者贝喜发回国
    【新华社十七日讯】根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问的德意志民主共和国的中国近代史学者贝喜发博士已在十七日离开北京回国。
    贝喜发博士在去年十月来我国后，曾向我国的历史工作者介绍了有关研究德国近代史的情况和问题，并举行了多次座谈。
    贝喜发博士还曾去过上海、广州和南京等地参观访问。
