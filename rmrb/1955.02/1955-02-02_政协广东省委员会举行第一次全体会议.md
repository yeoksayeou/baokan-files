Title: Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Holds First Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1955-02-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    The First Plenary Session of the First Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) was held in Guangzhou from January 27th to 29th. A total of 145 members attended the meeting.
    At the meeting, Li Minxin, a member of the former Guangdong Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, conveyed Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai's political report at the First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC; Chen Rutang, the former Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, delivered a work report on the Guangdong Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee. The meeting discussed the above reports. In their speeches, the members unanimously stated that they would abide by the Constitution of the CPPCC, support the declaration of the First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC, and sincerely strive for the various tasks put forward by the National Committee of the CPPCC.
    At the meeting, the members also angrily denounced the US imperialism's attempt to interfere in the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan by plotting the United Nations to arrange a so-called "ceasefire" scheme, and expressed their firm support for and full support of Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's statement on the US government's interference in the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan.
    The meeting elected Tao Zhu as the Chairman of the First Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPPCC by secret ballot, and Gu Dacun, Lin Liming, Zhang Wen, Du Guoxiang, Xu Chongqing, Ding Ying, Zhang Gengcun, Rao Zhangfeng, Xiao Junying, and Li Langru as Vice Chairmen.
    The meeting finally adopted the "Resolution on Supporting the Declaration and Political Report of Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai at the First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC," and also passed telegrams of condolences to the People's Liberation Army troops who liberated Yijiangshan Island and the people of Shantou and Fuzhou who suffered from bombing by Chiang Kai-shek's bandit planes.

### 政协广东省委员会举行第一次全体会议

1955-02-02
第3版()
专栏：

    政协广东省委员会举行第一次全体会议
　　中国人民政治协商会议第一届广东省委员会第一次全体会议一月二十七日到二十九日在广州市举行。出席这次会议的委员共有一百四十五人。
    会议由前广东省各界人民代表会议协商委员会委员李民欣传达了周恩来副主席在中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议上的政治报告；由前广东省各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席陈汝棠作了广东省各界人民代表会议协商委员会的工作报告。会议对以上报告进行了讨论。委员们在发言中一致表示要遵守中国人民政治协商会议章程，拥护中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议宣言，诚心诚意地为政协全国委员会所提出的各项任务而努力奋斗。
    委员们在会上还愤怒地斥责了美帝国主义妄想干涉中国人民解放台湾而策动联合国安排所谓
  “停火”的诡计，并表示坚决拥护和全力支持周恩来总理兼外交部长关于美国政府干涉中国人民解放台湾的声明。
    会议以无记名投票方式选举了陶铸为中国人民政治协商会议第一届广东省委员会主席，古大存、林李明、张文、杜国庠、许崇清、丁颖、张?村、饶彰风、萧隽英、李朗如为副主席。
    会议最后通过了“关于拥护中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议的宣言和周恩来副主席的政治报告的决议”，还通过了慰问解放一江山岛的人民解放军部队和遭受蒋贼飞机轰炸的汕头、福州两市人民的电文。　（新华社）
