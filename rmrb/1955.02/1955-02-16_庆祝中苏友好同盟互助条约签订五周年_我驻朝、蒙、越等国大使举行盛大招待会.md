Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance: Our Ambassadors to Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam and Other Countries Hold Grand Receptions
Author:
Date: 1955-02-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 15th: Pan Zili, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, held a reception in Pyongyang on the evening of the 14th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

Among those invited to the reception were Xu Zhenghua, Minister of Culture and Propaganda of Korea, Bai Nanyun, Minister of Education, Li Xinba, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zheng Lu and Jin Gang, Deputy Ministers of Culture and Propaganda, and others.

Also invited to the banquet were Razaliyev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in Korea, and Soviet Embassy staff.

Also invited to the banquet were diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in Korea.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the increasing consolidation and development of the great Sino-Soviet alliance, to the inevitable victory of the just cause of the Chinese people in liberating Taiwan, to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and the leaders of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government, to the achievements of the Korean people in post-war economic recovery and development, to the peaceful reunification of the fatherland, and to the health of Marshal Kim Il-sung.

A dance party was held after the reception.

According to Xinhua News Agency: He Ying, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Mongolian People's Republic, held a banquet on the evening of the 14th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

Among those invited to the banquet were: Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Sürünjavyn Shirindib, First Deputy Prime Minister, Maidar, Deputy Prime Minister, Jalkh-aajavyn Jamyan, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and heads of various departments of the Mongolian Party and government.

Styopkin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in Mongolia, and all embassy staff, as well as Soviet experts in Mongolia, were invited to attend the banquet.

Li Yongxie, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in Mongolia, and all embassy staff also attended the banquet.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to Sino-Soviet friendship and to the increasing consolidation and growth of the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 15th: Luo Guibo, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, held a reception on the evening of the 14th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, were invited to attend.

Lavrishchev, Ambassador of the Soviet Union to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and all diplomatic personnel of the embassy were also invited to attend.

Also invited to attend were the diplomatic envoys and diplomats of the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, and Mongolia stationed in Vietnam, and the head of the Polish delegation to the International Supervisory and Control Commission stationed in Vietnam.

The reception was conducted in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年  我驻朝、蒙、越等国大使举行盛大招待会

1955-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年
    我驻朝、蒙、越等国大使举行盛大招待会
   【新华社平壤十五日电】中华人民共和国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国特命全权大使潘自力十四日晚间在平壤举行招待会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年。
    应邀出席招待会的有朝鲜文化宣传相许贞琡，教育相白南云，外务省副相李新八，文化宣传省副相郑律、金刚等。
    应邀出席宴会的有苏联驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办拉扎廖夫和苏联大使馆人员。
    应邀出席宴会的还有驻朝鲜的各国使节和使馆人员。
    席间，宾主频频为伟大的中苏同盟的日益巩固和发展，为中国人民解放台湾的正义事业的必将胜利，为毛泽东主席和苏联共产党和苏联政府领袖们的健康，为朝鲜战后人民经济恢复发展、和平统一祖国事业的成就和金日成元帅的健康而干杯。
    会后还举行了舞会。
    据新华社讯：中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使何英十四日晚间举行宴会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年。
    应邀出席宴会的有：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理迈达尔，外交部长贾尔卡赛汗，蒙古党和政府各部门的负责人。
    苏联驻蒙古大使馆临时代办斯乔普金及使馆全体人员和在蒙古的苏联专家都应邀出席了宴会。
    朝鲜驻蒙古大使馆临时代办李咏燮和使馆全体人员也出席了宴会。
    席间，宾主频频为中苏友好，为以苏联为首的和平、民主、社会主义阵营的日益巩固和壮大而干杯。
   【新华社河内十五日电】中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使罗贵波十四日晚间举行招待会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年。
    越南民主共和国主席胡志明、越南劳动党总书记长征、越南民主共和国副总理范文同、越南人民军总司令武元甲应邀到会。
    苏联驻越南民主共和国大使拉夫里谢夫和使馆全体外交人员也应邀到会。
    应邀到会的还有德意志民主共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、蒙古驻越南的使节和外交官和驻越南国际监督和监察委员会的波兰代表团团长。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
