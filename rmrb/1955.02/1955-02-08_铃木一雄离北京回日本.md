Title: Kazuo Suzuki Leaves Beijing for Japan
Author:
Date: 1955-02-08
Page: 1

    Kazuo Suzuki Leaves Beijing for Japan
    [Xinhua News Agency, February 5th] Kazuo Suzuki, the Managing Director of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, left Beijing by plane on the morning of February 5th to return to his country. Kazuo Suzuki arrived in Beijing on October 19, 1954, at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade for a visit.

### 铃木一雄离北京回日本

1955-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    铃木一雄离北京回日本
    【新华社五日讯】日本日中贸易促进会常务理事铃木一雄五日上午乘飞机离北京返国。铃木一雄是应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请，在一九五四年十月十九日抵达北京进行访问的。
