Title: U.S. Intensifies Military Deployments in the Far East
Author:
Date: 1955-02-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 31st: TASS reports from New York on January 31st: American newspaper reports reveal that the U.S. command continues to conduct military deployments in the Far East, aimed at escalating tensions in that region.

    A United Press dispatch stated: "In the past few days, the U.S. Air Force has carried out new deployment work from South Korea to the Philippines." The news agency said that the peak of this deployment was the arrival of a squadron of F-86 Sabre jets in Taiwan. According to the "New York Times" correspondent in Tokyo, the U.S. Air Force in the Far East "has entered a comprehensive wartime posture."
    At the same time, U.S. naval vessels continue to concentrate in the Taiwan area. It is reported that U.S. Pacific Fleet Commander Admiral Stump has arrived in Taiwan.

### 美国加紧在远东的军事部署

1955-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    美国加紧在远东的军事部署
   【新华社三十一日讯】塔斯社纽约三十一日讯：美国报纸消息透露：美国司令部继续在远东进行旨在加剧那个地区的紧张局势的军事部署。
    合众社的一则消息说：“在过去几天中，美国空军从南朝鲜到菲律宾进行了新的部署工作”。这个通讯社说，这项部署的高峰是一个F—八十六型佩刀式喷气机联队到达台湾。据“纽约时报”驻东京记者报道，美国空军在远东“已进入一种全面的战时体制”。
    与此同时，美国海军舰只继续在台湾地区集中。据悉美国驻太平洋舰队司令斯图普海军上将已到达台湾。
