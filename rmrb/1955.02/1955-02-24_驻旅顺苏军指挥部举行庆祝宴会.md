Title: Soviet Military Command in Lushun Holds Celebration Banquet
Author: 
Date: 1955-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, February 23rd: The Soviet military command stationed in the Lushunkou area held a celebration banquet on the evening of February 23rd, the 37th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

Invited to attend were Peng Dehuai, head of the People's Republic of China delegation to慰问the Soviet army stationed in the Lushunkou area, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China; Song Qingling, deputy head of the delegation and President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China; and representatives Lin Feng, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Naiqi, Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Zongxun, Xiao Hua, Xiao Jingguang, Liu Yalou, He Wei, Qian Junrui, Lai Ruoyu, Shi Liang, Hu Keshi, Du Zheheg, and Guo Shushen.

Also invited to attend were Song Li, Mayor of Lüda City, Hu Zhonghai, Deputy Secretary of the Lüda Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Qiao Chuanjue, President of the Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and people from all walks of life.

Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and Consul General to Dalian Belyanov also attended the banquet.

At the banquet, representatives of the Soviet army stationed in the Lushunkou area and He Long, deputy head of the People's Republic of China delegation to慰问the Soviet army stationed in the Lushunkou area and Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, delivered speeches (full text to be published separately).

At the banquet, Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin also spoke. He congratulated the further consolidation and development of the great friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples and their armies.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to congratulate the close and unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples and their armed forces, and to wish good health to the leaders of the Soviet and Chinese parties and governments.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of close friendship.

Before the banquet, the song and dance troupe of the Soviet army stationed in the Lushunkou area performed wonderful programs.

### 驻旅顺苏军指挥部举行庆祝宴会

1955-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    驻旅顺苏军指挥部举行庆祝宴会
    【新华社旅大二十三日电】　驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部，在苏联建军三十七周年——二十三日晚举行庆祝宴会。
    应邀出席的，有中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团团长、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，副团长：中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄，中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻和代表林枫、章伯钧、章乃器、朱学范、张宗逊、萧华、萧劲光、刘亚楼、何伟、钱俊瑞、赖若愚、史良、胡克实、杜者蘅、郭述申。
    应邀出席的，还有旅大市市长宋黎，中国共产党旅大市委员会副书记胡忠海，旅大市中苏友好协会会长乔传珏和各界人士等。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金和驻大连总领事别良诺夫等也出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，驻旅顺口地区苏军代表和中华人民共和国慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团副团长、中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙先后讲了话（全文另发）。
    宴会上，苏联驻我国大使尤金也讲了话。他祝贺苏中两国人民及其军队的伟大友谊进一步巩固和发展。
    席间，宾主一再举杯，祝贺苏中两国人民及其武装部队的亲密的牢不可破的友谊，祝贺苏中两国的党和政府的领袖的健康。
    宴会在亲密友好的空气中进行。
    宴会前，驻旅顺口地区苏军的歌舞团表演了精彩节目。
