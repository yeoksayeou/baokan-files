Title: Notification from the Funeral Committee for Mr. Zhang Lan
Author:
Date: 1955-02-10
Page: 1

The remains of Vice Chairman Zhang Lan are lying in state at the Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. Organizations or individuals wishing to pay their respects may do so on Saturday, February 12, from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

The Funeral Committee office is located in the Zhongshan Hall, Zhongshan Park. Telephone numbers are San Ju 8218 and San Ju 8377.

### 张澜先生治丧委员会通告

1955-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    张澜先生治丧委员会通告
    张澜副委员长灵柩停于中山公园中山堂，凡团体或个人吊唁请于二月十二日（星期六）上午九时至下午四时前往。
    治丧委员会办公处设中山公园中山堂，电话三局八二一八、三局八三七七。
