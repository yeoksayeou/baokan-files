Title: Our Army on the Zhejiang Front Shot Down and Damaged One Chiang Kai-shek Plane Each
Author:
Date: 1955-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Front, 20th—At approximately 9:50 AM to 3:00 PM on February 20th, a total of six batches of twenty-three Chiang Kai-shek army F-84 and F-47 aircraft invaded the airspace over Taishan Island along the coast of Zhejiang Province, carrying out bombing and strafing. They were immediately met with fierce anti-aircraft fire from our army, which shot down and damaged one Chiang Kai-shek army plane each. The downed Chiang Kai-shek army plane crashed into the sea three li east of Taishan Island. The remaining Chiang Kai-shek army planes fled in panic.

### 浙江前线我军击落击伤蒋贼飞机各一架

1955-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    浙江前线我军击落击伤蒋贼飞机各一架
    【新华社浙江前线二十日电】二十日上午九时五十分到下午三时左右，蒋贼军F—84、F—47型飞机共六批二十三架侵入我浙江省沿海台山岛上空进行轰炸扫射，当即遭到我军高射炮火猛烈射击，击伤击落蒋贼军飞机各一架，被击落的蒋贼军飞机坠入台山岛以东三华里的海中，其余的蒋贼军飞机慌忙逃窜。
