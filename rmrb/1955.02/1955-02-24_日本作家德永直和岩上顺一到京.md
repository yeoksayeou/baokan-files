Title: Japanese Writers Tokunaga Sunao and Iwagami Junichi Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-02-24
Page: 4

    Japanese Writers Tokunaga Sunao and Iwagami Junichi Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, February 20th: Japanese writer Tokunaga Sunao and literary critic Iwagami Junichi arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of February 19th after attending the Second All-Soviet Writers Congress. They were invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Writers Association to visit our country.

### 日本作家德永直和岩上顺一到京

1955-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    日本作家德永直和岩上顺一到京
    【新华社二十日讯】　日本作家德永直和文艺批评家岩上顺一在参加第二次全苏作家代表大会以后，在二月十九日下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民对外文化协会和中国作家协会的邀请，前来我国访问的。
