Our Great Motherland
The first month of the opening of the Kangzang Highway - January of this year was the season when ice and snow blocked the mountains on the Kangzang Plateau, but the motor transport teams overcame all kinds of difficulties, drove as usual, and completed the transportation tasks for January according to the planned plan, transporting hundreds of tons of staple and non-staple foods from Chengdu to Lhasa. This is the situation of the motorcade passing through Erlang Mountain.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    康藏公路通车的第一个月——今年一月份正是康藏高原上冰雪封山的季节，各汽车运输队克服了种种困难，照常行车，按预定计划完成了一月份的运输任务，把数百吨主、副食品由成都运抵拉萨。这是汽车队经过二郎山的情形。
