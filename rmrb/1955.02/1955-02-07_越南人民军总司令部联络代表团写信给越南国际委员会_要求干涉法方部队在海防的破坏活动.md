Title: Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Army Writes to the International Commission for Vietnam, Demanding Intervention in the French Forces' Destructive Activities in Haiphong
Author:
Date: 1955-02-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 4th: The Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Army recently handed over to the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Vietnam a letter, accompanied by fifty-two petitions from Haiphong residents requesting intervention in the illegal removal of Haiphong public property by the French side.

    In the letter, the Liaison Delegation of the Vietnamese People's Army requested the International Commission to note the seriousness of these actions, which violate Article 15(d) of the Geneva Accords and contradict the International Commission's decisions on this issue.

    The letter stated, "These systematic destructive activities by the French Union forces not only create an unfavorable atmosphere for the current negotiations between the two sides but also create difficulties for the supervision and monitoring work of the International Commission."

    The letter finally requested the International Commission to intervene as soon as possible to end the destructive activities in Haiphong and ensure that the decisions of the International Commission are correctly implemented by the French Union forces.
    
    Vietnamese and French Sides Discuss Handover of Haiphong Issue

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 6th: The Vietnamese and French delegations continued to discuss the handover of the Haiphong area at the Central Joint Armistice Commission meeting on February 4th. Both sides introduced the representatives they had dispatched to participate in the Military and Order Subcommittee and the Administrative Subcommittee. The two sides also exchanged views on the working methods and working hours of these two subcommittees. The Vietnamese delegation particularly emphasized the two main issues to be discussed: the handover of public property and the phasing and number of days for the handover.

    Subsequently, the two subcommittees began work.

    At the same meeting, the two delegations signed a joint communiqué on the withdrawal of Vietnamese People's Army troops from the Fifth Interzone in South Central Vietnam.

### 越南人民军总司令部联络代表团写信给越南国际委员会  要求干涉法方部队在海防的破坏活动

1955-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团写信给越南国际委员会
    要求干涉法方部队在海防的破坏活动
    【新华社六日讯】　据越南通讯社河内四日讯：最近，越南人民军总司令部联络代表团已交给越南国际监督和监察委员会一封信，随信附去海防居民要求干涉法方非法搬运海防公共财产的五十二份请愿书。
    越南人民军联络代表团在信中要求国际委员会注意到这些行为的严重性，这些行为破坏了日内瓦协定第十五条丁项的规定并和国际委员会关于这一问题的决定相抵触。
    信件说，“法兰西联邦部队的这些有系统的破坏活动，不仅给目前双方的商谈造成了不利的气氛，同时也给国际委员会的监督和监察工作制造困难。”
    信件最后要求国际委员会尽快进行干涉，以便结束在海防的破坏活动，保证国际委员会的决定被法兰西联邦部队正确执行。
  越法双方讨论交接海防问题
    【新华社六日讯】　据越南通讯社河内六日讯：越、法双方代表团在二月四日中央联合停战委员会会议上，继续讨论了交接海防地区的问题。双方介绍了各自派出的参加军事和秩序小组委员会和行政小组委员会的代表。双方还就这两个小组委员会的工作方式和工作时间交换了意见。越方代表团特别着重指出要讨论的两个主要问题：交接公共财产的问题以及交接的分期和天数的问题。
    随后，这两个小组委员会便开始工作。
    在同一次会议上，双方代表团签署了关于越南人民军部队将从中越南部第五联区撤离的联合公报。
