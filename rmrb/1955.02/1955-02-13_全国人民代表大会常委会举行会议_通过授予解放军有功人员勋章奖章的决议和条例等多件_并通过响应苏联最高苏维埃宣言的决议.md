Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting, Adopts Resolutions and Regulations on Awarding Medals and Orders to Meritorious Personnel of the People's Liberation Army, and Passes a Resolution Responding to the Declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 12th: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its seventh meeting on February 12th.

After discussion, the meeting adopted the "Resolution on Stipulating the Award of Medals and Orders to Meritorious Personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army During the Chinese People's Revolutionary War Period" and the "Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Awarding Medals and Orders to Meritorious Personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army During the Chinese People's Revolutionary War Period." The meeting also adopted the "Resolution on Stipulating the Award of Medals and Orders to Meritorious Personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in Defending the Motherland and Carrying Out the Modernization of National Defense" and the "Resolution on Awarding Medals and Orders to Meritorious Personnel of the Chinese People's Volunteers for Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea and Defending Our Homes and Country."

During the discussion of the "Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Awarding Medals and Orders to Meritorious Personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army During the Chinese People's Revolutionary War Period," Lai Chuanzhu, Deputy Director of the General Cadre Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who attended the meeting, explained the regulations. Lai Chuanzhu said: Since its birth on August 1, 1927, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, together with the people of the whole country, has waged long-term revolutionary struggles with perseverance, arduousness, and indomitable courage for the cause of the liberation of the people, and finally defeated the counter-revolutionary armed forces, overthrew the reactionary rule of imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucrat-capitalism in China, and achieved the great victory of the people's revolution. Since the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has unremittingly striven to strengthen the modernization of national defense, defend the independence and security of the motherland, and defend peace in Asia and the world. Lai Chuanzhu said: The Chinese People's Liberation Army is an army with a high degree of political consciousness. In the long revolutionary war, it has produced countless heroic models and meritorious servants of the people, and created many earth-shattering and moving heroic deeds. In order to commend revolutionary merits and promote glorious traditions, the State Council, in accordance with the provisions of Article 31, Paragraph 14 of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, has formulated the "Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Awarding Medals and Orders to Meritorious Personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army During the Chinese People's Revolutionary War Period (Draft)," and submitted it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review. Lai Chuanzhu said: Through this reward, the sense of honor and responsibility of all revolutionary soldiers for liberating Taiwan, defending the independence and security of the motherland, and defending peace in Asia and the world will be further enhanced, their high patriotism and revolutionary heroism will be promoted, and they will be encouraged to give play to their initiative and creativity in combat and national defense construction, and further establish more meritorious achievements for the motherland. Lai Chuanzhu then made specific explanations on several issues in the regulations.

The meeting then adopted a resolution responding to the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

At this meeting, decisions were also passed on the establishment of representative offices of the National People's Congress and other matters.

Finally, the meeting adopted the list of appointments of members of the Adjudication Committee of the Supreme People's Court.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议  通过授予解放军有功人员勋章奖章的决议和条例等多件  并通过响应苏联最高苏维埃宣言的决议

1955-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    通过授予解放军有功人员勋章奖章的决议和条例等多件
    并通过响应苏联最高苏维埃宣言的决议
    【新华社十二日讯】全国人民代表大会常务委员会在二月十二日举行第七次会议。
    会议经过讨论，通过了“关于规定勋章奖章授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员的决议”和“中华人民共和国授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员的勋章奖章条例”。会议还通过了“关于规定勋章奖章授予中国人民解放军在保卫祖国和进行国防现代化建设中有功人员的决议”和“关于授予中国人民志愿军抗美援朝保家卫国有功人员勋章奖章的决议”。
    在讨论“中华人民共和国授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员的勋章奖章条例”时，列席会议的中国人民解放军总干部部副部长赖传珠对这个条例作了说明。赖传珠说：中国人民解放军自一九二七年八月一日诞生以来，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的英明领导下，为了人民的解放事业，同全国人民一起，坚苦卓绝、英勇顽强地进行了长期的革命斗争，终于战胜了反革命的武装力量，推翻了帝国主义、封建主义和官僚资本主义在中国的反动统治，取得了人民革命的伟大胜利。自中华人民共和国成立以来，中国人民解放军坚持不懈地为加强现代化的国防建设，保卫祖国的独立和安全，保卫亚洲与世界的和平而奋斗。赖传珠说：中国人民解放军是一支具有高度政治觉悟的军队，在长期革命战争中，产生了无数的英雄模范和人民功臣，创造了许多惊天动地可歌可泣的英雄事迹。为了表彰革命功勋，发扬光荣传统，国务院根据中华人民共和国宪法第三十一条第十四项的规定，拟定了“中华人民共和国授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员的勋章奖章条例（草案）”，提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审查。赖传珠说：通过这一奖励，将会进一步提高全体革命军人对解放台湾、保卫祖国的独立和安全、保卫亚洲与世界和平的荣誉感和责任心，发扬其高度的爱国主义和革命英雄主义精神，鼓舞其在战斗中和国防建设中发挥积极性和创造性，进一步为祖国建立更多的功勋。赖传珠接着对条例中的若干问题作了具体说明。
    会议接着通过了响应苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃宣言的决议。
    在这次会议上，还通过了关于设立全国人民代表大会代表办事处等决定。
    会议最后通过了任命最高人民法院审判委员会委员的名单。
