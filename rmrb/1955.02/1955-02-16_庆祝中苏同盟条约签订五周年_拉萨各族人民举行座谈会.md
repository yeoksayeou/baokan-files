Title: Lhasa People of All Ethnic Groups Hold Symposium to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Alliance
Author:
Date: 1955-02-16
Page: 2

    Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Alliance
    Lhasa People of All Ethnic Groups Hold Symposium

    More than 200 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Lhasa held a symposium on the afternoon of the 14th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

    Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, spoke at the symposium about the sincere assistance the Soviet Union has provided to China's socialist economic construction over the past five years, and the major victories achieved by the Soviet-led peace and democracy camp in the struggle for world peace. Dorje Phuntsok Rabgye, Kalon of the Tibet Local Government, and Wangdue, Representative of the Panchen Kanpo Conference Hall Committee in Lhasa, also spoke at the meeting. Dorje Phuntsok Rabgye said: The people of Tibet are determined to unite with the people of all fraternal ethnic groups in the country, strive to build the motherland with the help of the Soviet Union, and actively support the liberation of Taiwan. Representatives of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation and the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Federation also spoke at the meeting, expressing the gratitude of women and youth in Lhasa to the Soviet people and their determination to support the liberation of Taiwan and participate in the construction of the motherland.

    Ngawang Gyatso, Vice Chairman of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province, who represented the People's Government of Xikang Province to express condolences to the road construction personnel of the Kang-Tibet and Qing-Tibet Highways, also attended the symposium. He said at the meeting: "The Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance is in line with the interests of all ethnic groups in the country."

    While the symposium was being held, citizens listened to a live broadcast of the symposium. Many pictures about Soviet construction and Sino-Soviet friendship were also exhibited around the venue, and Soviet films were shown after the meeting. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 庆祝中苏同盟条约签订五周年  拉萨各族人民举行座谈会

1955-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏同盟条约签订五周年
    拉萨各族人民举行座谈会
    拉萨市各族各界二百多人，在十四日下午举行座谈会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订五周年。
    中国人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华在座谈会上讲了五年来苏联对我国社会主义经济建设的真诚帮助，和以苏联为首的和平民主阵营在争取世界和平的斗争中取得的重大胜利。西藏地方政府噶伦朵噶·彭错饶杰和班禅堪布会议厅委员会驻拉萨代表旺德都在会上讲了话。朵噶·彭错饶杰说：西藏人民坚决和全国各兄弟民族人民团结在一起，在苏联帮助下努力建设祖国，积极支援解放台湾。拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会和拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会的代表也在会上发言，他们表示了拉萨市的妇女和青年对苏联人民的感激和支援解放台湾、参加祖国建设的决心。
    代表西康省人民政府来慰问康藏、青藏公路筑路人员的西康省藏族自治区副主席阿旺嘉错也出席了座谈会，他在会上说：“中苏友好同盟互助条约是符合全国各族人民利益的。”
    在举行座谈会的同时，市民们听到了座谈会的实况转播。会场周围还展览了有关苏联建设和中苏友好的许多图片，会后放映了苏联影片。   （新华社）
