Title: Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement this year's Implementation Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1955-02-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 14th: After friendly and amicable consultations between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Polish People's Republic, the 1955 Implementation Plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Warsaw on February 11th.

    Hong Shen, head of the Chinese cultural delegation, signed on behalf of the Chinese side; Secretary-General Wende of the Polish Committee for Foreign Cultural Cooperation signed on behalf of the Polish side.

    Polish Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski and Vice Minister of Culture and Art Wierczek, and others attended the signing ceremony.

    Yang Qiliang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, also attended the signing ceremony.

### 中波文化合作协定今年执行计划签字

1955-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中波文化合作协定今年执行计划签字
    【新华社华沙十四日电】中华人民共和国政府和波兰人民共和国政府之间经过友好融洽的商谈后，于二月十一日在华沙签订了中波文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划。
    代表中国方面签字的是中国文化代表团团长洪深；代表波兰方面签字的是波兰对外文化合作委员会秘书长温德。
    参加签字仪式的有波兰外交部长斯克热歇夫斯基和文化艺术部副部长维尔契克等。
    中国驻波兰大使馆临时代办杨琪良也参加了签字仪式。
