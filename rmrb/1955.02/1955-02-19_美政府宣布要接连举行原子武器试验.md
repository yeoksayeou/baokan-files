Title: U.S. Government Announces Series of Atomic Weapon Tests
Author:
Date: 1955-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington: Following the announcement by the United States Atomic Energy Commission on January 29 that it would conduct a series of atomic weapon tests in February, the commission's chairman, Strauss, and the director of the U.S. Federal Civil Defense Administration, Val Peterson, jointly announced on the 9th that another series of atomic weapon tests would be conducted in April at the Nevada Test Site. The February tests are intended to test the use of various atomic weapons in war, while the April tests will mainly test so-called defenses against various atomic weapons.

The U.S. government's current series of steps to prepare for atomic warfare is clearly intended to intimidate peace-loving people around the world and is a provocation to their desire to ban atomic weapons and atomic weapon tests.

### 美政府宣布要接连举行原子武器试验

1955-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    美政府宣布要接连举行原子武器试验
    【新华社讯】华盛顿消息：继美国政府原子能委员会一月二十九日宣布要在二月份举行一连串原子武器试验后，这个委员会的主席斯特劳斯和美国政府联邦民防局局长华尔·彼得逊在九日又联合宣布四月份要继续在美国内华达试验场举行一连串原子武器试验。二月份的试验是要试验各种原子武器在战争中的使用，四月份的试验则主要是试验对各种原子武器的所谓防御。
    美国政府在现在采取一连串准备原子战争的步骤，显然是要恫吓全世界爱好和平的人民，并且是对他们的要求禁止原子武器和原子武器试验的愿望的挑衅。
