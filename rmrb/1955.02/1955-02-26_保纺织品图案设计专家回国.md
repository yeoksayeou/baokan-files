Title: Bulgarian Textile Pattern Design Expert Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-02-26
Page: 1

    According to Xinhua News Agency: Bulgarian textile pattern design expert Maria Kostadinova Yossifova, who visited China under the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing for home on the 19th. Yossifova came to China last October and visited Beijing, Shanghai, Suzhou and other places, collecting various patterns.

### 保纺织品图案设计专家回国

1955-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    保纺织品图案设计专家回国
    据新华社讯：根据中保文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问的保加利亚纺织品图案设计专家玛丽亚·科斯塔迪诺娃·约瑟弗娃已在十九日离开北京回国。约瑟弗娃在去年十月来我国，曾在北京、上海、苏州等地参观，搜集各种图案。
