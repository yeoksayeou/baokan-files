Title: Delhi Branch of the India-China Friendship Association Holds Photo Exhibition of Premier Zhou's Visit to India
Author:
Date: 1955-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 21st: The photo exhibition of Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to India in June 1954, organized by the Delhi Branch of the India-China Friendship Association, opened in New Delhi on the 19th. The exhibition also featured photos of Prime Minister Nehru's visit to China in 1954.

Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, said in his opening speech that it is now widely believed that the declaration of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence issued by Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru laid the foundation for peaceful relations between countries.

### 印中友好协会德里分会  举行周总理访印图片展览会

1955-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    印中友好协会德里分会
    举行周总理访印图片展览会
   【新华社新德里二十一日电】印中友好协会德里分会举办的周恩来总理一九五四年六月间访问印度的图片展览会十九日在新德里开幕。展览会里面还展出了尼赫鲁总理一九五四年访问中国的图片。
    全印和平理事会主席赛福丁·克其鲁博士致开幕词说，现在普遍认为，周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理发表的关于和平共处的五项原则的声明，替国家之间的和平关系打下了基础。
