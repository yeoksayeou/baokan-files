Title: Hungarian Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Celebrate Hungarian Publishing Festival
Author:
Date: 1955-02-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 1st: Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szkladán, held a reception in Beijing on February 1st to celebrate the Hungarian Publishing Festival and to entertain Chinese and foreign journalists working in the capital.

At the meeting, Press Attaché of the Embassy, Szabó, introduced to the more than sixty Chinese and foreign journalists attending the meeting the growth and development of Hungarian progressive newspapers and periodicals, and emphasized the important role played by newspapers and periodicals in Hungary's achievements in various fields under the people's democratic system. Szabó and others also answered questions raised at the meeting.

### 庆贺匈牙利出版节  匈牙利驻华大使举行招待会

1955-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    庆贺匈牙利出版节
    匈牙利驻华大使举行招待会
    【新华社一日讯】二月一日是匈牙利出版节。匈牙利驻华大使斯克拉丹一日在北京举行招待会，招待首都的中外报刊工作者。
    会上，大使馆新闻随员沙波向到会的中外报刊工作者六十多人介绍了匈牙利进步报刊成长发展的情况，并强调指出报刊对匈牙利在人民民主制度下取得各方面成就所起的重要作用。沙波等最后并回答了会上提出的问题。
