Title: Vietnam International Supervisory Commission Issues Communiqué Stating Haiphong Region Transfer Must Comply with Geneva Accords
Author:
Date: 1955-02-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 18th: The Vietnam International Supervisory and Control Commission recently released a communiqué to the press regarding the transfer of the Haiphong region.

    The communiqué states: “For the past two months, the International Supervisory and Control Commission has been considering how to smoothly and orderly carry out the transfer of Haiphong upon the conclusion of the three-hundred-day period stipulated in the Geneva Accords.” The communiqué mentions: During this period, the commission received petitions from some individuals in Haiphong concerning the removal of equipment and incidents caused by the prevention of such removal.

    The International Supervisory and Control Commission then clarifies in the communiqué: “Property belonging to state or public institutions, including municipal facilities and public utilities, should be regarded as public property that must be transferred.” The communiqué says: “The arrangements for the transfer must comply with the timeframe stipulated in Article 15(f)(2) of the Geneva Accords. There should be no interruption in the transfer of responsibility, and no damage or removal of public property should occur during the regrouping period.”

    The communiqué concludes: “In accordance with the supervisory responsibility stipulated in Article 36 of the Geneva Accords, the International Supervisory and Control Commission will supervise all relocation efforts in Haiphong. A special committee composed of representatives from India, Canada, and Poland has been entrusted with the task of maintaining regular contact regarding matters related to the transfer of Haiphong.”

### 越南国际监督委员会发表公报指出  海防地区移交工作必须符合日内瓦协定

1955-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国际监督委员会发表公报指出
    海防地区移交工作必须符合日内瓦协定
    【新华社十九日讯】据越南通讯社河内十八日讯：越南国际监督和监察委员会最近向报界发表了一个关于海防地区移交问题的公报。
    公报说：“过去两个月以来，国际监督和监察委员会一直在考虑如何在日内瓦协定规定的三百天期限结束时顺利地和有秩序地进行移交海防的工作的问题”。公报提到：在这个期间，委员会收到了海防一些人交来的关于迁运设备以及因为有人阻止迁运而引起事件的一些请愿书。
    国际监督和监察委员会接着在公报中说明：“属于国家或公共机构的财产，包括市政设施和公用事业在内，应该被看作是必须移交的公共财产”。公报说：“移交工作的安排必须符合于日内瓦协定第十五条己款二项所规定的期限，在责任的移交过程中不应该有任何中断，在集结的时期内不应该破坏或迁运公共财产”。
    公报最后指出：“根据日内瓦协定第三十六条规定的监督责任，国际监督和监察委员会将对海防一切迁运工作进行监察。已经委托一个由印度、加拿大和波兰三国的代表组成的特别委员会担负和有关移交海防的事情保持经常接触的任务”。
