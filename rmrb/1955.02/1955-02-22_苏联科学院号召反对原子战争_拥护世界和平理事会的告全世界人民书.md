Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Calls for Opposition to Atomic War; Supports the World Peace Council's Appeal to the People of the World
Author:
Date: 1955-02-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 20th: The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences issued a statement on behalf of Soviet scientists, expressing warm support and complete endorsement of the World Peace Council's appeal to the people of the world against the preparation of atomic warfare. The full text of the statement is as follows:

Recent events that have greatly intensified international tensions and the threat of a new war involving weapons of mass destruction have compelled us, Soviet scientists, to speak out loudly against the criminal plans for the use of atomic weapons.

To cater to the selfish interests of a small number of monopoly capitalists and producers of weapons of mass destruction, the ruling circles of the United States and Britain have forced Western European countries to accept the London and Paris Agreements on the reconstruction of the German Wehrmacht, disregarding the will of the people. The peoples of all countries have learned with great indignation that the drafters of the London and Paris Agreements are preparing to hand over atomic weapons to those Hitlerite generals who once ravaged European countries with fire and sword, slaughtered millions of people, and destroyed many of the most famous monuments of world culture.

The peoples of all countries have learned with unease and extreme indignation of the decision of the Council of the aggressive North Atlantic bloc, which in effect authorizes the American command to decide at will the location and timing of the use of atomic and thermonuclear weapons.

While preparing to use weapons of mass destruction, the imperialists, on the one hand, are talking loudly about the "defensive" nature of atomic weapons in an attempt to conceal their criminal activities. However, humanity has never considered the intention of a small number of monopoly capitalists to achieve world domination through atomic warfare as defensive.

The warmongers' talk of the "tactical" use of atomic bombs is merely intended to deceive the peoples of all countries. The first attempt to use such "tactical" weapons will inevitably lead to the large-scale use of atomic and hydrogen bombs. Aggressive forces are trying to use these hypocritical arguments of "defense" and "tactical" use of atomic bombs to convince the peoples of all countries that the use of atomic weapons seems unavoidable, thereby dragging them into an atomic war that would bring endless disaster to all mankind.

However, the facts irrefutably prove that atomic war can be prevented.

The era when ruling groups could disregard the will of the people and pursue their own wishful thinking is long gone. In Korea, despite the unwillingness of some American generals, popular indignation prevented the use of the atomic bomb. The conscience of mankind has stopped the aggressors' war in Vietnam.

The wrath of the people will also shatter the new criminal plans of the North Atlantic bloc partners. The actions of the peoples of all countries against launching an atomic war are becoming increasingly firm.

Soviet scientists, in dedicating their strength and knowledge to the noble goals of peaceful construction, further improving the welfare of the Soviet people, and developing the culture of the Soviet people, cannot tolerate the great achievements of human thought, such as mastering atomic energy, being used by imperialists for mass slaughter and destruction.

Atomic energy should be used to promote the infinite progress and peaceful development of mankind. The Soviet Union has taken the lead in using this great discovery for peaceful economic construction. The world's first atomic power plant has been built in the Soviet Union and has already begun generating electricity. Soviet scientists are conducting scientific work to study the use of atomic energy in technology, biology, medicine, and agriculture. The Soviet people, who have already put atomic energy to peaceful uses in practice, are ready to pass on their experience to the people of other countries. The Soviet government has expressed its full willingness to help China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, the German Democratic Republic, and other countries in the widespread use of atomic energy for peaceful purposes.

The Soviet people firmly advocate the prohibition of atomic weapons and any other weapons of mass destruction because they are concerned only with the interests of the peoples of all countries, and their starting point is the unshakable principle that the socialist system and the capitalist system can coexist peacefully.

Soviet scientists support the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on February 9, 1955, which reflects the long-cherished wishes and desires of all the Soviet people and all peace-loving people in all countries.

The declaration states, "The peoples of all countries are very concerned about strengthening universal peace. They have full possibilities to prevent another war, because the forces of peace are constantly growing and are now stronger than the forces of aggression and war."

The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences calls on all Soviet scientists and all the Soviet people to unanimously support the World Peace Council's appeal to the people of the world.

Great ideas awaken great forces. We believe that the forces of peace are invincible.

### 苏联科学院号召反对原子战争  拥护世界和平理事会的告全世界人民书

1955-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院号召反对原子战争
    拥护世界和平理事会的告全世界人民书
     【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十日电：苏联科学院主席团发表声明，代表苏联科学家表示热烈拥护并完全支持世界和平理事会为反对准备原子战争发表的告全世界人民书。声明全文如下：
    最近发生的一些大大加剧国际紧张局势的事件和使用大规模毁灭性武器的新战争的威胁，使我们苏联科学家不得不大声疾呼反对使用原子武器的罪恶计划。
    美英统治集团为了迎合一小撮垄断资本家和大规模毁灭性武器的生产者的自私利益，不顾人民的意志而强迫西欧国家接受关于重建德国国防军的伦敦和巴黎协定。各国人民异常愤慨地获悉：伦敦和巴黎协定的拟订者准备把原子武器交给那些希特勒的将军们，他们曾经用火和剑蹂躏过欧洲各国，屠杀过数以百万计的人，摧毁过许多极其著名的世界文化古迹。
    各国人民不安地和异常愤慨地获悉了侵略性北大西洋集团理事会的决定，这个决定实际上就是授权美国司令部可以随心所欲地决定使用原子武器和热核子武器的地点和时间。
    帝国主义一方面准备使用大规模毁灭性武器，另一方面却高谈原子武器的“防御”性质，企图借以掩饰自己的罪恶活动。但是，人类从来不认为一小撮垄断资本家想借助原子战争获得世界统治的意图是防御。
    战争贩子高谈原子弹的“战术”使用，只不过是为了迷惑各国人民。使用这种“战术”武器的第一个企图必将导致大规模地使用原子弹和氢弹。侵略势力竭力想用“防御”和原子弹的“战术”使用这些虚伪论调要各国人民相信使用原子武器似乎是不可避免的，从而将他们拖入会给全人类带来无穷灾祸的原子战争。
    但是，事实无可辩驳地证实，原子战争是可以防止的。
    统治集团可以不顾人民的意志来打自己的如意算盘的时代早就过去了。在朝鲜，尽管某些美国将军如何不甘心，人民的愤慨制止了原子弹的使用。人类的良心使侵略者停止了越南战争。
    人民的忿怒也一定会粉碎北大西洋集团伙伴们的新罪恶计划。世界各国人民反对发动原子战争的行动越来越坚决了。
    苏联科学家在把自己的力量和知识贡献给和平建设、进一步提高苏联人民的福利、发展苏联人民的文化这样一些崇高的目标，因此他们不能容忍像掌握原子能这样一个人类思想的伟大成就被帝国主义者用来进行大规模的屠杀和破坏。
    原子能应当用来促进人类的无限进步和和平发展。苏联在利用这个伟大的发现进行和平经济建设方面首先做出了榜样。世界上第一个原子能工业电力站在苏联建立起来了并且已经开始发电。苏联科学家正在进行科学工作，研究在技术、生物学、医学和农业中使用原子能。已经在实际上将原子能用于和平目的的苏联人民准备把自己的经验传授给其他国家人民。苏联政府已经表示完全愿意在将原子能广泛地用于和平目的方面帮助中国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、德意志民主共和国和其他国家。
    苏联人坚决主张禁止原子武器和任何其他大规模毁灭性武器，因为他们所考虑的只是各国人民的利益，而他们的出发点是社会主义制度和资本主义制度可以和平共处这个不可动摇的原则。
    苏联科学家拥护一九五五年二月九日苏联最高苏维埃的宣言，这个宣言反映了苏联全体人民和各国爱好和平的人们的夙愿和愿望。
    宣言中说道，“各国人民非常关心加强普遍的和平。他们有防止另一次战争的充分的可能条件，因为和平力量在不断增长，现在已经比侵略和战争势力强大了。”
    苏联科学院主席团号召全体苏联科学家、全体苏联人民一致支持世界和平理事会的告全世界人民书。
    伟大的思想唤起伟大的力量。我们相信，和平的力量是不可战胜的。
