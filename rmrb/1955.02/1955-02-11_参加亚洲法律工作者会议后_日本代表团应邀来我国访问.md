Title: After Attending the Asian Legal Workers Conference, the Japanese Delegation is Invited to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-02-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Japanese delegation, consisting of eighteen members, who attended the Asian Legal Workers Conference, led by Nagano Kunisuke, head of the delegation, and Tozawa Tetsuhiko and Okabayashi Tatsuo, deputy heads of the delegation, have arrived in Beijing on the 9th at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society for a visit to our country.

### 参加亚洲法律工作者会议后  日本代表团应邀来我国访问

1955-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

    参加亚洲法律工作者会议后
    日本代表团应邀来我国访问
    【新华社九日讯】参加亚洲法律工作者会议的日本代表团一行十八人，应中国政治法律学会的邀请，由团长长野国助、副团长户泽铁彦和冈林辰雄率领来我国访问，已在九日到达北京。
