Title: Malayan Guerrillas Fight in Johor

Author:

Date: 1955-02-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kuala Lumpur: Reuters reports that British forces suffered losses in Johor's jungles on the 10th when they came into contact with Malayan guerrillas.
    The report stated that one Fijian soldier in the British army was shot and killed by guerrillas while preparing to throw a grenade. The grenade exploded, killing another Fijian soldier.

### 马来亚游击队在柔佛州作战

1955-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    马来亚游击队在柔佛州作战
    【新华社讯】吉隆坡消息：据路透社报道，英国部队十日在柔佛州丛林中同马来亚游击队接触时遭到了损失。
    消息说，有一名英军裴济族兵在准备投掷手榴弹时被游击队开枪打死。这个手榴弹爆炸了，炸死了另一个裴济族兵。
