Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Condolences on the Death of Danish Prime Minister Hedtoft
Author:
Date: 1955-02-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram to Danish Acting Prime Minister and Foreign Minister H.C. Hansen on January 31st, expressing condolences on the death of Danish Prime Minister Hedtoft. The telegram stated: "Shocked to learn of the unfortunate death of Danish Prime Minister Hans Christian Hedtoft on January 29th, I hereby express my condolences."

Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd, Stockholm dispatch: Danish Prime Minister Hans Christian Hedtoft suddenly passed away on the morning of January 29th while leading the Danish delegation to attend the Nordic Council meeting in Stockholm. The King of Denmark appointed former Foreign Minister H.C. Hansen as Prime Minister on February 1st.

### 周恩来总理电唁丹麦首相赫托夫的逝世

1955-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理电唁丹麦首相赫托夫的逝世
    【新华社二日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来于一月三十一日致电丹麦代理首相兼外交大臣汉·克·斯·汉森，悼唁丹麦首相赫托夫逝世。电文说：惊悉丹麦王国首相汉斯·克·赫托夫在一月二十九日不幸逝世，特此致唁。
     【新华社二日讯】斯德哥尔摩消息：丹麦首相汉斯·克·赫托夫在率领丹麦代表团出席在斯德哥尔摩举行的北欧理事会会议时于一月二十九日清晨突然逝世。丹麦国王已于二月一日任命原外交大臣汉·克·斯·汉森为首相。
