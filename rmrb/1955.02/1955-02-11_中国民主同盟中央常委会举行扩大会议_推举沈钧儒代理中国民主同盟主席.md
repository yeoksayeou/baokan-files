Title: China Democratic League Central Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting; Shen Junru Elected Acting Chairman of the China Democratic League
Author:
Date: 1955-02-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 10th—The Central Standing Committee of the China Democratic League held a temporary enlarged meeting in Beijing on the afternoon of the 10th. Fourteen Standing Committee members in Beijing attended, including Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Luo Longji, Shi Liang, and Gao Chongmin. Twenty-four central committee members, alternate central committee members, and heads of various departments and offices in Beijing attended as observers. The meeting was presided over by Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the China Democratic League.

    The meeting began with a moment of silence for the late Chairman Zhang Lan. After hearing a report from Hu Yuzhi, Secretary-General of the China Democratic League, on Chairman Zhang Lan's illness and final moments, a resolution was immediately passed. The resolution called on all members of the China Democratic League to further strengthen unity, enhance learning, and intensify work in order to mourn Chairman Zhang Lan. They should struggle under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao to complete the general task of the state's transition period and oppose enemies at home and abroad.

    The meeting unanimously adopted the proposal put forward by Zhang Bojun, Luo Longji, Shi Liang, Gao Chongmin, and others to elect Shen Junru as the acting chairman of the China Democratic League.

### 中国民主同盟中央常委会举行扩大会议  推举沈钧儒代理中国民主同盟主席

1955-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民主同盟中央常委会举行扩大会议
    推举沈钧儒代理中国民主同盟主席
    【新华社十日讯】中国民主同盟中央常务委员会十日下午在北京举行临时扩大会议。出席的有在京的常务委员沈钧儒、章伯钧、罗隆基、史良、高崇民等十四人，列席的有在京的中央委员、候补中央委员和各会、处的负责人二十四人。会议由民盟副主席沈钧儒主持。
    会议在全体肃立为故张澜主席默哀，并听了民盟秘书长胡愈之报告张澜主席的病况和临终情况后，当即通过了一项决议。决议要求民盟全体成员，为了哀悼张澜主席，应进一步地加强团结，加强学习，加强工作，在中国共产党和毛主席的英明领导下，为完成国家过渡时期总任务和反对国内外敌人而斗争。
    会议一致通过章伯钧、罗隆基、史良、高崇民等提出的建议，推举沈钧儒代理中国民主同盟主席。
