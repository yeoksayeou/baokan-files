Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Berlin to Visit Leipzig International Fair
Author:
Date: 1955-02-26
Page: 4

    Visiting Leipzig International Fair
    Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Berlin
    Xinhua News Agency: The Chinese government delegation invited by the government of the German Democratic Republic to visit the Leipzig Technical Fair and Consumer Goods Sample Fair, led by Jiang Guangnai, Minister of Textile Industry and head of the delegation, and Wang Xinyuan, Deputy Minister of Light Industry and deputy head of the delegation, left Beijing on the 20th and arrived in Berlin on the 23rd.

### 参观莱比锡国际博览会  我国政府代表团到达柏林

1955-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    参观莱比锡国际博览会
    我国政府代表团到达柏林
    据新华社讯：应德意志民主共和国政府邀请前往参观莱比锡技术博览会和消费品样品博览会的我国政府代表团，在团长、纺织工业部部长蒋光鼐和副团长、轻工业部副部长王新元率领下，已于二十日离京，二十三日抵达柏林。
