Title: List of Members of the Supreme People's Court Adjudication Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Seventh Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress passed the appointment of Dong Biwu, Gao Kelin, Zhang Zhiran, Ma Xiwu, He Lanjie, Zeng Hanzhou, Jia Qian, Xing Yimin, Song Guangchang, Liu Yinxia, and Yang Qirui as members of the Adjudication Committee of the Supreme People's Court.

### 最高人民法院审判委员会委员名单

1955-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    最高人民法院审判委员会委员名单
    【新华社十二日讯】全国人民代表大会常务委员会第七次会议通过任命董必武、高克林、张志让、马锡五、何兰阶、曾汉周、贾潜、邢亦民、宋广常、刘寅夏、杨奇锐为最高人民法院审判委员会委员。
