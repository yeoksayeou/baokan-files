Title: Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement's This Year's Implementation Plan Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th: The 1955 implementation plan for the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Beijing on February 17th, after friendly and harmonious discussions between the two sides.

Representing the Chinese side was the Chief Negotiator of the People's Republic of China, Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin. Representing the Bulgarian side was the head of the Bulgarian negotiation delegation, Bulgarian Ambassador to China Dimov.

Also present at the signing ceremony were Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng, Vice Minister of Agriculture Cai Ziwei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs He Wei, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Chen Zhongjing, and Deputy Director of the General Office of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Cao Richang.

### 中保文化合作协定今年执行计划在京签订

1955-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中保文化合作协定今年执行计划在京签订
    【新华社十七日讯】中保文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划，经过双方友好融洽的商谈，二月十七日在北京签订。
    代表中国方面签字的是中华人民共和国首席谈判代表、文化部副部长丁西林；代表保加利亚方面签字的是保加利亚谈判代表团团长、保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使迪莫夫。
    参加签字仪式的有中华人民共和国高等教育部部长杨秀峰，农业部副部长蔡子伟，外交部部长助理何伟，对外文化联络局副局长陈忠经和中国科学院办公厅副主任曹日昌。
