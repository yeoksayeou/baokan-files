Title: The PLA Helped Me Understand Great Truths
Author: Tong He, Gu Tianwo
Date: 1967-08-15
Edition: 2

On a train, passengers were collectively studying the "Quotations from Chairman Mao." An elderly woman with white hair was listening intently but seemed to be struggling. Soldier Liu Chengke noticed this and walked over to ask kindly, "Grandma, can you read?"

The old woman replied cheerfully, "I can't read. But I love listening to Chairman Mao's words."

Liu Chengke opened the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and began to read with her, "Whatever the enemy opposes, we support; whatever the enemy supports, we oppose." He then explained this passage in simple terms, relating it to the current situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and discussed the hardships of the old society. The old woman said, "I suffered under the oppression of landlords and had to beg for food all year round."

Liu Chengke continued, "But the biggest capitalist-roader in power within the Party says that landlords oppressing and exploiting the poor is justified!" The old woman, upon hearing this, became so angry that her hands trembled. She said, "In the old society, the poor were exploited and oppressed, working hard all year round, and still had nothing to eat or wear. What kind of justice is that?"

Liu Chengke immediately added, "That's right. We must follow Chairman Mao's words and thoroughly overthrow the biggest capitalist-roader in power within the Party."

The old woman's eyes shone brightly as she said, "The PLA is truly great! You helped me understand the great truths of Chairman Mao."

### “解放军帮我懂得了大道理”
佟贺  古田沃
1967-08-15
第2版()
专栏：革命群众来信

    “解放军帮我懂得了大道理”
    列车上，旅客们在集体学习《毛主席语录》。一位头发花白的老大娘听得很认真，但又显得有些吃力。解放军刘成科看见后，走过去亲切地问道：“老大娘，识字吗？”老大娘开朗地说：“我不识字。毛主席的话，我最爱听。”
    刘成科翻开《毛主席语录》，就领着老大娘读起“凡是敌人反对的，我们就要拥护；凡是敌人拥护的，我们就要反对”这段话，并结合当前无产阶级文化大革命的形势，深入浅出地讲解了毛主席这个教导的伟大意义，随后又谈起了旧社会的苦。老大娘说：“我受过地主压迫的苦，成年要饭。”刘成科接着说：“可是党内最大的走资本主义道路的当权派，还说地主压迫剥削穷人有理呢！”老大娘听了，气得两手直抖，说：“旧社会穷人受剥削受压迫，一年四季累到头，还是没吃没穿，这叫啥有理？”刘成科紧接上说：“对，我们就是要按照毛主席的话去做，彻底打倒党内最大的走资本主义道路的当权派。”
    老大娘的眼里闪耀着光亮，说：“解放军真好！你帮助我懂得了毛主席说的大道理。”
                      佟贺、古田沃
