Title: The General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army Holds a Gathering to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1967-08-04
Edition: 5

The General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army held a gathering on the evening of August 1 to enthusiastically celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Lieutenant General Le Quang Dao, Deputy Director of the Political Bureau of the Vietnam People's Army, attended the gathering. Colonel Yuan Yunlou, Acting Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, was also invited to attend.

In his speech at the gathering, Colonel Nguyen Van Hieu of the Vietnam People's Army highly praised the great achievements of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in various fields. He also expressed gratitude to the Communist Party of China, the Chinese government, the Chinese people, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army for the tremendous assistance they have provided to the Vietnamese people in their struggle against French colonialism and in their current struggle against American aggression for national salvation.

Yuan Yunlou, in his speech, praised the unbreakable fighting friendship between the armies and peoples of China and Vietnam. He also expressed his firm belief that the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression for national salvation will ultimately achieve victory.

### 越南人民军总司令部集会庆祝我建军节

1967-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总司令部集会庆祝我建军节
    新华社河内二日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南人民军总司令部一日晚上举行集会，热烈庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年。越南人民军总政治局副主任黎光道少将出席了集会。中国驻越南大使馆代理武官袁云楼应邀出席了集会。
    越南人民军阮文效上校在集会上的讲话中热烈赞扬中国人民解放军在各方面取得的伟大成就，并且感谢中国共产党、中国政府、中国人民和中国人民解放军，对越南人民在反对法国殖民主义者和当前的抗美救国斗争中所给予的巨大援助。
    袁云楼在讲话中赞扬中越两国军队和人民之间的牢不可破的战斗友谊，并且表示坚信：越南人民的抗美救国斗争必将取得最后的胜利。
