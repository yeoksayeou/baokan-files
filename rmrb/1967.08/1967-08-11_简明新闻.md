	Brief News

	Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Four U.S. Aircraft and Damage One U.S. Ship

	From August 1 to 7, the military and civilians of the Vietnamese provinces of Yongling, Ha Tinh, and Quang Binh shot down four invading U.S. aggressor aircraft.

	On August 8, the coastal artillery of the Vietnamese People's Army damaged one U.S. pirate warship.

	Patriotic Armed Forces in Laos' Xam Neua Province Shoot Down Three U.S. Aircraft

	On July 10 and 30, the anti-aircraft forces of the patriotic armed forces in Laos' Xam Neua Province shot down two U.S. aggressor F-105 aircraft and one F-4B aircraft.

	Palestinian People Resolved to Oppose U.S. and Israeli Aggression

	On August 5, in the Sudanese capital of Khartoum, the Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization, Shukeiri, said: "The Palestinian people will continue with greater resolve to fight against U.S. imperialism and its lackey Israel, and to strive for the liberation of Palestine."

	Shukeiri stated that they will rebuild the Palestinian army in a manner suitable for guerrilla warfare. He said: "We will mobilize all the strength of our people to wage a people's war."

	Tanzania Federation of Labor Unions Holds Second Congress

	From July 27 to August 3, the second congress of the Tanzania Federation of Labor Unions was held in Dar es Salaam.

	Approximately three hundred representatives from various parts of the United Republic of Tanzania participated in the congress. Chinese trade union representative Lu Zongying, Albanian representative Kota, South Vietnamese representative Li Yingjie, and representatives from the All-African Trade Union Federation were also invited to attend the congress.

	Tanzanian President Nyerere, First Vice President Karume, and Second Vice President Kawawa attended the opening ceremony. Nyerere and Karume delivered speeches at the opening and closing ceremonies, respectively.

	Tunisian Government Continues to Persecute Protesters Against Imperialism

	The Tunisian government continues to brutally persecute the people who participated in the June 5 demonstration against U.S. and British imperialism's support for Israel's aggression against Arab countries.

	Following the July 19 unjust verdicts of two months to four years imprisonment for over fifty demonstrators, the Tunisian Special Military Court recently sentenced over fifty more demonstrators to two to twenty years imprisonment.

	The heaviest sentence was imposed on a Tunisian university student named Ben Rane. He was sentenced to twenty years imprisonment.

	Ben Rane sternly refuted the charges against him in court. He pointed out that participating in the demonstration against Israel's aggression against Arab countries is not a crime.

### 简明新闻

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南军民又击落美机四架击伤美舰一艘
    越南北方永灵区、河静省和广平省军民在八月一日到七日期间，击落了入侵的美国强盗飞机四架。
    八月八日，越南人民军海岸炮兵部队击伤美国海盗军舰一艘。
    老挝桑怒省爱国武装部队击落美机三架
    老挝桑怒省爱国武装部队的防空部队七月十日和三十日又击落美国强盗飞机F—105两架和F—4 H一架。
    巴勒斯坦人民决心反对美国以色列侵略
    巴勒斯坦解放组织主席舒凯里五日在苏丹首都喀土穆说：“巴勒斯坦人民将以更大的决心继续为反对美帝国主义及其走狗以色列和争取巴勒斯坦的解放而斗争。”
    舒凯里表示将用适合于进行游击战的方式来重建巴勒斯坦的军队。他说：我们将动用我国人民的一切力量来打一场人民战争。
    坦噶尼喀全国工人联合会举行代表大会
    坦噶尼喀全国工人联合会第二次代表大会从七月二十七日到八月三日在达累斯萨拉姆举行。
    参加大会的有来自坦桑尼亚联合共和国各地的大约三百名代表。中国工会代表卢宗英、阿尔巴尼亚代表科塔、越南南方代表黎英杰，以及全非工会联合会的代表等也应邀参加了大会。
    坦桑尼亚总统尼雷尔、第一副总统卡鲁姆、第二副总统卡瓦瓦出席了大会开幕式。尼雷尔和卡鲁姆分别在开幕式和闭幕式上讲了话。
    突尼斯政府继续迫害参加反帝游行群众
    突尼斯政府继续残酷迫害参加六月五日反对美、英帝国主义支持以色列侵略阿拉伯国家的示威游行的人民群众。
    突尼斯特别军事法庭继七月十九日无理判决五十多名参加游行的人两个月至四年徒刑之后，又在最近判决五十余名参加游行的人两年至二十年徒刑。
    判刑最重的是一位名叫本·惹奈的突尼斯大学学生。他被判二十年徒刑。
    本·惹奈在法庭上义正词严地驳斥了强加给他的“罪名”。他指出，参加反对以色列侵略阿拉伯国家的示威游行是无罪的。
                          （据新华社讯）
