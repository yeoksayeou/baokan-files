Title: Central Military Commission of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Chairman Chen Nanzhong Cables Comrade Lin Biao
Author:
Date: 1967-08-30
Edition: 2

The New China News Agency, Hanoi, August 29 - According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, on August 23, Chen Nanzhong, Chairman of the Central Military Commission of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, cabled Comrade Lin Biao, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, to warmly congratulate the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force on its brilliant victory of shooting down two invading U.S. planes and capturing a live American air pirate on August 21.

The cable expressed warm applause for the high alertness and modern technology of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, ready for battle at any time.

The cable said, "Your new brilliant achievement makes the military and people of South Vietnam even more excited and more determined to advance to thoroughly defeat the U.S. aggressor bandits."

The cable concluded by expressing warm congratulations on behalf of the people and armed forces of South Vietnam to the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the air force units that achieved the brilliant victory.

### 南越民族解放阵线中央军委主任陈南忠致电林彪同志  热烈祝贺我空军全歼美机两架的辉煌胜利

1967-08-30
第2版()
专栏：

    南越民族解放阵线中央军委主任陈南忠致电林彪同志
    热烈祝贺我空军全歼美机两架的辉煌胜利
    新华社河内二十九日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会军事委员会主任陈南忠二十三日打电报给中华人民共和国国防部长林彪同志，热烈祝贺中国人民解放军空军部队二十一日一举全歼入侵美机两架，并活捉美国飞贼的辉煌胜利。
    电报表示热烈欢呼中国人民解放军随时准备战斗的高度警惕性和现代化技术。
    电报说，“你们取得的辉煌的新战功，使越南南方军民更加兴奋，更加坚决地冲上前去，彻底打败美国侵略强盗。”
    电报最后代表越南南方人民和解放武装力量，向中国人民解放军和取得辉煌战绩的空军部队表示热烈的祝贺。
