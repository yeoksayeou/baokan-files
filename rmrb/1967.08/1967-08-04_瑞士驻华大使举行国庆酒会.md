Title: Swiss Ambassador Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-08-04
Edition: 6

Swiss Ambassador to China, Rossetti, held a reception on August 1st to celebrate the Swiss National Day. Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Yuan Xiang, attended the reception.

### 瑞士驻华大使举行国庆酒会

1967-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    瑞士驻华大使举行国庆酒会
    新华社讯　瑞士驻中国大使罗赛蒂一日举行酒会，庆祝瑞士国庆节。我外交部部长助理宦乡出席了酒会。
