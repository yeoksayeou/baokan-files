Title: Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception to Celebrate Army Day
Author:
Date: 1967-08-04
Edition: 5

	Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception to Celebrate Army Day
	Romanian Ministry of Defense Holds Meeting to Celebrate Chinese Army Day

	Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 1—Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Li Bin held a grand reception on the evening of July 31 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Attending the reception were: Romanian Minister of Defense General Ion Ioniță, Generals Teodor Jacob and Ion Tutoveanu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Romanian Armed Forces General Octavian Olbăn, Commander of the Bucharest Garrison General Ion Sârbu, Deputy Chairman of the Political Bureau of the Romanian Armed Forces General Constantin Oprișa, and other high-ranking military officers of the Romanian Armed Forces.

	Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Romania Mihai Marin also attended the reception. Invitations were also extended to diplomats from several countries.

	During the reception, guests viewed an exhibition of photographs titled "Long Live Chairman Mao" and "The Chinese People's Liberation Army Forever Loyal to the Revolutionary Line of Great Leader Chairman Mao" displayed in the banquet hall. Many guests requested copies of the Little Red Book and Mao badges.

	Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 1—The Romanian Ministry of Defense held a celebration meeting on July 28 to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Romanian Deputy Minister of Defense General Burca and Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Romanian Armed Forces General Olbăn, along with more than 300 officers, attended the meeting.

	Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Li Bin and other diplomatic staff from the Chinese Embassy in Romania were also invited to attend.

	General Burca and Chargé d'Affaires Li Bin both spoke at the meeting.

	After the meeting, participants viewed the exhibition "The Chinese People's Liberation Army Forever Loyal to the Revolutionary Line of Great Leader Chairman Mao" and watched a Chinese large-scale color documentary film "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts."

### 我驻罗临时代办举行酒会庆祝建军节  罗武装部队部集会庆祝我建军节

1967-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻罗临时代办举行酒会庆祝建军节
    罗武装部队部集会庆祝我建军节
    新华社布加勒斯特一日电　中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办李斌七月三十一日晚上举行盛大酒会，热烈庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年。
    出席酒会的有：罗马尼亚武装部队部部长扬·约尼查上将，特·雅科布大将、扬·图托维亚努大将，罗马尼亚武装部队第一副总参谋长奥·奥尔班中将、布加勒斯特卫戍部队司令扬·塞尔布上将、罗马尼亚武装部队最高政治委员会副主任康·奥普里查少将以及罗马尼亚武装部队的其他一些高级将领和军官。
    罗马尼亚外交部副部长米·马林也出席了酒会。应邀出席酒会的还有一些国家的外交使节。
    酒会期间，来宾们参观了陈设在宴会厅里的“毛主席万岁”和“中国人民解放军永远忠于伟大领袖毛主席的革命路线”的图片展览。许多来宾索取了红彤彤的《毛主席语录》和金光闪闪的毛主席像章。
    新华社布加勒斯特一日电　罗马尼亚武装部队部为庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年，在七月二十八日举行庆祝集会。
    罗马尼亚武装部队部副部长布尔卡上将、罗马尼亚武装部队第一副总参谋长奥尔班中将以及三百多名军官出席了集会。
    我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办李斌以及使馆的外交官员应邀出席了集会。
    布尔卡上将和李斌代办先后在集会上讲了话。
    会后，参加集会的人参观了“中国人民解放军永远忠于伟大领袖毛主席的革命路线”图片展览，并观看了我国大型彩色纪录影片《毛主席是我们心中的红太阳》。
