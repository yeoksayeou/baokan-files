Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Meets with Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-08-04
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Meets with Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegation

The Seventh Session of the Sino-Vietnamese Science and Technology Cooperation Executive Committee Signs the Minutes

	New China News Agency, August 3rd: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen of the State Council met with Dang Thi, the First Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission of Vietnam, and the entire delegation of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's Science and Technology Delegation participating in the Seventh Session of the Sino-Vietnamese Science and Technology Cooperation Executive Committee this afternoon. Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

	Vietnamese Democratic Republic Ambassador to China, Vu Minh Quan, was also present at the meeting.

	New China News Agency, August 3rd: The minutes of the Seventh Session of the Sino-Vietnamese Science and Technology Cooperation Executive Committee were signed in Beijing today.

	Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen attended the signing ceremony.

	Signing the minutes were Zhang Ben, the leader of the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation and the Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the State Science and Technology Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Dang Thi, the leader of the Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegation and the First Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission of Vietnam.

	Attending the signing ceremony were Cao Keqiang, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Vietnamese Ambassador to China Vu Minh Quan, and the entire Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegation.

	After the signing, Zhang Ben hosted a banquet to entertain the entire Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegation.

### 聂荣臻副总理接见越南科技代表团  中越科学技术合作执行机构第七次会议议定书签字

1967-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻副总理接见越南科技代表团
    中越科学技术合作执行机构第七次会议议定书签字
    新华社三日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻今天下午接见越南国家计划委员会第一副主任邓试，和由他率领的参加中越科技合作执行机构第七次会议的越南民主共和国科学技术代表团全体同志。聂荣臻副总理同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾也在座。
    新华社三日讯　中越科学技术合作执行机构第七次会议议定书今天在北京签字。
    聂荣臻副总理参加了签字仪式。
    在议定书上签字的，中国方面是中国科学技术代表团团长、中华人民共和国科学技术委员会革命委员会主任张本；越南方面是越南民主共和国科学技术代表团团长、越南国家计划委员会第一副主任邓试。
    参加签字仪式的有，外交部第二亚洲司副司长曹克强，越南驻中国大使吴明鸾和越南科技代表团全体成员。
    签字后，张本设宴招待越南科技代表团全体同志。
