Title: Revolutionary Masses in Qingdao Carry Out Military Support Activities on the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army
Author: Ding Keshi
Date: 1967-08-12
Edition: 2

    On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army, the revolutionary masses of Qingdao are actively carrying out military support activities. They performed a welcome show for the officers and soldiers of a certain naval fleet, and also visited the ship to learn from the spirit of the naval soldiers who are fearless in sacrifice and brave in battle.
