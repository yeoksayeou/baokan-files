Title: Premier Zhou Receives Mrs. Du Bois
Author:
Date: 1967-08-26
Edition: 4

Premier Zhou En-lai this evening received Shirley Graham, the wife of the famous black leader W.E.B. Du Bois, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with her.

Present at the reception was Chu Tu-nan, president of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

	The picture on the right shows Premier Zhou En-lai receiving Shirley Graham (third from the left) on August 25. The photo was taken during the reception.

### 周总理接见杜波依斯夫人

1967-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见杜波依斯夫人
    新华社二十五日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见著名黑人领袖杜波依斯的夫人歇莉·格雷姆，同她进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，对外友协会长楚图南在座。（附图片）
    右图：八月二十五日，周恩来总理接见了著名黑人领袖杜波依斯的夫人歇莉·格雷姆（左三）。图为接见时合影。　
                            新华社记者　刘建国摄
