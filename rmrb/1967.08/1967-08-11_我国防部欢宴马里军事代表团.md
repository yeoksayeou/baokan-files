Title: The Chinese Ministry of National Defense Welcomes the Malian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-08-11
Edition: 6

The Ministry of National Defense held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome the Malian military delegation led by Dibi Silla Diallo.

Vice Chief of Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Tianyou, and relevant responsible personnel attended the banquet.

Malian Ambassador to China, Guise, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Li Tianyou and Dibi Silla Diallo both enthusiastically praised the deep friendship between the peoples of China and Mali. Dibi Silla Diallo said in his speech that the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America should respond to the call of the great leader of the world proletariat, Chairman Mao Zedong, to unite as one and ultimately eliminate imperialism and all its lackeys.

The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere from beginning to end.

### 我国防部欢宴马里军事代表团

1967-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

    我国防部欢宴马里军事代表团
    新华社十日讯　国防部今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎由迪比·席拉·迪亚拉率领的马里军事代表团。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑，以及有关方面负责人出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有马里驻中国大使吉塞。
    李天佑和迪比·席拉·迪亚拉在宴会上热情颂扬了中国和马里两国人民的深厚友谊。迪比·席拉·迪亚拉在讲话中说，让亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲各国人民响应世界无产阶级的伟大领袖毛泽东主席的号召，团结得象一个人一样，最后消灭帝国主义及其一切走狗。
    宴会自始至终洋溢着热烈友好的气氛。
