Title: The Chinese Embassy in Sri Lanka and Afghanistan Holds Military Anniversary Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-08-06
Edition: 6

The Chinese Embassy in Sri Lanka and Afghanistan held a reception to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on August 1st, the Chinese Embassy in Sri Lanka and the Chinese Embassy in Afghanistan held receptions to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Approximately 600 people attended the reception held by the Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Sri Lanka, Delong Xi. The attendees included government officials, workers, farmers, progressive students, and civil servants.

The reception featured screenings of the Chinese films "The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought" and "The Tunnel War." The audience shouted slogans such as "Long live Chairman Mao!" and "Long live Mao Zedong Thought!"

The Chinese Military Attaché in Afghanistan, Pan Gan, held a reception to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army with 200 Afghan friends.

Attendees included high-ranking government and military officials such as Afghan Court Minister Ali Muhammad, Senate President Abdul Dawi, and Defense Minister General Khan Muhammad.

Other attendees included Sultan Mahmud Qazi, President of the Afghanistan-China Friendship Association, and various other dignitaries, as well as members of the diplomatic corps in Kabul.

The reception featured displays of foreign-language editions of Chairman Mao's works, which attracted the attention of Sri Lankan friends. Some read Chairman Mao's works on the spot, while others requested copies.

### 我驻锡兰阿富汗使馆举行建军节招待会

1967-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻锡兰阿富汗使馆举行建军节招待会
    据新华社四日讯　中国驻锡兰和阿富汗大使馆八月一日分别举行招待会，热烈庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年。
    出席中国驻锡兰大使馆临时代办奚业胜举行的招待会的约有六百人，其中包括政府官员、工人、农民、进步师生、公务人员等。
    招待会上放映了中国影片《毛泽东思想的伟大胜利》和《地道战》。观众们高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛泽东思想万岁！”
    中国驻阿富汗大使馆武官潘干举行招待会，同二百位阿富汗朋友共同庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年。
    出席招待会的有阿富汗宫廷大臣阿里·穆罕默德，长老院议长阿卜杜勒·达威和国防大臣汗·穆罕默德大将等政府和军队的高级官员。
    出席招待会的还有阿富汗—中国友好协会会长苏尔坦·马哈茂德·加齐等各界人士以及在喀布尔的外交使团的一些成员。
    招待会上展出的毛主席著作外文版吸引了锡兰朋友。他们有的当场阅读毛主席著作，有的索取毛主席著作。
