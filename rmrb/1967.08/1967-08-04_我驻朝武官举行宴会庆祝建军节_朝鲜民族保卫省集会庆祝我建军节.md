Title: Chinese Military Attache Holds Banquet to Celebrate Army Day; North Korean Ministry of People's Armed Forces Holds Meeting to Celebrate Chinese Army Day
Author:
Date: 1967-08-04
Edition: 5

	Chinese Military Attache Holds Banquet to Celebrate Army Day

	North Korean Ministry of People's Armed Forces Holds Meeting to Celebrate Chinese Army Day

	New China News Agency, Pyongyang, August 1st: Chinese Military Attache to North Korea, Chen Jianwu, held a banquet on the 1st to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Attending the banquet were: Vice Premier of the North Korean Cabinet, Chung Chun-sa, Alternate Member of the Political Committee of the North Korean Workers' Party and Minister of Finance, Han Sang-du, Director of the Cabinet's Bureau of Foreign Economic Affairs, Kim Kyong-yon, Deputy Director of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Vice Chairman of the North Korean-Chinese Friendship Association, Chon Byong-chol, Deputy Foreign Minister, Kim Chae-bong, Deputy Minister of People's Armed Forces, Marshal Choe Byong-hak, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, Lieutenant General Han Tae-suk, and Lieutenant General Yun Tae-hong, among others.

	Military attaches and diplomats from several countries' embassies in North Korea also attended the banquet.

	Chinese Charge d'Affaires in North Korea, Wang Peng, and the representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers to the Korean Military Armistice Commission, as well as representatives of Chinese experts assisting in North Korean construction, also attended the banquet.

	New China News Agency, Pyongyang, August 1st: The Ministry of People's Armed Forces of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a meeting on July 29th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Korean People's Army leaders and officers attended the meeting.

	Chinese Military Attache to North Korea, Chen Jianwu, was invited to attend the meeting.

	Lieutenant General Yun Tae-hong, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, and Chinese Military Attache to North Korea, Chen Jianwu, spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, a Chinese film was shown.

### 我驻朝武官举行宴会庆祝建军节  朝鲜民族保卫省集会庆祝我建军节

1967-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻朝武官举行宴会庆祝建军节
    朝鲜民族保卫省集会庆祝我建军节
    新华社平壤一日电  中国驻朝鲜大使馆武官陈健吾一日晚上举行宴会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年。
    出席宴会的有：朝鲜内阁副首相郑准泽，朝鲜劳动党政治委员会候补委员、财政相韩相斗，内阁对外经济总局长金炅炼，对外文委副委员长、朝中友好协会副委员长全炳哲，外务省副相金载凤，民族保卫省副相池丙学上将，朝鲜人民军副总参谋长韩泰淑中将、尹泰洪中将等。
    一些国家驻朝鲜大使馆武官和外交官员出席了宴会。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办王彭和朝鲜军事停战委员会中国人民志愿军代表团代表以及帮助朝鲜建设的中国专家代表也出席了宴会。
    新华社平壤一日电  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫省七月二十九日举行集会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年。
    朝鲜人民军将领和军官参加了集会。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆武官陈健吾等应邀出席了集会。
    朝鲜人民军副总参谋长尹泰洪中将和中国驻朝鲜大使馆陈健吾武官在会上讲了话。会后，放映了中国电影。
