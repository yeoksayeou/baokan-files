Title: Lenin: The Party's Organization and Party Literature
Author: 
Date: 1967-08-29
Edition: 4

We are talking about party literature and how party literature should be supervised by the party. Everyone can freely write whatever they want and say whatever they want without any restrictions. However, every free organization (including the party) can also freely expel those who use the party's banner to advocate anti-party views.

### 列宁：《党的组织和党的文学》

1967-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

    列宁：《党的组织和党的文学》
    我们说的是党的文学以及党的文学应受党的监督。每个人都可以自由地、不受任何限制地写他所愿意写的一切，说他所愿意说的一切。但是每个自由的团体（包括党在内），同样也可以自由地赶走利用党的招牌来鼓吹反党观点的人。
