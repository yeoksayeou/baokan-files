Title: Bombing the Headquarters
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1966-08-05
Edition: 1

	Bombing the Headquarters
	(My Big-Character Poster)
	(August 5, 1966)
	Mao Zedong

	The first nationwide big-character poster and the commentary by the People's Daily are written so well! I ask comrades to read them again. However, over the past more than fifty days, from the central to the local levels, some leading comrades have done the opposite. They have stood on the reactionary bourgeois standpoint, practiced bourgeois dictatorship, suppressed the ardent cultural revolution of the proletariat, confused right and wrong, repressed revolutionary factions, suppressed differing opinions, practiced white terror, felt self-satisfied, boosted the arrogance of the bourgeoisie, and crushed the spirit of the proletariat. How poisonous it is! Linking this to the rightist deviation of 1962 and the pseudo-left but actually rightist deviation of 1964, can it not make people deeply aware?

### 炮打司令部  （我的一张大字报）  （一九六六年八月五日）
毛泽东
1967-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    炮打司令部
    （我的一张大字报）
    （一九六六年八月五日）
    毛泽东
    全国第一张马列主义的大字报和人民日报评论员的评论，写得何等好呵！请同志们重读一遍这张大字报和这个评论。可是在五十多天里，从中央到地方的某些领导同志，却反其道而行之，站在反动的资产阶级立场上，实行资产阶级专政，将无产阶级轰轰烈烈的文化大革命运动打下去，颠倒是非，混淆黑白，围剿革命派，压制不同意见，实行白色恐怖，自以为得意，长资产阶级的威风，灭无产阶级的志气，又何其毒也！联系到一九六二年的右倾和一九六四年形“左”而实右的错误倾向，岂不是可以发人深醒的吗？
