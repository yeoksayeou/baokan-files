	Brief News

	Vietnamese People and Military Shoot Down Four Invading U.S. Aircraft

	On July 31, the people and military of Vietnam's Ha Tinh Province shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft and captured one U.S. air pirate. On July 28 and 29, the people and military of Quang Ninh Province shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft each day. On July 28, the people and military of Quang Binh Province shot down one enemy aircraft.

	Palestinians in Occupied Areas Continue Their Struggle

	In the occupied Gaza Strip and the West Bank of the Jordan River, the Arab people are intensifying their struggle against U.S.-Israeli aggression. In Gaza, some Arab youth attacked Israeli vehicles. On July 30, the people of Nablus city in the West Bank held a general strike to protest the Israeli occupation authorities' seizure of food from the city's market. In Nablus city, leaflets calling for the return of the West Bank to Jordan also appeared.

	France's Unemployment Reaches Post-War High

	By the beginning of July, France's unemployment rate had reached 400,000, the highest since World War II. According to a July 25 announcement by the French Ministry of Labor, the number of unemployed people registered at employment agencies but still without jobs increased by 60% in July compared to the same period last year.

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南北方军民又击落入侵美机四架
    越南北方兴安省军民七月三十一日击落美国强盗飞机一架，活捉美国飞贼一名。广宁省军民七月二十八日、二十九日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机各一架。广平省军民七月二十八日击落敌机一架。
    加沙等被占领区阿拉伯人坚持斗争
    在被占领的加沙和约旦河西岸的阿拉伯人民继续加紧反对美国——以色列侵略者的斗争。在加沙，一些阿拉伯青年，袭击了以色列的汽车。约旦河西岸纳布卢斯市人民三十日举行总罢市，抗议以色列占领当局没收这个城市市场的食品。在纳布卢斯市市内还出现了要求把约旦河西岸归还约旦的传单。
    法国失业人数达到战后以来最高峰
    法国失业人数在七月初已经达到四十万，从而创造了第二次世界大战以来的最高纪录。据法国劳工部二十五日宣布，已经在职业介绍所登记但是还没有找到职业的失业者人数，今年七月一日也比去年同期猛增了百分之六十。（据新华社讯）
