Title: Premier Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing and Others Meet with Representatives of Air Force and Naval Air Force Personnel Who Shot Down Enemy Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1967-08-03
Edition: 1

Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Cultural Revolution Group and relevant departments' responsible comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, Yang Chengwu, Guan Feng, Chen Boda, Yao Wenyuan, Ye Qing, today met with representatives of the Air Force and Naval Air Force who have distinguished themselves in shooting down enemy aircraft this year, and took a group photo with them.

The representatives of the Air Force who were received are: Hu Shouren and Dong Fucheng, who shot down a U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek F-104 fighter jet on January 13; Song Yimin, Cao Wenbin, and Wang Dewen, who shot down a U.S. F-4B fighter jet on April 24; Zhang Jintang and Wen Fude, who shot down a U.S. unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft on April 29; Zhang Dengke, Wang Zhaofeng, Zhang Minggao, and Wei Wulian, who shot down two U.S. A-4B fighter jets on May 1; Liu Guangcai and Huang Jinsheng, who shot down a U.S. unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft on June 12. The representatives of the Naval Air Force who were received are: Wang Zhushu, Lü Jiliang, and Wang Chaoyu, who shot down a U.S. F-4C fighter jet on June 26. Other distinguished personnel were also received.

Present at the meeting were Wu Faxian, Wang Xinting, Wang Hongkun, Wang Huichiu, Luo Yuanfa, and Zhang Xiuchuan.

### 周恩来陈伯达康生江青等同志  接见空海军击落敌机有功人员代表

1967-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来陈伯达康生江青等同志
    接见空海军击落敌机有功人员代表
    新华社二日讯　中共中央、中央文革小组和有关方面的负责同志周恩来、陈伯达、康生、江青、杨成武、关锋、戚本禹、姚文元、叶群，今天接见了空军和海军航空兵今年以来击落敌机作战有功人员的代表，并和他们一起照了相。
    被接见的空军作战有功人员的代表是：今年一月十三日击落美制蒋帮F—104型战斗机一架的胡寿根、董福成同志，四月二十四日击落美帝F—4B型战斗机一架的宋义民、曹文斌、王德华同志，四月二十九日击落美帝无人驾驶高空侦察机一架的张金堂、文富德同志，五月一日击落美帝A—4B型战斗机两架的张登科、王兆峰、张明高、韦万练同志，六月十二日击落美帝无人驾驶高空侦察机一架的刘光才、黄金生同志；被接见的海军航空兵作战有功人员的代表是：六月二十六日击落美帝F—4C型战斗机一架的王柱书、吕纪良、王朝玉同志。被接见的还有其他有功人员。
    接见时在场的有吴法宪、王新亭、王宏坤、王辉球、罗元发、张秀川等同志。（附图片）
