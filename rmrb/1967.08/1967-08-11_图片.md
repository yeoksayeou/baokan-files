Title: Lin Biao's Inscription for the Beijing Garrison's Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works Conference
Author:
Date: 1967-08-11
Edition: 1

Lin Biao, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, has inscribed the following words for the Beijing Garrison's Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works Conference:

	"Learn from the Red Army's fine traditions. Study Chairman Mao's works diligently. Carry out Chairman Mao's directives resolutely. Strive to become a revolutionary army that is absolutely loyal, wise, courageous, and disciplined to Chairman Mao and Chairman Mao's revolutionary line."
