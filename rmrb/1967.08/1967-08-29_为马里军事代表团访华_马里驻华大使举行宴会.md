Title: Reception for the Malian Military Delegation's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1967-08-29
Edition: 6

The Malian Ambassador to China, Gisse, held a banquet tonight to celebrate the visit of the Malian military delegation to China. The entire delegation, led by Dibi Shila Diarra, attended the banquet. Invited guests included Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Tianyou, and other relevant officials.

At the banquet, Ambassador Gisse and Vice Chief of the General Staff Li Tianyou toasted, jointly wishing the great leader of the world's people, Chairman Mao, good health and longevity.

### 为马里军事代表团访华  马里驻华大使举行宴会

1967-08-29
第6版()
专栏：

    为马里军事代表团访华
    马里驻华大使举行宴会
    据新华社二十七日讯　马里共和国驻中国大使吉塞为马里军事代表团访问中国，今晚举行宴会。由迪比·席拉·迪亚拉率领的马里军事代表团全体成员出席了宴会。应邀出席宴会的，有中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑和有关方面负责人。
    吉塞大使和李天佑副总参谋长在宴会上祝酒，共同敬祝世界人民的伟大领袖毛主席健康长寿。
