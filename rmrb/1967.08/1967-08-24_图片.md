Title: Revolutionary Youth Soldier
Author:
Date: 1967-08-24
Edition: 1

	Revolutionary masses in Qingdao are actively carrying out support-the-army activities. Revolutionary workers of Qingdao Plastic Factory No. 5 made Mao Zedong badges by hand and arranged them to form the words "Long Live Chairman Mao," which they presented to the People's Liberation Army. Photographed by Revolutionary Youth Soldier (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)
