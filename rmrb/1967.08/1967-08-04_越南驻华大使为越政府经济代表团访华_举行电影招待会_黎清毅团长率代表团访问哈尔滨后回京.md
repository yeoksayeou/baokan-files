Title: Vietnamese Ambassador Hosts Film Reception for Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1967-08-04
Edition: 6

Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Vu Minh Quan, hosted a film reception for the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation led by Vice Premier Le Quyen, who is visiting China.

Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Xiannian of the State Council, along with relevant officials from government departments and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as comrades who have visited Vietnam, were invited to attend the film reception.

Attending the reception were also members of the Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegation currently visiting Beijing, and Deputy Head of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front's Permanent Delegation to China, Le Songshan, among others.

The reception featured a Vietnamese film showcasing the Vietnamese people's brave resistance against American aggression.

According to Xinhua News Agency, the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation visited Harbin from August 1st to 3rd. The delegation, led by Vice Premier Le Quyen, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Ban, and Vietnamese Ambassador to China Vu Minh Quan, arrived in Harbin accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of China, Li Qiang. Upon arrival at Harbin Airport, the Vietnamese comrades were warmly welcomed by over 500 revolutionary masses and Red Guards.

During their stay in Harbin, the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation visited factories and enterprises, receiving enthusiastic welcomes from the revolutionary masses.

Vice Premier Le Quyen engaged in cordial conversations with revolutionary workers during the visits. He stated that the great achievements made by the Chinese people are the results of implementing Chairman Mao's directives and are great victories of Mao Zedong Thought. Vice Premier Le Quyen also shared with the revolutionary workers the great victories achieved by the Vietnamese people in their struggle against American aggression for national salvation.

Upon the delegation's arrival and departure from Harbin, the Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of Heilongjiang Province, Pan Fusheng, Deputy Chairman and Commander of the Heilongjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Jiadao, and other leaders of the Harbin Revolutionary Committee, were present at the airport to welcome and see them off.

### 越南驻华大使为越政府经济代表团访华  举行电影招待会  黎清毅团长率代表团访问哈尔滨后回京

1967-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    越南驻华大使为越政府经济代表团访华
    举行电影招待会
    黎清毅团长率代表团访问哈尔滨后回京
    新华社三日讯　越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾，为黎清毅副总理率领的越南政府经济代表团访问中国，今晚举行电影招待会。
    国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，政府有关部门和中国人民解放军有关方面的负责人，以及曾经访问过越南的同志，应邀出席了电影招待会。
    出席电影招待会的，还有正在北京访问的越南科学技术代表团的成员，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长黎松山等。
    招待会上，放映了反映越南人民英勇打击美国侵略者的越南影片。
    据新华社哈尔滨三日电　越南政府经济代表团从一日到三日在哈尔滨进行了参观访问，代表团已在今天乘专机离哈尔滨回北京。
    代表团由团长、越南民主共和国副总理黎清毅，副团长、外贸部副部长李班，副团长、越南驻中国大使吴明鸾率领，由我国对外贸易部副部长李强陪同到达哈尔滨。在哈尔滨机场上，越南战友们受到五百多名革命群众、红卫兵小将的热烈欢迎。
    越南政府经济代表团在哈尔滨期间，参观了工厂、企业，所到之处，受到革命群众的热烈欢迎。
    黎清毅副总理在参观中，和革命职工进行了亲切交谈。他说，中国人民所取得的各项巨大成就，都是贯彻执行毛主席教导的结果，都是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。黎清毅副总理还向革命职工介绍了越南人民在抗美救国斗争中取得的巨大胜利。
    在代表团到达和离开哈尔滨时，黑龙江省革命委员会主任委员潘复生，副主任委员、中国人民解放军黑龙江军区司令员汪家道，以及哈尔滨市革命委员会负责人等，曾到机场欢迎和欢送。
