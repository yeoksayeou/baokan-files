Author:
Date: 1967-08-10
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou Receives Malian Government Delegation

Gise Ambassador Hosts Reception for Malian Government Delegation's Visit to China

	New China News Agency, August 9th: Premier Zhou Enlai today received the entire delegation led by Sedou Badian Kouyate of the Malian government and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

	Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi and relevant department heads.

	The Malian Ambassador to China, Gise, was also present.

	After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet to entertain the Malian guests.

	New China News Agency, August 9th: Tonight, Malian Ambassador to China Gise hosted a reception for the Malian government delegation led by Sedou Badian Kouyate visiting China. Vice Premier Chen Yi and relevant department heads were invited to attend the reception. The Malian Republic military delegation led by Dibi Shira Diarra, who is currently visiting Beijing, also attended the reception. Attending the reception were also ambassadors from African countries to China. The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. (Photo attached)

	August 9th, Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire delegation led by Sedou Badian Kouyate of the Malian government. The photo shows a group photo taken during the reception.  Photographer: Lu Xiangyou, reporter of this newspaper.

### 周总理接见马里政府代表团  吉塞大使为马里政府代表团访华举行招待会

1967-08-10
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见马里政府代表团
    吉塞大使为马里政府代表团访华举行招待会
    新华社九日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了由塞杜·巴迪安·库亚特率领的马里政府代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在场的，有陈毅副总理以及有关方面负责人。
    马里驻中国大使吉塞也在场。
    接见以后，周恩来总理设便宴招待马里客人。
    新华社九日讯　马里驻中国大使吉塞今晚为由塞杜·巴迪安·库亚特率领的马里政府代表团访问中国举行招待会。陈毅副总理以及各有关方面负责人应邀出席了招待会。正在北京访问的由迪比·席拉·迪亚拉率领的马里共和国军事代表团也出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有非洲国家驻中国使节。招待会在热烈友好气氛中进行。（附图片）
