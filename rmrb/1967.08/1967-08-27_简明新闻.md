	Norwegian Youth Protest U.S. Aggression in Vietnam

	Hundreds of Norwegian youths protested in front of the U.S. Embassy for two consecutive days on August 23 and 24, condemning the U.S.'s escalation of the war in Vietnam. The protesters surrounded the embassy and held a sit-in demonstration, chanting, "U.S. out of South Vietnam!"

	South Vietnamese Puppet Army Soldiers Desert in Droves

	The morale of the South Vietnamese puppet army has been plummeting under the heavy blows from the liberation forces, with growing war-weariness and anti-war sentiments. Many soldiers have defected to the people's side.

	In the first half of this year, nearly 3,000 members of the puppet army's special forces in the central region deserted, including ten entire companies and two platoons that completely disintegrated. In late July, more than 40 puppet soldiers in Xuan Phuoc County, Quang Nam Province, surrendered to the people.

	British Unemployment Hits New High

	As the financial and economic situation of British imperialism continues to deteriorate, the number of unemployed in Britain in August reached a new high for this month since 1940.

    简明新闻
    挪威青年抗议美国侵略越南
    数百名挪威青年二十三日和二十四日接连两天在美国大使馆门前示威，抗议美国疯狂地进行侵越战争“升级”。示威青年包围了美国使馆，在使馆门前举行了静坐示威，高呼：“美国从南越滚出去！”
    南越傀儡军厌战纷纷开小差
    越南南方伪军官兵，在解放武装力量的沉重打击下，士气日益低落，厌战和反战情绪不断增长，纷纷向人民投诚。
    南部中区在今年头六个月有近三千名伪军别动队开小差，其中包括十个连和两个排完全瓦解。广南省先福县在七月底有四十多名伪军向人民投诚。
    英国失业人数达到新的高峰
    随着英帝国主义的财政经济情况不断恶化，英国八月份的失业人数已经达到了一九四○年以来这个月份失业人数的最高峰。
                                         （据新华社讯）
