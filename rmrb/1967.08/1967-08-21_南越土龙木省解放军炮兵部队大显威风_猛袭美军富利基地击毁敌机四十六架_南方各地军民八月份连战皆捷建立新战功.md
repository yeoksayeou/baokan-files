Title: Liberation Army Artillery Forces in Tuyen Duc Province, South Vietnam, Show Great Valor, Launching a Powerful Attack on the U.S. Military's Phu Loi Base and Destroying 46 Enemy Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1967-08-21
Edition: 5

The liberation army artillery forces in Tuyen Duc Province, South Vietnam, have shown great valor by launching a powerful attack on the U.S. military's Phu Loi base, destroying 46 enemy aircraft. Military and civilian forces across the South have achieved continuous victories in August, establishing new military achievements.

According to a report by the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, on the night of July 28 to the early morning of July 29, the artillery forces of the liberation armed forces in Tuyen Duc Province launched a fierce artillery barrage against the Phu Loi base of the U.S. 1st Infantry Division, located approximately 24 kilometers north of Saigon.

Thousands of shells from the liberation armed forces hit the base's airstrip, radar station, and missile launch pad, destroying 46 aircraft of various types, demolishing two radar facilities, one missile launch pad, and 25 barracks, killing and wounding over 200 U.S. invaders.

This was the sixth attack by the liberation armed forces on the Phu Loi base. According to statistics, these six attacks have destroyed 170 enemy aircraft of various types and killed over 1,000 U.S. invaders.

The liberation armed forces across various regions in South Vietnam have been highly active on the battlefield in August, achieving continuous victories and establishing many new military achievements.

According to a report by the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, from August 3 to 6, the guerrilla forces and local troops in the three counties of Son Tay, Thuy Y, and Son Ha in Quang Ngai Province bravely counterattacked enemy "sweeping" operations and attacked enemy strongholds, killing, wounding, and capturing over 300 U.S. puppet and South Korean mercenary troops. Among them, they annihilated a U.S. infantry company, five puppet platoons, and one "pacification" unit, burned down one fuel depot and one ammunition depot, destroyed nine military ships, five military vehicles, and seized a large amount of weapons and ammunition. In these regions, the guerrilla forces and local troops alone, in a single interception battle on August 4, annihilated hundreds of U.S. puppet and South Korean mercenary troops, including an entire U.S. airborne company.

On the night of August 6, the liberation armed forces in the Saigon-Cholon region launched a fierce attack on a U.S. 25th Infantry Division convoy in the Bau Me area. After a short battle, the liberation armed forces destroyed 20 military vehicles, including 18 armored vehicles of various types, killing and wounding hundreds of U.S. invaders.

On the night of August 13, the liberation armed forces in Sa Dec Province infiltrated into the city of Cao Lanh, attacking enemy logistics bases and some puppet strongholds. After more than an hour of battle, the liberation armed forces killed and wounded over 100 puppet troops, destroyed two ammunition depots and one communications equipment warehouse, and damaged two military vehicles. The liberation armed forces also shelled enemy barracks, artillery positions, and puppet military officers' quarters, causing significant losses to the enemy.

On August 2, the liberation armed forces in Binh Duong Province annihilated an enemy company in a blocking battle, seizing dozens of weapons and a large amount of ammunition and military supplies.

Following the capture of an enemy stronghold in Thanh Li village on August 3, the local troops and guerrilla forces in Phu My County, An Giang Province, intercepted and attacked enemy "sweeping" forces in the surrounding areas on August 5, killing 60 enemy troops, shooting down one helicopter, and destroying and damaging three military vehicles.

According to a report by the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the military and civilian forces in Ninh Thuan and Ninh Hoa provinces have taken the initiative on the battlefield this year, continuously attacking the enemy and achieving significant results in annihilating and disintegrating nearly 3,000 enemy troops.

According to statistics, the military and civilian forces in Ninh Thuan Province engaged in 170 battles in the first six months of this year, annihilating and disintegrating 1,276 enemy troops, including 448 U.S. invaders, shooting down 50 aircraft of various types, and destroying 12 "strategic hamlets."

The military and civilian forces in Ninh Hoa Province engaged in 158 battles with the enemy in the first half of this year, annihilating and disintegrating 1,637 enemy troops, including killing 207 U.S. troops, shooting down and damaging 17 aircraft, destroying four military vehicles, and blowing up four bridges.

The military and civilian forces in this province also conducted 72 armed propaganda missions deep into enemy-occupied areas, helping the people destroy 10 "strategic hamlets," and demolishing 4 kilometers of barbed wire obstacles.

### 南越土龙木省解放军炮兵部队大显威风  猛袭美军富利基地击毁敌机四十六架  南方各地军民八月份连战皆捷建立新战功

1967-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

    南越土龙木省解放军炮兵部队大显威风
    猛袭美军富利基地击毁敌机四十六架
    南方各地军民八月份连战皆捷建立新战功
    新华社河内二十日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方土龙木省解放武装力量炮兵部队，七月二十八日深夜到二十九日凌晨，以猛烈的炮火袭击位于西贡北面约二十四公里的美国第一步兵师的富利基地。
    解放武装力量的成千发炮弹击中了这个基地的停机坪、雷达站和导弹发射台，击毁各种类型飞机四十六架，摧毁雷达装置两座、导弹发射台一座和营房二十五幢，打死打伤美国侵略军二百多名。
    这是解放武装力量对富利基地发动的第六次袭击。据统计，这六次袭击共击毁敌人各种类型飞机一百七十架，消灭美国侵略军一千多名。
    新华社河内二十日电　越南南方各地解放武装力量八月份在战场上十分活跃，连战连捷，建立许多新战功。
    据越南南方解放通讯社报道，广义省山河、思义和山晶三个县的游击队和地方部队，从三日到六日，英勇反击敌人的“扫荡”和袭击敌人据点，共打死打伤和俘虏美伪军及南朝鲜帮凶军三百多名，其中歼灭美国侵略军一个连、伪军五个排和一个“绥靖”队，烧毁汽油库和弹药库各一座，击毁军用船只九艘、军车五辆，缴获大批武器弹药。这些地区的游击队和地方部队仅在四日的一场截击战中，就消灭美伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军成百名，其中包括歼灭美国空降部队一个连。
    西贡—嘉定地区解放武装力量，六日夜间在保梅地区猛烈袭击美国第二十五步兵师的一支车队。经过短时间的战斗，解放武装力量击毁军车二十辆，其中包括各种类型的装甲车十八辆，打死打伤美国侵略军成百名。
    沙沥省解放武装力量在八月十三日夜间潜入高岭市，袭击敌人的后勤基地和伪军的一些据点。经过一个多小时的战斗，解放武装力量打死打伤伪军一百多名，摧毁军火库两座和通讯器材仓库一座，击毁击伤军车两辆。解放武装力量还炮击敌人的兵营、炮兵阵地、伪军军官宿舍，给敌人造成严重损失。
    建祥省解放武装力量八月二日在一次阻击战中，歼灭敌军一个连，缴获武器数十件以及许多弹药和军用物资。
    永隆省平明县地方部队和游击队，继三日夜间攻克在成利乡的一个敌军据点后，五日又在上述据点周围的一些地方截击前去“扫荡”的敌人，消灭敌人六十名，击落直升飞机一架，击毁击伤军车三辆。
    据新华社河内十八日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方宁顺、林同两省军民今年上半年发挥战场上的主动权，接连进攻敌人，取得消灭和瓦解敌人近三千名的巨大战果。
    据统计，宁顺省军民今年头六个月共战斗一百七十次，消灭和瓦解敌人一千二百七十六名，其中包括美国侵略军四百四十八名，击落各种类型飞机五十架，捣毁十二个“战略村”。
    林同省军民在今年上半年先后同敌人作战一百五十八次，消灭和瓦解敌人一千六百三十七名，其中包括打死美军二百零七名，击落击伤飞机十七架，击毁军车四辆，炸毁桥梁四座。
    这个省军民还深入敌占区进行了七十二次武装宣传，帮助人民捣毁十个“战略村”，摧毁了长达四千米的铁丝网障碍物。
