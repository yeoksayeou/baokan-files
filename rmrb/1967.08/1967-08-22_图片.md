Title: PLA Troops in Inner Mongolia Build Their Own Barracks
Author: Shī Chángjiāng
Date: 1967-08-22
Edition: 2

The People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Inner Mongolia exemplify self-reliance and perseverance, constructing their own barracks. The revolutionary youngsters of Hohhot City, driven by their love for the PLA, voluntarily come to assist in building the barracks. The image shows PLA soldiers enthusiastically teaching the revolutionary youngsters from the Inner Mongolia Agricultural School how to bind steel reinforcement. Photographed by New China News Agency reporter Shī Chángjiāng.
