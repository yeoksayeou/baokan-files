	Brief News
	Military and civilians in Hanoi and other areas shoot down four U.S. planes

	On the afternoon of August 11, U.S. aggressor planes brazenly attacked the central and suburban areas of Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The vigilant military and civilians of Hanoi struck back fiercely, shooting down two enemy planes.

	On the same day, the military and civilians of Haiphong Province also shot down one enemy plane.

	According to a supplementary report, the military and civilians of Ha Tinh Province shot down one invading U.S. aggressor plane on August 9 and captured one U.S. pilot.

	Pakistan government protests Indian army's killing of Pakistani civilians

	On August 7, the Pakistani Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note to the Indian Commission in Pakistan, strongly protesting the Indian expansionists who infiltrated into Dhalagram in East Pakistan and killed innocent Pakistani civilians.

	The note pointed out that on August 1, residents of Dhalagram in East Pakistan, Ala Bash, Ali Akbar, and Nurul Sheikh, were harvesting rice in their fields on the Pakistani side of the border. At that time, five armed Indian border guards infiltrated the area and opened fire, killing Ala Bash and Ali Akbar on the spot. The Indian border guards then kidnapped Nurul Sheikh and dragged away the bodies of the two killed. Subsequently, the Indian army set up a sentry line around Dhalagram.

	Yemen government firmly opposes British conspiracy

	The Yemeni Ministry of Foreign Affairs recently issued a statement saying that the Arab Republic of Yemen firmly opposes the British attempt to internationalize the island of Perim.

	The statement said that if necessary, Yemen will use all possible means and mobilize all its forces to prevent the British conspiracy from succeeding.

	The statement concluded that the Yemeni people are determined to oppose all British conspiracies. All British colonial conspiracies will, like their past conspiracies, be doomed to failure.

	Emergency meeting of the Arab Lawyers Union concludes

	The emergency meeting of the Arab Lawyers Union concluded on August 10 in Khartoum. The meeting stated that the continuous armed struggle and comprehensive people's war by the governments of Arab countries are the only means to deal with aggression and imperialism, eliminate Zionist occupation of Palestine, and eradicate imperialist forces in Arab countries.

	The resolution condemned the imperialist plot to force Arab countries to hold direct talks with Israel and supported Egypt's blockade of the Suez Canal.

	The emergency meeting of the Arab Lawyers Union was opened on August 6 in Khartoum. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 简明新闻

1967-08-12
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南河内等地军民又击落美机四架
    美国强盗飞机十一日下午又悍然袭击越南民主共和国首都河内市的中心区和郊区。保持高度警惕的河内市军民给以迎头痛击，击落敌机两架。
    同一天，河北省军民也击落敌机一架。
    另据补充报道，广平省军民九日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架，活捉美国飞贼一名。
    巴基斯坦政府抗议印军杀害巴人民
    巴基斯坦外交部七日照会印度驻巴基斯坦专员署，强烈抗议印度扩张主义分子潜入东巴基斯坦达哈格拉姆地方杀害无辜的巴基斯坦人。
    照会指出，八月一日，东巴基斯坦达哈格拉姆地方居民阿拉·巴什，阿里·阿克巴尔和努鲁·舍伊赫正在边境的巴基斯坦一边他们自己的田地上收割稻谷，当时有持枪的印度边防部队人员五人潜入这个地方，并向他们开枪，阿拉·巴什和阿里·阿克巴尔当即被打死。印度边防部队人员然后又绑架了努鲁·舍伊赫，并拖走上述两名被杀害者的尸体。接着，印军还在达哈格拉姆地方周围布下了哨兵线。
    也门政府坚决反对英国策划的阴谋
    也门外交部最近发表声明说，阿拉伯也门共和国坚决反对英国企图使丕林岛国际化的阴谋。
    声明说，如有必要，也门将使用一切可能的手段，动员全部力量，不让英国的阴谋得逞。
    声明最后说，也门人民决心反对英国的一切卑鄙阴谋。英国的一切殖民主义阴谋，将象过去的阴谋一样，注定要遭到失败。
    阿拉伯律师联合会紧急会议闭幕
    阿拉伯律师联合会紧急会议十日在喀土穆闭幕。会议认为，阿拉伯各国政府进行持续不断的武装斗争和全面的人民战争，是对付侵略和帝国主义，铲除犹太复国主义对巴勒斯坦的霸占和肃清在阿拉伯各国的帝国主义势力的唯一途径。
    决议谴责帝国主义诱迫阿拉伯国家同以色列举行直接谈判的阴谋，并支持阿联封锁苏伊士运河。
    阿拉伯律师联合会紧急会议是六日在喀土穆开幕的。　（据新华社讯）
