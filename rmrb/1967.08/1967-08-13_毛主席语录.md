	Mao Zedong Quotations
	In places and units where it is necessary to seize power, the revolutionary "three-in-one" policy must be implemented. A temporary power organ with revolutionary, representative, and proletarian authority must be established. The name of this power organ is best called the Revolutionary Committee.

### 毛主席语录

1967-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    在需要夺权的那些地方和单位，必须实行革命的“三结合”的方针，建立一个革命的、有代表性的、有无产阶级权威的临时权力机构。这个权力机构的名称，叫革命委员会好。
