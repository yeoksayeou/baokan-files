Title: China and Somalia Sign Cultural Agreement Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-08-20
Edition: 6

	China and Somalia Sign Cultural Agreement Implementation Plan

	Xinhua News Agency, Mogadishu, August 19th — The Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Somalia, Zheng Peitang, and the Undersecretary of the Somali Ministry of Education, Muhammad Usman Osooble Arde, signed the 1967-1968 implementation plan for the China-Somalia Cultural Agreement on behalf of their respective governments on the morning of the 19th.

	After the signing ceremony, Zheng Peitang and Arde had a friendly and cordial conversation. Both sides emphasized the need to further strengthen friendship between the peoples of the two countries and cultural cooperation between the two countries on the basis of opposing imperialism and colonialism.

### 中索签订文化协定执行计划

1967-08-20
第6版()
专栏：

    中索签订文化协定执行计划
    新华社摩加迪沙十九日电　中国驻索马里大使馆临时代办郑佩堂和索马里教育部国务秘书穆罕默德·奥索布勒·阿德分别代表两国政府，于十九日上午在这里签订了中国—索马里文化协定一九六七——一九六八年度执行计划。
    在签字仪式结束后，郑佩堂和阿德进行了亲切友好的谈话。宾主双方都强调要在反对帝国主义、殖民主义的基础上进一步加强两国人民的友谊和两国之间在文化方面的合作。
