Title: Malian Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-08-10
Edition: 5

A military delegation from the Republic of Mali, led by Captain Diabi Silla Diarra, Governor of Kita Province and Commander of the Military Region, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense.

Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Li Tianyou, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Yuan Ziqin, Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department Zhang Xianyue, and leaders of the Navy and Air Force Zhou Xihan, Chang Qinkun, Deputy Director of the Office of the Ministry of National Defense Pan Zhenwu, as well as representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, officers and soldiers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force, and hundreds of Red Guards from the capital, warmly welcomed the Malian guests at the airport.

Upon disembarking from the plane, the welcoming officers and soldiers and Red Guards loudly recited Chairman Mao's words: "The Chinese people regard the victories of the anti-imperialist struggles of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America as their own victories and give enthusiastic support and sympathy to all their anti-imperialist and anti-colonialist struggles." They then presented the guests with French editions of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and Mao badges. Diabi Silla Diarra and others waved red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" in gratitude. Subsequently, the Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team performed songs and dances praising our great leader Chairman Mao and supporting the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, which were warmly received.

The Ambassador of the Republic of Mali to China, Jisse, and military attachés from various countries' embassies in China also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

The Malian military delegation was warmly welcomed by the leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in Shanghai when they arrived there yesterday.

### 马里军事代表团到达北京

1967-08-10
第5版()
专栏：

    马里军事代表团到达北京
    新华社九日讯　由马里共和国基塔省长兼军区司令迪比·席拉·迪亚拉上尉率领的马里共和国军事代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应我国国防部邀请前来进行友好访问的。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑，总政治部副主任袁子钦，总后勤部副部长张贤约，海、空军负责人周希汉、常乾坤，国防部办公厅副主任潘振武，以及外交部、北京市革命委员会的代表，陆海空三军指战员，首都红卫兵小将数百人到机场热烈欢迎马里客人。
    客人走下飞机后，机场上欢迎的指战员和红卫兵小将高声朗读毛主席语录：“中国人民把亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民的反帝国主义斗争的胜利看作是自己的胜利，并对他们的一切反帝国主义、反殖民主义的斗争给以热烈的同情和支持。”接着，向客人们赠送了法文版《毛主席语录》和毛主席像章。迪比·席拉·迪亚拉等走向欢迎的队伍，不断挥动红彤彤的《毛主席语录》表示谢意。随后，毛泽东思想宣传队为欢迎马里客人演出了歌颂我们伟大领袖毛主席和支持亚非拉人民革命斗争的歌舞，受到热烈欢迎。
    马里共和国驻中国大使吉塞和各国驻中国大使馆武官也到机场迎接。
    马里军事代表团昨天到达上海时，受到了中国人民解放军驻上海部队负责人的热烈欢迎。
