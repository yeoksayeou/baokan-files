Title: The Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) "Vanguard" Says Mao Zedong Thought is the Most Powerful Weapon
Author:
Date: 1967-08-27
Edition: 5

The Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) organ, the "Vanguard," recently once again called on the Australian people to study Chairman Mao's brilliant works, "On Practice" and "On Contradiction."

The "Vanguard" said that insisting on studying these two classic works of Marxism-Leninism will ensure that the people are armed with the most powerful weapon of dialectics. "On Practice" and "On Contradiction" are powerful weapons for solving practical problems.

The "Vanguard" pointed out that workers and laboring people possess knowledge that is directly linked to practice, and thus they are fully capable of mastering these two works.

The "Vanguard" said that on the 30th anniversary of the publication of "On Practice" and "On Contradiction," more Australians will study these two works.

### 澳共（马列）《先锋报》说毛泽东思想是最强大的武器  号召人民学习《实践论》《矛盾论》

1967-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    澳共（马列）《先锋报》说毛泽东思想是最强大的武器
    号召人民学习《实践论》《矛盾论》
    新华社墨尔本二十六日电　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）机关报《先锋报》最近再次号召澳大利亚人民学习毛主席的光辉著作《实践论》和《矛盾论》。
    《先锋报》说，坚持学习这两部马克思列宁主义经典著作，将保证人民用辩证法这个最强大的武器武装起来。《实践论》和《矛盾论》是解决实际问题的强大武器。
    《先锋报》指出，工人和劳动人民具有直接同实践相联系的知识，是完全可以掌握这两部著作的。
    《先锋报》说，在《实践论》和《矛盾论》发表三十周年之际，将有更多的澳大利亚人学习这两部著作。
