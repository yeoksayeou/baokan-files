Title: Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Xie Fuzhi, and Jiang Qing Receive Delegates from the Beijing Garrison's Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works Conference
Author:
Date: 1967-08-08
Edition: 2

Central leaders, members of the Central Military Commission, the Central Cultural Revolution Group, and responsible comrades from various sectors, including Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Xie Fuzhi, Jiang Qing, Liu Ningyi, Su Yu, Guan Feng, Chen Boda, Yao Wenyuan, Ye Qing, today evening received all the comrades attending the Conference of Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works in the Beijing Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

When the central leaders arrived at the reception hall, all the comrades waved red copies of the "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," enthusiastically shouting for a long time: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Chairman Mao is the greatest!" The central leaders waved the red revolutionary classic "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," warmly greeting everyone, congratulating them on their good results in studying Chairman Mao's works, and taking a group photo with everyone, watching the modern revolutionary Beijing opera "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy" performed by the Beijing Opera Theater.

Present at the reception were responsible comrades from the Beijing Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zheng Weishan, Fu Chongbi, and responsible comrades from various headquarters, military branches, and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

On August 7, central leaders, members of the Central Military Commission, the Central Cultural Revolution Group, and responsible comrades from various sectors, including Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Xie Fuzhi, Jiang Qing, received all the comrades attending the Conference of Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works in the Beijing Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The photo shows a group photo taken during the reception. Photo by reporter Lu Xiangyou.

### 周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、李先念、谢富治、江青等同志接见北京卫戍区学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会同志

1967-08-08
第2版()
专栏：

    周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、李先念、谢富治、江青等同志接见北京卫戍区学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会同志
    新华社七日讯　中央领导同志、中央军委、中央文革和各方面的负责同志周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、李先念、谢富治、江青、刘宁一、粟裕、关锋、戚本禹、姚文元、叶群，今天晚上接见了出席中国人民解放军北京卫戍区学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会的全体同志。
    中央领导同志来到接见大厅的时候，全体同志挥动红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，长时间地热烈高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”中央领导同志挥动红色革命宝书《毛主席语录》，向大家亲切致意，祝贺他们在学习毛主席著作方面取得了良好成绩，并和大家一起照了像，观看了中国京剧院演出的现代革命京剧《智取威虎山》。
    接见时在场的，有中国人民解放军北京部队负责同志郑维山、傅崇碧，以及中国人民解放军各总部、各军种、各兵种的负责同志。（附图片）
    八月七日，中央领导同志、中央军委、中央文革和各方面负责同志周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、李先念、谢富治、江青等同志接见了出席中国人民解放军北京卫戍区学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会的全体同志。图为接见时合影。　本报记者　吕相友摄
