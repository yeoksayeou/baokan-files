Title: The Chinese Embassy in Cuba Holds a Reception to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the PLA
Author:
Date: 1967-08-05
Edition: 5

The Chinese Embassy in Cuba held a reception on the evening of August 1 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Guests at the reception included representatives from the Cuban Revolutionary Armed Forces, various sectors of Cuban society, and friends from Latin America and other countries, totaling over 400 people. Diplomatic envoys from several countries to Cuba were also invited to attend the reception.

Some foreign friends who attended the reception expressed their love for Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin and their deep friendship with the Chinese people during their cordial conversations with the embassy staff. They were very excited to wear Chairman Mao's badges and receive copies of Chairman Mao's works.

The reception featured screenings of two Chinese films, "The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought" and "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts." The films were warmly received by the guests. Whenever Chairman Mao appeared on screen, the foreign guests applauded enthusiastically.

### 我驻古巴使馆举行招待会庆祝建军节  在古外国朋友们表现出对毛主席和林副主席的热爱

1967-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻古巴使馆举行招待会庆祝建军节
    在古外国朋友们表现出对毛主席和林副主席的热爱
    新华社哈瓦那三日电　中国驻古巴大使馆八月一日晚间举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年。
    出席招待会的有，古巴革命武装部代表、古巴各界人士和拉丁美洲及其他国家的朋友共四百多人。一些国家驻古巴的外交使节也应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的一些外国朋友在同中国使馆人员亲切交谈中，表现出对毛主席、林副主席的热爱和对中国人民的深厚友谊。他们非常兴奋地在胸前戴上毛主席像章和拿到了毛主席著作。
    招待会上放映了《毛泽东思想的伟大胜利》和《毛主席是我们心中的红太阳》两部中国影片。影片受到了来宾们热烈的欢迎。当毛主席在影片中出现时，到会的外国朋友多次报以热烈的掌声。
