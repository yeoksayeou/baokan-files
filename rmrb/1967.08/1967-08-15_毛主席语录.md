Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-08-15
Edition: 1

The socialist system will inevitably replace the capitalist system. This is an objective law that does not change with the will of individuals. No matter how the reactionaries try to stop the advance of the wheel of history, revolution will inevitably occur, whether sooner or later, and will certainly be victorious.

*From the Speech at the Celebration of the 40th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution by the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union*

### 毛主席语录

1967-08-15

专栏：

    毛主席语录
    社会主义制度终究要代替资本主义制度，这是一个不以人们自己的意志为转移的客观规律。不管反动派怎样企图阻止历史车轮的前进，革命或迟或早总会发生，并且将必然取得胜利。
    《在苏联最高苏维埃庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年会议上的讲话》
