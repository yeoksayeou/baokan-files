Title: Overview of the Situation of African Americans' Struggle Against Violence in the U.S.
Author:
Date: 1967-08-08
Edition: 5

Since the beginning of summer this year, the struggle of African Americans against violence in the U.S. has surged dramatically. In just the two months from early June to early August, the flames of resistance have spread from south to north and from east to west, engulfing more than ninety cities across dozens of states in the U.S.

The right-hand image depicts some of the major cities involved in the African Americans' struggle against violence.
