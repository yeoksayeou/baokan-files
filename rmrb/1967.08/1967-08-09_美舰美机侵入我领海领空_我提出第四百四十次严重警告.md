Title: U.S. Ships and Planes Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-08-09
Edition: 5

On August 6th, from 4:45 AM to 5:28 PM, a U.S. military vessel intruded into our territorial waters east of Baijian Island in Fujian Province. On the same day, from 10:01 AM to 10:18 AM, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding the military provocations by the U.S. military vessel and aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issued the 440th severe warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百四十次严重警告

1967-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百四十次严重警告
    新华社讯　八月六日四时四十五分至十七时二十八分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省白犬列岛以东地区领海。同日十时零一分至十时十八分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百四十次严重警告。
