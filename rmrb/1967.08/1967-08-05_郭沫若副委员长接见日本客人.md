Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-08-05
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo this afternoon met with the delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox) headquarters, led by Ko Kawa Hōtarō, which is on its second visit to China. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1967-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社四日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见日中友协（正统）总部派遣的以古川芳太郎为首的原在华日侨第二次访华代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
