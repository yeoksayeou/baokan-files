Military and civilians in North Vietnam's Quang Binh Province shoot down three U.S. planes

Military and civilians in North Vietnam's Quang Binh Province shot down two U.S. aggressor planes on the 11th and 13th.

It was also reported that the military and civilians in Quang Binh Province shot down one U.S. plane on the 7th.

Zimbabwean guerrillas ambush Rhodesian colonial troops

The chief representative of the Zimbabwe African National Union in Dar es Salaam recently announced in Dar es Salaam that a guerrilla unit of the Zimbabwe African National Union ambushed a Rhodesian colonial troop unit near the Makonde area of Karoi, killing 18 and wounding 8.

Nationalist fighters in South Yemen fiercely attack British colonial forces

Nationalist fighters in occupied South Yemen recently attacked the headquarters of the British occupying forces' first battalion in the Krat area on the outskirts of Aden with mortars.

Earlier, nationalist fighters also shelled the telephone exchange operated by British colonialists in the Sheikh Osman area, causing the exchange to be out of service for two days.

### 简明新闻

1967-08-18
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南北方广平省军民击落美机三架
    越南北方广平省军民十一日和十三日打落了两架美国强盗飞机。
    另据补充报道，广平省军民在七日还打落一架美国飞机。
    津巴布韦游击队伏击南罗殖民军
    津巴布韦非洲民族联盟驻达累斯萨拉姆首席代表最近在达累斯萨拉姆发表战报宣布，津巴布韦非洲民族联盟的一支游击队，不久前在卡罗伊附近的马贡杰地区伏击了一队南罗得西亚殖民军，打死十八人，打伤八人。
    南也门民族主义战士痛打英殖民军
    被占领的南也门民族主义者最近用迫击炮袭击了亚丁城郊的克拉特区英国占领军第一营营部。
    早些时候，民族主义战士还炮轰了舍赫奥斯曼区英国殖民者经营的电话局，使电话局的业务中断了两天。
                            （据新华社讯）
