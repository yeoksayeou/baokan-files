Title: The Military Program of the Proletarian Revolution
Author: Lie Ning
Date: 1967-08-26
Edition: 3

The use of armed force by the bourgeoisie to suppress the proletariat is one of the most serious, fundamental, and important facts of modern capitalist society. In the face of this reality, some people advise revolutionary Social Democrats to propose the "demand" to "abolish armed forces"! This is equivalent to completely abandoning the class struggle perspective and all revolutionary ideas.
