Title: Striking the Enemy from the Demilitarized Zone South to the Mekong Delta
Author:
Date: 1967-08-29
Edition: 5

The Liberation Armed Forces of South Vietnam have launched fierce offensives across the region, from south of the Demilitarized Zone to the Mekong Delta, striking at key military bases and cities occupied by the U.S. and puppet forces, achieving great victories.

According to Western news agencies, on August 28, the Liberation Armed Forces twice attacked the U.S. military base at Dong Ha south of the Demilitarized Zone, killing and wounding over 100 U.S. Marine Corps personnel, destroying five helicopters and two large transport planes.

On the same day, the Liberation Armed Forces heavily shelled the U.S. helicopter base near Danang, destroying ten helicopters and damaging seven to eight others, killing and wounding over 80 U.S. aggressors, and severely damaging six U.S. military barracks. On August 27, the Liberation Armed Forces also attacked two U.S. positions and five puppet outposts near Danang.

On the same day, August 27, the Liberation Armed Forces launched a fierce assault on the provincial capital of Quang Nam, Hue City. In the intense battle, the Liberation Armed Forces annihilated a puppet engineering battalion, captured the battalion commander, and destroyed three heavy artillery pieces and a fuel depot. On that day, the Liberation Armed Forces also attacked Pleiku in the Central Highlands and Can Tho in the Mekong Delta. According to Western news agencies, the U.S. and puppet forces suffered at least 500 casualties on August 27.

According to a report from the Vietnam News Agency in Hanoi on August 26, the Liberation Armed Forces in Quang Nam Province, South Vietnam, achieved a great victory from August 11 to 13, annihilating two U.S. companies, destroying one puppet battalion and severely damaging another, shooting down eight enemy aircraft, and destroying five artillery pieces.

The U.S. and puppet forces launched a large-scale "sweep" on August 11, mobilizing several puppet infantry and armored battalions to attack the tri-junction area of Tam Ky, Thang Binh, and Thuan Nam counties in Quang Nam Province. The Liberation Armed Forces bravely counterattacked on August 11 and 12, routing the enemy and then pursuing the fleeing remnants, directly assaulting the command post of an enemy regiment, scattering the enemy. On August 13, the Liberation Armed Forces attacked other positions of this regiment.

According to preliminary reports, the Liberation Armed Forces eliminated over 450 puppet soldiers in the Phu River and Phu Mountain areas in these three days of fighting, including annihilating one commando battalion and severely damaging another, shooting down five aircraft. Additionally, in the battle to attack a U.S. artillery position in the Phu Hiep area and a base of the 101st Airborne Division, the Liberation Armed Forces annihilated a U.S. paratrooper company and an artillery company, shot down three helicopters, and destroyed five 105mm artillery pieces.

### 从非军事区以南到湄公河三角洲到处打击敌人  南越解放武装力量在各地发动猛烈攻势  广南省解放军三天全歼美军两个连歼灭伪军一个营

1967-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    从非军事区以南到湄公河三角洲到处打击敌人
    南越解放武装力量在各地发动猛烈攻势
    广南省解放军三天全歼美军两个连歼灭伪军一个营
    据新华社二十八日讯  西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量连日来从非军事区以南到湄公河三角洲，向美伪军的重要军事基地和它们占据的重要城市发动一系列猛烈的袭击，获得了巨大的胜利。
    据西方通讯社报道，二十八日，解放武装力量两次袭击非军事区以南的美军东河基地，打死打伤美国海军陆战队官兵一百多名，击毁直升飞机五架和巨型运输机两架。
    同一天，解放武装力量用炮火猛轰岘港附近的诺曼美军直升飞机基地，击毁直升飞机十架，击伤七架到八架，打死打伤美国强盗八十多名，六座美军营房遭到严重破坏。二十七日，解放武装力量还袭击了岘港附近的两个美军阵地和五个伪军哨所。
    在二十七日的同一天内，解放武装力量猛烈袭击并且一度攻进了广南省的省会会安市。在激烈的战斗中，解放武装力量打垮了一个伪军工兵营，活捉了伪军营长，击毁了伪军的三门重炮和一个燃料库。这一天，解放武装力量还袭击了中部高原的波来古和湄公河三角洲上的芹苴市。据西方通讯社透露，美伪军在二十七日这一天内至少伤亡五百人。
    据新华社河内二十六日电　据越南南方解放电台广播，越南南方广南省解放武装力量在八月十一日到十三日三天中，取得了全歼美军两个连，歼灭伪军一个营和重创一个营，击落敌机八架，击毁大炮五门的巨大战果。
    美伪集团在八月十一日，纠集了好几个伪军步兵营和装甲营向广南省三岐、升平和先福县的三角地区发动大规模“扫荡”。解放武装力量在十一日和十二日两天中英勇地连续进行截击，把敌人打得一败涂地，并且在乘胜追击溃逃的残敌时，直捣敌军一个战团的指挥所，把敌人打得七零八落。十三日，解放武装力量又袭击了这个战团其他单位的据点。
    据初步消息，解放武装力量在这三天战斗中，仅在福河和福山地区就消灭伪军四百五十多名，其中包括歼灭一个别动营和重创一个别动营，击落飞机五架。另外在袭击福协地区的美军一个炮兵阵地和属于一○一空降师一个单位驻地的战斗中，全歼美军一个伞兵连和一个炮兵连，击落直升飞机三架，击毁一○五大炮五门。
