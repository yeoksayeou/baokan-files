Title: Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-08-19
Edition: 1

A large number of young revolutionaries who were previously unknown have become brave pioneers. They are bold and wise. They use big-character posters and great debates, speaking out loudly, exposing, and criticizing, launching a resolute attack on those open and hidden representatives of the bourgeoisie.
