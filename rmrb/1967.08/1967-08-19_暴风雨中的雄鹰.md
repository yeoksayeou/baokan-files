Title: Eagles in the Storm
Author: Zewat Shqepba
Date: 1967-08-19
Edition: 5

The Yangtze River's waves surge,
The Himalayas smile joyfully.
People from all five continents sing loudly,
Celebrating the great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China.

Wish long life to the great leader Chairman Mao,
Salute to the heroic Red Guards!

Red Guards, you are brave pioneers,
The vanguard of the Cultural Revolution;
You are the outstanding children of the working people,
The model of revolutionary youth in the 20th century.

You fire fiercely at the old world,
Your torch illuminates the entire world in red.
Ghosts and monsters are defeated by you,
Imperialism and revisionism tremble in fear.

A little red book lights your path forward,
Strive for the glorious cause of communism all your life.

Ah! Red Guards, dear comrades,
Salute to you!

We are the children of the homeland nurtured by the Party of Labor,
We bear the burden of revolution.
We are tempered in the storm,
We will fight to the death with the enemy,
With one hand holding a gun, the other a pickaxe.

Our slogan is eternal revolution,
Our communication station is in the deep mountains and dense forests.

Building railways, terraced fields,
Constructing the grand edifice of socialism relies on our hard work,
We are the heroes of the new era.

The hearts of Albanian and Chinese youth beat as one,
Our blood boils together.

Let us forever fight side by side,
Become eagles in the storm together!

Long live the great friendship between Albania and China!
Long live the great leader Enver!
Long live the great leader Mao Zedong!

### 暴风雨中的雄鹰
泽瓦特·什巴赫
1967-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    暴风雨中的雄鹰
    阿尔巴尼亚　泽瓦特·什巴赫
    扬子江波涛汹涌，
    喜马拉雅山喜笑盈盈。
    五洲四海纵情歌唱啊，
    欢呼中国伟大的无产阶级文化大革命。
    祝福伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆，
    向英雄的红卫兵战友致敬！
    红卫兵，你们是勇敢的闯将，
    文化大革命的开路先锋；
    你们是劳动人民的优秀儿女，
    二十世纪革命青年的标兵。
    你们向旧世界猛烈开火，
    你们的火炬把全世界照得通红。
    牛鬼蛇神被你们打得威风扫地，
    帝国主义、修正主义吓得胆战心惊。
    一本红书照亮了你们前进的道路，
    为共产主义的壮丽事业奋斗终生。
    啊！红卫兵，亲爱的战友，
    向你们致敬！
    我们是劳动党抚育的祖国儿女，
    革命的重担我们担承。
    我们在风暴中锻炼成长，
    我们要与敌人作拚死的斗争，
    一手拿枪，一手拿镐。
    我们的口号是永远革命，
    我们的通讯处是深山密林。
    修铁路，开梯田，
    建立社会主义大厦全靠我们辛勤劳动，
    我们是新时代的英雄。
    阿中两国青年心连心，
    我们的热血在一起沸腾。
    让我们永远并肩战斗，
    共作暴风雨中的雄鹰！
    伟大的阿中友谊万岁！
    万岁！伟大的领袖恩维尔，
    万岁！伟大的领袖毛泽东！
