Title: To Walk the Socialist Road, or the Capitalist Road?
Author:
Date: 1967-08-20
Edition: 2

The article "To Walk the Socialist Road, or the Capitalist Road?" by the editorial departments of Red Flag Magazine and People's Daily has been published in a single-volume edition by the People's Publishing House. Starting from the 21st, it will be distributed in Beijing and will gradually be distributed across the country.

### 《走社会主义道路，还是走资本主义道路？》单行本出版

1967-08-20
第2版()
专栏：

    《走社会主义道路，还是走资本主义道路？》单行本出版
    新华社十九日讯　红旗杂志编辑部、人民日报编辑部的文章《走社会主义道路，还是走资本主义道路？》已由人民出版社出版单行本，二十一日起在北京发行，并将在全国各地陆续发行。
