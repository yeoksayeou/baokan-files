Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-08-24
Edition: 1

In the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, we must hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and implement the leadership of proletarian politics. We must carry out the campaign of studying and applying Chairman Mao’s works among the broad masses of workers, peasants, cadres, and intellectuals, and use Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle for the cultural revolution.

### 毛主席语录

1967-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    在无产阶级文化大革命中，要高举毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，实行无产阶级政治挂帅。要在广大工农兵、广大干部和广大知识分子中，开展活学活用毛主席著作的运动，把毛泽东思想作为文化革命的行动指南。
      《中国共产党中央委员会关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定》
