Title: Prince Sihanouk Cables Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1967-08-24
Edition: 5

Prince Sihanouk of Cambodia cabled Premier Zhou Enlai to express his heartfelt thanks for the warm reception given to Cambodia's Foreign Minister, Prince Norodom Phurissara, during his visit to China.

The cable said: "The steadfast support given by our Chinese brothers to our struggle to maintain the independence, neutrality, and territorial integrity of our country, which is threatened by U.S. imperialism and its lackeys, has greatly encouraged all the people of our country and myself.

"I am pleased to reaffirm our confidence in the solidarity and friendship between Cambodia and China."

### 西哈努克亲王致电周恩来总理  感谢对柬埔寨外交大臣的热诚接待

1967-08-24
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王致电周恩来总理
    感谢对柬埔寨外交大臣的热诚接待
    新华社金边二十三日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二十三日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王打电报给周恩来总理，对柬埔寨外交大臣富里萨拉在中国访问期间受到的热诚接待表示衷心的感谢。
    电报说：“中国兄弟对我们为维护受到美帝及其走狗威胁的我国独立、中立和领土完整而进行的斗争所给予的坚定不移的支持，使我国全体同胞和我本人受到莫大的鼓舞。
    “我高兴地向你重申我们对柬中两国的团结和友谊满怀信心。”
