Title: Four Heroes on the "Ten Brave Warriors Hill" in Quang Tri Province, South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-08-20
Edition: 6

Four heroic soldiers on the "Ten Brave Warriors Hill" in Quang Tri Province, South Vietnam, are pointing at the guns seized from the American aggressors, laughing and discussing the feeble nature of American imperialism's paper tiger.
