Title: China Signs Economic Aid Agreement with Mali
Author:
Date: 1967-08-15
Edition: 5

The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Mali signed an agreement on economic aid today in the afternoon.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the signing ceremony.

Signing on behalf of the two governments were: Minister of the State Planning Commission, Fang Yi, and the head of the Mali government delegation, Sedou Badian Kouyate.

Also present at the signing ceremony were relevant officials from our country, all members of the Mali government delegation, and the Mali Ambassador to China, Gise.

### 中国向马里提供经济援助协定签字  周恩来总理等出席签字仪式

1967-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    中国向马里提供经济援助协定签字
    周恩来总理等出席签字仪式
    新华社十四日讯　中华人民共和国政府和马里共和国政府今天下午签订了关于中国政府向马里政府提供经济援助的协定。
    周恩来总理、陈毅副总理参加了签字仪式。
    代表双方政府在协定上签字的是：对外经委主任方毅、马里政府代表团团长塞杜·巴迪安·库亚特。
    参加签字仪式的还有我国有关方面负责人，马里政府代表团全体团员和马里驻中国大使吉塞。
