Title: Romanian Ambassador Hosts National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-08-24
Edition: 5

Romanian Ambassador to China Orel Duma hosted a reception tonight to celebrate Romania's National Day.

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi, and Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi, along with other relevant officials, were invited to attend the reception. The event was held in a friendly atmosphere.

An exhibition of Romanian industrial development, organized by the China International Cultural Exchange Association and the China-Romania Friendship Association, opened today in Beijing.

### 罗马尼亚驻华大使举行国庆酒会  董必武李先念谢富治刘宁一等应邀出席

1967-08-24
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚驻华大使举行国庆酒会
    董必武李先念谢富治刘宁一等应邀出席
    新华社二十三日讯　罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马今晚举行酒会，庆祝罗马尼亚国庆日。
    董必武副主席、李先念副总理、谢富治副总理、刘宁一副委员长，和各有关方面的负责人，应邀出席了酒会。酒会在友好的气氛中进行。
    由对外文委和中罗友协主办的罗马尼亚工业发展图片展览，今天在北京开幕。
