Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-08-01
Edition: 10

Premier Zhou received the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation this afternoon. The delegation, led by Vice Premier Le Thanh Nghi of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, included Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Ban, Deputy Minister and Vietnamese Ambassador to China Vu Minh Quan, and other members. Premier Zhou had a very cordial conversation with the Vietnamese comrades.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian and relevant officials Fang Yi, Peng Shao Hui, Li Qiang, and Han Nianlong accompanied the reception. (Photo attached)

On July 31, Premier Zhou received the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation led by Vice Premier Le Thanh Nghi of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam (front left four), Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Ban (front left six), Vietnamese Ambassador to China Vu Minh Quan (front left two), and other members of the delegation.  Photographer: Wang Dong, reporter for this newspaper.

### 周总理接见越政府经济代表团

1967-08-01
第10版()
专栏：

    周总理接见越政府经济代表团
    新华社三十一日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见越南政府经济代表团团长、越南民主共和国副总理黎清毅，副团长、外贸部副部长李班，副团长、越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾和代表团成员。周恩来总理和越南同志进行了十分亲切的谈话。
    李先念副总理和有关方面负责人方毅、彭绍辉、李强、韩念龙陪同接见。（附图片）
  七月三十一日，周恩来总理接见越南政府经济代表团团长、越南民主共和国副总理黎清毅（前左四），副团长、外贸部副部长李班（前左六），副团长、越南驻中国大使吴明鸾（前左二），以及由他们率领的越南政府经济代表团成员。　　　本报记者　王东摄
