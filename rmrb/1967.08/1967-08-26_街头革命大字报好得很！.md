Title: Street Revolution Big-Character Posters Are Great!
Author: 
Date: 1967-08-26
Edition: 3

Street revolution big-character posters are great!

Chairman Mao teaches us: "Big-character posters are an extremely useful new-style weapon."

Street revolution big-character posters are great. Street revolution big-character posters are an innovation of the proletarian revolutionary faction, serving as the frontline trenches in the resolute struggle against all kinds of bourgeois ideology.

Street revolution big-character posters are great. Each revolutionary big-character poster is like a shell, a dagger, concentrating fire on the bourgeois headquarters, exposing the counter-revolutionary restoration plot of China's Khrushchev. The proletarian revolutionary faction wields its pen, trampling on the former nobles.

Street revolution big-character posters are great. Reading one revolutionary big-character poster after another is like attending class after class of political education. The proletarian revolutionary faction uses revolutionary big-character posters to plant the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought throughout the streets and alleys of the city.

Street revolution big-character posters are great. The proletarian revolutionary faction writes big-character posters, protects big-character posters, and launches a mass movement, waging a resounding "people's war."

Street revolution big-character posters are great. Each revolutionary big-character poster is solid in content, sharp in penmanship, clear in stance, concise and powerful, and captivating. Those empty, verbose, off-topic, and lazy articles are swept away. Those articles filled with eight-legged essays and bad writing styles are also swept away. The style of writing that seeks to entertain the petty bourgeoisie will also be swept away. Street revolution big-character posters must truly become "a powerful weapon to unite the people, educate the people, attack the enemy, and annihilate the enemy, helping the people to unite and fight against the enemy."

In street revolution big-character posters, the brilliant flowers of our proletariat shine.

To welcome the new upsurge of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution nationwide, we, the proletarian revolutionary faction, must mobilize the masses more broadly to engage in revolutionary criticism and write more street revolution big-character posters.

We, the proletarian revolutionary faction, must mobilize more broadly to protect street revolution big-character posters.

We, the proletarian revolutionary faction, must mobilize more broadly to raise the level of Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought in street big-character posters.

Turn every street and alley into a stronghold of Mao Zedong Thought, and let the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought be planted throughout Shanghai and throughout China.

Turn every street and alley into an ocean to smash the bourgeois headquarters, and let China's Khrushchev and his agents in Shanghai be drowned in the ocean of the "people's war"!

### 街头革命大字报好得很！
上海《工人造反报》短评
1967-08-26
第3版()
专栏：

    街头革命大字报好得很！
    上海《工人造反报》短评
    毛主席教导我们：“大字报是一种极其有用的新式武器”。
    街头革命大字报好得很。街头革命大字报，是无产阶级革命派的创举，是与形形色色资产阶级思想作坚决斗争的前哨阵地。
    街头革命大字报好得很。一张张革命大字报，就是一颗颗炮弹、一把把匕首，集中目标，万炮猛轰资产阶级司令部，戳穿中国赫鲁晓夫的反革命复辟阴谋。无产阶级革命派激扬文字，粪土当年万户侯。
    街头革命大字报好得很。看一张张革命大字报，就是上一堂又一堂的政治课。无产阶级革命派用革命大字报，把毛泽东思想伟大红旗插遍全市的街头巷尾。
    街头革命大字报好得很。无产阶级革命派，人人大写革命大字报，人人保护革命大字报，大搞群众运动，打一场轰轰烈烈的“人民战争”。
    街头革命大字报好得很。一张张革命大字报，内容扎实，笔锋锐利，旗帜鲜明，短小精悍，引人入胜。那种言之无物，下笔千言，离题万里，懒婆娘的裹脚布式的臭文章，被一扫而空了。那种甲乙丙丁，ＡＢＣＤ，八股味十足的臭文风，也被一扫而空了。那种追求“噱、噱、噱”的迎合小市民的新闻作风，也必将被一扫而空。街头革命大字报要真正成为“团结人民、教育人民、打击敌人、消灭敌人的有力的武器，帮助人民同心同德地和敌人作斗争。”
    在街头革命大字报中，闪耀着我们无产阶级的灿烂的花，思想的花。
    为了迎接全国无产阶级文化大革命的新高涨，我们无产阶级革命派要更广泛地发动群众投入革命的大批判，大写街头革命大字报。
    我们无产阶级革命派要更广泛地发动起来，保护街头革命大字报。
    我们无产阶级革命派要更广泛地发动起来，提高街头大字报的马列主义、毛泽东思想水平。
    把街头巷尾办成毛泽东思想的坚强阵地，让毛泽东思想伟大红旗插遍全上海，插遍全中国。
    把街头巷尾变成摧毁资产阶级司令部的汪洋大海，让中国的赫鲁晓夫及其在上海的代理人淹没在“人民战争”的汪洋大海中吧！
