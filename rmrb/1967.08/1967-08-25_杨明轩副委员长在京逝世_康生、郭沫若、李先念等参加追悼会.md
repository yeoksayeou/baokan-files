Title: Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman, Passes Away in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-08-25
Edition: 2

Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, passed away in Beijing on August 22, 1967, due to illness. He was 77 years old.

The funeral for Vice Chairman Yang Mingxuan was held on the morning of August 24 at Babaoshan.

Attendees at the funeral included Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Kang Sheng, Guo Moruo, Liu Ningyi, Apho Angjinmei, and Zhou Jianren; Vice Premier Li Xiannian; Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Gao Chongmin, Cai Tingkai, Li Siguang, Fu Zuoyi, Xu Deheng, and Li Dequan. Also present were members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress in Beijing, leaders of various democratic parties, and Yang Mingxuan's family and friends.

### 杨明轩副委员长在京逝世  康生、郭沫若、李先念等参加追悼会

1967-08-25
第2版()
专栏：

    杨明轩副委员长在京逝世
    康生、郭沫若、李先念等参加追悼会
    新华社二十四日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员、中国民主同盟中央委员会主席杨明轩，因病医治无效，于一九六七年八月二十二日在北京逝世，年七十七岁。
    杨明轩副委员长追悼会，于八月二十四日上午在八宝山举行。
    参加追悼会的有全国人大常委副委员长康生、郭沫若、刘宁一、阿沛·阿旺晋美、周建人，国务院副总理李先念，政协全国委员会副主席高崇民、蔡廷锴、李四光、傅作义、许德珩、李德全，参加追悼会的还有在京的人大常委委员，各民主党派的负责人，以及杨明轩副委员长的家属和生前友好。（附图片）
