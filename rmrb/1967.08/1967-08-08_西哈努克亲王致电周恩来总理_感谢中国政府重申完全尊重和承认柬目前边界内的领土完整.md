Title: Prince Sihanouk Cables Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1967-08-08
Edition: 6

	Prince Sihanouk Cables Premier Zhou Enlai
	Thanks China for Reaffirming Respect for Cambodia's Territorial Integrity

	According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on August 6, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk cabled Premier Zhou Enlai, expressing gratitude for the Chinese government's consistent stance of fully respecting and recognizing Cambodia's territorial integrity within its current borders.

	The cable said: "The Royal Government and people of the Kingdom of Cambodia warmly welcome the statement by the Government of the People's Republic of China reaffirming its full respect and recognition of Cambodia's territorial integrity within its current borders. Please allow me to express our deep gratitude to you, to the Chinese government, and to the great Chinese people."

	The cable said: "At a time when U.S. imperialism and its accomplices are threatening and plotting to undermine Cambodia's territorial integrity, your support shows that the People's Republic of China is our good friend and the friend of all countries struggling for rights and justice in the world."

	The cable said: "We pay tribute to the stance taken by China, which has always respected our independence and sovereignty, and once again express our heartfelt admiration for the Chinese people, who are guided by the thoughts of Chairman Mao Zedong and bring great inspiration to the peoples of all countries fighting against imperialism. I am very pleased to take this opportunity to reaffirm our trust and complete solidarity with you."

### 西哈努克亲王致电周恩来总理  感谢中国政府重申完全尊重和承认柬目前边界内的领土完整

1967-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王致电周恩来总理
    感谢中国政府重申完全尊重和承认柬目前边界内的领土完整
    新华社金边六日电　据柬埔寨新闻社六日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王打电报给周恩来总理，对中国政府重申完全尊重和承认柬埔寨在目前边界内的领土完整的一贯立场表示感谢。
    电报说：“柬埔寨王国政府和人民热烈欢迎中华人民共和国政府关于重申完全尊重和承认柬埔寨在目前边界内的领土完整的声明。请允许我向阁下，向中国政府和伟大的中国人民表示我们的深切的感激。”
    电报说：“当美帝国主义及其同谋者威胁和阴谋破坏柬埔寨的领土完整的时候，你们的这一支持表明，中华人民共和国是我们的好朋友，也是世界上所有那些为争取权利和正义而斗争的国家的朋友。”
    电报说：“我们向一贯尊重我国独立和主权的中国所采取的立场表示敬意，向用毛泽东主席思想指导的并给反对帝国主义的各国人民带来巨大鼓舞的中国人民再次表示我们衷心的钦佩。我很高兴借此机会向你重申我们的信任和完全的团结。”
