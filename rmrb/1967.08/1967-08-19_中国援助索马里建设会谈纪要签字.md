Title: Memorandum of Consultations on China's Aid to Somalia's Construction Signed
Author:
Date: 1967-08-19
Edition: 5

According to Xinhua News Agency, Mogadishu, August 17th: The memorandum of consultations on China's aid to Somalia in the construction of rice and tobacco experimental stations was signed on the 16th by Zheng Peitang, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Somalia, and Ali Ali O. Mohamed Barre, Minister of Agriculture and Livestock of Somalia, in Mogadishu.

Present at the signing were Yu Shulin, Deputy Leader of the Chinese Agricultural Expert Group, and Abdul Rahman Amir, Director of the Office of the Somali Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock.

After the signing, Zheng Peitang, Chargé d'Affaires, introduced to the Somali Minister the great achievements made by the Chinese people in socialist construction under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao. The Somali Minister praised the great thought of Mao Zedong and expressed that Somalia would also develop agriculture in the spirit of self-reliance to solve the problem of food self-sufficiency as soon as possible.

### 中国援助索马里建设会谈纪要签字

1967-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    中国援助索马里建设会谈纪要签字
    据新华社摩加迪沙十七日电　中国援助索马里建设水稻和烟草试验站的会谈纪要十六日由中国驻索马里大使馆临时代办郑佩堂和索马里农业、畜牧业部长阿里·阿里奥·穆罕默德·巴雷在摩加迪沙签字。
    签字时在座的有中国农业专家组副组长游述麟和索马里农业、畜牧部的办公厅主任阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·阿米尔。
    签字后，郑佩堂临时代办向索马里部长介绍了在伟大领袖毛主席领导下的中国人民在社会主义建设中取得的巨大成就。索马里部长盛赞伟大的毛泽东思想，并表示索马里也要本着自力更生的精神发展农业，以便尽快解决粮食自给的问题。
