Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-08-17
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Honorary President of the China-Japan Friendship Association Guo Moruo today received the delegation of the Japan Youth Friendship Visit and Observation Group led by the delegation head Yamada Yuichi, deputy delegation head Tanmura Shinji, and Kayama Hiroshi.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo had a cordial conversation with the Japanese youth.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Honorary President of the China-Japan Friendship Association Guo Moruo today received the delegation of the Japan Teachers and Staff Visit and Observation Group led by the delegation head Ohiro Naoshi and deputy delegation head Takemoto Yoshimi.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo had a friendly conversation with the Japanese friends.

The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 11th of this month, visited Jinan on the 14th, and returned to Beijing this afternoon.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1967-08-17
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社十五日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中日友协名誉会长郭沫若今天接见了由团长山田雄一、副团长田村信子、影山博邦率领的日本学生访华友好参观团。郭沫若副委员长同日本青年进行了亲切的谈话。
    新华社十六日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中日友协名誉会长郭沫若今天接见了由团长大广直、副团长竹本良美率领的日本教职员访华参观团。郭沫若副委员长同日本朋友进行了友好的谈话。
    参观团是本月十一日到达北京的，十四日到济南参观访问，于今天下午回到北京。
