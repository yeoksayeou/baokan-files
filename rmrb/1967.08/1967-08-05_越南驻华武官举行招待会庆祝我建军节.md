Title: Vietnamese Military Attaché Holds Reception to Celebrate China's Army Day
Author:
Date: 1967-08-05
Edition: 5

Vietnamese Military Attaché Holds Reception to Celebrate China's Army Day

New China News Agency, August 3rd: Colonel Chen Wenpeng, the military attaché of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's embassy in China, held a reception on July 29th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Relevant officials from the General Staff Department and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army were invited to attend the reception.

Lý Sông Sơn, deputy head of the permanent delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, was also invited to attend the reception.

Ambassador Ngô Minh Loạn of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China was also present.

After the reception, films from both China and Vietnam were screened.

### 越南驻华武官举行招待会庆祝我建军节

1967-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    越南驻华武官举行招待会庆祝我建军节
    新华社三日讯　越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆武官陈文彭上校七月二十九日举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年。
    中国人民解放军总参谋部、总政治部等有关方面负责人应邀出席招待会。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长黎松山也应邀出席招待会。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾也在座。
    招待会后，放映了中越两国影片。
