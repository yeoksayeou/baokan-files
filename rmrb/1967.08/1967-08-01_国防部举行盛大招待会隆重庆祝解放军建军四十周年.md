Title: Ministry of National Defense Holds Grand Reception to Celebrate the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the PLA
Author:
Date: 1967-08-01
Edition: 10

The Ministry of National Defense held a grand reception at the Great Hall of the People to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Leaders from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, the Central Cultural Revolution Group, the Military Cultural Revolution Group, and the PLA accompanied foreign revolutionary comrades and anti-imperialist fighters from various countries who were in Beijing to attend the reception.

The following attended the reception:

Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China: Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Zhu De, Li Fuchun;

First Deputy Director of the Central Cultural Revolution Group Jiang Qing, Deputy Directors Zhang Chunqiao, and members Wang Li, Guan Feng, Chen Boda, Yao Wenyuan;

Members and alternate members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Secretaries of the Secretariat: Chen Yi, Liu Bocheng, Li Xiannian, Tan Zhenlin, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Li Xuefeng, Song Renqiong, Xie Fuzhi, Liu Ningyi;

Members of the Central Military Commission, the Military Cultural Revolution Group, the Ministry of National Defense, the PLA General Staff, the Navy, and the Air Force: Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, Su Yu, Ye Qing, Xiao Jinguang, Wang Shusheng, Li Zuopeng, Wu Faxian, Yu Lijin, Qiu Huizuo, Zhang Chiming;

Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the National Defense Council: Guo Moruo, Peng Dehuai, Zhang Zhizhong, Zhou Jianren, Fu Zuoyi, Cai Tingkai.

Guests of honor included:

Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Vice Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Head of the Economic Delegation Le Quoc Vui; Albanian Ambassador to China Natajelli and his wife; Deputy Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China Le Songshan; First Deputy Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Burma, Head of the Burmese Communist Party Delegation De Zhen Badengding; Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Writers' Association Senanayak and his wife; Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Liaison Committee Saikuni Kuniichi.

Tonight, the banquet hall of the Great Hall of the People was filled with a revolutionary and combative atmosphere. In the center of the stage, a large portrait of our most beloved and great leader Chairman Mao hung high. During the reception, PLA soldiers, revolutionary masses from various sectors, and Red Guards repeatedly approached Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and other comrades, raising their glasses to toast to the long life of our red sun, Chairman Mao! At this moment, the hall resonated with shouts of "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao! Chairman Mao is great! Chairman Mao is the greatest!"

Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the PLA, Comrade Yang Chengwu, spoke at the reception. He said, "Forty years ago today, the Communist Party of China led the armed uprising in Nanchang. This was after the Kuomintang had completely betrayed the revolution, under the condition that the correct line represented by Chairman Mao had defeated the right-opportunist line of Chen Duxiu, and under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought. This was the first shot fired at the Kuomintang reactionaries. Chairman Mao, based on the peasant movement in Hunan, personally initiated and led the great Autumn Harvest Uprising and the great march to Jinggangshan, establishing the first workers' and peasants' Red Army and the first revolutionary base area. This opened a new path for the Chinese revolution and the world revolution, that is, the path of encircling the cities from the countryside and ultimately seizing the cities and the country. This is a path of victory. This is a great feat in the history of proletarian revolution."

Comrade Yang Chengwu pointed out, "The Chinese People's Liberation Army is a new type of people's army personally created and nurtured by our great commander-in-chief Chairman Mao, armed with the great and invincible Mao Zedong Thought. This army, in the long and arduous struggle against domestic and foreign class enemies and in the struggle between the two lines, has always firmly stood on the side of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line. It is a truly proletarian revolutionary army that is always loyal to Chairman Mao's revolutionary line."

He emphasized, "Chairman Mao is the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, the greatest genius, and the most outstanding commander. Mao Zedong Thought is a new stage in the development of Marxism, and it is the guiding principle for all our work. Chairman Mao's military thought is the most complete, most scientific, and greatest revolutionary military theory in the history of Marxism, and it is the highest peak of contemporary Marxist-Leninist military theory. Every victory of our army is a great victory of Chairman Mao's revolutionary line and a great victory of Mao Zedong Thought. As Comrade Lin Biao said: 'The people's army built according to the military thought of Comrade Mao Zedong is invincible.'"

Comrade Yang Chengwu said, "Our deputy commander-in-chief Comrade Lin Biao has held the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought highest, studied Chairman Mao's works best, and used them best. For decades, Comrade Lin Biao has consistently, resolutely, and thoroughly implemented Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line. Comrade Lin Biao creatively applied Mao Zedong Thought, led our army to study and apply Chairman Mao's works, emphasized proletarian politics, and turned our army into a great school of Mao Zedong Thought, making outstanding contributions to the revolutionary and modernized construction of our army. Having the wise leadership of our great commander-in-chief Chairman Mao and the direct command of Comrade Lin Biao is our greatest happiness!"

Those attending the reception also included:

Major leaders from some military regions in Beijing: Chen Xilian, Zheng Weishan, Liu Geping, Yang Dezhi, Han Xianchu, Liu Peishan, Huang Yongsheng, Wei Guoqing, Xian Henghan;

Leaders from various departments of the PLA, the National Defense Science and Technology Commission, various military branches, military academies, and military control representatives, as well as revolutionary cadres from the PLA leadership, active learners of Chairman Mao's works from various units, "Four Good" units, "Five Good" soldiers, heroes and models, those who contributed to the atomic and hydrogen bomb tests, and representatives who made contributions to the "Three Supports, Two Militaries" work: Li Tianyou, Wang Xinting, Xu Licheng, Yuan Ziqin, Liu Xiyuan, Zhang Lingbin, Zhang Xianyue, Zhang Tianyun, Wang Hongkun, Zhao Qimin, Li Tianhuan, Chen Xianrui, Fu Congbi, Qian Renong, Wang Huijiu, Cao Lihuai, Chen Renqing, Wu Lie, Huang Zhiyong, Chen Shipeng, Luo Huasheng, Zhong Chibing, Luo Xunchu, Liu Huaqing, Cai Shungui, Chen Qihuan, Mo Wenhuai, Xiang Zhonghua, Jiang Wen, Zhang Xiuquan, Huang Zuozhen, Liu Shaowen, Luo Yuanfa, Liu Jiping, Xiao Wenjiu, Huang Zhengtang, Huang Wenming, Li Zhen, Shen Yuanquan, Liao Chujian, Nian Siyang, Qiao Xizeng, Zhang Fengming, Wang Zhushu, Shu Jicheng, Song Yimin, etc.

Representatives from various relevant departments and organizations: Wang Dongxing, Wang Zhen, Zeng Shan, Fang Yi, Zhang Zaiyi, Chu Tuonan, Yang Qiqing, Li Zhen, Yan Youmin, Li Qiang, Han Nianlong, Xu Yixin, Zheng Hantao, Lü Shiying, Liu Wei, Chen Huatang, Zhao Shaochang, He Guanghua, Qi Yiding, Hu Lanzi, Chen Ruiting, Zhang Yuanzhi, Bianjiang, Yan Kuyao, Gu Guangshan, Qian Xuesen, Zhu Guangya, Ren Xinmin, Tang Pingzhu, Zhao Yiya, Wang Weizhen, Jin Jingmai, Li Yingru, Lu Gongda, Liu Jucheng, Lin Jie, Zhong Ying, Kang Shujie.

Leaders from the Beijing Revolutionary Committee and the Revolutionary Committees of Workers, Peasants, Higher Education Institutions, and Middle Schools: Wu De, Liu Jianxun, Nie Yuanzi, Ding Guanyi, Zhou Jingfang, Kuai Dafu, Tan Houlan, Wang Dabin, Liu Xichang, Xu Jia, Miao Yongkun, Wang Laomin, Zhang Guifu, Li Dongmin, etc.

Foreign guests attending the reception included:

Ambassadors, military attachés, and diplomatic officials from various countries;

Members of the Political Bureau of the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Carlo Merlini, members of the New Zealand Communist Party National Committee Hugh McLeod, members of the Central Committee of the Brazilian Communist Party Benedito de Galvalo, representatives of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association Secretary-General Chardho, representatives of the Patriotic Front of Thailand Paron Chularat, representatives of the Malayan National Liberation League in China Shama, representatives of the Italy-China Friendship Association delegation leader Enrico Lambardi, Secretary-General of the Japanese Gear Seat Theater Jinmaru Chiyuki, representatives of the Union of African Students in France Ovide Laugo, etc.

Before the reception ended, everyone stood up and sang "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" in unison. (Accompanied by a photograph)

### 国防部举行盛大招待会隆重庆祝解放军建军四十周年

1967-08-01
第10版()
专栏：

    国防部举行盛大招待会隆重庆祝解放军建军四十周年
    中共中央国务院中央军委中央文革小组全军文革小组和解放军的负责同志陪同外宾出席。杨成武同志在会上讲话  周恩来陈伯达康生江青等同志和解放军指战员、革命群众、革命小将一起举杯敬祝毛主席万寿无疆
    新华社三十一日讯　国防部今晚在人民大会堂举行盛大招待会，隆重庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十周年。
    中共中央，国务院，中央军委，中央文革小组，全军文革小组，中国人民解放军各总部、陆、海、空三军的负责同志陪同在北京的各国无产阶级革命战士和反帝战士出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的有：
    中共中央政治局常委周恩来、陈伯达、康生、朱德、李富春；
    中央文化革命小组第一副组长江青，副组长张春桥，成员王力、关锋、戚本禹、姚文元；
    中共中央政治局委员、候补委员、书记处书记：陈毅、刘伯承、李先念、谭震林、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、李雪峰、宋任穷、谢富治、刘宁一；
    中共中央军委常委、军委文革小组、国防部、中国人民解放军各总部、海、空军主要负责同志萧华、杨成武、粟裕、叶群、萧劲光、王树声、李作鹏、吴法宪、余立金、邱会作、张池明；
    人大常委会副委员长和国防委员会副主席郭沫若、程潜、张治中、周建人、傅作义、蔡廷锴。
    应邀出席招待会并在主宾席上就座的有：越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国政府副总理、政府经济代表团团长黎清毅，阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使纳塔奈利和夫人，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长黎松山，缅甸共产党中央第一副主席、缅共中央代表团团长德钦巴登顶，亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克和夫人，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长西园寺公一。
    今晚，人民大会堂宴会厅内充满革命战斗的气氛。主席台正中高挂着我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席的巨幅画像。招待会进行过程中，人民解放军指战员、各界革命群众和红卫兵革命小将，一批又一批地走到周恩来、陈伯达、康生、江青等同志面前，举杯敬祝我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席万寿无疆！这时，宴会大厅内不断响起毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！毛主席万岁！毛主席万万岁的欢呼声。
    中国人民解放军代总参谋长杨成武同志在招待会上讲话。他说，中国共产党在四十年前的今天在南昌领导的武装起义，是在国民党完全叛变革命之后，在以毛主席为代表的正确路线战胜陈独秀右倾机会主义路线的条件下，在毛泽东思想的引导下，对国民党反动派打响的第一枪。毛主席在湖南农民运动的基础上，亲自发动和领导了具有伟大历史意义的秋收起义和向井冈山的伟大进军，建立了第一支工农红军，创建了第一个革命根据地，为中国革命和世界革命开创了一条崭新的道路，即用农村包围城市，最后夺取城市，夺取全国政权的道路。这是一条胜利的道路。这是无产阶级革命史上的伟大创举。
    杨成武同志指出，中国人民解放军是我们伟大统帅毛主席亲自缔造和培育起来的一支新型的人民军队，是用伟大的、战无不胜的毛泽东思想武装起来的英雄的军队。这支军队，在同国内外阶级敌人长期艰苦的搏斗中，在两条路线的斗争中，始终坚定地站在毛主席的无产阶级革命路线一边，是永远忠于毛主席革命路线的真正的无产阶级革命军队。
    他强调说，毛主席是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者，是当代最伟大的天才，最杰出的统帅。毛泽东思想是马克思主义发展的一个崭新的阶段，是我们一切工作的指导方针。毛主席的军事思想，是马克思主义发展史上最完整、最科学、最伟大的革命军事理论，是当代马克思列宁主义军事学说的最高峰。我军的每一个胜利，都是毛主席的革命路线的伟大胜利，是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。正如林彪同志所说的：“按照毛泽东同志建军思想建立起来的人民军队，是无比坚强的，是战无不胜的。”
    杨成武同志说，我们的副统帅林彪同志，把毛泽东思想伟大红旗举得最高，对毛主席著作学得最好，用得最好。几十年来，林彪同志一贯最忠实、最坚决、最彻底地贯彻执行毛主席的无产阶级革命路线。林彪同志创造性地运用毛泽东思想，领导我军活学活用毛主席著作，突出无产阶级政治，把我军办成毛泽东思想的大学校，对我军革命化、现代化的建设作出了卓越的贡献。有伟大统帅毛主席的英明领导和林彪同志的直接指挥，这是我们最大的幸福！
