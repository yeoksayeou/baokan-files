Title: The World's People Praise Chairman Mao's Army
Author:
Date: 1967-08-01
Edition: 11

The Chinese People's Liberation Army is the great teacher, great leader, great commander, and great helmsman, Chairman Mao, who personally created and led it, and Vice Chairman Lin Biao, who directly commands it, a people's army armed with Mao Zedong Thought. This army serves wholeheartedly for all the people of China and all the people of the world. It not only enjoys the enthusiastic support of the Chinese people but also receives the enthusiastic praise of revolutionary comrades and friends from the five continents. Below are excerpts from the thoughts, messages, letters, and articles of friends from various countries after visiting and touring a unit of the Beijing Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

A Completely New-Type People's Army

The Chinese People's Liberation Army is a great army armed with Mao Zedong Thought, an army from the people, an army that wholeheartedly serves the people, the newest and strongest proletarian revolutionary army in the world, and an army with a high sense of internationalism that is determined to carry the cause of socialism and communism through to the end.

		—Hito Chako, Political Department Director of the Albanian People's Army

The Chinese People's Liberation Army is a great army nurtured under Mao Zedong Thought, a completely and thoroughly united army with the masses of the people, and an army that serves the people.

		—Morita Akio, Japan

The Chinese People's Liberation Army is not only an army armed with modern weapons but also an army armed with Mao Zedong Thought. Therefore, it has become a powerful force, the force of the people of the world.

		—Ali Muhammad Zafar, Pakistan

You are a true people's army. I have never seen such a good army. You are truly the People's Liberation Army armed with Mao Zedong Thought.

		—Teodoro Losin, Philippines

The soldiers of the Liberation Army not only serve the Chinese people but also serve the people of the world. Your selfless spirit is very precious. The Chinese army is the most revolutionary army in the world.

		—Rashidi, Deputy Minister of Finance, Tanzania

You are true heroes, advanced elements who study Chairman Mao's works. You have not only made contributions to the liberation of the Chinese people but also to the liberation of the oppressed people of the world.

		—Michel Bindi, Head of the Security Department, Congo (Brazzaville)

A Great School of Mao Zedong Thought

Chairman Mao's thought of serving the people in building the army is very thorough. The Chinese People's Liberation Army, as a great school of Mao Zedong Thought, continuously carries out ideological revolutions, which is a remarkable thing. Only the Chinese People's Liberation Army is such an army in the world.

		—Nakajima Kazuo, Japan

The Chinese People's Liberation Army is a great school of Mao Zedong Thought. Every soldier strives hard to be a fighter, a producer, and a political worker, which is something you absolutely cannot see in other countries.

		—Masaki Masaki, Japan

The Chinese People's Liberation Army is a great school. It studies politics, military affairs, industry, and agriculture, and these are our models to learn from.

		—A comrade from the Burmese Communist Party

The Loyal Defenders of the Cultural Revolution

In the great, internationally significant proletarian cultural revolution personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao, the PLA not only supported the proletarian revolutionaries militarily but also politically and ideologically, contributing to the realization of the communist ideal.

		—Takano Hiroshi, Japan

The Chinese People's Liberation Army is closely united with the people, supporting the proletarian cultural revolution, and helping the people seize power where it is needed. This is also a great contribution to the revolutions of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

		—Rodriguez, Ecuador

The Chinese People's Liberation Army adheres to Chairman Mao's revolutionary line, actively participates in the proletarian cultural revolution, supports the revolutionary left, seizes power from the capitalist-roaders within the party, and thoroughly uproots the roots of revisionism, making a significant contribution to the world revolution.

		—A comrade from Spain

The Chinese People's Liberation Army is currently helping all departments across the country to carry the proletarian cultural revolution through to the end. We believe that the proletarian cultural revolution, with the firm support of the Chinese People's Liberation Army armed with Mao Zedong Thought, will definitely achieve thorough victory.

		—Sullivan, USA

The Strong Backbone of the World's Revolutionary People

With a neighbor like China and the Chinese People's Liberation Army as our backbone, we have a hundredfold confidence in defeating the American aggressors.

		—Nguyen Khang, Vietnam

The strong Chinese People's Liberation Army is the firm backbone of the people of South Vietnam in their anti-American national salvation war. The Vietnamese people's victory in the anti-American national salvation war is inseparable from the selfless aid of the Chinese people.

		—Phan Hong Chi, South Vietnam

I dare to guarantee: if imperialism dares to launch a war of aggression against China, it will definitely be annihilated.

		—Senanayake, Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Writers' Association

The Chinese People's Liberation Army, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, will fearlessly face any enemy, and no force can stop it from achieving victory. Its very existence and strength make American imperialism tremble with fear.

		—Ahmad Muhammad Ahmad, Eritrea

The Chinese People's Liberation Army, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, is the pillar of the dictatorship of the proletariat. It ensures that the base of the world people's revolution—the People's Republic of China—stands firmly, and it is the hope of the people struggling for liberation.

		—Malaya, Mabin

Under the leadership of the great revolutionary leader Chairman Mao, the great army instills in all oppressed peoples of the world the belief that the Chinese People's Liberation Army is the most reliable backbone of the world's revolutionary people.

		—Lek Salang, Cambodia
