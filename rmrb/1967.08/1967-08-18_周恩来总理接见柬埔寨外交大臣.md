Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Cambodian Minister of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1967-08-18
Edition: 3

Premier Zhou Enlai received and hosted a banquet for Cambodian Minister of Foreign Affairs Hou Yisala and other Cambodian guests this evening, engaging in friendly conversations with them.

Attending the reception and banquet were Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Hanlun, and others.

Cambodian Ambassador to China Zhanggang was also present. (Photo attached)

On August 17, Premier Zhou Enlai received Cambodian Minister of Foreign Affairs Hou Yisala (third from the left) and other Cambodian guests.

Photo by New China News Agency photographer Wu Jiguo.

### 周恩来总理接见柬埔寨外交大臣

1967-08-18
第3版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见柬埔寨外交大臣
    新华社十七日讯  周恩来总理今晚接见并宴请柬埔寨外交大臣富里萨拉和其他柬埔寨客人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    参加接见和宴会的，有国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长韩念龙等。
    柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也在座。（附图片）
    八月十七日，周恩来总理接见柬埔寨外交大臣富里萨拉（左三）和其他柬埔寨客人。
          新华社记者  武基国摄
