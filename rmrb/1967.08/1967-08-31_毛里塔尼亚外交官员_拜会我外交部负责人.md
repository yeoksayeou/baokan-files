Title: Mauritania Diplomatic Official Visits Chinese Foreign Ministry Official
Author:
Date: 1967-08-31
Edition: 6

Mauritanian diplomatic official Al-Mami Li, the second secretary who came to China to set up the Mauritania Embassy, paid a courtesy call on the responsible official of the Chinese Foreign Ministry this afternoon. The host and guest had a friendly talk.

### 毛里塔尼亚外交官员  拜会我外交部负责人

1967-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    毛里塔尼亚外交官员
    拜会我外交部负责人
    新华社三十日讯　毛里塔尼亚来华筹建驻中国大使馆的二等秘书阿尔马米·利，今天下午拜会了外交部负责人。宾主进行了友好的谈话。
