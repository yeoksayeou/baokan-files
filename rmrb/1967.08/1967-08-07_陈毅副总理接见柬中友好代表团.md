Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-08-07
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Delegation led by the second vice chairman of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association, Phean Thien, and held friendly talks with all the members of the delegation.

After the reception, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet in honor of the Cambodian friends.

Attending the reception and banquet were the vice chairman of the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association, Zhou Yiping, and others. The Cambodian Ambassador to China, Zhang Gang, was also invited to attend the reception and banquet.

### 陈毅副总理接见柬中友好代表团

1967-08-07
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见柬中友好代表团
    新华社五日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见由柬埔寨中国友好协会第二副主席披天来率领的柬中友好代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见后，陈毅副总理设宴招待柬埔寨朋友。
    参加接见和宴会的，有中国柬埔寨友好协会副会长周一萍等。柬埔寨驻我国大使张岗也应邀出席了接见和宴会。
