Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Mrs. Du Bois
Author:
Date: 1967-08-11
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received Mrs. Shirley Graham Du Bois, the wife of the renowned black leader W.E.B. Du Bois, this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with her.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairman Ding Xilin of the China-Africa Friendship Association and others.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见杜波依斯夫人

1967-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见杜波依斯夫人
    新华社九日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，今天下午接见了著名黑人领袖杜波依斯的夫人歇莉·格雷姆，同她进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中非友协副会长丁西林等。
