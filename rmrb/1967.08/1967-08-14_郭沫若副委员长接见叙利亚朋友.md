Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Syrian Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-08-14
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo received Syrian friends Salim Suleiman Sadi and his wife this morning and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Syrian friends Sadi and his wife arrived in Beijing on August 8.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见叙利亚朋友

1967-08-14
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见叙利亚朋友
    新华社十三日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，今天上午接见叙利亚朋友萨里姆·苏莱曼·萨迪和他的夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    叙利亚朋友萨迪和他的夫人是在八日到达北京的。
