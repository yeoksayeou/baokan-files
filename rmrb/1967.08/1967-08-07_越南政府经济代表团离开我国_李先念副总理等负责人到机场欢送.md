Title: Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation Leaves China
Author:
Date: 1967-08-07
Edition: 5

Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation Leaves China
Li Xiannian Vice Premier and Other Leaders See Them Off at the Airport

BEIJING, Aug. 6 (Xinhua) -- Vice Premier Le Quang Vinh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnamese government economic delegation he led concluded their friendly visit to China and left today on a special plane.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian and relevant officials Fang Yi, Peng Shaohui, Han Nianlong, Li Qiang, Ding Xilin, Zhang Xianyao, Yang Lin, and Nie Yuanzi, as well as over a thousand revolutionary masses and Red Guards from the capital, went to the airport to see them off.

When Vice Premier Le Quang Vinh and his delegation arrived at the airport, the crowd held high portraits of Chairman Mao and President Ho Chi Minh, waving red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao Tse-tung," and repeatedly shouting, "Down with U.S. imperialism!" "Down with Soviet revisionism!" "U.S. imperialism will be defeated! Vietnam will be victorious!" "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live President Ho Chi Minh!"

Vice Premier Le Quang Vinh boarded the plane, waving and bidding farewell to the crowd.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Ambassador Vu Minh Quan of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Deputy Chief of the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China Le Thong Son, and Ambassador Hyun Jun-gik of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China.

### 越南政府经济代表团离开我国  李先念副总理等负责人到机场欢送

1967-08-07
第5版()
专栏：

    越南政府经济代表团离开我国
    李先念副总理等负责人到机场欢送
    新华社六日讯　越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅和由他率领的越南政府经济代表团，结束了在中国的友好访问，今天上午乘专机离开我国。
    李先念副总理和有关方面负责人方毅、彭绍辉、韩念龙、李强、丁西林、张贤约、杨琳、聂元梓等以及首都革命群众和红卫兵一千多人到机场欢送。
    黎清毅副总理等来到机场，欢送群众高举着毛主席像和胡志明主席像，挥动红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，不断高呼：“打倒美帝！”“打倒苏修！”“美帝必败！越南必胜！”“毛主席万岁！”“胡志明主席万岁！”黎清毅副总理登上飞机，频频向欢送的同志们招手告别。
    今天到机场送行的，还有越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长黎松山。朝鲜驻中国大使玄峻极也到机场欢送。
