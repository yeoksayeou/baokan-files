Title: Vice Chairman Liu Ning Meets with Italian Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-08-11
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Liu Ning met with the full delegation of the Italian China Cultural Friendship Association, led by Chairman Enrico Lambardi, this morning. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

### 刘宁一副委员长接见意大利朋友

1967-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

    刘宁一副委员长接见意大利朋友
    新华社十日讯　刘宁一副委员长今天上午接见了由团长恩里克·拉姆巴尔蒂率领的意大利中国文化友好关系协会代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
