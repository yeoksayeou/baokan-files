Title: Indian National Congress Government's Foreign and Domestic Policies
Author:
Date: 1967-08-10
Edition: 4

The Indian National Congress government aligns itself with the United States and the Soviet Union while opposing China. Domestically, it ruthlessly suppresses democratic forces and brutally exploits the working people, leading to widespread suffering and famine. The image depicts a large number of starving Indian farmers living in dire poverty, facing an uncertain future day by day.
